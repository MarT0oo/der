When choosing your Castle it is important to note the Travel Dates and the weather in the area.  During the summer months (June – August) you’ll find less rain and overcast.  The later months are colder while the earlier can bring more rain.  The other items are getting your passports and visas.  Most of the time the trip becomes a vacation that will be remembered for a lifetime and not just a Wedding Celebration.  You can chose to take tours, have wine tastings, horseback riding, fly fishing, and make it a complete experience.  Additionally you will receive a wedding planner’s phone number, a menu selection, and a full quote on your special day.
