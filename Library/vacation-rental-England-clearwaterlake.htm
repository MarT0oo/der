Clear Water Lake
5 Bedrooms | 3 Baths | Lakefront                                                          
Cirencester, Cotswold, England
From £650 - £2000 per week
Description

Clearwater Lake is a 3 story / 5 bedroom/ 3 bathroom / 4 WC lakeside facing house (1 en suite in the master and 2 family bathrooms.
Sleeps 11 + captain's bed + travel cots in 4 doubles and 1 twin (1 of the doubles is a bunk-bedded room with standard double on the bottom and the single above)


Open plan living/ dining/kitchen/study is extremely spacious and the features a 2 story gable with a designer mood light, suspended from the 2nd floor ceiling.


Based within a private gated development with 24 hour on site security and takes advantage of a free membership of the renowned on site World class Spa with full height glass, gabled walls and only 1 minute walk from your front door. This award winning property offers 70 m2 of private and very secluded ground floor decking and grass, as well as 11 meters long first floor balcony, enclosed with glass balustrades makes the most of its location with full height south facing panoramic double glazing affording uninterrupted views from the edge of the lake, furnished to the highest standard with lots of iconic designer pieces guaranteed to wow the fussiest and even the most hardened of luxury seeker


Sited on a beautiful freshwater lake on a luxury 2nd home Development Lower Mill Estate, takes advantage of free membership of a renowned spa, 30 seconds walk from the house with its heated indoor and outdoor pools, gym, sauna, steam room and range of bookable treatments.


Clearwater Lake combines the tranquility of the beautiful Cotswold countryside with the very best of contemporary design whilst offering full width, full height, floor to ceiling panoramic glazing with amazing views and amazing vaulted ceiling in the open plan living/dining.


The property is located within a few minutes' drive of many traditional Cotswolds villages with a good range of shops and other facilities.
