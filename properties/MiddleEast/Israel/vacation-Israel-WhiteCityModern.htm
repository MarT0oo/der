Brand new luxury 2 bedrooms apartment located on a high floor of the upscale White City Tower. Halfway between Rothschild Boulevard, and Banana Beach, this place is the perfect spot for couples or young professionals looking for an overwhelming place.

Welcome to our apartment located in Neve Tzedek, Tel Aviv's most hip neighborhood. If you are attracted to shopping, beach and trendy restaurants, you have found the perfect place.

The apartment on a high floor of the White City Tower has spectacular views of the sea and the city of Tel Aviv. It includes a living / dining room with a fully equipped American kitchen, with a bed of 180x200 cm and a terrace overlooking the sea. The 2nd bedroom also has a bed of 160x200 cm.

The apartment has 2.5 Bathrooms and a separate toilet. Finally, there is a Walk-ing closet (mamad). The building has a synagogue and a Shabbat elevator. Moreover, the Holyguest team will be happy to recommend things to do, places to visit or help you with any booking.
