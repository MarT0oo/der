White City Contemporary, Tel Aviv-Yafo, Israel Vacation Rental

 

Brand new luxury 4 bed apartment located on a high floor of the upscale White City Tower, just between Rothschild Boulevard and the famous Banana Beach.

Welcome to our place in Neve Tzedek the most trendy neighbourhood in Tel Aviv. If shopping, eating and beach activities is what you are looking for, you have arrived to the right place.

The flat which is located on a high floor in the White City Residence complex has amazing views of the City and the Mediterranean sea from every room. It is comprised of a living area with a fully fitted modern kitchen, and a large terrace overlooking the sea. The master bedroom has a Queen size bed and its own bathroom. The other 2 bedrooms can be fitted with either 2 doubles beds or a 2 singles. Finally, there is second bathroom, a guest toilet and a walk-ing closet (mamad).

There is also a synagogue as well as a shabbat lift.

More than a job, hosting is a way for us to help travellers discover this amazing country and gives us the chance to meet great people on a daily basis.
