Kingdom of Fife Castle, Kingdom of Fife, Scotland


Kingdom of Fife Castle is a beautiful, privately owned, 16th century castle that guests occupy exclusively.  Kingdom of Fife Castle is not, and does not pretend to be, a hotel, we encourage our guests to treat the castle as their home for the duration of their stay. Kingdom of Fife Castle is awarded 5 Star Exclusive use by Visit Scotland.

The castle has exceptional charm and its dedicated team of staff create an atmosphere of quality and discretion. This coupled with outstanding cuisine prepared by the castle’s chef adds to the unique Kingdom of Fife Castle experience. Local produce is used wherever possible, sourcing some of the finest ingredients to tempt every palate. We are more than happy to accommodate any special dietary requirements. Our wine list is extensive, and we have a large selection of malt whiskies as well as a range of spirits and liqueurs.

Although our small team is always discretely on hand to assist, we like to afford our guests as much privacy as possible in order to maintain the ambience of a private home.  Meals are at your convenience served either in the formal Dining Room, or for a smaller group in the informal Victorian family kitchen. We serve a hearty Scottish breakfast; lunch can be supplied to suit guests’ requirements and is generally a relaxed occasion whilst dinner is normally the most formal meal of the day.

There are many activities available to people visiting the Fife area and we would be pleased to help organise activities both in and around the castle. Country pursuits are very popular and we can arrange stalking, shooting and fishing, dependent on season.

Some of the most famous golf courses in the world are within an hour’s drive of the Castle, notably St. Andrews, Carnoustie, Ladybank and Gleneagles and we would be delighted to try and arrange tee times.  As you will appreciate we cannot guarantee tee times but we will do our best to assist.

For the convenience of our guests we have a Helipad in the grounds and will be happy to arrange helicopter transfers to and from Glasgow or Edinburgh airports as well as to most of the Scottish Golf Courses. Private helicopters will, of course, be most welcome on our helipad.

We endeavor to provide our guests with a memorable and relaxing stay away from the rigors of office life. However to meet communication requirements all our rooms have direct dial telephones and broadband wireless is available in the study. A faxmachine is also situated in the study.

We can accommodate up to eighteen people in our nine, very individual, en-suite bedrooms.  The price is inclusive of accommodation, breakfast, light luncheon, dinner, and all non-alcoholic beverages and, of course, exclusive use of the Castle and grounds. Our minimum stay is normally for two nights.

Whether you are arranging a wedding, a special birthday, a corporate event or a house party for family and friends, Kingdom of Fife Castle is unrivalled as a venue.
