Mykonos Luxury
4 Bedrooms | 4 Baths  | Pool | Breathtaking Views                                           
Mykonos, Greece
  From €1500 per night

Mykonos Luxury is located on Mykonos, in Greece, in an area of 5 acres, facing the Aegean Sea (only 300m. walk distance from Ftelia Beach).  If you wish to leave the comfort of Mykonos Luxury , you can reach the cosmopolitan town or the famous beaches of Mykonos in 5-15 minutes by car.
   
Mykonos Luxury has four luxury suites built in cycladic style, each one with its own identity.  Old pieces from the owner's collection and unique works of art add style and character to the Villa:

The house of the moon consists of a bedroom with a queen size bed and a marble bathroom. There is also an open plan sitting area with a comfortable sofa, a dining table with great view, a separate room with kitchen facilities and a great shadowed veranda with breathtaking view.

The house of the sky consists of a bedroom with a queen size bed and a private marble bathroom with a bathtub, an open plan sitting room and a dining area with a kitchenette; a corner furnished with unique antique pieces and an authentic English card table.

The house of the sun consists of one separated bedroom with a queen size bed and en suite private marble bathroom with shower, as well as of an additional bedroom with one double or two single beds.  The suite has a sitting room with a comfortable sofa, kitchenette and dining area with a great view. It has also private balcony with a great sea view to enjoy your breakfast or watch the sunset in the evening.

The house of the star consists of a separated bedroom with a queen size bed and a private veranda with a great view and shadowed sitting area; a private marble bathroom with shower, fully equipped kitchen, a sitting room with a comfortable sofa and a dining area with sea view. The house of star has a spacious balcony to enjoy your breakfast and breathtaking sunsets.

At the highest point of the property, a Meditation Gazebo with breathtaking view for relaxation and alternative therapy, awaits our guests.

 

The harmonious composition of stone, old marble and wood create the unique atmosphere of the outdoor area.  The old marble pillar together with the other marble antiques dominate the pool area
