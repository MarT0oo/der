House Tea, Pučišća, Croatia

 

House Tea is situated in the centre of the picturesque village of Pučišća on the island of Brač. It is a traditional Dalmatian stone house, which was renovated in 2010.

This charming house can accommodate 3 to 5 people comfortably.

On the ground floor there is a kitchen / dining room / sitting room area, 1 bathroom and 1 WC. The upper floor has 2 bedrooms (1 double, 1 single).

To make your stay on Brač Island as pleasant as possible, the house has been equipped with all the equipment you might need and the interior and grounds have been tastefully designed.
