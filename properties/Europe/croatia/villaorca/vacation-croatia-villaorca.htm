Villa Orca, Šestanovac, Croatia Vacation Rental

 

Villa Orca can accommodate up to 12 people. In the main house there are 5 en-suite bedrooms with queen size beds (each with TV and phone). A guest cottage has an en-suite room and own kitchen. The outdoor swimming pool (heated) is asymmetrically shaped (circular waterfall, fountain, whirlpool for 6 people with a table in the middle). There is also an outdoor and indoor jacuzzi, an equipped gym (with sound system) and a Finn sauna (for 6 people). Behind the whirlpool and sauna there is a fantastic massage chair with different programs. In the winter garden there is a professional billiard table and darts board. An entertainment area with bar, sound system and poker table can be found in the attic. The terrace can accommodate up to 25 people and is also suitable for outdoor meetings. Next to the villa , beside the pool there is a small lawn made for playing soccer and a bowling field. Those that enjoy riding can practice their hobby with beautiful Lipizzaner horses nearby. The surroundings are a well known hunting area and there is also a trophy museum and a zoo located nearby.
