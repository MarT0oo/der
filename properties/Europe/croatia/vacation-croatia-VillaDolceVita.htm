Villa Dolce Vita, Vabriga, Croatia Vacation Rental

 

Treat yourself to an unforgettable holiday experience by opting for the premium Villa Dolce Vita! Rest and relax in tranquility and privacy in the villa surrounded by lovely Mediterranean garden in the midst of Istria.

Step into the elegant Villa Dolce Vita and experience full comfort with a touch of luxury on holiday! Its stylish interior furnished with designer pieces and equipped with state-of-the-art appliances is perfectly complemented by spacious outdoor area featuring a swimming pool, sunbathing zone and a gazebo with a dining table and a barbecue.

The commodious villa has 426 m2 of space which includes 5 large beautifully designed bedrooms with 4 adjoining bathrooms. It provides the perfect haven from daily stresses and complete privacy for up to 10 people.

Lie down on the elegant loungers to sunbathe by the pool and make use of the steam bath shower for pure relaxation. Share a delicious barbecue with your favourite people out in the open and visit local wineries and restaurant for a unique gastronomic experience.

At Villa Dolce Vita you have light housekeeping in the morning hours arranged for your carefree stay. Apart from that, our attentive staff is at your disposal to meet your needs and organize trips to your liking.

As its name suggests, Villa Dolce Vita invites you to fully enjoy every single moment of your unique holiday in a comfortable and luxurious setting. Its beautifully designed interior which features the artwork by renowned Croatian artists and wonderfully maintained garden provide you with the perfect spot for pure pleasure and relaxation.
