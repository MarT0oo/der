Villa Pola, Banjole, Croatia Vacation Rental

 

The modernly decorated Villa Pola in Banjole, located not far from the sea, will fulfill all your expectations. Ideal choice for anyone looking for a luxurious vacation, with six bedrooms it can accommodate up to 12 people and given the offer where nobody will miss anything.

Villa spreades over three floor, has 6 bedrooms, 5 bathrooms and much more.

The sophisticated interior of calming tones besides the beautiful living area and kitchen where you will enjoy the sea view and the unforgettable sunset offers a wellness area with a sauna for relaxing moments. If you want more activities and fun on holiday, Villa Pola is also equipped for those sports spirit and offers you a gym, table tennis, table football and billiards.

The exterior with private pool is no less enchanting, the covered part features a fully equipped outdoor kitchen with a dining table. Villa Pola in Banjole is decorated with much attention to the finest details to make your vacation perfect.

A wealth of Roman architecture lifts otherwise-workaday Pula (ancient Polensium; Pola in Italian) from the humdrum. The star of the show is the remarkably well-preserved Roman amphitheatre, smack in the heart of the city, which dominates the streetscape and doubles as a venue for summer concerts and festivals. Banjole is only 12km away from Pula and it is located in great position. The locals have always been turned towards the sea and fishing. For this reason, even today, we find here the best seafood restaurants. A well-known fact is that the best fish is prepared by fishermen themselves, but if you want to prepare seafood dishes then head to the small harbor at daybreak, wait for the fishermen to return from sea and buy the freshly caught fish. If you have found accommodation at a fisherman's home, perhaps you can join him on such an unforgettable experience at sea.
