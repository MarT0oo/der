House Sara, Selca, Croatia

 

House Sara is a stand-alone house situated on the slopes of the picturesque village of Selca on Brač, with views of the village on one side and views of the sea on the other. It is a traditional Dalmatian stone house, which was completely renovated in 2004. This charming house can accommodate 6 to 8 people comfortably. In front of the house there is a spacious terrace (70m2) with a big outside fireplace, and a big garden (500m2). On the ground floor there is a kitchen / dining room / sitting room area, 3 bedrooms 1 bathroom (with bath tub), and 1 en suite bathroom (with shower unit). 5 parking spaces are also available. To make your stay on Brač Island as pleasant as possible, the house has been equipped with all the equipment you might need and the interior and grounds have been tastefully designed.
