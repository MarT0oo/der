Villa Hermitage, Vabriga, Croatia Vacation Rental

 

Feel like royalty on your holiday at Villa Hermitage! Spend moments to remember surrounded by beauty and pure luxury in the elegant villa with a lovely private garden. Villa Hermitage captivates you with its timeless elegance and welcomes you to a wonderful stay in full comfort. The classy interior is perfectly complemented by a spacious outdoor area which includes beautiful Mediterranean greenery, a swimming pool and space to relax and socialize.

The luxurious villa can provide accommodation for up to 12 guests with its 511 m2 of space, which includes 6 large bedrooms with adjoining bathrooms, your oases of pure relaxation.

Enjoy marvellous views from the lounge area inside and admire the scenery from the upstairs balcony. Rest and relax by taking a swim in the pool, sunbathing on the stylish loungers and by delighting in delicious barbecue dishes.

Have your stay tailored to your wishes and preferences and let our friendly staff take care of all the details.

The interior of Villa Hermitage is dominated by an impressive spiral staircase and luxurious chandeliers, which create the impression of a palace. Artwork by renowned Croatian artists decorates the walls of the villa for a truly royal ambience.
