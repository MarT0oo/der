Villa Helena, Poreč, Croatia Vacation Rental

 

Make your dream holiday come true in the beautiful oasis of luxury – Villa Helena! Situated in the typical Istrian coastal town of Poreč, the villa is an ideal place for a relaxing and peaceful holiday.

Experience unique beauty and luxury and relax in complete privacy of Villa Helena! Spend your holiday in the sophisticated villa just a few metres from the sea enjoying the garden with a private swimming pool and unparalleled service away from the summer crowds.

With 300 square metres which include 5 wonderfully styled bedrooms and 4 bathrooms, the villa is an excellent choice for groups of up to ten people. Use the chance for endless enjoyment in the spacious interior with its unique thoughtfully chosen elements and the idyllic setting of the villa.

Swim a few strokes in the crystal clear Adriatic Sea or find refreshment in the luxurious outdoor shower in the garden. Relax by the swimming pool and enjoy the hot tub. Feel inspired and fulfilled while taking in the view of the blue sea on the terrace of the villa.

Find your piece of holiday heaven in Villa Helena and entrust our friendly staff with making all your wishes come true.

Villa Helena represents the ideal place for a relaxing holiday by the sea with its elegant design and luxurious furniture and accessories. With perfect location in Poreč, it also provides all you need for a one-of-a-kind holiday including a wealth of entertainment options and activities.
