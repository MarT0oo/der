Villa Lea, Vabriga, Croatia Vacation Rental

 

Welcome to your perfect oasis of peace and calm – Villa Lea! Situated in close proximity to the charming Istrian town of Poreč, the villa represents your haven for pure relaxation away from daily concerns.

Lie deeply relaxed on the stylish loungers by the pool while the sun rays caress your skin. Enjoy every single moment of your holiday in excellent company over a glass of exquisite Istrian wine and a lovely meal served outdoors.

With our dedication to provide exceptional service, your dream holiday can be fulfilled.

With the grand staircase, luxurious chandeliers and the cool marble floors, the stunning Villa Lea takes your breath away the moment you step inside, allowing you to feel like royalty in your very own holiday mansion.
