Luna Villa, Vabriga, Croatia Vacation Rental

 

Experience a perfect holiday in beautiful Istria by opting for the elegant and exclusive Luna Villa! Make your dream holiday with family and friends come true in the privacy of luxurious accommodation.

Luxurious Luna Villa exudes timeless classical style and invites you to indulge in complete comfort. Use the cosy living room and the fully equipped kitchen to spend quality time in addition to the roofed terrace by the pool surrounded by well maintained private garden.

With 233 m2 of space this spacious villa can accommodate 8 people and is, thus, an excellent choice for a large family or a group of friends. It offers you 4 sophisticated bedrooms with en-suite bathrooms for your comfortable stay.

Explore the vicinity by bike readily at your disposal at the villa and enjoy spectacular views over a glass of wine on the large terrace on the upper floor. Spend carefree time with your favourite people sharing a meal on the outdoor terrace and playing games in the pool and the garden.

Our attentive staff is committed to meet all your needs and fulfill your wishes for pure enjoyment on your dream holiday.

Luna Villa captivates you at first sight with its majestic look as well as luxury and comfort on every turn. It thrills you with carefully chosen details ranging from artwork by famous Croatian artists, attractive chandeliers to the Mediterranean touch in the bedrooms by the choice of colours, all of which contribute to your complete holiday experience.
