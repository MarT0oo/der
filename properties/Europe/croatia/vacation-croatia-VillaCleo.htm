Villa Cleo, Vabriga, Croatia Vacation Rental

 

Find your perfect piece of paradise in beautiful Istria at Villa Cleo! Escape the daily stress and find your peaceful oasis in the privacy of this modern luxurious villa for unforgettable holiday moments.

Villa Cleo impresses with its stylish interior full of natural light and wonderful details in combination with the spacious outdoor area featuring a swimming pool, sunbathing zone with elegant loungers as well as a lovely dining area with a barbecue. Share wonderful moments of joy and pleasure in the well-maintained private garden.

With its 306 m2 of space, which includes 3 stylish bedrooms with adjoining bathrooms in addition to 2 bedrooms with bathrooms in the guesthouse, the villa can accommodate 10 people.

Pamper yourself with L’Occitane cosmetics and plush Mastro Raphaël towels in the lavish bathrooms for pure pleasure. Share enjoyable moments in good company out in the open and take up activities to your liking for unique holiday experiences.

Have your trips and activities arranged by our friendly staff and communicate your wishes for a truly comfortable and memorable stay.

Villa Cleo exudes timeless beauty, absolute comfort and luxury. Enjoy all its exceptional details, from designer furniture to stylish home accessories and details, that contribute to its sophisticated appeal. This impressive villa represents your perfect heaven with the irresistible touch of luxury.
