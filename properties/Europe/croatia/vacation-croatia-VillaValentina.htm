Villa Valentina, Supetar, Croatia

 

Villa Valentina is located 1 km from the center of Supetar on Brač, in a beautiful bay on the ranch of 2,000 m2 with olive groves and vineyards, organically grown fruits and vegetables for guests, Mediterranean vegetation and plants.

It is a traditional Dalmatian stone house located by the sea near the roads along the coast and through the pine woods, olive roads, walkways and trails for recreation. It provides peace and quiet; you can even hear the waves, birds and crickets from the pine forests.

All the rooms are on the same floor. There are 2 bedrooms, 2 bathrooms, a kitchen, a sitting room, a dining room and a terrace of 50 m2. In the garden there is a Jacuzzi of 4.5 m2, a small playground, a swing and sun decks.

In Supetar and the surrounding area there are many restaurants, Dalmatian taverns as well as a ranch at a distance of 500 m.
