Abouth The Area

Primosten is one of the most picturesque small towns on the Adriatic coast with beautiful beaches. Primosten is a town in Croatia, and a part of the Sibenik-Knin County. It is situated in the south, between the cities of Sibenik and Trogir, on the Adriatic Coast. Primosten is famous for its huge and beautiful vineyards. A photo of the vineyards of PrimoÅ¡ten hung in the UN center in New York. The largest beach in Primosten is called Raduca, and its smaller part, Mala Raduca, is voted one of the 10 most beautiful beaches in Croatia. The area's vineyards are currently under consideration to become a UNESCO World heritage.

Primosten is nowadays a real tourist pearl. A crystal blue sea and numerous beaches, preserved old stone architecture, pleasant climate and open-hearted hosts will win your hearts forever. The extraordinary tourist value of Primosten has been confirmed by numerous tourist awards at home and abroad. A culinary specialty is lobster a la Primosten. In addition, you should taste the famous local wine Babic, which is derived from an indigenous variety. During the summer in Primosten numerous cultural and entertainment events.

The sports and leisure activities are varied and include tennis, table tennis, bowling, beach volleyball and sailing. The marina Kremik has 250 berths in the sea and 140 places on the land, a charter fleet as well. The another advantage of this small town Primosten is its location and the vicinity of many Dalmatian tourist centers such as Rogoznica, town of Sibenik, Trogir, town of Split and also two Croatian National Parks: Krka Waterfalls and Kornati Islands.
