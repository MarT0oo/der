Finca Parchite, Malaga, Spain Vacation Rental


A lovely country house surrounded by nature 10 minutes from Ronda, 45 from Marbella 90 km from Malaga airport.
An oasis of peace and tranquility in the hills near Ronda Located in the mountains of Ronda, this private estate of 135,000 m2, has been repeatedly called by our customers an “oasis of peace and tranquility.”


This stunning Andalusian Farmhouse, with a traditional, warm and elegant decoration can accommodate from 8 to 23 people. It consists of two houses, joined by an Andalusian patio, with four bedrooms and four full bathrooms each, plus two bedrooms with two bathrooms in the patio. In addition there is an independent housekeeper’s house for cleaning and catering services.


The house has a private pool in a garden of 15,000m2 in which native and tropical species blend with spectacular views of the Ronda Mountains.


Walking along the fire-coloured dirt paths of Finca Parchite you will find ancient olive trees and oaks. Follow the scent of the aromatic plants that cover the fields including rockrose, lavender, thyme or lemon verbena. At all times you will feel sheltered by the three natural parks surrounding Parchite, two of which have been declared Biosphere Reserves by UNESCO: Grazalema, Sierra de las Nieves and Alcornocales.


The estate and its fa Ronda, is world famous for its impressive bridge, the Tajo river and its stone bullring – the oldest in Spain and the only one of its kind. These buildings stand out among others of great interest, with some monuments preserved from the Muslim era.


This lovely city is surrounded by 3 natural parks (two of which are Unesco heritage sites): Grazalema, Sierra de Las Nieves and Alcornocales featuring many typical white villages.


Ronda is a very lively town with many tapas bars and restaurants, including gourmet restaurants and many local wineries.
