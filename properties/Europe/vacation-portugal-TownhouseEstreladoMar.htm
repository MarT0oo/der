Townhouse Estrela do Mar, Praia de Salema, Algarve, Portugal Vacation Rental

 

Set in the village of Salema, a perfectly preserved vestige of the Algarve of old, the Estrela do Mar townhouse comprises two former fishermen’s houses. Connected via an alley on ground level and a glass bridge on the upper floor, these two adjoining annexes combine old-world Algarve architecture with new-world comforts, and make an idea space for either two couples or a family of four.

Though the exterior still maintains its traditional form, so typical of this seaside village, the interiors have been refitted to deliver all the comfort and style of 21st-century living. As well as two fully-equipped kitchens and en-suite bedrooms, the property also has a shared living space and terrace with barbecue, dining area, sunbeds and beach views.

Praia de Salema beach—a sweep of sugar-fine pale golden sand, framed by cliffs and clear, calm waters—is mere seconds away.

Hall, fully equipped kitchen with freezer, oven, microwave, dishwasher, pantry/laundry with washing machine; elevator connecting the entrance level and the first floor, small staircase leading to a bedroom with large double bed, with TV and en-suite shower room.

It features a fully equipped kitchen with freezer, oven, microwave and dishwasher. Open space dining and living room, dining area with table and chairs for six people, large living room with TV, electric blinds and curtains. Wireless internet access.

Master bedroom with absolutely stunning views of the beach and Atlantic Ocean right in front, with king size bed, TV, en-suite shower room, electric curtains. Access to the rooftop terrace of House 2 is via a glass bridge.

Directly accessible from the street, House 2 is an independent annex located across from House 1.

The terrace features barbecue, table and chairs for four people, sun beds and sunshade, offering a spectacular setting overlooking the beach. Access to House 2 is from the outside, across a small alley. Wireless internet access.

Fully facing the beach (only 3 meters apart), it has outstanding views of the beach in front and allows for comfortable living.
