In the hip neighborhood of Bairro Alto, the 3-bedroom Bairro do Amor vacation apartment is guaranteed to give you a good time. Quiet and peaceful during the day, this district wakes up in the evening, with the best nightlife in Lisbon. Surrounded by ancient Portuguese culture, you can walk over to the National Museum of History (11 minutes), or visit the breathtaking churches in the area, such as the Basílica dos Mártires (6 minutes) or the ancient Igreja De São Domingos (12 minutes). The upscale shops and traditional bakeries of Chiado are 10 minutes away. Bus and metro stops are nearby.

Our local interior designer has combined antique and modern design elements to create a sunlit, sophisticated space. Large glass windows fill the lounge room with sunlight, and eclectic wall art adds character. The fully functioning kitchen and plush bedrooms have everything you need for a pleasant stay. Premium amenities include free Wi-Fi, cable TV, hotel beds and linens, luxury toiletries, and a Nespresso machine.

Perfect for groups or families, this spacious holiday apartment will make your trip to Lisbon unforgettable.
