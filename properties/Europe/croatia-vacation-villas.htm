About the Area

Croatia , also called the Kingdom of Croatia, is located in Central Europe.  For a very long time people did not know or travel to the area from countries other than those surrounding.  But with the advent of the internet as well as many viable ways that media comes into your house, these areas are wide open to new travelers from countries all over the world.  The area is rich with beaches, local foods, local shopping, and amazing architecture.  Some of the best examples of that can be found in the old Medieval Castles and Cities that dates back to the great Kings and Queens of the Centuries before.  These are remnants of a very strong and diverse culture, looking to get out and be free from its neighbors, which finally happened in 1989 after much upheaval over the years.

Now a safe and wonderful place to vacation, those same cities such as Opatija and Istria, are places of great wealth in history and in the new money flowing into the cities due to investors and tourism.  These areas have beautiful homes, overlooking the ocean, with amazing design and features not found in many places, other than the resorts around them.  The owners of these sprawling villas found peace and tranquility a big draw.  

Croatia is new and exciting, offering travelers great scenic trips, restaurants, night clubs, yachting adventures and more.  These areas are bustling with big city fever and ready to strike out on their own as the “place to be.”  Visit the city of Zagreb, Dubrovnik, Split, Kvarner, Northern Dalmatia, Central Dalmatia, Southern Dalmatia, Inland Croatia or Istria on the Coast.

We hope you enjoy Croatia!
