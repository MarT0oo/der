Villa Madama
3+2 Bedrooms with Ensuites | House & Cottage                                  
Maremma, Tuscany, Italy
  From €4722 - €9140 per week

Villa Madama is a hillside country retreat on a secluded family estate in the countryside of southern Tuscany. This property offers beautiful views  of the medieval town of Capalbio and the Tyrrhenian Sea beyond. The villa is surrounded by a typical Mediterranean style garden. The beach is only six kilometers from the villa.


The main house is to one side of a stone courtyard with built-in benches and a shaded orchard immersed in lavender bushes. Across the courtyard from the main house are two small guest houses connected by a covered patio area.


The main house is approximately 250 square metres and has three double bedrooms, each with its own full bathroom (two baths, one shower). The large, open kitchen area is fully equipped (large modern gas cooker) and has a dining/living area with a table that seats eight, as well as sofas, Cable TV and a fireplace; there is also a separate dining room, were diners can enjoy a view of the rolling hills and the distant sea, or open the French doors onto an outdoor patio area.


The kitchen area also opens onto a large, covered veranda, a lovely spot for relaxing or al fresco dining. Through the kitchen is a large living room with a fireplace and two large glass doors opening onto another patio with lovely views of the surrounding countryside. The bedrooms and respective bathrooms are all located off the living room and each has a garden view. The owner’s book and art collections add a personal and artistic feel.

 

Each of the two guesthouses has an independent entrance. The first has a large double bedroom and full bathroom with a decor matching that of the main house. The second has a double bedroom looking onto the courtyard and a bathroom (shower) with laundry area
