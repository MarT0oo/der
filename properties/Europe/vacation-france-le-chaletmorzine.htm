Tamazing uninterrupted views of the mountains across the valley.   With the town of Morzine just below, the chalet is only a short walk from the town centre and ideally located to drop you at the Prodain cable car or Super Morzine bubble and Pleney lifts, to get you straight up onto the slopes.

The chalet, which sleeps up to 17 people, has been recently refurbished with new luxury ensuite bathrooms, equipped with heated towel rails to provide you with the comfort you deserve whilst on holiday. With the latest technology in boiler systems, you’re guaranteed a hot shower or bath anytime of the day.

Comfort and relaxation is the key to your holidays. In the spacious lounge there are comfortable sofas on which you can relax after your day in the mountains; the chalet bar (an honesty bar) is stocked with cold beers, soft drinks, champagne and a selection of spirits.

There is a bookcase with an extensive selection, a selection of DVD’s, videos and satellite TV. Why not bring your own favorites to play?  There is also a boot/clothes drying room available for you to use.  The chalet is arranged on three floors with large spacious rooms. There are two south facing balconies with panoramic views across the valley of the Morzine ski area and the town and a large garden.
