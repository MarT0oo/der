Masia Pairal, Sitges, Barcelona, Spain


Masia Pairal is imbedded in the vineyards of the wine region El Penedes, just 5 minutes from the Mediterranean and the beautiful village Sitges. Even though the cosmopolitan melting pot Barcelona is just 30 minutes away, the absolute peace and calmness is one of the many unique elements one will be able to explore.

Entering the 4000 sqm private grounds of the Catalan manor house you will find the well-tended garden including the Patio, Chill out zone, barbeque area, sauna and much more. The charm of the house consists in the unification of the OLD and NEW, antique furniture coexist with high tech comforts. This place is ideal for families, friends and special occasions with the objective to enjoy nature and comfort.

The property has 10 bedrooms, all with double bed, and 7 bathrooms. These are divided between 2 floors. Some rooms have aircon, the others have fans.

Well equipped kitchen with extra storage room and connection to the outdoor barbeque area.

Living- and dining area with antique fireplace, 50inch plasma TV, Internet and connection to the outdoor chill out zone.

Masia Pairal is a place of calmness, nature & comfort, where the spacious inside- and outside areas of the house are flexible living areas with the main object to relax.
