Villa Corail, Villefranche, Cote D'azur, France Vacation Rental

 

A classic French villa enjoying a truly spectacular location above Villefranche with views over the sea and coastline between the Cap d’Antibes, Nice and Le Cap Ferrat. Everything you need is within 2km; shops, restaurants, the beach and train station in Villefranche, bus services, supermarket and even the fort and gardens of Mont Boron in Nice.

Built in a classic style and furnished with period pieces, this elegant Cote d'Azur villa has undergone some renovations in recent years, yet retains it's authentic character and appeal. Villa Corail’s layout makes the most of its privileged south-facing position, with large French doors in every room, ample terraces and lush vegetation. Tall cypress, fruit and olive trees, as well as bougainvillea, mimosa and lavender help create pleasant, shady and colourful areas in the garden.

Guests staying at Villa Corail will be able to visit beautiful seaside towns along the coast, sampling delicious seafood specialities and excellent wine in Villefranche sur Mer or Cap Ferrat. The Grimaldi Palace in Monaco is a popular destination, only 20 minutes away, and so is the charming stronghold of Eze, perched at the top of a steep rock, built in the 14th century against the Saracen pirates. The Italian border is only a few miles away so why not spend a day on the Ligurian Western Riviera, famous for its pleasant mild climate, food and wide variety of flowers?
