Croc Blanc, Courchevel 1850, France Vacation Rental

 

Chalet Croc Blanc is a traditional wooden property with three hundred and fifty square metres of living space and a unique location very close to the Cospillot ski slope and the resort centre. There are five bedrooms and a lift serves all floors of the house.

Croc Blanc has a fantastic living space with three big leather sofas and a stone fireplace. There’s a plasma television screen framed like a picture above the fire and unique furnishings and art dotted about the place. It’s a quirky property that has impeccable design qualities and the real essence of a traditional ski chalet. The dining area is open plan with a large glass table that sits next to full length sliding windows with balcony access and superb mountain views. The kitchen is also open plan and is equipped with everything you need for a great holiday.

Occupying the top level of the property is the very large master bedroom with en-suite bath and shower room, balcony access, sofa, TV and of course the best views in the house. There are three further standard en-suite doubles that are all furnished beautifully and there’s one family bedroom which is an en-suite double with interconnecting bunk room that sleeps two children.

On the basement level of the property there is a bunk bedroom.

Chalet Croc Blanc has an outdoor Jacuzzi, a hammam and a ski room.
