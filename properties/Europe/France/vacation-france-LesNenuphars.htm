Les Nenuphars, Var, La Croix Valmer, St Tropez, France Vacation Rental

 

Contemporary vacation villa with pool – This superbly appointed villa is set in a peaceful and secluded area of the countryside between Gassin and La Croix Valmer, a very short distance from the coast. Afine example of modern, streamlined architecture, Les Nénuphars is furnished in stylish cream and white colours with beautiful wooden floors and large windows for maximum exposure to the light and views over the surrounding hills. All bedrooms have sliding doors leading to large decked terraces on both floors.

The mature Mediterranean garden provides plenty of space for relaxation to suit every member of your party. The children will have a great time playing on the well maintained lawn or swimming in the large solar-heated infinity pool whilst adults will particularly enjoy soaking up the sun from the Balinese beds, relaxing in the Jacuzzi tub or playing boules.

During your vacation in Southern France you will have the chance to visit some picturesque villages nearby, such as Gassin or La Croix Valmer. The fine sandy beaches ofGigaro and Pampelonne are just a ten-minute drive from your villa and offer opportunities for sports and other leisure activities. Further afield, but still within 10 miles, the spectacular coastline of the French Riviera opens up in front of you offering an unforgettable panorama. Enjoy the views across the Gulf of St Tropez as you watch the yachts and the glitterati go by.
