Chamonix Chalet, Chamonix, France Vacation Rental

 

Chamonix Chalet is beautifully located in the Les Bossons area of Chamonix, in a stunning private mountain setting that provides unrivalled tranquillity!

Sleeping up to ten guests in five stunning en-suite bedrooms, each one inviting you to relax and unwind, with lavish bed throws, neutral fabrics, fluffy bathrobes, towels and gorgeous L’Occitane products.

Our flagship chalet boasts a vast living area furnished with beautifully soft chocolate leather sofas and trendy zebra armchairs. Original mountain paintings and alpine antiques all ooze charm and help set a relaxing atmosphere. The moment you arrive it will become clear why Chamonix Chalet really is the most peaceful chalet in the Mont Blanc Valley, next to a mountain stream.

In the summer months enjoy relaxing on a sun lounger on the large outdoor terrace, overlooking the beautiful garden, with breath-taking views of the Aiguille du Midi and Brevent. Setting this chalet apart during the summer months is the outdoor heated split level swimming pool, its unique surroundings offering the opportunity to swim while gazing up at the Mont Blanc Massif!

During the winter months Chamonix Chalet is the perfect luxury ski chalet, providing everything you need after a hard day on the slopes. Take advantage of the fully equipped luxury spa with hammam steam room, sauna, sunken indoor Jacuzzi, treatment room and outdoor hot tub. Or relax by the open log fireplace with a glass of wine while the kids enjoy a movie in the adjacent cinema room!
