Saint Germain Musique, 6th district, Paris, France Vacation Rental

 

Feed your soul in this high-brow haven in the heart of Paris' intellectual Saint Germain. With its original marble fireplaces, polished parquet floors, bookshelves groaning with literature, all it needs is someone to tinkle the ivories and fill the air with Ravel, Debussy or Fauré. A generous hallway leads to a large open-plan living-dining space filled with light and defined by a superb mirrored over-mantle above the original fireplace. Three large bedrooms offer masses of space especially suitable for children. The two bathrooms offer stylish contemporary tub and shower while a fitted kitchen has everything you need for a quiet night in. This apartment is a real home – and offers you the chance to live in someone's shoes for your stay - as a seasoned resident, while its personality is filled with style and comfort.

aint-Germain on Paris' left bank will forever be linked to its intelligentsia, especially the café debates of Sartre and de Beauvoir who called it home and made it chic. Although there is plenty of brain power still in the 6th arrondissement – the Sorbonne and the elite Ecole des Mines are both here – today you are more likely to see gallerists and fashion boutique owners than writers and philosophers drinking coffee on its famous pavements. This apartment is nestled between two metro stations - Rennes and Saint Placide - that connect you to anywhere in the capital, but there is a wealth to see right on your doorstep.The quartier retains a unique charm that for many is quintessential Paris: the ancient churches of Saint Sulpice and Saint Germain des Près; the many leafy squares and parks; the restaurant and café culture that define the City of Light.
