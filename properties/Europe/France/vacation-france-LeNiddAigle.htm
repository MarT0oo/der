Le Nid d'Aigle, Vence, Cote D'azur, France Vacation Rental

 

Luxury villa near Vence - Le Nid d' Aigle is an imposing, stone-built Vence villa rental, perched 360 metres above sea level, with sweeping 180 degree views across the surrounding countryside to the Bay of Cannes and the Italian coast. This is a stunning villa in an equally stunning setting, just 1 mile from the centre of Vence.

This Vence villa has been beautifully restored and elegantly furnished. With a private tennis court and boules pitch there is plenty of scope for activity, whilst large terraces with shady pergolas are ideally positioned to take in the vista over a relaxing glass of wine or a leisurely meal.

The old walled town of Vence is full of interesting art galleries, craft shops and cafes surrounding a classic French square and interesting church and the Chateau du Domaine St Martin restaurant boasts 5 Michelin stars. Nearby St Paul de Vence has small cobbled streets with tempting artisan shops and is home to renowned restaurant La Colombe d' Or. The beaches at Villeneuve Loubet and Cagnes-sur-Mer are a short distance from this Vence villa rental, and there are lots of activities for children along the coast between Antibes and Biot, both places that the adults will enjoy visiting too.
