Temple IV, Le Marais, Paris, France Vacation Rental

 

This stunning apartment is found in the historic Parisian district of Le Marais. It is located in the Rue du Temple, only a few minutes away from the George Pompidou Center and the Picasso MuseumIts interiors are designed with a contemporary flair in shades of grey and white, interspersed with refreshing accents of blue and yellow.

The welcoming curves and lines of the furniture charmingly offset décor elements such as the vibrant kilim in the sitting room and the geometric shapes among the bed linen in the sleeping areas.

From the eye-catching art on the walls to the mini gym area in the master bedroom; it is evident that each space of the apartment has been tastefully and skilfully designed. Found on Rue Vieille du Temple, the apartment is located in the centre of the Le Marais historical region.

Guests can avail themselves of an excellent selection of architectural marvels and venues for entertainment located near the apartment. For instance, soaring structures such as the centuries old Hôtel de Saint-Aignan can be experienced and enjoyed. Additionally, guests can savour delicious local cuisine at one of the many restaurants nearby or stop by a popular local pub to experience the authentic local atmosphere.

Travelling from the apartment is extremely convenient as the area is a central location in the transport network.
