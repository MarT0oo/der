Woodland, Ahetze, Biarritz, France Vacation Rental

 

If you dream of a luxury, eco friendly and sustainable retreat in the countryside and crave a connection with nature, this remarkable 5 bed newly renovated, Basque country villa with heated swimming pool will not only live up to, but also exceed all of your expectations.

Enjoy a special sense of remoteness and seclusion hidden away down a picturesque country lane, with just a handful of like-minded, out of sight neighbours each enjoying all the peace, quiet and tranquillity that this special location has to offer.

As remote as you dream to make it, it is worth remembering that this villa is located only a short 7 minute drive away from the nearest beach in Bidart and just a 12 minute drive from cosmopolitan city life in fashionable and chic Biarritz.

The villa itself boasts far-reaching, uninterrupted views over fields, woodlands, rolling hills and the Pyrenees Mountains spanning the horizon. These views can be enjoyed from any one of the many terraces, which bring the inside, outside and vice versa, perfect to enjoy an evening’s aperitif and al fresco style dining.

The split-level living arrangement throughout makes great use of the large, open-plan spaces and bright and airy rooms, all air-conditioned with luxurious, yet sustainable, hard tropical wood floors used both inside and out. The living/dining room area is vast, flowing effortlessly through to the large, ultra modern and high-end kitchen divided by a tastefully comforting fireplace.

On the ground floor you will find 2 bedrooms; one twin room with its own en-suite shower and wc and a further double room with a king-sized double bed, it’s own luxury bath, shower and wc. On the 1st floor, you will find 2 further bedrooms, mirror images of those found on the ground floor. The last bedroom is located above the garage with its own separate access. This is more akin to a luxury studio apartment enjoying the quiet and privacy from the rest of the house. Each of the bedrooms comes with it’s own flat screen TV.

This villa also has its own cinema style home entertainment room, perfect for the kids, a 2-4-person sauna, perfect for the adults and a laundry room all located on the lower ground floor. There is sufficient parking for up to 5 cars in the driveway, both private and secure with electric gates. There is also a secure garage. As well as the heated swimming pool, there is also a Petanque court in the large garden, a perfect game for a hot summer evening.

All Keyweek guests have special access to the spa, gymnasium and swimming pool facilities at half price in an exclusive beachfront Hotel in Biarritz.
