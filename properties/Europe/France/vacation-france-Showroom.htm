Showroom, Biarritz, France Vacation Rental

 

With its many pieces of local art and the closeness to the Grand Plage, Showroom grants you the complete Biarritz experience. Through the large living room windows on the first elevated floor of the building you can admire breath-taking sunsets, only a three-lined esplanade separates you from the ocean.

Part of an exclusive hotel, Showroom was originally built over 100 years ago. In fact, its spacious living room—which stretches to a remarkable 100 square meters—used to be the hotel’s dining room. Today, this exceptional holiday residence still adumbrates the glory of its glorious past. While most of Showroom furnishing is state-of-the-art—including original artwork from Biarritz’s most famous artist—details like the embellished, high ceilings and the typically Basque face of the building give you an idea of the days past.

Showroom provides 3 bedrooms with comfortable queen-size beds and a lot of roomy closets. An in-suite toilet and a flat screen TV add to the comfort of the master bedroom. In the rear, quiet part of the apartment, you will find the two other bedrooms; abutted by an entertaining area with a couch, a TV and video game console. Both guest rooms give on onto a narrow lane that runs along the back of the residence. In the hall between the large living space and the rear part, there is another toilet and two bathrooms with Italian showers.

To make sure your vacation exceeds all your expectations, a concierge is available only a phone call away. An entirely equipped, modern kitchen, a breakfast bar, electric shutters for the lofty front windows, a small balcony that overlooks the Grand Plage and the Hôtel du Palais and a garage are only some of the amenities that Showroom has to offer.

As our valued guests - for half of the official price - you can exclusively access the spa, the gym and the heated pool of the Hotel Sofitel One of the things we like to mention is that - like all downtown properties - the neighbourhood of Showroom can be very lively at night. However, this top-class location more than makes up for it. Separated from Biarritz´s Grande Plage by only a few meters, the historic Hôtel du Palais and the Casino just a few steps away and in the midst of various shops, restaurants, bars, this unique apartment rental is a long-time favourite of many of our guests.
