Villefranche Flat, Villefranche-sur-Mer, Côte d'Azur, France Vacation Rental

 

This property is one of the largest in the Old Town, Côte d'Azur region on the French Riviera. It provides sea views of the harbor and beautiful bay of the Mediterranean.

Everything in the apartment is new, with a very high standard of furniture, bedding, curtains, fabrics, flooring etc. The 3 bathrooms are clad in Pink Portugese Marble and the main bathroom has a high back bath at the window overlooking the port! All bathrooms have walk-in showers. Floors are Oak and the furniture is French Provencale style. The kitchen is Cream Provencale with a tiled floor.

This flat is on the second floor from the port side however, the entrance is on ground level from Rue Poilieu, meaning that there are no steps to climb. This is an unbeatable location to enjoy the views of the port de VIllefranche as it has 5 windows to the front (+ 6 at the back). There are 2 balconies to the front. One is fitted with table and chairs. All ceilings are high as it was constructed from 3 old apartments made into one! The basement has a separate entrance for bicycles etc if required.

Occupied since Roman times, the main attraction in Villefranche-sur-Mer is the old town. The beautiful ancient houses line the steep cobbled streets that wind through the town. The streets run along the hill (rather than up and down) and pass through arches UNDER the houses, creating a great atmosphere; The best known is called Rue Obscura. A car is not necessary in Villefranche however, there is a public car park 120 yards away (€ 8 / day) and a free car park on the old Citadel (300 yds away).

Fishing can be enjoyed nearby. Additional points of interest include the Chapel Saint Pierre and Mont Alban Fort. The nearest airport is Nice Côte d'Azur Airport, 6 miles from the apartment.
