Parisian Light, Paris, France Vacation Rental

 

This spacious, stately, and elegant four-bedroom apartment offers the true Parisian insider experience. With its expansive living areas, high ceilings, and streamlined décor, the 275m2 flat epitomizes comfort and charm in the City of Light. Perfect for families or groups wanting to share accommodation without sacrificing on space, everyone can find a piece of home in this Paris vacation rental.

Situated on the right bank of the Seine river, the exclusive 16th arrondissement proposes a Parisian way of life tucked away from the hustle and bustle of tourists, yet within easy reach of all of the city’s highlights. Fans of tennis will know that Paris offers quite the competitive scene, and a stay at our Parisian Light property will put you within easy access of the famous Roland Garros Stadium, home of the French Open. Admirers of Impressionist art need look no further than the Musée Marmottan Monet, housing the largest collection of Monet paintings in the world and located under a 10 minute walk from the flat via the charming Jardin du Ranelagh.

When you open the imposing double doors to your Paris vacation rental, you are welcomed by a gracious hallway leading to the formal dining area (with seating for 8), kitchen, and living room. These form an impressive living space divided by tasteful columns and an ornate wall with the same elegant wainscoting and crown molding encountered throughout the property. These beautiful living areas are ideal for sharing and preparing meals, relaxing by the marble fireplace, or even playing foosball! Indeed, this property is truly meant to be experienced and enjoyed, as attested by the large, plush sofas, flat screen TV, ample dining space, and ease of the layout. The modern kitchen is a delight for chefs, with its oversized island, two refrigerators, double sinks, gas burners, generous storage, and collection of cookbooks.

Master bedroom: Like much of the apartment, the master combines modern comfort with handpicked traditional design elements, such as parquet floors, marble fireplace, and occasional piece of antique furniture. You may feel at home, but the apartment will not let you forget that you are in Paris! The master features a California king size bed, large windows, and an ensuite bathroom.

The ensuite bathroom, which can also be shared by other guests through a second hallway entrance, is unique in its modern design; it boasts a double natural stone sinks, a large walk-in stained concrete shower, an oversized walk-in closet, a separate toilet, and ample natural light streaming through the floor-to-ceiling window.

Second bedroom: Across the hall from the master, this stylish yet understated private retreat offers two twin beds and its own ensuite bath with shower, sink, and toilet.

Third bedroom: With two large windows, built-in bookshelves, and a desk, this lovely bedroom is ideal for a quiet evening reading or relaxing. It features a queen bed, and like all the bedrooms offers quality linens for a restful night.

Fourth bedroom: Perfect for older children or adults in touch with their inner child, this charming room has a lofted double bed accessible by a ladder, a white marble decorative fireplace, plenty of storage, and a lovely color scheme and decor.

The third bathroom, ideally situated between the third and fourth bedrooms, offers a large and sleek tiled walk-in shower, complete with rain showerhead. It includes a large sink, toilet, and much storage for toiletries.

Perfect for vacation-goers and business travelers alike, your Paris holiday apartment rental also offers a large office space, convenient and elegant with its antique desk and Medieval artwork.

The laundry room, complete with Miele brand washer and dryer, is set apart in its own room off the main hallway.

The property’s large windows add to its stately appearance, but also give onto lovely balconies that overlook a private street accessible only by a secure gate, insuring privacy and tranquility. The Parisian Light is a true haven and home; a place of beauty, comfort, and convenience. Indeed, the property even includes a private parking space, a rarity in Paris, perfect for visitors interested in day trips outside of the city. This apartment provides the best of all worlds; you can stroll down the streets to shop for croissants, hop on the metro for a quick ride to the Eiffel Tower, or take a ride to Versailles in the comfort of your own vehicle, making this ideal vacation rental.
