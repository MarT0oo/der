Villa Frankie, Languedoc France Vacation Rental

 

In an idyllic beachfront position, Villa Frankie in Languedoc, southern France, sleeps up to eight guests in four en-suite bedrooms. There is a nanny en-suite double bedroom in the basement, which many of our guests favour as a perfect fifth bedroom for the teenagers! Villa Freya is right next door for larger groups of up to 20. Surrounded by perfectly manicured gardens, the villa’s terraces and outdoor infinity pool make for the perfect spot to unwind. With comfortable loungers to soak up the sun and a Webber BBQ to prepare a delicious lunch or dinner ‘alfresco’ on the terrace.

The magnificent entrance will be sure to create the ‘wow’ factor as you step into the incredibly light and spacious villa. Continue into the open plan living areas, perfect for entertaining and unwinding and equipped with plasma television, DVD library and sound system in the lounge. The modern open plan kitchen area is fully equipped with utensils and all the latest appliances. Use of the villa’s speedboat, kayaks, stand up paddles and mountain bikes for all ages ensure there’s plenty to do for those looking for a more “active” break. If keeping in touch is important whilst you are away, the villa is equipped with Wi-Fi access throughout

All bedrooms are suitable for double or twin occupancy and are equipped with plasma televisions, DVD iPod players and docking stations. The luxurious en-suite bathrooms are brimming with L’Occitane products and fluffy bathrobes and towels for your comfort. Work out in the villa’s air-conditioned leisure area complete with modern gymnasium and sauna or watch your favourite movie in the cinema room

The villa comes with a villa manager to ensure everything goes smoothly and to oversee all aspects of your stay while in resort as well as daily housekeeping. A complementary welcome pack awaits you and includes a bottle of Bollinger Champagne, a bottle of Saint-Emilion Grand Cru, a selection of regional cheeses and charcuterie as well as some basic essentials. A fresh bouquet of flowers will also have been arranged in the villa for your arrival. There is a fresh delivery of breads and pastries each morning, and a mid-week change of towels and linens
