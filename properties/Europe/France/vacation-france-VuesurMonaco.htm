Vue sur Monaco, Monte Carlo, Cote D'azur, France Vacation Rental

 

Villa rental with pool and stunning views over Monte Carlo – Superbly positioned on a hillside overlooking Monte Carlo and its bay, this villa enjoys a prime location from which to view the Grand Prix during the month of May from its large south-facing terraces; the shops and restaurants of Beausoleil are just within two miles and the beach is also close at hand.

With the accommodation all comfortably arranged on one floor, this villa provides extensive outdoor space perfectly equipped for every moment of your holiday. Whether dining al fresco under the pergola as you watch the sun set over the sea or just relaxing by the pool with a refreshing cocktail, you will soak up this unique panorama with its distinctive skyline and the Mediterranean as a backdrop. Formula 1 enthusiasts will also appreciate the view over the racing track winding through Monte Carlo’s lanes.

Everything you need is just within two miles of this villa. Countless designer boutiques, exclusive restaurants and endless entertainment is available in the small, yet prestigious Principality of Monaco. You can mingle with the jet-set as you spend an evening at the famous Casino, visit the splendid exotic garden and the Océanographique Museum or the old town of Monaco with its beautiful Palais du Prince, residence of the Grimaldi family. This stretch of the Cote d’Azur is exciting and easy to explore, either by car, by boat or by train. In fact, the elegant town of Menton is only six miles away from Vue sur Monaco along the coastal railway line. Venture along its winding lanes to discover its beautiful turn-of-the-century Italianate townhouses, or stroll along its lovely marina to find a pavement cafe where you can enjoy a coffee and a croissant whilst watching the world go by. If you proceed eastward for a few more miles you will soon cross France/Italy’s border for a taste of Italy’s gastronomic delicacies and relaxing atmosphere. Make sure you do not miss the Friday morning market in Ventimiglia for good quality bargains!
