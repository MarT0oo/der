Dalmore Chalet, Chamonix, France Vacation Rental

 

Chalet Dalmore is a modern variation of the traditional alpine chalet. Large panoramic windows surround the vast contemporary living spaces. While at the heart of the unique architecture, an open roof terrace offers unparralled views of Mont Blanc.

The five beautifully presented en-suite bedrooms boast floor to ceiling picture windows opening to individual balconies, each with spectacular mountain views. Every bedroom is equipped with a plasma smart TV and a wide selection of international channels. Each en-suite bathroom is supplied with luxurious towels, robes and slippers, plus gorgeous L’Occitane bath products.

The chalet also boasts a cinema room and a 'TV snug', sauna, relaxation and massage area. There is Wi-Fi throughout and Sonos sound system in the living area. At the exterior of the chalet enjoy exclusive use of the hot tub and outdoor heated swimming pool, operational in the summer months.

Located on the outskirts of the Chamonix golf course Chalet Dalmore invites all day sun. Nestled at the bottom of the sunny South facing mountain side (just a stone's throw away from the Flegere cable car), sunshine, mountain views and proximity to activities are at their peak. Chalet Dalmore is idealy located for your winter ski or summer vacation in Chamonix!

The perfect base for outdoor enthusiasts who want to get the most out of their alpine vacation. A triumphant return to Chalet Dalmore awaits with amazing facilities to soothe you, after an action packed Chamonix experience in any season.
