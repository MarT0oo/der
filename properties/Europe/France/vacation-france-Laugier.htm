Laugier, 17th district, Paris, France Vacation Rental

 

An effortless blend of color and glamour, this contemporary duplex offers a living space ideal for your longer stays in Paris. On the 3rd floor of a modern building (with elevator) on rue Laugier, near Place de Pereire in the 17th district, pure bliss awaits you behind the Laugier's front doors!

Extending across two floors at over 1000 sq ft, the Laugier will delight lovers of architecture, design, and open concept arrangement. Unwind in the grand living room, with its high ceilings and playful décor in tones of yellow. The large flat screen TV and Wi-Fi are also provided for your enjoyment. Nearby you will find the semi-separate kitchen that opens onto the dining table; every appliance you could need is provided for you to prepare meals at home. Constructed over two levels, take the stairs and discover two bedrooms, each with its own unique personality. One is smart and understated, and the other is spirited and colorful. A beautiful bathroom with bathtub, double sink and toilet complete this perfect duplex.

The 17th district gets its vintage allure from the place Pereire. Stroll down this neighborhood's cobblestone streets -- with its intimate cafés and small spice shops filled with local delicacies -- and discover this neighborhood's warm and friendly atmosphere! Satisfy your cultural appetite by visiting famous nearby attractions: la place Charles de Gaulle and its arc de Triomphe, the glamorous avenue des Champs-Elysées, the lush Monceau Park, avenue des Ternes with its boutiques, or the spirited neighborhood of Montmartre and the Saint-Pierre basilica. Well served by numerous methods of public transport (métro Pereire, line 3), connect to other districts in Paris with ease.
