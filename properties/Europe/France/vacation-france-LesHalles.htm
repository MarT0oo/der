Les Halles, Biarritz, France Vacation Rental

 

In the middle of downtown Biarritz this place will charm you with its tasteful pieces of art, extraordinary wall coverings, and bright lighting from both carefully placed illumination and the sun that shines through the tall windows. From the terrace you enjoy perfect views of downtown Biarritz and the lively city market.

A short walk to the beach, "Les Halles" features a large terrace, private parking, concierge services, access to a gym, spa and heated pool with an additional fee. The apartment offers two bedrooms—one with a king size bed and the other one with a queen bed, which can be converted into two single beds. The rooms are located, one facing the market hall while the other one looks onto a quiet street. The bathroom provides a lot of storage space and is equipped with a large rain-head shower.

The glossy, sun-washed kitchen is fully outfitted with integrated appliances, plus all the culinary tools necessary to craft a delectable dinner with fresh ingredients procured at the neighbouring market.

The shiny, fair kitchen offers all culinary tools and appliances you might need to prepare delightful meals with fresh and local products from the market just outside the apartment.

The adjoining, modern living room – with a comfortable leather sofa, an ottoman and a see-through armchair – contrasts with impressive wall art and a large variety of art books.

The dining table is a heart of the apartment: four chairs and a bench provide enough room for up to 6 people, to share a nice dinner and chat well into the evening, while Biarritz´s nightlife bustles. With the city’s best restaurants and bars just a short walk away you can change scenery whenever you feel like.

If you like to be located centrally with all its liveliness, combined with unique interior design, then "Les Halles" is the THE place for you. Please contact us today to reserve your dates, as our calendar fills quickly.
