Chalet Jejalp, Portes du Soleil, France Vacation Rental

 

Chalet Jejalp is a brand new chalet based in the family resort of Morzine in the Portes du Soleil, only a short transfer from Geneva airport, making it the perfect location for families. The chalet has been beautifully designed, retaining an alpine feel whilst being both luxurious and relaxing. Built around a central atrium which includes a double height glass wine cellar spread over two floors, this chalet is filled with lots of light and space.

Sleeping ten adults and four children in total, there is a superb master suite on the top floor, three double/twin en-suite bedrooms, plus a family suite comprising of a double/twin bedroom and separate 4 bunk beds room which share bathroom facilities.

The chalet has several separate living areas; a large bright living area with open fireplace located on the top floor of the chalet. There is a fabulous cinema room located next to a stocked bar with pool table which can be either open or closed off and hidden, which will keep both adults and children happy. The open plan kitchen and dining area looks out over the expansive terrace and affords beautiful views of the mountains.

The spa area is located on the middle floor of the chalet with floor to ceiling glass windows; it comprises of a sunken Jacuzzi complete with stepping stones which provide access to the sauna. There is a further relaxation zone to enjoy the sweeping valley views.

On the ground floor you will find a large and comfortable boot room alongside a gym for the more active members of the group. As with all Consensio chalets, Jejalp comes with its' own dedicated team comprising a manager, host, chef and chauffeur along with daily housekeeping, on hand to create your own bespoke, fully catered luxury ski holiday.
