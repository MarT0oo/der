Petite Atalaye, Biarritz, France Vacation Rental

 

On top of a small hill, in the heart of Biarritz, "Petit Atalaye" can pride itself with one of the best views of our portfolio. The large windows facing the ocean will draw your attention to the blue waters, the stunning architecture of Biarritz, and the lighthouse perched on a rocky cliff.

The main living area is a pool of sun, which enters uninterruptedly through the large French doors and the big windows…, the perfect spot to read a book while relaxing in deluxe, modern furniture.

Whenever you glance up from your lecture, you will be rewarded by breath-taking views. The open doors give you the feeling to be part of the stunning nature, the intimate balcony just a few steps away. Even while catching up on your e-mails - thanks to the complimentary Internet access and the small desk facing the ocean - you don´t miss the beauty of the endless sea.

In the modern Snaidero kitchen, the gourmets amongst you will find everything they might need to prepare delicious meals with the fresh products from Biarritz´s vibrant market hall: top-of-the-line appliances, a vine cellar, cookware and much more.

The dining table is part of the kitchen and allows you to have interesting conversations with your guests while you are performing magic…

Petite Atalaye provides two bedrooms, both with an en-suite bath. The modern Bisazza bathroom amenities of the first bedroom are a real eye-catcher! The second bedroom offers two single beds, which can easily be converted into a queen.

And not to forget, Petite Atalaye has another priceless privilege to offer: a full size private garage!

Apart from all these attributes, the central location of the apartment is another big plus. A short walk from Biarritz’s immaculate beaches, the aquarium, the harbour, various restaurants, as well as bars and cafes, you are also close to the main market “Les Halles,” where you will find yourself in a gourmet paradise: fresh veggies & fruits, seafood, regional delicacies and much more….

Our intention is to ensure that you spend an unforgettable vacation. Only a phone call away, our concierge service will reply to all your questions and requests, you only have to ask! As our valued guests - for half of the official price - you can exclusively access the spa, the gym and the heated pool of the Hotel Sofitel.

As you might expect, our villas and apartments tend to be booked out very quickly, better book your dates today!
