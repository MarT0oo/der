Les Collines, Montbazillac, Dordogne, France Vacation Rental

 

Les Collines is an impressivelarge Dordogne villa with heated private pool, in a private 120 acre estate, with lovely uninterrupted views.


A stylish use of colour adds great character. Each room has been uniquely decorated, using hand made fabrics. Modern art and photography adorn the walls. A tasteful use of antiques brings the design together in this classic home, with many original features.


There is plenty to do within the estate of this large Dordogne villa. Wander through the forest, enjoy an energetic game of tennis on the all weather court, bathe in the pool, or simply relax and enjoy the views!


Nearby Bergerac, set on the Dordogne River, has a selection of restaurants and bars. The local area also boasts world famous vineyards: St Emilion is nearby. Visit Perigueux, home of foie gras, quaint villages, rolling countryside and fields of sunflowers.


This large Dordogne villa with heated private pool is warm and inviting throughout the year.
