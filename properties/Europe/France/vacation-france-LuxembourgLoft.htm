Luxembourg Loft, 6th district, Paris, France Vacation Rental

 

Superbly located in the heart of Saint Germain, this chic and luxurious apartment is ideal to experience an authentic Parisian living. Completely renovated to a high standard, this Saint Germain duplex sleeps up to 6 comfortably. There are two queen beds and a sofa that makes a double bed to accommodate you and your party.

The spacious living room is the perfect space to settle in after a long day of sightseeing and exploring the wonders of Paris. It offers comfortable seating space, coupled with a large screen TV, high-speed Internet and an impressive collection of books and movies.

The apartment it is within walking distance to all of central Paris: St. Sulpice, Notre Dame, the Latin Quarter, Ile St. Louis and the Latin Quarter. Not to mention that the Luxembourg Gardens are just moments away by foot.
