Manoir Vanssay, Loire, France Vacation Rental

 

Manoir Vanssay is one of those places that you want to return again and again. A wonderful and ideal location, situated less than two hours from Paris. This lovely Manoir is where people go to escape the hustle and bustle of the world and yet enjoy the magic of the location. Take a daytrip and experience the local wines. The hosts will be happy to escort you from the home to the wineries and show you the beautiful region.

You will enjoy the 5 main rooms in the Manoir and two additional rooms in the guest houses. The house can be rented as one or individual rooms area available at different times of the year. During your stay you will discover the vineyards where the Manoir offers 150 different types of wine, some very old. Available for purchase during your stay and you'll take in some wonderful means with wine pairings as well. The large grounds offer so many wonderful opportunities. With an almost 5 star rating, this house is a one of a kind treat.

The Manoir has been in the family for more than 612 years. Elegance and authenticity meet with comfort and modernity in this elegant estate surrounded by 16th century dry moat fortifications, with its’ own chapel, flower gardens, fountains, and pond. In the great dinning room, masterpiece of the 18th century with its’ imposing dresser, 4 course candlelit dinners accompanied by a special selection of wines, are hosted by the family three times a week. All the bedrooms and suites, each sumptuously and individually decorated with beautiful period antiques, offer splendid views out onto the XVIth century fortifications, the vast garden, the pond and valley beyond. As well as airport and city transfers and sightseeing trips with a luxury private chauffeur guide.

Grand Siecle Dinner, french gastronomy, french wines, good restaurant… Meet the other guests of the Chateau during Champagne Cocktails in the Salon Rose. Then adjourn to a four course dinner by candlelight, served in the Xviii century dining hall with embroidered linen, family silver, fine porcelain and antique crystal. The Comte and Comtesse, hosting dinner, will introduce you to their home, its history, their Loire Region and its wines. After dinner, coffee and brandy can be enjoyed in the ornate Grand Salon, with its unique furniture collection made for the Marquis marriage at the home in 17. This special event at the home takes place only twice a week. Price : 185 euros per person wine and brandy included.

Come enjoy your stay and leave the planning to us.
