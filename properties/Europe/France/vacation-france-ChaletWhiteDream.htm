Chalet White Dream, Courchevel, France Vacation Rental

 

The chalet White Dream is white paradise resort of Courchevel 1850. With a surface area of 450sqm it can accommodate 10 adults and 2 children in 6 bedrooms.

Located in the famous district of Cospillot, at 100 meters from the slopes, the chalet tastefully renovated in a cozy and elegant style blends seamlessly with the warm atmosphere of the resort. Chalet White Dream has a true name.

The White Dream Chalet is an immaculate corner of paradise nestled amongst the pine trees of the Cospillot neighbourhood, just a few metres from the slopes. Accommodating up to 12 people in 6 superb en-suite bedrooms, White Dream also includes a superb relaxation area with swimming pool, spa, hamam and bar/cinema. Including two fireplaces, its spacious living room with dining area, and lounge provide unbeatable views of the valley
