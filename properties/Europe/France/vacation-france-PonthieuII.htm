Ponthieu II, Rue de Ponthieu 52, Paris, France Vacation Rental

 

This spacious four-bedrooms vacation rental in Paris, set in the glitzy area of Champs Elysees, will put you next door to many luxury boutiques, exquisite restaurants, and a sparkling nightlife. The monumental Arc de Triomphe, the Grand Palais exhibition hall, and the Place de la Concorde square are a five-minute drive away. Stroll in style through the posh streets to reach the Champs-Elysees Gardens (12 minutes). A bus stop is on the block and a metro station is nearby (4-minute walk).

Located on the fourth floor, this holiday apartment in Paris features angled windows that cast glowing light through the rooftop. Using soft colours and contemporary lines, our local interior designers imbued the whole two-floor space with a magical atmosphere. The living room – taupe with fun splashes of bright orange – boasts modern furnishings and ample seating for nine guests. A concrete table is the centerpiece of the large kitchen, which is equipped to prepare meals for a crowd. Three of the bedrooms have double beds, and the fourth has three single beds, ideal for kids. Whimsical décor, such as a swing hanging from the bedroom ceiling and a blackboard wall, turns this family holiday apartment in Paris into the ultimate setting for a fantastic vacation!

This vacation rental offers a unique combo of high comfort, convenience, fun, and refined style that will delight any family who appreciates the diverse beauty of Parisian life!
