Liege, Haussmann Saint Lazare district, Paris Vacation Rental

 

This beautiful 4 bedroom apartment is located in the historic Haussmann Saint Lazare district, one of the most popular shopping destinations in Paris. Popular Parisian department stores like Galeries Lafayette and Printemps are just a short stroll away, with the Palais Garnier Opera House also just a stone's throw away. This Parisian vacation rental lies at the heart of the 9th Arrondissement of Paris and puts you in prime location to get out and explore the French capital.

This elegant holiday apartment in Paris boasts 4 bedrooms and can comfortably accommodate up to 10 individual guests across 3 double bedrooms and one single bedroom. Expect open plan living when you check in here, with a sprawling lounge and dining area, fully appointed kitchen and no less than three different bathrooms. Every room is finished to the highest standards, with gleaming wooden floors throughout adding to the open plan vibe of the property. Soft textiles, quality upholstery and plush furnishings round this apartment off beautifully, providing you with a relaxing retreat to retire to when you're done exploring Paris for the day. This vacation rental also offers a surprising amount of storage space, perfect if you're planning on staying for a longer spell.

Whether you're visiting with family, friends or the other half, this holiday apartment in Paris is the perfect place to make your base during your trip to the French capital.
