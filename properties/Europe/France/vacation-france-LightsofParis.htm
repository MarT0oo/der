Lights of Paris, 2nd District, Paris, France Vacation Rental

 

Parisian Lights, a wonderful area of Haussmann you'll find this lovely town-home designed for the person who has family and wants a place to stay that is inviting and invigorating. The space is airy and sweet, with lovely modern decor and personal touches one would expect in a long term rental. The flat is designed to inspire and bring the life of Paris into your space. Truly inspirational with wonderful modern art pieces throughout the flat, a beautiful wood floor look and the white modern kitchen and eat in nook.

Situated in the 2nd borough, this apartment is THE place to stay to enjoy Paris in a warm and sweet atmosphere. The 100m2 of this apartment can welcome 9 persons maximum, thanks to 3 double bedrooms (including 2 big beds and 2 single beds), and a comfortable sofa bed. The full equipped kitchen and the high-speed internet access will help you to make you feel at home, in the heart of the 1st arrondissement.

At walking distance, you’ll find the Boulevard Haussmann, the Opéra Garnier, the Palais Royal and the Marais. This apartment allows you to discover the heart of Paris in the simplest ways.
