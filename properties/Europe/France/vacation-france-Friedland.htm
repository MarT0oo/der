Friedland, Champs-Elysees, Paris, France Vacation Rental

 

Set in the heart of Champs-Elysees, this four-bedroom holiday apartment in Paris will immerse you into the most prestigious area of the city. The tree-lined Boulevard Haussmann is a few steps away, putting luxury shopping, exquisite dining and a sparkling nightlife on your doorstep. The Arc de Triomphe is down the block, and the plush Le Lido cabaret is a five-minute walk. A short drive will bring you to the Place de la Concorde, Grand Palais hall, and the lovely Tuileries Garden (8 minutes). Bus and metro stations are two minutes away on foot.

This vacation rental in Paris is located on the ground floor of a classic Haussmannian building with a lobby that welcomes guests elegantly. Our local interior designers created an uplifting ambience in the living room, which has a lofty ceiling, large windows, traditional paneled walls and warm wood flooring. A soft sofa and a stylized chaise lounge provide ample place for ten guests to relax. Gleaming whites, deep mochas and beige hues are soothing, and mirrored ornaments reflect the soft Parisian light. The kitchen offers full amenities, along with an eating counter and leather bar stools. All of the bedrooms are furnished with roomy dressers and shelves, and the master bedroom features a chic vanity and spacious walk-in closet. Playful colours brighten one curtained-off bedroom, and another floral bedroom is upstairs in its own private loft.

A large family or party of friends will fit perfectly into this exclusive Parisian holiday apartment, which will transform any vacation into a high-class experience of the French capital city.
