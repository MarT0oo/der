Chalet Eglantier, Courchevel 1850, France Vacation Rental

 

Chalet Eglantier is a five bedroom property with three hundred and twenty square metres of living space. It has a ski-in/ski-out location on the Pralong ski slope and has a traditional exterior with highly modern interior.

Eglantier has an ‘L’ shaped open plan living area with a comfortable seating area set around a fireplace and a dining space that has a large table that can easily seat twelve. Both these spaces have a double-height ceiling and French doors that lead out onto a balcony terrace with superb views. There’s also a modern open plan kitchen.

The chalet has five bedrooms and sleeps up to ten guests. Each of the bedrooms has a cool design with modern furnishings and contemporary linens. There are four double en-suite bedrooms, two of these have bathrooms that are directly set in the bedrooms and two have shower rooms. The master suite is up on the top floor of the chalet and has an ethanol fuelled fireplace and a bathroom with both bath and shower. All bedrooms are equipped with LCD television, Satellite, DVD player.

Chalet Eglantier has a luxury spa area with a swimming pool and waterfall fountain along with a Jacuzzi and a steam room. There is also an outdoor Jacuzzi on one of the terraces.

The property also has a television lounge and a desk / study space. There’s garage parking, laundry and a ski / boot room with boot heater
