Grande Roche, Courchevel 1850, France Vacation Rental

 

Chalet La Grande Roche built in a traditional Savoyard style, has an immense living area where guests can enjoy the magnificent views over the Bellecôte glacier and the majestic Dent du Villard. Superbly well appointed and tastefully decorated, this jewel in Courchevel’s crown offers a subtle blend of authenticity and luxury. Just a stone’s throw from the slopes and the centre of the resort, Grande Roche represents the best in Courchevel and the Alps.
