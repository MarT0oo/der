Brune II, 14th arrondissement, Paris, France Vacation Rental

 

Located on the Boulevard Brune, this sunny four bedroom vacation rental in Paris is in the perfect spot to discover the city. Resting in the city where famous French artists, writers, and philosophers created their works, you can take a transit bus to the beautiful Montparnasse Cemetery (17 minutes) where Simone De Beauvoir and her lover Jean-Paul Sartre are buried. The Porte De Versailles can be reached by the tram as well (12 minutes), and is France’s largest exhibition center, where many around the world flock for shows, fairs, and conventions. The central apartment is also close to many beautiful parks, with Parc Montsouris (18 minutes) and Park Square du Serment de Koufra (7 minutes) both in walking distance.

This bright and spacious apartment in Paris, is the perfect holiday getaway for your family. Decorated by our local interior designers with classic blue colors and clean lines, this renovated apartment will steal your heart instantly with its sprawling clear windows looking out onto the Paris streets, and beautiful skylights for extra spots of sunshine. The spacious living room and dining room is an oasis with draping window curtains that lead to a large private deck, and a family of wood furniture to complement the bright blues. Accessorized perfectly with an eye for detail, you can find these beautiful blues all over the apartment with comfy cushions, artwork, and wall decals that add a splash to each room. Take your morning coffee to the deck, fully functioning kitchen, or even to the convenient work desk, settled right under a window looking out onto the city below.

Ideal for your vacation, this paradise holiday apartment in Paris is just what your family needs to travel in comfort and get excited to explore the creative town.
