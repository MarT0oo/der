Bastide De La Tour, La Tour d'Aigues , Provence, France Vacation Rental

 

Luberon country house with pool - Nestled at the foot of the Southern Luberon mountains within a large wine estate, this splendidly restored 17th century bastide exudes charm throughout and boasts stunning views over the valley and surrounding vineyards; all within just two miles of the typical Provencal village of Tour d'Aigues.

Tastefully decorated and furnished with a blend of country antiques and period furniture, Bastide de la Tour maintains a strong Provencal identity. Original paintings, an 18th century tapestry, open fireplaces and typical polished floor-tiles all convey a genuine Provencal atmosphere. The beautiful landscaped garden has an abundance of scented lavender, pink laurels and rose bushes, with century-old trees provide pleasant shaded areas. And what better way to relax than lounging by the pool or enjoying a drink on the terrace overlooking the valley and hills of the Grand Luberon National park!

There are delightful medieval villages scattered throughout the national park, many of them perched on rocky hilltops and centered around an ancient castle or tower. Tour d'Aigues, on the banks of the River Eze, is the nearest to Bastide de la Tour and a very pretty one too. Venture a little further within the Luberon area to discover the remarkable gems that contribute to the region's unique fame and appeal. Visit nearby Grambois or Pertuis in search of a good restaurant or Ansouis, Cucuron and Lourmarin to spend some time browsing among their weekly market stalls or just enjoying the atmosphere sitting in a local cafe. Endless opportunities for outdoor pursuits are available in this region, including hiking, horse-riding, cycling and even hiring a pedalo on the small Lac de Peyrolles
