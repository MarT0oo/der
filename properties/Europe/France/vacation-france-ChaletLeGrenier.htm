Chalet Le Grenier, Méribel, France Vacation Rental

 

Chalet Le Grenier is a stunning ski-in/ski-out chalet, situated in the Morel region of Méribel. It enviably located just across the road from the Morel chairlift and the Hulotte piste allowing access to the snow. The chalet is large and imposing with 550m2 of exquisitely designed rooms arranged over five floors, with the added convenient benefit of a lift. Chalet Le Grenier has been created with relaxation and enjoyment at the forefront of its design.

The entrance to the chalet leads to a central foyer with birds eye view of the living and dining area on the level below. A well-equipped boot room and cloakroom are located on this floor with their own separate entrance. Above, is the games room hideaway with snooker table, wide screen TV and comfy sofas.

Below the entrance foyer is the main living area with triple height windows and panoramic mountain views. Wide glass doors open up to the large sunny terrace with outdoor seating. Inviting comfy sofas surround a central fireplace before leading into the dining area and kitchen. The TV snug has a widescreen TV with Sky and Apple TV and this adjoins a study/library.

On the lower two levels, there are two master bedroom suites and an additional four double/twin bedrooms. Impeccably designed and furnished, all bedrooms are generously sized and have separate marble tiled en-suites with a bath and shower. Every bedroom has a Bose sound system and mood lighting. Five of the bedrooms have direct access to a balcony or terrace with wonderful mountain views.

The spa is located on the lower ground floor of the chalet and provides the perfect environment to unwind after a day on the slopes. The stunning sunken Jacuzzi is large enough to fit the whole family and leads to the sauna and massage room. If you need to keep up with training while away, there is a gym with a state of the art treadmill and bike and a fully integrated sound/TV system.

Chalet Le Grenier is the perfect ski-in/ski-out family chalet and the Consensio bespoke service provides guests with an unforgettable stay.

Wellness is at the heart of this chalet, with a stunning spa waiting for you at the end of the day. The beautiful sunken Jacuzzi is big enough for everyone to enjoy. The adjoining sauna will soothe your weary limbs before heading to the chalet massage room for a relaxing treatment from one of recommended masseurs or physios.

Guests wanting a little more exercise after skiing will find the chalet gym with a TRX, an Artis treadmill, bike and kettlebells.

For those who just like to relax watching the views of the mountains, there is comfortable seating on the terrace where you can wrap up and enjoy a hot drink or glass of Perrier-Jouët the house champagne.

Where else could you have your private chef whip up a bowl of buttery, salted popcorn and nestle up to get cosy and to watch a movie in the comfort of your chalet? Le Grenier’s TV Snug is complete with Apple TV and Sky, to showcase all your favourite movies on a 55cm widescreen television. A lovely addition to this family chalet, it makes for an evening of great entertainment and is perfect for those extra cold weather days or when the slopes are a whiteout. There is also a PlayStation 4 which will keep all the children entertained after a day on the slopes. Alternatively, enjoy a game of pool or watch the rest of the family play from the comfort of the upstairs games room.
