Eiffel Tower Avenue Rapp, 7th district, Paris, France Vacation Rental

 

Stay in this two-bed apartment with its stunning panoramic views and wrap around balcony and you will feel you are on a movie set. The views of the Parisian roofline, the Eiffel Tower and the Champs Elysée right up to the Arc de Triomphe are amazing in daylight but lit up at night they will take your breath away. The master bedroom has a queen sized bed and an adjacent bathroom with both bathtub and shower. A second bedroom – twin or double – is served by a separate shower room. A double sofa bed in the living room provides extra sleeping accommodation. This gem of an apartment is stylishly furnished in contemporary style and comes with a wealth of DVDs, music and books. Two elevators whisk you up to the eighth floor and parking can be made available.

Home to Paris' most iconic landmark, the Eiffel Tower, the 7th arrondissement on the rive gauche (left bank) of the Seine is also one of the city's prime residential areas. A little like the Upper East Side or London's Westminster, the area features the French National Assembly and a number of other government departments so has long been a favoured residential district. The 7th arrondissement houses the Musée d'Orsay, Musée Rodin and a number of wonderful parks the Champs de Mars and Esplanade des Invalides. The 7th has an upmarket, quiet vibe where you can escape from noisier touristy areas and feel like a true Parisian.
