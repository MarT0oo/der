La Contemporaine, Cannes, Cote D'azur, France Vacation Rental

 

Modern Cannes villa located in the exclusive area of La Californie above Cannes, La Comtemporaine is a striking, avant-garde villa with stunning views of the Bay of Cannes. Large glass doors open onto sweeping balconies on each of the 3 storeys and marble floors throughout create a cool elegance.

It is just 2 miles from the centre of Cannes offering a wide variety of restaurants, elegant shops and a busy harbour. Whilst staying at this modern Cannes villa take the opportunity to stroll along La Croisette with its bustling atmosphere and pavement cafes.

Head inland from this modern Cannes villa to enjoy the scenery around the perfume town of Grasse, or along to the coastal towns of Juan-les-Pins or Ste Maxime. A boat trip to the Iles de Lerins affords the opportunity to see this unique coastline in all its glory.

 

Nearest shops Vallauris 1.25 miles. Cannes & beach 2 miles. Juan-Les-Pins 15 mins. Grasse 30 mins. Nice airport 30 mins drive. Tennis in Cannes. Golf 10 mins. Boat trips to the Iles de Lerins
