Rue du Roi, Marais, Paris, France Vacation Rental

 

Paris Apartment Rental, The Rue du Roi is a two bedroom luxury rental located in a charming 19th century building in the trendy Marais neighborhood. This quiet and bright apartment has all the charm and authenticity of Paris with antique furniture, beautiful parquet floors, and fireplace. It's fully equipped, providing comfort, space and practicality while still remaining cosy and welcoming.

The two bedrooms have AC, king sized beds, and closets. The two bathrooms are spacious and modern. The kitchen is open on the spacious living room, it is fully equipped to allow you to cook delicious french meals. The second bedroom can also be set with twin beds, and two more quests can sleep in the living room queen sized sofa bed. Anti bed bug mattress protector in each bed.

The apartment is in accordance with the regulations of the city of Paris regarding short term rentals.

Location

The location is ideal, in the heart of the Marais, one of the most vibrant areas in the city, filled with unique shops and neighborhood restaurants. The apartment is located in the most beautiful building of the rue du Roi de Sicile (it won an award when it was built in 1898). It's 5 minutes walk from the Place Saint Paul where you can find a metro stop, bus lines, and a large taxi stand, as well as grocery stores, bakery, butcher, cheese, and chocolates stores, a pharmacy, and more. The apartment is in an easy walk to many tourist attractions such as Place des Vosges, the river Seine and the Islands, Notre Dame, the Pompidou Center, and in a reasonable walk to the Louvre and Saint Germain. Although this area is very touristic, you can feel that Parisians live here by the atmosphere of neighborhood life that emerges.
