Tour de la Baie, Ramatuelle , St Tropez, France Vacation Rental

 

L'Escalet Plage beachfront villa - Ideally situated a few metres from L'Escalet Plage and its splendid cove in the Ramatuelle area, this beautifully renovated family villa with air conditioning and Jacuzzi hot tub enjoys direct access to the beach as well as breath-taking views over the bay between Cap Taillat and Cap Camarat.

This single-storey villa offers extremely comfortable accommodation designed for relaxed, easy living in harmony with the surrounding seascape. Depending on the time of the day (and on your level of energy!), you can choose to jog on the beach or walk all the way to Pampelonne plage, St Tropez or Gigaro plage, in the opposite direction. Alternatively, you may prefer to watch the ‘world sail by’ from the exclusive comfort of your sunbathing terrace or Jacuzzi tub, whilst sipping a refreshing cocktail. During your stay at Tour de la Baie you will soon become accustomed to having the Mediterranean Sea on your doorstep and swimming in the blue waters of L’Escalet bay as if you were swimming in your own private pool; no doubt this will become your favourite pastime - or possibly an addiction!

St Tropez is only a short drive from this charming villa. Once a picturesque, small fishing port, the town still retains most of the original appeal as its bay keeps attracting multimillionaire yachts. A ferry service connecting St Tropez to Ste Maxime provides an excellent alternative to the car for exploring the area. A little further inland, nestled in the hills above St Tropez, is the medieval village of Ramatuelle, commanding striking views over the surrounding bays. A visit to this delightful village is highly recommended, and it’s only a few minutes’drive from this beachfront villa on the French Riviera!
