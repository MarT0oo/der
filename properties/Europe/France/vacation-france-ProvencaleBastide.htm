Provencale Bastide, Provence, France Vacation Rental

 

This is a spacious and charming mansion in huge grounds, nestling in the foothills of the Aix countryside. Entrance to the property is through majestic gated entrance, followed by a drive through acres of grounds, olive groves and Mediterranean shrubs, cypress trees, lavender fields and rosemary bushes till you reach the Bastide.

Apart from the beautiful bedrooms, each with their own character, there is a spacious family room featuring large screen TV, and billiard room. The kitchen is huge. This is an ideal location to explore the cultural and scenic delights of the Bouches du Rhone, as well as the timeless landscapes of the Luberon hills.

The property had two swimming pools, tennis court and gym. It is available for rent for one night or more and is a great venue for professional events, business meetings, conferences, photo-shoots and weddings.

The majestic gated entrance taunts onlookers as to what lies beyond. Although just 3 km from the vibrant city of Aix en Provence the property offers “off the beaten path ” seclusion with acres of olive trees and shrubs spread along the meandering road leading to the Bastide.

Provencale Bastide is the consummate setting for guests wishing to unwind and experience the “art of living” that is exclusive to this stunning country residence.

The ideal location gives easy access to the cultural and scenic delights of the Bouches-du-Rhone region and for the more adventurous, the challenge of cycling the timeless landscape of the Luberon or rambling up the slopes of Mont Saint Victoire.
