Vue Sereine, Plan De La Tour, St Tropez, France Vacation Rental

 

Plan de la Tour modern villa with pool – Tucked away amid vines in a tranquil area just a few miles from the quaint village of Plan de la Tour and its amenities, this immaculate, newly built contemporary villa enjoys sweeping views over the rolling countryside with its vineyards and olive groves. Many gems of the acclaimed French Riviera such as Ste Maxime, St Tropez, Grimaud, Gassin and Ramatuelle are within easy drive from this villa, offering scope for countless outdoor pursuits, sport-related activities and events, shopping, art and culture, excellent wine and food to whet the appetite.

Furnished in a minimalist style, this property offers ample space in which to relax and unwind with family or friends, set apart from all the hustle and bustle typical of more popular destinations along the coast. What better place to soak up the beauty of the surrounding landscape than swimming in the pool or relaxing on your balcony with a glass of rose wine!

If you manage to drag yourself away from this oasis of peace and tranquillity, you may wish to explore the surrounding area walking or cycling along trails crisscrossing meadows, olive groves and vineyards. The coast is within easy driving distance and, once there, you can take the ferry connecting Ste Maxime to St Tropez across the gulf. Inland hilltop villages such as Plan de la Tour, Grimaud, Ramatuelle and Gassin are very picturesque and have plenty to offer in terms of good restaurants, colourful weekly markets, far reaching sea views, castles and other historical buildings, as well as the chance to experience the typical lifestyle of a cheerful French country village.
