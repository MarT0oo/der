Sevres, Rue de Sèvres Paris, Paris, France Vacation Rental

 

In the historic Saint Germain des Pres neighborhood, in the 6th arrondissement, the 2-bedroom Sevres vacation apartment plunges you into the rich cultural heritage of Paris. Once home to the city’s most famous artists, the neighborhood is known for its wealth of art galleries and museums. Take in the striking sculptures of the Bourdelle Museum (9 min. walk) and the Rodin Museum (15 min), then enjoy the sunshine in the scenic Luxembourg Gardens (18 min). History lovers will enjoy the massive Army Museum (15 min). The Duroc Metro station is 2 minutes away.

With wooden floors and mirrored accents, our local interior designer has created a rustic yet elegant space. Vivid, colorful textiles and eclectic wall art give the space character, and huge windows fill the interiors with light. A fully equipped kitchenette has everything you need for in-house dining. Premium amenities include free Wi-Fi, cable TV, hotel beds and linens, luxury toiletries, and a Nespresso machine.

Spacious and centrally located, this apartment is the perfect home base for your family vacation in Paris.
