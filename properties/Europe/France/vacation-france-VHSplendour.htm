VH Splendour, St. Germain, Paris, France Vacation Rental

 

Located on the famous boulevard Saint Germain which was baptized in honor of Bishop Germain of Paris, this Prestigious property in the heart of the left bank is the quintessence of the Parisian spirit. You will be in the heart of the vibrant capital, on one of the main axes of the left bank, but a few minutes' walk from the most beautiful shops on the right bank (just as L'Esprit Parisien).

The neighborhood is among the most prestigious in the capital. Stroll along the boulevard and you will be able to breathe a concentrate of culture and art, in the heart of the French historical institutions: from the National Assembly (which can be visited), take your time to wander along the boulevard to feast yourself with many showcases.

A well-preserved secret, this Prestigious property in the heart of the left bank is hidden behind a splendid façade of the boulevard Saint Germain, which needs no further introduction. In other words, you will find yourself in one of the high places of the "Parisian gesture", at an address synonymous with French taste and refinement.

On entering the apartment, you have in front of you, on the other side of the corridor, a large bright living room. To your right, an immense dining room (which communicates with the living room), then the fully equipped kitchen, which communicates with the dining room. From the living room, you can also access the library.

To the left of the entrance, you will find four rooms: first, a large room adjoining the library, and which opens out to a shower room. This shower room is accessible from the first bedroom and from a second bedroom, an office room arranged as a relaxation corner and which can also act as a guest room. Finally, at the other end of the corridor, the last two rooms of this Prestigious property in the heart of the left bank: the children's room, and the last bedroom; a master suite with its en-suite bathroom. A third bathroom and toilet are accessible from the corridor.
