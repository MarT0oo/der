Villa Paradise, Bidart, France Vacation Rental

 

Our villa Paradise is the perfect home rental for an unforgettable holiday in a villa with extraordinary design architecture. In the peace and quiet of a lovely residential neighborhood South of Biarritz and right before the village of Bidart, it’s the ideal location. Near the beautiful beach of Erretegia, this recently renovated Basque home will know how to fulfill all your holiday desires, no doubt about that !

Passing through the entrance hall with a colorful floor tiling (very seventies like !), you then enter the main living area. The open living room with green, grey and wood design furniture is a relaxing space to spend quality family time.

Right on your left when you enter PARADISE, there is a large ground floor double bedroom with its own bathroom and Italian shower. You can access the garden from the bedroom and you get to hang out in a private little terrace. Outstanding !

On the first and top floor, 4 bedrooms have their own bathrooms with showers, one includes a bathtub. The master bedroom is majestic : the King size bed is facing a large square window, a real light shaft overlooking the property’s garden and pool…

Right next the living room you’ll find the open kitchen with a central island, a raw wood bar and a glass hood – nope, not an overhead projector ! Kitchen is fully equipped, perfect for cooking delicious meals for family and friends.

We love the harmonious blend of concrete and wood in this beautiful contemporary holiday home. A personal favorite ? The garden, swimming pool and upstairs terrace which turns out to be the perfect spot to read in the sun or for a delicious improvised brunch !

Paradise is located in a quiet and peaceful residential area, less than 2 km from Erretegia’s beach and golf course of Ilbarritz, and about 1.5 km from the town center of Bidart. You will then be very close to the Saturday markets to buy the best local produce of the region !

Biarritz is only located further North. For those flying in/out, the airport is only 20 minutes away by car and Biarritz train station is about 8 minutes by car too. Too easy…
