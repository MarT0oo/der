The Chateau Perla, Saint-Tropez, France


The Villa Pearl St Tropez Cote d'Azur is an amazing luxury vacation villa located just 5 minutes drive to Pamelonne Bay, Nikki Beach and a variety of other fan favorites in St. Tropez. This Fully serviced home has been at the top of the list of private luxury homes that extend a 5 star staff within residence. This wonderfully stylish villa is in a privileged position hidden away among the vineyards yet on the edge of the village of Saint-Tropez and just a five-minute walk to the beach. There is a stunning living room with fireplace, a fully-equipped kitchen, TV lounge, six bedrooms, five bathrooms, balcony, covered terrace and pool. There is also one-bedroom staff accommodation on site.

General Information: Located next to the Gassin Provence, just off the road leading to Nice and the Route des Plages. The living space of Villa Pearl is 400m2 and sits on a piece of land of 4000m2. The Villa has 3 living areas inside along with terraces on the first floor and on the ground floor, a veranda and pool area running along the length of the house. Sleeping Capacity: 12 Guests (including children) in 6 Double Bedrooms (Including 1 Master Bedroom), 4 with en-suites.

The Villa Pearl is ideally located and is only a 5 minute drive to Pampelonne Bay, Nikki Beach, Bagatelle Beach, Byblos Beach, Loulou, Verde Beach and the famous Club 55. So many love to enjoy the area via bike ride or enjoying a long walk along the streets. Photographers professional and novice love the idea of capturing the heart of what the Cote d'Azur is to this region of France. The music, the culture, the nightlife, the fashion, truly an inspirational journey for those wanting to experience more than just an ordinary vacation. We offer an experience at a level most only dream of.

Saint Tropez has immense character and it is easy to see why people embark on a lasting love affair with this sensational village. The town is home to internationally famous boutiques, bars, cafes and wonderful restaurants. It offers the perfect mix of quaint old town alongside jet set flavor on each turn in the meandering streets. The peninsula is filled with delightful beaches, such as the beaches of Pampelonne. Museums, art galleries, monuments, churches, a fabulous market, as well as endless events and sailing regattas means that your stay can be as action-packed or relaxing as you wish it to be.
