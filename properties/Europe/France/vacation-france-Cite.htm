Cité, 4th district, Paris, France Vacation Rental

 

Enjoy your stay in the heart of historic and emblematic area of l’île de la Cité. The two room apartment is located on rue du Cloître Notre-Dame on the second floor of a stately building with an elevator and paved courtyard.

Covering a surface of 430 sq ft, the apartment Cité has a bright living room with a comfortable dining area and unlimited internet access. Modern and authentic, let yourself be seduced by the serenity of this place. The fully equipped kitchen will make you feel right at home. The apartment has a Wireless internet connection. The Cité can host up to 4 people thanks to the double queen bed and the convertible sofa. The shower room and the WC complete this property. One of the amazing advantages of the apartment is the unique view on the Notre Dame Cathedral.

Known to be a secret village, l’Île Saint Louis offers numerous romantic walks along the Seine and its picturesque streets filled with mansions and art galleries. Cross the Pont des Arts to join the Marais or the Louvre and its majestic Tuileries gardens. Numerous public transport lines are at the feet of the apartment (métro Cité or Saint Michel Notre Dame, line 4, RER B and C), using those lines you could reach any Parisian districts you wish.
