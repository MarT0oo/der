La Colonnade, St Tropez, Grimaud, France Vacation Rental

 

This luxury villa with infinity pool and Jacuzzi hot-tub sits in an elevated position with commanding sea views over the Gulf of St Tropez, set against a backdrop of forested hills on the edge of Port Grimaud.

Part of an exclusive residential domain, this substantial villa is spacious and airy with many floor-to-ceiling windows, balconies and terraces allowing for maximum enjoyment of the stunning panorama. The interior is elegant and sophisticated, featuring marble columns, tiled floors and beamed ceilings. Outside, a marvellous lush garden surrounds the villa, with palm trees, agavi and oleanders creating the perfect Mediterranean setting. While-away your days by the poolside as you soak up these beautiful surroundings, and finish off with a delicious al fresco meal under the covered terrace or the poolside gazebo.

Port Grimaud is the nearest town to La Colonnade, just over a mile away, featuring a lovely marina, excellent restaurants and shops and a lively summer atmosphere. Within a couple of miles is Grimaud old town and its enchanting medieval castle perched on a hilltop; arguably one of the best viewing points of the bay. A little further afield is the medieval village of La Garde-Freinet for colourful, twice weekly markets and numerous shops and restaurants. The golf at Beauvallon is within a few minutes’ drive and countless watersports are available along the coast, not to mention horse-riding and hiking trails in the surrounding hills.
