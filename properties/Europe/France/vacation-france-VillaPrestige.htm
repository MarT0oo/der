Villa Prestige, St Paul De Vence, Cote D'azur, France Vacation Rental

 

This luxurious France villa in St Paul de Vence is ideally situated for exploring the Cote d'Azur. Some of the features which make Villa Prestige a superb location for a family holiday are the glass retractable roof, marble floors, ensuite bedrooms, exceptionally large swimming pool and dramatic views.

A large terrace overlooks the pool and spans the length of this modern villa. It is ideal for dining and admiring the views towards the hill top village of St Paul de Vence, or the distant sea views.

St Paul is just a short walk with narrow cobbled streets, restaurants and craft shops or for touring the Riviera itself. For art lovers, the Maeght Foundation Museum of modern art is also a short walk away.
