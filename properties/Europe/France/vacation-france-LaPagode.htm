La Pagode, Mougins, Cote D'azur, France Vacation Rental

 

Mougins luxury Cote d’Azur Villa – Tucked away among the verdant hills surrounding Mougins, only a ten-minute drive from Cannes and the splendid French Riviera, La Pagode is an elegant, modern villa with a unique exotic garden. Great care has been taken in designing this magnificent terraced garden adorned with a multitude of tropical and Mediterranean plants, palm trees, garden features such as ponds and a romantic Japanese-style bridge. As you enter the park of La Pagode you will experience an utter sense of peace and tranquillity, enhanced by the stunning views surrounding this villa.

The accommodation is very comfortable, bright and spacious with a large open plan living area and floor-to-ceiling glass panels enabling guests to appreciate the beauty of the garden whilst indoors. Ideally suited for two families or a large party, La Pagode lends itself to easy living with lots of space to enjoy and explore both indoor and outdoor. The pool area is the focal point of the park with a massive sun terrace and state-of-the-art pagoda-style summer kitchen, fully equipped for barbeques. You will love spending your evenings here by the illuminated infinity pool, maybe relaxing with a glass of wine in pleasant company.

If you manage to leave this little corner of paradise, the Valmasque Forest is an appealing location for trekking and other outdoor activities, while the quaint hilltop village of Mougins is only ten minutes away and offers a wide range of excellent restaurants to choose from. At approximately the same distance you can visit Cannes and its glamorous glitterati. As you browse among its exclusive boutiques you may even come across some celebrities in disguise. The beach in Cannes is lovely and many more can be found along the coast as well as many seafood restaurants, harbours and charming cafes overlooking the majestic Mediterranean sea.
