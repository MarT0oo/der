Chateau Des Legendes, nr. Siorac-en-Perigord, Dordogne, France Vacation Rental

 

Dordogne chateau for holiday rental - set in the heart of the country with stunning views of the Dordogne valley and surrounding countryside. Chateau des Legendes has been in the owners family for many generations and thus has a feel of real history.

This Dordogne chateau has been decorated in classic French style, with complementing antique furniture which careful clients are sure to appreciate, yet it has a cosy and inviting ambience. The stone walls create a naturally cool environment during the summer, whilst open fires are ideal for any winter stay. Dordogne offers thousands of historic sites, including the beautiful Renaissance town of Sarlat, the prehistoric caves at Lascaux and numerous medieval castles, churches, picturesque villages and towns seemingly unchanged by the passage of time.

Set privately within the chateau grounds are two delightful renovated farmhouses, each with their own pool, making this an ideal property for large groups and special celebrations. PR485 Ferme des Legendes & PR878 Maison des Legendes. Stay in this Dordogne chateau for full enjoyment of the famous cuisine, the wonderful countryside and the innumerable outdoor pursuits.

 

Coux et Bigaroque 1.25 miles for nearest local shops. Siorac-en-Perigord 3 miles. Le Bugue & Sarlat 14 miles, Bergerac & Perigueux 1 hr. Lascaux Caves 25 miles, Gouffre de Padirac 55 miles. Golf, tennis, horse riding and canoeing within easy reach.
