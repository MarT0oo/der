Grande Corniche, Les Gets, France


Grande Corniche is a brand new chalet offering the utmost in style, luxury and technology. Located in the beautiful resort of Les Gets. Situated in the vast "Portes de Soleil" ski area, Les Gets is a mere hour’s drive from Geneva airport. Its' location and expansive skiing make it a fabulous family holiday destination, whilst providing endless challenges for the more adventurous skier. The chalet is a short drive away from the ski lifts and charming resort centre, in your chauffeur driven 4x4.


Grande Corniche sleeps up to 12 guests in 6 en suite bedrooms. All rooms are equipped with plasma screen TV and enjoy stunning views across the valley. On a clear day guests may be able to spot the Mont Blanc from the open plan dining and living area. Grande Corniche's little sister the ‘Petite Corniche’, is a cozy mazot which can accommodate a further 4 guests and is located just beside the main chalet. Perfect for extended families, nannies, teenagers or grandparents.


As ever Consensio offers its' guests absolute luxury. You can relax in the glass sauna with 180 degree views of the valley, drink champagne in the conversation pit, or gaze at the stars from the outdoor hot tub. After being pampered in the wellness area, you and your guests will be invited for canapés and aperitifs around the chalet’s grand piano before taking a seat for the evening meal prepared by your private chef.


Grande Corniche has a number of unique features, be it the quirky slide from the cosy bar area on the fourth floor which takes you down to the wine cave on the lower ground floor, guaranteed to delight young and old. The snooker table can be converted to a dining table big enough to seat 22 people for those special occasions.


Everything in this chalet has been handpicked and created for your enjoyment – this really is a unique property, perfect for you and your family and a welcome addition to the Consensio portfolio.
