Sommet, Courchevel 1850, France Vacation Rental

 

Chalet Sommet is a traditional chalet with an authentic alpine atmosphere that has been carefully decorated in the Savoyard style. The property is located in the quiet ski-in/ski-out area of Le Plantret and can accommodate between twelve and fourteen guests in six doubles and one twin bedroom. There’s also a bunk bedroom and a staff room.

Anyone who is looking for the ultimate alpine pad will not be disappointed by the living space at Chalet Sommet. There are two lounge areas, one of which surrounds a big stone fireplace with comfortable seating and wood-clad walls. The dining table is large, seating up to sixteen guests and it’s open to the well-equipped kitchen. There’s a small games table for playing cards and table games and the living room has access to a balcony terrace.

On the top floor of the chalet there’s a master suite and a double bedroom. There are three further ensuite doubles on a lower floor and a family double with interconnecting twin bedroom. There is also a staff bedroom and a room with two bunk beds on the ground floor.

Chalet Sommet has a Jacuzzi and hammam along with a ski room for equipment storage.
