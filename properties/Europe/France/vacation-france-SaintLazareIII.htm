Saint Lazare III, Rue saint lazare Paris, Paris, France Vacation Rental

 

Cozy and bright, the 1-bedroom Saint Lazare III vacation apartment is a hidden gem in Paris’s sophisticated Saint Lazare district. Some call this neighborhood in the 8th arrondissement the true heart of the capital; it’s famous for its magnificent architecture, cultural institutions, and shopping. Only a 2-minute walk away, the Galeries Lafayette carries the latest fashion trends in a historic building. Museums such as the Museum of Romantic Life (8 min. walk) and the Fragonard Perfume Museum (12 min. walk) introduce you to Parisian style. The Saint Lazare Metro and train station is just steps away.

With funky wall art and a fashionable color palette, our local interior designer has created a cozy yet stylish space. The plush bedroom and a double sofa bed can accommodate up to 4 guests, and a sleek kitchenette is perfect for breakfast. The windows overlook a tranquil Parisian courtyard. Premium amenities include free Wi-Fi, cable TV, the finest beds and linens, and a Nespresso machine for your coffee cravings.

Ideal for couples, solo travelers, and families, this apartment puts Paris at your doorstep.
