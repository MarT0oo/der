Villa EZE, St. Jean Cap Ferrat, French Riviera, France Vacation Rental

 

Offering a beautiful panorama over the Mediterranean Sea and the Cap Ferrat, yet only a short walk away from the beach of Eze is the outstanding Villa EZE. A contemporary and modern house over 5 floors, it offers the best place to relax in this exclusive French Riviera enclave.

The dead end street and tall perimeter walls give you all the privacy you need, yet it also serves as a key contrast of what is to come. As the elevator brings your guests up, they are left speechless at the breathtaking sea views from every room on every floor; expansive glass windows and sliding glazed doors frame the glistening sea and invite you to take in the wonder of this unique villa that can be yours for a few weeks to months.

And when the time comes, Monaco is just 15 minutes away, offering cultural and historical sights, world class shopping, fine dining and a broad range of entertainment and cultural programmes (including the casinos, opera, exhibitions and nightlife) , in one of the most exclusive cities in the world. Monaco is also the home to the Formula One Grand Prix, the Yacht Show and other renowned international events.

The villa can accommodate very comfortably up to 10 adult guests to stay overnight in luxurious style. All five bedrooms have been beautifully designed with gorgeous en-suite bathrooms. 2 additional beds can be conveniently added in order to accommodate children.
