Château de Bonheur, Provence, France


Château de Bonheur is a wonderful Mansion and Luxury restored Farmhouse that can be rented as an entire estate or just the main Chateau. You can sleep anywhere from 16 to 29 guests comfortably with this wonderful space. The location is a perfect venue for large family vacations, weddings, corporate retreats or just a simply and enjoyable backdrop for photography and escapes for the soul. Learn to write, journey trough time and experience the romance that is set withing the rolling hills of Provence's wine regions.

The Chateau de Bonheur is a glorious 18th Century Castle completely restored and furnished with beautiful European and Provencal furniture and art. However, all the Chateau's services, including the heating, cooling, electrical and plumbing systems, are 21st century, as are the Kitchens, laundry rooms and all the bathrooms. The Chateau enjoys three Reception rooms (50 square meters, 30 square meters and 26 square meters), a Library (50 square meters), a large formal Dining room (which seats up to 15 persons - 35 square meters), and an informal large, combined Dining room and Kitchen (70 square meters). The Chateau's charming, internal Courtyard with its fountains, its aviaries and its jasmine covered walls can also be used for al fresco dining.

Within the Chateau there are three large master bedroom suites - with king sized beds and with one suite on the ground floor and two on the first floor. Each suite has an en-suite bathroom and a separate shower stall. There are a further six bedrooms (with four king sized beds and two with twin beds) on the second floor, each of which has its own en-suite bathroom (with showers in each bath) and all of which are tiled with lovely Provencal tiles.

The adjacent Farm building, of some 950 square meters, has been fully modernized and converted into, inter alia, guest bedrooms, and certain sports facilities (squash court and gym room). The accommodation consists of: (1) Guest room, which has two bedroom with a king sized bed, and one bedroom with a queen sized bed ; (2) Second Suite, which has one bedroom with king sized bed, and two bedrooms with twin beds; and - a separate Courtyard level bedroom, with a king sized bed. All these seven bedrooms have en-suite bathrooms, with baths and separate shower stalls. All the seven guest bedrooms overlook a beautiful internal Courtyard with its cloisters, various seating areas, lawn, lemon trees, jasmine clad walls and a fountain.

Enjoy this amazing and wonderful location but we can also make it that much better by combining it with a lovely tour and or cooking class. Learn to be a French chef and take in the wonderful spices and flavors of the area or take a journey from winery to winery and let us organize a tour of the wonderful wine regions. There is no limit to what dreamexoticrentals can arrange for your trip.
