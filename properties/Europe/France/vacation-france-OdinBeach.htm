Odin Beach, Anglet, France Vacation Rental

 

Odin Beach is located in the golf course of Chiberta in Anglet, between land and sea. A dreamy location! You will enjoy the peace and quiet of Anglet, its pine trees forest and sandy beaches, a 5-minute drive from Biarritz and its vibrant atmosphere. Stunning villa you'll love spending your holidays at!

The Space

Odin Beach is a stunning property which calls for an exquisite journey to the Basque Country. Imagine yourself sipping a smoothie or a glass of wine by the overflowing heated pool with panoramic golf and ocean views... Paradise!

The Villa

At first glance we are taken aback by Odin Beach's various architectural influences which include Asian and Scandinavian styles. The result is truly astonishing! Open to beautiful outdoor areas, this property is definitely worthy of the most prestigious homes from Hollywood.

You will spend your day basking in the sun on a lounger or relaxing in the pool. On warm summer nights you will love eating outside on the terrace while enjoying the gorgeous and vast tree-lined garden. The main living area features a large fully equipped open-plan kitchen which is very easy to use, a spacious dining room, and an extremely comfortable living room. You will find everything you need in this appealing kitchen and you will happily spend some quality time there while chatting away with your friends comfortably seated around the large table.

Odin Beach's living room is an invitation to unwind. Read a book all snuggled into the large light green sofas and cushions. In the evening, you have front row seats to watch in awe the colors of the remarkable sunsets over Anglet's beaches. The light is truly breathtaking, you will see...

On the first floor of the villa, you will find a second living/TV room to watch a movie in the utmost tranquility. A desk as well, for those of you who (really) need to work.

Please note that Odin Beach’s furniture and interior decor are very design yet quite fragile and not necessarily user-friendly. The roller blinds and blackout curtains should be handled with care. Also the villa is equipped with inside and outside cameras. But no need to smile because they’ll be turned off during your stay !

The Bedrooms

The villa features 6 bedrooms, each of which with their own private bathrooms. Novel and bright bedrooms with hotel-quality mattresses and beddings.

5 bedrooms are located in the villa : 3 double bedrooms, 1 bedroom with twin beds. On the 2nd floor, you will find the master bedroom which offers the most beautiful views of the property. Scale the heights and make the most of such heavenly views as soon as you wake up in the morning. The golf course, the sky, and the ocean as far as your eyes can see... With such panoramic views, it would be unreasonable to be in a bad mood to be honest !

There are also an extra double room in the basement, very convenient to invite your guests over and the pool house can also serve as a bedroom. A small kitchen and 4 single beds will make your kids very happy, they will feel as they have their own private little villa !
