Villa Freya, Languedoc, Occitanie, France Vacation Rental

 

A fantastic beachfront luxury villa in Languedoc, southern France, Villa Freya sleeps up to eight guests in four en-suite bedrooms. There is a fifth en-suite bedroom in the basement, perfect for the nanny or a hideaway for the kids, taking the occupancy to 10. Adjacent to Villa Frankie, the two villas are perfect for larger groups of up to 20!

The villa highlights include a spectacular glass walled swimming pool, manicured gardens, children’s, games room, cinema room and sauna. Use of the villa’s speedboat, kayaks, stand-up paddles and mountain bikes for all ages ensure there’s plenty to do for those looking for a more “active” break

The combination of traditional local stone, wood and glass provide for a spectacular entrance. The light and airy open plan living area provides a fabulous space for dinning, entertaining and just taking it easy. The kitchen is fully equipped with utensils and there is a plasma television, DVD library and sound system in the lounge and dining room. Enjoy ‘alfresco’ dining and evening BBQ’s on the terrace as the sun sets over the sea.

There are stunning sea views from the the master bedrooms and balcony. All bedrooms are suitable for double or twin occupancy and are equipped with plasma televisions, DVD players and iPod docking stations. The luxurious en-suite bathrooms are brimming with L’Occitane products and fluffy bathrobes and towels for your comfort

Children and big kids can enjoy table tennis and table football and other activities in the basement games room. Relax in the sauna or watch your favourite movie in the cinema room, both situated in the basement. Wi-Fi throughout enables you to keep in touch with the wider world should the need arise

The villa comes with a villa manager to ensure everything goes smoothly and to oversee all aspects of your stay while in resort as well as daily housekeeping. A complementary welcome pack awaits you and includes a bottle of Bollinger Champagne, a bottle of Saint-Emilion Grand Cru, a selection of regional cheeses and charcuterie as well as some basic essentials. A fresh bouquet of flowers will also have been arranged in the villa for your arrival. There is a fresh delivery of breads and pastries each morning, and a mid-week change of towels and linens
