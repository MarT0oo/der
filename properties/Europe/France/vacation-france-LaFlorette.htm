La Florette, Biot, Cote D'azur, France Vacation Rental

 

Exclusive Cote d’Azur villa – This grand, newly-built villa which exudes elegance throughout, is conveniently situated in one of the most sought-after areas of the Cote d’Azur, between Valbonne and Biot, a few miles from Antibes and its beach. Splendid, far reaching views over the hills of Sophia Antipolis stretch as far as the Mediterranean Sea and can be best enjoyed from the large oval terrace on the first floor, arguably a key feature of La Florette.

The tasteful combination of classic features and contemporary furniture help create the perfect atmosphere for a relaxing holiday in style, in the unique setting of the French Riviera. The spacious living area boasts marble floors, Greek-style columns, a crystal chandelier and contemporary, white furniture, including a dual-aspect fireplace. The garden surrounding the villa provides plenty of space as well as shaded areas with mature palm trees and cypresses, especially around the pool, where a music system will help you relax and enjoy this fabulous outdoor living space.

Just a few miles away from La Florette you can experience the "joie de vivre” and relaxing pace of life in the charming old villages of Biot and Valbonne, with their restaurants and pavement cafes, boutiques, art galleries and vibrant local markets. For a day by the sea, La Siesta beach in Antibes is only a stone’s throw away. Here you can also visit the Picasso Museum, with its rich collection of masterpieces painted while Picasso was a local resident, climb the dramatic heights of Castle Grimaldi, or stroll amongst the exotic plants of the Jardin Thuret botanic gardens in Cap d’Antibes. All in all, La Florette ‘ticks all the boxes’ and your holiday in the Cote d’Azur is bound to be a memorable success.
