Brune VI, 14th arrondissement, Paris, France Vacation Rental

 

Located in the calm and quiet 14th arrondissement of Paris, this three-bedroom holiday apartment in Paris is a pleasant stroll from many popular cafes and restaurants on Avenue Jean-Moulin (3 minutes). On foot, you can reach the unique Catacombs of Paris (18 minute walk). A short car ride (15 minutes) will bring you to the iconic Eiffel Tower or the Pantheon Mausoleum. Public transport is very convenient, with stations for both trams and buses next door (3 minutes).

With the exquisite taste of our local interior designers, this townhouse is a genuine work of Parisian art! The main living space is L-shaped, composed of a spacious dining area for eight guests, a basic kitchen, and a stylish salon. This vacation rental in Paris is a white canvas decorated with soothing, complementary colours, such as greyish-blue and yellow. Similar to the modern wall art, the furniture is geometric and quirky, with square cushions and circular chairs. A chunky cubist staircase reaches the second floor, and a glass wall with doors connects to the charming garden terrace. The bedrooms invite relaxation, with soft, shaggy pillows and a gentle mix of textures. Arched windows overlook traditional brick buildings and make the bedrooms glow with natural light. Convenient bedside tables all have angled light fixtures for easy reading from dusk to dawn. With a clean lines and tiny white tiles, two bathrooms supply all amenities.

This holiday apartment in Paris gives visitors a chance to eat, sleep, relax, and experience life within the artistic ambiance of this restful neighbourhood.
