Villa Ramatuelle, Ramatuelle, St. Tropez, France Vacation Rental

 

Holiday villa Ramatuelle near St Tropez - Villa Ramatuelle is in a superb position very close to the beautiful beaches and famous restaurants of the St Tropez peninsula and less than 2 miles from charming Ramatuelle, which is known for its open-air theatre and concerts in July and August. This spacious and private villa is tastefully decorated to a very high standard and boasts a stunning pool and breathtaking sea views.

With the accommodation arranged around a patio courtyard with a pretty feature fountain, this exceptional villa lends itself to easy indoor-outdoor living, enabling you to relax into your holiday from the minute you arrive. What could be nicer than relaxing on the terrace, letting your gaze travel across the beautifully maintained gardens down to the pool, with the trees and the stunning blue of the Gulf of St Tropez as a backdrop. Several beaches and beach clubs/restaurants are very convenient indeed, about a mile from the villa, which is situated on the much sought-after lower hillside of Ramatuelle, set apart from other villas in a tranquil location. However, the hustle and bustle of summertime on the Riviera is still at hand for those occasions when you want to enjoy it.

The Var region is such a lovely area for a holiday, with its long sandy beaches, its enchanting hilltop villages such as Ramatuelle, Gassin and La Croix Valmer, its mountains, forests and vineyards, its excellent restaurants, captivating St Tropez and, of course, wonderful sea views at every turn.
