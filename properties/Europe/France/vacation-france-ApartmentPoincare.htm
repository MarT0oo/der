Apartment Poincare, 16 Arrondissement, Paris, France Vacation Rental

 

The Apartment Poincare in Paris is a luxury vacation rental in the 16th Arrondissement. This amazing flat is what a Parisian might call this apartment a ‘typical Immeuble Hausmannien’: venerable, imposing, grand. But look closer. The entire property has been carefully reconfigured – walls moved, rooms re-proportioned – to offer the most modern of living spaces. The winding staircase gives way to the Regal flavor that can only be found while sipping espresso at the finest cafes throughout Paris. To truly see the sights you have to enjoy every moment even the most amazing spaces entwine us to want more.

Some of the most extraordinary features are our wonderful luxury kitchen, equipped with all of the wonderful necessities for even the most discerning of chefs A truly Euro Design with flat porcelain stovetop, a double oven, a stainless hood to extra and a glossy Euro design. A well placed and large kitchen with a summer kitchen and formal dining, seating 10 for a family dinner.

Each of our 5 bedrooms features it's own dynamic blend of contemporary and classic italian. You will find the Master Suite with it's delightful layout is made to feel like a true Royal Suite, within you'll find a large King sized bed with en-suite bathroom, within the second suite a Queen (larger than US) with en-suite bathroom, within the third bedroom as well a Queen en-suite bedroom, within the forth as well a queen bedroom en-suite and the forth a full sized bed. This apartment hosts 8 adults and 2 children or 9 adults, we feel the room layout is best for that situation. The apartment is very large and is equipped with an elevator located on the 2nd floor and very easy to maneuver.

Ask us about adding a chef to your next vacation.
