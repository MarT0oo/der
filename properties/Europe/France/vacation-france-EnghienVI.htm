Enghien VI, Rue d'Enghien Paris, Paris, France Vacation Rental

 

Located in the trendy Grands Boulevards-Opera neighborhood, the 2-bedroom Enghien VI vacation apartment is steps from the most beloved sites in Paris. Here in the 10th arrondissement, pubs and an electric nightlife are down the block, or head to the Grand Rex Theatre to spot stars and enjoy a show (5-minute walk). Shoppers will take delight in the close proximity to Galeries Lafayette (9 minute-drive) and to the famed market street of Rue Montorgueil (15-minute walk). Bus stops and the Bonne Nouvelle metro station are only steps away.

On the ground floor, this vacation apartment has been refurbished by our local interior designer for a light and airy feel. Soothing shades of blue and stately gold accents fill the space with Parisian elegance, while quirky pictures add a touch of humour. A fully equipped kitchen has everything you need for in-house dining. Premium amenities include free Wi-Fi, cable TV, hotel beds and linens, luxury toiletries, and a Nespresso machine.

Families will love this charming vacation apartment, a hidden gem in the heart of Paris.
