Ponthieu IV, Rue de Ponthieu Paris, Paris, France Vacation Rental

 

On elegant Champs-Elysees, the exquisite 1-bedroom Ponthieu IV vacation apartment brings you to the posh heart of Paris. Here in the 8th arrondissement, you'll be only steps away from chic boutiques, gourmet dining, and world-class nightclubs. Stroll down stylish streets to escape into the landscaped Champs-Elysees Gardens (12 minutes). A short ride will bring you to the Arc de Triomphe, the Grand Palais, and the lively Place de la Concorde (5 minutes). The Franklin Roosevelt and St. Philip du Roule Metro stations are within a 5-minute walk.

With splashes of color and floor-to-ceiling windows, our local interior designer has created a feast for the senses. An efficient kitchenette has everything you need for breakfast at home, and its attractive, tiled backsplash turns food prep into a work of art. The plush bedroom offers ample storage space. Premium amenities include free Wi-Fi, cable TV, hotel beds and linens, luxury toiletries, and a Nespresso machine.

Perfect for couples or solo travelers, this lovely apartment will be your home away from home.
