Maison Orchidee, Pernes Les Fontaines, Provence, France Vacation Rental

 

Exclusive stone built Provencal villa with pool - Encircled by a small secluded garden, private pool and terrace, this attractive mas has been built to the highest standard for comfortable, luxury living in the heart of Provence, less than two miles away from the market town of Pernes-les-Fontaines and in proximity of the Grand Luberon Park and its many quaint villages. The indoor is tastefully furnished in a contemporary style designed to complement the natural surroundings. Tall mature trees provide a very pleasant shade all around this attractive honey-coloured stone house which offers several relaxing spots on the large terrace and around the pool. Enjoy the secluded atmosphere of this little jewel of a house tucked in a typical Provencal setting.

This is quintessential Provence with extensive lavender and poppy fields, forested hills, the mountains of the Dentelles de Montmirail mountain range with Mont Ventoux proudly towering over them, the Grand Luberon park and its many quaint villages full of character and unaltered charm. Pernes-les-Fontaines is only two miles away from this villa, offering visitors the chance to sample local flavours and to fully immerse in Southern France's slow pace of life. This area is dotted with many attractive towns, all within easy reach of this property; Carpentras, Sorgue, Avignon and Orange all make very enjoyable day trips from this charming Provencal villa.

Ground floor: Large open plan living room, patio door to terrace and garden, outdoor dining facilities. Fully fitted kitchen/ breakfast area, door to terrace and pergola. Cloak room, WC. Double bedroom (2m) (TV) ensuite bathroom. Separate WC. Double bedroom (2m) (TV), ensuite shower room, walk-in shower, WC. Twin bedroom (2 x 1m), French door to small private terrace, ensuite shower room, walk-in shower, WC. First floor (attic accessed via spiral staircase leading directly into the bedroom - care with children): Family bedroom (4 x 1m), low sloping ceilings. Living area. Separate shower room, WC. Grounds: Small mature garden, with tall trees, shrubs and lawn areas. Private 7m x 4m heated pool (depth: 1.20m to 1.80m, Roman steps), decking area. Pergola, outdoor dining facilities, portable gas BBQ. Boule pitch, table tennis table. Parking for up to 3 cars.

Great little stone house with all that one needs to relax and enjoy life in Provence. Just 5 minutes away is the small gem of Pernes-les-Fontaines, a typical Provencal market village offering a bit of everything, from small mouth-watering melons, to plump tomatoes and juicy apricots, as well as olive oils, lavender honey and typical Provencal goat cheese quaintly wrapped in autumn-tinted chestnut leaves which are perfect match for Provence's delicious rose wines. What a place to experience the French lifestyle!
