Le Clocher, Cadenet, Luberon National Park, Provence, France Vacation Rental

 

Exclusive contemporary vacation villa in Provence – Built on a hilltop in the heart of the Luberon Natural Park, Le Clocher is a contemporary designer villa set in open countryside, with beautiful views over the surrounding fields, hills and village of Cadenet. The tall belfry tower of the local church stands out from this verdant landscape and it is clearly visible from the house, situated under a mile away from this typical Provencal village, with its feudal castle, weekly market and open air theatre.

The modern architectural style and minimal furnishing of this elegant property makes use of glass panels, sliding doors and large windows to maximise the light and increase the sense of space. The garden has a large covered terrace, perfect for outdoor dining, where you can sit, relax and fully immerse yourself in the gentle Provencal ambience.

Thanks to its superb location, Le Clocher is an ideal base for your exploration of the region. In fact, this area offers plenty of opportunities for outdoor activities such as horse riding, fishing, hiking, cycling and archery. Avignon is further afield but its dramatic ramparts, fascinating Palais du Papes and artistic treasures certainly deserve a visit during your holiday in Provence.

Separate shower room, walk-in shower, access to roof terrace. Double bedroom (140cm), views over the village and countryside, ensuite bathroom, walk-in shower. Separate WC shared by both bedrooms. Grounds: 2000 m2 enclosed garden, private 9m x 8m pool (Roman steps, depth: 1.10m to 2.50m), large covered terrace with canopy, gas BBQ, outdoor dining facilities, sun loungers. Ample shaded parking space for 4 cars. Please note that due to drops in the grounds and various low walls we do not recommend this property for very young children.

After a dark dreary winter this would be a great place to recharge your batteries, the villa being a contemporary house with lots of large windows, so exceptionally light. It is a simply furnished holiday house set in unspoilt terrain but with the convenience of being near the village, just five minutes walk to Cadenet. If you venture out to the village of Loumarin, you will find several restaurants: try the Auberge La Fenice for international cuisine, a lovely atmosphere and great views
