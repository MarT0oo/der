Oasis, Biarritz, France Vacation Rental

 

Step inside and discover the open space, luxury amenities, and stunning architecture that turn a French villa into a piece of contemporary art. Amidst the smaller apartments and townhomes common in Biarritz, Oasis stands out as one of the largest rentals available in the heart of the city—perfect for get-togethers and large, active families who want easy access to the city’s many attractions.

Designed into a U-shape, with a 15-meter in-ground pool (another rarity in Biarritz proper) forming one of the brackets, this villa includes 4 bedrooms and plenty of open gathering space. Raw building materials like wood, concrete, and floor-to-ceiling glass create a minimalist dwelling that—thanks to architectural magic—also feels warm and inviting. The fireplace in the living area adds to the warmth, as does the flood of natural light.

A sleek kitchen with built-in cabinets, gleaming white counters, top-of-the-line integrated appliances, and view of the pool and courtyard through sky-high windows gives the chef in your group a one-of-a-kind setting for preparing gourmet meals. Even better? The villa’s proximity to Biarritz’s fresh markets makes it a breeze to gather high-quality ingredients.

As the person in charge of the reservation for your group, you should feel welcome to claim one of two particularly outstanding sleeping options: the master bedroom with in-suite closet and a bathroom with a deep soaker tub and views of a private “winter garden,” or a guest house with modern white walls, private deck, and 2 bedrooms with their own bathrooms.

Very few guests have had the pleasure of visiting this Biarritz villa, as it was just constructed in the summer of 2013. Be among the first to see it in person, and discover beauty that even photos can’t capture.

Have a question? Call our on-call manager any time of the day. We also provide personal concierge services to make your stay as comfortable and carefree as possible.
