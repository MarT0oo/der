Villa Menerbes, Menerbes, Provence, France Vacation Rental

 

Provencal villa within walking distance of Menerbes with stunning Luberon views. Villa Menerbes is a beautiful Provencal, family home, located on a hill and surrounded by a landscape of vineyards, olive groves and cherry orchards with views across the Luberon massif, the Monts de Vaucluse and the Mont Ventoux. Just 500 metres away, the charming village of Menerbes, described as one of the most beautiful villages in France, offers an authentic taste of Provencal village life with the market, shops and restaurants.

Spacious and attractive, this villa was designed by an architect in traditional Provencal style with a large open fireplace and terracotta tiled floors. It is decorated with warm, Mediterranean colours and has a recently renovated kitchen and air-conditioned first floor bedroom. The enchanting garden offers views from every corner, whether dining under the shade of a tree, soaking up the sun around the pool, enjoying a game of boules, or just sitting and relaxing with a good book and a glass of local wine.

This is a perfect location for both walking and cycling. Apart from exploring the surrounding countryside, enjoy visiting the ‘Golden Triangle of villages’ Bonnieux, Lacoste, Gordes, Roussillion and Lourmarin, which host colourful Provencal markets and are all within easy driving distance. There are many local vineyards where you can enjoy wine tasting. This is also a great base from which to visit historic Avignon and Aix en Provence; there is plenty of opportunity for sightseeing.
