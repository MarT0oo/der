Panorama, Biarritz, France Vacation Rental

 

The moment you enter this impressing villa you will immediately understand why it got the name “Panorama.” While there are many highlights in this property —the villa’s closeness to Biarritz and numerous beaches, the expansive infinity pool and the four comfortable bedroom suites just to name a few—it’s the views of the Pyrenees and the scenery of the Basque Country that will take your breath away.

With high quality Moroccan linen and Indian artwork, Panorama boasts all the luxuries that can be expected from a five-star resort, with the convenience and the space of a private vacation home. The De Dietrich gourmet kitchen has everything you need to prepare exquisite meals at home.

From both, the brightly lit living room and the downstairs bedrooms you access the poolside decks, where from its two lounges you can enjoy the magnificent view. The rooms are furnished in a minimalist style, which compliments the villa’s natural environs. The villa’s open floor setting and generous rooms can comfortably accommodate up to eight guests with enough space for privacy. Additionally, Panorama features air conditioning in all rooms; a fully equipped gym and an on-call concierge will do its best to ensure your vacation exceeds all your expectations.

Panorama is located in a quiet surrounding, less than 5 minutes from Biarritz and only minutes from the charming fishing village Bidart. While Biarritz is famous for its exclusive restaurants and world-class surf, there is a lot to fall for in Bidart too, for example the many authentic Basque mansions or the fanciful cafés.

This original villa is sure to book fast, you better reserve your dates now!
