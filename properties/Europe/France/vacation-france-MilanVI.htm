Milan VI, Rue de Milan, Paris, France Vacation Rental

 

Found in the very heart of Paris, this stunning four-bedroom apartment unites refinement with comfort. Located on Rue de Milan in the Haussmann-Saint-Lazare-Opéra district, guests can take a short stroll from the apartment and into the heart of the city. With an abundance of excellent shops, entertainment venues and restaurants, and ten minutes walk from prestigious Galeries Lafayette; guests are ensured an unforgettable stay. From the grand Corinthian columns of the Madeleine Church to the stunning Chagall ceiling of the Opera Garnier; the apartment offers its guests some truly amazing opportunities for sightseeing. With the Haussmann-Saint-Lazare train station within walking distance, the apartment is also an ideal base for those who wish to travel further.

Imbued with an eclectic design in shades of grey, tan and blue; our local team of interior designers has transform this apartment into an oasis of tranquility in the Ville Lumiere. The elegant forms of the furniture complement the décor beautifully. Characterized by bold design elements, the apartment has many highlights such as the intricate, monochrome wall mural in the living area, the textured accent walls in a selection of the sleeping areas and the unique, unfinished ceiling that offsets the elegant interiors. With airy, light filled rooms and a stylish and functional kitchen; the apartment offers a wonderful combination of home comforts and hotel amenities, with the space and comfort of four bedrooms and two bathrooms.

Whether you're visiting with family, friends or the other half, this holiday apartment in Paris is the perfect place to make your base during your trip to the French capital.
