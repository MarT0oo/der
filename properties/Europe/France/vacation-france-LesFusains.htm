Les Fusains, Mougins, Cote D'azur, France Vacation Rental

 

Les Fusains is a lovely family holiday villa in France, set on a gated domaine near Mougins. It is spacious and modern with light, bright colours adding a welcoming touch. A large covered terrace offers a comfortable outdoor living space overlooking the pool and garden.

The hilltop village of Mougins, which has an excellent selection of restaurants, pavement cafes and art galleries, is less than 2 miles away. Cannes with its elegant shops, sandy beach, restaurants and pavement cafes is only 3 miles and Antibes, with its attractive harbour and daily Provençal market, is just a 15-minute drive. The town's medieval castle has been converted into a museum dedicated to Picasso, and children in the party are bound to love Aquasplash and Marineland.

Golf enthusiasts will appreciate the exclusive Royal Golf de Mougins course which is only a few minutes drive from this family holiday villa in France.
