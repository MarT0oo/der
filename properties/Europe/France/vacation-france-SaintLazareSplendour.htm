Saint Lazare Splendour, 8th district, Paris, France Vacation Rental

 

This apartment has a perfect mixture of traditional Parisian décor. High ceilings and elegant architrave paired with eclectic yet homely furnishings will impress you as soon as you walk through the door. Set across two floors and with a variety of communal areas including a study, dining room, and lounge, this is the perfect apartment to relax, entertain or attend to business. There are a 4 bedrooms with various size beds from Queen to Single, capable of housing up to 7 people making it an ideal rental for large families or groups. As with any AvenueStory rental the apartment comes with all kitchen utensils, household appliances and freshly laundered bed linen to make sure you feel right at home. Located in an area equipped with luxury fashion labels and many cultural landmarks within walking distance this would be an ideal spot to discover Paris.

Just off the Place Saint Augustin, this apartment is located between the glorious 8th and the charming 17th arrondissement of Paris. Here you are seven minutes from the Champs Elysées and surrounded by every French fashion and luxury brand. Apart from the chic shopping, there are museums and theatres as well as a slew of Michelin-starred restaurants. Some of the area's notable landmarks include the Arc de Triomphe, Place de la Concorde, Grand Palais and Petit Palais as well as the Museum of Modern Art of Paris. And you are also peacefully located away from the worst of the city's noise and yet within a heartbeat of central Paris. Whether you fancy an early-morning jog or a late picnic lunch, the peaceful space of the Parc Monceau lies at your feet. Painted frequently by Monet, it is marked by its informal, 'English' style of planting. No visit is complete before you have found the replica Egyptian pyramid, the Dutch windmill and the Chinese fort.

Just a few blocks away, you will find a delicious restaurant: the 114 Faubourg. For a Parisian chic dinner, you should go to this one Michelin star brasserie. The flavours are plentiful, service outstanding, and décor incredibly elegant. The artichoke soup with fois gras is memorable! Enjoy
