Monsigny I, Rue Monsigny 7, Paris, France Vacation Rental

 

Only 6 minutes’ walk from the Palais Royale, the 4-bedroom Monsigny I vacation apartment will plunge you into the charm and elegance of Paris. In the heart of the grand 1st arrondissement of Paris, on the banks of the River Seine, you and your family will be surrounded by stunning architecture and high culture. A 15-minute walk brings you to the world-famous Louvre Museum, and the Tuileries Garden is only 10 minutes away on foot. Restaurants, bars, and sidewalk cafes are everywhere you look, and several subway and bus stops put the rest of the city at your fingertips.

Blending modern, sleek surfaces with whimsical decorative accents, our team of local interior designers have brought together antique and contemporary Paris. The 4 luxurious bedrooms will make your whole party feel welcome, and the cushy hotel-style beds provide a haven for tired travelers. Prepare a French feast in the state-of-the-art kitchen, then enjoy it together around the 8-seater table, with views of the city from the picture windows. Or perch on the retro purple sofa with a cup of espresso, taking in the sunlight that streams into the living room. This apartment is located on the first floor, making for easy access to the city’s boulevards and sidewalks.

Perfect for family retreats or groups of friends, this spacious holiday apartment will give you a taste of Paris.
