Parisian Mansion, Rue Reaumur 81 , Paris, France Vacation Rental

 

Spacious, colorful, and full of surprises, the 5-bedroom Parisian Mansion vacation home is a Parisian dream come true. This villa is located in the central, historic Les Halles neighborhood, where classic Paris attractions and hip cafes await you. An 8-minute walk brings you to the iconic Notre Dame Cathedral, and a 12-minute stroll leads to the Royal Palace & Gardens. Walk 15 minutes to take in the art of the Louvre, then boost your energy at one of the area’s many fine restaurants or bakeries. The Sentier and Réaumur-Sébastopol metro stations, along with several bus stops, are just outside your door.

Our local interior designer has created a funky, light-filled holiday home that can sleep up to 12 guests. Each of the 5 bedrooms has its own unique color scheme, as do the comfy living areas, and the state-of-the-art kitchen has everything you need for a French feast. Bright colors and quirky patterns give the space character. The whole family will enjoy unusual perks such as a fitness room, a ping-pong table, darts, and a kids’ climbing wall. Premium amenities include free Wi-Fi, cable TV, hotel beds and linens, luxury toiletries, and a Nespresso machine.

With easy access to the highlights of Paris, this charming vacation villa is perfect for your next family retreat.
