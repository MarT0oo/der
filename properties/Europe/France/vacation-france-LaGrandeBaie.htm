La Grande Baie, Cavaliere, St. Tropez, France Vacation Rental

 

This is a modern holiday villa with a fabulous location within walking distance of Cavaliere beach: it overlooks the beach and the horseshoe-shaped coastline of the Bay of Cavaliere and Cap Negre, and also offers panoramic views towards Les Iles d’Or.

Equipped with all modern comforts in a contemporary style, La Grande Baie offers an ideal location for those wishing to experience the unspoilt charm of this renowned seaside region along the French Riviera. Wake up every morning to the lovely view of the Mediterranean Sea and enjoy your breakfast from either of the two large terraces. The uniquely shaped pool offers ledges within the water for sunbathing, a swim against currrent for exercising and hydrotherapy jets to help revitalise you.

Thanks to its ideal situation on the border between St Tropez/Var and Provence, La Grande Baie is an excellent base for exploring these very diverse regions. If you go inland you will encounter peaceful rolling countryside dotted with hilltop villages each featuring typical weekly markets. On the other side of your villa, the stretch of coastline towards Le Lavandou offers spectacular sceneries, fine sandy beaches, turquoise sea and a number of lively resorts with cafes, marinas and palm tree-lined promenades where you can spend unforgettable days during your vacation in South of France.

There is a private track for residents of the domaine only which runs along the side of this villa, leading downhill, through a secure gate and onto a small lane below where you can then choose to walk along the road into the village or take one of several tracks to the beach. Whichever route you take, you must cross the coast road (there are pedestrian crossings).
