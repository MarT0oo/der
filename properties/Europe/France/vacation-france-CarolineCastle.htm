Caroline Castle, Aix-en-Provence, France


Caroline Castle is a work of magic, wonderful views of the 12 Acre amazing property can be seen from almost every wonderful pained glass window. Located just 4 miles from Provence City of Aix-en-Provence, lending itself to the wonderful shops and dining of the area. Aix-en-Provence carries such riches as Aix Catherdral, Musée Granet a museum in the area devoted to painting and the amazing artists that painted this region, Pavillon Vendome is a wonderful Historical Home built in 1665 and it has been kept wonderfully. Along with amazing sites there are the fantastic cafes where each bite of Pastries reminds you why you came to France, to enjoy each wonderful bite of their best kept secrets.

The Castle has been completely updated to today's standards of comfort and luxury, while maintaining its French History and charm. Each suite has a wonderful feeling, from the tapestries on the windows to the hand sewn heirlooms throughout. The owners have let the Castle speak for itself, a truly magical experience. A mixture of formal and casual French antique furniture and decorations were used throughout the interior.

Most people come to walk our gardens, extensive gardens are a perfect spot for outdoor entertaining functions and the 1,800-ft² chapel could be used for an indoor event, such as a concert, wedding or dinner party. We have had many beautiful weddings here that fill the rooms with laughter and fun, each one more unique than the last.

Our Castle Caroline tennis courts and outstanding pool were built to sit beautifully within our 17th Century ruins.
