Villa Canoubwest, St Tropez, France Vacation Rental

 

Villa Canoubwest St Tropez France Luxury Vacation Rental is dreamexoticrentals’ compelling new 5-bedroom property, a grand Mediterranean masterpiece seated in 8,000 square meters of stunning landscaped gardens. Located in an exclusive residential area of St Tropez, this expansive property has direct access to the sea and a breathtaking 20m heated infinity pool. Its rolling, emerald lawns are perfectly bordered with Mediterranean planting, palms, pines and bougainvillea. The perfect venue for sophisticated garden parties or large family gatherings.

This traditional yet opulent terracotta villa is adorned with sumptuous fittings and finishes, gleaming marble, soft Italian stone and elegant parquet. The two-bathroom master suite, Bulthaup kitchen, bespoke furnishings and fine art complete this luxurious property. Its spacious terraces and large, airy rooms look out to panoramic views of Bay de Canebiers’ azure waters, while its closed, rear courtyard offers a more intimate space. The property’s vast plot, extensive pool, ample private parking and staff apartment make it both very private and a rare find

Villa Canoubwest is but a stone’s throw from the beautiful Plage de Canebiers, a secluded stretch of powdery sand, hidden away from the well-known Pampelonne Beach. This wonderful bay offers the possibility of mooring your boat within view of the property. As with all of dreamexoticrentals Properties, this magnificent villa is fully serviced and secure.

Adjacent to the residence of Brigitte Bardot, the actress who transformed St-Tropez to the glittering jet-set destination of today, Villa Canoubwest is located in an extremely private residential area of St Tropez. Tucked away on a quiet, private cul-de-sac, the other side of the peninsula from the popular Pampelonne Beach, this magnificent villa is a moment’s stroll from the endearing waters of the picturesque Baie de Canebier. The perfect bay to moor your boat and enjoy an enchanting stretch of beach with its powdery sand and soothing umbrella pines.

Despite its secluded location, Villa Canoubwest is less than a 10-minute drive from the centre of St-Tropez with its atmospheric Vieux Port where gleaming yachts are moored and lively cafes spill out onto cobbled streets. The most glamorous destination in France, St-Tropez oozes both charm and decadence. Here you will find everything from designer boutiques, gastronomic restaurants and gourmet food shops to bustling fish markets, art galleries, blissful beach bars and scenic hiking paths It is home to Arnaud Donckele’s three-Michelin-starred restaurant, La Vague d’Or, with a cuisine inspired by the flavours of Provence.
