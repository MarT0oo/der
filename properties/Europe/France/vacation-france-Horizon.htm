Horizon, Biarritz, France Vacation Rental

 

With its clean lines and bold furnishings, this ultra-modern villa has the feel of a modern art gallery or luxe showroom. It’s also the largest property in our portfolio—making it perfect for weddings, photo shoots, or corporate events.

Horizon offers unparalleled luxury for guests who want space, privacy, and all the amenities of a high-end vacation rental. The villa boasts seven bedrooms, four of which have in-suite bathrooms. The master bedroom is like an apartment in itself—it has a huge bed, a spacious walk-in dressing room, an office, a lounge area, and a private deck that’s almost the same size as the bedroom. The dramatic interplay of light in the room creates a space that is both sophisticated and inviting. The luxurious master bathroom includes an Italian shower, a bathtub, a separate toilet, and a towel warmer. All of the rooms are outfitted with hotel-quality linens, and a daily maid service is included with your stay. There is also a private office that, like the rest of the house, speaks to a modern sense of style. The villa’s colorful accents lend a sense of balance and whimsy to an otherwise clean interior.

In addition to the open-design kitchen in the main part of the villa, you’ll find a separate kitchen toward the back that is perfect for catering or hosting a personal chef. It has three ovens, two professional-size refrigerators, and everything else you need to throw first-class parties. The villa also has a game room, home theater, and a TV lounge that is separate from the formal living room.

Outdoors, you’ll find two terraces, a jai alai court, and an expansive lawn that melds into the surrounding countryside. The two-tiered pool is flanked by two wooden decks, and there are plenty of places to sunbathe while enjoying panoramic views of the Pyrenees. Off to the side, you’ll find a separate apartment for the groundskeeper and his wife, who are both warm and welcoming without getting in your way. Should any issues arise, the groundskeeper is more than happy to assist you, as is the on-call property manager.

Horizon is conveniently located less than 10 minutes from downtown Biarritz, making it the perfect retreat after a day of shopping, exploring, or lounging on the beach. As you sit on the terrace overlooking the Basque countryside, you’ll truly feel like you’re away from it all.
