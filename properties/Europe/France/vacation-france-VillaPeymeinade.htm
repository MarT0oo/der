Villa Peymeinade, Speracedes, Cote D'azur, France Vacation Rental

 

Cote d'Azur country villa with pool & views – Tucked away at the end of a cul-de-sac on the edge of Peymeinade near Grasse, this villa sits in a tranquil, residential location enjoying lovely views over the valley and surrounding hills towards Cabris and Speracedes. With an excellent restaurant and shops within two miles, and the beach of Cannes under 30 minutes’ drive, this delightful family villa is the ideal base from which to explore the Cote d’Azur.

This charming family home has lots of holiday appeal; dine on the balcony, sunbathe by the pool or just read a book under the covered terrace and you cannot help but unwind and relax in this tranquil setting. Opportunities for outdoor pursuits are also at hand; whether you fancy hiking, cycling, horse riding, playing golf and tennis or even practising water sports on the nearby Lac de St Cassien, don’t look any further, this is the place for you.

Moreover, you have the entire Cote d’Azur literally at your feet! Cannes is within half-an-hour’s drive for a huge selection of trendy restaurants and boutiques a la mode, for the Film Festival, the Casino and La Bocca beach clubs. Stroll along the palm-tree lined promenade for a chance to meet celebrities and film stars and experience the glamour of this exclusive holiday destination. Whatever you do, wherever you go, you will always be delighted to come back to your charming villa in the hills, and leave all the hustle and bustle behind.
