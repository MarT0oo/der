Duret, Rue Duret, Paris, France Vacation Rental

 

Nestled in the glamorous and popular Champs-Élysées neighborhood, this 2-bedroom Duret vacation apartment offers a home in the heart of Paris. Just down the street is the magnificent Arc de Triomphe (9 min). Venture a little farther and you come to the bustling Champs-Élysées Avenue (13 min), home to some of the best shopping in Paris. Spend an afternoon strolling in the Bois de Boulogne Gardens (14 min). The Argentine metro stop and buses are just a block away.

Carefully designed by our local interior designer, this apartment is meant to make you feel at ease. With cozy interiors and elegant furnishings, the spacious rooms provide a relaxing getaway. Bold, modern art and tall, classic windows give the space a true Parisian feel. Premium amenities include free WiFi, cable TV, hotel beds and linens, luxury toiletries, and a Nespresso machine.

Perfect for families or groups of friends, this apartment will welcome you to the heart of Paris.
