Miramar, Biarritz, France Vacation Rental

 

The most breath-taking beachfront property in our portfolio – Miramar – boasts views of the Bay of Biscay from its private terrace, which are incomparable.

Located in the very heart of historic Biarritz, this in 2011 fully renovated apartment can be called a perfect mix between contemporary design and the charm of the old, majestic days.

The centrepiece of this vacation home is definitely the open-concept living area. Only the huge sliding glass door separates you from the private terrace from where you have sweeping views of the endless ocean. The adjoining wall with its graceful glass-panelled French doors offers another splendid perspective of the lighthouse of Biarritz and the beach right below you.

With its modern interior, the high ceilings and the brick wall, the living room eradiates the feeling of a metropolitan loft. A bar area, which seats up to six people is part of the fully equipped kitchen. The shiny black cabinets, the stainless steel appliances and a stove you would normally only find in top class restaurants invite you to prepare exquisite dinners…

Miramar and its fully renovated three bedrooms provide a delightful 5-star- hotel feeling. The master bedroom boasts with a queen-size bed and an in-suite Italian shower. Another bathroom – for the other two bedrooms – is just next-door. While one can be converted into a king size bed or two single beds, you do also have one room with a queen-size bed. Our on-call concierge will make sure your stay exceeds all your expectations.

With the quiet and uncrowded Miramar Beach right under your luxury vacation home, you cannot get any closer to the beach. As our valued guest you can also access the heated pool, spa or fitness centre of the Sofitel, which is right next-door, for half of the usual price.

The neighbourhood has a long and illustrious past—the Hôtel du Palais and Imperial Spa are just a few steps away and downtown Biarritz is a 3-minute walk from Miramar. Two parking spaces are included and are located right next to the entrance.

As you might expect, the only beachfront property in our portfolio gets to be booked well in advance. Make sure you block your dates today!
