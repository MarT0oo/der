Rue de Bourgogne Chic, 7th district, Paris, France Vacation Rental

 

This apartment is exquisitely designed with the perfect mixture of old Parisian features and modern furniture. It fits in perfectly with the area in which it is situated as you will be surrounded by Paris’ elite as well as being within close proximity to many beautiful landmarks.

As you enter the flat you will be greeted with high ceilings throughout and a spacious living area. If you fancy picking up some local delicacies from nearby deli’s you will be able to cook up a storm in a chic kitchen and sit down to relax in your home away from home at the dining table which seats up to 6. There are also 2 generous bedrooms, the master is treated to a king size bed as well a beautiful ensuite, and the second room has a queen size bed and lovely views of the quaint streets below as well as a main bathroom completed with a bath.

Located on Left Bank, or as a Parisian would say the ‘Rive Gauche’, the 7th arrondissement is set in the heart of Paris. Many of Paris’ most beautiful landmarks, such as the Eiffel Tower, the Ecole Militaire, the Musee d’Orsay and the Hotel des Invalides call the 7th home but do not forget to explore the neighbourhood itself, as it is one of the most beautiful areas in Paris. It is a popular residential area with high-ranking officials and as a result has an upmarket, restrained atmosphere a little like London's Westminster or the Upper East Side in New York. Here you can jog in the Champs de Mars, enjoy art and lunch in the stunning Musee du Quai Branly and stroll around the wide, tree-lined streets feeling like a true, well-heeled Parisian. The 7th arrondissement has it all: beautiful landmarks, comfortable neighbourhood restaurants, bakeries, corner cafés and charming street markets.

Our Secret Tip:

Our very favourite terrace in Paris is le Faust! This amazing place is located not far from this apartment at the foot of the Esplanade des Invalides in the centre of the 7th arrondissement under the Pont Alexandre III, Paris’ most beautiful bridge. To us, this is the new must in the capital: chic and classic. Enjoy a nice glass of pink champagne while you watch the sun set over the Seine!
