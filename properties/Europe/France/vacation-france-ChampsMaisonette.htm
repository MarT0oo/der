Champs Maisonette, 8th Dist., Paris, France Vacation Rental

 

With tall ceilings and strong light, this one bedroom has a dramatic sense of space, particularly evidenced by the room-defining decorative fireplace in the main salon. Stay just two minutes away from the Champs Elysees in this recently renovated apartment with hardwood floors throughout.

The main salon has an open floor plan with modern architectural elements, and is styled with cool greys and whites against the blond wood accents of the room. A comfortably large L-shaped couch sits opposite the decorative fireplace and bookshelf, while over the couch hangs a stylish floor lamp. The salon also features two large French windows, two side chairs, and electronics for your use.

Just behind the sofa is the dining area with seating for four, a modern look, and window view.

To the left sits the recessed kitchen, which architecturally mirrors the focal point of the main salon. Well-equipped and with plenty of storage, the kitchen also provides a Nespresso machine.

The master bedroom offers a queen-sized bed and large windows that are easily opened for a view or a breeze. This room also has ample closet space and individual bedside lamps. The en suite bathroom is well styled, and has floor to ceiling mirrors, a state-of-the-art shower, towel warmer, washbasin, and WC.

Right off the Franklin Roosevelt stop, this rental offers up prime location. Things really don't get any better than the Golden Triangle. Located between the Champs Elysees and the Seine, this area is known for exquisite shops, galleries, dining experiences, and sightseeing opportunities.

Nothing could be better than a picnic at the Tuileries Gardens. Except maybe sampling the French Haute Cusine at Laserre. But, electing to stay in the Golden Triangle means that you can easily walk to both options.
