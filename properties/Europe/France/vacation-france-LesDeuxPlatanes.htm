Les Deux Platanes, La Mole, nr. Cogolin, St Tropez Var, France Vacation Rental

 

Cogolin private holiday villa with pool - This traditional Mas is located in peaceful countryside in the heart of a nature reserve near the charming village of La Mole. Built over one hundred years ago, this beautiful property sits on a gentle grassy slope at the edge of a protected forest, surrounded by vineyards and the luxuriant countryside of the Massif des Maures, yet is only 6 miles from the coast – it really is a rarity to find such a combination!

Tasteful French décor and traditional features combine with modern amenities to create a luxurious holiday home. What better way to enjoy this tranquil setting than to dine al fresco on the terrace under the dappled shade of two beautiful Plane trees.

The village of La Mole has a great local restaurant and an award-winning bakery, and Cogolin is within 5 miles for twice weekly markets. But the beauty of this location must be its proximity to St Tropez and the wonderful, sandy beaches along this stretch of coastline with their vibrant beachside cafes. Golf, tennis, horse riding and go-karting are just some of the activities you might enjoy, along with a diversity of entertainment ranging from children's fun fairs to polo matches.
