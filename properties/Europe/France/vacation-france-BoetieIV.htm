Boetie IV, Champs Elysées, Paris, France Vacation Rental

 

Steps from the chic Champs-Elysées boulevard, the fashion mecca of Paris, the 3-bedroom Boétie IV vacation apartment in the 8th arrondissement is as sophisticated as its surroundings and is near many cultural sites. Walk along the famous tree-lined boulevard to enjoy gourmet dining, exclusive boutiques diverse cafes, and theatres. Fabulous nightlife is only a stroll away; revel in a glitzy cabaret at Le Lido de Paris (5 min). Many classic sites can be reached easily on foot, such as the Arc de Triomphe and the Grand Palais (12 min). Bus stops are right outside the apartment, and the George V metro station is 4 minutes' walk away. Or, take a short drive to view the Arc de Triomphe (6 minutes), and then cross the Seine to visit the Eiffel Tower (10 minutes).

Our expert local interior designers echoed the elegance of the area within the rooms of this Paris vacation rental. With a mix of classic and contemporary style, eight guests can stay in sophisticated comfort. The five marble fireplaces topped by framed mirrors, and the 3m high ceiling and molded cornices, make the interior grand. Hardwood floors complement the refined color palette of crème, yellow, gold, pastels and browns. You can find in the living room plush sofa and soft chairs, and an amazing old fireplace, which invite relaxation, and the large dining room is perfect for enjoying local cuisine. The kitchen of this serviced apartments in Paris is sleek and modern, with full-sized appliances and all essentials, such as a Nespresso machine, microwave and breakfast nook.

Roomy bathrooms gleam with glass surfaces. Plush bedrooms, decorated with antique wallpaper and generous pillows, will lull you to sleep; the fully equipped kitchen has everything you need to prepare a feast. Large windows bring in natural light and provide a view of the fashionable area. Premium amenities include free Wi-Fi, cable TV, hotel beds and linens, luxury amenities, and a Nespresso machine.

Fit for royalty, this exclusive family holiday apartment in Paris is perfect for sipping a glass of Cabernet Sauvignon after exploring the cultural attractions of this upscale region.
