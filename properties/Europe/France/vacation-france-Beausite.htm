Beausite, Biarritz, France Vacation Rental

 

Nestled on top of a hill in Biarritz, in one of the area’s most exclusive neighbourhoods, Beausite offers guests utter privacy & seclusion. Beausite means beautiful place in French and this exceptionally beautiful villa certainly lives up to its name.

The luxuriously spacious and open plan living arrangement play host to modern and contemporary furnishings and Pop Art design whilst the villa’s classic architectural heritage is celebrated. Opulent features such as the wonderfully grand fireplace in the living room and exposed wooden beams throughout maintain an intimate feeling of character.

The perfect mix of modern comfort and rustic charm invite you to relax in style and allow you to enjoy sweeping and pleasant views of the well proportioned garden and green spaces that surround the property. Alfresco style dining can be enjoyed outside on any one of the numerous terraces that adjoin the property and the heated pool entices guests to bathe at their leisure.

Beausite easily accommodates up to 15 guests in its 7 bedrooms. The master bedroom on the top floor is particularly indulgent with its private living space, spacious dressing room, luxury bathroom and queen-sized bed. Far reaching ocean views can also be enjoyed from the bedroom window. The other 6 bedrooms share 4 bathrooms spread over two floors: 4 bedrooms are located on the ground floor and 2 bedrooms are located on the lower ground floor.

The villa also comes with a fully equipped kitchen, and ample parking for up to five vehicles.
