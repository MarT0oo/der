Chalet Totara, Courchevel, France Vacation Rental

 

The Chalet Totara is excellently positioned to enjoy and explore the expansive Three Valley region. Located in the Nogentil area of Courchevel 1850, approximately 50 metres from the Pralong Piste and chair lift.

The chalet interior is warm and welcoming, as well as being extremely spacious. There are two living rooms; one of which is 60sqm. with a central open fireplace and large windows to let in lots of sunlight and provide stunning views over the Alps. A large sun terrace is the perfect place to soak up the spring sunshine or you can relax and unwind in the outdoor hot tub surrounded by the magnificent mountain scenery; pure bliss! Chalet Totara sleeps up to 10 people in five bedrooms and there are five bathrooms in the chalet, four of these are ensuite bathrooms. All are beautifully furnished with cosy fabrics and completed with the latest mod-cons to make your stay as comfortable as possible.

Flexibility is offered when it comes to the chalet catering which is always a nice touch. A private chef and head waiter will be on hand to serve you a delicious breakfast and either lunch or dinner in the privacy of your own dining room.
