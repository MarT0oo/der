Champs Elysees Chic, 8th district , Paris, France Vacation Rental

 

Trip the light fantastic in this superb Haussmann classic, the subject of a complete and stunning renovation by a noted interior designer. You could only be in Paris here as the moulded plasterwork, marble fireplaces and great expanses of polished parquet flooring create an effortless, elegant backdrop to your own dramatic life. The renovation has emphasised the apartment's majestic proportions, the French doors opening from every room onto the wrap-around ironwork balcony. Here you can plan your day over a fresh croissant or write postcards with a glass of wine at your side: the quiet residential street and 5th floor vantage offer views and calm. The layout maximises flexibility: while the master bedroom is luxurious and spacious, a second room off the main reception room can be used as a further bedroom (there is a shower room adjoining) or as extra entertaining space – the choice is yours. The calibre of this refurbishment is outstanding: the bathroom fittings first rate; the statement lighting one-of-a-kind. Even washing up is poetic as the view from the sparkling new kitchen includes the Eiffel Tower herself. From the light-filled hallway, with a useful office corner, through the apartment the spaces flow one into another and ooze sophistication and style.

Location in the glorious 8th is as prime as this apartment's interior: here on the quiet, no through road of Rue Robert Estienne you are in the heart of Paris' golden triangle, between two of Haussmann's grandest triumphs - the Avenue Georges V and the Champs Elysées. Here you can visit the biggest Louis Vuitton store in the world and every French fashion and luxury brand too. Apart from the chic shopping, there are museums and theatres as well as a slew of Michelin-starred restaurants. Some of the area's notable landmarks include the Arc de Triomphe, Place de la Concorde, Grand Palais and Petit Palais as well as the Museum of Modern Art of Paris. Just 200 metres away is Franklin Rooseveld metro station that offers access to any point in the capital and beyond. For those willing to brave Paris traffic there are two public parking garages along the Champs Elysées too. No wonder this area of Paris has the most expensive real estate in France.
