Brune VIII, 14th arrondissement, Paris, France Vacation Rental

 

This three bedroom holiday apartment in Paris lies in a sophisticated part of the city close to the Parc des Expositions and the Porte de Versailles. Casual dining is plentiful in the area while the fascinating Musée Louis Pasteur is a ten-minute stroll away. Just a few minutes further is the Parc Georges-Brassens perfect for weekend family fun. When the shops call all the high-end names can be found at Beaugrenelle just a couple of Metro stops away.

Our talented local designer has really caught the atmosphere of this Parisian residential district with the chic colour palette and comfortable contemporary furniture of this incredibly spacious apartment. The addition of a double sofa bed in the living area means this vacation rental in Paris has plenty of room for eight guests. The large living and dining area contains a clever chef's table style kitchenette and is the main focal point of this rental. A colour scheme of cream and mocha has subtle blue highlights while a leather punchbag adds a quirky touch. Large arched windows let light flood into every corner of the apartment while the relaxed colours are continued throughout the bedrooms.

Everything about this holiday apartment in Paris tempts you to kick off your shoes and relax with a glass of French wine at the end of a day's sightseeing.
