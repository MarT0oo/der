Chic and sporty station

Val d'Isere is known around the world for the quality of its ski exceptional ski area: Espace Killy . But the resort also offers a range of high end. Services Elegance, quality, well-being are the watchwords of Val d'Isere which attracted vacationers worldwide.

The ski area offers an incredible variety of tracks. Thus, the resort delights both expert skiers and beginners. Learning to ski is easy and secure: many specialty areas are available in the resort. If Val d'Isère is above all an exceptional ski resort, it is also a village of a thousand faces ...

The Sports Centre is open for après-ski relaxation or workouts more intense: With 5,000 m² of which 780 m² of water bodies, holidaymakers are spoiled for choice: swimming, climbing room, multi-activity sports fitness, squash, sauna, steam room, spa, etc.

And after exercise, pleasure as Val d'Isère offers a myriad of gastronomic outings, festive and authentic ... Stroll through the elegant streets of the village is a real pleasure and shopping lovers will also be pleased that gourmets and night owls ...
