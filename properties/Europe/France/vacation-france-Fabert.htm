Fabert, 7th district, Paris, France Vacation Rental

 

Experience new adventures with splendor in this unique apartment that welcomes up to 4 guests. Situated on the 4th floor of a posh building (with elevator access), push open the doors and cut across a lush courtyard to find this abode in the heart of Paris' prized 7th district.

Extending 484 sq ft, the Fabert opens onto a cheery and bright living room that directly overlooks the Esplanade des Invalides and its majestic Golden dome. Elegantly decorated with portraits in gold leaf frames, the living room also has a convertible sofa bed, television, and unlimited internet access. The fully equipped kitchen makes it easy for you to have your meals at home, enjoying long dinners comfortably at the dining table. The double bedroom, with its refined decor, antique furnishings, and mahogany dressers for personal belonings, sleeps 2 guests. The spacious bathroom features an elegant bathtub and separate toilet for greater privacy.

Along the Seine, stretching from the Quai des Tuileries to the Quai Branly, the 7th district has long been considered one of the most sophisticated and historic districts of the capital. Most of your sightseeing and business affairs can be made on foot: the Esplanade and Hotel des Invalides where Napoleon is buried, the Eiffel Tower and Champs de Mars, the Orsay Museum, the Rodin Museum, the Quai Branly Museum, the National Assemby, the Bourbon Palace, as well as the Ministry of Defense. Well served by several lines of public transport (Invalides metro lines 8 and 13), join other districts of Paris with ease.
