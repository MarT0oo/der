Saint Lazare, Saint Lazare, Paris, France Vacation Rental

 

Located in the central area of Saint Lazare, this beautiful four bedroom holiday rental in Paris is a luxurious getaway surrounded by high end shops.You can find the classic Parisian stores such as Printemps or Galeries Lafayette (9 minutes). This shopping haven is also a cultural destination, with the 17th Century Opera Garnier only a short walk from the flat (9 minutes).

The stunning Haussmanian apartment sits in front of the Haussmann Saint Lazare station. You will surely enjoy a pampering vacation while staying in this spacious holiday apartment in Paris. Each room is fully furnished and uniquely decorated by our top local interior designers. Various styles of furniture were pulled together to give a charming and lively feel to the apartment’s style, and a fun treehouse bunk bed in the children’s bedroom makes it an ideal place for a family getaway. In the living room you will be greeted by geometric wall paper that highlights the detailed moldings upon the walls, while sweeping curtains emphasize the high ceilings above.

This apartment is truly a treat for the senses!
