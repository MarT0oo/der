Domaine de Vignoble, St Tropez, Grimaud, France Vacation Rental

 

French farmhouse in vineyards Grimaud St Tropez - located just outside the perched village of Grimaud near St Tropez, this former farmhouse with pool has been lovingly restored by its current owners and offers an outstanding base for a relaxing French Riviera holiday at any time of the year.

The house is centred around a large and comfortable living room, which is air conditioned in the heat of the summer but also has an open fireplace for use on cooler evenings. An outdoor 'au vent' - a covered seating area offering lovely views over the vineyards to Grimaud - a particularly magical sight in the evenings when the castle is floodlit and you can relax here listening to the crickets singing! The mature gardens are delightful, the rural setting idyllic, and the proximity to Grimaud and other attractive villages such as Ramatuelle and La Garde-Freinet highly appealing.

Days out in St Tropez or on the long sandy beaches which this coastline is renowned for, Pampelonne for example, are perfectly feasible. A holiday at this lovely French farmhouse in the vineyards will offer you the best of all worlds
