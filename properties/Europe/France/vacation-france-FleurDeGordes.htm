Fleur De Gordes, Gordes, Provence, France Vacation Rental

 

Provence family country cottage for rent - This charming period cottage, nestled in the heart of Provence near Gordes, boasts magnificent views of the surrounding hills and countryside and offers wonderful opportunities for bike rides and walks in the Luberon region. All the furnishings and decorations of Fleur de Gordes are typical Provencal to allow guests to fully absorb the atmosphere and lifestyle of this beautiful French region, where good food, excellent wines and plenty of opportunities for outdoor activities abound.

The enclosed grounds are laid to lawn, perfect for children to play, and are planted with numerous colourful oleanders, shrubs and maritime pine trees which give the garden an immaculate manicured look. In this charming oasis guests will have the chance to relax by the heated pool and dine al fresco soaking up the amazing views of the Luberon hill.

The quaint hamlet of Gordes, one of the most beautiful villages in the South of France, is on your doorstep and is well worth a visit. Its century old cobbled streets, tall stone houses built against the rock and the imposing castle are the unique setting for many art exhibitions and musical festivals taking place during the summer season.

The art town of Avignon, with its magnificent Palace du Papes, is one of the architectural wonders of France and can be found at 18 miles from your Provencal villa.
