Le Ciel Bleu, St Tropez, France Vacation Rental

 

Contemporary French Riviera villa with panoramic sea views and heated pool - Le Ciel Bleu offers luxurious and stylish accommodation in a superb setting on a private domaine, overlooking the beautiful Gulf of St Tropez.

If you're looking for prime location, stunning sea views, proximity to some of the loveliest places in the south of France, plus a high level of comfort and total relaxation, then look no further. Le Ciel Bleu ticks all these boxes and more, with its easy open-plan layout, its south-facing sun terrace and its sizeable, heated pool. Furnishings and design are of the highest standard, as befits a villa owned by an interior designer!

Situated on a pretty hillside in Beauvallon, Le Ciel Bleu is convenient for all the places you will want to visit during your holiday. There is a local beach with restaurants close by and, if you are keen to spend time on famous Pampelonne beach with its restaurant clubs, this is also easily drivable from the villa. If you don't fancy spending time in the car you can take a water taxi to St Tropez. Nearby Ste Maxime has several restaurants, plus year-round festivals with a theatre and cinema. A wide variety of activities are available in this part of the Var region, including tennis, golf, riding, polo, hiking and numerous water sports, so you will be spoiled for choice if you prefer an active holiday!

There are also museums and art galleries galore, and a short distance inland you will find plenty of attractive hilltop villages with typical squares, inviting pavement cafes and good restaurants. This modern French Riviera villa with sea views and heated pool really does offer an excellent base for a holiday that everyone will thoroughly enjoy.
