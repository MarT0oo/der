La Pervenche, Valbonne, Cote D'azur, France Vacation Rental

 

Cote d’Azur villa with pool near Valbonne – Set in large, private grounds in a quiet residential area near Valbonne, this spacious and comfortable villa is only a 10-minute walk from the village's lively market, elegant shops and highly-rated restaurants, with the beaches of Cannes and Antibes only 10 miles away.

The living area is spacious and light, with unusual curved walls and a large fireplace. Outdoor terraces offer plenty of space for dinners al fresco, including a fabulous pizza oven and built-in barbecue. Surrounded by a lush garden mainly laid to lawn, mature shrubs and large palm trees, this villa enjoys total privacy and therefore is ideal for those wishing to relax and get away from a frantic pace of life, but still be able to enjoy an evening stroll to a good restaurant.

As you leave La Pervenche, after a ten-minute stroll you will come across Valbonne which is a lovely village full of life and things to do. Why not start your day with a coffee and croissant in a local café, spend some time browsing among the market stalls and then enjoy a typical Mediterranean lunch in one of the many open-air bistros in the main square, as you watch the world go by. Your experience of the Cote d’Azur's lifestyle wouldn't be complete if you didn't include Cannes in your itinerary. Here you can choose to relax on the beach or enjoy some designer retail therapy. At nightfall you may want to enjoy the panorama of twinkling lights as you stroll along the promenade waiting to dine in one of the many restaurants a la mode.
