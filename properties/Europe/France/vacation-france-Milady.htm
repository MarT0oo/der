Milady, Biarritz, France Vacation Rental

 

Warm, classy and charming, Milady invites you to experience Basque Country in style. Everything about this exquisite five-bedroom villa emanates luxury—from the chic decor to the heated pool to the stunning views from the poolside deck, the master suite and the saloon.

Located in the south of Biarritz, Milady is located only 400 m from the beach. A short walk to one of Biarritz´s best surf breaks, the famous restaurant La Plancha and the Blue Cargo lounge/restaurant, where you can watch the sun disappear in the ocean while sipping your favourite drink.

If you’re up to more action, take the local shuttle, which brings you to the city centre in a few minutes time. The renowned golf course of Illbarritz – which is right on the beach and only a few minutes away by car – offers another nice leisure activity if you wish to practise your swing.

You are looking for romantic, peaceful settings? Then Milady is the perfect choice! The villa is perched on a quiet, arboreous hillside. Even though everything is close, you feel like above it all, hidden in your own private paradise.

Milady offers plenty of room for privacy and comfortably sleeps 10 persons in its five bedrooms on three floors. Deluxe in-suite bathrooms in two of the bedrooms and proper balconies with views of the city allow you to retreat into your own world whenever you feel like.

The other 3 bedrooms are equally intimate with only the highest quality linen, furniture and interior design.

You dream of true relaxation? The inviting indoor lounge area with the fireplace as well as the sunny terrace are the perfect setting for a small party or an elegant dinner. Or how about slide into the heated waters of your pool and watch the stars glimmering in the sky. Whatever you chose, Milady exudes a comforting tranquillity while fitting harmoniously into its environs with the wood details and clean, modern lines.

Milady is one of our most recent homes, but has already enchanted all of our guests who had the chance to spend their time under its roof. Get in touch with us today to book your unforgettable stay!
