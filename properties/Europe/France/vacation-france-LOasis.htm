L'Oasis, St Restitut, Provence, France Vacation Rental

 

Northern Provence luxury villa - L'Oasis is a luxurious villa set amid lush palm, pine and olive gardens with commanding views across the plains to the north of the Dentelles de Montmirail and Mont Ventoux; with the added bonus of being under 1 mile from the quaint village of St Restitut.

The spacious villa is stylishly decorated using warm colours and the large open plan living area has been cleverly furnished to create three individual seating areas, each with its own atmosphere.

This area of northern Provence has much to offer, from outdoor pursuits such as rock climbing, kayaking and white water rafting available in the stunning Gorges de l'Ardeche, to cycling through the beautiful villages of the northern Vaucluse, or a trip north to Montelimar, a delightful medieval market town with plenty of shops and restaurants, and most notably the home of Montelimar nougat, produced here since almond trees were imported from Asia in the 17th Century. This Northern Provence luxury villa is of a high standard.
