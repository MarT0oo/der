Etoile De Mer, Pampelonne beach, France Vacation Rental

 

Pampelonne beach vacation villa for rent – A few hundred metres from the renowned Pampelonne beach is this beautiful villa surrounded by a large mature garden whose tall Mediterranean pine trees ensure total privacy and pleasant shade for a relaxing holiday on the French Riviera.

Built in the typical style of Southern France and refurbished to the highest standards, Etoile de Mer is a comfortable villa, arranged mostly on the ground floor. Archways connect several reception rooms, all with terracotta floors, whilst three of the four bedrooms are conveniently located on the same floor with direct access to the patio and garden through French windows. The garden is provided with everything you may wish for: from the large salt-water pool, to the manicured rose garden, the boules pitch to the covered pergolas; ideal for those memorable barbecues with family and friends.

An array of restaurants can be found as soon as you leave your villa, most of them scattered along one of the most sought after seaside locations in Southern France. In fact, Pampelonne beach and its trendy restaurants are only a few minutes’ walk, whilst Tahiti Plage is just a little further away. Here a spectacular three-mile long white sandy bay is said to change colour with the current. During your stay at Etoile de Mer you will be able to practise a variety of water sports including sailing, surfing, water skiing, as well as horse riding, hiking, go-karting and even watch Polo matches in nearby St Tropez. However, if the charm of small traditional villages appeals to you a visit to Ramatuelle, Port Grimaud and Gassin is strongly recommended, especially on a market day.
