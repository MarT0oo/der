Chalet Le Blanchot, Courchevel 1850, France Vacation Rental

 

Chalet le Blanchot Courchevel 1850 is a lovely vacation rental located on altitude 1850 in the French Alps. Chalet Le Blanchot’s state-of-the-art facilities and features dovetail seamlessly with natural materials, selected to emphasise the moods, colours and textures of the mountains.

Each of the Chalet's 6 bedrooms feature en-suite bathrooms with 5 king Beds and 1 Oversized Queen. The rooms are spectacular and have the best of every comfort. One of the leading Parisian artisan ateliers crafted the wrought-iron staircase that links each floor; together with leather-clad wall panels bathed in soft, subtle lighting, this is a dramatic way to emerge into the high-ceilinged entertainment space, to dine at the refectory table or simply relax on deeply comfortable sofas.

For those who love to exercise we have a wonderful gym, together with the pool, steam room and Jacuzzi – its distinctive design, while the timber throughout has been painstakingly coloured to achieve a warmth and comfort befitting this unique mountain hideaway. You will never walk away feeling anything but wonderful and relaxed ready to come back for your next adventure.

Set in the exclusive Bellecôte area of Courchevel, this stunning chalet lets you enjoy the slopes of Les Trois Vallées in ultimate luxury. The world-famous ski runs of Courchevel 1850 lie just a 5-minute walk from the door of this charming timber chalet.
