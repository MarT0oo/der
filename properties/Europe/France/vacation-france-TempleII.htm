Temple II, Le Marais, Paris, France Vacation Rental

 

Designed with a refined contemporary style, this apartment is found in the extraordinary city of Paris and offers its guests an entirely satisfying experience. It is located in the Rue du Temple, only a few minutes away from the George Pompidou Center and the Picasso Museum. Showcased in shades of ice grey and white, offset with purple and blue accents; the pristine interiors exude a cosy opulence. With vibrant paintings and wall hangings, indoor plants and elegant décor; the light filled apartment is an elegant complement to la ville lumiére.

Located in an attractive building, guests are offered an appealing mixture of the familiarity of a home and the amenities of a hotel. Situated on Rue Vieille du Temple, guests are in the heart of the historic district of Le Marais.

Many spectacular sightseeing opportunities, fine restaurants and exciting venues for entertainment are located in close proximity to the apartment. Guests can take a short walk to experience structures steeped in centuries of rich history such as the Hôtel de Saint-Aignan.

Excellent French cuisine as well as many other delectable cooking styles can be savoured in the nearby restaurants. Guests can also visit a few of the city’s many entertainment venues that are popular with the stylish Parisians. As the district is a hub in the transport network, guests can also travel from the apartment with ease.
