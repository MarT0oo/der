This quaint stone-built cottage is one of the smaller of the four with a cosy atmosphere, ideal for small families. Each house has been sympathetically restored to retain its individual character and charm, but also stylishly enhanced to offer the latest in modern convenience and each enjoys a private swimming pool and garden, meaning they are equally suitable to be rented separately or as a whole for large groups and special occasions.
