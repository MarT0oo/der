Belle de Gassin, Gassin, St Tropez, France Vacation Rental

 

Situated in an exclusive residential area on the edge of St Tropez, just 50 metres from Bouillabaisse beach and within one mile of the Old Port of St Tropez, this highly sophisticated, fully air conditioned villa with exceptionally large pool is surrounded by a generous Mediterranean garden, brimming with palm and olive trees.

The contemporary design of the interiors, with neutral soft furnishing and high quality fittings and fixtures makes this villa extremely spacious and light; however, the exteriors are typically Provencal, with honey-colour stone walls, arched windows and a picturesque tower. Everyone can find their favourite spot in the magnificent lush garden; enjoy a game of cards under the covered terrace, sunbathe by the pool, or relax with a good book and a glass of white wine under a shady tree whilst the children play on the large, flat lawn. You can catch a glimpse of the sea from the Master bedroom and the terrace on the first floor, showing just how close you are to the Mediterranean! It is hard to believe that this verdant oasis is only a short stroll from a lovely sandy beach and the buzzing centre of St Tropez.

A footpath will take you to the very heart of St Tropez, with its pretty Old Port, pavement cafes and splendid yachts moored in the marina. Enjoy the sophisticated atmosphere of this magic place, still attracting celebrities from all over the world. Despite its fame, St Tropez still retains its original charm as a small fishing town, especially on a market day. The view across the bay is absolutely superb and if you take the ferry across the gulf you can easily reach Ste Maxime avoiding the local traffic. From here, exploring famed resorts along the French Riviera could not be easier, as is venturing a little further inland for a taste of typical French lifestyle, browsing market stalls in La Garde Freinet or walking up to Grimaud Castle for unrivalled views over the entire bay.
