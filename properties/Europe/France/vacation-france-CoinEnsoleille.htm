Coin Ensoleille, La Garde Freinet, France Vacation Rental

 

France holiday villa Garde Freinet - this South of France villa near St Tropez is in a fantastic location between two charming hilltop villages and is less than nine miles from the beach! With extensive grounds, a private salt water pool and lovely views, this tasteful, character villa would make an excellent base for a holiday in the Var.

Accommodation is in the main villa and in the annex, a former silkworm factory. Externally very appealing, these 18th century buildings are full of character, and internally the rooms are modern and bright.

Set in 4 hectares of land, Coin Ensoleillé is adjacent to protected forest and enjoys beautiful and extensive grounds as well as a lovely outlook over the wooded hills. If privacy and tranquillity are important to you, you will find them here: Coin Ensoleillé is hidden away at the end of a small track, deep in the heart of the countryside yet only a short drive to both La Garde Freinet and Grimaud. You will find a good selection of restaurants, as well as pavement cafés, small shops and local markets in both these villages. If you intend to spend time on the white sandy beaches of the Riviera, or in fashionable St Tropez, these are not far from this France holiday villa Garde Freinet.
