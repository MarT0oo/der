Chevalier Saint Georges, Chevaliers Saint George, Paris, France Vacation Rental

 

The lovely 3 bedroom Chevaliers Saint George holiday apartment in the heart of Paris’s most affluent neighborhood, Madeleine-Vendome, welcomes guests with classic modernity. Nestled halfway between L’eglise de la Madeleine (5 min) and Place Vendome (5 min), you’re a close walk to some of the city’s most famous boulevards, museums and attractions, including the Louvre (20 min), the Champs-Elysees (15 min), the Opera (5 min) and the Royal Palace (20 min).

At your doorstep you’ll find fabulous shops, restaurants, bars, sidewalk cafes and magnificent architecture. The Madeleine metro stop is nearby (5 min) and buses run around the corner.

Wooden floors, high ceilings and modern furnishings carefully chosen by our local interior designers give this spacious vacation apartment an opulence that is perfectly aligned with the magnificent structures in the external neighborhood. Each of the 3 bedrooms has a large, comfortable king-sized bed and its own quirky wall decor. The fully equipped, modern kitchen opens onto a large living room area with a fold-out couch that sleeps 2 and a dining table for up to 8 people. There are plenty of places to unwind with a glass of French wine after a long day of sightseeing. Guests will also love free Wi-Fi, Cable TV, plush towels, fine linens and a nespresso machine.

The perfect haven for a large family or group of friends seeking a comfortable and luxurious space in an ideal location, the only problem will be having to return home!
