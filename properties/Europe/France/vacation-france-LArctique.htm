L’Arctique, Courchevel 1850, France Vacation Rental

 

Chalet L’Arctique is located right in the centre of Courchevel 1850 providing the ultimate convenient location for all the fantastic shops, restaurants and nightlife of this world-class resort.

The living areas of L’Arctique are up on the top floor. The space is open plan and furnished / decorated to the highest standard. There’s a large seating area with a central fireplace, a spacious dining table and a contemporary open kitchen.

The property is split over four floors with five bedrooms and a self contained studio apartment. There is a master suite, two en-suite doubles, an en-suite twin bedroom and a double bunk room with en-suite shower room. The bunk room is ideal for up to four children. In the basement of the property is a self-contained studio with a seating area, en-suite and a kitchenette. The studio is ideal for a nanny, teenagers or any other guests who require a little bit of extra privacy from the main group.

There is a utility room and ski room in the basement for equipment / laundry.

Courchevel is multiple. First of all, because it is divided over 5 levels of altitude, then because each level offers its own face, panorama and way of approaching the mountain. Discover the ressort guide created by Cimalpes teams and identify the best of the ressort: restaurants, hotels, ski schools, activities, shops, ski, ski areas, etc..

Located in the prestigious area of Cospillot not far from the centre and the slopes. You can choose to enjoy both the amenities of the chalet and also the animations and restaurants of the resort.
