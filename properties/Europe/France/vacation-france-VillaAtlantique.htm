Villa Atlantique, Anglet, Biarritz, France Vacation Rental

 

Villa Atlantique is inspired by modern beach style living and Long Island architecture. It’s built on a base of natural stone and covered with wood frontage slats.

Designed by renowned Belgium architect Charly Wittock , with interiors designed by local artist Patrick Clairacq and construction completed by the finest local craftsmen.

Villa Atlantique is approximately 500 m2 and sleeps 14 people in great comfort.

The villa is located on a private road on La Madrague surf beach. It overlooks a par 5 hole on Chiberta golfcourse and has a spectacular ocean and surf view. A private walkway takes you straight onto the beach and into the water. Local restaurants and shops are only a few minutes away.

The living area, dining room and fully equiped kitchen on the main floor have stunning ocean and sunset view. Large sliding windows open up onto the covered, and wind sheltered, lounge and dining deck, giving indoor / outdoor options for relaxing and dining. You can jump straight into the beautiful black tiled heated pool or jacuzzi or relax on the stylish curved sunbeds. The three bedrooms on the first floor directly overlook the golf course, the ocean and La Madrague surfspot.

Villa Atlantique is a luxury beach holiday experience. The region is known for its large variety of activities and Michelin star restaurants. There’s no need to go far from the house to start enjoying everything the Basque country has to offer. A large variety of activities are only a few minutes away such as golf, tennis, surf lessons and horse riding to name but a few.
