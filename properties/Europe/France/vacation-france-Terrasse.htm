Terrasse, Biarritz, France Vacation Rental

 

In the middle of downtown Biarritz this place will charm you with its tasteful pieces of art, extraordinary wall coverings, and bright lighting from both carefully placed illumination and the sun that shines through the tall windows. From the terrace you enjoy perfect views of downtown Biarritz and the lively city market.

A short walk to the beach, "Terrasse" features a large terrace, private parking, concierge services, access to a gym, spa and heated pool with an additional fee. The apartment offers two bedrooms—one with a king size bed and the other one with a queen bed, which can be converted into two single beds. The rooms are located, one facing the market hall while the other one looks onto a quiet street. The bathroom provides a lot of storage space and is equipped with a large rain-head shower.

The shiny, fair kitchen opens onto a large porch overlooking the central market of Biarritz where you can find everything from fresh and local products to gastronomic specialties to prepare delightful meals.

The adjoining, modern living room – with a comfortable leather sofa, two big lounge chairs – contrasts with impressive wall art.

The dining table is a heart of the apartment: four chairs and a bench provide enough room for up to 6 people, to share a nice dinner and chat well into the evening, while Biarritz´s nightlife bustles. With the city’s best restaurants and bars just a short walk away you can change scenery whenever you feel like.

If you like to be located centrally with all its liveliness, combined with unique interior design, then "Terrasse" is the THE place for you. Please contact us today to reserve your dates, as our calendar fills quickly.
