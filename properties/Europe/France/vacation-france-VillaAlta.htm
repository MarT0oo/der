Villa Alta, La Croix Valmer, St Tropez Var, France Vacation Rental

 

This imposing La Croix Valmer holiday villa was constructed in the 1920s and has undergone complete renovation. The villa stands on three floors, with high ceilings throughout giving a light, spacious feel. The mature gardens include several magnificent large palm trees.

This French village villa is well situated in a mature residential area just 7 minutes walk from the centre of the village of La Croix Valmer on the Cote d'Azur, which offers a small selection of shops and restaurants.

The local beach is 2km away, while Pampelonne bay, which is a 20 minute drive, offers 4km of classic French Riviera golden sands fringed with chic bars and cafes, including the renowned Club 55. Golf enthusiasts will also enjoy this La Croix Valmer holiday villa.

 

La Croix Valmer centre 7 mins walk. Nearest beach 1.25 miles. St Tropez 15 mins. Toulon Hyeres airport 34 miles. Ramatuelle & Pampelonne Bay (Club 55) 20 mins. Tennis, golf course at Gassin & watersports within 5 miles.
