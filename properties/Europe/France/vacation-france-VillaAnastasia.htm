Villa Anastasia, Villefranche Sur Mer, Cote D'azur, France Vacation Rental

 

Cote d’Azur exclusive luxury villa - Perched on the top of a hill in a sought after residential area with striking views over the bay of Villefranche and St Jean Cap Ferrat, Villa Anastasia is the ultimate contemporary designer villa, providing the perfect accommodation for a truly luxurious holiday.

The French Riviera with its intense blue sea, prestigious yachts and white sailing boats is the inspiring setting of this three-storey villa with road access from the top floor and a very large private roof top terrace. The living areas are extremely spacious and airy with light flooding through numerous floor-to-ceiling windows and white coloured furnishings. Similarly, all ensuite bedrooms are elegantly furnished in a minimalistic style. The large home theatre on the lower floor offers an underwater view of the infinity pool. Here you can relax as you keep as you watch the children swimming.

At the feet of Villa Anastasia is the beautiful town of Villefranche, with a myriad of roofs and palm trees sloping down to the bay. Within a ten-minute walk you can reach the coarse sandy beach of Villefranche- sur-Mer as well as the elegant promenade with restaurants and trendy cafes. The coastline of this stretch of French Riviera is absolutely spectacular. As you drive along the scenic clifftop route you will come across secluded coves, lush terraced gardens, quaint fishing villages and lively harbours. The millionaires’ peninsula of St Jean Cap Ferrat is known for its exclusive restaurants and elegant shops, as well as the jet-set yachts passing by its harbour. Further along the coast, 7 miles away from your villa, is the Principality of Monaco and the glamorous Monte Carlo, with its Casinos and renowned Formula 1 track, and the Italian border is only 20 miles Eastward.

 

Nearest shops/ restaurants Villefranche sur Mer 1.2 miles, beach 1.2. miles, Nice town centre 10 mins, Nice airport 15 mins, Monaco 7 miles, Italy/France border 20 miles. Tennis 10 mins, golf course 20 mins.
