Villa Triton, Cote D'azur, Pegomas , France Vacation Rental

 

Large Cote d'Azur Villa with pool for vacation - Situated in an elevated position between Grasse and Cannes affording far reaching views across the Siagne Valley and the Tanneron hills, this large Cote d'Azur villa is surrounded by beautiful grounds with lush vegetation, a perfectly manicured romantic garden and full size tennis court.

Ideal for large parties, Villa Triton is very well equipped to offer a relaxing holiday in one of the most sought after regions of France. Most of the bedrooms feature large windows and balconies with electric awnings and outdoor seating facilities to admire the surrounding landscape. An enchanting Renaissance style pool with fountain and stone archway embellish the exotic garden, rich in palm trees, pines, mature oaks and shrubs offering plenty of shady areas during the hot summer days. A pool house with summer kitchen, sauna and Jacuzzi complement the splendind pool area which also encompasses a private tennis court and boule pitch just a few steps below.

A visit to the French Riviera and its pretty coastal villages is highly recommended, with Mandelieu being the nearest at just a fifteen-minute drive from this villa. Further inland you will find opportunities for fishing, walking, hiking and discovering typical French villages such as Mouans Sartoux and Mougins, full of character and charm.
