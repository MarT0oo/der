Baie d'Argent, St Tropez, France Vacation Rental

 

Perched on a hillside overlooking the magnificent bay of Ste Maxime, this is a superb contemporary villa with infinity pool and air conditioning, ideally set 100 metres from Ste Maxime tennis club, just 250 metres from the nearest beach and one of Ste Maxime’s most renowned restaurants. St Tropez is only a few miles away or even closer if you take the ferry across the bay.

This is a truly stunning brand new villa whose state-of-the art design follows the undulating profile of the hill, exposing the property to 180-degree views over the Mediterranean and the bay of St Tropez towards Ste Maxime. All ensuite bedrooms are arranged on either side of a spacious central living area and equally enjoy stunning sea views and access to the vast terrace. A large canopy stretched over the terrace creates a pleasant shade, making this the best place in which to while away the hours in company of your family and friends, enjoying one of the most inspiring vistas of the entire Riviera.

Directly below your infinity pool is a path leading to a lovely sandy beach with just a five-minute walk downhill (although it takes a little bit longer to walk back up). Here you can spend time on the beach, swim in the calm waters of the Mediterranean sea or stroll along the seashore enjoying the view of the bay. Wander about Ste Maxime, just a mile away from Baie d’Argent and take the ferry across the bay to St Tropez for a relaxing afternoon discovering the unique charm of this fishing port, its shops and famed pavement cafes which still attract celebrities from all over the world. Day trip destinations may include Port Grimaud, Frejus or Cannes along the coast. Alternatively, you may head inland to visit the quaint hilltop villages of Plan-de-la-Tour and La-Garde-Freinet, or around the headland to Ramatuelle and La-Croix-Valmer, all within a thirteen-mile radius; you will soon realise how much the French Riviera has to offer its visitors!
