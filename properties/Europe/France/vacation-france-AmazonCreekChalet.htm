Amazon Creek Chalet, Chamonix, France Vacation Rental

 

In the Les Bossons area of the Chamonix Valley, Chalet Amazon Creek sleeps up to ten guests in five stunning bedrooms, all with en-suite bathrooms. Our flagship chalet boasts vast living areas, a cinema room and a fully equipped luxury spa with hamman steam room, sauna and sunken jacuzzi.

The moment you arrive it will become clear why Chalet Amazon Creek is the most luxurious chalet in the Mont Blanc Valley. Nestled in woodland next to a mountain stream, our flagship property combines pure luxury with tranquillity.

Each of the five en-suite bedrooms is designed and furnished to the highest standards using neutral fabrics to give a fresh and warm feel. Wake to tea or coffee served in your bedroom, then enjoy a delicious breakfast and breathtaking views of the Aiguille du Midi.

After a day on the slopes, cosy the children into the cinema room and reward yourself with a massage in the superbly equipped spa with wonderful views of the forest. Or alternatively settle down with a glass of wine from our superbly stocked wine cellar
