Les Cascades, St Tropez, La Garde-Freinet, France Vacation Rental

 

An absolutely stunning newly built Villa just 2.5 miles from La Garde Freinet but totally secluded with wonderful views across the countryside. The well tended terraced garden of more than 4 acres with an infinity rock pool is surrounded by forest, ideal for groups looking for peace and privacy within striking distance of everything St. Tropez has to offer.

The covered dining terrace offers wonderful views of the mountain scenery, as does the pool, and there are various places to sit and relax for a chat, or maybe with a book and a drink, and soak up the Mediterranean sunshine!

Nearby La Garde Freinet is a delightful medieval village with numerous shops and restaurants and a colourful, twice-weekly market. The villages of Grimaud and Cogolin are well worth visiting, and St. Tropez, Club 55 and Pampelonne Beach are all within easy reach. This modern newly built villa is perfect for a relaxing French holiday.
