L'Oceanique, Biarritz, France Vacation Rental

 

Biarritz luxury villa with heated pool and sea views - Tucked away in a quiet residential area on a wooded hillside overlooking Biarritz and the ocean, this sleek, contemporary villa with heated pool is just a ten-minute stroll from Biarritz's lively promenade, fine shops and restaurants and sandy beach. Golf, surfing and numerous Thalassotherapy spas are on your doorstep in this enchanting Basque region.

Furnished in a minimalist style with smooth lines and plenty of light coming through large glazed sliding doors, this villa provides a very comfortable space to live, both indoor and outdoor. The large decked terrace which surrounds the pool area is the splendid setting for candlelit dinners as well as for relaxing among the lush vegetation, with enviable views across the sea.

This area is a golfers and surfers’ paradise boasting twelve first-class golf courses. Surfing and body boarding are very popular here due to the impressive high waves. You can also try the thrill of deltaplaning or kitesurfing. The long sandy beaches and the mild climate largely contribute to the region's appeal for families. A tourist train runs from Biarritz into the Basque mountains to discover this impressive, rugged landscape with dramatic views and vineyards galore. Visit Bayonne old quarter for a glimpse into its rich cultural heritage. a stop for a local hot chocolate drink is a must! The fashionable seaside resort of St Jean de Luz is only ten miles away from L’Oceanique. Further afield, beyond the Spanish border, the coastal town of St Sebastien provides an ideal day trip.
