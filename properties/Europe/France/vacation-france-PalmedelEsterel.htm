Palme de l'Esterel, Cote D'azur, Le Trayas, France Vacation Rental

 

Set against the striking red backdrop of the Esterel mountains, this air conditioned villa with heated pool and sea views enjoys an ideal situation in La Trayas, just a few minutes’ walk from La Figueirette harbour, sandy beach, twice-weekly markets and restaurants.

Palme de l’Esterel has a distinctive Mediterranean look, with warm terracotta colours mirroring the renowned chain of mountains which gives the region its name. The modern interiors are provided with the latest equipment and air conditioning in all bedrooms for comfortable, easy living. The garden is an oasis of tropical charm, stocked with different varieties of palm trees, cacti, oleanders and shrubs. In its midst is the heated infinity pool, complete with Jacuzzi; the perfect spot in which to while-away the hours on a hot summer's day. Splendid sea views can be enjoyed from the terraces and the attic, providing an idyllic setting for a relaxing holiday on the Cote d’Azur.

A short stroll away, is the lovely port of La Figueirette offering scuba diving, surfing, sailing and other water sports along its sandy beach. Further afield is Theoule-sur-Mer, offering a good selection of restaurants shops and more beaches. The larger town, Mandelieu-la-Napoule, is within ten miles and Cannes, is just five minutes beyond, for a glimpse of its chic atmosphere and lifestyle. For a complete change of scene, this villa in Le Trayas also offers great opportunities for walking along countless trails crisscrossing the Massif de l’Esterel, with striking views of the Mediterranean sea in the distance
