Villa Framboise, Le Cannet, Cote D'azur, France Vacation Rental

 

For a lovely family break near Cannes, Villa Framboise in Le Cannet is a fantastic choice. Stylish, yet comfortable with a distinctive-shaped private pool surrounded by palms and pretty flowers, this will be a dip to remember. Just a short distance from the residential area of La Cannet, once part of Cannes, you’ll find a wealth of treasures in this small residential area including museums, galleries, churches, restaurants and cafes around the popular Place Bellevue.

That’s if you can leave Villa Framboise. Elegant with a spacious living area that leads out on to the gardens and swimming pool, this is a lovely place to be, especially in the summer months, where you can relax in shaded areas and lounge in the pretty garden. It’s certainly somewhere where dining al fresco is a must. And with three welcoming bedrooms, decorated in an easy style, you’ll want to spend a lot of time indoors too. Le Cannet is nearby and is a great place to visit for a coffee or a leisurely stroll around the town, but if you’re looking for a little more adventure head on over to the film festival town of Cannes.

Bursting with glamorous boutique shops, trendy restaurants, delightful pavement cafes and a sandy beach, this is one trip worth making. Or, visit the historic town of Antibes for quaint cobbled streets, an ancient port, beaches and bays, all within a short distance of Villa Framboise.
