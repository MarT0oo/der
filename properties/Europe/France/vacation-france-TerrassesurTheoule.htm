Terrasse sur Theoule, Theoule-sur-mer, Cote D'azur, France Vacation Rental

 

Elegant Belle Epoque Cote d’Azur Villa – From its enviable hilltop location this classic, stylish villa boasts unrivalled views over the Mediterranean sea, the bay of Cannes, the Islands of Lérins, Juan les Pins and Antibes. Built in 1895, Terrasse sur Théoule has been refurbished to the highest standards in order to revive its Belle Epoque beauty and charm.

Tastefully furnished, the villa has been designed to maximize the impact of the sea view from every room, through several sets of French doors and balconies. The south-facing grounds feature a series of large terraces connected by a sumptuous stone staircase. On the lower level, a sizeable pool offers the ideal setting to relax in total privacy and soak up the enchanting beauty of the French Riviera at its best.

The small seaside resort of Théoule sur Mer is just a few minutes away from this elegant Cote d’Azur villa, and offers a wide variety of restaurants, cafes, shops and beautiful sandy beaches. Why not rent a boat to sail along the rugged coastline, or try out some popular water sports such as scuba diving, kayaking or water skiing? Lovers of trekking and hiking will be delighted to discover the direct access from this villa to the Esterel Mountains, with their distinctive red rock. From here winding pathways will take you to the best panoramic viewpoints across the bay of Cannes.
