Eléphant Blanc, Val d’Isére, France


something wonderfully uplifting about Eléphant Blanc. The Chalet is right at the very heart of the Espace Killy. The blue Solaise piste is literally on your doorstep and you can even ski right into the boot room at the end of the day. The nursery slopes are also conveniently close, making it perfect for families, while the centre of town is only a five minute chauffeured drive away.


Sleeping 10 guests in five ensuite bedrooms, the chalet is flooded with light from windows in every direction. The lofty drawing room with its high beams and roaring fire is the heart of the house, while the dining area is dominated by a huge table making it ideal for entertaining. Above, there is a cosy galleried office and TV snug, a perfect hideaway for children or adults.


Outside there are two large sheltered terraces with wonderful views to the Face de Bellevarde, plus a superbly private hot tub. Eléphant Blanc feels very personal and special: the perfect choice for a family holiday.
