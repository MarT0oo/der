Villa de la Plage, Ramatuelle, St Tropez Var, France Vacation Rental

 

L'Escalet beach villa south of France - just a short walk from a lovely sandy beach and tucked away in a quiet residential area, Villa de la Plage offers an ideal solution for those seeking a simple, relaxed beach holiday. It is simply furnished for easy summer living and set in beautiful mature gardens with a lovely pool overlooking the sea.

The accommodation is arranged across the main house and annex enabling friends and families to enjoy each other's company whilst benefitting from a measure of privacy. The delightful, mature garden provides a pleasant place to unwind surrounded by trees, shrubs and flowers. Additionally, the outdoor dining terrace overlooks the pool and faces the sea: ideal for long lazy meals before or after time spent at the beach, which is just a stroll away down the lane.

Proximity to the long sandy beaches of the French Riviera is undoubtedly the highlight of any holiday here, and if you should want a change from L'Escalet beach and the Cabane Bambou and Tropicana beach club restaurants, famous Pampelonne is less than 2 miles from this L'Escalet beach villa south of France.

Locally there is a small supermarket a very convenient 1.5 miles from the villa. The charming old village of Ramatuelle with its excellent selection of restaurants and summer music festival is less than four miles away, and lively St Tropez with its quayside cafés, restaurants, markets, art galleries and atmospheric old town just six miles.

 

L'Escalet beach 200 metres (walk from villa), Pampelonne beach less than 2 miles (Club 55 3.5 miles). Spar supermarket 1.5 miles, Ramatuelle 3.5 miles, St Tropez 6.5 miles, nearest airports Toulon 25 miles Nice 84 miles. Tennis in Ramatuelle: nearest golf at Beauvallon 13 miles.
