About The Area

Courchevel Moriond (also known as 1650) is situated in the beautiful Savoie region of France, on the edge of the Vanoise National Park. Courchevel Moriond has a friendly atmosphere offering many luxury ski-in ski-out chalets Courchevel on wide, easy blue runs; this resort is excellent for groups with children. The chic alpine ski resort has numerous hotels, bars, restaurants, shops, 3 supermarkets, its own local cinema and church – it is easy to see why some people prefer quieter 1650 to glamorous Courchevel 1850. Offering great access to Courchevel 1850 and the rest of the Three Valleys, 1650 is a great resort in which to find your luxury ski chalet!
