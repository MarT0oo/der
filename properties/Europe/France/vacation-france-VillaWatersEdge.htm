Villa Waters Edge, St Tropez, France Vacation Rental

 

Water's Edge Villa is Saint Tropez’s stunning new waterfront property, Villa Water’s Edge, is part of dreamexoticrentals Properties’ exclusive vacation rental portfolio, crafted to the highest of standards by our team of internationally renowned architects and designers.

The opulent Saint Tropez, sizzling on the picturesque shores of the Côte d’Azur, has long attracted the wealthy and glamorous, drawn to its exclusive, jet set reputation, lavish beach clubs and three Michelin-starred dining. The exclusive Villa Water’s Edge is located in Saint Tropez’s most prestigious private domain, Les Parcs, and is complete with a designer kitchen, bespoke furniture and fine art. This alluring and entrancing villa is designed with only the most luxurious of materials and boasts extensive covered terraces, breathtaking views, a heated infinity swimming pool, direct access to the sea and state of the art security.

WATER’S EDGE has a Master suite with 2 bathrooms and King suite, a second Bedroom 2 with double beds, a third with double bed or two single beds, en-suite bathroom then we have bedroom four garden room with salon double bed with en-suite shower room. There is a separate apartment with salon, kitchenette, double bed, bathroom, guest WC.

The house features a designer kitchen a lovely and very large Salon where you can enjoy reading or afternoon tea. Then we have the television room which is great for families who love to get away and just enjoy time together even at night while watching movies and sharing laughter. The luxury dining dining room is perfect for those shared dinners where the family comes together to talk and enjoy great food and drink. Outside you'll find amazing gardens, beautiful sea views and an infinity edge pool that will have your family or friends spending more time relaxing and enjoying just being together.

As with our other properties, guests of the Villa Water’s Edge will receive a truly tailor-made service provided by our team of world-class hoteliers. Our reputation is built on offering the very best in exclusive rentals to the elite and famous. For more information on short or long-term rental please contact our office.
