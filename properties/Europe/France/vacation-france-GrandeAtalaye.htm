Grande Atalaye, Biarritz, France Vacation Rental

 

Grande Atalaye is more than an extravagant home — it is a masterpiece of state-of-the-art design. From its very airy, vast rooms to its carefully selected pieces of modern art, this holiday home is the perfect setting for a prestigious vacation. Located in the very heart of Biarritz, the location is second-to-none.

Grande Atalaye was being renovated with care and even the smallest details were taken care of. The open living area invites you to either enjoy a delicious dinner at the large dining table with seating for ten or simply relax in sumptuous leather furnishings while watching a good movie on the flat screen TV, which is elegantly integrated in the furniture.

The dramatic glow along the walls – from very carefully placed lighting - and the gallant floor-to-ceiling windows give way to sensational views of historic monuments like Sainte Eugénie Church, the Casino, the Hôtel du Palais, and the old fishermen port. Apart from the views, you can also admire the city from a balcony, which stretches along two sides of the building.

The luxurious master bedroom – your private retreat – will charm you immediately with its walk-in dressing room, the Italian shower, the marbled double sink and access to the balcony. The other two guest rooms share a roomy bath including a tub and a shower, marbled countertops and even a towel warmer. Hardwood floors, hotel-quality linens, air conditioning in all rooms and a housemaid every three days add to the comfort of this outstanding apartment. We also have an on-call concierge who will make sure you get the best out of your stay in and around Biarritz. Not to forget the privileged access for our guests to the heated pool, Spa and fitness centre of the Hotel Sofitel for half of the usual price…

In a few words, Grande Atalaye is the ideal home base for all your explorations: the beaches and the aquarium, various sights and attractions, the casino and numerous shops are just around the corner. The prominent Chez Albert and many other restaurants, bars and cafes make part of the neighbourhood. You prefer to prepare a dinner at home? Just down the road, at the local market “Les Halles”, you will find everything from fresh fish, fruits and veggies to crisp baguette bread. The modern and perfectly equipped kitchen makes cooking a real pleasure!

Grande Atalaye´s enticing interiors and the privileged location are incomparable. Reserve your dates today!
