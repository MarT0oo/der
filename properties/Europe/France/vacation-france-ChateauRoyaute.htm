Chateau Royaute, Cote D’azur, France Vacation Rental

 

The chateau is perfect for entertaining with five charming and spacious reception rooms. There are three traditionally styled rooms with chandeliers, oak floors and original fireplaces, ideal for dinners and meetings. These rooms open onto the terrace and benefit from stunning views over the south facing pool gardens. There is also a Grand salon, an elegant and spacious room with marble floor and marble fireplace plus the Petit salon, a cosy and more relaxed sitting room with TV and doors to the cloisters. The rooms are complemented by a state of the art home cinema and games room with billiard table and private garden area, which suit either home entertainment or corporate presentations. Onsite activities include a gym, boules area and ping pong.

The property has air conditioning and heating throughout, Wi-Fi and quality music systems in all reception areas, atmospheric garden and cloister lighting and benefits from 2 lifts to assist guests with reduced mobility and families with young children.

The property features 15 Bedrooms; 12 luxury en suite bedrooms, 2 charming double bedrooms and a family bedroom. All the rooms are individually furnished in traditional style, benefitting from sea views over the Bay of St. Tropez or stunning views of the Esterel mountains. They provide charming accommodation, sumptuously furnished in a fresh and calm Mediterranean style accented with beautiful oak parquet floors, hand selected antiques and luxurious new bathrooms.

This is a very sophisticated property, in a glorious part of the world, and makes an excellent venue for any event, whatever the size. The chateau is let on a self-catered basis but a chef is available for professional catering, as well as a chauffeur, concierge, secretary and baby sitter.

The professional chef’s kitchen is perfect for preparation of party meals and can cater for large groups and cookery classes. The new, top quality family kitchen opens onto the pool gardens and is a wonderful space for enjoying quality time and relaxed meals.

There are numerous activities which can be arranged locally including sailing trips to St. Tropez and the Lerin Islands, windsurfing, kayaking and golf, tennis coaching, guided walks in the mountains whilst enjoying stunning sea views, mountain biking and horse riding, visits to fantastic Provencal vineyards and wonderful historic villages. Additionally private cookery lessons and art classes can be offered at Chateau Royaute and an experienced beautician is available for our guests.

The Roman town of Frejus with its famous amphitheatre, charming cafes and restaurants and bi weekly Provence market is located just 5 minutes from Chateau Royaute and gorgeous, large sandy beaches are close at hand. The wonderful beach of St. Aygulf is just 7 minutes by car and Les Issambres and charming St. Maxim can be reached in 15- 20 minutes. Agay Plage, located at 7 minutes drive is a beautiful beach and very safe for small children with the stunning red rocks of the Esterel in the background and the famous beaches and boutiques of Cannes can be reached in just 20 minutes.
