Blue Belle, 16th district, Paris, France Vacation Rental

 

Situated in the chic 16th arrondissement and within walking distance to many attractions, this triplex offers 3 levels of modern luxury, plus a rooftop garden! Simply stunning rooftop terrace with landscaping and an outdoor kitchen. 3 gorgeous bedrooms with queen size beds. Modern design with beautiful wood and stone accents throughout. Spacious and filled with natural light that pours in from the numerous windows.

This Triplex is a bright and refreshing space filled with fabulous amenities and modern decor. Enjoy the convenience of being in the fashionable and high-end 16th arrondissement where museums, parks, and gardens are all within walking distance. Entering the apartment, you will be struck by how open and airy the rooms are. The white walls and tiled floors create a perfect backdrop for the beautiful furnishings and warm wood accents. The living-room is bathed in light thanks to the expansive windows that make up an entire wall.

Relax on the leather sectional sofa or one of the modern chairs as you make use of the large flat screen TV. Make sure to admire the unique light fixture above your head as well. The open floor plan also features a dining-room with seating for 8 at its cleverly configured triangular tables.

Nearby you will find the sleek white kitchen with black granite countertops and plenty of cabinet space. Everything you need is at your fingertips to whip up multi-­course dinners or just coffee and simple breakfasts. With the large window that looks out over the neighbourhood, you’ll enjoy natural light along with a charming view.

The spectacular wood and metallic spiral staircase is a prominent feature of this luxury vacation home and leads you to the incredible rooftop terrace. This gorgeous space is furnished with cozy lounge areas and features lush greenery and topiaries throughout. The glass barriers allow for uninterrupted views of the stylish neighbourhood.

The added convenience of a covered outdoor kitchen means you can entertain your guests easily. What’s better than a rooftop cocktail hour or dinner party? The 3 comfortable bedrooms are spread throughout the 2 levels that this luxury vacation home encompasses, ensuring privacy.

The Master Suite features a queen size bed with an upholstered headboard, while the en suite bath is home to an oversized vanity and a soaking tub. The second bedroom also has a queen size bed and a soft colour palette for a serene environment. The delicate mix of modern and vintage furniture is a lovely combination. The third bedroom is also home to a queen size bed and also has a small office space and flat screen TV for yourconvenience.

These 2 bedrooms are served by a contemporary bathroom with intriguing tile and a glassed-­in shower.
