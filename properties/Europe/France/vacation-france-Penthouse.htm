Penthouse, Saint Jean de luz, France Vacation Rental

 

Saint Jean de Luz may not be as known as Biarritz, but it is undisputed that it offers a more authentic Basque experience. Not only was the town a favourite haunt of the pirates in 17th century, it’s also where Louis XIV secured an alliance by marrying a Spanish princess. Today, as you walk the streets of Saint Jean de Luz, you will see many 17th & 18th century mansions but also museums, churches and other cultural and historical sights.

It is in Saint Jean de Luz where we have one of our most spacious apartments. With its remarkable 4 bedrooms, 3 full bathrooms, and 2 half baths, the Penthouse comfortably accommodates 8 guests with lots of room to spare. A state-of-the art, aesthetic kitchen with high ceilings, granite countertops is brightly lit by natural sunlight.

On the first floor there are 3 bedrooms, including a master suite with a queen-size bed. The other 2 bedrooms - one with a queen-size bed and the other with bunk beds - share a bathroom. On the second floor, another large bedroom with 2 single beds and en-suite bathroom can be found, with a separated toilet, plus the extensive living area and ultramodern, perfectly equipped kitchen.

From the living room you have a perfect view of the main boulevard of the city. Only 200 meters from the pedestrian shopping street “Rue Gambetta” - the heart of Saint Jean de Luz - and 400 meters away from the Grande Plage (main beach) the Penthouse is perfectly located. The fresh market, a grocery store and wide-ranging bars and restaurants are all within a few minutes’ walk.

The Penthouse features also a private parking space, which is a priceless plus during the summer months. If you wish to visit Biarritz or Spain, both can be reached within short 20-minutes.
