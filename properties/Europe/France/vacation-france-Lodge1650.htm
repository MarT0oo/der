Lodge 1650, Courchevel, France Vacation Rental

 

Lodge 1650 is a luxury six-bedroom luxury villa, sleeping 13 people, in the center of Courchevel 1650. It is adjacent to the ski lifts, which will give you access to the three-valley area, the largest in Europe. The chalet was initially a barn in the original village of Moriond, in twentieth century it was a local café and patisserie and family home and in recent years has been a ski chalet. The current owners purchased it in 2008 and have completely renovated and refurbished it to high standards of luxury.

The accommodation, roughly 2,500sq ft or 250sq metres, is spacious and comfortable. The owners, who have been holidaying in different ski chalets in the Three Valleys for many years, sought to ensure that the chalet would provide a home from home for family and friends. There is a cozy lounge with space for 13 to sit and chat around the fire. A spacious dining table with wonderful breakfast-time views over the Courchevel valley and the ski slopes. A separate den/TV room where the kids can catch up with their favorite programs or DVDs or the adults can catch up with their favorite programs or Sports on Sky+ when the kids have gone to bed.

The chalet is furnished with lovely individual pieces, some old, but all chosen for practicality and comfort. The walls throughout are hung with a mixture of folk art and the work of local photographers and artists. The atmosphere is of comfort, coziness and relaxation.

All the bedrooms have marble-tiled en-suites with baths to soak in after a long day on the piste or the option of a fast shower if you wish to go to après ski in the local bars. There is a large ski room with a purpose built boot warmer and ski rack. And to ease out the aching muscles at the end of the day a hammam/steam room.
