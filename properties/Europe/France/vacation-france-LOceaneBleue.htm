L'Oceane Bleue, Biarritz, France Vacation Rental

 

Biarritz beach apartment - L' Oceane Bleue is an extremely spacious two-storey apartment with an enviable location on the seafront in Biarritz. Set in a beautiful period building, all rooms have stunning sea views and a balcony running the length of the building offers a wonderful place to dine or just relax and watch the surf.

Biarritz is a very smart town with a Casino, chic boutiques, fabulous pavement cafes and excellent restaurants. Visitors have long been drawn here for the wonderful Thalassotherapy centres, harnessing the natural healing properties of the ocean. It is the birthplace of French surfing and the Biarritz Surf Festival attracts boarders from as far afield as Australia and Hawaii.

With a choice of 12 golf courses, all kinds of seaside water sport from windsurfing to delta-planing, as well as tennis, horse riding, cycling and hiking all within easy reach of this Biarritz beach apartment, Biarritz will cater for even the most demanding of action holidaymakers.

 

On the seafront in Biarritz, 2 mins walk from local shops & restaurants (Access to beach 100m). St Jean de Luz 10 miles. Spanish border 16 miles. Airport 20 mins. 12 golf courses, water sports, horse riding, tennis and numerous Thalassotherapy centres nearby.
