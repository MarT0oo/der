Ravignan II, Rue Ravignan Paris, Paris, France Vacation Rental

 

The funky 2-bedroom Ravignan II vacation apartment in Paris is situated in the picturesque Montmartre district, in Paris's 18th arrondissement. Many of the French capital's most famous sights are a brief walk away: the Moulin Rouge, birthplace of the can-can (12-min); the Sacre-Coeur basilica with its towering white dome (6 min); the Place du Tertre, a gathering place for street artists (3 min). With restaurants and bars on your doorstep, you'll have everything you need for a quintessentially Parisian experience. The Abbesses Metro station is within a 6-minute walk.

With spectacular views of the city, this apartment has been refurbished by our local interior designer for an iconic Parisian experience. Quirky surprises such as vintage-inspired furniture, vinyl records, and a ‘70s-style projection room give this apartment its charm. A fully equipped kitchen has everything you need for gourmet dining, and an in-house sauna is perfect for evening relaxation. Premium amenities include free Wi-Fi, cable TV, hotel beds and linens, luxury toiletries, and a Nespresso machine.

Colorful and centrally located, this one-of-a-kind vacation apartment is fun for the whole family.
