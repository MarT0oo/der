Milan IV, 14th arrondissement, Paris, France Vacation Rental

 

This charming holiday apartment combines style with comfort. Its interiors are imbued with a contemporary design in shades of yellow, cream and tan.

The clean, bold lines and curves of the furniture complement the tasteful colour palette and create vibrant spaces where guests can relax and revive. The apartment comprises of ample rooms designed to let in the light and the minimal wood and rattan furniture create an atmosphere that is both stylish and comfortable. The interiors are enhanced with unique accents such as striking wall hangings, a handmade tepee tent, colourful cushions and hanging plants.

The large, trendy wall clock offsets the well designed and functional kitchen area. Located in the Haussmann-Saint-Lazare-Opéra district, there are many wonderful opportunities for sightseeing and entertainment. As the city centre is just a short walk away, guests can visit the Opera Garnier and enjoy the magnificent Chagall ceiling or the Madeline Church with its beautiful Corinthian columns with ease. Many locally popular venues, like the prestigious Galeries Lafayette, and excellent restaurants are just a short walk away.

Furthermore, the apartment is a wonderful central point for travellers as the Haussmann-Saint-Lazare train station and the city’s transport network is located nearby.
