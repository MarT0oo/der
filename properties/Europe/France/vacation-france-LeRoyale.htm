Le Royale, 16th district, Paris, France Vacation Rental

 

This apartment is exquisitely designed with the perfect mixture of old Parisian features and modern furniture. It fits in perfectly with the area in which it is situated – the golden triangle - as you will be surrounded by Paris’ high society as well as being within close proximity to many beautiful landmarks. As you enter this luxurious flat you will be greeted with an elegant hallway which opens up to a beautifully lit living and dining area. Its bright and spacious living area is the perfect place to relax with friends and family after a hectic day in the city.

This property also caters to your needs: you can enjoy a croissant and a nespresso in your modern fully-equipped kitchen, or settle down to a favourite movie at night. This apartment can comfortably accommodate up to 7 people. The 3 spacious bedroom come with a beautiful ensuite which boasts a large shower and/ or bath.

In the 8th arrondissement, you are in the heart of Paris's 'golden triangle,' between two of Haussmann's grandest triumphs - the Avenue Georges V and the Champs Elysées. Here you can visit the biggest Louis Vuitton store in the world as well as every French fashion and luxury brand. Apart from the chic shopping, there are museums and theaters as well as a slew of Michelin-starred restaurants. Some of the area's notable landmarks include the Arc de Triomphe, Place de la Concorde, Grand Palais and Petit Palais as well as the Museum of Modern Art of Paris. Just 200 meters away is Franklin D Roosevelt Metro station that offers access to any point in the capital and beyond. For those willing to brave Paris traffic there are two public parking garages along the Champs Elysées too. No wonder this area of Paris has the most expensive real estate in France.
