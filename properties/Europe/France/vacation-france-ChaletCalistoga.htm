Chalet Calistoga, Val d’Isére, France Vacation Rental

 

Chalet Calistoga is a brand new addition to the our portfolio, completely renovated in 2016 this luxury chalet is located in the exclusive domain of Du Cret in Val d'Isere. Sleeping ten guests in five ensuite bedrooms this chalet enjoys spectacular views from the chalet.

The double height living area enjoys panoramic views down the valley through the floor to ceiling windows, with doors out on to your terrace. The relaxed seating area is located around the fireplace and the adjoining dining area is spacious, to ensure you can relax and enjoy the meals prepared by your private chef. On the same floor you can find a TV snug suitable for young and old, perfect to watch movies or television together, complete with Sky and Apple TV. On the lower floor there is a children's games room with TV.

There are two twin/superking bedrooms which open out through double doors onto the terrace at garden level, the third double bedroom is located alongside a smaller bedroom twin/superking bed. The fifth Austrian twin/superking bedroom is on the lower ground level, enjoying amazing mountain views.

Spa facilities include a large swimming pool, sauna, shower and separate massage room. We can arrange for therapists to visit the chalet so you can enjoy a relaxing massage or other treatments after a hard days skiing.

Your private chauffeur will be on hand to whisk you from pillow to piste throughout the day, available exclusively to you and your guests, providing an in-resort facility from 8am - 11pm. The rest of the team will ensure your stay is exactly as you envisaged, planned right down to the last detail.
