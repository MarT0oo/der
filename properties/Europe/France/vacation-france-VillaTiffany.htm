Villa Tiffany, Ramatuelle, St Tropez, France Vacation Rental

 

Family villa with heated pool near Pampelonne beach - Within a couple of miles of Pampelonne beach and Ramatuelle, this family villa with heated pool sits in a typical Mediterranean garden and offers the ideal location from which to explore the St Tropez peninsula and the entire French Riviera.

Spacious and comfortable inside, this villa is perfect for families who also enjoy outdoor living; be it a table tennis game, playing in the large flat garden, swimming in the long heated pool or just relaxing in the courtyard with a good book and an icy-cold drink. The old village of Ramatuelle is within 10 minutes' drive with a typical Provencal market on Thursday , Jazz concerts during June and August, not to mention numerous excellent restaurants.

From your ‘home away from home’ you will also be able to visit quaint inland villages such as Gassin, Cogolin or Grimaud for interesting sightseeing and browsing the local market stalls. Alternatively, the splendid Mediterranean sea is almost on your doorstep, with a range of lovely sandy beaches. Pampelonne beach clubs are famous and still attract today’s celebrities mooring their yachts in the bay of St Tropez. Les Bronzes beach restaurant is your closest port of call for long lazy lunches, at the southernmost tip of Pampelonne bay, but be sure to book! All along the coastline from St Tropez to L’Escalet and Gigaro beaches you will find countless opportunities for water sports.

note: the caretaker lives within the grounds, behind the main property but has an independent entrance.
