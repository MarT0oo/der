Napoleon Luxury, 7th district, Paris, France Vacation Rental

 

This classic French apartment is on the second floor of a classic building with secure entry and elevator. From the French windows across the front of the apartment lies the wrap-around balcony. Around the corner you can catch a glimpse of the Eiffel Tower. The 105 square meter (1,130 square feet) apartment is tastefully furnished with a mix of 1940’s antiques and contemporary. The artwork was especially selected to give you the true feeling of being in Paris, the City of Light. The Master bedroom has ample storage together with a King-sized bed. It can be configured into two twins. The second bedroom includes a built-in wardrobe and a window looking down on the interior garden. For additional guests there is a comfortable third bedroom/secondary living room where there is also a flat screen TV with a Blu-ray player. The large bathroom contains a bathtub/shower, good shelving and lots of space; while the smaller one has a traditional shower and Metro tiles. The water closet, in French tradition, is separate. High-quality bath linens are provided throughout together with a hairdryer in each bathroom.

Home to Paris' most iconic landmark, the Eiffel Tower, the 7th arrondissement on the rive gauche (left bank) of the Seine is also one of the city's prime residential areas. A little like the Upper East Side or London's Westminster, the area features the French National Assembly and a number of other government departments so has long been a favoured residential district. The 7th arrondissement houses the Musée d'Orsay, Musée Rodin and a number of wonderful parks ¬– the Champs de Mars and Esplanade des Invalides. The 7th has an upmarket, quiet vibe where you can escape from noisier touristy areas and feel like a true Parisian.
