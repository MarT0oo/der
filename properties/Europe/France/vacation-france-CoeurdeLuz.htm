Coeur de Luz, Biarritz, France Vacation Rental

 

St Jean de Luz Pays Basque holiday villa - Coeur de Luz sits in a superb position right in the centre of St Jean de Luz, just 50 yards from restaurants, shops and pavement cafes and only a five-minute walk from the sandy beach.

Furnished in a minimalist style, this spacious architecturally designed penthouse will be sure to appeal to anyone who appreciates convenience and likes to be right in the heart of the action. A private elevator takes you up to the top of this bright and comfortable penthouse, from where you can enjoy dual aspect views across this little Basque beach town. St Jean de Luz is full of charm, with its delightful squares and attractive waterfront buildings, its fishing port lined with colourful boats and its fantastic seafood!

Heading south along the west coast, this is the last major French town before you reach the Spanish border. There are plenty of beaches nearby and you can enjoy water sports galore, such as windsurfing, kite-surfing and delta-planing. Fashionable Biarritz hosts the famous Surf Festival which attracts competitors from Australia and Hawaii. Golfers (and novices) are well catered for on the links in nearby Bidart. And heading inland are chateaux, evocative medieval villages and spa towns such as Dax. The bustling regional capital Bayonne is known for its chocolatiers and the Bonnat art museum, and elegant Pau in the foothills of the Pyrenees is another excellent choice for a day out.
