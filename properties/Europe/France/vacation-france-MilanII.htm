Milan II, 14th arrondissement, Paris, France Vacation Rental

 

Found in the romantic heart of Paris, this lovely apartment exudes opulent rejuvenation. Designed with a contemporary style in shades of tan, grey and white with alluring blue accents; the apartment interiors make a charming visual statement.

The sleek shapes of the furniture, creative wall décor and delicate accessories invite guests to feel at home in style and comfort. The apartment is bathed with light during the day and the spacious rooms and stunning city views make this the ideal place to enjoy a glorious holiday. From the stylish, functional kitchen to the elegant bedrooms; each section of the apartment has been skilfully designed to give its guests the best experience possible.

Located in the Haussmann-Saint-Lazare-Opéra district, guests will find themselves in one of the world’s best locations for entertainment and sightseeing. If you enjoy the opera, make sure to visit the wonderful Opera Garnier nearby. For those that love fine architecture, the Madeline Church with its majestic Corinthian columns and the Chagall ceiling of the Opera Garnier must not be missed.

There is also a splendid selection of entertainment venues, boutiques and restaurants just a short walk away from the apartment, and 10 minutes walk from famous Galeries Lafayette. For those who wish to see the rest of France, the apartment makes an excellent base as easy access to the nation’s transportation system is provided.
