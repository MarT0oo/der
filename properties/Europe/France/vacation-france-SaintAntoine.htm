Saint Antoine, 11th district, Paris, France Vacation Rental

 

Stay in the spirited neighborhood of Faubourg Saint Antoine in this apartment renovated with modern furnishings. Located on the 6th floor with elevator access from the 1st floor, this charming building with an area for bike storage is situated on rue Paul Bert. In Paris' 11th district, it won't take long for you to feel right at home.

Covering an area of 323 sq ft, the Saint Antoine opens to a cozy living room with a convertible sofa bed for a 3rd guest. Take advantage of the fully equipped American style kitchen and dining table to prepare and enjoy your meals at home. You will also find a double bedroom with access to the bathroom with a shower. Completely renovated in February 2013, the Saint Antoine is truly flawless. The exceptional view of the lush courtyard completes this property.

Home to some of the finest furniture and cabinet makers in Paris, Faubourg Saint Antoine stretches between place de la Bastille and Nation. Brimming with character, this neighborhood is a mecca for craftsmen and art lovers alike, with its art galleries, independent libraries, and the Aligre Market -- the first of its kind in Paris, open every day except Monday. Continue your sightseeing around the nearby neighborhoods of Oberkampf, Charonne and Ménilmontant, visiting Père Lachaise cemetery, the Saint Martin Canal, gare de Lyon or the Opera along the way! Explore Parisian nightlife in the area known for its trendy bars, bistros, and concert halls like the Bastille Opera house. Connect to other districts in Paris with ease from the closeby metro Faidherbe-Chaligny, line 8, and lines 1, 2, 6, 9, and RER A at Nation station.
