Le Loft, Biarritz, France Vacation Rental

 

Ultra modern 5 bedroom Loft with breathtaking living room in the heart of Biarritz

This aptly named New York style apartment, ‘Le Loft’, is a unique discovery in Biarritz.

Step out of the apartment and find yourself directly in amongst the action of the city with all the shops, cafés, bars, restaurants and beaches you could possibly ever want or need, all on your front door. Step back into the apartment and try to keep your jaw from dropping as you admire the enormity of the space and the beauty of the parquet flooring, the immensely high ceilings, the rich mix of historic and ultra modern architectural features such as the architraves throughout, the fireplace in the living room and the steel beams which support the upper mezzanine level as well as the glass see-through bridge/gangway which connects two of the rooms on the upper floor and allows light to illuminate the kitchen below.

The open plan living room and dining room is truly sensational with both a 10 person sofa and a 10 person dining table. The plasma screen TV is cleverly hidden behind a piece of wall mounted artwork which electronically slides up to expose the screen at a click of a button. The kitchen is partitioned off from the main room but the glass walls allow a simple and well transitioned connection. Perfectly proportioned, with all the mod cons you could possibly ever need for creating a culinary masterpiece.

There are 5 bedrooms in total. The first bedroom, with a double bed, TV and en-suite shower room is found on the ground floor just off the main living/dining room. Adjoining the bedroom and communal to all is a small office space.

On the upper mezzanine level, you will find the other 4 bedrooms. 3 of which (1 x twin and 2 x double) share a separate toilet and large shower room and the master bedroom is a real showpiece with a large double bed and open plan, en-suite, shower room with a beautiful rainforest style shower.

The apartment also benefits from a large combined washroom, wet room, linen and laundry room, located on the ground floor, perfect for washing off straight after the beach ; a pantry just off the kitchen and a wine fridge located in the dining/living room.

‘Le Loft’ comes with access to its own private parking and access to swimming pool, spa and gym facilities.
