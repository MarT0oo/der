Villa La Gabbia, Cote D'azur, French Riviera Vacation Rental

 

Villa La Gabbia in the Cote d'azur is a gloriously unique Luxury Vacation Rental perches at the water’s edge on St Jean Cap Ferrat, an exclusive peninsula home to the world’s most expensive real estate. Both rare and listed, this nearly 100-year-old villa has been lovingly restored to its former wonder, maintaining its original architecture but crafting a designer interior of marble, parquet, fine art and bespoke furniture. The villa’s five floors drape down the hillside in a whimsical fashion, its plethora of windows all with dazzling sea views. Facing west, La Gabbia is blessed with nightly sunsets over the Bay of Villefranche Sur Mer, one of the most beautiful bays on earth. These breathtaking vistas can be appreciated from everywhere in the villa including its extensive terraces and luxurious spa area with jacuzzi pool and sauna.

The property features 5 amazing bedrooms that have all been completely remodeled and furnished with luxuriously soft beds that you will melt into, the highest thread count linens and the most amazing layout with each room having a wonderful suite, with 1 King and 4 Oversized Queen Beds. There is such a warm feeling when you arrive and are taken to your room, you will feel the house speak to you. The chef and butler are there every day to make sure you are getting the best care possible, this isn't just another stay this is La Gabbia and at the villa you are treated to the Royal Affair.

As with all our properties, La Gabbia has been finished to the highest of specifications with the most up to date technical features to suit your every need. Security is paramount and the property includes a fully integrated alarm system.
