Maubeuge, 10th district, Paris, France Vacation Rental

 

You'll spend an enjoyable vacation with your family or friends in this splendid, well-decorated 3-room apartment. Located on the 2nd floor of a grand building on rue Rocroy, you'll be just a short walk from rue Lafayette and the gare du Nord train station.

Le Maubeuge apartment is an impressive 754 ft2 (​​70 m2). Within you'll find a double living room with a fully-equipped kitchen, an American-style bar, and a dining table. After a long day or business meetings or sightseeing you'll have the opportunity to relax on the large sofa and watch a movie on the flat-screen TV or surf the net with unlimited internet access. With two double bedrooms and a bathroom with an elegant bath and separate toilet, the apartment can comfortably accommodate four people,.

The 10th district is a growing neighborhood in the heart of Paris where you can explore the culture of Paris. You'll find yourself close to the Place de la République, Ménilmontant, Cimetière du Père Lachaise, Bastille, the parc des Buttes Chaumont, the Canal de l’Ourq, and the Marais area. In this popular and trendy district, you can go to crowded bars and mingle with the locals, or people-watch from the sidewalk cafes.

An abundance of other historical monuments are just a subway-ride away. Just in front of the apartment you'll find many subway lines (Poissonière Metro Line 7, or North Station, lines 4 and 5).
