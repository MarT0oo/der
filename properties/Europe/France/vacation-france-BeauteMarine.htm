St Tropez seaside villa – This is an exceptionally rare single storey 1920’s property situated only a few yards from the beachfront, with its own private jetty and outstanding views over the bay of St Tropez towards St Maxime. Wake up and enjoy a refreshing breakfast under the pergola covered terrace touched by a gentle marine breeze, then take a therapeutic stroll in the shallow water while the children look for colourful shells and starfish on the shore.

This villa’s private jetty is a perfect spot for sunbathing, fishing or boat trips along the coast and offers a privileged view of the most glorious sunsets across the bay of St Tropez. For a change of scenery you can venture inland to discover the hidden treasures of the Var region and nearby Provence: market towns, local gastronomic festivals, historical sites or the natural beauty of the Massif des Maures.

Trendy restaurants and shops in St Tropez are within reach as the famous Pampelonne Beach and Club 55, sought after by celebrities and seaside lovers alike.
