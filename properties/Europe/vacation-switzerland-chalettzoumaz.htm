Luxury Swiss Chalet
6 Bedrooms | 6 Baths Ensuite                                                                  
Laz Tzoumaz, Verbier, Switzerland
  From €325 - €1270 per week

The chalet is laid out over 2 floors plus a mezzanine level. Comfortably sleeping 12 (16 when using the bunk beds) in 6 bedrooms, each with its own en-suite facilities. Furnished with large comfortable beds which can be arranged to suit singles or as a super king size double. Our flexible sleeping arrangements allow 2 of the bedrooms to be arranged as family or children’s rooms, as the beds have been specially made to provide removable top bunks. 4 of the ensuite bathrooms have shower enclosures with luxury powerful showers. The remaining 2 have full-size baths with the same showers above. Quality Egyptian linen and fluffy towels come as standard.

 

The stunning open plan living/dining room has a ceiling that is open to the eaves, overlooked from the mezzanine library - a spacious area in which to relax in the squashy leather sofas in front of a real fire. Dinner is served around the elegant, large square table, allowing everyone plenty of elbow room. The entertainment system is linked to the internet for access to all your favorite music and videos.

Alternatively, take some time out and soothe away the day’s aches and pains in the sauna followed by a soak in the hot tub out on the terrace – wherever you are, you’ll have extraordinary views of the Swiss Alps.  We understand that guests need to keep in touch with the wider world (or even do some work – God forbid!) so the chalet is secure Wi-Fi enabled throughout and there is a desktop PC dedicated to guests use. There’s also a separate TV room, with big soft beanbags, a large flat screen, giving the kids their own space to play games, but also to make sure that you don’t miss the important match of the season.
