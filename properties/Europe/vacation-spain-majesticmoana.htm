Majestic Villa, Mijas Costa, Malaga, Spain


Holiday Majestic Villa is truly one of the most magnificent homes located on the Costa del Sol.

Majestic Villa is an oasis of luxury and exclusivity, where the pace of life is slow and relaxing. Here, you create your own definition of paradise and envelope the senses, and outside cares will be forgotten. A staff of seven, including a five star chef come with the villa.

Hidden from the outside world, a rather unassuming entrance leads you into another world. An expansive granite drive passes lush topiary, elegant fountains and native tropical blooms. Majestic Villa is first and foremost a home. The rich furnishings are intended to create intimate living spaces and stylish comfort. Invigorate both mind and body in two indoor health spas or you can step outside for a game of tennis, a run on the beach or enjoy a cooling dip in the fabulous outdoor swimming pool. This remarkable house is fully staffed to include a house manager, butler, household staff and an award winning private chef. Personal service here is considered and art form. It is delivered with absolute discretion and total commitment. Here you create your own definition of paradise.

Each of the nine bedrooms is an individual suite with it’s own well appointed bathroom, private terrace, a wealth of sumptuous finishes including canopied beds, French antiques, beautiful Persian rugs, separate dressing areas and jacuzzis. Each room rewards its guests with magnificent 180 degree ocean views.

The main entrance hall begs contemplation and appreciation. The drawing room, with its adjoining semi-enclosed sitting room, is an ideal setting for the ancient art of conversation. A discreet lift leads you gently up to the first floor where you will find an expansive living area. There are two sitting rooms, a glorious formal dining room, a large open plan living space with dining area and a superb terrace with sumptuous seating.

Stroll through the beautiful gardens to discover secret corners with stylish outdoor furniture, ideal for private moments of quiet contemplation. Discreet gates lead on to the secluded beach where guests can dip their toes into cool Mediterranean waters. A terracotta tiled sundeck surrounds the magnificent swimming pool and palm trees offer cooling shade on a hot summer’s day.
