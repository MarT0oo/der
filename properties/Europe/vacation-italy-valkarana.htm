ValKarana Villa Hotel, Sardegna, Italy


The ValKarana Villa-Hotel was developed to offer the best choice for anyone who wishes to enjoy an intense and relaxing vacation, in full privacy, minutes from Costa Smeralda.

ValKarana Villa-Hotel and Restaurant is located a short distance from Sant' Antonio di Gallura, a small village in the very heart of this beautiful corner of Sardinia. The Villa is quietly overlooking Liscia Lake, one of the most important artificial lakes of the entire island, a fundamental resource of water for the surrounding area and the famous Costa Smeralda.

From the white rustic beaches and crystal clear sea of Costa Smeralda, to the most interesting archaeological spots of Gallura, ValKarana is the ideal base from which to explore this wonderful island.  The ValKarana Villa-Hotel is composed by 18 double rooms each with an enchanting view of the Liscia Lake. All rooms are designed with Sardinian décor. Each is provided with air conditioning, satellite TV, refrigerator, private bath and parking.  In order to provide our guests the maximum freedom during their stay, all rooms have been designed with independent access.

Living Sardinia to the fullest means discovering its scents and tastes,  ValKarana Restaurant offers its visitors the most renowned dishes of the entire Sardinian Cuisine: from Porcetto Arrosto and Zuppa Gallurese, local salami, wines and cheeses, to Italian and International cuisine, satisfying every taste.

ValKarana is also an ideal location for weddings, events, meetings, concerts, fashion shows, banquets and ceremonies. Its position makes it a perfect choice also for executive business lunches and dinners. Set apart from the glamour of Costa Smeralda and immersed in the charming natural scenery, among the green of Gallura's wilderness and the blue of the sky and Lago Liscia.

ValKarana is also offered for rent as a private residence to the discerning client.  Upon your arrival in Sardinia, it is easy access to Sant'Antonio di Gallura and ValKarana Hotel and Restaurant. Olbia Airport, Private Jet Airport and the Ports of Olbia and Golfo Aranci are a mere 20 minutes away.   Thanks to the many offers of the main European low cost airlines throughout Italy and Europe, Sardinia is even nearer: Meridiana, Hapag Lloyd Express, Easyjet, Ryanair. With fares starting from 9,99 Euros, spending a weekend in Gallura is truely within reach!

“The perfect choice” for a pleasant and relaxing stay in Sardinia.
