Pebble Beach Retreat, Banyalbufar, Mallorca, Spain Vacation Rental

 

Literally translating as ‘red earth’ from the local Mallorquín language, this four-bedroom villa is situated near the edge of the Estate and a 20 minute walk from a pebble beach. Pebble Beach Retreat also has a private heated swimming pool (15 x 5.5 metres) and plenty of space on the terrace for sunbathing. This villa comfortably sleeps 8 adults in 4 en-suite bedrooms making it the perfect place to sit back, relax and enjoy the view.

Get close to the gorgeous scenery of Mallorca at Pebble Beach Retreat. This beautiful private hillside vacation rental, whose name translates to “red earth,” is set between the hills near Banyalbufar and the sea. Its traditional architecture, refined interiors, four suite-style bedrooms and personalized service create a wonderful luxury escape for up to eight friends and family members.

Your holiday at Pebble Beach Retreat includes a daily continental breakfast and housekeeping, as well as all beverages and lunch and dinner on six days out of a seven-day stay. Take in the panoramic sea views from the sunny sitting area on the terrace, a lounge chair by the heated pool or the al-fresco dining area, which is shaded by a pergola. The hammock is perfect for a blissful afternoon nap.

With a tile roof, stone walls and wood-framed windows, the villa takes after the traditional Mallorcan style. The charm continues inside, thanks to terracotta tile floors, beamed ceilings and brick fireplaces, while tuxedo-style sofas and oversized ottomans add both modern style and inviting seats to the living room. At mealtime, gather in the refined formal dining room or, on more casual occasions, perch on the bistro-style stools at the breakfast bar in the fully equipped kitchen.

Each of the four bedrooms at Pebble Beach Retreat has a king bed, en-suite bathroom and sound system, and one has ocean views as well. The same terracotta floors and beamed ceilings reappear in the bedrooms for a cozy, rustic feel, while built-in headboards add a sleek touch and vibrant artwork lends colorful personality.

Known for its dramatic mountains, balmy weather and breathtaking beaches, Mallorca has long been a favorite for everyone from jet-set couples on honeymoon to royal families on summer vacations. It’s just a 20-minute walk from the villa’s perch in the hills to the nearest pebble beach, and 2 miles to the center of Banyalbufar. Don’t miss the area’s ancient watchtower, seaside walks and tucked-away beach coves, but if you want to see more of the island, downtown Palma and several other beaches are within day-trip distance.
