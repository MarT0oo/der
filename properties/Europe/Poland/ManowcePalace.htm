Manowce Palace, Tzebieradz, Poland Vacation Rental

 

Manowce is a historic property uniquely located in northwestern Poland, surrounded by vast woods, far from residential areas and set right on the shore of a large bay next to the Baltic Sea. Despite its remote location, Manowce is only 2.5 hours from Berlin and its airports. Manowce is one of the very few luxury villas in this part of Europe where you can hire the property for exclusive holiday use. We also provide the group with a personal English and German speaking butler/concierge.

We charge a flat rate per person per day that includes the venue hire, accommodation and catering. For the common spaces in the palace building, you can hire either only the lower-ground floor or the lower-ground floor and the upper-ground floor. The charge that covers the lower-ground floor of the palace is 280 EUR (310 USD) per person per day. If you also want to hire the upper-ground floor, we charge 350 EUR (385 USD) per person per day. The minimum group size for the exclusive holiday hire is 15 adults or children aged over five, so the minimum total fee is 4,200 EUR (4,650 USD) per day. The minimum stay is 7 days and the maximum is 28 days. If guests choose self-catering, we reduce the rates by 30 EUR (35 USD) per each person per day.

Manowce offers 3 stunning, luxurious apartments and 13 double bedrooms, accommodating up to 33 guests in total, or 42 including sofa beds and extra beds in some rooms. All the rooms have en-suite bathrooms and large televisions, and there is Wi-Fi access throughout.

NEIGHBOURHOOD (THINGS TO DO)

At Manowce, you can sunbathe on the private shore of the lagoon, swim in the bay and hunt for mushrooms in the woods, and you will have complimentary use of our kayaks, fishing boat with rods, and bicycles. You can also enjoy our bio sauna free of charge. Other activities, such as horse riding and shooting, can be arranged for you.

GETTING AROUND

Despite its remote location, Manowce is only 40 minutes from Szczecin (Stettin) and 2.5 hours from Berlin and its airports. We will be pleased to offer your group a shuttle from Szczecin or Berlin, and to take your group to all historic, sports or cultural venues in our vicinity.
