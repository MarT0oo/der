Juliana, Quinta do Lago, Algarve, Portugal Vacation Rental

 

Juliana is a recently renovated well-presented two bedroom apartment located on the first floor in Encosta Do Lago. With 2 en-suite bedrooms with a bright open plan kitchen and lounge area.
