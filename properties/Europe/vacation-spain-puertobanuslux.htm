Puerto Banus Luxury, Marbella, Spain Vacation Rental


Puerto Banus Luxury is set in beautiful landscaped gardens complete with outstanding water features. The newly constructed the villa has every modern amenity. Offering high-speed wireless internet, and air conditioning throughout, as well as central heating to keep you warm during your Christmas holidays in Marbella. On the ground floor there are two double bedrooms both with en-suites, a large airy and fully equipped kitchen and a large living/dining room for 12/14. The living/dining room has a wall mounted 50’ HD Plasma TV, Blu-Ray DVD, plus 140 satellite channels. On the first floor there are a further 4 double bedrooms all with luxury en-suite bathrooms finished in Italian marble, double-ended baths, and large walk-in showers. There is a very large 20 x 12m feature pool complete with a tropical island, stunning views and underwater seating. Adjacent to the main pool is the heated Jacuzzi. There is also a table tennis table, cross-trainer and exercise bike that allows you to stay fit whilst topping up your tan.

The villa has been beautifully decorated by leading interior designers Hamilton Interiors, with very high quality furniture, hand-made works of art, sumptuous king-sized beds with luxurious Egyptian cotton sheets, extensive outdoor sofas and rattan loungers.

The villa is tastefully decorated to a high standard using Italian cream stone, authentic Spanish tiles and polished marble throughout the house. The villa offers air conditioning throughout, plus silent hand-casted fans with teak blades (for those who prefer to sleep without air-conditioning), as well as central heating should you require extra warmth in the winter months.

On the ground floor of the villa you have two double bedrooms both with en-suites, a large airy and fully equipped kitchen and a large lounge diner which comes complete with a wall mounted HD 50'' flat screen TV, DVD, Blu-Ray, Satellite TV, iPod dock and home cinema surround sound system. The dining area has seating for up to 12. From the lounge and the kitchen as well as one of the bedrooms you have direct access to a large free-form swimming pool and terrace areas, all offering you stunning panoramic views.

On the first floor there are a further 4 double bedrooms with beautiful en-suite bathrooms finished in polished marble complete with baths and large walk-in showers fitted with both wall and rain showers. From three of the bedrooms you have access to a private terrace overlooking the gardens and swimming pool, and from where you can take in the breath-taking panoramic mountain views. All bedrooms have 32" LED HD TV's with 140 satellite channels.

In addition three of the bedrooms also have private balconies giving dual aspect views. In fact all six bedrooms offer gorgeous views. The rear gardens would not be out of place in the finest boutique hotels and contain a stunning 20m pool complete with a tropical Island and underwater seating area for that ultimate long cool drink whilst admiring the stunning mountain views. Adjacent to the main pool is the heated Jacuzzi – the place to watch the sunset with a cooling cocktail.

Other features include games, videos, a Sony PlayStation 3, a table tennis table, an exercise bike and cross trainer, plus other gym/exercise equipment for those who want to stay fit while topping up their sun tan. The villa also has mountain bikes available for those who wish to enjoy 3 kilometres of enclosed private roads giving panoramic views within the estate.
