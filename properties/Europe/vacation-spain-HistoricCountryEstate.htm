Historic Country Estate, Deia, Spain Vacation Rental

 

A very impressive 17th Century country estate situated on the north coast of Mallorca at some 2km from Soller, with app. 250,000 m2 (60 acres) of land, including olive, orange and lemon grove, palms, fruit orchard and vegetable gardens with a large house of much character. Built around an authentic interior patio with an original olive press dining room,cinema & pool room, and a swimming pool barbecue pavilion with kitchen comprising a total area of some 1,600 m2 (17,500 sq.ft.).

Living rooms, a large country kitchen, a dramatic dining room, a lovely Master suite with dressing room, library and marble bathroom to outside gardens, as well as five other large double bedrooms with their bathrooms provides the main accommodation.There are also an additional two apartments; one Master Suite with separate entrance and living/kitchen plus large 4-poster bedroom leading to the pool terrace and the Second Apartment with two bedrooms,shared shower room and living area/kitchenette.
