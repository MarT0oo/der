Tivoli Lodge, Davos Klosters, Switzerland


The Majestic Tivoli Lodge has set a new benchmark in Alpine Luxury.

If ever there was a chalet destined to redefine Alpine luxury, Tivoli Lodge is it. One of the most desirable mountain retreats anywhere, this fabulous design-led chalet is intensely discreet, yet ski-in ski-out and only a two minute chauffeured drive from the centre of the village. The traditional exterior belies a chic and contemporary interior with more than 700m2 of living space and sensational views from the south-facing windows towards the highest peaks of the Engadine.

Tivoli is perfect for relaxing too. The property boasts a spa complex housing the stone indoor pool with a jet stream, massage waterfall and air-jet massage, jacuzzi and a separate sauna and steam room complex. For the younger guests the chalet offers an incredible indoor two story soft play gym for up to twenty children.

With 7 Bedrooms and Bathrooms this Lodge offers accommodations for up to 12 adults and 4 children.  There are 6 staff members that are there when you need them, which is included in the rate.  The Chalet is Ski in and Ski out and the nearest lifts are only 200m away on skis.   The center of the Village is a 5 minute drive. 

Our Facilities include:  Indoor swimming pool with massage and jets and jet steam, hot tub, relaxation area, library, study, media room with plasma television, satellite television and state of the art surround sound, ski room with heated boot racks, a la carte wine list, Nespresso Machine, 2 chauffeured 4X2s, Toys, sledges, games, books, iPod docking station, terrace overlooking the village, feather pillows and duvets, personal bathrobes and slippers, a hairdryer in every bedroom, Jo Malone Bathroom products, daily newspaper, study with telephone and fax, wireless internet.
