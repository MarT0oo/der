Eiffel Tower Art Deco, 16th district, Paris Vacation Rental

 

For those who like their luxury quiet and understated, step into this hushed art deco masterpiece – the home of an internationally-renowned architect right in the classy 16th district. The mesmeric views of the bateaux mouches on the Seine and the grand spire of Gustav's tower provide the dramatic back-story to what is a finely nuanced masterpiece of interior architecture. A muted palate unifies this third floor, two-bed, two-bath sophisticated home serviced by an elevator and fully equipped with everything you could possibly want.

Ah the 16-ième – home to Paris' old money. She resides in the tree-lined, balconied Haussmann buildings, shops in the rue Passy, strolls in the Bois de Boulogne and catches some culture in the neighbouring 7th - home to the Musée Rodin, d'Orsay and a dozen more galleries. Here is quiet style of the old variety – classy, sophisticated, intimate. Base yourself here, just a street away from the Seine and you have the city at your feet and yet are cocooned in a peaceful escape from the bustle and noise.
