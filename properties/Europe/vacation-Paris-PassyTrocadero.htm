Passy Trocadero, 16th district, Paris, France Vacation Rental

 

This elegant apartment is on the rue Lekain, a quiet street just a stone's throw from the bustle and café life of Passy and Trocadero. It features bags of reception space and superb original detailing from the parquet floors to the panelled walls and marble fireplaces. The apartment offers interlinking drawing room, dining room and television room that can also convert into a second bedroom if required. The spacious master bedroom features a huge bed and adjoining shower room. The flat is beautifully furnished and decorated with works by the artist Sainte Fauste. This tasteful apartment would particularly suit those looking for outstanding entertainment space in the heart of Paris' chic 16th arrondissement.

If it's glamour and style you crave then the 16th (or 16ième) is for you. Here the wide residential streets with Haussmann's elegant facades and intimate gardens are home to Paris' well-heeled elite. Encompassing the two prime neighbourhoods of Passy and Auteuil on the rive droite (right bank) of the Seine, the 16th is Paris' version of the Upper East Side in New York or Chelsea and Kensington in London. Stuffed with museums from impressionism to crystal, the 16th is home to the famous Bois de Boulogne, Avenue Foch and the oh-so-chic shopping along the Rue de Passy. Should you tire of the glamorous living, then head for the Passy Cemetery where Paris' rich and famous end their days in spectacular marble mausoleums.
