Situated in the picturesque Avenida da Liberdade neighborhood, the stunning 4-bedroom Liberdade vacation home puts you in the heart of Lisbon. Known for its elegant dining and shopping, Avenida da Liberdade is also home to some of the city’s most beautiful architecture. Stroll to the scenic Eduardo VII Park, with its world-famous greenhouse (8 min), or browse museums such as the Natural History Museum (10 min). Restaurants and bars abound, and public transportation runs nearby.

Our local interior designer has created a charming, traditional space that will make you feel like royalty. Rustic wooden accents, colorful textiles, and patterned tiles add a touch of Portuguese flair. With two double beds, two singles and a sofa bed, this apartment can host up to 8 guests. The fully equipped kitchen is perfect for casual dining, and full-length picture windows fill the space with light. Premium amenities include free Wi-Fi, cable TV, hotel beds and linens, luxury toiletries, and a Nespresso machine.

For an old-world retreat in a central location, your family or group can’t do better than this gorgeous apartment.
