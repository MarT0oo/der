Amsterdam Loft, Amsterdam, Netherlands Vacation Rental


This stunning NO smoking luxury vacation rental apartment overlooks the Singel in front and has a little patio at the rear and sleeps 2. The apartment has floor heating underneath the Carrera marble floor, which makes it very comfortable through the whole apartment.

The living space has a couch that converts into a child’s bed easily, for smaller children a cot is available. On request we can place an extra bed (extra person = surcharge).
   
In the living area you'll find a spacious desk and a PC with internet access; Whenever you need to work during your stay, a broad band internet connection secures you with fast access. On request we can install and configure a voip telephone (surcharge) in your room with your own telephone number.

The kitchenette, is equipped with Nespresso machine, kettle, fridge, ceramic cooker and a microwave/oven.  In the private and spacious bathroom you find a large Jacuzzi with mood lights and  a separate rain shower.

For sleeping you'll find a comfortable king size bed with a selection of pillows. With the secure entrance guests can relax and enjoy the option of a longer term let. We supply once a week Linen and Towel change and once a week & upon check out cleaning service is included in the rate.
