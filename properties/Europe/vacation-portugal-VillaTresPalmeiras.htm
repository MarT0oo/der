Villa Três Palmeiras Algarve Portugal


An imposing 3 or 4 bedroom villa located on the quieter Eastern Peninsular of Martinhal with stunning views over protected Natural Park and the Atlantic Ocean.

Villa Três Palmeiras has one of the largest private swimming pools at Martinhal (with hard electronic cover), surrounded by beautifully landscaped gardens and a raised sunbathing deck overlooking those fabulous views! Large terrace and BBQ area.

This spacious villa has 4 large bedrooms arranged as 2 doubles and 2 twins, 3 bathrooms, comfortable living room with Sky TV (basic channels), dining room, and fully equipped kitchen . All rooms on the ground floor open onto the garden and terrace, and the master bedroom has a huge sun terrace.

There is a direct pathway from the villa leading through the countryside leading directly to the remote Rebilinhos beach.


They say the Portuguese waters hold a mystery that lovers often find shown in the glimpse of the ships passing in the night. The shores of Portugal from The Porto (North Portugal) where you can find history afoot while visiting local historical sites: CASA-Museu de Guerra Junqueiro and the Museum of Quinta da Macieirinha. Or one could easily slip away to visit the city and visit the stock exchange and cathedral. Or if your prefer Tras-os-Momtes where you can do tons outside, such as canoeing or mountain trekking. On the North Coast of Minho you can discover amazing caves and superb beaches.
