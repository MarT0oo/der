Desejos, Quinta do Lago, Portugal Vacation Rental

 

A spectacular 6 bedroom magnificient family villa. The gated property is located in a quiet position within a cul-de-sac. Desejos is an ideal getaway in the heart of the exclusive resort of Quinta de Lago.

Desejos consists of 6 bedrooms that are all finished to a high standard and all have en-suites, built in wardrobes and plasma TV's.

The top floor of the villa is where two bedrooms are located. The first is the master bedroom and this room oozes pure luxury. It has everything that anyone could ever want, with a king size bed, an en-suite with twin basins and a double walk in shower. There is a separate kitchen with a traditional finish, walk in wardrobes an office, balcony, sun terrace and a Jacuzzi for those times when peace and quiet and privacy is required.

Also located on the top floor is Bedroom 2. This consists of a double bed and two private balconies with an en-suite that has a full bathroom with twin basins and a double shower.

The ground floor also holds two more double bedrooms that both have en-suites with twin basins and double showers. Both bedrooms give access to sun terraces that give each bedroom its own little piece of something special. Unwinding after a day in the sun or just digesting the spectacular views, the sun terrace captures the beauty of Quinta do Lago.

Bedrooms 5 and 6 are situated in the basement but they still contain the charm and same finish as the other bedrooms. Bedroom 5 consists of a double bed with a full bathroom en-suite that contains a modern and stylish sunken bath, twin basins and a double shower. Bedroom 6 has twin beds and an en-suite that consists of a full bathroom with twin basins and a double shower.

Each and every bedroom aims to offer the ultimate comfort and design that is expected from a property such as this. The design is sleek and modern with a traditional touch that helps it to blend into the Portuguese style seamlessly and effortlessly.

The living rooms are styled with class and finesse and they are both rooms that allow total relaxation as well as somewhere to sit and enjoy the beauty of Desejos. The sofas are large and luring with comfort that is hard to resist and the living room leads to the snug room. The snug room is the place to enjoy the large plasma TV with Sky television or even just to sit alone with your thoughts with the doors open leading out to the sun terrace.

The kitchen has the complete wow factor and this Mark Wilkinson designer kitchen is draped in marble with plenty of space for cooking and an island as the main feature. The kitchen has all the main appliances ranging from an American Fridge Freezer to a coffee maker. There is also a utility room with a washing machine, tumble dryer and Ironing board.

For those who like to entertain and have some fun there is also a games/party room, with plenty of space and a pool table.

The grounds of Desejos are simply breath taking, landscaped, luscious and with lots of colour it is a beautiful place to be. The grounds snake around the heatable pool and Jacuzzi as well as the sun terraces and the barbecue area and outdoor fireplace really help to maximize and improve the outdoor space. There is ample space for eating with a large dining table and chairs and more than enough sun loungers dotted around the pool.

All bedrooms are en suite and have built in wardrobes and plasma TV's.
