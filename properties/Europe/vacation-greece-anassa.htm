Anassa Mansion, Mykonos, Greece Vacation Rental


Built in traditional Mykonian style and located above stunning Panormos Beach on the island's chic and secluded northern side the Anassa Mansion and Guest House is an ideal choice for discerning guests who expect nothing less than an exclusive and unforgettable holiday experience. Featured in international design and decor publications such as houzz.com (*please see link below) and Ristrutturare con Casa Chic, the property has been decorated with antique furnishings and specialty finishes that enhance the overall ambiance of this exceptionally beautiful island retreat.

Anassa has 180 degrees bay views, spacious terraces, a large infinity edged swimming pool and spa with a pergola-covered outdoor dining area, huge dining table, two large built in seating areas as well as a traditional island hearth oven, BBQ and comfortable sun beds.

The spacious entry incorporates a Moroccan tiled guest bath while the large living room is elegant yet relaxed and has a wood burning fireplace. The dining room is an open space that is perfect for both formal and casual meals with family and friends and the kitchen combines traditional Mykonian design with the highest quality modern appliances.

On the completely private upper level is the master bedroom suite. Both the master bedroom and bath are delightfully decorated with exceptional attention to detail and have outstanding sea views from two large terraces with outdoor seating. The four additional bedrooms and three bathrooms are on the lower level of the house and are comfortable, spacious and quiet.

The guest house is a completely separate home adjacent to the main residence. It includes a complete kitchen, dining area, built in island style living area and private patio with gorgeous views. Upstairs is the master suite composed of a large bedroom with and en-suite bath. The lower level has a large bedroom, sitting area, full bath and spacious private terrace.

On the world renown island of Mykonos within a gated community overlooking stunning Panormos Bay our eleven villas are walking distance to the sea and have spectacular views of the bay, the beach and the untamed hillsides.

Situated on 55,000 meters the villas have been designed to integrate understated elegance and privacy with the leisurely island lifestyle and vibrant nightlife that has made Mykonos a world class destination.

Spend the day or evening swimming and lounging with the in-crowd at trendy Panormos with its bohemian beach bar and restaurant or experience the low key relaxation of secluded Agios Sostis beach and it's charmingly authentic Greek taverna.

The distinctive combination of natural beauty, location, privacy and luxury of these unique villas is what brings our guests back year after year.
