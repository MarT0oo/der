Lake Estate & Residence, Lake District England, England Vacation Rental

 

5 Star opulence on Ullswater, The Exclusive Lake House Estate is quite simply outstanding and luxurious in every way, arguably the most prestigious self-catering experience in the Lake District. Exclusively yours this magnificent private estate comprises Exclusive Lake House, accommodating 12 and Barn Retreat 8.

Both residences occupy a sublime, secluded setting within 26 acres of private grounds with ½ mile of lake shoreline, commanding simply breath-taking uninterrupted panoramic views as far as the eye can see.

Exclusive use of the estate includes these highlights: boathouse and jetty; spa and wellness sanctuary featuring a Laconium and bar leading out onto an infinity terrace with hot tub; function suite with minstrel's gallery in the Lake Room, perfect to indulge your special celebration, intimate wedding or luxury conference; cinema; and fully fitted barbecue terrace.

Leave aside the 'million dollar views and setting' for a moment and stroll through Exclusive Lake House and Barn Retreat - their sleek sophisticated interior styling and perfectionist attention to detail ensures that these luxury self-catering homes will inspire even the most discerning guest.

Whether you are celebrating a happy event, searching for the perfect setting for an intimate wedding or just spending time with your favourite people, here is a magical treat delivering sheer luxury and matching your high expectations.
