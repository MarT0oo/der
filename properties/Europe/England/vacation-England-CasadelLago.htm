Casa del Lago, Cotswolds, England Vacation Rental

 

Casa del Lago has uninterrupted views across Bowmoor lake, the largest of the lakes, with a glorious 500 square foot south facing deck. The property enjoys two superb master suites, both with full en-suites private balconies, a further adult twin bedroom & a children’s bunk room, sleeping 4.

The house is fitted to an exceptional specification – it is Soho House meets the White Company inside, with exquisite, unique pieces of artwork from the Owner’s private collection to punctuate the themes of mink, cream & mercury. The living space enjoys a large kitchen, with pantry & larder, vast dining space & beautiful formal lounge, along with a large snug, perfect for unwinding.

Please note, that because of the high specification of the property, security deposit for the house is £2,000, and the top floor is reserved for the Owner only.
