White Hill House 4, London, England Vacation Rental

 

White Hill House 4 in London offers accommodation with free WiFi, 2.1 miles from O2 Academy Brixton, 3.7 miles from Stamford Bridge - Chelsea FC and 3.7 miles from Natural History Museum. The apartment consists of 2 separate bedrooms, 2 bathrooms and a living room. A terrace is available for guests to use at the apartment.

Victoria and Albert Museum is 3.7 miles from White Hill House 4, while The All England Lawn Tennis Club Centre Court is 4.3 miles away. The nearest airport is London City Airport, 11.8 miles from the accommodation. Wandsworth is a great choice for travellers interested in tourist attractions, convenient public transport and architecture.

This well-equipped home is one of five apartments situated on the 1st floor in a secure, recently renovated, modernized and well-kept characterful Victorian building. The location is safe for families and close to central London. It has 2 en-suite bedrooms. The spacious master bedroom has a double bed and an en-suite shower room with a walk-in shower. The 2nd bedroom has two single beds and an en-suite bathroom with a shower over the bath. The open-plan living area has dining for four. Double doors lead from the living area on to a large, private and sunny terrace, equipped with table, parasol and chairs. It has double-glazed window and central heating. There is a well equipped kitchen. The comfortable living area has a flat screen HD television, DVD player and a Sky+ box with a media package including and sports, movies and children's channels. There is hi-speed fiber Wi-Fi internet. All bills are included in the price.

Just across the street is Clapham South Tube station in Travel Zone 2, which is on the Northern Line. The centre of London can be reached in about twenty minutes. Clapham Junction station, St Georges Hospital in Tooting, Wimbledon, Twickenham, Richmond and The Oval can all be easily reached. On your doorstep there are numerous shops, restaurants and the green open spaces of Clapham Common park. Balham and Clapham town centres, where there a wider variety of shopping, are only a 10-minute walk away; as are charming Clapham Old Town and Abbeville Village with their restaurants, boutiques, pubs and trendy bars.
