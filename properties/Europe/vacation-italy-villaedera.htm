Villa Edera
7 Bedrooms | 8 Baths | Hydro Massage Pool                                            

Pistoia, Tuscany, Italy

  From €6490 - €9985 per week

Classical Tuscan country style, this elegant and stunning property is situated amidst a glorious rolling countryside, just 30 minutes from Florence in the region of Pistoia.             

The property has been elegantly renovated with precious materials, Villa Edera lies in the middle of open farmland, surrounded by fields and woods which characterize the local environment. The garden is an lovely Mediterranean style garden with impressive olive trees and full of aromatic plants. Villa Edera is a large sunny house built in the traditional Tuscan style with a pervasive bucolic spirit.  The Villa features also its own vegetable plot.


The entire property, 6 hectares (15 acres) of fenced grounds, provides complete peace.

Although it feels remote and private, the property is within easy reach of the gorgeous Florence and Lucca villages of the Region and is a truly charming holiday escape.

The property includes a hydro massage pool, a fitness path and an impressive barbecue area, all offering amazing views of the hills.

The interior is warm, appealing cosy and colourful, and has been decorated in the typical Tuscan style. The colours used are Siena yellow, ochre, blues, terracotta, and brick”
