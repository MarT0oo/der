Villa Victoria, Barcelona Spain, Vacation Rental


The Area Surrounding the Villa


This beautiful and elegant Villa of over 550 m2 comes with the added advantage of over 330 m2 of terraces with swimming pool, jacuzzi and garden. Only ten minutes from 2 world heritage monuments: the Sagrada Familia and Hospital St Pau and 20 minutes from the historic center of town.


Well communicated by subway and bus and all high street stores, supermarkets, restaurants, bakeries – you have everything at your fingertips.

The Villa


This early 20th century neoclassical and artistic heritage listed villa has been completely refurbished in 2009 with style flair and great attention to detail.  All modern conveniences; air- conditioning, heating,  TV, safes and WI-FI in all rooms, modern bathrooms and the wonderful combination of hand-picked antiques with cutting-edge modern furniture and carefully placed lighting ensures a very comfortable stay in a modern and practical setting.


All of the bedrooms are spacious doubles and have individual climate control, WI-FI, safe box and 32”LED TV’s  some, a small sofa, dressing table and large wardrobes.


Entering through the front door, you find yourself in the impressive foyer with its majestic marble staircase rising to the upper floors.  With two stunning 18th century Chinese vases and bronze chandeliers you can fully imagine the majesty and elegance of the time.  The 250m2 which makes up this floor has been cleverly designed to ensure a feeling of relaxation and fun from the moment you enter.  The games and meeting room which takes up almost 200m2 and has been designed in a loft style, features pool and table-tennis tables, foosball, board games, meeting area, comfortable sofas, a 55” 3D TV (with 3D glasses), computer, bar and a quiet reading area – there is definitely something for everyone.  Also on this floor, you will find the first of the double bedrooms and a sleek bathroom complete with shower unit.


Going up to the first floor, you will find another 4 double bedrooms each stylishly and comfortably decorated.  There are two complete bathrooms, one with bath/shower combination and the other, which is en-suite to the master bedroom, has a corner bath with jacuzzi jets. 


Also on the first floor, you have the dining room with capacity for 12 guests and the spacious living area with comfortable white leather sofas where you can kick back, relax, plan your excursions or enjoy some of your favorite programs on the 60” TV with satellite channels.  Close at hand you have the modern and practical designer kitchen.

 
With Italian designed furniture, breakfast bar and preparation area and every electrical appliance that you could possibly need including a double fridge and dishwasher, cooking will be an absolute pleasure.


From the kitchen you have direct access to the pool and garden at the rear of the house.  The swimming pool is 8 x 4 metres and is surrounded by wooden decking and sun loungers.  There is also an exterior dining area with table and chairs.  To relax even more, the spacious 6 person heated jacuzzi is the perfect spot to unwind after a hectic day of sightseeing.

On the second floor we find a study, the final double bedroom suite with full bath/shower.


From both the study and the bedroom you can access to a 160m2 terrace.  Wooden decking, elegant lattice screens, comfortable seating and sun loungers provide guests with a wonderful chill-out space which can be enjoyed both by day and at night with some strategic romantic lighting.


As a little extra, at the other side of the terrace you come across a turret room, used as a reading room where you can hide away in this sunny and tranquil spot and lose yourself in a good book.


Everything has been carefully thought out and provided to ensure that your stay in this fabulous villa is memorable in every way.
