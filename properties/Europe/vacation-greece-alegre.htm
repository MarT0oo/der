Villa Alegre, Mykonos, Greece Vacation Rental


This lovely 2 bedroom villa is part of a larger compound that can be rented individually or together with the rest of the villas.  They are a set of magical villas that are perfect for those seeking serenity and leisure.  Setup individually but also uniquely together.  The Alegre is perfect for a small family or a honeymoon couple.

The Villas


All villas are exclusively constructed by Mykonian rock of 65cm length, which was inherent in the ground. Its architecture is minimal emphasizing the outdoor space, which embodies the wonderful giant rocks of the hill (a protected geological monument of granite boulders shaped as statues). Our purpose was the illustration of the natural environment and not the creation of an artificial garden.


The Advantages


All villas are in perfect harmony with the natural environment. The stones used to construct the villas, ensure to create a cool atmosphere during the warm days and a warm one during the cold ones.


The Interior


The interior is cozy and decorated with pristine, and tastefully appointed, comfortable furniture and artwork. The large windows, allow plenty of light to come in every room and offer a unique view no matter in which room of the Villas you are. On the ground floor are located a fully equipped kitchen, dining room, and a spacious living room with a couch for 8 persons and fireplace. The bedrooms have comfortable queen beds, with the option to change into two single beds in two of the rooms. All the bedrooms have en-suite bathrooms with shower panels. Rooms are comfortably spacious and tastefully furnished in a bright, minimalist style, incorporating white walls and ceilings with local rock, traditional features of Cycladic architecture, making this Villa a top choice for those who seek the best from traditional and modern design.
