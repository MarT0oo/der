Villa São Lourenço, Quinta do Lago, Portugal Vacation Rental

 

Villa São Lourenço is a stunning and unique holiday home that is sure to leave you with unforgettable memories. Once inside the grounds you will feel like you are hidden away from the world and can relax in luxury surroundings.

Outside there are various seating areas to enjoy the sunshine throughout the day. From the lounge area you are able to step onto the terrace that goes around the house with one section facing towards the garden and the other to the pool.

There is also a BBQ and an expansive green landscaped garden. The pool area is a few steps down to the left of the villa. The pool can be heated upon request.

Walking into the hallway you will enter the comfortable lounge area that has stunning views towards the patio, pool area and gardens. There is also a separate dining room.

Downstairs there are two beautifully presented large double bedrooms. The first double bedroom boasts views towards the pool area and access to a patio as well as a spacious shower room.

The second double bedroom has beautiful views towards the front courtyard and has an ensuite bathroom with a bath. Upstairs there are a further three bedrooms with ensuite bathrooms and wooden flooring to create a tranquil seaside vibe. The master bedroom is open and large with the bed facing towards a balcony and stunning views towards the pool area and San Lorenzo golf course beyond. The third double bedroom is also large and spacious with views over the green garden and an ensuite shower room. The fifth bedroom is ideal for children as there are two single beds and a set of bunk beds.

The kitchen is fully equipped. Beyond the kitchen there is a laundry room with a washing machine, tumble dryer, iron and ironing board.
