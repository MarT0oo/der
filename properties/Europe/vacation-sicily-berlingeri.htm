Casa Berlingeri, Masara del Vallo, Sicily Vacation Rental


Luxury villa, staff equipped , with private swimming pool and private spa center, located  in Trapani area. The nearest village is just about few km  far from the villa .

In the vicinity one can visit the most charming towns and archaeological sites , such as the medieval old town centre of Erice, the impressive temple of Segesta, the island of Mozia, the Zingaro’s natural reserve, the Egadi Islands, Marsala’s salt pans and the beautiful Trapani.

This exclusive Villa is located in a sunny, quiet but not secluded place and has a stunning countryside view. Its wide terraces and the wonderful swimming pool area  are the ideal spots to enjoy this lovely landscape. Its spacious exteriors and generously built interiors - that can comfortably accommodate up to 16 people – ideal for a large family or a group of friends.

 

The interiors  are furnished in a modern style  and all the eight bedrooms with en-suite bathrooms are equipped with air conditioning, minibar.
