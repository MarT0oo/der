Opulent Lago Luxury, Quinta do Lago, Algarve, Portugal Vacation Rental

 

Opulent Lago Luxury is a magnificent contemporary villa located in Quinta do Lago. This property is beautifully furnished, completed to a high standard and counts with a fresh, spacious and well-lit interior. On the ground floor there are 2 double bedrooms, both en-suite with shower and access to the outdoor area, a very bright living area with large doors to the terrace and pool, a charming dining room and a fully fitted kitchen with a small breakfast bar and also access to the terrace. On the first floor there are two double bedrooms, both en-suite with bath and separate shower and access to large terraces.

On the lower ground floor there are the remaining bedrooms, both with double beds, en-suite bathrooms with shower and access to the exterior area. There is also a cinema room and a games area with pool table and ping-pong table. The beautiful garden is the perfect place to relax and spend unforgettable moments with family and friends! The covered terrace, with dining area for 8 guests and a BBQ, great for "al-fresco" meals! The property has air conditioning throughout. The villa is located in the heart of the `Golden Triangle` just a few minutes from the beautiful beaches and renowned golf courses, Opulent Lago Luxury is everything you need for a relaxed sunny holiday!
