Chalet Bleu 3 Valleys, La Tania, France Vacation Rental


New to our Chalet Program is Chalet Bleu, which was previously owned by Alpine Action. Just a stone’s throw from Chalet Juliette, this is a lovely typical Savoyard Style Chalet, with high beamed ceilings and an open plan living area from where you can enjoy great views across to the other valley.

The chalet is warm and inviting with a wood burner and there are seven twin bedrooms all with en-suite facilities. The chalet is ski in, ski out, and only 100 metres from the Gondola. On the south facing terrace there is a hot tub to ease away those aches and pains after a hard day’s skiing. You will enjoy the under-floor heating feature as well as the hot tub for those nights you want to sip on a glass of wine with friends and family after a long day of skiing.  The Chalet is fully catered, so we will do our best to make sure all of your needs are met from the moment you arrive. 

Equipment Hire

We are happy to recommend the specialist shop, Ski Higher in La Tania. They supply a full range of skis, snowboards and boots. Your equipment will be fitted in the Chalet and collected at the end of your holiday, saving you the effort of going to the shop. Alternatively, if you prefer you can visit the shop in the resort. Payment is made by Euros or credit card. Ski Higher's specialist mobile technicians also offer servicing and repairs.
