Tenuta Agriturismo San Fabiano

Filippo and Rachel Fiorentini invite you to their Agriturismo San Fabiano country estate and open their farmhouse for splendid holidays in the heart of the Tuscan countryside. The Agriturismo San Fabiano estate of Count Fiorentini is located in the Val d'Arbia, 12 km south of Siena. You are welcomed with familiarity and kindness, Fiorentini family will be at your disposal to try to meet all your needs, giving you the best advice for visiting the area.

Podernovo farmhouse is the oldest on the Agriturismo San Fabiano country estate, dating back to 1750.  Beautifully restored, it has every amenity.   Apart from the owner's apartment, it offers 4 self-catering suites and an independent villa called Le Tortore; all of them with air conditioning.   The converted stable offers a special atmosphere around the fireplace for family get-togethers or cooking lessons, and for music (HI-FI, Organ, Grand Piano). Under the roofed area, you can prepare fantastic barbeques or just relax. From April to October you can refresh yourself in the solar heated pool.

The farm offers all of the areas where guests can make activities or simply relax with a good bottle of Chianti. There is a very large room with a fireplace where you can eat, do cooking classes, listening to good music. Outside un'ombreggiato pergola with barbecue and a relaxing swimming pool to cool off and get some sun.

A large bedroom with beamed ceiling, living room with two single beds a bathroom with shower, large kitchen and dining room with access to the patio, satellite TV with VCR, air conditioning.
