Mas Sant Pere, Sitges, Spain Vacation Rental

 

Mas Sant Pere is a beautiful property just outside of Sitges, an incredible area for beaches and fun and exciting times with friends and family. This modern and beautiful house was carefully renovated in the summer of 2015. The owner used her skills as a famed interior designer to transform the historical residence into a 21st century luxury home, with all the modern conveniences and high standard finishes that one would expect from a property of this outstanding caliber.

Mas Sant Pere is now an incredible 14 bedroom (all en-suite) authentic Spanish Mansion, superbly renovated with impeccable attention to detail. It offers 2 vast patios connected by a wonderful bodega, a four-sided infinity pool, several private sitting areas and 3 very large meeting rooms, with a dining room for 28 guests.

Mas Sant Pere is a very private and spacious villa, located at the edge of the small town of Sant Pere de Ribes, where you will find everything from shops and banks to fine restaurants. Your group will enjoy total privacy, with the possibility of taking advantage of all the facilities of a vibrant resort nearby.

Take a daytrip to Barcelona just a short 40 minute drive or go and layout on some of the most famed beaches in Sitges which are only 5 minutes away by car.
