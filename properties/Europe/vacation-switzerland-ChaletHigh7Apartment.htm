Chalet High 7 Apartment, Zermatt, Switzerland Vacation Rental

 

Fusing contemporary and classical Alpine design with the spirit of the mountains, Chalet High 7 evokes a feeling of peace and tranquility in the exclusive Alpine village of Zermatt.

Zermatt’s Chalet High 7 provides accommodation for 24 guests, the exquisite chalet is made up of a spacious 235m2 four-bedroom, duplex penthouse, two three-bedroom apartments and one two-bedroom apartment.

The chalet is located in Zermatt’s only ski-in ski-out district, Winkelmatten. This is known as the “Beverly Hills” of Zermatt and from the chalet you can ski through the meadow and down to the Klein Matterhorn Express Lift and you can ski back to the door of the chalet down the Moosweg ski-run.

The apartments are spacious, opulent and offer the comfort of a home-from-home.

Each apartment has been luxuriously finished to cater for your every need and can be accessed by stairs or lift
