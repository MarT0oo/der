Villa Calista
4 Bedrooms | 3 Baths | Pool | Rec Room | Gym
Icici, Opatija, Croatia
From USD €5,600 per week

The luxury Villa Calista is a superb Croatian villa which offers 4 comfortable bedrooms, private wellness center, big private garage for 6 cars, exceptional private swimming pool and beautiful large garden. Situated in Icici, at the edge of glorious Opatija, in calm and green area and only 150 m from the sea, it is ideal holiday home for Croatian elite villa accommodation.

This exclusive villa represents an impressive modern design and one of the finest examples of a contemporary Croatian architecture. Just marble stones around the villa are great value per se.

Self catering Villa Calista has 4 floor levels. The fourth and third levels are empty so guests on the second level have 100% privacy.

The second level on 190 m2 contains impressive hall with imperial white, labrador blue pearl and red Africa granite; big loggia with red Africa granite, elegant dining set and panoramic view to the sea; luxury kitchen/dining room with imperial white, labrador blue pearl and red Africa granite and all modern appliances and equipment; grandiose living room with the same granite, home cinema, elegant pure leather sofa set, paintings of Croatian eminent artist Claudio Frank, specially made for Villa Calista; master bedroom with king size bed, mahogany parquet and view to the see; comfortable bedroom with queen size bed and mahogany parquet; two twin size bedrooms with mahogany parquet; one extraordinary bathroom in natural onyx with Jacuzzi; one bathroom in luxury ceramic with steam-bath; separate WC and wardrobe room.

The first floor with140 m2 contains an intimate wellness center with sauna, shower and massage table, spacious gym, large recreational room with billiard and ping-pong table and space for central heating equipment.

Around the attractive Villa Calista, on 2500 m2, guests rent exceptional private swimming pool 13x6 m with extensive terrace, phenomenal sun loungers, dining space with barbeque and granite table and spectacular view to the sea, huge private garage for 6 cars, impressive Mediterranean garden with more than 500 flowers and trees and nice private playground for basketball and mini football.

In this supreme villa guests have, as rarely anywhere, excellent 100% silk bedding, specially designed for Villa Calista, and peerless 100% bamboo bath and swimming pool towels.

If absolute comfort, luxury and relaxation are your accommodation requirements, then this exceptional villa rental is your perfect choice. Villa Calista gives you everything needed to relax and entertain in truly luxury rental that is rarely matched in Croatia.
