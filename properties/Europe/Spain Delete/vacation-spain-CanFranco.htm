Can Franco, Mallorca, Spain Vacation Rental

 

This spanish style villa is in a totally private setting on the hillside overlooking the sea and cove of the pretty resort.

Inside the villa is decorated in the local traditional style with many Mallorquin features, rich dark wood, white washed walls and decorative tiles. Offering five bedrooms and five bathrooms, this villa is perfect for a large family or group of friends.

Outside it has a spectacular pool area with lush shady trees , mediterranean garden, pretty terrace and no neighbours!

The house also has a pretty courtyard adorned in bouganvillia and several upstairs terrace areas where you can sit and marvel at the sea views. Just a short drive from the resort where you can find all the amenities that you may require and just 15 minutes from the historical town of Pollensa.

Majorca is the largest of the four Balearic Islands; in fact its name means “the larger one”. Mallorca, like all of the Balearics, has many aspects to it which make it a wonderful holiday destination. Mallorca is often described as an island of contrasts; it certainly has something to interest many different types of visitors.
