Maison Mallorca, Mallorca, Spain Vacation Rental

 

Luxury 4 Bedroom, 5 Bathroom Town House with Sea View and a Private Swimming Pool , Air Conditioning, Wi-Fi, Satellite TV, near Golf, Sleeps a maximum of 8 people.

Maison Mallorca is elegance personified. If youre looking to spend a holiday in a beautiful traditional Spanish townhouse just a stones throw away from all the main attractions historic Pollensa has to offer, youve found it at Maison Mallorca.

Almost immediately you can tell that the owner of this superbly renovated property loves art. In fact, in addition to a collection of lovely artworks dotted throughout the mansion, youll find some splendid original paintings from his private collection.

So lets make our way inside.

When it comes to entrances, you dont get much better than this. The entrance hall is huge in true Spanish tradition, the high ceilings adorned by solid wood roof beams.

Appreciate those thick stone walls, admire the gorgeous ceramic floors, marvel at that grand stone archway, check out the lovely antiquesits all rather special here at your Mallorcan holiday home.

One of the first things you notice as you pass through the entrance hall is a lovely dining area featuring a completely restored stone fountain as its centerpiece.

Youll find a gorgeous bathroom complete with Italianate fixtures on this level too, as well as a state of the art kitchen fully-fitted with all the mod cons you will need, including a large American-style fridge-freezer.

The spacious first floor lounge/dining area is sumptuous to say the least. Its furnished to a very high standard indeed. Antiques abound and superb art hangs on the walls. (Just take a look at that fabulous antique French chaise longue beautiful!)

Large cosy sofas invite you to put your feet up and catch up on your favourite programmes on the plasma TV mounted on the wall. And if youre enjoying a touch of winter sun on your holiday, the wood-burning stone will keep you as warm as toast in the evenings.

Off from the lounge youll find two well-appointed double bedrooms, both with en suite bathrooms. The propertys master double bedroom with en suite shower room is also on this level. Open up the wooden window shutters and you are greeted with the most picturesque of views.

Up on the second floor youll find a roomy twin-bedded studio/apartment complete with ample wardrobe space and a large en suite bathroom and small kitchenette. This provides the utmost privacy for guests.

And then theres the amazing furnished roof terrace what a fabulous view over the pretty rooftops of Pollensa and the mountains beyond!

The exterior of Maison Mallorca is just as impressive. The star attraction here is undoubtedly the deep blue heated swimming pool, a haven of peace and privacy right in the centre of town.

The pool area is surrounded by lush foliage and a pretty terrace, and theres a delightful shaded al fresco dining area accessed via stone archways. (Theres a barbecue area too, should you fancy showing off your culinary skills.)

A large garage can be accessed from the main hallway and, should you fancy exploring the area on two wheels, there are four bicycles which you are welcome to use. And all this right in the heart of one of Mallorcas most beautiful old towns!
