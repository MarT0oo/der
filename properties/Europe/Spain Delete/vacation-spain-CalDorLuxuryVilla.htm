Cal Dor Luxury Villa, Mallorca, Spain Vacation Rental

 

Cal Dor Luxury Villa is a countryside residence of a very high standard with attention to detail, well proportioned rooms with the benefit of fabulous views. Shops, restaurants and beach all within 1km.

Complete privacy yet within a few minutes drive or a short walk of all amenities.

From the enclosed courtyard a large door leads through to the entrance hall. The large living/dining area has four doorways which open out to the poolside terrace with breathtaking views over open country side and to the sea in the distance. Fully fitted kitchen with modern appliances, electric oven/infra red hob, twin sinks, microwave, fridge/freezer and dishwasher. Laundry room with washing machine, Guest cloakroom, Double bedroom with en-suite shower room and door leading to the courtyard.

The stairs lead to a large open planned galleried area with sofa-bed, perfect for teenagers. Master twin bed room with en-suite large shower room, door leads to a lovely terrace with the benefit of south facing views.

Two further twin bedrooms with en-suite shower rooms, one of which has its own terrace with views over the courtyard and beyond.

The gorgeous infinity swimming pool with Jacuzzi, also has a poolside shower and grassed area. Plenty of room for relaxing on the comfortable sun loungers with parasol with far reaching views. Adjacent to the villa is a stone casita with covered patio, washing facilities and wc. To the rear of the property there is an enclosed courtyard with seating area and feature palm tree.

Majorca is the largest of the four Balearic Islands; in fact its name means “the larger one”. Mallorca, like all of the Balearics, has many aspects to it which make it a wonderful holiday destination. Mallorca is often described as an island of contrasts; it certainly has something to interest many different types of visitors.
