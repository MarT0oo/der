Escape to Paradise, Puerto Pollensa, Mallorca, Spain Vacation Rental

 

Located in the province of Calvia in the South Western part of Mallorca, this large and comfortable house is set inland in a secluded spot, and offers wonderful views over the surrounding countryside.

The gates open out to vast gardens full of flowers and vegetables, from which the house can be seen at the top of the hill. The ground floor is all on one level, designed to allow easy access between all room with minimal stairs between the house and outdoors. On this floor is the master bedroom as well as a dressing room and bathroom complete with bathtub. There is also the main hall, a guest bathroom, the living room with sofas, TV and round table dining area with chairs all located next to the traditional fireplace. The fully equipped kitchen can be accessed from the dining area.

The rest of the bedrooms are all spread out through separate floors, each floor with a private entrance. One of the bedrooms and it`s en suite can only be accessed from outside as it is a guest apartment. It has it`s own kitchen and dining area, and is attached to the main house. Overall Escape to Paradise can sleep up to 12 guests.

Outdoors there are many terraces which can be easily accessed from both the living room and kitchen. Out here you will find sunbeds and a great chill out area located next to the large swimming pool. The gardens also boast a covered outdoor dining area with a long table and ample seating space.

The villa itself is peaceful but the vibrant town of Calvia is just a kilometre away, meaning guests can enjoy the numerous restaurants, bars, services and amenities that the town offers. The nearby villages of Palmanova, Santa Ponsa and Puerto Adriano also offer a range of options, from traditional Spanish taverns to more modern cuisine
