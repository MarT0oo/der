Enchantment, Pollensa, Mallorca, Spain Vacation Rental

 

One of the most desirable places to rent a holiday property on the lovely island of Mallorca is the Pollensa area, highlighted by the quaint old town and its nearby Port.

The beauty of Enchantment, a delightful restored 6-bedroom single-storey holiday villa, is that it is situated in the picturesque countryside between Pollensa town and Port de Pollensa. So you get the best of both worlds!

The interior of Enchantment is fabulous. Spot the cool Philippe Starck designs. Drool over the gorgeous Italian Bontempi kitchen. Check out the superb lighting design. And appreciate the fabulous top quality furnishingstheres a touch of hotel chic about this superb holiday villa, characterised by cool, contemporary features and attractive dcor.

Theres a large flat screen satellite TV mounted on the wall so you can keep up with all your favourite TV programmes, and you benefit from air conditioning and wi-fi throughout.

One of the most appealing factors of Enchantment is that everything is on one low level. You dont have to bother with stairs. In fact, the property delivers a lovely spacious flow of living space dominated by the attractive, open plan living and dining area. Large, fluffy cushions everywhere add to your feeling of comfort. The sliding patio doors ensure that your holiday home is bathed with that soft Mediterranean light that we all love so much.

Access to the outdoor area is also easy there is just one step which can be accessed if necessary by using a small ramp.

The large, fully fitted main kitchen is a joy. Gleaming with design by the Italian company Bontempi, its all chrome and stainless steel with all the mod cons you need.

The villa sleeps up to 12 people in a total of 6 bedrooms; three doubles and a twin bedroom in the main house. The star attraction is the master bedroom with a four-poster bed. Several of the 5 bathrooms feature walk-in showers. Bed linen and towels are supplied.

Separate and across the way youll find a pretty 2-bedroom casita (little house) with a double and a twin bedroom that share a bathroom. Theres a chill out space too, the perfect place to wind down after a busy day exploring the area.

A nice little touch here is the covered terrace with pretty dining furniture, as well as a breakfast terrace on the west side of the villa.

The rectangular pool (approx. 10 m x 4 m) is illuminated at night, and with the backdrop of the gorgeous Tramuntana mountains, its a lovely place to relax and watch the sun go down.

Theres a covered barbecue area too, so you can dine al fresco if the mood takes you.

A beautiful, landscaped large garden featuring mature trees and a playground area with swings and a ping-pong table makes the property ideal for families with young children. An automatic irrigation system keeps the grass looking lush.

And if you want to keep fit whilst on holiday, there is a small gymnasium for your use. There is also ample parking space.
