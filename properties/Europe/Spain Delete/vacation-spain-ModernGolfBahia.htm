Modern Golf Bahia, Mallorca, Spain Vacation Rental

 

Luxury 6 Bedroom, 5 Bathroom Villa with Mountain View and a Private Heated Swimming Pool , Air Conditioning, Wi-Fi, Jacuzzi, Table Tennis, Satellite TV, near Golf, Sleeps a maximum of 12 people.

If youre looking for an exceptional holiday rental property in Pollensa, one of Mallorcas loveliest areas, Villa Modern Golf Bahia is most definitely it.

This absolutely fabulous villa is set in 750m2 of extensive gardens surrounded by mature pine, palm and olive trees, and is constructed of traditional Mallorcan stone and terracotta roof tiles, as well as more modern contemporary materials.

Its this perfect marriage of the old and the new that makes Modern Golf Bahia so special.

The exterior of Modern Golf Bahia is restrained elegance. Youll find large terraces complete with outdoor designer furniture. There are beautiful Buddha statues, lush lawns and the piece de resistance: the huge rectangular heated swimming pool which is illuminated at night time.

Whether you want to impress your house guests, or simply have some quality time with friends and family, Modern Golf Bahia has it all.

If you enjoy golf, the good news is that the villa is just a 2-minute walk from a challenging 9-hole golf course and superb club house.

The poolside area is exquisite. Lovely shaded dining facilities for up to 10 people can be found on the huge outdoor terrace, and for relaxing in the sun, why not make use of the chic double-bed sun loungers!

New additions to Modern Golf Bahia for 2016 include a fantastic jacuzzi which enjoys the same views a the swimming pool, as well as a sunken day bed and a new chill out set up under another terrace long from the outdoor dining area.

Theres a large dining area outside kitchen doors, perfect for a sunny Mediterranean breakfast. There is also a barbecue area if you fancy showing off your cooking skills.

Walking through Modern Golf Bahia is like a journey of exploration. Step inside this amazing villa and you enter a world of innovative design with clean lines and symmetry very much in evidence.

The spacious luxury lounge on the ground floor features an attractive Jerusalem stone fireplace. Huge windows let in that gorgeous Mediterranean light, and there are three double doors leading on to the pool/patio area.

There is a large, rather chic dining table with 10 leather chairs which also features an exquisite side unit holding a 48 LED satellite TV and state-of-the-art music system.

The hallway/office is area is light, bright and airy with glass doors leading you into a spectacularly-lit natural cave which in turn leads to a traditional cellar containing ancient wine barrels.

Next stop is the open-air atrium which leads into the family-sized lounge/TV room with all the latest must-have electronic games for the kids.

And then comes the kitchen. Wow! With a central island featuring marble tops and the very latest Miele appliances, crystal chandelier extractor hoods, a built-in breakfast booth plus fabulous views over the valley beyond, you soon realise that so much effort has been put into the design of this exceptional villa.

But its not all fantasy at Modern Golf Bahia. Through the next door youll find the utility room featuring a washing machine and dryer.

(If you dont fancy walking up the elegant staircase, there is lift access to the upper floor.)

Modern Golf Bahia sleeps up to 12 people in the lap of luxury. The 6 bedrooms provide a mixture of double and twin facilities. 4 of the double bedrooms benefit from en suite bathrooms, and there is are a further two twin bedrooms, one of which has an added sofa bed accommodating two people.

Needless to say, the master bedroom is something else. Spacious with two jaw-dropping marble baths side by side offering exquisite views out to the valley. It even features its own large steam room with stone basins, double shower, dressing table and walk-in dressing room.

Linen and towels are provided throughout the property.

Modern Golf Bahia is all about living the Mediterranean dream. Situated in the up scale Pollensa area of northern Mallorca, it will undoubtedly provide a holiday you will remember forever. We strongly recommend early booking.
