Modern Coster Blanes, Mallorca, Spain Vacation Rental

 

3 Bedroom, 2 Bathroom Villa with Sea View and a Private Swimming Pool , Wi-Fi, Satellite TV, near Golf, Sleeps a maximum of 6 people.

The great advantage of renting in Costa d`en Blanes is that the superior elevated position of this upmarket residential area offers the most spectacular views.

And it is here in the much sought-after residential complex of Voramar, that you`ll find the delightful villa that is Modern Coster Blanes.

Modern Coster Blanes is a cosy, comfortable semi-detached villa with its own private swimming pool providing fabulous views overlooking the prestigious yachting marina of Puerto Portals and the sparkling Bay of Palma.

Enter Villa Coster Blanes and you are immediately taken by now light bright and airy the living space is. Soft Mediterranean light floods in through the large windows giving you a lovely warm, relaxing feeling.

Even though a thoroughly modern villa with all the amenities you would expect from such a prestigious area, the terracotta tiled floors and exposed wooden roof beams remind you of a rather elegant Spanish heritage.

The large, open plan living/dining area is a joy. The long, cosy sofas are just great for relaxing on after a busy day exploring the island. The huge flat screen satellite TV shows all your favourite programmes. And the spacious dining area features a large table where you can enjoy an evening meal together with family or friends whilst enjoying fabulous views over the Mediterranean.

The fully equipped fitted kitchen is lovely yet practical. You`ll find all the appliances here to cook up some tasty meals. There is a downstairs loo, too!

Up the stairs now to the bedrooms which sleep up to six guests in comfort and style. There are three bedrooms altogether, two doubles and one twin. The master bedroom comes complete with en suite bathroom, the other two bedrooms sharing a modern, fully-equipped bathroom.

The exterior of Modern Coster Blanes is lovely. The light terracotta walls blend in perfectly with the lush green surrounds. The south-facing patio provides the most spectacular views over Puerto Portals and the crystal clear waters of Palma Bay. The palm tree laden garden is gorgeous. And the private swimming pool is just perfect for cooling off from the hot Mediterranean sun.

And that`s not all. Up on the roof of Modern Coster Blanes you`ll find a huge sun terrace which just screams for imbibing evening cocktails as the sun goes down. (It`s also perfect for cooking up a few tasty barbecue treats.)

As mentioned before, Modern Coster Blanes overlooks the prestigious yachting marina of Puerto Portals. Here you can hob-nob with the millionaire yacht owners in one of the fashionable bars or restaurants, or shop at one of the trendy boutiques.

Modern Coster Blanes is a fabulous 3-bedroomed villa overlooking the south coast of Mallorca.
