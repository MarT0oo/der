Distinctive Melody, Pollensa, Mallorca, Spain Vacation Rental

 

There is something special, something timeless about a Mallorcan country estate. And a perfect example of this is the beautiful Villa Distinctive Melody situated in the north Mallorcan countryside near the ancient town of Pollensa. It may as well be called Puig Gardens, as you will certainly not find gardens as vast as these elsewhere at the foot of the Puig and facing Pollensa!

After undergoing an extensive renovation, Distinctive Melody provides an idyllic place in which to escape from the pressures of the modern world and relax in a timeless Mediterranean setting.

This fabulous holiday property is constructed of traditional Mallorcan stone, its golden hue reflecting the suns rays as well as absorbing the warmth of the Spanish sun.

Villa Distinctive Melody combines the best of the old and new. Local stone, terracotta roofs, stone archways and solid wooden beams give you a glimpse of a rustic past, whilst all the modern comforts you would expect are catered for to make your holiday one to remember.

As you approach Villa Distinctive Melody one is immediately impressed by the location. Nestling snugly on the hillside of Puig de Maria, the views overlooking the Tramuntana mountains are delightful.

An array of pretty flowers welcomes you to your holiday villa, their petals covering the traditional stone faade in a riot of colour.

The large spacious living room at Distinctive Melody is ever so stylish. Impressive solid-wood roof beams, terracotta tiled floor, traditional stone fireplace, large windows letting in the soft Mediterranean light all complimented by chic contemporary furniture, cosy sofas and a large satellite TV.

Pass through the stunning stone interior archways and you enter the dining room with a large central table seating up to 10 people (perfect for a get together with family and friends.)

The fully-fitted kitchen is no less impressive. You have all the latest mod cons here to cook up a real treat, and the large, American-style fridge will keep your drinks cool in the hot weather.

There are five spacious bedrooms in Distinctive Melody, sleeping up to 10 guests in effortless comfort; in the main house there is a double and a twin bedroom on each of the two floors, and a fifth, twin, bedroom in an annex with it`s own independent entrance - this room also has a sofa bed which can sleep another 2 guests. All bedrooms are en suite.

The exterior of this fabulous property is a delight. Youll enjoy both sunny and shaded outdoor terraces perfect for an al fresco breakfast, or simply to watch the sun go down in the evening, gin and tonic in hand.

The swimming pool is surrounded by a delightful stone patio with loungers and parasols ideal for sunbathing. The pool is illuminated at night time and looks really beautiful. It can also be heated in the colder months if you fancy an invigorating swim.

The colourful, landscaped, mature gardens are lovely too; just perfect to relax in with a good book as you feel the cares of the world ebb away.

And what could be better than an evening poolside barbecue with drinks as the sun sets over the surrounding hills?

Distinctive Melody is a unique country home converted into a 5-bedroom villa within walking distance to the centre of Pollensa old town. It really is a fabulous rental property and early booking is most recommended.
