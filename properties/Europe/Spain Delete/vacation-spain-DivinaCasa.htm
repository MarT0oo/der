Divina Casa, Puerto Pollensa, Mallorca, Spain Vacation Rental

 

Inmaculate country estate in one of the best locations in Pollensa in the valley of Ternelles with breathtaking sea views over the bay of Pollensa.

In the beautiful valley of Ternelles lies this immaculate villa with stunning views towards the bay of Pollensa, over the old quarter of Pollensa town and of the final stretch of the Tramuntana mountains to the north.

Approaching the villa will take you across an age-old stone bridge, delivering you to a marvellously peaceful position, surrounded by protected land with old pine and oak trees and set in the midst of a wonderful and beautifully cared for Mediterranean garden.

The estate includes the main house and an adjoining independent guest apartment.

There are a total of five bedrooms all with bathrooms en suite bar one, a very fine living room with fireplace, dining room, TV-room/library, and charming country style kitchen. Completing this absolutely delightful country property is a double garage, and in the large cellar; utility room, workshop, lots of storage and a charming wine cellar.

Divina Casa also has table tennis and table football for guests to enjoy during their stay!

Perhaps the most enchanting features are the huge terrace upstairs and the equally large porch at ground level, both with immense views and a variety seating areas for every occasion, and the swimming pool with sunbathing terrace and barbecue.
