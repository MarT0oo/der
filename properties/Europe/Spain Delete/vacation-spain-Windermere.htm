Windermere, Bendinant, Mallorca, Spain Vacation Rental

 

Luxury villa located in the exclusive Bendinat area. Nestled into the hills of Cas Catala Nou, the villa has been elegantly constructed. There are numerous sun-protected terraces, a large swimming pool with 3-metre diving platform and a magnificent waterfall. There is also a hot tub and a second pool in front of the living area and a steam sauna.

The estate is divided in 2 independent villas, each offers a fully equipped kitchen, living area and separate entrance. A tennis court that is protected from the wind by high-growing cypress trees and which has a special Tartan (athletics) rubber floor to protect the joints.

The entrance area is beautifully lit with dimmable spotlights and the exterior signage is also lit up at Night giving the property a wonderful prestigious aura.

The entry level to the main house is over 145m2 and boasts a lift system which takes you up to the fourth floor of the penthouse. The main house offers a beautiful large living area with piano, dining table and office area. Large sliding glass doors open to the terrace with Jacuzzi, the small pool and comfortable outdoor furniture.
