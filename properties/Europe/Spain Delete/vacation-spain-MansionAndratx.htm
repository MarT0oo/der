Mansion Andratx, Mallorca, Spain Vacation Rental

 

Aptly named, Mansion Andratx is a grandiose property set amongst a quiet residential area in the much sough after southwest of the island.

Combining Mediterranean charm with modern architecture, Mansion Andratx ticks all the boxes for those looking to holiday in the highest of standards - just 5 minutes drive from Puerto Andratx, on one of the very few plots offering spectacular near 360-degrees views of both the harbour and the dramatic Tramuntana mountains, sits this luxury villa.

Steps lead up to the entrance where you enter into a beautiful foyer. The ground floor is a breath-taking open plan living area combining a lounge, kitchen and dining area which just ooze style and charm - and all with access to the outdoor terrace. Furnished with gorgeous, understated decor, the villa comes with all the modern conveniences to be expected from a Mallorcan villa of this calibre.

On the ground floor is also one of the five bedrooms, which enjoys an en suite bathroom.

Head upstairs and from the hall you have a choice of three wonderful bedrooms - each has an en suite bathroom and fantastic views of the sea and surrounding mountains.

Below the ground floor is a sunken basement level - there is a garage with parking for two cars here as well as a the fifth and final bedroom, once again with it`s own en suite bathroom.

All floors can be accessed via elevator.

Outdoors you`ll find large terraces with an 85 sqm infinity pool, bestowing total relaxation amid stylish entertaining space and lush gardens. Adjacent to the lounge is an outdoor chillout area, and sun beds line the poolside.
