Villa Macerio, Pollensa, Mallorca, Spain Vacation Rental

 

4 Bedroom, 3 Bathroom Villa with Mountain View and a Private Heated Swimming Pool , Air Conditioning, Table Tennis, Satellite TV, near Golf, Sleeps a maximum of 8 people.

Villa Macerio is a lovely countryside villa with a spacious interior. built in a high-quality traditional Mallorcan style, set in a country road leading from the Old Town of Pollensa towards Alcudia and approx. 7 mins drive from both. Villa Macerio is set back off the lane down a pretty, winding driveway and has large gardens with surrounding mountain views.

Inside, the villa is an elegant combination of traditional and modern design with large and high-ceilinged rooms with open rafters, white walls and dark wood furniture. On the ground level is a pretty country kitchen with granite surfaces and full appliances including a dishwasher and a microwave. Doors from the kitchen open out onto the large covered terrace which overlooks the gardens and pool.

There is also an amazing double-height dining room on the ground floor with a wood-burning stove for cooler evenings in early or late season. French doors lead onto the covered terrace and also onto a vine-covered terrace at the side of the villa. Stairs with fully-fitted stair gates (both at the top and bottom) lead up to a mezzanine lounge with TV, DVD and a CD stereo player.

There are 4 bedrooms in total, two twin bedrooms are on the ground floor and share a separate family bathroom with full-size bath and overhead shower.

Upstairs is a double bedroom and a third twin bedroom. These also share a separate family bathroom again with full-size bath with overhead shower.

All bedrooms have air-conditioning and heating.

The gardens and outside space at Villa Macerio are particularly well-designed with large lawns, an abundance of olive trees, pomegranates, figs and almond trees and lots of flowers and herbs in the summer. There is a fabulous brick-built barbecue on the vine-covered terrace at the side of the villa and a table-tennis table on the lawn.

There is also ample parking space at the front of the villa.

An exterior utility room on the back terrace houses the washing machine and a handy exterior WC. The pool is a generous size (8m x 4m, depth 0.90m - 1.50m) and is very private here in the gardens at the back of the villa.

*Please note this property has an optional pool heating facility which is charged extra at 250 EUR/per week should you require it
