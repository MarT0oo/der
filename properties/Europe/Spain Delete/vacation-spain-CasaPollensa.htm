Casa Pollensa, Pollensa, Mallorca, Spain Vacation Rental

 

Close to the pcituresque village of Pollensa in the quiet country roads of La Font lies the beautiful Can Casa Pollensa. Truly a gem of a property, features such as it`s own are seldom found in such abundance: large, old trees throughout the garden, well kept palms, wonderful flowers and plants can all be found in and around the extensive lawns.

The main entrance leads directly into the spacious living/dining room with it`s own fireplace. Offering a cosy atmosphere alongside comfortable furniture and a satellite TV, this really is a wonderful place to relax throughout the year. On the left there is the entrance to the kitchen, a shower room and a utility room. Turing to the right from the living room there is an annex contained within the villa. In the annex there is another living room (which can sleep two guests if need be), a small kitchen, a twin bedroom and a bathroom.

A staircase leads to the second where you will find the other bedrooms: a very spacious master suite with a 1.80m x 2.00m double bed and en suite bathroom, a bedroom better suited to children with two single beds and another en suite. Furthermore there are another two twin bedrooms, one of which has an en suite bathroom.
