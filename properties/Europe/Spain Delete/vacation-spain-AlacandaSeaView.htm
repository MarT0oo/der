Alacanda Sea View, Mallorca, Spain Vacation Rental

 

This comfortable holiday home is located in the north of Majorca , in the residential district Alcanada.

The semi-detached house provides accommodation for 8 persons is spread out over 2 levels and has a floor space of approximately 280 m2. From the terraces you can enjoy a wide view of the sea and in the garage you can accommodate 2 cars. The villa is situated on a plot of 500 m2 and has a beautifully landscaped garden with a terrace. with sun terrace, which is equipped with sun loungers, parasols, table and chairs. The garden has a table tennis table, an outdoor shower and a private swimming pool (Depth 0,90 m - 1.5 m 3 mx 6 m).

The town of Alcudia is located approximately 4 km to the south-east, where you can find the ancient city walls dating from the early fourteenth century which are preserved for the most part , as well as the excavated Roman theatre and ruins. The long sandy beach of Bahia de Alcudia and the famous Playa de Muro are just a short drive away towards Puerto Alcudia in the north which is also close to the nature reserve, Albufera

Majorca is the largest of the four Balearic Islands; in fact its name means “the larger one”. Mallorca, like all of the Balearics, has many aspects to it which make it a wonderful holiday destination. Mallorca is often described as an island of contrasts; it certainly has something to interest many different types of visitors.
