Mas Pollensa, Pollensa, Mallorca, Spain Vacation Rental

 

Mallorca`s Campanet Valley is something really special. Hidden away in a dreamlike landscape in a delightful part of the Mallorcan countryside, it is the ideal place for a `get away from it all` family holiday.

Here in the valley you`ll find Mas Pollensa, a luxurious holiday villa with its own private swimming pool and extensive grounds. Set in a sheltered position with magnificent views of the hills beyond, this delightful home from home sleeps up to ten people in the utmost comfort.

If you`re looking to escape the pressures of modern day life and enjoy possibly the most idyllic holiday you will ever have, Mas Pollensa awaits you.

It`s beautiful. This spacious converted hacienda-style farmhouse has all the traditional touches in which Mallorca excels: terracotta roof tiles, whitewashed walls, ancient wooden beams, shutter-style windows...a little piece of heaven on earth.

If al fresco life in the sun takes your fancy, you couldn`t find a better place in which to hang your holiday hat. Surrounded by extensive lush gardens with mature fruit trees and colourful shrubbery, the star attraction has to be your own private swimming pool (pool size is 12 x 5m, depth 1m-2m).

Rectangular in shape with gentle steps for easy access, what could be better than enjoying an invigorating morning swim before breakfast as the sun rises over the beautiful Mallorcan countryside.

Whether you prefer to relax on a luxurious sun lounger by the pool, or sip a cocktail on the large covered dining terrace, the villa has some lovely tranquil spots for you to choose from. As the sun sets, why not get the barbecue going and enjoy an outdoor meal with family and friends? You can even gather fresh herbs grown in the owner`s garden, which is lovingly cared for all year round.

Mas Pollensa is equally impressive on the inside too!

Proceed along the gated driveway and enter through the hallway set with local traditional stone and you are greeted by a spacious interior that offers all the facilities to make your holiday truly memorable.

Here, traditional features are strongly in evidence. Several rooms boast solid wooden roof beams and there is a traditional fireplace and wood burning stove to keep you warm should the nights become a little chilly.

The living area is luxurious to say the least. Cosy sofas abound and the art on the walls is impressive. The dining room features a large wooden table to easily accommodate several guests. There is also a separate cosy reading room should you need a bit of peace and quiet.

You`ll find two twin bedrooms on the ground floor, both with en suite bathrooms. What`s more, they are self-contained and share a fully fitted apartment suite with its own covered terrace.

The country kitchen is a delight and features all the latest appliances(including a dishwasher) you need to rustle up family meals. Doors lead out onto the patio terrace and there is a separate laundry room with washing machine.

Upstairs you come across the gorgeous master bedroom complete with four-poster bed, en suite bathroom and access to sun terraces.

Another double bedroom again features a four-poster bed plus en suite shower room and balcony. There is also an additional twin bedroom on this floor.

What`s more, you`ll find large flat screen satellite TV`s placed throughout the property so you can catch up on all your favourite shows or watch some DVDs.

You also benefit from private parking.

Mas Pollensa may be a piece of rustic paradise, yet it is only 15 minutes drive away from the gorgeous town of Pollensa and some of Mallorca`s most beautiful cove beaches.
