3 Wishes, Puerto Pollensa, Mallorca, Spain Vacation Rental

 

3 Wishes is a beautiful beautiful Mallorcan country home with stunning ornate gardens and a welcoming atmosphere - your own holiday oasis!

The interior of 3 Wishes has been beautifully decorated with a keen eye to detail in the style of a stately manor - entering through the front door in the hallway you can head right towards the downstairs bedroom and it`s en suite (there is also a utility room access from this hall), this in effect is the sixth bedroom; straight ahead into the kitchen which has plenty of workspace, storage and comes equipped with all the essentials as well as a coffee machine and dishwasher; or to the left, which leads into the large dining room with seating for 12 and great views out into the ornate garden.

From the dining room there is access to a covered terrace which has an outdoor dining table and chairs, or you can head further into the house - take a walk down the large hall to reach both a cosy living room with a TV (an independent bathroom is accessed from this room) and a huge drawing room.

Take the stairs and you`ll head up into a vestibule from which you can access any of the five air conditioned bedrooms which are split into two wings - on the left there are two double bedrooms which share a family bathroom, and to the right there are another three bedrooms; a double and a twin bedroom which share another bathroom, and last but not least the large master suite with a luxurious en suite bathroom, gorgeous views and even a private balcony with stairs down to the garden.

3 Wishes garden is truly a sight to behold! A lush green lawn sprawls over the outdoors, divided down the centre by topiarised hedges leading to a water feature. Plenty of trees dot the lawn as well, making it a great space to lay back with a book and enjoy the peace and quiet.

If you follow the garden path from the main house, through the "living" gazebo and down the path walled with plumbago and bourgeanvillea, you`ll discover 3 Wishes best kept secret - it`s enormous swimming pool (15m x 6m)! Total privacy is afforded by the well-kept garden and there is a covered terrace with both dining furniture, outdoor sofas and twelve sun loungers; perfect for a chilled afternoon.

3 Wishes is a great choice for large families and groups looking for a secluded getaway. Located just a short drive away from the historic town of Pollensa, it keeps you away from the hustle and bustle whilst staying within a reasonable distance of shops, beaches and things to do.
