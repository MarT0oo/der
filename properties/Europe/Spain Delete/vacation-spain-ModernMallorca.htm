Modern Mallorca, Mallorca, Spain Vacation Rental

 

This is one of the most opulent, luxurious and elegant properties in the whole of Mallorca. Built to an incredible standard, this villa offers amazing views, privacy and pure luxury to all who stay here.

This distinctive, waterfront villa, with captivating design and architectural elements offers sweeping views over the picturesque beach and bay of Formentor, and azure the Mediterranean.

On the first floor you find the master suite with designer bath tub, Dornbracht Rain Sky shower and dressing room. This amazing bedroom also has a home cinema system placed at the foot of the bed.

There are three further outstanding ensuite bedrooms, all with access to a balcony. The living room with a glass lift dominates the middle floor and is bathed in natural light. Here also stands the incredible media/ leisure room with bar, beautiful dining room, professionally equipped kitchen, gym and sauna.

The teak deck houses the fantastic heated infinity pool with illuminated cascade. Out on the decking you can also relax in the jacuzzi or watch the sun set whilst making use of the BBQ and wet bar.

The lower floor contains the impressive theatre style cinema and discretely located staff accommodation.

This is undoubtedly the height of modern luxury and one of the most exclusive properties on the entire island
