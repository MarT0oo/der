Can Valflor, Mallorca, Spain Vacation Rental

 

Luxury 6 Bedroom, 4 Bathroom Villa with Mountain View and a Private Swimming Pool , Wi-Fi, near Golf, Sleeps a maximum of 10 people.

Can Valflor is luxury personified. A grand, imposing modern villa nestled in the beautiful north Mallorcan countryside near Puerto Pollensa, it is ideal for a holiday to remember with friends and family.

This stunning, L-shaped hacienda style villa sleeps up to 10 people comfortably, but with the added attraction of accommodating an extra 4 children in an additional bedroom containing 2 bunk beds.

You get a feeling of understated luxury the minute you walk into Can Valflor. The reception area is grand indeed, bright and spacious with lovely antique furniture. The exquisite, wide traditional stone staircase takes you back to an age of elegance.

The living room is no less impressive. Cool contemporary chic is the order of the day here; the gleaming white walls, fabulous designer coffee table and sumptuous sofas adding to the overall feeling of luxury.

There is a large-screen TV and an iPod dock to keep everyone entertained in the evenings, and there is also an impressive fireplace. From the living room you gain easy access to the large terrace, gardens and private 6x12m swimming pool (depth 1.00m - 2.30m).

The connecting dining room at Can Valflor is light, bright and airy, featuring a large dining table seating up to 12 people.

There is also a thoroughly modern, island-style kitchen, fully equipped with all the modern conveniences you need.

The master suite bedroom is gorgeous and features an en suite bathroom. The 3 other bedrooms share 3 Bathrooms between them, and provide some lovely touches such as superb mosaic patterned tiles.

Can Valflor is so secluded and private that you can lie back in a hot bath and take in a view of the mountains through the large windows.

The outdoor area is just as splendid. There is an open, covered terrace with large dining table and comfortable seating. There`s also a traditional built-in brick barbecue/grill if you fancy dining al fresco.

The large rectangular pool is so inviting, particularly on a hot day. Or you may simply wish to rise early for a healthy swim before the sun gets up.

The pool is surrounded by lush, manicured lawns and mature trees. Bougainvillea is much in evidence here, adding a splash of colour. Needless to say, the mountain views from here are absolutely stunning.

Can Valflor is a grand, stylish modern villa located in the north Mallorcan countryside between Pollensa, Puerto Pollensa and Cala San Vicente.
