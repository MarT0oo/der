Can Perot, Mallorca, Spain Vacation Rental

 

Luxury 4 Bedroom, 4 Bathroom Villa with Mountain View and a Private Swimming Pool , Air Conditioning, Wi-Fi, Satellite TV, near Golf, Sleeps a maximum of 8 people.

Situated in the much sought after and exclusive area of Vall de March, just 15 minutes from the historical town of Pollensa, this elegant, modern and spacious finca sits nestled into the mountains, surrounded by some of the finest countryside Mallorca has to offer.

The interior combines uber modern design and all modern conveniences with neutral and natural tones throughout, creating a perfect blend for a very comfortable and relaxing stay.

This finca can accommodate up to eight guests with its four ample sized double bedrooms with fantastic countryside views, four bespoke bathrooms, open plan kitchen/diner, living room with doors onto the terrace, office area and reading room.

Take a stroll around the extensive gardens at dusk, then relax and enjoy your evening meal on the terrace overlooking the flood lit pool.

This extremely private villa will offer you natures true beauty as your daily backdrop, and complete relaxation, your perfect holiday getaway!
