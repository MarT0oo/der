Marcela, Puerto Pollensa, Mallorca, Spain Vacation Rental

 

4 Bedroom, 3 Bathroom Villa with Mountain View and a Private Heated Swimming Pool , Air Conditioning, Table Tennis, Satellite TV, near Golf, Sleeps a maximum of 8 people.

A private driveway leads you to the beautiful large open villa which is surrounded by open fields and with wonderful views to the mountains.

The entrance leads you an open-plan large hallway/dining area with stairs leading to the first floor. To the left of the entrance is a large lounge/Tv room.

The dining area is to the right of the entrance door. There is an abundance of light in this room from the large windows and patio doors at the far end of the room. These doors lead you to the covered outside terrace area which provides beautiful views across the pool and surrounding countryside. An American style fully-fitted and fully-equipped kitchen is accessed from the dining area. There are also patio doors leading from the kitchen to the outside terrace area.

Also on the ground floor, to the left of the staircase, is the main bathroom complete with bath and shower. Next to the bathroom is a large twin bedroom.

Upstairs there are three more bedrooms all of which enjoy beautiful views of the open countryside.

The main bedroom is on a split level divided by two small steps. On the upper level is a large comfortable sofa. On the lower level is a king size bed and an en-suite shower room.

The next bedroom is a large twin bedroom with an en-suite shower room. The final bedroom upstairs is a wonderfully light double bedroom with patio doors leading out to a large terrace area giving spectacular views of the pool, countryside and mountains.

Outside the villa there are two large seating areas to relax overlooking the swimming pool and cook on the portable Bbq.
