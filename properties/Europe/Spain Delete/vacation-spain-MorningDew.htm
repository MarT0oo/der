Morning Dew, Pollensa, Mallorca, Spain Vacation Rental

 

Morning Dew is a newly renovated 6 bedroom holiday villa in the leafy residential area of La Font, just a leisurely walk from the charming Mallorcan village of Pollensa in northern Mallorca.

Morning Dew is a detached villa set amongst large, lawned gardens, plenty of outdoor space and the all important swimming pool.

Entering through the front gate, there`s a short driveway where you can securely park your car and a garage containing bike racks and a cyclist`s workbench. To the right hand side of the garage is the front door - head on in and you`ll be greeted by Morning Dew`s immense sense of space, afforded by it`s modern makeover; bright white walls, fixtures and fittings are accentuated by pops of colour and wonderful decor.

Head down the steps from the entrance hall and you`ll enter the kitchen/dining area. Here you`ll find all modern conveniences, with essentials such as a coffee machine and dishwasher all present, and set around a large central island and enormous wooden dining table with seating for 12 guests.

On the other side of the dividing wall of this otherwise open plan space is the lounge, which is dominated by large, comfortable sofas and designer armchairs. There are two spaces here - a more grown-up, drawing room arrangement around a fireplace, and opposite is a more cosy arrangement, with a flatscreen TV mounted on the wall. The large windows on the south-east side of the living room slide open to access a large partially covered terrace with dining and chill-out furniture.

Morning Dew`s 6 bedrooms are all en suite - the first can be found to the left of the main entrance, through a small hallway where you will also come across a guest WC and laundry room - head up the stairs here to find a private double bedroom with access to an ample roof terrace with a pair of sun-loungers.

The second of the bedrooms is located just before you enter the kitchen, to your left after walking down the steps from the entrance hall - this bedroom also has a satellite TV. Opposite this door, to the right of the entrance, is the master suite which has a double bed, ample wardrobe space, en suite bathroom, a satellite TV and even access to the garden! Down from this bedroom is the fourth and last in the main house: it has twin beds and an en suite shower room.

The last two bedrooms are located off of the terrace in an independent apartment, once again with en suite bathrooms.

The highlight of Morning Dew`s garden is the kidney-shaped deep blue swimming pool, which is surrounded by a stylish patio paving and luxurious sun beds and parasols, whilst on the west side of the property are several lawned terraces (don`t worry, the height difference is minimal, and child friendly) which make a great play area.

There`s even a ping pong table for competitive guests to enjoy a match.

With Pollensa being just a gentle walk from the villa, and the beaches of Cala San Vicente just a 5 minute drive away, Morning Dew is the perfect rental property for large families and groups looking to be out of the way, but within reasonable distance to all attractions and amenities.
