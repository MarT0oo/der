Waters Edge, Pollensa, Mallorca, Spain Vacation Rental

 

There`s something about this luxurious Mallorcan country villa that screams "holiday of a lifetime". Brand new to the rental market and exclusive to our portfolio, you`ll struggle to find something better on the island!

Sleeping up to 10 people in the utmost comfort and style, Waters Edge really is the perfect place to enjoy an away-from-it-all holiday with family and friends.

Situated in a highly desirable rural spot between the north Mallorcan towns of Alcudia and Pollensa, Waters Edge is more than just a luxury holiday property, it`s a mini private estate in its own right.

Absolutely stunning landscaped gardens complete with mature trees and colourful shrubs overlook Pollensa Bay and the Tramuntana mountain range.

You will certainly enjoy relaxing in the villa`s lovely, heated rectangular swimming pool with stepped entrance, surrounded by wooden decking and luxury sun loungers.

Illuminated at night, the pool area creates a rather magical ambiance - ideal for enjoying a glass of wine or two as the sun sets and you fire up the barbecue.

Completed as recently as 2011, the interior of Waters Edge is stunning. If light, luxury, space and privacy are high on your priority list for a holiday to remember, you`ve got it in spades here.

The spacious, open-plan living/dining area is a dream. Chic, contemporary furnishings contrast with traditional features such as wooden roof beams and walls constructed of local stone. There is satellite TV so you can catch up with your favourite programmes, and wi-fi throughout the property to keep you in touch.

You have air conditioning to keep you cool when temperatures rise, and underfloor heating throughout should the evenings get a little chilly. An intelligent alarm system is also a feature of the property, combined with an automatic exterior gate for access.

Waters Edge`s five bedrooms are beautiful - all have en suite bathrooms and provide stunning views over the Mallorcan countryside; there are two double bedrooms and three twin bedrooms. The five bathrooms feature state-of-the-art fixtures and fittings and are ideal for enjoying a long, hot soak after a day in the sun.

The rather chic kitchen is light, bright and contemporary, with all the latest quality appliances for cooking up a real treat. There is a laundry area too.

And that`s not all. There is also a delightful covered outdoor terrace, so why not sink into one of the cosy sofas with a drink and good book in hand, and chill light years away from the stresses of the 21st century.

Due to its lovely rural location, you will need car transport when staying in Waters Edge and there is a double garage provided. You will also enjoy the freedom to explore the lovely northern part of Mallorca with its gorgeous mountains, lively culture, beautiful sandy beaches and crystal clear waters perfect for watersports.

And don`t forget those classy international restaurants which are a famous feature of the nearby towns of Pollensa and Alcudia!

Waters Edge is charming, elegant country mansion which promises a Mediterranean vacation you will remember for many years too come. With Mallorca being such a highly desirable holiday destination this year, we would suggest that you book early!
