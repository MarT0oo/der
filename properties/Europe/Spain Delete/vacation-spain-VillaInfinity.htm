Villa Infinity, Mallorca, Spain Vacation Rental

 

Elegant imposing villa in sought after residential area in Portals Nous, Majorca. The house Consists of two living floors and ground floor with a garage for 2 cars, the elevator connects all 3 floors.

The living space of the villa is distributed in the following way: the first floor has 5 bedrooms (master bedroom with wardrobe), 5 en suite bathrooms and a small office. 4 bedrooms have access to the terraces and to the garden, which surround the house, and the fifth leads to the patio. The upper floor consists of spacious lounge, 2 dining areas, an american kitchen with high tech appliances and a bar, a storage room and laundry room. The kitchen and living room has access to the garden and spacious covered terrace with sea view lounge area.

The stairs in the garden lead up to the swimming pool with stunning panoramic views of the Mediterranean Sea and is surrounded with solarium and covered patio with lounge. This luxury property is well designed and is equipped with air conditioning, built-in wardrobes, marble floors, DVD player, alarm and automatic entrance gates.

The villa is situated in 2 minutes by walk from sandy beach, situated in beautiful cove, restaurant, souvenir and food shop and in short driving distance from supermarkets, tennis and sports club, luxury restaurants, bars and Real Golf Bendinat
