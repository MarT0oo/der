Eden, Puerto Pollensa, Mallorca, Spain Vacation Rental

 

Luxury 4 Bedroom, 3 Bathroom Villa with Mountain View and a Private Swimming Pool , Air Conditioning, Wi-Fi, Теnnis Court, Table Tennis, Satellite TV, near Golf, Sleeps a maximum of 10 people.

As soon as you sweep into the driveway you will see why this fabulous country villa is called Eden! This really is a piece of paradise, nestled in the heart of the Mallorcan countryside, yet just a couple of miles from the beautiful Bay of Pollensa.

The original farmhouse was built from local sandstone which means that Eden stays naturally cool in the hot summer months (although you can put the air-conditioning on if you wish!) There is private parking for up to 15 cars.

As recently as 2011 this traditional Mallorcan farmhouse underwent a lavish refurbishment, transforming the property into a stylish modern villa with cool art on the walls, yet also retaining many of its original features.

Eden is the perfect place to get together with family and friends for the holiday of a lifetime. The large light, airy lounge is the ideal place to chill out after a day exploring the very best of Mallorca. SKY TV, cinema-quality DVD, Sonos music system and Wi-Fi throughout only adding to the overall experience.

Whatever time of year you choose to stay at Eden, you`re assured of the utmost comfort. You benefit from hot and cold air conditioning throughout, as well as solar heated water in the summer, and cosy, wood-fired central heating in the winter.

The kitchen area at Eden is large and, as you would expect, complimented by state of the art fitted features such as a La Cornue cooking range. Just perfect for some delicious home cooking!

Eden features no less than four double bedrooms, two of them en suite, plus two additional bathrooms. Each bedroom has a large double bed, and two include extra space for an additional bed or cot.

The outside area at Eden is paradise personified. Tree-lined gardens, stunning mountain views, a large 20m swimming pool plus sun terrace with comfy loungers and parasols certainly impress.

The whole family will enjoy the Swedish sauna house with large shower area, and there`s even a new floodlit tennis court complete with electronic ball machine to help hone your racquet skills!

Guests also enjoy a large barbecue terrace and al fresco dining area complete with wooden table that seats 12. This house is just made for entertaining!

You could even choose to be partly self-sufficient at Eden. The 10 acres of grounds enable you to pick your own fruit (grapes, figs, strawberries, oranges, lemons, avocados) and vegetables in season (tomatoes, onions, lettuce, carrots, potatoes, beans, cucumbers, cabbage, cauliflower.) The kids will love it.
