Casa Fendi, Benahavís, Spain Vacation Rental

 

A large and truly jaw-droppingly beautiful villa, Casa Fendi is one of the most luxurious, exclusive villas in the Benahavís area.

All interiors have been completed to a 5 star specification including Fendi designer furniture in the lounge and dining area. Many bathrooms have Villeroy & Boch oversize and jacuzzi baths, rain shower wet rooms and dressing rooms, and separate dressing rooms. With two pools, sauna and huge gardens it’s a very special place indeed.

Accommodating up to 25 guests in absolute comfort, Casa Fendi stretches endlessly over a large, secluded plot with extensive landscaped gardens, terraces, swimming pools (outdoor & indoor), hot tubs and sauna. The professional kitchen, BBQ and outdoor cooking area are designed to cater for large groups with ease and are simply magnificent! A new outdoor covered area allows spectacular al fresco dining for up to 40.
