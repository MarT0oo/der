La Grand Dame, Ibiza, Spain Vacation Rental

 

Situated just outside of San Jose and a few minutes away from some of the main spots of interest on the island, La Grand Dame is a magnificent private property of exceptional dimensions placed on a three-hectare plot of land. This space is conceived as a private park of sorts, featuring more than one-hundred varieties of plants and trees, paths fit for nice walks immersed in the nature and a private tennis court for those who can’t resist having a quick match, perhaps after a training session in the property’s private gym equipped with top-of-the-line Technogym equipment.

The oldest part of the property dates back to more than three-hundred years ago and was completely restored and renovated in 2013 in full respect of the traditional architecture yet enhancing it with all modern comforts. Along with the later added annexed units La Grand Dame offer a comfortable solution for particularly numerous groups, all within an residence that boasts a strong approach to accommodation as liveability of the all spaces.

With its fourteen bedrooms – all appointed with the best comforts – this property is able to cater flawlessly to the most demanding guests in an elegant and modern ambience, characterized by an airy and light atmosphere where the colour white dominates contrasted only by elements of design bearing bright colours.

Three living rooms with just as many kitchens, a tennis court, a gym and a helipad are only some of the features that make La Grande Dame one of the most important private properties on Ibiza.
