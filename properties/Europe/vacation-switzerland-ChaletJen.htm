Chalet Jen, Zermatt, Switzerland Vacation Rental

 

Chalet Jen is a lovely ground floor apartment with three double en suite bedrooms (two of which can be made up as twin beds).

There is a fireplace in the open plan lounge, dining area and kitchen.

The lounge has a stunning bay window with fantastic views of the Matterhorn and looks down over the village.

The kitchen has a hob, oven, dishwasher, fridge and all the modern appliances you would need.

The property is located on Zer Bannu Strasse which is a 7 minute walk to the centre of the village and the nearest lift station, which is Sunnegga
