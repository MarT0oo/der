Casa dos Diamantes, Quinta do Lago, Portugal Vacation Rental

 

A luxurious and contemporary six bedroom villa, with the finest of finishes, stunning pool, garden area, and cinema room. Located in the heart of Quinta do Lago, and close to the beach.

This unique villa has been designed and finished to the highest standards. The double electric privacy gates open to reveal a stunning villa. The landscaped grounds complement this property's and provides a great space to enjoy the outdoor lifestyle of the Algarve.

This stunning villa consists of 6 bedroom suites, 2 master bedroom suites on the first floor and 3 guest suites on the ground floor all with beautiful luxurious bathrooms. There are spacious living areas, a fabulous designer kitchen.

The large lower ground floor with entertainment area with bar, cinema and additional kitchen. On this level there is also an annex apartment with the 6th bedroom suite.

The features of this property include an intelligent home automation system with wall touch control pads and a walk about mobile unit. The system includes customised audio and video distribution throughout, Wi-Fi framework allowing full access from anywhere on the property and full music and film storage facility with viewing on all TVs and the customised cinema which has acoustic panels for enhanced sound. There is exceptional lighting with remote controlled RGB inside and out including in the swimming pool.
