Cabalgaderos Estate, Provincia de Cordoba, Andalucia, Spain Vacation Rental


Cabalgaderos is an Andalusian country estate (700 hectares) (Cortijo) decorated with beautiful works of art and Spanish antiques.  The house has a cook ready to prepare typical dishes with natural products and local spirits.  The estate has a private guard, who is in charge of the property security, at your service.  The house is provided with 5 double bedrooms, a porch, a dining room, two sitting rooms, a swimming pool, TV, DVD, a telephone and Digital channel, 5 bathrooms and toilets. For horse riding lovers, we have four horses available, with all the necessary equipment to practice Spanish mount.

There is also a six seats carriage pulled by two mules for those who enjoy harnessing and driving them.  You will find two four-wheel drive vehicles at your disposal, in case you wish to go on a trip-excursion within the country estate area. In these excursions you will enjoy watching deer and wild boar as well as well as all kinds of birds and varied vegetation such as oaks, heathers, cork oaks etc. Horse riding is a must in Cabalgaderos. The Cortijo is next to the XVIth century Saint Callixtus Convent, a place devoted to quiet and meditation, where the Belgian Kings Baudouin and Fabiola spent their honeymoon, and where you can also buy craftworks made by the nuns of a closed order. Two typical Andalusian white villages, Navas de la Concepcion and Hornachuelos, are less than a 20 minute drive away from the house. At a 45 minute drive you arrive in Palma del Rio, a village with outstanding monuments, restaurants and a shopping area. It takes an hour to get to Cordoba and one hour and a half to get to Sevilla.
