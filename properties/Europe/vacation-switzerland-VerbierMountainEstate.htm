Verbier Mountain Estate, Verbier, Switzerland Vacation Rental

 

This 1,000m2 mountain residence comprises two buildings, the North Wing chalet and the South Wing chalet, with a large central terrace and substantial spa area. This estate commands an enviable location which is ski in ski out with direct access to and from Piste Rouge. The property is approximately 200 metres above the sun terrace of Le Rouge Bar & Restaurant where the piste finishes. The centre of Verbier is a short distance from here.

The North Wing chalet and South Wing chalet each offer a spacious lounge with log fire and beautiful views. The estate combines contemporary design with mountain architecture and plenty of charm. The North Wing chalet (sleeping up to 12), and central Spa can be rented independently of the South Wing or the estate can be rented as a whole (to host up to 22 guests).

The Verbier Mountain Estate has views in the direction of the Mont Blanc massif as well as over the more immediate Le Rouge ski piste. The property has large terraces. On the upper terrace sits a massive outdoor barbecue and original Italian pizza oven. On the lower private terrace, in front of the Spa sits the jacuzzi.

In winter, the garden and piste alongside provide fantastic “private” tobogganing runs at night by torchlight once the last skiers have left the mountain.

In summer, the residence becomes one of the most beautiful locations in Verbier, as the ski piste on which it is located changes into a meadow of green grasses and wild flowers, with a feeling of wide open space and a wonderful play area for children.
