Villa Rose, Kremithas, Corfu Island, Greece Vacation Rental


Unforgettable moments of luxury and leisure…

Villa Rose is reached by a private road and it is quite clear as you approach that unlike other Villas, "Rose" is as groovy and as luxurious anyone could ever imagine.  Villa Rose has been equipped with the top of the range bathrooms, leisure equipment and hand crafted- sophisticated furniture.

This pretty corner of heaven incorporates extensive grounds of lawn along with an enormous swimming pool with heated Jacuzzi, outdoor kitchen, barbeque, and its own separate pool-house. The special designed pool area commands an unbelievable view over the Corfu channel and it is ideal for relaxing and perfect for those of a romantic inclination.

The villa is laid out over 3 levels with a lift between the levels as well as a staircase.  Entrance level open plan hallway and spacious living room with dining area. Large patio doors opening on to a covered balcony.

Supplementary kitchen. Double bedroom with en suite bathroom with Jacuzzi bath and balcony. Double bedroom with en suite shower room with hydro-massage shower and balcony.

Lower ground floor - Main kitchen with separate outside entrance for staff. Everyday sitting room and dining area with fireplace leading to garden. Playroom with billiard table. Spa area with hammam, massage shower, WC, gymnasium. Door to outside with path access to tennis court . Double bedroom with en suite walk in shower room.

First floor - Open plan office area with internet connection. Master double bedroom with king size bed and en suite bathroom with jacuzzi bath. Balcony. Double bedroom with en suite bathroom with Jacuzzi bath. Twin bedroom with en suite shower room. All these rooms have access to a large sea facing balcony.
