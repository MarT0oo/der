Villa Marianne, Vale do Lobo, Algarve, Portugal Vacation Rental

 

Villa Marianne is a beautiful high standard detached villa set into the hillside and overlooking pine trees in a central location on Vale do Lobo, approx 10 minutes walk to the beach and centre of Vale do Lobo.

Walk down for breakfast at Sandbanks restaurant and bar.

In the evening enjoy all the facilities of the praça, bar; restaurants, entertainment, etc.

Spacious well furnished living/dining area with separate TV room with cinema / sky satellite TV and DVD. Sliding doors lead from the lounge to a balcony with staircase down through the terraced garden to the pool. Fully fitted and well equipped kitchen with oven/hob, fridge/freezer, washing machine and dryer, dishwasher and microwave.

The bedrooms consist of one master twin bedroom (beds together) on the same level as the lounge, with en-suite bathroom with separate shower cubicle. A staircase leads down to the other 3 twin bedrooms, one with en-suite bathroom with separate shower and inter-connecting single bedroom, one other twin with en-suite shower room and one further twin with adjacent bathroom. All the bedrooms on this lower level have doors to the terrace leading down to the pool ( gated area)

The large rectangular pool (10 x 5m) which is set down a flight of stairs from the villa, has steps into the shallow end and is surrounded by pool terracing with a built-in BBQ. The pool includes a child safety fence/gate separating the swimming area from the rest of the garden and villa.
