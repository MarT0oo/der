Rosa Maria Villa, Algarve, Portugal Vacation Rental


This beautiful 4 bed villa is situated on the private Quinta Verde development on the outskirts of Quinta do Lago.  It is walking distance to restaurants and shops and there is an onsite gym and beauty centre. Fine Algarvian golf courses are a short distance from this villa, as are the beaches.  The villa has been furnished and decorated throughout to a modern very high standard.
 
Rosa Maria Villa is spacious and accommodates up to 8 people.  The home is equipped with Satellite TV, DVD/CD player, cinema room barbeque area, private outdoor pool and private garden.  Enjoy beautiful countryside views as you make Rosa Maria Villa your vacation home away from home.

Quinta Verde sits within the area known as the “Golden Triangle”. Located in the central Algarve and only twenty minutes drive from Faro International airport. The Triangle incorporates the exclusive developments of Quinta do Lago and Vale do Lobo together with the soon to be built 6-star Conrad Hotel. The area is renowned for its manicured golf courses, sophisticated cuisine, stretches of golden beaches and a variety of entertainment facilities and is a haven offering all the ingredients to enjoy a truly luxurious lifestyle.

ABOUT PORTUGAL
They say the Portuguese waters hold a mystery that lovers often find shown in the glimpse of the ships passing in the night.  The shores of Portugal from The Porto (North Portugal) where you can find history afoot while visiting local historical sites: CASA-Museu de Guerra Junqueiro and the Museum of Quinta da Macieirinha.  Or one could easily slip away to visit the city and visit the stock exchange and cathedral.  Or if your prefer Tras-os-Momtes where you can do tons outside, such as canoeing or mountain trekking.  On the North Coast of Minho you can discover amazing caves and superb beaches.
