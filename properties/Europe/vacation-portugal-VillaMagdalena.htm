Villa Magdalena, Quinta do Lago, Algarve, Portugal Vacation Rental


Located in the desirable area of Encosta do Lago and just a few minutes walking distance to the lake, where you can enjoy water-sports, the beach, and the ever popular Shack -bar-bistro and the dining experience of Casa do Lago restaurant.

Also close by is the new Quinta do Lago Sports Center, with wonderful world class facilities. The Campus hosts a state of the art gym, spa, outdoor heated swimming pool, tennis courts, paddle courts, football pitch and the ever popular restaurant/bar Dano’s.

The location of this stunning Villa really is great for those who love to be withing walking distance of all the main facilities that the five star Quinta do Lago resort has to offer.

Clients are greeted to a modern entrance hall with a staircase leading up to the first floor and into the comfortable lounge area. There are 3 bedrooms located on the first floor, two ensuite double bedrooms & a Master bedroom ensuite with access to a terrace. On the ground floor you have also two ensuite bedrooms, one double and one twin.

The Villa is beautifully decorated throughout. There is a spacious kitchen with a breakfast bar and a dining room. In the living room you can enjoy a lovely lounge area in front of the fireplace, having its own separate TV snug area.

The lower level of the property has an ensuite twin bedroom, which has natural light and access to the pool area.

The exterior of the property features a large swimming pool, sun loungers, outdoor seating area & a charcoal BBQ.
