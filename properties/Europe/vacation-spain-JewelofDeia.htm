This traditionally stone built 4 bedroom villa is in the picturesque village of Deia. It is tastefully furnished and boasts the most wonderful views over the village of Deia, the Mediterranean Sea and the mountains. It is situated in a quiet area on the sunny side of the village.

Sa Tanca is in a quiet cul-de-sac immediately above the world famous La Residencia Hotel and hidden from tourists and traffic. The house is literally a stone’s throw from the hotel and only a few minutes’ walk from anywhere in the village. It is the perfect holiday retreat for those who want to avoid the use of the car, be private and inconspicuous and yet still feel very much part of Deia. Beautifully built in the Finca style the house has views of the church, the Tramuntana Mountains and across the rooftops of La Residencia to the sea beyond. The house combines the best of the past with the luxury of the present.

The interior of the property is spacious, light and airy. It is tastefully decorated throughout, featuring old and new handcraft furnishings as well as comfortable easy chairs and sofas and a variety of original artwork.

Evergreen oak furniture, beamed ceilings and white walls all combine harmoniously, creating a relaxed and comfortable environment. Care and attention to detail is also apparent in the exterior of the property; a private pool sits next to a lawn with a olive tree and a very private garden stocked with palm, olive, citrus fruit and cypress trees, bougainvillea and climbers.

There are a various places to enjoy al fresco dining; the loggia in the central courtyard is ideal for breakfast, the pool area is great for lunch in the shade of the pergola, and the privacy of the garden. The wisteria covered pergola on the front terrace is perfect for evening drinks or dinner whilst enjoying the wonderful views of Deia village, the sea and the sunsets.

The centre of the village is just a short walk away, with its numerous restaurants, bars and boutiques.

GROUND FLOOR: The double height entrance hall leads to the left into an open living/dining and kitchen area, straight ahead is the loggia, courtyard and pool and on the right to the sitting/TV room. The ground floor living areas have French windows opening into the garden.

A shower room that communicates with the pool and dining area. A large kitchen, with Corian worktops, is fully equipped with modern appliances, including plumbed-in steam oven, electric oven, Gaggenau 2-ring gas hob, 2 ring electric hob, electric barbecue, toaster, Nespresso, juicer etc. A separate larder with dishwasher, large fridge, chiller, freezer and ice maker. Utility Room: At the end of the loggia is a utility room with washing machine and tumble dryer.

The sitting room has a Flat screen TV with Satellite, DVD player, Music System (with Bose speakers piped through the living room), and opens out onto the front terrace and rear loggia.
