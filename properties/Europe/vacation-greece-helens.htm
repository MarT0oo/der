Helen's Stately Home
4 Bedrooms | 3 Baths | Pool                                                                               
Crete, Greece
  From €190 - €240 per night

The Helen’s Stately home has been built with care on the family home grounds. It is a destination which combines the aristocracy of a renovated mansion and the dreamy environment of the village. The first level has a handmade wooden kitchen fully equipment, a spacious living room with a big fireplace and two bedroom with their own separated bath.

The second level has a big terrace with breathtaking mountain view, a handmade wooden kitchen fully equipment, a spacious living room and a loft room with an internal wooden stair. The serenity of the village makes the high walls that surround this property fairly unnecessary, but to guarantee the utmost privacy, they produce a romantic and spacious courtyard of outstanding beauty, hidden from the sight of anyone passing by. Big trees offer shade during mid day heat, the pool dares you to refresh yourself any time you wish, the ample outdoor sitting- and dining facilities provoke to avoid the interior with little organic vegetable garden.

The village, with its ancient history and traditional ways of life, is an ideal place to learn about customs, traditions and experience an authentic rural life. Throughout the area the visitor is invited to walk in a region of particular natural beauty, to admire important monuments and to experience the customary local hospitality. We are pleased to offer our delightful region for excursions or holidays, combining naturalist and archaeological routes that, in their total, constitute an important ‘map' of experiences. The Municipality of Kouloukona organizes every year many interesting cultural events open to residents and to visitors alike. The "Talea Festival" with theatre performances, art exhibitions, concerts and various other ravishing activities is a yearly summer festival well worth visiting.
