Bowhill, Kingdom of Fife, Scotland Vacation Rental

 

Bowhill is set in the picturesque hamlet of Peat Inn just 10 minutes from St Andrews, the home of golf. Bowhill sleeps up to eight in four luxury ensuite bedrooms with super king size beds which can also be configured to suit a twin share.

There is a sitting room in which you can congregate, relax and chill with wall mounted flat screen TV with full SKY package. This is also the dining room where you will be served your breakfast specially created for you by John and Jo-Anne. In the evening we open John’s Bar for residents where you can enjoy a beverage beside the roaring fire.

Each bedroom includes flat screen digital TV, ensuite bathrooms have a power shower, three of the ensuite bathrooms also have a stand-alone roll top bath, all with luxury toiletries, robes and slippers, memory foam super king size beds, hospitality tray and mineral water, hairdryer, shaving point, and complimentary wireless internet access.

There is a lovely terrace in which to enjoy and look out to the garden and beyond to the tranquil, beautiful Fife countryside and across to the Firth of Forth. Guests of Bowhill have full access to our 270-yard private driving range, subject to availability. Bowhill is ideal to book individual rooms for business, a leisure break or dining at the Peat Inn restaurant which is only minutes away. Our bedrooms are also perfect as an overflow to our self-catering properties, Hawkswood House and The Roundel.

We can offer a pre-booked transport service to and from the Peat Inn restaurant and the town of St Andrews. Price on request.
