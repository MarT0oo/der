Petra Villa, Patmos Island, Greece Vacation Rental


Proudly under the exclusive management of the Petra Hotel & Suites, the Petra Villa is one of the most luxurious and graceful private villas on the island of Patmos. The magnificent four-bedroom / four-bathroom Petra Villa is an ideal luxury property for a privileged Patmos vacation.

Located in the heart of the truly beautiful settlement of Chora, one of the most picturesque villages of the Aegean and a UNESCO World Heritage Site, the Petra Villa provides a truly fascinating arrangement. Ideal for extended families or a group of friends, the Petra Villa was originally built in 1542 and as such is one of the oldest houses of the captivating maze of white-washed houses and alleys that comprise the unique village of Chora. Offering modern amenities that blend perfectly with antique furniture and local touches while respecting local architecture and traditions, the fully renovated Petra Villa is indeed one of the most beautiful Patmos villas that will certainly enchant even the most demanding visitors.

The Petra Hotel & Suites, located in Grikos, is within a three-minute drive from the village of Chora, where the Petra Villa is located. It is important to mention that the Petra Villa can be reached only on foot (a five-minute walk from the area where cars are allowed), or by scooter. More precisely, visitors can conveniently drop off their car at the entrance of Chora, and walk in the car-free zone of this jewel town of Patmos in order to reach the Petra Villa.

The LIVING Areas

With hardwood floors, wooden ceilings, and impressive antique furniture, the Petra Villa is one of the most impressive houses on Patmos. Entering the house from the serene external patio, visitors meet a comfortable space that serves as an indoor sitting area and dining room. Adjacent to the living room and dining area, albeit without visual contact from it, you will find the wonderful kitchen decorated in authentic Patmian style and offering all modern amenities. The kitchen is comfortably situated next to the patio for easy access to all the areas on the main floor.

Of course visitors of the Petra Villa should not worry about cold or warm temperatures: the entire house offers climate control through sophisticated radiant cooling and heating systems that are particularly friendly to the environment.

The ACCOMMODATION Areas

The master bedroom and two additional bedrooms are located at the mezzanine level of the Petra Villa, while a fourth bedroom is situated on the ground floor.


A small sitting area on the mezzanine level provides additional comfort and serves as an entry area to a corridor that leads to the rooms. The master bedroom and a second bedroom are equipped with en-suite bathrooms. In fact, the en-suite bathroom of the second bedroom is situated at a different level, combined with an area offering an additional sofa-bed that may also be used as a couch. Connected to the bedroom through a small stairway, this additional space is a perfect illustration of local architecture and a playful tradition of Patmian houses.
