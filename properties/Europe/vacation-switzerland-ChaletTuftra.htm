Chalet Tuftra, Zermatt, Switzerland Vacation Rental

 

Chalet Tuftra is located in Zermatt’s only ski-in ski-out district, Winkelmatten, it is right on the of the forest and you often see the deer feeding from the balcony .

This area is known as the “Beverly Hills” of Zermatt and has some fantastic restaurants and bars, children’s play areas and shops. From the chalet you can ski through the meadow and down to the Klein Matterhorn Express and you can ski back to the door of the chalet down the Moosweg ski-run.

This property consists of two luxury, adjacent, penthouse apartments which can be booked separately or together, one has two double/twin en suite bedrooms (and sleeps 4-6) and the other has three double/twin en suite bedrooms (sleeps 6).

Booked together they sleep 10 comfortably and you then command the entire top floor. The decor has been done in a beautiful alpine style. Both apartments have a spacious open plan lounge and dining area, open fires and modern fully fitted and equipped kitchens.

The spacious dining and living areas are cladded with mountain spruce, oak wood floors, Swarovski chandeliers and this with the open fireplaces exude comfort and coziness. From the balconies you can enjoy a beautiful panoramic view.

The apartments can be connected or disconnected to accommodate a maximum of 12 guests. All bedrooms are en suite and equipped with bath or shower, two bathrooms also have steam showers. Each room has flat screen TV for reception of satellite TV, the living rooms each have a large flat screen. The entire penthouse is equipped with Wi-Fi.

Each apartment has its own private laundry with clothes dryers
