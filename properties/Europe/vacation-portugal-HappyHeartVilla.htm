Happy Heart Villa, Vale do Lobo, Algarve, Portugal Vacation Rental

 

Beautiful recently renovated 4 bedroom villa located in the Vale do Lobo resort within walking distance to the Praça and beach. This property comprises of an open plan dining and lounge area featuring a fireplace and direct access to the pool terrace and a fully fitted and equipped kitchen with breakfast bar.

Two of the bedrooms are located on the ground floor, which share a bathroom. The master guest bedroom is en-suite and features a shared terrace with the master suite which features stunning sea views and an al-fresco seating area. The terrace includes a plunge pool with enough space for the sun loungers perfect to enjoy the hot summer days. The award winning Vale do Lobo resort offers a wide range of holiday activities such as; golf, water sports, mini golf, kids centre, night life, superb restaurants and a wellness centre.
