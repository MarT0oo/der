The Architect House, Cap de Barbaria, Spain Vacation Rental

 

The Architect House an exclusive luxury property in the heart of the Formentera. The Villa is located in the Cap de Barbaria area of the island, the structure emerge from the nature with spectacular views of the magical rock of Es Vedra and the rest of Ibiza.

The sparkling 350 sq. m villa blends into the landscape with it’s design. Such a marvellous property of its kind happens also to be near the wonderful beach of Cala Saona. Inside the tasteful decor is a sophisticated mix that blends contemporary architecture with vintage décor and bespoke furnishings. The overall design concept is one of continuous, bright and vast open-spaces symbiotic with the external areas on to which they outflow effortlessly. From the roof terrace guests can be thrilled by a phenomenal sea and 360 view of the island panorama, which alone makes stay at this impressive residence an all the more unforgettable experience. Overall, there is ample space in which to enjoy the company of others without feeling crowded and also to find a comfortable corner for more private rest and relaxation. With its state-of-the-art technology and the extraordinary bonus of a pool, facing west with views of the beautiful sunset which sets right next to Ibiza.

Up to a total of 18 people (14 guests + 4 staff) can stay at this spacious luxury villa arranged with double bedrooms en suite for each guest with spectacular views of the sea on the ground floor and 2 double bedrooms with one bathroom in the basement. Having also a living room with dining area and a private outdoor terrace that is spacious enough to double as a private open lounge and romantic al fresco dining area. The main terrace is a wonderful social place and an ideal setting for large private dinners with an unique sunset sea views on Es Vedra. The TV area offers privacy for lazy afternoons.

The villa is a private and paradisiac haven within an exclusive luxury retreat and it is an elegant holiday home, particularly for a group of couples, friends or a family who desire a fabulous setting with lots of space and opportunity of independence for private summer bliss.
