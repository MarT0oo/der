Germany

Germany has always been place that many people have dreamed of seeing, from it’s fabulous architecture and history, to the wonderment of the new hustling and bustling cities that put some of the most mainstream to shame.  This country has undergone such a revolution from its days at war to its days in the sunshine, no matter how you look at it, Germany is always a memorable experience. 

Some of most famous places to travel are Berlin, the Capital of Germany.  So many wonderful places to start when enjoying a tour, such as  Brandenburg Gate, the Checkpoint Charlie Museum, and the Berlin Wall.  For those who are looking for some nightlife, Berlin offers plenty of great nightclubs and restaurants, there is no lack of cultural diversity and wonderful transportation throughout.   In addition for those going to the Berlin International Film Festival it’s a place like no other.  You will see Films from all over the Globe and experience the city like you’ve never seen it before.  As well for Music Lovers we have the Berlin Jazz Festival held every year.

Some other cities that may interest you are Hamburg, the second largest city  for its ports.  Then you have Dresden, that although it may be sad to see the destruction of the war, it’s also a great way to field through time and find bits of the history that was once this great city structure.  You can also take a trip to Frankfurt and it’s commerce Mecca, Munich for its style and culture and maybe take a tour of the Porsche facility in Stuttgart to get an impression of this Automobile Giant’s historical roots. 

No matter where you go in Germany, you can enjoy beautiful Apartments, Villas, Lofts, Castles, and Private Mansions that are available for rental during your stay.  Reside in an accommodation on the water or a wonderful oceanfront home, but no matter where you go, you’ll always be welcome in Germany.
