Perched atop the hills of the Baixa neighborhood, the 3-bedroom Martim Moniz I holiday apartment presents a marvelous view of Lisbon. This quiet, lovely neighborhood is known for its historic buildings and museums. The apartment is within walking distance of Rossio Square (6 minutes), Restauradores Square (8 minutes) and Praca do Comercio Square (14 minutes), where you can visit famous monuments, museums, and boutiques. In the evening, walk over to the Bairro Alto district (17 minutes), the heart of Lisbon nightlife. The apartment is five minutes away from the Rossio and Restauradores metro stations, giving you easy access to the entire city.

Decorated by our local interior designer, fun surprises await you around every corner in this apartment. Clean lines, white walls, and huge picture windows give the space a sunlit feel. A fully equipped kitchen has a backdrop of Lisbon’s hilly views beyond, and plush bedrooms will make you feel at home. Premium amenities include free Wi-Fi, cable TV, hotel beds and linens, luxury toiletries, and a Nespresso machine.

Perfect for families or groups, this gorgeous retreat will be the home base for your next adventure.
