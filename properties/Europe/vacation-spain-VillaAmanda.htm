Villa Amanda, Alcudia, Mallorca, Spain Vacation Rental

 

Extraordinary luxury villa situated in the North of the island of Mallorca, between Alcudia and Pollensa bays, in a quiet residential area and just few meters away to two of the most beautiful white-sand and crystal-clear water beaches overlooking the bay of Alcudia. The luxury villa consists of a distinctly furnished and fully equipped main house and different outbuildings. This property can accommodate 20 guests, distributed between the main house and the service area. With a great extension of private land and surrounded by stunning gardens and lawns, this is the ideal setting to enjoy a fantastic holiday with friends and/or family. It has extraordinary terraces distributed in different areas, an outdoor dining area at the same level of the main house and another covered outdoor dining area with barbecue by the large saltwater swimming pool and jacuzzi. All with highly comfortable furniture, umbrellas and awnings.

The contemporary style of the interior design of the main house has been carefully chosen creating an atmosphere of great comfort and tranquillity. High quality materials have been used to dress all the spaces, with stone pavement for the day zone and the warmth of the oak flooring for the night zone, all combined with light colours and bespoke furniture for the different spaces. All bedrooms, in line with the rest of the house decoration, have spacious and modern en-suite bathrooms. The attic of the first floor could be ideal as a play area for the youngest. The master bedroom deserves a special mention because, in addition of its own living room, a dressing room and a bathroom with a bathtub and a shower, has the first floor private terrace with overlooking views to the sea and Formentor. Villa Amanda is definitively one of the most spectacular luxury villas that you will ever find on the island, which without a doubt will let you enjoy an unforgettable holiday with friends and or family.

Mallorca the “Mediterranean Lady”. The wonderful climate, beautiful landscapes as well as its cosmopolitan ambience make it a privileged holiday resort, with a cultural offer comparable to the one of many European capitals. Mallorca is the perfect location for a golf holiday or golf break due to the excellent golf courses available on the island. In addition to the other benefits Mallorca has more than 70 beaches, ideal for water sports or just for relax and sun bathing. Mallorca has stunning landscapes and magnificent cities, like Palma de Majorca with its impressive gothic cathedral. Palma offers a wide cultural choice, with new museums and guided walking tours and sightseeing, small enough to walk across in an hour, big enough and sophisticated enough to support Michelin-starred restaurants, boutiques and art galleries, a thriving musical scene that embraces jazz, folk, rock and classical, and festive activities enough to keep anyone busy all day, all year.
