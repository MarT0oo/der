Luxury Villa 26 Algarve Portugal


Villa 26 at Martinhal Beach REsort & Hotel enjoys an elevated position and commands a stunning panorama across Martinhal Bay.

Accommodation is arranged to take advantage of the privileged location, with generous living and dinning areas situated on the upper level that open onto a spacious balcony. The four large bedrooms are housed on the ground floor; two bedrooms are en-suite, with the other two bedrooms sharing a third bathroom. There is an additional guest cloakroom situated on the upper level. Zip and link beds are supplied to allow for a additional flexibility in terms of sleeping accommodation.

Amenities include a modern and fully fitted kitchen, laundry room and satellite TV and there is air conditioning throughout. The grounds are enhanced by a private 9m x4,5m swimming pool that is sunk into a large, landscaped garden. A fully removable pool fence makes Villa 26 especially suitable for families with young children.
