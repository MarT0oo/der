Parco Fiorito, Cortona, Italy


In an ancient sixteenth century convent, nestled on a green farm surrounded by woods, fields and pastures, just six kilometers from Cortona and at the center of the "Golden triangle of culture" created by Perugia Arezzo and Siena, the Parco Fiorito holiday farm, on the Umbria - Tuscany border, is the ideal solution for tourists who want to spend their vacation in a homey and authentic atmosphere enhanced by all the most modern comforts and services.

The Russo family welcomes guests to the farm and farmhouses that include the reception, breakfast room and hall where they can sample the farm's typical organic produce certified by AIAB (Italian Organic Agricultural Association).

Guests can enjoy the two pools, an exercise trail for outdoor sports fans and the Parco Fiorito's restaurant with a terrace overlooking Cortona.

On the Umbria - Tuscany border, in the heart of the verdant hills between Cortona and Lake Trasimeno, in the town of Piazzano, the Parco Fiorito holiday farm is the ideal destination for a vacation surrounded by nature near delightful cities and exciting archeological ruins.

Cortona is just 6 kilometers away, Tuoro sul Trasimeno 19, Arezzo 35, and Perugia, Montepulciano, Montalcino, Siena, Assisi, CittÃ  della Pieve and Pienza are all within 50 kilometers of Parco Fiorito. Outdoor lovers can take their pick of either trekking, mountain biking or horseback riding on the shores of Lake Trasimeno and Trasimeno Park trails.

Parco Fiorito is a delightful working farm where all products (meats, olive oil, wine, etc..) are home grown and raised. The Parco Fiorito is an organic farm! The Parco Fiorito offers three different Menu for those who wish to taste the recipes of the Chef Roberto Russo
