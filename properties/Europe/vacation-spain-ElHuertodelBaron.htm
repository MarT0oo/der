El Huerto del Baron, Marbella, Spain Vacation Rental

 

Experience a world of luxury at El Huerto Del Baron, a private and secluded home from home, just minutes from Marbella’s hub of clubs, restaurants and bars – a perfect blend of luxury and Andalucian tradition, charm and elegance.

This exclusive villa is located in the outstanding Marbella Hill area, just a 10 minute drive from the fashionable Marbella Club and close to Puerto Banus, the famed shopping centre and marina. It is quite simply, Andalucia’s finest.

Stylishly Designed for Comfort

Extensive, private and peaceful, El Huerto Del Baron is beautifully decorated throughout, focusing on a modern, yet classic Spanish style.

This villa can sleep up to ten guests, in five beautifully decorated bedrooms, each with elegant en-suite bathrooms and its own Bang & Olufsen entertainment system and Sky TV. The master suite has its own shared terrace for surveying the property – perfect for a private breakfast or pre-dinner cocktails.

The two air conditioned interior sitting rooms are wonderful spaces to escape from the midday heat, whilst not compromising on comfort. The interior dining room provides a easy alternative to the outdoor dining terrace – for breakfast, lunch or dinner.

The fully equipped gym and sauna are at your disposal – and if you don’t have the luxury of a complete break from reality, the extensive study area or the library are ideal for catching up on some emails in private. A traditional Spanish courtyard provides another space for sitting, entertaining or hiding away.

Entertain Your Guests, No Matter What the Occasion

The spacious interior and beautiful gardens make El Huerto perfect for entertaining – just ask our team, who will be happy to help you host any event you want, accommodating up to 150 guests.

Take in Breathtaking Andalucian Vistas

The substantial 3,750 square metres of landscaped garden look out onto an exceptional view of the Sierra Blanca mountain range on one side and the Mediterranean Sea on the other. It’s unbeatable.

Guests can sit in the shade of the fruit trees which give this villa its name ‘The Baron’s Orchard’, enjoying the cooling breeze blowing off the ocean. Alternatively, the magnificent pool is perfect for a relaxing dip, followed by some sunbathing, with a few cocktails from the bar.
