Gothic IV, Carrer d'en Serra, Barcelona, Spain Vacation Rental

 

Located in the laid-back, authentic neighborhood of Las Ramblas, the Gothic IV 3 bedroom apartment is the perfect place to experience Barcelona’s local culture. A short walk to the Plaza Catalunya, this area is best known for its bohemian vibe, local artist studios, multi-cultural restaurants, quaint cafes, trendy clubs and famous attractions. It’s easy to reach the Guell Palace (5 min), the History Museum of Catalonia (8 min), the Basilica de Santa Maria (10 min), the maritime museum (7 min), the zoo (15 min), the aquarium (8 min) and of course, the beach (8 min). The metro takes you all over the city, including to La Sagrada Familia (15 minutes), where you'll find many of Barcelona’s most famous museums, cathedrals and art galleries. The Drassanes metro stop is 6 minutes away on foot, and buses run around the corner.

Inspired by the warm Spanish sun, our local interior designers have carefully chosen a blend of soft pastels and warm wooden furnishings to accent the clean lines in this contemporary space. Large windows and high ceilings give the apartment an extra roomy vibe. Visitors will love cooking a spicy Spanish meal in the chic modern kitchen or relaxing in the cosy living room with a glass of local wine after a long day of exploring the area’s local attractions. Premium amenities include free WiFi, cable TV, high-end toiletries, hotel beds and linens.

Ideal for families or a group of friends, this lovely vacation apartment will make you feel right at home in one of Barcelona’s best locations.
