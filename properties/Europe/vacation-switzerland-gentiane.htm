Chalet Gentiane, Valais, Switzerland Vacation Rental


Chalet Gentiane benefits from an exceptional location at the heart of a hamlet of chalets built in old wood. Blessed with the maximum amount of sunshine, it faces the « Imperial Crown » of 4000m mountains and a panoramic view of the whole valley.  This property is located just a few minutes’ walk from the centre of the village and 1 km from the pistes. You can also take advantage of the free shuttle bus and leave your car in the garage.

Experience a memorable holiday in this recently completely and beautifully furnished chalet. Elegant and refined, the sumptuous interiors provide you with a truly luxurious property. The chalet is spread over four floors and accommodates up to 8 people. It consists of 4 bedrooms, of which two have en suite bathrooms. Three of the rooms have direct access onto the garden terrace; the fourth has its own large balcony.

You can enter the chalet from your private garage or through the entrance hall, on the same level as the first three bedrooms. At the heart of your residence, you will marvel at the superbly spacious double-height living and dining room which lead out onto a terrace with stunning panorama. There is also a separate snug with cable television.  The master bedroom with dressing room and en suite bathroom has a stylishly rustic feeling, positioned as it is, in the eaves of the chalet.

After a great day on the mountain, stretch out in front of the large fireplace and take the opportunity to relax with friends and family.
