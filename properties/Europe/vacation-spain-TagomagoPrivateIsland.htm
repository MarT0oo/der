Tagomago Private Island, Tagomago Island, Ibiza, Spain Vacation Rental

 

Enjoy both the nightlife and scenery of Ibiza and an intimate getaway at Tagomago Private Island. Just a short boat ride from the famed beaches and clubs, the island is a private paradise with beautiful rolling hills and panoramic sea views. But you won’t be alone—the property’s contemporary five-bedroom villa is fully staffed.

You can reach Tagomago by boat, docking at the private jetty, or by helicopter, arriving at the private landing area. You’ll be welcomed—and pampered—by 24-hour security, a water-sports captain, island manager, cook, kitchen staff and waiter. Spend your stay basking in the sun in the lounge area on the terrace by the saltwater pool, sampling local fare from the barbecue at the al-fresco dining area and watching the stars from the jetted tub on the deck. Keep up with your routine in the outdoor fitness area, or simply relax indoors by flipping on the sound system or browsing via the Wi-Fi.

The natural textures of the island’s low shrubs and rocky cliffs dropping to the sea are contrasted by the villa’s clean, contemporary lines. Fold-back glass doors hung with gauzy white drapes give the open-plan living areas a breezy, relaxed feel, not to mention beautiful sea views. Between a mod red sectional and fun white poofs, there’s ample seating in the living room, and the dining room can accommodate at least ten in whimsical brush-inspired chairs. The fully equipped kitchen continues the sleek vibe with white cabinets, black countertops and plenty of stainless steel.

Each of the five bedrooms at Tagomago has a queen bed, en-suite bathroom, air conditioning, TV and access to a terrace. The master bedroom has its own dressing area and walk-in closet, and three of the other bedrooms have sea views as well. As in the common areas, the bedrooms’ dark gray stone-tile floors, white walls, bedding and drapes and large windows frame panoramic views of the landscape.

From the house, it’s a 1-minute walk to the island’s dock, and from there, it’s just over a half-mile to Ibiza or 30 minutes to the Formentera beaches. Once you’ve landed on Ibiza, hit the beach at Platja des Figueral, 3 miles away, or Paraiso Beach, 7 miles away, or play a round at Club de Golf Ibiza, 12.5 miles away. Plan on a 21-mile drive from the port to Ibiza Airport.
