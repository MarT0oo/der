Villa Jacinta, Paguera, Mallorca, Spain Vacation Rental

 

Brand new villa in a residential area with walking distance to the village and beach. The modern villa has 6 bedrooms and 6 bathrooms, guest toilet and a laundry room. Open plan living area with super modern fully equipped kitchen with extra wine cooler.

The villa is spread over 2 floors. Each floor has a large terrace. The second floor has a large Tv lounge with flat screen. The main living room with large flat screen has also a dining area indoor and outdoor. The villa has air-conditioning and heating in all rooms and internet. The villa is fitted with marble and wooden floor of high quality.

The pool is surrounded by a further terrace with sun loungers and umbrellas, bbq and a covered area with lounge sofas.

Paguera is a lovely village with many shops, restaurants and a large beach for all watersport activities.
