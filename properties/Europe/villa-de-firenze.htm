Amore a Firenze, Londa, Tuscany, Italy Vacation Rental


This impressive and unforgettable 18th Century Villa is one of the most important structures in the area. It comfortably sleeps 14 people with 7 bedrooms and 7 baths. Beautifully tiled floors, fireplaces, antique furnishings, arched doorways, gracious suites, porcelain and stone bathrooms, vaulted ceilings, balconies and shuttered windows with views of the surrounding hills punctuate the spaces. Every room recalls the past yet is made comfortable by modern conveniences. Guests will feel very much at home here.

 

Come get your taste of La Dolce Vita at this breathtaking country mansion in Tuscany… the unforgettable Villa , owned by The Spagnoli Family for 200 years. This exclusive 18th century Florentine Villa sits just 35 minutes from Florence, and is a short drive or train ride away from many of Italy’s key destinations: Rome, Venice, Siena, Pisa, Arezzo, Lucca, Cinque Terre and San Gimignano.

 

Surrounded by terraced acres of gardens with a private swimming pool above the quaint lake town of Londa, this Luxury Villa is a dream setting for any family or group vacation.

 

Furnished throughout with exquisite antiques, the Villa comfortably accommodates up to 14 people. Every aspect of this spacious, 8,000 square foot country home speaks to its Tuscan pedigree. From its aged façade to its rustic angles to its sloping earthenware rooftops, Villa Luciano reflects the region’s traditions, architecture and culture.

 

Additionally: Telephone, Satellite TV, 5-speaker DVD home theatre system, Washer, dryer, dishwasher, Oversized refrigerator, Two modern kitchens, Wood-burning pizza oven in original working kitchen,  Balconies and patios, Ten acres of gardens and fruit tree groves .
