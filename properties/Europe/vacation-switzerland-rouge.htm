Chalet Rouge, Grimentz, Switzerland Vacation Rental


Chalet Rouge is one of several luxurious properties in the prestigious « Beauregard La Résidence », a collection of unique upmarket properties offering some contemporary zest to the traditional Swiss chalet. This property is just a few minutes’ walk from the village centre and direct access to the pistes and lift system.

You can take advantage of a free shuttle bus system and thus leave your own vehicle in the complex’s private car park. Recently completed, this duplex apartment combines natural and noble materials and provides the comfort for an elegant and unforgettable stay.


Its idyllic setting and stunning views will help you forget the daily grind and provide you with a weekly lodging in magnificent surroundings.


From your balcony you can discover the spectacular view over the picturesque village of Grimentz and its snowy peaks.
