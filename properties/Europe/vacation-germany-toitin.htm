Toitin Luxury Country Estate, Pomerania, Germany Vacation Rental


Toitin Luxury Country Estate is one of the oldest fiefdoms in Pomerania, its history dating back almost five hundred years.  In 2007, the estate was transformed into one of the most luxurious holiday houses in Germany. The manor house and a cottage are set among beautifully landscaped park and gardens with unobstructed views into the countryside.

The Manor House:
The 4000 ft² manor house breathes traditional elegance with high stucco ceilings, hand-crafted Prussian tiling and carefully selected antique furniture. At the same time, large windows, tone-in-tone decorating and a particular attention to textures give the property a contemporary, homely feel. All rooms have under floor, low-temperature heating, providing a particularly healthy, comfortable indoor climate in winter.

The ground floor boasts a grand hall, a library, a drawing room with a marble fire place, and a formal dining room with grand piano and a second fire place. The beautiful 450 ft² luxury kitchen with La Cornue stoves and an informal dining area should be of particular interest to the ambitious hobby chef. The historic brick wine cellar holds a careful selection of European and New World vintages and is open to guests.

The first floor accommodates five cozy, en-suite bedrooms. Bathrooms all feature tubs, rain showers, Czech & Speake fittings, wood and marble finishing. Satellite flat screen television, DVD, local telephone and wireless internet are provided.

Two large north and south facing terraces provide 180 degrees, unobstructed views into the endless Pomeranian countryside. A rose garden is reserved for the exclusive use of the manor house guests. The estate park has an eco-pool.

The Cottage:
The 2000 sq.ft. cottage matches its surrounding with a modern country house furnishing. Low, wooden beam ceiling, earth colored loam walls and a rustic Prussian terracotta floor give the house a unique, cozy atmosphere. All rooms have under floor, low-temperature heating, providing a particularly healthy, comfortable indoor climate in winter.

The ground floor holds an open plan luxury country kitchen, a dining area, and a lounge with an open fire place. There is one large, ground floor bedroom with a bespoke luxury country bathroom (disabled easy access). The cottage honesty bar holds a selection of wines and spirits.

The first floor accommodates two light and airy bedroom studios with bespoke bathrooms. Satellite flat screen television, DVD, local telephone and wireless internet are provided.

The cottage has a south facing terrace as well as a secluded herb and scent garden for the exclusive use of the cottage guests. The estate park has an eco-pool.
