Villa Star, Vale do Lobo, Algarve, Portugal Vacation Rental

 

A charming 2 bedroom apartment situated in the renowned Vale do Lobo resort, within walking distance from the beach and local amenities. This property comprises a spacious entrance with stairs leading up to an open plan, fully fitted and equipped kitchen with breakfast bar, a dining area and a lounge with a log fireplace and access to the exterior terrace which features an “al-fresco” dining area perfect to enjoy the hot summer evenings. This terrace also leads down to the swimming pool which is surrounded by terracing and space for the sun loungers, there you can also find the BBQ. The master bedroom is en-suite, includes built-in wardrobes and has access to the balcony. The second bedroom has a separate bathroom. This apartment is the perfect holiday getaway and ideally located!

The award winning resort of Vale do Lobo offers a wide range of holiday fun: golfing, laying on the beach, mini golf, horse riding, fantastic restaurants, tennis, water sports, night life, wellness centre... With a beautiful and diverse landscape this resort boasts golden beaches, clear waters and a Mediterranean climate. Members of the prestigious Vale do Lobo Club Resort
