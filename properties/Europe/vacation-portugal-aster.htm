Atlántico Aster Villa
6 Bedrooms | 6 Baths | Pool | Gym | Spa
Algarve
From €11,000 per week

This magnificent 6 bedroom villa is in an outstanding location with beautiful views to the Ria Formosa and the ocean.  The property has been furnished throughout to a very high standard. It is only a few minutes’ walk from the Quinta do Lago beach and water sports. The local restaurants, bars and shopping plaza are only minutes away (by car).

The property is on three levels and can comfortably accommodate 12 guests.  An interior elevator is provided on addition to the staircases. Air conditioning throughout and pool heating (supplement payable if required).


On the ground floor is a large entrance hall with cloakroom, a spacious sitting room leads out to the extensive terraces and pool area, a formal dining room and a large fully fitted kitchen with breakfast room.   Television Lounge with cable TV.  Two bedrooms, one double with adjacent bathroom and one twin, with en suite bathroom.

On the first floor is the extensive master bedroom suite, with bathroom, shower room, dressing room, TV and large terrace overlooking the Ria Formosa to the ocean.  Two double bedrooms each with en suite bathroom and both leading out to balconies.

On the lower ground floor is the Games Room, with snooker table. Gym and Sauna (for Sauna supplement £100/week payable if required), cinema room.  And a twin bedroom, suitable for teenagers or staff, with en suite shower room.

The Villa offers beautiful large terraces, private heated (£150/week payable if required) pool approximately, Gas BBQ and landscaped gardens.

Quinta do Lago is without a doubt one of the most exciting and exclusive resorts in Europe and understandably boasts some of the most spectacular and beautiful villas in the Algarve. Founded in 1972, this 800 acre residential estate borders miles of unspoiled golden beaches and the pristine Atlantic Ocean, undulating pine forests and some of the best golf courses in the world.

Quinta do Lago is a major destination for golf enthusiasts. Two thousand acres of land has been transformed into a resort dedicated to a golfer's fantasy lifestyle, with villas spaced strategically apart, all the comforts of a metropolitan centre and championship and non-championship courses for any golf aficionado. Of course, aside from golf, the resort has other attractions, such as horseback riding, tennis, two busy shopping malls, two miles of golden beaches, a fine selection of restaurants and nightclubs, and three thousand hours of sunny blue skies each year.

Quinta do Lago is a very appealing summer holiday destination because of its easy access to the rest of Europe. Only twenty minutes drive from Faro Airport, Quinta do Lago is only two hours away from both Lisbon and Seville, Spain.
