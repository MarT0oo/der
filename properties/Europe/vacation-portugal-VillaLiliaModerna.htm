Villa Lilia Moderna, Quinta do Lago, Algarve, Portugal Vacation Rental

 

Set in a luxury gated community Villa Lilia Moderna is a stunning and contemporary villa, equipped to a high standard, which offers a spacious and luxurious setting. Located just five minutes from the beach and golf courses of Quinta do Lago.

Very spacious area, split level, all open plan on the ground floor level. The large glass sliding doors can be opened to the pool to create a feeling f indoor/ outdoor living.

1st Floor: 2 double bedrooms. Bedroom 1: is a double room with a separate dressing area, en suite shower room and wall mounted flat screen TV. Bedroom 2: is a double room with a separate dressing area, en suite shower room, wall mounted flat screen TV and doors to a terrace overlooking the pool.

2nd Floor: Master bedroom and bedroom 4: Master bedroom: is a double room with a large dressing room, flat screen TV, ultra contemporary en suite bathroom with bath and separate shower unit and doors to the excellent main terrace overlooking the pool, furnished with comfy seating. Bedroom 4: Is a double room with a wall mounted flat screen TV, separate dressing area, en suite shower room and doors to the main terrace. Stairs lead to the 3rd floor of the property, the roof terrace and Jacuzzi.

The basement is finished in the same quality as the house itself. There is a glass wall giving light from the swimming pool. A cinema room with blu ray DVD player, steam room and shower, laundry room with washing machine and tumble dryer and a garage.

The interior is spacious, open plan and modern, exuding subtle and sophisticated elegance. The spacious sitting room offer confront and elegance, the expansive dining room table seats 8 with similar capacity for outdoor dining on the terrace, and the four contemporary bedrooms all have bathrooms en suite and open out onto patios, terraces and the pool. The lower level of the villa also has a large games room with a spectacular pool window brings in light, a home cinema room, spa area with steam room and garage. The furnishings throughout are of the highest quality and standard. The roof terrace has a Jacuzzi and the terraces offer great views to the surrounding hills.

Quinta Verde sits within the area known as the “Golden Triangle”. Located in the central Algarve and only twenty minutes drive from Faro International airport. The Triangle incorporates the exclusive developments of Quinta do Lago and Vale do Lobo together with the soon to be built 6-star Conrad Hotel. The area is renowned for its manicured golf courses, sophisticated cuisine, stretches of golden beaches and a variety of entertainment facilities and is a haven offering all the ingredients to enjoy a truly luxurious lifestyle.
