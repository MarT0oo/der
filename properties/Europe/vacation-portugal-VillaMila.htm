Villa Mila, Algarve, Portugal Vacation Rental

 

Inserted in a plot of 4000 square meters this villa is the synonym of peace and tranquility with its manicured gardens and living spaces that surround the villa. It is the ideal place to relax and enjoy the Algarve.

The double entrance doors open to a large hall which is the heart of the house, where it is divided in three different areas.

On the left is located the living room, kitchen, dining room, an office and a toilet. On the right are 3 en-suite double bedrooms with access to the garden and the magnificent master double en-suite bedroom with a full bathroom with basin for him and her, walking in shower and a round bath that is perfect to spend moments for two, this bedroom also has an office and access to the garden.

Still in the entrance hall are the stairs that lead to the lower level of the Villa, where is located another living room with TV, garage, two double bedrooms sharing a bathroom with shower and a small kitchen. The best part of this level is the Spa with a sauna, a relaxation area and a plunge pool with cold water.

The open kitchen is fully equipped and is combined with a breakfast area from where you can access the outdoor dining area with gas BBQ. The living room is also connected to the kitchen and is divided into three living areas, giving place to the whole family, this large living room with large sliding windows gives direct access to the pool area.

The exterior of Villa Mila is the perfect combination of all the qualities it has inside.

A large pool, two dining areas perfect for dining in the fresh air, and a game of basketball for everyone to have fun.

Villa Mila is the sublime villa to enjoy your holiday.
