Casa Ordinga, Punta Prima, Spain Vacation Rental

 

Casa Odinga is a delightful private property located in the area of Punta Prima, a few steps away from Es Pujols yet far enough to grant a good level of privacy. The privileged position allows easy access both to the sea and to the nearby town with its picturesque boardwalk full of colourful stands that light up at night and are a popular hang-out spot for the island connoisseurs.

The property is completely fenced and immersed in a blossoming garden where the free-form private pool finds space just a few steps away from the main house. An enticing outdoor lounging area with cozy summer couches and the outdoor dining area enjoy the shade of the pergola accessible from the indoor spaces while a second outdoor area with BBQ is located closer to the swimming pool.

The interior decoration of 9612 is sober and elegant - different colors distinguish the various spaces with unique furnishings setting each bedroom apart from the other. Modern amenities were installed throughout the property providing comfort to its layout on a single floor, particularly appealing for families with children.

This property is indeed a hidden gem of Formentera, it boasts a unique mix of quality and unique location that allow guests to enjoy their stay on the island, either surrounded by nature at home or by strolling through the bustling Es Pujols.
