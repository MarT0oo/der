Dunas Douradas, Dunas Douradas, Algarve, Portugal Vacation Rental

 

This charming 4 bedroom villa is located in the private and secure Dunas Douradas development within walking distance to the golden beaches and short driving distance to the renowned golf courses of the area. This property comprises of an open plan fully equipped kitchen with a breakfast bar, a dining area and a spacious lounge featuring a wood burning fireplace, Cable TV and DVD Player and has access to the exterior terrace.

There are 3 bedrooms located on the ground floor, two of which are en-suite. The master bedroom suite is located on the first floor and features a spacious bathroom with His and Hers sinks, a walk-in wardrobe and has access to a private terrace boasting views across the garden and development. The exterior provides a beautifully landscaped garden with enough space for the sun loungers, a large heated swimming pool, a BBQ and dining area perfect to enjoy the hot summer evenings. This property provides air conditioning and Wi-Fi throughout.
