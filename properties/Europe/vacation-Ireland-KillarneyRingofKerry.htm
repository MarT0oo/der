Killarney Ring of Kerry, Killarney, Co. Kerry, Ireland Vacation Rental

 

This seven bedroomed detached property is located in the much sought after scenic area of Fossa on the main N72 Ring of Kerry road just minutes drive from the thriving tourist town of Killarney.

The area is known for its fishing amenities, cycling routes, equestrian centres and its three Championship Golf Courses which undeniably boast some of the best golfing facilities in the world.

Killarney Ring of Kerry is privately owned and has been built to the highest of specifications. It has been architecturally designed with large landscaped gardens and offers magnificent views of the lakes and mountains from the front. The property is approached through two stone pillars and a long gravelled driveway. A large decking and patio area offers the perfect retreat to enjoy the beauty and tranquility of your surroundings. There are 7 en-suite bedrooms that sleep 17 people, five of which have mountain views.

THE ACCOMMODATION
The inside of the property has a superior finish throughout with the highest quality of fixtures and fittings. The entrance porch has a marbled tiled floor. The dining room has lovely double doors leading out onto the patio area. The lounge has a log effect modern electric fire, TV with DVD player. The large sitting room has a limestone fireplace housing a log effect modern electric fire. There is a downstairs bathroom with electric shower. The utility area is fitted with a washing machine, a tumble dryer, iron and ironing board.

There are two stand-alone showers in the en-suites and five within bath tubs. There is also a stand-alone shower downstairs. In all, Fossa Lodge can accommodate up to 17 people very comfortably
