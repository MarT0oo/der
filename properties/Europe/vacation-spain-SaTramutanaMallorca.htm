Sa Tramutana, Deia, Mallorca, Spain Vacation Rental

 

Sa Tramutana, named for the stunning mountains that oversee the house. You'll find the Sa Tramutana at the end of the winding road from Deia to Son Ripoll with electric security gate access, on Mallorcas spectacular northwest coast, this three bedroom house forms part of an ample villa set in vast private grounds with a backdrop of the Sa Tramuntana Mountains.

The property is perfect for those that enjoy the outdoors, and are looking for a real escape. Around the house are some beautifully maintained gardens, orchards of olives, mandarins and grenadines and the aroma of bougainvillea. Guests can choose to dine either on the terrace by the house or by the swimming pool. The pool is found on a large upper terrace and offers breathtaking views of the sea and the mountains that cradle this region of Mallorca. A little removed from the pool, along a stone path is a floodlit tennis court.

The house itself is inviting and informal, and is distributed over two floors. The master bedroom, situated on the upper floor, has a traditional Majorcan double bed and offers panoramic views from its French doors and adjoining terrace. The main living area of the house is on the lower floor and includes living room, kitchen and a dining room. A further two double bedrooms are located on this floor.

The pool area is well equipped with 5 sun loungers, outdoor shower, iron and stone dining table for 8 people, barbecue terrace with full kitchen facilities including oven, hob and fridge under veranda. An indoor shower and changing block with a fridge half way between the pool and the hard-court floodlit tennis court. Natural countryside with hill and mountainside walks, surrounded by mandarin and grenadine orchards, well-kept gardens, waterfall features and olive groves.
