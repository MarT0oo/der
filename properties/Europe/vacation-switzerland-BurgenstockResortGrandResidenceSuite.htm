Bürgenstock Resort Grand Residence Suite, Lake Lucerne, Switzerland Vacation Rental

 

The opulent Grand Residence Suites invite you to enjoy a splendid getaway in the Swiss Alps.

Your suite at a glance

The imposing architecture of the former Grand Hotel, dating back to 1873, is matched with stylish interior décor. The spacious 276 m2 (2,970 sq ft)Residence Suite has four bedrooms and a room for staff, and provides a view of Lake Lucerne and the idyllic hills and mountains. Two of the bedrooms and staff room are with en-suite bathrooms. Precious materials such as marble, fine saddle leather, floors of smoked oakwood and works of art by aspiring international artists add to the modern and relaxed setting.

A living room with a cozy fireplace, a dining room with a Juliet balcony and lake view, a large fully equipped kitchen, a multimedia room and seven private balconies, three of which have a lake view and four a sunny mountain view provide room for relaxation in a private and luxurious setting for up to nine guests.

Located at Switzerland’s top luxury resort

The Panorama Residence Suites are integrated in the award winning Bürgenstock Resort Lake Lucerne. By staying at the residence suites you can enjoy the comfort of the luxury resort with its outstanding culinary offer, the 10’000 m² Alpine Spa, a Health & Medical Excellence centre, housekeeping, concierge service and countless leisure activities.

Wellness & Wellbeing

Enjoy access to the Bürgenstock’s 10,000m² glass-clad Alpine Spa. Facilities include 13 treatment rooms, three private spa suites, wet areas with hammam, sauna, whirlpools and hydrotherapy, a 24-hour fitness center and a Kids Club. Enjoy the views from the dramatic 1,383-foot-long outdoor infinity pool that wraps around two sides of the spa building.

Crisp Alpine air offers the perfect setting for outdoor pursuits, including 70km of hiking and biking trails, three Davis Cup standard tennis courts, a nine-hole golf course, curling and ice-skating. A private railway whisks guests to the glistening shores of Lake Lucerne. Alternatively discover the Hammetschwand Lift, Europe’s highest open-air lift built in 1905 or watch a new release film at Bürgenstock’s private cinema.

World-class dining

Dining in one of ten restaurants and bars may be arranged by the Residence Concierge to sample Michelin star French fine dining at RitzCoffier, authentic Asian at Spices Kitchen & Terrace or Persian dishes at Sharq Oriental. Alternatively, relish in world-class cuisine from the comfort of home thanks to in-residence dining.

Lake Lucerne Area

Guests can use the Bürgenstock Shuttle Boat as well as a funicular connecting the Resort with the charming city of Lucerne with the historic center ant the picturesque wooden bridges free of charge during the stay. The lake and nearby mountains offer countless possibilities for excursions, whilst the city is also known for its art, culture and festivals.
