La Norjeanne, Verbier, Switzerland Vacation Rental

 

The chalet is staffed by a private chef and two hosts. Prices include breakfast, afternoon tea, four-course dinner with complementary wine, beer and soft drinks. Ski guiding and childcare can also be provided free of charge.

With 6 bedrooms and 6 bathrooms, the chalet is a great place for large groups or families with children as there is plenty of space. All 6 rooms can be made up as doubles or twins and the chalet can accommodate 12-14 guests in comfort.

There's a large living area with an open fireplace, plus a separate TV/playroom where you can watch movies or play on the Nintendo Wii. The spa room with sauna and steam-room, and large outdoor Jacuzzi are always popular too.

Our service includes full English or continental breakfast, afternoon tea, four-course evening meal with unlimited complementary wine beer and soft drinks.

If you are travelling with young children we can serve them a menu adapted to their tastes before the main dinner service.

We only run one chalet & like to offer our guests a very personal, bespoke service. With a 24 hour concierge service, we can help with arranging transfers, booking ski school, ski hire, lift passes, lunchtime restaurant tables, childcare, massage and nightclub tables, plus any other additional services you might need. If you have any questions we’d be delighted to offer any help we can in organising your trip.
