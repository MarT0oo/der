St. Tropez Jewel, Plan de La Tour, France Vacation Rental

 

The perfect destination for family and friends. Ideally suited for guests seeking luxury, tranquility and privacy only minutes from St Tropez, numerous beaches, restaurants and charming villages. This villa is furnished to an exceptionally high standard offering the perfect blend of true Provencale living with all modern requirements. There is a large private heated pool which has an additional shallow area for babies and young children.

The pool is bordered by lawns, a running fountain and shaded terraces for relaxation with family and friends. The property is set in 5 acres (20,000sq.m) of private gated land where you will find many olive and fruit trees, lavender plants and well manicured mature gardens. Of particular note is the large terrace that extends across the front of the whole property offering various seating and sun worshipping spots as well as a great outdoor banquet table seating up to 20 guests with amazing views and an adjacent summer kitchen with BBQ.

Outside There are numerous terraces attached to the property offering both privacy and stunning vistas to accompany the beautifully landscaped gardens. The property also boasts a heated swimming pool 6m x 7m, many pool toys and inflatables and a Court for playing Boule. 12 sun loungers with cushions that can be placed around the pool or on the terraces. There are also 2 day-beds, one of which is situated under the Mulberry tree covered terrace along with numerous other outdoor seats and recliners. In fact, everywhere you look around the extensive grounds of 5 acres (20,000sq.m) there are seating areas, private terraces and sun-traps.

Coast/Beach There are numerous beaches all within easy reach. The famous Club 55 at Pampalonne beach is a 25 minute drive, the private beaches at La Nartelle and Saint Maxime are a 10-15 minute drive and Port Grimaud is also a 15 minute drive. Boats can be easily rented within a 10-15 minute drive, along with numerous other water sport activities. Day trips by boat to Cannes or the local islands also go from Saint Maxime bay, 10-15 minutes from the villa. Please ask for more information.

Other: There is an outdoor covered terrace which opens out from the kitchen area. It is equipped with a gas BBQ and sink. There is a custom made oak banquet table which runs the length of the terrace and can seat up to 20 guests.

Maid service: Towels and linen provided. All bed linen is in high-count Egyptian cotton and the towels come in 3 sizes. Separate pool towels included. Cleaning on Saturdays included plus a weekly linen change. Maid service can be arranged on extra days upon request.


Notes on accommodation The property is ideal for families with children or groups. There is central heating throughout and air-conditioning in 3 bedrooms, room fans in the other 3 bedrooms. The pool is alarmed.
