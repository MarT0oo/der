Drimnamore House, Sneem, Co. Kerry, Ireland Vacation Rental

 

Based around the structure of an original stone cottage, this home has been lovingly restored from top to bottom and is now an incredible mix of old and new located on the Wild Atlantic Way.

This wonderful home has been architecturally designed for maximum impact on a private site of 2 acres. The moment you approach this stunning home you will be intrigued and will want to look inside. Recently landscaped and refurbished this holiday homes has been architecturally designed to combine a stone cottage from the 1900's and an enlarged simulated barn living structure surrounded by acres of gardens and newly planted trees.

This is a large spacious and very impressive house. Everything about it is tasteful and has been carefully designed and selected to ensure maximum enjoyment and comfort during your stay. When you enter the main front door there is a cosy sitting room area closed off from the rest of the house with a sliding door. This is the perfect place for taking time out in the evenings to cosy up in front of the fire with a good book or a movie.

One of the bedrooms is downstairs and it has its own ensuite shower room.

The rest of the ground floor is made up of a large open plan living room cum dining room cum kitchen area - the perfect space to enjoy your holiday. This area opens out onto the outside patio areas almost bringing the "outside" "inside" and creating a bright, airy, spacious, cosy and comfortable feel to the house. The kitchen is bright and modern and has been equipped with every appliance you could possibly need to cater for everyone during your stay.

The remaining three bedrooms are located upstairs - all bedrooms have double beds in them however there are beds and space to accommodate up to 12 people in this house as there are two double sofa beds in the "den" area upstairs.

Drimnamore House is located 1 mile from the village of Sneem which has lots of pubs, places to eat, small shops and tourist attractions. It is also just 1.5 miles from Parknasilla Resort & Spa. The house is located exactly half way along the road to Oysterbed Pier which is just one mile long where yachts and sail boats can be anchored.

The closest sandy beach for swimming is Casey's Cove and it is just a 15 minute drive away. The famous Ring of Kerry town of Kenmare is 16 miles away and it is 28 miles to Killarney.

Waterville Golf Club is 15 miles from Drimnamore House.
