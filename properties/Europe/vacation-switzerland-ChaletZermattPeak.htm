Chalet Zermatt Peak, Verbier, Switzerland Vacation Rental

 

Chalet Zermatt Peak is a landmark chalet in one of the most desirable locations in the world. Set into the side of the mountain, it offers unrivalled panoramic views across the village and to the Matterhorn. No expense has been spared in creating Zermatt’s ultimate and most luxurious chalet.

Chalet Zermatt Peak occupies a unique position within the village. Centrally located so only a few minutes walk from the bars, restaurants and shops, yet perched up high on the side of the mountain, offering unrivalled views coupled with complete privacy and tranquility.

The panoramic views offered by the floor-to-ceiling windows blur the lines between indoors and outdoors. Guests can feel themselves as part of the landscape rather than just a bystander to it. The mountainside is close enough to touch.

Watch the sunrise on the opposite side of the valley and the sunset behind the glorious Matterhorn from the comfort of the sofa, or in the fresh, clean air from the balcony. From the chalet, take the complimentary taxi to either ski-lift, which is within 5 minutes, or stroll into the village to take in its charm and atmosphere. Hot Gluewein and roasted chestnuts mingle in the crisp winter air, and the jingle of bells from the horse and carriages add to the sense of a winter fairytale.

During summer, enjoy a promenade along the streets, discovering the boutiques and stadels of Olde Zermatt, and take time to sit and drink in the beautiful alps around you.

5 individually designed bedroom suites, all offering deluxe double beds, seating and dressing areas and spectacular panoramic views

All rooms have HD cinema TV’s with digital surround sound, remote controlled lighting, curtains and audio-visual, and balconies or terraces. The bathrooms are equipped with hairdryers and straighteners, as well as beauty products and skincare from several luxury Swiss brands, and are stocked with designer robes and towels. All showers have overhead rain-shower fittings, as well as handshowers and body jets to refresh and rejuvenate. Our master suite also offers a private Jacuzzi
