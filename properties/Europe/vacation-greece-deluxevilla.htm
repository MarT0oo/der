Deluxe Villa, Mykonos, Greece Vacation Rental


A lovely and spacious vacation home for the perfect Mykonos holiday. This villa is fully equipped with the highest quality furniture, appliances, mattresses and linens and is walking distance to Panormos beach. It also has a large patio for entertaining or lounging with uninterrupted views of the bay and surrounding hillsides. There is a tastefully decorated living and dining room, full kitchen as well as 3 bedrooms and 3 baths.

On the world renown island of Mykonos within a gated community overlooking stunning Panormos Bay our eleven villas are walking distance to the sea and have spectacular views of the bay, the beach and the untamed hillsides.

Situated on 55,000 meters the villas have been designed to integrate understated elegance and privacy with the leisurely island lifestyle and vibrant nightlife that has made Mykonos a world class destination.

Spend the day or evening swimming and lounging with the in-crowd at trendy Panormos with its bohemian beach bar and restaurant or experience the low key relaxation of secluded Agios Sostis beach and it's charmingly authentic Greek taverna.

The distinctive combination of natural beauty, location, privacy and luxury of these unique villas is what brings our guests back year after year.
