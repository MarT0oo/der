Villa Terracotta, Deia, Spain Vacation Rental

 

The Islands of Spain are a gathering place of those who love to experience the best views, wonderful music, the sounds of the ocean and wonderful outdoor experiences. Shop in the Sineu market, one of the oldest in Mallorca where you can shop for fresh fruit and vegetables, eggs and fish. You will find the stores bustling with the sounds of people enjoying the open market and all it has to offer. A truly family friendly place to stay.

The Villa Terracotta is one of the top villas on the island offering a wonderful view of the island from each corner. Go outside and visit the natural setting, hike up the mountain and take a stroll into the past. Find some special rocks as you walk and explore the vast hilltops and create your own memories at Villa Terracotta.

Traditionally stone built Mallorcan House – idyllic location – with fantastic sea views, its own garden and private swimming pool. The house has the traditional Mallorcan high ceilings, wooden beams and terracotta floors, is furnished to a very high standard and decorated with artwork from local artists. Only 5 minutes walk from Deia town and 20 minutes walk from beautiful Cala Deia beach.

House has Terraces and garden area . Pool and Parking . BBQ.

Please note that for the comfort of all our guests, the house is a non-smoking house, and pets are not allowed. Pool and garden maintenance is included. Wireless broadband connection. Outside: The house has a private large swimming pool (13×4 mtr) with lovely sea and mountain views equipped with wooden sun loungers up to 8 people. Planted gardens, dining terrace to the front of the house, BBQ and dining area near the pool. Private parking on the property. Sunbathing terrace near the pool area. The perfect place to relax!

Utility Room: Washing machine, separate dryer, iron and ironing board. Cleaning / Towels / Linen / Maid service: The rates include weekly cleaning, towels, change of bed linen and welcome pack on arrival. Swimming pool towels are also provided. Additional maid service and baby-sitting may be arranged by prior request, paid for locally. Please advise when booking. Other: BBQ and large wooden dining table to accommodate up to 8 people near the pool area.

The House is in a residencial area 10 min walk to Deia center .
