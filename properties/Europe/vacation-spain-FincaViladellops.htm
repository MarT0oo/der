Finca Viladellops, Sitges, Spain Vacation Rental

 

Dreamexoticrentals welcomes Viladellops Catalan Villa & Running Winery into it's Portfolio. A lovely Finca in the middle of the Vineyards, Finca Viladellops. Viladellops is a private little town where you can find a cellar, a thirteen century chapel, and visit the most important Private National Archives of Catalonia, owned by Desvalls Family. The house is located 1 minute walking to these 3 places. By car is only 5 min. from Sitges, 25 from Barcelona airport and 40 from Barcelona City. The location is very close to the bucolic sandy beaches of Sitges, el Garraf, and Castelldefels.

Description of the house:
The grounds at the villa have an infinity swimming pool and a garden with seating porches. The furnishing is contemporary and the all villa is decorated to the last detail without losing the traditional character.

The house has a very rustic and charming feeling to it, the owners do not believe in taking away from the character of the Finca. You will find some every day amenities like Wi-Fi and modern day kitchen, however there is no local phone number so you will have to use your cell phones while at the house. We recommend that you try an application like Whats Ap, so that you can use the Wi-Fi from your phone to accept calls at a lower price. A total of 6 double bedrooms, 5 in the main house featured en suite bathrooms, and 1 in the cottage with a bathroom.

Ask about our wines and one of our staff will be happy to setup a tour.
