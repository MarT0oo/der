Villa Azorea, Sao Miguel, Azores, Portugal Vacation Rental

 

Villa Azorea an amazing vacation rental in the Azores Portugal, where life stands still and the beauty speaks to you in moments of silence and pure bliss. This is a place that offers an elegant and authentic holiday experience on the sublime island of Sao Miguel in the Azores, "the Hawaii of the Atlantic".

Facing the ocean, Villa Azorea is designed to allow true interaction with nature, while offering a design and relaxing living environment just 10 minutes from the capital of the Azores: Ponta Delgada and 15 km from the international airport where you can rent your car. Sao Miguel is the main island of the archipelago, it’s here that live 50% of the Azoreans (140,000 people). Directly connected to New York JFK, Boston, Toronto, London, Frankfurt, Amsterdam, Brussels, and Lisbon (up to 8 daily flights), it’s the island that we visit first in the Azores.

Sao Miguel is a huge lush garden of 740 km2 where Iceland would have mixed with New Zealand. Wherever one goes, the roads are adorned with massive hydrangeas, exotic plants and impressive ferns. Japanese cedars cover entire valleys, wrapped in Hedychium, a kind of wild ginger from the Himalayas, colonizing the entire island. There are also tea plantations (unique in Europe), fields of bananas, pineapples or even fruit trees such as passion fruit, grown for its passion fruit.

Sao Miguel is also a gourmet island, renowned for its many cheeses, fresh fish and excellent beef. Trekking, canyoning, paragliding, kitsurf, sea trips with dolphins and whales, and also golf (with the 2 most beautiful courses in Portugal), are all activities that make this green island with a mild ocean climate, a real small ecological paradise, far away from the stress and pollution of the major continental cities.
