Skipping Stones, Dunas Douradas, Algarve, Portugal Vacation Rental

 

This beautiful 4 bedroom villa is situated in Dunas Douradas, a highly regarded family resort, it is within a 3 minute walk to the beach and a short distance from local amenities and the renowned golf courses of the Vale do Lobo and Quinta do Lago resorts.

The villa comprises of a fully fitted and equipped kitchen, a spacious lounge accessing the exterior terrace and an open plan dining area that seats up to 8 people. Also on the ground floor is the laundry room which includes a washing machine and dryer and 2 bedrooms both of which are en-suite and have access to the pool terraces. The remaining 2 bedrooms are located on the first floor, the master bedroom is en-suite and features its own private terrace with sea view and the guest master suite also features a private terrace. The exterior is ideal for embracing the true Algarvean lifestyle.

It includes a large heated swimming pool, plenty of space for the sun loungers, beautifully landscaped gardens, 4 different seating areas with shading and a BBQ perfect for "al-fresco” evenings. Sound system, air conditioning and high speed Wi-Fi are provided throughout.
