Chalet Razzie, Courchevel, France Vacation Villa Rental


Courchevel 1850 is one of the most famous ski resorts in the world which combines elegance with luxury. Situated at the highest residential point in the exclusive resort of Courchevel 1850, Chalet Razzie offers superb alpine accommodation. It is nestled just behind the Pralong chair lift, close to Belcote lifts, offers easy access to the slopes yet the desired privacy and exclusivity.

The sumptuous contemporary interiors are second to none and provide the ultimate in comfort and style. The infusion of the Savoir touch completes the sensational elegance and ambiance of the chalet. Designed to accommodate 10 people, all 5 bedrooms have luxury en-suite bathrooms, plasma televisions and terraces offering awe-inspiring views across the snow-capped region.

The south facing terraces offer an idyllic 'sun trap', for sunbathing, 'après ski' relaxation, reading and pre dining drinks. Dining facilities are superb and include a 'state of the art' kitchen and dining room. For in-chalet 'après ski' entertainment, there is a large HD TV, an extensive selection of DVD's and games to create a fun filled atmosphere.

 

The Chalet makes for the perfect mountain escape, both for those looking for a ski adventure or those simply wanting a relaxing break. The location also offers access to all grades of ski runs, allowing for all ages and abilities. There is a chalet manager available throughout your stay at your disposal to help make your holiday more enjoyable.
