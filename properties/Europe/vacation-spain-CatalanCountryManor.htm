Catalan Country Manor, Sitges, Catalonia, Spain Vacation Rental

 

If you are looking for an outstanding location in Sitges or near Barcelona, Catalan Country Manor will fulfill all your requirements. This amazing 400 m2 villa is luxuriously furnished, combining the charm of an ancient masia (Catalan country manor) with a fresh contemporary style.
