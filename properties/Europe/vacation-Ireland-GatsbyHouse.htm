Details on the area surrounding the property/accommodation

Gatsby House is located in the heritage town of Ardara in County Donegal, the most northwesterly county in Ireland. Gatsby House is set in the heart of the heritage town of Ardara, Co.Donegal. The town has bars, restaurants and shops all within walking distance, including the iconic Nancy's Bar, a must for any visitor to Co.Donegal. Live Irish music can often be heard at the Corner Bar and there are three supermarkets within walking distance. The Nesbitt Arms Hotel has a popular Bistro and there is fine dining available at the Woodhill House, just outside Ardara.

Getting there & away/local amenities

Getting There: There are regular flights to City of Derry Airport with Ryanair from London Stansted, Luton, East Midlands, Liverpool and Glasgow Prestwick. Gatsby House is a 1hr 30 min drive from the City of Derry Airport via Letterkenny, Donegal Town and then over to Killybegs on the N56. There are also flights from Glasgow Prestwick and Dublin to Donegal Airport with Aer Arann at Gweedore which is a 50 minute drive away from Gatsby House.
