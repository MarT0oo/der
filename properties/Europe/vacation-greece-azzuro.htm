Interior

The interior is cozy and decorated with pristine, and tastefully appointed, comfortable furniture and artwork. The large windows, allow plenty of light to come in every room and offer a unique view no matter in which room of the Villas you are. On the ground floor are located a fully equipped kitchen, dining room, and a spacious living room with a couch for 8 persons and fireplace. The bedrooms have comfortable queen beds, with the option to change into two single beds in two of the rooms. All the bedrooms have en-suite bathrooms with shower panels. Rooms are comfortably spacious and tastefully furnished in a bright, minimalist style, incorporating white walls and ceilings with local rock, traditional features of Cycladic architecture, making this Villa a top choice for those who seek the best from traditional and modern design.

Azzuro Villa (4 bedroom,  4 bathroom)

The first level has its own separate entrance and therefore it is easier for people with different tastes of entertainment to spend their holidays together without bothering each other. It is also easier for families to enjoy private moments without losing the advantage of living all together.
