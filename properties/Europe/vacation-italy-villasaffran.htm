Villa Saffran
3 Bedrooms | 3 Bathrooms                                                                          
MONTEPULCIANO (VAL D’ORCIA), Siena, Italy
  From €3455 to €5805 per week

Villa Saffran, with its 7 hectares of land, used to belong to the nearby village parish, Castiglioncello sul Trinoro. On the hill behind the house, an old chestnut grove produces the best chestnuts of the region, and the woods offer plenty of mushrooms. Terraced orchards and fields slope down towards the old road. Cars are parked below the house, but easy car access to the house itself is provided for unloading the baggage.


The house has been decorated with the original colors of the Sienese farms: pale yellows, pinks and blues, with borders in a burnt terracotta color, terra di Siena. The furniture is local, simple and attractive, with a few choice antiques.

Meals can be taken in the large country kitchen, but on warmer days you might prefer the loggia whit its barbecue and traditional wood-burning bread oven, or again you might wish to sit under the pergola along the front of the house, looking over the valley.
