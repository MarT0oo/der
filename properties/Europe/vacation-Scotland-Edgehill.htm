Edgehill, East Lothian, Scotland Vacation Rental

 

Edgehill is perfect exclusive-use property to host golfing parties, corporate outings, birthday parties or simply an excuse to spend time with friends and family. You will have an experience to remember at Edgehill.

Its location close to Edinburgh makes it ideal for bringing people together in particular for golfing parties. East Lothian is one of Scotland’s best regions for golf with 21 courses catering for golfers of all standards and tastes. Some courses you will recognise – Dunbar, North Berwick, Musselburgh, Gullane and The Open Championship course at Muirfield. A whole variety of courses to choose from whether traditional links, classic inland golf courses, courses steeped in history and modern to challenging courses. Come and discover them for yourself.

Edgehill is new to the exclusive use market and has an exceptional location overlooking the Gullane golf courses and is located within its own private gardens. With flexible space for corporate events whether board meetings or private dining, we pride ourselves on designing and delivering an exceptional and bespoke service for each guest.

Spend the night in one of our 7 luxurious bedrooms. All rooms are individually styled, spacious and have breath-taking views. Guests can then relax in one of our many rooms from the Lounge, TV Lounge, Dining Room and Dining Kitchen. For further entertainment during your stay we offer a cinema room, gym and indoor heated swimming pool.

At Edgehill we tailor each proposal to your individual requirements and we offer self-catering or we can arrange full or partial staff including private chef, butler and housekeeping. We can also assist with transport arrangements and full itineraries for you and your guests
