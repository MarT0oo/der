Vila Moura, Boliqueime, Portugal Vacation Rental

 

This stunning 3+1 bedroom villa offers the best of both worlds; a retreat environment in a spectacular garden oasis, yet only a 10 minute drive from the vast variety of restaurants, golf courses and entertainment of Vilamoura and Pine Cliffs 5 Star Resort. Only a 25 minute drive from the airport, Vale Do Lobo and Quinta Do Lago, Vila Moura is utterly idyllic and boasts a huge open plan living room, with a spacious dining area. The kitchen is newly installed and well equipped, and all of the bedrooms are also spacious and each have their own bathrooms. The villa is contemporary in style but with a beautiful authentic Portuguese flair, and is single storey, making it safe for children or older guests. The house benefits from an enormous terrace which winds its way around the house, offering a number of places to enjoy a glass of wine al fresco, or read a book in peace and quiet. The swimming pool is large with a terracotta terrace surround with plenty of space for sunbathing.

If you are planning a family holiday, this villa is ideal, as it is only 15 minutes drive from the nearest beach and aqua water parks, however, the villa is set on a large plot of land amongst stunning orange and olive groves, so if you need not be afraid of making a noise or disturbing the neighbours!

If golf is what you are after, Vila Moura is a perfect choice, as you are only a 10 minute drive from the nearest golf course, making it easy to enjoy your favourite sport. We can also arrange for a private chef to cook in the evenings should you wish to stay at the villa in the evenings rather than eat out every night. The villa has a BBQ and the garden is a perfect environment to enjoy the balmy evenings with your friends. If you wish to eat out, we can organise transfers to take you to Vilamoura for dinner.

This property really will provide you with the perfect setting whatever the occasion.
