Villa GranSollazzo
5 Bedrooms | 5 Ensuite Baths | Pool | 8 Acres
Florence, Italy
From €3,043 per Night

In the Chianti hills, 6 miles (10 km) from Florence, 43 miles (70 km) from Siena and 31 miles (50 km) from Arezzo, give yourself and your family a dream vacation in an exclusive, 4,50o ft² (450 sq. meter) villa.  The structure is located in the picturesque Tuscan hills with 8 acres (3 hectares) of fenced property and a splendid 75 x 36 ft. (25 x 12 meter) pool, and tennis court.

The Villa is ideal for those seeking calm and privacy, and who prefer not to give up the comfort and warmth of a 4–star hotel.

Villa GranSollazzo is surrounded by greenery, vineyards and woods, yet merely 15 minutes from Florence. With your friends you can spend a splendid vacation pampered by staff that will take care of you during your entire stay.

Each day you can choose to have breakfast on the pool deck or in the shade of horse-chestnut trees, whether to eat your light lunch under the gazebo overlooking the pool or leisurely in your room.  You can sip a long drink while watching the sun set and waiting for dinner to be served in the wooden gazebo illuminated by candles and Morocco lanterns.

Your children can use this vacation to learn how to play tennis, learn to swim or speak their first Italian words, while you can discover the secrets of Tuscan cooking and astound your friends when you return home.

The Villa is spread out over one floor in which we find the entrance, the living room with fireplace, the dining area, the reading/study area, and the entertainment area containing a 54” flat screen satellite TV (sky channels service) and  home theatre sound system, DVD and CD players.

Through a door in the living room we reach the part of the villa where the 5 bedrooms and 5 baths are located; each room is furnished with a wardrobe, air conditioning and a TV 28” Cd/DVD player, private terrace.  Each bathroom includes a shower and hairdryer.

The dining area leads to a utility room where we find the ice machine, the tea and coffee makers, a cupboard containing dishware, silverware and glassware; from the utility room we continue on into the kitchen which is equipped with a 6-burner stove, oven, microwave, refrigerator, freezer and dishwasher.
