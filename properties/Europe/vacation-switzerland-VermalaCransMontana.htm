Vermala Crans Montana, Valais, Switzerland Vacation Rental

 

Located in Crans-Montana in the Valais region, Vermala Crans Montana, with ski-to-door access, is 1.9 km from Crans-sur-Sierre. It provides a terrace, a garden and a children's playground, in addition to a well-equipped kitchen. Free Wi-Fi is available.

There is also a view of the mountain. At Vermala Crans Montana, the kitchen is equipped with an oven, a stovetop and a dishwasher. It also has a microwave, a refrigerator and a coffee machine.

Relaxation options include a hot tub and a sauna. The chalet provides a fireplace, a flat-screen tv and a dvd player. Additional entertainment facilities include satellite channels.

In the bathroom you can find a bath or shower, a hairdryer and a bidet. For your convenience, a washing machine and free toiletries are also provided.

On-site facilities include a fitness centre and ski storage. Area activities include skiing, cycling and hiking.

Nationale Express is 1.1 km away, Pas du Loup is 1.3 km away and Crans-Cry d'Err is 1.9 km away. Belp Airport is 66 km away.
