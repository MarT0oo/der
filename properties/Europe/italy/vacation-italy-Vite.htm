Vite, Rome, Italy Vacation Rental

 

Minutes from the Spanish Steps, the spacious 3-bedroom Vite apartment will be your haven in the heart of Rome. Surrounded by upscale shopping and restaurants of all flavors, this vacation apartment exudes Roman sophistication. Tour remnants of old-world Italy, including the Piazza de Popolo (10 min. walk) and the Villa Borghese palace and gardens (15 min). Go farther back in time to the ancient Pantheon (8 min) or check out the outdoor Campo de Fiori market (18 min). Bus stops border the apartment, and the Spagna metro stop is only a 9-minute walk away.

With gentle colors and whimsical patterns, our local interior designer has put together a home away from home. The living room features comfy couches and lots of natural light. Three plush bedrooms and a sofa bed can accommodate a total of 8 guests. High ceilings and vintage mirrors add elegance to the space. The apartment includes premium amenities such as a state-of-the-art kitchen, free WiFi, cable TV, high-end toiletries, the finest beds and linens, and a Nespresso machine.

Great for family retreats or groups of friends, this lovely apartment lets you experience Rome to its fullest.
