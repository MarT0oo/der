Tenuta Selinunte, Selinunte, Italy Vacation Rental

 

Tenuta Selinunte is a beautiful old farmhouse on the island of Sicily. It is just few minutes away from the wonderful ruins of Selinunte and lovely beaches.

The villa, built around an old observation Tower, is on a hill with citrus and olive groves, palm and cypresses.

On the lawn beside the villa there is a large swimming-pool and a small lake with papyrus and bamboo.

The villa and its gardens are decorated in an elegant mixture of old and new styles that create charming, comfortable environment.
