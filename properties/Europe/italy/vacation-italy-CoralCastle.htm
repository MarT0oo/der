Coral Castle, Positano, Amalfi Coast, Italy Vacation Rental

 

Coral Castle, it’s an historical house of 1700 totally decorated with antic furniture and painting like a museum house.

The villa is composed by 4 floor with a S.P.A included. The principal entrance is from the second floor, but you can access to the house also from the forth floor. From this entrance you cross a Mediterranean garden full of bougainvilleas, lemon and orange tries, from which we make the natural juice and home made marmalades that we serve for the breakfast.

On the first floor there is the S.P.A, which also has an independent entrance, that it can be used also from the service. Here, on the terrace there is a fountain made in a pompeianan style which is a Jacuzzi looking on the see. Inside there is a heat swimming pool full of Jacuzzi and an oxygen therapy with different position to swim in, to get “healthy and beauty”. The S.P.A is composed also of one Sauna with therapy shock( hot- cold) a Tiepidarium, showers, and a massage place where a professional doctor do the anti-stress Massage, Manual lymphatic drainage, strengthening massage, sport massage(post-event or pre-event), deep tissue massage. Every massage last 50 minutes. In all this area there is a relaxing music and a plasma tv.

From this part of the house you get to the second floor ( by an interior stairs) and there is a big kitchen, where you can have the typical local dishes made in a traditional way from our cook. On the same floor there is a big room which you can use like a dining room, relaxing room, or working room, because there is a computer with all the services( Wi-Fi,Adsl line, fax, mail…) to work in total relax. Here there is also a big library full of old and new books, where you can get a look at the books write by John Steinback, writer who gets in this house his own inspiration.

At the third floor there are two big suites with the correspondent bathroom.

On the fourth floor ( which has an independent entrance) there is a penthouse with two bedrooms, decorated in Mediterranean style (white wood beam on the roof) with one big bathroom full of antic Neapolitan ceramics mosaic of 1700 for both bedrooms.

This area is perfect from young people or guest of the family.

All the house is full of terraces, garden with a spectacular view on the beach of Positano. We offer also two persons for the services and one butler.
