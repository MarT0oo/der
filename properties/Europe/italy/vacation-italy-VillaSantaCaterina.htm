Villa Santa Caterina, Siena , Tuscany, Italy Vacation Rental

 

Large air conditioned villa near Siena - Villa Santa Caterina is an imposing and fully restored 13th-century monastery, subsequently used as a farmhouse, which has been transformed into an elegant country holiday house that exudes charm throughout. Precious antiques, period furniture, yellow Siena marble, wooden ceilings create an exclusive home with spectacular, far reaching views over the peaceful Tuscan countryside.

As part of the latest refurbishment project, this villa also benefits from the addition of a range of modern comforts such as sauna, gym, long swimming pool, tennis court and football pitch so that guests can enjoy a good balance of relaxation and activities. At the very heart of this property is a delightful inner courtyard, providing the ideal setting for dinner al fresco.

A huge park comprising centuries-old trees, mature shrubs, lawns and woodland surround this beautiful villa, creating an enchanting backdrop to your holiday. Siena, with its artistic treasures, lively food and craft shops and period costume events such as the Palio, is only 15 minutes away. San Gimignano, Volterra, Montalcino are all within easy reach of the villa. For a chance to taste some exquisite wines produced in this region, why not follow the wine route that will take you past all the renowned wine estates of the Chianti valley between Siena and Florence
