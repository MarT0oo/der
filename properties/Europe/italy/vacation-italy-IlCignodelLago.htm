Il Cigno del Lago, Vassena, Lake Como, Italy Vacation Rental

 

Villa Il Cigno del Lago is a wonderful property located in Vassena a beautiful hamlet on the shores of Lake Como just a short drive from Bellagio. From the villa you can take in the most amazing Lake views as the property is located right on the Lake shores and surrounded by the peace and total seclusion that the private garden area has to offer.

The property is composed of two prestigious luxury villas, located in a spectacular position, directly on the waterfront of the famous Lake Como, and set in a beautiful “Italian style” garden of 2000 square meters (21.500 sq.ft.) with secular trees and their own private beach and private harbour. With its perfect location, close to the famous town of Bellagio, this property offers a central point for touring Northern Italy, Como, Cernobbio, Milan as well as Switzerland.
