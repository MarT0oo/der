Casale Vigneti, Panzano in Chianti , Tuscany , Italy Vacation Rental

 

This charming Chianti Villa Rental is set in the midst of the gently sloping hills of the Chianti Classico wine-growing region near the village of Panzano. It is ideal for families and/or friends travelling together as it offers spacious and flexible accommodation both in the main house and in the separate annex. There is a wonderful infinity pool, and there are lovely rural views over the vineyards, woods and hills.

Casale Vigneti is surrounded by beautifully-kept gardens, with cypress and olive trees, mature flowering shrubs, climbers and pots of flowers. The owners of this Chianti Villa Rental have maintained the character of the original 13th century farmhouse and outbuildings, tastefully restoring them in a mellow stone to provide very welcoming, comfortable and modern accommodation.

The delightful local village of Panzano is only 1.5 miles away from this Chianti Villa Rental for shops and restaurants. Casale Vigneti enjoys a peaceful, countryside location in the heart of Tuscany, yet is ideally placed for easy day trips to Colle di Val d'Elsa, Greve in Chianti, Siena, San Gimignano and Florence. An ideal base if you want to experience and explore the heart of Tuscany.
