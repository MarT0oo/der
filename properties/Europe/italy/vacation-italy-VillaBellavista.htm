Villa Bellavista, Civenna, Lake Como, Italy Vacation Rental

 

The beautiful Villa Bellavista is located in the heart of Civenna, a small typical Italian town located just 6 kms from Bellagio. The location of the Villa is perfect – it offers picture postcard views of the Lecco Branch of Lake Como and the Grigna Mountain Range from the elegant veranda and it is surrounded by a beautiful park with century old trees, plants and flowers.

A stay at the Villa Bellavista will not only allow you to experience an elegant Italian home in the heart of a typical town where you can easily walk to the local restaurants, post office, general store, pastry shop and join the locals but it also gives you very easy access to one of Lake Como’s most beautiful town, that of Bellagio.
