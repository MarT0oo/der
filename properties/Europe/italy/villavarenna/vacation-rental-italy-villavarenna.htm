Villa Varenna, Lake Como, Italy Vacation Rental

 

A centrally located gem!

Enjoying a fabulous location in the heart of Varenna this beautiful holiday home has been furnished to a high standard throughout and is ideal for families and friends.

Internally the accommodation boasts a spacious open plan living room with sitting area with SAT TV and DVD player for guests entertainment. A modern fully fitted kitchen provides all the desired appliances for an enjoyable self-catering stay including a gas oven hob, microwave and dishwasher. A dining area is perfect for dinner parties and the fabulous private garden is perfect for alfresco dining, with a BBQ and wonderful lake views!

The sleeping accommodation is spacious and flexible for large groups of up to 8, with 3 double bedrooms, 3 single bedrooms and 1 single bed with a pullout single. All bedrooms have been beautifully furnished with some boasting exceptional lake views.

The villa also provides guests with 3 modern fully fitted bathrooms and a washing machine for maximum convenience during your stay.
As well as a splendid garden a parking facility is also provides for guest’s use.

This beautiful villa is the perfect Lake Como retreat for guests who are looking to both relax and explore during their holiday, with the stunning hillside popular with hikers and cyclists and the touristic lakefront centre of Varenna all very nearby.

The picturesque centre is very popular with visitors who come to explore the ‘Portofino of Lake Como’ with its pretty pastel coloured townhouses which lead up ancient cobbled streets to the medieval quarter with its tree-lined piazza and utterly charming shop and cafe lined streets which lead past churches and breathtaking lake vistas. The idyllic lakefront with its small harbour, its scenic walkway with floral displays and charming lakefront restaurants which lead along cobbled streets to wine bars and fine hotel restaurants paint a highly romantic scene for any visitor and the town also boasts the visitor attractions of Villa Monastero with its famous botanical gardens and the medieval Castello di Vezio.

A car and passenger ferry port operates from the town to the other famous ‘golden triangle’ towns of Bellagio and Menaggio and a convenient train station also runs a frequent Lecco-Milan service.

Central lake activities include swimming, cycling, tennis, golf, sailing, boat hire, wind surfing, kayaking, paragliding and skiing.

PLEASE NOTE: This property is situated on the opposite side of the road to Varenna-Esino train station.
