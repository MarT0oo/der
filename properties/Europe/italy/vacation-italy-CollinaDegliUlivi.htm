Collina Degli Ulivi, Arezzo, Tuscany, Italy Vacation Rental

 

Lucignano holiday villa with pool to rent – Collina degli Ulivi is a charming 17th century stone farmhouse set in the verdant Tuscan countryside, known as Valdichiana, just a few miles away from the well preserved Medieval village of Lucignano.

This rustic villa is surrounded by a glorious garden, gently sloping towards the private pool and its romantic gazebo, set among 12 acres of vineyard and olive groves. Here the owners produce their own olive oil and put their vegetable garden at the clients’ disposal to enrich their dining ‘al fresco’ with genuine local flavours.

The villa is an excellent starting point for visiting many important Tuscan cities of art, such as Arezzo, Pienza, Montepulciano, Cortona, Siena and Florence. Country paths from the villa will take you to an idyllic lake, ideal for relaxing walks and picnics in this picturesque Tuscan holiday location.
