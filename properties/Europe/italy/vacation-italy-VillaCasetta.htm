Villa Casetta, San Giustino Valdarno, Tuscany, Italy Vacation Rental

 

Villa Casetta is a renovated Tuscan home set in the vineyards of the Relais Il Borro estate. Guests staying at Casetta have full access to the facilities of the hotel estate including the spa, restaurants and private medieval village.

Set out over two floors and with an overall interior living space of 350m², the main villa sleeps up to six adults. On the ground floor is a large entrance hall, cosy living room with fireplace, dining room and a kitchen/breakfast room. A staircase leads upstairs to three attractive furnished double bedrooms with ensuite bathrooms with bathtub and shower and views of the gardens.

New for 2019, the former pool house has been converted into a separate bedroom suite. Images to follow as soon as they become available.

Villa Casetta has a large garden with an outdoor dining terrace and an infinity swimming pool with views of vineyards and Tuscan hills beyond.

The property is situated 2.5 kilometres from the hotel facilities and restaurants, therefore a car is highly recommended as there is no transfer service between the villa and the hotel.
