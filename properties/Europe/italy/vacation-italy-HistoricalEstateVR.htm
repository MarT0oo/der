Historical Estate VR, Chianti, Tuscany , Italy Vacation Rental

 

The Historical Estate is nestled in the heart of Chianti Classico area, between Florence and Siena, surrounded by it's vineyards, luxury Villa is exactly what you would expect from a film by Bertolucci.

The Villa was once a monastery dating back to the XVII century, it has been restored and furnished, combining antique furniture with pieces collected during our travels.

An integral part of our lives here are the animals: four friendly dogs, four elegant horses, two cute ponies, a shy cat, chickens and geese who provides delicious fresh eggs for breakfast.

There are 9 double rooms, 8 on suite bathrooms, large living room with fireplace, a library full of interesting books, gazebos, pergolas, a large garden with swimming pool and a tennis court. In the hall of the first floor there is also a beautiful grand piano for guests who feel inspired. At VR, the cook prepares Italian dishes with fresh ingredients taken from our own organic vegetable garden, just a few steps from the kitchen. combined with our production wines and olive oil. Like the pure tradition of the Italian Dolce Vita, you can enjoy dining with a view under the rose gazebo.

Everything is provided for you to live the Italian experience of being a guest at a friend's house, with, in addition, the comforts like being in a luxury hotel.

The Estate is the ideal place for those who want to have parties and weddings in the privacy of a villa in Tuscany. The beautiful vineyards create a picturesque backdrop for a romantic outdoors wedding party. Services can be held in the historical Monastery of Badia a Passignano.

Catering, marquee/tents, flowers, music and fireworks are some of the services that can be provided. For large groups, there are nearby accommodations for extra guests.
For those who want to celebrate their event in a tuscan atmosphere, you will feel as if you were at home.

FOOD & WINE IN THE HISTORICAL ESTATE

Whether it's a dinner with wild boar from Antica Macelleria Falorni, pasta with truffles and a Florentine T-bone steak Dario Checchini, tiramisu made of goose eggs, or a simple pizza cooked in a wood oven, the cooking is always healthy and genuine.

Olive oil, tomatoes, asparagus, fennel, zucchini, artichokes, rosemary, thyme, sage, beef, wild boar and wine, are all fresh products, a few steps from the kitchen! With these ingredients, the chef of the house and I create for you exceptional Tuscan dishes, proposing ancient Tuscan recipes handed down from generation to generation.

Against the backdrop of the great combinations of food and wine produced on site, the dining table at the Villa has been the scene of many wonderful conversations, captured in stunning photos and in the many memories.
