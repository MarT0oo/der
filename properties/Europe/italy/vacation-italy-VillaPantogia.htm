Villa Pantogia, Porto Cervo, Costa Smeralda, Italy Vacation Rental

 

Villa Pantogia is located in the lower part of the Pantogia Area in Porto Cervo. The property boasts an enchanting sea view overlooking the Pevero Golf. It is located a few minutes away from the most beautiful beaches of the Costa Smeralda, Porto Cervo Marina and Piazzetta of Porto Cervo.

The Savin Couvelle Architecture is the prevailing design with modern decor, the property is composed of 3 levels. The ground floor has a wide living room, dining area, kitchen, verandas and terraces where is possible to enjoy ‘Al Fresco’ meals. BBQ area, with wood oven and table for dining outside.

The property has 5 double bedrooms all with en suite bathrooms, one of which can be turned into 2 double independent bedrooms plus 1 bedroom for staff. The property is not children friendly with several stairs so children are to be supervised. Open-air gym area and a wellness room with sauna and massage bed. Covered parking place for 2 cars, laundry area, fully air -conditioned, Wi Fi connection, Sky channels in the living room and Master Suite.
