Villa Madonna di Costantinopoli, Praino, Amalfi Coast, Italy Vacation Rental

 

Villa Madonna di Costantinopoli exudes charm and gives amazement, it is a luxurious historic home completely overlooking the sea and the amazing wonders that are the background to the village of Praiano heart of the Amalfi coast.

Located between lemons, olive trees surrounded by the Mediterranean, the Villa has a spectacular view suspended between sky and sea and overlooks a wonderful country, Praiano, in front of one of the most beautiful views in the world: the island of Capri, the Faraglioni, the Peninsula Sorrentina, Positano, and the Li Galli Archipelago (already owned by Nureiev as well as the Island of the Sirens in the Odyssey of Ulysses).

This prestigious villa preserves a fascinating history that dates back many centuries and some traces are still found today in authentic architectural and decorative details kept inside the villa.

Praiano is a beautiful small town on the coast of Amalfi that boasts the privilege of being located in a unique corner for the incomparable views of the coast and the sea, from the Villa you can witness some of the most beautiful sunsets in the world.

The door that gives access to the magnificent property consisting of about 500 square meters surrounded by terraces and gardens, lets immediately perceive the vibrant sensation of being completely extended to the sea.

The house is all in white lime, wrought iron wood and stone, restored on ancient walls of fascinating Arab architecture nothing is left to chance. The rooms and the rooms are all finely furnished with period furniture, and unfold in a succession of barrel vaults, cross vaults, columns, and ancient capitals. The Villa is equipped with every comfort: sauna, Jacuzzi, solarium and a beautiful infinity pool with rock and waterfall. The orientation of the house is towards the healthy South, every architectural element and every piece of furniture perfectly match the surrounding environment. Staying in this Villa is a unique and unforgettable experience.

In the well-kept interior and in the vaulted rooms an old kitchen with Neapolitan tiles from the 18th century was refitted, where the most traditional Mediterranean cooking school of the ancient fishing village can be learned and tasted.

Villa Madonna di Costantinopoli, the ruler of the beautiful Amalfi Coast, unique and exclusive piece that allows those who stay there to enjoy nature, art and wellness and experience magical moments in one of the most beautiful places in the world.
