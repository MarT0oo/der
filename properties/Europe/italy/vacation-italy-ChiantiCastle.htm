Chianti Castle, Chianti, Florence, Tuscany, Italy Vacation Rental

 

Exquisite restored Medieval property in Chianti, Tuscany dates back to the year 1000 with panoramic view of the hills, vineyards, olive groves that are typical of the Tuscan countryside, and beautiful view to the nearby castle Santa Maria Novella and in the distance the Italian Alps.

The first evidence of the existence of the Uglione Castle is an act of the year 1127, where the castle were part of a gift to Florence’s bishop. The castle was also the residence of the very famous Taddeo, the “Villan d’Uglione”, who is mentioned by Dante in the Divine Comedy.

This estate is set on a private hilltop starting at the grand remote controlled iron gate. You enter the property along the driveway which brings you to the Villa at the top. The park-like grounds have been beautifully landscaped with plants and trees which are illuminated at night to create a beautiful atmosphere in any season of the year.

The main Villa has a surface of 400 mq, another building with a tower of 200 mq, and a spa of 80 mq for a total living area of 680 mq. The property is surrounded by more than one hectare of grounds: garden, pool, terraces, lawn with fruit-trees, trampoline, pétanque, playground, olive grove and wood.

The property has been totally renovated and was finished september 2016 with the highest level of quality materials that will satisfy the most discerning expectations. Climate controlled, with Wi-Fi. Car park for 5 cars
