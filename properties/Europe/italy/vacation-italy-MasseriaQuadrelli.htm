Masseria Quadrelli, Salento, Puglia, Italy Vacation Rental

 

In the heart of Salento, set in an olive grove "Masseria Quadrelli" is located only 10 km from Otranto and just a few km from Santa Cesarea Terme, renowned resort famous for its spa and the splendid sea.

Built respecting local traditions, the central farm is characterized by the white "leccese stone", from vaulted ceilings and simple, natural materials.

Inside the complex, there are also two independent Suite, picturesque buildings shaped trulli, located in the olive grove and designed like ancient Pajare.

It is the ideal place for those seeking tranquility and comfort and to spend a holiday in close contact with nature.

Masseria Quadrelli is made from the farm: the main building with 2 suites and 2 superior rooms and independent annexes: the two trulli and 4 garden suites.

TRULLI

The 2 Trulli, immersed in the olive grove at a distance of about 50 m from the Masseria, are modeled on the ancient "pajare" Terra d'Otranto.

These buildings, formerly made with the dry stone technique, were used by farmers of Terra d'Otranto, today become comfortable residences for stays in close contact with nature. They have an area of about 70 square meters each and a total capacity of up to 4 people. They are characterized by the presence of a living room with kitchenette and sofa bed for 2 people, a large and bright double bedroom, a b1athroom with shower and an exclusive and private patio, covered and equipped with barbecue. The tasteful decor recalls the simple but refined area of the ancient farmhouses, where stone and wrought iron that creates an atmosphere of times.

GARDEN AND POOL

Masseria Quadrelli is located in an ancient garden of trees and plants typical of the Mediterranean, such as olive trees, oleanders, strawberry trees, figs, bucanville, broom, that scent the environment around the spectacular freeform pool, with integrated whirlpool.

Here you can enjoy the privilege of stepping into the past, living in close contact with nature, to discover the aromas, flavors and authentic colors of Salento.

The pool is enriched by beautiful water features created with rocks and a solarium, which with its sofas and lounge chairs is an irresistible invitation to indulge in the warm sunshine.

Here the hours pass slow, serene, with loved ones, away from the noise except in the hottest hours of summer, the sweet sound of cicadas, reading a book, chatting, enjoying a delicious aperitif on the sofas outside.
