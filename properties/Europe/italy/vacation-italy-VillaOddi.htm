Villa Oddi, Tuscany, Italy Vacation Rental

 

The beautiful six-bedroom Villa Oddi exudes sophistication and charm. Suffused with natural light, the home’s interiors are spacious and elegantly furnished. The residence features state-of-the-art technology, a private screening theater, a private tennis court, an infinity pool and breathtaking views of Montalcino. Villa Oddi is perfect for a family reunion or as a private business retreat. 466 sq. m. / 5,016 ft²

Sleeps up to 12 guests. Main house ground floor: living room with Tuscan fireplace; dining area; kitchen; TV room with conference facilities; and two double bedrooms with walk-in closets and en suite bathrooms. First floor: master bedroom with king-size bed, walk-in closet and en suite bathroom; double bedroom with walk-in closet and en suite bathroom; and twin bedroom with en suite bathroom (445 sq. m. / 4,790 ft²). Pool house: twin bedroom, en suite bathroom and outdoor patio (21 sq. m. / 226 ft²). Breakfast at Ristorante Campo del Drago €35 p.p. Private outdoor heated swimming pool. Tennis court. Wood-burning oven. 82” LCD flat-screen television in the living room and LCD flat-screen televisions in all bedrooms. Wi-Fi broadband Internet access
