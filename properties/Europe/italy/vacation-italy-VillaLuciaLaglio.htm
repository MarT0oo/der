Villa Lucia Laglio, Laglio, Lake Como, Italy Vacation Rental

 

Located along the main lakeshore road of Laglio, this modern 4 bed villa for 10 provides an exclusive home-from-home setting, whatever the season!

Fully-equipped for lavish holiday stays, the villa offers partial Air-con, SAT TV, a full entertainment system, a private outdoor jacuzzi and a luxury outdoor plunge pool (fitted with a variable speed swim jet). A private garage is also on hand for added convenience.

The chic and inviting living space effortlessly lets you relax and entertain; with a sumptuous L-shaped sofa featuring alongside a sleek kitchen with chic breakfast bar and dining area for 10.

The flexible 3-storey villa retreat is ideal for accommodating luxury groups and grown-up families of 10. The versatile sleeping arrangements comprise a romantic loft-style Double bedroom with en-suite shower, 1 Double lake view bedroom with balcony and 2 twin joinable lake view bedrooms (each with balcony).

Outside, the poolside dining area allows guests to dine ‘alfresco’, whilst enjoying the sun and partial lake views from the sheltered terrace garden.

The villa’s location offers close proximity to Laglio village centre (3 minutes’ drive away), whilst two lovely authentic Italian restaurants (within 50 metres of the front door) offer every excuse to wine and dine your holiday away! Some highly-recommended restaurants can also be savoured in nearby Brienno, Argegno and Cernobbio (all within 5-12 minutes’ drive away), so it truly offers a great location for foodie fans and wine buffs.

The picturesqe setting of Laglio also offers a lovely base from which to relax and explore; with bus and road links to the fashionable lake resort of Cernobbio (home to boutique gift shops, a Lido and Villa D’Este Golf Club & Hotel), the famous Central Lake tourist attractions (15-20 minutes’ away) and the cosmopolitan highlights and Milan transport links of Como (24 minutes’ away).

A host of leisure pursuits all await your enjoyment too; from hiking, cycling, boat rental, kayak hire and water sports to seaplane trips, golf, tennis, lake swimming and Italian Alp Skiing.

The nearest airport Milan Malpensa, can be reached by car in just 46 minutes.
