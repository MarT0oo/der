Villa Capricorno, Ischia, Italy Vacation Rental

 

Ischia, with its internationally renowned thermal baths and natural beauty, is a paradise on earth just waiting to be discovered during your stay at Villa Capricorno, a 19th century luxury villa located in the vibrant center of this island, known as the "Green Island". Here you can indulge your need for privacy without missing out on cultural daytrips and lively local nightlife.

With its linear and classic design that is uniquely elegant, Villa Capricorno will seduce you with the captivating simplicity of its decor. The villa's furnishings and fabrics have been carefully chosen to render the interior bright, welcoming, and stylish. Marble floors and fine antiques embellish all five suites of the villa, each boasting the most modern comforts for a luxurious stay.

An intimate summer dinner on the veranda while you're watching the sun set over the sparkling sea on the horizon... a daydream that becomes a daily event at Villa Capricorno!

Lose yourself in the beautiful garden of the property, which stretches right to the seaside at Ischia's tiny, lovely English Beach. Villa Capricorno will make you fall in love with its sweeping outdoor estate, carefully furnished for leisurely relaxation. At the garden's the most scenic spot, you can enjoy a swim in the large saltwater pool and a relaxing soak in the outdoor thermal pool, followed by a lazy afternoon basking on the sundeck

Ischia is famous worldwide for its spas and curative thermal springs, so it's no surprise that Villa Capricorno has a deluxe wellness area dedicated to recharging mind and body...let yourself be pampered by the indulgent services and amenities.

The villa boasts a fully equipped fitness area and a Jacuzzi with thermal water, where you can unwind after your workout!
