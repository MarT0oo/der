Villa L'Eau, Puntaldia, Sardinia, Italy Vacation Rental

 

Semi-detached house featuring 4 double bedrooms with ensuite bathrooms, 2 living areas, dining area and modern kitchen with a comfortable peninsula where you can enjoy quick meals while admiring the sea view. The kitchen is fully furnished with induction hob with integrated hood, oven, combined fridge, dishwasher, coffee maker, toaster, kettle, etc.

Very spacious outdoor veranda boasting a stunning wide sea and pool view. It’s furnished with comfortable sofas and a large table with chairs and a comfortable gas barbecue. Another large porch downstairs near the fitness area.

The house is gated, garage for cars, air conditioning, WIFI, 2 TV 50’.
