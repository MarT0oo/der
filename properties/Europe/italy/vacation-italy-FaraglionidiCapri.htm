Faraglioni di Capri, Island of Capri, Amalfi Coast, Italy Vacation Rental

 

Luxury Capri villa with pool – Set on the cliffs overlooking the world-famous Rocks of Capri, Faraglioni di Capri is an elegant Mediterranean-style villa in an absolutely stunning location, within walking distance of the rocky beach of Marina Piccola Bay and the lively Piazzetta in the heart of Capri village centre.

Awake each morning to unparalleled sea-views and enjoy your first morning espresso from the comfort of your terrace, among colourful bougainvillea, scented rosemary and Jasmine. The beautiful garden offers several shaded areas, you only have to choose whether to relax under the tall pine trees or the stylish gazebo by the pool conveniently located next to the bar area, in case you fancy a refreshing cocktail. A gentle sea-breeze will reach you wherever you are and will be particularly appreciated in the hottest hours of the day.

The mountaintop village of Capri and its famous Piazzetta with resturants, cafes and boutiques is only a stroll away from your villa and as is the rocky beach of Baia di Marina Piccola, accessible via a steep, scenic path just outside the villa. Boat excursions depart from the pretty harbour and are strongly recommended to fully enjoy the beauty of this idyllic region, the cobalt blue Tyrrhenian sea, the rocky coastline with striking panoramas and cheerful coastal villages with their whitewashed houses such as Amalfi, Positano and Ravello.

 

Capri Piazzetta (village centre) 15 mins walk or 5 mins’ drive. Nearest rocky beach in Marina Piccola Bay 3 mins’ walk via steep path just outside the villa. Sorrento, Positano & Amalfi coast 30-50 minutes by hydrofoil/boat. Nearest airport Naples/Capodichino (50 mins by hydrofoil + approx. 30 mins by taxi or bus); heliport at Anacapri. Tennis at Anacapri and Capri.
