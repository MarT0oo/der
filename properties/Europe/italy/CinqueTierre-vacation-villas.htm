About Cinque Terre & Italian Riviera

The Cinque Terre & Italian Riviera is the very romantic and historic Southern Stretch of Coastline in Italy.  It is by far the most scenic and well traveled area of the Coasts of this region, bringing in thousands of tourists annually.  Known for its Cliffside architecture, seen easily from the boats sailing by or even from a high hill at night, it’s no wonder the location lends itself to movies and romantic getaways.  It is truly a wondrous place to visit and for some becomes their second home.

The beaches are used year round by visitors as the Coast has a very mild winter, however you may find the occasional cold winter day so bring a coat with you if traveling during that time period.  The regions of the Coast people often visit are: Positano, Ravello, Scala, Priano, Atrani and lay very close to Sorrento as well. 

You will enjoy the level of vacationing here, offering luxury villas, vacation rentals, luxury home lettings, as well as mansions that are seaside.

Arriving at the Cinque Terre & Italian Riviera you would fly into Naples, then hire a car or take a bus to your final city of choice.  You can also drive but the roads are often difficult to navigate, especially for tourists.  Try www.positanocarservice.com which have had good reviews on trip advisor.  It will cost about 100 – 200 Euros for the transfer, depending on where you go.  Most of the busses stop running very early afternoon, so you will not find a late night bus.  Be sure to plan ahead and reserve a car, a limo, or your bus.
