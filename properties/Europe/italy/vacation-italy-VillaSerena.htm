Villa Serena, Sorrento, Italy Vacation Rental

 

Villa Serena Sorrento is a welcoming and elegant three-story Luxury villa, from Villa Serena you will enjoy a great view over the bay of Naples and Mount Vesuvius, the Villa is 300 square meters (3230 square feet) and can accommodate up to 10 people on 5 Bedrooms. It features a private swimming pool, private terraces with wonderful views sea view, a private loved garden, is fully air conditioned, it has winter heating and Fast WI-FI.

Bedrooms: Total of 4 Master suite and 1 bedroom without en suite bathroom, all the bedrooms are fully furnished, all the bathrooms have shower, no bath.

The first bedroom is located on the top floor and accessed via the corridor, the room has a double bed (160 cm/62 inches, wider than a queen-size bed), it has en suite full bathroom and a terrace.

The second bedroom also on top floor has a double bed (160 cm/62 inches, wider than a queen-size bed) , it has en suite full bathroom and a terrace.

The third bedroom is on the top floor it has two French beds (120 cm/47 inches), and a the terrace sea view, its bathroom is located in the corridor.

The fourth bedroom is on the ground floor, has a double bed (160 cm/62 inches, wider than a queen-size bed), it has en suite full bathroom and a terrace.

The fifth bedroom is on the ground floor, has a double bed (160 cm/62 inches, wider than a queen-size bed), it has en suite full bathroom and a terrace.

From the Villa, you will be about 2 km (1.2 miles) away from Sorrento center, and about 300 metres (330 yards) from some Restaurant, shops and the stop for the SITA bus Amalfi-Sorrento line.

You will find the closest sandy beach of Puolo 2 km (about 1.25 miles) away an enchanting fishermen’s village.

About 1 km away (about 0.6 miles) you’ll find the wonderful historic site of the “Bagni della Regina Giovanna” famous for being a place where according to the legend the Queen Giovanna used to swim, is a natural seawater lagoon inside the ruins of an old Roman Ruins Villa.

You will not need a car to stay at Villa Serena but if you do have one, you will be able to park it on the premises, free of charge inside the entrance gate.
