Villa Riva, Lake Como, Italy Vacation Rental

 

A modernist retreat of grand proportions and enjoying a prominent lakefront position!

Situated overlooking the exclusive Nobiallo Marina on the waters-edge, and conveniently placed just 5 minute’s drive north of Menaggio, this exclusive residence provides the very highest quality of accommodation for discerning parties of families and guests holidaying together on Lake Como.

The air conditioned property provides lake view accommodation over two floors and comprises of two Open plan living rooms which both provide luxurious sofa suites, flat screen satellite TV with DVD player and beautiful formal dining areas. The first floor living room additionally features a working fireplace and access out onto a panoramic lake view terrace of generous proportions and with ample alfresco dining, whirlpool spa Jacuzzi and BBQ facilities.

The villa also features a fully fitted designer kitchen to each floor with dishwasher and washing machine.

To the first floor 6 Double bedroom suites with en-suite facilities and a seventh bathroom are situated and to the penthouse level 3 further Double bedroom suites with en-suite facilities and an eleventh bathroom are situated.

Parking is conveniently situated in this exclusive residence for guests.

By special arrangement a chartered yacht may be hired from the marina so that you and your guests can explore the Lake’s famous lakefront villas, gardens and towns and enjoy a private tour with champagne and aperitifs!

The pretty lakeside village of Nobiallo is conveniently located just a 5 minute’s drive north of the famous lakeside resort town of Menaggio which provides a wide choice of amenities including supermarkets, delicatessens, cafes, bars, restaurants and boutique designer shops. A popular and newly refurbished Lido with beach, swimming pools, volley ball, cocktail bar and pizzeria can be enjoyed during the warm summer months and a frequent car and passenger ferry operate from the town taking visitors to the golden triangle towns of Bellagio, Varenna and also to Cadenabbia, Tremezzo and Como city.

Tennis courts are situated in the Menaggio hillside town of Loveno and the world famous Menaggio & Cadenabbia Golf Club is also situated very nearby on the Menaggio hillside. The famous Villa Carlotta and Villa Balbianello are also both situated very nearby and are well worth a visit. The lake also offers many other activities including sailing, kite surfing, hiking, bike riding, golf, a jungle adventure park and it is also possible to take a boat plane ride or even paraglide!

In the evenings guests can try a cocktail in Menaggio’s lakefront cocktail bar lounge or try wine tasting in the historical wine cellars of Cantina Follie in Tremezzo before going to the Lido di Lenno to dance the night away with the locals and the Milanese. There are also some outstanding restaurants on the Lake including the lakefront ‘La Darsena’ in Tremezzo, the authentic agriturismo restaurants of ‘La Fagurida’ in Tremezzo and ‘La Barcola’ near Porlezza, ‘La Baia’ Pizzeria in Cremia and the sophisticated ‘Il Gatto Nero’ in Cernobbio.
