Bellisimo, Sorrento, Italy Vacation Rental

 

Bellisimo, once known as Villa Perrone, is a period residence dating back to 1866, now fully restored and converted into a prestigious and charming villa. It is equipped with all the amenities, ensuring an elegant and exclusive stay.

Strategically located at Capo di Sorrento Bellisimo, enjoys a beautiful view of the Gulf of Naples, the Mount Vesuvius, as well as Ischia and Procida Islands.

Surrounded by about two acres of park and garden , it is perfect for a pleasant holiday, in complete serenity and privacy.

In a setting of great charm, with spectacular views of the Mount Vesuvius, as well as the Islands of Ischia and Procida, surrounded by two acres of park and garden with olive trees and beautiful flowers, Bellisimo is the ideal place for a fabulous wedding reception.

Two internal halls with beautiful frescoes and a lovely terrace, overlooking the sea, create a refined atmosphere during sunless days, offering your guests a truly unforgettable experience. Bellisimo is also available for exclusive weekly rentals. Exclusive rentals of Bellisimo are suitable for private events, wedding celebrations, business meetings, family reunions, movie sets and commercials

How to reach us

By car from the International Airport of Naples Capodichino
Take the A3 motorway (Napoli - Salerno - Reggio Calabria) to Castellammare di Stabia.
Continue along the SS145 to Sorrento Peninsula.
Once in Sorrento, follow the signs to Sorrento, continue for 3 km before reaching the property .

By car from Rome

From Molo Beverello of Naples there is a daily hydrofoil service to Sorrento.
