About Lake Como Region

The Lombardy Regions of Italy are well traveled and some of the most beautiful in the world.  Where else can you find towering mountains on the Alpine side and lovely lakes on the Lombard Lakeside?  It’s a region that is known for its richness in culture, art, and history, people who come will often find themselves immersed in the Fashion of Milan and the lazy days on Lake Como Beaches.  It’s no wonder celebrities make their way here every year and stay on the shores of these great lakes or visit the cities fine cultural centers and rent a flat.  There is no way to describe it unless you visit, a true inspiration to be seen.

You can take a drive through the country during your stay and stop off at some lovely wineries, such as Azienda, La Versa and Angelo, all found in the Wine Region.  You can always schedule a tour of the wineries with any of the concierge who help you during your stay or we can help with planning prior.  There are so many reasons that Lombardy is great and will continue to be one of the richest, if not the richest region in Europe, so don’t miss out and keep this on one of your top things to do list. 

There are many areas to visit, such as Milano a cultural and stylish city, Lake Como a wonderful and classy lake community, Varese on the Alpine Foothills, Monza & Brianza in Western Lombard and regions that sit within.  You will find Cottages, Farm homes, Skiing Chalets, Large Estates, Fully Catered Mansion Rentals and so much more.  Don’t miss out on this truly wonderful adventure.

Arriving at Lombardy you will fly into Milan and find many International Airports at  your disposal.  You can then hire a car or get a limo to any destination you wish.  There are a number of different local agents at the airports always ready and willing to assist.
