Villa Le Selve, Lake Garda, Lombardy, Italy Vacation Rental

 

Tenuta Le Selve is an elegant and exclusive hillside property located on the western shore of Garda Lake, a wonderful environment for unforgettable holidays and stays. Enveloped by the green of the olive grove, which lies on the surrounding plateau, you can enjoy an incomparable panorama that spans across the lower lake and the church of the quaint village of Gaino.

In the estate there is the prestigious Villa designed in the twenties by the architect Giancarlo Maroni, first of all known as the designer of the Vittoriale degli Italiani, the residence of the poet and writer Gabriele D'Annunzio. The Villa, the core of the estate, was partially renovated in 2008 to host a Guest House, a refined and elegant residence for those guests who want to rent it for indulging in periods of relaxation and tranquillity.

A unique solution both for couples who wish to sidestep the hustle and bustle of everyday life, and for large families who want to share time together surrounded by every comfort and attention.

In the Villa there are three master bedroom suites to welcome our guests in elegancet and comfort.

The suites consist of large rooms fitted with every comfort, boasting a private open space bathroom where guests can make use of a large shower and bath-tub for relaxing moments.

Every room has been finished and personalized in every detail; bathroom marble, wooden decorations, sheets and sponges made of precious textiles, tenuous nuances and stylish furniture have been chosen and matched with care to exalt every room.

As in every comfortable home, the Villa has a spacious living room, dining room and kitchen.

These are located within the characteristically elegant and sophisticated iron and glass conservatory facing the lake, which, through its glass surfaces, allows in not only the light, but also the intense colours of nature to blend in and create a unique atmosphere. The white ceiling curtains made of fine linen gauze give a touch of refinement that completes the work.

Here, the guests of the Villa can come together around a table for elegant lunches and dinners, prepared in the kitchen which is located behind the dining room. This develops around a central bar with marble top, provided with every convenient and funcional service, where you can meet for breakfasts and snacks.

To pamper and spoil its guests the Villa also features a wellness area overlooking the garden and lake. Here you will find a large whirlpool tub where you canimmerse yourself for a revitalizing bath, a Finnish sauna, large shower and a relaxation area with comfortable beds.

The quality materials and soft colours make the atmosphere ideal to indulge in relaxing massages and beauty treatments, booked and performed at the request of the customer.

To be enchanted by this unique landscape, immersed in the pool with an horizon that mergess with the blue of the lake, is certainly a privilege only for the few.

Besides the large infinity pool sterilized by a natural salt-based treatment , the external environment of the Villa offers a large whirlpool tub set in the stone wall and an area around the pool equipped with deck chairs and tables for outdoor dining.

All this is framed by flowerbeds with essences and scented flowerings and by the expanse of the olive grove. A further fine detail: on customersâ€™ request the main pool can be heated to enjoy the outside environment in all seasons and to appreciate the natural environment and its different colours in different seasons.

Tenuta the Selve, located 300 m above sea level above sea level, and with a stunning view overlooking Lake Garda, is surrounded by 10 hectares of olive cultivation.

The villa is located in a strategic position for those who want to quickly reach places of history, interest, culture and entertainment. A few kilometers away is

Gardone Riviera - the 'garden city' that hosts the Vittoriale of the Italians, home of the Italian poet Gabriele D'Annunzio - historical and cultural city, Verona - the city famous for opera in the Arena and for lovers Romeo and Juliet, a place of art and history, but that is well suited for shopping and entertainment.

These are just some of the attractions that can be found a short distance from the estate, but the area offers much more like typical festivals, culinary events and fireworks.

The Villa, surrounded by green hills and overlooking Lake Garda, allows those who love sports and nature many choices for outdoor activities (golf, sailing, hiking, climbing, horseback riding or mountain biking, paragliding, windsurfing and kitesurfing).
