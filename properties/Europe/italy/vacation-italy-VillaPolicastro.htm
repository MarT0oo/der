Villa Policastro, Scario, Amalfi Coast, Italy Vacation Rental

 

Situated within the Cilento National Park, just a short stroll away from the beach and the village of Scario, this stunning villa with an exceptionally large pool and lush Mediterranean garden, enjoys breath taking views over the Gulf of Policastro and the surrounding hills.

The accommodation of this typical seaside villa is spread over three floors, each with private terrace and outdoor dining facilities; imagine waking each morning and stepping out to splendid views over the cobalt-blue sea. Each floor is only accessible externally which means added privacy for everybody within the same party. Inside, local hand-painted tiles are widely used to add vibrant colours and mosaic patterns so typical of this region of Southern Italy. Villa Policastro offers a poolside gym for those who like to keep fit, and a sizeable swimming pool with panoramic views over the gulf to cool down after. A tennis table provides a little light sporting diversion, and a lush garden stocked with colourful flowers, shrubs and fruit trees completes this idyllic picture.
