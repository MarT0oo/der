Villa IL Pino, Sant'Agata sui due Golfi, Sorrento, Italy Vacation Rental

 

VILLA IL PINO is an approximately 360 m2, four-story building that can accommodate a maximum of 10 people. It has six bathrooms, a private swimming pool, private parking, pool salon and a computer with internet connection. The main characteristics of the villa are the serviced garden rich of colourful plants, the pool with sunbathing area, the independency and the fantastic view of Naples' bay. The place where one feels like a prince or a princess...

N.B. The villa is connected to the villa ‘Il Noce' by a private passage; they can also be rented out together as they have a communal garden that surrounds the entire complex.
