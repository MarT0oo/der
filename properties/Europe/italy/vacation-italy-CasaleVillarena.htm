Casale Villarena, Nerano, Italy Vacation Rental

 

Villarena is located in Nerano, Massa Lubrense, one of the most relaxing place in Sorrento Coast, just a few kilometres from Sorrento town, Positano and Amalfi Coast drive. Nerano is a small and typical italian fishing village that it’s worlds away from the hustle and bustle of well-known surrounding tourist destinations. There you will find tranquility and privacy, beautiful beaches with clean sea water that neighboring towns simply cannot offer. The best memories of your Italian vacation will be the ones that just happen. When it comes to planning your Italian get away, it is best to have an amazing place to stay. Then, let spontaneity and freedom take over. Casale Villarena is just that amazing place. With the owner personally welcoming each guest and the entire staff being at your complete disposal, Casale Villarena is a full-service, village resort that offers you an experience unparallel to any other hotel in Italy. With such amenities as pool with massage jets, children's play area with playground equipment, full service restaurant, organic garden, relaxation areas, air conditioning, free continental breakfast, private parking, courtesy car pick up, laundry room with complimentary use of washing machine and iron, and breathtaking sea views throughout the property.

Casale Villarena is a combination of a private luxury hotel and your own home. Be sure to leave some time for some amazing experiences at Casale Villarena: Enroll in a cooking class with a professional Italian chef. Here, you and twelve other friends, family, and guests can take beginner to advanced level cooking courses, where you learn how to make such local favorites as gnocchi, parmigiana, and limoncello. Enjoy a cheese and wine tasting in a "Farisani" style room and wine cellar, which stores reasonably priced and higher priced wines. Tempt your taste buds with some of the best cheeses that Italy has to offer.

Expertly paired wines will surely make this experience something to tell your friends and family about. This breathtaking, romantic, old-style room is also available for private parties and romantic dinners. Speed away in a Fiat 500 convertible for a day of shopping in Sorrento or Amalfi. Relax on a half-day or full-day boat excursion to the islands Capri and Ischia. Throughout the day, unwind with a glass of local wine or limoncello in the resort's upper or lower relaxation areas.

Just steps from the pool, the lower relaxation area provides peace and tranquility , where you can doze off for a nap, read your favorite book, listen to music, chat with other guests, watch television, or play a game on Nintendo-wii. Accessed from Casale Villarena's "Dolce Far Niente" restaurant, the upper relaxation area allows you to soak in the sun, sit under the gazebo, delve into your favorite book, and enjoy the beautiful panoramic view of the Mediterranean Sea and Li Galli Islands. Book a private pizza party.
