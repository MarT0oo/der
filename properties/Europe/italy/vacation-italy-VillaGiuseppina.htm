Villa Giuseppina, Talla, Tuscany, Italy Vacation Rental

 

Elegant Tuscan villa rental – Nestled among the unspoilt Casentino mountains, Villa Giuseppina is a newly restored country house whose understated elegance and tasteful décor make it a hidden gem of rural Tuscany where genuine character seamlessly blends with modern comforts. Terracotta floors, chestnut beamed ceilings, Travertino marble and natural stone walls are some of the features of Villa Giusepppina, and so is the original double-sided stone fireplace between two living rooms.

One of several villas built in a secluded private domain accessed through a private road, this villa enjoys a particularly peaceful atmosphere only broken by sounds from the surrounding countryside, a lovely complement to a relaxing holiday. Far reaching views can be enjoyed from most rooms and from the pool terrace, where forest clad mountains, meadows and valleys are the inspiring backdrop of a truly blissful Tuscan holiday.

Many pretty villages, such as Talla, with traditional country restaurants and little shops are a stone’s throw away from the villa, whereas the magic triangle of Florence, Siena and Arezzo is within an hour’s drive. An array of local wine and food fairs, music festivals and cultural events contribute to make Tuscany a very appealing destination for your family holiday.
