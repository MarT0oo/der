Villa Adina, Forte dei Marmi, Tuscany, Italy Vacation Rental

 

Hidden away, yet very close to Forte dei Marmi town center, the home style encompasses traditional elements brought into the now with updated add-on’s for today’s lifestyles: there is a heated pool equipped with beach umbrellas and deck loungers, offering a breathtaking 360° view of the coast; for fitness enthusiasts, the estate features a shady corner gym equipped with Technogym machines, a comfortable sauna with an outdoor shower. The home design, approached with research in the estate contextual, technical, and historical contexts, results in a new-classic style with stone, resin floors, quality washed wooden boards and marble. Modern, comfortable and soothing, this is a bohemian-chic retreat full of light and a welcoming vibe.

The living areas include two large rooms where watching TV or reading a book in front of the fireplace, the breakfast area with the fully equipped open kitchen. The refined atmosphere is also enhanced by its light shades of nude and grey, enriched with vintage pieces and Italian design ’50s and’ 60s pieces. The lush garden, studded with Mediterranean plants, citrus and flowers, enables guests to enjoy many corners to regenerate and to live a relaxing time.

This estate offers eight bedrooms decorated in elegant style with color washed walls in pale shades, contemporary fabrics, books and items, which lend a very personal touch, each with private bathroom, decorated in shades of grey or green and furnished with pieces of modern design, all feature Calacatta marble flooring in bathrooms, some also a Danish fireplace and large windows with various panoramic sights. Bedrooms scent with the aromatic notes of the fragrances of choice of the owner, a restaurant and beach club owner and her family.

Each room is exclusive and original, with custom furnishings and accessories, pieces of art and Italian design from the 50’s and 60’s. Guests will always find fresh flowers, a kettle with a selection of teas and herbal teas; our lady guests will also find everything they need to remove their make-up: cosmetic pads, cotton wool and tissues.

On the ground floor we find a living room, a fully equipped kitchen, and a service bathroom, a patio and the external area with pool and views.
