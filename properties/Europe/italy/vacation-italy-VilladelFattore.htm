Villa del Fattore, Lucca , Tuscany, Italy Vacation Rental

 

Villa del Fattore is a fine example of a Tuscan country house, situated at the heart of an outstanding 18th-century estate. The villa is set in a beautiful, floodlit park on the edge of a lush, wooded hill and surrounded by orchards and olive groves.

The front of the villa features a large lawn and spacious swimming pool, surrounded by comfortable lounge chairs and umbrellas. On both sides of the building are original patios in Tuscan stone where wrought-iron tables and chairs with cream umbrellas provide an elegant setting for a lunch.

The house itself has recently been restored to the highest standards while retaining itstraditional Tuscan character. Beautiful terracotta tiled floors and exposed beams adorn every room, and several of the downstairs living rooms have frescoed walls. As well as a lounge and satellite television, the first floor of the property boasts a formal dining room, fully equipped kitchen, two guest bathrooms and a beautiful internal conservatory where breakfast may be enjoyed. With ensuite facilities in every bedroom, Villa del Fattore offers the best of modern comforts and convenience in an elegant yet rustic setting.
