Castello di Magrano, Umbria, Italy Vacation Rental

 

The magnificent Castello di Magrano was built in the middle ages (1200 AC), in defence of the town of Gubbio against Perugia. The castle has retained it defensive caracter from the outside, but four years of intensive renovation have turned the interior into an extremely welcoming and comfortable house for 14 to 35 guests.

The castle is surrounded by a park whith century old trees, large lawns and beautiful pool which looks out over the hilly 1400 acre estate. The estate is signposted with beautiful walks and in the appropriate season you can even go truffle hunting with local experts.

The owners of the estate are descendants of the family of Napoleon Bonaparte and the castle has been in their family since 1830.

The local village can be reached in 5 minutes by car. Gems such as Gubbio, Perugia, Assisi, Cortona, Spoleto and Todi are all whithin easy reach.

The accomodations are split into two buildings - the main castle, which sleeps 14, and the guest house, which stands to the side of the castle and connects to the castle through a corridor, which sleeps another 16. The most exciting rooms are of course up in the watchtower!

The ground-floor kitchen is modern and functional, a professional stainless steel kitchen. It opens up onto the courtyard and the back of the castle, both equipped for outdoor meals.

The rent includes daily maid service from Monday to Saturday as well as the preparation of breakfast
