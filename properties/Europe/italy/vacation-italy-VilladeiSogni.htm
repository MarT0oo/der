Villa dei Sogni, Bellagio, Lake Como, Italy Vacation Rental

 

The beautiful Villa dei Sogni, overlooking the town of Bellagio, known locally as the Pearl of Lario, is perfectly situated to live up to its name- the Villa of Dreams. Each and every corner of this amazing property has been lovingly thought out and finished to the highest quality assuring its house guests the most comfortable stay.

Villa Dei Sogni is the perfect spot for you to immerse yourselves in an unforgettable Italian experience that will be a memory to cherish forever.. It is literally the most magic place for a timeless and unique experience, in one word a dream! The large outdoor terrace with views as far north as the Alps is the perfect location to enjoy the most amazing Lake views while celebrating special evenings and eating ‘al fresco’. The villa is the perfect base for exploring the area, Bellagio with its beautiful villas and gardens and the villages scattered on the Lake shores. For the city lovers Como and Lecco are both within an easy 45 minutes drive of the Villa while Milan and Bergamo are only 1,5 hours drive away. If you prefer walking, you can head into the hills behind Villa dei Sogni, this area offers some wonderful walking, hiking & mountain biking options that should not be missed!
