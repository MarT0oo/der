Isola Bella Giardino, Musso, Lake Como, Italy Vacation Rental

 

Incredible view lakeshore apartment. Offering a top lakefront setting, couples and honeymooners can easily savour all the thrills and escapism of a Lake Como stay!

Situated to the first floor of an inviting lakefront residence and easily accessible by lift, guests are within easy reach of the shared facilities and lakeside grounds. Inside a bright and spacious living room offers a restful setting in which to curl up and enjoy quality moments together. French doors lead directly out to the splendid private lake view terrace.

Providing a spot of welcome shade, the covered terrace offers an idyllic setting for year round enjoyment. From here a sunny decked private terrace can also easily be reached. Furnished with sun loungers, a parasol and stylish outdoor furniture set, couples can easily soak up the rays and savour romantic evening drinks at sunset.

A modern and fully equipped industrial kitchen enables guests to wine and dine their stay away. With all the must-have appliances to hand, including an oven, cooker hob, toaster, coffee machine and kettle; the scene is set for tasty dinner parties and relaxed morning breakfasts!

The spacious 5 bedroom apartment offers the perfect sanctuary in which to enjoy a guilt-free holiday slumber, with a modern double bed complemented by beautiful lake views.

Within the shared lakefront grounds, a shared outdoor swimming pool offers the perfect place in which to unwind in the sun and enjoy a refreshing swim.

Private parking and resident boat mooring are also on hand, allowing easy access for those with rental cars and boats.

From the authentic village setting of Musso, guests are within a 13 minute drive away from the windsurfing club in Cremia and the hiking routes of San Siro, as well as being located just a 15 minute drive from the restaurants, shops, family Lido and central lake ferry service of Menaggio.

The surrounding central lake boasts a wide range of activities to enjoy, from water sports, sailing, spa days and world class golf, to tennis, hiking, horse riding and even skiing!

The central lake also offers easy access to Lugano (44 minutes drive/bus ride away from Menaggio), Como city (46 minutes drive/bus ride away from Menaggio) and Milan (by train from either Varenna or Como/ a 1.5 hoursâ€™ drive away from Menaggio).

By booking this holiday home you will get free acces to the lido di Menaggio. The Lido di Menaggio has swimming pools, a restaurant, a bar/discotheque, a kids club, boat rentals, a sandy beach, kite surfing and various sportive activities to do.
