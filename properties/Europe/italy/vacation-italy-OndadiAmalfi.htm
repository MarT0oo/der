Onda di Amalfi, Amalfi, Italy Vacation Rental

 

Luxury Amalfi villa rental- Nestled on a hillside along the coastal road leading to Amalfi, Onda di Amalfi is a newly refurbished Mediterranean terraced villa with striking views over the gulf and lush vegetation cascading down to the cobalt blue sea. This is a truly romantic setting for the ultimate holiday experience in the renowned Southern Italy Riviera. The versatile accommodation is split between two levels and includes a further annex with its own independent entrance from the terrace. Each of the well-appointed, ensuite bedrooms is tastefully decorated with brightly coloured Italian style furnishing, lavish use of majolica tiles from Vietri sul Mare and fine marble, so typical of this region.

Spectacular views ov the Tyrrhenian sea can be enjoyed from all rooms and terraces. Defying the steep and rocky nature of the terrain, several terraces have been created in the picturesque garden with many stone steps leading to shady corners, charming terraces for your dinners al fresco and the pool area with a spacious sun terrace at the lower level.

The beautiful town of Amalfi is only a couple of miles away and the glittering reflections of the sun on its mosaic-clad cathedral dome can be spotted in the distance. Here the white-washed walls of the houses create a striking contrast with their colourful hand-painted tiles which can be purchased in many local hand-craft shops. Arguably, the best perspective from which to contemplate the beauty of the fishing villages scattered along the coast is from the sea. Boat excursions can be arranged on request to visit Ravello, Positano, Sorrento and the isles of Capri and Ischia. Hydrofoil transfers from Naples and Salerno are also available, as an appealing alternative to travelling by train or car.
