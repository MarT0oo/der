Villa Orta, Lake Orta, Italy Vacation Rental

 

FIRST FLOOR of 100 m2: room kitchen dining area, 1 double bedroom, 1 twin bedroom, 1 bathroom with Jacuzzi bathtub.

GROUND FLOOR of 100 m2: Comprised of a large country-style room a bathroom with shower and a kitchen. Large garden directly overlooking the lake. The guests have a garage available to them at the beginning of the pedestrian street.

The village where the Villa is located is one of the most beautiful villages in Italy: you enter the village by passing through elegant 17th-18th century palaces with loggias opening up the gardens sloping down towards the lake. The wrought-iron balconies on the old slate-roof houses are particularly charming.

The Villa is located just a short distance from the beginning of the village. The garden touches the shore of the lake and the private swimming pool looks like it flows into the lake’s water. The villa is furnished with quality furniture and is equipped with car park, WI-Fi, satellite TV, Internet connection, washing machine. The guests have a garage available to them at the entrance to the town so that they are free to move around as they like.
