Villa Mon Repos, Positano, Amalfi Coast, Italy Vacation Rental

 

A stunning location with a postcard-perfect view: Villa Mon Repos welcomes you to 800 square meters of pure luxury and beauty for a once-in-a-lifetime holiday on Italy's most breathtaking stretch of coast.

Just a short walk from the center of Positano and the Spiaggia Grande beach, with spacious interiors and comfortable living areas, Villa Mon Repos is perfect for a holiday with family or a group of friends on the Amalfi Coast, boasting enough space for everyone to enjoy their privacy.

See the Best of Positano. The large terrace at Villa Mon Repos covers a full 200 square meters and is accessible from every level of the villa, so guests can enjoy one of the most exclusive views over Positano. In addition to the sun deck furnished with sun beds and tables and chairs, the terrace is home to the gorgeous rectangular overflow pool with a view, dramatically lit after sunset.

The interior design of Villa Mon Repos is elegantly sophisticated, with a refined décor featuring pieces of exquisite antique furniture, making a stay feel like living in a fine art gallery. On the first floor, guests have access to four bedrooms and bathroom, two large living areas, two fully equipped kitchens, and the gym. The first living room has a billiard table and opens onto a private terrace, which the second living room opens onto a second large terrace. The gym is equipped with a variety of machines to keep fit.

The second floor is split onto to levels: the first includes a living area, bathroom, and kitchen with direct access onto the large terrace with a pool. The second level can be reached with a flight of stairs and has two additional bedrooms and bathroom.

All the bedrooms boast a fantastic view over the pool, coastline, and Mediterranean Sea.
