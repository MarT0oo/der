Villa Sant’anna, Tuscany, Italy Vacation Rental

 

Perched on a ridge overlooking the Castiglion del Bosco golf course and Sangiovese vineyards, Villa Sant’Anna enjoys enchanting views from dawn to dusk. The home’s elegantly understated interiors are notable for their exquisite details, stonework and furnishings. The villa features a reading/home theater room as well as a private tennis court located within its extensive gardens. 655 sq. m. / 7,050 ft²

Sleeps up to nine guests. Main house ground floor: living room with genuine Tuscan fireplace; double bedroom with king-size bed, walk-in closet, en suite bathroom and terrace; single bedroom with extra-wide single bed and en suite bathroom; reading/home theater room; dining room; kitchen; powder room. First floor: king-bedded master bedroom with en suite bathroom; king-bedded double bedroom with en suite bathroom; and twin bedroom with two single beds and en suite bathroom (587 sq. m. / 6,318 ft²). Pool house: living room with fireplace, bathroom and outdoor living area with shady pergola (68 sq. m. / 732 ft²). Breakfast at Ristorante Campo del Drago €35 p.p. Private heated outdoor swimming pool. Outdoor wood oven. Flat-screen LCD televisions in the living room, reading room, bedrooms and pool house. Wi-Fi broadband Internet access. Tennis court. Air conditioning. Terrace with shady pergola
