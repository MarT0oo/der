Villa Bianca Grande, Menaggio, Lake Como, Italy Vacation Rental

 

Come and be marvelled by this 4 bed lake view jewel!

Overlooking the Central Lake and just 6 minutes’ drive from central Menaggio, this Wi-Fi-enabled retreat with private pool offers a carefree hillside haven for couples and flexible groups of 11.

With lake views, contemporary-style home comfort and all the must-have luxury features including a private outdoor pool, sauna, dining terrace and a separate TV room, the holiday Duplex promises a truly unforgettable year-round escape.

The ground floor kitchen diner leads down a few steps to a lovely open lake view living room with SAT TV. Made for effortless entertaining and home comfort whatever the season, the open plan layout is fully air-conditioned and benefits from a lovely ambient fireplace as well as direct access out to a sunny private lake view terrace. A fabulous Movie room also features to the lower ground floor.

The 4 lake view bedrooms and 4 modern bathrooms (1 with Jacuzzi) offer both a versatile dose of family-friendly and couple-friendly appeal; with 2 Double lake view bedrooms, a Twin Joinable lake view bedroom, a spacious child-friendly lake view bedroom for 3 and a Double sofa bed to the lower ground floor TV room.

Outside, a sunny private garden lets you kick off and relax in style. A covered decked poolside terrace invites guests to uniwnd here throughout the day, whilst a lovely outdoor pool is sure to be a big hit with everyone. The flexible holiday retreat further benefits from a private parking space and a garage.

From its conveniently-placed hillside location along the main road, guests are within easy reach of the Menaggio & Cadenabbia Golf Club, the Loveno Tennis Club, some highly-recommended local Agroturismo restaurants and the many amenities and ferry links of Menaggio (6 minutes’ drive away).

Active fans can enjoy close proximity to the surrounding hiking routes, horse riding centres of Porlezza, the lake swimming spots, Lido di Menaggio and kayak and power boat hire opportunities of Menaggio, as well as the sailing and wind surfing clubs of nearby Cremia, Dongo, Domaso and Gravedona.

The perfect starting point for exploring the Central Lake; guests can easily reach Varenna, Bellagio, the villa attractions, Tremezzina hiking routes, Piani di Bobbio ski resort and the shore of Lake Lugano.
