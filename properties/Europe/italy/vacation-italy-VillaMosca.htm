Villa Mosca, Alghero, Sardinia, Italy Vacation Rental

 

Villa Mosca Charming House is embedded in a unique location. Charm, history and culture. Spectacular views and lush grounds along Dante promenade in Alghero. The Charming House is a few steps from the sea and from the old town, protected by ancient ramparts. The sea view, the breathtaking panorama on Capo Caccia promontory and on the surrounding villas add a relaxing atmosphere to Villa Mosca Charming House.

Hotel
Villa Mosca Charming House is a prestigious residence, characterized by an octagonal shape and large windows. It is an historic property safeguarded by cultural and architectural assets. Art nouveau style, featuring soft lines, floral elements and whiplash motifs match perfectly with services you would expect of a high-quality hotel Design.

Rooms
The 9 rooms at the Villa Mosca Charming House are remarkable because of their style and comfort; these are subdivided in the Comfort, Superior Sea Side, Superior and Deluxe categories. All rooms dispose of a bathroom with shower, air conditioning, telephone, hair drier, Sat TV "32, mini fridge, wi-fi, a safe deposit box and a courtesy toiletry set. Two sunbeds will be reserved for guests in the grounds. The Superior and Deluxe offer also a sea view.

The Villini accomodations are instead ideal for those who prefer a personal living room to discuss business and a fully equipped kitchenette at your leisure. If necessary, a sofa bed can accommodate a third person. Surrounded by the greenery and about 20 meters away from the Villa, these independent buildings dispose of the same comfort of the rooms.

Restaurant
A rich breakfast buffet is served in the relaxing public rooms of the Villa Mosca Charming House. You can choice sweet and savory dishes in a welcoming and elegant atmosphere. From April to October there is the possibility to reserve a table for lunch and dinner at the exclusive restaurant where you can enjoy the speciality of the house in a relaxing and unique atmosphere.

Services & Activities
At disposal the reading room and lounge bar, gym, jacuzzi and free internet wi-fi throughout the hotel. In the grounds that surround Villa Mosca Charming House are placed some sunbeds and, on request, you can arrange business meetings and weddings.

Cost of the stay includes:

• welcome drink and buffet at the arrival;
• hotel staff at complete disposal for the whole stay;
• international buffet breakfast, is served from 7.30 to 10.30 and offers fabulous homemade cakes and muffins, eggs and bacon, local cheeses and sausages, fresh fruit and more;
• the use of the Jacuzzi and solarium (temperature 36°C) in the park;
• internet wi-fi in the common areas and rooms;
• bike rental.
