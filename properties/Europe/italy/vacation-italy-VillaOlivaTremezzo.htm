Villa Oliva Tremezzo, Lake Como, Italy Vacation Rental

 

With its magical mountain view setting and enviable central lake location, this 6 bed 4 bath villa in Tremezzo promises an exclusive taste of Lake Como luxury! Boasting its own private pool and garden, the beautifully presented modern villa provides a flexible and carefree setting for year-round stays.

The stylish open plan living room features a sumptuous L-shaped sofa with a large SAT TV, free Wi-Fi access and an ambient fireplace to cosy up to. Adjoining this restful scene is a sleek dining area for 8 and an accompanying Kitchen Diner for 4. Alfresco dinner parties also await your enjoyment, outside on the private covered poolside terrace.

Benefitting from 6 flexible Double bedrooms (3 walk-through) and 4 modern bathrooms, the layout offers that welcoming feel of home. The family-friendly villa also benefits from private laundry facilities and a private garage and parking space.

Situated just a 3 minutes’ drive or a 10 minutes’ stroll away from the lakefront, the very best of Tremezzo is close by on the doorstep. From its authentic Italian restaurants and ambient wine bars to its scenic walking routes, popular Lidos, lake swimming spots, famous Villa attractions and carefree ferry links; Tremezzo offers a classic as well as vibrant holiday ambience. The famous resort also offers close proximity to neighbouring Cadenabbia (for Villa Carlotta and the Central Lake ferry stop), Lenno’s Villa Balbianello and Lido attraction (7 minutes’ away), the famous L’ Isola Comacina attraction (12 minutes’ away) and also the family-friendly Lido, sport activities and timeless resort appeal found in Menaggio (13 minutes’ away).

Perfect for active guests, the convenient Central Lake stay places guests within enviable reach of hiking, cycling, tennis, golf, water sports, power boat rental, sailing, extreme sports and Italian Alp skiing opportunities.

Its desirable central western shore location also promises easy access to Como (49 minutes), Lugano (53 minutes) and Milan (82 minutes).
