Villa Magia, Positano, Amalfi Coast, Italia Vacation Rental

 

Climb the famous Pyramid of Positano along winding staircases and characteristic narrow lanes lined with small boutiques and spectacular sea view to Villa Magia, where you'll find one of the most enchanting vantage points on the whole of the Amalfi Coast.

Three Floors of Beauty

Three floors of luxury and beauty in a former 18th century watchtower, Villa Magia can host ten guests in the sweeping 5000 square feet property which boasts flowering gardens and panoramic terraces overlooking Positano.

The main floor is devoted to the common rooms, including an indoor kitchen and a large outdoor kitchen with barbecue, pizza oven, a small bath, and a wonderful orchid terrace; a large living room with fireplace, and a modern dining room elegantly decorated with artwork and elegant furnishings.

On the upper level, two of the property's four airy suites await: luxurious, spacious rooms with private ensuite bathrooms and independent terraces, furnished in a contemporary style.

Beneath the main floor, the tower level is the original and oldest part of the villa, once a watchtower guarding against Saracen pirate attacks.

Here you'll find two suites furnished in the traditional Positano style, another fully equipped kitchen with a living area, and a wonderful dining patio with a gorgeous sea view. From here, guests can directly reach the swimming pool area located on a magnificent terrace with sea view. Are you ready to take a dip surrounded by the beauty of Positano?
