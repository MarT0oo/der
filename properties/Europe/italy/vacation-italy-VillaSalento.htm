Villa Salento, Salento, Puglia, Italy Vacation Rental

 

The Trulli stand out like stone tents in the green landscape of olive trees, exhibiting their white pinnacles and displaying mysterious signs on the convex side of their cones: crosses, crescents, stars and angels protecting the hearth – ancestral traditions of an agricultural and deeply spiritual culture, profoundly linked to natural symbolism. The trulli have been declared a Unesco World heritage site and are the most exciting discovery in a land that has such a rich architectural and environmental identity.

This Trullo, located in the heart of the Salento region, was designed for those who want to escape the hustle and bustle of city life and stay in an exclusive oasis where sun, pure air, luxury and relaxation reign supreme. The property is a Trullo composed of 11 cones. Inside there are four double bedrooms, three living rooms and two kitchens. The interiors have modern style and top designer furniture. This exclusive environments recreate a homely atmosphere, with an extra touch of elegance and constant attention to details.

Outdoors, it is surrounded by a typical maquis shrubland oasis with more than 800 trees: secular olive trees, as well as almond, apple, pear, citrus trees and many more. There is also a pool with a relaxation area. On the left and right side of the Trullo there are two stone courtyards which are used as outdoor lounge areas with direct acces from the Trullo, where guests can relax in an intimate atmosphere. The Two courtyards are furnished with sofas, tables, sun umbrellas and tv.

The Trullo has been designed to guarantee utmost privacy and functionality. In fact, a section of the Trullo, despite being part of the same building, has a private entrance, ideal for those who prefer going on holiday with their own domestic stuff.
