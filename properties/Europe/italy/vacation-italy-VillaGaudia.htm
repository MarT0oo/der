Villa Gaudia, Florence, Tuscany, Italy Vacation Rental

 

Come enjoy Villa Gaudia in the heart of Tuscany. Surrounded by vineyards and olives farms, the silence and tranquility will be the background noise of your holiday. This 5-acre estate is located about 30 minutes southeast of Florence; Perfect countryside spot to host your events, festivals, private parties, weddings, and receptions. Can accommodate hundreds of guests. Accommodations for additional guests can be found just a short, 10-minute, drive away.

Set in San Donato in Collina, Villa Gaudia’s decor is Tuscan charm blended with a dash of country chic. Warm and welcoming! Bedrooms are spacious, each features its own private terrace to combine comfort and privacy. Let yourself be served breakfast on the pool terrace, swim in the largest private pool in Italy (300 square meters with 750,000 liters of water) with a reverse negative edge. Chill out under the pergola by the pool, lounge on sumptuous soft white sofas, sipping your cocktail of choice! The pool is seasonal from May 18th thru September 30th. Play a game of tennis and relax in the five-person, 32 jet Jacuzzi. If you are interested in learning to make homemade pasta, ask us about a cooking class (ravioli, tagliatelle, lasagna, tortellini).

There is an on-site restaurant serving a variety of Italian dishes. Luxury shopping at The Mall is nearby. Local farms feature wine and cheese tastings.
