Colosseo View, Rome, Italy Vacation Rental

 

This luxurious 3-bedroom vacation apartment, the Colosseo View, is located near the many historical and cultural attractions of the Piazza Venezia and Roman Forum neighborhoods. Just steps from the apartment is the Roman Colosseum (1 min), the Roman Forum (7 min), Piazza Venezia (12 min), and Circus Maximus (11 min). The flat is a 6-minute walk from the Colosseo Metro station, and 1 minute from the Celio Vibenna bus station.

Refurbished by our talented interior designer, the Colosseo View combines Roman elegance with the comforts of home. Rich wooden flooring, a warm, inviting color scheme, and upscale furnishings create a sumptuous Italian ambiance. A fully-equipped kitchen provides for all your culinary needs. Enjoy a relaxed evening on the patio, with an unbeatable view of the Colosseum. Premium amenities include free WiFi, cable TV, hotel beds and linens, luxury toiletries, and a Nespresso machine.

Perfect for business travelers, a family, or group of friends, this magnificent apartment offers guests an exquisite home in ancient Rome.
