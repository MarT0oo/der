Villa Affresco, Positano, Amalfi Coast, Italy Vacation Rental

 

A stirring and elegant atmosphere just a few minutes from the main beach, Villa Affresco is an 18th-Century historical villa in Positano ideal for those wishing to spend a completely relaxing holiday with all the mod cons on the Amalfi coast.

The splendid solarium garden with “Ulisse” bar and “Afrodite” swimming pool, elegant drawing room with open-plan kitchen and terrace decorated with period amphorae make Villa Affresco in Positano the fairy-tale location for those wishing to hold their wedding in Positano.

The fabulously elegant suites with all mod cons and overlooking the sea ensure guests total privacy .

Choosing Villa Affresco in Positano for your holidays and weddings means you will be steeped in a truly unique historical atmosphere.

Villa Affresco in Positano on the Amalfi coast is a wonderful 18th-Century historical home for unforgettable holidays and events.

The villa has a total area of 750 sq.m on three floors, all of which overlooking the sea and Positano bay:

The main floor, leads to the large drawing room with period furniture and genuine masters.

This floor has the suite brothers Montgolfier.

All the rooms on this floor lead to the villa’s large terrace, which is an excellent location for breakfast, lunch and dinner.

The upper floor has 4 bedroom suites overlooking the sea and with balconies opening onto a long terrace with a breathtaking view.

The suites have frescoed domes with original paintings, period furniture, marble fireplaces, doors painted by artists from the School of Naples and terracotta floors with ceramic decorations. all the bathrooms have been restored recently and are decorated with antique tiles.

The “Romeo and Juliet” suite with an independent entrance located on the lower floor has an enchanting atmosphere, which can be experienced by looking out over the balcony of the suite to admire the wonderful view of Positano and the elegant “Afrodite” swimming pool.

The floor with the swimming pool also has a solarium with showers, bar, and dressing rooms with toilet.

This property has all the mod cons: swimming pool, telephone, fax, wi-fi, independent heating, air conditioning, high-quality linen, dvd player, stereo, satellite tv, ice-maker, washing machine, dryer, hair-dryer, dish-washer, freezer and fully-equipped professional kitchen.
