La Vallata, Lucignano, Tuscany, Italy Vacation Rental

 

Holiday villa in Tuscany near Arezzo - with its beautiful hillside position and panoramic views across the unspoilt Val di Chiana countryside, La Vallata offers an ideal base if you wish to experience the very best that Tuscany has to offer.

Comprising two buildings, the main villa sleeping eight and a smaller annex close by sleeping four, this Tuscan accommodation offers everything you need for a really relaxing holiday. La Vallata is full of classic Tuscan charm, with beamed ceilings, tiled floors and elegant décor. The properties have recently been restored and the thoughtful owner has included various modern conveniences, ensuring guests enjoy a very comfortable stay. You can enjoy fantastic views over the Valdichiana valley from the pool, and there are also lovely views of the nearby walled town of Lucignano.

The whole region is steeped in history. Atmospheric Lucignano, where you will find restaurants, shops and a local food market on Thursdays, is on your doorstep, as are other quintessential Italian hilltop towns such as Cortona, Montepulciano and Arezzo, just twenty minutes away. Siena, Florence and Lake Trasimeno (for water sports and lakeside beaches) would also make easy and very rewarding day trips from this holiday villa in Tuscany.
