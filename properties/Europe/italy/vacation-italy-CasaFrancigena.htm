Casa Francigena, Montaione , Tuscany, Italy Vacation Rental

 

Montaione Luxury villa - Casa Francigena enjoys an enviable location in the heart of the Tuscan countryside. Surrounded by its own vineyards and olive groves, this elegant two-storey villa has a history dating back 1,000 years to its origins as a cloister on the Via Francigena, the ancient road linking Rome and France.

Beautifully restored by its current owners, the villa offers very comfortable accommodation in a tranquil setting. Two flights of stone steps lead down from the house to the large swimming pool, where you can enjoy the sun or sit in the shade of the mature trees. An attractive loggia for summer dining overlooks the extensive lawns surrounding the pool.

The nearest shops and restaurants are to be found just ten minutes from this luxury villa - in the small town of Montaione. Ideal destinations for day trips would include cities of major historic importance such as Florence, Siena and Pisa, all of which are within an hour's drive, as well as the smaller, charming hilltop towns of San Gimignano, Volterra and Certaldo
