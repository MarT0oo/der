Le Torri di Bagnara, Umbria, Perugia, Italy Vacation Rental

 

Le Torri di Bagnara Medieval Historic Villas is an elegant resort on an estate of about 600 hectares in the heart of Umbria, near Perugia, the splendid city of art, and from its hill dominates a large stretch of the Alta Valle del Tevere, also called “Valle Museo” (Museum Valley), because of its beauty, history and culture, dotted with castles, abbeys and medieval villages.
The resort has also been created out of a picturesque complex of ancient buildings dating back to the end of the 11th century.

The Tower, the Pieve San Quirico Abbey, a 17th century villa and the fantastic panoramic swimming pool are set among floral parks and large terraced gardens full of lavender, rosemary and broom bushes, fig, laurel, peach, pomegranate, cherry and olive trees, which all form a luxuriant framework for this unique and exclusive oasis.

The refined structure and class welcome characterise this comfortable residence which, set in an enchanting out-of-this-world scenario, offers all the comforts and luxury of a 5-star hotel.

The elegant, light apartments of various dimensions are all situated in the Tower, while the bedrooms and suites are in the Abbey and have authentic period furniture and ceilings with wooden beams.

Added to the ample choice of activities on the estate are a stables, golf course, tennis courts and covered swimming pool just 5km distance.

Perugia

Situated in Perugia, this guesthouse is in the same region as Church of San Michele Arcangelo, Arco Etrusco, and Oratorio di San Bernardino. Regional attractions also include Cathedral of S. Lorenzo and Fontana Maggiore.

Free shuttle, breakfast

In addition to a restaurant, Le Torri Di Bagnara features an outdoor pool. Other amenities include a bar/lounge and a rooftop terrace.

Views of courtyard

In addition to flat-panel televisions with satellite channels, guestrooms include refrigerators. Bathrooms come with rainfall showerheads and makeup/shaving mirrors.
