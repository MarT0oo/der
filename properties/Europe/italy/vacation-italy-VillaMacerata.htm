Villa Macerata, Macerata, Italy Vacation Rental

 

Located in a commanding position over the ancient Town of Tolentino, in the heart of Marche, this Villa has stunning views over the undulating countryside, the Adriatic coast on one side and the Sibillini mountains on the other.

Recently restored to the splendour of the original 19th century building, the restoration has captured the essence of its original purpose, that of a holiday Villa fro the aristocracy of Turin. The Villa with its luxurious accommodation for 10 people sits within a discreet and private level garden with its centuries-old trees and new swimming pool. One intriguing feature of the Villa besides its stunning architectural form is the small private chapel now fully restored.

With its classical elegance and the luxury style of the interiors and furnishings, the Villa provides an exclusive location of sophistication and charme surrounded as it is by over 10 hectares of green and lush woodland.

Grounf Floor: hall, fully equipped kitchen with direct access to the garden, dining room (sits for 12), living room with fireplace, bathroom with anteroom, fully restored Chapel.

First Floor: living room with fireplace, double bedroom with ensuite bathroom, other two double bedrooms, bathroom with anteroom, stair, laundry, a double bedroom with two single beds.

Second Floor: Double bedroom (50 sqm) with ensuite bathroom.

Outdoor you can find balconies, covered loggias, private swimming pool, garden furnitures, 6000 sqm level garden and the parking.
