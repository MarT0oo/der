Villa Margherita, Impruneta, Tuscany, Italy Vacation Rental

 

Villa Margherita is a 300 sqm (3230 sqft) Tuscan villa, dating back to the 17th century, built entirely using ancient terracotta and stones. It was purchased in 1963, by the abstract painter Francesco Clemente, whose artwork can still be admired inside the estate.

The villa is situated in Impruneta, just 1.5 km (half mile) from the center of the town, on a hillock that dominates the Chianti hills, and 12 km (8 miles) from Florence.

In front of the main entrance to the house, there is a beautiful covered patio with dining table and a living area with bamboo furniture. On the backside of the villa, there is an open-air dining area with a round table in stone, situated in front of a swimming pool surrounded by laurel and cypress hedges.

Villa Margherita is located in the Tuscan Chianti countryside, surrounded by 20 acres of land, which is cultivated with vines and olive trees; we produce San Giovese and Trebbiano wine and Extra Virgin Olive oil.

On the ground floor, there is the dining room, a double living room with fireplace, a typical fully equipped Tuscan kitchen, and a double bedroom with bathroom en-suite with independent entrance.

On the first floor, there are three double bedrooms, 3 en- suite bathrooms, and a closet room. In the tower, there is a single bedroom with twin size bed

The guest house, which is next to the villa, consists of a double bedroom with bathroom en-suite, and can be rented separately by the villa guests, in case they need an additional room. Including the guest house, the villa has a total of 5 double bedrooms, 5 bathrooms en suite, 1 single bedroom, and it can therefore accommodate up to 11 guests.

Villa Margherita is available for short term rentals of minimum a week, preferably from Saturday to Saturday, from April to October, and of minimum 4 nights, from November to March.

The villa can also host private events, meetings and weddings.
