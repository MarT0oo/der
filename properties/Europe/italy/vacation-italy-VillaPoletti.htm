Villa Poletti, Bellagio, Lake Como, Italy Vacation Rental

 

Villa Poletti is a wonderful property located in Bellagio, the Pearl of Lake Como, and close to the small hamlet of San Giovanni. From the property you can enjoy beautiful views of the lake from different corners and the small outside garden terrace is the perfect place to relax outside, enjoy a glass of red wine and take in those breathtaking views!

From the property you can reach the centre of Bellagio by car and it is just 5 minutes away and here you can have everything you might need readily available from grocery shops to restaurants, bars, and pharmacy. From the villa itself you can also walk down to the small harbour of San Giovanni to enjoy the lake or take the small local boat that goes to Bellagio or down to Como.

If you are interested in sports the area is perfect for hiking, biking, walking and doing water sports! If you instead prefer to visit the local towns a tour of the lake is for sure a must and you can discover the hidden treasures of this beautiful corner such as Villa Balbianello and Villa Carlotta.
