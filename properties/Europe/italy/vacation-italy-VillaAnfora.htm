Villa Anfora, Molin Nuovo, nr Arezzo, Tuscany, Italy Vacation Rental

 

Traditional country farmhouse holiday on the Tuscan/ Umbrian border – Villa Anfora is a delightful period country house, very tastefully decorated, full of character and authentic charm. Enjoying a peaceful hilltop location, overlooking verdant valleys, this villa is surrounded by a picturesque landscape of rolling hills, unspoilt woodland and quaint hamlets.

At Villa Anfora guests will find the perfect balance between a genuine rural atmosphere, the legacy of its farming past, and all modern comforts, including a real Italian bar with an espresso/cappuccino machine. The friendly owners, who reside in the vicinity, will be happy to introduce you to an array of local organic delicacies and excellent wines from this generous land.

Thanks to its enviable location on the border between Tuscany and Umbria, some of the most beautiful towns of central Italy are within easy reach of this traditional country farmhouse. The attractive hilltop village of Monterchi is nearby, and Anghiari, another atmospheric little town in this area, is considered one of the "most beautiful villages in Italy". Make sure you find some time to visit the nearby medieval town of Arezzo, famous for its gold jewellery and precious artistic masterpieces.And just on the other side of the Appennine mountains is the Marche region, where you can visit the lovely Renaissance town of Urbino and the Republic of San Marino.
