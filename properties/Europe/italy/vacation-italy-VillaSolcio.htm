Villa Solcio, Lake Maggiore, Italy Vacation Rental

 

Peacefully located in the private residential area of Solcio di Lesa, this villa is quite simply one of the finest holiday villas to be found on Lake Maggiore, Italy.

This luxurious, waterfront property is the perfect place to relax and enjoy your vacation in one of the most beautiful locations in the Italian lake district. It provides a modern, stylish and totally exclusive environment in which to rel

ax with friends or family. Designed to appeal to those who appreciate the finer things in life, the villa offers all the creature comforts of home and much, much more. The luxury vacation property accommodates up to 10 people:

The main villa (350m²) sleeps 8 people in four large double bedrooms, fully air conditioned, and each with telephone, electronic safe and en-suite bathroom. In addition to the main villa, an independent guest cottage (50 m²) with 2 single beds, kitchen and bathroom is available at extra charge.

The interior has been designed in a high quality Italian style with a beautiful living room including cosy couches and antique fireplace, a dining area, a large fully-equipped kitchen and a private fitness room. The children will love the upstairs mansard with sitting area, junior billiard table, electronic piano and numerous games. The house provides all modern comforts including satellite TV, DVD player, telephone and wireless internet. Outside you will find a large covered terrace with outdoor furniture, ready for enjoying 'al fresco' dining with spectacular views over the lake.

This idyllic lakefront estate is a private paradise set within a large park (80,000 m²) which extends right up to the shores of Lake Maggiore, where privacy, tranquillity and relaxation are guaranteed. The grounds offer direct lake access, heated swimming pool, tennis court and a 9-hole golf course. Bikes, canoes, motorboat and a sailing dinghy are also available for use. In addition to all this, it’s possible to go waterskiing or book a sight-seeing trip with the owner in his private helicopter, sailing yacht (15m) or vintage wooden motorboat (12m). A private chef and massage service are also available upon request. See the rental conditions for all available services.

The friendly owners live in a second house in the same resort, approximately 50m from the villa. They are ready to meet every demand and to offer you the finest service while at the same time respecting your privacy: they will not interfere with your stay but will be happy to provide you with tips and local information about the region.

The private residential area of Solcio di Lesa, lies half-way between Arona (7 km) and Stresa (10 km), and is the ideal starting point for visiting Lake Maggiore and the surrounding countryside. Stresa is the most popular tourist town of Lake Maggiore with its many shops, restaurants and a beautiful lakeside promenade. From there you can hop on any ferry and explore the lake, its magical islands and the natural beauty in both Italy and Switzerland. Easy day trips are possible to the cities of Milan, Locarno and Lugano, the famous Piedmont vineyards, the roman tic Lake Orta and the nearby mountain valleys.
