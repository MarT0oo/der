TUSCANY AND THE VAL D´ ORCIA

Tuscany and the val d ‘Orcia, Italy´s golden heart, is associated with the essential Italy: a unique mixture of the picturesque terracotta colored villages, olive groves and Cyprus lined roads, bathing in the mythical Tuscan light.

If you choose Tuscany as your holiday destination it will be difficult to avoid the Val d´Orcia. The valley is surrounded by seven Etruscan, Roman, medieval and Renaissance towns, which together with the undisturbed environs, have been declared a national park and listed on Unesco´s world heritage list.
