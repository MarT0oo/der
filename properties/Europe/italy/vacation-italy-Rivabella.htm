Rivabella, Cortona, Tuscany, Italy Vacation Rental

 

Holiday property near Lake Trasimeno Italy – From its hilltop position above Lake Trasimeno, this luxurious country farmhouse commands striking views over the blissful Umbrian and Tuscan countryside, especially at sunset when dramatic red and gold skies are reflected in the waters of the lake, with the added benefits of being within walking distance of the local trattoria.

Rivabella is a tasteful renovation offering the original charm of natural stone and thick chestnut beams, combined with stylish interiors and all modern comforts, including air conditioning, hammam, Jacuzzi and chromo-therapy showers using the latest light technology to create a most relaxing experience. This is an ideal setting for holidays surrounded by unspoilt countryside and genuine Italian culture. Your dinners al fresco, evening cocktails and lazy morning breakfasts can be enjoyed under the gazebo, with stunning views of the lake setting the perfect scene.

The surrounding area is one of the most beautiful in central Italy and yet off the beaten track of mass tourism. As well as a fully equipped beach at Lake Trasimeno, just a couple of miles from Rivabella, there are many towns and villages with historic interest, artistic value and gastronomic delight. Within the Umbrian border you will find Perugia, whose summer Jazz Festival is renowned all around the world, Spoleto, Assisi and Todi, rich in Medieval charm. Within the Tuscan border are Cortona, Montepulciano, Montalcino, Siena and Florence, all within easy reach of this Italian vacation villa.
