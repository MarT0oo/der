Villa Medici, Florence, Tuscany, Italy Vacation Rental

 

Villa Medici, located at the very heart of the Chianti region, crowns a hill with magnificent views over Florence, which is just 15 minute drive away. It is both a wine and olive estate and a hospitality business open for vacationers and corporate executives for short and extended stays, and for corporate and leisure events including weddings. The Malenchini family, the owners of the estate, have carefully and tastefully carried out a restoration project that has brought the Villa Medici and the wine estate into the new millennium. The estate offers authentic Tuscan ambience, with bespoke personalized experiences and modern comforts and amenities.

Social and corporate events The perfect combination of Medici architecture and the latest technology, Villa Medici, offers customized and flexible solutions for events, ranging from the newly refurbished Limonaia Banquet Hall to historic rooms, beautiful courtyards and gardens. Services include Wi-Fi, state-of-the-art lighting systems, air conditioning and heating. The estate's venues are the ideal locations for dream weddings, corporate meetings, conferences, product launches, photo and video shoots.

Villa Medici offers stylish self-catered accommodation. All suites and villas are completely renovated with all the modern comforts, such as air conditioning, Wi-Fi, two new swimming pools, flat screen TV, laptop-size safes. La Corte Villa - 5 ensuite bedrooms With generous room for up to 10 (+6) people, La Corte Villa is right at the heart of Villa Medici: next to the Medici Villa and courtyard, and adjacent to the Limonaia Banquet Hall, our reception point and other event venues. So if you’re staging an event at Villa Medici, you can book La Corte Villa to accommodate your most important guests close to the heart of the action. When it’s not booked for an event, you can choose any of its five suites. La Torre Villas - 5 individual villas (from1 to 3 bedrooms each) A short walk from the center of Villa Medici, La Torre Villas are private and amidst a typical Tuscan village environment. The villas, large communal terrace and swimming pool all have spectacular views of Florence and its Duomo, and the Chianti countryside. Each villa is self-contained, peaceful and comfortable, ideal for holidays or for business executives. It’s also possible to rent villas together for groups.

Authentic experiences on and off our estate Vacationers can tailor everything to their ideal break. Villa Medici offers unique opportunities designed exclusively around special requests and can arrange customized activities. Innovative and contemporary ideas come together with history to provide customers with customized and flexible solutions in an elegant and informal style. Experiences includes Wine Tastings, Cookery courses, Massages, Yoga classes, City tours and visits to art collections, palaces, gardens normally not open to the public. The epitome of Tuscan lifestyle and casual sophistication, Villa Medici is a prestigious, historic private family estate, which you can share and enjoy. The tranquil terraced gardens, courtyards and swimming pools are surrounded by 70 hectares of vineyards and olive groves. From this oasis of calm nestling in the hills of Chianti Colli Fiorentini, it‘s hard to believe you are only 14km and 15 minute drive from the center of Florence, and 25 minutes from Amerigo Vespucci Florence airport.

When you stay at Villa Medici, we want you to feel at home. So our personalized services ensure everything is comfortable, convenient and just how you like it.

We invite you to stay at Villa Medici any time of the year, and for as long as you wish. Secluded and relaxing yet only 15 minute drive from the center of Florence, and 25 minutes from its airport, it’s perfect for a weekend break, a longer holiday or a home-away-from-home extended stay for the business executive.

There are six villas for self-catering, with a range of sizes to suit your needs. Each historic, building has been tastefully restored and refurbished to include air-conditioning, heating and Wi-Fi, with fully equipped bathrooms and kitchens, and contemporary Tuscan interior design.

You can rent a villa exclusively for your use, or simply rent a bedroom and share your villa with like-minded guests who also appreciate the fine food and wine, fascinating history and leisure possibilities of this area.

You can rent our beautiful halls, courtyards, terraces and gardens individually or in your preferred combination. The newly restored Limonaia Banquet Hall also has disabled access, and we have a central Reception and Meeting Point where your guests can gather.

Villa Medici is only minutes away from the center of Florence. As the former capital of Italy, it has excellent links with the rest of the country by road, rail and air. Close to the beating heart of Florence, and at the same time surrounded by Chianti’s peaceful, rolling hills, you have the best of both worlds – city buzz and country calm.

Whether your visit is for leisure or business, Villa Medici brings you the best of the authentic Tuscan lifestyle.
