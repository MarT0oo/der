Villa Fiore Bianco, Santa Margherita di Pula, Sardinia, Italy Vacation Rental

 

Villa Fiore Bianco is set among a thick and luxuriant pinewood along a white and fine sandy strip in Santa Margherita di Pula, the beach area of the lively town of Pula in south Sardinia. The villa is new and immaculate, its shape resembles an old times countryside church with soft and round shapes. Eclectic in its design, décor and fittings it strikes for its harmonious shapes and its glamorous like feeling, a true blend of architectural beauty and simplicity.

The villa is on two floors and features a fully equipped kitchen, dining and living area, 4 double bedrooms with en-suite bathrooms. The outdoor is generous with covered veranda and portico fully equipped with sofas, lounge chairs, cushions and four posters sundecks. Beautiful manicured garden with palm trees, outdoor dining equipped area with barbecue, walking distance to an enchanting white and fine sandy beach.
