Villa del Pavone, Praiano, Amalfi Coast, Italy Vacation Rental

 

Luxury Amalfi Coast villa with pools and sea view - Perched on a cliff top on the edge of the charming village of Praiano, between Amalfi and Positano, this exclusive villa boasts two heated pools, garden terraces and ensuite bedrooms with sea views, each one equipped with all modern comforts. All this within the spectacular setting of the Tyrrhenian sea and a lush Mediterranean garden, just minutes away from the local shops, restaurants and beach.

The extensive renovation carried out in recent years has revived the former splendour of this XVIII century aristocratic residence where contemporary design and silk soft furnishing seamlessly blend with antiques and period furniture under high vaulted ceilings and hand-painted archways. The interior is light and airy, with whitewashed walls and cool ceramic floors throughout; the accommodation arranged over three floors has natural sunlight and sea breezes flowing through tall arched windows. Take a cooling dip in the outdoor pool and sunbathe on the terrace overlooking the sea, or pamper yourself with aromatherapy, chromo-therapy and hydro-massage in the softly-lit indoor spa pool. Either way, you will be spellbound by Villa del Pavone’s magical atmosphere and the first class service offered by the staff. No dinner at a restaurant can beat a meal al fresco on the garden terrace within the intimacy of your private villa surrounded by scented lemon trees and bougainvillea whilst the sun sets over the sea.

The experience continues as you set off to discover many hidden treasures along the Amalfi Coast. Praiano is a picturesque fishing village just a short stroll away from this villa and very convenient for your daily shopping, for sampling the catch of the day and other local specialities, or simply for a day at the beach. Within a few miles are Amalfi and Positano, Ravello and Sorrento, with boats departing regularly to take visitors on exciting tours of the coast, Capri and the mesmerizing Grotta Azzurra. A little further afield is also the awe-inspiring Mount Vesuvio, towering over the archaeological Roman sites of Pompei and Ercolano. All in all, Villa del Pavone epitomizes the best of everything this region has to offer.
