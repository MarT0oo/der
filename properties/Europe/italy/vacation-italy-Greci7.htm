Greci 7, Via dei Greci Rome, Italy Vacation Rental

 

In the historic Spanish Steps neighborhood, the 2-bedroom Greci 7 vacation apartment puts you in the ancient heart of Rome. With the banks of the Tiber River on one side and the lush gardens of the Villa Medici on the other, this apartment will introduce you to the city’s most iconic sights. A 5-minute walk brings you to the central Piazza del Popolo, and the Spanish Steps are within a 5-minute walk in the other direction, with many other museums and historic sites along the way. The Spagna Metro is nearby.

Inspired by the rustic Italian countryside, our local interior designer has created a space that is both comfortable and elegant. Pastoral wicker and wooden accents give the apartment its character, and Renaissance-inspired wall art adds a funky touch. Two bedrooms and two sofa beds can accommodate up to 6 guests. Premium amenities include free Wi-Fi, cable TV, luxury toiletries, hotel beds and linens, and a Nespresso machine.

Perfect for families and business guests, this apartment brings Rome to your doorstep.
