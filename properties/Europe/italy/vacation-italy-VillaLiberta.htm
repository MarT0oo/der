Villa Liberta, Italian Lakes, Lake Como, Italy Vacation Rental

 

Lake Como luxury villa - This stately villa sits majestically on the shores of Lake Como, near the village of Limonta in the heart of the 'Oliveto Lario' just a short distance from the well known town of Bellagio. Dating back to the 19th century, the property has been built in a Liberty style and is surrounded by a landscaped park with century-old trees.

There are picture postcard views stretching away across the lake and inland, towards the Grigna mountain range, the Rhaetian Alps and towards the picturesque town of Varenna. Cherished for the past century by the owners, the villa also benefits from modern conveniences including air conditioning, Wi-Fi internet and a heated swimming pool. The end result is an exclusive residence and an ideal place to enjoy Lake Como in style.

There is a small trattoria within walking distance for a convenient meal out, whilst nearby is the well known town of Bellagio, with its historical centre to explore and a wide range of shops and restaurants. There is a local boat service in Bellagio that serves the numerous towns and villages around the lake, so this is a good starting point to explore Como itself. Further afield is Como town, Milan, Bergamo and Lugano, all within day trip range.
