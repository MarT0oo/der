Villa Larius, Lake Como, Italy Vacation Rental

 

A Secluded family retreat overlooking the beautiful Lake Como!

This luxuriously spacious and modern Villa located above Lenno offers guests a very special stay here on the Lake, offering a sunny lake view garden, large outdoor Swimming Pool and plenty of entertaining space.

This Villa offers guests a stunning Living area with comfortable seating, working fireplace, Satellite TV and DVD Player. The separate Dining room can seat upto 10 guests and is perfect for a large dinner party. There is a spacious fully fitted Kitchen also located on this ground floor.

The accommodation is very comfortable and spacious with charming beamed ceilings providing a unique atmosphere to the 5 Double Bedrooms and 5 fully equipped Bathrooms.

Outside a BBQ and eating area allows you to enjoy 'Alfresco' Dining whilst enjoying the sun, lake and mountains. The private Swimming pool is also located in the grounds and is perfect for a morning swim or Pool Party!

The Lakeside town of Lenno offers a rich heritage as well as many local amenities such as Cafe's, Bars, a Ferry and Bus Service, the popular Lido di Lenno Beach and Nightclub and not forgetting the famous Villa Balbianello, which can be approcahed by either walking throught the lush woodland gardens or by taking an enjoyable Taxi Boat ride.

The central Lake location also means that guests can enjoy easy access to the many local attractions and towns on offer, such as Tremezzo which boasts many Cafes, Bars and beautiful Villa Carlotta and Menaggio which offers a variety of Boutique shops and which runs a frequent Ferry Service to the other very attractive Lakeside towns of Bellagio and Varena.

For Golfers the world renowned Menaggio & Cadenabbia Golf Course is located just a 15 minute's drive from Lenno and boasts some of the most romantic scenery, due to its elevated position over the Lake.
