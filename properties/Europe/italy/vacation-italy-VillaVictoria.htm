Villa Victoria, Argegno, Lombardy, Lake Como, Italy Vacation Rental

 

This Villa is a newly renovated detached Rustico, located on the desirable Argegno hillside overlooking the central part of Lake Como.

This beautiful property enjoys a large garden with great facilities as well as flexible sleeping accommodation making this the ideal holiday home!

Inside, the property provides a very stylish and elegant interior offering a very spacious Living room which provides two very comfortable seating areas, one of which surrounds a romantic fireplace and the second of which features a large wall-mounted Flatscreen TV and large windows with panoramic mountain and partial Lake views.

There is a separate fully fitted Kitchen with a central Dining table seating upto 6 guests. Upstairs the accommodation is flexible and perfect for a family staying, with a spacious Master Double Bedroom, a second Bedroom with Bunkbeds and single Bed, a third Bedroom with twin single Beds and a fourth smaller separate sleeping area with a Futon for any additional guests.

This Villa also offers a modern and newly fitted Bathroom with Bathtub and Shower as well as a convenient second Toilet.

Outside guests can enjoy the well maintained and sunny Garden, which is mainly laid to lawn with a terraced area ideal for 'Alfresco' Dining, and guests can also enjoy the large unheated Swimming Pool. There is also adequate space in the grounds for Parking.

Access to the property is along a well-maintained secondary Road, which is located just 4 km from the Argegno Lakefront. The charming town of Argegno offers guests an interesting, and very Italian blend of medieval romanticism with modern amentities, which include trendy Restaurants and Bars.

A frequent Ferry service runs from the town offering a car-free alternative to exploring Lake Como if desired. The world famous Vista D’Este Golf course is also located just a 10 minute’s drive away in Cernobbio and the cosmipolitan Lakeside city of Como is just a 15 minute’s drive away, making Argegno a great place to both relax and enjoy your Lake Como holiday!

By booking this holiday home you will get free acces to the lido di Menaggio. The Lido di Menaggio has swimming pools, a restaurant, a bar/discotheque, a kids club, boat rentals, a sandy beach, kite surfing and various sportive activities to do.
