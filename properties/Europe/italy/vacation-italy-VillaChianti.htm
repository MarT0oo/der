Villa Chianti, Chianti, Italy Vacation Rental

 

The Villa is completely immersed in the Tuscan countryside of Chianti. It is surrounded by high density vineyard where the wines are produced. It is located between Siena and Arezzo, very close to charming little villages where it is possible to buy typical food. This amazing property was built in 1400 by De’ Medici Family.

It was restored in 2008 to allow all the best comforts to the guests. The Villa, about 1000 square meters is spread over two floors so distributed: the Ground Floor has an entrance hall with wardrobe, large kitchen with fireplace, pantry and adjacent terrace with a gazebo for outdoor dining and living room, moreover a suggestive loggia is facing the private swimming pool. Dining room, courtyard, large living room composed by fireplace room, piano room, tv lounge and bathroom.

The First floor encloses six large double bedrooms and suites with travertine bathrooms. Handmade wooden floor and air conditioning alla over the floor. Beautiful italian garden on two levels surrounded by the Tuscan countryside.

The upper garden has an historical path of plane trees, private chapel and large panoramic terrace overview the Chianti hills.The garden below has a private swimming pool with showers and laundry.
