La Casa Positano, Amalfi Coast, Italy Vacation Rental

 

La Casa Guest House & Villa is one of the most beautiful, alluring and oldest places here in Positano. Thanks to the ideal location, just in front of the sea, La Casa offers breathtaking views. As you enter the gates and walk down the curving stairway and to the lobby, you will enter a private oasis. A hidden gem hiding amidst the lush botanical garden severed by bouganville trees, hibiscus flowers, banana plants and tropical palm trees. A magnificent Jacuzzi (working from June to September) can be found hidden in a natural cave where you can relax your senses whilst enjoying the aromatic atmosphere.

La Casa is one of the few villas in Positano that can be reached from the main street with a short walk (120 meters) and just 18 steps and that's it! All of the 5 bedrooms have high vaulted ceilings with modern elegant fixtures, air conditioning, TV (with national channels) & private terrace. Each bedroom features its own private bathroom. The living room has FREE Wi-Fi, TV with Sky Cable (international channels), a sea-view terrace with a 100 year old brick pizza oven.

Just imagine yourself relaxing on the balcony, sipping a delicious drink, basking in the sun and soaking up the amazing view, all the while surrounded by a quiet and relaxing solarium. From La Casa you can easily reach one of the most famous beaches here in Positano by walking down a set of magical stairs(434). This beach is named Spiaggia di Fornillo.

The other renowned beach of Positano named Spiaggia Grande (Main Beach) and the Town Center is just 10 minutes by bus or 20 minutes by foot from the Villa. There are many great attractions nearby, including Church of Santa Caterina, restaurants, art galleries, boutique clothing stores, ceramic/wine bars and Positano's local delicatessen. Positano is a place not just to live your dream, but to live in a dream.

We invite you to come and be our guest to experience the wonders only Positano can offer.... “The crown jewel of the Amalfi coast!”
