Villas of Positano, Positano, Italy Vacation Rental

 

The Villas of Positano are situated in a wonderfully suggestive landscape with marvelous views and only a few minutes from the beach in Positano. It is the ideal location for your holidays on the Amalfi Coast, immersed in an atmosphere of peace and tranquillity. The Bougainville and other Mediterranean plants frame the terraces and the infinity pools shaded by the lemon trees, and the solarium facing the Isola Li Galli creates a magical dreamlike scenery for its guests.

The APARTMENTS AND LUXURY SUITES decorated in Mediterranean style all have large sun kissed terraces overlooking the sea and surrounded by plants of aloe, grape vines, jasmine and geraniums. This is the ideal place for those who wish to spend their holidays in peace, quiet and complete privacy.

Choosing one Suite or the entire Villa for your holiday or wedding is an excellent way of spending a fairy tale dream. The villa is bathed by the sun till sunset and offers its guests an unforgettable scenery to admire from its terraces and INFINITY POOLS. Sitting on a rock high above the sea and only a few minutes from the beach, the villa is built in the typical Positano style and is made up of apartments and suites equipped with every comfort.

It has four floors and large sun-trap terraces. It can host up to 30 people and is the ideal choice for a charming and relaxing holiday. On the higher floors of the villa there are the panoramic infinity pools for the exclusive use of the guests, a spa pool overlooking the sea for a relaxing hydro massage and the infinity bar for cocktails and drinks with a your own personal barman. A continental breakfast is also served every morning directly to the suites and apartments and there is a daily cleaning service.
