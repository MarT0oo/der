La Livunati, Vibonati, Cilento Coast, Italy Vacation Rental

 

Village house with pool near beaches - La Livunati is an elegantly furnished townhouse with light and spacious rooms, a beautiful pool and two generous south-west facing terraces with views across the valley to the Gulf of Policastro. Sandy beaches are just 2 miles away, and the stunning mountain scenery of the Cilento National Park is on your doorstep.

Impeccably presented, the villa offers generously proportioned rooms with tastefully selected furniture and high quality soft furnishings. On the ground floor the open-plan layout lends itself to relaxed, sociable living and an indoor/outdoor lifestyle: you also have additional space to relax with your family and friends on the top floor, as well as on both panoramic terraces. The views are lovely - a sweep of green mountains opposite with the village church to the north, and the sea - the stunning Bay of Policastro - to the south.

Popular with Italians in the summer, this part of Campania feels very relaxed and undemanding, but if you do want to get to know the area there is plenty here for all. Vibonati is on the edge of the Cilento National Park & Val di Diano (a UNESCO world heritage site) where hiking, canoeing and riding are all on offer. You can explore inland villages such as Teggiano, but don't miss the waterfalls at Casaletto Spartano, the Ancient Greek site of Velia (close to Ascea), the Certosa di Padula monastery or the spectacular caves at Pertosa. There are boat trips along the coast to see the sea caves (grotte), some of which easily stand comparison to their more famous counterpart at Capri. Fishing and night fishing trips are available too. And the Cilento coast is known for its long sandy beaches, many of which have Blue Flags. A holiday at La Livunati will certainly be one to remember for all the very best reasons.
