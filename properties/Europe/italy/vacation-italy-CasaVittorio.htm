Casa Vittorio, San Giovanni d'Asso, Tuscany, Italy Vacation Rental

 

Tuscan period villa rental - Centred around a pretty garden, Casa Vittorio is a notable, historic residence set over several levels, elegantly furnished with antiques and typical Tuscan furnishings. The house itself enjoys a village location with other properties in close proximity, but still retains its privacy.

There is an elegant Italian garden complete with lemon trees, a lawn and a good-sized pool with a terrace and sun loungers. The refrigerator by the pool means you can enjoy cool drinks whilst relaxing in the sun. Inside, the reception rooms are decorated in a classic Tuscan style and the bedrooms are comfortable and well furnished.

This ancient village with historical connections dating as far back as the Etruscans has a restaurant, café/bar, supermarket, bakery and butcher. In addition, the castle hosts a white truffle market each year. Slightly further afield is the town of San Quirico d' Orcia (less than 15 minutes by car), whilst Montalcino and Pienza are only about twenty minutes away.

Please note there is a low wall to the rear of the garden with a steep drop behind it; active toddlers and children need supervision.
