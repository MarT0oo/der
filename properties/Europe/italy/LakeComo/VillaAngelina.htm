Villa Angelina, Pianello Del Lario, Lake Como, Italy Vacation Rental

 

New stunning villa with pool and beach access in Pianella del Lario, Lake Como.

Villa Angelina is a lakefront property situated on the northwest shore of Lake Como. The villa is located in a unique setting and provides access to the beach. The beautiful exterior includes a heated swimming pool, a panoramic sun patio, a large private garden directly overlooking the lake promenade and the beach.

The interior design is quite precious: from the ground floor pavement in antique Lombardy terracotta tiles, to the stairs and first floor in chestnut wood, aged for 20 years before being treated and placed. The windows are all soundproofed for the perfect peace of the guests.

The villa (700 m²) can accommodate 12+2 people in 6 double bedrooms with 6 private bathrooms and an additional staff bedroom with dedicated bathroom. The villa with its spacious interior is the ideal holiday home for large parties, consisting of two floors (250 m² each) and a basement (200 m²) with laundry room.

There is a large and sunny living room with comfortable sofas and satellite TV, a dining room with an antique wooden table for up to 18 people, a cozy sitting room with a magnificent grand piano, a modern and fully equipped kitchen (2 ovens, one blast chiller, 2 dishwashers, a large fridge with freezer and a wide kitchen island), a large dining room with a wooden table for up to 18 people. A double bedroom, with en-suite bathroom, accessible by people with disabilities. On the back of the kitchen there’s a staff bedroom (9 m²) with a dedicated bathroom (6,50 m²).

Going up the stairs you’ll reach a sitting room with terrace and you’ll find the other 5 air conditioned bedrooms, each with private en-suite bathroom. All the beds of all the bedrooms can be double or twin.

From the entrance on the back of the villa, descending a few steps, you’ll find the laundry room, equipped with washing machine, dryer and a large sink.

The Villa Angelina is located directly on the shore of the lake, a few meters from a public pebble beach. In the large private garden (2500 m²) there is a swimming pool, heated from mid-May till mid-September (during the other seasons, it can be heated at an extra cost).

This villa lies less than 1km from the village of Pianello del Lario where you can walk to along the beautiful lakeside promenade. The village provides grocery shops, a restaurant, bank and post office. Just 10 km to the south is the beautiful lake town of Menaggio, which can also be reached by an enchanting ferry ride. The picturesque town of Bellagio, the “pearl of the lake”, and the mediaeval village of Varenna are both reached in 30 minutes by ferry. The neo-classical Villa Carlotta in Tremezzo, with its botanical garden can be reached in 20 minutes. Como, with its historic town centre, elegant shops, restaurants, cafes, a panoramic promenade, ferry port, and a railway station where you can catch a train to Milan, is 45 km away.
