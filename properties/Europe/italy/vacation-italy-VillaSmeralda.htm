Villa Smeralda, Porto Cervo, Sardinia, Italy Vacation Rental

 

Aspire to a sensational luxury villa in Porto Cervo where to experience unspoilt nature and unwind; a true inspirational place for soul and mind affording breath-taking views over the bay of Cala di Volpe, private and yet convenient. It is a fabulous location for relishing the sophisticated atmosphere of Costa Smeralda, while always maintaining your private sanctuary at a peaceful remove. Gorgeous and well-appointed alfresco living spaces combine with exquisite and cool interiors to create the perfect setting for sweet, summer escapes.

With an ample pool terrace the villa's exterior invites you to enchanting days and evenings in the exquisite Porto Cervo light and breeze. The spectacular interior centres around an atrium that illuminates the central lounge opening directly to the pool terrace — the perfect setting for evening parties.

The main living area features a second lounge with home theatre room and an adjacent dining room with ample sea-view windows. Air conditioning, security system, state of the art AV equipment to ensure total comfort. Over 1,500 sqm of living area distributed in between the Master Unit and the adjacent Guest House.

Master Unit: 4 elegantly appointed guest suites including the Master Bedroom with en-suite bathroom and walk-in closet. Living room, Home Theatre Room, Dining Room, Kitchen, Wine Cellar, Massage and relax rooms, terrace, infinity swimming pool. Garage, Security System.

Guest House: 3 elegantly appointed guest suites with en-suite bathroom and dressing room. Living room with dining area opening onto the wide terrace and dedicated guest-house swimming pool, kitchen. A wellness area with equipped gym, Hammam, hair-dressing room, yoga room, football pitch.
