Casale Lucca, Capannori, Lucca, Italy Vacation Rental

 

Casale Lucca rents in Vorno, a small Tuscany village, just outside Lucca, Italy. Though the mill wheel no longer turns, the absolute simplicity of the timeless atmosphere remains today. Serenity is the philosophy of life offered here, as Villa Casale Lucca is brought back to life for your enjoyment. Here in the warm golden afternoons you can breath in the simple pleasures of the past.

Today the rooms of the old mill have become intimate and comfortable living spaces, full of warmth and welcome. The old stable on the ground floor is now the dining room where one can admire the vaulted ceiling, the stonework, and other traditional workmanship.

The entry hall’s large window looks on to the garden, bringing in the outside. The study and powder room continue in the same rustic but elegant style. And how can one forget the two stone fireplaces, once so indispensable, that still awaken the warmth and memories of family gatherings, old stories, and friendly conversations.

On the upper floor of villa are five double bedrooms, each with private bathrooms. Three rooms face north with views of the panoramic valley and hills and two look southwards over the beautiful garden and brook. Of the five bedrooms, one is a large master suite with sitting room.

The furnishings were carefully and patiently selected during the restoration to provide the utmost in comfort and luxury. The villa is filled with period furniture and splendid decorative objects. Nothing has been left to chance. The luxurious bathrooms are fitted with both tubs and showers.

The spacious garden is filled with potted lemons, olive trees, pines, camellias, wisteria, roses, lavender, rosemary and fruit trees, all forming a splendid backdrop to the swimming pool. One can enjoy relaxing by the pool, conversations under the gazebo or natural wisteria pergola, or dining al fresco in the place of your choice.
