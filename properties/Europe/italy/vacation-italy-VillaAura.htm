Villa Aura, Menaggio, Lake Como, Italy Vacation Rental

 

With fabulous and flexible family-friendly accommodation on offer, this modern three storey villa offers the last word in luxury.

Enjoying a private and elevated hillside location overlooking the famous Bellagio peninsular, the sleek and stylish villa provides up to 9 guests with a truly unforgettable stay with a full host of modern day comforts and luxury features to enjoy.

The 200sqm villa boasts an open plan layout with the principal living and dining area located to the lake view first floor. Here guests can relax and entertain in style, with a fully fitted contemporary kitchen with gas hob, oven, microwave and breakfast bar situated in an inviting and relaxed living area which is furnished with L-shaped sofa, a sleek gas fireplace for added ambience and a flat screen TV for your entertainment. Perfect for dinner parties, the French doors lead directly out to an alfresco lake view covered terrace with access beyond to the sunny private garden.

A modern cloakroom WC is also conveniently situated to the first floor of the villa and a staircase leads to the two principal bedroom suites located to the spacious second floor.

These two luxury bedroom suites both enjoy plenty of light, space and comfort with a stylish double bedroom with luxury en-suite shower room and a private lake view terrace to each.

Additional accommodation for a further 5 guests is situated to the ground floor of the villa. A double bedroom with flat screen TV is the perfect choice for a third couple staying in the villa and a further bedroom furnished with a set of two single beds and flat screen TV is ideal for children. An additional guest can also be accommodated on the single sofa bed to the third bedroom-come-sitting room. A spacious contemporary shower room is also situated besides the bedrooms on this floor.

Guests can easily access the private garage which conveniently features a laundry with washing machine.

The villa is perfectly placed to enjoy the surrounding breath taking mountainside scenery, by hiking or taking a bike ride. The prestigious Menaggio & Cadenabbia Golf Club and Loveno Tennis Club are also an easy drive away and the famous resort town of Menaggio can easily be reached by car in just 10 minutes.

This picturesque lakeside town enjoys a prominent central lake location with easy access by car and passenger ferry to Varenna and Bellagio. The town itself boasts a wide selection of supermarkets, delicatessens, boutique shops and a host of pretty cafes and restaurants which line its charming piazzas.

The beautiful lakefront promenade enjoys outstanding central lake views and is the perfect spot for enjoying a walk or an Italian ice cream. The family-friendly Lido di Menaggio also provides guests with a relaxed day of swimming, sunbathing and volleyball as well as delicious pizzas and a stylish outdoor lakefront cocktail bar which comes alive as dusk sets.

Evening aperitifs can be enjoyed in one of the many authentic Lake Como bars during your stay, as well as at the stylish ‘Aqua Cheta’ Boathouse bar and famous wine cellar bar of ‘La Cantina Follie’ which are both to be found in Tremezzo.

Dining out on Lake Como is a pure delight with a vast range of options to choose from, including family-friendly pizzerias, the renowned hillside Agroturismos and the elegant and romantic lake front restaurants which are dotted along the Tremezzina shore all the way southwards, to Como city.

By booking this holiday home you will get free acces to the lido di Menaggio. The Lido di Menaggio has swimming pools, a restaurant, a bar/discotheque, a kids club, boat rentals, a sandy beach, kite surfing and various sportive activities to do.
