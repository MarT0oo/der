Il Delfino, Atrani, Amalfi Coast, Italy Vacation Rental

 

Amalfi luxury villa near beach - Il Delfino is a very special villa situated halfway between the picturesque villages of Amalfi and Atrani, and within easy walking distance of both. Perched on the hillside, this exceptional property offers a stunning panoramic view over Atrani, the sea and the coast.

Il Delfino is impeccably presented: newly refurbished, the interiors are tastefully decorated in neutral colours, with splashes of colour provided by the frescoed, vaulted ceilings of the living room, choice pieces of furniture and the hand-made majolica tiles used for the floors and in the bathrooms. From the living room, with its views of the sea and Atrani, you can access both terraces, which offer wonderful places to relax, enjoy the sun, take a leisurely lunch or hold a dinner or pizza party. Chefs and catering can be organised for you, of course. The terraces are stunning at night time, when you can gaze down on the lights of the towns whilst enjoying a drink under the stars

This villa is in a truly unbeatable and historic location, within easy walking distance of both Amalfi and Atrani, where you will find restaurants, bars, shops and a pebble beach. And not only do you have your own private pool at Il Delfino, you can also use the local hotel's private beach platform, which is a particular bonus on this stretch of the Costa Amalfitana. You are also welcome to dine at the hotel's magnificent restaurant just a short stroll from the villa. A holiday at this unique luxury villa Amalfi Atrani with views would certainly be an experience to treasure.
