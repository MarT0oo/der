Villa Petriana, Maruggio, Apulia, Italy Vacation Rental

 

The property is immerse in the famous Manduira vineyards which produce the wonderful Primitivo wine and just a few kilometers beyond are the splendid white beaches along the Ionic Sea.

The property itself extends for over 12,500 sq. m. of typical Ionic rocky terroir filled with wide secular olive, almond and fig trees, all sprouted from what was a seabed hundreds of years ago giving the whole area a mysterious sense of elegance.

270 sq. m. of living space is divided into four double bedrooms each with its own en suite bathroom. All of the furnishings were also designed by architects reflecting, using and emanating elements of local nature evoking the sea, the surrounding colors and shades that harmoniously fuse well with the stones and other materials used for the structure itself.

A glassed in covered patio of about 60 square meters leads to the main entrance where you will find three double bedrooms with en suite bathrooms on the ground floor. Fully equipped modern kitchen with refrigerator and dishwasher, pantry/laundry room, powder room are all also on the ground floor. Up to the first floor, is the fourth double bedroom with en suite bathroom, and four terraces. In two bedrooms there is a further bed that can be set on request.

Next to the pool area there is now a new 5th bedroom with en-suite bathroom. this is a 20 sq. m. double bedroom, furnished in a local and minimalist style. The bedroom is air-conditioned.

The outdoor area consists of about 200 sq. m. of pine forest, ceramic outdoor table decorated with Majorca tiles, wooden gazebo over a water-well with seat and table, fully equipped external cooking area and a 6 x 12 m. pool.

A new organic vegetable garden is at guest disposal with its fresh fruits and vegetables.

In the garden immersed in the olive trees you’ll find also a relaxing area for meditation and yoga with a wooden platform. A new fitness area is on the top of the roof with a multifunction bench.
