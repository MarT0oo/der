Acquamarina, Siracusa, Sicily , Italy Vacation Rental

 

Sicily seafront villa with pool – Situated in Syracuse’s most exclusive residential quarter of Plemmirio, this splendid seaside family villa affords breath-taking views over the Mediterranean sea and a rocky beach, just a short stroll away. Shops and restaurants are also close at hand, to make food shopping easy and pleasant.

The typical whitewashed exterior of this seaside villa creates a striking contrast with the deep blue sea, whilst its curved lines clearly mirror the gentle motion of the waves. The interior is equally light and cool, with a maritime inspired décor. The stunning, panoramic sea views can be best enjoyed from the terraces of this villa, furnished with high quality garden furniture, outdoor dining facilities, parasols and large awnings; a table football will keep the children amused whilst you sit back and relax. The pool is simply marvellous, both in the daylight and at night, large and perfectly set on a terrace overlooking the sea.

A few miles away from Acquamarina lies Siracusa, one of the most beautiful Greek cities in Sicily. Enjoy a tour of the Neapolis Archaeological Zone encompassing the impressive Greek theatre where ancient tragedies and comedies are still performed every other summer. Walk across the bridge to visit the Ortigia island, separating the city’s two ports. As well as countless ancient and baroque buildings, castle ruins and elaborate church facades, Ortigia boasts a plethora of restaurants and a beautiful seafront promenade. Acquamarina is within easy reach of a number of magnificent historical towns, such as Noto, Ragusa Ibla, Megara Hyblaea and renowned seaside resorts all along the Gulf of Noto, featuring both sandy and rocky beaches
