Villa Vista, Porto Cervo, Costa Smeralda, Italy Vacation Rental

 

This is a semi detached property that sits on the hill of Pantogia, boasting a breathtaking sea view of the the Grande Pevero Bay. This completely renovated property, keeps the Sardinian style while offering a fresh and contemporary décor. The villa boasts a wide living and an indoor dining area, fully equipped kitchen and an outdoor veranda with dining table and relaxing lounge terrace. The house has in total 6 bedrooms 4 are double, 1 twin and 1 single that can be turned into a tidy double for staff all with en suite bathrooms with showers.

The bedrooms are located between first and ground floor through a flight of steps and a second living with kitchen on the ground floor. Laundry room with washing machine, fully air conditioned.

Outdoor swimming pool and a small Jacuzzi with solarium with full privacy completes this sunbathing area. A manicured garden surrounding the property, enriches the ambience with fresh Mediterranean scents.

Private car park for 2 cars. The property is gated.
