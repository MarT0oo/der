Carate Urio Deluxe, Carate Urio, Lake Como, Italy Vacation Rental

 

Enjoying a prominent lake view setting along the main village road, this luxury 4 bed apartment offers a scenic and convenient holiday base in the up-market village of Carate Urio.

The luxury lake view accommodation is immaculately presented throughout, and boasts a stylish integrated Kitchen, a spacious open plan Living and Dining room with large flat screen TV and a fabulous lake view terrace with BBQ and chic sitting area. Four luxury Bedrooms (3 with full En-suite Shower rooms) provide a boutique lake view ambience too, whilst one offers added flexibility for family groups staying (featuring a Double bedroom area with an adjoining room with modern bunk beds). Equipped with air-con to the bedrooms and private laundry facilities too, the apartment really offers that home-from-home comfort and convenience.

From the lake view setting of the modern 3-storey residence, public parking and the idyllic delights of Carate Urio are on the doorstep. A plethora of leisure activities also await your enjoyment in the local area, including hiking, lake swimming, Lido days, boat rental, seaplane trips, fine dining, water sports, golf and more. The fashionable lakeside village is within easy reach of neighbouring Laglio (2 minutes’ drive), the Central Lake tourist attractions (20 minutes+) and also the resort facilities of Cernobbio and cosmopolitan amenities and transport links of Como city (14-29 minutes’ drive away).

Carate Urio is easily accessible by bus, passenger ferry and also by road. The charming village is also perfect for the jet-set traveller, being just 47 minutes’ drive from Lugano Airport and 56 minutes’ drive from Milan Malpensa.
