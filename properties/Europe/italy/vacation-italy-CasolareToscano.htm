Casolare Toscano, Between Siena and Arezzo, Tuscany, Italy Vacation Rental

 

Set in a large wine and olive oil-producing estate, this beautifully restored Tuscan villa with air conditioning enjoys 360-degree views over the surrounding rolling countryside towards Siena and the charming village of Rapolano Terme, renowned for its hot springs and natural spa.

The property is immaculately kept and surrounded by manicured gardens with ample lawn areas for children to play and a private swimming pool with striking panoramic views over the valley. You have plenty of space here to enjoy unforgettable days relaxing in the Jacuzzi tub or lounging by the pool soaking up this magical atmosphere of this place. Prepare some lovely dishes dressed with the local extra-virgin olive oil to add some authentic flavour to your meal, accompanied by wine produced on the estate; an excellent blend of Cabernet Sauvignon and Sangiovese - what a perfect souvenir to bring back home!

This is quintessential Tuscany featuring idyllic rolling countryside with the distinctive cypress-topped hills, dotted with vineyards, olive groves and natural hot springs. Hilltop villages boast ancient castles and medieval churches, reminder of the rich cultural heritage of this land. A few miles away from Casolare Toscano are the picturesque villages of Rapolano Terme, Asciano, San Giovanni d’Asso and Trequanda where you can spend time strolling along narrow cobbled lanes to discover spectacular vistas and taste local gastronomic delicacies – the white truffle being the most sought after. A little further afield are some of the gems of Southern Tuscany; Arezzo, Siena, San Gimignano, Montalcino are famous worldwide for a very good reason! Ancient routes across vineyards and olive groves will lead you to discover some of the finest wines in the world. Wine tours and olive oil tasting can be easily arranged at your villa, as well as other additional services.
