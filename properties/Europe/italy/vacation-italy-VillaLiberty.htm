Villa Liberty, Sorrento, Amalfi Coast, Italy Vacation Rental

 

Villa Liberty is a luxurious Italy villa rental residing on the prominent Amalfi Coast. Mature citrus and palm trees surround the villa along with vibrant foliage and stunning views of the sapphire sea. These Italy accommodations are elegant and comfortable while displaying lovely antiques, walls of vintage books and arched doorways capturing the historic and beauty of this luxury villa rental in Italy. A large swimming pool framed with palm trees and Mediterranean plants is located on the terrace and furnished with whicker chaise lounges and large umbrellas. Guest will savor in the marvelous views rendered from the swimming pool deck area while absorbing the Mediterranean sunshine on the grounds of Villa Liberty.

Villa Liberty interiors are classic in design with parquet flooring, beautiful artwork adorns the walls and comfortable stylish furniture is throughout this Italy villa rental. A beautiful blue and yellow ceramic tiled gourmet kitchen with state of the art equipment is featured at Villa Liberty. Large copper pots hang over the preparation island and side wall adding to these fine Italy accommodations. Large ceramic urns sit beside a wonderful pizza oven with wooden dining table complete Villa Liberty kitchen accommodations. Outside the kitchen is an incredible terrace with large potted palms giving way to the gorgeous swimming pool with rattan chase lounges for enjoying the incredible beauty bestowed on guests visiting the majestic Amalfi Coast in Italy.

The beauty of Villa Liberty carries on into the elegant and spacious bedrooms. All of these stylish bedrooms offer beautiful furnishings, gorgeous bedding and picturesque vistas. These Italy accommodations and amenities continue with plasma televisions, mini bar, safe and air conditioning in all bedrooms. A professional and dedicated staff is provided to the guests of this luxury villa vacation rental in Italy that include; maid/laundress, housekeeper and cook.
