About Umbria

Umbria is a lovely and picture perfect region of central nothern Italy.  With it's hills and rivers and amazing topography, it's perfect for farming and winelands, for which many visitors come to travel to see.

Umbria however is completely landlocked, it is the only region in Italy like this, which makes it very unique and charming.  Of course you'l see the rivers, which run through, but this region is mainly known for it's historic and argicultural presense.

The Romans Conquered this land, however if you look hard enough you'll see that many of the residents stil feel quite like they are in another country.  The are boasts tours that are fantastic and enchanting, with stories of the Etruscans and their fight to the end.  The hills are filled with little dips and valleys that if one is lucky enough to look, one can find lots of great old treasures.

Yet for Italians this is a place to drive through and relax, many residents leave their doors open and you can always see children on a sunny or rainy day, playing outside. So if you're looking for a wonderful family vacation with lots of great history, this is the place to go.
