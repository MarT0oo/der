Castel Monastero, Chianti, Tuscany, Italy Vacation Rental

 

The landscape is classic Tuscany: vineyards, wheat fields and long rows of cypresses. A world-renowned setting. And it is right in the heart of this countryside, near Castelnuovo Berardenga, that a small 11th century medieval village and former monastery, 23 km from Siena, has become a refined resort and true Tuscan Retreat: Castel Monastero.

The 74 rooms pay homage to the rustic elegance and harmony of the ancient walls – the restoration project has preserved the charm of the Tuscan residences, adding elements of contemporary design, with full respect for the local culture and paying particular attention to the environment. The resort is set in green surroundings, providing spectacular views of the vineyards, the Piazza and the gardens all around from every angle. The villa, immersed in greenery in close proximity to the Spa, enjoys a spectacular view of the medieval village and, upon request, the privacy and exclusivity of the guests’ stay are assured by a private chef and butler.

In keeping with the Tuscan food and wine culture there are two restaurants: the gourmet restaurant “Contrada” where guests can have a sophisticated gastronomic experience tasting Tuscan cuisine interpreted by our Resident Chef together with world-famous Gordon Ramsay. The wine cellar, “La Cantina”, holds the best Tuscan and Chianti wines produced in the region and in the vineyards surrounding Castel Monastero and guests can sample the local specialties.

The SPA at Castel Monastero is truly the ideal place to regain your equilibrium and rediscover your inner balance – within its 1,000 square meters everything is designed to take away the mind from daily life and provide relaxation and wellbeing - attention to detail and the professionalism of our staff create a unique and unparalleled atmosphere of harmony. Here guests may choose from a series of treatments complemented by innovative and exclusive lines such as Natura Bissè and Dr. Murad. Guests can experience our wellness programs – detox, weight loss and energizing programs, based on specific Ayurvedic treatments and procedures performed by expert therapists and with the assistance of a pool of leadingItalian doctors and nutritionists. All the programs start in the morning with yoga sessions, followed by treatments during the day and a diet with specific menus devised by our Resident Chef with the supervision of Gordon Ramsay. Every program is based on the deep marma massage, which works on the acupuncture points and on the energetic meridians moving the metabolism, thereby helping the body to react to the diet and get the best results from the program.

The SPA experience is enhanced by the indoor water area which features a Finnish Sauna, Hammam, sensorial showers with chromo and aroma therapy, and two indoor pools, of which one with an exclusive patent designed by the medical team at Forte Village, featuring water with a high saline concentration that has draining, revitalizing and muscle relaxant properties.

For the ultimate in exclusivity and privacy, our Spa Suite offers a double treatment room with private Hammam and jacuzzi. An experience to share with your loved one in the most peaceful atmosphere. Around the three spectacular outdoor pools, you can relax surrounded by nature and tranquillity, or sip herbal teas and infusions at the Spa’s Herbarium, these are prepared using only the best ingredients from the private herb garden.

ROOMS

The rooms at Castel Monastero have outstanding views of either the Tuscan countryside, the antique courtyard or the gardens. Rooms vary in size and all offer the finest details of Tuscan tradition with a contemporary style and comfort. In all the rooms guests will find beautiful details of the best of Made in Italy, from fabrics to sofas. Many rooms have Terracotta floors and wood beams on the ceilings. All the rooms have king size beds or two twins beds, desks, and many have a spacious sitting area (Prestige and Junior Suite). The large bathrooms are all in Volcanic stone and many have separate bath and shower. Many rooms connect and a few have sofa beds.

Facilities: Minibar, safe deposit box, cordless telephone, complimentary Wi-Fi, bathrobes, hairdryer slippers, air conditioning, LCD TV, satellite TV, video-on-demand, all-news channels. i-Pod dock station on request.
