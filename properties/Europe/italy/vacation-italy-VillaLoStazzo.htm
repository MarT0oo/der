Villa Lo Stazzo, Monticanaglia, Porto Cervo, Costa Smeralda, Italy Vacation Rental

 

A modern country property located in the countryside area of Porto Cervo at no more than 10 minutes driving from downtown, 5 bedrooms countryside villa at a few minutes from Porto Cervo. An estate for truly “lovers” of beauty and design.

An enchanting location away from the glitters and yet in the true heart of it. A magnificent architectural work with stone and glass within a landscaped park of 4,000 sqm hosting the swimming pool and the Jacuzzi bath. Fully equipped veranda for both dining and relaxation, 300 sqm is the size of the property including living room, dining area and the kitchen. 3 large bedrooms with en-suite shower bathrooms on the ground floor and 2 double bedrooms with en-suite shower bathrooms on the lower ground floor. A studio room ideal for staff, laundry area. Parking place within the estate, fully air-conditioned, Internet connection.

Great finishing and contemporary furniture. Ideal for those looking for a modern property in a large outdoor ground, full privacy.
