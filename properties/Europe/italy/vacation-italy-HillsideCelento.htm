Hillside Celento, Salerno, Italy Vacation Rental

 

Inserted in the beautiful Mediterranean vegetation of the Cilento National Park, this characteristic country villa offers lazy guests and those fleeing from the city in search of peace, a true oasis of relaxation in a refined and comfortable setting, overlooking rolling green hills and blue wedges of sea.

An enchanted place that is – at the same time – also the ideal starting point for adventure lovers, for the tireless walkers and for those who want to spend an active holiday by the sea, seasoned with good food and wine, enriched by excursions among the wonders of nature and archeology.

The villa has a sleeping area on the first floor served by stairs, with 4 double bedrooms each with private bathroom and 1 quadruple room consisting of two bedrooms and private bathroom. All rooms have a balcony or terrace, air conditioning or heating and TV. Three of the rooms can have an extra bed.

On the ground floor there is a well-equipped professional kitchen, large panoramic living room with a corner bar and fireplace, 3 bathrooms (one for handicapped) and some service spaces.

Outside a beautiful panoramic terrace and gardens overlooking one side the charming village of Castellabate and on the other side the bay of Ogliastro Marina, all around the wild beauty of the Mediterranean garden. The villa is also an ideal location for parties and private events. A wide parking area completes the property.

The following services are also available separately: Housekeeper service for general cleaning and laundry; Concierge service for transfers, excursions and various activities on the territory.

Personal Trainer, Yoga teacher and Massotherapist, Kitchen Service with Private Chef and room staff.

The kitchen service, in particular, is possible for breakfast, lunch and dinner. The chef offers a simple and Mediterranean cuisine with local ingredients paired with local wines. The house is equipped with an organic vegetable garden for the seasonal production of vegetables and vegetables, figs, almonds, olives and extra virgin olive oil.
