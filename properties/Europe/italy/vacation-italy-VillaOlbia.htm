Villa Olbia, Sardinia, Italy Vacation Rental

 

Modern 600 sq m (6.900 sq ft) villa in secluded position, set within the private estate of Capo Coda Cavallo, just 50 m. (165 ft.) from a beautiful sandy beach.

The villa is located in a large and beautiful private property in north eastern Sardinia so there are virtually no constructions in the eyesight. The villa was built in 1976 by a famous Italian Architect called Luigi Caccia Dominioni.

The villa is located in a small bay and has straight access to its sandy beach which is 50 meters from the villa. The bay forms a well protected natural harbour even for large yachts. The villa enjoys exceptional views.

There is a further beach available for your use just a short 10 minutes walk from the villa, which is famous in the region for its crystal clear water.

The property is a remarkable one floor Villa and has 9 double bedrooms, 7 bathrooms, a fine dining room, one patio, one covered terrace, one living room, one study, one fully equipped kitchen and one laundry room.
