Villa Rudalza, Rudalza, Costa Smeralda, Italy Vacation Rental

 

Overlooking the surrounding greenery of gentle and soft hills decorated with olive trees and thick Mediterranean bush, this newly built house stands within unexploited, beautiful landscape with views reaching out to the sea. Granite stones and vaulted beam ceilings are those of the ancient Sardinian traditions and yet its style and design are deep into the most contemporary flair and character .

With its convenient lay-out it offers ample grounds and enchanting views, it sits within a well manicured garden with swimming pool and barbecue area equipped for relaxation and outdoor dining ideal for day and night gatherings. The main unit comprises of a living room with dining area and open plan kitchen with views on both the countryside and the sea and opening onto the covered terrace and the swimming pool, the Master bedroom and a double bedroom, both with en-suite shower bathrooms. Just next to the main but with separate access a double bedroom with en-suite shower bathroom. Close to the main unit a guest house with double bedroom and ensuite shower bathroom. The property is gated, fully air-conditioned, covered parking area.
