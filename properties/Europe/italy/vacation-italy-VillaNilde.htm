Villa Nilde, Ragusa, Sicily, Italy Vacation Rental

 

We are glad to offer in its selections of luxury Italy villas for rent, This air-conditioned luxury villa near the sea is in a perfect location, in the countryside near Marina di Ragusa, with a fantastic view of the Mediterranean Sea.

At few km from the sea, from Marina di Ragusa, staing in villa Nilde make you abel to visit all south area of sicily from Agrigento to Taormina, Catania, Siracusa, Etna volcano mountain, and Barocque area as Ragusa, Noto, Scicli, Modica…all Unesco protected cities of art.

The most seside resorts in area are: Marina di Ragusa, Marina di Modica, the protected area of Vendicari, Marzamemi, Donnalucata, Pozzallo

This Sicily luxury villa for rent is furnished with a fine modern taste, with the best luxury minimalist furniture that offers a fresh and elegant atmosphere, an atmosphere suitable for memorable holidays.

On the ground floor are the kitchen, dining room and living room, a fully equipped laundry room, a single bedroom, a double bedroom and a bathroom with shower. A staircase leads to the first floor where there are the other 2 double bedrooms (1 with private bathroom with shower), a twin bedroom and a bathroom with shower and Jacuzzi. The villa is protected by an alarm system.

The infinity pool (10m * 4mt) allows moments of peace and tranquility, lying on a comfortable chaise longue to laze under the Sicilian sun.

The beautiful terrace, which surrounds the villa, gives access to an outdoor bowling alley and also allows you to enjoy the beauty of the surrounding olives and carobs, typical of the Ragusa area.
