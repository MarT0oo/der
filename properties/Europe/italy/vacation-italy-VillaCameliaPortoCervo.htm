Villa Camelia, Porto Cervo, Costa Smeralda, Sardinia, Italy Vacation Rental

 

Sitting on a hill above Porto Cervo, this elegant property boasts a stunning sea view over the Cala di Volpe Bay. This 3 storey - property with six-bedrooms is the perfect gate away for family reunion and friends getaway.

Entrance opens on to the opne space living and dining room overlooking the Cala di Volpe Bay, fully equipped kitchen and 3 bedrooms with bathrooms. Upstairs the Master bedroom with stunning view and terrace overlooking the property and the wonderful sea view comes with en-suite bathroom. Further two bedrooms with bathrooms are located on the lower floor, where an extra kitchen is found and laundry area with washing machine and tumble dryer. The outdoor features a wide swimming pool, sunchairs and outdoor Al Fresco Meals.

The property is close to the Pevero Golf Club, Beaches and a few km away from Porto Cervo Center.
