La Divina, Positano, Amalfi Coast, Italy Vacation Rental

 

Ultimate luxury on the Amalfi coast; nestled in an enchanting Mediterranean garden adorned with a riot of colourful hand-painted tiles and elegant furniture, La Divina is a splendid villa with air conditioning and private plunge pool, affording unrivalled views over Positano and the Mediterranean sea to the island of Capri. This small private estate was formerly home to famous film director Franco Zeffirelli and host to many celebrity guests including Maria Callas from the operatic world.

The villa consists of two luxury bedroom suites opening onto a large private terrace affording one of the most romantic views along the Amalfi coast; Positano’s whitewashed houses cascading down to the colourful marina and cobalt sea; you will be mesmerized by this breathtaking vista! After breakfast, find your favourite spot under the long pergola or take a refreshing dip in the plunge pool nearby. You can also stroll down to the pebble beach where the private beach facilities await. Here you also have a private boat shuttle for trips to Positano, ideal for a spot of shopping or an evening meal.

The unique situation of this elegant property within a private gated domain, means you can enjoy the privacy of a private villa as well as benefiting from the facilities and services of a boutique hotel. These range from the daily maid and breakfast service, a Lounge Bar and restaurant serving your daily 2-course meal inclusive of all drinks and snacks, a round grotto pool with Jacuzzi and outdoor fitness centre, use of the beautiful landscaped gardens built over eight levels and direct access to the beach. If you can bear to leave this idyllic paradise, you have plenty of things to discover along one of Italy’s most acclaimed coastlines. Positano is on your doorstep, and Amalfi and Ravello are just a few miles away. North of La Divina you have Sorrento, and directly opposite you have the island of Capri; you will be spoilt for choice!
