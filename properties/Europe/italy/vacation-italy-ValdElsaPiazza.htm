Val d’Elsa Piazza, Siena, Tuscany,Italy Vacation Rental


Val d’Elsa Piazza is situated in the heart of the Tuscany countryside, between Siena and Florence, and is the ideal accommodation for anybody who loves nature. Encircled by luxury gardens, the villa stands on top of a hill and enjoys a breathtaking view over Siena. The main building, owned for centuries by one Roman Aristocratic Family, dates back to 1400 and was restructured with loving care, while additional construction was added between the 18th and 19th century, transforming the building into a splendid holiday villa in Italy

The Villa, a beautifully executed rent house, has 13 bedrooms, each one elegantly furnished with different colors and patterns, which can accommodate up to 25 people. As many luxury Tuscany villa, it is possible to rent it for holidays, family reunion, weddings, business meetings, and film events. Both inside and outside there are several types of entertainments, in order to satisfy the requests of our guests.

In the garden, next to the bar, there is a large swimming pool and a sun deck with gazebo, with lovely views over the Tuscan countryside. The interiors of this wonderful luxury Tuscany villa, with exclusive amenities, are cared for in every detail with spacious living rooms, one of them with a big flat tv screen, a game room and three adjoining dining rooms furnished with tables. A large and spacious kitchen, fully and professionally equipped, suitable also for cooking classes, is reserved to the Villa staff.

It’s possible to select on demand different wines from our wine cellar.
