Castello di Procopio, Umbria, Perugia, Italy Vacation Rental

 

Imagine the perfect fairy-tale setting, an imposing medieval castle with turrets and thick stone walls surrounded by rolling countryside and enchanting views over olive groves and charming hilltops villages…and you have found Castello di Procopio! Completely restored in recent years, this former fortress was originally built in the 12th century and is now an exclusive residence complete with all modern comforts, including air-conditioning, spa, gym and swimming pool, set in 3 acres of peaceful parkland on the border between Umbria and Tuscany, just 15 miles from Perugia airport.

Built by the Holy Roman Emperor Federico Barbarossa, from its hilltop situation this castle witnessed the unfolding of crucial events in the history of Italy and Europe. Spacious and easily accessible via internal and external staircases, as well as lifts, the accommodation is split into two separate wings, each featuring individually appointed, air-conditioned bedroom suites with spectacular views over the valley below. Original antiques and precious works of art adorn this castle, in which every piece of furniture and soft furnishing has been carefully selected following meticulous research, in order to preserve the historical accuracy and revive its former glory. Sumptuous and majestic, yet comfortable and easy to live in, Castello di Procopio is the perfect setting for a family celebration or a holiday of a lifetime.

The castle is ideally located near the border between Umbria and Tuscany, regions which boast an incredibly rich heritage. For a true taste of Umbrian country life, why not join a truffle hunt in the woods, followed by a taste of delicious truffle-based dishes and local wine! To explore the surrounding area, horse riding and cycling are also highly enjoyable outdoor activities suitable for children and adults alike. Moreover, within the splendid seclusion of your castle you can enjoy a variety of spa and beauty treatments, cookery classes, meals prepared by a chef or just relax in the idyllic tranquillity of this fairy-tale setting.

Please note: the discreet caretaker lives in a separate cottage near the pool and is available to help, if needed.
Also, there are many stone steps everywhere in the castle so young children must be supervised at all times.
