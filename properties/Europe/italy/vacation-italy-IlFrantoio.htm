Originally an olive press and still surrounded by beautiful terraced olive groves, Il Frantoio has been lovingly restored into a charming and comfortable home (fully heated and air-conditioned) with 4 spacious and well appointed guestrooms.

Il Frantoio was originally an olive press and is still today surrounded by beautiful terraced olive groves. Its recent restoration has turned it into a charming and comfortable home (fully heated and air-conditioned) with 4 spacious and well appointed guestrooms each with modern en-suite bath and shower facilities for up to 8 guests.

Ground Floor: Main entrance with hall and staircase, fully equipped Tuscan kitchen and breakfast room, Spacious lounge with comfortable sofas and armchairs, music system, satellite TV as well as a desk area with computer, printer, fax, telephone and internet facilities, Dining room, Bathroom

First Floor: 4 sunny and spacious queen bedded rooms with beamed ceilings and original terracotta floors each with modern en-suite bath and shower facilities.
