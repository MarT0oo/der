Ruby Knights, Knightsbridge, Central London, England Vacation Rental

 

Ruby Knights is a five bedroom, six bathroom home in Knightsbridge. It has a provocative art collection spread throughout the three sitting rooms (one with emerald damask wallpaper, one wood-panelled and curio-filled, the other lined with diverse artworks) to the ruby-red cinema room, this home’s living areas are certainly characterful.

The five ensuite bedrooms are too – you’ll find antiques, unusual pieces of art and flamboyant accessories in every one. You’re beautifully situated here, between this area’s renowned museum district and Hyde Park. Upscale boutiques and eateries line the surrounding streets, and it’s only around a ten-minute walk to Knightsbridge tube station.

The fireplaces in this home are ornamental. This home accepts babies, but does not accept toddlers or children. The communal garden is shared with a number of other homes.

This home doesn't have air-conditioning. Between June and September, we'll place fans in each bedroom. Please let us know if you'd like fans outside the summer period. There is limited phone reception at the lower ground floor of this home. This home's collection of political art is intentionally provocative and controversial. If you'd like any more information before booking, please let us know.

The homeowners' cleaner will visit from Monday to Friday between 11:30 am to 2 pm to water the plants. While the TV in the cinema room does not feature cable, there is an Apple device for your use. There is adult-themed art in this home.

This home is spread over seven floors with no internal elevator. Since the photo shoot, the canopy bed on the terrace and the television in the Master Bedroom have been removed.
