The Islands of Spain are a gathering place of those who love to experience the best views, wonderful music, the sounds of the ocean and wonderful outdoor experiences. You will find the stores bustling with the sounds of people enjoying the open market and all it has to offer. A truly family friendly place to stay.

The Villa Monticello is one of the top villas on the island offering a wonderful view of the island from each corner. Go outside and visit the natural setting, hike up the mountain and take a stroll into the past. Find some special rocks as you walk and explore the vast hilltops and create your own memories at Villa Monticello.

Situated in the heart of the village, in the Son Canals Community, this detached villa offers panoramic vies of the village, church and mountain.
