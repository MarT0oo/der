Villa Cordelia, Quinta do Lago, Portugal


A beautiful and unique villa fronting on the Atlantico North section and facing the São Lourenço Golf course. The villa faces south and west to capture the day long sunshine to enjoy the large pool and terrace areas.

4 of the bedrooms are around a central courtyard with direct access to the main house where the master and the guest bedroom are both situated, plus a large lounge, dining room and the extremely well equiped kitchen. Separate beer tap on request.

This is a great villa for a family reunion. The owners art collection and eclectic taste of furnisher offers a combination of both modern and traditional.


Quinta do Lago is without a doubt one of the most exciting and exclusive resorts in Europe and understandably boasts some of the most spectacular and beautiful villas in the Algarve. Founded in 1972, this 800 acre residential estate borders miles of unspoilt golden beaches and the pristine Atlantic Ocean, undulating pine forests and some of the best golf courses in the world.

Quinta do Lago is a major destination for golf enthusiasts. Two thousand acres of land has been transformed into a resort dedicated to a golfer's fantasy lifestyle, with villas spaced strategically apart, all the comforts of a metropolitan centre and championship and non-championship courses for any golf aficionado. Of course, aside from golf, the resort has other attractions, such as horseback riding, tennis, two busy shopping malls, two miles of golden beaches, a fine selection of restaurants and nightclubs, and three thousand hours of sunny blue skies each year.

Quinta do Lago is a very appealing summer holiday destination because of its easy access to the rest of Europe. Only twenty minutes drive from Faro Airport, Quinta do Lago is only two hours away from both Lisbon and Seville, Spain.
