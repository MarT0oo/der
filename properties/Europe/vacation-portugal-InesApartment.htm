Ines Apartment, Quinta do Lago, Algarve, Portugal Vacation Rental

 

Ines Apartment is a freshly renovated spacious 2 bedroom, 3 bathroom apartment located on the ground floor with a stunning golf & garden view. This bright and tasteful apartment is a perfect choice for your holiday destination. The modern kitchen is fully equipped (powered by Bosch), also equipped with a Nespresso coffee machine. The welcoming and feel-good living room has cable TV with excellent international and national programs. The apartment has a large sunny terrace with an outdoor dining table & lounge chairs for relaxing and enjoying the Algarve weather. The Apartment comes with free wifi (in the resort / pool). Upon arrival you will receive a welcome basket with fruit and goodies.

Ines Apartment is set in a prime location within walking distance to the Quinta do Lago beach, lake watersports, San Lorenzo Golf Course and Casa do Lago and Bovino Steakhouse and others. Great Resort onsite with tennis courts, outdoor pool, poolside snack & drink bar. There is private parking and 24/7 Security. It is a 15 minutes drive from Faro Airport, 10 minutes drive to Almancil, 5 minutes drive to Quinta Golf Course & Academy and Quinta Shopping.
