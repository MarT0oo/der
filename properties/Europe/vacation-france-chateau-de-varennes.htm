Le Petit Chambord
10 Bedrooms | 10 Baths                                                                                  
Loire Atlantique, France
  From £12500 per week

A Beautifully restored Luxury Castle, in the Loire Valley, 30 min from angers and 50 min from Nantes 20 min from Chateauebriant, with Trains that can take you to Lyons or any other destination of your dreams.  There are various daytrips along this Historic Route, where Kings and Queens have walked before.

 

The house contains 13 bedrooms in all, 6 of which are main suites, 4 of which are guest suites, and then the additional rooms that can be used for staff of additional guests.  Each of these rooms has been completely remodeled, from the bedrooms, to the bathrooms, flooring, new woods throughout, and upscale tapestries and cabinetry. 

 

The grounds are amazing; from the moment you walk onto the grounds you can feel the magic around you.  The grounds are suitable for weddings, corporate events, family picnics, or anything your heart desires.  The owner is a Wedding Coordinator and is happy to help you make the most of your day.  As a specialty lodging home, this can be rented as a Bed and Breakfast or in its entirety.  The house does offer daily breakfast for its guests and there is a staff to take care of all of your needs.

 

Situated on beautiful grounds, the Chateau complex makes up one corner of a small village, which also has shops, a cathedral, and many summer events. The Chateau is now open for both overnight guests and day visitors, and also hosts wedding receptions and other events, your chateau holidays in France.
