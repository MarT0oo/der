Villa Fern, Algarve, Portugal Vacation Rental

 

This luxury villa stands in landscaped gardens with views to the sea. It is finished and re-furnished to a very high standard and air conditioned throughout and has cable T.V., DVD and Hi-Fi.

The villa has 3 double bedrooms and 2 twin bedrooms all with bathrooms en-suite. 1 double bedroom and 2 twin bedrooms on the ground floor and 2 double bedrooms including the master bedroom with dressing area on the first floor.

The ground floor has also cloakroom, living room with fireplace and cable television, dining room and lovely hand painted fully fitted breakfast kitchen with oven, microwave, hob, fridge’s and dishwasher. There is also a small laundry with washing machine plus tumble dryer.

The villa has a lovely pool with outside cloakroom and open air shower. The pool can be heated if client requires. There is a normal BBQ and a gas BBQ also on a dining terrace overlooking the pool and pound with views to the sea.Walking distance to the beach.
