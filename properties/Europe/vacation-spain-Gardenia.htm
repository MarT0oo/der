Gardenia, Cala Tarida, Ibiza, Spain Vacation Rental

 

Gardenia is tucked away amidst a lush forest of pine and eucalyptus trees halfway between Sant Josep and Cala Tarida. This fantastic surrounding adds on to the slightly elevated location of this property, boasting stunning sunset views over the islands of Conillera and S'Espartar in addition to Es Vedrà just a bit further out.

The architecture is signature contemporary Ibiza style - all white surfaces and ample windows that open up onto the outdoor area with its appealing private pool. Both sun beds and lounge areas are present abound accompanied by a shaded dining area, fitting for gathering at any time of the day.

The same signature style is evident indoors where a sober and elegant vibe is perfectly conveyed by refined materials and colors - a dominant presence of white mixed with teak wood and grey micro cement surfaces. The carefully selected furnishing further the elegance of the interiors without being overly excessive or over the top.

Gardenia is a delightful property under all aspects, great for small families or couples looking for relaxing and pampering holiday.
