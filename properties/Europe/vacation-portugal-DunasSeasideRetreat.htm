Dunas Seaside Retreat, Vale do Lobo, Algarve, Portugal Vacation Rental

 

An elegant and traditional 4 bedroom villa situated within Vale do Lobo, in between the exclusive Dunas Douradas resort and Julia’s beach. This property comprises of a fully fitted and equipped kitchen with a breakfast table, a spacious lounge with a fireplace accessing the covered terraces and garden featuring a breathtaking sea view. Also on the ground floor is the dining room, a guest cloakroom, a snug room also including a fireplace and cable TV and 2 bedrooms. The double bedroom and twin bedroom include fitted wardrobes, share a bathroom and have access to a separate kitchenette, a seating and dining area. The remaining bedrooms are located on the first floor, the master suite features a spacious shared terrace where one can really admire the scenery. The master guest bedroom is also en-suite and features fitted wardrobes, a fireplace and a shared terrace. The lower level includes a sauna, steam room, gymnasium and games area featuring a table tennis and football.

A lift is provided to access all 3 levels and WIFI are provided throughout. The exterior is truly unique allowing guests to relax and make the most of their stay. It includes a large heated swimming pool with cover, landscaped gardens for the sun loungers to enjoy the Algarvian weather, covered terraces with “al-fresco” dining and BBQ area, a putting green and a kid’s playground featuring a trampoline! Dunas Seaside Retreat allows its guests to experience the fabulous life in the Algarve, being within walking distance to the golden sandy beaches and a short drive from the renowned golf courses and amenities.

Please note that there is only air conditioning in the first floor, the rest of the property uses underfloor cooling to cool the villa. The driveway is slightly steep.
