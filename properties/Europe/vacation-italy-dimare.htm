Dimora Di Mare, Ravello, Italy


We invite you to experience the unique charm and romantic atmosphere of this ancient 8-bedroom Villa overlooking the sea, just below Ravello, set in a magic place of natural beauty with private access to the sea in the heart of the Amalfi Coast.

Enjoy the sea, the music, the traditions and the gastronomy, and discover it all without sacrificing privacy and relaxation. The Dimora di Mare Villa offers spacious rooms for weekly stays, all with breathtaking sea views, furnished with antique furniture and equipped with the best modern comforts.

Dimora di Mare is an ancient villa built in the 19th century directly upon the sea of the Amalfi Coast. It has just been renovated, keeping the architectural features of the building intact. It has been transformed into a vacation rental with all the comforts imaginable, while preserving the special character of historical homes with rich antique and elegant furnishings and majolica tiled floors.

Its four stories are connected by an ancient stairway with artistic inlaid wood handrails and arched windows overlooking the beautiful sea. At the Villaâ€™s entrance, on the ground floor, you can find Prelude and Overture, which are the reception areas - with internet point & tourist information - and the living room with a fireplace and ancient library.

The equipped panoramic roof garden on the top and the bougainvillea terrace on the bottom, both of about 100 sqm, are the most beautiful setting for guests' special and unforgettable events.

The property has a private garden of about 1.500 sqm, with private access to the sea by a stairway: one hundred and thirty stone steps run through bougainvillea terraces and lemon and orange gardens, until they reach the equipped solariums, the natural caves, the pool with water massage set in the panoramic viewpoint and a small private pier set in the enchanting inlet of the sea cliff.

A full Spa has just been built at the Villa, including a large Swim Spa Jacuzzi of 4.30 x 2.3 metres with water massage, aromatherapy, cromotherapy and heated water, a small gym, an infrared Sauna for two people set into the natural grotto within a massage table and a relax area. The spa manager, for wellness and beauty treatments, is available on request.

The Villa is managed directly by the owners who live in an independent apartment within the property on the garden floor.
