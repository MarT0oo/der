Villa Lighea Positano, Italy Vacation Rental


Villa Lighea is located in one of the most beautiful and elegant parts of Positano, the small town on the Amalfi Coast known around the world for its exclusive beauty. The villa is a typical Positano construction, in which the influence of Byzantine architecture merges perfectly with the skills of local master-builders. The view from the wide balconies and verandas perched high above the multi-colored sea is breathtaking.  In front of the villa, Li Galli, the Siren Islands; to the right, romantic Positano.

The villa has two stories and is surrounded by gardens and terraces. The sitting room is spacious and airy, has a wide terrace and is furnished with comfortable sofas, a large dining table, widescreen LCD television with Bose acoustics, satellite system and DVD player. The Internet connection is wireless so that our guests can connect easily from anywhere in the villa or the garden. The kitchen adjacent to the sitting room is professionally equipped with everything required for fine cooking.  A cook may be requested for the entire day or for special events.

The five bedrooms are each uniquely furnished in Mediterranean style and have either double or twin beds, each with a balcony facing the sea.  All the bedrooms have bathrooms, roomy showers or hydro-massage tubs, safes, independent air-conditioning and mosquito netting. We have chosen not to place televisions in the bedrooms.  The view is simply too beautiful ... we feel that that it is undoubtedly preferable to let oneself be carried away by the sighing of the sea or the sound of personally-chosen music.
