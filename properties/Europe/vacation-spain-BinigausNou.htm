Binigaus Nou, Menorca, Spain Vacation Rental

 

Cala Binigaus is an exclusive villa located in a natural reserve paradise in the south of Menorca, one of the most outstanding landscapes of the isle.

With 120 hectares, direct access to the beach and 800 meters of coastline, it has privileged views to the sea, the island of Mallorca and Binigaus cliff. All the rooms have spectacular views.

The building dates from 1896 and it has been recently restored keeping at all times its historical aesthetics, considered Menorca patrimony it preserves the charm of the past with the comfort. Designed by the famous architect Femenias.

The carefully-decorated interior transports us to the past and to the beauty of the great Danish masters of modern design. Each room has a story to be told, great inheritance and far away places memories: the Manila shawl from last century with which the grandmother and the great-grandmother of the owner won numerous prizes, the bed headboards from last century. Danish furniture classics, Asian and India antiquities, all integrated with the Menorca spirit of this peculiar villa. A paradise full of beauty, peacefulness, romanticism and history.

The villa comprises one suite with terrace, bathroom with shower and tub, with views to the sea, two double rooms, with bathroom, one with views to the sea and the other with views to the countryside. Another double room, sharing the bathroom with the kids bedroom, one kids bedroom with bunk beds, sharing the bathroom with the previous one. Another bedroom with twin beds and a bathroom (possibility of adding an extra bed) and a room service with bathroom. It also has an infinity swimming pool, barbecue and old firewood oven.

Possibility of an adjoining apartment under construction with two double rooms, bathroom, kitchen and lounge.
