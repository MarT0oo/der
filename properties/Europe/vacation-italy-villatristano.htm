Villa Tristano
11 Bedrooms | 11 Bathrooms                                                                  
Orvieto (Umbria), Italy

The access road to the property is private.  The property has a traditional accent. The house is filled with antiques, books and paintings and has French windows opening on to flower-filled terraces. Situated 10 minutes from Orvieto amidst glorious rolling countryside that cross the edge among Tuscany, Umbria and Lazio (Rome's region).  The whole property, 8 Ha of fenced, gated grounds, provides absolute peace and tranquility enjoying countryside vistas.

The gardens surrounding the buildings have been lovingly landscaped; a wonderful selection of flora includes roses, olives, vines, and fruit surround this heaven for nature lovers. The immaculate property dates back 1800 years and has been tastefully renovated. Although it feels remote and private, the property strategically located in the centre of Italy (70km from Perugia, 80 from Assisi, 130 from Siena e Roma e from Firenze.) The pool is one of a kind and the views from the garden superb. Approximately 1.200 sqm of habitable space including the main house and the small house; each with bedroom suites. The white outdoor swimming pool is a regular  and  measures  15m 5 m.
