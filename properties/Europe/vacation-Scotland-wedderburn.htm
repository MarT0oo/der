Wedderburn Castle, Duns, Scotland Vacation Rental

 

The castle has the ambience of a private Georgian family house with all original features still in place, maintained to the highest standard and with all of the modern comforts expected today.

Wedderburn offers guests the greatest possible level of privacy and freedom of choice. This is a country house that becomes your own. Whilst retaining much of the convenience of a hotel, there is no reception desk and visitors continually comment upon the relaxed and informal atmosphere that they enjoy so much.

These pages offer a comprehensive guide to the castle and the service we provide. Please feel free to contact us to discuss your requirements or for a personal consultation on all aspects of your stay.

Wedderburn is the perfect location for your Scottish castle wedding and reception. The sweeping stone stairs provide a dramatic entrance, leading to four spacious and elegant reception rooms.

12 unique bedrooms for a resident party of up to 26 people.

Whichever bedroom you choose at Wedderburn you'll be sleeping in style. Every room is different and all have kept their original period features and fabulous views over the castle grounds and the rolling farms and woodland of Berwickshire.

Bedrooms are supplied with bathrobes and luxurious thick towels. For individual room details please click on the photographs here to enlarge.

Weddings

We welcome civil, religious and humanist wedding ceremonies at Wedderburn Castle. Your wedding can take place in any of our licensed rooms, or outdoors on the castle lawns. There are also several historic churches to choose from nearby. After your wedding ceremony, the reception can be held in the castle grounds or there is ample space for your reception and wedding photography indoors.

In the castle itself the light, spacious ballroom is a fitting place to hold your wedding ceremony. Up to 92 guests will be comfortably seated, while more intimate parties might choose the elegant stair hall or the drawing room for the ceremony.

We will, of course, provide you with unlimited practical advice on arranging both your wedding ceremony and reception party. Alternatively our events management staff will be delighted to provide a full wedding planning and co-ordination service.

Receptions

Wedderburn will comfortably host a wedding reception and banquet for up to 92 guests. Dinner can be served in the spacious ballroom, or the dining room will seat more intimate parties of up to 42. All reception rooms at Wedderburn will be available for your guests to enjoy throughout their visit, simply to relax with drinks from the bar or catch up over after-dinner coffee.

Your reception can be held outdoors in the beautiful castle grounds, or of course indoors throughout the elegant reception rooms. Our staff will deliver any drinks or canape service ordered wherever your reception takes place on the day, and of course we welcome photography throughout the castle and grounds.

 

Entertainment

As Wedderburn is your exclusive-use 'home away from home' your schedule and choice of entertainments is not limited by the presence of other paying guests. Pipers, bands and any other entertainments are welcomed, and the ballroom is an ideal place for a ceilidh or dance. During the day, outdoor activities including clay pigeon shooting, falconry, hot air ballooning and Highland Games are easily arranged in the grounds of the castle.
