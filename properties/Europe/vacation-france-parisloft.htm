Designer Loft, 2nd district, Paris, France Vacation Rental


Originally a 19th Century Industrial Printing House built by Gustav Eiffel, the Designer Lofthas been transformed into an exclusive, contemporary loft space, offering a warm atmosphere and modern facilites for professional or residential use. Designed to be modular according to differing requirements, the property offers diverse spaces that can be adapted according to needs, including two lounge areas, a kitchen with a mobile island that can be moved if required, floating passageways and up to 4 double bedrooms that can be made up on request. Furniture can be stored or re-arranged according to requirements.

The Designer Loftis fully equipped with state of the art Wi-Fi and internet link-ups, home automation and 3 multi-media audiovisual systems - the main lounge is equipped with a giant home cinema screen (3.2 meters/18 feet wide). An office provides a printer, scanner and fax - this room can be isolated with sliding doors, or remain an open extension to the mezzanine area.

The Designer Loftoffers a terrasse overlooking the private courtyard and has access to a charming and well preserved 19th Century paved passage way providing a smoking area and second access for deliveries.

The Designer Lofthas a relaxation room on the lower level, including a sauna, a hydro-jet massage shower an eliptical trainer and multi-gym.

About The Area

The Designer Loftis centrally located in the heart of Paris on the edge of the Marais and near to the Louvre and the Tranoi, with easy taxi access, parking and nearby public transportation only a few steps away (3 metro stations all within 2 minutes walk):

The Designer Loftis situated in the Quartier Montorgueil - a lively pedestrianised quarter that hosts many fashion brands, designer boutiques and showrooms. Montorgueil also offers a wide range of good dining and has many convivial bars and cafés.
