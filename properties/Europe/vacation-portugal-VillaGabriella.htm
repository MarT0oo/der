Villa Gabriella, Quinta do Lago, Portugal Vacation Rental

 

Villa Gabriella is a traditional 4 bedroom villa with large landscaped garden with lovely pool and covered terraces. This villa has all the bedrooms ensuite, has air conditioning throughout, cable TV and Wi-Fi internet.

There are large landscaped gardens. The private pool has an open air shower and is surrounded by terraces with a dining area and two sofa areas as well as eight sun loungers. There is also a charcoal BBQ area. On the ground floor to the right the living room is the dining room and kitchen.

All bedrooms have en suite bathrooms. On the ground floor to the left it has the two twin bedrooms, one with shower and other with bath en suite. Both rooms have sliding doors to the garden and pool terrace area. On the first floor you will find a further two double bedrooms, both with bathrooms ensuite, one of the rooms has cable TV. Both bedrooms have sliding doors to a balcony with a staircase to access gardens and pool terrace.

The kitchen is fully equipped with an oven, microwave, hob, large fridge, dishwasher, coffee machine, toaster etc. There is also a laundry area with washing machine and dryer.
