Crom Castle, Lough Erne, Co. Fermanagh, Ireland Vacation Rental


The Lough Erne Castle exudes all the charm of a personal residence, whilst offering the most modern facilities in line with its five star accreditation from the Northern Ireland Tourist Board. With its own drive and entrance, the Castle gives you all the privacy you need and, at the same time, the opportunity to experience life in a castle. Decorated in warm colors, with wonderful views, the Lough Erne Castle radiates a welcoming atmosphere. It sleeps up to 11 people, however, if you are a smaller group there is flexibility.

 

The high ceilings of the yellow Drawing Room opens directly onto the west Terrace, the Gardens, and the Tennis Court . Both the Drawing Room and the barrel vaulted Dining Room have open fireplaces. Guests can further relax in the Study which has a large screen TV with DVD and Video and a desk with telephone and fax and a separate line for your laptop. There is also the very necessary boot room, for storing boots, coats, fishing rods etc., a cloakroom, laundry room and domestic accommodation. The six spacious bedrooms, all with en-suite bathrooms, lead off the Castle's two long inviting corridors.

 

The bedrooms consist of the Buff Room (Four Poster Double Bed), The Print Room (Double) with attached Dressing Room (Small Single), The Crichton Room (Double), The West Room (Large Single), The Blue Room (Twin), The Rose Room (Twin).

 

Guests are provided with logs for the fireplaces in the Drawing Room and Dining Room. Under NO circumstances should fires be lit in the bedrooms. Smoking is strictly forbidden in the bedrooms. You may, however, smoke in the Drawing Room, Study or Dining Room.
