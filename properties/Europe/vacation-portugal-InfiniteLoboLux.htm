Infinite Lobo Lux, Vale do Lobo, Algarve, Portugal Vacation Rental

 

Infinite Lobo Lux is a luxurious property located in Vale do Lobo, only a few minutes’ walk from the Praça, beach, golf, restaurants and bars. The property is decorated to a very high standard and, on the ground floor compromises a fully equipped kitchen with a breakfast bar and dining area seating 6 guests.

The wonderful living room is very bright and sumptuous, with large windows to the outdoor area. Still on the ground floor there is a guest cloakroom and 2 double bedrooms, both en-suite with walk in shower. On the first floor there is the master suit, with walk in shower and access to two private terraces, one of them overlooking the pool area. On the lower ground floor there is a self-contained area with two bedrooms, one double, en-suite with walk in shower and a twin bedroom with separate bathroom with walk in shower.

This area also has an open plan living area with a small fully fitted kitchen comprising oven, microwave, kettle, mini fridge and dishwasher. The outdoor area has a very zen feeling, with private pool, gas BBQ, dinning table seating 8 guests and a very relaxing covered seating area.
