Villa Margaret, Sant Pere de Ribes, Sites, Spain Vacation Rental

 

Situated between the coast and the mountains, this stylish country villa is great for a relaxing break in the Catalan countryside. In addition to the wonderful 14 bedrooms with ensuite bathrooms, Villa Margaret offers a marvellous outdoor pool and extensive gardens.

The villa dates back to 1669 and still has many of the original features, while having been renovated to modern standards. Each enuite room is equipped with air conditioning/heating and private sitting areas.

Villa Margaret also features a lovely dining room with views to the countryside, as well meeting rooms and a beautiful wine cellar for wine-tasting or gatherings.

The villa is located in the village of Sant Pere de Ribes, on the edge of the Garraf Natural Park. With the charming town of Sitges just a 5 minute drive away, everything is your fingertips, from stunning beaches to lively nightlife. Barcelona city is just a 30-minute drive away.

This property has been consistently ranked one of the highest-rated locations in Sant Pere de Ribes!

Group Holidays

If you have a big family or many friends, Villa Margaret is the ideal place for you. Enjoy the utmost in privacy on the edge of the Garraf Natural Park, while being steps away from restaurants, shops, and grocery in the town of Sant Pere de Ribes.
