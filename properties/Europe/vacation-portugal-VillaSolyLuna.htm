Villa Sol y Luna, Vale do Lobo, Algarve, Portugal Vacation Rental

 

This 5 bed villa is traditional in style. It is conveniently located close to the entrance of Vale do Lobo and only 15 minutes walk from the beach and restaurants.

This property benefits from a private swimming pool, internet access and TV. The kitchen is fully equipped with fridge, dishwasher, washing machine and microwave and leads on to the dining room. The large living area has a fireplace and cable TV. Outside there is a large garden with private pool, BBQ and plenty of garden furniture.

There are 2 championship golf courses within Vale do Lobo. The Praca and beach are a five minute drive from this self-catering villa. Cleaning, linen and bath towels are included. Cots/highchairs/fans are available upon request.
