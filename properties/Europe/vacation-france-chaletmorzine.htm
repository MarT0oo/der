Chalet Morzine, Morzine, France Vacation Rental

 

The Villa is a massive chalet of 400m2 with unparalleled living accommodation. It was designed and built by a local architect (Morin) for his own use and is truly unique. The lounge is vast at over 550 square foot, with lots of seating and an incredible inglenook fireplace area that alone will seat 8 people. In the lounge there is widescreen TV with Sky, digital film and music libraries as well as free internet access.

 

The chalet has 11 bedrooms & 7 bathrooms.  There is a separate dining room which seats up to 26 people, a fully equipped kitchen with a large range cooker , dishwasher, microwave and American style fridge freezer.  There is a hot tub in the huge garden (2,500m2), we have recently added a new decking area around the hot tub for the 2007/08 season. 

 

In the basement there is a fully equipped games room with table tennis, pool, darts, table football, Xbox and Play-station games consoles. There is also a separate large TV room with books, games, Sky TV, DVD and video, great if the kids (or adults) want to watch different programs.   The Chalet has free wireless internet access throughout and for those without there own laptops an internet access terminal is installed the TV room.
