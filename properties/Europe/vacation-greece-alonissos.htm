Villa Alonissos
3 Bedrooms | 3 Baths  | Pool | Walk to Beach                                              
Alonissos Island, Greece
  From £300 - £800 per night

In beautiful countryside, on the North-West coastline of Alonissos, just 5 km from the cosmopolitan and extremely picturesque port of Patitiri, a beautiful villa is has been completed. The Villa is set on a wooded seaside, home to olive and pine trees, which slopes down to the sea, offering superb views of the coast of Alonissos and Skopelos.

The house stands perched, just steps away from a beautiful beach area, allowing you to enjoy the wonderful ocean views during your stay.  Each part of the house was designed with the traveler in mind, from the open rooms, to the rich colors and textures throughout.  This is a stunning piece of architecture by any standard.  The Master Suite, which has it’s own bath with Jacuzzi and kitchenette, is setup for those wanting some privacy even when traveling with a group.  The room features a platform bed with wonderful views, sitting area, and flat screen television.  The Guest Bedrooms share a beautiful full bathroom.  One guest suite has a full sized bed while the other is perfect for children or singles with two twins (singles).

 

As you walk through the house you will find an amazing gourmet kitchen that opens up to a fantastic garden area with amazing views.  The living area is inviting and features a flat screen television for your pleasure, also enjoy complementary local and cable channels.  The dining area seats 6 for dining and if you have friends over, there are some additional chairs throughout the house.

 

Most people do not come to Greece to spend their time indoors, but to experience all the romance and charm of a country that has withstood the test of time through the courage and determination of its people.  You can visit the beaches along Alonissos, or take a drive and see some great ruins and historical sites.   No matter what you do, you are bound to enjoy every moment in this very special villa.
