Nice Holiday Villa, Nice, France


“Nice Holiday Villa” is a stunning private holiday home located in the green hills directly surrounding the Nice-City-Centre. Because of its location (250 meter / 850 feet above sea level) the detached villa offers spectacular views on the Mediterranean Sea, Nice and the Southern Alps. It is only a 5 minutes drive to the Nice City-Centre and the beach. With all the advantages of the city-centre of Nice nearby you can at the same time enjoy the space and privacy of our 3500 square meter (40000 square feet) property: a private heated pool, a big tropical garden, 2 spectacular outdoor terraces, a 8-persons spa with direct seaview.


The luxurious villa features a spacious 65 square meter (700 sq feet) livingroom with big baywindows, a modern Italian kitchen, 7 bedrooms and 6 ensuite bathrooms (accommodating up to 16 persons). Modern facilities include airconditioning, Satelite TV, Wi-Fi-Broadband-internet, DVD, ... Nice Holiday Villa is a perfect location for your relaxing holiday, family-gathering or business-event !

Nice Holiday Villa is a perfect and central base for trips in the surroundings; famous places on the Cote d'Azur such as Cannes, Antibes and Monaco are within only a 35 km (20 miles) range from the villa !
Also many other places of interest are within a short drive: the Italian border: 35 km (22 miles), San Remo: 55 km (35 miles), St. Tropez: 99 km (64 miles), St. Paul de Vence: 22 km (14 miles), Aix-en-Provence: 175 km (175 miles), Genua: 190 km (115 miles) and Valberg: 85 km (53 miles) a ski-resort in the Southern Alps.
