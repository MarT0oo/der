Relais Claro de Lua Villa, Latina, Italy Vacation Rental


Relais Claro de Lua is located a short distance from Rome International Airport and is a destination for discerning and top travelers attracted by the beauty of the coast, the warm climate and the white sandy beaches, which spread over 0.5KM.  The Villa is located a few minutes from the historic center of San Felice Circeo.

Still on the subject of welfare there is a large Jacuzzi that can offer moments of pure relaxation in a magical, contemplating a fabulous view overlooking the sea.  Relax to the jet stream and waterfall of our Jacuzzi and be wrapped by the regenerative power of water, which gives relief to the body and spirit, while meanwhile sip an excellent aperitif on board tank.
