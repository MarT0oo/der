Vltava, Prague, Czech Republic Vacation Rental

 

This spacious apartment comprises 280 sm / 3014 sft and is located on the first floor of a grand apartment block, on the banks of the Vltava river, with wonderful views to the Castle across the river, and is a few minutes from the Old Town.

The apartment is situated on the first floor of a turn of the century apartment block, situated overlooking the Vltava River, a ten minute walk from Charles Bridge. The main entrance hallway, leads through into the Living and Dining Room which boasts wooden floors and expansive views across the river to Prague Castle. The kitchen is fully equipped with a hob, electric oven, microwave, dishwasher and American style fridge freezer with integrated filtered water and ice maker. There is a private elevator which opens up directly into the apartment.
