Prague Luxury Penthouse, Prague, Czech Republic Vacation Rental

 

Four bedroom 600 sqm² penthouse with 360 degrees view from rooftop terrace with jacuzzi, sauna and BBQ. This is an exclusive, spectacular and highly representative duplex apartment with a total area of 600 m².

It is located on the last two floors of the house and offers magnificent views of the Vltava River and the Prague Castle panorama on the west side and views of St. Peter and Paul Basilica on the south side and Vyšehrad is in the palm of your hand. There is also a huge 200 m2 rooftop terrace offering stunning views of Prague. The terrace has a beautiful floor made of exotic wood, many flowers, shrubs and trees for privacy, barbecue, seating area and a large jacuzzi for 5 people.

The apartment has 4 bedrooms, 3 bathrooms, 1 living room, study, dining room with fully equipped modern kitchen plus another fully equipped kitchen on the upper floor, library and entertainment room with its own bar, high-quality home cinema and other kitchen.

One of the bathrooms has a sauna and a steam bath next door.

Everything is done to a high standard, in all bedrooms there are spacious built-in wardrobes, marble bathrooms, solid wood floors with high carpet for added comfort and furniture is mostly from exotic countries combined with European antique furniture.

The apartment is situated in a quiet and safe neighborhood filled with traditional Czech pubs and trendy international cafes all within walking distance. The tram station Výtoň is just a 2-minute walk away. From there, you can take a quick ride to the National Theater and Old Town Square.
