Villa Locket, Locket, Czech Republic Vacation Rental

 

The villa Locket is located in the historic center of Loket, built on walls of a former castle, with a fantastic view of the valley. Situated in the west part of the Czech Republic, it is close to all famous spa towns in this region: Karlovy Vary, Marianske Lazne, Frantiskovy Lazne, Lazne Kynzvart.

This is ideal for a group looking to do a lot of touring and traveling throughout the historic region, collecting fine art and visiting the antique shops during the day.  The food is divine and the culture is very laid back and relaxed, with a mix of old world and modern architecture.  You can take the holiday to ski or to enjoy the summers and the city side scenery.  Or do some lovely backpacking during your stay and sleep under the stars as you visit the beautiful cultural sites. 

Close to mountains so ideal for skiing holiday: Bozi Dar, Klinovec
