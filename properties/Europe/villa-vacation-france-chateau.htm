Dordogne Castle, Dordogne, France


The magnificent French Dordogne Castle was built in 1550 and is sited in a dominant position contemplating the valley of the river Isle. After a conscientious modernization and restoration, Dordogne Castle opens its doors as a luxurious, exclusive self-catering rental accommodation and Bed and Breakfast in France.


Inside the Chateau you will enjoy the craftsmanship of those who built it. Monumental fireplaces,  ancient wood paneling, oak beams covered in intricate plaster work, mosaics and wooden floors, all are part of an ancient theme that has only improved in appearance with time.  It is this attention to detail that makes Dordogne Castle one of the best bed and breakfasts in France and renowned for its excellent accommodation facilities.

Outside you will be delighted by a wealth of wild vegetation and formal gardens that stretch over an area of 40 acres. You are welcome to explore as much as you wish of this plentiful environment. We are located at only 10 km from Perigueux, the capital of the Dordogne, which constitutes an ideal starting point for the discovery of some of the most beautiful towns of France.

The Dordogne valley, Sarlat, Domme, Brantome, Les Eyzyes and Saint Emilion are within easy reach from the Chateau. And of course, there is also much to explore in Perigueux itself: the Roman ruins, museums,or  the medieval center with its wonderful open market on Saturdays. Without a doubt, the Dordogne captures the essence of France, and we are at its heart.
