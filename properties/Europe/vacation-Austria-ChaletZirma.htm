Chalet Zirma, Zürs, Austria Vacation Rental

 

The Bentleys House is divided into three boutique chalets. Guests are invited to choose from Chalet Jazzi, Chalet Zirma and Chalet Pazuel. All chalets have the same luxurious finish, as your home away from home should be.

The architecture is characteristic of the Arlberg region and are arranged over two floors offering abundant natural light and featuring a blend of old timber and bricks. Each chalet is individually decorated to provide maximum elegance and comfort in an entirely private environment with state-of-the-art amenities, including satellite TV, music system and wireless internet connection.

Chalet Zirma, is a truly comfortable chalet that can cater for a small family. With 2 spacious bedrooms and comfortably sleeping four, Zirma offers the perfect sanctuary for winding down and relaxing after a day on the slopes. The en-suite bathrooms are adorned with the highest quality amenities complete with luxury cotton rich towels and robes.

The Wine Cellar is a purpose built Chalet Zirmaerature and humidity controlled cave with a 1000+ bottles a la carte selection of fine Old and New World wines and champagne. The wine list offers a vast collection of top producers, mature vintages, large-format bottles and excellent reach across several wine regions. Chalet Jazzi offers unique wine-degustation packages combined with highlights from the kitchen.
