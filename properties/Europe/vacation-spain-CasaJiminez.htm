Casa Jiminez, Ibiza, Spain Vacation Rental

 

6 bedroom villa located in the exclusive area of Cap Martinet, 2 km from the town of Jesús, 5 km from Ibiza Town, Marina Botafoch, Pachá, and 10 minutes walk from the beach of Talamanca. It enjoys spectacular panoramic views of the Mediterranean, Formentera and Dalt Villa.

The villa has 600 m2 built on 2 levels, with a total of 6 bedrooms and 8 bathrooms, 2 living rooms, 2 kitchens and several living rooms, 2 laundry rooms, garden, swimming pool, sauna and jacuzzi. Air conditioning, Fiber Optic (100Mbps) internet, Wi-Fi all around, safes, alarm system with cameras, stereo systems, DVD/VHS and 6 TVs with TDT and satellite connection.

The upper floor consists of an entrance with a guest toilet, which leads into 2 large and illuminated living rooms. One of them has a well equipped kitchen, a large dining room and access to a large terrace with spectacular 180-degree views. The other living room has a breakfast bar, fireplace and access to a terrace with sea view, sauna and an outdoor shower. Each of the living rooms lead to a different bedroom. A master suite with a king size bed, bathroom with tub and shower, sea views and direct access to the pool. The other bedroom has a double bed and a bathroom.

The pool area features a beautiful garden and spectacular panoramic views of the sea and Formentera, several chillouts with shadow areas, outdoor dining for over 25 guests, a large pool of 10 x 5 meters with saline chlorination system (does not use chlorine -beneficial for eyes, skin and hair) and jacuzzi for 8 people.

The villa counts with 2 full-time service personnel, who reside in a separate section from the rest. They perform housekeeping, maintenance and security; are trusted and well qualified, speak fluent Spanish and English.
