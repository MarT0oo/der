Scotland

Scotland is well known for Its strong and brave people and astonishing backdrop, offering one of the best places to go if you want to see various historical castles and if you want to stay in your own holiday castle.  Edinburgh is the Capital of Scotland and one of the places people love to travel to trek around the city and taste the local cuisine and shop in the town center for great gifts to take back home.  Every August Edinburgh has the World’s largest Arts Festival and for those who love art it’s something not to miss.  To find out more see this site http://www.edinburghartfestival.com/. 

Aside from Edinburgh, visit Glasgow for its design inspiration, winning various design awards including: European titles of City of Culture, City of Architecture and Design, and City of Culture.  You’ll find amazing Opera, Theater, and various arts centers throughout the city.  It is a place you have to experience to believe and a city that is known to have some of the best shopping outside of London with its amazing designer shopping outlets and jewelers. 

There are so many reasons to travel to Scotland, while there take a tour through: Ayrshire, Inverness, Kingdom of Fife, West Lothian, Stirling, Dumfries, Dundee, or Aberdeen.  And make note of the regions of The Borders – (eastern) The South West (Scotland's Riviera), The Central Belt, The Highlands (NW) where you’ll find Loch Ness, North East (great tours given), The Hebrides (Islands off the NW Coast), The Orkney Islands (North) and the Shelter Islands.
