Deia Joya, Deia, Spain Vacation Rental

 

Located in the heart of Deià, this traditional Mallorca house is called Casa Deia Joya which means House Master or House of the Teacher and was originally used as a school. Complete renovation was finished at the end of 2011 and our aim was to update the facilities but retain the original character. The house has wooden beams and terracotta floors throughout and the original well is still a feature in the kitchen. There are spectacular views from the completely private rooftop terrace and a beautiful, very private courtyard with a brand new Hot Tub to the rear of the property.
