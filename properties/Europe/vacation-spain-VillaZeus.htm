Villa Zeus, Puerto de Andratx, Mallorca, Spain Vacation Rental

 

The offered estate with its vastly dimensioned rooms is located on a piece of land of about 2,700 m2 which is unique in its kind.

The main house is divided into a representative residential area with a fireplace and a connected TV-sector, a library as well as a spacious dining room. Each of the window elements of the lower sector of the house are sliding elements which facilitate a direct access from all rooms to the pool area and the extensive terrace space. On the ground floor of the main building there are additionally a big kitchen with an industrial area and an access to the exterior dining sector. The upper floor has three huge bedrooms with bathrooms en suite.

The lower area of the house offers a spacious cinema and entertainment area with a bar, a „bodega“ (wine cellar“), the personnel area as well as two separate guest apartments. In addition to this, the basement has an interior pool, a spa zone with sauna and vapour bath. A separate apartment with an own kitchen is available. A special highlight is the garage with storage possibilities for up to ten vehicles and a power-wheel as parking support.

GARDENS
Special value was attached to the planting of the plain gardens. The Mediterranean garden in which the unique pool landscape can be found, is bounded by different palms and guarantees an absolute private sphere.

SAFETY & EXCLUSIVITY
This exclusive estate oriented to the south, which was designed in great detail and with a lot of minimal aspects, fulfils the highest technical and constructive standards. The aim in this was luxury, purism coupled with the highest safety standards.

The philosophy of „Las Brisas“ includes a 24-hour security service, the concierge and maintenance service, which look after the estates if their owners are travelling. The further tasks include the monitoring of the cleaning personnel, the gardeners, the pool service and other commissioned service providers.

The exclusive luxury location „Las Brisas“ is located beyond Puerto de Andratx with a unique view of the bay of Cala Llamp. In the southern direction the view is dominated by the Cape of the Cala Llamp, the Mediterranean Sea and the neighbouring island Ibiza, to the north by the mighty Tramontana Mountains.

Puerto de Andratx is located in the south west of Mallorca and captivates with international and prominent audience. The romantic fishing port is embedded in an enchanting, Mediterranean landscape of mountains, green hills and steep rocky coasts. The location at the south west coast of the island offers a constantly mild and balanced climate.

The complex of “ Las Brisas” consists of fifteen mansions with a total constructed area of approx. 100.000 m2, the owners of which are of various nationalities.
