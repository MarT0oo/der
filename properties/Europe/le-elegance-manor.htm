Edwardian Mansion, Eardisland, Herefordshire, England Vacation Rental


Covered in wisteria, this Edwardian country mansion is a sight to behold. Just five miles from the historic cathedral city of Hereford, The Lodge has undergone a full refurbishment. As such it offers just as much luxury as it does heritage. Combine this with its eight acres of grounds and it’s the perfect backdrop for both business and pleasure.

Not only does it boast 12 en-suite bedrooms, there’s a swimming pool, sauna, tennis court, gym and trampoline. Add to that three large reception rooms, a Zen garden and games barn, and it’s as much a holiday resort as a country retreat. One where your every whim has already been catered for, allowing you to just relax and focus on enjoying your stay.

Even with a full quota of 30 guests, it is impossible for The Lodge to feel overrun. Its 12 en-suite bedrooms, three large communal rooms, spacious kitchen and expansive grounds, mean there’s more than enough room for one and all.


However don’t think that space is all this Edwardian mansion has to offer. Making sure that no detail has been overlooked in its design, The Lodge’s décor and finish are the embodiment of luxurious living.

While set in eight acres of grounds, themselves surrounded by the rolling Herefordshire countryside, The Mansion is in no way isolated. Far from it, just five miles from the cathedral city of Hereford, it actually offers the best of both worlds. The serenity of its seclusion with all the trappings of a modern city close to hand.


To make the most of your time in Herefordshire and for your convenience, we would recommend driving to The Lodge. However as long as some of your guests do so, others may choose to take advantage of the direct trains to Hereford from London Paddington (3 hours). Or if they’re coming from further away, either Bristol or Birmingham airport are approximately 70 miles from the house.
