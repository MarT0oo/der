Vienna, Vienna, Austria Vacation Rental

 

This elegant and spacious 196 sq. metre apartment is situated on the first floor of a historical mansion block situated on a quiet street in a central location near the famous shopping street of Mariahilferstrasse.

The apartment comprises of two double en-suite bedrooms, a large drawing room with historical wooden detailing and a separate sitting/TV room along with a kitchen/dining room, all with high ceilings and antique plaster mouldings. The detail throughout the apartment is breath taking.

Vienna is not just a city of monuments. It is a city which is alive with music and opera and dance; it is a city of traditional coffee-houses, offering mouthwatering cakes and chocolates, and wine taverns featuring wines from the Wachau Valley along the Danube River. A stay in your home in Vienna can easily be combined with a visit to your home in Prague, only a short train journey away
