Chalet Clarité, Swiss Alps, Switzerland Vacation Rental

 

Chalet Clarité is set in one of the most astounding and exclusive locations in the Swiss Alps, surrounded by snow capped peaks and adjacent to the national forest, it sits perched on the mountain high above Verbier. Access to the chalet is via a 12 person Snowcat & 2 ski-do’s, making the journey in and out very special.

This luxurious and elegantly composed chalet is perched on the mountain above Verbier surrounded by forest and km’s of snowy pistes. It was designed with privacy, space and every comfort in mind. Chalet Clarité is cosy and elegantly styled with rich textured features, wood panelling and custom stone work. Chalet Clarite has 3 south facing terraces, all day long sunshine, with BBQ and heated outdoor dining area. These are the ideal surroundings for dining Al fresco or relaxing with a glass of bubbly at the end of the day.

Chalet Clarité sleeps up to 11 guests in 6 en-suite bedrooms. The master suite has its own fireplace with en-suite bath and shower room. 5 other en-suite bedrooms are spread over 3 levels. The main living area features a massive stone fireplace which dates back to the 1700’s. It really is the centrepiece of this room. There are huge panoramic windows to take full advantage of the breathtaking views. Great care has been taken to create a warm and cosy ambiance.

There is a wonderful “Carnotzet” Cellar dining area for raclette and fondue, ideal for families/groups entertaining in the evening time after a day on the slopes. The chalet features a wellness / spa area with 5m “Jet” pool, sauna and gym. Through large double sliding doors, you can take in all the mountain peaks, with views as far as the eye can see.
