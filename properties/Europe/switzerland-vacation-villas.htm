Traveling to Switzerland

When people think of Switzerland the normally think of The Swiss Mountains and the wonderful skiing from The Alps to Jura.  Switzerland is also known for its multicultural languages, it is a haven for those from all over the world.  It also has one of the lowest unemployment rates, maybe due to the fact that its tourism, banking, and luxury production of Swiss watches, knives, and clothing keep it going year after year.

Places to visit include: Lake Geneva (one of the largest lakes in Europe with a high population of French speaking citizens), Jura Mountains (for its skiing, hiking, lakes), Bernese Lowlands (Agricultural), Bernese Highlands (mountainous region with many beautiful towns to visit), Central Switzerland (Historical Sites to visit), Zurich (A major city where you can find all the luxuries of a big town, including shopping, dining, entertainment), Valais (Beautiful Glacier Views), Ticino (Italian Region).

Other places to consider are: Locarno ,Solothurn, Interlaken, Zermatt, Davos, St. Moritz great ski villas, Grindelwald, Gstaad, Crans Montana, Verbier (for great hiking).  No matter where you go in Switzerland we’re sure you will enjoy your stay.  Visit our cottages, vacation homes, castles, and ski chalets.
