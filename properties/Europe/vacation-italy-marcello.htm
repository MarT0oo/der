Venetian Marcello Luxury, Venice Italy Vacation Rental


The Venetian Villa Luxury is owned by the original aristocratic family the Marcello family. The Marcello family has had a great role in Venice military, social and political history throughout the centuries, being one of the twelve most powerful families of the city.

The Marcello family offers an exclusive vacation apartment for 8-9 people to be rented, which is located in the left wing of the villa and faces the Italian garden. The walls have original marble plaster surface and most rooms have parquet floor and plaster decorated ceilings. The bathrooms are furnished with hand painted ‘Vietri’ tiles and high standard bath furniture. All the bedroom windows overlook the main Italian style garden and the villa’s façade. Ca’ Marcello lays in the middle of the southern Veneto region. The air conditioning has independent climate control in every bedroom. A private fenced garden with swimming pool, gazebo, barbeque set and other facilities is available at exclusive use of the guests. The kitchen is fully equipped and there are a washing machine and a dryer available for the guests. Dinners can be self-arranged by the guests, but can also be cooked by a private chef and served in the garden, everywhere the guests want.

Count Jacopo Marcello and his family are very welcoming and appreciate allowing their guest be part of the house and its day-by-day life. During their stay, guests are usually accompanied by the owner through a private visit of the main building which ends with an aperitif together in one of the living rooms, to appreciate the real Italian lifestyle.
