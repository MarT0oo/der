Villa Alegre, Praia de Salema, Algarve, Portugal Vacation Rental

 

The gardens of this elegant villa slope down to the smooth golden sands of Praia de Salema beach, a secluded and uncrowded beach where you won’t have to battle with the masses for space on the sand. Guarded by ochre cliffs, this quiet stretch of sandy shoreline is free from high-rise development and retains its untrammeled, yesteryear feel. Further inland sits the village of Salema, a traditional town known for its delicious seafood restaurants.

Built into the shoreline just steps from the sand, this four-bedroom villa blurs the boundaries between indoor and outdoor living, with large glass sliding doors opening onto roomy terraces. The show stealer is the rooftop, where you’ll find a furnished terrace dotted with shade-providing parasols and an infinity pool overlooking the Atlantic.

Inside, sophisticated, modern-style interiors mirror the colours of the Mediterranean, with pops of various shades of blue incorporated into the white-dominated space. In addition to the four bedrooms, the villa also has a spacious open-plan kitchen, living room and dining area.

Entrance hall with staircase leading to a lower floor and the first floor. The entrance level has two bedrooms with front line ocean views, each featuring a large double bed, flat screen TV, an en-suite shower room and separate dressing room.

One bedroom with a large double bed, flat screen TV, an en-suite shower room and separate dressing room, with direct access to the property´s enclosed parking area.

You will enjoy the east and westward facing terraces with fabulous views of the coastline of Salema beach, with the picturesque view of the village and the fishing boats below. To the left, the master bedroom, which features a large double bed, flat screen TV, an en-suite bath/shower room and a standalone bathtub, separate dressing room, all with full frontal breathtaking water views. All areas have natural light and access to a large terrace, overlooking the coastline and the nearby Salema beach.

Private infinity pool overlooking the coastline with fully furnished terrace and parasols, allowing for outdoor living and entertaining.

On first floor an open space design featuring a modern state-of-the art, fully equipped open plan kitchen, with an American double fridge/freezer, induction hob, oven, microwave, dishwasher, coffeemaker and toaster. This area also includes a contemporary living room, decorated with comfortable elements, evoking beachside living, with flat screen TV, and a dining area that is equipped with a table and chairs, seating 8 people – an inviting place for a morning coffee or a leisurely dinner.
