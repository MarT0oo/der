Superb Melissa Villa
5 Bedrooms Ensuite | 5 Baths | Pool | BBQ                                           

Algarve, Portugal

  From €4950 – €6950 per week

This superbly located villa is in the sought after area of ‘Monte Golfe’. The property is built in traditional Moorish style and has been maintained by the current owners to a very high standard.

The villa is spread over 3 levels with five en-suite bedrooms, four of which are on the ground floor, with the master-suite occupying the entire upper level. The master suite has its own living area, large bathroom with Jacuzzi bath and separate shower, walk-in wardrobes and a large private terrace.

The spacious living accommodation opens onto the extensive entertaining areas and gardens, with a feature stream and a pond, outside bar and covered dining area, plus an orchard. There is a large basement and living/games room with billiard table, fireplace, ducted air cooling and electric central heating, marble floors in the living areas and hard wood floors in all bedrooms.

Superb Melissa Villa accommodates up to 10 people.  The home is equipped with Satellite TV, DVD/CD player, barbeque area, private outdoor pool and private garden.  Enjoy beautiful garden views as you make Superb Melissa Villa your vacation home away from home.

Quinta do Lago is without a doubt one of the most exciting and exclusive resorts in Europe and understandably boasts some of the most spectacular and beautiful villas in the Algarve. Founded in 1972, this 800 acre residential estate borders miles of unspoiled golden beaches and the pristine Atlantic Ocean, undulating pine forests and some of the best golf courses in the world.

Quinta do Lago is a major destination for golf enthusiasts. Two thousand acres of land has been transformed into a resort dedicated to a golfer's fantasy lifestyle, with villas spaced strategically apart, all the comforts of a metropolitan centre and championship and non-championship courses for any golf aficionado. Of course, aside from golf, the resort has other attractions, such as horseback riding, tennis, two busy shopping malls, two miles of golden beaches, a fine selection of restaurants and nightclubs, and three thousand hours of sunny blue skies each year.

Quinta do Lago is a very appealing summer holiday destination because of its easy access to the rest of Europe. Only twenty minutes drive from Faro Airport, Quinta do Lago is only two hours away from both Lisbon and Seville, Spain.
