Chalet Pazuel, Zurs, Austria Vacation Rental

 

The Bentleys House is divided in three boutique chalet apartments. Guests are invited to choose from three Bentleys House apartments, Chalet Jazzi, Chalet Zirma and Chalet Pazuel. All chalets have the same luxurious finish, as your home away from home should be. The architecture is characteristic of the Arlberg region and are arranged over two floors offering abundant natural light and featuring a blend of old timber and bricks. Each chalet is individually decorated to provide maximum elegance and comfort in an entirely private environment with state-of-the-art amenities, including satellite TV, music system and wireless internet connection.

Chalet Pazuel offers delightful rooms, each individually decorated with contemporary flair. Enjoy en suite bathrooms and state-of-the-art office facilities for executives. Spread across two levels, the first floor opens out into the chalet’s spacious kitchen, dining and lounge areas. The generously sized rooms, intriguing cutaway display areas and wall-mounted 60inch LCD screen, make the Chalet a winning mix of stylishly professional and the endearingly playful. On the top floor is where you find two master bedroom and en suite bathroom. Guests will be looked after by a team of multilingual professionals, who are there to facilitate all your needs.

You will be provided with breakfast, a fabulous afternoon tea and a 4 course gourmet dinner on a daily basis. The Chef de cuisine captures guests with exquisite international and traditional regional cuisine. Indulge in the finest menu creations from the highly acclaimed chef, carefully selected vintage wines and champagne from the chalets wine cellar in your private and charming retreat.

The Wine Cellar is a purpose built temperature and humidity controlled cave with a 1000+ bottles a la carte selection of fine Old and New World wines and champagne. The wine list offers a vast collection of top producers, mature vintages, large-format bottles and excellent reach across several wine regions. The chalet offers unique wine-degustation packages combined with highlights from the kitchen.
