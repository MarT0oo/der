Chalet St. Clair, Valais, Switzerland Vacation Rental


Chalet St Clair benefits from an exceptional location at the heart of a hamlet of chalets built in old wood.   Blessed with the maximum amount of sunshine, it faces the « Imperial Crown » of 4000m mountains and a panoramic view of the whole valley.   This property is located just a few minutes’ walk from the centre of the village and 1 km from the pistes. You can also take advantage of the free shuttle bus and leave your car in the garage.

Recently completed, chalet St Clair has been designed with taste and elegance, combining authenticity with comfort. Natural materials such as wood and stone combine perfectly in a warm, welcoming and classic style.   The chalet extends over three floors and accommodates up to 8 or 9 people.  It consists of 4 bedrooms, of which 2 have en suite bathrooms. The rooms on the lower level all have direct access out into the garden.

You enter into a spacious hall way, which overlooks the double-height living / dining room, which gives your chalet a sense of space and comfort.   From the living / dining room, with its stone fireplace, stroll out onto the balcony and marvel at the splendid panorama of the Val d’Anniviers.

After a hard day on the mountain, you can ease those stiff muscles in the sauna, before relaxing and enjoying precious time with your friends and family in this charmingly authentic and luxurious chalet.
