Chalet Meteze, Grimentz, Switzerland Vacation Rental


Chalet Mélèze is one of several luxurious properties in the prestigious « Beauregard La Résidence , a collection of unique upmarket properties offering some contemporary zest to the traditional Swiss chalet.  This property is located just a few minutes’ walk from the centre of the village and close to the piste. You can also take advantage of the free shuttle bus service and leave your car in the private underground car park.

Recently completed this chalet has been finished with taste and an eye for detail.  The « mountain loft » style interior is designed and furnished in a manner to help you relax and free yourself from the stress of daily life, combining natural and noble materials to create elegant and comfortable surroundings.

This 3-floor chalet enjoys a stunning location overlooking the rest of La Résidence and the picturesque village of Grimentz.  Stroll out on to you balcony and take in the magnificent view over the Val d’Anniviers and its snowy peaks.  Accommodating 6 people, this chalet has three bedrooms, one of which has an en suite bathroom and is under the eaves with floor to ceiling panoramic windows and breathtaking views.  At the heart of your holiday retreat, you can stretch out in front of a log fire after a long day on the slopes, and share a drink with your family and friends before an excellent dinner.

The chalet’s layout works well for both families and groups of friends.  Allow yourself a little respite from the daily grind and discover a gentler pace of life in Grimentz and the Val d’Anniviers.
