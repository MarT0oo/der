Galé Beachside Villa, Praia dos Salgados, Algarve, Portugal Vacation Rental

 

Gale Beachside Villa is a gorgeous villa located just steps to the sandy beach, with views of the ocean from almost every room. The 5 bedroom villa is 28KM from Faro Airport making this a wonderful and convenient location for people traveling to the Algarve area. The house has every resort amenity you could want, from the bedding to tapestries, this is a one of a kind home with every luxury. Here we can also arrange a chef and maid daily, which is perfect for families traveling with small kids and those who want to stay home and enjoy the location.

For back-to-nature experiences, the nearby Salgados nature reserve, a protected stretch of coastal wetlands, promises excellent birding, walking and biking opportunities. Includes access to resort amenities such as restaurants, bars and spa—as well as top-shelf chefs, DJs, security, or anything else you may need for an in-villa event.

Galé Beachside Villa enjoys one of the most privileged locations in the Algarve, positioned directly on the gorgeous Galé beach, on the outskirts of a peaceful nature reserve. The Salgados lagoon is one of the most important coastal freshwater wetlands in the Algarve, offering opportunities for birding, walking and biking throughout the year. The towns of Albufeira and Guia are close by with a plethora of local cultural experiences as well as other attractions including water parks, a sand sculpture festival and extensive shopping at Guia Shopping Mall. Night owls can take advantage of the Algarve´s lively summer hotspots and buzzing nightclubs.
