Villa Vicentina Algarve Portugal


This is a spectacular villa, which has been finished to an amazing high-end specification, offering endless open-space and luxurious, contemporary interior design.

The villa is arranged over two floors, with the main living area upstairs affording direct views over Martinhal Beach and the Ocean. There are 4 large bedrooms and a huge, open-plan first floor living area/kitchen, opening out onto a fantastic terrace with amazing ocean views.

There is a large, private swimming pool (with electronic hard cover) in the rear garden, adjacent to a spacious, covered al-fresco dining area and BBQ.


They say the Portuguese waters hold a mystery that lovers often find shown in the glimpse of the ships passing in the night. The shores of Portugal from The Porto (North Portugal) where you can find history afoot while visiting local historical sites: CASA-Museu de Guerra Junqueiro and the Museum of Quinta da Macieirinha. Or one could easily slip away to visit the city and visit the stock exchange and cathedral. Or if your prefer Tras-os-Momtes where you can do tons outside, such as canoeing or mountain trekking. On the North Coast of Minho you can discover amazing caves and superb beaches.
