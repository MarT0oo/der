Villa Vianci, Siena, Italy


Villa Vianci is an ancient mansion dating back to the eighteenth century, located in the heart of Chianti Sense, just 17 ​​km away from Siena and 45 Km from Florence. It is located in a vantage position on a hill above the old town of Staggia Senese. Recently subjected to a fine renovation and restoration, that has enhanced typical charm of the Tuscan villas without altering the original structure, Villa Vianci is today an ideal place to enjoy a pleasant and exclusive stay under the Tuscan sun and to visit the most important art cities of the area.

It was originally founded to be a manor to house the noble families during the hottest months of summer. The villa belongs to our family from several generations and is now managed by the young owner and his girlfriend who take care of their guests with attention and respect in a friendly and genuine family atmosphere. Its location in the center of Tuscany, a few km from the highway Firenze-Siena and near the historic intersection between Via Cassia and Via Francigena, makes Villa Vianci the perfect location to undertake tourist paths among enogastronomic, cultural and art-historical sites in the many typical places and villages of the area.
