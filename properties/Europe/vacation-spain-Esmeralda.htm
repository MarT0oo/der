Esmeralda, Cala D'Hort, Ibiza, Spain Vacation Rental

 

Just a 5 minute drive from the beaches of Cala D'Hort, Cala Vadella and Cala Carbò this delightful private property is tucked away on a natural reserve on the southwestern side of the island. Thanks to its location Esmeralda enjoys the utmost privacy being one of the few private villas in the area and boasts a picture-perfect sunset view overlooking Es Vedra.

True to its natural setting an inspired stonemasonry paves and connects the two main terraces, each with their own private pool overlooking the stunning scenery around. Inviting lounge areas were designed on each level of the terrace, duly covered by wooden canopies and fitted with cozy summer furniture. The al-fresco dining experience is sure to inspire memories thanks to its thoughtful position on the most panoramic corner of the lower terrace.

The interior design of Esmeralda is a winning concept, a fusion of iconic design furniture, decapé wooden elements and pan-religious icons that pop-up throughout the property's common spaces and bedrooms in a stylish manner. The appreciation for natural elements spills also into the indoor spaces where cacti and small palms trees find space and solid rock walls were left in their original position.

Esmeralda is an idyllic getaway with a unique style sure to amaze families or groups of friends looking to enjoy a more natural side of Ibiza. The property's layout also allows for added privacy due to the original structure being left intact and the location of some bedrooms.
