Villa Cleopatra, Vale do Lobo, Portugal


This is a unique opportunity to rent a superb coastal property. The kitchen has also a wall-mounted espresso and a separate preparation area that can be closed when you have guests. The airy double height hall gives these rooms a wonderful sense of space.

The German designed fold back glazed units open up the entire space achieving a very clever indoor / outdoor feeling to the entire ground floor of the property.The Italian designed Gervassoni external furniture on the various terraces at ground and first floor level provide those renting the property superb external spaces in which to relax and appear to be a natural extension of the interior design. The heated 10 meters pool with electric cover is surrounded on four sides by idyllic relaxation and dining areas. The pool pavilion with its sensational views to the beach provides a covered dining area comfortably seating 10 and has a flat screen TV and gas fire for those who wish to avail of either. The BBQ entertainment area is discreetly positioned to the rear of this pavilion.

The formal reception rooms mentioned earlier are only two of the five available living spaces in the property. The lower ground floor entertainment room is fully equipped with Blue Ray DVD player, WII, Play Station and again a full sky package.Also on the downstairs level is the fifth bedroom, slightly less good that the other bedrooms but still of high quality, with its en suite shower room.The house is equipped with an exceptionally high spec music and entertainment system including an I-pod doc for personal music choices. This system also houses the CCTV security, which is linked to the main security at Vale do Lobo.

All 5 bedrooms are fully air-conditioned, en-suite bathrooms and there is Sky TV in 4 bedrooms.There are two bedrooms at the ground floor level, two at first floor and the fifth is in the lower ground.On the ground floor are two bedrooms: a guest room opposite the living room has doors to the side of the house, twin beds, walk in shower and its own small dressing area.

The second guest bedroom has king size bed and door leading to the garden and pool terrace.The exceptional master suite beautifully decorated in tones of grey is a totally private space encompassing a king sized bed, en-suite bathroom with walk-in shower, standing bath and separate toilet.
