Chalet Nadine II, Courchevel 1850, France Vacation Rental

 

This exceptional 360 m2 chalet offers an indoor swimming pool, hammam, sauna and a Jacuzzi. Very spacious, it can host 12 people. It has a large living room, a beautiful terrace facing south, a fully equipped kitchen, 5 bed rooms and 5 bath rooms as well as a garage for 2 cars and a laundry room.

Close to the Pralong Ski Lifts for your stay, this makes it much easier to get to and from the slopes.  The facilities include: Room 1 = 1 double bed 1.60 x 2.00, Room 2  with 1 double bed 1.60 x 2.00, Room 3 with 2 single beds 0.80 x 2.00, Room 4 = 2 single beds x 2.00, Room 5 = 1 double bed 1.40 x 1.90, Fully equipped kitchen, Living room with fireplace, 5 W.C. (+ WC in Spa) and 5 bathrooms.

There is a separate SPA area, including swimming pool, hammam and sauna, private Jacuzzi in master bedroom, television, satellite television, VCR, telephone with individual counter, internet access (ADSL)/Fax services, mezzanine with videos for children, terrace, balcony, ski room, laundry room with washing machine and dryer, garage for 2 cars, 3 hours of house cleaning per day, Linen provided (including bathrobes)
