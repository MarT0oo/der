Vardy Chalet Meribel
6 Bedrooms | 7 Baths | Billiard Room
Meribel, France
From £20,200 per Weekly

Chalet Vardy - utterly private but never isolated: the village of Meribel is close by but not too close for comfort. The unusually large grounds see to that, and there's plenty of space for epic family snowball fights.  Fabulous state-of-the-art facilities for comfort and relaxation, as well as entertaining and of course business, come as standard in this beautiful, unique chalet.  But all this loveliness would be nothing without top class skiing on the doorstep. Set amid an enormous area served by arguably the world's best lift system, the chalet attracts skiers of every ability to a smorgasbord of runs across Les Trois Vallees.

Bedrooms are as individual as the spirit of the chalet, with that woody Alpine charm combining seamlessly with state-of the-art technology.  You'll need a microscope to calculate the thread count in the sheets. Bathrooms go for total immersion with deep tubs for soaking ski-weary muscles. Downstairs, there are steam and massage rooms and, for after-dinner competitiveness, a billiard room as well as a cinema.  As distinctions between work and life seem to vanish, there's plenty of space and technology to satisfy all needs without encroaching on those bent purely on fun.

You will be whisked from airport to mountains, to find a full complement of staff waiting for you. Chef can fulfill your every culinary fantasy - luckily Les Trois Vallees' unparalleled skiing burns off the calories.  Go on ask for something wild, he's bound to be able to deliver. It's that thing where you get what you want, when you want it. Ice bucket filled with snow, anyone?  Nothing is too much - no really. Early morning powder session? Breakfast and boots, warm and waiting as early as you like. Spontaneous bash for friends staying nearby? No problem. The huge entertaining space cries out for parties. Just ask.

 

Chalet Vardy is all about everything you need to support the sublime ski experience of the Trois Vallees. Sauna, outdoor hot tub, steam and massage to soothe those weary muscles. Billiards, cinema, television with DVDs and satellite and iPod docking station for chilling at the end of the day.  For the children, there are toys, sledges, games, books, CD’s, DVDs and masses of space outside for snowballing, while the grown-ups relax on fur-lined loungers on the wide terraces. Those who cannot separate business from pleasure will find broadband, telephones, meeting facilities and fax.
