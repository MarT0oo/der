Mas Torroella, Palafrugell, Costa Brava, Spain Vacation Rental

 

This delightful 14th century “Masia” enjoys a most privileged & unique location amidst the peaceful meadows & tree clad hillsides of the Gavarres mountain range. Spread spaciously over three floors and tastefully restored to retain much of its original character the property sleeps up to 20 pers. in 8 bedrooms, each with en suite facilities & air-conditioning. In addition to numerous comfortable sitting areas the property also boasts a private spa which comprises of an indoor heated swimming pool with incorporated Jacuzzi, Finnish sauna & fitness room.

As befitting a property of this stature, manicured lawns, colourful flower beds, lush shrubbery and mature trees grace the surrounding gardens where guests can enjoy the large, private swimming pool (heated upon request) in total privacy, as well as a full size tennis court, a table-tennis court, and a children’s playground area.

Mas Torroella has been tastefully restored to the highest standard of luxury, elegance & refined comfort whilst retaining the charm of a traditional 14th century Catalan “Masia”.

Spreading spaciously over three floors, an abundance of exposed stone work, vaulted & hand painted ceilings combine to create an ambiance of rustic elegance that is complimented by an exquisite mixture of traditional and contemporary fabrics & furnishings. For those seeking an idyllic holiday amidst romantic surroundings, this unique property offers the perfect mix of privacy & luxury.

Located across the courtyard opposite the main house, this attractive property, originally the Groundsman`s cottage, has been lovingly restored to the highest standard using traditional materials that reflect its rustic history. La Caseta has one double bedroom and a twin bedroom, each with en suite facilities. A utility room is situated just off a spacious open plan kitchen/ living/ dining area. Patio doors conveniently open out to a sun drenched terrace, equipped with outdoor furniture. The property sleeps up to 4-6 people in 2 bedrooms, all with central heating and air-conditioning.

Mas Torroella is the perfect starting point for walks and routes, as it is in the middle of the mountain massif of the Gavarres. Much of the Gavarres is covered by the hiking network, and the paths are clearly signposted and well-suited for hiking or cycling. Our district has a network of high-quality nautical services offering visitors a wide range of possibilities for doing water sports, making cruises or following courses, as well as scuba-diving or snorkelling, kayaking, windsurfing, water-skiing or jet-skiing, take a sailing course, go fishing…

Today, anyone has the chance to do just that in an ultralight (delta wing), helicopter or balloon. For the most daring there is the option to do parachute jumps.

Catalan cuisine and specially from Girona are enjoying an exceptional moment, thanks to its deeply rooted, hundred-year-old culinary cuisine, to its high-quality raw materials found in the territory and in the Mediterranean and to the skill of its chefs of worldwide prestige with their restaurants here, such as Ferran Adrià, Joan Roca and many others.

Visit one of these restaurants is a must and it will give you the opportunity to try some of most emblematic dishes such as rice dishes, fish stews or suquets and our mar i muntanya dishes of seafood and meat.
