Campanet Estate, Mallorca, Spain Vacation Rental

 

A Spectacular Holiday Villa set behind gates with a sweeping driveway. The villa is totally private with just a short walk to the village of Campanet and a 12 minute drive to Pollensa.

The private outdoor pool is exceptionally large with a bridge going over it. There is also a heated indoor pool. You can relax on several beautiful terraces for dining, for sun, for shade, for lounging etc. Mature tropical gardens surround the superb swimming pool and BBQ terraces. Here you totally relax in complete privacy in this truly impressive location with far reaching views across seasoned cypress trees to mountain ranges.

The master suite has direct terrace access with views over the pool and gardens. There is a games room with large plasma screen, pool table and bar. Wii and Wii fit available.

The main house has four bedrooms.
The wonderful casita has two bedrooms and two bathrooms and the apartment has one bedroom and one bathroom.

There are mature gardens with fruit trees.

From the huge front terrace six steps lead down to a balustrade terrace with seating area and a few further steps down the magnificent 14.00-8.00m swimming pool has a "central bridge".

Large sun terraces surround the pool and are comfortably furnished with cushioned reclining sun loungers. A very special feature is the Caribbean style BBQ casita with spacious seating areas and circular dining table.

The property is not overlooked and you can enjoy dining up by the main house, or barbecue down by the pool or over at the Japanese barbecue.

The accommodation is perfect for groups of friends or family sharing. There are so many different rooms, levels and terraces.

There is air conditioning throughout the entire property.
