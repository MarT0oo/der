Casa Alegre, Varandas do Lago, Algarve, Portugal Vacation Rental

 

Casa Alegre is a pretty 3-bedroom property located in Varandas do Lago. Varandas do Lago is a luxurious development of exclusive villas which is located between Quinta Do Lago and Vale Do Lobo. The area is surrounded by some of Europe's best golf courses, beaches and the area's best shopping and spa facilities. Ancao beach is only a few minutes’ drive and features a water sports center and a number of beach front bars and restaurants.

This lovely villa is welcoming and bright. The fully equipped, modern kitchen overlooks the terrace, which has a private pool. There is plenty of space for you to enjoy the Algarve sunshine on the number of sun loungers provided. Also located on the terrace is a BBQ and al fresco dining area, the perfect way to enjoy the glorious Algarve evenings and sunsets. There are 2 charming double bedrooms and 1 bedroom with 3 single beds, which all have a relaxed feel to ensure you have a peaceful night’s rest. The well-presented living and combined dining area is naturally well lit, with a vibrant style. The games room is equipped with a pool table, foosball table and a mini putting green area. Also located in the games room is a cycling machine and weights.

This attractive villa is a great choice for your holiday, it is in a fantastic location and really does have everything you need to have amazing time!
