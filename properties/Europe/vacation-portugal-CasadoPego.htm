Casa do Pego, Comporta, Portugal Vacation Rental

 

Casa do Pego in Comporta Portugal is one of the most beautiful beach villas located in Comporta, one of the most exclusive places in Portugal. With a unique modern design, rising from the sand dunes, just a few meters away from the best portuguese beach - Praia do Pego. This architectural gem created by Pedro Ferreira Pinto features a private sunny terrace with a heated swimming pool, a garden, a front deck with breathtaking views and an interior patio for total privacy and relax.

Casa do Pego is a special retreat where Nature meets architecture and great design, on a 5 minute walking distance to the beach. An exquisite, clean design where concrete, glass windows and pine wood decks blend perfectly.

Filled with iconic mid century modern classics, Casa do Pego is the neverending "art project" of our Lisbon-based family. It’s our second home and we love to spend our free time by the beach on this natural paradise. The suspended fireplace and heated floor make it irresistible throughout the year, ideal for off season breaks.

This fully equipped house has 3 bedrooms, accommodates 6 people and it's just one hour away from Lisbon and its International Airport. Casa do Pego is available for rent for a memorable family holiday and also for film, photoshoots, design and fashion productions and commercials.

On top of the house, lies a sunny terrace with a stunning private heated swimming pool The concrete pool was finished with aquabright onyx for an exquisite look and feel. The spacious terrace has an elevated position to ensure maximum privacy. Covered in pine wood, it's an ideal place to sunbathe and relax. From here you can watch the sunset while hearing the sound of the ocean, facing the green crops field and the Arrabida Mountain. The controlled temperature pool allows nightswimming under the starry comporta sky.

If you would like the full experience dreamexoticrentals offers you the full touring experience along with day trips to Lisbon and boating excursions, all with a guide to show you the way.
