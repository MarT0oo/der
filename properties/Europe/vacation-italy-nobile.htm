Villa Nobile, Florence, Italy Vacation Rental


Villa Nobile is an elegant and charming property, recently restored, it is plunged into a wonderful Tuscan landscape, the country-house is scattered on a “knoll” overlooking the Tuscany hills. Two floors, joined by a great central staircase, compose the house. The large and elegant rooms make the atmosphere ideal for a comfortable and relaxing holiday. In the summer, a large garden sloping down to the swimming pool offers the opportunity to appreciate completely the charmer beauty of one of the most beautiful country all over the world. On the ground floor you find two wonderful big rooms divided by a large central corridor.


On the right hand side there is a large kitchen communicating with the dining room, and the dining room, it is furnished with antique furniture.


On the first floor the house has six large bedrooms which join charm and sobriety of the details together with the functionality. The house is completely furnished with antique furniture, rooms are air-conditioned and equipped of TV sat and bathrooms with showers. Some of them have arranged facilities for handicapped people. The Villa has a wonderful garden with an heated swimming pool and Jacuzzi, rich of relaxing and evocative natural sceneries. Ancient trees and green meadows make the ambient rich of shadows and quiet places: the ideal place to relax and rest the body and mind.
