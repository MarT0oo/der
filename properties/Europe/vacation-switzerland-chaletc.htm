Chalet C By Chivoone, Grimentz, Switzerland Vacation Rental


Chalet C By Chivoone is one of several luxurious properties in the prestigious « Beauregard La Résidence », a collection of unique upmarket properties offering some contemporary zest to the traditional Swiss chalet.  This property is located just a few minutes’ walk from the centre of the village and close to the piste. You can also take advantage of the free shuttle bus service and leave your car in the private underground car park.

Recently completed this chalet has been finished with taste and an eye for detail. The « mountain loft style interior is designed and furnished in a manner to help you relax and free yourself from the stress of daily life, inviting you to make the most of every moment spent with your friends and family.

The chalet is spread out over 3 floors, each enjoying a magnificent view over the old village of Grimentz and its snowy peaks.  Available for 6 to 10 people, it consists of 4 bedrooms, of which two have en suite bathroom or shower. In the eaves of the chalet, the stunning master bedroom enjoys a free-standing path and large floor to ceiling windows.

The pivoting fireplace provides a warm and stylish centre piece to the spacious open plan living and dining room.  After a beautiful day’s skiing or hiking, you can take advantage of your private Jacuzzi and allow yourself to wallow in the beauty of Grimentz and the Val d’Anniviers.
