Villa Stylish, Sagres, Algarve Portugal Vacation Rental


A stylish and beautifully presented 3 or 4 bedroom villa with a south facing garden and private swimming pool, just a few minutes walk to Martinhal Beach.

There are 3 large bedrooms in the main villa, all 3 benefitting from ensuite bathroom/shower rooms and having direct access to a terrace with sea views. The fourth bedroom is a large double bedroom with ensuite that has been converted from the original garage (which is just a couple of steps from the main villa).

The villa has been professionally interior designed, and is fully equipped with everything you need for your holiday. The garden is secluded and has a great outside dining area, with gas barbeque.


They say the Portuguese waters hold a mystery that lovers often find shown in the glimpse of the ships passing in the night. The shores of Portugal from The Porto (North Portugal) where you can find history afoot while visiting local historical sites: CASA-Museu de Guerra Junqueiro and the Museum of Quinta da Macieirinha. Or one could easily slip away to visit the city and visit the stock exchange and cathedral. Or if your prefer Tras-os-Momtes where you can do tons outside, such as canoeing or mountain trekking. On the North Coast of Minho you can discover amazing caves and superb beaches.
