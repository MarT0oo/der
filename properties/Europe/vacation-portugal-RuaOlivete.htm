In Lisbon’s funky Bairro Alto neighborhood, the 3-bedroom Rua Olivete vacation apartment will give you an unforgettable vacation experience. By day, you’ll be surrounded by medieval architecture and some of the city’s most impressive museums; by night, the neighborhood comes alive with a huge variety of nightlife. Stroll 3 minutes to Lisbon’s Botanical Gardens, or take in the stunning views from the Miradouro de São Pedro de Alcântara viewpoint (9 min). Metro and bus stops are nearby.

Our local interior designer has created a romantic, colorful space. Vintage furniture and antique bronze accents transport you back in time; you’ll love the apartment’s many fun surprises, including a fireplace in the living room. The fully equipped kitchen and three plush bedrooms will make you feel at home. Premium amenities include free Wi-Fi, cable TV, hotel beds and linens, luxury toiletries, and a Nespresso machine.

Perfect for families or groups, this elegant holiday apartment brings Lisbon’s culture inside your walls.
