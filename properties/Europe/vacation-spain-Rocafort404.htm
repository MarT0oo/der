Rocafort 404, Carrer de Rocafort, Barcelona, Spain Vacation Rental

 

Located in the vibrant Eixample district, the Rocafort 404 3 bedroom apartment is the perfect place to experience Barcelona’s local culture. A close walk to the Parc Joan Miro, Eixample is an area filled with local artisans, funky restaurants, quaint cafes and pubs. The metro takes you all over the city, including to La Sagrada Familia (15 minutes), where you'll find many of Barcelona’s most famous museums, cathedrals and art galleries. It’s a lovely walk to The Maritime Museum (30 min), Guell Palace (30 min) and the Aquarium (30 min). The Rocafort metro stop is 2 minutes away on foot, and bus stops are just around the corner.

With large windows that afford plenty of natural light and upscale furnishings carefully chosen by our local interior designers, visitors will love the great mixture of style and comfort. You’ll find everything you need to make a great meal with fresh local ingredients in the fully-equipped kitchen and get a great night’s sleep on beds of the highest quality. Premium amenities include free WiFi, cable TV, high-end toiletries, hotel beds and linens.

An upscale vacation apartment ideal for families or a large group of friends, this lovely space will make you feel right at home.
