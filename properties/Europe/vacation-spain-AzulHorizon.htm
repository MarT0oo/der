Azul Horizon, Formentera, Spain Vacation Rental

 

Hidden amongst the lush Mediterranean vegetation Azul Horizon is a jewel of technology in a great panoramic position, this villa set a new standard of comfort by introducing a revolutionary approach to the most modern amenities while maintaining at the same time a simple, traditional and somewhat wild feeling that characterizes the entire island.

The architecture is composed of square shapes in warm colors with the evident purpose to emphasizes the contact with nature and allow guests to enjoy the most of the time outdoors; many relaxation areas are arranged wisely, wooden platforms, comfortable seating and couches full of pillows invite guests to relax while admiring the stunning views, or tasting an aperitif accompanied by music diffused discreetly from the sound system throughout.

Azul Horizon develops ideally around the living room completely opened on two sides to get maximum benefit from the favorable position, the atmosphere is sober and elegant with comfortable sofas and furniture in brown and cream tones that create a cozy feeling; particularly interesting is the combination of handmade artworks with technological features like iPod dock, HD screen, and a dedicated iPad application that allows guest to “control” the house. Connected to the living room is the functional and fully equipped modern kitchen, which an attractive wooden dining table with twelve chairs.

There is not a similar property like Azul Horizon anywhere on Formentera, recommended to the most demanding guests, who can appreciate and take advantage of technological and innovative amenities at their best, in a position with great suggestions that ensures maximum privacy.
