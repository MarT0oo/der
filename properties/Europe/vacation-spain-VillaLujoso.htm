Villa Lujoso, Puerto Andratx, Mallorca, Spain Vacation Rental

 

Truly an awe inspiring luxury villa on the island of Mallorca with breathtaking panoramic view of the sea; in good weather conditions you can even look over as far as Ibiza. Surrounded by nature and located at the end of a cul-de-sac, you can enjoy this view in complete tranquility and privacy. Constructed in 2014 with amazing tech and fantastic sea views for year round sunsets in Port Andratx. The beach and the center of Port d’Andratx are only a few minutes away.

This modern interpretation of a Mallorcan villa was built on a hillside on 1,890 square meters. The villa’s 950 square meters spans three-stories and is stylishly furnished, with an eye for individual detailing and exquisite works of art. This is a place to savor privacy at its most lavish. There is ample space and luxury for up to 12 people – in this one-of-a-kind location surrounded by unique natural countryside. Includes 6 bedrooms with breath taking sea views and all with ensuite bathrooms. Relax in the KLAFS-made sauna with its SANARIUM® (turn your sauna into five different bath types), splendid shower, separate half bathroom, and changing rooms. Take time to keep fit on Technogym equipment and with a magnificent view of the sea in the adjacent gym. This villa features a spacious kitchen with utility room/second kitchen, a dining room with a wonderful garden and sea views, living room with fireplace and bar, a wine cellar, a cloakroom and a guest toilet.

This villa offers wireless internet throughout the property; as well as underfloor heating, air conditioning, alarm system and automatic time-controlled blinds. A key feature is the high-quality BOSE audio system (indoors) and B&W system (outdoors). Everything is controlled by iPads, which are provided in the house. The villa has been designed with care and taste and fitted out with only the very best equipment. For instance, the high-quality fabrics have been produced from the only weaving mill in Majorca and all bathrooms have been equipped with Dornbracht taps. The spacious main kitchen is equipped with Gaggenau appliances and a wine fridge. There are other kitchens on the terrace which make it easy to barbecue.

Spacious terraces, garden and lawn areas, a 15-metre swimming pool and a jacuzzi characterize the outdoor area. A large outdoor dining area, outdoor kitchen / BBQ and a lounge including gas fireplace round out the exterior. The fitness and spa area includes a sauna, a spacious shower, changing facilities, a separate toilet and a fitness / relaxation area with fantastic sea views.

The highlight of the villa is undoubtedly the panoramic view of the sea; Yet the technology is impressive too! Includes wireless internet throughout the property, underfloor heating, automatic time-controlled blinds, automatic covers Ospa technology for the very best water quality in the pool and jacuzzi (LED lighting can be selected to match your taste or mood), BOSE audio system (indoors) and B&W system (outdoors) and

Following a reception service with welcome champagne, savor the luxury of the Villa Lujoso all to yourself. You have exclusive use of all the villa’s facilities. In addition, pool maintenance, housekeeping and assistance services are all part of the villa’s inclusive service. Top-quality bathrobes, towels and bed linen are provided. Five-star bath and beauty products also await you. Your personal housekeeper will bring the daily newspaper, maintain the pool and attend to your every need (shopping is charged). Come to Villa Lujoso and be welcomed.
