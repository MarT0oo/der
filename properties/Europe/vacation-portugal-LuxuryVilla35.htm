Luxury Villa 35 Algarve Portugal


A stunning property enjoying spectacular views over Martinhal Bay and Sagres, Villa 35 enjoys and enviable position near the beach.

Accommodation is arranged as one Master bedroom suite with and additional lounge room area on the first floor – which could allow for an optional sleeping area for two children.

There is one further double en-suite room and one twin bedroom, and three bathrooms in total.

Living areas in Villa 35 are spacious and stylish. The well-appointed amenities include a modern, fully fitted kitchen, laundry room, satellite TV and air conditioning throughout.A generous first-floor terrace overlooks a pretty south-facing garden with BBQ facilities, and a private heated swimming pool.
