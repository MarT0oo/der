Villa Alegria, Vale do Lobo, Portugal


This stylish and modern townhouse overlooks the Golf Course in Vale do Lobo with a private terrace and pool. This property can sleep up to 7 people with a spacious bedroom on the lower ground floor with 3 single beds.

The award winning resort of Vale do Lobo offers a wide range of holiday fun: golfing, laying on the beach, mini golf, horse riding, fantastic restaurants, tennis, water sports, night life, wellness center.

With beautiful and diverse landscape this resort boasts golden beaches, clear water and Mediterranean climate.

Lounge with dining area , fireplace and cable TV leading to exterior terrace.First floor: Master bedroom with double bed and access to terrace and walk in wardrobe and en-suite bathroom.

Guest bedroom with single beds and en-suite shower room.Lower ground floor: Bedroom with 3 single beds. Utility area with washing machine and tumble drier. Cloak room.Private pool and terrace with Gas BBQ.
