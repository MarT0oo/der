Bailen, Carrer de Bailen, Barcelona, Spain Vacation Rental

 

In the tranquil residential district of Eixample, the 4-bedroom Bailen vacation apartment is within easy reach of the city's attractions. Nearby Passeig de Gràcia is lined with high-end shops and fine dining, while a visit to Barcelona Aquarium, just 20 minutes away, is fun for the whole family. Gaudi's unforgettable Sagrada Familia and the architecture of his Casa Batlló and Casa Mila are right on your doorstep. Nearby bus and metro stops will take you all over the city with ease.

Our talented local designer has added modern flair to this spacious and light-filled apartment. With three bathrooms and a double sofa bed in addition to the four bedrooms, this vacation apartment in Barcelona has plenty of space for ten guests to enjoy city living. The gentle color palette in the bedrooms is just right for peaceful nights, and the modern kitchen is perfect for preparing a Spanish feast. Premium amenities include free WiFi, cable TV, hotel beds and sheets, high-end toiletries, and a Nespresso machine.

Open a bottle of Spanish wine, sink back into the sumptuous sofa, and relive the memories of a busy day's sightseeing.

Please note that the sofa bed can only accommodate one person, one bedroom has no natural light and the living room is a bit dark.
