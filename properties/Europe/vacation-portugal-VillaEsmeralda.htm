Villa Esmeralda, Algarve, Portugal Vacation Rental

 

Villa Esmeralda is situated in the quiet cull de sac of The Village, a sought after location very close to Vale do Lobo and Quinta do Lago. The recently rennovated property boasts contemporary interiors and all necessary mod cons.

The villa is within five minutes of beaches and golf courses, and established restaurants such as “ la Parrila “ and “ Gourmet Natural” are just two minutes away.

The villa is made up of six en suite bedrooms, of which three have private patios. The lounge and kitchen are open plan and lead out to a beautiful pool and BBQ area.

The swimming pool faces south for optimum sun and is a perfect spot for an evening cocktail.
