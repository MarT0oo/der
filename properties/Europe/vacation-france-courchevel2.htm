Courchevel Chalet II, French Alps, Courchevel, France

 

This Luxury Chalet in the French Alps, just minutes from everything in Courchevel 1850, is a modern and fully equipped ski in and ski out Chalet that is  situated in the Hameau de Cospillot. The chalet is built on four floors with a modern four person lift serving all floors.

 

The Lounge is located on the 3rd floor and enjoys sunshine and views with 2 balconies. It seats 10 in three couches and has a fully equipped bar in one corner. It is equipped with a wide screen TV connected to Sky Digital (including sports and movie channels) and incorporating surround sound, DVD player, CD player and radio.

 

The Kitchen is open plan with the Lounge. There is a full working kitchen complete with all necessary cooking facilities.  The Dining Area is adjacent to the kitchen and comfortably seats 12 people. There is also a fully equipped bar. 

 

The Master Bedroom is situated on the second floor and at the front of the chalet. It has an ensuite bathroom including a Jacuzzi bath.  Etoile has a full ensuite bathroom while Roc Merlet is a little smaller and has an ensuite shower room.  The Jardin Alpin has built in wooden bunk beds with an ensuite bathroom.  The Grange room contains a single couch/bed with an extra pullout bed. This room also contains a TV and can be used as a children's lounge. 

 

The garage has been converted to a fully equipped ski-room with racks for skis, timed boot-warmers and shelving.  On the entrance level there is a six-person sauna, which is perfect for those nights when you get in late from your day on the slopes.  Additionally you will find that the first level has a glove warming and drying area, so you are assured the owners have thought of everything.
