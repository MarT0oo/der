Almiral de la Font, Sitges, Spain Vacation Rental

 

Almiral de la Font is like no other villa. This boutique property is located just south of Barcelona near the beautiful seaside town of Sitges. It caters for 16-28 guests and has been luxuriously renovated with high tech comforts such as a 19 metre heated infinity pool, hot tub, billiard room and state of the art home cinema. Staying at Almiral is a unique experience: renovated with great care and attention to detail, this beautiful masia (Catalan manor house),offers high-tech modern comforts whilst retaining the historic charm and character of the original building. Set high on a hillside in the Penedes wine region, the masia is surrounded by the Garraf National Park and offers dramatic sea or mountain views from every room or terrace. This tranquil haven has more than 300 acres of private grounds with vineyards, olive groves and fruit trees, yet is only a 30 minute drive from Barcelona and 10 minutes from the lively beach resort of Sitges.

This historic masia is ideal for families and corporate groups, and for occasions such as weddings, filming and production launches.

When you stay at Almiral de la Font Masia you will enjoy a substantial private residence with a living area of over a thousand square metres allowing you as much space and privacy as you like. It has a large tiled courtyard with palm and citrus trees and an atmospheric moroccan lounge providing welcome shade.

Almiral has over 300 acres of private grounds with 14 kilometres of tracks in which you will find large areas of vineyards, olive groves & fruit trees. Almiral produces its own artisan brut nature and white wine.

You will also enjoy sea views from 200 meters altitude onto the coastal resorts of Sitges and Vilanova Y La Geltru. There is easy access to the closest town, Sant Pere De Ribes and proximity to numerous good local restaurants and vineyards. The famous Penedes and Cava producers including Torres Freixenet & and Cordoniu are within visiting distance for wine enthusiasts. Almiral has direct access to Garraf national park for excellent hiking and mountain biking and yet is ony 45 minutes from Tarragona and the much acclaimed theme park - Port Aventura.

You will not be short of entertainment. Almiral has its own infinity solar heated swimming pool(19 x 6 metres) and children's pool, a jacuzzi hot tub, a snooker/pool room, a children's play room, an exercise area, a table tennis table and basketball net and a trampoline.

Almiral can also provide extra services on request ; excellent chef, extra cleaning, catering, chauffeur for private excursions, baby sitter, outdoor gas heaters, marquee and catering;equipment hire and personal laundry.

History. Almiral de la font, meaning "admiral of the (water) spring", enjoys huge grounds whose xarello grapes are used for its own brut nature and white wine. Amongst the 14 kilometres of private tracks you will also find pear, olive, cherry, walnut and almond trees in addition to many naturally growing herbs and vegetables (asparagus, rosemary and mushrooms). The house, a protected building and fortified masia, was built before 1592 and was extended in 1800. It has been and continues to be a significant landmark in the Garraf area and was formerly used exclusively for winemaking and farming. Though totally renovated and adapted to a high standard of modern comfort, the original details have been preserved, in particular the wine presses and bodega. The windows have been enlarged to allow in more natural light. Balconies and terraces have been added.

Cottage adjoining the villa - self-contained with its own kitchen & bathroom and separate access.
