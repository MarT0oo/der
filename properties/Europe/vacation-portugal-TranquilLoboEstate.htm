Tranquil Lobo Estate, Vale do Lobo, Algarve, Portugal Vacation Rental

 

Built in the vernacular style of this community, yet the interpretation is very modern and stylish in my opinion, contemporary and minimalist, tranquil and spacious but I feel it’ll be one of those villas that you either love or loathe.

There are no tiles, the floor is polished concrete that feels so smooth to bare feet, all the surfaces in the bathrooms and even the sinks are crafted in polished concrete with glass screens to contain the water. Swinging doors where possible are eliminated in favour of chunky sliders that are often glazed to frame beautiful views of the pool, the gardens and the sky. Even the air conditioning has been integrated into the building so the usual wall-mounted boxes are eliminated and replaced with elegant vents supplying cooled air.

The master suite is on the first floor, with double bed facing the huge sliding window that opens to the private terrace, en suite bathroom with twin wash basins, toilet and walk-in shower. On the lower floor is a guest suite with a double bed and en suite shower room. Two more bedrooms each have twin mattresses on a king-size base so can be made up as king or twin beds depending on requirements. Each has en suite shower rooms and access to a private side terrace to enjoy the open air. The fifth bedroom is ideal for children, with twin beds, en suite shower room and doors to a side terrace.

Down a couple of steps into the stunning living space, from the glossy kitchen to the rustic dining area and the lounge, you’re drawn to the perfectly presented and framed view of the pool and gardens in perfect alignment. But enjoy the interior too, the kitchen is wonderful and has all you could wish for. The dining space leads naturally to the outdoor dining space and the lounge is so comfortable and welcoming, filled with art and beautiful things, the polished floor covered with a simple rug and vintage lounging cushions. It is perfect!

Open the sliding doors to a shaded sitting area beneath a pergola with two cushioned, wooden benches, the terrace of polished concrete a seamless extension from the living space. The large amazing swimming pool has integral steps for easy access, then relax on one of the reclining loungers. Occupying one of the highest spots in the community, there are views outside the perfectly manicured confines of the villa although surrounding hedges increase your privacy.
