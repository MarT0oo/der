Mas Vilanova, Costa Dorada, Spain Vacation Rental

 

Driving in to the private estate of “Mas Vilanova” and feel total calmness take over. Quietude is only interrupted by the abounding nature and tranquil waterfall sound from the Pool. A large yet cozy classic Catalan manor, of over 250 years old, 10 bedrooms comfortably accommodates up to 20+people, making this villa an ideal rental to share with your extended family or friends.

Drive up to the property and see that the lush is lined with majestic trees and Palm trees. Lounge chairs and a relaxing daybed surround the sun-drenched pool. Alfresco dining with charming lanterns and pillowy seats provide the ideal gathering spot for sharing relaxing meals.

Inside, the unique decor is a mixture of classic Spanish and touches of modernity. The lovely settee with cozy throws and hand-crafted heavy oak wood eettafels epitomize the homes effortless elegance.. The colorful eat-in kitchen with stainless steel appliances is a dream for those who love to cook; just off the kitchen is a refurbished harvest table made for enjoying many courses with the entire clan.

The master suite in the main villa has a queensize bed , en-suite and a private terrace with charming views. The villa’s “wing” area also has a master suite, en-suite bathroom with double sinks and shower, a spacious walk-in closet at a private patio. The remaining eight bedrooms have either king or queen sized beds, and the home’s seven bathrooms ensure there’s ample space for guests to have their privacy. A private detached cottage with a queen size bed and en-suite bathroom and Kitchen is ideal for teens or a couple.

Though the relaxation you’ll feel at Mas Vilanova gives little influence to venture further afield, the many delights of Spain are within arms reach. For a day of tasting Spanish wines, within few Km distance. A tennis club is only two minute drive. The mediterranean, long stretched beaches of Cubelles and Cunit are just authentic Spanish tourism. For more international Tourime is Sitges and Vilanova is within 10 minutes drive. And the city of Barcelona, with its endless opportunities for exploring, is just 40 minutes away by car.
