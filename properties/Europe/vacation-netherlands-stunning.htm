Stunning Amsterdam Apartment, Amsterdam, Netherlands Vacation Rental


This NO smoking short stay design apartment has a stunning view on the Singel canal and sleeps 4. The apartment has its own heating system, which makes it very comfortable through the whole apartment. It has 2 bedrooms one with a king and one with a queen size bed, for smaller children a cot is available.

The living space has a couch, chairs and dining area. On request we can place an extra bed (extra person=surcharge).  On the vide you'll find a desk with a PC with internet access and ipod/ mp3 docking station; Whenever you need to work during your stay, a broad band internet connection secures you with fast access. On request we can install and configure a voip telephone (surcharge) in your room with your own telephone number.

The full kitchen, is equipped with Nespresso machine, kettle, fridge, gas cooker and oven.  In the private and spacious bathroom there's a toilet, bath and separate shower.   Bedroom 1 has a comfortable king sized bed and bedroom 2 a queen-sized bed. With the secure entrance guests can relax and enjoy the option of a longer term let. We supply once a week Linen and Towel change and once a week and upon check out cleaning service is included in the rate.
