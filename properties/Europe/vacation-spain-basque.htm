Palacio Basque, Oiartzun, Basque Country, Spain


Palacio Basque is an elegant 600 square meters palace suitable for meetings and family travels. Basque is decorated with exquisite taste, divided in four floors, six bedrooms, five bathrooms and a toilet. On the ground floor guests will find parking for two vehicles and the sport area; equipped with a little Basque pediment to play squash, frontennis or Basque pelota; exercise bikes, regular mountain bikes and a dart game.  Also found on the ground floor are the common areas; equipped with a brand new kitchen, eating room, living room with sofas and TV, and a relaxing area with sofas in front of the chimney. Each room has a view overlooking the splendid little forest surrounding the building.

On the second floor there are six bedrooms, two of them equipped with double bed and en-suite bathrooms.  Three of the other bedrooms are double-single-bedded; and the last bedroom has a double bed. There are two bathrooms, each of them on one side of the corridor; and one of the modern showers has a sauna function.

Basque is surrounded by large terraces and gardens in a private forest of beech and oak. The forest is perfectly designed for relaxation and contact with nature.  The palace sleeps 14 guests (12 adults and 2 children), provides free wi-fi, is close to the city, and is the perfect destination for those looking for peace and relaxation.
