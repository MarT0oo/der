Chalet Jade, Courchevel, France Vacation Rental

 

Chalet Jade is the quintessential ski chalet: surrounded by pine trees, built from giant wooden beams and with large balconies giving views over the mountains and snowfields.  Decorated in contemporary eclectic style, the chalet has a double-height, open-plan lounge with a roaring log fire, home cinema and large, leather sofas.

 

The dining room and kitchen are ideal for breakfast, uniting with balconies for a buffet lunch or for dinner cooked by one of our private chefs.  Après-ski, staff collect, dry and store all of your clothes, boots and skis ready for your next session.

 

A massage, afternoon tea or champagne and canapés are there for the asking. Relax in the sauna and Jacuzzi, watch a film or satellite television on the home cinema.

 

Six guests sleep in three bedrooms all with en-suite bathrooms. Those on the ground floor have a private balcony while the master bedroom is set in a gallery over the lounge.

Jade – winter skiing at its peak, 1850 metres to be precise
