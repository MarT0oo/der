Luxury Villa 29 Algarve Portugal


Orientated towards the beach, with wonderful views across Martinhal Bay, Villa 29 “Casa da Praia” is appointed throughout to exceptionally high standarts.

Villa 29 comprises of three large twin bedrooms of which two are en-suite and one large double bedroom en-suite.

The living spaces generate a welcoming ambiance that is enhanced by stylish interior décor. Under-floor heating, air conditioning and an open fire add the finishing touches.

Modern and fully equipped with kitchen, laundry roomand satellite TV among other amenities, the property features and attractive covered terrace with a built-in BBQ and a set of patio furniture. A private 7,5m x 4m heated swimming pool is sunk into a generous landscaped garden. A fully removable pool fence makes Villa 29 especially suitable for families with young children
