Twin Villas
4 Bedrooms | 3 Baths | BBQ | Scenic Views                                             
Crete, Greece
  From €130 - €150 per night

Two twin villas, luxuriously constructed and decorated with care, are built in a quite village Prinos, 10 km away from Rethymno city and only a couple minutes from the beach of Scaleta. An ideally destination available for vacation during the whole year.


The villas are located amongst olive and orange trees. The scenery offers an escape of calm and peacefulness by the view of the mountain hills of Philoritis and on the other the wide blue of the sea.

Each villa consists of a garden with a barbecue and balconies fully equipped with wooden tables and comfortable chairs. In the interior of the villa we meet a specious living room, fireplace and a modern fully equipped kitchen. At the same level there is a bedroom with a separate balcony and a bathroom. A few stairs above there are two more bedrooms which they share a big balcony with a breathtaking view and an extra bathroom.

The village, with its ancient history and traditional ways of life, is an ideal place to learn about customs, traditions and experience an authentic rural life. Throughout the area the visitor is invited to walk in a region of particular natural beauty, to admire important monuments and to experience the customary local hospitality. We are pleased to offer our delightful region for excursions or holidays, combining naturalist and archaeological routes that, in their total, constitute an important 'map' of experiences. The Municipality of Kouloukona organizes every year many interesting cultural events open to residents and to visitors alike. The "Talea Festival" with theatre performances, art exhibitions, concerts and various other ravishing activities is a yearly summer festival well worth visiting.
