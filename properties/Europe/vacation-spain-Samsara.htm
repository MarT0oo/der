Samsara, Ibiza, Spain Vacation Rental

 

Samsara finds space halfway between San Rafael and Santa Gertrudis in the Ibizan inland. It is a grand private property located in a position that allows to draw away from the fast paces of the local nightlife, which remain nonetheless within reasonable distance, along with some of the more popular beaches of the island.

Entering the estate through the main gate one accesses the garden, a clear anticipation of the spaces Samsara boasts throughout. The property was conceived to host large groups with great comfort so it comes as no surprise that the private pool is surrounded by sun beds and parasols, a fitting furnishing choice that is also shown in the outdoor dining and relaxation areas.

Indoors all rooms adhere to the spaciousness already previewed in the outdoor spaces, ample rooms are fitted in a sober and functional style where comforts reigns abound without useless excess. The bedrooms are divided amongst the main house and the various guest houses scattered throughout the estate, grating a reasonable level of privacy to guests.

Samsara is without a doubt one of the largest property properties on Ibiza, designed and built to cater to the demands of large groups who wish to spend time together in the privacy of a great setting. Furthering even more this concept there is a small private club on the premises, with DJ console and professional audio system, available to guests who wish to party within the intimacy of their rental property.
