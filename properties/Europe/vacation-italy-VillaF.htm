Villa F, Venice, Italy


Villa F is a newly restored 16th century villa, originally home to a number of Venetian noble families and in the early part of the 20th century a famous guesthouse, the Casa Frollo. This guesthouse catered to an eclectic clientele of painters, writers, and actors including Gabriele D'Annunzio, Eleonora Duse, and Mabel Holland.

The villa, divided into independent units, some of which connecting, is the first and only all-suite residential Villa with hotel service in Venice, offering unique privileges to its guests such as:
•free access to the facilities of Palladio Hotel and its three-acre garden
• use of the adjoining Palladio's 450m² Spa
•use of Restaurants of The Bauers Collection
• complimentary transport to the Bauers S. Marco.

With original wall frescoes, Italian and Venetian 'Terrazzo' floors, bespoke pure linen and silk fabrics by Rubelli, Donghia & Jesurum, and equipped kitchens with wine cabinets by Brummel, the Villa F is a place of understated luxury beyond anything else seen in Venice and totally world class...put together with the fabulous views, access to a three acre private garden, as well as the Spa, it really is the ultimate space to be in.
