There is a Music System with two speakers in the outside covered BBQ area. The 8 sun loungers and day beds are enough for eleven people. The dining area has a large Italian wooden table and chairs which seats a maximum of ten people. There is a smart TV room with music system and PlayStation 3, which includes a three seat sofa and two puffs. On the ground floor is the first twin bedroom with an en suite bathroom, a terrace, flat screen TV and dressing room. The second en suite bedroom can be prepared as either a twin or double bedroom and also has a terrace, flat screen TV and walk-in closet. Both rooms have a table and chairs on the terrace. One the first floor is the master bedroom which can be prepared as either a twin or double bedroom, with a wall mounted flat screen TV, en suite bathroom and walk-in closet.
