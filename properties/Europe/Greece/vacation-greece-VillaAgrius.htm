Villa Agrius, Mykonos,Greece Vacation Rental

 

This unique Mykonos Grand Villa is located in the posh area of Lia on the top of a hill overlooking most of the southeastern part of the island with a breathtaking view almost in every direction. Situated between two of Mykonos most beautiful and popular southern beaches, Kalaphatis and Lia, a jewel of the Mykonos Grand Villa collection of rental villas in Mykonos, Villa Agrius provides striking panoramic views of the arid, wild Mykonian landscape and the majestic Aegean Sea with the Islands of Paros, Naxos, Amorgos and Donusa in the distance. Sitting at the wind protected outdoor living area one is surrounded by the natural rock formations alleged to be the petrified remains of Giants slain by the legendary Hercules, while from the wind protected outdoor dining terrace located at a level below the overview of Lia and Kalafatis bays, amply compensates for winters long anticipation till the arrival of spring and summer. The spacious pool being placed at an even lower terrace provides the opportunity to swim at a cloud level view and striking panoramic views of the arid, wild Mykonian landscape.

Entering Villa Agrius, a spacious living/dining area for eight to ten guests together with the fully equipped and fully functional kitchen welcomes you in for a long and glamorous stay. Furthermore, this level includes a guest bathroom and a bedroom with two single twin beds ideal for the youth and with an independent entrance. Up a few steps and at a mid-level landing a bedroom en-suite with a double bed is perfect for a couple. Located on the first floor /horizon level, is a spacious master en-suite double bedroom appropriate to satisfy demanding guest needs. The master bedroom shares a veranda with the adjacent guestroom en-suite, having two twin single beds and an independent entrance that makes ideal for a couple of guests who seek privacy from the rest of the group.

The Agrius Grand Villa “throne” position awaits its guests to come and feel at home end experience a royal vacation with comfort and glam. Its hosting flexibility, due to its layout, makes this rental villa in Mykonos appropriate in accommodating a variety of guest specifications.
