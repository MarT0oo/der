Villa Eliz, Aleomandra, Mykonos, Greece Vacation Rental

 

An exclusive private retreat, or rather a mini private village where groups of friends or families can come together and share a fascinating getaway, villa Eliz is located in the area of Aleomandra, close to the town of Mykonos.

With a total living space of 700 square meters, this luxury compound consists of the main villa and five independent guest houses that can welcome up to 20 guests in superior style. The interior and exterior common areas are large and inviting, still, since the guest houses are autonomous, the respective parties can always find privacy. Depending on their mood, the guests can head to the bustling town or unwind in the serenity of the complex.

The property is in a whitewashed cube and stone traditional design, surrounded by charming Mediterranean gardens and stone paved paths. An amazing large private pool with sun beds, daybeds, and umbrellas is set in front of the majestic views of the Aegean, and equipped with showers and changing rooms. On the various terraces there are multiple lounge and seating areas all overlooking the sea, charming outdoor dining areas and a built-in barbeque area.

Vibrant colors and a décor of refined comfort prevail in the spacious interiors, against generous views and the white surfaces that are representative of modern Cycladic architecture. The airy ambience, cozy furnishings and modern amenities make villa Eliz feel just like home.

To top it all off, the compound provides lots of opportunities for fun and group activities. An exciting perk of the complex is the triple functioning sports area which converts to a tennis, soccer or basketball court. There is also a fabulous playroom with table soccer, air hockey, and ping pong tables and gym equipment. This mini village would not be complete without its charming little white chapel, the perfect place for intimate weddings or baptisms.
