Villa Super Paradise One, Super Paradise, Mykonos, Greece Vacation Rental

 

Just above the renowned beach of Super Paradise, lies the private complex Super Paradise Villas that comprises three villas. The first of which, has its own infinity pool with clean view of the Aegean Sea with built-in spa, separate entrance and private outdoor area where a big dining table, a BBQ, a big bar and a state of the art sound system lie. In the site of the stone-built dining room which is surrounded by huge rocks, just a few steps away from the pool and the kitchen, you can enjoy wonderful meals, quick and refrishing snacks all day long and relax with your friends chatting or having a drink at night. The internal of SuperOne is ergonomically designed with modern style and decoration. On the upper level there is a fully equipped kitchen along with the main dining room and the living room that has a view to the infinity blue. One level down lie the five bedrooms, each of which has two twin beds or a double bed, en-suite bathroom, as well as direct access to the terrace.

All properties of the Super Paradise Villas complex are ideal for a large group of friends or families that fancy spending their vacation together in a pleasant and safe environment. The privacy that every semi-detached offers, is also advisable for smaller groups or families that seek a friendly and comfortable environment in a majestic location.
