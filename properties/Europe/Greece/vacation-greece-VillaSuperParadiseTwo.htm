Villa Super Paradise Two, Super Paradise, Mykonos, Greece Vacation Rental

 

SuperTwo, which is part of the Super Paradise Villas complex, sits above the renowned beach of Super Paradise, offering a sensational view of the beach, the Aegean Sea and the islands of Paros and Naxos.

The private pool by the villa and the teak furnished pergola, dominate in the space. The BBQ, the mini bar with the sound system and the stone-built lounge create the ideal space for the guests to relish and relax. The villa in its interior is specially designed in order to keep separated the bedrooms from the other parts of the property. Hence, the level of the entrance consists of an ergonomically designed kitchen, along with a spacious living room. The whole decoration and furnishing is a combination of minimal and traditional cycladic style with the excessive presence of stone. All three bedrooms with en-suite bathrooms are arranged on the lower level with access to the terrace through the staircase that leads to the pool. Each bedroom is equipped with all the modern amenities, including air-conditioning and TV set.

SuperTwo, can host both large and small groups that are in search of relaxation and comfort in a modern and refreshing environment, just a few steps away from one of the most renowned beaches globally.
