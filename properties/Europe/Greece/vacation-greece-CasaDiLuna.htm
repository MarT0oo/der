Casa Di Luna, Mykonos, Greece Vacation Rental

 

Casa di Luna is a unique 300sq.m villa, located in a 4000sqm plot on the south-eastern tip of the island and very close to the beaches of Kalafatis (walking distance, 10-15 minutes), Agia Anna, Lia, Kalo Livadi, Elia, Ftelia, Panormos and Agios Sostis. Its’ minimalistic design renders it very appealing to young spirited people who want to have the time of their live in Mykonos and it has been featured in the magazine Mykonos villas for three years in a row.

The estate is composed of the main villa and 2 guest houses. It can accommodate 7 couples or 14 people. It has 7 bedrooms, each one with an en suite bathroom, four living rooms, three kitchens, WC, outside dining area covered by a pergola, bar, bbq, outside bathroom with shower &WC. A stunning 95 square meters swimming pool suitable for large parties which we would be happy to help you organize, Wi-Fi, satellite TV, home cinema, indoor and outdoor surround sound system, central A/C, a beautiful garden full of olive trees, parking space for 10 cars and a power generator in case of power cut from the electricity company.
