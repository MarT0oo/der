Villa Perseus, Agios Lazaros, Mykonos, Greece Vacation Rental

 

Welcome to Villa Perseus, a luxury Mykonos villa in Agios Lazaros, perfect for 13 guests. Either family type or party lover, you couldn’t have picked a better spot; totally wind-protected, private and with amazing view to the Aegean Sea. You will fall for the 105 m2 infinity pool, but if you want to explore beaches as well, the famous Psarou is only 2.8 km away.

Shall we start from the inside? 5 master bedrooms with en-suite bathrooms spread throughout the upper and the lower level. 2 guest rooms are available as well. Cozy living room with fireplace and a fully equipped kitchen with dining area create a homely atmosphere dressed with a luxurious feeling.

But that’s not all. Stepping outside you can choose to relax on sunbeds, built-in benches and tables and shaded pergolas. The element of the water is dominant of course as there is the infinity pool as well as a jacuzzi! You will have a lot of photos in blue, that’s for sure; press the escape button and leave the rest to us. Our concierge will be there to help you with everything you need! From breakfast to transfer, and yoga to island hopping, don’t hesitate to ask! We have your back.
