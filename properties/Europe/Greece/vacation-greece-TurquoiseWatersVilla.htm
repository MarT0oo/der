Turquoise Waters Villa, Mykonos, Greece Vacation Rental

 

This newly built luxury Mykonos villa of 450sqm built in a 5.500sqm lot, has a breathtaking view of the bay of Ftelia, its sandy beach, gourmet restaurants, archaeological site and its impressive waves that have established Ftelia as the wind surfers’ and kite surfers’ paradise.

This luxurious venue is ideal for the guest in search of tranquility and privacy as well as for the active traveler. You can dwell in the 60sqm pool, have fun barbequing in 2 barbeques or hike down 500m and enjoy the lovely turquoise waters of the sandy beach and surf on the waves or simply relax in the serenity of the villa gazing at the sea and stars in the 4 outside sitting areas.

At a distance of 2 km from the picturesque village of Ano Mera, 6 km from the sizzling night life of Chora, and 4 km from the beaches of Elia, Kalafatis and Kalo Livadi, Ftelia Retreat is in the middle of the Mykonian universe.
