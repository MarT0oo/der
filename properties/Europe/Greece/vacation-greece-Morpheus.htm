Morpheus, Mykonos,Greece Vacation Rental

 

Named after the Greek god of dreams, Morpheus is a villa truly nothing short of a dream itself. Set above the bay of Agios Lazaros, it is part of an ultra exclusive estate where comfort, quality and views blend to give guests an unforgettable experience. The villa’s terrace features an infinity pool built in a panoramic position as well as a patio with barbeque and a marble table ideal for outdoor dining. A small veranda has also been built right next to the pool.

The villa is built on three different levels. The main level features the living/dining areas and a double bedroom with direct access to the pool. The large modern and fully equipped kitchen can be found on this floor as well as a large living room with access to a balcony and the charming dining room with their views over the pool and Aegean sea. The master bedroom is located on the upper level and has its own private balcony, a four-poster bed, a walk-in wardrobe and a Jacuzzi tub in its en-suite bathroom. Two identical double bedrooms are located on the lower level along with a kitchenette with a marble coffee table. Air-conditioning in all rooms, Wi-Fi internet and 24-hour security enhance the aforementioned high standards of the exterior and interior spaces of Morpheus. If relaxing in a wonderful setting and comfort is all you want to be concerned about when in Mykonos, Morpheus should be a prime candidate for your stay.
