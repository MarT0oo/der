Where beauty well exceeds its size Antiparos is a small Greek island located at the heart of the Cyclades. Antiparos is an island waiting to be discovered outside the usual routes of those who enjoy the island hopping in the Aegean.

Over the last years many Greeks and foreigners have explored it and some of them have even built their villas here. Others hang around in their yachts and sailboats along its magical coasts with the caves and crystal clear waters.

The cave of Ai Giannis Spiliotis as well as the Kastro and the old mines, which are unique from a geo-technological point of view, are the island’s most prized monuments. So close to the cosmopolitan Paros, this secret paradise of the Cyclades called Antiparos is waiting for you to get to know it and love it.
