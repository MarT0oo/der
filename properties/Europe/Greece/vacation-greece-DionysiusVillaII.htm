Dionysius Villa II, Fokos Beach, Mykonos, Greece Vacation Rental

 

Neutral tones and white washed walls give a sense of understated luxury. Split over three levels, and designed to take advantage of the light, the property has an abundance of space.

With seven bedrooms and eight bathrooms, the property can accommodate seventeen guests which makes it perfect for social gatherings and family get togethers. There are two master bedrooms with king size beds, and five double bedrooms with four en-suite bathrooms all fitted with quality amenities. Each bedroom has AC. You will have the freedom to escape in total privacy.

The fully equipped kitchen will leave you looking for excuses to prepare meals. The kitchen leads to a bright dining area, sleek in its design set in a background of prevailing white. The reception area is incredibly generous in space, and has all your entertainment needs in mind, allowing for you to have some down time or entertaining space when needed.

A church at the property makes this villa a perfect setting for a fairytale wedding. We can't think of any place more fit to host your special day.

This property would be the envy of any interior design magazine. A property that simply exudes indulgence, while inhabiting a unique character.

A beach front is one of the villas highlights. Simply step out of this luxurious villa and find yourself on the sandy beach of Fokos, making this a perfect setting for your getaway.

After a long day at the beach sit back and watch the sun and your worries melt away. Heart melting sunsets and colorful mornings can be anticipated. The private pool has unobstructed views of the surrounding area allowing you to escape reality. The views are simply breathtaking. Stretch your feet on the sun beds or in the shaded pergola. Enjoy alfresco meals from the BBQ whilst sitting in the outdoor dining area.

If this wasn't enough there is also an outdoor jacuzzi, allowing for more recreational space. Boasting a seafront location staying in this villa means you are lucky to have the option of escaping Mykonos famous nightlife, whilst being conveniently located to partake in the islands hotspots at your choosing.
