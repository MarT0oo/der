Villa Haumana, Corfu Island, Greece Vacation Rental

 

With a wide choice of terraces and balconies to choose from this built-in-nature villa is ideal for those who enjoy outdoor living. The beautiful infinity pool with shallow children's section is placed to enjoy the views over the village of Agios Stefanos and the Corfu Channel.

The jacuzzi whirlpool, sitting on its own shaded terrace and surrounded by olive trees and many flourish plants offers even more spectacular views from the elevated position and comfortable terrace loungers and sofas beg you to simply laze around and relax.

The shaded dining area with its pergola naturally covered by a lovely lilac tree is the ideal spot for al fresco meals. What will certainly make a success of your evening parties is the original built in rock barbecue.

The interior decoration has been inspired by a country chic style and what will charm you are the very comfortable sized bedrooms all featuring open plan and the innovative and glamorous en suite bathrooms.

In the ground floor a natural rock is coming into the master bedroom plunging you in an exotic atmosphere. The living/dining room and the open plan kitchen have been newly refurbished for 2010 and in addition new outdoor dining table/chairs have been provided and the whole villa has been repainted with pale earthy colors.
