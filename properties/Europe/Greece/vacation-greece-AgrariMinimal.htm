Agrari Minimal, Mykonos, Greece Vacation Rental

 

The villa Agrari Minimal is distinguished by its coveted design and the elevated setting which redefines the open-air living. Feel the sea breezes from a variety of angles and enjoy your amazing stay in the 50 shades of grey overlooking the Aegean Sea. The Villa offers spectacular views of the two southeast beaches, Agrari and Elia combined with the absolute privacy you have ever desired. Even more its special location capitalizes on the glorious sunrises which you can see even while you are in bed. The outside terrace will lead you to the large swimming pool area supplied with comfortable beds, sound system and a pool bar.

MAIN HOUSE
Agrari Minimal loves grey. It matches perfectly with the rocky scenery of the Agrari area.
The main house consists of three different levels.

On the upper level the master bedroom with en suite bathroom makes you feel the luxury of the simplicity. The wooden double bed with the white strips falling from the top, overlooks the crystal blue waters of Agrari Beach. From this room you have only to open your eyes to watch the sunrise from the bed.

In the middle floor you will find the impressive dining area supplied with a large, wooden table for 12 people. The amazing sea view will dominate your vision while enjoying your meal. Four boles separate the dining area from the perfectly equipped kitchen. At the other side there is a large and relaxing living room decorated with minimal furniture designed by Ralph Lauren. The huge couches, the lounge chairs, the astonishing sea view combined with the atmospheric lightning of the house and the perfect sound system, gives you the kinky mood of the 50 shades of grey.

On the ground level you make yourself comfortable in the living room area but if you want to be naughty, just check the room under the stairs and be surprised with the rocky scenery and the bar. Two more sew view bedrooms are one the ground floor.

Specifically, one master bedroom with en suite bathroom and a double bed and one more bedroom with two single beds and its own bathrooms. Both bedrooms have private access to the beautiful verandas with the great view to Agrari beach as also to the gardens.

GUEST POOL HOUSE
The Villa offers an independent guest house at the pool area which can accommodate up to 4 guests. The master bedroom with the double bed on the upper floor offers you an excellent sunriseview while the rocky bedroom on the ground floor overviews the swimming pool. Both bedroomshave en suite bathrooms. The guest house also offers a fully equipped kitchen.

GUEST SECRET SUITE
A small living room area with a comfortable sofa, one double bed and a marvelous en suite bathroom composing the picture of the mystery room behind the main house with its own veranda. The secret guest suite can accommodate up to 2 guests.
