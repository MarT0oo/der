Aeolos, Houlakia, Mykonos, Greece Vacation Rental

 

Aeolos is a very large hilltop property situated in the area of Houlakia, above the homonymous bay. Thanks to its dominant position, the house offers uninterrupted views of Houlakia, Mykonos town and several Cycladic Islands: Delos, Rhenia, Syros and Tinos. The views dramatically transform at night, when thousands of tiny lights shine in the distance bringing the island and its neighborhoods to life in a romantic glance.

The very large terrace enjoys the sun and the sea breeze all day long, while a spacious Camares - a Greek pool house - provides shade and relaxation during hot summer days. At night, it is a perfect place to entertain guests with its very well equipped bar, round dining table, and large sliding windows which access the swimming pool.

The house is built on three levels; the lower one features a large living room and three large bedrooms with wardrobes, en-suite bathrooms and private access to the garden. The middle floor has a modern and well-equipped kitchen with an adjoining dining room, while another comfortable sitting room has access to the main terrace and the BBQ area.

The impressive white staircase leads to the top floor master bedroom, which has amazing views of the garden, the sea and the pool. It has also an extravagant open-space bathroom and a large double vanity.

The interior of Aeolos is a combination of vibrant colors, innovative creation, large common spaces all boasting a traditional feeling. Ideal for those who like to entertain in total comfort but also for those looking for a relaxing holiday where the privacy is the main prerequisite.
