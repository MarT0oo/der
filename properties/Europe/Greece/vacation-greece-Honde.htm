Honde, Mykonos,Greece Vacation Rental

 

The ingenious minds of two extremely talented architects created this distinctive property to share with elite travelers looking for a unique experience in Mykonos. Perched just above Super Paradise beach, Honde is a prime example of modern design applied to provide great comfort in a stylish atmosphere.

The spacious pool terrace covers the entire front of the property with its graceful stone tails. The private infinity edge swimming pool, with its unique structure, is located in the most panoramic position of the terrace directly overlooking the golden sands of Super Paradise beach. The two large pergolas are located on each side of the pool, one with clever summer furniture for time-off and the other with the formal outdoor dining area equipped with a stunning wooden dining table and a wood-fire BBQ.

Indoors, the property’s dominant concept of the extensive use of glass gives guests a chance to marvel at the impressive views right away. Space and light throughout are definitely the main factor in the architecture of Honde. The modern open style kitchen is fully functional with top of the range amenities and connects directly onto the formal dining area. All bedrooms feature superb fittings and elegant materials, which combined with the vast space, assure great comfort throughout.

Honde is a must for guests searching for an elegant summer residence located right above one of the most active and cheerful beaches on the island. The astonishing design and attention to detail mixed with the airy atmosphere both indoors and outdoors are certain to endow with memorable moments in this unique property.
