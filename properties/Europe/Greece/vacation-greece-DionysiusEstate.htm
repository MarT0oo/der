Dionysius Estate, Fokos Beach, Mykonos, Greece Vacation Rental

 

The space and light in these villas will captivate you. The large white surfaces emphasize a Greek touch. Neutral tones and white washed walls give a sense of understated luxury.

The spacious living rooms, along with the dining rooms create a perfect place to gather. Both kitchens are fully equipped to cater for all your needs. Built with high end appliances and coupled with an ergonomic designs they are guaranteed to ensure a pleasurable cooking and entertaining experience. You will be looking for excuses to prepare meals.

The two properties have thirteen bedrooms and fifteen bathrooms combined, eleven of which are en-suite. The two properties can accommodate thirty guests. There are four master king bedrooms with king size beds, and nine double bedrooms with seven en-suite bathrooms. All incredibly generous in size, designed to convey a sense of comfort and to ensure full living flexibility. All of the bedrooms are fitted with AC.

An indoor church makes these villas a perfect setting for a fairytale wedding. We can't think of any place more fit to host your special day.

No expense has been spared in the creation and design of these two villas.

Simply step out of these luxurious villas and find yourself on the sandy beach of Fokos. What better experience to have for an unforgettable holiday.

The outdoor spaces of these villas have been designed to perfection. The epitome of excellence. The star feature is undoubtedly the expansive pools which stretch into the horizon. The view from both pools can only be described as spectacular.

Heart melting sunsets and colourful mornings can be anticipated. Take in the view from the sun loungers or relax under your shaded pergolas. Allow yourself to be mesmerised by the cascading waves. These villas are the perfect setting to experience absolute relaxation. Enjoy alfresco meals from the BBQ whilst sitting in the outdoor dining areas.

A church at the property makes these villas a perfect setting for a fairytale wedding. We can't think of any place more fit to host your special day.

There is also an outdoor jacuzzi, allowing for more recreational space.

Boasting a seafront location, staying in these villas means you are lucky to have the option of escaping Mykonos famous nightlife, whilst being conveniently located to partake in the islands hotspots at your choosing.
