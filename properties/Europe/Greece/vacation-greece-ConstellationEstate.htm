Constellation Estate, Houlakia, Mykonos, Greece Vacation Rental

 

Spacious verandas, breathtaking views of the Aegean Sea and awe-inspiring sunsets await you here. As night falls, the flickering lights of passing sea vessels provide a fantastic tableau-vivant. Located less than a 5-minute drive from bustling Mykonos Town, amidst 5.000 m2 grounds, Constellation Estate Villas is the ideal option for unforgettable vacations in a tranquil and relaxing environment.

The 11-bedroom Constellation Estate consists of 2 independent properties, Villa Moonlight (5 bedrooms) and Villa Starlight (6 bedrooms), each one with its own private pool, spacious indoor & outdoor areas, amazing view and it can accommodate up to 22 guests.

The property is located at the fashionable Houlakia Bay area, just a short drive from Saint Stefanos beach and Mykonos Town well protected from the wind.
