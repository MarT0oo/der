Villa Agamemnon Mykonos villas, Mykonos,Greece Vacation Rental

 

This unique Mykonos Grand Villa is located, in the posh Agios Giannis area, on the top of the hill overlooking most of the southwestern part of the island with a breathtaking view in every direction. Sitting at the huge wind protected outdoor living area one overlooks the Islands of Delos, Siros, Paros etc. while from the wind protected outdoor dining (seats12+) the overview of, Mykonos town, Ornos, Korfos and Tourlos bays, amply compensates for winters long anticipation till the arrival of spring and summer. The spacious infinity pool being placed at the very edge of the terrace provides the opportunity to swim at a cloud level view.

Entering this Mykonos Grand Villa at pool level, a spacious living/dining area for eight to ten guests and together with the open plan fully equipped kitchen welcomes you in for a long and glamorous stay. Furthermore, this level includes a guest bathroom and bedroom with a single bed. The first floor /horizon level has a spacious master en-suite double bed room with a walk-in closet appropriate to satisfy demanding guest needs. The garden level has three studios en-suite (one having independent entrance) with their own living rooms (TV?s) two of which have single twin beds and the one having a king size double bed. Last but not least, at the lowest level one more en-suite studio with its living room (TV) having single twin beds and exiting to a terrace can host a couple or single adults. The Agamemnon?s Grand Villa “throne” position awaits its guests to come and feel at home end experience a royal vacation with comfort and glam. Its hosting flexibility, due to its size, makes Agamemnon appropriate in accommodating a variety of guest specifications.
