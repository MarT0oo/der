Paraga Retreat, Mykonos, Greece Vacation Rental

 

Paraga Retreat is an exceptionally unique property located at the Southern part of Mykonos, overlooking the islands of Paros and Naxos with stunning views over the Aegean sea and its secluded PRIVATE BAY.

Make the ultimate vacation dream come true.. Enjoy Mykonos at it's Best ! The villa is superbly located in the most vivid area of the island, just walking distances from the famous beaches of PARADISE and PARAGA which have established Mykonos as an internationally cosmopolitan destination.

Within a private land of breathtaking view, the villa enjoys internal privacy and comfort, whilst externally is offered the opportunity to relax or party at the adjacent verandas or the surrounding spaces.

The extensive grounds with their walkways, surrounding the villas, have been landscaped in such a way as to allow privacy and seclusion either in the two "infinity pools" with magnificent sea views or to the two mini pools which are ideal relaxation spots for the grownups or playgrounds for the little ones. The spacious outdoor facilities, including its two barbeques, can form a unified surrounding ideal for partying, weddings or other similar activities demanding large gatherings.

Paraga Retreat is one of the biggest rental properties in Mykonos offering luxurious accommodation for 32 guests in its 16 guest bedrooms with their en suite bathrooms.
