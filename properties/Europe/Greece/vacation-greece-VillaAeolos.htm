Villa Aeolos, Mykonos,Greece Vacation Rental

 

This Mykonos Grand Villa is located in an area known as Fanari, at the northwestern part of Mykonos Island. Situated on a hillside that overlooks the unique and due to its morphology protected, volcanic stone beach of Choulakia, Villa Aeolos offers spectacular views of Mykonos Town, referred to as "Chora", the Island of Delos, Tinos and Syros and the sun setting into the Aegean Sea.

Villa Aeolos is built on three terraced levels, each with its own independent hosting facilities and can amply host fourteen (14) guests. The entry level hosts four (4) guests in two bedrooms with twin double beds that share a bathroom. At this level there is a living room and a kitchen with dinning facilities. At the exterior there is a large terrace with a covered dinning area, bar and a BBQ. A built in shaded sitting area with a view of the lower pool area, the Aegean Sea and Islands provides privacy for cozy moments. A stairwell leads to the upper level that can accommodate four (4) guests in two (2) independent studios en-suite.

At the level below (pool level), which hosts six (6) guests, is located the master bedroom en-suite with an adjacent sitting area, two (2) twin double bed bedrooms that share a bathroom and a large living and dining area with an open plan kitchen. There is also a separate guest bathroom. Exiting on to the terrace our guests can sunbathe and enjoy the swimming pool and shaded sitting area during the day, or dine with a view of the sunset in the evening. Villa Aeolos is suitable for a group of adults who wish to discover the Mykonean way of living in a most elegant and luxurious fashion.
