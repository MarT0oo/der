Villa Androminus, Mykonos, Greece Vacation Rental

 

This distinctively designed villa that is perfect for an unforgettable island escape. A beachside haven, the villa is fully equipped with modern amenities and caters to all of your holiday needs. The interior has a tastefully decorated living and dining area, a fully equipped modern kitchen as well as 4 bedrooms and 3 baths. The layout is organically designed to optimize the beautiful sea views and the seamless flow between indoor and outdoor spaces.

Admire the Aegean laying on a comfy sun bed that overlooks your private pool. Enjoy a sunset cocktail at your pool bar or prepare a delicious BBQ meal with fresh fish. It’s an experience you’ll never forget, living the Greek island lifestyle in the comfort of your own villa by the bay.

On the world renown island of Mykonos within a gated community overlooking stunning Panormos Bay our eleven villas are walking distance to the sea and have spectacular views of the bay, the beach and the untamed hillsides.

Situated on 55,000 meters the villas have been designed to integrate understated elegance and privacy with the leisurely island lifestyle and vibrant nightlife that has made Mykonos a world class destination.

Spend the day or evening swimming and lounging with the in-crowd at trendy Panormos with its bohemian beach bar and restaurant or experience the low key relaxation of secluded Agios Sostis beach and it's charmingly authentic Greek taverna.

The distinctive combination of natural beauty, location, privacy and luxury of these unique villas is what brings our guests back year after year.

Location Information

Just 5k. from Mykonos Town, the airport and both ports, Mykonos Panormos Villas provides its guests with the perfect combination of proximity to beaches, restaurants and entertainment as well as privacy and serenity.

From: Mykonos Airport

Make a right turn out of the airport and proceed straight until you reach a roundabout. At the roundabout turn right at the first street and proceed straight until you reach a second roundabout. From that point turn right at the first street toward “Ano Mera” and proceed straight. As you proceed you will go down a large hill. At the bottom of the hill you will see a sign on the left saying “Panormos”. Turn left at the sign and proceed following the winding road until you reach a fork where the road ends. Make a right turn and proceed on the winding road until you see a Greek flag on the left…..Turn left into the driveway…..you have arrived!
