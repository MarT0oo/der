Villa Moonlight, Houlakia, Mykonos, Greece Vacation Rental

 

Spacious verandas, breathtaking views of the Aegean Sea and awe-inspiring sunsets await you here. As night falls, the flickering lights of passing sea vessels provide a fantastic tableau-vivant. Located less than a 5-minute drive from bustling Mykonos Town, amidst 5.000 m2 grounds, the 5-bedroom Villa Moonlight (185 sq.m) is the ideal option for unforgettable vacations in a tranquil and relaxing environment.

Villa Moonlight is part of a private estate which consists of 2-independent properties, located at the fashionable Houlakia Bay area, just a short drive from Saint Stefanos beach and Mykonos Town well protected from the wind. Built by Mykonian laborers with respect to the Cycladic architecture, Villa Moonlight is welcoming guests who want to combine an authentic Mykonian accommodation experience while being very close to the center of the world renown Mykonos’ nightlife.

Choulakia Bay, with uniquely shaped pebbles of natural interest is very close (300m), as well as Mykonos Town (4 km) and Saint Stefanos Beach (1km). The villa is under renovation at the moment and will be ready to welcome guests with a fresh look for their summer vacation on 2018!
