Rocky Retreat Two, Agrari, Mykonos, Greece Vacation Rental

 

Rocky Retreat Two is the perfect villa for those seeking a hidden gem, completely immersed in the island natural rocky landscape and vegetation, complemented by open views of the Aegean sea. A privileged secluded and wind sheltered position overlooking the tranquil, beautiful and family friendly Agrari Beach.

A generous lagoon shaped infinity pool, built with special sand-colored materials with rock elements, donates a very natural feel to the property outdoors, located in front of the house and next to a large wooden shaded pergola. The outdoors offer guests a beautiful and spacious outdoors dining area with a traditional wood fire BBQ, perfect for entertaining day and night. An open bar with stools overlooking the sea completes the picture.

The villa's charming interiors are a combination of minimal architecture with Provencal beach house elements. A fully equipped open-plan kitchen with modern, bright-red and steel Smeg appliances, gives onto a living-sitting area directly accessing the lovely pool and its wide terraces with sea views.

The upper level of the house comprises of one master bedroom with en-suite bathroom and private balcony and two generous double guests bedrooms with a shared bathroom, all equipped with comfortable heavenly double beds. A separate guest studio below the swimming pool, with garden entrance offers two double beds, an en-suite bathroom, and is ideal to host children or one family.

Rocky Retreat Two and its adjacent sister-villa Rocky Retreat One, each guarantee own privacy to their guests, while offering the convenient possibility to be rented together for a large group of friends or for two-three families.
