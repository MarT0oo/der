Villa Armonia Paradise, Mykonos,Greece Vacation Rental

 

Villa Armonia, adjacent to Eros, located on the typically “wild Mykonian” in landscape hill top area of Kalamopodi, in the south of Mykonos island, overlooking the bays of Paranga and Paradise with the Aegean Sea Island of Naxos in the distance. It is a new, ultra-modern, fully furnished, vacation home, blending traditional island architecture with all modern comforts for an unforgettable experience in outdoor and indoor living. On entry, a private pool-BBQ area and an inviting outdoor living and dining space with its shaded terrace and splendid view, captivate the imagination. Everyone adores these outdoor amenities and this is the most popular gathering spot. Many guests can be accommodated in this area for receptions or informal parties.

One enters this Mykonos Villas into a spacious, tastefully decorated living and dining area with a fully equipped open-plan kitchen and fireplace, perfect for relaxation. In the hall way of this level, there is also a guest bathroom. Down the conch shaped staircase and at garden level, one finds two (2) en-suite bedrooms with double beds (one exiting to the garden) and an additional living/dining area and guest bathroom. In addition at this level, this Villas in Mykonos has an independent double bedroom en-suite that exits to the garden, and a cozy, private living room with an unfolding sofa bed. On the top level an independent studio/apartment, with an outdoor living area on a shaded terrace, is suitable for a couple that requires flexibility and independence.

This impressive, traditional style Villa is recommended for a family and a couple of guests. The added advantage of security as part of a gated vacation complex, it is the perfect playground for adults and young people. Its close proximity to Paranga beach with its fine sand and rock formations, bars and taverns that serve quality Mediterranean cuisine, and the world-renown for its round the clock parties Paradise beach, make Villa Armonia a top choice in our selection of "Myconos Grand Villas”.
