Light Blue Villa, Kalafatis, Mykonos, Greece Vacation Rental

 

Located at the area of Kalafatis and just a short drive to the famous Kalo Livadi beach, Kalafatis beach and Lia beach, Light Blue Villa is a cozy 88 sq.m. property ideal for a family, a small group of friends or couples, and is part of the Endless Summer Complex.

Consisting of 3 bedrooms (accommodates 5 guests) with TV, air-conditioning, Wi-Fi, living area, dining area, kitchen equipped with modern appliances and a beautiful outdoor sitting area with a 18 sq.m. private pool and 2 parking spaces.

Mykonos is an island in the Cyclades group in the Aegean Sea. It's popularly known for its summer party atmosphere. Beaches such as Paradise and Super Paradise have bars that blare thumping music. Massive dance clubs attract world-renowned DJs and typically stay open well past dawn. Iconic landmarks include a row of 16th-century windmills, which sit on a hill above Mykonos town.
