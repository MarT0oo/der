Villa Python, Mykonos,Greece Vacation Rental

 

Villa Python is located above the main town of Mykonos, also referred to as Chora. This area (Evagelistraki) is perfectly suitable for an easy going crowd that prefers the close proximity of shops, restaurants and night clubs, while it's easy access to main routes leading to the most spectacular beaches (north or south), make it a prime choice. This Mykonos Grand Villa provides a spectacular view of the main town, the Aegean Sea, and the islands of Delos, Naxos and Paros etc.

This Villa in Mykonos can easily host a party of six (6) guests in two (2) en-suite bedrooms and a double bed room which is located on the entry level. Upon entry one is welcomed in a bright spacious living room and a fully equipped open plan kitchen that is cleverly hidden behind an indoor dining room. A room with a double bed that is suitable for a teenager or the family help is situated just at the side of the kitchen. On this level, there is also one bedroom, with an elegant double bed and a bathroom. This is perfect for the younger crowd that stays up late enjoying the famous Mykonos night life. Up the stairs and on the highest level is the Master bedroom en-suite with its private shaded veranda and its exceptional view due to the Villa?s ideal location.

A spacious outdoor living/dining area with a pool, surrounded by high walls, provides a quiet and discreet retreat. This terrace can accommodate dozens of guests and special events in a magnificent setting. Sitting in the bright, clear light sparkling on the sea and the islands in the distance is what Mykonos is all about. The Python Villa is a unique family get-away suitable for a family who wish to discover Mykonos in an easy going fashion.
