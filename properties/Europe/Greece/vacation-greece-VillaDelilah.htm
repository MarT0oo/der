Villa Delilah, Mykonos, Greece Vacation Rental

 

Villa Delilah is a 260 square meters villa and consists of three bedrooms with en-suite bathrooms (the master bedroom has a Jacuzzi), a large closed kitchen, indoors living room and dining room, breakfast solarium room, outdoor dining area covered by a pergola, a WC, a beautiful 130 square meters swimming pool.

In addition to the main villa there are two guest houses both equipped with a fridge and one with a kitchenette as well. The villa has free Wi-Fi access, satellite TV & open parking space area. The villa can accommodate up to 10 guests.
