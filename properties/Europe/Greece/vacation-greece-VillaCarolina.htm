Villa Carolina, Pyrgi, Mykonos, Greece Vacation Rental

 

Villa Carolina is a beautiful villa with a private pool, part of The Paradise Estate, located on a land of 10.000sqms in Pirgi, between Paradise bay and Super Paradise beach, just a few-minute drive from the famous beach restaurants Jackie ‘O and Cavo Paradiso. It has 230 degrees of uninterrupted sea view of the islands of Naxos and Delos.

The Estate consists of three villas (Villa Delilah, Villa Carina and Villa Carolina), 4 guest houses, a helipad, a chapel, several outdoor areas and it accommodates 17 couples or 34 people + 2 kids. The property can be booked as a whole Estate, where guests have the possibility to use the helipad and the small church in order to host events, weddings, parties, retreats, baptisms etc. which will be happy to help you organize, or just the villas independently. The Estate is also equipped with a power generator.

Villa Carolina consists of 4 double ensuite bedrooms, kitchen equipped with modern appliances and sitting area. Outdoors there is a private swimming pool, pergola covered sitting area and guests enjoy beautiful sea view.
