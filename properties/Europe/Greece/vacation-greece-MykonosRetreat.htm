Mykonos Retreat, Ftelia, Mykonos, Greece Greece Rental

 

Mykonos Retreat is something of a modern marvel, a brand new private property where technology meets a contemporary take on trademark Cycladic architecture in a natural setting overlooking Ftelia beach, the windsurf Mecca of Mykonos and one of the last unspoilt beaches on the island.

The outdoor areas all face towards the scenery allowing for views that stretch out over the countryside and the turquoise waters of Ftelia bay. A grand pergola covers the outdoor dining area equipped with a wood-fire oven and large BBQ in order to cater to any culinary taste. The main pool is located a few steps down and makes the most of the view. The contact with the element of water is a redundant theme of the outdoor spaces as, in addition to the main pool, a built-in Jacuzzi tub was conceived on the lower terrace and a plunge pool was just recently built adjacent to the lower guest suite.

A remarkable attention to details is evident throughout the indoor spaces of Mykonos Retreat. Each room is appointed with style, each piece of furniture was carefully chosen to contribute to an airy, elegant atmosphere. Modern amenities and domotics complete the picture, ensuring guests feel both pampered and entertained. For the ultimate relaxation experience a new hammam, massage room and small private gym were added to the property near the lower guest suite. True to Cycladic style the property was built in units, with some bedrooms having independent access for added privacy.

Mykonos Retreat checks all the boxes when it comes to comfort, style and quality, making it the perfect summer retreat for guests looking to enhance their Myconian experience in a real standout private property.
