Poseidon Two, Kalafatis, Mykonos, Greece Vacation Rental

 

Poseidon Two is part of a group of three brand new, semi-detached properties freshly inaugurated in the area of Kalafatis Beach, on the southeastern side of Mykonos. The location grants easy access to the beaches of Kalafatis – a windsurfer’s delight – and Agia Anna, a beautiful small cove, relatively unknown to international crowds.

The property was built in traditional Cycladic architecture, pristine white surfaces and stone structures. Posedion Two’s infinity pool grants fascinating views over the water of Kalafatis’ bay. A cozy outdoor dining area with BBQ on the side of the terrace is ideal for al-fresco dining or relaxation benefiting from sea views.

The beautifully furnished lounge room with its adjoining dining area have a stylish atmosphere and soft colors embellish the space. The modern kitchen is well designed and fully functional with all necessary amenities. Another great asset of the property is the jacuzzi tub on one of the upstairs veranda. The other strong point of the property, on the lower level of the house, is the unique aquarium style window which actually looks into the pool.

The possibility to visit less known beaches of Mykonos combined with the stylish design of this property make Poseidon Two the perfect spot for groups or families looking to embrace the “Mykonos experience”. It would certainly appeal to larger groups given the availability to rent it along with Poseidon One - also part of the complex and our portfolio - for guests who wish to travel together but retain their own privacy in doing so given the two houses are on the opposite sides of the complex.
