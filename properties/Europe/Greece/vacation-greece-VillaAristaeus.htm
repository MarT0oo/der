Villa Aristaeus, Agios Ioannis, Mykonos, Greece Vacation Rental

 

Live the ultimate dream holidays in the luxury Mykonos villa, Villa Aristaeus. You will find it in Agios Ioannis, the most enviable location on the island, since it is wind sheltered and close to town and famous beaches. The villa is independent and can sleep 16 guests, It is situated in a gated complex with two other traditionally built, flat-roofed, cube shaped villas, so if you are planning on a bigger group, go for it! Ready for a small tour?

The three-level villa has 8 double bedrooms and a small studio for staff. Cycladic architecture and minimal white elegance prevail in the interior design. 3 kitchens await for the ambitious cooks to experiment with Greek cuisine. Don’t bother if you fail; our concierge can arrange a superb gastronomical experience with our chef.

The best part, of course, is the pool terrace! Infinity pool, loungers, sun beds and pool bar make the superb view look even better! Covered pergolas, gardens, BBQ, dining areas complete the scenery, outdoor music system and wine cooler with 70 bottles capacity! Make a playlist and get ready for a party!

If you want to live the famous nightlife of Mykonos, the town is close too. Our concierge will be happy to arrange your transfer, as well as everything else you might want. Just think of it and we take care of it!
