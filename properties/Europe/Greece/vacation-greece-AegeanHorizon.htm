Aegean Horizon, Lia, Mykonos, Greece Vacation Rental

 

Aegean Horizon is an exceptionally large and stylish property located close to Lia Beach area. It is facing South in a privileged wind-sheltered location with magnificent views of the Aegean sea.

The property features a large and elegant infinity pool surrounded by a very spacious sun deck. The outdoor equipment and setting include 8 sophisticated and comfortable sunbeds, a large outdoor and pergola-shaded lounge area, paired with a sumptuous dining table with own woodfire BBQ that are ideal for entertaining. The interior of the house is tastefully decorated with fine art and furnished with modern design pieces.

The main entrance leads directly to a bright and large living room, featuring comfortable sofas and equipped with state-of-the-art flat screen TV and sound system.

The formal indoor dining area communicates with a beautiful open plan "gourmet" kitchen equipped with impressive appliances such as Gageenau oven. Three of the four guests bedrooms are located on the ground floor, all very well appointed and decorated in different pastel colors, featuring own en-suite bathrooms, great views and direct access to the pool terrace. A grand staircase from the main hall leads to the upstairs master bedrooms. The main one features a vestiaire and an en-suite bathroom with a Philippe Stark design bathtub, which enjoys magnificent sea views and a private roof terrace. The second master bedroom with twin beds and en-suite bathroom also has access to the roof terrace, which enjoys the same views.

A detached guests house, with garden entrance, has its own small living room and smart open plan kitchen offering privacy and stylish space for two more guests. Another spacious studio with separate entrance, living room and kitchenette is available at the basement of the property. Aegean Horizon is a unique home, both in terms of style and size, offering a high level of privacy within a very elegant and pleasant setting, while the friendly live-in staff ensures a precise housekeeping service. The perfect choice for a discerning clientele looking to experience Mykonos at its best.
