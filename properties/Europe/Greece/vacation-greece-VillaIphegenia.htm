Villa Iphegenia, Mykonos,Greece Vacation Rental

 

Iphegenia is a modern designed and elegantly furnished Grand Villa, located in Aleomandra on the South-western part of Mykonos. It overlooks the islands of Naxos and Paros to the South. It is one of the few Villas on Mykonos that is protected from the strong northern winds of August. The outstanding aspects of this villa are its well designed floor plan and the harmony that exists between the villa and the existing landscape. It offers an unobstructed sea view from every room. The 120mÂ², L-shaped infinity swimming pool with the large pergola shaded sitting and dining area is a favourite gathering place for the guests.

The well designed layout of the entry level includes the dining area, living and sitting spaces with a fireplace flat-screen TV and a home cinema area. There is also a fully equipped kitchen, a guest bathroom and one twin/double bedroom en suite.

The first floor has two twin/ double bedrooms en suite with verandas ideal for a morning coffee alone with view after a long night. The garden level has one independent studio with a double bedroom en suite and built-in sofa-beds and one independent twin/double bedroom en suite.

The Villa is completely air conditioned and tastefully furnished in a bright clean uncluttered minimalistic style at the same time incorporating the white walls and wooden beams that are part of the traditional Cycladic architecture. The rooms are all comfortable spaces with en suite bathrooms and off-white wooden floors making this Mykonos Grand Villa a top choice for guests combining friends and family vacationing.
