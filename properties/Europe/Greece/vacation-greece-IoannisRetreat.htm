Ioannis Retreat, Mykonos,Greece Vacation Rental

 

This very exclusive property, located in a privileged position over Agios Ioannis bay, enjoys extensive sea views over Delos and Rhenia islands; it is also blessed with colorful sunsets, superb outlook and proximity to Kapari beach. Various terraces and relaxation areas embrace the property and its oversize swimming pool.

Amidst the rocks and garden above the pool, a wooden deck with comfortable sun-chairs promise a perfect chocolate tan, while a small gorgeous private chapel dominates the scene. Two separate outdoor dining areas equipped with Greek-style BBQs are perfect for entertaining guests during hot sunny days or for romantic al fresco dinners.

The entrance of the house opens directly onto a majestic split-level hall, where two living rooms and a formal dining area are appointed with unique pieces of furniture and flair. Adjoining, a fully equipped kitchen leads directly to the pool terrace BBQ. Three bedrooms are located on the ground level, all beautifully appointed with style and different shades of colors. An impressive open staircase, from the entrance hall, leads to the upstairs quarters, where a beautifully decorated and romantic master bedroom with en-suite bathroom offers stunning sea views of the bay and beyond.

Ioannis Retreat is by far one of the most exclusive properties on the island, both for its unique privileged position and for the unparalleled comfort and beauty it offers to its guests.
