Bluewave Villa, Kalafatis, Mykonos, Greece Vacation Rental

 

This modern new villa is located on the Cycladic Island of Mykonos, near Kalo Livadi (2km), Kalafatis beach (1,5km) and Lia beach (3km) coastline, 10 km from the port of Mykonos and 11 km from Mykonos airport. It features magnificent sea views and two amazing infinity swimming pools with grand shaded surrounding areas of over 400 sq. meters, suitable for dining and lounging at all hours. Not far from Bluewave XL Villa you will find the idyllic beaches of Kalo Livadi, Agia Anna, Lia, Elia, Kalafatis, Ftelia, Agios Sostis, Panormos beach, renowned for numerous watersports activities as well as for their great restaurants such as Solymar and Blue Marlin beach club at Kalo Livadi, Spilia at Agia Anna, Allemagou at Ftelia and Liasti beach club at Lia beach.

Consisting of a main building, 700 m2 at the ground floor and upper level, three guesthouses of 45 m2 each at top upper level and three extra guestroom of 25 m2 at entrance level as well as a large gym and spa area of 100 m2 and outdoor shaded areas of over 400 m2. Plot area in total is 3.000 sq. meters.

Bluewave XL Villa consists of 10 spacious bedrooms with en-suite bathrooms, plus 7 large guest rooms with individual entrances.

This spacious summerhouse is decorated in harmonic shades and features, sleeps 34 guests and offers a selection of versatile living spaces for easy entertaining and uncomplicated holidaying. Enjoy a good night’s sleep on a Simmons mattress, relax at the soft touches of elegant sophistication and Greek colors sofas and the view from the bedrooms. Every bedroom and living room is equipped with 50 inches smart TV, hairdryer, safe box, ceiling fan and A/C, so that each member can be independent.

It combines high quality finishes, impressive views, a tranquil ambiance and large indoor and outdoor spaces for a distinctive and elegant experience. From the crystal-clear water of your infinity pool all that separates you from the glittering sea is your imagination. This slender dash of blue will be your home for many happy hours – a private oasis in a secluded retreat.

This villa is ideal for family groups, who wish to enjoy each other’s company, without ever feeling crowded inside the premises since it is designed to respect both the privacy of parents individually or as a group and at the same time the privacy of the group of children or young adults.

A perfect spot for guests looking to enjoy Mykonos in a luxury and stylish to the last detail environment spending time close to a great choice of famous beaches and clubs/restaurants and yet not far from the night life for which the island is famous.
