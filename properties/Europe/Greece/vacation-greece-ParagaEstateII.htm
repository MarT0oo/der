Paraga Estate II, Mykonos, Greece Vacation Rental

 

The Paraga Estate II villa is the sister villa of Paraga Estate. The residence is built on a 2,000 sq.m. private property next to the sea with garden and a pergola. It is located on the small peninsula between Paraga beach and Paradise beach with eastern orientation. Both beaches are 5 minutes walking distance. The distance from the center of the town is 4.5 km, which is connected with regular buses every half hour during the summer period. The distance from the airport is approximately 2.0 km. The area is structured with residential villas, two 4-star hotels and several restaurants / taverns.

The villa consists of the 1st floor, ground floor and the pergola, garden and pool next to the sea.

1st Floor
The 1st floor is a autonomous house of 150 sq.m. comprising of 1 bedroom, 1 large living room and dining area of 100 sq.m., a fully equipped kitchen, 1 bathroom, a large closet and wardrobe and 1 WC. Facing east there is a large veranda of 40 sq.m. overlooking the garden with an excellent view of Raradise Bay and beach and a table that serves 12 people. The living room has a terrace of 80 sq.m. facing west with a view of Paranga Bay and Beach. There are 2 fireplaces, one large in the living room and the second one in the bedroom.

Ground Floor
On the ground floor there are 3 independent studios with eastern orientation and sea view which have direct access to the garden and there is a 4th studio with westerly orientation with side view. The 1st studio has an area of 65 sq.m. with bedroom, bathroom, closet and living room with a large built-in sofa which can be used as a bed. There is also a small kitchenette / bar with refrigerator and small sink. The 2nd studio has an area of 33 sq.m. , with 2 single beds, one large built-sofa /bed, a kitchenette with fridge , wardrobe and bathroom. The 3rd studio has an area of 31 sq.m. with 1 double bed, wardrobe and fridge and a bathroom. The 4th studio has an area of 28 sq.m., 1 double bed and one bathroom.

Pergola, garden and pool
The pergola which is at the northeastern end of the property has an area of 160 sq.m. It is made from stone and has built-in BBQ and BAR and is overlooking the garden, the house and the pool. The total garden area is 1,600 sq. m. and is covered with plants that are supported by the local climate.
