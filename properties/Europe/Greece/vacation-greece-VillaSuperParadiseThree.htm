Villa Super Paradise Three, Super Paradise, Mykonos, Greece Vacation Rental

 

Above the Mykonian bay where Super Paradise Beach lies, with a view to the infinite blue of the Aegean Sea, nests the third villa of the Super Paradise Villas complex. The architecture, similarly to the other two sister villas, complies with the traditional cycladic style. The villa consists of an external space in which are arranged a BBQ, a Bar with a sound system, a pergola where you can enjoy local dainty morsels and a private pool which is accessible either from the separate bedrooms or from the staircase that leads to it. All bedrooms lie on the lower floor and feature modern furnishing combined with all the necessary comforts, since all the bedrooms have en-suite bathrooms. On the entrance level lie the living room with the lounge and the dining area, all of them having access to the terrace.

SuperThree, like the other two villas of the Super Paradise Villas complex, with the presence of all modern amenities, combined with high aesthetics, lures people that desire to taste wonderful moments that they will be forever engraved in their minds and their hearts.
