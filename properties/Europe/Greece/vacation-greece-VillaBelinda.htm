Villa Belinda, Aleomandra, Mykonos, Greece Vacation Rental

 

This luxury villa is located in Aleomandra, one of the finest locations in Mykonos, well known for its high end summer residences, its privacy and its close proximity to the most cosmopolitan spots of Mykonos. The orientation of the house is astonishing, offering open sea views from the outdoor and indoor areas. A pebbled beach located a few steps away from the villa is ideal for a morning swim and the extra-large infinity pool uses the Aegean blue as its natural extension.

The residence has a very outdoorsy atmosphere, featuring a bbq area, a sitting-dining area with a superb wooden table under a large pergola and three verandas, and is ideal for groups of friends or families seeking for fine living.

Indoors, the villa features large and comfortable areas with minimal decoration in white washed and black tones, elegant floors, spacious bedrooms and bathrooms decorated in chic island style.

The villa comprises of one building volume and a guesthouse, comfortably accommodating 19 guests ( property is leased for 12 guests - for additional guests only upon request) plus 4 staff.
