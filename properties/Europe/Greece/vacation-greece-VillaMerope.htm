Villa Merope, Mykonos,Greece Vacation Rental

 

Villa Merope, consisting of two independent apartments and a guest studio, is situated in a residential area on a terraced hillside above Elia Beach at the southern end of Mykonos Island. Upon entry into the top level apartment of this Mykonos villa, our guests are welcomed into a bright, open space with a dining area that comfortably sits six (6) guests, lounging area and a fully equipped open plan kitchen. The view from the wide folding glass pane doors, invites guests to step out onto a large veranda with a pergola shaded dining area that can accommodate eight (8) guests. This spacious, unobstructed veranda with its BBQ is ideal for outdoor cooking and dining in the evening and offers a great view of the Aegean Sea, Elia Beach below and the surrounding, typically Mykonian wild landscape. To the left of the entrance and along a corridor adjacent to the open plan kitchen area, there is a bedroom en-suite with two twin single beds and a bedroom, en-suite, with a double bed and excellent view that exits to the front veranda. A separate guest restroom is also located along this corridor. A flight of steps lead to the master bedroom, en-suite, with a double bed and which exits to a private veranda with a splendid view.

A garden guest studio, en-suite, with a double bed and a view of Elia Beach, is located at the furthest eastern end of the terrace and is ideal for a couple who wish to share their Mykonos vacation with family or a group of friends while maintaining privacy.

The lower level apartment of the complex is accessed by descending a flight of external steps that lead to the garden terrace. Upon entry our guests are welcomed into bright, spacious, minimalist living room and dining area that can comfortably accommodate six (6) to (8) eight guests, and a fully equipped and fully functional open kitchen. Wide windows and folding glass pane veranda doors offer great views of the Aegean Sea and Elia Beach just below. Adjacent to this open space there is a bright, spacious master bedroom en-suite with king size futon bed and a wonderful view of Elia Beach and witch exits to the front garden terrace. A second bedroom en-suit also with a king size bed and a futon sofa bed. Exiting to the terrace and with an independent entrance, it is ideal for a couple who wish to enjoy greater independence. A third bedroom en-suite is reserved for younger members of the party. Bright, spacious and cheerful, it has two twin beds mounted on the walls and is ideal for older children or young teenagers.

The large pergola shaded terrace with a BBQ is ideal for outdoor dining in the evening and can comfortably accommodate eight (8) guests. A path leads to the lower level of the terraced grounds where there is a garden table with a built in sofa, an alternative outdoor dining area, ideal for dining on warm nights under a canopy of stars.

Due to its layout this Mykonos Grand Villa offers hosting flexibility. Elia beach below, the longest in the south of Mykonos Island, with a wide choice of taverns and bars and offering a variety of water sports (water skiing, parasailing wakeboarding or windsurfing), is on the “kaiki” (traditional fishing boat) route, used to taxi passengers to the world renowned nudist beaches in the south of the island. Villa Merope’s proximity to Elia Beach make it ideal for guests who wish to have easy access to a variety of activities, enjoy some independence and maintain privacy while vacationing with family or a group of friends.
