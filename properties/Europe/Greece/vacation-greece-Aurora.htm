Aurora, Mykonos,Greece Vacation Rental

 

Aurora is a stylish property in the exclusive area of Agios Lazaros on the southwestern side of Mykonos. The villa enjoys a privileged position with breathtaking views over Ornos bay, Delos island and the memorable Mykonian sunsets. The location is enhanced by the vicinity to Psarou beach, the “in” spot on the island and a 5 minute drive to Mykonos Town.

The vast scenic views are best enjoyed from the property’s pool terrace situated at the front of the villa. A large pergola behind the marvelous infinity pool and jacuzzi includes both a large lounge/sitting area, an elegant dining table and a barbeque. On the lower terrace, there is a great space for togetherness for moments of privacy shaded by a pergola. A half basketball court located at the back of the property is a rare amenity of this property for sport lovers.

Aurora is structured on three levels with the interior design flawlessly combining natural elements such as stone and wood with a traditional Cycladic architecture. Precious artifacts and unique pieces of furniture adorn throughout and convey the charisma of a stylish summer residence. The entrance level includes two distinct living rooms connecting to one another, both granting outstanding sea views. The villa’s spacious and fully equipped main kitchen shares its space with the charming indoor dining area.

This property is truly the idyllic holiday residence for a large family or group of friends looking for a setting that allows postcard-worthy scenery, modern amenities, elegant design and vicinity to the main spots of Mykonos
