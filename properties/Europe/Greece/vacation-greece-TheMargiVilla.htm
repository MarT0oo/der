The Margi Villa, Athens Riviera, Greece Vacation Rental

 

With spectacular views of the Aegean Sea, the Margi’s beachfront Villa offers the privacy of a luxurious holiday home combined with the perks of a five-star hotel. Flooded with light, the 200-sq.m of living space has a bright contemporary feel. With the beach at its doorstep, this villa gives guests unique and unparalleled access to the sea.

Outdoors, guests can dine alfresco on the spacious patio, unwind in the Jacuzzi or simply savor the sunset on the chic outdoor furnishings. Indoors, guests can lounge in the elegant living room with its floor to ceiling windows or cook in the fully equipped modern kitchen. All four bedrooms feature en suite bathrooms with rain showers, closets and air conditioning. Three of the bedrooms have their own private terrace with uninterrupted sea views.

Guests have exclusive services such as a private butler and fresh breakfast daily as well as a range of a la carte services to choose from whether it is a private chef creating a delectable dining experience or a private yoga session. Located just 1km away from the hotel, guests also have complimentary access to all the hotel’s facilities.

With beautifully designed living areas, along with luxury services, The Margi Villa creates the perfect holiday retreat for families or groups of friends.
