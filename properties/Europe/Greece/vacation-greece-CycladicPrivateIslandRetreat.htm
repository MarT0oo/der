Cycladic Private Island Retreat, Private Island, Cyclades, Greece Vacation Rental

 

This unique beach house is situated at the beginning of the Cycladic islands. It looks on the seashore of a large bay in a peaceful location offering exceptional privacy within a preserved archipelago of islets.

The property is part of a privileged historical grand estate of 900 acres planted with tall cypress, centennial olive and pine groves, pistachio and fig trees set within a mature Mediterranean vegetation and dry stonewalls. Yet, this luxurious hideaway is minutes away by boat to a fisherman's wharfor to a beautiful medieval city with its Venetian Castle and fish restaurants by the sea. It is also the gateway for day excursions to the near by Cycladic islands or to Athens’s cultural venues. This exceptional beachfront property comfortably houses 8-9 guests.

Contemporary furniture and elegant decoration adorn the house and each bedroom has en-suite marble bathrooms featuring stylish old- fashioned English accessories.

The patios and numerous shaded terraces provide a comfortable setting at any hour of the day. The villa offers breathtaking views of a unique bay with outdoor sitting and dining areas, beaches and private piers at your doorstep. Walking out of your bedroom and stepping into the crystal clear turquoise water lying in front of you is truly a Mediterranean experience unlike no other.

In a matter of days, you will feel yourself anew, it is the perfect match for those who love nature, water sports, fishing, boat rides and sailing, relaxing on a beach, or just enjoying a drink at sunset on the pier in front of the house.

Numerous trails on the estate also provide great hiking and biking escapes. The property is ecologically managed and a gourmet selection of seasonal products from the organic vegetable garden and the farm will complement your menu
