Νuxta Mia, Almyrida, Chania, Crete, Greece Vacation Rental

 

Νuxta Mia is the ideal retreat for a small family or couples who wish to rent a villa in Greece in total luxury, serenity and privacy. The villa has 2 bedrooms, 2 bathrooms, fully equipped kitchen, living room with fireplace and its own little pool with waterfall. The integrated sitting bench in the pool invites you to sit and relax under the sun in hot summer days. If you rather like to refresh yourself, you can take a refreshing shower in the waterfall.

The villa has impressive architectural design and decor style with the elegant mix of stone, glass, wood, earthly tones and quality fabrics.

With the sound of the waves from the sea, the sandy beach nearby and the view of the beautiful White Mountains in front, Νuxta Mia is the perfect place for those who are seeking a peaceful and relaxing atmosphere for their holiday home. The Beach and the village is only a few steps away if you like to go for a morning swim in the sea, activate with watersports, go for a meal at the cafes and restaurants, or just take a walk.

Guests are given the opportunity to relax in the nice and stylish living spaces, sit back in the cozy living areas and enjoy the luxurious facilities provided. The modern and fully equipped kitchen allows guests to prepare a meal of their choice flavored with herbs from the garden.

Whether seeking for a private luxury villa in Greece or a relaxing place for your holiday, this is the perfect place! Imagine laying on the sun lounger after a bath in the pool or a refreshing shower in the waterfall, overlooking the scenic landskape of Almyrida and the beautiful White Mountains.
