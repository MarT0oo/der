Aries, Paradisia, Mykonos, Greece Vacation Rental

 

The concept of space is relative from one individual to another however the unique signature of the most famous architect of Mykonos on the magnificent villa Aries definitely provides an interpretation that is hardly disagreeable. The stunning scenery of the views from the property’s premises can be seen as a starting point of the villa’s dominating theme with its 180 degree range of view over Mykonos Town, the islands of Delos and Rhenia and the breathtaking Mykonian sunsets.

Aries’ outdoor area offers a variety of possibilities through its thoughtful design where three distinct living areas can be found – the one to right wing of the property with immaculate white furnishing inspires an idyllic setting for a sunset cocktail, the one to the left wing of the villa with designer wood furniture and a proper outdoor kitchen giving possibilities of relaxation in its comfortable lounge area or of fine dining at the elegant outdoor dining table and finally the pool terrace to the front of the villa with its woven black summer couches, wooden sun beds and a marvelous infinity pool. Three outdoor living areas to mirror the three generous indoor living rooms, each with its own décor design and exquisite fittings.

The granite-surfaced kitchen with its Gaggenau appliances and central island will prove to amaze those fond of cooking and is perfectly enhanced by the attractive formal dining area with its design table combining three classic elements of nature in a clever blend of steel, wood and stone.

Generous use of spaces is also recurrent throughout the five bedrooms of the property, respectively the master bedroom and a guest bedroom located on the first floor, the two guest bedrooms on the ground floor and the further guest bedroom located on the lower level featuring also a cozy sitting area. All graciously fitted they combine traditional elements of Cycladic architecture such as Cimentogonia surfaces with a contemporary and fresh décor while all granting also access to the outdoors. Aries writes an important page in the portfolio of luxury retreats on the island of Mykonos with its range of options for unconditional relaxation surrounded by an array of modern comforts and amenities – the ideal getaway for those who dream of slowing the hands of time while spending their Mykonian days in an undeniably laid-back and fresh atmosphere.
