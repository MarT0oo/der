Apollo Retreat, Agios Ioannis, Mykonos, Greece Vacation Rental

 

Perched on a small hill above Agios Ioannis beach, Apollo Retreat offers panoramic views of exceptional beauty. A truly stunning combination of colors ranging from the golden beach of Agios Ioannis to the intense and inspiring blue of the Aegean Sea with the islands of Delos and Rhenia completing a priceless picture. The beautiful infinity pool - with a built-in Jacuzzi - overlooks the sea as well as the large outdoor terrace full of spots to enjoy outdoor living and views with great comfort. The terrace features a spacious pergola with dining table and a large, fully equipped bar - barbeque area.

It is difficult to do justice to the complexity and taste of the interiors through words. Marvelous marble with its shades of grey and white has been used to pave the floors of the spacious rooms of Apollo Retreat. The villa has been furnished with great attention to detail and in way that best enhances the beauty of its design. The large living room and television room both feature large windows built to allow guests to enjoy the views also when indoors. The kitchen is modern and fully equipped with all useful appliances. A small hallway separates the living area from the two bedrooms. The master bedroom has its own private terrace, sitting room and walk-in wardrobe respecting the standards one could expect from a luxury hotel suite. The second bedroom features a double bedroom and has its own private bathroom en-suite. Below the main house is the guesthouse, located a few steps down from the swimming pool area. This part of the villa has its own private entrance and parking. It includes a large sitting room with a separate kitchen, a master bedroom with sea views and access to its own private terrace and a further bedroom also with double bed. This level fully reflects the beauty and standards of the main part of the villa.

The villa is a perfect retreat for those who wish to live their Mykonian days to the fullest in an atmosphere of great luxury, extreme comfort and privacy. We specially suggest this villa to those who appreciate the combination of Greek architecture, furnishing with European reminiscence along with the innovative design of the outdoor areas.
