Villa Proteus, Fanari, Mykonos, Greece Vacation Rental

 

Enjoy your private stay in Villa Proteus, the luxury Mykonos villa located in Fanari. Quietness and view is the concept since it is 8 km from the town and 5 km from the beach and is on the highest region on the island. Ideal for a group of 10 friends, but in case you are a bigger party, the rest can go to the detached villa.

Admire the natural stone of the villa that blends perfectly with the unlimited view. Take photos of the sunset and the sea and prepare to go viral! Whether you are on your bed or outside by the pool, you will definitely relax. When you are full of energy, you can go for a round of tennis, and then relax again.

You can always grab your friends and see for yourself the wild side of Mykonos. If you need car or a chauffeur, let our concierge know. She will be of precious help throughout your holidays. Pick the date and let her take care of everything you ask for.
