Eros, Mykonos,Greece Vacation Rental

 

Guests looking for a property with views over the various shades the Aegean Sea's blue waters and the rocky south-eastern coast lines of Mykonos need to search no further: Eros is an exclusive, unique villa overlooking the Bay of Agios Lazaros and offers stunning panoramic views. The villa guarantees privacy thanks to its location and the 24 hour security service offered, while also providing all options to VIP clientele including availability (upon request) of a private helipad and small dock access.

The outdoor areas all boast unforgettable views over the Aegean Sea, particularly the Bay of Agios Lazaros. The outdoor dining area, outdoor living area and the pool terrace are meticulously furnished with designer furniture to assure comfort at any time of the day. The attractive infinity pool's edge fades directly into the stunning blue shades of the Aegean sea, and can be heated upon request.

Designer furniture is also featured throughout the interior, along with top-of-the-line appliances in all rooms, offering the utmost in comfort, luxury and technology. The fully air-conditioned main house can accommodate eight guests in its four bedrooms, and provides additional accommodation for two people in the staff quarters. The main kitchen is located on the main level along with the former dining area and living room. A second, smaller kitchen is located on the lower level, leading to the staff quarters. Each bedroom boasts sea-views and access to a terrace via French doors, be it on the main level one or the lower level/pool terrace.

Eros is a 360-degree experience that rivals high-end summer accommodations anywhere in Europe. The quality of every detail will pleasantly surprise the most demanding and pamper from the moment of arrival.
