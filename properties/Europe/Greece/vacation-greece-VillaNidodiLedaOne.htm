Villa Nido di Leda One, Mykonos,Greece Vacation Rental

 

Located in the peaceful and residential area of Houlakia in the Northwest of the island this villa on Mykonos is a true “Gem” within our Mykonos Grand Villas collection. Built within a gated estate on a terraced hillside overlooking the granite bay of Houlakia and the west coast line of the Island, this villa on Mykonos offers guests with memorable breathtaking views of the majestic Aegean Sea and the Islands of Delos, Siros and Paros in the distance. Following the sea side road from the beachfront village of Agios Stefanos guests arrive to the private estate, “home” to all three Nido di Leda Villas. A cobblestone road leads to the main entrance where this Villa in Mykonos welcomes guests into an elegant, spacious, open plan living/dining area with its off white wood beam ceilings, a charming fireplace and marble floors. A bright, fully equipped and functional kitchen located separately and beyond the dining area invites the ?cook? amongst our guests to feel right at home. The majestic view of the magnificent Aegean Sea through the patio doors will tempt our guests to stay indoors. However, once exiting onto the shaded veranda with a dining table suitable for ten, our guests realize where the ideal setting is, for a romantic evening dinner or a cocktail party. Finally also found on this level, is a cleverly hidden auxiliary bathroom.

Down a flight of stairs and onto the lower level is the charmingly furnished spacious master bedroom en-suite, with its king size build-in bed, that is suitable for a couple seeking something special. In addition, two elegantly furnished and bright bedrooms, sharing a bathroom, one with a build-in double bed while the other with a double bed made of leather, are also found on this level. All three bedrooms have direct access through patio doors to the pool terrace and the elegantly designed, shaded living/dining area, with a table for twenty, a fully equipped bar and a built-in BBQ. A convenient guest room and shower are also found at the pool area. The sun beds scattered around the infinity pool and the shaded outdoor gym area, are always appreciated by our guests, providing them the opportunity to start the day with a private workout and finish with a refreshing dip in the infinity pool.

The proximity of this villa on Mykonos to the main town of Chora” and its hosting flexibility, make it ideal for a family, or a group of easy going friends who wish to savour all that a Mykonos vacation has to offer, in comfort and style.
