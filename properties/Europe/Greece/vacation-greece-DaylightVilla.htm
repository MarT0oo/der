Daylight Villa, Almyrida, Chania, Crete, Greece Vacation Rental

 

Daylight Villa is contemporary style, incorporating a mixture of stone and concrete with light surfaces, large windows and sliding doors, steel and wooden floors. High-quality materials, aesthetic interior design, distinctively furnished with luxury in mind.

Entering this villa you will experience the breath-taking view overlooking the pool area, the village, the White Mountains and the blue sea. Sliding doors leads you out to a spacious terrace where you can enjoy the beautiful sunset in the evenings.

There are two bedrooms on the first floor and two bedrooms on the ground floor. All the bedrooms have its own ensuite bathrooms with bathtub and a furnished veranda or terrace. Spacious wardrobes, safe box, dressing table and Satellite TV in all bedrooms.

On the ground floor, it is a large living area with fireplace and an open and spacious kitchen, fully equipped with modern appliances and a large dining table with ample room for eight persons. From the kitchen, you have access to an intimate backyard/garden with barbecue and a big dining table.

The tiled pool area with sunbeds, umbrella and sitting areas where you can enjoy the view and swim in your private pool (45 m2), is perfectly incorporated with the house.

In the basement, you will find a well-equipped gym and anopther big bathroom with hamam (steam bath). A private little “wellness center”….. perfect for recreation!

Whether seeking for private luxury villas overlooking the scenic landscape of Almyrida, or a villa with view to the blue Cretan sea and the beautiful White Mountains, Villa Karga is the choice for you. Our spacious villa offer an incredible array of facilities & services ensuring a holiday filled with comfort and wellness.

Guests are given the opportunity to relax in the nice and stylish living spaces, unwind in the hamam or work out at the gym, embrace the benefits of wellness or sit back in the cosy living areas and enjoy the amenities provided. The modern and fully equipped kitchen allow guests to prepare a meal of their choice and relish the flavours on the atmospheric outdoor area or simply catch the sun as they lay on the sun beds after a refreshing swim in the pool.
