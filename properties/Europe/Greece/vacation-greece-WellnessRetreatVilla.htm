Wellness Retreat Villa, Mykonos, Greece Vacation Rental

 

Wellness Retreat Villa Mykonos villas, located at a distance of 7 minutes from Mykonos town and 5 minutes car ride from Ag. Stefanos beach, covers 600sq.m. and sits on a terrain of 4,900sq.m., in a serene environment, surrounded by the fabulous traditional Cycladic stonework.

Its grounds accommodate up to 16 guests in 8 bedrooms with air condition units and ceiling fans in all rooms. This secluded villa offers to the guests the sense of privacy and relaxation, well protected from the north Mykonian winds all summer long. The villa’s design ensures a spectacular view to the sea from all the outdoors sitting and dining areas. The large outdoor spaces are equipped with sun beds, Jacuzzi, barbecue, & a dining area.

The villa extends over two levels. Each level has its own entrance.

The upper ground/pool level consists of a spacious sitting area with TV, Blu-ray player, fireplace, dining area, a fully equipped kitchen with . Three of villa’s bedrooms with en/suite bathroom are on this level.

The lower ground floor consists of a fully equipped kitchen and dining/ office area, a spacious, fully equipped gym, massage room, spacious Hammam with bathroom. Five of villa’s bedrooms are on this level.

Outdoors the villa features an infinity pool at the ground level with al fresco shower, and a kid’s pool at the lower level. Both pools overview the sea. There is a Jacuzzi with a resting area and sea view, a shaded dining area for up to 10 guests with panoramic view, two sitting areas, one shaded and the other open air with stunning sea view and an outdoor parking space for more than 4 cars.

Our guests enjoy high quality VIP services 24/7. Heliport, infinity pool, a special pool for children, outdoor Jacuzzi, gym, steam room, barbeque, outdoor airplay sound system, security alarm, Wi-Fi internet, private parking for more than 4 cars, are only some of the features of the Wellness Retreat Villa.
