Cayenne, Mykonos,Greece Vacation Rental

 

The delightful Cayenne lies atop the hills above the new harbour of Mykonos, an elevated position that allows for spectacular views of the Mykonian sunsets and a panoramic outlook stretching from the Chora out to the islands of Delos, Rhenia and Tinos. The property’s location in congenial to those who wish to not be in the middle of the action but still within easy reach, thanks to the reasonable distance from the restaurants and nightlife of Mykonos Town.

A lush garden surrounds Cayenne featuring typical Mediterranean vegetation, in a perfect balance with the traditional shapes shown throughout the villa’s exterior architecture. The outdoor spaces can be basically divided in two separate levels. The upper level hosts the villa’s private pool with its infinity edge overlooking the Aegean sea, in addition to hosting summer lounge areas and an elegant teak deck perfect for soaking up the Greek sun. The lower outdoor area is mainly dedicated to the aforementioned garden, giving space to nature on the property’s premises.

Indoors there is a clear contrast between contemporary and vintage décor, embodied throughout the various rooms and blending in a stunning harmony. The colors are those typical of a summer residence, where the dominance of white is complimented by washed colors and soft tones of wooden furnishing and decorations. The main houses is divided amongst two levels, with the lower level being on the same as the pool terrace and hosting an ample open space which includes two distinct living area and the indoor dining area.

Cayenne is the ideal property for families or group of friends looking for a panoramic and conveniently located position mixed with a tasteful décor and modern amenities, where a guest has the liberty of living Mykonos on a day by day basis given the various options offered.
