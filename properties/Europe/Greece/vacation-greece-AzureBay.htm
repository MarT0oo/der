Azure Bay, Faragas Beach, Mykonos, Greece Vacation Rental

 

Owing its name to a privileged waterfront position, Azure Bay is a grand private villa designed entirely to the specifications of its discerning owners who wished to create the ultimate retreat for large families or groups of friends within the idyllic setting of the property’s location. Generous spaces, traditional Cycladic architecture and minimal interior design combine in this villa’s airy and fresh ambience, providing unrivalled comfort to guests throughout indoor and outdoor spaces - from the spacious front terrace with the large infinity pool and various living/dining areas to the voluminous living room, dining room and bedrooms.

The concept of space was wisely matched with all modern comforts and attractive minimal furnishings and fittings. Interiors boast a strong presence of white cleverly interrupted by contrasting décor. The property’s bedrooms are all fitted to the same standard, featuring each en-suite bathrooms, air-conditioning and TV. Divided between the main house and two gracious guesthouses they provide accommodation for large groups while also allowing privacy at any time.

Azure Bay is the perfect getaway for guests who wish to combine stylish living with a “postcard-worthy” surrounding – an experience of pure relaxation whether its having a glass of wine on the pool terrace at sunset or having a swim in the enchanted bay below the villa.
