The Crown Estate, Mykonos, Greece Vacation Rental

 

Welcome to The Crown Estate in Psarou. This remarkable, newly renovated, 3-story villa, located above the most prominent beach of Mykonos, epitomizes the pure elements of Cycladic architecture in a masterful and affluent design. This essence of Mykonian luxury radiates across 550 square meters of interior living space, with towering views over the most notable beach of the island.

Just 400 meters away from the most fashionable beach of the island and the highly celebrated Nammos Beach Bar & Restaurant, the extraordinary Villa with 6-bedrooms (each with en suite bathroom) plus 1 personnel room, accommodates up to 13 persons. The villa has a sophistication enveloped in light and air to reflect the freshness of Mykonian style and the natural details of the island, while its state of the art facilities reflect the personality and tastes of those accustomed to the best in quality, design, details, and lifestyle.

The Crown Estate in Psarou features unparalleled 24/7 luxury services. A live-in private housekeeper maintains the villa in its pristine condition and meets every guest’s need or want; offering guests an experience, a unique indulgence, and the perfect summer escape.

Spacious living rooms with vaulted ceilings elevate expectations and illuminate throughout the villa’s 3-stories and across 550 sqm of interior space. Guests will surrender to the villas naturally chic aesthetic as they escape into one of the villa’s six private bedroom suites, each with a sleek en-suite bathroom featuring luxurious Greek toiletries and amenities, generous closets, and wonderful views of the Aegean. The master bedroom is the ultimate lavish retreat facing Psarou Beach; complete with an in-room oversized bathtub, en suite bathroom with shower, and built-in luxurious king bed. The master walk-in closet is designed to match the tastes of those accustomed to the highest ambience and style of living.

The Crown Estate in Psarou was conceived for its guest to enjoy the never-ending views of Psarou and the sparkling Aegean waters while the rolling mountains of the famous Cycladic island form the perfect backdrop for letting go next to the sea blue infinity pool. The shaded outdoor dining area with barbecue surround the pool and form the perfect entertainment area while custom lounge furniture offers guests infinite moments of relaxation under the warm sun, or by night under the effervescence lighting of the stars and the shining lights of the famous Psarou bay forming an unforgettable backdrop. Offering more than an accommodation, The Crown Estate in Psarou guarantees its refined guests will embark on a journey of bliss and elevating moments next to Mykonos’s most exclusive beach. With a team of professionals ready to offer an unforgettable luxury experience tailored to meet the needs of its guests 24/7. The distinctive location offers many facilities and parking for two cars in the private driveway.
