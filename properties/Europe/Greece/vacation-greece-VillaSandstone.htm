Villa Sandstone, Kastro, Mykonos, Greece Vacation Rental

 

Villa Sandstone is a beautiful 7-bedroom stone-built villa at the area of Kastro, with amazing sea and sunset view, the perfect option for your vacations in Mykonos! The property is close to Agios Stefanos beach, Mykonos’ new port, Mykonos Town (Chora) and Choulakia Bay, with uniquely shaped pebbles of natural interest are close.

The villa is on the North-West side of Mykonos and consists of a main house, 3 independent guest houses, several outdoor sitting areas, dining area and an outdoor Jacuzzi. Guests enjoy moments of relaxation at the outdoor areas with comfortable sofas, floor pillows, dining table etc.
