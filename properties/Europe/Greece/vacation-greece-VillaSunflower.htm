Villa Sunflower, Kalafatis, Mykonos, Greece Vacation Rental

 

Located at the area of Kalafatis, just a short drive to the famous beaches of Kalafatis, Lia and Kalo Livadi the beautiful Villa Sunflower invites guests to have the time of their life at the cosmopolitan island of Mykonos. The villa is built in the traditional Mykonian/Cycladic architecture of white washed cube houses with a contemporary touch in the design and furniture.

Villa Sunflower is around 250 sq.m., formed in 3 levels and comfortably accommodates 14 guests at 7 double bedrooms. All outdoor areas of the villa are surrounded by pergolas and stone pedestals that protect the house from the northern winds. The guests can enjoy top facilities: infinity private swimming pool (20 sq.m.), heated Jacuzzi, pool bar & lounge area with BBQ.

The villa is located in a 4.000 sq.m. plot along with 2 other properties.
