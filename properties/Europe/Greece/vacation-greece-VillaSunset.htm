Villa Sunset, Mykonos, Greece Vacation Rental

 

Villa Sunset is an exceptional and rare jewel on the island of Mykonos. It is located in Aleomandra facing Delos and its colorful sunsets. The construction and architecture of the villa is a flawless combination of modern and traditional Mykonian style.

The house is surrounded by a very spacious white-stone terrace and superb huge pond-style waterfront infinity pool. Various terraces and relaxation areas embrace the property. In the pool, the beautiful marble deck with comfortable sun-chairs promises a relaxing morning. The two spacious outdoor dining and BBQ areas are perfect for cocktail parties and open-air dinners.

The living room is decorated with elegant and modern white furniture. The living room, combined with an open-style kitchen and formal dining area, features large windows and balconies overlooking the pool and the sea, offering a panoramic view of the Aegean even while indoors. Two double bedrooms with en suite bathrooms are located on the ground floor.

The upper floor features a master bedroom and two more similar bedrooms, all with their own en suite bathrooms and private terraces. One guesthouse is located below the swimming pool. The guesthouse can be accessed by two side stairs from the swimming pool and it has its own kitchen, bathroom, a double bedroom and living room area. One more guesthouse (staff room) is located at the entrance of the Villa, and has a king sized bed with en suite bathroom and view of the sea and Sunset.
