Luxury Honeymoon Villa, Firostefani, Santorini, Greece Vacation Rental

 

Luxury Honeymoon Villa offers all the necessary comforts in an idyllic environment and is one of the most beautiful villas designed for couples looking for a romantic destination for honeymooners.

Guests can enjoy breathtaking views of the caldera of Santorini in the spacious terrace of 62 sqm and can relax in the outdoor Jacuzzi, watching the sunset. The fully equipped kitchen, king size round bed in the master bedroom and an office space with Apple computer and high speed internet access are just some of the elements that make Luxury Honeymoon Villa really special.

The luxurious 107 sqm of this house is enough to comfortably accommodate a couple or a family and its premises also includes a second fully equipped kitchen and a second bedroom with a lovely bathroom painted in blue palette
