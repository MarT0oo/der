Syros Passion Villa, Syros, Greece Vacation Rental

 

Located on one of the most beautiful bays of Syros, just outside the popular village of Phoenikas, Syros Passion Villa is a stone built, luxurious retreat with a large pool and all the amenities a modern traveler seeks.

Syros Passion Villa surprises its guests with the panoramic view of the Aegean Sea. Nothing can be compared to the vastness of the sea, the mystical sunrises and romantic sunsets. Syros Passion Villa can offer idyllic moments - memories for a lifetime – that will compensate the visitors when it’s time to say goodbye.

Syros Passion villa offers seclusion and privacy, as the nearest residential area is 200meters away. In the outdoor area, there is a large swimming pool with comfortable loungers and a dining area with breathtaking view of the Aegean Sea. Syros Passion villa is surrounded by a beautiful garden of 3.700 sqm that consists of a scenic vineyard, palm trees and cypress trees. The garden is a traditional greek landscape full of beautiful colors and tasty flavors.

Syros Passion villa offers its guest a change to enjoy the view of the Aegean Sea, sunbathe in privacy in the large pool and to walk in the vineyard in the garden. All in all, Syros Passion Villa can create an unforgettable experience to its guests.

The house has a big, modern and stylish swimming pool where one can relax and enjoy the view, together with cold refreshments or even a martini.

The main house consists of three (3) bedrooms with en suite bathrooms, a fully equipped kitchen and a spacious living / dining room overlooking the Aegean Sea.

Syros Passion Villa has a guest house as well. It consists of two (two) bedrooms, a second fully equipped kitchen, a living room, and a bathroom.

The house is ideal for families and large group of friends who desire a beautiful and private estate for their holidays.
