Panormos Retreat, Panormos, Mykonos, Greece Vacation Rental

 

Perched on a hill, Panormos Retreat is a marvelous renovated Cycladic property set in the most natural area of Mykonos. The location makes the outstanding Panormos and Agios Sostis beaches reachable within minutes while Chora remains at a little less than a 15 minute drive away.

This very traditional home shows an exquisite and authentic style whether it is from its architecture or other elements. The swimming pool terrace displays various relaxation areas and shaded pergolas. An extensive view over Lake Marathi and the sandy beach of Panormos completes the outdoor experience.

The charming decor throughout the house is really appealing owed to its preserved original features and cozy atmosphere. Panormos Retreat was built as three separate entities being the main house with living room, kitchen and master, a pool studio and a guest house.

Panormos Retreat is set to become a favorite amongst those who wish to become accustomed to one of the most beautiful areas of Mykonos and for those who have a passion for comfort enhanced by beautiful decor within tradition.
