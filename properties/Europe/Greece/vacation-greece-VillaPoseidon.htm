Villa Poseidon, Fanari, Mykonos, Greece Vacation Rental

 

Escape in your private paradise, Villa Poseidon. The luxury Mykonos villa is located in Fanari, the highest region on the island. Imagine the view, right? The town is 8 km away and the beach 5 km away. Grab the chance for private parties! The villa can sleep 13 people, or 12 and keep the single bedroom for staff. But that is details; shall we start with a tour?

The 3-level villa is full of cozy corners; from the king-size beds to the living room and pool loungers, the homebodies will find many places to sink in and relax. The fitness enthusiasts will be thrilled to learn that they can play tennis or work out in the gym when they are not in the pool.

The outdoor dining area will be favorite spot during sunset. Eating delicious meals as the sun melts into the sea is fantastic. In case you don’t feel like experimenting with Mediterranean cuisine, call our concierge. She will arrange delicious meals with our chef! You just sit back and enjoy!
