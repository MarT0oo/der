Villa Hacienda & SPA, Corfu Island, Greece Vacation Rental

 

The garden offers a lovely outside dining and barbecue area under the shade of the olive trees - placed to fully enjoy the stunning views over the harbour of Agios Stefanos and across the straits to Albania.

The accommodation is arranged in the shape of the Greek 'pi' with two bedroom wings with outside access on either side of the lovely mosaic-tiled swimming pool with Jacuzzi overflowing into it. A wonderful covered terrace borders this Jacuzzi, curtained and with supremely comfortable lounging furniture this is the ideal spot for totally indolent relaxation! On the first floor the dramatic stylish Master Suite has all the ingredients of extreme comfort: nuptial bed separated sitting area with a sofa bed and an egocentric bathroom from when you can enjoy the yachts sailing through the Corfu Channel.

The lower level offers a fully equipped Spa, including sauna and steam bath, where private health and beauty treatments (bookable locally) can be taken in a muted, relaxing atmosphere with window looking into the swimming pool. In addition on this level a twin bedroom with en suite shower room more suitable to teenagers or staff is also available.

Hacienda and Spa is within close proximity to both Haumana and Pierre de Lune making them suitable for large groups holidaying together.

Villa Layout

Open plan living/dining/kitchen leading to covered terrace overlooking the pool. Steps lead down to the two outside bedroom suites (one with twin beds, the other with a double bed) and en suite shower rooms and separate WC (each suite has a small fridge).

First floor reached by stairs from the living area lead to Master Suite with a double bed with en suite bathroom and separate WC.

A further separate by doors study/double bedroom also having access to the bathroom would be ideal for other members of the family. Private balcony. An additional twin bedroom with swimming pool window more suitable for teenagers or staff is also available at the Spa level.

The Spa features a shower room, separate WC, sauna, Turkish steam cabinet, small gym area with exercise bike, body toner machine, stepper and weights and massage bed. Any treatments booked are payable locally.
