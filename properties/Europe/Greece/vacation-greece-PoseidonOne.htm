Poseidon One, Kalafatis, Mykonos, Greece Vacation Rental

 

Located to the right hand side of a three semi-detached property complex, Poseidon one is situated on the Southeastern area of Mykonos within short distance of the beaches of Kalafatis – well known for being the island’s main water sport spot – and Agia Anna with its marvelous bay and turquoise waters. Completed in year 2010 the property is a fine example of the islands characteristic residences with its white structures, which cleverly blends with stone architecture.

The beautiful infinity pool surrounded by wooden sunbeds plays a central role in the front terrace directly facing the bay of Kalafatis. Elegant stone tails are featured throughout this area, as well as in the the outdoor dining area conveniently placed under an attractive pergola to the side of the property. The presence of a wood fire BBQ adjacent to dining area also guarantees potential for great grills at any time of the day for the property’s guests.

The interior décor and design of Poseidon One is based on the common concept of an island summer home featuring carefully chosen furnishing and artifacts all displays typical light tones of pastel colors. The property was developed on the three levels, the entrance one being the one dedicated to the spacious living room which spills directly onto the formal indoor dining area, both boasting sea views. The four bedrooms of the property are equally divided among the upper and lower level, both accessed by cimentogonia staircases. Upstairs the master bedroom and a guest bedroom share access to their panoramic terrace overlooking Kalafatis bay while the two further bedrooms downstairs still offer “water-related” views given their aquarium-style windows looking into the clear waters of the swimming pool – an intriguing concept to provide lighting to the lower level of the property. All bedrooms are well appointed and display the unmistakable touch of the property’s interior designer.

Poseidon One – either rented alone or with its sister property Posiedon Two for larger groups - is ideal for those who are fond of thoughtful décor and a position which shows a different side of the Mykonian life, the relatively unspoilt atmosphere of Kalafatis is a brilliant contrast to the busy moments of Mykonos Town or the more crowded southern beaches.
