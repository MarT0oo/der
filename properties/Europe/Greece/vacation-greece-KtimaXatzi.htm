600 square meters of pure delight

Enveloped by 7 acres of Mediterranean garden with olive grove, orchard, 1 acre of lawn & a variety of colorful tropical and exotic flowers. Villa & the gardens are guarded 24 hours by private security and high intelligence thermal cameras.

The castle-villa has 5 full equipped, luxury bedrooms with baths, jacuzzi in the master suite, 2 wooden attic rooms, Internet, private phone line, LCD television.

In the main floor exists 2 sitting rooms, 2 dinning rooms with marble floors, kitchen, luxury bathroom, play-room for the children. The house also provides, home cinema, sauna and a gym. Outdoor there is a 140 square meter swimming pool with a children's play area, a barbecue house with sofa and monastery table and an authentic old wood oven. Dine Al fresco in this stunning setting.

Follow Ktima Xatzi and the mediteranean breeze on white beaches where the rumors of the traffic is only a fade memory.

Enjoy the unique red sunsets and let your heart breath again.

The ancient Temple of Poseidon will astound you. Cape Sounion is noted as the site of ruins of the ancient Greek temple of Poseidon.

The market at Plaka will surely enchant you, with locals plying their goods from small and quaint little shops. Enjoy a family day at the beach. Ktima Xatzi is significant close to a variety beaches. All are around 5-10 minutes by driving.

One of the amazing aspects of Ktima Xatzi is the secluded position of it, and yet it is close enough to the City of Athens, the Ancient remains and the beach.

Enjoy a boat trip out for the day with the family to the lovely island of Tzia or Kythnos.
