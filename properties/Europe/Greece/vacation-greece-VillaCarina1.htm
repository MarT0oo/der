Villa Carina 1, Mykonos, Greece Vacation Rental

 

This 250 square meters amazing villa is part of a complex of two villas and it is located in Pirgi, between Paradise bay and Super Paradise beach. The well known beach restaurants Jackie O’ and Cavo Paradiso are just a few minute drive away.

This luxurious villa offers 230 degrees of uninterrupted sea view of the islands of Naxos and Delos and it comes with a stunning 110 square meters swimming pool.

Villa Carina consists of six bedrooms, each with its own en suite bathroom, while the master bedroom has a Jacuzzi. There is also an open kitchen, large indoor dining and living rooms, an immense outdoor dining area, bar and BBQ, all covered by a pergola, a comfortable outdoor living room-lounge area covered by a pergola, surround sound system all around and a WC.
