Acqua Di Mare, Mykonos, Greece Vacation Rental

 

Boasting a bold position with outstanding sea views, Acqua Di Mare is a brand new villa located above Panormos Beach. Spawned from the imagination of its discerning owner the property was built as a former vacation retreat where traditional architecture and Italian influenced design mix effortlessly with the enchanting backdrop of the Aegean sea and the long sandy beach minutes below the house.

The five bedroom property features a main entrance level where the living and dining area are directly connected on to the other in the smart use made of this large open space. The kitchen displays a surprising contrast of wood, marble and stone while providing the perfect spot to prepare summer meals thanks to the presence of top of the line appliances. Meals that can be appreciated either indoors at the wooden dining table overlooked by a local artist’s paintings or outdoor under a pergola with the marble based dining table, also fruit of a local artist’s deft. This entire area of the main level’s indoors enjoys direct access to the pool terrace and lounge area.

All bedrooms are designed and fitted to the same standard with characteristic cimentogonia floors, wood beamed ceilings, airy atmosphere and beautiful large en-suite bathrooms (each tinted in a different color). A master bedroom and guest bedroom are located on the main floor, both with sea views and access to the outdoors. On the lower level are two more bedrooms: a second master bedroom and one guest bedroom. A large TV room with comfortable sofas is located on this level, perfect to entertain adults and children. The lower level of the house hosts a new apartment, which can sleep a maximum of six extra guests. The apartment features an entrance room with 2 single beds adjoining a second “children bedroom” with 2 bunk beds (4 single beds), artistically handcrafted in wood, a true paradise for children but also suitable teenagers or adults if needed, such as a nanny. The room has a generous en-suite bathroom and access to an outdoor staircase leading to the garden.

The outdoors mirrors perfectly the style of the interiors of Acqua Di Mare. A long rectangular infinity-edge swimming pool in surrounded by a spacious terrace which provides options for any time of the day thanks to the shaded outdoor living area with sound system, the bar area, the pool deck and the outdoor dining area. All spaces are intelligently furnished and designed for the pure purpose of relaxation and allowing guests to share their time in the privacy of this villa. A discrete glass paneling was installed on one side of the terrace to grant protection from the northern winds while preserving the far-reaching views one can experience from the elevated position of Acqua Di Mare.

Those seeking a combination of privacy, modern and comfortable living along with fabulous natural surroundings will find Acqua Di Mare to be the ideal retreat for their vacations on Mykonos, enjoying this fresh property’s features and its privileged position.
