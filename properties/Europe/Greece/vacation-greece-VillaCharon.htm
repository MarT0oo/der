Villa Charon, Agios Ioannis, Mykonos, Greece Vacation Rental

 

Grab your friends and head to Villa Charon in Agios Ioannis, in the southwestern part of Mykonos. The totally wind protected location gives you the chance to spend endless hours by the pool and admire the view to Delos island. And in case you want to take in the view by the sea, Agios Ioannis beach is only 850 m away.

The cozy villa can sleep 8-10 guests, but is part of a 3-villa complex in case you want to come with a larger group! The villa is divided into 3 levels and offers 4 double bedrooms with en-suite bathrooms which you will love. There is also a fully equipped kitchen for those who love gastronomy experiments; the rest of the group can relax outside!

The sun beds in the terrace are perfect for stretching by the pool. Take selfies in the blue background or have a dinner under the pergola. Our concierge can book our lovely chef only for you. And she can actually book whatever you wish for, from popular tables at hot spots to an island-hopping experience! Just ask for it!
