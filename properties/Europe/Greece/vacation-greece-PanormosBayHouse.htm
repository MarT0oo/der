Panormos Bay House, Panormos Beach, Mykonos, Greece Vacation Rental

 

Panormos Bay House in traditional Mykonian style is located above beautiful Panormos Beach, on the island's northern side, the villa enjoys stunning sea and surrounding hill views. The spacious, verdant and flowering terrace features a large oval swimming pool with pergola covered dining area, BBQ and comfortable teak sun beds.

Inside, a large open-space combines a fully equipped kitchen and dining-sitting area tastefully decorated with antique furniture and ethnic artifacts. Adjoining this living space there is a large, guest bedroom with own sitting area, terrace-entrance and swimming pool access.

On the upper level, both the master and another bedroom share outstanding sea views from a spacious front terrace with outdoor seating. The regular live-in housekeeper has own separate room in the main house, while on the lower level of the villa, there is a separate studio which is ideal for a young couple.

Close-by Panormos Bay and Agios Sostis have long unspoiled sandy beaches, this special villa location offers guests a real getaway experience in a lovely and charming environment. When on the beach, choose between the fashionable in-crowd Panormos restaurant or the exceptionally charming Sostis-setting taverna, for some memorable Mykonos island moments.
