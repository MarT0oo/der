Gracias A La Vida, Mykonos,Greece Vacation Rental

 

Gracias A La Vida is a splendid property situated on one-acre of land in Costa Ilios overlooking the Island of Delos. The position, one of the most exclusive on the Island, offers handy access to the close-by beach of Agios Ioannis and stunning views of the sea and Delos Island with its bright and colorful sunsets.

The main house with private pool and detached guest house have separate entrances and parking and both entities are attractively furnished in tasteful Greek Island style with modern appliances. The main house features a large terrace with sea views, a Camares (pool house) and an attractive bean-shape swimming pool; this area is equipped with comfortable deck chairs, an outdoor dining area and a Greek-style BBQ. The modern and functional kitchen leads to a bright dining room and a large double sitting area, with direct entrance from the pergola-shaded terrace.

Gracias A La Vida is a very large property offering to its guests high-level of comfort in every room, making it the ideal choice for large families or group of friends.
