Super Paradise Three, Super Paradise, Mykonos, Greece Vacation Rental

 

Boasting views over the bay of Super Paradise Beach, the Aegean Sea and the island of Naxos, Super Paradise Three is the third villa included within its brand new villa complex, including other two semi-detached sister properties published in our portfolio.

Similar to its neighboring properties, Super Paradise Three features a well-thought outdoor area comprehensive of a stone-built bar with BBQ, a pergola aptly furnished for al fresco dining and a pool terrace with views over the beach to surround the villa’s private swimming pool. This area can be accessed though small staircases both from the villa’s TV room and all the bedrooms on the lower level.

As mentioned all bedrooms are located on the lower floor and feature smart furnishing along with modern amenities and en-suite bathrooms. The entrance level is instead fully dedicated to the fresh living room, the indoor dining area, the well-equipped kitchen and an interesting TV room, all having access to the outdoor terrace via French doors and all air-conditioned.

As for its sister villas, Super Paradise makes a wonderful escape when combined with the other two properties, ideal for large groups or families looking to spend quality time together while still retaining their own privacy. The vicinity to Super Paradise’s sandy beach and the presence of all amenities allow it to also be appealing to smaller groups who wish to rent it independently and cherish their moments on the Cycladic island of Mykonos.
