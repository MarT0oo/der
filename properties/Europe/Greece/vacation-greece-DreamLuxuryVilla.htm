Dream Luxury Villa, Firostefani, Santorini, Greece Vacation Rental

 

One of the best vantage points on the island is the Dream Luxury Villa, an architectural jewel that was created only in 2010. Guests will enjoy a nice view of the volcano from the best point of the island, in a private villa that combines all modern comforts on the edge of the cliff!

Both the outdoor spaces and the interior of this unique villa welcome visitors to a panoramic view of the Aegean and enjoying quiet relaxation from the hectic pace of the island. The 120 sqm covered by Dream Luxury Villa includes two bedrooms and two bathrooms, but most of this space is outdoors, with 70 sqm for the swimming pool and Jacuzzi, both rare amenities for the Caldera of Santorini.

The master bedroom is beautifully decorated according to the Cycladic style, and has a king size bed, while the whirlpool bath makes the experience truly luxurious.

Another bedroom and one bathroom with Caldera view are located on the second level of the villa, making it the ideal holiday destination for families with children or groups of friends.
