Infinity, Mykonos,Greece Vacation Rental

 

Infinity is a stunningly built hilltop property, overlooking renowned Agrari Bay and its natural island surroundings. The villa's privileged position allows complete privacy and offers amazing panoramic views of the Aegean Sea and Naxos Island. Agrari Beach, with its intimate charm and crystal clear water, is just a quick walk downhill or a few minutes away by car.

The house, beautifully decorated and furnished by an Italian interior designer features attractive art works and antiques from France and Italy. The spacious, relaxed and quality appointed living room shows a lead-through study and dining room area, all boasting wooden floors which add further warmth and style to this beautiful interior.

The pergola-shaded dining terrace, with superb sea and garden views, has a typical Greek BBQ with a long dining table ideal for all types of entertaining. The villa's veranda leads, via a short natural stone path, to a very large and elegant infinity swimming pool sheltered by giant rocks and cacti. A large pergola with an outdoor pool bar adds further space and character to the outdoor and swimming pool area.

Certainly one of the best and largest properties in Mykonos Island, Infinity - a perfect getaway - is ideal for those discerning spirits searching infinite beauty, rare sophistication and Mediterranean glamour
