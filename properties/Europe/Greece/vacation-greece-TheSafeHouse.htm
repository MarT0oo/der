The Safe House, Psirri, Athens, Greece Vacation Rental

 

Enjoy your stay in a cinematic, secret agent's Safe House. A former escape room opens its doors to accommodate those that want to enjoy a secret agent's Safe House and live in a themed loft just 5 minutes from Acropolis. A spacious, 1,400 ft² loft, that accommodates up to 4 adults. If you solve the loft's puzzles during your stay, and discover the hidden safe, a special gift awaits you!

The apartment has 1 bedroom and 1 bathroom with shower and free toiletries. An industrial approach in design, renovated and modernized and at the same time a cinematic scenery that will make you feel like you are starring in a James Bond movie. There is a dining area and a kitchen complete with a dishwasher and a toaster. There are no partitions separating the bedroom from the rest of the apartment. Therefore, it’s mainly suitable for 1 couple, families, tourists and businessmen. You have free access to Wi-Fi. There is a 55” LED TV with satellite channels in the living room and a fully equipped office with a dual screen PC for your enjoyment.

Located in the heart of Athens, just 400 meters from the Ermou Street-Shopping Area, the Safe House and the themed loft near Acropolis offers amazing mountain and city views. Situated only 400 meters from Monastiraki Square and 500 meters from Monastiraki Flea Market. Omonia Square is just a 6-minute walk from the apartment, while the National Theater of Greece is 500 meters away and only 2 minutes from the best night life Athens has to offer. The nearest airport is Elefthérios Venizelos Airport which is 20 km from the property.
