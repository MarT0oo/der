Villa Fedra, Mykonos, Greece Vacation Rental

 

Located in the beautiful area of Ftelia, just 80m from the beach in a small bay lays the beautiful villa Fedra. Ideally located very close to the famous Alemagou beach-bar-restaurant, villa Fedra is the perfect option for groups of young people looking to have the time of their lives in Mykonos! Guests also benefit from a unique villa service and order breakfast, lunch, dinner from Alemagou and have it delivered at the property. Note that clubbing and events are neighboring the property at Alemagou beach-bar-restaurant and are ideal for groups that enjoy all day and night entertainment!

The house includes the Main Villa, 2 independent guest houses and a studio, a private pool, numerous outdoor shaded areas, beautiful garden, and a chapel. There is also a generator covering every need the villa may have.

The Villa enjoys a panoramic sea view, spacious lounge areas, BBQ and comfortable sunbeds, ideal for moments of fun under the sun!

The Main Villa is formed in 2 levels and consists of 1 bedroom with a spacious bathroom & Jacuzzi, a dining room, a fully equipped with modern appliances kitchen, a spacious living room, a utility room and a wc. A beautiful staircase leads to the 2nd level where 2 additional bedrooms and a spacious independent bathroom are located.

Apart from the main villa there are 2 independent guest houses. The 1st one consists of 2 roomy bedrooms with en-suite bathrooms, a wc, a beautiful living room, a dining room, a fully equipped kitchen and a playgroundlocated next to the kitchen while the 2nd one consists of 2 sizable bedrooms with en-suite bathrooms, a living room, a lounge area, an auxiliary kitchen and a wc.

The last unit which comprises Villa Fedra is an independent studio, with a bedroom with an en-suite bathroom and an auxiliary kitchen.

Villa Fedra is a true Mykonian summer gem, the one and only option for your summer experience in Greece!
