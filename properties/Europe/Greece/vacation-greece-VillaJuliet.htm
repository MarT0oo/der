Villa Juliet, Mykonos, Greece Vacation Rental

 

Villa Juliet is a magnificent 400 sq.m. villa located in a 4.000 sq.m. plot at the beautiful golf of Ftelia and is one of the most beautiful beachfront villas on the island of Mykonos.

This luxury villa is only 2 minutes away for the famous beach of Ftelia and only 10 minutes from Mykonos town center by car; concluding to be a quiet residence, that offers full privacy and at the same time being very close to the famous beach restaurants and the unique nightlife of Mykonos Island. Villa Juliet is suitable for a company of individuals and perfect for a big family.

The villa has a beautiful sea view and consists of the main house & 2 guest houses, accommodating up to 12 guests. All rooms have access to the pool and at the same time they may be connected with each other. The outdoor areas include a private pool with sunbeds, pergola covered dining area, bar, living area & a private helipad etc.
