Villa Sparkle, Mykonos, Greece Vacation Rental

 

Ideally located in Agia Sofia, Villa Sparkle, is part of a luxury villa complex that combines state of the art amenities and exclusive VIP services with the traditional Mykonian Style. The architecture of the villas is based on natural material such as stone and wood, to ensure light abundance and the open space experience.

Imagine a long sunbathing session, after taking breakfast on the large veranda, just before driving to one of the nearby spectacular sandy beaches that, among other, have made Mykonos one of the world’s top holiday destinations. Whether you travel with family or friends the wisely designed 156m2 Villa Sparkle will create the perfect ambiance for you to have the time of your life and enjoy every single moment.

Its’ amenities include 2 double bedrooms with ensuite bathrooms on the ground floor, a living area, a fully equipped open plan kitchen and a toilet in the entrance level and 2 master bedrooms with a sharing bathroom on the top floor. On the outside there are spacious dining and lounge areas.

Only a few minutes away from Mykonos town, the small region that took its name from Agia (Saint) Sofia is the ideal location for those seeking a relaxing holiday in a most stylish setting, while being so conveniently near to some of the hottest restaurants and clubs in Europe!
