Villa Adonis, Houlakia, Mykonos, Greece Vacation Rental

 

Take your holidays in Villa Adonis and you won’t regret it! The private villa in Mykonos Sleeps 6 people, so it is ideal for a family or a group of friends. For those who are planning for a bigger group, they can stay in the twin villa located in the same complex and can sleep 6 more people. It is in Houlakia, in the northwestern part of Mykonos; the beach is only 950m. away and the noisy town center is 5.8 km away. Sounds pretty relaxing, right?

Come on, walk with us! A private swimming pool surrounded by a spacious deck with sun beds and deck chairs, an area for relaxing, a large shaded pergola equipped with comfortable sofa, dining table for 6 guests, BBQ and a superb view over the endless blue sea and the historical island of Delos is what you should expect outside.

In the interior design, the elegant furniture and the stylish decoration create a warm and homely atmosphere in the living room, dining area, and fully equipped kitchen. You will find Dunlopillo mattresses and linen made of Egyptian cotton in the bedrooms. The two spacious bathrooms have comfortable showers with rain spray showerheads, Apivita bath amenities, as well as towels and bathrobes of excellent quality.
