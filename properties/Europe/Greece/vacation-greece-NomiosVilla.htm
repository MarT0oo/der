Nomios Villa, Tzia, Kea, Greece Vacation Rental

 

Nomios is a brand new modern villa for the Aegean fanatics with ultra-modern Cycladic aesthetics, natural materials and a rare to find set of amenities, ideal for families or a big group of friends. The residence, located at a secluded area on top of a hill, in a plot of land of over 108,000 ft², exploits to the utmost the unique natural features of the protected landscape and offers its guests hard to find privacy and relaxing vistas.

The white washed facades and the local stone walls, coupled with the natural oak tree surroundings and the stunning sea view, create the perfect setting for a peaceful stay and a memorable vacation. Split in two levels, this 5,400 ft² mega villa can accommodate up to 12 guests in various modern accommodation options; a perfect villa for a large group or a family vacation. Its outdoor dining area and the infinity pool, are a rather realistic extension of the bright blue waters that dominate the views.

A winery is attached to the villa. In addition to an outdoor pool, Nomios Villa provides an outdoor tennis court and a fitness center. Guests can unwind with a drink at one of the villa's bars, which include a poolside bar and a bar/lounge. A complimentary breakfast is offered each morning. A computer station is located on site with complimentary wired and wireless internet access.

Nomios luxury villa let you design the vacations of a lifetime. We want you to feel that anything is possible, and we’ll do our best to make it happen. The Kea luxury villa is your ticket to exclusivity, privacy and bespoke services from the moment you step on the island until your very last day of vacation.

Our aim is to surpass your expectations in every way!
