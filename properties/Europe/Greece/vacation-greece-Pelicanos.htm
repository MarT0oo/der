Pelicanos, Pyrgi, Mykonos, Greece Vacation Rental

 

Hailing from the experience and imagination of the owner of a worldwide renowned resort, Pelicanos is a majestic, brand new property perched in a panoramic position between Paradise beach and Super Paradise beach on Mykonos. The views from the property stretch 180 degrees over the Aegean sea allowing the guests to enjoy one of the most soul moving backdrops during their time at Pelicanos. An infinity edge private pool functions as the “heart” of the villa’s grounds, separating the main house from the guest house with its lounge area and annexed outdoor dining pergola – BBQ area.

The concept of Pelicanos comes across as soon as the guests walk through the main entrance, inviting the guests to dwell and enjoy their time in the luxury of all modern amenities and comforts. A generous living room greets guests upon entering the property, featuring modern furnishing throughout and spilling directly onto a private, furnished terrace. The ultra modern kitchen, fully equipped with top-of-the-line appliances, boasts elegant marble surfaces and is easily accessible both from indoors and outdoors.

The bedrooms are equally divided between the main house and guesthouse. Each bedroom has been fitted to high standards with a design mixing wood and marble in a wise manner, providing a refreshing ambience while not denying any modern comfort. They each have their own furnished terrace apt to enjoy moments of intimacy while enjoying the suspenseful sea views. A new guesthouse was recently added in a lateral position of the property; it includes two guest bedrooms sharing a bathroom, small living room and kitchenette all meeting the standards displayed in the other units.

Pelicanos is certainly a 360 degree experience thanks to the property’s impressive features, it allows guests to enjoy every aspect of the Mykonian life while treasuring moments in one of the island’s most inspiring private villas.
