Villa Salacia, Kalafatis, Mykonos, Greece Vacation Rental

 

Villa Salacia is a stunning private property in a 13,474 m2 lot, perched on a privileged position overlooking the bays of Agia Anna and Kalafatis on the southeastern side of Mykonos and one of a few selected villas that features a private helipad that our VIP guests can use if they wish to arrive to or leave the island in absolute privacy.

This villa encapsulates the island’s uniqueness and reflects its renowned beauty. The elevated and waterfront location allows for inspiring views over the turquoise waters of the Aegean, stretching as far as the eye can see. A private staircase leads down to the sea, where one can dive in directly off the rocks below the property and potentially swim to a nearby sandy cove.

The property’s ample outdoor spaces are ideal for lounging and dining and are everything a large family or a group of friends could ask for. The concept of Salacia is without a doubt that of enjoyment and entertaining guests with style. A true testament to this statement is the massive pool terrace stretching out in the most panoramic position of the property. The swimming pool plays a catalyst role with its central position, sidelined on one side by a chic wooden sun deck and on the other by a grand pergola. Two distinct dining tables find space under the pergola along with a massive lounge area furnished with summer furniture and a plenitude of cushions. An extended bar area with a proper outdoor kitchen completes the exterior experience, forming a full circle of options for any mood throughout the day.

Style is a constant also indoors where the space developed on three levels conveys a calming sensation - various shades of white and grey were thoughtfully used, enhancing the overall minimal decor of the property. The bedrooms are all appointed to a high standard and all share a view over the bay below the property, exception made for the staff bedroom located to the back of the property. On the entrance level the functional kitchen connects directly with the outdoor bar area while the airy living room spills directly onto the pool terrace.

Salacia is all a rental property needs to be, in order to cater to even the most demanding travelers. It offers the luxury of arriving by air, adding an extra touch of glamour to an already exclusive holiday experience. A grand mix of space, style and quality in a location that allows both privacy and access to the sea - the ultimate way to experience Mykonos at its finest.
