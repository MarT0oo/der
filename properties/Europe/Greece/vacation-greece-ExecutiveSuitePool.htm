Pure Indulgence in Suite with Private Pool

Encompassing an area of 38 sq. meters, the elegantly adorned Executive Suite with infinity pool is a haven of privacy, luxury and comfort ideal for a romantic escape in the cosmopolitan island of Mykonos.

Enjoy the astonishing sea view from the sun kissed veranda, take a dip in the refreshing private infinity pool, and tempt your taste buds with gourmet dishes on the grand terrace. Sense the upmost of luxury at one of the best.

Hotel Bill & Coo is a 4-star hotel in Mykonos, which invites guests into a tranquil retreat within a stunning setting. The intimate hotel is just a 10-minute walk from Mykonos Town, and 150m from Magali Ammos Beach and the shimmering sea. Mykonos Port and Airport are 2.5km away.

The glistening hotel infinity pool is the perfect place to cool off whilst gazing at spellbinding vistas. At sunset, guests can watch the sun dip below the Aegean Sea with an expertly shaken cocktail. The hotel restaurant is a romantic spot to dine on seasonal Mediterranean dishes overlooking panoramic views.

Each sumptuous room in the Bill & Coo Hotel combines traditional Greek island architecture with captivating contemporary features. Swathes of crisp white linens and hand-picked vibrant fabrics sit alongside comfortable furnishings and modern amenities. Guests can sit on spacious private verandas - some complete with luxurious Jacuzzis to relax in.
