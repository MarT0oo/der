Casa de Piedra, Deia, Spain Vacation Rental

 

The Islands of Spain are a gathering place of those who love to experience the best views, wonderful music, the sounds of the ocean and wonderful outdoor experiences. Shop in the Sineu market, one of the oldest in Mallorca where you can shop for fresh fruit and vegetables, eggs and fish. You will find the stores bustling with the sounds of people enjoying the open market and all it has to offer. A truly family friendly place to stay.

The Casa de Piedra is one of the top villas on the island offering a wonderful view of the island from each corner. Go outside and visit the natural setting, hike up the mountain and take a stroll into the past. Find some special rocks as you walk and explore the vast hilltops and create your own memories at Casa de Piedra.

In the village of Deià, this beautiful stone house is at walking distance from all the local tapa bars, restaurants, boutique shops and the historic local church. Main house: 180m2. Accessed through the terrace you enter the living area on your right equipped with two large white sofas, sofa table and fire place. Left is the open pan kitchen and dining area with table and chairs for 8 people. Grounds: enclosed garden with private heated swimming pool (on request), sun chairs covered dinning and seating area, BBQ and upper level terrace.

Open plan living room with sofa and tea table in front of chimey. Large flat TV screen with satellite TV, Music System for iPod. Dining room off of living room on different level separated by 3 stairs, seating area and table for 8 and open to kitchen.
