Mountain Side Mansion, Westlake, Cape Town, South Africa Vacation Rental

 

This modern double story home overlooks the Constantia Valley and is ideally suited for relaxing family holidays. It is situated in a security estate which is nestled on the slopes of the Silvermine mountains. The house is close to some of the best wine farms, golf courses, forests and beaches Cape Town has to offer. The home consists of 4 bedrooms, 3 with ensuite bathrooms and all with their own private balcony. The kitchen, dining and lounge areas flow into one another and are perfect for cooking up a storm while entertaining guests. The pool and braai area have an incredible view and are great for outside entertaining.
