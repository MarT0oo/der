Villa Verdana
3 Bedroom | 3 Bathrooms | Infinity Pool                                            
Mauritius
       From €550 - €1020 per night

The tranquil atmosphere and the large open spaces of your villa will quickly make you feel totally at ease.  Traditional plantation-style detached villa with garage, designed in rectilinear form and offering comfortable indoor and outdoor living, elevated terrace and infinity pool set within a lush, tropical landscaped garden. 

The warm surroundings of the landscaped garden will provide you with a welcome sense of privacy, but you are never far from an extensive range of 5* services - many of which can even be enjoyed in your villa.

There are 4 styles of villas - Veranda, Terrace, Village and Courtyard - for you to choose from, ranging from spacious 3-bedroom villas to 4-bedroom villas up to 400 sqm. Built with excellent quality finishes, the villas have air-conditioned master bedrooms with en-suite bathrooms, infinity pools, large verandas, state-of-the-art communications, a barbecue area, a gazebo and landscaped tropical gardens.

Reading on a long chair under the gazebo or lying next to the large infinity swimming pool while the mouth-watering aromas from the barbecue enchant your senses...this is your time for relaxing!

Facilities

Guests are spoilt for choice with access to an exceptional range of facilities and will enjoy privilege access to: 18-hole Championship Golf du Château as well as preferential rates at two other top golf courses in the South West. Two of the most beautiful well-being centres in the Indian Ocean - Seven Colours Spa and Umuzi Sports Centre. The beautiful ‘Frederica’ Nature Reserve, which presents a unique environment for observing the wildlife, fauna and flora of Mauritius. 11 restaurants at the Domaine de Bel Ombre including the renowned Château de Bel Ombre.
