Star Fish Lodge
4 Bedrooms | 4 Bathrooms Ensuites | Salt Water Pool                
Robberg Beach, Plettenberg Bay, South Africa
       From R26,278 per week

Welcome to Star Fish Lodge in Plettenberg Bay.  Overlooking a landscaped indigenous garden with Salt Water Pool, Starfish Lodge is a two minute walk from the swimming beach of Robberg.

This 4 bedroom lodge offers Bed and continental breakfast accommodations as well as the option of exclusive use of the lodge on a self catering basis.  Only the finest linen and bedding has been provided as well as under floor heating, heated towel rails, safety deposit boxes and hair dryers. Each of the 4 rooms has beach towels, beach chairs and sun umbrellas, that can be taken to the beach.

There are communal tea and coffee making facilities and telephones in each of the rooms. Each room has a patio/balcony overlooking the indigenous garden and salt water swimming pool.

Emergency lighting is available in each room – fluorescent that kicks in immediately power goes off and free standing lanterns. All four rooms have balconies with views of the pool and garden and have full en suite bathrooms..
