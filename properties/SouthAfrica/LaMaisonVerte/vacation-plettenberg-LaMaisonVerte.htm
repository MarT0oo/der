La Maison Verte, Knysna, South Africa


Providing an ideal getaway for couples, small families or friends traveling together, La Maison Verte oozes character and charm. This pretty eco-friendly cottage is situated in the quiet, safe suburb of Rexford and is just minutes from the lagoon, Knysna Heads, town centre and two golf courses.

Recently refurbished to a high standard, the accommodation is spacious yet cozy with polished wood floors throughout, luxurious bathrooms and distinctive French touches.

The entrance hall leads to an attractive open plan kitchen/diner and comfortable lounge area. The well-equipped kitchen includes a gas hob, electric oven, large fridge/freezer, a host of small appliances, ample storage space and dining table.

In addition to a sofa, armchairs, coffee table and wood burning stove, the lounge features a 32” flat-screen TV (with DSTV channels), DVD player and patio doors leading to a stylishly furnished covered deck and braai – perfect for outdoor dining and relaxation.

The first bedroom is located on the ground floor with patio doors opening on to the deck. In addition to a king bed (or twin beds if preferred), there’s a spacious ensuite shower room and toilet, hanging space, dressing table and ceiling fan. A washing machine is also located in the bathroom.

Stairs from the entrance hall lead to the stunning master bedroom. Occupying the whole of the first floor, it features a king/twin bedroom, additional sofa bed, large walk-in wardrobe, dressing table and electric heaters. Double doors lead out to a private deck with sweeping views over the Knysna lagoon, golf course, town and Outeniqua Mountains beyond. The huge, elegant bathroom includes ‘raindrop’ shower, separate toilet, ample under sink storage and heated towel rail.

There’s a good-size safe lawned garden to the rear of the cottage and parking for two vehicles in the paved driveway at the front.

As its name suggests, La Maison Verte has many eco-friendly features including solar geyser and rainwater storage tank. Only biodegradable products are used for cleaning.

Excellent facilities for sailing, kayaking, fishing, cycling, hiking, surfing, bird and whale-watching and game viewing. Several outstanding golf courses (including Pezula, Simola, Knysna, Plettenberg Bay, Goose Valley, George & Oubaai) are all within easy reach.

There is no dedicated Internet service for the house but there is a strong 3G signal and a dongle with 1gig data capacity will be provided to guests on arrival
