Sea House, Knysna Heads, South Africa


At the heart of the property is an impressive double-height lounge (seating 10 people) and dining area (seating 8 people) with attractive gas fireplace and solid wood floors. This leads to a beautifully presented and equipped kitchen with every possible amenity including dual fuel cooker, dishwasher and separate utility room with washing machine and dryer.

Each of the 4 bedrooms features a private patio or balcony. The 3 double rooms have stylish en-suite bathrooms, whilst the twin has a separate shower room. Additional features include under floor heating, driveway parking for up to 4 vehicles and double lock-up garage with electric doors. All linen, crockery, glassware and cookware are provided, along with pool towels. (Guests are asked to bring their own beach towels.)

Sea House is secure and safe and is surrounded by a walled garden laid to lawn and indigenous plants. It is perfect for large groups of friends or for families seeking a relaxing holiday in stunning surroundings.

Knysna enjoys a unique location in an area of unrivalled natural beauty and is the oyster capital of South Africa. Overlooking an 11km long lagoon, the town nestles between the majestic Outeniqua mountains and the Indian Ocean. Surrounded by deep forests and vast expanses of natural fynbos, Knysna is dominated by the Heads - the impressive sandstone cliffs at the mouth of the lagoon where Sea House is located. There are numerous fine restaurants and shops in the Knysna waterfront, quays and town centre, whilst the surrounding area offers excellent opportunities for sailing, cycling, hiking, surfing, bird and whale-watching and game viewing. Golfers will be in paradise as the superb Pezula and Simola courses are close by.
