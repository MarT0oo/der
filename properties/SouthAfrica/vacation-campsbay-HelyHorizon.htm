Hely Horizon, Camps Bay, South Africa Vacation Rental

 

Hely Horizon is perched high above Camps Bay with spectacular views of the beach below, the majestic Twelve Apostles mountain range and Lions Head above.
This villa is ideal for unwinding and relaxing, quiet and away from the hustle and bustle, yet close enough to take part should you so wish.
It is a short drive down to the main beach and the famous Camps Bay strip of restaurants and entertainment.
The city centre, V&A Waterfront, known for its world class shopping, and everything Cape Town has to offer is all within easy reach.
