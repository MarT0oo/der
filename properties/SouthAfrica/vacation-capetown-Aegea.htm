Aegea, Bantry Bay, Cape Town, South Africa Vacation Rental

 

Perched high above the waters of Bantry Bay with endless horizon views, this ultra-luxurious 5-bedroom retreat is the epitome of opulent seaside living. Spacious double-volume living spaces are stylishly furnished and the fully equipped kitchen and scullery is an entertainer’s dream.

The open-plan kitchen flows directly into the spacious living and dining area and a dumb-waiter connects the upper and lower levels of the house, perfect for entertaining. The dining table comfortably seats up to 8. The second level of the villa houses a bar and entertainment area with a 10-seater dining table and lounge with gas fireplace, all of which flow out onto a sheltered pool terrace with beautiful ocean views. Here guests can enjoy sun loungers, a gas barbeque, outdoor dining and a rim-flow pool with ocean views. Situated on the first level, the sea-facing master bedroom offers a spacious en-suite bathroom boasting an Amazon shower (three showerheads), bathtub and an Art Deco-styled vanity. Of the remaining four guest bedrooms, three are situated on the lower level with sea-facing and terrace views and one on the upper level with views of the Twelve Apostles mountain range. Stylish en-suite bathrooms accompany each of these rooms and all enjoy king-sized beds, air-conditioning, state-of-the-art entertainment systems and ample cupboard space.

The villa offers two double garages and off-street parking for two cars, is fully secure and has a dedicated housekeeper to ensure your every need is taken care of.

Just a short drive from Camps Bay and Sea Point, the villa is perfectly positioned to enjoy Cape Town’s cosmopolitan lifestyle
