KaapKloof Manor lays nestled on the backdrops of the Twelve Apostles Mountains, overlooking the Atlantic Ocean on top of Hout Bay, one of Cape Town’s most exclusive and tranquil suburbs. Hout Bay, as a quaint family and fisherman’s village, is an ideal and central starting point to discover the tremendous diversity of the Cape Peninsula region. Shops, markets, exquisite restaurants, the most beautiful natural beaches and the lively harbor are only a few minutes down the road.

Unfold your picnic blanket on lush meadows at Kirstenbosch Botanical Gardens (15 min.) or visit the fantastic wine estates on the Constantia Wine Route (10 min.). Drive towards the South, over the famous Chapmans Peak Drive (5 min.), passing Noordhoek with a breathtaking 8km long beach (20 min), Simons Town with their adorable penguin colony (30 min.) until you reach a point which feels like the end of the world: the Cape of Good Hope (90 min.). Up north lies Camps Bay’s chic beaches, Cape Town’s Waterfront and vivid city centre, which are easily accessible in only 20 minutes along one of the most beautiful coastlines Africa has to offer.

Some people spend a lifetime exploring these astonishing surrounds – start your journey at KaapKloof Manor, your home in Africa.
