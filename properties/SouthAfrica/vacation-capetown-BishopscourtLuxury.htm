Bishopscourt Luxury, Cape Town, South Africa Vacation Rental

 

Neighbouring Cape Town’s famous Kirstenbosch Botanical Gardens, Bishopscourt Luxury, in the sought after and prestigious suburb of Bishopscourt, is an exclusive luxury villa offering respite from the busy Cape Town city centre. Guests here are spoilt with views over the back of Table Mountain and the surrounding tree lined lush avenues. Bishopscourt Luxury is as exclusive as it gets, surrounded by a sprawling landscaped garden and greenery.

Bishopscourt Luxury, a large five-bedroom en-suite villa situated in the upmarket neighbourhood of Bishopscourt, is an ideal base for those guests that want to experience the natural sights of Cape Town nearby such as Constantia vineyards with its many restaurants, Kirstenbosch Botanical Gardens all with mere minutes of the villa.

Enjoy breakfast al fresco on the outdoor deck, overlook the glistening infinity swimming pool and landscaped garden or enjoy a friendly game of tennis on our dedicated courts. Dedicated members of staff are always on hand to assist with any requests, while elegant dining can be arranged for lunch or dinner on request.

“Located in a suburb that has seen the likes of Desmond Tutu and Nelson Mandela taking up residence, ‘Bishopscourt Luxury’ is one of my finest acquisitions with its spectacular mountain views and close proximity to the world famous Kirstenbosch Botanical Gardens.

For those who wish to relax within the comfort of this private mansion, the infinity pool and tennis court set in tranquil lush gardens make for ideal holiday activities.

However, I love the convenience of this property. It is the ultimate base from which to explore the many scenic attractions of Cape Town, with easy access to highlights such as Cape Point further to the south or the renowned V&A Waterfront adjacent to the city centre.

Cape Town is such a beautiful city and is surrounded by kilometres of coastline and I’m convinced you’d be amiss to visit and not sail the sea. This leads me to one of the X factors in staying at ‘Bishopscourt Luxury’ – the opportunity to cruise along the Atlantic Seaboard in my private 56 foot yacht called Ocean Princess. This nautical addition to a suburban stay will without doubt, be a highlight of any guest’s stay.

It’s opportunities like this that make the Bishopscourt villa the perfect choice for visitors who wish for a unique, tailor-made experience when visiting one of the world’s best cities”.

Each of the large five bedrooms ranging from 20 m² to 60 m² with full en-suite bathroom and offers the utmost in luxurious living including a king-sized bed dressed in lavish percale bed linen, satellite TV, air conditioning, heated towel rails and an electronic safe.

The bedrooms are split across two levels with two bedrooms situated upstairs, and three bedrooms on the lower levels. Each room is north facing and showcases a spectacular view of Table Mountain.

Facilities at Bishopscourt Luxury including a choice of two swimming pools, tennis court, a relaxed outdoor porch with sun loungers, a gas barbecue and elegant dining area, and lounge area with satellite TV.

Children are welcome at Bishopscourt Luxury as the residence is well suited for multi-generational families.

Villa rates are inclusive of daily housekeeping, dedicated House Manager and daily continental breakfast. Additional services such as butlers, private chefs, child minders on request and at an additional charge.

Please be aware that South Africa has introduced strict immigration regulations when it comes to travelling with children. Please ensure that you are carrying the correct documentation when travelling as a family.

If you are comfortable with driving on the left side of the road, hiring a car is a great option to get around in Cape Town, with many attractions and wineries nearby to Bishopscourt Luxury.

Bishopscourt Luxury is a 25min drive from the airport. Airport transfers are available on request.
