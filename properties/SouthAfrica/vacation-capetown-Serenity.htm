Serenity, Camps Bay, Cape Town, South Africa Vacation Rental


Being in the Cape, one of the world’s richest floral kingdoms, Serenity is grounded in its own little piece of virgin fynbos heaven. Untouched and unspoilt. Naturally beautiful in every way. A sun deck wraps around the perimeter of the villa, maximising views of the Twelve Apostles peaks, Table Mountain, Camps Bay beach and the painterly sunsets that the Cape’s Atlantic horizon is famous for.

Located in the cosmopolitan seaside village of Camps Bay, Serenity is an easy walk to Cape Town's best rated restaurants, vibrant beach bars and pristine palm-lined beaches. Camps Bay is internationally known for its startling natural beauty.

Serenity is situated 8kms from the waterfront and city centre, 1km from Camps Bay village and 90kms from the Cape of Good Hope.

A choice of suites provides capacity for ten to twelve guests to stay comfortably, with a cleaning service on hand Monday to Friday. Extensive entertainment facilities are an integral aspect of the villa, including an outdoor fireplace, jacuzzi, and L-shaped heated pool. A well equipped gym has stunning design features, such as a view directly into the inky jet black swimming pool beyond. Internet access is available throughout the villa and satellite connection on every widescreen TV.
