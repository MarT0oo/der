Beachscape, Plettenberg Bay, South Africa Vacation Rental

 

Beachscape enjoys a stunning location on the renowned Keurboomstrand, 5km east of Plettenberg Bay. Less than 100m from the pristine sandy beach and dunes, this ultra-modern property enjoys uninterrupted sea views with endless opportunities for dolphin and whale watching. The famous Robberg Peninsula and the ever-popular coastal town of Plett can been seen in the distance.

Accommodating up to 10 people in 5 luxuriously-appointed ensuite bedrooms, Beachscape has recently undergone extensive refurbishments and now comprises:

Secure entrance and adjacent private north facing courtyard laid with exotic wooden decking and comfortably furnished. The ground floor includes a large, beautifully appointed kitchen with stunning views across the beach and coastline, a scullery, dining area (with log burning stove) opening onto an exterior entertainment space with full braai.

The ground floor also features a large open plan library/relaxation area, and TV lounge, an entrance hall with cloakroom and steps down to a large double garage. All ground floor areas have fold and stack doors offering free-flow access to the decks, pool and entertainment areas.

There are 4 bedrooms on the 1st floor: a very spacious master bedroom with king size bed, dressing area and en-suite shower and bathroom. There are 2 further double bedrooms, both with en-suite shower rooms and queen size beds, plus an additional twin-bedded room [which can convert to a queen] with en-suite shower room and a large walk-on balcony. The 2 double rooms have shared access to a wide sun deck directly above the dining area and pool deck below.

A large separate 5th bedroom is located below the pool deck and features an en-suite shower and bathroom. This suite is served by stacking doors that open onto a private deck that, in turn, leads to a private walkway to the beach.

Outside there is a secluded walled courtyard to the front and a huge deck to the side and sea-facing aspect. The sea-facing decked area boasts a round fire pit – perfect for intimate moments or group relaxation. There is also a built in braai with a family size outdoor dining table. A well-proportioned family swimming pool and steps to the private walkway to the beach complete the picture.

The owners of Beachscape are committed to environmental sustainability. A rainwater storage facility and extensive use of solar energy for power and hot water are just some of the measures incorporated into this beautiful, state-of-the-art holiday home.
