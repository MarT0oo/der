About Mauritius

Mauritius Is a lovely location to visit if you are looking for an experience.  This location offers everything you could want and more, from the beautiful waterfalls to the lovely beaches and private villas throughout.  This Island Nation includes Mauritius, the islands of Cargados Carajos, Rodrigues, Agalega Islands, all part of the Mascarene Islands and  is the perfect escape for anyone.  Located off the SE Coast of Africa in the SW Indian Ocean and only 900 miles to Madagascar.  The lush and tropical setting offers the vacationer looking for an adventure everything under the sun.

Getting to Mauritius

Sir Seewoosagur Ramgoolam International Airport (MRU) - http://aml.mru.aero/

Be aware that Malaria is an endemic there and you may be required to have a variety of shots prior to visiting provide both blood and urine once on the island as a precaution.  Also note that the immigration process can be quite long, so be patient, you will be in line for a while and brining a book to read or some work to do may do you some good.
