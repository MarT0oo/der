Back to South Africa
Plettenberg Bay

Dubbed the Jewel of South Africa, it is a place where families gather to enjoy the splendor that is “Beach Living.”  With golden sands and turquoise beaches, there is a hint of excitement and fantastic beauty in the air, unlike anything you will ever experience in your life.  You are in the homeland, the birthplace of life, that in itself is a really magical feeling.  Yet there is so much more to expect, so much than you will get from any other Beach Destination.

So what to do in Plettenberg? 

The Keurbooms River and  beautiful tours you can take of the amazing landscape, beaches, and wildlife.  Professionally tour the rich forests that can make anyone feel like they are truly living the “adventure vacation.”  There is Birds of Eden http://www.birdsofeden.co.za/ with so many species of exotic birds and entertainment that you’ll surely have a great time.  There is Monkey Land http://www.monkeyland.co.za/ the first “Free Roaming” primate sanctuary, wonderful for kids and families.  There is the Elephant Sanctuary http://elephantsanctuary.co.za/ where you get to interact with these amazing animals.  There is Mitchell’s Knysna Brewery where you can tour the establishment and have a tasting.   

For those who want to do stuff outside, there is mountain biking,  bungy jumping, Abseil down the Kranshoek gorge, scuba diving, day trips to the Shamwari Game Reserve, and of course the wonderful shops and local dining areas.  As well try sailing, which is a great activity for families and friends alike. 

We offer Villas, Cottages, and beachfront rentals in Plettenberg bay.  From Beachyhead, to Robberg Beach, Keurbooms River Rentals, and all kinds of great Holiday Homes to select from.
