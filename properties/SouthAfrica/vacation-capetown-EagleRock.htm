Eagle Rock, Bantry Bay, Cape Town, South Africa Vacation Rental

 

Eagle Rock is an impressive retreat, erected around massive mountain boulders at the end of a private road in Bantry Bay. This exclusive villa, set in the most picturesque location in Cape Town, offers pure luxury living with breathtaking panoramic sea views of the Atlantic ocean. Every attention to detail has been given, using only superior finishes designed to ensure your every comfort has been accommodated. The villa offers large, harmonious spaces for pure holiday relaxation and sophisticated entertaining. Eagle Rock is a unique haven designed for the truly discerning traveller.

Bantry Bay is located on the coastal road between Sea Point and Clifton, centrally located for easy access to entertainment venues, world class shopping centres and restaurants.
