Romansbaai Villa, Cape Town, South Africa Vacation Rental

 

Romansbaai Villa offers tranquil estate living surrounded by a fynbos landscape with bird- and wildlife, walking trails and a 3km stretch of pristine, private beach. Designed with the well-heeled traveller in mind, Romansbaai Villa is appointed with the finest appliances, finishes and furniture. Come and enjoy serene and stylish living that is only a quick drive from city conveniences.

Romansbaai Villa is located within the Romansbaai Beach & Fynbos Estate near Danger Point in the Western Cape of South Africa. Romansbaai is a unique fynbos and beach estate near Danger Point and Gansbaai in the Overberg. Residents have direct access to a 3km stretch of private beach, wildlife, fynbos landscapes and breath-taking mountain and ocean views. Guests can drive to Romansbaai Villa with ease on tarred roads; the estate is a 5-minute drive from Gansbaai town centre, and a 2-hour drive from Cape Town.

Designed to optimize your interaction with the natural surroundings, Romansbaai Villa is situated on a plot of 2,300 square metres beautifully landscaped to blend in with the indigenous fynbos and beach schematic of the estate. The living areas and bedrooms boast splendid views over the fynbos landscape and the Atlantic Ocean.

Constructed over 3 levels, the villa comprises an open living and dining area that leads onto a hardwood deck with a heated swimming pool, a full kitchen perfectly appointed with high-end appliances, an enclosed entertainment veranda, and 6 bedrooms complete with high thread count linens and spacious en-suite bathrooms.

The living and dining area features an Italian fireplace, flat-screen satellite TV, wireless Bose sound system, and leads onto hardwood timber decking and a heated swimming pool. There is a DVD library and games for families to enjoy together on cold days spent cosied up in front of the log fire.

This holiday home is made for family getaways! With social spaces such as an enclosed entertainment veranda, an expansive terrace on the first floor with fantastic views, and a furnished courtyard – there is plenty of space for family activities, as well as quiet retreat for each individual.
