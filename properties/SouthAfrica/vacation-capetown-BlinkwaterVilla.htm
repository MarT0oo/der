Blinkwater Villa, Camps Bay, Cape Town, South Africa Vacation Rental

 

The 5-star graded Blinkwater is a spacious, luxurious and modern upmarket villa located in Cape Town's trendiest suburb, situated a few minutes walk from the main beach of Camps Bay, restaurants and nightspots of the strip.

This architect-designed house has amazing views of the bay, Lion’s Head and Table Mountain, nestled in the tranquil setting of a quiet greenbelt – the best of all worlds. The expansive entertainment area flows onto the deck with large pool. The modern kitchen is equipped with luxury appliances including a built-in Siemens coffee machine for guests’ enjoyment.

The villa has 4 double bedrooms sleeping 8 guests. All rooms are en-suite with individual satellite television and Fibre Optic Internet. All rooms lead to outdoors with indigenously planted terraces. Special attention to superb quality bed-linen and block-out blinds ensures a luxurious and comfortable stay. Secure garage parking for 4 cars with direct access to villa. Selected areas feature under-floor heating and air-conditioning. The villa is serviced daily and a private chef can be arranged on request.
