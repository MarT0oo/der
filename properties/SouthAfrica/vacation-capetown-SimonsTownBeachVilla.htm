Simon’s Town Beach Villa, Simons Town, Cape Town, South Africa Vacation Rental

 

Simon’s Town Beach Villa is located on Boulders Beach in the quaint village of Simons Town. This sheltered beach is made up of inlets between granite boulders and is home to a breeding colony of over 2000 endangered African Penguins that nest along the boardwalk at Simon’s Town Beach. Simon’s Town Beach Villa is a fully serviced vacation rental comprising of 4 en-suite bedrooms, a splendid balcony overlooking the ocean, and a generous living area that includes a modern kitchen, dining area and lounge. This private villa features a fireplace, underfloor heating, and Wi-Fi.

This sea facing Simons Town beach villa is conveniently located on the sheltered cove with a sandy beach and boardwalk with free-roaming colony of African penguins part of the Table Mountain National Park. One colony who have made a home directly in front of the villa. They can be seen sunning, preening and guarding their nests.

Boulders has become world famous for its thriving colony of African penguins and magnificent wind-sheltered beaches. Simons Town is part of the South Peninsula which is made up of Hout Bay, Chapman’s Peak Drive, Noordhoek, Kommetjie, Scarborough, Cape Point, Fish Hoek, Kalk Bay, St James and Muizenberg.

Enjoy the sights and sounds of the indigenous bird life and watch the whales as they complete their annual pilgrimage to the warm Indian Ocean waters of False Bay.

In the main house there are 3 spacious en-suite bedrooms. The first bedroom which is suitable for children and is fitted with three quartered beds. The second room is fitted with two single beds which is converted into a king size bed. The master suite is fitted with a king size bed and sea views of False Bay. The fourth bedroom is fitted with its own private kitchen and sitting room with a separate or private entrance.

The living area is open planned and spacious, featuring large, glass panels optimizing the ocean views, and leads out to a large open veranda with built in bbq.

Cape Town’s Simon’s Town Beach is ideal for kids as immense granite boulders shelter the cove from currents and large waves – but please always take care. Do not touch or feed the penguins. They might look cute and cuddly but their beaks are as sharp as razors and if they feel threatened they have no qualms about nipping the odd finger or nose.

The most popular recreational spot is Simon’s Town Beach, however the penguins can be best viewed from Foxy Beach, where boardwalks take visitors to within a few meters of the birds.
