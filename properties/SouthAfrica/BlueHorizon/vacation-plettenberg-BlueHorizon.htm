Blue Horizon, Pezula Golf Estate, Knysna, South Africa


Enjoying breathtaking views over the Indian Ocean, Blue Horizon is a luxurious residence offering the ultimate in comfort and relaxation. Newly built to the highest specification, the property occupies a spectacular clifftop location on an exclusive, secure development: Pezula Golf Estate (formerly Sparrebosch Estate). This estate holds the world famous Pezula Golf Course and is next to the Pezula hotel and Spa and Gym.

The house, in a private corner of the estate, looks out onto a secluded “private” beach which is accessible by steps starting from the house.

A large, single storey property built around a sheltered central courtyard, the open plan layout maximizes natural light to create a serene and restful ambiance. The master bedroom and living/dining areas open out to a long verandah overlooking on one side the beautiful shoreline of the Sparrebosch Nature Reserve (a 45-hectare Afromontane forest which is part of the Knysna Coastal Conservancy) and on the other side the beach and ocean beyond – the ideal spot for a sundowner or a morning of whale and dolphin watching.

Modern and elegant
The overall architecture is of a timeless, radically modern design.

Using quality finishes, furnishings and objects d’art throughout, Blue Horizon’s interiors exude elegance and simplicity. State-of-the-art kitchen appliances, audio-visual equipment and underfloor heating throughout, offer the last word in 21st century comfort.

Sleeping up to 8 people, Blue Horizon comprises 4 double bedrooms. The spacious master bedroom features patio doors leading to the verandah with fabulous sea views, king bed, dressing room and a superb bathroom with bath and shower. Bedrooms 2 & 3 feature queen beds, wardrobes and stylish ensuite shower rooms. Bedroom 4 overlooks the courtyard and has a kingsize bed (convertible into 2 single beds), fitted wardrobes and en-suite shower room.

Designed for entertaining
At the centre of the house is a stylish, fully-equipped kitchen with an island worktop unit with sink and storage space. There’s a stainless steel cooking range, extractor hood and large double-door fridge/freezer, microwave oven, kettle, toaster, etc. The utility room, located to the side of the kitchen, has a washing machine, dryer, dishwasher and an additional sink.

The adjacent dining and lounge areas are bright and spacious and lead seamlessly to the verandah by means of ‘fold & stack’ frameless glass doors. One end of this huge living space features top quality audio/visual equipment, including a large LCD TV (with DSTV), hi-fi system and DVD, comfortable couches, massive coffee table and pebble gas fire. At the opposite end is an additional seating area with couches and fireplace.

Mostly laid to lawn, the central courtyard offers an outside entertainment area with built-in duel-fuel braai (electric & gas) and a deck with an attractive driftwood dining table

In keeping with the objective to make life at Pezula Golf Estate as relaxing and carefree as possible, the Estate rules relate primarily to safety, security, privacy and protection of the environment. The elevation of the Estate affords it a great deal of natural security and privacy, which is reinforced by 24-hour permanent manned access control at all entrances. Home owners can walk, jog or cycle around the estate in safety. Children can play happily outdoors.


The world-renowned Pezula Resort Hotel & Spa adjacent to the Golf Estate development provides numerous 5-star amenities which residents have access to, including Zachary’s gourmet restaurant, a Champagne & Whisky Bar, Cigar Lounge, executive boardrooms, a boutique and a multi-award winning Spa & Gym with heated indoor pool, sauna, Jaccuzzi and steam room (Additional charges apply to some). Residents also have free access to the Pezula Golf Estate communal pool, tennis court and children’s outdoor playing area located in a quiet corner of the estate. The outdoor pool is surrounded by a sundeck and lounges. It gives a bird’s eye view on the majestic Knysna lagoon. Those amenities come with restrooms, shower facilities and also a waited bar during peak season.

Tenants also get 1 complementary free round of golf playing rights (1 person) on the Pezula Golf Course per 2-day rental
