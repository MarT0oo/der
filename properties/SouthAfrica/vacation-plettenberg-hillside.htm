Plettenberg Hillside Villa
5 Bedroom | 5 Ensuite Baths | Oceanview                                          
Plettenberg Bay, Garden Route, South Africa
       From R3500 - R10000 per night

A modern self catering villa situated in Harker Street on the hill overlooking Central Beach and the Beacon Isle in Plettenberg bay.

The house is within 5 minutes walk to the beach and 3 minutes to Plett's town centre, ideal for the guest who wants to park the car and be able to walk to the beach, restaurants and shops.

Come and soak up the sun and relax while taking in the beautiful Robberg Peninsula, frolicking whales or playful dolphins.

There are 5 en-suite bedrooms, sleeping 10, all with magnificent views of the beach and sea.

• Main bedroom/penthouse upstairs - king size bed, underfloor heating, heated towel rail.
• Bedrooms 2-4, queen size beds, bathrooms, underfloor heating, heated towel rails
• Bedroom 5, 2 x three quarter size beds, bathroom with underfloor heating plus heated towel rail.
• Downstairs has a TV area for the children and bar near the pool.
• Garaging for two cars.
• Kitchen and lounge, with plasma TV, are open plan with glass folding doors onto a large patio with braai.
• Beach towels are provided.
