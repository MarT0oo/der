16 on Nautica, Camps Bay, South Africa Vacation Rental

 

This stylish 3 bedroom apartment is located in a secure complex in Camps Bay and is also walking distance to a Clifton beach. The living areas are on the lower level of the apartment and boast stunning views.

As you enter the apartment, the fully equipped kitchen and breakfast bar are on your right. The open-plan living and dining room have comfortable seating and access to the patio with a splash pool.

All 3 bedrooms are on the upstairs level. The Master bedroom has beautiful views, an open-plan bathroom and access to a small shared balcony with the second bedroom.

The second and third bedrooms share a dedicated bathroom. This gorgeous apartment is the perfect seaside getaway to enjoy with family or friends.
