Apostles Edge, Camps Bay, Cape Town, South Africa Vacation Rental

 

This luxurious 6 bedroom villa is situated at the end of a quiet cul-de-sac on the mountain’s edge of Camps Bay. The entire villa is secured by an alarm system and electric fencing and offers ample parking for visitors and guests.

The property is structured across three levels, each with breathtaking mountain and ocean views.

The large double garage is on the top level and has a door which opens into the top level passage way. There are two master bedrooms on this level. The first master suite has floor to ceiling windows which boast uninterrupted views of the Twelve Apostles mountains and the sea. Both of these spacious rooms have open plan bathrooms with bath, shower and double vanity. The second master bedroom has access to a balcony.

On the second level of the property is the main entertainment area and one bedroom. The double size living room has seating for 12, a TV area. The open plan dining room on the left of the lounge has a large dining table and seating for 12. Further to the left is the spacious kitchen fitted with all the necessary amenities as well as a breakfast bar with seating for 6. This entire room boats beautiful views of both the mountains and ocean. The third bedroom is to the right of the living room and past the guest bathroom. This open plan bedroom and bathroom has a Juliette balcony, single vanity, bath and shower.

The lower level of the property has a welcoming bar with seating for 6 as well as a comfortable couch that seats 5. To the right of the bar is the fourth open plan bedroom with a double shower and vanity. It has floor to ceiling stacking doors which open onto the outside deck. The fifth and sixth bedrooms are to the left of the bar. They are both open plan. The fifth room has access to the main outdoor deck and the sixth room has a private deck and access to a separate entrance.

All the bedrooms in this grand property have a King sized bed, TV and air conditioning.

The outdoor deck has a built in braai as well as seating for 18 to 20 people and a dining table for 10. It is the perfect entertaining space. There is also a swimming pool and five sun loungers.
