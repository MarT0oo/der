Castle Residence, Plettenberg Bay, South Africa


This Private Castle on Noetzie Beach is situated on one of the most beautiful and secluded beaches in the world. Built into the rugged coastal cliff-face, it offers breathtaking panoramic views of the beach and the ocean and is the ultimate in ultra-luxury beach accommodation.

Guests of the Castle have every conceivable wish catered for by a team of hand-picked personnel including a resident chef, housekeeper and executive butler. The rate is fully inclusive of all meals, drinks, snacks and refreshments as well as selected activities such as horse riding, hiking, canoeing, tennis and use of the Field of Dreams golf practice range. Gourmet menus are created daily using the freshest available ingredients from the Resorts organic vegetable garden and local suppliers.

Guests have access to all the facilities of The Resort Hotel & Spa including helicopter excursions, golf at the Championship Course, luxurious spa treatments at the multi-award-winning Spa & Gym and fine dining at Zachary's gourmet restaurant, recently voted one of South Africa's Top 10 Deluxe eateries in the prestigious 2008 Dine Awards.
