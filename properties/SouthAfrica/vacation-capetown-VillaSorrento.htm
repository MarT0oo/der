Villa Sorrento, Cape Town, South Africa Vacation Rental

 

An exquisite five-bedroomed villa with a beautiful lap pool, stylish open plan living spaces, and uninterrupted sea views. Overlooking the Atlantic Ocean in the glamorous seaside suburb of Camps Bay, Villa Sorrento is the perfect retreat for discerning guests looking for luxurious levels of privacy and comfort. The villa is split over two levels, with one bedroom on the lowest level, spacious, airy living spaces and four bedrooms on the main level.

The property is entered on the lowest level, which features a sunny lounge with couches and flat screen television and one bedroom. The bedroom has a super-king bed and an en-suite bathroom with shower and bath, as well as air-conditioning and underfloor heating throughout. The second level houses the living spaces, kitchen and remaining four bedrooms. A contemporary-chic lounge with wood burning fireplace has comfortable couches and a large flat screen television connects effortlessly with the dining area, which features an eight-seater dining table.

A state-of-the-art kitchen is fully equipped with modern amenities, including two ovens, double fridge/freezer, microwave and coffee-machine. A separate scullery has a washing machine, tumble dryer, dishwasher, and ice-machine. The master bedroom commands breath-taking ocean views with a luxury vi-spring super king size bed, spacious en-suite bathroom with double shower, bath and twin vanities and a private balcony is the perfect place to unwind and soak up the views.

The remaining three guest bedrooms have super king-sized beds, en suite bathrooms, air-conditioning, and ample storage space. Each room has beautiful views of Lion’s Head, Table Mountain, or the vast Atlantic Ocean. Both the living and dining spaces open out onto a shady sea-facing wide terrace with a barbeque area and outdoor dining for eight, sun loungers, and a heated lap pool. The house is fully secure with an alarm, has a double garage and ample on-street parking for guests. Villa Sorrento is conveniently located in the heart of Camps Bay and a short drive will get you to the beautiful beaches of Camps Bay and Clifton, the V&A Waterfront and Table Mountain.
