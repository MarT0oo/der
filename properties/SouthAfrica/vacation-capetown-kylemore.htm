kylemore, Victoria & Alfred Waterfront, Cape Town, South Africa Vacation Rental

 

Furnished in a modern but classic way with unique and original artwork, the sophisticated Kylemore apartment has everything you need for the perfect stay for business, vacation or just a weekend getaway. The balcony is an excellent place to relax and enjoy the magnificent views of the Waterfront marina and canal. This luxurious self-catering apartment includes so many unexpected pleasures – underfloor heating, air conditioning are but a few of the exhaustive list of features, and offers the discerning traveller privacy and easy access to the best of what Cape Town has to offer.

Situated in the heart of Cape Town’s working harbor with the dramatic backdrop of the majestic Table Mountain, the Victoria & Alfred Waterfront is widely acknowledged as Cape Town’s premier shopping and tourist destination and a well-known entertainment hotspot. Abundant with upmarket shopping, excellent restaurants and breathtaking views, it is the ultimate base from which to see and do as much as possible in Cape Town. A ten minute drive will take you to the Cape’s most famous beaches and the city is within walking distance. Within walking distance are also a winery, live theatre, cinema and an amphitheatre featuring music concerts, drama and dance performances.
