Home by the Sea, Keurboomstrand, South Africa


Home by the Sea is a beach-lover’s dream. The property’s elevated position just 5km east of Plettenberg Bay offers uninterrupted sea views and is less than 100m from the Indian Ocean and the pristine beaches of Keurboomstrand where dolphins and whales can be viewed almost every day.

Cleverly designed to take maximum advantage of its stunning location and year-round sunshine, the house is the last word in elegant simplicity. From the huge frameless fold-back doors and free-flow living/dining areas, to the sparkling swimming pool and intimate and private walled courtyard, this property unquestionably has the ‘wow’ factor.

The furnishings and finishes are of the highest quality and the home entertainment system (including two LCD flat screen TVs, DVD and hi-fi) are state-of-the art, creating a luxuriously serene environment for those who want to be close to nature while enjoying every conceivable creature comfort

On the first floor there are four beautifully appointed bedrooms. The huge master bedroom affords stunning ocean views and features a king size bed, floor-to-ceiling fold-back doors and balcony. There’s a stylish en-suite bathroom with shower, bath and double-sink vanity unit, plus a dressing area with ample wardrobe and storage space.

Two further double bedrooms, both with en-suite shower rooms and verandah, also enjoy spectacular sea views. There’s an additional twin bedroom with en-suite shower that looks out north across the indigenous forest.

Climb a few more steps and you’ll reach a lounging area where you can escape from everything and enjoy views across the whole of Plettenberg Bay. Where better to watch the Dolphins and Whales, enjoy sundowners or just gaze at the African stars at night
