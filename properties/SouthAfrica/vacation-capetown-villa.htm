Cape Town Modern Villa
5 Bedroom | 5 Bathrooms | Pool Home                                        
Cape Town, South Africa

This extraordinarily beautiful home has magnificent views and a mountain stream running alongside providing a constant soothing melody.   It is a symphony of glass and stainless steel with travertine floors and African rose wood. There are 5 en suite bedrooms. A double volume formal living room is overlooked by a Romeo and Juliet balcony.

 

The outdoor area is a perfect entertaining spot with a BBQ and a sizeable infinity swimming pool. In the kitchen you have clinical stainless steel work surfaces, top of the range Siemens appliances and rosewood cabinets.

 

In addition to having everything you would find in a Resort setting, there are 9 balconies, 3 studies and 4 lock up garages, gym, home theatre, and emphasis on security with 360 CCTV, alarm systems, 2 Fire places, under floor heating, walk in Cold room, all bedrooms carpeted and top of range bathroom fittings, Phillipe Stark (Hans Grohe) Taps, Italian light fittings. The house is Ideal for entertainment with bar, wine cellar and pool table adjacent to Natural River. Plasma TV in the informal lounge with DVD, and Cable TV linked to Master Room and Cinema.
 
The Mansion is built on a TWO lots, half an acre which is very rare for Camps Bay. This Luxury Home is known to be one of the largest homes in Camps Bay. There is ample parking for 16 cars, level Lawns adjacent to home and pool.  There is also a Jungle Gym and swing for the Kids on the opposite side of the natural river. 2 Bridges, and is an ideal playground for kids. CCTV Cameras surround the property and can be viewed on every TV, including the Sound Proof Home cinema.

 

This Villa is perfect for people who want complete privacy and luxury at the same time.  Bring your friends and family and enjoy one of the best holidays you’ve ever imagined.
