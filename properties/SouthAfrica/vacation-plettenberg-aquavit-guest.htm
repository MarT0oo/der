Aquavit Guest House, Garden Route, Plettenberg Bay, South Africa Vacation Rental

 

Situated above "Bahia Formosa" (which means beautiful bay), Aquavit has panoramic views of the Tsitsikamma and Outeniqua Mountains and the coastal beaches of Plettenberg Bay, on the Indian Ocean. Aquavit is situated within minutes of the charming town of Plettenberg Bay, offering beautiful pristine beaches, great shopping and fine restaurants.

We welcome you to experience, firsthand, why Plettenberg Bay is called "the jewel of the garden route." Come see this magnificient country, where the people are kind and gracious. Enjoy the pleasant climate and beauty of the Indian Ocean. This is the perfect place for exploring, hiking, whale watching or just relaxing poolside in the sun while on your South African holiday.

Each of the five smoke free en-suite guestrooms, including one superior room, are individually decorated in soft warm earthy tones of stone, creams and crisp whites with personal attention to details. The contemporary design invites a mixture of coastal and antiques using only natural materials. The rooms are spacious and well appointed equipped with King, Queen and Twin sleeping accommodations.

Our guests enjoy a full English breakfast, served on the terrace, with magnificent views of mountain, sea, and sky. Attention to detail is important to us, so your breakfast will look as good as it tastes - a perfect start to your day!
