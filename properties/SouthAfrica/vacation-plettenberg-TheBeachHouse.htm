The Beach House, Plettenberg Bay, South Africa Vacation Rental

 

Take a short walk on a wooden boardwalk, through dune vegetation to pristine Robberg Beach. Relaxed beach décor allows families to kick off their shoes, unwind at the pool and enjoy all that Plett has to offer. The house sleeps 14 people in 3 separate areas so is ideal for families and friends to share!

 

Plett The Beach House has 2 Bedrooms (3 double en-suite; and 2 self-contained areas with 2 bedrooms each.) Each tastefully decorated in blue & white. Ideal for an extended family or 3 families sharing a house for the a holiday break.

The upper level has a sea-view and an open balcony for sun tanning and sundowners with lovely views of Robberg Beach.  An open Dining area leads into a fully equipped kitchen and laundry with granite surfaces. A sunny TV lounge with flat screen TV provides entertainment and relaxation. A quieter reading lounge leads off the dining area.

 

Live-in Manager - Maria Phillips - is available at all times to greet guests, supervise staff and take care of any special needs. She has separate accommodation on the property!  The house is serviced daily!  The town of Plettenberg Bay buzzes with many restaurants and novelty shops.
