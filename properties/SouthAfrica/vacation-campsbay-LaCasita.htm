La Casita, Camps Bay, South Africa Vacation Rental

 

Tucked away on a quiet street in the heart of Camps Bay, La Casita is a stylish 4 bedroom retreat offering views of both the mountain and the sea. This Villa is ideal for families or groups who want to explore all that the Mother City has to offer. The house is flooded with light in the afternoons, and you can spend your days lazing by the pool or dining al fresco on the terrace. This is the perfect place to enjoy the spectacular sunsets and relax after a long day in the sunshine

Split over two levels, with almost every room on the ground level opening out onto the terrace and pool to take advantage of the views. As you enter the property you will find the dining room, open plan kitchen and plush living room. This Villa has been designed for your enjoyment. The lounge offers ample comfortable seating, a gas fireplace and Full HD Smart TV. The dining room offers seating for 10 guests and the open plan kitchen is fully equipped with everything you could need including a Nespresso machine.

Further down the passage you will find the bedrooms, all of which have been beautifully designed and will ensure a relaxing stay for the whole group. Each. On the top level of the house is the loft room. This room has been converted into a fourth bedroom with twin beds.

Outside you will find a dining table for 8, outdoor couches as well as sun loungers. This house also offers Fibre internet, garage parking for 3 cars, security and housekeeping.

Enjoy its close proximity to Cape Town's best beaches, Camps Bay and Clifton, as well as numerous restaurants, bars and boutiques.
