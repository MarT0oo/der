Lookout Villa, Plettenberg Bay, South Africa Vacation Rental

 

Lookout Villa overlooks one of South Africa's most beautiful bays. Here you'll be spoilt with magical sunrises, pods of whales breaching in the winter months and dolphins swimming by throughout the year. This family friendly three-bedroom villa marries contemporary African design with an easy-on-the-eye seaside palette.

Picture soft, sandy and ocean colours complimented by a mixture of textures and lighting that gives The Lookout Villa a warm and distinctly relaxing atmosphere.

This spacious villa boasts an open plan lounge and dining room, a well-stocked galley kitchen, access to a private swimming pool and garden, air-conditioning throughout, flat screen televisions, satellite TV, wireless Internet access, DVD players, full service, parking and all other modern conveniences.
