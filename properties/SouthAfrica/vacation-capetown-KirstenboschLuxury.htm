Kirstenbosch Luxury, Cape Town, South Africa Vacation Rental

 

Kirstenbosch Luxury is situated in the Cape's small, affluent and lush, residential suburb, Bishopscourt, approximately 20 minutes from the Center of Cape Town, which neighbors Constantia which is home to some of the oldest Vineyards in Cape Town and is less than 50m from the Kirstenbosch Botanical Gardens entrance. It sits high above on the highest hills of Bishopscourt which enjoys some of the best views offered in this region and gives you the sense that you are almost in the clouds.

This 6 bedroom villa with its exquisitely tended gardens which is serviced by a borehole, overlooks the mountain and ocean and enjoys various generous reception rooms and entertainment areas and pool room. Kirstenbosch Luxury Villa, offered as an exclusive use only in the heart of the Constantia’s wine region and Kirstenbosch Gardens is a decadently indulgent yet informal for the perfect family vacation villa.

Kirstenbosch Luxury accommodates up to twelve guests in its six luxurious suites and private bathrooms. Four bedrooms upstairs consisting of the Master Suite, Double Ensuite and two smaller Children’s Rooms all with sweeping views over the mountain and Kirstenbosch Gardens with two bedrooms on the ground level. Each room in Kirstenbosch Luxury has been tastefully decorated with a modern yet classic interiors. Its several generous lounge areas, inside and outside, allow for various options to enjoy its elegant interiors and it lush outdoors.

Its Pool Room and entertainment area is equipped with BBQ, glass stackable doors which open up to the garden and pool area, bar and kitchen counter, TV room and indoor wood burning fireplace which make for many fun nights and is the heart of the villa.

Should you need to stay connected at work, a working office is available.

Kirstenbosch Luxury is ideal for families with children of all ages, with children sleeping rooms, playroom and jungle gym in the gardens. The swimming pool is fitted with an electric cover for safety.
