Planning a trip for a large group? Need enough in-house entertainment to please everyone? Want luxury and opulence at every turn? Desire privacy and spaciousness even with a large group? Well, you are asking the right questions and there is only one right answer—“Serenity Mountain Pool Lodge!

Situated in the Great Smoky Mountains, this luxury Pigeon Forge cabin is the perfect vacation solution for any type of large group—whether a family reunion, a church retreat or any other opportunity to gather up great friends and have a little fun!

With luxury accommodations for up to 52 people, this nine bedroom cabin offers space and privacy while also providing ample sleeping room. The two master suites feature 55” flat screen TVs, private balconies, private baths, Jacuzzi tubs and a sit-in shower! The other bedrooms are certainly not shabby either, with amenities such as 43” flat screen TVs and private baths.

Now that everyone is assured a restful night’s sleep, you can check out all the REALLY cool things about this cabin. “Serenity Mountain Pool Lodge” features bedside fireplaces, two massively oversized hot tubs, an indoor heated pool (pool temperature set at 76 degrees) and a home theater system with surround sound and theater seating. Think that sounds impressive? Well, there’s more….much more!

The young and young at heart will be thrilled with the game room on the lower level that includes a pool table, nine stand-up arcade games, two sit-down arcade games and even a four-kid carousel! Once you pull them away from the game room, you can entice them outside with the lure of the country porch rockers and the porch swings.

Whether you stay in and prepare a fabulous dinner in the fully-equipped kitchen or check out one of the many fabulous restaurants in Pigeon Forge or Gatlinburg, you will enjoy both the food and the company. You can then have coffee together and recap the day in front of the stone fireplace in the great room.
