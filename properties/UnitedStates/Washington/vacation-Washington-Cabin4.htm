Cabin 4, Mt. Baker, Maple Falls, Washington Vacation Rental

 

This brand new 2-story 1-bedroom 1-bath cozy cedar cabin is the perfect couples retreat! The 1st floor also includes a fully equipped kitchen, and a 3/4-bath with a laundry area including a stack washer/dryer. The entire 2nd floor is a large loft sleeping area with 1-queen bed and 1-twin bed, which is great for the kids! Just steps away from the cabin is a gazebo covered HOT TUB and a gas grill! This pet-friendly vacation property will sleep 4 to 5-persons
