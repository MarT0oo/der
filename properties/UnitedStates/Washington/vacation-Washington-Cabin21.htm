Cabin 21, Mt. Baker, Maple Falls, Washington Vacation Rental

 

Your family will have a delightful time in this 2-story, 3-bedroom, 2-bath log cabin with a natural pine interior.

The first floor features a large, open and airy, kitchen/dining/living area with vaulted ceilings, complimented by a wood stove, (firewood provided) and a breakfast bar. There is one bedroom with one queen bed, a second bedroom with one set of bunks, and one full bath augmented by a laundry cubby with a full-size washer/dryer. The second floor features a loft sitting area, a master bedroom with one queen sized bed and an adjoining full bath. Exterior features include a wrap-around deck with a gas grill, and an enclosed summer porch for spending lazy afternoons.

This pet-friendly vacation property will sleep up to 6 - 8 persons .
