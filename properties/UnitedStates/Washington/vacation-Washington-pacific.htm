Pacific Beachfront, Washington, USA Vacation Rental


Pacific Beachfront is an ocean view property located in Seabrook, Washington which sleeps up to 14 people.  Relax in our outdoor hot tub or cook out on the charcoal grill while enjoying the ocean views.

There are two dwellings located on this property. The main house features a master bedroom with a king size bed and full bathroom, one bedroom with two twin beds and full bathroom, a second bedroom that features a double bed, a sleeper sofa in the living room, and a large game room with two twin beds and a full bathroom.  The carriage house features a bedroom with a queen bed and a second bedroom with two twin beds, a full bathroom, and a kitchenette.


All bedrooms include TVs with cable, embroidered spa robes, fine all cotton sheeting, 2 100% Down + 2 Poly fill pillows per room, downy soft featherbeds, and a library of books.

Our game room is located on the entire third floor of the main house with digital HDTV cable, PS3 with games, Blu-ray player with a large Blu-ray and DVD library, a foosball table, board games, puzzles, a child's play kitchen, toys, books, and more.

For your comfort the home features antique Fir hardwood floors, central air, full size washer and dryer on bedroom level, one garage parking space and one off street space.  The kitchen is well-outfitted with dishwasher, microwave, stove, oven, refrigerator and freezer, pots and pans, dishes, utensils, Henckels knives, and much more.

Seabrook is located on the west coast of the United States in Washington State. From Seattle, go 27 miles South and 85 miles West (to the coast) and you are in Seabrook.
