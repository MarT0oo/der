Waikiki Diamond head Modern, Waikiki, Oahu, Hawaii Vacation Rental

 

This is a high end, modern, relaxed home situated at the base of world famous Diamond Head and walking distance to Waikiki. With 5,000 square feet, this spacious home offers vacation guests 5 bedroom suites, each with its own bathroom, a very spacious living room, kitchen, and dining room, and an office space and half bath.

Outdoor space includes a refreshing saltwater pool and hot tub, landscaped backyard with fruitful mango tree, two large outdoor lanais with incredible views of Diamond Head, city skyline, and Kapiolani Park.

This property is arguably the best location on Oahu just a block away from Kapiolani Park with beautiful city skyline views. It is a few minutes walk from beautiful white sandy beaches, Honolulu Zoo, Waikiki Aquarium, and all the excitement of bustling Waikiki.
