Travel to Hawaii

Oahu is the 3rd largest island in the Hawaiian Archipelago  596.7 square miles, the name meaning “gathering place.”  The area is one visited often for the wonderful sandy beaches, such as Kailua Beach.  Oahu is also home to the Capital of Honolulu where you can find many wonderful shops and restaurants, and lovely historical sites such as the  Iolani Palace, the Kawaiahao Church, the Mission , Bishop Museum , Aloha Tower, King Kamehameha I Statue, the Duke Kahanamoku Statue and the historic Hawaii Theater. 

In addition to being a lovely island to vacation on, it’s also home to Pearl Harbor in Central Oahu, about 30 minutes from Waikiki, draw people in from the mainland every year in order to remember the 1941 events that changed America.  There are also 7 miles of Pristine Beaches that attract surfers worldwide to beaches like Kawela Bay, Sunset Beach, Waimea Bay famed for its Surf, Waimanalo Beach, and so much more.  To see a full list of beaches please go to http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Oahu.

There is no lack of Vacation Rentals, Cottages, and Mansions in Oahu.
