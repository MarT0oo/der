Chill Out, Snowmass, Aspen, Colorado Vacation Rental


The Chill Out is part of our Snowmass luxury property rental collection. This magnificently decorated home is located 250 yards from Adams Avenue, providing indirect ski access to Snowmass Mountain. This 4 bedroom Snowmass luxury property is entirely decorated in an Alpine castle style with rich colors and fabrics, beautiful woodwork and furniture, enticing you to stay home and relax after a day playing in the mountains. Take full advantage too, of the two decks, outdoor private hot tub and unbeatable views this house has to offer.

Luxury 4 bedroom /office/4.5 bath home, Indirect ski access: 250 yards to Adams Avenue.  The first King bedroom with private bath, Jacuzzi tub, gas fireplace, 27" TV.  The second King bedroom with private bath, Jacuzzi tub, 27" TV.  The third Queen bedroom with private bath, 27" TV/DVD.  The forth has two sets of bunk beds with private bath, 27" TV/DVD.  We also have an office with queen sleeper sofa, 27" TV, High Speed Internet Access, and Fax machine.

There is also a 32" TV/DVD in the main living room, 15" LCD screen TV in the kitchen.  The house features spectacular views, two large decks and two car garage.
