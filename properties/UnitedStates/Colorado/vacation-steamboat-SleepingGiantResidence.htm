Sleeping Giant Residence, Steamboat Springs, Colorado Vacation Rental

 

The Sleeping Giant Residence at One Steamboat Place is your home-away-from-home located right at the Gondola with unrivaled ski-in/ski-out access. Beautifully appointed you will feel right at home after a day of adventures on the mountain.

The slope side Private Residences at One Steamboat Place are located at the Gondola and offer ski-in/ski-out ease.

The Sleeping Giant Residence features a large living area with a wonderful south-valley view. The spacious open floor plan connects the dining and living areas to the kitchen with plenty of room for a group to relax together. State of the art a/v equipment provides hi-definition entertainment after a full day of activity.

The professionally equipped kitchen offers Viking appliances and outdoor grilling is a cinch at the built-in Viking gas barbecue on the deck.

The adjoining dining area features a handcrafted dining table that beautifully complements the fine craftsmanship of the contemporary interior in this residence.
