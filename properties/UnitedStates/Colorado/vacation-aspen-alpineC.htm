Alpine Cottage C, Aspen, Colorado Vacation Rental


The Alpine Cottage C is an Aspen luxury 4 bedroom rental. Located 5 blocks from downtown Aspen and a block from the free shuttle, it offers great deluxe amenities, such as a gourmet kitchen, media room, and enough space to accommodate large groups and/or families. Book this deluxe home now and have a great, relaxing time during your next Aspen vacation!

Luxury 4 bedroom plus media room 4.5 bath home located on the east side of town off Alpine Ct just five blocks from Downtown Aspen.  One block from free skier shuttle making easy access to town & slopes.  The Gourmet Kitchen with granite counters, breakfast bar, Stainless Steel Sub Zero fridge and appliances, and thermador stove.  The Formal Dining room is wonderful for those who want to spend time with family and friends.  There are hardwood floors throughout, living area features gas fireplace and leather sofas. 

Outside you have a Small outdoor deck with gas grill, there is a garage with parking for one vehicle for your convenience.  The Master bedroom features a King bed, 36" TV, gas fireplace, private deck, large walk in closet, private bath with Jacuzzi tub and Steam shower.  The second Guest Master bedroom features a Queen bed, 24" TV, gas fireplace, private bath with Jacuzzi tub and Steam shower.  The third Guest bedroom features a Double bed with private bath (shower only).  The fourth Guest bedroom features a bunk bed (double, single), and two trundle bed. This room comfortably sleeps seven children and has a private bath (shower only). 

As well the house features a media room with 42" projection screen television, pool table, wet bar, and gas fireplace.
