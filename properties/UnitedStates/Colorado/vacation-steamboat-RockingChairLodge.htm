Rocking Chair Lodge, Steamboat Springs, Colorado Vacation Rental

 

Guests at Rocking Chair Lodge will love this enormous 6 bedroom home located at The Porches, a luxury home community at the gateway to the Steamboat Ski Area. With 10,400 finished square feet this home is an amazing opportunity for a multi-generational family or corporate group to spend time together. The custom finishes on this spectacular home are top of the line throughout, including beautiful wood and stone floors, floor to ceiling tile in all bathrooms, and four rock surround gas fireplaces with custom iron work. The large great room is open to the floor above with exposed wood beams creating an expansive and inviting space.

Your group can relax on the over-sized couches while enjoying the fire or tv. If it’s warm, enjoy a rocking chair sitting out on the attached deck in front of the outdoor fireplace and drink in the mountain views.

The professional kitchen is any chef’s dream. It is equipped with SubZero double refrigerators, freezer drawers, ice maker, wine and beverage refrigerators. The AGA gas stove consists of 6 ovens, 4 gas burners, 2 hot plates and a warming plate. To finish out the chef’s dream, there are 2 Asko dishwashers, a built in Miele coffee maker, convection oven/microwave, Vent-a-Hood hood, copper farm sinks and a double island made of stunning banded iron stone. The outside island has seating for 4 people with a large tv overhead. For indulging in the food your chef has put together, there is a stunning 12 person granite table for everyone to sit around.

It’s the perfect place to gather and share the adventures of the day. The master suite on the main floor features a one of a kind, custom, ninety sq ft bed, rock fireplace, and access to the front patio. The spacious and luxurious master bath consists of a waterfall/multi-jet/steam shower, a towel warmer and large two person jetted bath tub that will welcome you after a winter day on the slopes. The upper level of the home has 5 additional bedroom suites. The bedrooms consist of a king room with full kitchen and access to the upper deck with hot tub.

There is a bunk room with 3 twin and 3 full beds and a bedroom with a queen bed. The final room is a second master with king bed. All bedrooms have their own beautifully tiled bathrooms. The lower level of this home is where entertaining is at its best. There is a media room with large movie screen and reclining theater seats for enjoying a movie night. For your refreshments there is a full wet bar, with beverage refrigerator and freezer. There is a pool table and fireplace, next to the bar. In the next room over, you’ll find a ping pong table and foosball table. If that isn’t enough entertainment, there is also a 14 ft tall climbing wall, play room and large open conference or game room. There are 3 half baths through the lower level. This home boasts the largest deck at The Porches with an outdoor fireplace and magnificent view of the ski mountain.

The Porches amenities are one of the biggest attractions to this neighborhood and include a full-service concierge, 6000 ft² "Barn" with heated outdoor pool and hot tubs, outdoor BBQ deck & fire pit, fitness center, kid's play room, business center, and great room.

Ski Access is easy with on-call shuttle service to the ski mountain and downtown district at night for restaurants and shopping. There is a ski valet at the base of the mountain so you won’t have to bring your skis back and forth. The largest home in The Porches will amaze and please anyone who stays here.
