Vail Penthouse, Vail, Colorado Vacation Rental

 

Perched six floors above Vail Square and claiming an entire wing of the exclusive Arrabelle Resort, this 4,000 square foot, five-bedroom chalet is a vision of alpine design. In the great room’s distinctive octagon sitting area, a moss stone fireplace, rich hardwood accents, original art, marble coffee table, and slate-blue mohair upholstery are illuminated by walls of windows that provide a king’s view of the mountain and the European-inspired square below.

Step onto the 280 degree balcony that hugs the great room and survey skiers swooshing down the ski runs, ice skaters circling the rink, and the Arrabelle’s Glockenspiel clock marking the slow and pleasant passage of time—all while tucked cozily in a blanket with a steaming latte warming your hands

This Penthouse is located at an ideal ski-in, ski-out location at the base of the gondola, and just a ten-minute walk from Vail Village’s five-star restaurants and elite shopping. Not to mention your access to Arrabelle’s lavish amenities like a rooftop hot tub, lap pool and spa services—amenities that are yours in addition to the solitude and quiet provided by a private residence.

Bronze Master Suite: One of two master suites, the Bronze Master presents a warm glow with its corner fireplace, walls coated in a Venetian bronze plaster finish, and custom bedding and upholstery in shimmering hues of gold and alabaster. From the Bronze Master, watch skiers on the slopes or access the wrap-around balcony right from your room.

Mornings in this suite are indulgent, thanks to the attached marble bath with Jacuzzi soaking tub, steam shower, and TV for watching the day’s ski report while you get ready.

Two Queens Suite: Two queens, one castle. Enjoy this double-occupancy suite with Vail Square views, attached bath, antique trunk-style dresser, and two queen-sized beds that include all the trimmings, plus leather headboards to lean against when you settle in with a book.

Vail Square King Suite: The Vail Square King Suite offers an en suite bathroom, king-sized bed with a custom leather headboard, classic and beautiful lighting fixtures, and a plush sitting area. Open the curtains, settle into the roomy chair, stretch your feet onto the leather ottoman, and take in the royal views of Vail Square’s European architecture and the Glockenspiel.

Turret Master Suite: The Turret Master Suite is the ultimate indulgence. It entices with its craftsman stone fireplace, king-sized bed with cream leather headboard, and grand windows that ensure the Vail Square and ski mountain will be the first sights to greet you in the morning.

A turret sitting area surrounds you in even more glass for taking in Vail’s beauty. But the view doesn’t stop there: a five-piece marble bathroom offers another vantage point, plus spa-like comforts like a Jacuzzi soaking tub and a marble vanity.

Denim and Wool Bunkroom: The bunkroom is a kid favorite—the perfect place to hide away and slip into dreams of one day winning Olympic gold on the slopes. The bunkroom is conveniently adjacent to the laidback, Secondary Family Room, with its kitchenette, large flat screen, plush sectional and fun games—like a Nintendo Wii—for the whole family.

Fully Equipped Chef-Style Kitchen: If you have a culinary need or want, it likely have the tools to create it. Equipped for the master chef, our kitchen shines with Viking stainless steal appliances and a plethora of small appliances, including a Nespresso machine. The cabinets were custom-painted in light gray tones and accented with moss rock—the perfect complement to the white marble counters. Custom, hand-crafted glass pennants are suspended over four barstools—a place to gather for casual breakfast or a glass of wine while the chef works their magic.

Great Room: Our fully stocked liquor cabinet and wine rack provide all the ingredients for a relaxing conversation in the great room’s stunning octagon sitting area, which is lavishly furnished with two mohair sofas draped in velvety faux fur blankets.. A state-of-the-art Savant entertainment system provides Netflix, Hulu and Pandora as a backdrop while you entertain.

The formal dining area seats eight and overlooks the ski mountain. Adorned with original art and antiques, including a French Regency buffet with vintage stone top and authentic Trumeau plaster mirror with original artwork, the dining area is a fitting backdrop for a memorable meal.

French doors open to the wrap-around terrace with bistro table—a perfect perch for an espresso while people-watching—and an outdoor cooking area with gas grill.

About The Area

Home to the iconic Rocky Mountains, it’s no wonder that Colorado is the number one destination for ski trips in the United States. With 21 first-class ski resorts scattered throughout the Rockies, ski and snowboard enthusiasts travel far and wide each winter to experience the mountains firsthand. Colorado is truly a year-round destination, however, with renowned dining, shopping, and golfing bringing in tourists long after the ski season has ended.

Featuring several world-class ski resorts, such as Vail, Aspen and Beaver Creek, Colorado is firmly grounded as one of the most sought after luxury vacation spots for affluent travelers. Given a plethora of luxury mountain cottages, villas, chalets and rental homes, it’s no surprise Colorado is often host to celebrities and royalty.
