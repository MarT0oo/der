Crystal Peak Lodge, Breckenridge, Colorado Vacation Rental


Luxury surrounds you at Crystal Peak Lodge #7501. Come and visit the ultimate Breckenridge vacation getaway, with true ski in and out access and ski area views you have to see to believe. Crystal peak lodge is located at the base of peak 7, just steps from the Independence super chair and the breck connect gondola, and the true ski in and out that is waiting for you. You can take the gondola into town from your front door, or the free shuttle is at your beckoned call, if you just don’t feel like dragging your car out of the heated garage. Convenience is a phrase many promise, but only Crystal Peak Lodge will deliver.

Once finished taking in the beauty of Breckenridge, you can enter into another dimension of total luxury once you step into the property. After your foot passes over the threshold high ceilings and ornate décor surround you. Entering the oversized main room you will be greeted with a chef’s dream kitchen outfitted with Viking appliances, granite countertops, and plenty of accessories to prepare gourmet meals. Just off the kitchen, past the granite bar and stools, is the dining area with plenty of seating for the while family. Adjacent to the dining area is the lush and expansive living area with a flat screen LCD TV and pillowy seating. Leading out from the living area is the sprawling private deck overlooking the majesty of the Breckenridge ski area, right under your chin.

Sleeping has never been as inviting, with 3 master suites, everyone has the best bedroom. Two bedrooms are furnished with king sized beds, with a queen in the 3rd and twins in the 4th, and private washrooms for the ultimate in spacious comfort and discreet privacy. Every bedroom has a spectacular view of the wonder Breckenridge offers, from Baldy Mountain to the ski area. No expense has been spared or corner cut in any of the master suites, you will enjoy the richest linens, thickest mattresses, coziest bedding, roomiest surroundings, and best night’s sleep in Breckenridge money can buy.

 

To fully accentuate your bountiful Breckenridge vacation the lodge and your hosts offers plenty of amenities to choose from that will enhance the luxury of your vacation. Experience the onsite hot tubs, fitness center, steam room, recreation areas, and cozy fire pits. You can also order in a myriad of services including massage, full spa treatments, private chef, catered meals, and even ski rentals.
