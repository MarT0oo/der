1038 A Eagle Drive, Beaver Creek, Colorado Vacation Rental

 

Views delight in this stunning 7 bedroom property nestled above the Eagle-Vail golf course between Vail and Beaver Creek. Chef’s kitchen, game room, and multiple hot tubs only add to the abundant features in this luxurious residence.

Some of the most incredible valley views greet guests of this stunning 5,400 square foot property atop Whiskey Hill in Eagle-Vail between Vail and Beaver Creek. The main entrance to the home opens up to a spacious great room and an incredible chef kitchen not only featuring a wolf range, in-counter blender, and ice maker, but the formal dining table is ready for your next dinner party with seating for 12 guests, plus additional seating at the breakfast bar for 6.

The residence features a master suite with en suite bath, fireplace, office, and private outdoor hot tub on the top level.

One floor below the kitchen level guests will find 4 guest bedrooms. One houses a king bed, kitchenette, and en suite bath, the remaining three share two hall baths and are comprised of a queen, two twins, and the last bedroom a twin bunk bed + additional twin bed.

Through two glass double doors, on the the main floor guest will find a large game room with Mrs. Pac-Man arcade game, shuffle board, billiards table, theater-size TV screen, wet bar, and wine fridge. Balcony access to the second hot tub can be found here along with comfortable outdoor gathering and dining spaces. Two additional guest bedrooms are also located off of the game room area. One features a king bed with fireplace and en suite bath, and the other a queen bed.

Guests have the choice to spend their day at either Vail or Beaver Creek as the property lies half way between both resorts. Vail is 8.5 miles away and Beaver Creek is 5.5 miles away. In the summer months, guests staying at the home have complementary access to the Eagle-Vail community pool. The pool is located a little more than half a mile from the home, only about a two minute drive. This salt water pool is open daily from 10AM to 8PM. Hang out on the grassy lawn and use their BBQ grills while taking a break from swimming laps!

About The Area

Home to the iconic Rocky Mountains, it’s no wonder that Colorado is the number one destination for ski trips in the United States. With 21 first-class ski resorts scattered throughout the Rockies, ski and snowboard enthusiasts travel far and wide each winter to experience the mountains firsthand. Colorado is truly a year-round destination, however, with renowned dining, shopping, and golfing bringing in tourists long after the ski season has ended.

Featuring several world-class ski resorts, such as Vail, Aspen and Beaver Creek, Colorado is firmly grounded as one of the most sought after luxury vacation spots for affluent travelers. Given a plethora of luxury mountain cottages, villas, chalets and rental homes, it’s no surprise Colorado is often host to celebrities and royalty.
