Sly Fox Chalet, Steamboat Springs, Colorado Vacation Rental


Sly Fox Chalet is a luxury 4 bed, 4.5 bath Steamboat vacation home sleeps 10 and offers five-star accommodation in a private neighborhood near the base of the mountain area.

The great room is warm and inviting with vaulted ceilings and large windows through which guests will enjoy a spectacular view of the entire Steamboat Ski Area as they enjoy the gas fireplace. The spacious open floor plan layout connects kitchen dining and living spaces so that the whole group can relax in comfort together. The dining room table seats 8 with room for 4 at the bar. Granite countertops including a large island will make any aspiring chef happy along with the GE appliances, a 5-burner gas range and a wine refrigerator. Also on the main level is a rec. room complete with pool table, card table and its own flat screen TV.

The queen bedroom suite is located on the main floor with a private bathroom. Upstairs each bedroom features a private bathroom. The master suite bathroom come equipped with a Jacuzzi tub and a walk-in shower. The master suite also features a gas fire place, flat screen TV and a covered balcony with a panoramic view of the Steamboat Ski Area. On the ground level enjoy the same view of the ski mountain from the hot tub on the covered patio.
