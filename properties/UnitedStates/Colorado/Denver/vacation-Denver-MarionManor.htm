Marion Manor, Denver, Colorado Vacation Rental

 

Discover true luxury minutes from downtown in Cherry Creek North at Marion Manor Denver, a stunning 1887 Queen Anne mansion. We offer an unparalleled level of service to our guests: if you need a personal chef, driver or airport transfer, in-home massage, personal trainer, grocery delivery or restaurant booking, we are on call any time. We offer daily tidy-up service.

This 7,700+ square foot updated estate is filled with antiques, marble, wood floors, and designer decor. It is located in the prestigious and historic Denver Country Club neighborhood.

Slip into the outdoor hot tub for a soothing soak or swim laps in the year-round heated outdoor pool. Have a quiet family BBQ or sunbathe in the relaxing new outdoor furnishings.

Taking up 4 rooms of the estate, the home gym includes free weights, kettle bells, TRX, Bosu, treadmill, stationary bike, elliptical, and numerous Cybex and LifeFitness machines for body building and much more.

Six bedrooms including two immaculate master suites accommodate up to twelve fortunate guests (16 adult guests possible including 4 on air mattresses, and up to 4 additional children will fit). This is perfect for guests seeking perhaps the ultimate in mile high luxury.

Steeped in history, this home was designated a landmark in 1997. Browse old letters, photographs and other historical documents from the original owners. Cozy up to the grand fireplace, or enjoy reading a good book in the historic reading room next to the grand player piano.

The spacious kitchen is a chef’s dream with a Diva de Provence gas range, stainless steel appliances, large skylights, a Thermador refrigerator, a long center island, and a butler's pantry.

Cherry Creek North is just a short 12 minute walk to experience luxury shopping and fine dining. A 5-10 minute drive will get you to downtown Denver and nearly all of the city's top-rated tourist attractions.

Dogs are a part of the family, and are not only welcomed but encouraged at Marion Manor (no fee).
