Webb Residence, Aspen, Colorado Vacation Rental


Spend your winter ski vacation in this deluxe four bedroom victorian style town home; enjoy the proximity of downtown and Aspen Mountain, as well as the comfort of this Aspen deluxe Town home. The house features a den with Queen sofa sleeper and entertainment system, Master Bedroom with King bed and private bath with Jacuzzi tub, 2nd Bedroom with Queen bed, 3rd Bedroom with 2 twin beds and 4th Bedroom with 2 twin beds.
