Beaver Dam, Vail Village, Colorado Vacation Rental

 

This luxurious home is ideal for extended families and friends. Indulge in a glass of wine or a cup of coffee in one of the many outdoor living spaces or relax your ski legs in one of the two private, outdoor hot tubs. Custom stonework, wood finishes, and fine fixtures ensure a unique stay that is sure to impress.

Located 1 block East of the Eagle Bahn Gondola and the Born Free lift, this home offers a quiet, private location for skiing, mountain biking, hiking and year round enjoyment.

If you've ever seen a picture of Vail, you've likely caught a glimpse of the iconic pedestrian village. Many of its buildings were originally built by Vail's founders in the early 60s with a nod to classic European alpine architecture. Vail Village retains a peaceful, small-town ambiance even with its world-class restaurants, shopping, cafes and late-night hot spots. The heated streets make for an easy walk up Bridge Street to Gondola One. The dining scene is amazingly diverse and one of Vail's main attractions, with Restaurant Kelly Liken, Matsuhisa Vail, Elway's, Larkspur, Sweet Basil and many more.
