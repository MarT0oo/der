Diamond Estate, Arrowhead, Vail, Colorado Vacation Rental

 

Diamond Estate in Arrowhead is a luxury residence is located within the gated golf and ski community of Arrowhead, the final village of Beaver Creek Resort and showcases floor to ceiling windows with gorgeous sunset and mountain views.

A perfect alpine setting for entertaining larger groups or multi-generational family gatherings, the home has a main floor great room with a centerpiece gas fireplace and an open floor plan, plus a lower level game room with a large sectional sofa, flat screen tv, wet bar, ping pong table and an over-sized wall-mounted scrabble game! The outdoor hot tub and on-demand ski shuttle service, combined with access to the Arrowhead Alpine Club's outdoor heated pool, tennis and pickle ball courts (summer only) and our personal concierge services to assist with all aspects of trip planning, ensures that everyone will have a remarkable Colorado getaway no matter what season you choose to visit!
