Dolomite Townhome, Aspen, Colorado Vacation Rental

 

Located at the base of Aspen Mountain and just 3 blocks from the Silver Queen Gondola, this Dolomite townhome has one of the best locations in town!

If there is fresh snow on the ground, renters can ski across Monarch Street from the unit to Lift 1A. The free skier shuttle is just over two blocks away and the rest of Aspen’s shopping and night life are only a few steps beyond. This property appeals especially to large families and groups who want to enjoy Aspen together.

The unit features a gas fireplace, TV's in every bedroom, modern kitchen, and a balcony facing Aspen Mountain. Three skylights give the condo a light and spacious feeling throughout the 1900 square feet of living space. Additional amenities include washer & dryer, wireless internet and outdoor hot tub.
