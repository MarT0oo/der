Olympian 301, Steamboat Springs, Colorado Vacation Rental

 

This spacious 3 bedroom condominium is tastefully decorated with modern furnishings and bold colors. The open floor plan of the living room, dining room, and kitchen lends a roomy feel and encourages togetherness. With granite counter tops, stainless appliances, and a large island with double sink, the kitchen offers everything you need to fix breakfast or a holiday dinner for the whole family. The master bedroom features a king bed, flat screen TV, and a plush master bath with his and hers sinks, a soaking tub and marble tiled shower. The second bedroom has a queen bed and flat screen TV, while the third bedroom features a twin over full bunk bed.

A queen sleeper sofa is located in the living room allowing 8 people to sleep comfortably. The second and third bedrooms share a full bathroom, and there is a powder room in the entryway. There is complimentary Wi-Fi available in the condominium. Enjoy the views of Howelsen Hill from the windows and oversized deck. This is a great place to watch the fireworks during Winter Carnival or Fourth of July.
