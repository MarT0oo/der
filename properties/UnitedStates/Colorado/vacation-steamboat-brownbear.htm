Brown Bear Chalet, Steamboat Springs, Colorado Vacation Rental


Brown Bear Chalet is an exquisite 4 bed, 5 bath home which accommodates 12 guests and is located in the Burgess Creek neighborhood in Steamboat Springs.

This chalet offers a spacious great room with covered deck and mountain views.  Leather sofas invite you to relax in front of the large fireplace and 45" plasma TV.  The adjoining dining area features a hand-crafted 10-person alder dining table that beautifully complements the fine craftsmanship of the interior woodwork.  The large open floor plan kitchen is fully equipped and includes the finest appliances from Wolf and SubZero. Custom alder cabinets and granite counters make a rich environment to inspire the creativity of any chef.

Brown Bear offers a guest suite and junior master suite each with a king-sized bed and en-suite bath on the main level; a master suite with a king-sized bed and en-suite bath and a bunk room with 2 set of bunk beds and en-suite bath on the upper level; and a queen-sized sleeper sofa is available in the recreation room on the lower level.  The luxurious beds have high thread-count sheets, featherbeds and Euro comforters. Bedrooms also offer individual DVD players and flat panel LCD TV's, alarm clocks with MP3 / IPOD input, robes and slippers.  The flexible bedding configuration provides the option of as many as 11 individual beds.

The recreation room on the lower level combines as a media and games room with a 50" big screen TV with surround sound, Playstation 2 video games and a pool table. The separate mud room includes a boot dryer and storage cupboards for all your ski equipment.  Brown Bear Chalet is equipped with an elevator serving all floors.
