Pines Townhome, Beaver Creek, Colorado Vacation Rental

 

Enjoy world class skiing from the convenience of an updated, modern luxury townhome nestled amongst pine and aspen groves. This Upper Pines Townhome offers the best location in this development.

Steps from the ski run, on the complimentary shuttle to the heart of Beaver Creek and Bachelor Gulch, a short stroll to Splendidos, one of Beaver Creek premier restaurant will provide a wonderful experience for both sports and non-sports enthusiasts.

This townhomes features a three-story floor plan with an open kitchen, living room and dining area. Gorgeous Fireplace and Gourmet Kitchen: The family room features a gas fireplace for the utmost in convenience. The cooks in your group will appreciate the well-equipped kitchen and stainless steel appliances and a gourmet convection oven!

BEDROOM CONFIGURATION: The bedrooms are located on the first and the third floor. There are 4 bathrooms including a bathroom with shower on the main level. The master bedroom is located on the third level with a King bed and en-suite bathroom On the third level there are 2 guest bedrooms, one with a Queen bed and the other with two twins which can also be converted to a king. Additionally, there is a bonus room with 2 sets of twin bunks. There are 2 full bathrooms on this level. Each bedroom has a flat screen TV. Amenities include gas fireplace, wireless high-speed internet, cable, washer, dryer and a heated garage. Guests of Upper Pines enjoy access to the pool, hot tub and spa located at the Pines Lodge; just steps away.

The Pines Lodge featuring a luxurious outdoor heated pool and spa, fitness center, and AAA Four Diamond Award-winning Grouse Mountain Grill. You can even stop by for a complimentary newspaper. Resort Amenities include the Beaver Creek complimentary shuttle service which is aBeaver Creekable year-round and is the most convenient way to get around! This resort shuttle provides door to door service. The shuttle driver will pick you, take you to your destination and then bring you back home again. No need to drive around and find parking.

CONCIERGE: When staying at this home, you will also be able to enjoy the complimentary services of your very own concierge who will arrange ground, lifts, ground transport, outdoor activities, grocery delivery, and much more.
