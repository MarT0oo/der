Rendezvous Lodge, Steamboat Springs, Colorado Vacation Rental

 

Rendezvous Lodge is the perfect base for a group or family ski trip. Enjoy the amenities of a luxury custom home with great access to the ski trails that are almost on the doorstep, just 200 yards away

Rendezvous Lodge, just a short walk from the Thunderhead Express Chairlift, offers alluring accommodations in a prime location, that will delight families and groups seeking luxury, privacy and convenience.

This spacious home boasts sophisticated and modern décor throughout with elegant finishes alongside playful bursts of vibrant color. The foyer sets the tone, with beautiful natural stone, leading you into the great room, filled with natural light, where views of the mountains are perfectly framed by the living room and dining room windows.

The gourmet kitchen flows right off the dining room, an entertainer’s delight, with top of the range appliances, a gorgeous copper hutch. The large marble topped island which runs the length of the kitchen is the heart of the home.

Designed for entertaining and spending time together the round dining table can seat eight to ten, with an additional four stools at the island.

The master bedroom is private and secluded as the only bedroom on the main floor. A stunning sanctuary with views above the treetops, the King bedroom has a gas fireplace, and large walk-in closet off master bathroom. The bathroom embellishes elegance with double sinks, large, marble top tub and separate glass steam shower. There are two more bedrooms on the lower level, each with private baths; a guest suite with a Queen bed and a bedroom with two twins. The large laundry boasts double washing machines and double dryers. The top floor King guest master offers spectacular views of the Steamboat mountain resort, vaulted ceilings, private sitting area, and another master bathroom with soaking tub. The other bedroom on the top floor has two twin beds, a queen and a private bathroom.

On the lower level, the second living room overlooks the Burgess Creek ravine below, surrounded by nature, the perfect setting for a soak in the hot tub on the lower level patio. This relaxed rec room has a flat screen tv, fun multi colored arm chairs, wet bar, with wine cooler and a half bath.

A bonus to this lodge is the massive “ski locker” room which is located off the attached two car garage, with an abundance of cubbies, storage, boot dryers and seating, the essential room for your winter toys. Our ski shuttle will collect you from the driveway or you can access the ski trails on foot, just a minute from the door
