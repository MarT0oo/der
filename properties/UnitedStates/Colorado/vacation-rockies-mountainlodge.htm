Rockies Mountain Lodge, Mt. Crested Butte, Colorado Vacation Rental


The Rockies Mountain Lodge is a boutique mountain lodge providing luxury accommodations for groups. This 11,500 square foot facility has been completely remodeled to provide the perfect setting for visitors seeking the best the Colorado Rockies have to offer.

The Lodge sits high on a hillside above the ski resort town of Mt. Crested Butte, a short walk from the ski lifts. Our guest rooms have panoramic views of the surrounding aspen and evergreen covered mountains and valleys. The structure has been designed with a blend of traditional and modern Colorado architecture and building materials including hickory and maple floors, soapstone stoves and slate and travertine bathrooms. The large open spaces contain soaring, cathedral open-beam ceilings and ensure a range of comfortable spots for guests to gather and relax.

The spacious common areas include a living room with 100” projection TV, dining room with seating for 24, gourmet kitchen with two refrigerators and two dishwashers, office, and lounge with bar.   There are numerous decks and outdoor spaces to enjoy the sun, stars and alpine climate at our 9,375 ft. elevation. Other facilities include spa and massage room, outdoor Finnish sauna and a fully equipped gym and fitness room.

The location and design of the property provides for privacy while enhancing the views of the entire valley.  Plenty of space for guests traveling with extended family, nannies, assistants, or security detail.  For an additional fee, full concierge services are available from our onsite caretaker to provide you with everything you’ll need for a relaxing and energizing stay in Crested Butte!

The Lodge has 10 guest rooms, including five master suites with their own outdoor private hot tubs, and fireplace or gas-burning stove.  Each room, named after a local mountain peak, is thoughtfully and tastefully decorated, with its own en-suite bathroom, writing desk and chair, television, bathrobes, and thermostat control.  Everyone will feel as though they’ve checked into their own private room at a boutique hotel.

Come enjoy our Colorado hospitality. You can challenge yourself and pamper yourself at our Lodge. We are confident you will leave relaxed and rejuvenated.
