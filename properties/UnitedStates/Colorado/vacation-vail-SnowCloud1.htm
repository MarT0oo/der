Snow Cloud 1, Vail, Colorado Vacation Rental

 

Snow Cloud 1 is a luxury mountain getaway located in an exclusive area of Vail Valley. Just outside your window is an express high-speed quad lift, providing convenient access to all of the prime skiing in Bachelor Gulch, Beaver Creek and Arrowhead. Summer months also bring great hiking and golf opportunities, as well as native birdsongs. During your visit to Colorado, you will be sitting pretty at Snow Cloud 1!

This lovely ski-in/ski-out lodge boasts magnificent views of the surrounding scenery. The Ritz-Carlton’s 4 Diamond Spa, heated outdoor pool and five star facilities are just minutes away. Or stay at home and enjoy a well deserved soak in the Jacuzzi. A home entertainment system, Internet access and a gas grill are also provided for your convenience.

The beautifully designed interior features hand trowel-textured walls, white oak floors, knotty pine trim and cabinetry, granite slab kitchen counters and marble tile bathrooms. The dry-stack moss rock fireplace in the cozy living room is an ideal spot to share après-ski chuckles. Aspects of Snow Cloud 1 evoke the warmth of the country, while contemporary design themes also provide a modern edge.

Three comfortable and stately bedrooms accommodate up to nine guests at Snow Cloud 1. Each bedroom features an en-suite bathroom fitted with the latest amenities. The rough hewn “beaver log” bunk beds are sure to be a hit with the children!

Located in the heart of skiing country, The Red Sky Golf Club is also located nearby with its world renown Norman Course. In mid-summer, the Bravo! music festival welcomes top caliber classical ensembles that echo their celestial sounds through the mountainside. Whether you are racing down the slopes in the winter, or taking in the fresh summer air, Snow Cloud 1 offers all of the refinements required for a splendid alpine stay.
