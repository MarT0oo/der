Humming Bird 1, Vail, Colorado Vacation Rental

 

Spectacular Ski-in/ski-out luxury privately owned penthouse with sweeping views overlooking the ski slopes. Guests enjoy complimentary access to the The Ritz hotel & Spa. Amenities include a full gourmet kitchen with granite finishes, TV/VCR/DVD's, high speed wireless internet, large deck, W&D, underground parking with elevator to unit, ski storage, common area hot tub & workout room & changing area.

This luxury residence offers the some of best views in Bachelor Gulch from the living area & master bedroom. It has an open floor plan, tasteful modern upscale furnishings, clean lines, roomy master bedroom and master bath, click-in/click-out ski access, 10 foot ceilings with windows to match, lots of natural light and air conditioning. Three comfortable bedrooms sleep up to 8 guests in 1 king, 2 double, 1 king and a queen sofa in the loft.

Kitchen is fully equipped and will delight the chefs in your group! One of the best ski locations in Bachelor Gulch! Literally steps from one of Bachelor Gulch premier ski runs to your private ski locker!!
