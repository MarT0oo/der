Lodge at Timber Ridge, Snowmass, Aspen, Colorado Vacation Rental

 

The Lodge at Timber Ridge is newly built Snowmass luxury vacation Home. Located in the Two Creeks Subdivision, it has direct ski access to Snowmass Mountain via Two Creeks run. One of our top vacation home, this house features out-standing mountain lodge style decor with warm colors and a lot of wood, inviting you to relax during your next stay with us. You will find too, that this spacious house, big enough to sleep 12 people, offers great amenities such as an outdoor private hot-tub, a media room, and large, functional gourmet kitchen.


Newly Constructed Home in Two Creeks Subdivision with direct Ski Access onto Two Creeks.  The Master bedroom with King Bed, gas fireplace, 25" flat screen TV, steam shower and Jacuzzi tub.   The second master bedroom with King Bed, gas fireplace, 25" flat Screen TV, steam shower, Jacuzzi tub.  The King bedroom with private bath, 42" flat screen TV.  A Queen bedroom with private bath and steam shower.  And one set of bunk beds with full on the bottom and a twin on top, and one twin bed and private bath.


The living room has a 42" Flat Screen TV, DVD, and CD player.  Our media room with gas fireplace, 42" Plasma TV, CD, DVD, Satellite, IPOD Capability and Central Stereo System throughout.  We have a great office with gas fireplace, 20" TV, High-Speed internet access and fax machine. 


There is a formal dining Room table that seats 12 people, a lovely gourmet kitchen table as well that seats 6 people.  The house also features a outdoor Deck with Wood burning Fireplace, a private outdoor hot tub with a maximum of 11 Guests.
