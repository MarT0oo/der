Mountain High Chalet, Steamboat Springs, Colorado Vacation Rental

 

Mountain High Chalet sits upon a mountain ridge in a quiet neighborhood just minutes from the slopes.

The hand shaped logs are beautifully finished and provide a cozy living area centered in front of the stone fireplace. The Great Room with high ceiling and loft shows off the fine craftsmanship involved in the construction of the chalet.

The kitchen is fully equipped. However, with our Luxury Catered Chalet option, our chefs will tantalize your taste buds with healthy, gourmet delights that will energize you for a day on the mountain and warm your bellies after a full day on the mountain.

The dining table will comfortably seat eight guests for an elegant dinner setting as the alpenglow lights up the mountain.

Mountain High Chalet offers 3 charming bedrooms and baths in the main house and a detached apartment garage with fireplace, bath and kitchenette.

The outside decks offer one of the most spectacular views of the area with a 180-degree view down the valley to the Flat Top Mountains and up to the Steamboat Ski Area. This is same view that you can enjoy from the hot tub on the lower deck.

Complimentary shuttle included for super-easy access to the ski trails.
