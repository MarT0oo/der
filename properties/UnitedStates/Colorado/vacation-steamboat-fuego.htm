Chalet Fuego, Steamboat Springs, Colorado Vacation Rental


This newly constructed multi-level five bedroom home has warm, inviting tones blending mountain and modern touches that will set your heart on fire!

The cozy living area invites you to take in the expansive valley views curled up before the double-sided fireplace. The open kitchen allows conversation and interaction with seating for eight at the wood dining table and an additional four in leather barstools at the gorgeous, marble countertop.

The professionally equipped kitchen has double Wolf ovens, Sub Zero refrigerator, and a wood with glass front bakers cabinet with extensive stemware. A half bath is located off the kitchen between the laundry room and attached two-car garage.

The master bedroom is private and secluded as the only bedroom on the main floor. The King bedroom has a gas fireplace, accesses the hot tub from its own deck, and large walk-in closet connects to the master bathroom. The bathroom has every convenience with double sink, large, marble tub and separate glass shower. The spacious bunk room has a “full over full” bed and “full over king” bed. The top level is just three more steps and has an inviting sitting area with chaise lounge for reading in front of another gas fireplace or gazing out the windows. A King guest master with private deck enjoys spectacular views of the Sleeping Giant and Emerald mountains.

In the lower level, the recreation room has large, flat screen tv, gas fireplace, sleeper sofa, half bath, a hand-made wood table with board games, bar, and walk out to the side yard, fire pit and covered hot tub. There are two more bedrooms each with private baths.

Chalet Fuego can be rented in conjunction with its sister property, Chalet Beliza , next door. Combined, they are 10 bedrooms, 9 full baths and sleep 26.
