Senator’s Penthouse, Steamboat Springs, Colorado Vacation Rental

 

The Senator’s Penthouse at the Olympian offers a sanctuary in the heart of downtown Steamboat Springs with spectacular views of Howelsen Hill and Emerald Mountain over the Yampa River.

This delightful penthouse residence offers ultra-convenient access to the heart of downtown Steamboat Springs with its extensive shopping and a flourishing culinary scene and World-class skiing easily accessed by free bus or our on call private winter ski shuttle service. Additionally, you are just a stone's throw from the Rodeo grounds and the trails of Emerald Mountain.

The spacious open plan living room with handscraped hickory wood floors and luxurious carpet is the heart of the Senator’s Penthouse with floor to ceiling windows that bring the views into this beautiful residence. Tastefully furnished throughout you will be drawn to relax before the large gas fireplace. When weather or seasons allow the large deck with built in snow-melt system offers the opportunity to relax and live-a-little in the outdoors with views of Howelsen Hill, and the mountains all around. In summer, this is your springboard to biking and hiking trails and the weekly Rodeo. The private hot tub allows you to soak your tired muscles and enjoy the view at the same time.

The fully equipped kitchen with Wolf and Sub Zero appliances has everything that you will need to entertain and cook-at-home. Custom cabinetry and granite countertops create an elegant workspace for the aspiring chef in your group.

The elegant dining table seats 6 guests with additional seating for three at the breakfast bar.

The master bedroom offers a Queen bed and private bathroom. The second bedroom offers a Queen bed private bathroom as does the third bedroom. There are extensive closets and drawers to allow you to fully enjoy these spacious rooms.

Although a vehicle is not a necessity this residence also offers the luxury of an underground heated parking space. Ski access is very easy, complimentary on-call private shuttle service which picks up from the Olympian and deposits you right at the Gondola.
