Storm Peak Chalet, Steamboat Springs, Colorado Vacation Rental

 

This beautiful new home is the perfect base for your family ski trip as it is conveniently located close to the Steamboat mountain village.

The cozy great room allows you to relax by the gas fireplace and enjoy a spectacular view of the Steamboat ski mountain. The spacious and open floor plan that connects the living spaces including the kitchen and dining area means you can gather in comfort with your family or friends. All floors are served by an elevator.

The fully equipped kitchen allows for easy entertaining. The granite countertops, large island, and Wolf gas range are among the highlights that will bring out the "Iron Chef" in those that like to cook.

The dining room table seats eight with room for four at the bar so everyone can be together for meals.

The four bedrooms of Storm Peak Chalet, all with private bathrooms, will comfortably sleep 10. The master bedroom with King bed enjoys a private deck. The guest master with private bath offers a choice of King bed or can be set up with two twin beds. Another bedroom offers a set of double bunk beds and the 4th bedroom offers two double beds. The media room offers a queen sleeper sofa.

On the main level, the vista of the ski mountain can be enjoyed from the hot tub on the covered deck. The upper level media room is the perfect place to enjoy a movie or play games. A ping pong table on the lower level offers a competitive outlet for apres ski activities.

Complimentary private shuttle is included for convenient and speedy access to the ski trails.
