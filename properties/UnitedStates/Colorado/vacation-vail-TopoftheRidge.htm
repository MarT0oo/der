Top of the Ridge, Bachelor Gulch, Vail, Colorado Vacation Rental

 

One of the most striking and luxurious vacation rentals in Bachelor Gulch, Colorado, Top of the Ridge Road sits just above the Bachelor Gulch Chairlift and features ski-in ski-out access. Architects utilized massive stone archways, beautiful woodwork, and modern design to create a collection of truly opulent living spaces. With commercial appliances, premium furnishings, and ample bedding throughout, this home is an ideal location for the luxury minded traveler who demands the absolute best. The home features an enviable list of amenities including billiard room, home theatre room, and a private swimming pool with magnificent views of the Vail Valley with the backdrop of the Gore Range.

The kitchen’s list of appliances and amenities is endless, but includes a stone wood-burning pizza oven, built in Miele system, sub-zero refrigerator, and separate wine cooler.

The master bedroom, in its own wing, has a king bed, gas fireplace with additional seating area, loft, flat screen television, walk-in closet, and a bathroom one can be envious of with its steam shower, Jacuzzi tub, and sitting area.

The other 5 bedrooms of the home each have access to their own bathroom and are wonderfully placed throughout the home’s 4 floors.

A theater room, billiard’s table, additional family room, and outdoor patio with ample lounge chairs and hot tub provides something for everyone for their next ski vacation; convenience, luxury, and memories to be made.

Have a larger party? For an additional charge and approval ahead of time we may be able to open up the 7th bedroom of this home offering three more beds. One is a captain’s bunk with a full bed on bottom, twin bed on top, in addition to another lofted twin.
