Pop Art Villa, Vail, Colorado Vacation Rental

 

Pop Art Villa is not just your average Arrabelle penthouse. This is for the discerning traveler who seeks the highest level of luxury in a perfect slope side location.

The AAA 4-Diamond-rated Arrabelle at Vail Square is known for high quality luxury accommodations and excellent standards of customer service. The hotel’s timeless character and European charm give you the feeling of visiting a Bavarian mountain village. The location and accommodations are unparalleled in Vail, providing an unforgettable and relaxing experience. Upscale fine dining, entertaining, and retail options are available right out your door.

Discover the convenience and fun of the world’s most iconic ski resort with direct access to Vail Mountain via the Eagle Bahn Gondola at your doorstep. With the ski valet available to handle all of your equipment needs, getting on and off the mountain has never been easier.

The butler and concierge are available 24 hours a day to meet your every need. From grocery delivery, gear rentals, dinner reservations, to finding you a private chef and much more, our staff are fully trained with the exacting standards of luxury.

In the winter months, head out to the mountains for a beautiful, fun-filled day of powder skiing or stay close by to enjoy the ice skating rink. The heated year-round roof top lap pool with sweeping mountain views is a family favorite. In the summer months, enjoy many of the Vail Mountain adventures such as mountain biking, white water rafting, kayaking, and hiking. Whether skiing or biking, you can count on the valet service to take care of your equipment and transportation needs. The variety of elegant shops and entertainment are just what you need to occupy any spare time you may have left in your day.

You will discover our private residential living has superior amenities. These suites are furnished with new custom designer furnishings, Bulgari toiletries, automatic blinds, custom drapes and pillows, as well as plush luxury bedding and amenities.

Each room features numerous sophisticated contemporary art pieces, many from local artists.

There is a full gourmet kitchen with gas stove, large refrigerator, and enough supplies to satisfy any cook.  There is also an outdoor grill with a sweeping mountain view! A private chef may also be booked for an additional charge.

There are sweeping ski mountain views from the living room, patio, and all bedrooms. The entire place was recently renovated with well-known designer Jeff Elliot and includes many custom pieces of fine furniture. There is an automatic stone fire place in the living room and in one of the master suites. Watch your favorite shows on a 75" Samsung flat screen TV.  Free high speed WIFI included. You won't want to leave your suite, but if you do, Vail Mountain awaits you.
