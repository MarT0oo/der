616 Forest Road West, Vail, Colorado Vacation Rental

 

We are pleased to offer the newest property on West Forest Road. This 5 bedroom, 5.5 bathroom luxury Vail vacation rental is located just steps from the ski slope in Lionsheads and has commanding views of the Gore Range, Vail Valley, Gondola and Lionshead Village. The 4 floors offer a ski and boot room, media room with wet bar, vaulted ceilings and floor to ceiling windows, elevator throughout and 3 sets of washer/dryers. The master bedroom occupies the whole 4th floor and has a private balcony, and the second master also has a walk out patio. All of the bedrooms have their own full bathrooms, and there are 2 additional half baths. The gourmet kitchen features state of the art appliances, including a Wolf Range and has marble counter tops. In the summer, enjoy a beautiful outdoor patio area surrounded by Aspen trees, with a built in gas grill, 8 person hot tub and outdoor dining table. The front deck features comfortable seating and amazing views. This house sleeps 10 people, with 3 rooms being able to convert from King beds to 2 twin beds.
