Potato Patch, Vail, Colorado Vacation Rental

 

Nestled in the heart of Vail Mountain’s exclusive Potato Patch neighborhood, this desirable townhome is one of the most stunning vacation rentals in Colorado. Seated on the north side of Vail Valley, you can expect plenty of sunshine and impressive views of the mountain and valley below. Whether you are racing down the slopes or taking in the nature’s summer tones, the Potato Patch Club Townhome offers something for everyone.

In the summer, you will have access to a lovely swimming pool and tennis courts during your stay at Potato Patch. The villa includes a home entertainment system and Internet access for your convenience. The complimentary concierge service will be happy to help you with any of your needs or special requests.

Inside this three story remodeled end unit, you’ll find modern decor with elegant accents—from the tiled fireplace to the cherry-colored hardwood floors. The fully equipped kitchen features stainless steel appliances, cookware and a three seat breakfast bar—a perfect place to start the day with a solid meal before heading out for fantastic skiing or epic hikes. The gallery windows throughout capture much natural sunlight and majestic mountain views.

The four bedrooms at Potato Patch accommodate up to twelve guests. All bedrooms include en-suite bathrooms and TVs. One bedroom has a king sized bed and the three bunk beds are sure to be a hit for families traveling with children. The two panels of windows in the bedroom with two queen beds makes one feel as though they are floating up in the trees.

While this Potato Patch town home’s location offers privacy and seclusion, it is also but a five minute drive to Vail Village and the ski lifts. If you don’t feel like driving, hop on the free shuttle. For avid golfers, the Red Sky Golf Club with its famed Norman Course is also located nearby. Whatever the season, you are well situated for some alpine fun at Potato Patch Club Townhomes.
