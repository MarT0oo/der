    You will love the Gidlow, one of our Snowmass luxury vacation property, this newly remodeled 6 bedroom offers you all the comfort of home, with that personal touch that makes it so unique. Each room is different and all the space offered in the common areas make it easy to circulate and enjoy each other’s company without feeling overwhelmed. Enjoy indirect access to the slopes, a private out-door hot tub, and a media room, all of it thought through to make your vacation all the more relaxing.


    Luxury, re-modeled 6 bedroom, 5.5 bath home located in Wood Run with indirect ski access - ski out 100 yard walk to the end of street, direct ski in back to the house.  The main master features King bed, fireplace, 55"Plasma TV, private bath with Jacuzzi tub, steam shower with two shower heads, 15" LCD screen TV, private stackable washer/dryer.  There is also a private gym located off of the master bedroom with treadmill, elliptical, weights and more.  The second Guest which is a King bedroom has a  private bath and 27" TV.  The third guest with a King bedroom has a  private bath and 27" TV.  The fourth guest has a King bed also with private bath, 27" TV.  The fifth guest has a king bed with private bath and 27" TV.  There is also an office with loft above and two twins and an additional 27" TV.  We also have a Music/Reading room with twin day bed, stereo with CD and 15" LCD screen TV.


    There is a high definition 52" TV in the living room with DVD and VCR and digital cable for those who enjoy a nice movie or a sporting event while on vacation.  There is a large wood burning fireplace in the living room, for cozying up after a long day of skiing.  As well there are 2 large decks with outdoor hot tub and Weber Grill.


    You will truly enjoy a gourmet kitchen with surrounding granite bar and 8 barstools, perfect for those who love to cook and save on costly restaurant meals.  There is in addition a formal dining room seats 8 people, billiard Room with full size pool table, CD sound system and 27" TV.
