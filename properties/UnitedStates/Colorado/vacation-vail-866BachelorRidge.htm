866 Bachelor Ridge, Beaver Creek, Vail, Colorado Vacation Rental

 

This extraordinary mountain house is perched slope-side in the exclusive community of Bachelor Gulch. Guests will find expansive views, large and ample gathering areas, and exquisite touches of mountain luxury with its exposed timbers, stone fireplaces, and private outdoor hot tub.

This 5 bedroom, 7 bathroom home could not be more perfect for your next mountain vacation any time of the season. Sleeping 12 adults comfortably, and up to 14 with children, guests love not only what this mountain lodge has to offer inside, but outside as well.

During the winter months you need only walk slightly downhill less than 100 yards to the groomed blue run “Ridge Rider” or less than 100 yards slightly uphill to the groomed green run “Everkrisp.” To end your day these runs will bring you almost to your front door, or should you find yourself at the Ritz Carlton or in Beaver Creek Village the free dial-a-ride service can pick you up and drop you back at your front door. During the summer months this home is surrounded by wildflowers, wildlife, ample hiking trails, and a disc golf course. Your personal concierge can also help you get set up with whitewater rafting, horseback riding, let you know of the summer concerts, and more!

Stepping inside this beautiful chalet you can’t help but notice the luxurious finishes as well as the grand piano, great stone fireplace, and views that will take your breathe away as they span Bachelor Gulch Mountain and Beaver Creek. The dining area is surrounded by timbers, plush chairs, and a large natural table with seating for 10. The kitchen not only has granite counter tops and gourmet appliances, but also a cozy hearth room off the side as well as a breakfast table with seating for 8.

The Master Suite can also be found on this main level and includes a king bed, flat screen television, fireplace, additional sitting area, and an en suite bathroom with soaking Jacuzzi tub, his and her vanities, and a walk-in closet.

The lower level of this home houses the remaining four guest bedrooms, one of which is the Jr. Master Suite also containing a king bed, flat screen television, fireplace, and en suite bathroom with Jacuzzi tub. The remaining three bedrooms each have their own en-suite bathroom as well. There is a fitness room for those who wish to continue their workout regimen while on vacation, as well as a poker table, bumper pool table, wet bar, and additional family area with ample seating, flat screen television, and stone fireplace. Let’s also not forget the private outdoor hot tub and fire pit as well as the expansive deck with gas grill perfect for those hot summer nights and never-ending sunsets!

The homeowners of this residence do own a dog and it does visit the home once or twice a year. If you have a severe pet allergy we do not recommend this home for your stay.

Ritz Carlton Bachelor Gulch Access:
The Pool and Tennis Courts are FREE to anyone staying or living in Bachelor Gulch. Guests may have to provide a property address but it is 100% FREE.

The fitness center and spa are for guests of members only and it is $35 per day per person. This charge will go to the member. The member has to call with the dates desired and names of guests in advance. They will then issue passes which can be picked up at the Club desk or the spa front desk. The spa is limited to people over 18 only.

Access to the Bachelor Gulch Club is for members or guests accompanied by members. This is only a lounge for members to hang out in, eat and drink for free.
