Top of Mill, Aspen, Colorado Vacation Rental

 

For those who agree that skiing and snowboarding are two of the greatest winter sports on the planet, Top of Mill ski-in and ski-out luxury estate on Aspen Mountain is one of the best places to stay! Indulge in contemporary mountain living at its finest in this modern 2-story villa with rustic charm. You have direct ski access from your door on Schuss Gully and Little Nell ski runs, and just a short walk to Aspen Mountain’s base.

This villa features 6 bedrooms each with elegant ensuite bathrooms and spacious closets. The master bedroom has a king size bed with an ensuite bathroom a shower and dual vanity. The convenient gas fireplace will keep you warm during those cold winter nights. Individuals seeking additional privacy will enjoy the cozy guest house with 1 bedroom and ensuite bathroom. Enjoy playing pool in the entertainment room and relaxing with family and friends. Entertainment room includes a wet bar. The outdoor space features a fireplace and barbeque. Massage room and gym are superior additions to this villa. Stay in tip top shape at Top of Mill’s exercise room includes a Peloton bike, treadmill, elliptical machine, free weights, and more. At the end of a thrilling day, enjoy the Jacuzzi and relax sooth your muscles. Top of Mill even make laundry a breeze with two washers and two dryers. This villa comes fully stocked and staffed with a resident House Manager and housekeeping staff.

Top of Mill is only a 5-minute walk to the center of town or guest may choose to take the household golf cart (seats 5). Enjoy all that Aspen has to offer from restaurants, shopping, and nightlife!

Although Aspen is known for their world renowned skiing, it is also a well-loved year round natural mountain escape. Enjoy outdoor adventure, arts & culture, and restaurants that bring fresh menus and new talents to provide culinary options to please any pallet. Winter in Aspen is characterized by warm sun and heavy snowfall on the mountains, making it the ideal time for skiing. The summer months are warm and dry, with daytime highs of up to 90°F, great for sightseeing and exploring. Fall conditions are wonderful for travelers. Snow is common on the peaks, so winter sports are still a possibility, and the moderate temperatures keep the chill at bay. The end of September and beginning of October also promise dazzling autumnal foliage.
