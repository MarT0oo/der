Scandinavian Lodge 300, Steamboat Springs, Colorado Vacation Rental

 

Nestled high alongside Steamboat's iconic Mt. Werner, The Penthouse at the Scandinavian Lodge was fully renovated for the owner's personal use and comes equipped with new modern large kitchen with Kitchenaid Professional appliances including a gas burning stove and convection oven.

Flat screen TVs in all bedrooms, new 55 inch HD TV Xfinity 1 cable and Wi-Fi. Large living room with plenty of seating, wood burning fireplace and approximately 2,000 square feet of living space. The master bedroom comes equipped with new furnishings & bedding, HD TV, fabulous views and private balcony. Master bath with large Jacuzzi soaking tub, separate shower and double sinks and walk in closet.

The second master also has private balcony and wonderful views, king bed, private bath with tub, TV, ceiling fan. The third bedroom has two twin beds and a day bed with trundle bringing the sleeping to 4 twins in the room, the pass through closet provides guest in this room direct access to full guest bath.
