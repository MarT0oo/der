Winter Castle, Steamboat Springs, Colorado Vacation Rental

 

Located on the mountain in a private neighborhood, Winter Castle offers privacy with great access to skiing just minutes from your door. Recently remodeled, this home has rich mountain accents; large beams, reclaimed wood floors, custom antler railings and a stone fireplace to create a cozy luxury lodge.

Your home-away-from-home includes the Great Room with vaulted ceilings, big screen TV and gas fireplace providing a comfortable airy setting for living, dining, and entertaining for the week. The plush leather couches invite you to relax and enjoy a good book or watch a movie.

The professionally equipped kitchen provides everything that your group will need to dine and entertain at home. A full complement of appliances, utensils, and cookware will allow you to take advantage of staying in a home and not feel that you have to eat out on vacation.

It’s a great home for entertaining with a dining table that seats up to 10 with additional seating for three at the breakfast bar.

The master bedroom on the main level is just one of 5 king bedrooms in this large home, three of which have private bathrooms. The lower level has a King bedroom with private bathroom and two king bedrooms that share a bathroom. On the upper level is another king with shared bath and a bunk room that has two sets of bunks and two trundle beds to sleep up to 6 guests – ideal for kids.

The rec room provides an additional living area with a 42” TV, Wii video games system, and a foosball table. The outdoor hot tub allows you to relax and soak away the stresses of a day on the mountain.

Winter Castle offers covered parking although vehicles are not needed in the winter as complimentary shuttle service picks up from the driveway.
