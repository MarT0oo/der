Edgemont, Steamboat Springs, Colorado Vacation Rental

 

With ski-in, ski-out access to the slopes of the Steamboat ski area and above the base area village, Highland Thistle offers the unique opportunity of six slope side bedrooms. A large group can gather together with ample room for all to relax and enjoy their surroundings.

This residence is warm and inviting with a native Colorado sandstone two-way fireplace which creates a focal point of the living spaces.

The professionally equipped kitchen will provide an inspirational setting for the chef in your group with the 6-burner Viking Range and GE Monogram appliances.

Large dining room tables and kitchen bar seating create the perfect setting for meals to be enjoyed with friends.

The tasteful furnishings throughout this home create a sense of balance and serenity.

The onsite amenities of Edgemont include a heated outdoor pool and hot tub, outdoor fire pit, and indoor lounge with gas fireplace.

Slope side access is easy with the community ski locker room next to the slopes. You can store your equipment and dry off at the end of the day. There is also exercise equipment onsite.

Thistle Ridge and Highland Ridge can be booked individually for 8 guests respectively.
