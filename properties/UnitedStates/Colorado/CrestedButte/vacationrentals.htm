About Crested Butte

Crested Butte, a lovely and richly scenic location for those who want to escape to a ski destination that offers serene beauty and a lovely setting.  Mount Crested Butte is where everyone flocks to during the winter months.  The skiing has become one of the great pastimes of both locals and visitors alike.  The mountain has also attracted some really amazing skiing activities to the area, including the extreme skiers who often flock to the mountains for some over the top fun on the slopes.  The mountain also offers some great trails for cross country skiing for those who prefer a slower and more timed pace. 

If you are looking for something during the summer there are some great trails for walking, running and Biking.  Steamboat is actually the one area that if you enjoy extreme biking that you would love to visit.  It has been known as the Capital for the Mountain Biking Craze, and where youâ€™ll find the Mountain Biking Hall of Fame. 

The area offers many festivals, including their own version of Mardi Gras during the summer months, so you can always enjoy the amazing atmosphere while taking in the scenery.  The area also offers some of the best vacation rentals, chalets, condos, and lodges available in the Colorado Rockies and is a perfect place for your nuptials.  

Arriving at Crested Butte you can fly into Denver and get a connected flight to Gunnison Regional Airport.  And if you are lucky enough to fly private or charter, you can always just fly directly into Gunnison.  A drive from Denver would take you about 4.5 hours plus to Crested Butte.
