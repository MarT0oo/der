Firelight Lodge 2, Vail, Colorado Vacation Rental

 

Get in the spirit of the majestic West at this stately condo, seated at the top of the exclusive Bachelor Gulch Village. This modern and charismatic alpine villa is among the best vacation destinations in Colorado. Especially favorable is the lodge’s easy ski-in/ski-out access to the neighboring Roughlock run. For the warmer months, avid golfer’s will appreciate teeing off at the Red Sky Club nearby.

Perfect for either summer or winter fun, why not take a well deserved soak in the hot tub after your day of skiing or hiking? Internet access is also provided for your convenience. Firelight Lodge is also wheelchair accessible and perfect for families.

Inside the lodge, guests are treated to an ample living room furnished with plush sofas, modern accents, a widescreen TV with cable, a DVD player and stereo system. The well equipped gourmet kitchen will inspire the chef in you with top-of-the-line appliances and beautiful wooden cabinets. A gas grill rest outside and a large dining room table is the perfect place for those special announcements. If you’re feeling casual, pull up a chair at the breakfast bar with comfortable seating for four, or dine alfresco on the patio for a change of pace and a breath of fresh mountain air.

Firelight Lodge readily sleeps up to ten visitors with its four stately bedrooms. The master bedroom includes a king bed and an en-suite bathroom with a Jacuzzi and stand-up shower. Youngsters will especially enjoy the two log-finished sets of bunk beds with plenty of room to bring along toys!

Remember to pack a camera, especially since the private balcony offers stunning panoramas of the rugged mountain peaks. As an added bonus, take advantage of Firelight Lodge’s complimentary concierge service, which can handle everything from shipping your skis to booking your lift tickets. In mid-summer, keep an eye out for the Bravo! music festival that welcomes classical ensembles to echo their celestial notes throughout the mountainside. However you spend your time in Colorado, you will rest easy at Firelight Lodge!
