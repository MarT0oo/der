European Elegance, Beaver Creek, Colorado Vacation Rental

 

European elegance meets mountain splendor in this luxuriously appointed 9th floor Penthouse at The Ritz-Carlton, Bachelor Gulch. European and custom details capture European Elegance, like the drop-down lighting made with Italian hand-blown fixtures, installed to showcase original artwork and bathe the rooms in a warm glow. A moss rock fireplace anchors the Great Room at the heart of the penthouse.

Claim a front row seat on your private terrace with seating for four, and also watch the après-ski entertainment, with live music at the patio fire bowl just under the terrace. In summer, the pools below splash with activity.

Sear a steak on the terrace’s Lynx gas grill while you people-watch. Full access to the Ritz-Carlton’s services and amenities is another perk of your stay here.

Each suite-style bedroom provides an attached bath. Flexible sleeping arrangements, like a queen-sized bed with twin trundle underneath, and two twin beds that convert to a king-sized bed, make this penthouse perfect for families or couples traveling together.

Master Bedroom Suite: The master suite features a four-poster, king-sized bed and a window seat overlooking the ski mountain—no better place to tuck under a blanket and enjoy a book. A walk-in closet provides plenty of storage, and a computer area with computer, printer and fax machine gives you a great place to get work done, if you must. The en-suite bathroom indulges with a two-person Jacuzzi tub, steam shower, heated floors to warm your toes in the morning, and a WC.

Guest Suite 2: This room’s queen-sized bed has a twin trundle underneath. Plus, this suite also boasts a door that leads to a balcony overlooking the ski mountain, and an en-suite bathroom with walk-in shower.

Guest Suite 3: This spacious bedroom suite provides two twin beds that convert to a king, views of the ski mountain, and an en-suite bathroom with bathtub/shower. Adding to this room’s versatility is an additional twin trundle and a twin pullout sleeper. A lovely sitting area with a leather loveseat provides extra space to sprawl out.

Fully Equipped Chef Style Kitchen: A chef-designed kitchen caters to the epicure with stainless steel appliances and cupboards stocked with every utensil and culinary device one could need to prepare a meal on the mountain. The kitchen is adjacent to the dining and family room and features a set of barstools for mingling.

Great Room: At the heart of the penthouse, find a great room that is both cozy and opulent, with an expertly blended motif of mountain charm and European style. Hardwood floors and 9-foot wood coffer ceilings add to the elegance. A moss rock fireplace will hold a flickering fire, and custom-made furniture from EJ Victor is so comfortable. A formal dining room seats eight, and the handmade rustic chandelier suspended above it makes any meal look gourmet.

About The Area

Home to the iconic Rocky Mountains, it’s no wonder that Colorado is the number one destination for ski trips in the United States. With 21 first-class ski resorts scattered throughout the Rockies, ski and snowboard enthusiasts travel far and wide each winter to experience the mountains firsthand. Colorado is truly a year-round destination, however, with renowned dining, shopping, and golfing bringing in tourists long after the ski season has ended.

Featuring several world-class ski resorts, such as Vail, Aspen and Beaver Creek, Colorado is firmly grounded as one of the most sought after luxury vacation spots for affluent travelers. Given a plethora of luxury mountain cottages, villas, chalets and rental homes, it’s no surprise Colorado is often host to celebrities and royalty.
