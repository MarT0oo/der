Snowshoe Chalet, Steamboat Springs, Colorado Vacation Rental

 

Consisting of 4 bedrooms and a generous layout, your family will feel right at home in the Snowshoe Chalet. The stunning wood floors throughout encourage you to enjoy the house in socks or barefoot just to get a better feel for them. When you enter the home you and your family will be drawn to the open living area with vaulted ceiling, large flat screen TV and gas fireplace. It is the perfect spot to unwind after day out exploring Steamboat.

When you are ready to prepare dinner the chefs in your group will love the kitchen. It comes fully equipped with everything you need to prepare a feast for your group. If snacks are in order, there are 3 bar stools in the kitchen for a quick bite or overflow seating from the dining area. There is also a breakfast table with seating for 6.

The formal dining area is located just off the kitchen. It has a table that seats 6 with views onto the patio and the park beyond.

When the day is over there are 4 bedrooms consisting of 2 King beds, 1 Queen and 2 Twin beds. The master bedroom is on the main floor and has a large ensuite bathroom with double vanity and large double headed shower. Also, on the main floor you find a bedroom with 2 twin beds and a shared bathroom. Upstairs you will find a Queen and King bedroom. These share a bathroom with a double vanity and tub/shower.

To relax and unwind Snowshoe Chalet has an outdoor hot tub that you can enjoy as you take in the tranquil surroundings.

There is a 2 car garage with ski racks and plenty of space for your ski gear. The mudroom offers space to hang jackets and dry gloves and is located next to the washer and dryer for easy laundry. There is high speed internet and an office nook if you need to get some work taken care of.
