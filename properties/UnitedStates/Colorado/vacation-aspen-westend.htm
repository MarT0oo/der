Contemporary West End Home, Aspen, Colorado Vacation Rental


Contemporary West End Home in Aspen Colorado is a Luxury vacation home featuring 4 bedrooms and 4.5 baths. Modern decor with hardwood floors in the living area, gas fireplace, office and large media and bar area. Sit on your private deck, relax in the hot tub and enjoy views of Aspen Mountain.  This house is architecturally striking luxury 4 bedroom, 4.5 bath home. It is located in the historic West End with beautifully maintained gardens. Two- blocks from the free skier shuttle and 1 block from Triangle Park with walking distance to town and Festival Music Tent.

The Master suite features king bed, flat screen HD- television with surround sound private bathroom with Kohler bath products, steam shower and Jacuzzi tub. Private back deck with views of Aspen Mountain.  The second master bedroom also comes with king bed, HD-flat screen television, private bath with shower/soaking tub, double vanity & Kohler bath fixtures. Views of Aspen Mountain.   The third master bedroom has a  king bed, HD-flat screen television, private bath with shower/soaking tub & Kohler bath fixtures. The fourth bedroom has a lovely set of four  bunk beds ( 8 twin beds total), with four mini flat screen DVD-players, hall bathroom double vanity and large steam shower featuring Kohler bath fixtures. The house features Pratesi Italian linens & duvets in all bedrooms with Molten - Brown bath amenities.

As well this luxury home has a wonderful gourmet kitchen featuring Viking range/oven, All-Clad & Cuisinart appliances, Villeroy-Boch & Juliska china, & Christolfe flatware. There is a formal living room with gas fireplace and a Large dining room.   The Nana-glass wall door that opens to slate terrace and landscaped fenced yard, private hot tub, and gas BBQ. Our office area off of the kitchen with laptop, printer/copier and fax line. Wi-Fi throughout the house. In addition there is a large Media Room with game table, bar area, HD -flat screen television with surround sound.   You will also find a 2 car heated garage with ski storage racks which is perfect for those who like to ski right in.
