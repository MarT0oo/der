    Casa de Gondola is one of the best ski Access home in all of Snowmass. Enjoy views of the Elk Camp Gondola from the house and access it via Funnel Ski Run. This newly remodeled Snowmass luxury ski vacation rental offers beautiful and tasteful new furnishing throughout the house, a private hot tub, gourmet kitchen, a wood burning fireplace and everything you need for a vacation where your only worry should be to ski as much as possible.

    This Luxury 6 bedroom, 5.5 bath home is newly re-modeled, with all  new furnishings throughout.  It’s at the bottom of the Funnel Ski Run- Ski In/Out to the brand new Elk Camp Gondola, the Best Ski Access home in all of Snowmass!! 

    The Master bedroom has a lovely king bed, private bath, separate shower and tub, 32" LCD TV/DVD.  The large second Master bedroom has a king bed, private bath, 32" LCD TV/DVD.  The 3rd Guest room has a King bedroom with private bath, 32" LCD TV/DVD.  The 4th Guest room has a King bedroom with private bath, 32" LCD TV/DVD.  The 5th guest features two twins bedroom with private bath, 26" LCD TV/DVD.  And the 6th Guest room has two twins and one trundle bed bedroom, 32" LCD TV/DVD.

    The Main Living Room is nice and toasty during the winter months and comes with wood burning fireplace, 42" LCD TV/DVD, Stereo with surround sound, IPOD dock and CD Player.  The Gourmet Kitchen is a great place for people to come and test their cooking skills or hire a professional to cook for you, it’s easy with a Wolf 4 burner stove/griddle/grill, Sub Zero refrigerator, Two Dishwashers, Ice Maker, Wine Cooler and Island with prep sink.

    Enjoy your skiing holiday.
