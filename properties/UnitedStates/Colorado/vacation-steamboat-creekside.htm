Creekside Chalet, Steamboat Springs, Colorado Vacation Rental


The expansive Great Room with cathedral-ceiling offers spectacular views of the mountains. Step out on the sunny south-facing deck for a view down the valley. This room offers plenty of room for all to lounge in. Our over-stuffed leather furniture invites you to relax with your feet up!

The spacious kitchen is fully equipped with professional grade gas stovetop and griddle, double ovens and double Bosch dishwashers. With our full-service option our chefs will tantalize your taste-buds with healthy gourmet delights that will energize you for a day on the mountain.  Children can also enjoy meals at their own pace in the breakfast nook with seating for 8, while adults enjoy relaxed meals in the spacious 10-person dining area in the Great Room.

On a side deck that borders the National Forest looking up to the ski mountain, is an 8-person hot tub. Soak, unwind and plan adventures for the day ahead under a starlit sky or while fresh "Champagne" powder coats the mountain.

On the lower level of the chalet is a large game room with foosball, Wii video games system, home theater 50" big screen TV with surround sound and full stock of books and board games for young ones.  Here movie lovers can dim the lights and turn up the sound or children can play and let off steam without disturbing the rest of the chalet.
