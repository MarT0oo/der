Time Flys Lodge, Steamboat Springs, Colorado Vacation Rental

 

Time Flys Lodge is the perfect home base for a Steamboat mountain vacation. Recently remodeled from top to bottom it combines contemporary elegance with western flavors and all the comforts of home. Located just steps from Thunderhead ski lift it’s essentially a new large and stunning home in a prime location.

This modern home offers luxurious comfort and great convenience for your ski vacation. Located a short 160 yard walk to the Thunderhead high-speed ski lift, you have everything you will need and a few extras that just make it a great choice to stay here. The views of the mountain and valley wrap around you through the large windows and deck areas providing beauty in all directions.

The cozy great room offers a place to relax before the large gas fireplace. The playful barn wood accents remind you that you are in the west while you take in a movie on the big screen or soak in the mountain views. The elegant dining area with seating for 10 is the perfect setting for family or group dinners and this room provides a central focus for your stay with convenient access to the spectacular kitchen. There is also a table with seating for 5 that is perfect for a game night. Just off the great room is a large deck with breathtaking views of the ski mountain and valley beyond.

The gourmet kitchen surrounds a huge central island and offers an abundance of counter space for your chef to use. The modern white cabinets compliment Kitchen Aid appliances that include a 5-burner gas stove, oven, microwave, and dishwasher. This kitchen is fully equipped for five-star entertaining and also enjoys breathtaking views through the large windows that face the mountain. The island has seating for 6 people so this can be a setting for breakfast or additional seating for dinner guests.

On the main floor the master bedroom with extra high ceilings offers a comfortable King bed. This room allows you to take in the mountain views from the large windows without even leaving the bed. The master bathroom has dual vanities, a 6 headed shower and large, free standing, soaking bath tub to help you relax after a day in the outdoors. Upstairs, overlooking the kitchen you can retreat to the reading deck complete with desk and printer in case you need to check-in with the office. This area connects to three bedrooms – the first with a King bed, which connects to a large bathroom with dual sinks, shower and large soaking bath tub. Through this leads you to a bedroom with a twin bunk bed and twin bed to sleep three. Across the reading deck is the next bedroom with a King bed and private ensuite bathroom with a shower. Finally, on the lower level is the fifth bedroom with queen bunk beds and an extra queen to sleep up to 6. The adjacent open plan bunk and rec room also offers another four queen bunks neatly outfitted with individual lighting and charging stations.

The downstairs bunk/rec room is a fun gathering spot. In addition to the built in bunks described above there is a monstrous 75 inch flat screen TV for ultimate movie nights or sports events. From here you also walk out to your private hot tub where you can look up at the mountain and relive your days’ adventures.

With easy walk-to-ski access, or shuttle service to the base area from the door this home offers ideal convenience for skiers. There is a heated ski room for storing equipment and boot dryers to ensure you start off your day on the right foot. The on call shuttle service eliminates the need for rental cars. All the comforts of home, combined with luxury and convenience make this a great choice for your next stay in Steamboat.
