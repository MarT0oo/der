    Large and spacious 7 bedroom, 6 bath deluxe home in Ridge Run. Featuring 4 gas fireplaces, gourmet kitchen with granite countertops and stainless steel appliances and deck with private hot tub, fire pit and gas grill.  This Deluxe  7 bedroom 6 bath home is located in Ridge Run, great for those who want direct ski access.  As well there is Dial-a-ride access.

    The Master Suite is equipped  with King bed, sitting area, gas fireplace, private bath with steam shower and 36" TV.  The first guest Master with King bed, gas fireplace, private bath with steam shower and 36" TV.  The second guest room has a King bedroom with private bath and 22" TV. The third guestroom has a Queen bedroom with private bath and 22" TV.  We then have a fourth guestroom with two Queen beds with private bath and 22" TV.  A large fifth room with two sets of bunk beds with shared bathroom and 22" TV.  And finally a 6th guest room with king bed with shared bathroom and 36" TV. 

    The large living room features a  40" TV, wet bar & wine cooler, mini fridge & gas fireplace.  The family room is perfect for late night movie watching and features a 40" TV, home theatre, game table & gas fireplace.  There is also a formal dining area for those nights you want to eat in and avoid the long lines and high cost of meals, the table seats 10 comfortably.

    We hope you enjoy your stay at this lovely Snowmass Vacation Rental.
