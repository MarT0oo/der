Bear Creek Lodge 407, Telluride, Colorado Vacation Rental

 

Enjoy the perks of the Bear Creek Lodge in this spacious four-bedroom condo overlooking the woods and mountains. Enter this condo on the 4th floor and walk into the open dining area and living room with a gas fireplace, flat screen TV and balcony facing the mountainside (mountain view). The living room is open to the kitchen with granite countertops.

On the main level is a bedroom with a queen bed, TV and an ensuite bathroom with jetted tub and separate shower. The 2nd bedroom on the main level has 2 twin beds, TV and a bathroom in the hallway with shower/tub combo. Upstairs is the master bedroom with a king bed, TV, walk-in closet and ensuite bathroom with double sinks, jetted tub and separate shower. There is also a safe in the master bedroom. On this floor is another bedroom with a queen bed and a twin bed, TV, walk-in closet and ensuite bath with jetted tub and separate shower.

Bear Creek Lodge offers free wireless Internet access, free outdoor parking, fitness center, indoor and outdoor hot tubs, outdoor heated pool, sauna and a free shuttle for guest use within the Mountain Village. An outdoor fire pit and BBQ area is popular in the summer. There is easy ski access in the winter season via a semi-private lift to the Lower Village Bypass run.
