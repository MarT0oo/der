Cascades A-1, Telluride, Colorado Vacation Rental

 

Located in downtown Telluride, The Cascades Condominiums at Element 52 offer seclusion, privacy and spectacular views. Surrounded by open space to east and with the San Miguel River to the north, The Cascades mirrors the rich palette for which Colorado is renowned.

The Cascades complex has a host of amenities: the funicular (small trolley on a rail) will transport you directly from the complex to a ski trail that accesses both the Gondola and the base of Lift 7 at Coonskin; massive soaking pools cascade from one to another, and a ski valet and ski storage are at your disposal.

The complex also boasts a brand new club room, The Te-Bar (Te is element 52 on the scientific chart and it stands for Tellurium!) for apres ski enjoyment. Cascades unit A1 features a full gourmet kitchen with a Viking stove and soapstone counters, a gas-log fireplace, one bath per bedroom, a steam shower in the master bath, and a washer and dryer.

Cascades A1, a two-level unit, has a king in the master suite, a Queen and twin sofa sleeper chair in the second bedroom, 2 Fulls in the 3rd bedroom and 2 Twins in the fourth bedroom. This unit has parking for two vehicles in the heated garage. Stay at Cascades A1 and let it become your Telluride spot!
