Creek Residence , Telluride, Colorado Vacation Rental

 

The Creek Residence couples the services and amenities of the finest resort with the privacy and touch of a private residence. Offered for the first time as a rental, this is one of the finest custom residences in the historic Town of Telluride. This 5600 square foot residence, completed in 2008 overlooks the year-round flowing waters of Cornet Creek and offers five-bedrooms plus a flexible den and six and a half bathrooms.

As you enter the home, you can't help but notice the detail and sophistication that went into creating this one of a kind retreat. All of the bedrooms offer private en-suite bathrooms, complete with steam showers and white marble. The first three bedrooms on the entry level offer a Double, Twin Bunks and a King respectively. As you continue past the bedrooms, you enter the open and expansive living room with a flat panel tv, towering fireplace and access to the patio set on Cornet Creek with a 10 person hot tub. The dining and kitchen, just off the living create an open area perfect for entertaining in the top of the line kitchen and enjoying a meal after a day on the mountain.

Upstairs a Queen suite is to the left and to the right, the Master offers a private fireplace and views of the ski area. Just one level below the main entrance is the den/flex area that can sleep three on the oversized couch with a full luxurious bathroom in the corner.

Guests at the Creek Residence also enjoy all the services and amenities of Element 52 - an Auberge Resort including a private bar, ski valet + storage, SUV service and gym. If you would rather drive yourself, there is a Porsche Cayenne or an electric car for a quick trip to the store. With the finest finishes and first class amenities, the Creek Residence offers the ultimate Telluride Resort vacation retreat.
