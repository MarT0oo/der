Rocky Mountain Dreams, Mountain Village, Telluride, Colorado Vacation Rental

 

Custom built 6 bedroom log and stone residence located on a beautifully wooded and private 1.19 acre lot with excellent ski access just one lot off the slopes from the Lower Galloping Goose.

Enjoy the privacy and amazing views of the Rocky Mountains at this luxury Telluride ski vacation rental. Completely renovated in 2018! The property features a year-round, spring-fed, flowing stream off the master suite and is situated adjacent to Open Space and Hood Park.

Come in and enjoy the brand new hot tub, pool table, mountain bikes, jukebox, board games, DVD collection and much more!

Utilize the free gondola and Dial-A-Ride for all your Mountain Village and Telluride needs.
