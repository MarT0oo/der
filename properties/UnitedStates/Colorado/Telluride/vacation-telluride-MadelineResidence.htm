Madeline Residence, Telluride, Colorado Vacation Rental

 

Come home to the private residences at Madeline in the heart of Mountain Village. The convenience, comfort and style of a private vacation home, plus world-class resort amenities and atmosphere. Choose from one- to four-bedroom ski-in, ski-out residences with stunning views, balconies and heated floors. The Residential Suite has the option to combine two more guest rooms on the same floor and make the floor private.

Our largest luxury residences offer modern mountain living at its finest with an open living area and four separate bedrooms. Like a private ski home, each one-bedroom residence features a fully equipped kitchen with stainless steel appliances and washer/dryer. Enjoy the open dining area for gathering with friends & family. Most units offer a private balcony for evening cocktails and gazing at the stars. The gas fireplace in the spacious living room sets the stage for relaxing. The elegantly-appointed bedrooms offer a king- or queen-sized bed and en-suite bathrooms with steam showers, and deep soaking bathtubs for relaxing after a day outdoors. The couch is furnished with a pull-out bed.

This stunning alpine resort sits mid-mountain and is connected via the complimentary gondola to the historic town of Telluride. The 14,000-foot peaks of the San Juan Mountains and the unparalleled amenities of Mountain Village set the scene for your exclusive getaway home. Enjoy multiple restaurants, full-service spa, fitness facilities, pool, hot tubs, and other world-class amenities and services during your stay.
