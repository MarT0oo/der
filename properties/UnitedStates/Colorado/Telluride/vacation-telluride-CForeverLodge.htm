C Forever Lodge, Telluride, Colorado Vacation Rental

 

C Forever Lodge is a classic western ski lodge with direct ski in/ski out access on to the Galloping Goose ski run. This 5 bedroom home can sleep 10 guests over 3 spacious level. The main level of the home features a huge great room with cathedral widows. These windows have stunning, sun filled views to the west. There is a stone fireplace surrounded by a comfortable lounge area, another seating area and access out on to a large deck. There is a full gourmet kitchen as well as a dining table for 12 guests. On this level, there is also a half bathroom. There is a king guest suite off to the side of the great room, with it's own private bathroom. There is also a ski room, through which guests can access a second outdoor deck with a hot tub and direct slope access.
