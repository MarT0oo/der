Touchdown Estate, Mountain Village, Telluride, Colorado Vacation Rental

 

Touchdown Estate is one of the most private properties in Mountain Village, yet only 175 yards to the ski run and a five-minute drive to the Village Core. This estate sits on the top of a knoll on nine acres of beautiful forest with a private tennis court and the only private indoor swimming pool in the Telluride area.

This majestic home offers over 15,000 square feet of luxury and high-end finishings and furnishings. Reminiscent of a European castle with wide hallways, vaulted ceilings, antique massive doors, separate private dinning room, great room with massive wood-burning fireplace and endless views from every room in the house. The kitchen is designed to cater to a large crowd with four ovens, two dishwashers, eat-in area, huge pantry and lots of space.

There are two very large master suites, each with huge bathrooms with steam showers, tubs, walk-in dressing rooms. The top floor is occupied by one master suite and also has a separate study. The main floor has the common areas and a queen bedroom (can be used as a separate nanny/pilot room) as well as the second master suite and a den.

The lower level of the home has a third master with a king, and three other guest bedrooms as well as a third den with large TV, surround sound and foosball table. The jewel of the home is a massive private pool which is separately enclosed (lock on door). The pool is extremely large by any standards and the room itself could compete with any top level spa in the world. In the spa/pool area there is also a large group steam room and separate bathrooms.

The home is surrounded by private decks and has a large deck with grill off of the great room. This home has probably the best all around views of any property in Telluride.
