West Pacific 340A, Telluride, Colorado Vacation Rental

 

With an unyielding attention to detail in an unbeatable location, intrepid travelers will appreciate everything that this recently completed residence in the Heart of Historic Telluride has to offer.

Located in the Gondola district one short block from the center of town on West Pacific Avenue. Enter the front door and step into a charming space where the high level of finish and the long view lines make it the natural spot for your group to gather. The kitchen was designed with both a family and a discerning chef in mind, providing ample space to gather and all of the tools needed to create your own delectable dinners at home.

The dining room provides seating for eight and the living room has a gas fireplace enhancing the ever-present mountain ambiance. The flat screen on the wall will ensure you catch your team's game or provides music to match the scene. A half-bath and kitchen pantry complete this main floor.

The lower level of the home features three bedrooms. The first bedroom at the bottom of the stairs has double bunk beds and twin bunk beds, flat screen on wall and use of the hall bath that has a stand-up shower. The second bedroom on the right also shares this hall bath and is also furnished with a queen bed and a gas fireplace. The third bedroom features a king bed, large closet, and en suite with a stand-up shower. The private laundry is on this level as is the door to the garage, leading to the alley and the one-block walk to the Gondola.

The top level of the home is where the master suite is located, featuring a king bed, TV and den with a pull-out couch, a private balcony with a hot tub boasting "the best view" in town, vaulted ceilings and up close views of rugged Ballard Peak. There are two closets and the master bath is finished with a large soaking tub, a rejuvenating steam shower and double vanity sinks.

Guests of this newest addition to Telluride's list of compelling homes can take advantage of free concierge services from the friendly, local staff of Sea to Ski. Whether tackling Telluride's legendary slopes or taking in one of its world-renowned festivals, the central location and quality spaces this property boasts will help ensure that your holiday is as ideal as possible.
