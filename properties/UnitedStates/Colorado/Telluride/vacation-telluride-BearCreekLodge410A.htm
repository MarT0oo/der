Bear Creek Lodge 410A, Telluride, Colorado Vacation Rental

 

Located in the heart of the most stunning ski and recreational resort on the planet, Bear Creek Lodge is the perfect luxury vacation resort for your entire family or group of any size!

This all-season mountain hideaway, complete with special event facilities for those who desire them, offers convenient access to shops, five-star restaurants, and the gondola, Telluride's unique free transportation system. Nestled in the core of Mountain Village, surrounded by snow-capped peaks, ski runs, hiking and biking trails, Bear Creek Lodge affords immediate access to every winter or summer recreational activity - from hiking to heli-skiing! While vacationing at Bear Creek Lodge, the attentive staff will cater to your every need. Enjoy the amenities and services of a five-star hotel, all while enjoying the spaciousness, convenience and comfort of a fully-equipped, luxury condominium--your home away from home! No need to squeeze your family into cramped hotel rooms! This slopeside property features a private funicular ski lift to whisk you right onto the slopes; indoor valet parking, a free, private shuttle, with driver, at your beck and call; an on-site fitness center; in-ground heated pool; massage room; hot tubs, sauna and steam rooms; game room and a stunning great room with a roaring fireplace! Gather around the huge, outdoor barbecue pit after a day of skiing, riding, biking or hiking. Enjoy a massage in the on-premise massage studio or in the privacy of your own home away from home!

The unit features complimentary wireless internet, four brand new flat screen HD televisions, an iPod docking station, DVD player, a fully equipped gourmet kitchen, washer/dryer, and a beautiful fireplace in the gorgeous, spacious, comfortably furnished living room. Relax after a day on the slopes and share a meal with family and friends in the privacy of your own home away from home! Or if you prefer, head out for a night on the town and enjoy close proximity to Telluride's nightlife!

The Bear Creek Lodge offers free wireless internet access, free outdoor parking, a fitness center, newly-remodeled outdoor year-round heated pool and hot tubs, grills, a fire pit, sauna and a free shuttle for guest use within Mountain Village. A funicular is located onsite for easy ski access to the Lower Village Bypass run in the winter.

The pool, hot tub and shuttle operation hours vary. Please consult the front desk for hours during your stay.
