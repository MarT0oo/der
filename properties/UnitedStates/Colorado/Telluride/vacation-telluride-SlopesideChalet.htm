Slopeside Chalet, Telluride, Colorado Vacation Rental

 

Privacy & Views Alpine ski-in/ski-out private home located trail-side on the Galloping Goose ski-run in Mountain Village, at the heart of the Tellluride ski resort. Adjacent to the National Forest at 10,001' altitude with spectacular mountain views. Surrounded by towering Aspens and Pines and hidden at the end of a private road for complete seclusion.

Winter Trail-side. 300' of direct ski-run frontage makes it easy to stop for lunch, hot chocolate, gear changes or a warm fireplace. Invite friends to stop Après-ski on the last run of the day. Book a ski instructor to ski to your back door in the morning to pick up the kids for lessons. When it starts to snow, pop in for powder skis or snowboards to take advantage of the fresh pow-pow. Enjoy 'fresh tracks' each morning as the first down the ski run, before the lifts even start turning!

Telski. World-class skiing with 18 lifts turning, 2 high-speed gondolas, 148 ski trails, Nastar course, terrain parks, back-country, hike-to, extreme & heli-ski terrain. Telski boasts the finest ski school in Colorado. Also, Nordic skiing, ice skating, snowmobiling, snowshoeing, sleigh rides, horseback riding and ice-climbing.

Mountain Village (population: 978) is Telluride's sister town at the heart of the ski area. A european-style alpine community nestled high above Telluride at 9,500+ elevation. The towns are connected by a free gondola system operating over the mountain tops. There is no need for a car as homeowner guests enjoy free SUV service (fleet of MB Sprinter vans) anywhere in Mountain Village.
