Dream House, Telluride, Colorado Vacation Rental

 

Dream House, the ultimate in Luxury, providing ski in and ski out for your family while keeping you in a heart of Mountain Village where you'll find the best skiing. This private and peaceful property with four bedrooms, five bathrooms above the ski resort sleeps up to twelve people.

The chalet brings a new standard of luxury with its open floor plan; majestic vaulted ceiling, well-appointed gourmet kitchen, fireplaces, cathedral windows and private outdoor hot tub. The third floor of this home is the master suite. This spacious room includes a huge master bath, king-size bed, lounging/reading area, and gas fireplace for the ultimate in relaxation.

Conveniences include a ski-in/out mudroom with ample storage for skiing equipment, complete with boot warmer. There are TVs throughout the home with wide ranging channel subscription, stereo and DVDs.

This ski-in and ski-out property is the “home away from home” that you have been dreaming about.
