Rocky Road, Telluride, Colorado Vacation Rental

 

Rocky Road is a bright and airy artist's retreat, sitting on a very private block of land just near the Bridges ski run. This 4 bedroom, 3.5 bathroom home sleeps up to 9 guests in total. On the upper level of the home is the kitchen, living & dining areas. The newly remodelled kitchen has high end details including granite countertops and stainless steel appliances. There is also a grand piano in this great room area. There are 3 guest suites on this upper level – the king master, and 2 queen suites. The king master en suite has a jetted tub and a steam shower. The other 2 queen guest suites share a bathroom. Downstairs, there is another king guest suite with an en suite bathroom, and a large games room with a billiards table, juke box & flat screen TV. Rocky Road also has a “zen den” which serves as an office/study.
