Horse Thief Mountain 103, Steamboat Springs, Colorado Vacation Rental

 

The Horse Thief Mountain Residence, located on the ground floor of this grand Steamboat's Mountain Village, has the perfect location with no elevators or staircases to climb. It has convenient access to the OSP community Game Room and Little Tykes room for the kids.

As a ground floor unit with a quiet location, the Horse Thief Mountain Residence is unique among other residences at One Steamboat Place. This exclusive private residence is only steps away from the Ski School gathering place, One Steamboat Place state-of-the-art fitness facility, pool and hot tubs, the community game room and Little Tykes room for the kids, and all Steamboat's recreational opportunities.

The spacious open floor plan connects the dining and living areas to the kitchen with plenty of room for a group to relax together.

The Viking appliances of the professionally equipped kitchen will delight the chef in your group.

The kitchen and dining area, adjacent yet separate from the living room, will allow you to enjoy relaxed meals just across from the majestic fireplace.

The master bedroom sets the mood with a gas fireplace, and the master bath features a steam shower with a rain shower head and Jacuzzi tub. Three other guest bedrooms with private bathrooms offer spacious, premium accommodations for an additional eight guests to comfortably accommodate 10 guests in total.

Enjoy all the signature touches throughout One Steamboat Place provided by Timbers Resorts including exquisite gathering areas, wine cellar and tasting room and one of the greatest mountain spas in the West. In addition to Steamboat's recreational opportunities, One Steamboat Place has a state-of-the-art fitness facility, pool and hot tubs, a community game room and Little Tykes room for the kids. With numerous in-residence catering options, free continental breakfast during the winter, concierge and ski valets, the Private Residences at One Steamboat Place have defined the first-class experience at Steamboat.
