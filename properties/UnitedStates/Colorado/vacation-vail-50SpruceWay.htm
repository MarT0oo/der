50 Spruce Way, Beaver Creek, Colorado Vacation Rental

 

This stunning estate is located on beautiful Lake Creek in Edwards. Just a short drive to Beaver Creek Resort, this property is perfect for those looking for a year-round rental as the Riverwalk and town of Edwards are just 1 mile down the road

Make this luxury estate your next home away from home. Available for a short term stay or a rental for 12+ months, this 5 bedroom single family home has all of the comforts you need to enjoy the Vail Valley year-round. With 1.3 acres of private property, including a private trout pond and Lake Creek running through the property, you can sit back and enjoy your natural surroundings. This home has great outdoor space, including a heated patio with gas outdoor fireplace and hot tub, flat green lawn, and a sitting area down by the creek.

In the winter, cozy up with a cup of tea and watch the snow fall quietly around out the 2 story floor to ceiling windows in the main living room. The gourmet kitchen intricately weaves granite, stone, copper and wood to create a space that will inspire even the most novice chef. The dining area and sitting room off of the kitchen create a great space for everyone to hang out while the pizza cooks in the pizza oven. The formal dining room comfortably seats 10, and the wet bar makes it easy to refill drinks during dinner. The main floor also holds the Master Suite, which includes a gas fireplace and stunning glass bathroom, which invites in scenic beauty of the surrounding forests. The master bedroom walks out to a private patio with it’s own outdoor fire pit.

Upstairs you will find high ceilings as you walk along the corridor to the recreation room. The rec room is a great space to hang out and watch the game or a movie, and includes a wet bar with wine cooler. Off of the rec room you will find a guest bedroom with Queen bed and full bathroom. Down the hall you will find a second master bedroom with King bed, gorgeous vaulted ceilings with exposed reclaimed wood beams and a full bath, complete with soaking tub. The second guest bedroom has 2 twin beds and a full attached bath, and the 3rd guest bedroom has a Queen bed and full bath.

Downstairs from the main level is where you will find the custom stone wine room, complete with tasting table. You can also relax at the end of a day with a movie in the theater room or kick start your morning with a work out in the exercise room. Walk out this level to the private hot tub on the heated patio, and enjoy an evening in the hot tub, next to the fire pit, listening to the babbling creek.

We also provide short-term rental guests with pre-paid visas to use for parking at both Vail and Beaver Creek during the ski season.

This home is truly a one of a kind rental estate in the Vail Valley. To make this your vacation retreat, give us a call today!
