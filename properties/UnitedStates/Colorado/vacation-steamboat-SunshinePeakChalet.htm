Sunshine Peak Chalet, Steamboat Springs, Colorado Vacation Rental

 

Newly constructed and located just a short walk from the heart of the Steamboat Ski village, Sunshine Peak Chalet offers fabulous views and a home-away-from-home for families or a group seeking a close connection to the mountain village.

This exceptional property offers a location close to the Steamboat mountain village, the comforts and privacy of a home and amenities that make it just plain fun to stay here.

The intimate great room allows you to gather and relax while taking in your surroundings before the gas fireplace. The spacious and open floor plan that connects the living spaces including the kitchen and dining area gives you room to breathe while together with family and friends.

Entertaining at home is a cinch with a fully equipped kitchen that boasts custom cabinetry, granite countertops, a large island, and Wolf gas range.

The dining room table seats ten with room for four at the bar so everyone can be together for meals.

Sunshine Peak Chalet offers four bedrooms with bedding options to sleep up to 13 guests. The master bedroom has a King bed and a large ensuite private bathroom. The guest master, also with private bath, offers a Queen bed with an optional roll-away twin bed and pack 'n play. Another bedroom offers two sets of twin bunk beds, each its own individual tv/dvd combo built in. The 4th bedroom offers the option of a king or two twin beds. The media room has a queen sleeper sofa with Tempurpedic mattress. All bedrooms include HD LED TV's. Smart TVs are in the great room, media room, and two guest suites with Wi-Fi access for streaming video from the day on the slopes, surfing the web, streaming a Netflix movie, or playing online apps.

The hot tub on the upper rear deck is the ideal place to soak away your day on the ski trails. Enjoy a Netflix movie, a 3D Blu-Ray movie, or play games on the Wii in the media room. The in-home Sonos audio system provides an unlimited streaming music library all controlled through an Iphone or Ipad throughout.

Complimentary private shuttle is included for convenient and speedy access to the ski trails.
