Butler Home, Snowmass, Aspen, Colorado Vacation Rental


This newly constructed Snowmass, CO luxury rental is perfectly located at walking distance from two runs: the upper Guggenheim Trail and Adams Avenue, so just take your pick of a run on those wonderful powder morning you have been dreaming of! And after a day of skiing on Snowmass Mountain, enjoy the Butler house, your new home away from home in Snowmass; check-out the spectacular views of the area and the Village, soak in the private outdoor hot tub or just cozy-up in front of the fireplace.


This Luxury newly constructed, 4 bedroom, 4.5 bath home with indirect Ski access for novice to expert skiers via the upper Guggenheim Trail. For beginner skiers, Adams's Avenue is about 220 yards down Wood Road.  The Master has a king bed, private bath, private deck, and 27" TV/DVD player.  The second guest has a King bedroom with private bath, private deck, and 15" LCD TV.  The third guest has a Split king bedroom with private bath, Love Seat that converts to a twin bed, private deck,  and 27" TV/DVD/VCR.  The forth guest has a Split king bedroom with private bath, private deck, and 15" LCD/DVD TV.


The Living room features gas fireplace, 32" TV/DVD/VCR/ surround sound/CD player.  There is also a formal dining room seats eight, bar area seating for four people.  The house has spectacular views of Snowmass area and Village.
