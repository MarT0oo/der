Highlands 2, Vail, Colorado Vacation Rental

 

Highlands Townhome 2 is a two story townhouse containing new furnishings and classic western accents to epitomize the modern country luxury villa. The large windows in every room capture much natural sunlight during the day and allow wonderful natural views. Whether embarking on an adventurous hike in the summer months or racing down the slopes in the winter, Highlands Townhome 2 offers its visitors an elegant ambiance in which to relax and rejuvenate.

When you’re ready for adventure, step out the back door, step into your skis and you’re quickly on your way to the Centennial Lift or the new Children’s Gondola and world renowned ski school. This convenient location provides true ski-in/ski out access at its best! In the summer, you can start your hikes right from home. Why not take a relaxing soak in the Jacuzzi after your day of activities?

Walk into the front entrance and you’ll enjoy an expansive view of the entire lower level. Make cocoa in the kitchen while overlooking family and friends while they play cards at the huge dining room table, or relax in the spacious and inviting living room, complete with soaring ceilings and a river rock fireplace. All throughout the villa, rich textures of wood and leather create a truly resplendent atmosphere.

All three bedrooms are quietly tucked away upstairs, separate from the lower level activities. Two individual master suites feature comfortable king beds and private baths. Children will especially enjoy the third bedroom, with two sets of log-cabin style bunk beds, a Play Station 2 game console and private bath. All bedrooms have their own flat screen TVs. Family friendly, Highlands Townhome 2 can accommodate up to eight guests.

Located in the heart of skiing country, various nature walks make great activities for the warmer months. The Red Sky Golf Club also resides nearby with its world renown Norman Course. In mid-summer, the Bravo! music festival showcases classical music ensembles for your listening pleasure. When you return from your forays onto the mountainsides, you will no doubt appreciate the warm luxuries that surround you at Highlands Townhome 2.
