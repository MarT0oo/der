Mountain View Residence 405, Vail, Colorado Vacation Rental

 

Mountain View Penthouse #405 is a brand new 4 bedroom luxury penthouse on the banks of Gore Creek steps away from Vail Village. This unique Vail, Colorado condo is perfect for those who want good Vail Village access while being in close proximity to all Golden Peak has to offer. Spectacular mountain views combined with Provencial mountain design and open living spaces create a truly ideal Vail vacation home! Featuring elegant flooring, luxe decor, vaulted ceilings, an on-site pool and a private roof top patio hot tub, guests find it difficult to leave this home at the end of a memorable ski vacation. In addition to the large family room with a flat screen tv, this home includes a fireside dining and formal living room, a powder room, a fully equipped kitchen with custom cabinetry, wolf brand gas range, slab granite counter tops, and breakfast bar seating. A deck right off of the formal living area offers panoramic views of Vail Village, Gore Creek, and Golden Peak. The lower level of this home contains 3 guest rooms, while the fourth guest room is on the second floor off of the family room.

Skiers and snowboarders can walk to the Vista Bahn (Lift #16) for quick Vail Village ski access or they can walk to Riva Bahn (Lift #6) at Golden Peak for fast access to ski school or the big bumps on the East side of Vail mountain.
