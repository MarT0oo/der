VAC Inc., Snowmass, Aspen, Colorado Vacation Rental


The VAC Inc. is 6 bedroom Snowmass Village luxury vacation rental conveniently located in Wood Run with direct access to the Funnel, right under Alpine Spring Lift. This beautiful luxury rental easily sleeps 16 guests and offers amenities sure to please you and your whole family or large group of friends. Take advantage of a fully equipped gourmet kitchen, a hot tub to relax in after a day out on the mountain and both a game room, with pool table and wet bar, as well as a family room to entertain people of all ages.


Luxury 6 bedroom, 6 bath home located in Wood Run with direct Ski Access- located on Funnel, right below Alpine Springs Ski Lift.  The Master bedroom with king bed, private bath, 27" and Flat Screen TV.  The Second Master bedroom with king bed, private bath and 27" Flat Screen TV.  A Queen bedroom with private bath and 25" Flat Screen TV.  Another Queen bedroom with private bath and 25" Flat Screen TV.  We then have one set of bunk beds and one twin with trundle with private bath.  There is also a double sleeper sofa in the office.


The house also features large den with pool table and bar and 30" Flat Screen TV.  The main living room has a 52" Flat Screen TV.  The family room is equipped with 42"Flat Screen TV and great for those who travel with small kids.  The gourmet kitchen features professional appliances and a great space for even the best of chefs.  Our formal dining room seats 10 people and is great for nights at home.
