Spruce Heights, Snowmass, Aspen, Colorado Vacation Rental


Spruce Heights is a beautiful Snowmass luxury ski-in/ ski-out, 6 bedroom vacation rental with direct, easy access onto Cascade Run in Two Creeks. With 5,500 square feet, this home offers a unique and exquisite decor entirely decorated by Thomas Bartlett of Napa valley and offers top of the line quality furnishings including various antiques and elegant artwork.

The gourmet, state of the art kitchen makes cooking for your friends and family easy and pleasurable. Additional amenities include 8 fireplaces throughout the house, private outdoor hot tub, sauna room, billiard room, as well as a family/media room.
