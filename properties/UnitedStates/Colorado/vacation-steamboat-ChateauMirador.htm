Chateau Mirador, Cordillera, Steamboat Springs, Colorado Vacation Rental

 

This stunning residence perched atop The Divide at Cordillera has sweeping views in every direction. This nearly 9,000 square foot luxury home features seven bedrooms, 8.5 bathrooms and sleeps up to 14 guests. Multiple levels and wings provide plenty of space to spread out and take in the 360-degree views of various mountain ranges.

A spectacular drive up to Cordillera ends through an archway into a heated courtyard. Entering the home, guests are treated to an exposed timber and stone great room with dual gas fireplaces, a formal dining area with seating for 12 and a sitting area with a flat-screen TV. Beyond the great room is the home office with a minibar and a powder room just off the hall. The kitchen features a breakfast table with seating for seven in front of a fireplace and additional counter seating for three. The kitchen boasts over-sized countertops, a Viking range, warming drawer, and dual sinks. Past the great room is a small sitting area and library that leads to a king bedroom with a crib, flat-screen TV, walk-in closet and en-suite bathroom with a walk-in shower.

The master suite is also on the main level just before the kitchen. The suite's foyer leads to the bathroom with his and her vanities, a Jacuzzi tub with mountain views, a steam shower and a closeted stacked washer and dryer. The master has a king bed, writing desk, gas fireplace, flat-screen TV with DVD player and stereo system.

From the main level up the curved staircase is an "in-law" suite with a queen bedroom, kitchen, sitting area, fitness equipment, and a full bathroom with a walk-in shower.

The four other bedrooms are all on the lower level, one of which is the junior master suite with king bed, flat-screen TV, walk-in closet, patio and en-suite bathroom with Jacuzzi tub and walk-in shower. A second bedroom has a queen bed, TV, walk-in closet and en-suite bathroom with a shower-tub combo. The third bedroom on this level has a king bed, walk-in closet, flat-screen TV and en suite bathroom with a shower-tub combo. The final bedroom features a twin daybed with twin trundle, walk-in closet and en-suite bathroom with a walk-in shower.

The lower level also has a family room with a full bar, game table for six, fireplace, ample seating and a flat-screen TV. There is a full bathroom off the family room with an over-sized steam shower. A custom wine cellar, as well as the main laundry room also live on this lower level of the home; and the laundry room has one washing machine and two dryers.

SKI ACCESS: Guests will need to drive to the base of Arrowhead, Beaver Creek or Vail Mountain to access the trails. The Arrowhead lot is 9.3 miles away, the parking garage at Beaver Creek 13 miles and the Vail parking structure is a 21-mile drive.
