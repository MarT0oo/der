Graystone Lodge, Steamboat Springs, Colorado Vacation Rental

 

Graystone Lodge is a spectacular new mountain home overlooking the Rollingstone Ranch Golf Course at the base of the mountain area. This newly built home is loaded with extras to accompany the magnificent alpine views that wrap around you.

With 5 bedrooms, generous floor plan and expansive views, Graystone Lodge delivers “wow” throughout. Luxurious furnishings, modern appliances and 5 star amenities abound in this spectacular home.

With vaulted ceilings, wide planked wooden floors and rock wall fireplace, the spacious great room and picture windows provide a magnificent venue to gather at the end of a day on the mountain. Leading off this room is a superior west-facing deck which bathes in the valley views; an inspiring location for outside dining in summer.

Above the fireplace the 50” Flat screen with Apple TV and 7:1 surround sound will provide entertainment to try to compete with the views.

The fully equipped professional kitchen provides an inviting setting for the creative chef to prepare delightful meals, to fuel your stay and dazzle your taste buds. A gas grill awaits the grill master on the covered main deck, allowing you to use this year-round.

The large dining table seats 10 with 5 additional seats at the adjacent breakfast bar, making this a great home for entertaining and hosting a large group.

All bedrooms have private bathrooms and TVs. The Master bedroom on the main level offers a California King bed and a 50” TV with surround sound. On the next floor down the second and third bedrooms offer a queen bed. The guest master also has a California King and another 50” TV. Finally the enormous bunk room, themed with cowboy charm, offers 3 sets of twin bunks (6 beds in total). This would be the perfect hang out zone for the younger ones in the group with, a TV, X-Box and Blu-Ray to keep them entertained.

The lower level of this home also offers a spectacular media and games room with pool table, 55” TV with 7:1 surround sound, Blu-Ray DVD player, X-box, along with a wet bar and microwave for popcorn.

If your day on the mountain doesn’t wear you out then the exercise room offers you three pieces of cardio equipment and a 50” TV to distract you from the pain (or pleasure). After you can relax in the adjacent dry sauna, or a soak in the hot tub, which is located off the lower level rec room.

The spacious 2 car garage offers a rack to accommodate skis and boards and boot dryers will have your footwear ready to go in the morning. Access to the ski mountain is easy with a private on-call ski shuttle, picked up right from your doorstep and only 5 minutes to the ski trails and mountain village.
