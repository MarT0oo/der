Chateau Two Creeks, Snowmass, Aspen, Colorado Vacation Rental


Chateau Two Creeks is located in Snowmass Colorado with indirect access to cascade Run, this Snowmass luxury ski property is the perfect home for large groups or families, as it sleeps up to 12 people in 5,500 square feet, has a large kitchen and dining room, a media room and so much more. This property makes it easy for you and your group to have all the space needed to enjoy and relax during your next ski vacation in Snowmass.

This Luxury 5 bedroom 5.5 bath home in Two Creeks with indirect ski access to the Cascade run.  This house is fun an contemporary flairs give it that really great resort feeling that we all need while vacationing.  The house features a Master with king bedroom and private bath, Jacuzzi tub, walk in steam shower, gas fireplace and 32" TV/DVD.  The first guest has King bedroom with sitting area, private bath with steam shower.  The Second Guest room has King bedroom with private bath and 30" TV.  The Third Guest Room has Queen bedroom with private bath.  The Fourth Guest Room has a Bunk room with twin on top and double on bottom and double bed with private bath and 32" TV/DVD.  These rooms are all designed especially with the skier in mind.

The Dining room is lovely with seating for 8 people, it is complimented by the large open kitchen with butcher block island, breakfast nook with seating for 4 people.  In addition there is a media room off the kitchen with 46" flat screen HD TV, a living room with gas fireplace and a private outdoor hot tub.
