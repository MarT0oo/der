Starwood Estate, Aspen, Colorado Vacation Rental

 

Incredible 5 bedroom estate situated in the prestigious, gated residential community of starwood located just minutes from downtown aspen.

The private home sits on 3 acres and features more than 13,000 ft² Of luxuriously appointed furnishings and decor, and has unobstructed views of all four aspen mountain peaks There’s a reason John Denver dubbed this place his snow-covered paradise. The majestic Rocky Mountain backdrop. The canopy of evergreens. The captivating mountain homes and winding roads.

Experience Starwood for yourself at Starwood Estate, a breathtaking 5-bed, 6.5-bath vacation home designed by rowland+broughton.

Starwood has maintained its off-the-beaten-path mystique, while also evolving into an exclusive setting for special events and celebrity vacations. Situated on three acres of land, Starwood Estate was once owned by Prince Bandar, Saudi Arabia’s Ambassador to the United States. It is now available for rent for the first time ever.

Inside, you’ll experience kick-off-your-boots charm, with inviting chairs and couches huddled around warm fireplaces. Meanwhile, floor-to-ceiling windows showcase Aspen’s Four Peaks and ensure the mountain scenery is as much a part of this home as its luxury appointments.

Designed for entertaining, Starwood Estate features a secondary catering kitchen with a dumbwaiter connected to the upper-level kitchen. Elevators allow guests to easily move between the first and second floors, and a heated patio is gorgeous across all seasons, as is its Jacuzzi tub. Each bedroom and living area offers access to the alpine paradise just outside. When the slopes call, a 2013 Denali SUV will be waiting in the garage to take you there.

Master Bedroom #1 – Upper Level:
Rest in a king-sized bed with an intricately carved four-post frame. Glass doors offer sweeping views of the mountains and open onto a private deck. Blackout curtains create privacy and an intimate fireplace lends warmth. Enjoy an enormous walk-in closet and en-suite bath with a Jacuzzi tub.

Guest Bedroom #1 – Main Level:
This spacious room offers a king-sized bed with rustic wooden frame and television. An en-suite bath features a Jacuzzi tub for premier relaxation.

Guest Bedroom #2 – Main Level:
Friends will adore this guest room with quaint wooden finishes including a large, artful armoire for storage. This bedroom offers two double beds, television and an en-suite bathroom.

Guest Bedroom #3 – Main Level:
Rustic furnishings reminiscent of a classic log cabin and plush throw pillows and blankets are featured in this bedroom with two double beds, a television and an en-suite bath.

Guest Bedroom #4 – Main Level:
This bedroom is an intimate nook brimming with comfort. A soft king-sized bed, oversized armchair, Victorian-style desk, television and en-suite bath are all found here.

Living Spaces:
This home’s vast and stunning upper-level living room has a gravitational pull: you’ll find your whole group gathering here. With floor-to-ceiling windows overlooking the majestic Rocky Mountains, you can take in the view from outside-facing armchairs. Or get closer to the view on the deck, which opens from this space.

Next door, the den/office space serves as a place to tackle real world responsibilities, and adjoins an enchanting sunroom in the treetops.

The main level atrium offers an alternate gathering space and access to a covered patio that opens onto a deck overlooking the mountains. Wander to the hot tub for truly special nights under a star-studded Colorado sky. On the lower level, a home theater offers sumptuous seating in front of a large flat-screen TV.

Kitchen & Dining:
Dine with ease, comfort and style at a round table with leather chairs. The dining area is on the upper level, just off of a gourmet-inspired kitchen with granite countertops and bar-style seating. The second catering kitchen can be found on the lower level.

Alfresco meals are something special at this Aspen home. An outdoor dining area features a wooden table and the snowcapped peaks of the Rockies offer an ideal, John Denver-approved backdrop.
