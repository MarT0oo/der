225 Forest Road, Vail, Colorado Vacation Rental

 

This home is 3,600 square feet, has 5 bedrooms, and is located on Forest Road between Lionshead and Vail Village. Guests can easily walk to either Village or to the Bear Tree Run on Vail Mountain for Ski & Trail Access

Once you have stayed a week at 225 Forest Road you will discover why many guests of this outstanding ski residence have to reserve this property year after year. The welcoming atmosphere, spaciousness, and fabulous views all combine to make it a perfect location for a Vail vacation, winter or summer.

The main level features a large living room with a massive rock fireplace, large flat screen TV w/DVD, dining room for 8, and an exceptionally large kitchen that opens up to the living room with a bar that seats 5. The living and dining areas have large picture windows with beautiful views of the forest land. There are two guest rooms on this main level, one bedroom with a king bed, and a second bedroom with a split king bed.

The lower level has three more guest rooms with full bathrooms. The first room contains a king bed, the second bedroom has two queen beds, and the final guest bedroom houses two twin beds. The den, great for family gatherings, is also on the lower level and has a large flat screen TV, DVD player, and stereo. Just outside is a large deck with access to the pool during the summer months and a Jacuzzi all year long.
