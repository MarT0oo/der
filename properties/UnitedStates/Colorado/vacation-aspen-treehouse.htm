Treehouse, Snowmass, Aspen, Colorado Vacation Rental


Located at the top of Faraway road, this luxury ski-in/ski-out estate has direct access to the Two Creeks ski run. Sit on your expansive deck and gaze at your amazing mountain views or soak in your private outdoor hot tub after a long day of skiing. Some of your luxurious amenities include a built in BBQ, vaulted ceilings with wood beams, bedroom suites with private baths, family room with 55” flat screen TV, hardwood floors and wireless internet.


Luxury 5 bedroom, 5 and 2 half bath home with direct ski in/ski out to Two Creeks.  The Master bedroom with king bed, jacuzzi tub and steam shower, 42" flat screen, private deck, gas and fireplace.  The second King bedroom has a  private bath, 32" TV and gas fireplace.  The third King bedroom has a private bath, 32" TV and gas fireplace.  The forth Twin bedroom has a private bath.  The bunk room sleeps 4 with private bath and is great for kids or singles.


In the Main living area you have a wood burning fireplace and 55" flat screen TV with wet bar and floor to ceiling windows.  The family room has a 55" flat screen TV.  The Gourmet Kitchen features granite counter tops and Subzero refrigerator.
