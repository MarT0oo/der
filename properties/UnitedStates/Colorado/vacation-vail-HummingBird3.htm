Humming Bird 3, Vail, Colorado Vacation Rental

 

This lavish alpine condo was made for those looking for one of the most sought after ski-in/ski-out vacation rentals in Colorado. Avid skiers will be amazed at the ultimate convenience offered by Hummingbird A1 in Bachelor Gulch—mid-mountain, and directly level with the slopes. Glide directly from the condo’s private ski door to the base of the express chairlift and enjoy the neighboring Roughneck and Cabin Fever ski runs, as well as the Evercrisp skiway.

Stop in at Hummingbird A1 for a lunch break and let your gear warm up on the complimentary boot heaters and dryer. Fully refreshed, you will be back on the slopes in no time! Or feel free to whittle away the hours in the lodge’s private clubhouse, hot tub, fitness room and games room. Internet access is also available. Outdoors by the fire pit or indoors by the fireplace, Hummingbird A1 is sure to soothe the soul. Hummingbird’s fine attention to detail includes high-end furnishings, wool carpeting, butternut doors and cabinetry, top-of-the-line appliances, natural stone wainscoting and slab marble countertops. Equally extraordinary are the private decks and patios offering brilliant panoramas of the mountain scenery. In the Great Room, you will find large windows, wide-plank hickory floors and a floor-to-ceiling granite fireplace that features a wrap-around steel mantel.

Four fabulous bedrooms accommodate up to fourteen guests at Hummingbird A1. All bedrooms feature en-suite bathrooms and the master bath features a Jacuzzi. Air conditioning is also available for the warmer months. The roughly hewn bunk beds in the children’s room are a pièce de résistance.

Located in the heart of skiing country, various hiking trails and nature walks make great activities for the warmer months. The Red Sky Golf Club is also located nearby with its world renown Norman Course. In mid-summer, the Bravo! festival welcomes top caliber classical music ensembles. However you spend your time in Colorado, you will appreciate the comfort and resplendence offered at Hummingbird A1 all year long.
