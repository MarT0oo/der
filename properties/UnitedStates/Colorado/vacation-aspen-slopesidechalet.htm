Slopeside Chalet, Snowmass, Aspen, Colorado Vacation Rental


The Chalet at Slopeside is the perfect Snowmass, CO deluxe ski property. Located in the Pines, the house has direct ski access to Snowmass Mountain through Two Creeks. This 5 bedroom deluxe property has numerous fireplaces, a private outdoor hot tub, a media room and enough space to sleep 12 people comfortably, making it the perfect Snowmass deluxe ski property for a family vacation.

This Duluxe five bedroom, five bathroom home located in the Pines near Aspen Colorado in Snowmass with direct ski access onto Two Creeks.  The house is perfect for those who enjoy skiing daily and who really want the best ski lodge possible that will give them quick access to the slopes.  The house has all of the things you would expect from a luxury skiing vacation home.

The Master bedroom features a king bed, outdoor patio, private bath and Jacuzzi tub, gas fireplace, and 27" TV.  The first Guest features a queen bedroom with private bath and Jacuzzi tub, gas fireplace and 27" TV.  The second Guest features a queen bedroom with private bath, 13" TV.  The third Guest features one set of bunk beds (2 twins) and double bed with private bath.  The forth Guest features a  set of bunk beds (twin on top, double on the bottom) and double bed with private bath.

The Living Room with  wouldn’t be complete without the  61" Plasma which makes watching your games, soap operas or listening to some great music videos in the morning, a perfect thing to do.  Nearby you have the wet bar and wood burning fireplace, as well you have the family room with gas fireplace.  The house also features a media room with 27" TV and gas fireplace for those who want to get away from the group and maybe watch something on their own, also great for kid and cartoon time. 

Outside you have the private outdoor hot tub, the grill with snowmelt patio, a 2 car garage with snowmelt driveway, and a great view all the way around.  You have over 5,600 Sq. feet to run around in, which is more than plenty for up to 12 guests.
