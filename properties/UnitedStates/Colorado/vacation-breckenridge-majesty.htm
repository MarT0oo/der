Alpine Majesty, Breckenridge, Colorado Vacation Rental


There is something special about Alpine Majesty with 5 levels of post and beam glamorous log charm. Alpine Majesty a premier Colorado Rocky Mountain luxury vacation rental ski haus rises five stories and 6500 square feet. Once you’ve stayed, you will return year after year - winter or summer beckons in your dreams.

On the first level park in an oversized 3-car heated garage. The driveway flat pad is heated, allowing safe passage for guests between the home and outside transportation. Front or 4 wheel drive is necessary in winter.

The second level has the family media room with large screen LCD TV, wet bar with icemaker, beverage refrigerator, microwave, pool table and LCD TV. A 'Cub's Den' (bunk room perfect for the kids) with four bunk beds and TV/DVD off the family media room, has a bathroom with large shower. Laundry closet on this level.

The third level of this new luxury home is beautifully arranged with a gorgeous panoramic Great Room with exposed log beams, stone fireplace and beautiful hickory floors; large kitchen with Viking and GE Monogram appliances. The separate dining room has a large table and seats 8. Great Room deck featuring log railings, barbeque grill, private hot tub and outdoor furniture. Off the great room is a bedroom with a queen size bed and TV.

Take the stairs to the fourth level, where you will find a bridge that overlooks the interior moss rock fireplace. Smokers - You will also find the exterior cigar porch over the front entry with a gas infrared heater. There is another laundry closet off the cigar porch with front-load washer and a front load gas dryer. On this level you will find two suites with their own Jacuzzi tubs. The North suite has a king size bed, TV, private bathroom and private balcony. The South suite has a king size bed with a private bathroom.

The fifth level consists of the Loft Suite. The suite (perfect for brides-to-be) has a king size bed and sleeper sofa, walk in closet, and private balcony. There is also a large sitting area with love seat, sleeper sofa and TV. The 'Sunrise' deck is perfect for your early morning coffee. The luxury bath with moss rock and patina copper accents, has a huge double shower and Jacuzzi tub.

Decks and balconies are located on all sides of this custom designed home. The hot tub, located off the great room and on the great room deck, seats 6 people. Featuring deck furnishings, log railings, stone walls; this deck can be enjoyed year round. Area Information

Every season in Breckenridge is magnificent. Winter in Breckenridge is every skier’s dream with its champagne powder days and thousands of acres of ski terrain. Breckenridge is less than a two-hour drive west of Denver, and is one of the most popular ski resorts in the U.S The majestic, stunning mountain vistas of Breckenridge’s 13,000-foot peaks along its ten-mile mountain range thrills every guest. The winter season not only boasts spectacular ski conditions, but an array of activities, festivals and ski/snowboard competitions.

In the summer months, most Breckenridge guests stay outdoors from sunrise to sunset. Whether playing golf, sightseeing, hiking, biking, rafting, fishing, horseback riding, or even gliding down a mountain glacier, there are endless options for everyone. Downtown Breckenridge presents a full calendar of outdoor summer festivals, along with galleries, shops, restaurants, bars and nightlife.
