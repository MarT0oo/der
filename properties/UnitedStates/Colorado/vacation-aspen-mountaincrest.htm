    The Mountain Crest Lodge is a brand new, luxury Snowmass vacation rental, featuring breathtaking views and convenience to the slopes. Indulge yourself and your friends and family in this spacious home featuring fabulous amenities such as a outdoor built in grill, outdoor hot tub, media room with game table and a bunk room featuring individual flat screen TV's . Gorgeous views and expansive deck await your arrival for a one of a kind vacation.


    Luxury 6 bedroom, 6.5 bath home located in the heart of The Pines with Direct Ski Access.  The Master bedroom with king bed, gas fireplace, private bath with Jacuzzi tub, large steam shower and 46' flat panel TV.  The 2nd Guest is a King bedroom with gas fireplace, private bath with Jacuzzi tub, large steam shower, private balcony and 46' flat panel TV.  The 3rd Guestroom is a King bedroom with gas fireplace, private bath with Jacuzzi tub and 32' flat panel TV.  The 4th Guestroom is a Queen bedroom with gas fireplace, private bath with shower and 46' flat panel TV.  The 5th Bedroom is a bunk room with 4 Queen Captains' Bunks each with personal 20' flat panel TVs, private bath with shower.  And then we have an office with Queen Murphy Bed and private bath.


    Our Large Great Room has gas fireplace, wet bar, 42' flat panel TV and expansive deck.  The Gourmet Kitchen features granite counter tops, double ovens and a breakfast nook that seats 8.  That is aside our lovely Dining Room that seats 10 for your at home family meals.  The house includes a lovely Media Room with a wet bar, game table,46' flat panel TV, Wii video game console and so much more.


    Additionally our Main Level deck boasts a built in grill and gas fireplace.  The lower deck boasts a gas fireplace and hot tub.
