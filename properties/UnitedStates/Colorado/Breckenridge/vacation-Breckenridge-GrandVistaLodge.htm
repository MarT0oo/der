Grand Vista Lodge, Breckenridge, Colorado Vacation Rental

 

Perched above Breckenridge on Peak 10 with over 6500 square feet designed in a modern mountain design offers the height of sophistication in accommodations and amenities. Upscale travelers seeking a unique experience with revel in the 7 bedrooms, open floor plan living room with vaulted ceilings, exposed beams and stunning views of Breckenridge and Baldy Mountain.

The extraordinary commitment of the owners to create the ultimate mountain home with their recent remodel that conveys a real sense of privacy and serenity succeeded magnificently. Located a mere 5 minute drive from Breckenridge on Peak 10 at the end of a cul-de-sac. A mere 5 minute walk to the trail access for the ski area for winter downhill skiing and riding activities and summer hiking or mountain biking. In winter the ski trial ski out access is to the Beaver Run six chair or Quicksilver chair for experienced skiers on non-groomed trails. Ski in access to Grand Vista Lodge is on expert trails only.

Area Information

Every season in Breckenridge is magnificent. Winter in Breckenridge is every skier’s dream with its champagne powder days and thousands of acres of ski terrain. Breckenridge is less than a two-hour drive west of Denver, and is one of the most popular ski resorts in the U.S The majestic, stunning mountain vistas of Breckenridge’s 13,000-foot peaks along its ten-mile mountain range thrills every guest. The winter season not only boasts spectacular ski conditions, but an array of activities, festivals and ski/snowboard competitions.

In the summer months, most Breckenridge guests stay outdoors from sunrise to sunset. Whether playing golf, sightseeing, hiking, biking, rafting, fishing, horseback riding, or even gliding down a mountain glacier, there are endless options for everyone. Downtown Breckenridge presents a full calendar of outdoor summer festivals, along with galleries, shops, restaurants, bars and nightlife.
