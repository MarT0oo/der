GoldenView Lodge, Breckenridge, Colorado Vacation Rental

 

The peaks of the Breckenridge Ski Area and the magnificent Baldy mountain await you at Goldenview Lodge. Soar above the treetops in this exquisite custom log home with amenities galore. Open floor plan on main level with great room, dining, kitchen and access to expansive decks all surrounded by towering evergreens complete the woodsy atmosphere of GoldenView Lodge. Inside the home, massive logs, post and beam work, plank floors all remain true to the Colorado luxury log home. Guests can gather around the massive river rock gas fireplace in the great room for a welcoming warmth after a day of adventure in Breckenridge.

GoldenView Lodge is situated on three levels featuring six bedrooms and four and a half baths – sleeping up to 16 total. Enter through the front door into the large entryway to the expansive Great Room with floor to ceiling windows and access to the Deck. The Great Room features Gas Fireplace, Flat Screen TV and Wet Bar with wine refrigerator. Dining Area and Gourmet Kitchen sit adjacent to Great Room. The Dining Table can seat up to 10 with additional seating at the game/breakfast table located in the Great Room. The Kitchen features granite countertops, custom cabinetry, two ovens and six-burner gas stove. Butcher Block island completes the space and allows for a great place to gather!

The Mother Lode Master Suite is located off the Great Room on the Main Level and features a King Bed and Ensuite Bath with two sinks, Jacuzzi tub and separate walk-in shower. The Suite also features flat screen TV and access to the expansive deck. On the deck you’ll find the private outdoor hot tub, wood-burning fireplace and BBQ Gas Grill. There is also an outdoor dining table as well as comfortable seating to enjoy the tranquil forest.

On the Upper Level you’ll find Fools Gold Queens Bedroom (sleeps 4 in two queen beds) and the Silver Vein King Bedroom (sleeps 2 in king bed). These bedrooms share a hall bathroom.

Moving to the Lower Level of GoldenView Lodge, you’ll find more fun amenities: Billiards Table, Den with Flat Screen TV, Wet Bar and another Large Deck. Great space for Kids of all ages to relax and create wonderful memories together. You’ll find the remaining three bedrooms on this level. The Quartz Queen Lode Bedroom (sleeps 2 in 2 Queen Beds) sits adjacent to the Billiards Table and the Copper Tailings King Suite (sleeps 2 in King Bed) and Coyote Hold Bunk Room (sleeps 6 in 2 captain’s bunks).

Area Information

Every season in Breckenridge is magnificent. Winter in Breckenridge is every skier’s dream with its champagne powder days and thousands of acres of ski terrain. Breckenridge is less than a two-hour drive west of Denver, and is one of the most popular ski resorts in the U.S The majestic, stunning mountain vistas of Breckenridge’s 13,000-foot peaks along its ten-mile mountain range thrills every guest. The winter season not only boasts spectacular ski conditions, but an array of activities, festivals and ski/snowboard competitions.

In the summer months, most Breckenridge guests stay outdoors from sunrise to sunset. Whether playing golf, sightseeing, hiking, biking, rafting, fishing, horseback riding, or even gliding down a mountain glacier, there are endless options for everyone. Downtown Breckenridge presents a full calendar of outdoor summer festivals, along with galleries, shops, restaurants, bars and nightlife.
