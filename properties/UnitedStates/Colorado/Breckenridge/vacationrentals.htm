About Breckenridge

Most think of Breckenridge as just a great place to ski, a part of the Rocky Mountains that most would say offers a more balanced family vacation, aside from the hustle and bustle of the bigger ski destinations in Colorado and in the Mountains.  If you wanted a vacation home, this would be the place to buy it, not only affordable but beautiful. 

However, what the area is really known, are the arts.  Breckenridge is host to a variety of art fairs and art shows each year, bringing artists and buyers alike.

This is really the heart of Breckenridge during the summer you can enjoy the Ice Sculpting Championship and that is just a start, in the summer months you can find the Full Orchestra at the Riverwalk Center Downtown and the Breckenridge July Arts Festival the weekend of 4th of July, the Breckenridge Mainstreet Festival normally held the end of July and Gathering of the Great Divide Arts Festival held in September.  The Arts are very important and unique in this area and charming for those who really had no idea. 

For the people who come for summer outdoors activities we have: The Annual Imperial Challenge Breck’s Triathlon not for the faint of heart, The 5 Peaks North America's biggest ski mountaineering race with 10,000 feet of climbing and of course the Spring Fever Month where we celebrate the human body as it changes from winter to summer months.  The Spring festivals include concerts and lots of outdoor activities, including skiing and mountain biking.

And for those who love to dine, you can enjoy some 5 star quality in Le Petit Paris for French Cuisine or Mimi’s Fried Pies for those looking for a family atmosphere and then last but not least Modis for that Bar Feeling with a welcoming touch.  No matter where you want to eat, Breckenridge offers it all!

This area offers lots of affordable Villas, Chalets, Lodges, Cottages, and luxury condos for your next vacation in the Rocky Mountains.

 

Arriving at Breckenridge:  Fly into Denver Colorado International Airport (90 Miles Approximately).  Plan to fly in the daytime to avoid night driving in strange areas.
