A Mine Shaft, Breckenridge, Colorado Vacation Rental

 

A Mine Shaft on Peak 8 in Breckenridge reflects the golden era of Colorado mining in a bold and glorious fashion. Closest home to the main chairlifts: Colorado SuperChair and Rocky Mountain SuperChair – Approximately 30 steps and you are on a semi-private skiway that leads you to the Claimjumper trail and the chairlifts. Ski out of the home and in 1-2 minutes you can be on the Colorado SuperChair or Rocky Mountain SuperChair. It doesn’t get any better than this!

Unique features of this incredible home are reclaimed Wyoming snow fence, rejuvenated oak floors from a barn in Kentucky, bar foot rails from a Montezuma Colorado mine ore cart, banisters fashioned from recycled ski lift cable, forged copper accents, handmade front door from Durango, interior old growth distressed Douglas fir beams, skip-sawed wood siding intertwined with cross timbers and rusted metal siding. All elements combine to form a symphony that embraces mountain mining history, rustic while comfortable and livable. The owner calls it “an artistic creation”. We think he’s right!

Area Information

Every season in Breckenridge is magnificent. Winter in Breckenridge is every skier’s dream with its champagne powder days and thousands of acres of ski terrain. Breckenridge is less than a two-hour drive west of Denver, and is one of the most popular ski resorts in the U.S The majestic, stunning mountain vistas of Breckenridge’s 13,000-foot peaks along its ten-mile mountain range thrills every guest. The winter season not only boasts spectacular ski conditions, but an array of activities, festivals and ski/snowboard competitions.

In the summer months, most Breckenridge guests stay outdoors from sunrise to sunset. Whether playing golf, sightseeing, hiking, biking, rafting, fishing, horseback riding, or even gliding down a mountain glacier, there are endless options for everyone. Downtown Breckenridge presents a full calendar of outdoor summer festivals, along with galleries, shops, restaurants, bars and nightlife.
