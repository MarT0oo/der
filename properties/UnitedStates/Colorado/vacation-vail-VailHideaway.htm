Vail Hideaway, Vail, Colorado Vacation Rental

 

Fully renovated in 2016, this private mountain-modern home sits on an acre of land in between Gore Creek and the only private pond in Vail. The house itself is located on one of just three Vail Mountain streets, between Vail Village and Lionshead.

Tucked away amongst a forest of towering pines and surrounded by water on three sides, this home on the exclusive Beaver Dam will make you feel like you’ve found your own modern-day oasis. Enjoy views of Gore Creek, Vail’s only private pond and a quaint, babbling brook. Walls of windows throughout make you feel like you are in a luxurious treehouse.

Indoors and out, gorgeous views abound. Kick off your snow boots for a little après-ski, soak in the hot tub or enjoy your afternoon cuppa on the outdoor deck as you take in the scenery: sun filtering through the trees; fresh mountain air; water rushing over rocks…utter perfection.

Inside, sleek modern design and a palette of matte grays combines with ski-lounge-worthy furnishings to make a truly modern mountain retreat. Completely renovated in 2016, the house boasts four en-suite bedrooms, many with stunning forest views, plus a bunk room with full bath in the hallway. A cozy media room with huge flat-screen TV and mini bar beckons after a day of hitting the slopes, and the stunning kitchen with professional range, sunken sink and wine fridge is as functional as it is pretty.

Nestled in Beaver Dam in the heart of Vail – one of the most coveted locations in all of Colorado’s ski towns – the house is just a short walk from the slopes. You’re located just minutes from Vail Village and Lionshead Village, where you’ll find gourmet restaurants and top-notch shopping. Ski enthusiasts will love the world-class slopes and gorgeous views, but there’s something for all seasons: hiking, mountain biking, and fly fishing are perfect summer activities. Or visit during the holidays for a true White Christmas among the sparkling lights of Vail.

About The Area

Home to the iconic Rocky Mountains, it’s no wonder that Colorado is the number one destination for ski trips in the United States. With 21 first-class ski resorts scattered throughout the Rockies, ski and snowboard enthusiasts travel far and wide each winter to experience the mountains firsthand. Colorado is truly a year-round destination, however, with renowned dining, shopping, and golfing bringing in tourists long after the ski season has ended.

Featuring several world-class ski resorts, such as Vail, Aspen and Beaver Creek, Colorado is firmly grounded as one of the most sought after luxury vacation spots for affluent travelers. Given a plethora of luxury mountain cottages, villas, chalets and rental homes, it’s no surprise Colorado is often host to celebrities and royalty.
