Firelight Lodge, Vail, Colorado Vacation Rental

 

Ski in and out of your own luxurious private condo at Firelight Lodge 302. Part of Beaver Creek Village, the four-bedroom villa is set at the top of Bachelor Gulch, so you can enjoy mountain views and a ski run at your doorstep. To reach other ski lifts, dining and the spa, simply take the complimentary shuttle.

Leave your car in the villa’s heated underground parking garage and your gear in the ski locker room and let the private elevator whisk you upstairs. The property has its own deck with a gas barbecue that’s equally welcoming after a day of golf or skiing, as well as a shared swimming pool and hot tub. Relax in front of the TV or stereo system, post shots of the day’s best runs via Wi-Fi and refresh equipment in the washer and dryer. When it’s time to head back out, be sure to use the boot warmers before stepping into the snow.

Large windows with panoramic views of the Gore Range and vaulted ceilings at Firelight Lodge 302 make the villa feel bright and open, while a stone fireplace and leather and wood furnishings keep it cozy. The great room, which includes the living room, dining room and kitchen, has plenty of spots to curl up after a day outdoors, from leather sofas around the fireplace to a dining table that seats eight. The state-of-the-art kitchen is fully equipped and has a breakfast bar along one of its granite countertops.

The villa’s four suite-style bedrooms, each with an en-suite bathroom, are perfect for families or groups of friends. There is one master suite with a king bed, ceiling fan and panoramic mountain views, two bedrooms with queen beds and one bedroom with two twin bunk beds. Wood trim, deep natural tones and leather accents continue the luxury-cabin vibe in the bedrooms.

If you’ve come to Vail for the slopes, Firelight Lodge 302 is right on Roughlock run, about 4 miles from Arrow Bahn Express Lift 17, 5.5 miles from Lower Beaver Creek Mountain Express Lift 15 and Riverfront Express Gondola 7, 6 miles from Beaver Creek Ski Resort and 7 miles from Bachelor Gulch Express Lift 16. If you’ll be visiting during the summer, the villa is about 4 miles from Country Club of the Rockies, about 8 miles from Beaver Creek Golf Club, Beaver Creek Village and Beaver Lake Trail, just over 8 miles from Vail Valley and about 9 miles from Eagle Vail Golf Club. If you’ll be flying into the area, choose from Eagle County Regional Airport, about 24 miles away, or Aspen-Pitkin County Airport, about 87 miles away.
