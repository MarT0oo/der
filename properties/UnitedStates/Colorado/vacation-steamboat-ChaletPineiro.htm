Chalet Pineiro, Steamboat Springs, Colorado Vacation Rental

 

Picture yourself enjoying a glass of wine with a great view of the ski mountain as you and your family or friends relax in Chalet Pineiro. This delightful 5 bedroom home offers space for a large group, or two families to stay together in comfort with privacy for all, yet close to the action of the ski village.

Chalet Pineiro is located in a friendly mountain neighborhood that will make you feel right at home. There is plenty of room for your whole family to relax and truly enjoy Steamboat Springs and the surrounding areas.

The open and airy living room is the gathering center of the house. Everyone will enjoy the vaulted ceiling, large rock fireplace and gigantic windows to absorb the natural beauty of the surrounding mountains. There is plenty of space to relax and talk about your day’s adventures on the ski slopes in this stunning room.

Just off the living room is the gourmet kitchen. Any chef will be delighted to use the gas range to cook up a delicious meal for your famished group after a day of mountain adventure. There is plenty of counter space to spread out and all the cookware you will need.

Surrounded by windows, the round dining table with seating for 10 is the perfect setting for any meal your chef prepares. Located as part of the open living and kitchen area no one will be left out to compliment the chef! For a larger group, the kitchen island has seating for 4 more at the counter.

When the day winds down, you can relax in any of the 5 bedrooms. Upstairs there is the Master bedroom with access to the large main deck. The master bathroom includes a large jetted bath tub to soothe sore muscles, 2 sinks and a shower. Also upstairs there is a bedroom with Queen bed and ensuite bathroom. Going downstairs there is a bedroom with a Twin bunk bed and room with a Full and Twin bed and a final bedroom with Queen bedroom. These 3 bedrooms and lower living room share 2 bathrooms.

Located on the lower level there is a private tv room if your group isn’t all in the main living area upstairs. Just off the lower living is your private hot tub to soothe any aches and pains from the day’s adventures, while enjoying a view out to the valley. This home includes everything you would expect from a luxury home, including, free Wi-Fi, washer/dryer, plush towels, and luxurious bath robes. Moving Mountains’ homes are fully stocked with essentials like laundry detergent, dishwasher tablets, cling film, foil wrap, sandwich bags, trash bags and paper products as well as our premium all-natural toiletries so everything you need is on-hand.

The spacious 2 car garage and mudroom offer plenty of space to store all your gear. Our private ski shuttle will collect you from the driveway and run you to lifts and back in the daytime, and to downtown or shopping in the evening so a rental car is not needed here.
