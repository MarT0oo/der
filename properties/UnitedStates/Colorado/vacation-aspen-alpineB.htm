Alpine Cottage B, Aspen, Colorado Vacation Rental


Are you looking for an Aspen luxury vacation home rental? Look no further! the Alpine Cottage B is where you want to stay for your next Aspen Vacation. Located 5 blocks from downtown and 1 block from the free shuttle, this home offers all the luxury and practical amenities needed to make it your home away from home; amazing amounts of space, a gourmet kitchen, a media room, hardwood floors, a hot tub and so much more. Book it now with Alpine Properties.

Luxury 4 bedroom plus media room 4.5 bath home located on the east side of town off Alpine Ct just five blocks from Downtown Aspen, one block from free skier shuttle making easy access to town & slopes.  The house features gourmet kitchen with granite counters, breakfast bar, stainless steel sub zero refrigerator and freezer and appliances, 2 thermador stoves, and dumbwaiter.  There is also a dining room seats 8.

The Living area features a 42" HD flat screen TV, tumbled granite fireplace and pine doors and trim.  The Master bedroom features a King bed, gas fireplace, 27” TV, private alcove, large walk in closet, outdoor hot tub on deck, private bath with Jacuzzi tub and Steam shower.  The second Guest Master Bedroom features a Queen bed, 19” TV, gas fireplace, private bath with Jacuzzi tub and Steam shower.  The third Guest Bedroom features a Double bed, 12” TV, private bath with tub and shower.  The fourth Guest Bedroom features two twin beds, private bath on the same level shower only. 

There is a media room with 42” flat screen, gas fireplace, wet bar, stairmaster, and a Queen pullout couch.
