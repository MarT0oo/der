Highlands 1, Vail, Colorado Vacation Rental

 

Highlands 1 provides all the comforts and convenience required for a picturesque winter getaway. This luxury condo is situated in the heart of the ski haven Beaver Creek. Just steps from the Centennial Lift, you’ll be hitting the slopes in no time! Beginners will appreciate the villa’s proximity to the world renowned ski school, and parents will be happy to know that the new Children’s Gondola is just minutes from your door.

When hunger hits, the large open kitchen contains stainless steel appliances, sleek finishes and a breakfast bar. Or go more casual with a barbecue on your private patio. To truly unwind, take a well deserved soak in the outdoor hot tub. In the warmer months, you can lounge by the pool or tee off at the lovely golf course nearby. The lodge also includes Internet access and a home entertainment system for your convenience and enjoyment.

After a day on the slopes, make yourself at home in the living room—plunk down on the leather sofa and cuddle up next to the river rock fireplace with a cup of tea. The dining room table is perfect either for a gourmet meal, a board game or a round of cards. All throughout the villa, rich wooden floors and furniture instill a feeling of bucolic tranquility on visitors.

This cozy townhouse is one of the most family friendly vacations rentals in Colorado. Three bedrooms accommodate up to eight guests. The main bedrooms contain two well appointed king beds, and all bedrooms feature en-suite bathrooms. Kids, and the young-at-heart, will delight in the two sets of log-cabin inspired bunk beds, along with a Play Station that includes two game controllers.

Located in a premiere skiing area, the warmer months offer hiking trails and nature walks for various skill levels. The Red Sky Golf Club is also located nearby with its world class Norman Course. Look for the Bravo! music festival in mid-summer that welcomes top caliber classical ensembles to chime their celestial notes across the mountainside. Just minutes from Vail Village, at Highlands 1 you are perfectly poised for the ultimate alpine holiday!
