Elk Ridge Retreat, Snowmass, Aspen, Colorado Vacation Rental


Located in the desirable Ridge Run neighborhood of Snowmass Village this home is located on Elk Ridge Lane, a very private and peaceful street. The main level has an open floor plan with cathedral ceilings and French doors that open the home up to the breeze of the mountain air. The Master Suite has a fireplace, private deck, and spacious master bathroom. The kitchen opens up to the dining room and great room which is perfect for entertaining family and friends. The outdoor living space is perfect for entertaining with a large wood burning fireplace, hot tub, and beautifully landscaped lawn and patios.

Master with King and 32” TV and private bath with Jacuzzi tub and gas fireplace, queen with private bath and gas fireplace, queen with private bath, captains bunk with full on bottom with private bath.  Additionally our living room with wood burning fireplace is great for those late nights where you want to unwind with family and friends.  AS a bonus there is a media room with 32” flat screen TV and gas fireplace, Billiard table, Outdoor hot tub with wood burning fireplace and a 2 car garage.
