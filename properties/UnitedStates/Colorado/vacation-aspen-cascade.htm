Cascade Lodge, Snowmass, Aspen, Colorado Vacation Rental


The Cascade Lodge, a Snowmass luxury ski vacation rental, is located in the Two Creeks area and offers direct ski access onto Cascade Run. Indulge yourself, as well as your friends and family to a luxurious ski vacation in this beautifully decorated home. Featuring great amenities such as numerous gas fireplaces throughout the house, an outdoor private hot tub, a large great room with panoramic views of the surrounding mountains, and a media room with a 36" TV and game table.

Direct ski access onto the Cascade Run, what else can you ask for?  This is the crème de la crème of locations for those who love to ski.  The house has all the bells and whistles for those seeking a complete package and has all the feel of a rustic mountain cottage.  The house is setup like this: Master bedroom with king bed, 27" TV, gas fireplace, private bath with Jacuzzi tub, large walk in shower.

King Guest bedroom with private bath, 27" TV.  Queen Guest bedroom with private bath.  Queen Guest  bedroom with private bath, 19" TV. Two double size bunk beds Guest Room with private bath, 19" TV.  These bedrooms have a true Ski Lodge feeling, you’ll truly enjoy them.

The Main Living Room has 27" TV/CD Player, gas fireplace, the media room has 36" TV/DVD/Game table, there is an outdoor private hot tub and a large gas grill on the deck.  This space is really great for those who like the outdoors and cooking.  A great spot even off season for those who like to trek around or want a Destination wedding in the mountains, really a special treat.

The house features as well a gourmet kitchen with granite counter tops, bar seating for 6 people.  In addition the dining room seats up to 10 people for dinner and you can enjoy those cold nights in with family in front of the fire having some family and friend time.  There is also a great  room with Panoramic Views, so you can sit and unwind, read a book and really get some R&R.
