Paradise Mountain Pool Lodge, Pigeon Forge, Tennessee


This fabulous cabin has everything you could ever desire in a mountain getaway. Located just minutes away from the downtown strip in Pigeon Forge in a resort setting with paved and easily navigated roads. With a private indoor heated pool on the bottom floor as well as a movie theater and arcade style games on the upper level of the cabin your group will find endless hours of entertainment without ever leaving the cabin!


Enjoy a large and spacious kitchen with a dining room table big enough to seat your group with space left. The kitchen and dining room open up to the living room allowing for plenty of interaction with the guests while preparing your meals. The kitchen has been updated with state of the art appliances including a convection oven and a French style refrigerator. Any cook will feel as if they are in heaven in this dream style kitchen!


Two master suites are on the main level with private Jacuzzi tubs in the room and private balconies overlooking a gorgeous mountain view. Enjoy the lavish bedrooms that are easily accessible on the main floor saving you a trip up the stairs to enter these two main floor bedrooms.


The second floor features the game room with a pool table, several arcade style games and a second living room with leather sofas and a large screen plasma TV with surround sound and a DVD player. There are two more master suites on the second floor that also feature private Jacuzzi tubs, private bathrooms and lavish appointments. Both of these bedrooms also feature their own privately accessible balconies with deck rockers and a spectacular mountain view. The third floor up features two queen size beds and is set up loft style with approximately 1100 sq feet of room that looks over the game room. This room has windows with a view of the mountains surrounding the cabin and plenty of room the enjoy rest and relaxation in.


The second floor down from the main floor houses the movie theater where you will spend many evenings enjoying your favorite movies with your friends and family without ever leaving the cabin! Off each side of the movie theater room are two more bedrooms featuring queen over queen bunks. The second floor down from the main floor features the swimming pool with windows that overlook a view of the mountains.
