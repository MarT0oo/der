Makena Aloha Estate, Lahaina, Maui, Hawaii Vacation Rental

 

The Makena Aloha Estate offers the all-inclusive feel of a private Maui estate located in the most ideal paradise setting. With over 4,400 square feet of living space, this fantasy-dwelling can more than comfortably accommodate up to 18 guests.

The main house has four spacious bedrooms, 3 of which are ensuite, and an extra 5th bedroom adjacent to the main master suite, which is perfect for a small child. The Makena Aloha Estate also hosts a detached 2 bedroom, 1 bathroom cottage on the property. This allows additional capabilities for a larger party, still preserving a sense of privacy amongst a larger estate.

Throughout the property, vibrantly colored gardens flourish with a variety of fresh herbs, coffee plants, papaya, coconut palms, avocado and an abundance of mature tropical plantings. Expansive lawns surround the swimming pool, spa Jacuzzi and outdoor living space. This space provides grounds for children to run and play, a hammock for relaxation, tiki torches at night, and a giant communal table for outdoor meals and celebrations.

Perched at the base of the West Maui Mountain range, Makena Aloha Estate offers sprawling 180-degree views of Iao Valley, West Maui Mountain valleys, the vast blue Pacific Ocean, and the outer islands of Moloka’i, Lana’i and Kaho’olawe.With year-round picture-perfect sights, this home proves to be the best location to watch Humpback Whales breech in the ocean during the winter months. It is easy to imagine intimate and awe-inspiring sunset gatherings, and cool late nights watching shooting stars with a nightcap.
