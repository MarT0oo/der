Hibiscus Paradise Estate
3 Bedrooms | 3 Ensuite Baths | Pool | Jacuzzi
Napili, Maui, Hawaii
From $3,465 per Week

The Hibiscus Paradise Estate in Napili, Maui, Hawaii, is perfect for romantic getaways, a gathering place for family and friends, or a place for some much needed R&R. It offers a private master en-suite upstairs with 2 additional bedrooms and full baths downstairs. It is nestled among wonderfully fragrant plumeria trees Hawaiian Hibiscus flowers with a beautiful view of Napili Bay. Enjoy Maui's sensational sunsets while sitting on your open air lanai.

During the winter season you can also watch the whales frolic away. This bungalow has a private back yard with a built in BBQ area, lawn and Jacuzzi that is perfect for entertaining small groups for some outdoor fun!

Hibiscus Paradise Estate is situated in the small and scenic town of Napili. Just a 5 minute walk to Napili Bay and the Gazebo Restaurant (a must for breakfast on the ocean-side at least once). The estate is perfectly located between Kapalua and Ka'anapali.
