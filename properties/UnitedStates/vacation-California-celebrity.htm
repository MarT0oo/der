Celebrity Style, Beverley Hills, California Vacation Rental


This chic, modern architectural compound hidden down a private, quiet, celebrity-laden lane in Beverly Hills will truly amaze you.  The lines, the glass windows that flood in natural light, the modern furnishings throughout, one of a kind art, and spectacular location.  Just minutes to Downtown Beverly Hills, this Mansion brings the best of both worlds, even for the most discerning travelers.

Celebrity Style has over 5,600 ft² of living area with 6 bedrooms and 6.5 bathrooms (6 en-suite). Three of the 6 bedrooms have California King size beds and the other three have Queen beds. The main house has 4 bedrooms and 4.5 bathrooms with over 4,400 ft² of actual living space.  In it you will find two stories of open space, a huge main floor plan opens through walls of glass to pool and views. A large master suite with a contemporary en-suite bathroom with a large Roman tub and an open shower with views.  The large guest suites with their confidant and sophisticated style all have en-suite baths. 

As you enter the living area you will find lots of space for sitting, having your morning coffee, or just lounging around watching movies.  What better place to take in a movie than in Beverly Hills, where you are now neighbors with the Celebrities you have come to admire. 

The gourmet kitchen is fully-equipped with high-end stainless steel appliances and granite countertops with fantastic mountain views.  The island with its own sink make this kitchen a great place to prepare your families meals or hire a chef and have it done for you, either way it's a dream! 

The dining area is large and modern, very inviting for a family or group of friends.  It overlooks the pool and makes eating fun every time.  On the other side of the pool is the large guest house, 1,200 ft² with 2 bedrooms and 2 bathrooms, with a full kitchen and laundry room, each bedroom has a private deck and full en-suite baths. Live like a star in Beverly Hills surrounded by nature. Experience the glamorous LA lifestyle at its best!

Additionally there are 10 decks, a large heated pool and spa, 2 kitchens, 2 laundries, 3 fireplaces and 4 car garage.
