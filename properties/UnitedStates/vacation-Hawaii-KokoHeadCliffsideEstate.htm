Koko Head Cliffside Estate , Oahu, Hawaii Vacation Rental

 

Just 20 minutes from Waikiki and Honolulu, this gated estate is part of a very small class of Oahu vacation rentals that sit at the top of a cliff in Hawaii Kai. This 3 story, 5,700 square foot home has expansive panoramic views of the Pacific Ocean for as far as the eye can see. While lounging on one of the 3 lanais or inside the pool or Jacuzzi of this Honolulu vacation rental, many have witnessed sea turtles, dolphins and whales pass by. The opportunities for amazement are endless at this unique Honolulu vacation rental.

Most bedrooms have ocean views and three even have direct access to our outdoor private lanais. The kitchen has such magnificent views that you might find yourself too distracted to actually cook which won't be a problem since we are moments away from numerous restaurants including the world famous Roy's Hawaiian Fusion. You will find your stay at this Oahu vacation rental one that you will never forget
