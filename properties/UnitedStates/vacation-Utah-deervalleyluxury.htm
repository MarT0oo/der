Deer Valley Luxury Villa, Deer Valley Utah Vacation Rental


Nestled within a private gated community just moments from Park City, Deer Valley Luxury Villa is one of the most exquisite properties in ski country. The stunning chalet expertly blends into the landscape, and offers guests over 9,000 square feet of luxury that is nothing short of magnificent. Upon entry guests are warmly enveloped in a décor that evokes Americana through time worn leathers and western inspired patterns, resulting in pure charm, comfort and luxury. Enjoy the awe inspiring scenery, as expansive windows offer panoramic views of the lake, mountains and forest.

Deer Valley has consistently ranked as one of the top three ski resorts in the country in recent years by the readers of SKI magazine. When you walk outside Deer Valley Luxury Villa each morning you can ski down Aspen Loop to the high-speed Deer Crest Gondola. At the end of the day you can ski right to the chalet’s back door from either Bald Eagle Mountain or Deer Crest. Enjoy the multiple private Deer Valley ski runs that lie within community boundaries then warm up in the chalet’s generous ski prep and mudroom complete with boot & glove heaters, ski work bench, and air conditioned ski lockers. In the evening, enjoy the spectacular stars from multiple terraces or from the in-ground Jacuzzi and spa complete with outdoor steam and sauna rooms. Warm your toes and roast marshmallows around the oversized stone fire-pit!
