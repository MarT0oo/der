Marvelous Rock Estate, Ka'anapali, Maui, Hawaii Vacation Rental


Black Rock Estate is a phenomenal 11,000 square foot Maui villa available for vacation rental. This luxurious villa is ideally located on one acre of beachfront perfection in Kaanapali, and offers lucky travelers an exotic, private world that incorporates Balinese and Hawaiian fantasy architecture and design.

All areas of Black Rock Estate have been carefully planned to create stunning visual vignettes, merging the Hawaiian landscape and interior living space in a harmonious design that incorporates light, air and water.

Black Rock Estate is a large Maui vacation villa located in the ever-popular Kaanapali resort area; it can comfortably accommodate up to 16 guests, making this home an ideal setting for milestone birthday celebrations, multi-generation family escapes and family reunions.

The villa offers a total of eight bedrooms. Two bedrooms come with king-size beds and five with queen-size beds, all of which offer en-suite bathrooms. The eighth bedroom features two full-sized beds, a ceiling fan and comes with access to the patio. There are a total of nine bathrooms under one roof.

The indoor space is as opulent as its grounds and setting, and comes with a stunning open-air living room that incorporates views of the blue Pacific as a backdrop, a spacious gourmet kitchen, two private dens, a game room with a Ping Pong table, a billiards room with a pool table, a library and a wet bar, a TV media room, and an indoor gym.

To keep guests comfortable, there is central air conditioning, and to create the right mood, the villa comes with a Surround Sound system with MP3 docks. For peace of mind, there is a security system, and gated entry with a secure three-car garage and a tarmac.

The outdoor living space is truly sublime, with numerous Balinese-inspired outdoor lounge areas, a private cabana perfect for afternoon massages, a gorgeous swimming pool and spa Jacuzzi area, a BBQ grill space, an outdoor dining table, and one of the best views of West Maui from all vantage points. Here at Black Rock Estate, you will awaken to the melodic sounds of rolling waves and palm trees dancing in the wind.

Set beachfront, the home's design reflects Hawaii's luxury resort architecture, with spaces open to the cooling breezes that caress the Islands.

Created for the most discerning of travelers, come to Black Rock Estate for an unrivaled experience in Maui villa living, while still being only minutes away from Ka’anapali Beach Resort, Whalers Village, and a variety of Maui activities and adventures.
