Hale O' Ola
6 Bedrooms | 7 Baths | Gated Community | Pool
Maui
From $14,000 per Week

Breathtaking panoramic ocean and outer island views, warm tropical breezes intoxicate the senses and renew the spirit. An easy walk to Palauea Beach and the many world-class hotels, shops and restaurants that make up the Makena- Wailea resorts. The Villa is located in the new and exclusive private gated community of One Palauea Bay.

The Hale O Ola will take your breath away form the moment you walk in. There is a welcoming stature of a Hawaii girl inviting you in with Leis, opening her arms in a way that open up to the extravagance of the home. Each feature is elegantly laid out to draw attention to your eye. As you enter the living area you will find it is a wide open layout with gorgeous travertine floors. The views of the ocean and nearby islands is stunning, each feature making you feel like the inside and outside of the home melds into one.

The house suites are just as lovely as the home itself; the Master Suite has a King Sized bed with sweeping Ocean views. The Master Suite bathroom can only be described as art, with one solid stone carved into a bathtub, where you can soak up the wonderful views and sip a glass of wine. It's truly an inspirational room. There are 5 guest suites with King sized bedrooms while the last guest room has a plush queen bed. Each room has its own bathroom, with waterfall showers in 4 of them and the rest with Rock tubs. Each bedroom has been uniquely designed to make you feel as if you've reached the Heart of Maui and from our point of view, you have.

The formal entry opens to an exquisite outdoor courtyard that connects each wing of the home. A magnificent 35' waterfall serves as a celebration of sight and sound. The sunken cabana with swim-up bar, full outdoor kitchen, vanishing edge lagoon pool and hot tub is perfect for hosting lavish parties.

With over 10,000 square feet under roof, the gracious interior combines tranquil, tropical ambiance and refined European elegance. The sophisticated style and design is displayed in the many hand-carved stone columns, rich wood cabinetry, impressive appointment of natural stones, materials and custom made imported furnishings. The expansive floor plan lends itself to the indoor/outdoor feeling of the home. A spacious living room with motorized pocket doors opening to a lanai with stunning ocean and island views. Culinary masters will delight in the large, gourmet kitchen with leathered marble counter tops, professional appliances, walk-in pantry and wine room.

You have reached a very luxurious destination; we hope you enjoy your stay. Ask about our fully catered options.
