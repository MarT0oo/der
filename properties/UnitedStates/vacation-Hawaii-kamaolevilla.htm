Kamaole Villa, Kihei, Maui, Hawaii


Kamaole Villa Maui County Permit # 3909 is a beautiful well-appointed home located in Kihei near Wailea. A short drive to shopping, restaurants, tennis, golf and other activities. Kamaole Beach is just steps from the tastefully landscaped backyard which includes both a 75' lap and child's pool. Generously sized lanai offers chaise lounge relaxation as well as dining. Spectacular sunsets the yearlong.


Large open and welcoming, the Living Room is part of a Great Room that includes the Dining Area and Kitchen. Furniture is upscale, comfortable with overstuffed sofas and chairs. Well-equipped entertainment center completes this gathering place. Perfect home for 3 couples, each bedroom has its own bath and Bedroom 3 has 2 twin beds that can be converted to king with prior notice. Please let us know your preference at time of booking.

Master and Queen bedrooms have ocean view, bedroom 3 has garden view.

Ocean Swimming: On Kamaole Beach. Perfect for swimming, sunbathing & walks in the sand. Superb sunsets, a picture taking dream!

Perfect central location to spend a day exploring Haleakala, the dormant volcano known as The House of the Sun or the lush greenery and waterfalls on the road to Hana. Then take a day and hop aboard the ferry to Molokai or Lanai to enjoy their special island contributions. Nellie's excellent proximity to some of the best golf courses, shopping and dining pleasures. All the while luxuriating in your backyard paradise watching the ocean sunset as you plan your next Maui excursion. And if you happen to be on island during whale season, suddenly you scan the ocean and see a whale breech! At once you have an overwhelming desire to see these amazing creatures from a boat tomorrow. Your Maui Holiday will plan it self if you go with the flow of relaxation and leave the stress behind!
