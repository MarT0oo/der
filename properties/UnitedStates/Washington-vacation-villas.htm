Enjoy Washington

The State of Washington is Diverse and lovely, with the parks and beauty of nature running free.  Mount Baker is a lovely and beautiful mountain to ski on as is the lovely town of Maple Falls to visit during the summer and fall months to see the leaves change.  The state is so diverse that it attracts both lovers of nature, skiers, and those who enjoy shopping and long scenic drives.  It really is a breathtaking and affordable location to both ski and vacation in. One of the best features is you are very close to the boarder of Canada, so if you decide you want to skip over to our Northern Friends, you can.

Each Chalet and luxury home is equipped with everything you will need for your stay. They are professionally managed and handled with care.  We take pride in hand selecting every home on our site.  Be sure to let us know how your stay went.
