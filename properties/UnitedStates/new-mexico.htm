About New Mexico

Rich in American Indian Culter and History with a topography that is strewn with lovely mountain ranges and beautiful rolling hills, New Mexico is a long forgotten paradise, where many escape to rejoin nature and find their serene inner voice.

The state is located just West of Texas and Just East of Arizona and boarder Okalahoma and Utah.  So you can say it's really in the middle of everything.

While visiting you can see: The Aztec Ruins, Lincoln National Forest, Gila Wilderness, The Old Spanish National Historical Trail, Carlsbad Caverns a true natural wonder and so much more. 

Visiting New Mexico is like going to a new Country, if you haven't seen it, now is your chance.  We have vacation rentals, mansions, and condos available for short term rentals and many lovely places where you can enjoy your next holidays.
