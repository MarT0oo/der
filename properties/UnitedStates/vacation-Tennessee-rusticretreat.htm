Rustic Retreat, Pigeon Forge, Tennessee


Treetop views, tasteful cabin decor, and a spacious floor plan are just a few of the first things you notice about Rustic Retreat. It is a comfortable space with families (even the canine members) in mind - a luxury rustic retreat! It is the best of both worlds without compromising luxury or comfort.


Rustic Retreat is pet friendly, and can accommodate up to 18 people with 6 bedrooms on three levels and an extra queen sleeper sofa. Two king master suites include deck/balcony access, private baths, TVs, and whirlpool tubs. Another bedroom has its own fireplace. The bunk bed room sleeps four - the kids' idea of a vacation retreat.


Relax in the great room by the stone fireplace and gather your friends and family around to watch a DVD on the 42” flat screen TV. Can’t agree on a movie? No problem! There are 7 satellite TVs. You are sure to love the home theater, complete with wide screen TV, theater seating and surround sound system. Or maybe you prefer interactive games with a pool table or arcade game.


If you feel more like heading outdoors to take in the beauty of the Smoky Mountains, you don’t have to go any further than the deck of Rustic Retreat. You can relax in your private hot tub inside a screened porch, or wile the time away in country porch rockers and take in the beautiful mountain views. Don’t worry about loosing touch with the rest of the world, Rustic Retreat has free unlimited long distance and high speed wireless internet access.


Rustic Retreat is located just minutes away from Pigeon Forge, in the beautiful Smoky Cove Log Home Resort in a wooded setting with a creek. You will enjoy additional luxury amenities in the Resort, including a custom log cabin shaped outdoor swimming pool! You can also enjoy walking trails, a lighted picnic pavilion, a fire pit with benches, and a horseshoe pit.


Make Rustic Retreat your Smoky Mountain getaway, and retreat with your family and friends to this luxury Pigeon Forge cabin rental!
