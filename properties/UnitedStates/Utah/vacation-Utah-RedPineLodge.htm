Red Pine Lodge, Park City, Utah Vacation Rental

 

Slopeside Chalet at Canyons Village with Theater Room, Hot Tub and Mountain Views. A ski-in, ski-out chalet in the vibrant Canyons Village area of Park City Mountain Resort, Red Pine Lodge is conveniently situated between Main Street and family-friendly Kimball Junction for easy access to a wide array of restaurants, shops, galleries, theaters and even Olympic history.

The three-level home sits on a quiet, wooded lot less than half a mile from the Canyons resort base with mountain views and ski-in, ski-out access. Designed in the style of a private lodge, the impressive log home features hardwood floors and beams, double-height ceilings and stone accents.

An open-concept living and dining area invites lively gatherings with views of soaring evergreens through floor-to-ceiling windows. Mix a cocktail in the gourmet kitchen and relax on the modern, full-length sofa, accented by fur throws and pillows, or grab one of two elegant armchairs. A stacked-stone fireplace adds warmth, while a large-screen SmartTV adds entertainment.

Steps away, an 8-seat dining table sets the scene for formal dinners, with 2 additional seats at the kitchen bar. A 3-seat breakfast nook provides an ideal kids’ table for families, or allows for even larger groups. The gourmet kitchen will inspire your inner chef with top-of-the-line appliances and ample counter space. A Keurig coffee maker will help you rise in time to catch first tracks.

After a day on the slopes or trails, a private hot tub with mountain views awaits to soothe tired muscles. When you feel like staying in, a stadium-seat theater provides the ultimate in-home entertainment. Guests can make popcorn in the wet bar and get comfy on the plush leather recliners. In the summer, take advantage of the spacious, furnished deck for meals prepared on the BBQ grill.

Ideal for large families or groups, this 5-bedroom home sleeps up to 13 guests with a privacy-friendly layout and en-suite bathroom in every bedroom.

From your central location near Canyons Village, you’ll be just 10 minutes from Main Street, 15 minutes from Deer Valley Resort, and 10 minutes from Kimball Junction. In the winter, enjoy outdoor music at the Canyons Village base, tubing at Gargoza Park, and a network of nearby Nordic ski trails, all within 10 minutes. In the summer, explore hundreds of miles of hiking and biking trails, family-friendly adventures at the Utah Olympic Park, open-air farmer’s markets and outdoor concerts. With two covered parking spaces, you can easily explore the surrounding area.

Whether you’re seeking a family-friendly ski retreat, a group-friendly homebase during the Sundance Film Festival, or a convenient summer escape, this ideally situated home puts all that Park City offers at your fingertips.
