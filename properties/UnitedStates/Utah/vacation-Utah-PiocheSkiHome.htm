Pioche Ski Home, Deer Valley, Utah Vacation Rental

 

This newly remodeled ski-in ski-out chalet offers 10,000 square feet of living space with panoramic views of the surrounding mountains and Jordanelle Reservoir. Conveniently located on the private groomed Pioche ski trail, this home features access to the Deer Valley gondola and entire Deer Valley Resort.

After a day on the slopes, store your gear in the ski prep room then relax in the large dry sauna or outdoor Jacuzzi. At night, stay warm next to either the indoor or outdoor fireplaces, snuggle up to watch a movie in the theater room or enjoy some friendly competition in the billiards room. The spacious patio and workout room complete this spectacular ski chalet.

Relaxing dry sauna and private hot tub, inviting outdoor fire pit, projector-screen theater room, and den with pool table and wet bar. This home also offers a heated two-car garage and spacious deck with views of the surrounding mountains and Lake Jordanelle.
