953 Empire Home
4 Bedroom Ensuite | 5.5 Baths | Hot Tub
Park City
From $1,200 per Night

The unique architecture of 953 Empire evokes the feeling of a contemporary city residence with the warm decor and unforgettable mountain views of a luxury ski chalet. The multi-level design gives each guest privacy with an en-suite bath and flat-screen TV in every bedroom. The top-level master, a floor of its own, even boasts a plasma screen above the bathtub. Gas fireplaces, inviting living spaces and a private outdoor hot tub welcome skiers after a day at Park City Mountain Resort, Deer Valley or Canyons -- all located less than five minutes away. This Old Town home is also less than a mile from Main Street.

The house is decorated and designed in a modern feel, unlike most homes in Deer Valley.  The house has all the touches you could want in the Euro Style Kitchen to the lavished bedrooms and dining area.  The managers are happy to accommodate anything else you may need during your stay, including private maid services and chef for an additional cost.
