Harmony Haus 2, Deer Valley, Utah Vacation Rentals


This 6,000 square-foot townhome is nestled mountain-side for stunning views of Deer Valley's slopes and Lake Jordanelle. Beautifully designed with granite counter tops, state-of-the-art appliances and elegant décor, this home features a theater with leather recliner seating, a wet bar, gas and wood fireplaces and a spacious deck with private outdoor hot tub. A heated two-car garage leads to a ski prep area with custom hardwood ski lockers. Take the Private funicular down to the lower section of Jordanelle ski run which takes you directly to the Jordanelle Express Gondola. Main Street Park City is 3 miles away.

During the Sundance Film Festival this home requires a security deposit.
