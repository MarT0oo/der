Silver Star 15, Park City, Utah Vacation Rental

 

This Park City ski lodge provides excellent ski access to Park City Mountain Resort. Located at the base of Silver Star ski lift, you are only a short lift ride away from the mid-mountain slopes at Park City. Complimentary shuttle service makes it easy to get to the base at Park City Mountain Resort for ski school as well as shopping, dining and entertainment on Main Street.

These 4 bedroom luxury homes offer amenities such as gourmet kitchen with top of the line appliances, beautiful mountain decor, spacious floor plan and much more. Silver Star Lodge offers heated outdoor pool and hot tub, fitness center, onsite front desk and concierge staff, ski rental shop, Park City Mountain Resort lift ticket window, complimentary shuttle service and heated underground parking. Silver Star has everything to provide an unforgettable Park City Utah ski vacation.
