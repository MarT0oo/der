Alpine Sanctuary, Park City, Utah Vacation Rental


Nestled on the intermediate Sanctuary Run at Canyons Resort, this 11,000 square foot alpine vacation home offers backyard access to the slopes for both skiers and snowboarders. This house has all the bells and whistles you can imagine.  From the massive entryway to the large Great room with open glass windows and mountain views, to the Bedrooms which have been created with the luxury skier in mind.  Every whim will be recognized, it’s absolutely a beautiful home for your next Park City trip.

The most impressive part of this house may be the amazing Movie Theater with Stadium seating.  When friends and family arrive and realize they can relax after a long day watching their favorite cartoon, sporting event, or movie, they just slip into the mood of their vacation.  Simply put there is nothing better than getting what you pay for.

As well the house includes a wine cellar for those who love to engage in social drinking and especially the special wines that you can find throughout the boutique shops of Park City and Deer Valley.  There are also several sitting areas, a living room and breathtaking living room with fantastic open views.

After a day on the slopes, store your gear in the spacious ski locker room and relax in the secluded outdoor hot tub or sauna.

What to do

A shopper’s paradise, Park City offers everything from unique fashion to original works of art. Not only will you find quaint shops and galleries on Main Street, but if you’re looking for sales, the Tanger factory outlet mall offers a wide range of popular brands at exceptional value.

Enjoy Summer or Winter Activities in this beautiful location, from fishing, to snowboarding or Destination Weddings.
