White Pine Retreat South, Park City, Utah Vacation Rental

 

Conveniently located, White Pine Retreat South is a ski-in/ski out property in The Colony at Park City. Access to the slopes couldn’t be easier since the Flat Iron Lift is just steps away from the property. Newly constructed, White Pine Retreat South has striking architecture and has been completely customized creating an interior full of character.

Spanning three floors and an area of 6,393 square feet, this luxury retreat has spacious open plan living with high ceilings, wooden floors, stylish furnishings and floor-to-ceiling windows allowing plenty of natural light in and framing the picturesque landscape. Chefs will enjoy the spacious and fully-equipped kitchen which overlooks a formal dining area where guests can gather to eat delicious meals. Alternatively those that enjoy dining al fresco can gather round the fire pit outside on the terrace and enjoy the mountain views.

White Pine Retreat South is able to accommodate 6 adults and 4 children in 3 bedrooms, 2 of which are doubles whilst the other is a bunk room with an en-suite bathroom and is ideal for children. The master bedroom has a King-size bed, walk-in wardrobe and an en-suite bathroom with a shower.

There is no shortage of entertainment at White Pine Retreat with endless amenities on offer. After a day on the slopes guests can relax in the outdoor hot tub, play a game of pool, have a drink at the bar or put their feet up in front of a movie in the stylish cinema room. Those who still have energy to burn can do so on the property’s own interior sports court with a game of basketball or badminton.

Other features include wireless internet, laundry facilities, and parking for 3 vehicles.

For larger groups, White Pine Retreat South can also be rented in conjunction with adjacent property White Pine Retreat North which is able to accommodate 12 guests in 6 bedrooms.
