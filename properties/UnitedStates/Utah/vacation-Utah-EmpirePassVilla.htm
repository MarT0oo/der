Empire Pass Villa, Deer Valley, Utah Vacation Rental

 

Empire Pass Villa is located in the brand new community of Empire pass in Deer Valley, with dramatic and scenic views to the valley below. The Villa is two dozen easy steps to the new Silver Buck run and the Silver Strike chairlift. Our mid-mountain location allows you to get the jump on the base mountain traffic for easier access to 'FIRST TRACKS' skiing.

Undoubtedly one of the most beautiful and convenient ski locations in the world, Larkspur at Deer Valley is a truly special place. Only 5 minutes to Park City Mountain ski resort and only 15 minutes to The Canyons Ski Resort allowing for easy access to 3 world class ski resorts on your stay. Snowbird, Alta, and Snow Basic, Brighton, and Solitude ski resorts are all within one hour to further expand your Ski and Snow opportunities.

Located an easy 45 minute drive on 1-80 from SALT LAKE CITY UTAH International airport, many guests take morning flights and are on the SLOPES the same afternoon!

Creatively designed and impressively decorated, with a spacious great room where you have a front row seat to 20-mile downrange views of the Wasatch Valley, the Silver Strike Ski run and the natural vegetation amidst the greenspaces in the elegantly designed Empire Pass community. This 3150 square feet townhome has lots of entertainment space as well as private areas.

Designed for maximum luxury and comfort, the large, open great room includes vaulted and beamed ceilings, and a huge wall of windows to take full advantage of the wonderful big-valley views. Exquisitely decorated, the living area, dining area and upscale kitchen include a marble stone bar, stainless appliances and NO WALLS to obstruct your views. Relax in front of the large stone fireplace or take in the big games on the widescreen 50-inch Sony HDTV.

Luxuriate in the vaulted-ceiling main-level master bedroom suite with its grand 4-poster bed, and adjoining private bath with a jetted tub plus a large, walk-in shower.

Three bedrooms on the lower floor provide for a variety of sleeping accommodations for every need, with each of the spacious bedroom having their own bathroom and multiple-window down-valley views. The huge second bedroom is designed as a kids room/media room, with two sets of log bunk beds, large mountain sofa which can sleep a 5th person in the room, and a huge 55-inch Sony LCD-projection TV.

The third bedroom is decorated in Victorian style, with tall 4-poster bed and matching décor. The fourth bedroom is very large and contains two queen sized mountain-style beds, making it ideal for several adults or a number of children. The upstairs loft includes a twin sleeper sofa and writing desk and central open location that facilities its use for kids playroom or ‘king of the mansion’ office space. Wireless internet is provided throughout the chalet.

Relax or BBQ on the large wood-beam balcony which overlooks the Wasatch Valley provides the best location for gazing out across the magnificent scenery. In the summer, the Deer Valley resort and surrounding ski areas contains many walking nature trails, trails and lifts for mountain-biking, paths for road-biking, and many opportunities for fishing, golfing, and sightseeing.

Located just 2 miles from downtown Park City's many unique and memorable shops and restaurants. Garage parking is provided.
