Last Chair at Park City, Park City, Utah Vacation Rental

 

Early birds are eager to get first chair, but only the true skiers stay for the last chair of the day before heading out for some fun on the town. Last Chair at Park City is a home designed by Robin Mazzone of All In A Day Design, to embody the authenticity and unique character of our beloved town.

Its prime location close to ski access and Main Street offer a one-of-a-kind experience to guests selecting a vacation home in this locale.

Accessibility is a key element of this home. Just 130 steps from ski access at the largest ski resort in the U.S.A., and only three blocks from the vibrant après ski atmosphere of Park City’s historical Main Street.From start to finish this home embodies the town and the ski culture, from first chair to last chair
