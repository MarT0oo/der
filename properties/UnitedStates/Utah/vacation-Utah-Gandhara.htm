Gandhara, Deer Crest, Utah Vacation Rental

 

Once an ancient kingdom in the foothills of Hindu Kush, "Gandhara" is now a stunning ski chalet in the Wasatch Mountains. Designed by renowned architect Michael Upwall, the 11,000 square-foot home sits seamlessly on the slopes of Deer Valley Resort in the gated community of Deer Crest. Filled with whimsical paintings, including works by Banksy and Yue Minjun, Gandhara maintains a distinct, colorful personality. This 6-bedroom ski-in, ski-out villa features a stadium-seat theater, pool table, indoor bar, private salt-water hot tub, fitness suite, elevator and inviting outdoor fire pit -- perfect for après ski.

Named for an ancient kingdom in the foothills of Hindu Kush, "Gandhara", this 11,000 square-foot residence is whimsically decorated in a modern style with paintings by Yue Minjun and Banksy.
