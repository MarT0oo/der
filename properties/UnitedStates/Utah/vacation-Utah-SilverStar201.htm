Silver Star 201, Park City, Utah Vacation Rental

 

Modern ski condo, ideal for couples with on-site chairlift, concierge and winter shuttle service. This ski-in ski-out community offers direct access to Park City Mountain Resort via the on-site Silver Star ski lift. The complimentary Park City bus service makes it easy to reach the base at Park City Mountain Resort for ski school. Guests can also take advantage of Resorts West's complimentary shuttle service based on availability. This 3-bedroom, 1,900 square foot Park City condo features a gourmet kitchen, en-suite baths and Satellite TV.

Guests of Silver Star enjoy access to a heated outdoor pool and hot tub, fitness center, on-site front desk and concierge staff, ski rental shop, on-site cafe, lift ticket window and heated underground parking. Main Street Park City is only a 5 minute drive away.

There is a private hot tub, gourmet kitchen with top-of-the-line appliances, gas fireplace, washer & dryer, Satellite TV, iPod docking station, air conditioning, BBQ grill, Central humidifier
