Great Escape, Deer Valley, Utah Vacation Rental

 

This spacious ski home sits in a quiet residential area of Deer Valley Resort within 30 steps of the green rated Silver Dollar ski run. Guests can easily ski to the base via green and beginner-friendly wide blue runs, making 10 Bellemont an ideal home for families and groups of skiers with mixed abilities. After a day on the slopes, guests can relax in a private outdoor hot tub or in one of two spacious living areas with large-screen TVs and cozy fireplaces.

Walk to Deer Valley Resort; Silver Lake; 5,400 sq. feet; Sleeps 14

Ideal for groups of friends or couples traveling together, this inviting residence offers six spacious suites and two living areas with cozy recliners and large-screen TVs. Enjoy the peace and quiet of mid-mountain accommodations with beautiful views. This home also features a private slope-side hot tub, deck, and heated two-car garage.
