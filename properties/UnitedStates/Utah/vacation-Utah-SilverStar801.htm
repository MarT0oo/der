Silver Star 801, Park City, Utah Vacation Rental

 

Mid-mountain luxury townhome, walk to Silver Star lift -- winter shuttle service, on-site concierge. Located at the base of Silver Star ski lift, this ski-in ski-out community is nestled on the mid-mountain slopes of Park City Mountain Resort. This 4-bedroom, 2,500 square foot luxury condo offers a gourmet kitchen with top-of-the-line appliances, beautiful mountain décor and spacious living areas including a den with large-screen TV. Silver Star offers a heated outdoor pool and hot tub, state-of-the-art fitness center, on-site front desk and concierge staff, ski rental shop, Park City Mountain Resort lift ticket window, complimentary shuttle service and heated underground parking. Complimentary Park City bus service service makes it easy to get to the base for ski school or to Main Street for shopping, dining and live entertainment. This unique community offers everything to provide an unforgettable ski vacation. Main Street Park City is only a 5 minute drive away.

Top 5 Things To Do

Ski the Greatest Snow on Earth™: It’s a scientific fact, Utah produces the most perfect snow conditions for skiing and snowboarding and with Park City’s two world-class resorts there is no better place to hit the slopes.

Sundance Film Festival: Attend the largest independent film festival in the United States, renowned for showcasing ground-breaking films in unique venues throughout Park City.

Big Stars, Bright Nights Concert Series: Savor the vibrant summer sunset, mountain silhouettes, and a gourmet picnic, while enjoying legendary music performances at Deer Valley Resort’s (tm) Snow Park Outdoor Amphitheater.

Mountain Bike the Wasatch Crest Trail: Stunning 360-high alpine views across the Wasatch Crest from Big Cottonwood Canyon to Park City and the vast valleys makes this physically challenging ride worth every effort.

Kimball Arts Festival: For three days, historic Main Street is taken over by 220 jury selected artists, 30 live bands, local restaurants and 50,000 art and outdoor loving visitors.
