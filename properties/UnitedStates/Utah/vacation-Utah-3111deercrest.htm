6 Bedroom Ensuites | 7 Baths | Private Ski Trail

This stunning chalet is located in the beautiful gated community of Deer Crest at Deer Valley Resort, offering ski-in/ski-out access via the private Pioche ski trail. The luxurious 9,000 square-foot home features 6 bedrooms and 6.5 baths, sleeping a total of 17 people. Wood and gas fireplaces keep this large residence cozy, while a pool table, theater room, iPod docking stations, X-box and Playstation 2 ensure guests of all ages remain entertained both on and off the slopes. This home also features an office, wet bar and two sets of washers and dryers. There is a 7 night minimum for this home.
