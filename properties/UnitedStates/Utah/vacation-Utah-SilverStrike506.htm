Silver Strike 506, Deer Valley, Utah Vacation Rental

 

Silver Strike is a ski-in ski-out luxury lodge located in the Empire Pass area of Deer Valley Resort. Each 4-bedroom condo offers stunning mountain views, top-of-the-line appliances and electronics, stylish mountain décor and a private deck overlooking Deer Valley Resort. Guests enjoy ski equipment storage lockers with boot dryers, an oversized outdoor hot tub, fitness facility, on-site front desk and concierge service, heated underground parking and complimentary shuttle service. With the privacy of Empire Pass and Main Street Park City less than four miles away, Silver Strike is sure to meet your highest expectations in Deer Valley lodging.
