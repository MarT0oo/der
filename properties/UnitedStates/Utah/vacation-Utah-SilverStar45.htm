Silver Star 45, Park City, Utah Vacation Rental

 

Modern contemporary cottage with sweeping views, hot tub and winter shuttle service. Located at the end of a quiet court with mountain views, steps from a ski access funicular shared only among Silver Star Cottage residents, this bright 4-bedroom, 6-bath cottage offers rare mid-mountain access to Park City’s slopes with full Silver Star amenities, including on-site concierge service and a heated swimming pool.

A three-level property with 3,200 square-feet of living space, Silver Star #45 features rich hardwood beams and iron accents reminiscent of Park City’s mining heyday, a signature of the Silver Star community. With vaulted ceilings and picture windows, the light-filled great room offers an inviting space to gather. A leather sofa, lounge chair and twin armchairs frame a stone brick fireplace, where a striking, colorful painting of a winter sky captures attention. Bold, abstract and nature-themed artwork accent every living area, complemented by intriguing Southwestern sculptures and light fixtures.

An amber glass light box hangs above a 10-seat formal dining table, with two additional seats available for larger parties. The gourmet kitchen features top-of-the-line appliances and granite countertops to easily cook homemade meals. You can even arrange to have the kitchen stocked before you arrive, but with a custom elevator ready to sweep you from floor to floor, you won’t have any trouble carrying groceries from your heated garage.

You can also take the elevator down a floor to the second living area, which features a leather sofa, a Southwestern-style armchair, wet bar and flat-screen TV. Double doors open onto a covered deck, where you can access the private tub. This lower level also features two bedrooms, including a junior master with a king bed, en-suite bath and private outdoor space, and a guest bedroom with two queen beds and en-suite bath.

The light-flooded master and guest master bedrooms are located on the top floor with vaulted ceilings and picture windows. Both have king beds and en-suite baths. The master also features a gas fireplace, flat-screen TV, and an office area.

Outside this cottage, you’ll find yourself surrounded by mountains at the end of a quiet court, a short walk from the Silver Star Cottage access lift. At the main Silver Star Plaza, you’ll find the express Silver Star chairlift, an on-site restaurant with year-round live music, an equipment rental shop, and an open-air lounge area.

As a guest of Silver Star, you’ll also enjoy access to a heated swimming pool, fitness center, fire pits, ski lockers, and concierge service. Free winter shuttle service and year-round complimentary city bus service make it easy to explore Main Street Park City, or access the Park City resort base. To easily explore all of Park City, including the family-friendly Kimball Junction area or Nordic skiing trails at Round Valley, a heated garage makes it easy to have a rental car. In the summer, an on-site trailhead offers easy access to hiking and biking, and the Park City Golf Club is steps away.

However you choose to explore Park City, the Silver Star Cottages offer a relaxing home base with full lodge amenities.

Top 5 Things To Do

Ski the Greatest Snow on Earth™: It’s a scientific fact, Utah produces the most perfect snow conditions for skiing and snowboarding and with Park City’s two world-class resorts there is no better place to hit the slopes.

Sundance Film Festival: Attend the largest independent film festival in the United States, renowned for showcasing ground-breaking films in unique venues throughout Park City.

Big Stars, Bright Nights Concert Series: Savor the vibrant summer sunset, mountain silhouettes, and a gourmet picnic, while enjoying legendary music performances at Deer Valley Resort’s (tm) Snow Park Outdoor Amphitheater.

Mountain Bike the Wasatch Crest Trail: Stunning 360-high alpine views across the Wasatch Crest from Big Cottonwood Canyon to Park City and the vast valleys makes this physically challenging ride worth every effort.

Kimball Arts Festival: For three days, historic Main Street is taken over by 220 jury selected artists, 30 live bands, local restaurants and 50,000 art and outdoor loving visitors.
