Canyons Escape, Park City, Utah Vacation Rental

 

Luxury mountain refuge, ski-in ski-out, perfect for families or groups. Escape to Park City for your next vacation in this spacious eight-bedroom ski home in The Colony at Canyons Village. The bedding accommodates up to 20 people and there are multiple dining, media and entertaining areas - ideal for families traveling together or a small business retreat. The on-mountain location provides sweeping views from every room. This private 10,688 square foot residence has a ski prep room with lockers and boot warmers to let you enjoy ski-in, ski-out access to the Serenity trail at Park City Mountain Resort (near Canyons Village) in comfort. This home also includes a steam/sauna room, hot tub and an indoor/outdoor heated pool! On-mountain accommodations let you spend quality time together whether skiing or relaxing in a serene mountain setting.

There is an Apple TV, Steam and Sauna room, with an Indoor/Outdoor pool, Two Master Suites, Two Garages with entrances, perfect for multiple families traveling together. During the Sundance Film Festival this home requires a security deposit.
