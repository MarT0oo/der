Banner Haus, Park City, Utah Vacation Rental

 

Luxurious ski-in ski-out home in Empire Pass with heated patio, hot tub, perfect for families

Located in Deer Valley’s exclusive Empire Pass community, this ski-in ski-out property has unadulterated mountain views and well-appointed amenities. Sitting at 8,200 feet, nestled along Banner Ski Run and just steps to the Silver Buck Ski Run and the Silver Strike Chair Lift, this home offers unmatched ski access to Deer Valley’s meticulously groomed runs. With four guest bedrooms and one spacious master bedroom, all with en suite bathrooms, Banner Haus is perfectly suited for a family getaway or corporate retreat with space and design features to comfortably host 14 guests.

Enjoy a massage or relaxing yoga session before taking to the slopes in the home’s private yoga studio. After a long day of skiing relax by the fire pit on the heated patio or in the private sunken hot tub surrounded by pine trees and deep snow banks. With a fully stocked chef’s kitchen it is pleasure to prepare a feast and an ideal workspace for a personal chef to take over. After dinner, gather in the Great Room fireside or cozy up in the family room with its drop-down screen/projector system for movie-time.

The master bedroom is located on the third floor and has a king bed with en suite bath and standing shower, as well as a soaking, jetted tub. The second bedroom is located on the second floor and has a king bed with an en suite shower. Another king bed is located on the second floor and has an en suite bath/shower combo. The 4th bedroom is located on the second floor with a king bed and an en suite bathroom with a bath/shower combo. The 5th bedroom on the second floor has two Captain's bunk beds and an en suite bathroom with a bath/shower combo. In the loft area which doubles as a massage/meditation room, there is queen sleeper sofa.

No amenity has been overlooked at Banner Haus, including heated driveways and walkways with ample garage parking. With its perfect location this stunning home will make for unforgettable Deer Valley ski vacation.
