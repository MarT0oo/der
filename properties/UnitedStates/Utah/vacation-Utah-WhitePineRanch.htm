White Pine Ranch, Park City, Utah Vacation Rental

 

White Pine Ranch in the Colony at Park City Utah is nestled on 100 acres of naturally landscaped grounds, including evergreens and aspens, wildflowers, and a private pond, White Pine Lodge is a tranquil retreat with the amenities to host a corporate retreat or private event. On the property sits a 4-bedroom, 3-bathroom home with a private hot tub, fire pit, and sprawling backyard porch furnished with a BBQ grill and outdoor seating for dining with a view. Inside, revel in the feeling of a luxury mountain lodge with log beams, hardwood floors and soaring ceilings. An open-concept living area, styled in modern decor with a Southwestern flair, invites cozy evenings by the wood-burning fireplace or in front of the large-screen TV, and lively meals around the 12-seat dining table.

The adjacent gourmet kitchen offers plenty of space for catering or home-cooked meals, with two seats at the breakfast bar for tranquil mornings over coffee. Between 4 spacious suites, the home comfortably sleeps up to 12 guests comfortably. Like every one of our lovely Park City offerings this Villa offers that mountain loveliness we all dream about. Upstairs you have two amazing luxury suites, one master and one guest master with King en-suite bedrooms. The Master suite features vaulted ceilings and a sitting area with television and fireplace, luxurious bathroom with step in shower. The Guest master also has lovely vaulted ceilings and TV with a beautiful guest master bath. On the Main level you have one Queen bedroom with shared bath and in the 4th bedroom you have a Captain's Bunk with XL Twin/Queen plus a bonus Twin Trundle. This is the kid friendly adventure home with great stuff for everyone to enjoy.

A game room and lounge on the upper level with a pool table, library and TV add entertainment for the whole group, while a laundry room and mudroom add convenience -- particularly if you prefer to spend your days outside exploring the grounds, hitting the slopes, or finding adventure on the trails. For large groups, the property features four comfortable barns designed for break-out sessions or receptions, while a meditative yoga studio provides space for instructor-led classes or private practice -- indoors or out.

A three-car garage makes it easy to explore the surrounding area, including the Canyons Village resort base at Park City Mountain, a 5-minute drive, and restaurants, shopping, theaters and family-friendly entertainment at Kimball Junction, a 10-minute drive.
