Palm Desert Luxury House, Palm Desert, California


Located in the beautiful Coachella Valley (also home to Palm Springs), Palm Desert is the ideal luxury get-away and offers the perfect climate from October through May with days of constant sunshine and low humidity.  The desert is the ideal spot for relaxing, as well as enjoying a wealth of recreational opportunities: golf, tennis, hiking, and swimming.

 

Palm Desert Luxury House is your dream get-away.  This 6,000 square foot home is designer furnished, offering the best in comfort and style.  Award winning architect Kristi Hansen created an indoor/outdoor oasis, complete with slide-away walls of glass, stunning views of the mountains, the Valley, and the golf courses.   Featuring stone floors, the latest in audio/visual electronics, and stylish finishes and materials, this is truly a fantasy escape. The home features the best in luxury and comfort, including Jim Thompson Thai silks, custom upholstery, antiques selected in Hong Kong, China, and Thailand, custom Wenge cabinetry, and over sized stone flooring.

 

This 3 bedroom, 4.5 bath home, is fully equipped and loaded with amenities.  The chef's kitchen has Thermador, Sub Zero, and Miele appliances, multiple table settings of Vietri dinnerware, multiple sets of barware and a wine cooler. The granite countertops and marble island are ideal for entertaining, and the island offers seating for four.
