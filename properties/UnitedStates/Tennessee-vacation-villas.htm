About Tennessee

Tennessee  has often been called the Golden Gem of the South because it’s where people would come to search for their pot of gold and in the process laid down their roots. Tennessee is strewn with lovely mountains and city central locations like Memphis and Nashville, where you can find plenty of local hot spots for singers and song writers year round.  As well Tennessee welcomes those seeking skiing, chalets near the lakes, and hiking trails. It’s truly and inspirational place to visit no matter what you are doing or where you are planning to stay.

Right now we are offering Gatlinburg and Pigeon Forge TN Log Cabins, Chalets, and Vacation Rentals Lodging and Accommodations, just minutes from Dollywood, enjoy Pigeon Forge, Gatlinburg and all that Tennessee has to offer with luxury being in mind. 

You can drive or fly in, Tennessee is very easy to visit any time of year.
