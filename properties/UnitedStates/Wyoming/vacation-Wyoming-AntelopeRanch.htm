Antelope Ranch, Jackson Hole, Wyoming Vacation Rental

 

Sitting on the east bank of the Snake River and gazing up at the Teton Mountain range, Antelope Ranch combines high-end accommodations, complete privacy, and immense natural beauty for your stay. Our three classically Western-style homes are set among picturesque spring fed creeks, aspen groves, and incredible vistas; all without a neighbor or roadway in site. Though tucked away, Antelope Trails is centrally located, with easy access to all of Jackson Hole’s main attractions, including downtown Jackson, the Jackson Hole Ski Resort, Grand Teton National Park, and Yellowstone National Park.

Antelope Ranch consists of three homes situated throughout the stunning natural landscape. The Main House is the crown jewel of the property and typically serves as “home base” for visitors. The nearby Woods House and Gate House offer additional bedrooms and unique features, all within a few minutes’ walk. In total, the property can accommodate up to 28 guests comfortably in a rustic-elegant style that is unique to Jackson Hole.

With 67 acres entirely yours to enjoy, you can remain on the premises for your entire stay without a hint of cabin fever. Some examples of on-ranch experiences are listed below, but we are happy to work with you to design your ideal itinerary and help arrange guided activities, if desired. Jackson Hole offers some of the most incredible natural beauty on earth, and with it comes endless options for how to enjoy your time in the area. Your itinerary can be packed with adrenaline-boosting activities and nonstop adventure – or not. Because sometimes, the most luxurious thing to do is nothing at all.

Antelope Ranch is an ideal destination for a family reunion or other gathering. The surrounding area provides ample on- and off-premises activities conducive to relationship-building and group recreation for people of all ages. Guests may opt for different service levels – from standalone, self-catered to a full luxury experience with on-site chef, daily housekeeping, and concierge / errand services – and anywhere in between.
