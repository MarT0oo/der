Chalet Pataheya, Jackson Hole, Wyoming Vacation Rental

 

Chalet Pataheya in Jackson Hole Wyoming is one of the top ski destinations in the world. You will find every amenity within this beautiful home equipped with five bedrooms with king sized beds and detached from one of the king rooms is a wall for four twin bunks.
