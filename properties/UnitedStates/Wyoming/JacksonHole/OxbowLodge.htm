Oxbow Lodge, Wilson, Wyoming Vacation Rental

 

Oxbow Lodge is a modern 6 Bedroom Ranch, located minutes from Teton Village and entrance to Grand Teton National Park. With 6 bedrooms and bathrooms, overlooking the beautiful Tetons, you really feel like you have arrived. Nothing is left to chance at this amazing Lodge home. The serenity and luxury of this home can be seen all of the luxury touches that this home was constructed with. The charm, the beautiful location, the more than luxurious amenities are why the Oxford is one of the top rated vacation rentals in Jackson Hole.

The house features a wonderful array of things for the entire family to do, from a recreation room that offers you a Foosball table and Projection television for hours of fun. Our Great room includes 2 wood fire places and patio access, as well we have a wonderful family room for those days you just want to lounge around and see your favorite movie or television show with the family. An amazing Fitness room for the people who love to travel but also like to take their fitness with them, enjoy Nautilus Equipment a Kitchenette, then jump into the Sauna and shower. The amazing level of luxury is usually just seen in resorts, as most owners like to fill there rooms with beds, but here at the Oxford we know that with luxury and busy lives comes the need to bring the wonderful world of fitness with us. We even have instructors available if you so desire.

Our Kitchen is also quite a construction of beauty, from the hard woods and rare granite to the very favorable cooking area that includes any chef's dream of 2 Sinks, a Pizza oven, 2 Sub Zero Refrigerators, a Butler's Pantry, patio access, bar seating for 3 and an amazing dining area for 10 that also includes patio access. So you can be in and out of the house, enjoying one another's company and never skipping a beat. Close to the kitchen we also have a powder room and this is great for cleanup just before eating or if you have guests.

Now we didn't forget some of the most important things which are the access to skiing. We have a wonderful mud-room and you can call and order skis most of the year to be sized and delivered to you. There is also a washer and dryer, and if you want help with laundry we do have maid service we can arrange and you'll be very happy to know they do a wonderful job making sure you are very happy.

Our bedrooms as well are in a class of their own, the Master Bedroom features a King Bed, Television, Office, 2 Sinks, Shower, Toilet, Gas Fireplace, and Patio Access. No master is complete without a luxury bedroom and the master meets and exceeds most guests expectations. In Guest room 1, there is a Queen bed, television and it's of course en-suite. The 2nd Guestroom features a King bed, Television and is also en-suite. The 3rd Guestroom has 2 twins but we can convert to king if needed, a Television and it's also en-suite. The nice thing about this house is it also features a North Wing Apartment, just in case you're traveling with friends or a young couple that want space. This is a wonderful apartment that has a Kitchenette, living area, queen bed, sofa bed (queen), deck access, a television, and a wonderful bathroom for private use.

We think this house is amazing and we hope you do as well.
