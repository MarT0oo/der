Snow Ridge, Teton, Wyoming Vacation Rental

 

Snow Ridge is a bright, chic, contemporary condo set right in the heart of ski paradise: at the base of the slopes, only a five-minute walk to Teton Village and Jackson Hole Mountain Resort, with epic, fifteen-mile views all the way across the Hole to the Gros Ventre Range.

A shared, bridge-like walkway leads to Snow Ridge’s front door; the private lawn in front dappled by big shade trees. Inside, Snow Ridge is open, airy, and inviting, its rooms filled with light. A wood-burning fireplace is ambassador of the great room, its joyous glow and crackle visible from the living area, dining table and the elegant, streamlined kitchen. A long, U-shaped counter creates a half wall separating the kitchen from the great room and deck, while at the same time providing ample space for food prep, two sinks, a five-burner stovetop and a wine cooler. The counter serves as a convivial gathering spot, a place to pour drinks, serve food, cook and converse. Standing at the stove, you can look out through the great room’s glass doors to the deck, and all the way across the valley

Snow Ridge's master bedroom is set on the main level, close to the action, yet peaceful and private. Three closets and a full bath round out the master bedroom area. The upper level of Snow Ridge has three bright guest bedrooms. In the first, a cloud-soft queen bed awaits under a row of four windows, which hold a wide panorama of Sleeping Indian and the whole Gros Ventre Range. In the second bedroom - perfect for kids - matching twin beds with classic iron bedsteads stand side by side, with a nightstand between them, and space to play on the floor. Both of these bedrooms have closets, and they share a full bath.

The third guest room, at the end of the hall, is big enough to be called a “junior master.” Here a four-poster, Shaker-style queen bed dressed in snowy white linens is flanked by reading lamps; a deep leather chair waits under a bank of windows that frame the front lawn. A second bathroom, with a walk-in shower, is just down the hall, steps from the bedroom.

Snow Ridge has four sybaritic, spa-like bathrooms: one on the lowest level (which has a steam shower) the master bath on the main level, and two on the upper level. Each has a modern, clean, organic feel, with an elegant vessel sink, tiled walls and floors, and a huge walk-in, glass-walled shower. The master bath has a deep heated soaking tub as well, and a wonderful river-rock “rug” inlaid upon the floor.

Snow Ridge was designed with an eye toward maximizing space and a sense of peace and welcome. Throughout the home, warm wood tones glow against creamy white walls. An office/sitting area, a laundry room, a media room, and ample coat-and-gear storage are set near the main and lower entries – easily accessible and yet out of the way.

A dining table and grill wait out on Snow Ridge’s spacious deck, blessed with a no-holds-barred view. Jackson Hole spreads out like a magic carpet, shockingly gorgeous in every season, full of possibilities. Just a few minutes’ walk from Snow Ridge are the two tennis courts, heated outdoor pool, children’s pool and Jacuzzi of Sundance Tennis and Swim Club. And all around… the mountains. The ski slopes, the hiking trails, the rushing, trout-rich rivers, the National Parks that seem like playgrounds of the gods. Snow Ridge is right in the middle of it all, a wonderful, welcoming place to stay for a week, a month or a season, perfect for a family or a group of friends on the adventure of a lifetime
