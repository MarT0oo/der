Moose Creek 35, Teton Village, Wyoming Vacation Rental

 

Yes, that is a ski lift above the front driveway. Out of all the town home/condo developments at the base of the Jackson Hole Mountain Resort, none have a better location than the Moose Creek town homes. The 4-person Moose Creek lift is only a couple of hundred steps away. Skiers will love the easy access to the mountain from here.

Moose Creek homes are a great base for hikers too. Once the snow melts, you can walk out the front door and be on a hiking trail in 5 minutes. Best of all is that this convenience comes with quiet, here you don’t have to share your hot tub with anyone else, and, while Teton Village’s main shopping and dining area is close, it’s not so close that concerts or events there will affect any quiet, lazy afternoons at home you might plan.

This home is ideal for guests who prioritize function as well as form. Of course all of this home’s suites—a master suite with its own private deck, and two suites that each have two twin beds—have luxurious overstuffed down comforters and luxurious linens. Throughout the home you will find framed prints by Donna Howell Sickles, a western artist famous for her contemporary and colorful portrayals of cowgirls. The kitchen is well appointed with everything needed for a quick snack or preparing a gourmet meal. Moose Creek 35 has everything you need.

The cute-and-cozy vibe is set right in this home’s entry, where a modern rustic bench and side table seem to be waiting for you to set down your hiking or ski gear. Walk up the stairs into the combined kitchen-dining-living room and cathedral ceilings, a wood-burning rock fireplace, and an elk mount greet you. The kitchen island has seating for 4 and the round dining table for between 4 and 6. It is a perfect space for entertaining.

Directly off the kitchen and facing the ski resort is an intimate deck. As nice as it is, it’s not our favorite outdoor space at this home. That honor is a tie, between the covered, southeast deck off the master suite and the ground floor patio home to a private, 8-person hot tub. But really, a cocktail enjoyed on a sunny afternoon from any of these three spaces will feel pretty nice.

With the home’s public spaces all on the top floor, the main and ground floors allow for quiet bedrooms. The master is on the main floor and the two rooms with twin beds are on the ground floor. (Also on the ground floor is a stacked washer/dryer and storage room.) Each bedroom has ample natural light. The master in particular gets great morning light.

Guests staying in a Moose Creek townhome have access to the Sundance Tennis and Swim Club, which has a seasonal (summer only) heated pool and three tennis courts and year-round two hot tubs and changing rooms.
