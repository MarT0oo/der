Escape to the Hamptons, Westhampton Beach, New York Vacation Rental

 

This magnificent Oceanfront beach house on Dune Road has panoramic ocean views, features six bedrooms (two oceanfront master Bedrooms, an additional four bedrooms), five and a half baths, unique family room with a cozy fireplace and an exquisitely detailed living room. It contains an open gourmet kitchen with granite counter-tops equipped with the latest top-of the line-appliances and breathtaking views of the ocean. A mahogany Oceanside deck surrounds a heated pool and a private boardwalk allows you to enjoy the privacy of your very own beach. Enjoy this beautiful home all year long.

Your vacation will not be complete without the sunshine, which we promise will be around for most of your stay in the summer months. The house is positioned to let the warmth of the sun fill up the rooms, modern and airy, bright and sublime. Walking on the beach you may run into a celebrity or two, which is nothing new, as they own homes around The Escape. It’s so great to know you are in good company and it shows with every day you spend.

You can enjoy the shopping in town, with a short 20 minute drive. Take in the local scenery and shops, or just cross over to Bridgehampton in about 30 minutes. There is nothing you are lacking here, we have Wi-Fi, Televisions, pots, pans, towels, and a house you would be glad to call your own person “vacation escape.” Let us help you find paradise.
