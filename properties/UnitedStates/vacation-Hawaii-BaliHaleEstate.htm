Bali Hale Estate, Maui, Hawaii Vacation Rental

 

Bali Hale is a fabulous Maui vacation villa that echoes, "dream vacation" through all aspects of the property.

An incredible amount of attention to detail has been applied to allow this Villa to languish in eastern style design, extreme comfort and all around visual pleasure. Set upon a 5-acre estate and sustainable farm, walk the orchards and graze upon a variety of Hawaiian fruits including papaya, lilikoi (passion fruit), bananas and citrus varietals. Breathe in the fresh island air, and allow your senses to be tantalized by the sweet aroma of plumeria, night blooming jasmine and gardenia.

Be embodied by the outside living space of perfection with a handful of lavish relaxation areas including; an expansive covered lanai, a fully equipped wet bar, a spa Jacuzzi and a beach entrance swimming pool. Enjoy breathtaking sunsets with panoramic views of the Pacific Ocean and the outer islands of Lana’i, Moloka’i and Kaho’olawe as the backdrop.

Conveniently located to nearby Launiupoko Beach Park, the Bali Hale Villa makes enjoying ocean activities, and long warm days in the sun just a minute away. Situated only 5-minutes away from the historic town of Lahaina, you can adventure to shopping, restaurants and nightlife in ease, knowing you won’t be away from your paradise for too long.
