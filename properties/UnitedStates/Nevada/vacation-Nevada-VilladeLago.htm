Villa de Lago, Lake Tahoe, Nevada Vacation Rental

 

VIlla de Lago, The Lake House, is located in a secluded enclave north of historic Cave Rock Nevada. Nestled in pines that sweep down through the riparian shrubbery from the home to the properties private pier and buoy. Whether by day or night the lighted staircase will carry you to the magic of the bouldered shoreline where you can spend your time relaxing to the sound of the waves and Tahoe’s ever changing panorama or take a dip from off the pier and swim in waters as clear and brilliant as our skys.

De Lago, brings the outside in with expansive windows that reach from floor to ceiling and doors that sweep open to bring the lakes vistas in uninterrupted to our second living and dining area on the deck above the always playful waters of Lake Tahoe.

De Lago’s décor is rich in eclecticism with antiques, comfortable furnishings and beds; appointments and original art that show cased by stunning vintage chandeliers. , We like to think it’s fun, cozy, comfortable and smart with a deference to the traditions of lake nostalgia that will give you memories and stories to last a lifetime.

Fireplaces

The predominant feature of the main hall is the floor to ceiling fireplace in the living room. With an oversized wood burning firebox capable of keeping you in the mood during your escape with logs up to 30 inch. All the irons are hand wrought and oversized to accommodate large logs. A plentiful supply of firewood is complimentary. The master suite fireplace is gas log and can be operated by remote allowing for the convenience of instantaneous warmth and glow.

Great Hall

The main hall is an open split level floor plan allowing guests to share in all the homes main entertaining activities; cooking, dining, sitting and reading area, television and a powder room on top of beautiful hardwood floors, and carpets and unique and comfortable furnishings. The main deck is accessed off both the living and dining areas through large 6 foot glass doors in between wall to wall and floor to ceiling windows. Dining accommodates 8 plus 3 at the counter, Living room accommodates 6 on the sofas plus 2 on the leather ottomans and many more on the large stone hearth. The reading area accommodates 2 with leather chaise lounges.

Main deck

The main deck is accessed off the living and dining areas through sweeping doors. Lounging area seats 8 on sofas and ottomans and the dining area seats 8 at one table and of course there are panoramic lake views and a bbq. Access to the dock is off a stair case from the main deck down through trees and shrubs with subtle stair lighting.

Master Suite and Bath

The lake view master suite is one floor lower than the main hall and is secluded from the rest of the home. King size sleigh bed, chaise lounge next to the fireplace, hardwood floors and carpets, large wall to wall windows that access the lower deck, and stairs that pass to the dock and upper deck. The master bath is generous and airy with double vanities and a dry sauna, lake views from the double sized double zone shower and tub.

Upstairs Bedrooms

Both upstairs bedrooms have queen beds with luxury linens and bedding and you will enjoy Lakeview’s from either choice. Both bedrooms are furnished with antiques and modern appointments. The bathroom is shared. The loft is accessible on the same level as the bedrooms.

Loft

The loft is directly above and behind the main hall and has lake views on par with the main hall. Queen sleeper sofa comfortable seating and relaxing, unique furnishings, decorations and art.

Recreation Room

The rec room is accessed off the inside of the garage, for just a short time longer. Truly a man cave with a view, a pool table and large screen tv and stools.

The Dock and Boating Facilities

The wood dock is approximately 100 feet long above a beautiful bouldered bottom. The boat lift is not accessible to guests but the 15,000 pound boat capacity buoy is as well as tie up to the dock. The boating closet houses a refrigerator and ice chests for your convenience and leisure on the lake. 1 double seat kayak and 2 single kayak available for guest use. Some life preservers available.
