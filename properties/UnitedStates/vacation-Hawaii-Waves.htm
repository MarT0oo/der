Waves, Maui, Hawaii Vacation Rental

 

Located on the slopes of a West Maui mountain rising up above the beach, the Waves earns its names from its elegant and surf-inspired design ethos, which incorporates surfing memorabilia and imagery as well as cool, beachy colors.

This new Maui villa is perched over the beaches of West Maui, near Lahaina, with local surfing spots with in eyeshot. Want to see if the surf is up? Walk to the edge of the yard and look down at the coastline. Not into surfing? No worries, relax around your amazing pool and soak in the heated spa.

Entering the home, one is struck by the incredible living room views, which frame the island of Lanai, floating on the horizon like a mythical Polynesian paradise. This view is truly one of the most striking in the Hawaiian Islands because the outer islands create dramatic elements, especially at sunset.

The main living space of this superlative Maui vacation home is designed to allow for seamless indoor-outdoor Hawaiian vacation living, with a wall of pocket doors that retract and connect the living room and dining room area to the covered lanai flanking the pool.

The rectangular pool and spa are the centerpiece of the terraced backyard and offer day and nighttime enjoyment. A built-in barbecue conveniently positioned under the shade of the lanai invites guests to embrace outdoor Hawaiian villa living and grill up some of Maui’s fresh ocean bounty.

The kitchen boasts a modern design, with clean white cabinets and counters, and a breakfast bar capable of seating five. The rustic yet stylish picnic-style table sits just beyond, in a corner of the living area, and adds to the overall relaxed surf-chic vibe of the home. Black and white photos of beaches and surf spots adorn the walls, completing the look.

There are five bedrooms, each offering lanai and pool access. Four bedrooms are located in the main home and feature king-size or queen-size beds: three offer en-suite bathrooms with walk-in and outdoor showers, and one a bathroom adjacent. A fifth bedroom with a king-size bed is located in a detached cottage, offering guests an additional level of privacy. The cottage bedroom offers a luxurious outdoor shower, a covered lanai and direct pool access. All bathrooms are new, with gorgeous stone and contemporary fittings. While the home benefits from the naturally cooling and fresh Maui trade winds, Central AC ensures comfortable sleeping conditions at all times!

This Maui vacation home may cater to your inner surfer, but it does so with a clean look that says Malibu or Montauk as much as it says Maui surf house. Enjoy sunny days, starry nights and commanding ocean and beach views from this epic Maui villa!
