Water’s Edge, Lahaina, Maui, Hawaii Vacation Rental

 

This stunning villa in Ka'anapali, along Maui's western shore, features spectacular sunset views of the Pacific Ocean and the nearby islands of Lanai and Molokai. Situated just three minutes on foot from Kahekili Beach Park and within easy reach of some of the finest beaches and golf in Hawaii, the villa enjoys the exquisite serenity and comfort of a private resort, with outstanding alfresco living areas, superb interiors, and four enchanting bedrooms, including two master suites.

Secluded amid lush tropical gardens, the ample terrace invites you to dreamy days in the Pacific breeze. Take a morning dip in the beautiful swimming pool, luxuriate on elegant sun loungers, and savor the breathtaking views. Returning from the beach in the afternoon, cook a barbeque lunch and relish it alfresco, before climbing into a hammock for a seaside siesta. At sunset, gather in the lounge for delicious cocktails and sublime vistas.

Open walls join the terrace with the interior, creating a lovely flow of space and air. Savor a bottle of fine wine in the sumptuous central lounge under soaring ceilings, or watch your favorite shows in the adjoining living room. The beautiful dining table sets the scene for festive feasts, while the luminous kitchen, under a rustic ceiling, is fully equipped with chef-grade appliances. A sitting area beside the kitchen is perfect for sipping local coffee in the morning. The villa also features an excellent fitness room.

The four bedroom suites are wonderfully elegant, spacious, and cozy, and all include deluxe ensuite bathrooms. There are two master suites, each with king bed, high ceilings, ample sitting areas, private balconies, and ocean views. One of the guest suites features a king bed, ocean views, and direct access to the terrace, while the other has two double beds, garden views, and a lovely stand-alone bath in its bathroom. With comfortable accommodations eight, Hale Hawaiiana is ideal for families and destination wedding guests.

About a three-minute walk to Kahekili Beach, you're also just two miles to Kaanapali Beach, less than three to Black Rock, and an easy drive to Puamana Beach Park and Launiopoko Beach Park. The town of Lahaina is just four miles away. Golfers are just seven miles from The Plantation at Kapalua Golf.

You’ll be close to the best restaurants, beaches and activities that the island offers, but you may just decide to stay home watching the swaying palms and the setting sun.
