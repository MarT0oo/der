Kailua Beachfront Cottage, Kailua, Oahu, Hawaii


This multi-million dollar Beachfront home has over 3,100 spacious square feet of living space. The 6 bedrooms and 5 baths (plus a dry heat sauna) surround the large courtyard and pool creating a very unique and private layout. This home is the perfect gathering place for friends and family to enjoy an amazing experience together without having the hassle of renting out numerous hotel rooms.

Located on the World Renowned Kailua Beach, this home’s location is unsurpassed. At the end of our private street of just 10 homes, you will find our Beachfront Retreat with our own private beach access that is unknown to even many of the locals. With 100 feet of your own private beachfront access and white sand at your feet, paradise is found.

Island Activities
Every outdoor activity in the Hawaiian Islands can be found on Oahu. Go sport fishing, kayaking, snorkeling, surfing, windsurfing, horseback riding, hiking, bicycling and much more. Watch cliff diving and Polynesian cultural shows at Waimea Bay Park, visit the Polynesian Cultural Center, experience the world's largest maze at the Dole Pineapple Plantation, tour Pearl Harbor or just layout and unwind on the beach.
