Hale Kohola, Kaanapali, Maui, Hawaii


This palatial Kaanapali Villa, offers the perfect location for families or 5 couples. Spacious and elegantly designed with lush tropical views, this home will delight all visitors. Pool with turtle mosaic, Jacuzzi and large sun deck wrapped in verdant foliage highlighted with foot bridge and pool waterfall. Grill and enjoy your dinner poolside, this is your own private resort!

Entering Hale Kohola you immediately notice the marble floors, indoor waterfall with whimsical sculptured fish and inviting layout. Both Master Bedrooms are located on 1st level, each with ensuite bath. King Bedroom 3 and Queen Bedroom 4 are located first floor as well with an inviting living area central to all. A two story atrium rises before you in the from of an open winding staircase. Above is an amazing Humpback whale sculpture with several other artistic surprises in store. Hale Kohola has won several design awards, it is a villa of unique pleasures!

Reaching the 2nd level you realize just how expansive this home is! An enormous open 'U' shaped floor plan incorporates large welcoming kitchen, dining and living area complete with gaming table, large screen TV, stereo and DVD player. This truly is a home designed for large scale entertaining and fun. But wait, there's more, open the French doors to lanai in living and dining areas to view ocean sunset, surrounded by layered tropical foliage. Also on the 2nd level is your 5th Bedroom, a King. A full bath is located just around the corner. 10 guests will enjoy ample space and privacy. A 'bonus' room is located just behind the kitchen which includes a daybed.

Hale Kohola is located on one of the Kaanapali golf courses, it's also only moments from beaches, restaurants and shopping! You simply can not find another home for this price and value! Guests who stay at Hale Kohola are happy guests!
