Hau 'oli Villa, Kamuela, Hawaii Vacation Rental

 

This stunning home offers the best of both worlds: the comfort of a spacious, private home and access to all of the facilities of the Mauna Kea resort - one of the most beautiful resorts in all of Hawaii. Come relax in the privacy of what we hope you’ll feel is like your home away from home that we have outfitted with many extras for the whole family to enhance your stay. A well-stocked high-end kitchen including wine refrigerator, games, beach towels and toys, coolers, snorkel gear, fast Wi-Fi, flat screen TVs with AppleTV with Netflix and DVD players in every room are just some of the things we provide. In addition, you will have access to private concierge services to facilitate every aspect of your trip, from purchasing groceries to stock the refrigerator before your arrival, to restaurant recommendations and reservations, and helping arrange activities throughout the island. Our local property manager is available to you 24x7 for any needs that might arise.

Staying in this house entitles you to full use of the amenities of the Mauna Kea resort for a nominal fee (see below). Championship golf, tennis, beaches, heated pools, restaurants, spas, fitness centers, shops, business center and valet parking are just some of the options available to you at both the Mauna Kea Beach Hotel and the Hapuna Beach Prince Hotel with full signing privileges. The beautiful white sand beaches are consistently rated the best beaches in all of the Hawaiian islands. Swim, snorkel, boogie board, paddle surf, play in the sand, or relax on the hotel-provided lounge chairs under beach umbrellas. Or just hang out by the wonderful heated pool. Scuba diving, whale watching, shopping and farmer’s markets are all just minutes away.

Located in the Uplands at Mauna Kea, the house is designer-decorated inside and landscaped by a renowned Hawaiian landscape architect. The beautiful furnishings and appointments set this never before rented house apart from others like it. Inside the house, the spacious master bedroom suite opens out onto the lanai and features a king-sized bed, an abundance of closets, and a spacious en suite bathroom with a deep soaking tub and a large walk-in shower. The second bedroom features a queen-sized bed with an en suite bathroom. The third bedroom also has a queen-sized bed with a nearby bathroom with shower. The fourth bedroom is a large ohana suite (“ohana” is Hawaiian for “family”) with a private entrance, and features two queen sized beds, en suite bathroom with shower and a kitchenette.

The communal areas are just as inviting. The kitchen is equipped with Viking appliances including a gas range, and granite countertops. It is a joy to cook in with a huge selection of pantry items, cookware, and dishes. Relax in the oversized couches and chairs in the living room or family room. Gather in the family room to watch HDTV with Apple TV enjoying movies, live sports and news, music, and even content from your iOS devices or Mac. Sit the whole group down at a beautiful dining table that seats up to 10 people or have casual meals indoors or out at smaller glass tables. With a central stereo system, you can enjoy your music throughout the house.

Outside, enjoy the incredible views of the ocean, Maui, and breathtaking sunsets over the foreground golf course from the spacious lanai. Swim and frolic in the 40’ saline infinity pool, which is heated at no additional cost. Relax in the couches, chairs and lounge chairs with afternoon smoothies and cocktails or in the spa for a post-swim or end-of-day soak. Enjoy your meals at the additional outdoor dining table so you can watch the views, or your kids.

If you are ready to leave the resort, great restaurants and shopping in Mauna Lani, Waikoloa, and Waimea are close by. Talk to us and we can fill you in on our experience with the wonderful mix of luxury stores, department stores, boutiques, art galleries, dining, farmers markets and even dive shops!
