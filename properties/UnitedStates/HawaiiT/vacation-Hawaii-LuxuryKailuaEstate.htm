Luxury Kailua Estate, Kailua, Oahu, Hawaii Vacation Rental

 

Luxury Kailua Estate is one of the newest , most striking and contemporary executive beachfront estates on world renowned Kailua beach. Constructed in 2008, the estate features two homes (main house and guest house) separated by a resort-style pool with elegant umbrellas and lounge furnishings. Other features include a steam sauna, movie theater, beautiful manicured yard and pristine sandy beach access.

Every part of the two homes, impressively designed with striking hardwood and granite is designed for the most discriminating guests to feel satisfied and luxuriate. The two spacious master bedrooms in the main house facing the ocean boast breathtaking, panoramic views from private lanais, remote controlled curtains, beautiful interiors and spacious bathrooms with hot tubs. Two more suites are located downstairs.

The main kitchen area is extremely elegant with custom shaped dining table, elegant chandelier lighting, full kitchen with stainless steel appliances, open living room attached, and all open out through sliding glass doors onto the spacious yard and glittering ocean !
