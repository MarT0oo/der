Kahala Beach Apt, Honolulu, Oahu, Hawaii Vacation Rental

 

Luxurious Kahala beach at its best. Prestigious Kahala Beach vacation rental apartments. Beautifully upgraded. 2 bedrooms, 2 bathrooms fully furnished with garden view and 1 parking. The large covered balcony provides lots of indoor-outdoor living space, and whimsical natural lighting. Hardwood floors in the living areas and kitchen and bathrooms add comfort to this perfect, relaxing hideaway.

The building grounds are romantic and beautiful with white pillars, lush and colorful tropical gardens, and winding clear koi ponds. There is a large, resort-style, sparkling blue water pool adjacent to the soft white sand beach and clear turquoise waters of Kahala.

The building facilities also include a charming, shady library, and comfortable exercise room with work-out machines. Highly recommended hideaway to singles, couples, or a small family.
