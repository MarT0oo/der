Horizons of Wailele, Diamond Head, Waikiki, Hawaii Vacation Rental

 

Located on the private slopes of Diamond Head. Architect designed home that captures stunning views of the ocean, coastline, and a tropical landscape. Spacious living areas with indoor waterfall & pond that lead to a dramatic pool deck with water feature. A second terrace features a spa for quiet relaxation. Panoramic views for most rooms in the house. Large master suite with private deck and 180 degrees of blue ocean.

Short 10 minute walk down the hill to a sandy beach, and in 20 minutes you can walk into the action of Waikiki for shopping, fine dining and nightlife.

Master bedroom - Encompassing the entire upper level of the home, the master bedroom provides spectacular ocean views and wonderful sea breezes. Complete with a CA King-size bed and an en-suite bathroom with His & Hers sinks and large soaking tub. Also features private entrance with security panel, safe, laundry room, large closet, wall-mounted TV with DirecTV, central air-conditioning, sliding glass doors lead to a large deck with magnificent views!

Bedroom 2 - Located on the ground floor of the home, this large bedroom has a CA King-size bed, en suite bathroom with a spacious shower, large walk-in closet, TV with DirecTV, central air-conditioning, security alarm panel, safe, and a lanai with garden and partial ocean views.

Bedroom 3 - Located on the ground floor, this bedroom features a CA king-size bed, floor-to-ceiling windows, TV with DirecTV. Central air and shared bath.

Bedroom 4: Located on the ground floor, this bedroom features a double-size bed, TV, safe, window seat for relaxing and reading, wall-mounted air conditioning unit, and shared bath.

*Please note there is no child safety fence option for around the pool area. All children must be supervised by adult. Pool is not heated.
