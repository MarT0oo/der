Massachusetts

One of the Most Beautiful Locations to travel in the world, that is strictly my opinion, but it’s truly a special adventure when you visit Massachusetts.  Home of the Brave and Forever known as the stronghold of freedom, it’s a place that brings history back to life.  It is uncompromising, everlasting, and magical without compare.  When most people think of Mass, they think of Boston especially.  Boston is historically known to have welcomed Presidents, contained amazing such history as the: Boston Tea Party, The Revolutionary War, the Boston Massacre, Battle at Bunker Hill and So much more.  You can stay in a house that is as old as the history of the US Colonies and trek the freedom trail in Boston either alone or by Tour guide.  You can shop Newberry Street where all the fancy shops still stand or you can test out the wonderful foods at the Square where you can eat at Quincy Market.  But come during the fall or summer season so you can enjoy a ball game or the changing of the leaves.

Right outside of Boston you’ll find Salem Mass, home to the Salem witch trials.  You will find the wonderful mix of European culture, such as the Irish, Portuguese, and Italian.  The smaller towns are a treat to visit, setup tours of Cambridge, Fall River, New Bedford, and don’t forget Plymouth.  These are all great places to take in and enjoy while visiting us.

In addition to Boston, people flock to Mass because of the wonderful Large Estates for hire in Nantucket, Martha’s Vineyard, and Nantucket Island.  Additionally you’ll find that people love to travel to the West of Mass where you’ll find some great treats, like the Basketball Hall of Fame in Springfield.  There are just so many wonderful treats in New England, don’t miss out on the beaches up the Coast, including a local favorite Hampton Beach.  It’s  just the perfect place to have history come alive.
