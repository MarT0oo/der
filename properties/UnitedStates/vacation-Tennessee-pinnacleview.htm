Pinnacle View, Pigeon Forge, Tennessee


Pinnacle View is the perfect cabin for spending a luxury Pigeon Forge vacation with family and friends. With 8 bedrooms, additional queen sleeper sofas, and 6 bathrooms Pinnacle View can accommodate up to 26 people.
This spacious cabin offers a stone fireplace in the great room, fully equipped kitchen, and 4 master suites with a king size bed, fireplace, Jacuzzi, satellite TV, and DVD player. You will love the luxury amenities that you have to enjoy in Pinnacle View.


Pinnacle View also includes a game room with entertainment for all ages! Enjoy a game on the pool table, foosball table, or sit down video arcade. You can relax and spend time together while you play and watch your favorite TV shows or sports game on the satellite TV with DVD.


Pinnacle View offers amazing views from three decks. So enjoy a relaxing soak in the hot tub and then enjoy the fresh mountain air and beautiful view from the country porch rockers. This is a fantastic way to spend a relaxing evening enjoy the Smoky Mountains from your cabin!


Located in Black Bear Ridge Resort, Pinnacle View is just a few miles from the main parkway in Pigeon Forge, giving you easy access to the attractions and entertainment of Pigeon Forge and Gatlinburg! You also have access to a seasonal community swimming pool.


Take advantage of the fabulous amenities that you and your family can enjoy in Pinnacle View. We are sure you will want to start planning your next vacation before you even leave!
