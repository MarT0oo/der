Spectacular Paradise Estate
5 Bedrooms | 4 Baths | Pool | Beach
Launiopoko, Maui, Hawaii
From $10,500 per Week

The sprawling estate features 5 bedrooms and 4 bathrooms.  You can have it all - including a panoramic ocean view of Molokai, Lanai, Kaho’olawe, and Molokini.

Perfect for large families or multiple couples, Spectacular Paradise Estate was built to take advantage of the tropical Maui weather with large outside dining and living areas featuring spectacular ocean views. Watch magnificent sunsets, and whales breaching, without having to look over rooftops.  If you are not getting married here, you will wish you were! If you are having a wedding or party, it is great to have everyone under one roof.  There is parking for 30+ cars off the street.

With gated acreage far from the street, this property is a sun seeker and skinny dipper’s paradise, all while enjoying the view and sunsets.   The large pool with beach entry is the perfect place to soak in the rays or take a swim into a cave through the natural rock waterfall. Enjoy cold refreshments at the outside bar as you play in the pool with built in grotto or take a quick to trip to the beach, which is less than a five minute drive.  

With the expansive marble decks for large parties, barbeque area and traditional imu pit (pig roast)-  you could have your very own Hawaiian luau with plenty of room for the dancers.  There is 12,000 square feet of lawn surrounded by palm trees, fruit trees and tropical flowers. Pick your own bananas and papayas.   Spectacular Paradise Estate has many natural decorations such as rocks, trees and other environmental art including a large koi pond stocked with elegant koi fish and lush lily pads.

Enjoy the large gourmet kitchen without missing a sunset.   Feel like a King and Queen while seated at your one of a kind dining room table with 14 of your favorite subjects.
