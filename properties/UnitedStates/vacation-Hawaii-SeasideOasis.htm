Seaside Oasis, Maui, Hawaii Vacation Rental

 

Mountain view or ocean view? It can be tough to choose, but at Seaside Oasis, you don't have to! This ultra-private villa on five pristine acres in Lahaina is a masterpiece where the past meets the present with clean, modern architecture sprinkled with mid-century flair.

Seaside Oasis's bright interior provides a sleek and beautiful canvas for the delightful, pops-of-color decor. The blues of the living room complement the ocean views visible through wall-to-ceiling sliding glass doors and a stunning gold bathtub is showcased against the neutral travertine in the master bathroom.

Nothing is an afterthought at Seaside Oasis. Five bedrooms and six bathrooms are situated throughout the sprawling 4,300 square-foot villa, providing ample room for up to 10 guests. The spacious and open floor plan provides an effortless flow, with views from virtually every room in the house. Glass doors lead to the covered patio where guests can enjoy a peaceful breakfast, afternoon cocktails or an al fresco dinner. Be sure to light the tiki torches for the quintessential Hawaiian backdrop.

Take a leisurely swim or keep up your fitness with laps in the pool. At the end of a busy Maui day, be sure to enjoy a relaxing soak in the attached hot tub that cascades into the pool. The pool area has six sleek chaise lounges and seating areas furnished with comfortable, colorful chairs. Don’t miss the hammock on the veranda that complements the bedroom’s decor.

The living room is a comfortable haven with three sofas and additional chairs situated in front of a large, flat-screen TV. It is a perfect place to gather for a movie, and of course delight in the ocean views from the glass doors leading to the veranda.

The adjacent dining room has seating for eight, and the sleek gourmet kitchen has a center island with bar seating for seven more. Seaside Oasis is an ideal property to enjoy extended family or a vacation with multiple couples.

Seaside Oasis is the perfect name for this house as every room is an oasis. The Master Bedroom has a king-size bed, ceiling fan, direct access to the veranda and a desk. The en-suite bath’s pièce de résistance is the hammock-style tub. Unwind in this sleek piece of art while enjoying views to the outside.

Bedrooms Two and Three are also equipped with a king-size bed, ceiling fan and veranda access, in addition to flat-screen TVs. Bedroom Two has a beautiful en-suite bath while Bedrooms Four and Five each have a queen-size bed. Bedroom Four has its own en-suite bath with shower.

Garage parking available for your rental car. In the immediate vicinity you can go horseback riding at Lahaina Stables and surfing at Launiupoko Beach Park.

Find great shopping and dining within five miles at Front Street Lahaina and Lahaina Cannery Mall. World-famous Ka’anapali Beach and Golf Course are a beautiful eight-mile drive away, and you will find more shopping at Whalers Village. For nature lovers, snorkeling and scuba diving is available at Black Rock, zip lining at Kapalua Adventure Center, and hiking can be enjoyed on the Napili Bay Beach Trail.

The beauty of Seaside Oasis combined with all of the available activities in and around Lahaina will make for one unforgettable holiday.
