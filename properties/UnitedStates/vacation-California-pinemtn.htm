Pine Mountain Luxury Lodge, Lake Arrowhead, California


Would you like a completely unique experience, tucked away, yet close enough to everything you and your family would like to do? If you are looking for the ultimate family getaway, where you can enjoy the absolute in luxury lodges and family fun, then Pine Mountain is the place for you. Nestled in a private 40 Acre Estate, overlooking Lake Arrowhead, with its own paths and trails, you will never stop finding new things to do.

The Main Lodge (sleeps 13) is the larger of the two buildings and contains a Master suite with an attached full bathroom, and a queen bed on the main floor. Also on the main floor is a bunk room that has one twin and three bunks (six beds). Upstairs, there are two bedrooms. One has a queen bed, and one has two twin beds. On the main lodge floor there is a gender-neutral bathroom with two private toilets and two private showers. The Chef's kitchen is fully equipped with cooking equipment, two Viking ranges, a microwave, dishwasher and a 48" refrigerator with an ice maker and filtered water dispenser. Of course, all glassware, plates and utensils are provided. Professional chefs love this kitchen. The living room contains a large fireplace (firewood is provided), flat-screen TV, (cable and DVD player), i-Pod docks and Wi-Fi. Outdoor living spaces include a large wrap-around deck with luxury seating, 3 outdoor dining tables, an additional fireplace, a large natural-gas grill (gas provided), serving bar and an additional restroom, with two private stalls and a private shower.

The Guest Lodge (sleeps 9) is a real log building consisting of three rooms; great-room, kitchen/dinette, and gender-neutral bathroom with two private stalls, a private shower and two sinks. The great-room has a fireplace and seating area, with a flat-screen TV (cable and DVD), Wi-Fi, and i-Pod dock. Next to the seating area is a unique bunk bed with a double mattress below with standard twin bunk above. In the center of the room there is a large round dining table that seats 8 people. At the far end, there are three 3 bunks (sleeps 6), which can be screened from the great-room with a large privacy curtain. The kitchen/dinette is fully stocked. The outdoor patio has a fantastic view of the lake, and contains a gas grill, and outdoor fireplace, covered eating area and luxury seating.

Apartment: (at extra charge). The apartment is located adjacent to a covered parking area, below the Main Lodge with a separate entrance. It is a one-room apartment with a queen bed and a double futon/couch, with a fireplace (gas, not wood burning) and its own a half bath.

For the treat and enjoyment of the children or the children in us all, this unique holiday lodge also has two tree houses. These two tree homes can be used for the kids to camp out or just enjoy a lazy afternoon in, the views are simply breathtaking. And for the children who want to camp out, well, we have a teepee as well. The kids will enjoy camping outside and you will enjoy the weather resistant materials, we have left nothing to chance.

In the area you will find Hiking, Mountain Climbing, Stargazing (telescope provided), sledding, wireless internet access, cooking classes upon request (at additional cost), massage therapy upon request (at additional cost). At Lake Arrowhead you can rent a canoe or enjoy the multiple activities the area has to offer, including water skiing at Makenzie School, fly fishing, snow skiing, restaurants, movie theater, shopping, dining and more! Enjoy one of the most amazing vacations you’ve ever had in a lifetime!
