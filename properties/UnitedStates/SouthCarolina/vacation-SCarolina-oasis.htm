Amazing Oceanfront Oasis, Hilton Head Island, South Carolina Vacation Rental


This amazing Hilton Head oceanfront home offers spectacular ocean views and luxurious furnishings. 12 steps lead to the entry where you will find a great room with sleeper sofa, TV, DVD, stereo, powder room, bar area with a sink, refrigerator, and icemaker. This level has five bedrooms. The first has a king bed, TV, door to the pool area, ocean view, and private bath. The next two bedrooms have queen beds, TVs, and private baths. The fourth bedroom has a queen bed, twin bed, TV, and a private bath. Next is a bedroom with 2 queens, a private bath, and doors leading to the pool and spa.

A dual staircase leads to the second floor of this beautiful home. The main living area, kitchen, dining area, and master plus a powder room are located on the second floor. The main living area features a sleeper sofa, plenty of seating, and a door to a spacious balcony overlooking the Atlantic. The beautiful living room also has a plasma TV with DVD player. The kitchen/dining area features 2 stoves, 2 dishwashers, granite countertops, and laundry room with full size washer and dryer. There is seating at the dining bar for 8 plus a formal dining table for 8. Off the dining area is a screened porch overlooking the pool and ocean with propane grill. The enormous master suite is furnished with a king bed, TV, door to the oceanfront balcony and large walk-in closet. The magnificent master bath has a luxurious shower with body sprays and rainfall shower head plus a Jacuzzi tub. A spiral staircase from the master leads to a private observation loft overlooking the ocean. Outdoor amenities include a gas grill and fabulous oceanfront pool and Jacuzzi.
