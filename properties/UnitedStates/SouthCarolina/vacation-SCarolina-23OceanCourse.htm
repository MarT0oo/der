23 Ocean Course, Kiawah Resort, South Carolina Vacation Rental

 

This near beach home provides spectacular views of the Ocean Course and the Atlantic Ocean from the living area and verandas of this 6 bedroom 5 bath home. 4 bedrooms on the first floor are connected by a cozy sitting area with a flat screen television and immediate access to the home’s private pool and hot tub. Travel up the stairs, or the elevator, to the 2nd floor living area which is decorated with smart, sophisticated seaside furnishings. The spacious gourmet kitchen has a large island and is equipped with Viking and Subzero appliances.

A generous dining table and adjacent screened porch make this home a perfect place to gather for quality time with friends and family. The master bedroom on the third floor offers the same sweeping views of the Atlantic Ocean as the 2nd floor living area. It also features a separate lounge with a flat screened television, a spacious bathroom and a large walk-in closet. Guests are guaranteed to enjoy the basketball hoop along the driveway and grilling along the pool deck.

Pools

Night Heron Pool Complex: Mere steps from the beach in our popular 21-acre park and recreational facility, all resort guests will enjoy exclusive, complimentary access to the Night Heron Pool Complex. The children’s pool features two flume slides, water cannons and a giant dumping bucket, plus a toddler splash area with gator slide and baby bouncers. The Junior Olympic-sized family pool is surrounded by a spacious sundeck and offers poolside dining from Night Heron Grill (open seasonally). Both pools are heated in the spring and fall and cooled in the summer for your complete comfort.

Swimming & Recreation

West Beach Pool Complex: All resort guests can also take advantage of exclusive access to our West Beach Pool Complex (open seasonally). Perfect for play or relaxation, this pool offers tube slides and a five-foot depth area as well as a kiddie slide and splash zone with interactive spray features. Hungry for more? Dine poolside on Mexican cuisine with an island twist from West Beach Cantina.

Recreation: Golf Savings & Preferred Tee Times, Resort guests save up to 25% at all five of our fully renovated championship golf courses—including the iconic Ocean Course—and enjoy preferred tee times to make the most of their stay.

Tennis Discounts & Preferred Reservations: Resort guests save up to 20% on tennis programs and court time, plus receive access to preferential advance reservations to take their game to the next level at our top-ranked tennis resort.

Discounted Recreation & Nature Programs: Only resort guests have access to discounted rates on our most popular resort recreation and nature programs—that includes a savings of $20 per child, per day at the always-fun Kamp Kiawah.

Fitness Room Access: All guests of the resort are welcome to use the well-equipped fitness room in Night Heron Park. You can keep up your workout regimen on the fitness room’s cardio and strength training machines.

Transportation

Complimentary Island Transportation: Get to any resort-owned venue across Kiawah’s 10-mile island with door-to-door transportation available only to our resort guests.

Dining

Preferred Dining Reservations: Kiawah Island Golf Resort delivers an extensive dining collection with over a dozen eateries, including fine and smart casual restaurants, poolside and outdoor cafes, and more. Our guests enjoy preferred privileges at all resort-owned restaurants that accept reservations.

Services
