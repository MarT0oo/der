Beach Beauty, Palmetto Dunes, Hilton Head, South Carolina Vacation Rental

 

This amazing Hilton Head oceanfront home offers spectacular ocean views and luxurious furnishings.

Ten steps lead you to the front entry of this beautiful home where you will walk in to the great room with cathedral ceilings and a wall full of windows with oceanfront views. Here you will find two decorative sofas and two chairs. There is also a wall-mounted flat-screen TV and a sliding door leading out to the very large deck. In the dining room is a table with seating for up to ten and a beautiful china cabinet. Just beyond this room you will find the open kitchen featuring white corian countertops with a paneled fridge, 4 high top chairs, bar with ice machine, wine cooler, and a chair with armoire. The breakfast table, leading out to the sun room, has a table for four. In the sun room you will find a 24” flat-screen TV, loveseat, and a door leading out to the deck with ocean views.

On the first level you will find the first two-bedrooms, both with king beds, flat screen TVs, and private bathrooms. Sixteen steps will lead you to the second level where you will find the remaining four-bedrooms.

The first bedroom you come to is the master-bedroom with a king bed, two chairs, wall-mounted flat screen TV, and a door leading out to a private deck with spiral steps leading down to the pool deck. There is also three-walk in closets and an amazing private bathroom with his/her sinks, Jacuzzi tub, and a stand-up shower. Down the hall is the media room which offers a 40” flat-screen TV, DVD player, wall unit with a sink, two leather chairs and a sofa. This looks down to the kitchen and breakfast just below you. From here you will find the fourth bedroom, which offers a queen bed, flat screen TV, and a shared bathroom in the hallway.

Nine steps down will lead you to the fifth bedroom with two twin beds, flat screen TV, and a Jack and Jill bathroom that is shared with the sixth bedroom offering a king bed, flat screen TV, and cathedral ceilings.

Out your back door is the amazing backyard with a large deck and pool (13’x26’), gas grill, lots of seating, and a separate sunning deck with amazing ocean views. This home offers everything you could ask for!

Palmetto Dunes with its bike paths, golf, tennis, boating, shopping and dining is a world-class destination. Enjoy your beach vacation strolling or biking alongside loggerhead turtle nests, watching the dolphins swim by or admire the array of herons and ospreys in their own special habitat.
