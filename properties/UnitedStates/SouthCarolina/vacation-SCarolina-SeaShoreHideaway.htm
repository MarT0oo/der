Sea Shore Hideaway, Hilton Head Island, South Carolina Vacation Rental

 

This great Hilton Head Island home offers 7 bedrooms, 3 sitting areas, and is just a short 7 minute walk to the beach. 5 steps lead to the entry where you will find a formal dining table for 10. The adjacent formal sitting area features a sofa, 2 chairs, and French doors leading to the pool area. A spacious den offers a large screen TV, DVD, and opens to a wonderful kitchen with granite counter tops, an eat-in bar that seats 4 and a second dining table for 8. Doors from the den lead to the pool area and lagoon.

A lovely master suite on this level offers a king bed, sitting area with a queen sleeper sofa, TV/VCR, and a private updated bath with a jetted tub and separate shower. The next bedroom contains a king bed, TV, and shares a bathroom with the third bedroom which features a twin day bed with pull-out trundle (to access this bedroom you must walk through the king bedroom). This bedroom also offers a TV and is only accessible from the king bedroom.

15 steps lead to the 2nd floor with a family room and 4 more bedrooms. In the family room you will find a pool table, TV, and stereo. French doors lead to a 2nd floor balcony overlooking the beautiful pool and lagoon. Bedroom #4 contains a king bed, TV, and a shares a bathroom with bedroom #5 which has 2 twin beds and a TV. The next bedroom has a queen bed, TV, and shares a bath with the 7th bedroom which contains 2 twins and a TV.

Outdoor amenities include a fabulous pool area complete with spa and gas grill. This ample-sized home offers enough living space for 2 families – it is a wonderful choice for your next Hilton Head getaway.
