Hilton Spectacular, Hilton Head Island, South Carolina Vacation Rental


HILTON HEAD. This spectacular oceanfront home is absolutely amazing. The living room is located directly off of the grand entry. It has a sofa, love seat, two chairs, large flat screen TV, DVD, stereo, decorative fireplace, ocean view, and a bar area. The spacious, fully equipped kitchen is located to the right of the living room and features granite counter tops, a small flat screen TV, a bar area that seats five, breakfast table that seats seven, door to the deck, and a wonderful ocean view.

The first bedroom is located to the left of the entry and has two twin beds, TV, DVD, and a private bathroom with a tub/shower combination. The second bedroom has a king bed, TV, a door to the deck with an ocean view, and a large private bathroom with a jetted tub and separate shower. The remaining four bedrooms are located on the second floor. The third bedroom is located to the left of the stairs and has two twin beds, TV, DVD, and a private bathroom with a tub/shower combination. The fourth bedroom has a king bed, TV, VCR, door to the balcony with an ocean view and a private bathroom with a jetted tub and a separate shower. The fifth bedroom has a king bed, TV, DVD, door to the balcony with an ocean view, and a private bathroom with a shower only. The sixth bedroom also has a king bed, TV, door to balcony with an ocean view and a private bathroom with a shower. The upstairs grand hall has a game table and leather sofa that looks out at the ocean.
