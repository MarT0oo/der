Moon River Premier, Kiawah Resort, South Carolina Vacation Rental

 

Enjoy the quintessential Kiawah Island experience in this magnificent 6000 square foot home with breathtaking views of the Kiawah River and tidal marshes from every room. Located at the easterly tip of exclusive Rhett’s Bluff and surrounded by water on three sides, this 6 bedroom/6.5 bath retreat constructed on an acre of land offers privacy, security and exclusivity unique even to Kiawah. Features that allow guests the optimum connection with the home’s natural surroundings include an outdoor swimming pool, deck and spa, a large screen porch with additional dining accommodations, and a private dock.

The property, named “Moon River” by its owners for the spectacular moonlit evening views over the river and marshes, features an open and spacious floor plan with high ceilings, elevator access at every level, a family room, sitting room and library, and a beautifully appointed kitchen with granite countertops, built-in refrigeration, ice machine, two dishwashers, a six- burner professional stove and separate butler’s pantry. Every bedroom features a private bath and flat screen television. The large master bedroom on the second floor boasts an expansive balcony and floor-to-ceiling glass. Due to the complete privacy of the master bedroom setting, its panoramic windows remain untouched by a shade or curtain. The third floor king bedroom suite boasts a separate sauna off the full bathroom.

This serene home is perfect for large families on vacation, or executive golf or strategic retreats, and offers close proximity to all Kiawah amenities, as well as ample parking in and out of the garage. Entertain your family, friends and colleagues in a truly world class setting.
