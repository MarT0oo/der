Rum Runner Getaway, Palmetto Dunes, Hilton Head Island, South Carolina Vacation Rental

 

This newly renovated home overlooking the Robert Trent Jones golf course offers a great layout and awesome outdoor space for the entire family! With three bedrooms and three bathroom, this house offers plenty of space for you and your family.

The master bedroom offers one King bed, a flat screen TV, and overlooks the pool and golf course. The master bathroom features a walk-in shower and a large, modern bathtub. The second bedroom, located in the back of the house, includes two Queen beds, a flat screen TV, and a private bathroom with a walk-in shower. The third bedroom has one Queen bed with a flat screen TV and a private bathroom with a walk-in shower.

The beautiful, spacious backyard includes a back deck with outside seating and a grill in addition to the private hot tub and pool. All outside amenities overlook the beautiful Robert Trent Jones golf course.

*Free shuttle service through Palmetto Dunes Buggy available April through September.

*Pool ans spa may be heated upon request for an additional fee.

Palmetto Dunes with its bike paths, golf, tennis, boating, shopping and dining is a world-class destination. Enjoy your beach vacation strolling or biking alongside loggerhead turtle nests, watching the dolphins swim by or admire the array of herons and ospreys in their own special habitat.
