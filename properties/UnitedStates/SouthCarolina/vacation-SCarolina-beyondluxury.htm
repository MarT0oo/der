Beyond Luxury Estate, Hilton Head Island, South Carolina Vacation Rental


Over an acre of grounds surround this spacious oceanfront estate which is located near the south gate of “Sea Pines Plantation”. The grand entrance, via a beautiful landscaped private cul-de-sac, sets the opening stage for a fantastic vacation. From the champagne and martini glasses to the poolside cabana to the wide quiet beach you can feel the luxury everywhere.

The kitchen has state-of-the-art appliances including; 2 Miele dishwashers, gourmet chef’s range and ovens, granite countertops with spacious island and just steps away from the kitchen is a gas grill for barbeque aficionados. Two separate spacious dining rooms, a breakfast bar plus outdoor dining areas offer space for large and small groups to gather. The Media Room is equipped with a 60+ inch TV, a video library, stereo, a wet bar w/ ice machine, a computer with internet access (free wireless internet throughout house also), and plenty of seating.

The outdoor area has three pools – large main pool, spa, and a baby pool. The 2nd kitchen is located in the poolside Cabana making it convenient to chill your favorite beverages or prepare snacks. The cabana, painted in a whimsical tropical design, has a refrigerator, ice machine, dishwasher, microwave, and a roomy Corian bar/seating area. For your outdoor enjoyment there is: a generous raised Trex deck, spacious poolside decking, lighted raised boardwalk to the beach and an ocean side beach pad – for daytime activity or evening happy hour. There is also an outdoor shower to use as guests come up from the beach!
