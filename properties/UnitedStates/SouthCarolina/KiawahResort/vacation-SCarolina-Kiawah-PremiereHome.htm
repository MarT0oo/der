Premiere Home, Kiawah Island Resort, South Carolina


Prepare to create unforgettable memories within this 3-story Premier Ocean View Home. This home boasts 8 spacious bedrooms; including 5 oceanfront king bedrooms and 3 double bunk bedrooms with 3 bonus trundles!  Soak in spectacular ocean views along the covered porches where friends and family will have room to spread out. Two spacious family rooms are equipped to entertain with large flat screen T.V's and plenty of seating.  Enjoy family meals at the dining room table, along the kitchen island, or take the meal outside and relax on the screened porch. Outdoors you will find yourself lounging by the heated pool and hot tub, grilling under the veranda, and strolling to the ocean along your private beach access. This home also features 8 full bathrooms, 3 half baths (1 on pool deck), two laundry rooms, an elevator, wireless internet, and upgrades at every turn. Don't miss out on this unparalleled Kiawah experience!
