96 Windsong Premiere Home, Kiawah Island Resort, South Carolina


Your search for the ultimate multi-family beach house on Kiawah Island is over. Unmatched on the island, this home sits as close to the ocean as they come. With 8 spacious bedrooms, including 5 oceanfront king rooms, each with spectacular ocean views and private porches to enjoy the breezes, and 3 rooms with double bunks, friends and family will have room to spread out.

Two large family rooms boast theatre sized flat screen T.V's and plenty of seating. Enjoy family meals at the 14 person kitchen table, or take the meal outside and relax on the covered veranda which boasts a fireplace, bar, flat screen, full outdoor kitchen/grilling area, and quite possibly the most stunning view of the ocean on the island. Adults and kids will enjoy the spacious pool and spa, and parents will rest easy with the automatic pool cover. This home also features 10 bathrooms, game systems, wireless internet, and upgrades at every turn. From the moment you drive up the stately driveway on lovely Surfsong Lane, your dream vacation has begun!
