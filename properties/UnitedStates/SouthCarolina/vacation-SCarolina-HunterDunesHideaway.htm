Hunter Dunes Hideaway, Palmetto Dunes, Hilton Head Island, South Carolina Vacation Rental

 

6 steps lead to the entry of this lovely 3,000 square foot home in prestigious Palmetto Dunes. You will find hardwood flooring throughout the living and dining areas. The living room has a sleeper sofa, TV/DVD, stereo and slider to a back deck with a grill and seating for up to 6. Off the living area is a dining table for 6. The kitchen comes fully equipped with double oven, granite countertops, and a breakfast nook seating 4.

The master suite features a king bed, TV/DVD, and private bath with a tub and separate shower. The second bedroom has 2 queens, TV, and uses a hall bath with a tub/shower combination. The 3rd and final bedroom has a double bed and a private bath with a tub/shower combination.

This great Hilton Head Island home, on the lagoon, is close to tennis, golf, and the beach. It is truly a great value and a fabulous choice for your next vacation.

*Free shuttle service through Palmetto Dunes Buggy available April through September.

Palmetto Dunes with its bike paths, golf, tennis, boating, shopping and dining is a world-class destination. Enjoy your beach vacation strolling or biking alongside loggerhead turtle nests, watching the dolphins swim by or admire the array of herons and ospreys in their own special habitat.
