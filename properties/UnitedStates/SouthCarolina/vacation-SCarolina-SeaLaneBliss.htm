Sea Lane Bliss, Palmetto Dunes, Hilton Head Island, South Carolina Vacation Rental

 

This spacious, 3 bedroom, 3 bath home is located in a wooded setting overlooking the Robert Trent Jones golf course in Palmetto Dunes. Spacious and bright with vaulted ceilings, the living area has a TV/DVD, stereo system, game table, wet bar, and two large sofas, one of which is a queen sleeper sofa. The very private master suite has a king bed, TV and private bath with jacuzzi tub. The second bedroom has a queen bed, its own TV, and private bath. The third bedroom is over the garage, and has two double beds and a TV. It also has a private bath, and an adjacent study offers a queen sleeper sofa, love seat, and TV. Added features of this Hilton Head home include many skylights, full size washer and dryer, grill, pool, an expansive deck, and a private dock on the lagoon. This home is a 7 minute walk to the fabulous beaches of Palmetto Dunes.

*Pool heat available May-Sept for an additional charge.

*Free shuttle service through Palmetto Dunes Buggy available April through September.

Palmetto Dunes with its bike paths, golf, tennis, boating, shopping and dining is a world-class destination. Enjoy your beach vacation strolling or biking alongside loggerhead turtle nests, watching the dolphins swim by or admire the array of herons and ospreys in their own special habitat.
