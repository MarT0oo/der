Pristine Palmetto Paradise, Hilton Head Island, South Carolina Vacation Rental


Gracing the pristine beaches of Palmetto Dunes, this fabulous 6-bedroom home offers 3 living areas, beautiful furnishings, and a kitchen that is a true gourmet lover’s delight. 13 steps lead to the entrance of the home. From here, you will find an exquisite dining area with seating for 8. Just beyond the dining area is the first sitting room with a TV, VCR, DVD, and wet bar. The kitchen boasts granite countertops, stainless steel appliances, a stereo, and a breakfast table for 6. Just off the kitchen is a second living room with a TV, VCR, DVD and desk with high speed internet access. A luxurious master suite has a king bed, TV, DVD player, 2 walk-in closets, and a large private bath with a jetted tub and separate shower. A second bedroom on this level offers 2 double beds, a TV, VCR, and DVD player. This bedroom also has a private bath and a walk-in closet.

19 steps lead to the second floor where you will find a third oversized sitting area. This room has floor to ceiling windows with panoramic views of the Atlantic. You will enjoy the state of-the art sound system complete with TV, VCR, DVD, and stereo. There’s also a table that seats 4, a wet bar with seating for 3, a small refrigerator, and icemaker.

Also located on the second floor is the superbly appointed 2nd master suite. Like the downstairs master, this room offers a king bed, TV, VCR, DVD player, 2 walk-in closets, and a large private bath with a jetted tub and separate shower. The 4th bedroom has a king bed, TV, VCR, DVD player, and door to the 2nd floor balcony. A private bathroom has a shower only. The 5th bedroom offers a king bed, TV, VCR, and private bathroom with a tub/shower combination. The 6th bedroom has two twin beds, TV, VCR, and private bathroom with a tub/shower combination. Enjoy the sun in the elegant ocean-side pool and Jacuzzi. This secluded spot offers a lounge area, gas grill, and a table for outdoor dining. Pristine Palmetto Paradise is a superb choice for guests with the most discriminating of tastes.
