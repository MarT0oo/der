Marvelous Dunes Palace, Hilton Head Island, South Carolina


Located in the heart of Palmetto Dunes Plantation, the Marvelous Dunes Palace offers direct access to the famous Hilton Head beach and is just a short walk to the tennis center and Robert Trent Jones Golf Course. Upon entry guests will find an open floor plan with the kitchen, living and dining areas. The kitchen features all stainless steel appliances with an ice maker, wine cooler and granite countertops. It has an additional sink at an island with 2 barstools. The laundry room is located off the kitchen with a full size washer and dryer. The breakfast area has a table for 6 and access to the porch, which can also be accessed from the living room. The two-story living room offers plenty of seating with two sofas, two chairs, and a game table for four. There is a large TV with a DVD player and a decorative fireplace to relax and unwind. Next to the living area is the dining room that has a large table for 8.

On the first floor you will find 3 bedrooms - two are master suites with king beds and direct access to the porch. The third bedroom has 2 twin beds. The second level of the home offers 2 more master suites with king beds, 2 bedrooms with queen beds and a bedroom with 2 double beds. All of the bedrooms have direct access to a bathroom, with the exception of the double bedroom which shares a bath directly across the hall with one of the queen bedrooms. Both king suites and the double bedroom have access to the second level porch that has a table for four, sofa and additional chairs. There is also a small kitchen with a refrigerator, sink, microwave, dishwasher and stovetop. All rooms have TVs. In the basement level of the home you will find a media room including a 52 inch TV, pool table, game table, ping pong table, sleeper sofa and 4 recliners. From the basement you also have direct access to the pool area. A pull out sofa is located in the media room and two of the oversized masters to accommodate a total of twenty-four. This is a perfect home for sharing with three or four families.

The large covered porch on the first floor has a built-in fireplace, dining table for four, side chairs, a gas grill and overlooks the pool area. 15 stairs lead down to the pool and spa where you will find lounge chairs and additional seating to sun bathe.
