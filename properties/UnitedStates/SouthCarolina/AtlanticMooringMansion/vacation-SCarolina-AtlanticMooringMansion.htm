Atlantic Mooring Mansion, Hilton Head Island, South Carolina


Beautifully appointed, this 6-bedroom Hilton Head home is just moments from the sand only third row from the beautiful Atlantic Ocean and beaches! With a 20’ x 40’ pool one of the largest on the Island!!

Four steps lead to the entry with the main living area and dining room. The living room has a TV/VCR/DVD, stereo with CD player, and a door to the pool area. The dining room seats 8 and leads to a spacious kitchen with tile flooring, granite counters, TV, stereo, table for 4, and a door to a screened porch overlooking the pool. There is HD TV throughout. The first bedroom has 2 twins, TV/DVD, and private bath. The next bedroom is a master suite with king bed, TV/DVD, walk-in closet, and a large private bath with a jetted tub and separate shower. Sixteen steps lead to the second floor with 4 more bedrooms. The first has a king, TV/DVD, walk-in closet, and private bath with garden tub and separate shower.

The next two bedrooms share a bath. Both have twin beds and TVs and DVDs. The final bedroom has a king bed, TV/DVD, private bath with jetted tub and separate shower, and a small kitchen with a small refrigerator and microwave. This suite also has a door to a small private balcony with steps leading to the pool area.

This home offers a magnificent pool area complete with gas grill and plenty of room for lounging. The pool itself is 20’ x 40’. It is very private and gets a lot of sun. A private beach path to the right of the home leads to the gorgeous beaches of Palmetto Dunes. No detail has been spared in this wonderful Hilton Head Island home.
