Dogwood Cottage, South Forest Beach, Hilton Head, South Carolina Vacation Rental

 

This lovely cottage home offers guests an oasis with its warm and comforting modern, yet nautical décor. With large gathering space and open floor-plan, this home is great for your next group getaway! The beautifully decorated living room gives off an airy, coastal feel with its vaulted ceilings and classic, nautical décor. There is a flatscreen TV, two sofas, and two armchairs, offering plenty of seating for everyone! The completely renovated kitchen has granite countertops, stainless steel appliances, and eat-in bar with seating for two. The dining room, located just off the kitchen bar, has a large table with seating for six.

The master bedroom has a King bed, flatscreen TV, and private en-suite bathroom with double vanities and beautiful walk-in shower. The second bedroom has a King bed, flatscreen TV, and shares the hall bath that has a tub/shower combination. The third bedroom has two Full beds and flatscreen TV. This bedroom shares the hall bath as well.

The outside amenities include a large, fenced in patio. This Hilton Head home is approximately a 3 minute walk to the beach.
