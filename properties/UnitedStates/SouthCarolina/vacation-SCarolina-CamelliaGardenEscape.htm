Camellia Garden Escape, Palmetto Dunes, South Carolina Vacation Rental

 

This amazing Hilton Head oceanfront home offers spectacular ocean views and luxurious furnishings.

17 steps lead to the entry where you will find a sunken living room, two steps down, with two matching sofas and chairs, decorative fireplace, wall-mounted flat screen TV, and large windows with amazing oceanfront views. Sharing the ocean views is a second living room with large entertainment center, flat screen TV, DVD player, plenty of additional seating including a sofa, loveseat, and chair with ottoman. This room also offers entry to the pool deck. In the open kitchen you will find a bar with ice maker, breakfast table with seating for up to seven, door to the pool deck, two high-top chairs, custom cabinets, and granite countertops. Here you will also find top of the line stainless appliances including a built-in double oven and microwave, gas range, and Keurig coffeemaker. A large laundry room with full-size washer and dryer and plenty of cabinet and countertop space is located off of the kitchen area as well as a half bath.

Located on the first floor is the master suite offering a king bed, armoire with flat screen TV, decorative fireplace, sofa with two matching chairs, beautiful ocean views with a door leading to the pool deck, and a large private bathroom with his/her sinks, walk-in closet, Jacuzzi tub, and separate stand-up shower. The second bedroom has a full bed, flat screen TV, and a private bath with stand-up shower.

Eighteen steps lead to the second floor where you will find the additional three bedrooms. The first bedroom has two queen beds, large dresser with flat screen TV, door to a private balcony with ocean views, and a large private bathroom with Jacuzzi tub, separate stand-up shower, and walk-in closet. The third bedroom has a king bed, flat screen TV, BluRay DVD player, two chairs, walk-in closet, ocean views, and also has a private bathroom with garden tub and separate stand-up shower. The last bedroom offers two twin beds, each with a trundle, large dresser with flat screen TV, walk-in closet, a door to a private balcony with beautiful ocean views, and a private bathroom with tub/shower combination. In the landing area you will find a small room with game table and four chairs as well as a private patio facing the front of the home with two chairs and small end table, perfect for reading the daily paper.

The backyard is sure to please everyone who stays in this five-star home offering a large *pool with plenty of seating, including eight lounge chairs, table with four chairs, and a separate *hot tub. Six steps down in where you will find the beach path, small yard, and outside shower as well as a private Camellia garden.

No details have been spared in this oceanfront beauty!
