White Grass Lodge, Big Sky, Montana Vacation Rental

 

With amazing views of Lone Peak, the White Grass Lodge is the perfect home for your extended family vacation, and in winter ski in/ski out access to White Otter chair lift makes this a dream home.

Ideal for large families, this luxury home boasts 6 Bedrooms, each with a king bed, and a bonus room downstairs. There are additional beds in the upstairs bedrooms, one with a twin bed and one with a full. One downstairs bedroom has a full bed in addition to the king, and the bonus room downstairs houses a queen bed and a twin daybed. The master on the main level offers a private bathroom, while the other downstairs bedroom offers access to the deck.

The family room is a fantastic place for quality time together around the gas fireplace, the Wii gaming system, foosball and pool tables, two TVs, and a wet bar. End a fun day of skiing by soaking in the luxurious hot tub on the main level.

For your convenience there is a two car garage with two additional refrigerators, as well as a laundry room. This beautiful mountain home is a skier’s dream, with a ski room downstairs for equipment storage.
