Spanish Peaks Cabin 168 Nighthawk, Spanish Peaks Mountain Club, Montana Vacation Rental

 

Ski in/ski out and on the first fairway in Spanish Peaks! Located on in the picturesque privacy of Big Sky's Spanish Peaks Mountain Club on Andesite Mountain, this convenient ski in/ski out cabin offers the perfect year-round getaway from the hustle and bustle of city life. In summer, walk off the back deck and onto the first fairway of the award winning Spanish Peaks golf course. Relax and enjoy the mountain views on one of the many private decks after a day exploring Yellowstone or cozy up with a great book by a roaring fire in the den while all of your cares fade away.

This home is a short walk (or drive) to the beautiful Spanish Peaks clubhouse. The home offers 4 bedrooms plus a loft. There are three bedrooms on the main level, each with a king bed, TV, private bathroom, and access to a deck. Also on the main level is the den, which has a wood burning fireplace for comfortable nights in. The fourth bedroom is located upstairs and houses a king bed and a TV. The loft offers a twin bed with twin trundle. A laundry room, gas grill, detached one car garage and private outdoor hot tub are available for your convenience.

Temporary guest membership at the private Spanish Peaks Mountain Club is required for an additional fee, applied to persons 12 years and older.
