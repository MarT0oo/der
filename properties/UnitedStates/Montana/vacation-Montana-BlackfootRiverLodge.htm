Blackfoot River Lodge, Greenough, Montana Vacation Rental

 

Nestled in a beautiful private setting on the banks of the Blackfoot River, this private yet family-friendly vacation home blends the charm of an authentic Montana dude ranch lodge with exquisite western furnishings and décor. This four-bedroom, three-bathroom home features the following: master bedroom suite with an enormous master bath; private office; full, kitchen with slate flooring; large dining room and living room with large stone fireplace; laundry room; four exterior decks and spectacular views of the mighty Blackfoot.

The Blackfoot River Lodge also features a private luxury tent located just outside. Situated on the banks of the Blackfoot River, this chic canvas abode provides the perfect outdoor, overnight wilderness adventure for up to two people. The private tent option at this home is available late May through Sept. This home also comes with a complimentary personal vehicle for transportation around the Resort.
