Andesite Lodge, Big Sky Resort, Montana Vacation Rental

 

With easy access to Yellowstone National Park, Andesite Lodge is just 10 minutes away from year-round fun including skiing, white water rafting, hiking, golfing, and fishing. When your day of adventure is done, sit back on the sweeping deck or soak in your own private hot tub while you take in the gorgeous mountain views.

This home boasts 4 bedrooms, 3.5 baths, and is beautifully appointed. Two bedrooms offer king beds and one offers a queen, each with private bathrooms. Each room that has a king bed also has its own TV. There are four twin beds in the fourth bedroom, making it the perfect room for the kids. Additional sleeping is available on the queen sleeper sofa in the bonus room, which also has a TV.

A wood burning fireplace sits in the living room for chilly evenings around the fire. The kids and adults alike will also love to gather in the loft, which offers a TV, a half bath, and a deck. Your vacation will be made more convenient by the laundry room, gas grill for preparing delicious meals at home, and a two car garage.
