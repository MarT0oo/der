Renegade Chalet, Big Sky, Montana Vacation Rental

 

Tucked on the slopes of Lone Peak, this stunning Big Sky vacation home offers all the comforts one could need after a long and enjoyable day of adventure. Located in Moonlight Basin, this beautiful vacation rental is a short drive from Big Sky Resort and other shops and amenities right in town.

In the winter, take advantage of the direct ski in ski out access and enjoy the comforts of a dedicated ski room. Located not far from the Bootlegger Lift at Big Sky Resort, rest easy knowing you can return home, slip off your boots, and relax in the private outdoor hot tub. Explore everything winter has to offer, all from this central Montana luxury rental.

In the summer, enjoy the long hours of daylight while relaxing on the deck and cooking dinner on the grill. Regardless of which part of this gorgeous home is your favorite, the Big Sky Montana views from every window are stunning! The location of Renegade Chalet also makes it a perfect Yellowstone vacation home, with a short hour drive to the entrance of Yellowstone National Park.

This home is perfect for the adventure seeker or family looking to get away, with direct ski-in/out access to Big Sky Resort. There are two bedrooms on the main level, each with a king bed, private bathroom, gas fireplace, and access to a deck. The third bedroom is located downstairs complete with a king bed and private bathroom. Downstairs there are two more bedrooms, one of which has a full bed, twin bunk bed, and private bathroom. The other offers a queen bed, twin bunk bed, and private bathroom. Please note, this Big Sky rental home is not dog friendly!

A two car garage is also available for your convenience, as well as a ski room with boot dryers and lockers and a private hot tub. Book the Big Sky getaway of your dreams during any season at the remarkable Renegade Chalet. Luxury amenities, a private location, and central access to everything Big Sky Montana has to offer make this retreat perfect for your next vacation.
