Spanish Peaks Cabin, Big Sky, Montana Vacation Rental

 

Located on Andesite Mountain in the picturesque privacy of Spanish Peaks Resort, the Spanish Peaks Cabins provide the perfect getaway from the hustle and bustle of city life. Relax and enjoy the mountain views on one of the many private decks or cozy up with a great book by a roaring fire in the den.

There are two bedrooms on the main level, one with a king bed and the other with a queen sleeper sofa—each with a private bathroom. The king bedroom also offers access to the patio. Upstairs there is one bedroom, which offers a king bed, private bathroom, office and a deck. The last two bedrooms are located downstairs, one with two sets of twin bunks and the other with a Captain’s bunk bed set. Each has a private bathroom. The family Room is also downstairs and has TV, gas burning fireplace and wet bar. A laundry room, gas grill, detached two car garage and private outdoor hot tub are available for your convenience.

This home is a short drive from Big Sky Resort's Southern Comfort lift. Temporary guest membership at the private Spanish Peaks Mountain Club is required for an additional fee, applied to persons 12 years and older.
