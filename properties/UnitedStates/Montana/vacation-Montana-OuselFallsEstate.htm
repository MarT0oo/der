Ousel Falls Estate, Big Sky, Montana Vacation Rental

 

Luxury ski cabin with club amenities, a private hot tub and direct access to the Ousel Falls Trail. This luxurious Big Sky vacation home sits perched directly above the beautiful Ousel Falls, tucked away on a quiet, private lot.

Guests are just a few minutes away from the clubhouse at Spanish peaks Mountain Club which offers ski access, ski valet service, outdoor heated pool, fitness center, golf course and much more.

In the summer, guests are a step away from hiking, swimming, and other outdoor activities. There is a footpath from the backyard of this home down to the base of the falls providing direct Access to the Ousel Falls Trail and Yellow Mules Trail in the Gallatin National Forest, offering year-round recreation opportunities.

This 5 bedroom home has a spacious deck off the main living room with gas grill and private hot tub. In addition, there is a sitting area on the bluff overlooking the picturesque Ousel Falls and Gallatin National Forest.The kitchen has all stainless steel appliances with a double dishwasher and wine fridge. There are dining seats for 8 people, four seats at the breakfast bar and an additional four seats at the breakfast nook. The living room features a wood stove, a couch, two armchairs and flat screen TV with DirecTV and satellite radio system controlled by Ipads.

The master bedroom has a king bed, large walk-in closet and en-suite bathroom with soaker tub and steam shower. The master bath attaches to a private yoga studio and fitness room. The second bedroom located on the upper floor and has a dual zone firmness control king sized bed with en-suite bathroom and shower. The third and fourth bedrooms are located on the upper level. Both have queen beds and share a Jack and Jill bathroom with a shower/tub combo. The fifth bedroom, located on the lower level, features a queen bunk bed and shared bath with shower/tub combo.

There is a large family room with a custom theater located on the lower level as well as a boot dryer in the mud room on the main level. A two car attached garage is also available.

Temporary guest membership at the private Spanish Peaks Mountain Club is required for an additional fee, applied to persons 12 years and older.
