Cowboy Heaven Cabins 3 Rustic Ridge, Near Big Sky Resort, Montana Vacation Rental

 

Located near the Moonlight Lodge, directly on the groomed run Gambler, the Cowboy Heaven Cabins enjoy beautiful views year-round, and easy ski access to all of Big Sky Resort's terrain in the winter.

This comfortable cabin-style home is suited for a family, offering 2 bedrooms and 2 bathrooms. The first bedroom provides a queen bed and private bathroom, and the second provides twin bunk bed sets.

When your day of adventure comes to an end, take a dip your private hot tub and enjoy the majestic view of Lone Mountain and the valley below. 3 Rustic Ridge sits just at the tree line, giving you a private place to retreat and soak in the incredible surrounding scenery. During chilly winter evenings, slip inside, gather by the fireplace, and enjoy Direct TV and Wi-Fi. For your convenience, the cabin offers a washer and dryer.

Whether you join us in the summer or winter, everyone will have the ultimate Montana experience in the cozy Cowboy Heaven Cabins.
