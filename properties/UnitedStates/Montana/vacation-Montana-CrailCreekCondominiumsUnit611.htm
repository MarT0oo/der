Crail Creek Condominiums Unit 611, Big Sky Resort, Montana Vacation Rental

 

Located in the heart of the Big Sky Meadow Village, the Crail Creek Condominiums provide great access to the Big Sky Golf Course in the summer as well as miles of groomed cross country skiing in the winter.

Relax in the hot tub overlooking the Big Sky Golf Course and enjoy frequent sightings of moose and elk walking right through the yard, or fire up the grill and enjoy a scrumptious outdoor meal.

The condo offers three bedrooms, three bathrooms, and a beautiful open living room with vaulted ceilings. The first bedroom has a king bed and the second has a queen, each with a private bathroom. The third room is ideal for kids, offering a Captain’s bunk bed and twin bunk bed. A loft upstairs provides a comfortable lounging area, and overlooks the incredible mountainous landscape beyond the home.

For your convenience there is a laundry room and a one car garage assigned to the condo.

Natural Retreats guests in Big Sky have access to exclusive Moonlight Basin Premium parking for only $30 per day. This offers a personal greeting by one of the Moonlight Basin valet members at the parking kiosk, personal ski or snowboard delivery from the car to ski racks on the snow to begin the day as well as delivery from the mountain back to the car at the end of the day. In addition, guests will receive a premium parking spot and concierge services that include lunch/dinner reservations, après ski information as well as mountain information with run suggestions for all levels of skiers
