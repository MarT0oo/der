See Me Lodge, Steamboat Springs, Colorado Vacation Rental

 

See Me Lodge is majestic mountain home that ranks among the most impressive in Steamboat Springs. This magnificent 6-bedroom luxury vacation home offers ultimate privacy and commanding views of the valley from its perch above the base area with easy access to the ski trails.

Originating from Glacier National Monument, the huge timbers of this finely crafted Colorado Log Home form the structure of the large great room. Here you can relax in front of the wood-burning fireplace and enjoy striking valley views over the Steamboat Ski Resort through the giant picture windows. The large curved leather couch is also the perfect spot to take in the big game or watch a movie on the LCD TV. The enormous wrap-around deck offers the ultimate view over the mountain village and is a great place for al fresco dining in summer.

The fully equipped kitchen with unique granite countertops, custom cabinetry, and slate flooring offers all that a chef could want. Appliances include Sub-Zero refrigerator, Viking gas range, electric convection oven.

Adjacent to the kitchen is a 12 person dining table to relax or watch your chef prepare dinner – especially if you've selected our Luxury Catered Chalet option!

The six spacious bedrooms combine contemporary with mountain lifestyle. On the main level the master bedroom with King Bed, gas fireplace, large walk-in closet and reading nook is the perfect retreat. The private ensuite bathroom includes double vanities, steam shower, and a jetted tub. On the upper level is the second master with a plush queen bed and private bathroom. On the lower level leading off the family room is the bunk room with two twin-full bunk beds that kids just love. This room includes a full private bathroom. A fourth bedroom is also found on this level with Queen bed, and the fifth bedroom with King bed, closet, a study with built-in bench seats and office desk, deck access and flat-screen TV. These two bedrooms share a large full bathroom with double vanity sinks.

Another unique feature of See Me Lodge is the fully equipped Guest House that is attached to the main house by a covered walkway. This is ideal for those needing a little extra space or peace and quiet. It’s perfect for teenagers, in-laws, friends, or for those just seeking extra solitude. Here you also can enjoy having your own additional kitchen and living room with gas fireplace and flat screen TV plus an additional washer and dryer. In the upper loft there is a Full bed and two Twin beds. On the main level, there is another bedroom with a Queen bed. These two sleeping rooms share a large full bathroom with double vanities.

The large family room on the lower level offers additional space for relaxation. Enjoy movies on the 52" big screen TV with surround sound and black-out curtains so you can create your own home movie theater. Outside is another deck area with additional outdoor seating areas. Overlooking the great room is a magnificent bar with another TV so you can catch the game in your own “pub”. The adjacent pool room offers a venue where family rivalries can be settled in style.

Après ski cannot be surpassed once you've sat in the leather, rocking chairs in the sunset room and watched the alpenglow envelope the mountain. The outdoor hot tub is a tranquil retreat to soak and relive the days' adventures. Complimentary private ski-shuttle is included for super-easy access to the ski trails so you don’t need to rent a car, but if you do then there is a large driveway with four enclosed heated garage parking spaces.
