Seven Rose, Big Sky Resort, Montana Vacation Rental

 

Seven Rose is a cozy retreat for the whole family. The Powder Ridge Cabins are only minutes from the shopping, dining and activities offered in the Big Sky Mountain Village. This comfortable home is, beautifully decorated and offers direct ski-in ski-out access to Big Sky Resort via the White Otter lift.

In the winter, guests can enjoy ski-in ski-out access via the White Otter Lift and more a 5 minute drive or 15 minute walk to the Mountain Village Base Area. Store all of your snow gear in ski lockers conveniently located on the front porch. Take advantage of the private hot tub to warm you up after a day on the slopes.

In the summer, enjoy the outdoor living area perfect for entertaining. The location of this Powder Ridge home also makes it an ideal Yellowstone vacation spot, with a short hour drive to the entrance of Yellowstone National Park. If you want to stick a little closer to home, look no farther than outside your front door, Big Sky has renowned fly fishing, hiking, horseback riding, white water rafting and golf. With so many activities at your dispense, you are sure to have an adventure filled vacation!

The kitchen is equipped with new state of the art appliances. The dining area will seat 6 people with an additional 2 stools at the breakfast bar. The living area is located on the main level, with a couch and love seat, guests can relax in front of the wood burning fireplace or enjoy the flat screen television with DVD player and sound system.

The master bedroom on the upper level has a king bed and en suite bathroom with dual shower heads. The second bedroom is also on the main level and has a queen bed with a shared bathroom with newly updated features. A third bedroom is found on the main level and has a queen bunk and shares the bathroom with the second bedroom. The fourth bedroom is located on the lower level and has a king bed and shared 3/4 bath with the fifth bedroom/family room. The family room is located downstairs with a queen sleeper sofa and 3/4 bathroom. In order to preserve the quality of this home please note, this Big Sky vacation rental is not pet friendly.

This cabin has a private deck off the kitchen with a gas grill, private patio on the lower level with an outdoor hot tub and a wood burning fireplace in living room. It also features a laundry room with washer and dryer as well as a detached one car garage.
