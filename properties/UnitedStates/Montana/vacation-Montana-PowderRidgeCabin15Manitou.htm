Powder Ridge Cabin 15 Manitou, Near Big Sky Resort, Montana Vacation Rental

 

This Powder Ridge Cabin is a cozy retreat for the whole family. The Powder Ridge Cabins are only minutes from the shopping, dining and activities offered in the Big Sky Mountain Village.

This home is comfortable and beautifully decorated with direct ski-in/out access to Big Sky Resort via the White Otter lift. Bedroom #1 is on the main level and offers a queen bed and shared hall bathroom. Bedroom #2 is also on the main level and has two twin beds and shares the hall bathroom with #1. Bedroom #3 is located upstairs and has a king bed, TV, Blu-ray player, and private bathroom. Bedroom #4 is located downstairs and offers a king bed, TV, Blu-ray player, and private bathroom. The lower level family room has a queen sleeper sofa, gas burning fireplace, and a TV.

This home features a private outdoor hot tub, gourmet kitchen with seating for 8 at dining table and 2 at the bar. The living room has a TV, Blu-ray player, and wood burning fireplace. The home also offers a laundry room and one car garage.
