Wilderness Estates, Greenough, Montana Vacation Rental

 

Escape to a wonderland of palatial rustic glamour at The Wilderness Estates, a collection of luxury ranch homes nestled a world away from the everyday at our breathtaking Montana resort. Whatever you can imagine is at your fingertips. Total indulgence. Complete relaxation. A secluded wilderness sanctuary, all discretely hidden behind gated chaparrals. Your expansive luxury ranch accommodation comes complete with panoramic vistas, uber-privacy and an extensive list of features including a great room with a soaring vaulted ceiling, a cook's kitchen, a loft game room, and three master suites featuring en-suite master bathrooms outfitted with a spacious slate shower, a jetted spa tub, sumptuous spa products and plush towels.

When you're ready to fully disconnect, saunter out to your sprawling private deck for a soak in your rejuvenating, six-person hot tub. If and when you're ready to reconnect, high-speed wireless Internet access, surround-sound entertainment systems and 42" flat-screen TVs are at your beck and call. Each Wilderness Estate comes with a complimentary personal vehicle for your use during your stay. The Wilderness Estates - the wilds of Montana have never been so civilized.
