Moondance Lodge, Big Sky, Montana Vacation Rental

 

Luxury home with great views of Big Sky! Private hot tub, game room, home theater, close ski access. With expansive decks, and views to match, Moondance Lodge is surrounded by the natural beauty of Big Sky. In winter, this property is ski accessible to the White Otter/Cascade lifts. Moondance Lodge is equipped with a two car garage, a laundry room, and a boot warmer in the mud room.

Upstairs, you'll find a bedroom containing a king sized bed and a flat screen television, as well as access to a private deck and a spacious private bathroom. There are three bedrooms downstairs, two of which feature a queen bed. The first queen bedroom has a private bath, while the second queen bedroom has a shared bath with the third downstairs bedroom. The third downstairs bedroom contains a Captain's bunk bed set, great for kids or adults. Above the garage is the home's fifth bedroom which has four twin beds and a private bathroom.

Downstairs is a family-friendly game room with a wet bar, a pool table, and a home theater. The home also offers a hot tub and gas grill on the private deck.

Early season and late season ski access is condition-dependent and dictated by Big Sky Resort operations.
