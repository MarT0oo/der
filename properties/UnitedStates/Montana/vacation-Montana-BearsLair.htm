Bear's Lair, Big Sky, Montana Vacation Rental

 

Fantastic Big Sky home for families and groups - Private hot tub, spacious deck, and updated features. Bear's Lair is the ideal Big Sky getaway in winter and summer, newly updated for families and larger groups. This 4 bedroom home is located in the Mountain Village area of Big Sky and is ski accessible via the Cascade and White Otter Lifts.

Just a short drive to the Beehive Basin trailhead, this is the perfect home for outdoor adventures. Beehive Basin is a perfect hike for all ages, offering some of the best wildflower viewing in the lower 48 during the summer months, and snowshoeing and backcountry skiing during the winter months.

The living room on the main floor has floor to ceiling views of Lone Peak and is the perfect spot for everyone to gather and catch up after a long day of skiing. Cozy up to the gas fireplace on the sofa or one of the two arm chairs and enjoy the sound system or large flat screen TV with streaming capabilities and Netflix. Just a few steps away is the spacious kitchen with updated appliances, seating for eight at the dining table and an additional four stools at the kitchen counter. There is a large outdoor deck off the main level with an outdoor dining table, fire pit, gas grill and spectacular views from Andesite to Lone Peak.

The master bedroom is located on the main floor has a king bed with en-suite bath, walk-in closet, steam shower and a private walk out to the hot tub. On the upper level, the second bedroom with a queen captain’s bunk has mountain views; this shares a shower/bath combo with the third king bedroom. On the upper landing, a foosball table and game area offers another get-away in the house. The large fourth bedroom on the lower level can accommodate a family of four with a queen bed, twin bunk bed, en suite bathroom, and big screen TV with X-box. An additional bathroom with shower is also located on this floor. The lower level mudroom with gear storage and boot drying racks provides easy ski access to Cascade trails through the 2 car garage.

Early season and late season ski access is condition-dependent and dictated by Big Sky Resort operations.
