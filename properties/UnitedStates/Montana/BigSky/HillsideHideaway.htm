Hillside Hideaway, Big Sky, Montana Vacation Rental

 

A contemporary retreat in the heart of Meadow Village, Hillside Hideaway offers panoramic views and a feeling of seclusion just 5 minutes from Town Center and 15 minutes from the slopes of Big Sky Resort.

Nestled in a quiet neighborhood with unobstructed views from an impressive wrap-around deck, the 4-bedroom home creates a feeling of unity with the outdoors through double-height ceilings, floor-to-ceiling windows and log finishes inside and out.

On the main level, an open floor plan creates seamless flow between the kitchen and dining area, and a sunny great room. Pour a cup of coffee from the Keurig machine in the chef’s kitchen to sip at the breakfast bar, or curl up on the comfy loveseat in the great room by the gas-burning stone fireplace. You’ll also have two leather armchairs with ottomans. After a day on the slopes, gather around the six-seat dining table for a home-cooked meal, with two additional seats at the breakfast bar.

The sprawling main level features a separate family room with two recliners and a large-screen Satellite TV, a spacious laundry room, and mud room leading to the garage. The master bedroom is also on the main level, offering privacy as the only suite on this floor. Across all four suites, you can comfortably sleep up to 11 guests
