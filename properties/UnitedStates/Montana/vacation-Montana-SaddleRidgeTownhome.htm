Saddle Ridge Townhome, Big Sky Resort, Montana Vacation Rental

 

Conveniently located adjacent to Moonlight Lodge, the Saddle Ridge Town Homes are Moonlight Basin's original accommodations and offer spectacular mountain views. In the winter, enjoy easy ski access. In the summer, enjoy the outdoor living space of the multi-level decks. The five level town homes are perfect for couples traveling together or just a family. With bedrooms that are separated by the entry level with a full kitchen and living space, there is room and privacy for everyone!

This home is comfortable and casually appointed and has Ski-in/out access to Big Sky Resort and Moonlight Basin via the Iron Horse chair lift. There is one bedroom upstairs which offers a queen bed, twin bed, queen sleeper sofa, and a private bathroom. Two bedrooms are downstairs, one of which has two twin bunk sets and a private bathroom, and the other offers a king bed and private bathroom. There is a gas fireplace in living room and a private outdoor hot tub is available.
