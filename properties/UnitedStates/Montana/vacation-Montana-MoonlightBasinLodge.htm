Moonlight Basin Lodge, Big Sky, Montana Vacation Rental

 

Moonlight Basin Lodge sits in a prime location, right next to the entrance of Moonlight Basin and mere steps away from a groomed access run to Big Sky Resort via the White Otter and Cascade Lifts.

In the summer, there are many outdoor activities for guests such as fly fishing on the Gallatin, mountain biking, hiking and much more. Beehive Basin is a popular hike in both summer and winter, and leaves from the trail-head just 1.5 miles from this home.

In the winter, this home has ski access via the Cascade and White Otter lifts. The ski access runs alongside the edge of the property and is a 25-yard walk to the slopes.

This 6 bed, 4.5 bath home has a living room complete with large windows, high ceilings, and decorated with true, western flair. This home sleeps up to 16 guests. Enjoy cozying up by two wood burning, stone fireplaces or sit around the fire feature on the back patio and gaze at the pristine mountain panoramas. In addition to the private outdoor hot tub on the lower level patio, the main-level deck has a gas grill for evening cookouts. With granite counter-tops and stainless steel appliances, this kitchen is perfect for all levels of chefs. The dining area seats 8 people with ample room for others with another dining area accommodating an additional four guests.

On the main level, you'll find the first bedroom, which has a king sized bed, a private deck, and a private bath. The master bath features a claw foot tub, double vanity, and shower. The four remaining bedrooms are on the lower level of the home. The second bedroom has a queen bed with an en suite bathroom and shower. The third bedroom has a queen bed with en suite bath and shower over tub combo. The fourth bedroom has queen sized bunk beds. The fifth bedroom has a king bed with an en-suite bath. On the upper level you will find two additional double beds tucked way in their own private space.

On the lower level, there is another family room with a couch and two over-sized chairs. Guests can cozy up to the wood burning fireplace and watch the flat screen TV after a long day out on the slopes.

This home is equipped with a laundry room, mud room, boot warmers, and a two-car garage. Little Plume comes with radiant heat in the winter and air conditioning for the warmer summer months.

Traveling with two or more families? Moonlight Basin Lodge is just down the street from Moondance Lodge.

Early season and late season ski access is condition-dependent and dictated by Big Sky Resort operations.
