Lone View Lodge, Big Sky Resort, Montana Vacation Rental

 

This deluxe home in Mountain Village is ski accessible to Big Sky Resort via the White Otter and Cascade chair lifts, and just a short drive from Yellowstone National Park in the summer. The secluded location of this home affords the most beautiful mountain views from all angles.

There are four bedrooms in total, two with king beds and private bathrooms, a third with a queen bed, and a fourth with a twin bunk bed set and Captain’s bunk bed set. The third and fourth bedrooms share a bathroom. There is a family room downstairs with a home theater and gas fireplace, making it a comfortable retreat for the family in addition to the living room, which is equipped with a home theater and wood burning fireplace. This home also offers a private outdoor hot tub as well as a gas grill, Direct TV, and wireless internet.

For your convenience, this Big Sky vacation rental offers a laundry room and a two car garage.

Natural Retreats guests in Big Sky have access to exclusive Moonlight Basin Premium parking for only $30 per day. This offers a personal greeting by one of the Moonlight Basin valet members at the parking kiosk, personal ski or snowboard delivery from the car to ski racks on the snow to begin the day as well as delivery from the mountain back to the car at the end of the day. In addition, guests will receive a premium parking spot and concierge services that include lunch/dinner reservations, après ski information as well as mountain information with run suggestions for all levels of skiers
