Modern Beachfront Villa, Maui, Hawaii Vacation Rental

 

Modern Beachfront Villa is a brand new private villa situated on 18,700 square feet of gated property in Kihei, Maui. This Hawaiian villa is the ultimate luxury island destination. Enjoy peerless views from the inside of this ocean-front gem. When you want to get closer to the Pacific Ocean a beach path will lead you directly to the sandy shores and turquoise waters. You deserve your own private paradise during an unforgettable Hawaiian vacation.

This ocean-view villa features 5,700 square feet of interior living space split between two spacious floors. A flowing open floor plan with 21 ft wide bi-folding glass doors combine the great room and spacious pool deck, ideal for entertaining family and friends. 14 ft high recessed ceilings showcase high-end finishes and colorful art and custom furnishings.

Up to 12 guests can escape to this palatial Hawaiian vacation retreat. Modern Beachfront Villa features six bedrooms, six full bathrooms, and one half bathroom. An interior clad with leathered quartzite marble countertops, tumbled Belgian limestone tile, custom scraped French oak wood floors and solid white oak cabinets are just a few of the top of the line materials used in this gated estate.

During your stay, you will want to spend plenty of time in the villa’s stunning outdoor living space. Modern Beachfront Villa has manicured lawns, a private courtyard, and a large, private pool area. Take a dip in the heated saltwater pool, or soak in the attached hot tub. When you are not swimming, you can recline on one of the poolside chaise lounges or one of the hanging chairs. Simply soak up the sun, or enjoy food fresh from the outdoor barbecue grill.

Wide pocket doors make it easy to move between the pool area and the indoor living room. The open concept living room has multiple seating areas perfect for enjoying ocean breezes that waft in from the open pocket doors. The living room also has a high ceilings and a large flat screen TV.

The wide open space of the main floor also encompasses the gourmet kitchen and dining area. The fully equipped kitchen has stainless steel appliances, including a gas stove and a double oven. Enjoy a fresh cup of coffee at the kitchen’s bar seating, or grab a bottle of white or red from the wine cooler to go with the sunset view. The indoor dining table features seats for 10 guests.

One of the villa’s bedrooms is located on the main level. This room has a king-size bed and access to an adjacent bathroom with a bathtub. The upper level of the villa has four bedrooms. The largest bedroom features a king-size bed, a walk-in closet, additional seating, and a washer and dryer. Guests of this room will also enjoy direct access to the upper level terrace and an en-suite bathroom with a walk-in shower and bathtub.

The sixth bedroom is located in a detached cabana. This room has a king-size bed, ceiling fan, and en-suite bathroom with a walk-in shower. The separate bedroom is perfect for couples who want a little extra privacy.

Modern Beachfront Villa comes with all of the modern amenities you could hope for in a luxury villa. Smart Home capabilities such as Lutron electronic black out shades, custom adjusted lighting, Heos Home Audio System, and video surveillance all controlled by personal device. Get a little work done in the villa’s office, and stay connected with complimentary Wi-Fi. The villa also has central air-conditioning and two sets of washers and dryers.

One of the best features of the villa is its spectacular location. Walk directly to the beach to enjoy the beauty of the Pacific Ocean. Swim, paddleboard, or fish at this pristine beach. Conveniently located less than five minutes from several shopping plazas with a variety of local restaurants, coffee shops, stores, nightlife, supermarkets, food trucks, weekly Farmer’s Market, Powerhouse Gym, Maui Hot Yoga Studio and Afterglow Yoga Studio. Other nearby attractions include Wailea Golf Course, The Shops at Wailea, Maui Ocean Center, Kalepolepo Beach Park, Iao Valley, and Maalaea Harbor. Whether you want to relax in the solitude of your private villa, or explore all that Maui has to offer.

Vacationers who stay at Modern Beachfront Villa will be treated to a glamorous interior and beautiful outdoor living space. Welcome to paradise.
