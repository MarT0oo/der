Kauai Beachfront Villa, Kauai, Hawaii


Imagine an island, a lush green paradise in the far-off reaches of the deep blue sea. Mango trees sway in a gentle breeze, while the warm and sparkling Pacific waves roll out upon golden sands. Imagine a beach house, expansive and bright, airy and inviting, carefully crafted down to every detail, and filled with every amenity  for your every need – just a few steps from the sand… Welcome to Kaulana Naue.

This 5,000 ft², well-appointed, ocean-front home is everything you need for your  dream Kauai vacation. Designed with artful detail in mind, the home is a perfect blending of the natural Kauai landscape with modern craftsmanship and amenities. Invite in the tradewinds and turquoise Pacific views with our large picture widows and sliding doors. Cook a gourmet Hawaiian feast in the spacious, well-equipped kitchen. Each of the bedrooms are large and spacious, each having it’s own bathroom.

Heaven Defined…
Take a stroll at sunset on the isolated sands of Tunnels Beach and enjoy a cocktail as you soak in the hot tub on our large ocean-front lanai. Sip your morning coffee at sunrise with your toes in the sand, and dream up the day ahead. Snorkeling, surfing, swimming, sand-castle building or just sunbathing with a good book ~ all without having to go more than 15 steps out the door. Kaulana Naue offers a perfect measure of privacy, in it’s well-manicured grounds and pristine stretch of lesser-populated beach front property. Join family and friends at the beach, or lull yourself to sleep in the over-sized hammock perched perfectly between two old-growth Kamani trees, and sheltered by lush tropical foliage.

Everything You Will Need for a Perfect Vacation…
When booking this 5,000 ft² luxury beach home, you will have everything you could possibly need for the perfect Kauai vacation. There is a 5th bedroom (an additional 500 sq. ft of living area) on the lower level. This unique set up is perfect for a large group/family (up to 12 people) using the additional 5th bedroom as a more private area off of the main house. This works well for guests that are either bringing their babysitter or personal chef. The additional 500 ft² which we call the “5th bedroom” is actually a 1 bedroom apartment equipped with a full size sub zero fridge as well as a small kitchen. This luxury vacation home rental has everything you will need to enjoy a most amazing vacation here in Paradise. The home is simply spectacular and can accommodate up to 12 people comfortably.

Stunning Kauai North Shore Location
Located 2 miles from the end of the road on Kauai’s exclusive North Shore, where you will find Tunnels and Ke’e Beach as well as the beginning of the NaPali Coast and the Hanakapi’ai/Kalalau hiking trail. Haena is a quaint, peaceful beach neighborhood fronting Kauai’s most stunning white sand beaches. There is great hiking nearby as well as wet and dry caves to explore, beach parks, snorkeling, surfing and a Botanical Garden offering self-guided tours. Whether you frequent nearby Hanalei (the closest town, about a 10 minute drive) to dine at gourmet restaurants, or never leave the house, you will feel right at home in our spectacular Kauai vacation home by the sea.
