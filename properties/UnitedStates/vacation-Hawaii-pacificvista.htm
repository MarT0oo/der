Pacific Vista, Napili, Maui, Hawaii


Pacific Vista, is an incredible 5 Bedroom, 6.5 bath ocean front home. Just the right spot for a family reunion or a couples holiday getaway. With well over 5000 square feet there is plenty of room for everyone to spread out. Newly remodeled, beautifully appointed and comfortable. Limestone, travertine, exotic cumaru hardwood, fireplace, spacious decks with the island of Lana'i within your ocean vista and a petite sandy beach cove. Your Jacuzzi and pool is positioned just above the beach cove. This is your slice of Maui heaven! Ultimate in private relaxation, Pacific Vista welcomes you to soak up sun and ocean grandeur that pictures can't describe. You will luxuriate so happily, all your cares and stress will melt away!

When you are ready to venture forth and explore the island you'll find more than you expected so close. Beaches, restaurants, shopping, golf, water sports, sunset dinner cruises, ferry to neighboring islands, historic Lahainatown and that's just the beginning! Then when you are tired, ready to relax, head back to Pacific Vista for your afternoon sauna or a dip in the pool.

Each of the 5 bedrooms has its own private bath and most have ocean views. Hotels just don't come close to offering the privacy and quite peacefulness of a private home where the entire family or group can gather away from the hustle and bustle. Grill some delicious fresh fish, float in the pool, swim in the ocean and while away the evening with cocktails in the Jacuzzi...all with the sun setting and moon rising on another perfect Maui day.
