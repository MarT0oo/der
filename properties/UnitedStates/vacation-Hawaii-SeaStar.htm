Sea Star, Maui, Hawaii Vacation Rental

 

Nestled within a backdrop of the West Maui Mountains with spectacular panoramic ocean views, this 6-bedroom 4,225 ft² single-story gated island home sits within its own private, resort-like setting. Enter Sea Star vacation villa and leave the world behind!

Relax amid the palms on your shaded lanai overlooking the neighbor islands or soak in your private swimming pool and spa. Slip into island-time as you enjoy a front row seat to Maui's dramatic sunsets from your own back yard. This sleek and modern home, accented throughout with rich wood trim and professionally decorated in warm island tones is ideal for family gatherings, corporate retreats or a well-deserved vacation. From the main foyer, a spacious great room offers a comfortable gathering place with plenty of seating and leads to a 1,000 ft² covered lanai, blending indoor-outdoor living for the ideal Maui lifestyle.

Inside, a gourmet kitchen, outfitted with high-end appliances including a Sub-Zero built-in refrigerator and 6-burner Wolf gas stove, offers ocean views of its own. Stop by the docks in Lahaina and pick up some fresh fish for a sunset barbecue in your own backyard with your built-in Viking grill. For indoor dining, a formal dining area is situated in the open space between the kitchen and media room, where a comfortable sectional sofa invites you to enjoy a movie after a day in the sun or watch your favorite ball game on the huge, flat screen television.

The five bedrooms in the main home feature flat screen TVs, king beds and designated bath, offering the perfect accommodations for large families or friends traveling together. Four of the main home bedrooms feature sliding doors and personal lanai areas that face the pool. Two master bedrooms on either end of the home feature an additional stone-enclosed alfresco shower. A convenient powder room sits off the main hallway, next to a full-size and fully-stocked laundry room.

Separated from the main home, a secluded pool cabana is furnished as a sixth bedroom with its own half bathroom inside and an alfresco shower. A personal, ocean view lanai makes this a perfect option for a romantic Honeymoon Suite for those tying the knot in the islands. This private retreat is just a few steps from the main home, with air conditioning and a flat screen television.

The waters off of Maui are the winter home to hundreds of humpback whales year after year. Watch them frolic and breach from the comfort of your own lanai - binoculars provided!

Our homes are noted for an upscale beauty, craftsmanship and design that have become the vacation choice for celebrities and business elite who are looking for island homes that match their level of luxury and exclusivity.

Although you’ll feel miles away, you’re just a short 5 minute drive to the best restaurants, shops and galleries of famous Front Street in Lahaina. Behind the giant Banyan Tree is the Lahaina dock where you can catch a ferry to Lana’i for the day, or take a snorkel trip or whale watching tour.

If you’re looking to explore some of Maui’s 81 beaches, start with Ka’anapali Beach just up the road, where cliff divers light torches along the cliff and take their nightly swan dive into the sea. A mile long beach walk meanders behind several resorts along this beach. This is also one of the best places on the island to dine on the water.

Although you’re close to everything that the island offers, don’t be surprised if you decide to remain in your private retreat under the swaying palms and in the quiet bliss of Sea Star.
