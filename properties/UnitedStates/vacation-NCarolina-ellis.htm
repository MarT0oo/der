Asheville, North Carolina has long been a place that families went to spend some time together enjoying the Trails, the Majestic Asheville Waterfalls, and the beautiful views that can only come from a panoramic view from what seems like the top of the world.  Situated on Elk Trail, this 4 bedroom, 4 bathrooms, 5,013 sq. foot home, offers you every bit of luxury that you would expect from a 5 star Resort Accommodation.

 

The Master Suite leaves nothing to the imagination from the wonderful King Sized Sleigh bed to the inviting fireplace, and just through the double doors you’ll find the most stunning bathroom, featuring marble floors, hardwoods, Jacuzzi tub, and granite countertops.  This is truly every woman’s dream bathroom!!

 

The guest suites are no less lavished, each one with a Queen sized bedroom and private bath.  The bedrooms are all professionally decorated with the highest regard to the Luxury Traveler.  You will be happy to find your towels and bedding are resort quality, which makes the accommodation that much more desirable.

 

The Chateau is setup for those families traveling who are used to renting a luxury suite at a local resort and our now thinking of hiring a villa.  The house is equipped with Game room, including a billiards Table, flat screen television, a bar and plenty of space.  The lower floor offers an office/library for those traveling on business, and the media room is set up with Large Flat Screen Television and Cable which comes standard with this luxury home.

 

For those aspiring chefs in us all, the gourmet kitchen it setup to suit even the most discriminate of professionals.  You have everything you need at your disposal, from the stainless steel appliances, to the granite countertops, and a great chef’s island to prep the foods.  You can also enjoy a wonderful eat in area, where your friends and family can help or watch as you delight in cooking your favorite foods.

 

Things to do:


Skiing is a short 45 minutes from the villa. Hiking, the Mountain to Sea Trail or the Blue Ridge Parkway trails within 3 minutes.  Golfing, try the Asheville Country Club and the Grove Park Golf Course in Asheville within 15 minutes. Within an hour you will find Tom Fazio, Jack Nicklaus, Arnold Palmer and Donald Ross designed golf courses. Tiger Woods is building his first golf course and his home here.  Hot Air Balloon Rides - 30 minutes.  The Biltmore Estate - tour America's largest home with gardens designed by Frederick Law Olmsted, who also designed N.Y.'s Central Park. Winery, Restaurants, Canoes, kayaks, horse back riding. Bike Rentals - 15 minutes.  Hot Springs - 45 minutes. 

Nantahala Outdoor Center - home to 15 whitewater Olympians, world-wide reputation for teaching and guide services in white water sports, mountain biking, mountain climbing. Ashville was Rated #1 vacation destination by Good Morning America and National Geographic.  Grandfather Mountain - Believed to be the world's oldest mountain, a preserve with abundant wildlife, trails, the Mile High Swinging Bridge.  Lake Lure - 45 minutes. This lake is nestled in a valley surrounded by steep granite mountains. This Lake offers swimming, a beach, boating, fishing, golf, beautiful scenery. You may recognize some of the scenery as the back-drop in the film Last of the Mohicans and the vacation resort in Dirty Dancing. 

Dining in Asheville is fresh, unique and wonderful - 15 minutes to Steak and seafood, Tex-Mex, Vegetarian, Japanese, Italian, Mediterranean, Cajun, Thai, Chinese, Indian and more. Asheville prides itself on using local growers and not allowing franchise restaurants downtown. 

Art Galleries - This region of the Blue Ridge Mountains is known for its long history in Arts and Crafts. Some of the nearby craft schools have national recognition since the 1920's. Several Art studios in town have Craft Guild exhibits in the mediums of glass, ceramics, clay, metal wood and fabrics. 

Cherokee Museum - 1 hour drive to a new and Cherokee Nation built museum.  Smokey Mountains National Park - can be reached in an hour.  Elk Pastures - 30 minutes to see Elk grazing in the meadows below one entrance to the Smokey Mountains National Park.  Cherokee Gambling Casinos - 1 hour
