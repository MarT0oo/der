Hanalei Dolphin Estate, Kauai, Hanalei, Hawaii Vacation Rental

 

On the sand in Hanalei Bay, this elegant, recently remodeled 5 bedroom, 4 bath home, plus 1 bedroom, 1 bath guest house, is perfect for a romantic getaway or for a group to 10 people. Beautifully furnished throughout, including 4 king beds and one queen in the main house and 2 queen beds in the guest house. Other features are a pool table, large plasma HDTV's, gorgeous chef's kitchen and comfortable patio furniture for enjoying the sunsets over the NaPali coast and ocean.

Walk directly out your large, private back yard, onto the sand and into the sparkling Pacific Ocean. The home is located near "The Cape" which is a relatively quiet and private section of the world famous Hanalei Bay. Enjoy surfing, swimming, kayaking, jogging, beach-going, or any outdoor yard activity just steps from your vacation home.
