Cedar Shores, North Lake Tahoe, California Vacation Rental

 

The rustic exterior of this 7,652 square foot, lakefront vacation rental home was achieved by mixing cedar logs, poplar bark shingles, grizzly granite veneer, and copper with a combination asphalt shingle and living roof.

You will find breathtaking views of Lake Tahoe from the great room, kitchen and two bedrooms. Oversized French doors bring the outside in. You'll find endless ways to relax and enjoy your Tahoe getaway. A home theater in the lower level seats 16. An adjacent billiards room is complete with a wet bar, dual large screen televisions, and shuffleboard for entertaining the adults while the kids enjoy the built-in climbing wall and playroom.

The exterior rear patio features a pizza oven and barbecue. The gas-lit fire pit nestled by a boulder outcrop on the lawn faces Lake Tahoe. This luxury lakefront vacation rental truly has it all!

Please Be Advised: Many highways, access roads and/or driveways are steep in Tahoe/Truckee and may be icy in the winter. To ensure your safety, it is critical that during November 1st - April 30th brakes, windshield wipers, defroster, heater and exhaust system are in top condition. Tires need to be properly inflated and the tread must be in excellent condition. Always carry tire chains, even if driving an AWD/4WD vehicle. Make sure the chains are the proper size for your tires and are in working order.
