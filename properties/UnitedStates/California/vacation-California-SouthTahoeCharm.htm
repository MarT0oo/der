South Tahoe Charm, South Lake Tahoe, California Vacation Rental

 

Take a look at the most unbelievable vacation rental in all of South Lake Tahoe! This one-of-a-kind home is absolutely amazing with 5700 square feet of living space sitting on over 1/2 acre.

You will find yourself celebrating the perfect and most relaxing vacation of your life! The home is spread out the length of the lot with the living room and kitchen on the ground floor right in the middle of the property. Above the living room you will find the loft/family room where you will find a 60" flat screen TV and wet bar. To the west you will find the main master suite with private wrap around deck, views of the lake, gas fireplace, and enormous bathroom.

The master bath has dual sinks, dual walk in shower, and 2 person Jacuzzi bathtub, all with views of the lake and surrounding creek. There are 3 bedrooms upstairs, 2 with Jack and Jill bathrooms, the final room is for the kids with 2 bunk beds and a Foosball table.

Take a quick walk outside on the covered elevated walkway to find a private apartment where you will have a king size suite with kitchenette and private Jacuzzi bathroom.

All bedrooms have decks with tree and lake views, there are 6 total full bathrooms throughout, Tallac creek surrounds the property, and there are 3 gas fireplaces! This home is unbelievable and should please the most discriminate travelers!

***Please note: Unfortunately the home owners association is strict regarding vacation rentals, there is no access to the private community beach, but you can drive just 5 minutes to glorious choices of Lake Tahoe Beaches.
