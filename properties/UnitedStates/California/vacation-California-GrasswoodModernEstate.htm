Grasswood Modern Estate, Malibu, California Vacation Rental

 

Welcome to the Sacred Path, A unique Architectural Masterpiece with a twist. Nothing here is overlooked, no corners cut. This property is a sanctuary, inspired by tranquility and ease of the Malibu life. You can see the preferred aesthetic of Artisan design with open, spacious rooms, custom tile and stonework and furnishings to inspire. This home is full of highlights to fascinate the high-taste eye, the detailing throughout will surely keep you on your toes.

After admiring the edgy zen exterior, step inside and find every inch is carefully designed and crafted. Thoughtful architectural characteristics take center stage here; in this simple design huge ocean views and amazing masonry, and woodwork flow and blend together.

Adjacent to the airy and orderly great room is your chef designed kitchen with all the extras you would expect in a luxury rental.There’s a striking balance between elegance and luxury, yet includes a functional space for cooking and entertaining all while enjoying the breathtaking views.

The pool area is relaxed, elegant and spacious, with nearby herb gardens and an expanse of fruit trees. In addition to the amazingly large pool, this home comes with a key to a private , very much in demand beach. So you have the best of both worlds here…

4 Bedrooms & 4 Baths make up the main house plus a comfortable designer guest house with bath.

Home gym, equestrian area and tranquility garden round out the estate. Situated on 1.2 flat acres, there is enough room for solitude anywhere on site, many areas with ocean views. This home is luxurious and simple all at the same time.
