Sand Acre Estate, Palm Springs, California Vacation Rental

 

5 bedroom Spanish Estate in Movie Colony neighborhood, visited by Marilyn Monroe

Located in the heart of The Movie Colony neighborhood, Sand Acre is a circa 1933 Spanish Estate situated on a coveted one-acre in-town lot. Rumored to have been frequented by Marilyn Monroe, it is noted to be where she and Joe DiMaggio often spent time together while in Palm Springs.

Sand Acre features five bedrooms, each with en-suite baths. The master suite offers a king sized bed; flat screen TV. The first and second bedrooms are interconnected- The first bedroom features a king sized bed and flat screen TV while the second offers a queen sized bed and flat screen TV. The fourth bedroom features a full sized bed. The fifth bedroom is located in the “tower”, rumored to be where Marilyn Monroe stayed. It features a queen sized bed and flat screen TV. The large chef’s kitchen is recently updated with a full-suite of stainless steel appliances including a Viking professional gas range.

Sand Acre is surrounded by manicured grounds with a private tennis court. The in-ground pool and spa feature an oversized patio area and poolside cabana.
