The private estate gazes over expansive views of dreamy hills, filling your days and evenings with Southern California light. Large glass walls—controlled with a simple button—open from the pool deck and lawn to the interior great room, creating a single alfresco space. Inside, gather in the marvelous lounge beside the fireplace and large TV, mixing cocktails at the superb indoor bar and plucking bottles from the wine cellar. Then repair to the media lounge with its supple viewing chairs, wet bar, and classy games table, or savor the night air on the ample deck.
