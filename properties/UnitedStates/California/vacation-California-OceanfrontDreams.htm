Oceanfront Dreams, La Jolla, San Diego, California Vacation Rental

 

Sophisticated and modern, with stunning beach views, Oceanfront Paradise is the perfect example of a California beach house—and not just because it’s been featured on one of Hollywood’s longest-running reality TV shows.

Whether you’re getting away from the paparazzi or just getting away from it all, you’ll appreciate the gorgeous contemporary interiors, sun-kissed outdoor spaces, sweeping ocean views, and its premiere location within Bird Rock Village of La Jolla & minutes to downtown San Diego, Little Italy, Sea World and Old Town.

Thanks to southern California’s near-constant sunshine, you can enjoy the villa’s outdoor heated pool, multiple lounge areas, and heated hot tub - all year around.

A street-legal, 4 passenger golf cart is included during your stay for a fun ride to beautiful Tourmaline Beach when, the day calls for beach play and relaxation. The beach can also be accessed, during low tide, by walking a few minutes north, to the beach access stairway near the corner of Chelsea Street and Sea Ridge Drive.

Whether working up a sweat in the sauna, rooftop tennis court, or fully furnished gym with a bath and sauna, preparing dinner on the barbecue and watching the sunset from the al-fresco dining area, or enjoying popcorn and a movie in the home theater room - this spectacular home has it all - just like a private resort!.

The thoughtfully appointed interior of this ocean inspired 7 bedroom, 7.5 bath, 9,100 square foot, 3-story home is show-stopping in its own right without distracting from the stunning ocean views through floor to ceiling windows – from the 2 living rooms, the dining room, a dining nook, and the 2 fully furnished kitchens – one on the upper level and the 2nd on the middle floor near the pool area for beer, wine, and snacks. There are 2 master king bedrooms, each with ocean views, a large flat screen TV, and en-suite baths and one with a fireplace. There is a 3rd king bedroom with a seating nook and en-suite bath. There are also two queen bedrooms – one with an en-suite bath and a second on the first level with a separate bath with a walk-in shower.

The 6th bedroom has a separate entrance, and will be perfect for grown kids/teenagers, or the nanny or Grandma and Grandpa to get away from the noise! It will sleep 4 with a queen bed and pull out sofa. The 7th bedroom is a full size gym, and has a Murphy bed and a full bathroom – perfect for the teenagers in the group. This home comfortably sleeps 16.

There is a full, in-home laundry room with washer and dryer for your use and garage parking for 2 cars, driveway parking, and ample street parking in front.
