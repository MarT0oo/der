Cozy Chalet, North Lake Tahoe, California Vacation Rental

 

What a spectacular home perfect any time of year with a lake view and is pet friendly! This newly constructed, absolutely gorgeous home, sits on an acre and a half, backs right to up to National Forest Land for Privacy and Serenity, and has tons of wonderful deck space with views of the surrounding mountains, trees and the Lake!

Walk right out the back door for back country skiing, cross country skiing, and sledding in the winter plus spectacular hiking and biking trails in the summer! You can bring your dog with a $300 refundable deposit, the home has a Wii with games for some evening family fun on the 60" flat screen in the living room, and since the owners are new parents you'll find a Pack N' Play and high chair available for use as well!

Please make sure you have a 4 wheel drive to access the home in the winter, the unique setting of this home will make the long driveway somewhat difficult to maneuver. For a great value, this is just wonderful, winter or summer, you will have the vacation of your life!
