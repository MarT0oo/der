Beachy Breeze, Venice, California Vacation Rental

 

Very nice 4 bedroom 3 bath Bungalow that is located 1 block to the beach and a block to all the great restaurants and shops that are along Abbot Kinney Blvd. on a quiet walk street..

The Bungalow has 2 bedrooms, 2 baths, a living room with fireplace, a kitchen, and laundry on the 1st floor, then on the 2nd floor there are 2 more bedrooms (one of which is very very small), 1 bath, another full kitchen, another living room with fireplace, and another laundry room.. This main bungalow can be used as 2 separate units or as single family home and would provide a spacious and relaxing environment for a larger group. There is also a common inside stairway that connect the 2 floors..

If you need more room there is a separate 2 bedroom 1 bath guest house at the rear of the property available..

The bungalow was all recently renovated with two nice full kitchens that has higher-end appliances..Refrigerators with ice makers, dishwashers, and gas ranged/stoves.. New tiled bathrooms, new wood, tiled, and carpeted floors, new comfortable decor and furnishings…There are 2 small balconies with a BBQ, chairs and tables..Also parking for 3 small cars or 2 medium cars..

We have DSL, local phone service, quality linens and towels, fully stocked kitchens, 4 plasma TV’s with Direct TV Satellite, beach chairs, and body boards..

As for bedding; the 2 lower bedrooms both have queen beds and there is a queen sofa bed in the lower living room, and up on the 2nd floor there is a queen bed in one bedroom, a twin bed in the very very small 4th bedroom, and a sofa bed in that upper living room that makes into a single bed. We also have a twin roll away and air bed if needed..

We can arrange for bicycle rentals for $50 each per week.
