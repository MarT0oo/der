Estate With A View, Napa County, California Vacation Rental

 

This luxurious 4 bedroom St. Helena compound is set on 8 acres off of Silverado Trail on Sage Canyon Road, just 5 miles to the restaurants and boutiques of downtown St. Helena. A full time gardener tends to the magnificent grounds which include 90 foot tall redwood trees, a small vineyard, olive and citrus orchards, a swimming pool surrounded by lawn and a tennis court.

The meticulously renovated main building features Provencal style decor with antiques and French fabrics. There is a very spacious king bedroom with en suite bathroom, large great room with wood burning fireplace, large gourmet kitchen with breakfast area and adjacent seating with a second fireplace and a formal dining room. Across the breezeway is a second large master bedroom with en suite bathroom. Beyond the pool is a guest house with two additional en suite king bedrooms, one with a fireplace.

A green lawn surrounds the large swimming pool, and the terrace outside of the main building has seating and a large outdoor dining room table. To one side of the pool is a very spacious covered gazebo with a wood burning fireplace and outdoor couches and additional seating and an overhead fan. There is a caretakers unit on to the rear of the guest compound housing a winery manager and winemaker/chef who are available for special wine tastings and chef dinners. However, guest enter the property through a separate gated entrance and have plenty of privacy.
