Lemon Estate, Rancho Mirage, California Vacation Rental

 

Lemon Estate is a wonderful 6/7 bedroom home located in Rancho Mirage, within a private enclave. This amazing home is a perfect blend of perfection and luxury amenities for those who like the best in life when they travel. There is nothing like having your own private home where no one will bother you and you can enjoy the fantastic backdrop of the landscape.

This luxurious 5,000+ square-foot home consists of 6 bedrooms on a 3-building private compound. The private, 6-bedroom, 7-bathroom estate sits on a corner lot and includes a main house, casita, nanny’s quarters, secured main entrance, and the largest residential lap pool in the desert. Fully air conditioned, the home has wireless internet throughout, a state-of-the-art theatre, sauna, private office, and an open-concept kitchen, dining, and living space overlooking the pool area.

The main building has 4 bedrooms and the stand-alone Casita offers 1 bedroom,2 full baths, fully equipped kitchen, and a living area with a daybed. The Nanny’s Quarters has 1 bedroom, 1 full bath, and is connected to the garage with it’s own entrance and mini-fridge.

Enjoy the view of the pool from the outdoor seating area, which features a BBQ area, outdoor kitchen, fire pit, and air misters. The swimming area consists of a children’s pool, hot tub, and 26-metre lap pool for those serious swimmers. The home theatre is outfitted with the Control 4 home system and boasts a 120-inch projection screen. The room seats 6 comfortably in reclining leather sofas. And you can play music right from your Apple device throughout the home.

The property has secured parking for 5 vehicles. The Lemon Estate is wonderful for private groups and we can arrange a private chef and maid.

About Palm Springs

Palm Springs has long been the place people go to get away from it all, a short drive from Los Angeles and plenty of flights daily. The area is booming with high class golfing which everyone takes advantage of while in town, from the smaller less advanced courses to the professional courses where we can arrange a private lesson with a PRO if requested. Palm Springs is also home to some great tours, if you want to see where the stars lived and spent their leisure time. Find the amazing tours impressively fun and lots of great character in the architecture surrounding the 50's and 60's iconic movie stars. It's no wonder people flock here.

And of course who can forget Coachella which draws hundreds of thousands each year. See your favorite indie bands perform or just walk around and enjoy the laid back scenery and take in some foodie events with some great local food trucks. You won't be disappointed that's why year after year Palm Springs is a #1 Destination.
