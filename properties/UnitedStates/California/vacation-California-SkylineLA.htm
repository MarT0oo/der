Skyline LA, Hollywood Hills, Los Angeles, California Vacation Rental

 

See the Skyline in LA from this beautiful Masterpiece. Enjoy every convenience this house has to offer, from the amazing luxury pool to the upscale furnishings and proximity to everything. We can also arrange a wonderful chef during your stay or beautiful dinner party. Just a wonderful option for your next holiday.

With 5 Bedrooms and 4.5 Bathrooms you have plenty of space for 10-12 guests. The rooms are spacious and the decor modern A space you will truly appreciate as a backdrop to your holiday. Enjoy a day shopping within 10 minutes of the house, or enjoy the sun and sunbathe in your walled and gated private yard. Around you there are trees and tropical splendor. The glass doors open onto the 2000 sq ft back patio, pool and spa area. The pool features fountain jets, and a jacuzzi spa, while the patio area which is great for entertaining, has ample seating, BBQ, lounge chairs, and umbrellas. There is also a large TV for viewing outdoors with comfortable seating and a fire pit. This is a perfect LA vacation spot for the modern design lover!

There are 5 bedrooms; 3 kings and 1 full upstairs and 1 king next to the pool on the ground floor. Floor to ceiling windows bring brilliant light into the rooms while remote controlled blinds control the light easily. The master has a double shower walk in, with steam side, as well as a bath and makeup area, with two his and her walk in closets.

The open plan ground floor has superb entrance with circular stairway upstairs, leading to the bedrooms. The modern sitting room and two dining areas surround the state of the art kitchen, with all chef equipment, allowing for easy entertaining areas. A chef and small events can be requested.
