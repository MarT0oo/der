COH1211 Lake Tahoe, California

 

Recently constructed in 2007, this beautiful home designed with the Tahoe vacationer in mind is still shiny and new. Perfect for groups of up to 12, this four-bedroom, three-bath home has stunning hardwood flooring throughout.

There are three wall-mounted LCD televisions that can tilt and rotate, so everyone will get to enjoy their favorite shows. Check out the 37-inch screen in the game room, the 42-inch LCD screen in the master bedroom, and the huge 47-inch TV in the living room. If you prefer to surf, take your laptop along to enjoy high speed wireless Internet. Make some music in the living room with a totally new electric piano and the stereo surround system wired to iPod docks. Other features include granite kitchen counter-tops, an outdoor gas grill, and a bubbling Jacuzzi tub in the master.

The games room is perfect for parties, with its impressive pool table, two mini refrigerators, and wet bar. Heavenly, Sierra-at-Tahoe, sandy beaches, and exciting casinos are only 15 minutes away, making this beautiful new home a perfect choice any time of year.
