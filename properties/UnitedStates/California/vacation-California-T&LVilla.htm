T&L Villa, Palm Springs, California Vacation Rental

 

Modern Desert home on Public Golf Course with Swimming Pool, Fire Pit and sweeping Mountain Views.Located on the first hole of the Escena Golf Course, a 5-minute drive from downtown Palm Springs and less than an hour from Joshua Tree National Park, T&L Villa is a stylish retreat with sweeping views of the San Jacinto mountains and designer furnishings in every room.

Set on sprawling, zero-scaped grounds. The 3-bedroom home opens onto an expansive outdoor area with multiple sitting and dining areas, a private lap pool, and hot tub featuring a fire pit.

Catch morning rays by the pool on one of chaise lounge chairs, or gather with friends on the chic sectional sofa, accented by fur throw pillows. On cool summer nights, fire up the BBQ grill and enjoy dinner al fresco with room for up to 8 guests at an outdoor dining table with additional seating at a separate high-top table.

Floor-to-ceiling windows marry the outside with a spacious, gallery-inspired living area with original sculpture and artwork -- each a bright pop of color against a clean, minimalist background. Relax on the large sectional sofa, watch an old Hollywood film on the flat screen tv, or turn up the gas fireplace for ambiance.

A seamless floor plan opens the living room to a gourmet kitchen boasting KitchenAid appliances and a long marble island with 6 stools. Steps away, a contemporary dining table seats up to 10 guests beneath a chic, designer chandelier, bookended by royal blue mid-century egg chairs.

The thoughtful design of the living areas carries to the bedrooms, each styled in a unique aesthetic.

The master suite, warmly decorated with wood-paneled walls and plush carpet, has a California King-sized bed with luxurious, deep purple bedding facing a large TV and a sapphire chaise lounge. The minimalist en-suite bathroom, accented by modern art, features a deep soaker tub and walk-in shower. This room opens onto the back deck through sliding glass doors.

The second bedroom, styled in clean, white decor with nature-inspired accents, features a queen-sized bed, sitting area and floor-to-ceiling window. Guests of this room have a stylish bathroom in the hall with a walk-in shower.

The third bedroom channels the whimsical culture of the 1950s, featuring two twin beds with pink walls and bedspreads, fur accent furniture, and pop-art portraits of Audrey Hepburn and Marilyn Monroe. This room has a luxurious bath with walk-in shower and vanity.

A private washer and dryer add convenience, along with parking for three cars to explore the surrounding area, from the Palm Springs Aerial Tramway, a 10-minute drive, or to Joshua Tree National Park, accessible in under an hour.

Whether your ideal day involves lounging by the pool, playing a round of golf on the public course, or exploring galleries and shops or simply reveling in the chic details of a designer home, T&L Villa offers a chic retreat that uniquely embodies Palm Springs.
