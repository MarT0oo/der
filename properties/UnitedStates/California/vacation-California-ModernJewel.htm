Modern Jewel, Hollywood Hills, Los Angeles, California Vacation Rental

 

Now available for short and long-term rent, the "Modern Jewel" is luxury living at its finest. Built in 2017, this modern mini-estate highlights the quintessential LA indoor-outdoor lifestyle. Nestled in heart of Hollywood among other homes rich with history, its cosmopolitan sensibility is unique to the area and even LA itself.

Inspired by Italian Modernism and Japanese architecture, this 4 bedroom + 3 bathroom has been shown in Mansion Global, Angelino, the LA Times, and Variety. Developed with a focus on all five senses, this ultimate retreat features 15+ dedicated-use spaces, breathtaking views, gallery-style windows, giant patios, high ceilings, limited-edition fixtures and installations, top-of-the-line appliances, and so much more.

Those who like a healthy routine will find its proximity to the Runyon Canyon hiking trails (a celebrity favorite), tranquil salt-water pool, and gorgeous bathrooms rejuvenating. Modernists will appreciate its convenient location; the Sunset Strip, Beverly Hills boutiques, shops, and restaurants, the Hollywood bowl, and nightlife are just minutes away.
