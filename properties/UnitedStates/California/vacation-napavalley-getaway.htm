Extraordinary Getaway, Napa Valley, California Vacation Rental


Located on 10 acres in the Heart of the Napa Valley, nestled in the World renowned Stag’s Leap District off the famous Silverado Trail, the Eastern side of the Wine Country.  This fabulous 3,800 ft² home was custom designed on the top of the knoll overlooking the incredible mountain tops and the sweeping floors of the Napa Valley.  The location of the home is absolutely perfect, feeling quite secluded and private in the country, but minutes to the World Class Dining and Premium Wineries.  The setting is to embrace the extraordinary location with absolutely intoxicating views from every room in the house.

The Great Room and dining space provide guests to enjoy the chef while he is cooking a gourmet meal, or just a gathering of friends to enjoy the magnificent sunsets through the glass walls of the kitchen and living areas.  Custom cut 12 foot granite oversized center island provides and elegant work surface and stainless steel appliances, double ovens  with a confection oven are any chef’s dream.

The living area is drawn together by the granite fireplace and Chenille luxurious section couch that allows for spirited conversations or provide and agreeable setting you want anywhere is the house.  Double sliding glass doors open to the terrace where you can dine outside and or lounge on the couch with a glass of Napa’s finest.  Multiple doors to the home’s exterior provide unfettered access to the outdoors: one of the treasures of wine country living.

The library/office space s down the glass hallways with both east and western exposure with a large desk and sitting area to read and or work.  Wireless internet is provided throughout the house for up to five computers. 

The Quest Bedroom is all views with a stunning soda light blue granite double sink, with en-suite walk in marble shower.  The sumptuous Master Bedroom, vaulted ceilings with glass doors that open to a private balcony.  The walk in closet has built in shelving, storage and an upper level for your seasonable wardrobe.  The master bath is a contemporary oasis with all marble finishes a marble walking shower with more views and a 3 people tub to relax in after a day of wine tasting.

The very large Bonus Room or fourth bedroom is a private retreat with a living room and granite word burning fireplace.  The generous bedroom has two queen plush beds with a television and surround sound stereo for a completely away from it all. The bathroom en-suite for your guests convenience. 

The bucolic setting for the swimming pool is in its own private valley below the main house sheltered with beautiful trees, market umbrellas, and appointed with very comfortable patio furniture.  The 2000 sq. foot deck has a great BBQ, 2 burners and a sink for entertaining while lounging around and getting some sun after a day of imbibing with friends.
