About Palm Springs

Palm Springs has long been the place people go to get away from it all, a short drive from Los Angeles and plenty of flights daily. The area is booming with high class golfing which everyone takes advantage of while in town, from the smaller less advanced courses to the professional courses where we can arrange a private lesson with a PRO if requested. Palm Springs is also home to some great tours, if you want to see where the stars lived and spent their leisure time. Find the amazing tours impressively fun and lots of great character in the architecture surrounding the 50's and 60's iconic movie stars. It's no wonder people flock here.

And of course who can forget Coachella which draws hundreds of thousands each year. See your favorite indie bands perform or just walk around and enjoy the laid back scenery and take in some foodie events with some great local food trucks. You won't be disappointed that's why year after year Palm Springs is a #1 Destination.
