Modern Escape, Palm Springs, California Vacation Rental

 

This 1959 redefined mid-century ranch residence is located in the Vista Las Palmas neighborhood with four contemporary bedrooms and many modern appointments.

This vacation home is full of contemporary design and updated amenities, while still showcasing original elements like the floor to ceiling rock-wall fireplace. The kitchen is fully stocked with updated appliances, perfect for casual or formal dining. The property offers a large and private backyard including a modern cabana with comfortable seating, refreshing wing-shaped pool with ocean blue tiles, separate elevated spa, and outdoor grill with sit at bar. The outdoor living space is an updated interpretation of mid-century life including a shuffleboard court surrounded by lush landscape, lawn area with surrounding citrus trees, and the legendary mountain views of the desert.

The bedrooms offer plenty of comfort and relaxation, appointed in contemporary style. The spacious master bedroom includes an en suite bathroom with spa qualities that opens to the backyard and adjacent spa. An adjacent guest suite also with en suite bathroom has backyard access with private patio and water feature. All bathrooms feature contemporary style with modern fixtures.

About the Area

Palm Springs, a city in the Sonoran Desert of southern California, is known for its hot springs, stylish hotels, golf courses and spas. It's also noted for its many fine examples of midcentury-modern architecture. Its core shopping district along Palm Canyon Drive features vintage boutiques, interior design shops and restaurants. The surrounding Coachella Valley offers hiking, biking and horseback riding trails.
