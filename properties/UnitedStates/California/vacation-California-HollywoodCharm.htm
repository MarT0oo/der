Hollywood Charm, Malibu, California Vacation Rental

 

This architectural gem recently underwent an inspired restoration, transporting the home from its midcentury roots to a modern, light-filled oasis. The towering post and beam gallery, the contemporary furniture, and the amazing art collection all contribute to the chic, modern feel of this gorgeous home.

The main entrance to the home includes a soaring vaulted gallery with a central strip of skylights leading into the main living room. With dark, softly polished concrete and bamboo floors, windows instead of walls, and whitewashed interiors, this home is the epitome of minimalism. The dominant use of white and economical décor, showcases an art collection that blends beautifully with the gallery-like structure of the home.

Sleek and modern, the open floor plan is nevertheless warm and inviting. An interior courtyard divides the main house and the guest studio. A master bedroom includes an en-suite bathroom with its own dressing area, as well as an adjacent office and library. The large, private guest quarters sleep two.

The newly updated state-of-the-art chef’s kitchen is fully equipped with the finest appliances and accommodates dining for six. The original fireplace has been updated to combine brick and a polished platform, creating a warm focal point for the room. The home also features audio systems throughout the house and gardens.

Spend your Los Angeles vacation in style at this stunning modern retreat.
