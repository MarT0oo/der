Pacific Beach Luxury, Pacific Beach, San Diego, California Vacation Rental

 

This newly constructed luxury home located in the Crown Point area of Pacific Beach, San Diego provides you with one of the most relaxing locations to spend your vacation. The tropical landscape and salt water pool add to that island like experience!

Your tropical adventure in San Diego begins when you open the custom mahogany front door of the Pacific Beach Luxury rental. Pacific Beach Luxury is one of our premiere properties, and is the perfect way to spoil yourself, your family, and your friends when you visit San Diego. This completely remodeled home offers 2,400 square feet of luxurious living space that includes four bedrooms and four bathrooms, as well as 10,000 square feet of outdoor space.

A Perfect Island Escape
Pacific Beach Luxury is decorated in a tropical, contemporary design. You’ll notice how spacious and welcoming this large house is the moment you step in the door. Original artwork decorates the walls, and the granite countertops throughout the house add a feeling of elegance. Large windows and skylights let in plenty of light, so you can really get a taste of the tropics.

Bedrooms and Bathrooms
Pacific Beach Luxury offers two king beds, one queen, and a pull-out sofa for your group. This rental is the perfect option if you want to bring the spouse and kids to San Diego along with other members of your family. It can also be a great choice for a group of friends. Two of the bedrooms include private baths, with beautiful tiled walk-in showers and lots of sink space. Some of the bedrooms also include large, flat-screen televisions with Direct TV.

Kitchen
San Diego offers lots of amazing restaurants, but you might not even want to go out to eat with all that this spacious kitchen offers. The kitchen is completely open, allowing multiple people to cook together at the granite countertops or central island. You can eat at the breakfast bar in the morning, or settle down for a more formal meal at the dining room table below a capiz chandelier.

Living Room
Relax after all your San Diego adventures in the large, open living room, which features a comfy couch, large fireplace, and picture window that looks right out at the salt water pool. A 65-inch flat screen television with surround sound is a great option for catching the game or watching a movie together at night.

Outdoors
Of course, this is San Diego, so you can’t spend all your time inside. Step outside and enjoy a dip in the salt water pool. The hours will speed by as you play in the water or catch up on some reading poolside. When the night grows chilly, it’s time to move to the warm Jacuzzi, or get ready for a meal under the stars. Enjoy sipping wine or some of San Diego’s famous craft beer under the large, custom Palapa Bar with built in barbecue, sink, and outdoor refrigerator.

For an even better view of the property and more relaxation options, head up to the roof-top deck. Lean back in the comfy chairs, and watch the fireworks from Sea World at night.

Nearby Attractions
Pacific Beach Luxury is located in the bustling city of Pacific Beach, a hip area filled with bars and restaurants. This coastal city offers quick and easy access to the beach and to Downtown San Diego. Within a short drive, you can take the children to Sea World, or enjoy the classy wine bars and fine dining in San Diego. A quick car trip can also take you to some of the best golf courses in the country, as well as luxury spas and tennis clubs. If you’d rather keep the car in the garage, take a walk right from the house to Mission Bay or Crown Point Shores Park. All that San Diego has to offer is practically outside the door!

Extra Guest House
A small guest house is located on this property and can be added to this rental for an additional fee upon specific request. The guest house is a great option for older children, parents, or a child’s nanny. It offers privacy while still keeping the residents close to the main house.
