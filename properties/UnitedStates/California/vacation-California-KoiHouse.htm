Koi House, Palm Springs, California Vacation Rental

 

Situated in the Historic Tennis Club neighborhood of Palm Springs overlooking the San Jacinto Mountains, this modern home is filled with original art, custom furniture, and top quality appliances and finishes throughout. Only steps away from downtown, you can stroll to restaurants, galleries and shops yet still enjoy the exclusivity of being located on a private cul-de-sac.

Beaming with color in every room, the open floor plan, high ceilings and clerestories allow natural light to flow throughout the house. The dining room and living room, which contains a stunning baby grand piano and gas fireplace, boast beautiful garden views.

Located in their own separate wings of the main home, each bedroom is equipped with king beds, sitting areas, ensuite bathrooms, flat screen tvs and very generous dressing rooms and closets. The first bedroom has access to the pool and koi pond and the second bedroom has its own private patio. The separate casita offers a king bed with en-suite bathroom, flat screen tv, outdoor shower, and direct access to the pool.

The extremely private gardens are extensive with multiple hidden spots to relax and gaze at the mountains. Enjoy the covered lanai which encloses the outdoor living and dining room. Rejuvenate by the pool or in the spa and take in the luxuriant views of palm trees. Take a stroll through the gardens and meditate by the large koi pond. Or snuggle up by the fire pit under the secret pergola.

For your added pleasure, this home also includes an Apple tv, outdoor grill, home office, security system, touch screen blinds, and whole house control stereo system.

The unique architecture and customized interior design has earned Koi House some substantial press coverage including publication in Palm Springs Life this coming January and the house will be included in the prestigious Modernism Week house tour this coming February.
