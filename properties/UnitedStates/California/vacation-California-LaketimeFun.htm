Laketime Fun, Lake Tahoe, California Vacation Rental

 

Enjoy the serenity of Lake Tahoe in this large luxury rental in South Lake Tahoe located on a private cul-de-sac. The large back-deck is perfect for entertaining, and includes a filtered view of Lake Tahoe. The gourmet kitchen with granite counter tops is perfect for anyone who enjoys cooking. And the large living room features a 70 inch big screen TV with surround sound.

With 6 bedrooms, and 4 baths, and 4400 SQFT, this luxury home can comfortably accommodate 14 people. The master suite includes a huge master bedroom and bathroom with a jacuzzi tub and gas-log fireplace. Enjoy the game room which includes a card table, pool table, and wet bar. There is also a ping-pong table in the garage, and horseshoe pits in the back yard. Relax in the private hot-tub on the back patio after a great day of skiing! Heavenly Ski Resort is only 10 minutes away!
