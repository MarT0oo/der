Lakefront Cabin, South Lake Tahoe, California Vacation Rental

 

Our Lakefront South Lake Tahoe vacation rental is located amidst breathtaking scenery and provides multiple opportunities for fun and relaxation. Whether you are looking to unwind in our Jacuzzi after a long day of skiing or a place to moor your boat in the summer, this South Lake Tahoe vacation rental is well equipped to fit everyone`s needs. It is available for a weekend getaway or as a long term Lake Tahoe vacation rental.

The gated driveway provides privacy in a quiet residential setting. The main rooms large windows have magnificent lake views and allow for lots of natural light. There is a lakefront deck with a Jacuzzi and private pier. The large family room is a great place to gather around our rock fireplace, watch TV on our flat screen or play a game of Billiards. The master bedroom has magnificent views and also offers a Jacuzzi tub and a two-headed rock shower. This is a great South Lake Tahoe vacation rental to enjoy with your family and friends.

Winter: Just minutes from skiing at three different resorts including Heavenly Valley and close to shopping and restaurants. Summer: We are right on the water and Regan Beach is about 5 houses down from us.
