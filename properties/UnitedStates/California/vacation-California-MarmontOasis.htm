Enjoy a magical Summer getaway in this classic and historical Hollywood treasure. It is nestled behind the infamous Chateau Marmont. (Food can be delivered from there). Perfect location for relaxing by the pool or entertaining friends in the large private garden while also being within walking distance to Sunset Blvd and a short drive from all the wonderful restaurants and attractions the West Hollywood, Beverly Hills area's have to offer.

Hollywood lies about eight miles northwest of downtown Los Angeles in the foothills of the Santa Monica Mountains, the home of the famous Hollywood sign. Hollywood is the birthplace of the American film industry, its name eliciting images of wealth and fame. It is filled with attractions that celebrate the entertainment industry and its larger-than-life persona, from behind-the-scenes tours at Universal Studios to high-end shopping and people-watching on the Sunset Strip and Hollywood Boulevard.

Most tourists come to Hollywood to experience the glamour and excitement of Tinseltown while keeping a hopeful eye open for celebrity sightings, but you will also find a variety of natural and cultural attractions and things to do, including hiking, horseback riding, and museums.
