Beach Duplex – 4 Bed 3 Bath, Venice Beach, California Vacation Rental


Steps to beach and to nice restaurants, walk or bike to everything, partial ocean views, roof top deck that has ocean views, a BBQ, and patio furniture.

This is one of our most popular 4 bedroom properties, can sleep up to 12 persons… This is located in one of the best Venice Beach locations.. Close walk to Abbot Kinney Blvd shopping and Marina del Rey. Easy drive to other Southern California destinations.

In this beautiful 4 bedroom Beach Home the kitchen, dining area, and living room are an open floor plan. There is a dining table that fits 6 persons and the breakfast bar fits another 4 persons..There is a large master bedroom that has a queen bed, a twin sofa bed, a large walk-in closet and private bath.

The 2nd bedroom has a queen and a twin bed, the 3rd and 4th bedrooms have queen beds, and there is a queen sofa bed in the living room..In the 4th bedroom there is an efficiency kitchen that has a small refrigerator, sink, microwave, and a 2 burner stove.
