The Pond Estate, Palm Springs, California Vacation Rental

 

The Pond Estate is truly a magical opportunity wrapped in the historic spiritual nature of the old Palm Springs Canyons. Passing through the gate you are surrounded by an oasis with ponds, bungalows and an incredible perspective of the mountains.

A large formal main house enjoys grand spaces perfect for more formal entertaining while the 3,500 square foot "guest house" is the ultimate retreat for visitors, complete with its own tennis court. There are two other guest quarters several, several garages, a horse stable along with the riding area, and heli-pad.

Far and away The Pond Estate is the most substantial property in Palm Springs with endless opportunities to create your own world in one of the country's most in-demand resort cities.
