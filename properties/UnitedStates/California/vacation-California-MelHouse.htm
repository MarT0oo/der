Mel House, Palm Springs, California Vacation Rental

 

Recently updated, this four-bedroom retreat includes southwest views of the mountains in a central Palm Springs location. This family dwelling offers traditional accents and furnishings with the perfect blend of modern pop artwork displayed throughout.

A large media room that seats 12 includes action film-noir movie posters for the ultimate theater experience. The sleeping quarters offer plenty of relaxation in bright hues and modern furnishings and appointments. All bedrooms have direct access to the pool area and include en suite bathrooms.

The terrace and completely private yard feature abundant lawn space, which surround the pebble-tech pool and spa.

Three blocks away is Ruth Hardy Park amidst the original estate homes of the historic Movie Colony. This is a great neighborhood for morning walks, enjoying the many architectural styles of homes from mid-century, modern, to Spanish revival. Just blocks to down-town Palm Springs for nightlife, shopping, and near the Uptown Design District on Palm Canyon Drive, this home is convenient to all sorts of Palm Springs fun.
