Sunset Terrace, Palm Springs, California Vacation Rental

 

This luxurious 5 bedroom Palm Springs vacation home is located in the very desirable Vista Las Palmas neighborhood and is within walking distance of the world famous Palm Canyon Drive with restaurants, shops and art galleries. Guests can enjoy the rooftop terrace overlooking the pool and bocce ball court with beautiful views of the San Jacinto Mountains. Perfect for sunset cocktails.

The main house has three bedrooms and the casita has an additional two bedrooms, each with its own entrance and en suite bathroom. All five bedrooms are equipped with California king beds, en suite bathrooms and private patios.

Completely remodeled in January 2017, guests can enjoy the most updated appliances with quartz countertops in the large chef's kitchen with ample dining space. The open concept living area and kitchen are bright and airy with glass sliders opening the house to the pool and lawn for a wonderful Palm Springs experience.

For your added pleasure, this home includes 7 flat screen TVs, gas fire place, an outdoor gas grill, fully groomed bocce ball court, game table in the den, ping pong table, saline pool with attached spa, and outdoor fire pit.

About the Area

Palm Springs, a city in the Sonoran Desert of southern California, is known for its hot springs, stylish hotels, golf courses and spas. It's also noted for its many fine examples of midcentury-modern architecture. Its core shopping district along Palm Canyon Drive features vintage boutiques, interior design shops and restaurants. The surrounding Coachella Valley offers hiking, biking and horseback riding trails.
