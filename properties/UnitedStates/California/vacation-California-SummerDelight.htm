Summer Delight Lake Tahoe, California

 

Check out this luxurious Heavenly Valley rental! Up to 12 can stay in style in this grand home. Situated on a cul-de-sac, this lakeview vacation rental is peaceful and quiet. Admire the sight of the mountains and casinos, and experience breathtaking views of Lake Tahoe from the family room on the lower levels, the mid-level kitchen and living room, and the master suite and deck with a fabulous hot tub on the top level. The chef of the house will love the gas stove in the gourmet kitchen, which also has a 10-person dining table. In the upstairs living room you'll find gorgeous hardwood flooring. Take in the view from the massive deck off of this area. There's a living room downstairs too, which has a table, television, and a DVD player. Feel like royalty in the master suite, boasting a sitting area with a comfy sofa and a television, as well as a private bath with a detached shower and a classic claw-foot tub. Heavenly Valley is a super-convenient one mile away, while the casino nightlife and Tahoe's beaches are within a ten-minute drive. A perfect setting for a corporate getaway or a retreat with your extended family! VRP# 001028
