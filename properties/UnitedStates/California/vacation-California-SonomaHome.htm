Sonoma Home, Saint Helena, California Vacation Rental

 

Stonehenge depicts wine country living at it's best. You will enjoy the home as much as you'll love exploring Napa Valley. The home is professionally designed and furnished (mostly Restoration Hardware) and comes with a gorgeous pool, patio with fireplace, fire pit, BBQ, 1/2 outdoor basketball court and 1/4 planted acre of Cabernet vines.

It is perfectly located in the heart of the Valley and only 2 miles from Main St. in St. Helena where you will find restaurants, shops, tasting rooms, etc. If you prefer to walk or rent a bike, there are wineries near by, such as, Del Dotto, Raymond, Louie Martini, Flora Springs, Plump Jack tasting room, and others. Dean & Deluca is also just a walk through the vineyards for pastries, coffee, sandwiches, wine and any other gourmet treats that may tempt your palate. Enjoy Napa Valley at it's finest.
