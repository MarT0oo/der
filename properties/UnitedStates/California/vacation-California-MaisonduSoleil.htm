Maison du Soleil, Palm Springs, California Vacation Rental

 

A four bedroom Spanish style estate in the Las Palmas neighborhood, Maison du Soleil offers a large game room and bar as well as an impressive great room and media area for all your entertaining needs. In addition, the home is refreshingly tranquil, with a courtyard that features a large three-tiered fountain.

The four bedrooms each offer flat screen TVs, DVD players, sitting area and private en-suite bathrooms. Two of the bedrooms are located at the Eastern end of the home, and the other two on the Western end. The oversized master suite features a king sized bed and offers direct pool patio access. Two other rooms feature king sized beds, and the last offers a queen sized bed.

The outdoor living areas of Maison du Soleil provide abundant seating for all guests, lush green space and fantastic Western Views. The poolside Palapa provides a unique gathering space for guests enjoying the outdoors. This home is also within walking distance of the Uptown Design District.
