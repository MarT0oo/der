Desert Solstice, Palm Springs, California Vacation Rental

 

Located on a 2/3 acre corner lot in the heart of Las Palmas, this four bedroom features 4,960 square feet of living space in its “U” shaped floor plan. On the western side of the property is a private hard surface tennis court.

The master suite features a custom California king-sized bed, with large sliding glass doors facing the pool to the east. Its en suite bath is a true spa experience, with a free-standing jetted tub, stacked stone fireplace, and separate water closet with bidet. A sliding glass door opens onto a semi-private patio with western facing mountain views. The remaining bedrooms are located off the main entrance and offer 40” HD Flat Screen TVs, each with en suite baths.

A true desert retreat, Desert Solstice brings the indoor living to the pool patio, with an outdoor dining area, gas fire feature, and a rectangular pool with elevated spa and spillover. Extremely private, Desert Solstice is only a short drive to Downtown Palm Springs’ fine dining, art and culture.

About the Area

Palm Springs, a city in the Sonoran Desert of southern California, is known for its hot springs, stylish hotels, golf courses and spas. It's also noted for its many fine examples of midcentury-modern architecture. Its core shopping district along Palm Canyon Drive features vintage boutiques, interior design shops and restaurants. The surrounding Coachella Valley offers hiking, biking and horseback riding trails.
