Mid Century Escape, Palm Springs, California Vacation Rental

 

Located in the vibrant Movie Colony neighborhood -- steps from shops, galleries and restaurants -- Mid-Century Escape offers a private escape in the heart of Palm Springs.

The gated mid-century estate welcomes guests with lush green lawns, towering palm trees, and a meticulously manicured garden surrounding a swimming pool, hot tub and fire pit. Chaise lounge chairs surround the pool for relaxing days in the sun, while a bench-style dining table provides plenty of space for group dinners with views of the surrounding mountains.

Glass doors open to the backyard along with a spacious living area with high ceilings, a gas fireplace and a charming wet bar. A mid-century style sofa and club chairs face a large-screen SmartTV for days inside, enjoying central air conditioning. Fusing elements of minimalist decor with the technicolor soul of the 1960s, the decor of Mid-Century Escape features clean white walls and furniture accented by cheerful lemon and lime hues, hardwood cabinets, exposed brick, and intricate tilework throughout.

The great room opens seamlessly onto a modern kitchen with stainless steel appliances and ample counter space, offering inspiration to one’s inner chef. The adjacent dining area features a chic, white oval table, surrounded by retro inspired saucer chairs.

Conveniently located down the hallways of this single-level home are 3 spacious bedrooms, each with unique decor, an en-suite bathroom, and a private entrance to the backyard. An additional 4th bedroom is located in the Casita.

A stacked washer and dryer and outdoor shower add convenience as you enjoy lounging at the pool and hot tub, while a two-car garage makes it easy to explore the surrounding area -- including nearby hot springs, golf courses, trails, and even Joshua Tree National Park, an hour’s drive away.

However you choose to experience Palm Springs, Mid-Century Escape offers a chic retreat with an ideal location in the heart of it all.
