Heavenly Ski Resort Chalet, South Lake Tahoe, California Vacation Rental

 

Absolutely, unbelievable, gorgeous brand new construction located directly on the slopes at the Stagecoach Lift of Heavenly Ski Resort! You won't even have to walk or drive to skiing, the slopes are directly out your front door! Amazing!
