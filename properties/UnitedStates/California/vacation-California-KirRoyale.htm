Kir Royale, Palm Springs, California Vacation Rental

 

This ridge-side luxury residence is located in Vista Las Palmas with more than 4,200 square feet of indoor/outdoor desert living.

The residence sits on rugged terrain as a contemporary showcase rising out of the foothills of Mt. San Jacinto. Living quarters include a master suite on the upper level with private deck, exterior stairs to the pool and Yoga and Meditation room. The master bedroom with hardwood floors offers an intimate fireplace and a spa-quality master bathroom that includes duo rain shower heads and a free standing Boffi tub. The two additional bedrooms on the lower level offer outdoor access to the pool deck. A fully self-contained casita features a private entrance poolside with a private patio for the fourth bedroom option.

Enjoy the desert lifestyle while taking in the breathtaking views of the adjacent bolder field and mountains from the outdoor living room featuring a Viking BBQ and range and Sub-Zero appliances perfect for dining alfresco or relaxing. Also enjoy the refreshing saltwater pool and tanning shelf, desert Zen garden, or rejuvenate in the elevated spa or outdoor misting system. This lifestyle home integrates modern architecture and mid-century influences with warmth and accessible living for today.
