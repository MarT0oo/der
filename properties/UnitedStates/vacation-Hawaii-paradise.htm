The moment you walk through the front door you will feel as though you have found a bit of paradise! This spacious 4 bedroom, 4 bathroom home (3 of which are en-suites) will provide you with memories to last a lifetime.

On the covered outdoor deck with alfresco dining or out by the pool stargazing is where you may choose to spend most of your evenings. The large outdoor venue may prompt you to grill out as you visit with family and friends and watch the sunset in paradise.  This open and airy custom home invites you in to relax and stay for a while.

The five-star accommodations at Paradise Makena Villa will leave you relaxed and reinvigorated.  There may be days when all you want to do is just relax around the pool, listen to some of your favorite music or read a book on the hammock- this is just the perfect place to do it!
