Malibu Colony Beach Mansion, Malibu, California


Malibu Colony Beach Mansion is part of the exclusive guard-gated neighborhood that is world renowned for its celebrity residents. "Malibu Beach Motion Picture Colony" was the first name given to this famous address in 1926. Some of the first Colony residents were Bing Crosby, Ronald Colman, Gary Cooper, Gloria Swanson, Marie Dressler, Warner Baxter, Jackie Coogan and many others. When the land was finally offered up for sale in 1936, celebrities such as Burgess Meredith, Cary Grant, Brian Donlevy and June Lockhart grabbed up homes in this neighborhood only 20 miles from Hollywood.

Recently completed in January 2009 Malibu Colony is a completely oceanfront home with private stairs that access your personal ocean (technically, the property includes approximately 150 feet out into the ocean as well). The home comprises approximately 4200 square feet and consists of Master Bedroom with wood burning fireplace, full whitewater ocean views, fully automated blackout privacy shades on all windows, three sets of French doors leading onto oceanfront master deck with a private Jacuzzi spa on its own deck. Full master bathroom with fireplace at master tub and oversized rain shower and two walk in closets with custom built cabinetry. Master bedroom electronics include private Crestron video zone which connects plasma TV. to five separate TiVo systems as well as video gaming systems and your personal computer, independent audio zone with iPod, xm satellite and CD capabilities, Guest Suite features a Queen bed with it's own fireplace, sitting area and flat screen television featuring a private Crestron video/audio and dvd system, private deck and guest bathroom. Two additional Bedrooms with trundle twin beds feature their own flat screen televisions connected to the TiVo video system and own private full bathroom. Maids Room on separate floor with private flat screen television and closet. There is a separate full sized bathroom for the maid as well.

Large kitchen with work level wood-burning fireplace, La Cornue range, in drawer microwave, walk-in butler's pantry complete with professional high speed dishwasher and sink inside, custom cabinetry, imported French chandelier, flat-panel recessed television with full Crestron video control zone capabilities and touch panel control. Outdoor Entertainment Areas with amazing features including an all-weather, outdoor flat screen television which connects to the Crestron video controls and accesses five TiVo’s under a gas heater to warm you.
