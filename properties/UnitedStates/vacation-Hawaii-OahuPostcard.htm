Oahu Postcard, Kahala beach, Maui, Hawaii Vacation Rental

 

Oahu Postcard, is situated on a beautiful ocean front setting with picture postcard views accompanied with the melodic sounds of the Pacific Ocean. This contemporary island home is luxuriously appointed with all of the comforts and amenities that every discriminating and well traveled guest comes to expect.

Oahu Postcard is located along the golden coast of Oahu's sunny southern coast with calm weather and gentle waters. The interior is custom decorated with rich woods and sleek lines. Five spacious bedrooms, including two Master suites easily sleeps 12-14 guests. Two Master Suites on the top level offer en-suite spa baths and ocean views. One Master features a King bed. The second Master features a Queen size bed. The third upstairs bedroom offers two Double beds and en-suite bath. The two downstairs bedrooms each offer a King size and Queen size bed and share a lovely remodeled hall bath. With cable TV in every bedroom, everyone has a private space to retreat to, for some quiet time.

Enjoy your days and evenings spending quality time with your special friends and family in the large swimming pool and Jacuzzi. Or sun and relax in one of the chaise lounges with a good book and watch the festivities. While the little kids (and big kids too) are enjoying the pool, you can grill & prepare your lunch & dinner in the gas BBQ. Next to the swimming pool is a large covered lanai providing ample seating space for a comfortable respite from the sun. At the end of the day, enjoy a sunset stroll along the beach or just relax with a glass of wine on your ocean side pool lanai. Exploring Waikiki, shopping for souvenirs or luxury goods, dining at local favorites or five star restaurants is a snap as Oahu Postcard is just ten minutes from world famous Waikiki. World famous Hanauma Bay, a natural preserve offering a myriad of tropical fish is just ten minutes in the other direction.

Oahu Postcard offers a central, beachfront location and private pool; everything to create perfect postcard memories of your Hawaii vacation!
