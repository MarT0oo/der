Midtown Jewel Sapphire, Manhattan, New York Vacation Rental

 

Midtown Sapphire is an extraordinary apartment that offers stunning views of the iconic Manhattan skyline from the bird’s eye vantage point of a quintessential New York building. This contemporary vacation rental makes the perfect pied-à-terre for a small family on holiday in the Big Apple, a couples’ retreat or even a lively honeymoon for epicureans with a taste for something a little more fast-paced. Be a part of it, in the one and only New York City!

At Midtown Sapphire, you will find something to look at both above and below. Amazing helicopter views peer down to Times Square while the west presents scenery with the Hudson River. Guests of Midtown Sapphire will enjoy an array of exclusive amenities, including a private leisure lounge with a beautiful rooftop and fitness center. The perks inside this private apartment include an ice maker, cable TV, Wi-Fi, dishwasher and laundry facilities.

Take time to absorb the mesmerizing views of Manhattan through a series of floor-to-ceiling windows that enhance both light and space. Furnishings in the living and dining area are chic and contemporary. You will also find stunning wooden floors and a custom Poliform kitchen with Miele appliances, a Sub-Zero refrigerator and generous stone countertops.

Two exquisite bedrooms accommodate up to four guests at this non-smoking luxury apartment. Each bedroom includes a king sized bed, en-suite bathroom and television. With the breathtaking views of the city to wake up to each new morning, you will no doubt have that old Sinatra song playing your head in no time!

During your stay at Midtown Sapphire, you will be within walking distance of the Empire State Building, Times Square and Central Park. Explore the crisscrossing streets spotted with telltale yellow taxis and restaurants offering cuisine from every corner of the globe. Whether it’s a show on Broadway or neighboring Carnegie Hall – or just discovering the best martini in the city – get the fabulous New York vacation you deserve
