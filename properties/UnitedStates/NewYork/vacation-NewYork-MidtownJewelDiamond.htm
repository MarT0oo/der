Midtown Jewel Diamond, Manhattan, New York Vacation Rental

 

This large apartment is located in a world class luxury building downtown. This 2,650 square foot apartment is among the largest four bedroom apartments that this amazing building has to offer, as it is a combination of two interconnected units.

Amazing helicopter views looking down at Times Square with west facing views to the Hudson river. Soaring 60 stories high, this magnificent building offers mesmerizing views of the Manhattan Skyline through a series of floor to ceiling windows which enhance the light, space and view from your room.

This unit offers stunning wooden floors, custom Poliform kitchens with Miele appliances, a subzero refrigerator and stone countertops. Four full and two half beautiful bathrooms featuring soaking tubs, marble floors and walls as well as stunning fixtures throughout. Guests will enjoy an array of exclusive amenities, including a private leisure lounge with a beautiful roof top and fitness center. Staying here is truly a dream of luxury at its best!
