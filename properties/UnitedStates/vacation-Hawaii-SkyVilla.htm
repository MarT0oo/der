Sky Villa, Maui, Hawaii Vacation Rental

 

A magical Sky Villa in Maui, with 5 bedrooms, ocean views and more amenities than you can imagine. The beautiful location in Historic Lahaina with amazing views is the perfect location to enjoy your holiday.

Come to Sky Villa Maui for ocean adventures, long beach walks, , calm warm waters that are perfect for swimming and paddle boarding, and balmy nights. Facing west you will see dramatic sunsets and bright night skies. Whether you are looking for an active family vacation or a relaxing getaway with friends, Sky Villa Maui welcomes new and returning visitors with beauty and Aloha. Sky Villa Maui is a designer updated vacation home. The entire house has New A/C for your comfort. Paddle board right off the back of the house. Learn to surf down the beach or come in November – March to watch whales spouting from the ocean-front lanais over looking the pacific. Our meditation tower has great views of the sunset, jumping whales and miles of blue ocean. Relax, unwind and experience paradise!

Maui royalty. A magical abode close to the setting the ancient Hawaiian kings selected from all the islands and all the places on Maui. Architecture that swirls and soars in the manner of the ocean and sky before it. Includes perhaps the premiere master bedroom in America where one feels floating on a cosmic ship of azure. Step out onto the splendid balcony and ascend the open air winding stairs to your private meditation tower like an ancient castle suffused on all directions by ocean and mountain vistas including Lanai, Kahoolawe, and the sublime peaks and contours of Pu'u Kukui. The refined luxury of the master bathroom will take your breath away, including sharing the same mesmerizing views. There are four additional generous bedrooms with equal comfort, including five additional bathrooms. One of the finest lap pools in Hawaii - long enough to actually swim - together with a Two Bunch Palms-inspired soaking pool and hot-tub. The living room, cook’s kitchen, dining room and media room are all grandly designed and appointed, gracefully synergized by the long line vision of the designer. Staying in this ocean front villa is truly experiencing paradise designed to match and enhance the wondrous beauty of Maui ... and succeeding!

Sky Villa is a private oceanfront 5 bedroom home on the beach in Lahaina, Maui Hawaii. Sleeps 10 - 12 comfortably. Each bedroom has wireless access. The open-air house was build to enjoy the natural beauty and breezes of Maui. Doors fully retract in master bedroom, living and dining room. Sky Villa is walking distance to Lahaina Town and a short drive to beaches on the south and west side. This is a convenient location for exploring all of Maui.
