Sea Song, Kauai, Hawaii Vacation Rental

 

Nestled on a spectacular ocean bluff, this exquisitely designed residence offers guests an opportunity to enjoy one of Kauai's most breathtaking ocean, mountain and river views. Situated amidst lush tropical gardens, this 12 acre estate is a 10 minute walk down our private beach path to Kahili Bay, one of Kauai's most secluded and scenic beaches.

Great care was taken in the design and construction of this remarkable home built with the help of many of Kauai's finest craftsmen. Beautiful materials, fine woods, stone and tiles are highlighted by a unique blend of tropical colors; nature's "garden palette" is truly reflected inside and out. The dramatic colors of the Great Room's high-beamed ceiling coupled with the custom-made Teak sliding doors perfectly frame our inspiring ocean and river panorama. In fact, every room in the Main Residence affords guests stunning ocean views.

The Main Residence and charming Garden Studio and Library Bungalow are joined by meandering stone paths surrounded by exotic orchids, gingers, heliconias, and other colorful ornamentals. Dramatic tropical trees provide a swaying canopy of light and color and are home to many of Kauai's delightful songbirds. Unwind on the spacious ocean and garden lanais or take a leisurely stroll through the lovely gardens.

From the expansive windows in each room one can watch the ever-changing estuary of the Kilauea River as it flows into Kahili Bay. At this "one-of'-a-kind" estate, each day is an opportunity to enjoy ocean breezes, fragrant flowers and the song of the sea. From glorious sunrises to dazzling moonrises and star filled nights, Sea Song at Kahili Bay offers guests a peaceful, serene, joyful and enchanting experience.
