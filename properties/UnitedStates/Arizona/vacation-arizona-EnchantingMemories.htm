Enchanting Memories, Scottsdale, Arizona Vacation Rental

 

Enchanting Memories is indeed a grand space. A total of 6 bedrooms, all of which have access to an exterior courtyard can accommodate up to 12 guests, with an additonal two on the Living room Queen sleeper. Four of the guest bedrooms feature spa-lke bathrooms, and the 5th bedroom has 2 extra long twin beds. All bedrooms have flat screen TVs. The Master suite has a king bed w/ entertainment hookups, a fireplace, and a gym next to the suite. The large open living room features a 55" flat screen with stereo system, a pool table, a poker table, and a wet-bar with a popcorn machine. The kitchen is adorned with stainless steel, with an adjacent dining room.

The oasis like grounds feature a large private pool with a slide, a large hot tub, a tennis/basketball court, private putting green and large open grassy areas. A ramada with BBQ grill, and outdoor dining table add to the entertainment factor.

Silverspur is a pravately gated home for extra security amongst the beautiful lush grounds. Available for extended stays and corporate events.
