Camelback Mountain Retreat, Camelback Mountain, Phoenix, Arizona Vacation Rental

 

Camelback Mountain has long presided over the surrounding terrain. Whether you are on the hill looking down, or down below looking at the storied landmark, the views are nothing short of spectacular. The Camelback Mountain Retreat neighborhood lies just to the West of the Camel's head, which highlights the Praying Monk along with a myriad of shapes and textures on the mountain itself.

The three bedroom main house is all en suite. One guest bedroom features two Queen beds with are ideal for the small travelers. The master suite has a king bed and the second guest bedroom has a queen. All beds are adorned with Italian linens, a flat screen TV, and a private patio.

The spacious master suite is designed to coddle. Open and airy, large windows offer endless views, and a fireplace will warm on the crisp nights. By the time you finish counting each of the 19 separate shower heads, you will be clean as can be, yet will not want the moment to end. The adjacent lady-slipper footed soaking tub enjoys a lovely view of the outdoors.

The lofty ceilings are lined with massive timbers reminiscent of the territorial style homes, which once occupied the area. Rustic treatments, such as a roughed out stone wall frame a fireplace and meld into rustic yet elegant stone flooring.

At the rear of the property lies the guest house. Two bedrooms; one with a queen bed and the other two Queen beds, and each has a private bath.
