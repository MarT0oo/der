This estate boasts amenities that rival any home in the world.  The media room offers great privacy for classic games or friendly tournaments. The gymnasium works as a sixth bedroom that can be configured with 2 twin beds for kids. The gourmet kitchen has enough area to entertain and prepare, with easy access to the dining room and great living room.

Hale Luxury Estate has a state-of-the-art lighting system that can enhance the visual effects of the home. The outside lanai area – with adjacent fire pit and fireplace overlooking the infinity pool – is great for sunset cocktails. The drop-down sandy beach pool offers kids the enjoyment of having their own sandbox to play. The dual Jacuzzi on both north and south ends of the pool offer privacy for both sides of the home. The outdoor massage area also has privacy in a relaxing setting for guests to pamper themselves.

This estate is considered the epitome of grand design, decor, and luxury living on Maui!!!
