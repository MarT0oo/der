Trail Creek Villa, Sun Valley, Idaho Vacation Rental

 

Located on Trail Creek in the Bitterroot neighborhood of Sun Valley, this Contemporary Luxury home with Private Hot Tub is just steps from downtown Ketchum via the Wood River Trail System Pedestrian Bridge. Centrally located, both Dollar Mountain and Bald Mountain (River Run Lifts) are less than 2 miles from the home.

The Main floor encompasses the common living areas as well as one of the master suites. The gourmet kitchen is fully equipped and features top of the line appliances including a Double Oven, Gas Cooktop, and a Stainless Steel Fridge. The adjacent family / breakfast room includes Bar Seating, a Round Table (seats 4) Leather Chairs and a Flat Screen TV.
