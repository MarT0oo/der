River Rock, Ketchum, Idaho Vacation Rental

 

Featuring amazing indoor and outdoor space, this luxurious 4 bedroom, 5 bath, 4500 square foot home is located 3 miles south of Ketchum in a very private neighborhood, directly on the Big Wood River. From the moment you enter the home, you will be both captivated and surrounded by the beautiful sight of the flowing river sparkling through the Aspen trees.

In the winter, guests are only minutes away from many activities and attractions such as Downhill and Nordic skiing, snowshoeing and ice skating.

Summer adventures are also plentiful in Sun Valley. There are fantastic biking trails nearby as well as a handful of great mountain biking trails. Bald Mountain is nearby and boasts incredible hiking and guaranteed views. Guests are also minutes away from world class fly fishing.

The master bedroom is on the main level and features a king bed, fireplace, walk-in closets and large master bath with separate shower and soaking tub. There is also a half bath on this level.

On the lower level are the remaining bedrooms, 2 bedrooms with queen beds and an additional bedroom with a set of twin beds, all with en-suite baths. There is also a laundry room, garage, and half bath as well as a family room featuring a large, flat screen TV. From this room, you can access the lower outdoor patio featuring a full outdoor kitchen with a keg bar.

This home also features a bocce court, pickle ball court, and a remodeled sheep wagon that is available for sleeping in during summer months.
