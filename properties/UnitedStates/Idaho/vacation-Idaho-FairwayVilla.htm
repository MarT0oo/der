Fairway Villa, Sun Valley, Idaho Vacation Rental

 

This newly built 7,000 square foot mountain home located in Sun Valley features 5 bedrooms, 6.5 baths and windows with gorgeous Bald Mountain views.

Located just 10 minutes from town, access to the Sun Valley Resort is easy. After a long day on the slopes, guests can cozy up to the wood-burning fireplace located in the double-story great room. The home also features a mudroom with electric boot warmers to keep your ski gear dry. As an added amenity, there is radiant heating throughout the home.

In the summers, guests are just a step away from the links as this home overlooks the Sun Valley Golf Course and features air conditioning upstairs. Sun Valley is the perfect destination for outdoor excursions, with fishing on the Big Wood River to mountain biking at the River Run Base area, the adventures are waiting!

In total, this vacation home sleeps 12 and has a separate living and dining room as well as a media room. A gourmet kitchen with 2 refrigerators. 2 dishwashers, and an upstairs and downstairs laundry facility, makes this the perfect entertaining space.

The master bedroom is located on the second floor and has a King bed, en suite bathroom with glass enclosed shower and separate tub, flat screen TV, gas fireplace and incredible views of Baldy Mountain. Also on the second floor is the second bedroom with a Queen bed, built in twin bed, and en suite bathroom with a glass enclosed shower/tub combo. The third bedroom comes with a bunk suite, one set of built in bunk beds and two full beds with en suite bath and a glass shower. The king guest suite is located on the first floor and has a flat screen TV, an en suite bath, and views of Baldy Mountain . The fifth bedroom is located on the first floor and has an en suite bathroom and private deck.

With no shortage of amenities, there are 5 flat-screen TVs, wireless internet, an outdoor hot tub and fire pit, a stone patio with a barbecue grill and a 2 car garage. Full Sun Valley amenities with use of year-round heated pools, Jacuzzi and sauna plus tennis and golf at discounted rate are included. To preserve the quality of this home pets are unfortunately not allowed.
