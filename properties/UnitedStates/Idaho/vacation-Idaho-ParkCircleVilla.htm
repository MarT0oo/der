Park Circle Villa, Sun Valley, Idaho Vacation Rental

 

This charming, updated 2,400 square foot home is located on the Big Wood River with a pleasant little creek and a small bridge over to the connecting island on the river. This lovely property is in a quiet, exclusive part of Ketchum with incredible access to the bike path and easy access to the town of Ketchum.

The bedrooms are located upstairs. The king master suite has a wood fireplace, flat screen TV and a private balcony. The master bathroom is a delight, with heated floors, large soaking tub with river views, a steam shower and double sinks.

Both of the guest rooms also have private full bathrooms. The first guest room has 2 twin beds that can be bridged to a king bed and flat screen TV. The second guest room has a king bed, armoire, mounted flat screen TV and a small loft.

This home is located very close to the YMCA facilities and the Mountain Rides bus stop. To help maintain this property, no smoking is permitted.
