Shady Lane Villa, Sun Valley, Idaho Vacation Rental

 

Experience the ultimate in luxury lodging with family and friends at this quintessential Sun Valley retreat. Located in the Warm Springs area directly in front of Bald Mountain, Shady Lane 322 encompasses 5000 square feet.

Guests are less than 1 mile from the Warm Springs lifts in the winter and after a long day on the slopes, can enjoy the private hot tub. The Sun Valley Lodge and Village is also only about 3 miles away.

In the summer, guests have access to the bikes located on the back wall of the garage and can easily hop on one of the many mountain biking trails that make Sun Valley such a fantastic summer destination.

The open kitchen is perfect for cooking and entertaining with a dining table that can seat up to 10 people. With a Thermador 6 -burner stove as well as a Subzero fridge/freezer, and wine fridge, no luxury is overlooked in this home. The living room has a large wood-burning fireplace, flat-screen TV and expansive windows that look out onto the patio. The patio is equipped with two tables that can each sit 6, a BBQ-grill and a fire pit for the ultimate entertainment. At the back of the home is another large media and den room with its own sound system and a large gas fireplace.

This 2-Level home features four en suite bedrooms all furnished with a king bed, large tub and separate shower. Each level has two bedrooms. One bedroom on each floor features not only a king bed, but also includes a twin-size trundle bed; giving these rooms the ability to sleep 4.

Sun Valley is the ultimate summer and winter destination and Shady Lane 322 is the perfect home to relax and enjoy all the adventures that await guests outside the front door.
