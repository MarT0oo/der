Huffman Villa, Warm Springs, Idaho Vacation Rental

 

This exquisitely designed 4025 square foot home is the perfect combination of Sun Valley luxury, warmth, and comfort.

The main floor has an open spacious living room with breathtaking views of Bald Mountain. From the living room, glass French doors open to a large wrap-around deck with a gas grill.

The master bedroom is located on the main level, with a king bed, a large walk-in closet and an elegant bathroom. There are three guest bedrooms downstairs, the first of which has two double beds and a private bath. A second guest room with a queen bed is further down the hall with a full bath. Across the hall is another guest room with a king bed and a full bath.

This lovely home is minutes from the mountain via bus, on a quiet street, and three minutes to the center of town. Access to Sun Valley's extensive world class biking and hiking trail systems is minutes away. The Warm Springs area of Ketchum is wonderful; minutes from the mountain for skiing or hiking, and downtown is also just a short trip for all restaurants, galleries, and shopping.
