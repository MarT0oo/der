Golden Eagle Villa, Mid Valley, Idaho Vacation Rental

 

You will be amazed by the splendor and warmth of this Sun Valley vacation home from the moment that you enter the driveway.

This spacious 4 bedroom home is located in the exclusive Golden Eagle neighborhood which surrounds a large pond. The property includes wonderful outdoor space with spectacular views all around. Truly experience the home and surrounding beauty from a large patio area, lawn outdoor fire pit, and barbeque.

When you enter the house it offers a surprise around every corner, including exquisite artwork throughout. The large formal dining room has a unique round dining table that seats eight, surrounded by high ceilings, large fireplace, and spectacular views.

Off of the dining room is a library to the left and a lovely the living room and kitchen to the right. The library boasts impressive walls of books, a stone fireplace and a set of large leather chairs to sink into. Bookworms will especially appreciate this room!

The living room has two walls of windows that overlook the lake and the surrounding mountains in Sun Valley. It is perfect for entertaining with its two story rock fireplace, high ceilings, a Steinway piano, two seating areas and a 46 inch Flat Screen TV. Adjoined to the living area is the large gourmet kitchen, a breakfast nook, new appliances with an open concept plan, perfect for the chef in the group.

The main floor has two large guest bedrooms, each with a king bed and en suite bath. Upstairs is the master suite with his and hers master baths, two walk-in closets, a large jetted tub, a steam shower and a private massage room.

This floor also has a sitting area with a stone fireplace, library and flat screen TV. Just off this room is another bedroom area with a King sized bed and provides access to private a balcony with amazing views. From the main entry continue down the hall to the first bedroom.

Downstairs is the fully equipped media room with a projection screen TV, media library, pool table, large couches and theater seating. There is a home gym that has all the state of the art work out equipment which includes a treadmill, elliptical, stationary bike and free weights. Down the hall is the fourth bedroom which has a queen sized bed, sitting area and private bath. From this bedroom you can also access the living area and garage from a second stairway.
