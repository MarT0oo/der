Osprey Cabin, Mid Valley, Idaho Vacation Rental

 

Located on the Osprey estate across Highway 75 from the Valley Club, this luxury home sleeps 10 people in three bedrooms, including a main floor master, each with their own bathroom. The Osprey property is surrounded by abundant wildlife, including moose and elk, and offers private access to fly fishing on the Big Woods River.

The open-concept great room, dining and sun room provides a beautiful place to relax by the fire, enjoy sweeping views, or entertain from the full kitchen and spacious dining areas. This home also has a heated single-car garage, separate outdoor parking, and a large patio. As an added bonus, the cabin provides an additional self-contained one bedroom nanny or grandparent apartment located on the bottom level.

The cabin is centrally located just 9 miles from River Run ski resort and 3 miles from Hailey.

*For larger groups or intimate gatherings, this home can be combined with the Osprey Main House (please view Osprey Estate rental).
