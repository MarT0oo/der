Downtown Logger Luxe, Sun Valley, Idaho Vacation Rental

 

Completely remodeled in 2016, this Gorgeous Ketchum 3 BR / 2.5 Bath Log Home with A/C is a a beautiful mix of modern comforts and amenities nestled in the rustic charm of a Log Home with Wood Burning Fireplace and Mountain Views. Downtown Ketchum restaurants and shops are a mere 5 minute walk from the home while the River Run Ski Lifts and Big Wood River are less than a mile or 5 minute drive.

Filled with natural light, the living area features Cathedral Ceilings, Flat Screen TV, and a Wood Burning Fireplace with Gas Starter. Leather couches- centered around the Fireplace and TV- comfortably seats 6. The small gourmet kitchen with open shelving features a Wolf 4-burner Gas Range, K-cup Coffee Maker, Stainless Steel appliances and back splash. Granite Counter Bar Seating for 3 is a great spot for a quick snack.

Adjacent to the Kitchen, the Dining Area easily seats 6 with built-in leather seating wrapped around the dining table. Enjoy a cup of coffee in one of the Adirondack Chairs on the front Porch or head out to the furnished Deck with Gas Grill for al fresco dining. (deck dining table and chairs available seasonally)
