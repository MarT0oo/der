Sheep Meadows, Ketchum, Idaho Vacation Rental

 

This 4 bedroom, 4 bath log home sits on a gorgeous lot situated on the Big Wood River, with views from the house of the river and a pond to the side of the house. This is a beautiful lot surrounded by Aspen trees and a lovely expansive lawn that sits alongside the river.

On this main floor are two bedrooms, one with a king bed and bath with tub/shower combination. The other bedroom offers one set of bunks and one additional twin bed. There is an en suite bathroom with walk-in shower, small refrigerator and kitchen space with a separate entrance to the outside. The laundry room is on the first floor, just on the other side of the mudroom entrance.

The second floor houses the master with a king bed, sitting area in front of the fireplace, great views of Baldy, the river and the pond from the bedroom, and a balcony overlooking the river. There are two bedrooms upstairs, one with a king bed overlooking the river, and one with a queen.
