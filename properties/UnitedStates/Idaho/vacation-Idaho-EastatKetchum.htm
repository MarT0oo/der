East at Ketchum, Ketchum, Sun Valley, Idaho Vacation Rental

 

You will adore this stunning 4 bedroom, 5 bathroom townhome, centrally located in downtown Ketchum. Beautifully styled with tasteful furnishing, a modern kitchen with Wolf range 6 stove burner and griddle, and unique dining area, this home is great for entertaining and gathering the whole crew.

Sitting at the base of Bald Mountain, the views from this townhome are unmatched, and guests can soak in these incredible views from a comfortable balcony accessible from the living room.

With 3 bedrooms and 1 guest bedroom with 4 twin beds for the kids or singles, the unit is perfect for families or groups of couples. The master bedroom offers a king-sized bed with dual sinks, jetted tub, walk-in closet and private balcony access with great mountain views and views of Ketchum. The other two rooms provide queen sized beds, and all bedrooms are elegantly appointed for a true luxury experience. The 4th bedroom offers four twin beds and a pull-out couch, comfortably sleeping 5 in the room. Each of the bedrooms has an en-suite bathroom, and the master bath is truly spectacular.

There is also a one car attached garage, washer/dryer, garage and air conditioning for your convenience. Experience the beauty of Sun Valley in this equally gorgeous townhome!
