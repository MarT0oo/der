Garnet Villa, Sun Valley, Idaho Vacation Rental

 

Townsend Lodge is on a quiet street in old Ketchum. This wonderful 2,200 square foot home, which looks out on a large open field, offers spectacular views of Mount Baldy - Ketchum’s main ski area.

This updated home offers a fully equipped kitchen with gourmet appliances and renovated bathrooms. The great room is a spacious living area with a wood burning fireplace, a dining room table for 8 and a small library/sitting area. The two master bedrooms each have king beds and their own bathrooms. French doors open out on a deck to the backyard and a spacious slate lined hot tub. Down the hall, there is an additional queen bedroom and an Adirondack style bunk room which comfortably sleeps 4. Both bedrooms share a hallway bath close by.

Appointments for guest use include a washer/dryer, fully stocked kitchen, high speed internet, 4 televisions (one 40” high definition flat screen,) 2 DVD players, CD player with iPod dock, Sony Playstation 2, and a gas grill.

To help maintain the beauty of this property, smoking is not allowed. This home is pet-friendly with an additional pet fee.
