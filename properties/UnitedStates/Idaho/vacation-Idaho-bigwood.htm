Luxury Big Wood Riverfront, Ketchum, Idaho Vacation Rental


Luxury Big Wood is located in Ketchum, this extraordinary home sits on the Big Wood River and is less than 5 minutes to downtown Ketchum or the lifts.  With a large and abundant living space, including a separate Guest Wing, this luxury home provides ample room for extended families or friends vacationing together. As you enter the grand Foyer, you may access the Guest Wing through a door to the right head through the Double Wood Doors to the left to enter the Main home. If desired, the Guest Wing may be completely closed off from the Main home.

The centerpiece and heart of the home is the Great Room with Cathedral Ceilings and Big Wood River views. The Great Room is comfortably furnished and features a Wood Burning Montana Rock Fireplace- perfect for socializing or curling up with a book in front of a fire. French doors to the south open up to the front entry alcove with a Rock Fountain while windows to the west and north offer Big Wood River views.

The adjacent Dining Area seats six and has French Doors leading to the Riverfront Patio. For the little ones, there is a kids' table that seats four. The adjacent Gourmet Kitchen offers additional bar seating along the concrete counter.

The Gourmet Kitchen is fully equipped with top of line equipment including a Sub Zero Refrigerator and Freezer, Dacor 6-Burner Gas Range, Wall Oven, Microwave, and Warming drawer. Adjacent to the Dining Area as well as the Patio with Built In Gas Grill, the kitchen is conveniently located for either dining inside or alfresco. For catered events or large gatherings, there is a Butler's Pantry with a second dishwasher and refrigerator.

Also located on the ground floor is the spectacular Master Suite with a King Bed, Montana Moss Rock Fireplace, Big Wood River views and French doors leading to the Riverfront Patio. The Private Bath includes a Jetted Tub, Dual Vanities, Glass Shower, and a huge Walk-In Closet with built in shelves and drawers.

Upstairs and open to the Great Room below, the Family Room is complete with a TV, DVD Player, comfy couch and Office area with Desk. French Doors lead to the Riverfront Balcony with stunning Mountain and Big Wood River Views.

The Library with overstuffed chairs is a great escape to relax with your favorite book or if you need to burn a little energy head to the Game Room with Ping Pong table and play a friendly game of table tennis. Additionally, there is separate office for those on a working vacation.
