4 Lazy F Cabin, Teton Springs, Idaho Vacation Rental

 

This cabin is located on Bannock Circle and overlooks a peaceful fishing pond. It also borders the Caribou-Targhee National Forest, so there is an abundance of hiking trails. Or if you prefer to relax, enjoy the luxurious hot tub or peaceful balcony.

The master bedroom is located on the main level and has a king bed, large jetted tub, glass walk-in shower and entrance onto the balcony. Another king bedroom is located on the lower level with a glass walk-in shower. There are 2 extra long full beds on the lower level with a shower/tub and entrance onto the patio. Another room on the upper level has 2 extra long full beds and a glass walk-in shower.

All of the custom, luxury mountain log homes are close to the trails in the adjoining Caribou-Targhee National Forest, allowing easy access to mountain biking and hiking. The resort’s 25 meter pool and children’s Geyser Land, hot tubs, Fitness Center, tennis and basketball courts are also conveniently located. Teton Valley is a Mecca for hiking and mountain biking and Teton Springs is the gateways to unlimited trails in and around Teton Valley.
