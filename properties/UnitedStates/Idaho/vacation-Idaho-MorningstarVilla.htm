Morningstar Villa, Elkhorn, Idaho Vacation Rental

 

Outdoors, the slopes of Sun Valley; indoors, Western luxury at Morning Star Road 715. Tucked onto a five-acre lot with views of Bald Mountain and the surrounding peaks, this Elkhorn vacation rental is just minutes from skiing, golf, tennis and more. Its spacious interiors and five suite-style bedrooms can accommodate ten friends or family members in modern comfort.

Morning Star Road 715 opens to a beautiful patio with plenty of lounge chairs, a pergola-covered sitting area, a serene water feature, a barbecue and fantastic views. When there’s snow on the ground, entertain indoors in the inviting games room, which features a pool table, ping-pong table and wet bar. A fireplace is the perfect spot to curl up with a book, while Wi-Fi lets you stay in touch with friends at home.

The villa’s bright, roomy interiors are warm and welcoming. A vaulted ceiling with exposed wood beams stretches over the great room’s living and dining areas, with sofas around the stone fireplace at one end and a table that seats ten at the other. The fully equipped kitchen is just off the great room and has state-of-the-art gourmet appliances and a built-in stone pizza oven.

Each of the five bedrooms at Morning Star Road 715 has an en-suite bathroom. There are two bedrooms with king beds that open directly to the terrace, and three bedrooms with queen beds. High ceilings, wood trim and neutral tones make the bedrooms feel chic yet tranquil—perfect if you’re filling your holiday with activities like skiing or hiking.

The area is known for some of the best skiing in Idaho, and thanks to its Sun Valley Village location, the villa brings you close to the slopes. It’s just a 5-minute drive to the Elkhorn lift at Dollar Mountain, and about a 4-mile drive to Sun Valley Ski Resort. If you’re visiting during warmer weather, make the 2-minute walk to play tennis or swim in the pool at the Harker Center, or the 5-minute drive to the nearest golf course. The nearby towns of Sun Valley and Ketchum are your best bets year-round for everything from quick après-ski bites to multi-course, honeymoon-worthy dinners.

Please note: there is a hot tub on the property that is not operable.
