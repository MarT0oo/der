Highland Villa, Elkhorn, Idaho Vacation Rental

 

Highland Villa is a warm and welcoming 4,600 square foot, 4-bedroom, 4.5 bath home located on a quiet street in Elkhorn. This homes sits above the Elkhorn Golf Course’s 10th fairway and has both gorgeous mountain and golf views. This is the perfect home for families or a large group.

This thoughtfully furnished home is laid out over two levels with the kitchen, sitting area, dining room, living room, and master on the main level. Three bedrooms, den and game room are on the level below. The spacious kitchen has updated appliances and an adjacent sitting area with a large flat screen TV, fireplace, and large windows to enjoy the views. The dining room can sit up to ten people and just beyond is the living room with two sofas and two oversized armchairs, as well as another fireplace, making this the ideal home for entertaining.

The large master bedroom is located on the main level and has a king bed with en-suite bathroom that includes a jetted tub and a separate shower. The lower level of the home has three remaining bedrooms—two of the bedrooms are configured with two twins in each and en-suite bathrooms. The other bedroom is a queen with direct access to a bathroom, which also doubles as a shared lower level bathroom. All bedrooms have private doors and access to the patio that includes a hot tub. Also on the lower level, is a large den and game room with another large flat screen TV.

Guests have access to a one car garage.

Guests are not far from all of the attractions that make Sun Valley such a famed winter ski destination. Sun Valley’s beginner hill, Dollar Mountain, is just a mile away; the River Run Lodge and Resort Base is only 4 miles away; and the Sun Valley Nordic Center is under 3 miles from the home. In the summer, enjoy golf and miles of walking and biking trails right out your door. Activities at Sun Valley’s Village and in town festivals are also only a few miles away.
