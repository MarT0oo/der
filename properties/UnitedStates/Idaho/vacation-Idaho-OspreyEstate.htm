Osprey Estate, Mid Valley, Idaho Vacation Rental

 

This private, luxury retreat sits on 85 acres of woodland reserve in the Wood River Valley. The gated estate consists of two newly updated log homes with over 10,000 square feet of living space. Guests of the estate will enjoy serene wooded privacy, access to fly fishing on the Big Woods River and an abundance of wildlife roaming the property.

The main house offers four bedrooms, three of which are king suites, including a main floor master. The fourth is a bunk room with a full bathroom, complete with its own TV and lounge area. A 2-car heated garage and large laundry room offer plenty of storage for your group’s ski, fishing, biking and other recreational gear.

A stone-paved walkway leads to the guest house, which sleeps 8 people in three bedrooms, including a main floor master, all with their own bathroom. On the bottom level is a private one bedroom apartment. This home also has a heated single-car garage and a large patio.

Osprey Lane Estate is centrally located across Highway 75 from the Valley Club, just 9 miles from River Run ski resort and 3 miles from Hailey. Small, intimate gatherings available for booking; please contact Xplore for additional information and pricing.

*For smaller groups, the Main house and Guest house may be booked separately (see Osprey Main House and Osprey Cabin rental).
