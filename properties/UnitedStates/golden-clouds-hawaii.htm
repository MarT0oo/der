Oahu Estate and Spa, Oahu, Hawaii


Welcome to the Oahu Estate and Spa Retreat, the first holistic anti-stress centre on earth. Located directly on the North Shore of Hawaii’s island of Oahu and just 40 minutes from Honolulu’s world class shopping and dining. Private, secure and inviting we tempt you to indulge in the luxury of the estate or experience nearby surfing, whale-watching, golf, tennis and many more.

 

Extensively renovated in 2006 and 2007, The Oahu Estate and Spa Retreat offers its guests all the amenities of a seven star plus resort & SPA within the intimacy of 5 1/2 acres. The stunning six bedroom mansion and extensive walkways, surrounding water features, and tropical landscape offer the best views of Oahu’s legendary North Shore.

The Oahu Estate and Spa Retreat is incomparable; truly one of Pacific's and Hawaii's most exquisite properties and a North Shore/Oahu landmark. Guests often speak of the property's sense of tranquility, which is enhanced by the offering of our new JK7™ – OlaLoa, The SPA Retreat for the 7 Senses™ with SPA-Treatments never experienced before and the Estate’s very personalized service. The Estate offers the best of everything – whilst applying deep respect for our green environment and being energy conscious.  Are you in a workout mood?  The Oahu Estate and Spa offers it all – cardio, weight, balance and resistance, with or without Personal Trainer.

 

Enjoy an evening with your family and friends watching a movie in the fully equipped Multi-Media Room or hold a meeting with your Business Partners while your spouse, family or friends enjoy a SPA Day.

 

At The Oahu Estate and Spa Retreat we care for the environment and enhance “green living” in every way. Combining the biggest project in Hawaiian residential Voltaic solar energy with innovative SPA treatments, the Estate is a truly holistic and eco-friendly relaxation, health, well-being and fun refuge.
