Seamless Luxury, Kilauea, Kauai, Hawaii Vacation Rental

 

A secluded and serene Modern Balinese-Style Estate located on the North Shore of Kauai is the perfect get-a-way for relaxing in Paradise, where all you will hear are the sounds of nature and be amazed by the plethora of stars in the sky at night.

Surrounded by 4 acres of stunning landscaping and tropical foliage, this 4 bedroom 3.5 bath retreat is on its own 45 acre private lake where you can go fishing, paddle boarding, or take a kayak out with a glass of wine and enjoy the breath-taking views of Kauai’s renown Mount Namahana.

The home itself is over 5,000 sq ft, with an additional 4,000+ sq ft of exterior decking, including covered Lanais, outdoor dining and lounging areas for lots of indoor-outdoor living. With an oversized great room, including large gourmet kitchen, living and dining areas and panoramic pool and lake views, this romantic setting is perfect for a couple who wants to spoil themselves, as well as can accommodate a total of up to 8 guests. In addition to the great room, there are 3 additional separate structures, interconnected by covered breezeways .

The upstairs master overlooks greenery and has its own separate office looking onto the pool and lake, a two bedroom wing (both with desks), also has a TV/media room, and downstairs master bedroom (900 sq ft) overlooks expansive views of lotus ponds and tropical palms. Lastly, there is a small bedroom house and deck with arched roof, (without a bathroom) that is secluded among bamboo trees closer to the lake, with more extensive views of the lake. The infinity-edge pool and Jacuzzi are salt-water and no chemicals are used on the property. Other features of the house are a private outdoor shower nestled among bamboo trees.

With so much to enjoy on the property, you’ll need a reason to leave. Yet Kauai’s best beaches are just down the road, including Anini and Kalihiwai Beaches…10 minute drive from Princeville, and a 15 minute drive into Hanalei town and more beaches, all of which are great for surfing, snorkeling, and or just surrendering into the beauty of it all.
