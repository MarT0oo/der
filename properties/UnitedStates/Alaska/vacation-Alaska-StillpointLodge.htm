Stillpoint Lodge, Halibut Cove, Alaska Vacation Rental

 

Located at the base of the mountain fjord of Kachemak Bay, this boutique, all-inclusive luxury resort can be found set on the edge of the wilderness. With 11 cabins and suites, each with spectacular views of either the mountains, cove or ocean, our guests comment on the stillness of the area. With only the sound of whales breaching nearby or of bald eagles souring overhead, this property allows you to escape the noise and enjoy all that Alaska offers. Adventure can be found nearby from kayaking through glaciers to bear viewing by helicopter to fishing from a float plane on a remote lake, our team can organize your stay. Guests enjoy our gourmet cuisine prepared by our Chef using the freshest of Alaska ingredients including some from our own gardens. Berry picking, photography, relaxing on your private deck with a good book, morning yoga, afternoon meditation or even mountain biking, the choice is yours to do as much or as little as possible, while enjoying the luxury you have become accustom to. Stillpoint Lodge welcomes individuals, couples, families (children ages 6 and older) or complete property buyouts
