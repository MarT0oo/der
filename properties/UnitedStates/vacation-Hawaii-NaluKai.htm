Nalu Kai, Kauai, Hawaii Vacation Rental

 

This extraordinary 30-acre estate perched on the bluff above Kahili bay, offers expansive ocean views, a pool, spa and tennis court. Nalu Kai is a modern “pod-style” home with 5 bedrooms connected by outdoor paths and breezeways.
