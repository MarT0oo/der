The Lodge at Lana'i, Lanai, Hawaii Vacation Rental

 

The Lodge at Lana'i is a private getaway located on the famed Koele Golf Course on the exotic Hawaiian island of Lana’i. This exclusive property has a country feel due to its comfortable and rustic design motifs. Included in this five-bedroom home are two master suites, both with king-size beds and en-suite bathrooms. One of the master suites uniquely provides an attached baby’s room along with a crib and changing table, making the home perfect for young families looking for a private, relaxing and unique Hawaiian vacation escape. Also included are two guest suites, one with a king-size bed and the other with two double-size beds; both guest suites include en-suite bathrooms.

Open the folding glass doors to create an expansive indoor/outdoor living space with a lush mountainside backdrop. Enjoy cooking meals in the gourmet kitchen alongside modern amenities such as a Sub-Zero refrigerator, Wolf oven/stove and two dishwashers - a Fisher-Paykel and a Bosch dishwasher on either side of the farm-style sink. The cooking amenities don’t stop there, as you also have the choice of using the fully equipped outdoor kitchen with a built-in BBQ grill.

While your family is swimming in the heated pool or the kids are playing in the private playground, relax in the hammock set among the pine trees where you can unwind and connect with the nature that surrounds the home. You can also take the kids on a "field trip" to explore the self-sustaining organic vegetable garden with fifty different fruit trees.

The Lodge at Lana’i is truly an "exotic" estate and perfect for those who know and love Hawaii and are ready for a new experience. You may find your perfect paradise in this little-visited, yet beautiful and exclusive, Hawaiian island.
