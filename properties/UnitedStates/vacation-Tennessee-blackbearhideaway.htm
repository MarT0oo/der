Black Bear Hideaway, Pigeon Forge, Tennessee


This luxurious 5 bedroom log cabin covers a spacious three levels. From the moment you step out of your car, parked in your private garage, and see the custom carved doorway and the waterfalls, you know you have made it to a special hideaway.


Black Bear Hideaway features a serene screened in sun porch with an entrance onto one of two decks with country porch rockers. A second deck includes a full size hot tub where you can take in the scenery or follow the short path from the cabin to a screened in gazebo with a picnic table. This is only the beginning of the extra luxurious perks you will find in Black Bear Hideaway!


Once inside you will find a spacious great room that is perfect for family and friends to gather in. With a beautiful stone fireplace, a 42” Flat Screen TV, DVD Player and VCR, you may be surprised to find that there is also a family media room! Your family media room includes a Big Screen TV, Home Theater, and surround Sound System.
Black Bear Hideaway also includes a fantastic game room for your family to enjoy! This game room features a pool table, air hockey table, 37 game arcade game unit, a stereo, and a card table so everyone can sit and enjoy in the activities!


Black Bear Hideaway is located in Cedar Falls Resort, in the beautiful valley of Walden's Creek. You are sure to enjoy the many luxuries of having extra amenities right at your own resort including, a community swimming pool, playground, picnic area with fire pit, basketball, volleyball, horseshoes, nature trail, and a catch and release pond. You will have so many things to do in Cedar Falls Resort, that it will be hard to tear yourself away to see the attractions of Pigeon Forge and Gatlinburg!
