Come enjoy breathtaking mountain views from this new 4,800 ft² home.  This contemporary Adobe home offers three bedrooms and three full baths, as well as a guest powder room.

The Grand salon or entryway welcomes you with a Baldwin Grand Piano overlooking the mountain views.  The living area has a 56” flat screen TV for your entertainment pleasure, as well as a comfortable seating area and fireplace.  Direct TV/music.  The mountain and sunset views from this area are spectacular.  Sliding doors open up to a full wrap portal with Stark outdoor furniture, completely walled-in with a banco and outdoor fireplace.  The outside walled-in portal is the perfect place to have a glass of wine and watch the most amazing sunsets â€“ private and relaxing!

There is a private office for those who must have a working vacation; including a fax, printer, and internet for your convenience.  Sliding doors open up to your own private aspen grove.

There is a formal dining area for six with fireplace, French doors, and a private portal.  Formal dining ware, placemats, and cloth napkins provided.

The kitchen has an informal black oak serving area, SubZero refrigerator and wine cooler.  There is a Wolf gas stove, Wolf convection oven, and microwave.  In addition, the owner has provided an Espresso/coffee/tea/hot chocolate machine.  All cookware, cooking utensils, informal dining ware, and glassware provided.  The kitchen is fully equipped to meet all of your possible cooking needs; and also provides a drinking water purification system.  The entire home has filtered water and a soft water system.

Adjacent to the kitchen is a breakfast/family room with dining table, 48” flat screen TV, and fireplace.  Here is another portal with great mountain views, completely walled-in with two teak sun chairs; which provides the perfect spot to have your morning cup of Joe!  Guests can hear hummingbirds abound in the late summer.

Around the corner from the breakfast room is the mud/laundry room, with steam washer and dryer and ironing area.  Exit door opens to a heated 3-car garage.

The master suite has a king-sized bed, fireplace, ceiling fan, Infinity “Jacuzzi” tub with separate walk-in shower.  There are French doors to open a private portal.  Generous walk-in closet for two and full length dressing mirror.

The first guest suite has a queen-sized bed, ceiling fan, and bath with combination glass tiled bath and shower.  The suite has generous closet space and is quiet and private.

The second guest suite is a fun room with two twin-sized beds, ceiling fan, and a great fold-out Kartell bed.  (For monthly rentals the owners will furnish this room with a king-sized bed if it is desired).  The bath is a combination glass tiled bath and shower.  In this suite, the full length windows open up to the private Aspen Grove.

The house is also equipped with all organic, bamboo linens, Turkish cotton towels, hair dryer, shampoo, and soaps.

The property provides full-time security and gated entrance/exit, plus an ADT security system.  There is 24-hour on-site emergency medical response kit with a defibulator.

This spectacular property has been professionally decorated with every detail in mind for your perfect Santa Fe vacation.  There are miles of walking/jogging paths and hiking trails just outside your front door.  The property is located 5 minutes to the OPERA, 10 minutes to the Plaza, 10 minutes to the Farmers Market, 15 minutes to the Casino and 30 minutes to skiing.
