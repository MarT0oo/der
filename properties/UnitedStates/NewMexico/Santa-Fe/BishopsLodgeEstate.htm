Bishops Lodge Estate, Santa Fe, New Mexico Vacation Rental

 

This Luxury Getaway embodies the best of Santa Fe. The property is set in the picturesque Tesuque Valley known for its lush landscapes and flowing streams, and just 7 minutes to the Santa Fe Plaza! The estate is an exquisite work of art, with great attention to detail; a blending of traditional Santa Fe architecture with modern lines, and interiors that are as comfortable as they are elegant. It is an oasis in the desert - a dreamlike setting to be enjoyed privately during your stay.

The Estate is 5,000 sq ft, with 4 bedrooms, 4.5 baths, including a 1 bed, 1 bath casita. Featured in Sunset Magazine, the gardens are delightful at any time of day, and hiking trails can be found nearby. With more than an acre of landscaped gardens and mature trees, lavish manicured lawns, and several water features, this beautiful setting is unlike any other.

Bishops Lodge Estate is designed for open living, with bright, naturally lit rooms from large skylights and windows opening to the landscaped gardens and courtyards. It is a showpiece, handcrafted from floor to ceiling, while tastefully incorporating modern conveniences, including a state of the art lighting system, whole home audio, and a Dolby Atmos entertainment center. The home is decorated with lush rugs and rich tapestries, a thoughtfully appointed chef’s kitchen, large open family space with luxuriously upholstered furniture, and old-world details. You will also find radiant heat for those cold winter days, a steam shower to recoup from the dry desert air, and air-conditioning for the hotter summer moments.

This Luxury Getaway is the perfect combination of a five-star hotel stay and the comfort of a private home. Included in your stay are daily housekeeping, Frette linens and robes, Le Creuset kitchenware, Wolf and Miele appliances, and luxury bath amenities.

Retreat, relax and find yourself a world away.
