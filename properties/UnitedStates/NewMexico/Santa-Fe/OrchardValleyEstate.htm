Orchard Valley Estate, Santa Fe, New Mexico Vacation Rental

 

Experience this quintessential Santa Fe Estate with its' natural mountain setting just 5 minutes to the Plaza! Orchard Valley Estate is located just 3 miles north of downtown, perched atop Camino Encantado in one of Santa Fe’s most coveted areas. The 3 bedroom main estate boasts a very Grand “Great” room with views of the mountains and plenty of space for a family reunion. The main house features all en suite bathrooms. The professionally designed interiors and exteriors of the property are unparalleled. There’s a level of history and classical appeal that the Estate has in spades! The two bedroom guest house is perfect for accommodating the younger folks, etc., and here’s plenty of room in the main house to gather.

Orchard Valley Estate is situated on several acres in the foothills of the Sangre de Cristo mountains, there is ample space to explore nature and have private adventures within the comfort of your ‘home’! The gated Estate has ample covered and open parking, and please reach out to us for concierge services or any provisioning that you may desire.
