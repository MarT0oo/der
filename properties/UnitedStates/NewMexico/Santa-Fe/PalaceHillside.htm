Palace Hillside, Santa Fe, New Mexico Vacation Rental

 

Palace Hillside is nestled on a gentle hillside above the Santa Fe River. Canyon Road - the Art and Soul of Santa Fe- only steps away, and will lead you to the Plaza as you stroll past the best galleries and restaurants in town! Or, you can walk alongside the tree lined Santa Fe River on your way to the Plaza. This 3 bedrooms 3.5 bath Estate is all ensuite, and features a wrap around garden, outdoor lounging, and a wonderfully unique floor-plan.

Relax and experience the historic charm of this 1800's double adobe home with vigas, archways and gorgeous plaster with modern amenities!

Palace Avenue is itself a landmark of the historic east-side, enjoy 150 year old shade trees as you follow the bricked sidewalks to downtown, or to the lovely park up the way. From your hillside oasis, views of the treetops and the sound of the Plaza cathedral bells will help you leave the city life behind!
