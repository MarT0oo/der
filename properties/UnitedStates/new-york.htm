Finding the Right Vacation Home

New York, the city of lights, sounds, free theater on the park grounds or just plain old fashioned fun!  New York City is Synonymous with the most amazing and insightful schools, histories, and cultural diversity that we will ever find.  It is no wonder that the French thought her worthy of a Guardian to guide her travelers through the night as they came searching for better and fuller lives.  Not only is NY City a wonder but so isn’t New York state.  The State with some of the best Skiing, the Best Foods, the most amazing beaches, and the most amazing views you will ever find.  If you think you know New York, think again.

Just some of the best skiing is found in Thunder Ridge just one hour from the city , a family friendly ski area.  Catamount has a wonderful Park for kids as well as skiing. In Lake Placid you have the Olympic Jumping Complex which offers Nature Treks, Skating, Ballet School and more or you can try. Woods Valley which really gives you a wonderful feeling of family.  No matter where you go to ski, it’s well worth the travel time.

And don’t think that’s it. There are also the Beaches, also known as the Hamptons Beaches to the stars and celebs.  You will find some of the best things to do in these locations, from the wonderful Art Galleries such as The Parish Art Museum, The Hampton Road Gallery or Guild Hall.  

If love dining out, the Hamptons offers Le Chef Bistro, a top rated Restaurant, Almond Restaurant for fine French Bistro Style, or Regular Music Café for some Fun with the kids.  You can always venture out and find all kinds of great little places to stop and eat in.  It’s all about finding the right place for you and the Hamptons has it all.

Most people love New York for more reasons than can be expressed, visit for the city life, Manhattan, New York City, or the essence of the smaller suburbs of Queens and the Bronx, or visit for the radiance of New Years and the Great Apple Falling with showers of confetti.  No matter why you chose to come to New York, you’ll always chose to return.
