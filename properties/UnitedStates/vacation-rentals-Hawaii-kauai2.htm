These Kauai Beachfront villas are located just 15 minutes from the town of Hanalei and just steps to Tunnels Beach, the best snorkeling location on the island. The Kalalau Trail, which marks the beginning of the Na Pali Coast and Kee Beach, are about 2 miles from the property. The famous Princeville Resort (golf, tennis, spa, and restaurants) and Hanalei Bay Resorts are just 20 minutes away. The north shore of Kauai offers a multitude of fine restaurants and daily activities; a list of recommendations will be available to you for your stay.

 

Beautiful island style furnishings fill these two luxury homes. Each home has 3 bedrooms, 2.5 bathrooms, a fully equipped modern kitchen, formal dining room table, casual outdoor dining on the lanai, complete laundry facilities and 100% cotton Ralph Lauren linens.
