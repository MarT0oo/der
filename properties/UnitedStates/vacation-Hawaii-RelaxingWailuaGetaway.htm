Relaxing Wailua Getaway, Wailua Beach, Kauai, Hawaii Vacation Rental

 

Relax to the sounds of the ocean lapping at the shoreline right outside your bedroom or watch the dolphins play just off shore. During the winter months, whales frolic in their winter play ground, which can all be viewed right from your lanai! Kick back and relax in this brand new 5 bedroom home and make memories that will last a lifetime.

The Relaxing Wailua Getaway has 5 bedrooms, 6-bathrooms that can sleep 12-20 guests. There are 2 large wrap around lanais for guests to sit and watch the incredible sunrise and sunsets.

Four of the bedrooms have TV's and a foldout ottoman. The 5th bedroom has two Queen beds with a TV. There's additional space in the upper sitting room with a pull out sofa bed and three folding beds in the bedroom closets. Each room has its own air-conditioning unit that can be controlled independently of each other.

Have a great time at the beach and make use of the following available amenities: beach towels, boogie boards, surf boards, snorkeling gears, beach chairs, and a kayak. There are basic kitchen utensils including coffee maker and rice cooker. Enjoy BBQ with family and friends while you stay in this home. High chair and Pack N Play are available for your little ones. Paper towels, toilet paper, bath towels, and shampoo are also available. If you need to do laundry, there's a washer and dryer available for guest use. There is parking available at the home.

If you want a quiet and relaxing vacation, this is the place to be. It's located in a quiet and laid back neighborhood and it's near the beach. The home is set back 60 feet from the beach and has a beautiful lawn from the home to the beach. The beach is protected by a reef from large waves which creates great snorkeling and swimming. On the outside of the reef there is great surfing. Kayaking is good both inside and outside of the reef The area has a walking/bike path all the way into town, it is only 2 miles away from the popular surf town Haleiwa. Haleiwa also has plenty of shopping and a large variety of restaurants to choose from. There is no traffic getting to and from the house, it is 35 minutes from the airport and 35 minutes from the Polynesian Cultural Center. It is quiet and has a country feel and is located on a beautiful beach that is secluded.
