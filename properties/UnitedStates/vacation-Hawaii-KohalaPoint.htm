Kohala Point, Kauai, Anini, Hawaii Vacation Rental

 

One of the north shore’s most spectacular homes, Kohola Point enjoys 180 degree views across Anini Beach, from Bali Hai to the Kilauea Lighthouse. The entrance-way boasts a fabulous ocean/infinity 700 square foot pool and octagonal spa with three waterfalls seemingly falling to the sea.

The Master Suite features a raised king bed, Ipe flooring, retractable screens for privacy and a pop-up 40” plasma television at the foot of the bed. The artistically tiled Master Bath is spacious and equipped with a steam shower, spa tub, and out-door shower. There are three additional bedrooms, two with California king beds and one queen. Bedrooms all have full bathrooms and television as well as phone and high speed internet. Designer bedroom with super soft sheets, pillow choices, and comfortable mattress covers to be either hard or Tempurpedic soft. Each bedroom offers your own private deck for an afternoon massage or rocking away reading a book.

The airy designed living room has a large home theater with surround sound and retractable screen. Indoor and outdoor kitchens are fully equipped with top-of-the-line kitchen equipment and serving pieces. The home is a place that is perfect for entertaining or quiet solitude with plenty of dining, bar and outdoor lanai seating.

The home is 5,320 square feet with an additional 3000 square feet of lanai and deck area.
