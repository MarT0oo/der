Palmilla Luxury, Reunion Resort, Orlando, Florida Vacation Rental

 

Enjoy the perfect vacation in one of Orlando's most beautifully designed luxury rental homes. With a prime location overlooking the picturesque Nicklaus designed PGA course at Reunion Resort, this spectacular 6 bedroom villa presents a perfect opportunity for a special family vacation.

With a mediterranean accent and a tasteful blend of wooden flooring, natural stone, tall ceilings, and rich contemporary furnishings, the home has been hugely popular with discerning guests in search of an idyllic retreat in the theme park area of Orlando.

Best of all, the highly sought after home enjoys spectacular golf views from its prime location on Reunion Resort's Palmilla Ct. Close to Disney, but long enough away from home to slow down, relax and enjoy a well earned escape!

Located in the Reunion Resort, this beautiful home backs onto a conservation area. Reunion is probably the most prestigious rental community in Central Florida having every amenity that you could want to make yours a memorable vacation from restaurants, bars, swimming pools, 3 PGA golf courses, spa and water park though membership is required for certain amenities. The resort is also ideally situated for all the major parks, Disney, Universal & Seaworld with many local attractions situated on the US192 a ten minute drive away. The main entrance is only two minutes from I4 giving easy access to both the Gulf and Atlantic coast lines for great beaches.
