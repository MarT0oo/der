Nautical Treasure, Edgewater, Florida Vacation Rental


Make lifelong family memories at this enchanting waterfront garden property.  Perched behind an oyster shoal overlooking a serene stretch of state protected islands on the Intercostal waterway in New Smyrna Beach.  Nautical Treasure offers Old Florida Style in a nature lovers’ paradise.  Marine life  constantly visible from nearly every room (dolphin herding bait fish on to sandbars, manatee plying the waters beneath your private dock, an abundance of water birds) the villa offers 100 feet of direct intercoastal waterway frontage with spectacular views. 

World class fishing for Redfish, Trout, Flounder, Snapper, Grouper, Snook, Tarpon with abundance of shrimp, Oysters, Clams, Stone Crabs and Blue Crabs.

Nautical Treasure’s Old Florida Style Playhouse with its own dock and rowboat to explore protected lagoon enchant children and adults alike.  Enjoy a completely outfitted top-of-the-line gourmet kitchen, butler’s pantry and chef’s garden to table vegetable/herb garden (depending on season), a meal produced almost entirely from the waters surrounding the private dock and chef’s vegetable garden is not uncommon here.

Host a champagne and oyster roast on your private dock atop Nautical Treasure’s Sky Platform which features 12 bar stools, propane BBQ and stereo system.  Then kick back by a roaring fire in the dock fire bowl or poolside fireplace.

Centrally located 1 hour from Disney World/Universal/Sea World, 45 minutes from Kennedy Space Center, 30 minutes (by boat) from New Smyrna Beach inlet and lighthouse, 10 minutes from Historic Flagler Ave Restaurants, shops, full-service spas and bars, 10 minutes from the Atlantic Center for the Arts, 10 minutes from the beach, 15 minutes (by boat) from Cape Canaveral National Seashore, 20 minutes from Daytona International Speedway.

New Smyrna Beach’s Ponce Inlet break was recently named by National Geographic as one of the top 20 surf breaks in the world.  Local outfitters also offer kite surfing instruction and kayak or stand up paddle board excursions in the ocean surf or through fascinating networks of backwater mangrove formations.

Fourth Generation backwater guide available to pilot Nautical Treasure’s 10 person deck boat ($25/hour) for fishing, nature, Cape Canaveral National Seashore, beach, oystering, shrimping, and waterfront restaurant trips.
