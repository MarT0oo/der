Castello Royale, Reunion Resort, Orlando, Florida Vacation Rental

 

Whilst this luxurious custom built home looks impressive from the outside, your dream truly begins when you enter the home through the grand hallway with its featured staircase, that is a central aspect highlighted by its gallery landing. The games room is situated off the hallway and features an array of games that are guaranteed to keep the children occupied and entertained for hours and convenient to the purpose designed 'bunk' room that will be a haven for the children in the group, three sets of two full size bunks with a wall mounted TV and Playstation will be a haven for the younger children. If that wasn't enough, across the hallway is the games room offering air hockey, foosball, arcade game, wall mounted TV and games Xbox games console. There are four further king size bedrooms on the ground floor with en-suite bathrooms, two of them are king-size masters and located at the rear of the home, both offering access onto the pool deck, beautiful wooden floors, and bathrooms with twin vanities. One has an oval tub and walk in shower, and the other an over sized walk in shower with multiple shower heads. The open plan living area incorporates a separate powder room, a huge fitted kitchen complete with a long granite breakfast bar, two built in refrigerators, double oven, two dishwashers, and a large center island houses the commercial size hob. This truly is a gourmet kitchen with walk in pantry, two dishwashers, two refrigerators and an ice maker. The family seating area offers comfortable sofas from which to enjoy the large wall mounted TV and a formal dining area, a wall of glass doors open onto the covered lanai and overlooks the pool with golf course beyond.

Upstairs there are six further bedrooms all en-suite with two bedrooms being the third and fourth king-size masters with access onto the rear balcony with its breathtaking views. Two of the bedrooms offer two queen size beds in each and the final two are king size. At the front of the home is the exercise room with elliptical machine, exercise bike, and work bench. Also located on this floor is a second powder room and the impressive three tiered movie theatre which will be the perfect place to gather for movie night or for corporate groups to hold their presentations. The second gathering area of this magnificent home boasts a huge purpose built bar and preparation area offering a refrigerator, microwave, ice maker, and dishwasher all built into the granite counter top providing ample seating. With a card table, pool table, multiple TV screens perfect for game night and access onto the large wrap around style balcony overlooking the pool area and golf course this really is the perfect space to return to after a busy day at the parks or a conference.

Outside the heated pool and spa are surrounded by lounging and seating spaces, a covered dining area perfect for your alfresco meals, and a built in summer kitchen which will rival any commercial space. This really is living at its best!
