Poolish Dreams, Panama City Beach, Panhandle, Florida Vacation Rental

 

Renovated corner penthouse unit with breathtaking, ocean-front/views throughout. Located directly on the beach, this condo is newly decorated with custom-made cabinets and quartz countertops throughout. The Master and Third Bedrooms are newly carpeted with planked tile flooring laid throughout the rest of the unit. Custom-made, linen window treatments, along with beautiful, distressed furniture, give the unit a crisp, clean atmosphere made for relaxation. The large sliding glass balcony doors & Dining Room windows offer ample opportunities to take in the breathtaking views of the sparkling, emerald green waters, as well as, the many families of dolphin that frequent the waters.

The Kitchen is fully-equipped with new stainless steel appliances, as well as, a Reverse Osmosis Water Filtration System. A booster highchair is also provided. A brand new front load washer and dryer were recently added to complete this unit's beautiful transformation.

For your relaxation and enjoyment, the Living Room includes a new, 55" Flat-Screen Smart TV with a Blue Ray DVD Player. Relax on the couch and admire the beautiful waters of the Emerald Coast, or spend some quality family time playing cards and classic board games (conveniently located inside the coffee table.) There is also a wet bar located in the Living Room which is stocked with an array of cocktail glasses, a blender, an ice bucket, and other bar accessories.

Whether you desire to lay in the sun while your child naps or choose to enjoy a meal with friends and family, our large deck with a newly-furnished lounger, bar-height dining table, and chairs will provide the atmosphere you need.

Mixing pleasure with business? Have no fear. This unit provides an unshared, "Private," High-Speed Wi-fi connection with Streaming Capabilities.

The Beach-Front Master Bedroom has a new, king-size hybrid mattress, along with new bedding and furnishings which includes a Flat-Screen TV and DVD Player. Step out of your private patio door to enjoy the sound of the surf. For your storage needs, the Master Bedroom offers a walk-in closet. The Master Bathroom offers a spa-like atmosphere with a relaxing whirlpool tub and walk-in shower. A wall-mounted vanity mirror and stool are provided for your comfort.

The Second Master Suite offers a spacious atmosphere including a king-size bed and relaxing chair. A Flat-Screen TV and DVD Player are provided for your enjoyment. The Bathroom has its own spa-like whirlpool tub and walk-in shower. Also included in your amenities are a wet-bar sink, a refrigerator to store your drinks, and plenty of cabinet space to store snacks. There is also a large walk-in closet with a portable twin bed (bedding provided.) Enjoy your morning coffee and danish on your own private, plexiglass balcony with ocean views along with a great view of Destin. The patio deck is furnished with two comfortable chairs and a bistro table.

The Third Bedroom is designed with children or teens in mind; it offers a comfortable, full-size bed with new mattress and a twin trundle below. The amenities include a 32' Flat-Screen TV & DVD Player. A full-size Bathroom with tub and shower is located off the hallway.

Bath&Body Works Stress Relief toiletries are provided, as well as, upgraded linens in all the Bedrooms and Bathrooms. Each Bathroom is equipped with wall-mounted hair dryers. A starter kit of dish soap, laundry detergent, trash bags, paper towels, and toilet paper is provided, so you may enjoy your first day without rushing to the grocery store.

Want to save yourself some money? This unit provides Beach Service which includes two lounge chairs and an umbrella; this is a $30 daily savings. An outside storage closet (located directly across from the front door) stores extra beach chairs and an umbrella, loads of beach toys, several floats, noodles, and small boogie boards. All you need to provide for your personal use is a cooler and beach towels.

An assigned, covered parking space assures that you can always load & unload in comfort; Unit 801 has the closest parking spot to the elevator.

Poolish Dreams is meticulously maintained. It boasts a large pool with chairs, umbrellas, and loungers, as well as, a hot tub and a grilling area with several large grills and seating. A large Ice Maker (for a fee), vending machines, and bathrooms are conveniently located near the pool area. There are two walkways that take you directly to the beach.

Poolish Dreams is near attractions, shopping, restaurants, and right on the beach. You are just a short 10-minute walk to the Back Porch Restaurant. Directly across the street, you can find pizza, subs, beverage store, beach store, and ice cream/yogurt parlor. Also located across the street is The Track- a place to ride go-carts, play putt-putt, bungee jump, ride bumper boats, explore an arcade, and so much more.
