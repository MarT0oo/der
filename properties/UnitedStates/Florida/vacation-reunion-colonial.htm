Villa Colonial
4 Bedrooms | 3.5 Bathrooms | Pool | Gym
Reunion, Florida
From $2,604 per Week

This stunning 4 bedroom, 3.5 bath home boasts views of the Tom Watson championship golf course.  Villa Colonial is just a 5 minute drive from the heart of Disney, restaurants, supermarkets and shopping.  The villa is decorated in soothing contemporary style, with high-end customized soft furnishings. The villa has its own private pool and spa with a large sun deck with sun lounges and plenty of room for outdoor dining.  Colonial is equipped with a dining room, family room, games/TV room, luxury kitchen, breakfast area, large lanai, and extensive balconies on both the front and back of the home.

Villa Colonial offers free high speed internet access, free local telephone calls, and a 5.0 integrated surround sound system throughout the home; including the outdoor covered patio. All bedding and towels are provided.  The villa is a 2 minute walk to the resort centre, the Club House, bars, restaurants, spa and Seven Eagles Pool Pavilion.

Villa Colonial is 25 minutes from Orlando International Airport (MCO), 25 minutes from the Downtown Orlando Train Station, and 50 minutes from Sanford International Airport (SFB).
