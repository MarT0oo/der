Inspiration, Watersound, Florida Vacation Rental

 

This beautiful three bedroom, third-story, breathtaking UNOBSTRUCTED GULF VIEW, BEACH VACATION GETAWAY, located OCEANFRONT on SCENIC Highway 30-A, is DIRECT BEACHFRONT!

Exquisitely decorated and fully equipped with everything to make your vacation a memorable and wonderful event.

In our garage you will find 4 bikes you can use for free! You'll also find an assortment of kids beach toys, boogie boards, and beach chairs that you are welcome to use.

Only three miles to SEASIDE, WATERCOLOR, and SEAGROVE VILLAGE with all the local casual and fine dining. Only three miles to ROSEMARY BEACH. Right in the heart of all the sparkling BEACHES of the EMERALD COAST, right off magnificent highway THIRTY-A in beautiful SOUTH WALTON COUNTY, FLORIDA.

Be prepared for the fantastic views that await you the instant you step into this luxurious, gulf front condo, in the complex of The Crossings, located in the private and gated community of WaterSound Beach.

Sitting directly overlooking a private, pristine beach front, The Crossings provides the perfect location for enjoying Mother Nature's glory. Elevators take you to your third floor unit making this location convenient for everyone.

Creatively designed and impressively decorated, our condo provides a spacious great room where you have a front row seat to a panoramic view of the Gulf, the beach and the natural vegetation amidst the dunes and walkways along the beachfront. Exquisitely decorated with a large great room that includes the living area, dining area and kitchen with upscale stainless appliances with granite bar and stainless appliances and 'no walls' to obstruct your views.

Luxuriate in the grand master bedroom suite with its large king bed, sitting area and adjoining private bath with a Jacuzzi plus a large, marble-and-glass enclosed walk-in shower. Awake to views of the emerald green waters or go out on the porch accessible from your bedroom. The second master, also with a King Bed bed, features a temperpedic-style foam mattress for your sleeping comfort. The bedroom also features a private marble bathroom also offers incredible gulf views and access to the gulf front porch. A third bedroom suite features a queen bed, and a twin bunk bed, to sleep 4 guests comfortably. The third bedroom also features a private marble bathroom.

Relax on the third floor extremely large balconies that overlook the gulf front pool and provides the best location for gazing out across the magnificent beachfront. Beach walkway is just steps from your door. You can see the main Crossings Pool from the expansive balcony, and the Watersound Bridges clubhouse and pool is just a short walk away. Located midway between Seaside and Rosemary Beach, there are many shops and restaurants and coffee shops convenient to you. Parking for two cars. Also, you are only minutes from the Camp Creek Golf Club.

For your swimming pleasure, three pools are available for your use. The Crossings swimming pool is located just a few yards from the door and is used exclusively by Crossings residents and guests. New for 2016, the Crossings pool has been totally renovated, re-tiled and furnished with all new top of the line furniture. The pool area is surrounded by a large deck area, grassy yard, and beautiful outdoor fireplace. There are two commercial grills that you may use. You may also use the community pools and pool houses named Bridges and Dunesider. These also have grills and grassy areas. WaterSound Beach Club is a private, members only, club. VRBO Guests staying in WaterSound Beach are not allowed to use this pool.

WaterSound has more than one mile of private, white, sandy beaches for you to enjoy. Other amenities include Puttering Park (18 hole putt-putt course), Yacht pond, playground, walking paths and boardwalks throughout the community and access to Camp Creek Lake. A state of the art fitness center is available for a small fee. Beach chair and umbrella rentals are available.

The surrounding area is a haven for outdoor enthusiasts. Over 20,000 acres is state-owned and therefore protected from future development. It is home to one of the most beautiful beaches in the nation, South Walton Beach, and also has 17 fresh and salt-water lakes and the Choctawhatchee Bay.

The 30-A bike trail will take you from Santa Rosa Beach to Inlet Beach. Along the way you can enjoy the communities of Blue Mountain Beach, Grayton Beach, Watercolor, Seaside, Seagrove, Alys Beach, Seacrest, and Rosemary Beach. Deer Creek State Park is located next to WaterSound Beach and other state park are just a short bike ride or drive away. The area has some of the best restaurants and most unique shops along the Scenic Emerald Coast of South Walton County.

Golfers can enjoy numerous courses in the area including Santa Rosa Country Club, Kelly Plantation, Regatta Bay, Emerald Bay and Indian Bayou.

If shopping interests you, WaterSound Beach is located about halfway between Destin and Panama City Beach. Destin features Destin Commons, Grand Boulevard and Silver Sands Factory Stores. Panama City Beach is home to Pier Park Mall with over 120 stores to choose from.

Close to Rosemary Beach, WaterColor, & Seaside. Near Scenic 30A communities like Seaside, Seagrove Beach, Grayton Beach, WaterColor, WaterSound and Rosemary Beach.
