South Yachtsman Home, Sanibel, Florida Vacation Rental

 

Spend a month or longer at the South Yachtsman Home on beautiful Sanibel Island, Florida.

The home was originally designed and built by a local artist and those unique touches are found throughout the home and property.

Enter on the main living level with a spacious living room, open kitchen, dining area and half bathroom. There is a screened lanai off the kitchen to relax and listen to the local wildlife. Off of the opposite side of the kitchen is the sliding doors to access the pool area complete with two dining tables, lounge chairs, flat panel TV, sink, BBQ grill and of course the huge pool.

The entire area is screened in for your convenience. The second level of the home has a family room, table along with two bedrooms, one bathroom and the laundry room with a full washer and dryer. On the top level of the home are two master suites with a desk and reading area in between. With plenty of room to sleep up to eight guests, South Yachtsman Home will surely be your top pick for your vacation rental home year after year.
