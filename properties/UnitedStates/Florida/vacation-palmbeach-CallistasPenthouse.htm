Callista’s Penthouse, Palm Beach, Florida Vacation Rental

 

Located on the island of Palm Beach, the Palm Beach Hotel Condominium (built in 1926) combines classic Spanish and Mediterranean architecture with modern conveniences.

The condominium, which features a large pool and in-house spa, is conveniently located in the “Main Street” area of Palm Beach within walking distance of numerous restaurants and shops. The sparkling waters of the Atlantic are just one block to the east; the bicycle trail which runs the length of the island along the Intracoastal Waterway is just one block to the west.

The trendy Royal Poinciana Plaza is also walking distance for your shopping pleasure. Please note that there is no parking at this hotel. There is 1 hour parking out front to unload luggage. This home is 950 square feet and is managed locally by Palm Beach Vacation Rentals.
