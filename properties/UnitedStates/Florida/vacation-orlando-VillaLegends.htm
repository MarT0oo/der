Villa Legends, Reunion Resort, Orlando, Florida Vacation Rental

 

This outstanding home offers over 4,000 ft² of luxurious living located on the Legends Corner area of the resort with breathtaking views over the 14th green of the Nicklaus golf course, one of three PGA courses within the Reunion Resort. The open plan living area having been recently redecorated is bright with colorful accents and access onto the large rear patio. This home will provide the perfect setting for those looking for a high standard of surroundings and amenities with that 'home from home' feel. Beautiful furnishings and decor have been used throughout the home.

This Mediterranean style home offer a spacious bright living space utilizing the whole of the ground floor with the exception of one queen size bedroom and a bathroom with a walk in shower. For entertaining or celebrations in elegant surroundings a formal dining room is located to the front of the home. There is a gourmet kitchen with stainless steel appliances and granite counter tops which include a long breakfast bar for casual dining adjoining the family living area with its comfortable leather sofas and accents chairs for viewing of the large wall mounted flat screen TV. The full width of the room has glass sliding doors overlooking the pool and patio area.

Upstairs the landing area has a dedicated work station for those guests who need to keep in touch with work. The first king-sized spacious master suite is located at the rear and includes a bathroom with garden tub, walk in shower and twin vanities with the added luxury of a private balcony with a double size day bed for your relaxation. The second king-sized master bedroom has a walk in shower and Juliet style balcony overlooking the front of the home. A further two bedrooms, one queen size and the other having two twin beds share the family bathroom which has a tub/shower combo. All bedrooms have TV/DVD's and the home is equipped with Wi-Fi.

Outside the area has a combination of covered patio, perfect for your alfresco meals or taking shade from the heat of the day, a built in Summer Kitchen, a paved area surrounding the pool and spa for lounging and an adjoining lawn area perfect for children to play.
