Champions Elegance, Orlando, Florida Vacation Rental

 

Brand New Vacation Home has 6 bedrooms, 6 bathrooms and is located in the Retreat, Champions Gate's newest vacation community. This home stands on a prime location and will overlook the water park.

This beautiful home has been professionally decorated and furnished in a neutral contemporary style to give a fun, relaxed atmosphere in this gorgeous Florida setting. With two king-size master suites, two or more families can easily be accommodated in absolute comfort and luxury.

Includes Unlimited Free Access To The New Oasis Water Park!

The downstairs of this home has two bedrooms, a king-size with the ensuite having a walk in shower and twin vanities and the second bedroom a queen size having a tub/shower combination. The living area is open plan incorporating a fully equipped kitchen with breakfast bar for casual dining adjacent to the formal dining and seating area with it's large flat screen TV. There is a fully equipped laundry room and pool bathroom for your convenience. A games room with pool table, air hockey and foosball will provide hours of fun and entertainment. The living area overlooks the pool deck which has a long covered dining area perfect for relaxing evenings and al fresco meals of simply taking shade after a swim in the heated pool or an evening jacuzzi.

Upstairs the loft area provides a second seating area with the added attraction of an Xbox games console. Surround the loft are four bedrooms, the second king-size master having an ensuite bathroom with twin vanities, a tub and walk in shower. A queen size bedroom and a bedroom with two twin beds share a 'jack n jill' bathroom with walk in shower and the sixth bedroom also with two twin beds is opposite the family bathroom with a walk in shower. Every bedroom has a flat screen TV and the home is served by Wi-Fi

A games room with pool table, foosball and air-hockey will provide hours of fun for children and families alike. A spacious patio with heated pool and spa surrounded by lounging space and a covered dining area. If you're not spending time at one the areas many theme parks and attractions just a short drive away, you can play or relax in your very own luxury home.

Best of all, your luxurious and comfortable dream home is less than 15 minute's drive away from Walt Disney World's main gates!

The Retreat at Championsgate is the newest vacation resort in Central Orlando. This amazing resort has been purposed designed to create the ultimate in vacation lifestyle.
