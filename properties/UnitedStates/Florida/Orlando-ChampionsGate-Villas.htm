About Orlando, Florida

Orlando has been called the “City of Dreams and Magic” because it is the single place where you will find summers filled with Disney Dreams and the Magic Kingdom and the Disney Theme Parks.  A truly joyous place for children and adults alike where kids will learn the true meaning of happiness as they go from park to park.  This city has welcomed visitors from every continent and every year grows and grows, bursting with tourists from anywhere you can imagine. 

 

The draw of course is the weather and the location.  The Orlando area, which also includes Kissimmee and Davenport, are always warmer in the winter time, which means you and  your family can visit year round.  The kids can enjoy holidays in a lovely Vacation Home that offers a heated pool, themed rooms, and lots of games for their enjoyment.  Mothers and fathers can enjoy adult entertainment in Orlando’s many famed bars and nightclubs as well as an entire Island, Treasure Island to be exact, where you can dance and party the night away.  A vacation in Orlando is truly a dream come true for any family or just friends looking to get away.

 

We offer a variety of accommodations, including smaller villas that sleep 8, all the way up to 9 and 10 bedroom villas that can accommodate your entire extended family.  The holiday homes don’t stop there, you can hire a few villas near one another in Formosa Gardens or Emerald Resort Community, it’s really just up to you.  Each home is individually owned and operated, while we take care of the professional marketing and reservations for these lovely rental homes.
