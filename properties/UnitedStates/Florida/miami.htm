Everyone loves the Idea of Visiting Miami Beach.  This city has recently undergone some major changes, including a Live Theater that can be seen along Miami’s New Downtown Center area.  Miami has undergone such a transformation that many are dubbing it the New City of Miami.  The areas that our Vacation Rentals are in are quite different.  Miami, unlike other cities, spans many smaller sub-sections, such as: Coral Gables, Sunny Isles, Normandy Isles, Aventura, North Miami, North Miami Beach, Miami Beach, Pinecrest, Palmetto bay, Old Cutler Bay, and many more.  Some people describe it as a melting pot and it can be described in many ways, but certainly never boring.

The place most people are familiar is Miami Beach.  In recent years Miami Beach has seen a host of High Profile Celebrities buying up property in very exclusive neighborhoods.  These celebrities are a great draw to our beaches, but they are not the only reason people come.  You can walk down Ocean Drive and find a host of “one of a kind” designer stores, that you will find nowhere else in the world.  Models come here to get their start as do designers, all hoping to catch the crowds that are often drawn to these locations.  In addition to shopping, you have a host of restaurants and café’s lining the streets.  You will never be at a loss of where to go, it’s just impossible to try them all.

And of course, Miami is known for it’s beaches, they are truly a draw for people from all over the world.  The draw may also be the beautiful people who often visit the beach, making it a hot-spot for singles looking to enjoy the sunshine and some night life.  No matter what the draw, Miami Beach is a great place to visit.  We must give it an Adult Rating due to the area and destination specifications.  It is great for Adults, we do not recommend it for small children.

You can now rent a home in allot of different areas of Miami.  The newest places are Eastern Shores and Normandy Isle.  Eastern Shores is a great place for those looking for a residential community, away from all the crowds, this is a plus for those with small children.  The area is often a draw to families traveling with groups of children, looking for the beaches, the pool, and gorgeous views.  There is so much you can do in Miami, from the Metro Zoo with your family, where you can find incredible shows, rides, and children’s activities.  There is also the Monkey Jungle, a great draw for nature lovers, this fun and fantastic place let’s you feed the animals while you walk through the park, where else can you do that?  If you enjoy the sea, you have to try Miami Seaquarium, where the fun is never ending.  They host water shows where they interact with the public, they also have animal shows, and if you register you can swim with the dolphins, it’s truly an amazing treat.  To round off the things for families to do we highly recommend the Fairchild Tropical Gardens, the Children’s Museum, and the Parrot Jungle. All of these are great places for you and your children to visit while in town.

Don’t forget that we also have pet friendly homes.
