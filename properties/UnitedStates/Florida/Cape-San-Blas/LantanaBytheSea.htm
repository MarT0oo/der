Lantana By the Sea, Cape San Blas, Florida Vacation Rental

 

Splendid gulf-front beach vacation rental with private pool & hot tub. Equipped with four-bedrooms, private pool & hot tub, and gulf views it's easy to see why! Located in the south section of Cape San Blas, Lantana boasts modern upscale furnishings & fixtures, gulf facing balconies including a screened in porch on the floor with the living space, and two king master suites. The two additional bedrooms have a queen bed and full over full + twin trundle bunk-bed, bringing the sleep count to 9 comfortably.

Other guest amenities include: cable TV, free wi-fi, gas/charcoal grill, fully stocked kitchen, outdoor hot/cold shower, easy beach access, and parking for up to 4 vehicles. An added bonus is the proximity to Scallop Republic, so you'll be able to hop on bikes and enjoy some live music while trying some local brews while watching the sunset.
