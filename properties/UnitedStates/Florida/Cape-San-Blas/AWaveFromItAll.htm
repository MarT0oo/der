AWave From It All, Cape San Blas, Florida Vacation Rental

 

Get “AWave From It All” at this newly constructed 6-bedroom, 6-bath home, nestled in a Gulf County premier gated community with resort amenities and boardwalk access to the beach.

With a striking coastal architectural design that enhances its gulf-front location, this 4-level home boasts multiple porches, picture windows and two towers with unobstructed views of the Gulf and the Bay to the east. Access every floor from a private elevator. Distressed wood-look and Italian porcelain tile floor is throughout the house.

At the heart of the home, located on the second floor, you are greeted by two oversized custom-made alder wood barn doors, an expansive great room with high ceilings, and a seamless flow between the living area, gulf front dining room, and gourmet kitchen.

Sprawl out on the large, sectional sofa in front of the 55-inch, flat-screen TV, gather around the counter height live-edge dining table with views of gulf water and swaying palm trees, or spend a lazy morning making pancakes in the chef’s kitchen. The kitchen comes complete with high-end stainless-steel appliances including but not limited to a 5-star range with 6-burners and 3 ovens, wine cooler, stand-alone ice maker, and dishwasher. Five counter stools at the artistically illuminated leathered granite island add seating for up to 12 guests.

On cool summer evenings, choose a bottle from the kitchen wine cooler and relax on the screened porch, steps from the dining room, to enjoy sweeping views of the surrounding beach and ocean. Moreover, on cool spring and fall days, the screened porch double gliding WinDoors transform the four rooms to create a splendid indoor/outdoor gathering space.

This floor also features one of the home’s six bedrooms, offering queen-sized sofa bed, a 40-inch TV, and access to a full bath in the hall with a step-in shower with custom-made cabinetry, sink, and lighting fixture.

An outdoor bridge connects this level to a carriage house, ideal for teens and older kids. This room has three extra-long twin beds, a 40-inch TV set up for gaming, and an en-suite bath with a step-in shower.

A level above, accessible either by private elevator or a finely crafted open staircase, is the bay facing east bedroom, featuring two twin beds, a wall-mounted TV, and an en-suite bath with a tub/shower condo. This floor also features two gulf-front guest master bedrooms, each with a king-sized bed, flat-screen TV, and en-suite bath with a step-in shower. A covered gulf-front porch can be accessed from either bedroom.

As you start your day, take advantage of the hall coffee bar on this floor -- and as you unwind in the evening, use the private laundry room to toss towels and swimming suits in the dryer so they’re toasty for your next day.

The fourth and top floor opens onto an airy cocktail room with scenic bay views and amazing sunrises for the early birds. This room features a sitting area with a stylish queen sleeper sofa, a wet bar, and a wall-paneled TV. From the cocktail room step onto a gulf-front sun deck to feel the sun’s rays or to toast the sunset with friends in the adjacent crows’ nest furnished with Adirondack-style furniture, including a table and chairs. The top floor also features a third master suite with a king-sized bed, vaulted ceilings and a private en-suite bath with a step-in shower and double vanities.

Although you’ll have plenty to explore and enjoy at the home, a boardwalk access to the beach is just outside your door. One of the 2 community pools is steps away across the street from the driveway. The other is an infinity pool located in the clubhouse area along with fitness center and access to the St Joseph bay for fishing and kayaking which is a short walk away. Tennis courts and easy access to an 8-mile walking and biking path, making this an ideal home for guests looking to explore the natural beauty and relaxed atmosphere of Cape San Blas.
