Dreams Do Come True, Cape San Blas, Florida Vacation Rental

 

Soaring three-story oasis on Cape San Blas that sleeps 10, with ocean views and a private pool. Follow the path down the beach lane until you arrive at a place where Dreams Do Come True— a three-bedroom home on a pristine stretch of sand that curves into the peaceful Gulf of Mexico. With sleeping space for up to 10 people, this bright, three-story oasis has enough square footage for your whole family to spread out, relax, and savor the serene, beachy vibe of “the Cape.”

The ground floor of Dreams Do Come True has all the amenities your crew needs for an unforgettable beach getaway. A large deck and a privacy fence surround a sparkling heated pool and spa. *Please note that there is an additional fee for heating the pool and spa. Soak up the sunshine on comfy lounge chairs. Grill juicy burgers or freshly-caught fish from the Gulf on the gas grill. A quaint welcoming foyer on the ground level features a convenient washer and dryer. There’s also a spacious outdoor shower for washing off salty skin and sandy toes before heading inside.

Dreams Do Come True has a unique layout. The living and dining spaces are located on the top floor while most of the bedrooms and sleeping spaces are located one story below. The light-filled living area is wide open and features tasteful, neutral furnishings with pops of beachy teal and salmon. The updated kitchen has crisp white cabinets and a charming subway tile backsplash. A peninsula bar has seating for three, while a driftwood-inspired dining table has bench-style seating.

Slide open the glass door and step out onto the spacious third-floor balcony. From up here above the swaying palm trees, you’ll have a view of the turquoise water of the Gulf of Mexico. It’s an ideal place to slip off your flip flops, get lost in a juicy novel, chat with friends, or dine al fresco under the stars. Relax out here in the mornings with a warm mug of fresh coffee and listen to the rustle of the palm fronds in the sea breeze and the call of coastal birds. In the evenings, pour a glass of wine or mix a mojito and kick back on the deck as the sun dips into the Gulf.

Dreams Do Come True sleeps 10 people across 4 Bedrooms and one flexible living/sleeping space.

At Dreams Do Come True, you’re never more than a few short steps away from feeling the warm, sugary sand beneath your flip flops. The pristine beaches of Cape San Blas are only a quick stroll from your front door— simply throw a beach towel over your shoulder and you’re good to go. Dip your toes in the clear, aquamarine water and watch for playful dolphins in the waves. Rent a kayak or paddleboard and get out into the surf. Build sandcastles, collect seashells, watch the pelicans, or simply sit back in your beach chair and relax. You’ll also enjoy the laid-back, old-Florida feel of locally-owned Cape San Blas restaurants and shops to explore.
