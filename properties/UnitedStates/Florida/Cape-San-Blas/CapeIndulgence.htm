Cape Indulgence, Cape San Blas, Florida Vacation Rental

 

Elegant Gulf-Front Beach House with Private Swimming Pool, Boardwalk and Sweeping Views. Located in the gated South Beach community of Cape San Blas, Cape Indulgence is an elegant gulf-front retreat with panoramic ocean views, hotel-like amenities, and a private boardwalk to the beach.

With spacious entertainment areas indoors and out, the four-level home opens with a covered al fresco dining and grilling area with bar, a small fire pit, and an outdoor lounge area on the ground floor. Just beneath the covered entry is a private swimming pool and spill-over spa. An outdoor shower and fish-cleaning station add convenience, while a doggie door and fenced-in pet area make it easy to bring the four-legged members of your family. We are happy welcome up to 2 dogs for an additional non-refundable pet fee of $200.

Just inside the formal entryway is a private elevator, which whisks you -- and your luggage -- to the second level, where an open-concept great room welcomes you with natural light and elegant decor, including a self-playing mini grand piano. Claim a leather recliner and unwind in front of the ultra-high definition 4K TV, get cozy in the seating area by the gas fireplace, or take in the sea air on the impressive wrap-around deck. You’ll find plenty of lounge furniture.

In the evening, gather around the 10-seat formal dining table or one of 4 seats at the breakfast bar for a large family dinner. When you’re in the mood to indulge your inner chef, a gourmet kitchen offers granite countertops, high-end appliances, two refrigerators and a wine cooler.
