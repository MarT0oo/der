Reunion Golf Palace, Reunion Resort, Orlando, Florida Vacation Rental

 

This outstanding custom built home has been a private residence within the Reunion Resort until this year. No expense has been spared in the design, decor and furnishings to complete this dream residence whilst still retaining that 'homely' feeling throughout its 6 bedroom 5 bathroom floor plan. With 5,400 ft² of air-conditioned living space, 8,000 ft² of covered area your personal space is guaranteed.

Amenities abound throughout the property and include one of the most spacious pool decks found within Reunion with a large built in Summer kitchen, a games room, dedicated tiered cinema room, office, golf views and access onto the large rear balcony via the outdoor spiral staircase making this the perfect home for gracious entertaining.

Located on one of the most mature sections of the exclusive 'Legends Corner' area of the world renowned Reunion Resort, overlooking the Jack Nicklaus PGA golf course is where you will find this beautiful home. The Spanish style covered entrance way leads you into the hallway of natural stone with its showcase stairway and chandelier. The large master bedroom suite is located to the right off the hallway, it includes his and hers walk in closets and a magnificent bathroom which comprises of twin marble vanities, a Jacuzzi sunken tub and oversized walk in shower with multiple heads and jets. On the other side of the hallway is a king-sized bedroom with wall mounted TV, a bathroom with walk in shower and fully equipped laundry room. The main living area on the ground floor includes a large formal dining area with adjacent granite wet bar perfect for serving those pre dinner cocktails. A feature half wall incorporating a dual sided fire place separates the formal dining from the family seating area with its comfortable sofas and chairs arranged for relaxed viewing of the large flat screen TV or simply enjoying the views through wall of glass that showcases the outdoor living space, golf course and conservation beyond. The dream kitchen is gourmet heaven and has custom made cabinets, granite work tops and island, a large gas range, stainless steel appliances and functional breakfast bar as well as a casual dining area also overlooking the outdoor area.

Upstairs there are four additional bedrooms, two king-sized bedrooms, one to the front of the home having a private balcony and en-suite bathroom with walk in shower. A third bedroom has two full size beds, an en-suite bathroom with walk in shower. The final bedroom is a queen size and offers access onto the rear balcony with its breathtaking views and shares the family bathroom incorporating a tub and shower with one of the king-sized bedrooms. Upstairs is also the location for the two tiered cinema room with theatre chairs, cinema screen, projector and surround sound perfect for a relaxing family evening watching the classics. To the rear is the games room where you can have hours of fun and entertainment using the pool table, poker table and built in wet bar with refrigeration and when the excitement gets too much walk out onto the balcony and watch the sun go down across the golf course.

The outdoor living area will guarantee you hours of pleasure with its heated pool and spa, built in Summer kitchen, covered dining area to enjoy your alfresco meals, spacious lounging areas to relax or top up your tan and views every golfer would envy, come live the dream!
