Gordon Majestic, Naples, Florida Vacation Rental


Gordon Majestic is the top of the line Luxury Homes in Naples Florida from an exclusive property manager in Naples.  This home has every amenity you would desire in a Villa, including spacious layout, waterfront, beach access, and heated pool.  There is nothing missing from this home, it’s made to Wow the moment you walk through the doors.

The house features a breath-taking breezeway as you make your way into the home.  You will find the floors are a mix of rich natural stonework and elegant tiles, within a romantic setting that is truly spectacular.  This truly stunning luxury home will wow you with every step you take. Customized with intricate details in every room.  The bedrooms feature an all en-suite bath resort style layout, with each bathroom being unique and designer decorated.  There are two master bedrooms, one King and one Queen, the detail that is placed into the sitting areas and the balconies overlooking the waterway will stun.  The owner has spared no expense, even putting in a romantic shower and bath in each room, making it a unique and inviting experience.  You will feel as if you are in a Majestic Hideaway.

The Kitchen is a lovely Gourmet kitchen with eat-in nook, easy to entertain within, masterfully ready for a novice or true chef.  Everything you can think of can be found within the kitchen area.  Aside the kitchen you will find a lovely dining area, for those nights when you want to dine together and enjoy the best of both worlds.  The house speaks volumes on what you can do while on a luxury vacation.  As well there are various sitting areas, a wine room, and lovely reading area for those who like to lay back with a glass of wine or sherry and just soak in the “get away,” experience.

Outside you will find a pool and spa, fully ready for use and sparkling with direct views of Champney Bay.  There is seating, dining set and BBQ all ready for use while you stay within this lovely home.  And for those who just can’t get enough of the beaches of Naples, all you do is walk directly across the street to be right on the beach!  What else can you ask for?
