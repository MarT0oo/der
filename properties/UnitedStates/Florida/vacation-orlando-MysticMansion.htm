Mystic Mansion, Reunion Resort, Orlando, Florida Vacation Rental

 

This 8 bedroom luxury home is located within the Reunion Resort, just a 12 minute journey down the road to the happiest place on Earth! Reunion Resort offers PGA golf courses right on property as well as a waterpark with a lazy river!

Sleeping up to 24 guests, this home provides bedrooms across both floors, privacy, and spacious living quarters including 2 children's themed bedrooms such as superheroes and princesses.

The upper floor loft area provides endless entertainment with a pool and foosball table as well as the balcony with an air hockey and ping pong table! Don't forget about the themed home theater where you and your family can watch your favorite movies at any time of the day on a large projection screen! You also have 2 children's playrooms as well as a hidden one and a home gym with a sauna and basketball court!

Right outside your patio doors, you can also enjoy a private swimming pool and spillover spa with a summer kitchen and grill for all your outdoor needs!
