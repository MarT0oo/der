Luxury Condo III, Marco Island, Florida Vacation Rental

 

Stand in the sunshine, listen to the waves crash and breathe the salty air—all just steps from your front door.

Grab your own slice of paradise at the beachfront Somerset Condominiums, located on the sunny south end of Marco Island! With million dollar views and plenty of that spectacular Florida sunshine, Somerset’s prime location and amenities allow plenty of time for relishing your personal piece of paradise.

Somerset reaches 10-storys into the blue Florida sky and contains 122 units. This stunning property is tastefully landscaped, complete with tropical gardens. Pathways wind their way across the gated property and are perfect for a jog or an evening stroll with your sweetie. Her multiple heated pools, private tennis courts and barbeque area are sure to keep you on your feet --when they aren’t in the sand.

The real star of Luxury Condo III is her incredible location directly on the powdery white sands of Marco Island’s pristine beach. Simply grab your complementary beach chairs, umbrella and fully stocked cooler and hop in the elevator. You can be sitting and sunning in front of the gulf in just minutes. She is also located within walking distance of many of Marco Island’s premier restaurants, shopping and entertainment. Walk to the famed ' Marco Walk Plaza ' which features Marco Movies, Beebe's Ice Cream, O'Donoghue's Irish Pub, Nacho Mama's Authentic Mexican, the Marco Prime Steakhouse, DaVinci Fine Italian, Marco Island Fish Company, and numerous Island Shoppes such as Chico's and Beach Daisy!!

You will not have to sacrifice any of Marco Island’s spectacular scenery when you return to the condominium. With sweeping views of The Gulf of Mexico, watch dolphins play in the waves and watch the colors change as the sunsets—all from your lanai.

This coastal condo was fully dressed anew in late 2014, a definition of modern elegance. Stone wood plank floors, wall mounted HDTVs with Apple TV, solid surfaces, and stainless steel appliances are just a few of the luxurious features of this exquisite beachfront condominium.

Luxury Condo III is a fantastic choice for those who are seeking a true beach getaway!
