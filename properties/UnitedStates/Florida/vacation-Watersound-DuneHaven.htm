Dune Haven, Watersound, Florida Vacation Rental

 

Get ready to watch dolphins while eating on the balcony. DUNE HAVEN IN THE CROSSINGS AT WATERSOUND BEACH is a GULF FRONT condo with SPECTACULAR unobstructed PANORAMIC COASTAL VIEWS. It is the perfect retreat for a BEACH VACATION GETAWAY located next to the beach in the Crossings complex at the private, gated community of WaterSound Beach on coastal Highway 30-A.

We are one of the closest condos to the beach... only 3 have a shorter walk! And, yes, the views you see in our pictures were taken from our condo.

Designed by professionals, Dune Haven’s 2168 square feet of living space features spectacular views of pristine white beaches and emerald waters. This unit has the view, space and modern amenities to make your vacation perfect.

The wall of windows in the great room provides this luxury residence with extraordinary natural light and it leads to a very private balcony overlooking the magnificent waters of the Gulf of Mexico and Camp Creek Lake. Hardwood floors are found throughout this exceptional property that offers the utmost comfort in a bright, airy and chic atmosphere.

Coastal living is defined by the fantastic view from the large living, dining, and kitchen areas on the second floor. There is plenty of seating around the table with additional seating at the breakfast bar. The dining area is open to the gourmet kitchen for the ultimate in entertaining and gathering among family and friends. Enjoy a glass of wine or a delicious meal utilizing a fully stocked kitchen including stainless steel appliances and granite counter tops. The upscale kitchen features new custom cabinets.

The second floor features two bedroom suites; one with a King bed, and ensuite bathroom, the other with a Queen bed and ensuite bathroom.

The spacious gulf front first floor Master suite features a King bed with a Tempur-Pedic style gel topped mattress, new furniture, two closets, spectacular marble bathroom with a large jetted whirlpool tub, walk-in marble shower and double vanity. The Master suite has its own private over-sized deck with a view of the beach, the water and the dunes. Very few first floor condos can make this claim. It is the perfect place to take a nap, read a book or relax to the sound of waves.

On the first floor, there is also a bunk room with a twin over twin bed for the kids, and a fully equipped laundry room.

Only a handful of units in the Crossings at WaterSound have their own private elevator inside of the unit and this is one of them. The elevator makes moving in and out a breeze. There is also an attached garage and your own private parking space.

For your swimming pleasure, three pools are available for your use. The Crossings swimming pool is located just a few yards from the door and is used exclusively by Crossings residents and guests. The pool area is surrounded by a large deck area, grassy yard, and beautiful outdoor fireplace. There are two commercial grills that you may use. You may also use the community pools and pool houses named Bridges and Dunesider. WaterSound Beach Club is private and not accessible to non-members.

WaterSound has more than one mile of private, white, sandy beaches for you to enjoy. Other amenities include Puttering Park (18 hole putt-putt course), Yacht pond, playground, walking paths and boardwalks throughout the community and access to Camp Creek Lake. A state of the art fitness center is available for a small fee. Beach chair and umbrella rentals are available.

The Hub 30A, only a 5 minute walk, is home to lots of eateries, shops and family entertainment.

The surrounding area is a haven for outdoor enthusiasts. Over 20,000 acres is state-owned and therefore protected from future development. It is home to one of the most beautiful beaches in the nation, South Walton Beach, and also has 17 fresh and salt-water lakes and the Choctawhatchee Bay.

The 30-A bike trail will take you from Santa Rosa Beach to Inlet Beach. Along the way you can enjoy the communities of Blue Mountain Beach, Grayton Beach, Watercolor, Seaside, Seagrove, Alys Beach, Seacrest, and Rosemary Beach. Deer Creek State Park is located next to WaterSound Beach and other state parks are just a short bike ride or drive away. The area has some of the best restaurants and most unique shops along the Scenic Emerald Coast of South Walton County.

Golfers can enjoy numerous courses in the area including Santa Rosa Country Club, Kelly Plantation, Regatta Bay, Emerald Bay and Indian Bayou.

If shopping interests you, WaterSound Beach is located about halfway between Destin and Panama City Beach. Destin features Destin Commons, Grand Boulevard and Silver Sands Factory Stores. Panama City Beach is home to Pier Park Mall with over 120 stores to choose from.
