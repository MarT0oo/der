Diamante, Kissimmee, Orlando, Florida Vacation Rental

 

Welcome to the Diamante, a home built and furnished for the traveller who demands the highest standards. This elegantly furnished home has six bedrooms, five and one-half bathrooms and numerous amenities including a private pool and spa. The four master suites with king size beds and private bathrooms allow several couples to have their privacy.

As you enter the home, you are presented with a beautiful conversational burgundy sofa and a square cocktail table in the cozy living room. A few more steps and you are at the beautiful dining room with its double pedestal table and six Marietta Black Pepper upholstered chairs. The foyer table with marble top provides just the right piece for the hallway going into the kitchen and family room.

As you enter the family room, you are overcome by the vaulted ceiling with its eighteen foot long ceiling to floor drapes custom made with beautiful butterfly fabric. The eight foot wide glass doors out to the pool deck are available from the family room, the kitchen eating area and the master bedroom. The cocktail table provides a place for drinks and all those miscellaneous things you accumulate. Two full size couches, covered with the comfortable microfiber cocoa fabric facing the large screen TV, invite you to relax on their reclining end seats. The fully stocked kitchen with an abundance of small appliances has everything you need to cook a many course dinner. These amenities provide you with the option of skipping the fuss and expense of going to restaurants while you relax and enjoy the pool and house.

You may want to go out the door to the lanai and spend some time soaking up the Florida sun. The covered ten foot pool deck has two ceiling speakers so you can enjoy music from the stereo on the family room entertainment center without disrupting those inside. You could step into the fifteen by thirty foot crystal clear water of the heated pool or really relax in the one hundred degree Jacuzzi or you can just lay back on one of our comfortable loungers. The pool and lanai are south facing which gives you sun all day long. Of course you can stay under the covered deck if you already had enough sun. The pool has an easily removable safety fence providing extra protection for young children.

Sleeping accommodations are exceptional. The wood panel king-size bed in the first floor bedroom is breathtaking as it faces the paneled armoire with TV behind its beautiful paneled doors. Immediate access to the pool and spa is available using the glass doors on the back wall. On the other side of the bed is the bathroom with double sinks, an over-sized bath tub and a separate shower stall. There are three bedrooms on the second floor with king-size beds. The first has the spectacular leather headboard bed with high quality bedding that makes sleeping a true pleasure. This bedroom also has a private bathroom, a TV, a ceiling fan and a clock radio. In addition, this room also has a full-size crib with beautiful Winnie the Pooh bedding.

The next bedroom has an iron king-size bed with high quality bedding, a private bathroom, a TV, a ceiling fan and a clock radio. The third bedroom on the second floor with a king-size bed also has the exquisite iron bed with the high quality bedding, a private bathroom, a TV, a ceiling fan and a clock radio. The children will want to spend a lot of time in the Cinderella and the Buzz Light-year bedrooms that share the SpongeBob bathroom. The four low post panel beds offer maximum comfort for busy children while providing them with an environment they love. The beds are big enough to accommodate two children up to the age of about ten making it very comfortable and cozy.

And for the person who must do some work, the loft has a beautiful desk and hutch combination with an extremely comfortable chair where any desk work can be done with the computer provided. Or you can use your laptop with the wireless Internet access.

For those families with small children, we provide the beautiful crib in the upstairs bedroom as well as a high-chair, a stroller, a dressing table and a bassinet. For your convenience, we also provide a clothes washer and dryer in the utility room.

When you approach the Emerald Island Resort, you see the beautiful entrance followed by the dramatic palm tree lined entrance with the beautiful foliage leading up to the security gates. Following that, you see the dolphin fountain and the very popular Clubhouse. The exquisite Emerald Island Resort Clubhouse of four thousand square feet has a heated pool and spa, a fitness room, a Sauna, a Tiki Bar, a gathering room, computers with internet access and a Video game room. On the side of the clubhouse are a playground and a volleyball court. Tennis courts and a basketball court are nearby on the Emerald Island Resort property.

All of these amenities are available for your use as a renter. All of this is located in the exclusive gated resort of Emerald Island which is only three miles from Disney World. Two stop signs and two traffic lights is all that separates you from the Disney property.

The owners of this splendid home took every opportunity to give you and your guests the ultimate vacation luxury in your temporary home away from home.
