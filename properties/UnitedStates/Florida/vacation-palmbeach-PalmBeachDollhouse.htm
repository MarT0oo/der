Palm Beach Dollhouse, West Palm Beach, Florida Vacation Rental

 

Palm Beach Dollhouse is a Key West style cottage located in the beautiful historic neighborhood of Flamingo Park in Downtown West Palm Beach. The property is lushly landscaped and tastefully decorated with a mix of Florida and the Caribbean, popularly known as “Floribbean” style. The cottage is walking distance to many area attractions including City Place which features shopping, dining, nightlife and entertainment in a beautiful outdoor setting. The cottage is also walking distance to the Convention Center, intra-coastal waterway, The Kravis Center and Norton Museum of Arts. A 5 minute drive will get you to Palm Beach Island and the amazing beaches, Worth Avenue and all the upscale splendor it offers. A 10-15 minute drive will also get you to Clematis Street, The Palm Beach Outlet Mall, The Palm Beach Zoo, The Palm Beach International Airport, Rapids Waterpark plus numerous other area attractions.

The Patio

The patio is spacious and furnished to fit everyone in your party. It is the perfect place to relax, sip lemonade and enjoy the warmth of the Florida sun. A large gas grill is also provided, it is a great place to enjoy a backyard bar-b-cue in our large fully fenced backyard. The spacious patio and lagoon pool make it “the spot” for casual laid back family vacations or for couples who desire spacious quarters.

Common Areas

The open floor plan, with those big French Doors is perfect for entertaining. The living area has a plush slipcover sofa bed that pulls out to a queen bed. There are additional comfortable chairs in front of a 50” flat screen Smart TV with full cable and HBO. Netflix, You Tube and Hulu can also be watched on our Smart TV’s. There's the fully equipped chef's kitchen, a beautifully designed space with gourmet appliances created for those who LIVE in the kitchen (when you're not dining at one of the amazing nearby restaurants). Off the kitchen is a lovely dining area that overlooks the open living area.

The Accommodations

The property is newly renovated in 2017 and features a brand new kitchen which includes quartz countertops and stainless steel appliances. The bathrooms are also newly renovated and gorgeous new bamboo floors throughout. Shiplap paneling accents the living and dining room. Whitewashed coastal accent walls adds a relaxed beachy feel to the home. It sleeps eight if the queen sofa bed is utilized. The master bedroom features a grand King size bed, flat screen tv and plenty of closet space. The master bathroom is en-suite and brand new. The second bedroom features twin beds, smart flat screen tv and spacious closets. The third bedroom features a queen size bed. A brand new second bathroom is fully remodeled and stunning. Free parking for two cars are included and plenty of free on-street parking is available throughout the neighborhood.

The Neighborhood

Flamingo Park is one of West Palm Beach’s most prestigious historic neighborhoods with homes dating back from the 1920’s to present. The houses are characterized as mixture of Spanish Mission, Mediterranean Revival, Frame Vernacular and mid-century Ranch architectural styles. The tree lined streets are picturesque and a pleasure to tour when you are not busy enjoying the numerous area attractions. If you are interested in purchasing your own vacation home in Flamingo Park or the surrounding areas we can direct you to a Realtor that specialize in helping people find second homes.
