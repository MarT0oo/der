Three Bedroom Garden Townhome, Key West, Florida Vacation Rental

 

Discover luxury in our three-bedroom garden view villa which includes a spacious guest room with two queen beds, along with an adjacent suite featuring a king master bedroom and a top floor queen bedroom. The villa has a shared main entrance. Upon entering the garden view villa, there is a foyer with separate doors to the guest room and suite, offering ample privacy between the shared villa. Please note that the villas do not have kitchens but a dry bar area and mini fridge.

The queen guest room includes two queen beds, a porch and garden views. Enjoy a porch entrance surrounded by Parrot Key's lush tropical foliage and towering palm trees. This 375 ft² guest room is just steps from 4 sparkling pools. This guest room features 2 plush queen beds, a mini fridge, coffee maker, 27" flat screen TV, a DVD player, walk-in shower, a hair dryer, and Wi-Fi access. Enjoy Key West style handmade wooden furniture and original artwork.

The connecting suite includes a master king room, second queen room, living room, and garden tree top balconies. The spacious living room features a 42" flat screen TV, a dry bar area, mini-fridge, coffee maker and toaster. Both the master king and queen bedrooms feature en-suite bathrooms, their own 27" flat screen TVs and DVD players, and Wi-Fi access. The suite is accessed by a flight of stairs with the master bedroom on the second floor and the second bedroom on the third floor. Our three-bedroom Key West villas offer both privacy and ample shared space for family or friends traveling together.
