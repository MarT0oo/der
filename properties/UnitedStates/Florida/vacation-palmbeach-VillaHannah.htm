Villa Hannah, West Palm Beach , Florida Vacation Rental

 

The newly constructed Vacation Home is located at the highest point in the peaceful Grandview Heights Historic District, which is within the West Palm Beach Arts & Entertainment District immediatly adjacent to downtown West Palm Beach . This luxuriously furnished, fully equipped vacation rental features 3 bedrooms, 2 1/2 baths, open kitchen, laundry room, private patio area and a charming front porch for relaxing.

This property is located just a few blocks from The Intracoastal Waterway and a short leisurely walk or bike ride to the heart of downtown West Palm Beach . Howard Park with jogging paths, 9 public tennis courts and the Armory Art Center is located just a few hundred feet from this home. The Villa Hannah top location is only a few minutes walk to the West Palm Beach Hilton, where guests can access the expansive pool, jaccuzzi and resort area, which features food and cocktail options. The West Palm Beach County Convention Center and CityPlace, Kravis Center for the Performing Arts, Norton Museum of Art and Grandview Public Market are all within easy walking distance.
