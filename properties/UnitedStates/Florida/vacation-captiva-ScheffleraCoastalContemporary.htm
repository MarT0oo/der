Schefflera Coastal Contemporary, Captiva Island, Florida Vacation Rental

 

This beautifully decorated beachside home offers 4 bedrooms and 4.5 bathrooms. With an open floor plan decorated with a Coastal Contemporary flair, this is a perfect private retreat ideal for a getaway with family and friends alike. Steps from the beach with its own private pool and several different screened lanais, allow for entertaining or enjoying a Captiva sunset. The living room has sliding glass doors which open to the main lanai, providing spectacular views of the Gulf of Mexico.

The master suite boasts a king bed, a large flat panel TV, its own private seating area, plus an en suite bathroom with a large walk-in shower, and separate bathtub. The second bedroom offers a king bed and an en suite bathroom with a garden view. The third bedroom has 2 queen beds with a large flat panel TV, views of the Gulf, and an en suite bathroom complete with a separate tub and a walk-in shower. The 4th bedroom has 2 queen beds, with a large flat panel TV, views of the Gulf, and an en suite bathroom.

Your guests will be sure to enjoy the many activities and amenities that South Seas Island Resort has to offer from this fantastic home that sleeps 10 guests.
