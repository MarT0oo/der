Welcome to our Resort Collection, villas that take their service to the next level by offering 5 Star Resort Amenities in a Luxury Vacation Home Rental.  This Villa is located in Manalapan Florida, home of the Rich in Famous in the Palm Beach area.  This location has long been a place for jet setters from all over the world to visit, to play golf, to shop, and dine in some of the most exclusive places in the World.

The villa includes 4 bedrooms, designer decorated, each with their own style and theme.  The Master has a King Sized bed, en-suite bathroom and a flat screen television for your enjoyment.  The guest suite upstairs has a Queen sized bedroom and bathroom, the second guest downstairs has a Queen Sized bed and bathroom, the 3rd guest downstairs also has a Queen sized bed and bathroom.  The best feature of this, you’ll get daily maid service with your rental, that way each room is made up daily and house picked up, for a Resort Villa service to remember.

The 5,700 sq. foot home also features a lovely gourmet kitchen, with 3 sinks and 3 center islands.  This is great for those who will be hiring a chef, making for easy preparation and cleanup.  There is a billiards room overlooking the waterway, Coffee wine and liquor bar that are fully stocked, wine cooler in master, a Nintendo WII with many different games, and satellite television for your viewing.

In addition to this wonderful list of Resort Villa Amenities, the house also features a beautiful pool area with a private sandy area for the guests.  On the Sandy area you’ll find a buggy, beach chairs, umbrella, cooler, toys to take to the Atlantic, private dock and use of jet ski, indoor steam room and Jacuzzi, outdoor gourmet grill and dining, outdoor table tennis, outdoor poolside projection television, 5 Star Quality Hotel Duvets and Linens, house stocked with staple foods, complementary fruit and pastries daily, mini-bar stocked with beverages and snacks, and concierge service for your stay.

The house is 1 ½ blocks from the Atlantic Beach and there are over 6 restaurants within a 3 block radius.
