Rendezvous by the Sea, Sanibel, Florida Vacation Rental

 

Available for January 2016! Discounted by 20%!! Rendezvous by the Sea is a beautiful Sanibel Island vacation home with nearly every amenity you could imagine available. Once you have stayed here, you will not want to rent another home. 28 night minimum stays are required, please inquire with our office regarding any flexibility in arrival/departure dates
