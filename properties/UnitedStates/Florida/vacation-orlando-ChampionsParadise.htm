Champions Paradise, Orlando, Florida Vacation Rental

 

This brand new 8 Bedroom home has a great location on The Retreat at Champions Gate with open space behind it. The community is one of the newest vacation developments in Central Orlando and will boast a host of amenities when completed in 2015, also within close proximity to Disney and all major attractions. Purpose built as a vacation home, the skillful designers have ensured that the floor plan utilizes every square foot to it's maximum potential. Large family groups or friends will have numerous areas allowing them to socialize or take some time out for themselves.

This home benefits from the artistic touch from one of the area's most renowned vacation home interior designers. Their efforts have created a fantastic transitional effect, that's not too traditional and not too modern, with the help form the home's beautiful mirrored furnishings.

The entire feel is light throughout with complimenting accents. Having a movie room and games room will ensure all ages are entertained and catered too.

Includes Unlimited Free Access To The New Oasis Water Park!

A fabulous vacation home with two king-size masters on the ground floor, one to the front with the bathroom having a walk in shower and twin vanities, this bathroom can also be accessed from the hallway. The bedroom at the rear has a sunken tub, walking shower and twin vanities with the toilet separate and also accessible as a pool bath. The hallway leads into the living area which is open plan and spacious incorporating a formal dining area for 10, large seating area, a fully equipped kitchen with stainless steel appliances and granite counter work surfaces which include a breakfast bar for casual dining all overlooking the outdoor living area which can be accessed from the patio doors. Off the hallway is a games room, the perfect space for children and adults alike to enjoy hours of fun with a pool table, air hockey, TV and PS4 games console.

Upstairs is a loft area which provides a second seating space with a large flat screen TV. The loft is surrounded by six more bedrooms, The first bedroom having a full size and twin bed, the second 2 queen size beds and the third a king-size all served by the large family bathroom which has twin vanities and a walk in shower. The fourth and fifth bedrooms, one a king-size and the other with a full size and a twin have a jack and jill style bathroom with walk in shower and twin vanities. The sixth bedroom is a king-size and has access to a bathroom with a shower/tub combination. There is a movie room complete with over-sized leather seating on tiered levels and a huge wall mounted screen and blu-ray.

Outside the extended pool deck provides a large covered dining area where you will have both dining and comfortable seating making it the perfect space for those al fresco meals and relaxing evenings. The upgraded heated pool and spa is surrounded by lounging space for those wanting to top up their tan.

The Retreat at Championsgate is the newest vacation resort in Central Orlando. This amazing resort has been purposed designed to create the ultimate in vacation lifestyle. When completed there will be an outstanding water park facility available to all guests and a brand new golf course. The location is supberb having the centre of Championsgate just minutes away offers a selection of stores, shops, takeouts and restaurants. The landmark of Championsgate is the Omni Hotel which is home to a host of oustanding restaurants and spa facilities.

Having easy acess to both the US27 and the I4 make this the perfect location for those guests visiting the local theme parks, attractions and nearby beaches on either coast.
