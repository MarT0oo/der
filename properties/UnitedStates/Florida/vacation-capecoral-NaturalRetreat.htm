Natural Retreat, Cape Coral, Florida Vacation Rental

 

Natural Retreat is a gorgeous 2 story house located on the Spreader Waterway opposite the Nature Preserve. Very peaceful and quiet location. It has western exposure so unforgettable sunsets are guaranteed. The house has 3 bedrooms and 4.5 Baths. The open kitchen with island, breakfast bar, granite counter tops and high end stainless steel appliances is very well equipped. The living-room has an open fireplace.

For your convenience you have Wi-Fi. The lanai has an outdoor dining area, pool furniture and a lounge set. Pool and spa are electric heated. Perfect for entertaining outside as it has a huge outdoor kitchen with BBQ grill and fridge. Entire property is fenced. Shopping and restaurants are only 10 min. away.

A wonderful idea for families looking for a laid back and relaxed setting with family and friends. At the house you can rent a boat for a day or the entire week to make the experience even that more memorable.
