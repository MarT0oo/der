3708 Island Paradise, Holmes Beach, Florida Vacation Rental

 

3708 Island Paradise is just that- the best view on Anna Maria Island. Panoramic views of the beautiful blue waters of the Gulf of Mexico and the white sand beaches. And don't forget the legendary sunsets. It simply does not get any better than this. It is like you are sitting on the deck of a cruise ship- just in more luxury! This premium condo has it all including tile floors, granite countertops, the finest cabinetry and appliances.

The powder sugar sand beaches of Anna Maria Island await. Far away yet close to plenty of the great island charm that the area has to offer.

The living area has a very comfortable sofa and 50" flat screen TV. Oh, and an incredible view of the Gulf of Mexico! You won't be able to surpass the view from your dining table. No better place to relax and read a book on vacation.

The Master bedroom has a King bed, personal TV and ceiling fan. Also has a very nice view of the beach and ocean as well. The second bedroom has (2) full size beds that can comfortably sleep 6. Has its own private TV and ceiling fan.
