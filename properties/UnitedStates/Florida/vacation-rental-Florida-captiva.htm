Captiva Beachfront Luxury Cottage, Captiva Island, Florida


Attached to one of the most stunning private beaches, this home is far from any public beach access. The backyard deck is just steps from the beach with an 8 person hot tub. The new lighted inviting Tiki - Hut includes a foot wash and cool shower for after a long day at the beach. Outside the Guest and Master bedrooms of the house are heated nature showers for your convenience. Enjoy the sights of elegant dolphins as well as other native wildlife. You can watch the beautiful sunset and stars from the wonderful swing on the beach.

 

Some Amenities Include:

 

Lighted Tiki hut on beach, hammocks, outdoor whirlpool spa hot tub, three outdoor showers (one at the Tiki hut), coal burning grill, gourmet kitchen with 2 dishwashers and 2 ovens, gas grill, washer/dryer, Cable TV in each room with HBO and DVD players (flat screen TV with DVD in master bedroom), VCR, stereo, separate hers and his bathrooms in Master suite, hers with a Jacuzzi tub and his with a large shower, another Jacuzzi tub in guest bath, central air conditioning, 2 screened porches, 1 open porch, 1 open deck.  You will find original artwork throughout the house.

 

Additionally, for a large gathering, there are a dozen folding chairs; dining table can expand to seat 14. Two port-a-cribs, one baby rocker, toddler rocking chair, booster chair and pack and play on site.

 

The house is over 5,000 sq. feet and on 1.5 Acres of Private Property.  There are 3 King Bedroom Suites, 1 Queen Bedroom, and sleeping loft with Daybed and trundle.  There are separate family rooms upstairs and downstairs. 

 

This is a perfect family retreat.
