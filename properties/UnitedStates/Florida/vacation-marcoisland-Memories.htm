Memories, Marco Island, Florida Vacation Rental

 

Memories are what you make when you go somewhere that hits the mark, making it just the right combination of mood and magic. You are immersed in a setting of upscale privilege, with spectacular Southwesterly views. Two canals with direct access to the bay come together just off the dock of this ""tip lot"". The dock includes a berth for your boat and an inviting sitting area over the water viewing two long, wide canals. There's also a fish-cleaning station to for preparation of your catch. With a southwest exposure, the sun favors the pool almost all day. The pool is screened, heated and has an adjacent hot spa for relaxation after a day of play and shopping. Room after room reveal accents and details to remind you that you are a special person in a special place.

The master bedroom has a decidedly presidential aura. Under a double tray ceiling with crown molding, a king bed invites our slumber. A area with fine period furnishings suggests a good place for a good read . Just beyond sliders to the pool deck is a casual breakfast area. A massive flat screen TV completes the bedtime experience. The en suite master bath is luxuriously fitted with glass block walls around the step in spa pool and walk-in spray-jet shower.

Upon entry to the home, you are greeted with elegance and style. Bahama ceiling fans slowly rotate to circulate the air. Period furniture blends into contemporary wicker seating under cover on the pool deck. Library shelves line the living room wall, while an elegant formal dining room awaits easy service from the adjoining kitchen, where sophisticated black appliances compliment easy-care granite countertops. Topping it off are tray ceilings and crown molding through-out, and expansive glass walls framed by designer fabric window treatments.

No skimpy office nook here, the home includes a full office room, with a regency-style desk, computer peripherals, a wi-fi router (serving the whole house) and a plush black leather couch.

Just off the open kitchen are a pair of semi-circular upholstered banquette benches around a circular table, featuring a view through a corner glass wall to the pool area. You'll enjoy family breakfast or lunch in this cozy corner. The family room accommodates the whole family in warm leather chesterfield chairs and sofa for watching the large flat screen wall TV. Two comfortable additional bedrooms, one with twin double beds and the other with a king, share a generously roomy bath.

The two car garage, accessed off a quiet cul-de-sac, holds an assortment of bicycles and pool toys for your use.
