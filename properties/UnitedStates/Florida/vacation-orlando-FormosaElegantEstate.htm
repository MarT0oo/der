Formosa Elegant Estate, Formosa Gardens, Orlando, Florida Vacation Rental

 

Formosa Elegant Estate has long been recognized as one of the most prestigious vacation communities in Central Florida. This fantastic home commands a large corner lot on Formosa Boulevard and Emperor Orchid Court. The elegance and style of the decor furnishing will not fail to satisfy your every need. The superb location of Formosa Gardens makes this the perfect base for all your requirements; being just two minutes from US192 with its huge selection of shops, restaurants, and take-outs.

This 6 bedroom (2 master suites), 4 bathroom, 2-story home has been professionally decorated and beautifully furnished throughout with a perfect blend of colors and fabrics. There is a queen-sized bedroom to the front of the home off the hallway that leads to the family seating area complete with comfortable sofas and large screen TV. On one side of the family room is the extremely large king-sized master suite and to the other side is a second queen-sized bedroom and bathroom that also serves as a pool bathroom for your convenience whilst outside. Also located on the ground floor is the laundry room and games room with pool table, air hockey, and large screen TV with games console. The spacious fully-equipped kitchen separates the informal and formal dining areas.

The stairway leads onto the gallery landing overlooks the family room and leading to two bedrooms each having two twin beds and a family bathroom. On the opposite side of the landing is the second master suite and bathroom having twin vanities, bath and walk in shower. The large pool deck incorporates a covered area ideal for Summer evening meals overlooking the pool and garden with its own citrus trees. Many amenities are included, TV/DVD in every bedroom, free international telephone calls, wireless internet access and a large games/entertaining room.
