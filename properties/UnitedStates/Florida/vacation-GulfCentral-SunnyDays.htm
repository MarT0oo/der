Sunny Days, Indian Rocks Beach, Florida Vacation Rental

 

This family friendly, multi-level house sits directly on the gulf and offers plenty of space to relax and get away, both indoors and out. Featuring 5 bedrooms, 4 full bathrooms, stunning gulf views, easy direct beach access, private pool and a spill-over spa.

This spacious vacation home features a very unique layout, catering to large families, multi-family gatherings, reunions, events and even small weddings. This is one of the most booked properties we currently offer.

Our beach house accommodates 14 guests, making it the perfect location for a large family vacation. Sunshine’s Hideaway is a warm and comfortable home away from home.

From the moment you arrive you will be mesmerized by the wonderful appointments, furnishings, amenities and sweeping views of the Gulf of Mexico. The home has 3500 square feet under a/c with more than 4000 square feet of living space including beachfront decks.

Our Sunshine’s Hideaway vacation home is composed of 3 units all in one home allowing families to be together but have their own privacy.

The ground level consists of the first unit (Unit A) which features a studio with a full size bed and a pull out sofa. It also features a kitchen and a dining area. Easy access to the pool as well.

The second level consists of the second unit (Unit B) and third unit (Unit C).

The second unit (Unit B) is located on the north side of the house. This unit features two bedrooms and one full size bathroom. The first bedroom has two full size beds and the second bedroom has one full size bed with a small tv for dvd movies omly.This unit accommodates 6 guests total in this unit. This unit also features a fully equipped kitchen, dining and living area, where you can enjoy beach views.

The third unit (Unit C) is located on the south side of the house and it has two levels. The second level has one bedroom with two full beds and one full bathroom and a second bedroom (master suite) with a queen size bed with a private bathroom on the third level. Savor the view of the dazzling water and powder white beaches from your master bedroom, the living and dining rooms. If you are looking for some peace and quiet, sit out on the small upstairs balcony and breathe in the gulf air. The living room, dining area and full kitchen are located on the second level. Access to this unit is available from the ground level and from the large balcony on the second level.

This beach house has everything needed to make your stay comfortable and entertaining!

Our Sunshine’s Hideaway beach house features two large balconies, one on the second level and a small one in the third level off of the master suite. Sit out on one of the large balconies overlooking the Gulf of Mexico and savor the breathtaking view as the waves slowly roll onto the shore.

All units are bright, spacious and have an open floor plan. You can enjoy the gulf views from the living, dining and kitchen areas of all three units. Another bonus is beach views from the master suite.

Have a family cookout on the private grill on the large patio. Walk down to the beach and soak up some Florida sunshine. There is no better place to spend your vacation than in a beach home in Indian Rocks Beach.

This deluxe Beachfront Home has a magnificent Pool. Thatched roof Tiki Hut with lights makes every meal feel like a party. Barbecue available for outdoor cooking.

Spacious decks with unimpeded views of the Gulf surround each unit, providing private outdoor eating areas with a choice of sun or shade. Plenty of indoor lounging areas designed for great TV viewing and entertaining. Everything you need in the way of bedding, linens and appliances.

Things to note: Only 3 parking spaces available. The spa is not a hot tub, its only a spill over. Events are allowed at this property, an event fee and restrictions may apply, please call our office for more information. Pool heat is an extra cost of $35 per day - up to 85 degrees
