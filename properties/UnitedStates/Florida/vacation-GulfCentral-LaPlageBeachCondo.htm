LaPlage Beach Condo, Holmes Beach, Florida Vacation Rental

 

LaPlage Beach Condo is Island luxury at its finest! An exquisite example of Mediterranean architecture nestled right on the sands of the Gulf of Mexico, this beachfront enclave will exceed all your expectations. Relax in the tropical splendor of this ultra-luxurious beachfront property on Anna Maria Island in a gated community that features expansive verandas for sunbathing, relaxing and enjoying the beautiful beaches and the crystal blue waters of the Gulf of Mexico. LaPlage Beach Condo is light and airy with extra high ceilings and large closets. It is one of the very few 4 bedroom Beachfront condo units available on the island.

Soak up the sunshine and take a swim to cool off in the large pool, enjoy a therapeutic soak in the jacuzzi or stroll on the white sugar-sand beach just a few steps away from the pool.

Polished granite counter tops, solid wood cabinetry and top of the range stainless steel appliances make the gourmet kitchen a great place to prepare anything from a light breakfast, to a family lunch or fully fledged dinner party. Laundry is no problem with your own separate large capacity washer/dryer.

Park in the cool private, covered garage, enjoy the large heated pool and spa that looks over the Gulf of Mexico, cook out with the Weber gas grill and picnic in the shade of the pergola beside the pool. This property provides the ultimate vacation destination.
