Encore Bliss, The Encore Club at Reunion, Orlando, Florida Vacation Rental

 

This 12 bedroom home is located at the Encore Resort, less than a 10 minute drive to Walt Disney World and their 4 incredible theme parks! Conveniently located for you and your loved ones to experience and enjoy, Walt Disney World's proximity from this vacation home is an added bonus while staying on the Encore Resort property! The Encore Resort also offers exclusive amenities right on property including a waterpark with thrilling drop and twisting slides, multiple restaurants and bars, and an amenity center with a fitness room and kid's area!

Sleeping up to 24 people, Encore Bliss has bedrooms across both floors including 3 children's themed bedrooms such as Galactic and Pirates as well as a home theater, themed games room with a foosball table, and upper floor loft area with a pool table, for all your entertainment needs, so the fun does not have to end at the theme parks!

You and your family also have the added luxury of your very own private swimming pool and spillover spa right outside your patio doors to enjoy Florida's sunshine and warm weather!
