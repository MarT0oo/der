Sola Mia, Panama City Beach, Panhandle, Florida Vacation Rental

 

Sola Mia Seven bedrooms, six baths, sleeps 16: three kings, three queens, two (Twin top and full lower) bunk beds, full sofa sleeper. Grandeur, spaciousness, and attention to detail combine to make this beach house the perfect vacation spot. Many special features including three master suites on top floor - each with private porch access, kid's bunk room , and a huge great room - perfect for large family gatherings or special get-togethers.

Every amenity imaginable to insure a 'grand' vacation for your entire family. Open porches allow a view of the Gulf only 25 yards away. Private community beach access. Parking for four cars. Located in a cul-de-sac, making it safe for kids to walk freely.

Barbecue grill and beach chairs are available for your use. There is a private community pool with restrooms.

Sola Mia is located 1 tier back from the gulf without a road or street in-between. This beautiful beach is rated one of the best in the United States.

It has white sand beaches and clear blue water. It is three minutes west of Santa Rosa Beach. The outlets in Sandestin are 15 minutes away. Located to the east you will find Grayton Beach three minutes away and Seaside six minutes away.

There are convenience stores located two blocks away, including bike, moped and kayak rental.A health food store, juice bar, and several new restaurants and shops are within walking distance.

New amenities include all new hardwoods,new great room furniture....Plus High Definition tv and wireless internet.
