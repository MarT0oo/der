High Design Villa, Lighthouse Point, Florida Vacation Rental


High Design Villa is a spectacular gated estate with 3 bedroom, a media room, large open space with high end fixtures and designed as a luxury vacation rental.  You will find everything you need in this house, from the lighting to the magnificent pool is of high standard.  Nothing can compare to this perfect house, it truly is a renters paradise.

The house features a Point Lot setting for complete privacy with a pool and summer kitchen that would make any novice chef envious.  It is graceful and refined and perfect for those outdoor family meals.  The interior is a designer inspired gem, with travertine flooring throughout the main area, the room flows from space to space.  Your eye wanders through the house as you come to the open gourmet kitchen, with hard woods and granite counter tops, equipped with all the Viking has to offer in Appliance from Range to Dishwasher and Frig.  The second you enter that kitchen you feel really impressed that the finishing touches match throughout.

The owner has made this into an oasis for just the type that want a totally private and totally luxurious property to call their own.  The master features a King Bed with lovely en-suite bathroom, the Guest Features a King Bed, also with its own bathroom, and the 3rd Guest Features a Twin.  This house is best for 4 adults with 2 kids with can be arranged to fit 6-8 guests if needed.  There is a media room as well, perfect for those who love to sit and relax with surround sound and large picture. Kids will get a thrill out of this room. 

The house owner has a boat that can be rented with Captain docked at residence, as well he owns a second cigarette boat that can be rented as well.  So ask if you want to enjoy the house to its fullest.
