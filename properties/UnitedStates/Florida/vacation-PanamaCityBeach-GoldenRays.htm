Golden Rays, Panama City Beach, Panhandle, Florida Vacation Rental

 

With oceanfront views from an expansive and private balcony, stunning living spaces and quiet, private bedrooms, this 4820 square foot home is the definition of luxury.

One of the first things you’ll notice is the wall of glass pane doors that go from the living room out onto the large balcony. You’ll love being able to plop down in on one of the lush living room sofas and stare out onto the crystal clear waters and white sandy beaches of the Gulf.

The living room is set up into two different sections, and has beautifully tiled floors, one large sofa, two leather recliners and two upscale wicker chairs, all positioned perfectly for a great view of the water, or the flat screen TV. The second section of the living room has a bright and comfortable wrap around sofa, two plush armchairs and gorgeous accents and furniture that make the room look like the cover of a magazine.

The color scheme and tasteful beach decor throughout the common spaces of this beach penthouse tie the entire home together in a way that is welcoming, inviting and really feels like “vacation.”

The expansive living room flows effortlessly into the chef inspired kitchen. This gorgeous kitchen has granite counter tops, custom cabinets, state of the art stainless steel appliances, and much more.

The over extended counter space doubles as a snack bar, and multiple padded bar stools make this a great hang out spot for guests to enjoy a drink, link their laptops up to the Internet and check emails, or mingle with other family members in the kitchen. Bonus features of this amazing kitchen include a coffeemaker, toaster, starter packs of dish soap and coffee, cookware, and all of the utensils your family will need to enjoy a delicious meal together.

A quick trip to the local grocery store is all you’ll need to stock this kitchen with your family’s favorite meals and snacks, and can save you crazy money compared to taking the whole gang out to eat for every single meal.

When it’s time to eat, a formal dining area provides a comfortable place to sit together as a family and enjoy a meal.

The view from the dining room table is unbeatable, as everyone will have the best seat at the table looking out onto the water. When dinner is finished, bring the whole family out onto the balcony of this stunning corner unit penthouse and take in the views. Multiple chairs and tables provide plenty of seating, both in the shade and in the sun, and make this a popular family hang out spot.

Enjoy a meal out on the balcony under the shade, or put on your swimsuit and soak up the sun on one of the many upscale patio loungers. This penthouse balcony is the perfect place for a family photo, with the gorgeous blue waters of the Gulf as the backdrop.

This waterfront penthouse sleeps 10 people comfortably, and the unheard of 4.5 bathrooms is set up perfectly for multiple families traveling together, families with a lot of kids, and even families coming with kids AND in laws!

One incredible feature of this home is that all four bedrooms have King size beds! All of the bedrooms have high quality mattresses and luxurious linens and TVs. Two of the master bedrooms have private access out onto the balcony. The upscale bedding in all of the rooms will have you asleep before your head hits the pillow, and the brilliant layout of the bedrooms gives guests peace and quiet when they need some down time.

For even more sleeping options, the sofa in the living room pulls out and converts into a Queen bed.

The four stunning bathrooms allows big groups to prepare for, or settle down from, a busy day without having to wait in line to shower or brush their teeth. Two of the bathrooms have gorgeous, glass walk in showers and separate garden tub, and two of the bathrooms have a shower/tub combo, upgraded with duel glass doors.

A half bathroom offers even more extra space, an amenity that large groups find very helpful.

This incredible penthouse is located on the popular Sterling Breeze resort, a place where families can take advantage of heated pools, spas, a fitness center, meeting space w/ movie theater, and much more. Guests can take advantage of discounts on local attractions like dolphin cruises, fishing trips and boat rentals.

There are countless sidewalk boutiques, local restaurants, major shopping centers, golf courses, bike paths, bars, theme parks, playgrounds and live entertainment venues just minutes from the resort, making this the best place in Panama City Beach to have your next family vacation.

This unbelievable penthouse is for families who work hard and play harder, who want to experience Panama City Beach like a true VIP, and who want to give their families the luxury of a waterfront penthouse, combined with the comforts and conveniences of home.
