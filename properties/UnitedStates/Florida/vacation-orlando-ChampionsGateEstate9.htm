Champions Gate Estate 9, Champions Gate, Orlando, Florida Vacation Rental

 

The Resort community of Champions Gate is a place to pine over. Here we have a house affectionately named “The Retreat,” with its 6 bedrooms and 6 bathrooms offering a 12-person capacity. You can fill this Orlando vacation rental full of couples, individuals, or a large family; two rooms feature dual twin beds while the others have queens and kings, all equipped with TVs.

The backyard area features a patio covered by glass, creating the illusion that you are outside without having to sacrifice control to nature. The main feature of this area, however, is the large private pool and spa. The pool can, without trouble fit all your guests, and the heated spa sits just a foot above it for easy transition. There are plenty of pool chairs and an outdoor kitchen, allowing for great outdoor barbeques.

Inside is an open living room and dining room, which will make you want to share dinners as a group. Weather it’s a breakfast buffet on the countertop, or a feast on the large wooden dining table, this is the environment to do it. Burn off the food in the blue-walled game room, which offers a billiards table, or go upstairs to the media loft to relax with a good movie.

You probably won't want the leave this Orlando vacation home, but if you do want to get out for a bit, it's easy. Champions Gate is right near the I-4 Highway, so you can get anywhere that comes to mind. The big parks, like Walt Disney World, are just a short drive, and there are plenty of beaches in the area to allow for oceanside fun.

Don’t forget this is a resort style community, meaning you have a great environment within Champions Gate. If you simply walk to the Oasis Clubhouse, you’ll become infatuated with all the amenities. The large community pool is a joy itself, with its lap pool and mini water park equipped with slides and water spouts. There’s cabanas to eat and drink under. This clubhouse offers a fitness center and large studio for the best of yoga or pilates. There’s also a movie theatre and golf course! Last but not least, guest especially love the clubhouse restaurant and bar, offering great food and drinks.
