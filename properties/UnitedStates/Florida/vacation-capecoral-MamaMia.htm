Mama Mia, Cape Coral, Florida Vacation Rental

 

Mama Mia is a large 2700 sq ft home will be furnished/decorated right now. The villa is located in the SW area of Cape Coral on a canal with western exposure. and has 4 bedrooms (2 kings, 1 queen and 2 twins) and 2.5 baths. Three bedrooms have TV. The large living room has a flat screen TV, stereo and DVD. You have Wi-Fi for your convenience. Gourmet kitchen with granite countertops is well equipped kitchen and has a breakfast bar. In a separate room you have a pool table. The pool is electric heated and has a comfortable patio area with BBQ grill. Convenient central location close to shops and restaurants. Gulf access.

Cape Coral Florida, known for its islands like Miami Beach or the Marco Island area, it offers a blend of residential living with nautical living, which has become a staple for the community. The housing boom saw a big influx of Europeans buying housing, as well as a market change, now giving Cape Coral access to some very posh villas not only as private residences but also for rentals. The market brought the gulf front mansions, the beach homes, and the Intercoastal Direct home offering those with yachts direct access to the gulf waterway.

Those who love fishing can enjoy year round fishing charters from Cape Coral, there is no lack of sportsmanship and adventure for those who love the water.
