Visiting Naples, Florida

Taking its name from the famous Naples, Italy, this famed Naples location has long been a favorite of the jet setters and the fisherman worldwide.  This location not only offers you the most amazing scenic beaches, large estates, and first class shopping/dining.  This location is home to some of the best money can buy, both in real estate, and in piece of mind.  Prior to this jewel being home to the rich and famous, it was just a simple and laid back city.  Vacationers would flock here for the amazing nautical escapes, from renting fishing boasts for short excursions to living the life on a yacht for a week while going up and down the coastline. 

In the past 10 years Naples has attracted high line investors into the real estate scene, adding a more flavorful mix of German, English, Irish, and Norwegian residents to the city.  There is no escaping the very international Airports and the signs you can find in various languages as you make your way around.  The vacation rentals market can often go hand in hand with these same investors, often buying real estate and then using it as a second home, they make a return on their investment while not utilizing the home.  Some investors have made quie a business out of the city, buying up various homes and creating a little nest of vacation homes that can be rented year long.

So what is there to do in Naples, Florida?  That is what you’re probably wondering, right?  Well, aside from:

               Bird watching in the Everglades

               Naples Zoo and Gardens

               Golfing

               Air Boat Tours

               Shopping in tin city

               ...and so much more.

For the adults, enjoy the dining at:

               Chop City Grille

               Cloyde’s Steak and Lobster House, or

               M Waterfront Grille

In Olde Naples you can also find plenty of dining, shopping, and great beaches.  There is just too much to do, don’t forget to bring your fun hat!!
