Grandview Heights Escape, West Palm Beach, Florida Vacation Rental

 

In one of the most charming sections of the Grandview Heights Historic District, this 1923 historically landmarked home has been recently restored, updated and luxuriously appointed.

This spacious home sleeps up to 6 guests. The main house features 2 bedrooms, 1 bathroom, a large family room, living room, dining room, den/office, a deluxe kitchen, a charming outside dining terrace, pool deck, lounge chairs and a heated private pool. In addition, there is a covered walkway to a detached king bedded master suite with its own full bathroom and lovely french doors opening towards the pool. The house also features a separate laundry room, wi-fi, HDTV, HBO and a high quality audio system.

The house is located just 10 minues walking distance to CityPlace, the rejuvenated area of downtown with its pedestrian zone, restaurants, shops, department stores, outdoor cafes and movie theaters. The Kravis Center for the Performing Arts and Norton Museum of Art are also within 10 minutes walking distance. The beaches are within 5 - 10 minutes driving distance. The famous island of Palm Beach with its popular points of interest (Worth Avenue, Flagler Museum, Breakers Resort) are also 5 - 10 minutes driving distance.

This home is 1300 square feet.
