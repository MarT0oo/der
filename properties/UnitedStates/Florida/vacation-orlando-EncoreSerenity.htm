Encore Serenity, Encore Resort at Reunion, Kissimmee, Orlando Vacation Rental

 

This 12 bedroom villa, located at the Encore Club, is less than a 10 minute drive to Walt Disney World and their 4 incredible theme parks where you and your family can create magical and unforgettable memories that last a lifetime! You and your loved ones can also enjoy exclusive amenities right on property including a Aquapark with thrilling water slides, an arcade, multiple bars and restaurants, and an amenity center with a fitness room!

Sleeping up to 24 people, Encore Serenity has bedrooms across both floors including 6 themed children's bedrooms!

The home theater is the perfect place for the whole family to watch a movie together or play friendly competitions of pool and foosball in the loft area! The outdoor deck is also a great place to soak up some Florida rays in your very own private swimming pool and spillover spa.
