Casa Maria, Miami Shores, Florida


Casa Maria is a villa aside the waters of the Bay, overlooking the city with amazing views. A 5 bedroom plus guest room, this house is large and spacious for those traveling with family. The house also features a beautiful wooden stairway to the bedrooms; a private elevator is also available. The rooms are lovely and decorated for families looking for a large vacation home with plenty of space. The master suite has a large King ensuite and the guest rooms, aside from the downstairs guest room, all have queen sized beds.

Your family will love the views from the water or fishing off the dock. Downstairs you'll find a wonderful large gourmet kitchen with beautiful unique barracuda blue granite. As well the wonderful upscale appliances and all the things you need to make a meal complete. Your family can enjoy cooking in or eating out. Just a short drive from the house you can find lots of great restaurants and things so do, including great places for children to adventure to. This is a wonderful area for those seeking a great time in Miami.

Outside you'll find a pool (heated), grill and outdoor dining facilities. The outdoors is what the house is all about, rent a boat for a day or just lounge around the pool. Your family will love this house.
