Villa Valentina, Miami, Florida Vacation Rental

 

Valentina is a spectacular 6 bedroom/6.5 bathroom, 6,500 ft² waterfront villa that boasts a commanding bayside view of Miami, which pours in from almost every vantage point of its large, panoramic windows and multi-level terraces. A stunning vanishing-edge pool subtly blends into the bayline horizon for days of outdoor relaxation under the warm Miami sun.

The clean contemporary lines of the San Marino Modern Estate’s exterior gives way to an interior that is a symphony of design: an atrium ceiling and open floor plan creates a sprawling living and dining area, complete with one-of-a-kind furniture pieces, exquisite modern art masterpieces, and ultra-modern gourmet kitchen.

Upstairs, 6 roomy, magnificent bedrooms pamper guests with an enriching balance of modern design and comfort, with ultra-contemporary en-suite bathrooms and amazing views of the bay.

Master suite has 1 king bed with en-suite bath; guest suite with 1 king bed and en-suite bath; guest suite with 1 queen bed and en-suite bath; 2 bedrooms with 1 double bed in each with en-suite baths; guest suite with 2 double beds and en-suite bath.

Amenities: Gated, waterfront, roof-top terrace, home theater, piano, bar, steam room, steam room in master bathroom, cascading Waterfall, Barbecue, and heated infinity pool
