The St Tropez is a trio of towers offering waterfront condominium residences nestled on the Intracoastal Waterway. The towers themselves are true works of Italian art reflecting a sleek and modern Italian design. The towers also have an aura of an old world Mediterranean Village which exemplifies the charm and charisma of the South of France. The street level of this Mediterranean marvel will soon become the new Town Centre of Sunny Isles City, featuring beautiful upscale dining and a shopping Mecca. This is an excellent feature for St Tropez residents and outside patrons. The town centre will be surrounded by pedestrian friendly manicured walkways and garden like pathways complete with bubbling fountains and lush landscaping; thus, becoming the new retail and commercial centre of the city.
