La Valentina, Miami, Florida Vacation Rental


Beautifully renovated in 2004, this 6,240 ft² Colonial style estate is located just 20 minutes from Miami Beach and situated on more than 1/2 acre of waterfront manicured grounds with amazing views of Miami from almost every room. Private gated entrance & paved courtyard. Inlaid marble, hardwood & granite floors in living areas. Full balcony overlooking pool and Bay stretches across 2nd story with access from 3 of 6 bedrooms. 100ft on Bay, 80ft dock, full shore power & electric lift for more toys.


Totally Renovated Home in Gated Waterfront Community. Elegant Living & Entertaining Areas. Beautiful Billiard Room w/Wet Bar & Media Center. Magnificent Views of the Bay Throughout the House.
