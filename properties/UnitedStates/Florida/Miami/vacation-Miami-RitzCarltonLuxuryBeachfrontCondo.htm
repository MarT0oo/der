Ritz Carlton Luxury Beachfront Condo, Bal Harbour, Florida Vacation Rental

 

The Ritz-Carlton Bal Harbour is a luxurious 5 star condominium / hotel resort in Bal Harbour.

The building has a contemporary design offering a variety of services that creates a resort-style appeal for its guests and permanent residents. Located right on the water, this beautiful condominium presents probably the best view you can get in all Miami Beach (Atlantic Ocean, Sunny Isles Beach Front, Biscayne Bay and Oleta River State Park).

Ritz-Carlton Bal Harbour also comes with outstanding amenities like private beach, superb shopping and dining areas nearby, concierge, security, a 10,000 sq ft SPA with 10 private treatment suites with ocean views, restaurant, swimming pool, ballroom and conference facility.

This two bedroom two and a half bathroom condominium 1 King and 2 queens) suite with balcony boasts ocean and bay views.

Other amenities include a private elevator, infinity tub, 2 terrace, fully equipped kithchen, Wi-Fi, room service, concierge servive, access to the pools and beach with beach chairs and towels.
