Sunset Luxury Estate, Miami, Florida Vacation Rental

 

Sunset Luxury Estate is a 7 bedroom, 5 full bathroom and 2 half bathroom villa with 6 Kings and 3 Single beds. The estate has a charming exterior with over 6,400 sq. ft. of living space, fresh interior remodel and sits on one of the most desirable pieces of property in all of Miami.

The 28,500 sq. ft. villa has 210 feet of water frontage on Biscayne Bay. Updates include iPhone controlled Sonos sound with 6 zones, Nest climate and LED lighting throughout as well as a brand-new Italian furniture package throughout. Second floor master contains an oversized shower and Jacuzzi tub overlooking the same pristine waters of Biscayne Bay.

Exterior highlights include an outdoor kitchen by the dock, large heated swimming pool and hot tub as well as plenty of covered areas for sitting and relaxing with the breathtaking sunset views of Downtown Miami and the Islands.
