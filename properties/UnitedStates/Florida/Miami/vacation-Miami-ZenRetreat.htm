Zen Retreat, Miami, Florida Vacation Rental

 

A lovely waterfront cottage on Biscayne with a very Zen Magical feel right as you enter the house. Villa Jasmine has a great quality most homes do not possess, the ability to make you feel at ease and tranquil. The house offers you a blend of natural woods and stones, great spacious layout, and 3 bedrooms and bathrooms.

The area is safe and fun to discover with family and friends. You are within a short distance of everything, from minutes’ drive to Miami Beach to a minutes’ drive to Bal Harbour Shopping and dining.

The bedrooms feature one King Sized Bedroom in the master en-suite, then Two Queen Bedrooms with their own bathroom, plus a daybed for additional children or friends who are joining you. We recommend the house for up to 8 guests.

Outside there is plenty of space, a great view of the canal and the lovely boats floating by, a wonderful pool with great sitting area and so much more. The house is truly built for a family experience. The kitchen is large with lots of space, there is a great dining area and updated media space.

Find your Zen in our retreat.
