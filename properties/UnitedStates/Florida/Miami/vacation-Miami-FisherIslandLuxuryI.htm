Fisher Island Luxury I, Fisher Island, Florida Vacation Rental

 

Enjoy all Miami has to offer while relaxing in an upscale villa located on luxurious Fisher Island, Florida! Just minutes away from Miami Beach, since development Fisher Island has been a favorite destination for the rich and famous. Come rub-elbows while vacationing in classy accommodations.

You get an 1,800-square-foot villa, fully stocked kitchen (food not included, plush king bed in master suite/2 queen beds in a separate bedroom, all with premium bedding, pull-out queen sofa bed in living room, roll-away bed available upon request, granite counter-tops throughout, private balcony, private dining area, private washer & dryer, flat screen LCD TVs with DVD players, complimentary Wi-Fi, complimentary Netflix, private golf cart included.

As a Fisher Island guest, you have the opportunity to access a multitude of luxurious island amenities for a daily membership fee of $100 per day, per couple. Children—anyone 24-years-old or younger—are included in this daily member fee. There is a maximum of 5 children allowed per fee.
