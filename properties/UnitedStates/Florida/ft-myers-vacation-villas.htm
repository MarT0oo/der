About Fort Myers, Florida

Fort Myers has long been a favorite of European Travelers, boasting a large population of Germans seeking a home away from home for their holidays.  The draw is not only the amazing beaches and nautical lifestyle, but the location allows those who love the beach and the water to enjoy both.  Most who rent a home in this location prefer to be on the water so they can have access to the Gulf for fishing or just yachting around.  It’s a wonderful way to spend a holiday.

Additionally from Fort Myers you are just an easy trek to various theme parks, including Disney, Busch Gardens, and various water parks.  This is great for people who want to enjoy the area with children and like different activities to keep everyone busy. 

Additionally you have some great dining and shopping on the Boardwalk of Fort Myers beach as well as the Barbara B. Mann Performing Arts Hall, Broadway Palm Dinner Theatre, Florida Repertory Theatre, Seminole Casino,Southwest Florida Symphony and various Golf Courses with over 50 in the Fort Myers area (Estero, Cape Coral, Lehigh Acres, Sanibel Island, Bonita Springs and Venice).

In Addition to being a wonderful place for families Downtown Fort Myers offers some great outlet shopping in the Tanger Outlet Center, Fleamaster's Fleamarket, Miromar Outlets, and the Edison Mall.  It’s truly a shoppers paradise and when you are finished shopping you can stop for Coffee in one of several coffee shops or have lunch in a local diner.  It really is an amazing place to visit and we’re always ready to welcome new travelers to one of the most affordable and best places for family travel.
