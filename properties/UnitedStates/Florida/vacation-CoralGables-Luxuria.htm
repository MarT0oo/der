Luxuria, Coral Gables, Florida Vacation Rental

 

Luxuria in Coral Gables takes you down a road of luxury, from the moment you walk in you'll see that you have arrived somewhere spectacular. Nothing has been left untouched in this remodel, from the top of the line kitchen and bathrooms to the state of the art music and video equipment and the Designer Decorated home with high end furnishings. The 4 bedroom home features all of the classy elements that any Miami Home should inspire, located in one of the most prestigious locations in Miami, private and secure for your use.

The upstairs spacious master suite offers a spacious terrace overlooking the Gables Waterway. The luxury bathroom features an incredible soaking tub with wonderful large layout, granite counter tops and upmarket towels and linens. One of our guest suites has two twins that are perfect for kids or two singles. The room has it's own bathroom with luxury shower and amazing layout. The suites are all inspired with that Miami Flair in mind, even our powder room features chandelier and luxury wallpaper with metallic elements that make the room come to life. That is really what this house is all about, Luxuria.

The downstairs area features voluminous living space with TV in every room, open luxury kitchen and fantastic dining area for 8-10 guests, spacious garden area with sparkling heated pool, romantic gazebo and beautiful pergola. Going outside you will feel like you're in a private luxury resort, with your own amazing outdoor space. Your loungers, your outdoor dining and seating areas are all placed perfectly for your relaxation.
