Contemporary Paradise, Reunion Resort, Orlando, Florida Vacation Rental

 

Located on Reunion Resort's Golden Bear Drive, this amazing multi-family home offers comfort, luxury and grandeur overlooking the 15th fairway of the Nicklaus PGA course.

Perfect for discerning guests that are looking for a special luxury retreat near Disney, this grand 3 floor residence has it all.

The owners have worked with one of Reunion's leading builders to create the ultimate vacation home that is packed full of fabulous and unique features to make your stay extra special.

Whether you are looking to relax by the pool, enjoy a movie in the 3 tiered theatre room or enjoy some quality family time in the games room, this home will become an unforgettable part of your dream vacation.

The grand entrance lobby to this magnificent luxury vacation home offers a beautiful welcome as guests arrive. The double height celling, and large panoramic glass views out onto the beautiful pool area bend the fabulous air conditioned living spaces with the adorable landscaped views across the nearby lake and golf course.

The spacious open layout living areas of the home provide the perfect opportunity to relax and enjoy some quality time with your fellow guests, family and friends. A large gourmet kitchen, formal dining and the home's casual dining area is also complemented by a fabulous lanai with covered dining area, summer kitchen, luxury pool, spa, and large lawned area.

After a day at the parks, the fun can continue, thanks to the professionally installed 3 tier home theatre, and spacious games area with wet bar and comfortable seating. Fitness fans also can keep to their training program thanks to the home's private Gym.

Located in the Reunion Resort, this beautiful home commands an enviable elevated position on the Jack Nicklaus golf course with spectacular views of the Reunion Grande Hotel in the distance. Reunion is probably the most prestigious vacation rental community in Central Florida having every amenity that you could want to make yours a memorable stay, from restaurants, bars, swimming pools, 3 PGA golf courses, spa and water park though membership is required for certain amenities. The resort is also ideally situated for all the major parks, Disney, Universal & SeaWorld with many local attractions situated on the US192 a ten minute drive away. Just 100 yards from the main entrance you will find a great selection of shops and stores that will meet your day to day needs and I4 is only two minutes away giving easy access to both the Gulf and Atlantic coast lines for great beaches. Travel time from local airports is 30 minutes for Orlando International and 50 minutes from Sanford International.
