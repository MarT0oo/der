Strawberry Estate, Marco Island, Florida Vacation Rental

 

Rebuilt by Thomas Martin Construction and professionally designed & dressed 2013, this model showhome could be found in Florida Design Magazine. Rich details and craftsmanship meet casual coastal styling in this marquee island villa. Value neverending, this waterfront gem rests on an island bay and is blessed with strong, continuous sunshine in the pool and lanai nearly all day long - even during the winter months!! Enjoy the spectacular wide water views while sunbathing in the privacy Strawberry Estate offers. Light up the large outdoor LCD and dine outdoors while taking in the stellar island sunsets. Afterward, grab the fishing gear we supply and walk down to our oversized dock for the night bite... Cast into the snook lights!!

Strawberry Estate of Marco Island rests in a perfect central location - walking distance to the popular shoppes, marina, restaurants of The Esplanade as well as the countless shoppes and grocers in The Marco Island Town Center Mall. Best of all she's easy striking distance to Tigertail Beach!!

Strawberry Estate is dressed with HDTV, Wi-Fi, full compliment of finer linens throughout, fully stocked gourmet kitchen, electrically heated (year round) pool, beach gear, and far more ... just pack your swimsuit!!
