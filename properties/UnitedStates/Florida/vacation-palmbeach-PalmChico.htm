Palm Chico private oasis is a two bedroom, one bath home with a hot-water outdoor shower. It has a private heated swimming pool and is five minutes to the ocean

Palm Beach County is truly an amazing place to venture to. Located just 1.5 hours from Miami, 1 Hour from Fort Lauderdale, and only a 2.5 Hour drive to Orlando makes it one of the more central locations that you can vacation in. Not only is Palm Beach a great place to visit but many of the truly Elite have called Palm Beach their home, from popular rock stars to business tycoons and fashion icons, Palm Beach is really the “place to be.”

The most amazing part about Palm Beach is that it has the unique advantage of having a very posh lifestyle setting. The shops on Clematis and the Luxury Car and Mansion Rentals abound in these locations. Why is that? Well, Palm Beach was indeed home to a Railroad Pioneer and tycoon of sorts, Henry Flagler, who made his home in Palm Beach. He was also instrumental in starting the very luxurious trends.

Aside from being a Millionaire’s paradise, Palm Beach also aspires to have some of the best beaches, boasting a “pet friendly beach in West Palm,” and having some of the whitest and softest sands on the Florida Eastern Coast. Maybe they cheat a little, but don’t we all just love that imported sand!

The homes in Palm Beach can only be compared to the Palaces of England. Some are quaint and Cottage like, while others are sprawling and large estates.

Why else would the Kennedys and the Trumps both call it home from time to time. In Palm Beach you’ll also find some of the best golf, such as Palm Beach Par 3, Palm Beach PGA Golf Course, and if you can get in or know someone who knows someone, you can always try Trumps Golf Course.

No matter what you like to do, shopping, dining, entertainment, you can find it here. Please visit the Palm Beach Chamber of Commerce for more information...
