Playa 3 Water View, Florida Keys, Marathon, Florida Vacation Rental

 

These charming Florida Keys beach houses offer two-level living ideal for up to six people. The well-appointed first floor features a fully equipped gourmet kitchen, separate dining area for six, half bath and living room with 42-inch wall mounted plasma TV, premium cable and wireless internet for a convenient stay in our Florida Keys accommodations. Whimsical painted furniture, locally inspired art, premium bed and bath linens, French doors on both levels lead to spacious porches with spectacular water views of the Gulf of Mexico for breathtaking Marathon Florida lodging. Laundry facilities and bedrooms are upstairs. In short, our beach houses are fully equipped and ready to make you feel at home in our luxury Florida Keys resort.

Enjoy the extra space of a roomy three-bedroom beach house near our spectacular swimming pools and just a short walk to the beach. This waterview beach house is accessed via a lushly landscaped lane and features water views from the 2nd floor balcony off the master bedroom. The master bedroom has a king sized bed and the two guest rooms offer one queen and two twin beds. This spacious, 2-story accommodation gives you 2.5 baths, a living room and a dining area with a fully equipped gourmet kitchen. The living room has a 42” flat screen TV with DVD/CD player. The king bedroom has a 27” inch TV and the queen bedroom has a 20" TV. The house also features a washer and dryer upstairs. Luxurious linens are provided, as is Wi-Fi wireless internet access. Please note: Property-wide smoke-free resort.
