Homestead Court, Reunion Resort, Orlando, Florida Vacation Rental

 

Luxury 4 Bedroom Villa with Screened Pool and Private Guest Suite

Pet Friendly Home year round for all durations. This beautiful custom built home is located in Homestead which is the centre of the resort and convenient for the Grand Hotel, Club House,Annika Golf Academy and several of the resort's communal pools.

The home has a beautiful position at the end of Watson Court next to a conservation area, the pool is positioned in the courtyard providing privacy and bordered by the conservation. The home is fully equipped to cater to your family and pets needs and comfortably furnished throughout.

You enter this home into a picturesque courtyard which has a heated pool and spa, outdoor dining and relaxing space. There is a guest suite with private entrance perfect for inlaws or older children, it has a queen size bed and ensuite bathroom with walk in shower. Walking past the pool you enter the main house which has formal seating and dining areas that open onto the rear patio providing a great spot for those looking for some quiet time or creating some great BBQ dinners on the built in Summer Kitchen. From the entrance hall you access the first kingsize master suite, the bathroom has a jacuzzi tub, walk in shower and twin vanities. The kitchen is fully equipped and has granite counter tops with a breakfast bar perfect for casual snacks and overlooks the family room with comfortable sofas, feature fireplace, wall mounted TV and games console. Off the family room is the third bedroom which has two sets of bunk beds providing four twin beds, the bathroom has a walk in shower and also provides access from the pool area.

Upstairs is the fourth bedroom which is a second kingsize master suite with the ensuite bathroom having a jacuzzi tub, walk in shower and twin vanities. The loft area provides a great games area having a pool table and foosball with access onto the rear balcony.

Located in the Reunion Resort, this beautiful home is situated within the Homestead division. Reunion is probably the most prestigious rental community in Central Florida having every amenity that you could want to make yours a memorable vacation from restaurants, bars, swimming pools, 3 PGA golf courses, spa and water park though membership is required for certain amenities. The resort is also ideally situated for all the major parks, Disney, Universal & Seaworld with many local attractions situated on the US192 a ten minute drive away. The main entrance is only two minutes from I4 giving easy access to both the Gulf and Atlantic coast lines for great beaches, just outside the entrance you will also find a selections of shops, stores and takeouts to meet most of your day to day needs.
