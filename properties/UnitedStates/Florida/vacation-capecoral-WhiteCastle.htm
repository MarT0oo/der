White Castle, Cape Coral, Florida Vacation Rental

 

This White Castle is a great place to escape into the quick boating access to the Gulf of Mexico. The house has over 3,000 SF. The outstanding location makes this property a true boater’s dream. Guests will immediately feel right at home. Situated at the cul-de-sac you not only get exclusivity and privacy, you know you are getting the best of the best. You are in the yacht club domain where you can find tennis courts at the Yacht Club and the lovely beach on the Caloosahatchee River.

The Castle Features soaring columns hints at the luxury of this vacation home an expect inside. The home offers four bedrooms, each with a TV and an adjoining bathroom, including two master suites that open to the patio. Bedroom 1 features a King size bed and a small writing desk. The en-suite bathroom consists of a large walk-in shower with five shower heads, a separate tub and a spacious sink area. The other master suite also has a King size bed and a walk-in shower. The third bedroom, offers another King size bed. The bathroom features a walk-in shower as well. Two Twin beds can be found in the fourth bedroom. Again, the bathroom was designed with a walk-in shower.

Guests will enjoy the spacious living room with its comfortable sofa and chairs. A competition-size pool table will allow for hours of fun, and if you bring your i-pod, you can hook it up to the stereo system. The open dining area and kitchen complete this area. Fully equipped, the kitchen includes a large refrigerator with ice maker, stove, microwave, juicer etc, also plenty of dishes and silverware. High speed internet access is available at no extra cost. Pool and spa are electric heated. The pool area is perfect to enjoy a sunny vacation day. To the left and right of the pool, two sundecks with lounge chairs invite guests to relax for hours. You can end the day with a great BBQ on the summer kitchen. There is even a TV on the patio. You have gulf access.
