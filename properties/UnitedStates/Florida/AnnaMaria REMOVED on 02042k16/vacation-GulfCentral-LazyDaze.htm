Lazy Daze, Anna Maria , Florida Vacation Rental

 

Lazy Daze may well be one of the most exquisite private homes available for rent on all. Enjoy the amazing amenities is 4+ bedroom/4 bathroom 5300 ft² custom home has all the fine touches and professional craftsmanship one would expect in a high-end custom home. There are hardwood floors, custom cabinets, granite, ornate crown moldings, wainscoting and the finest fixtures and furniture. Once you walk in this amazing home, you know you have arrived in a truly amazing vacation destination.

There is a full-sized heated pool and spa, boat dock, elevator, 3 levels of porches, a deluxe kitchen- and so much more. The architecture is splendid with unique touches rarely seen in a vacation rental home. Combined this with being only a few minutes’ walk to both the Bay and Gulf beaches, great shops, restaurants and Pine Avenue, this property will keep you coming back again year after year!

The 4 bedrooms all have their own private baths. There are 2 King beds, one queen bed, 2 twin beds. Sleeps 12 in beds comfortably.
The main living area in this home has beautiful vaulted ceilings and large windows looking out towards the 2nd level balcony and canal. There is a fireplace as well as a large flat screen TV.

There are beautiful hardwood floors, crown moldings, decorative window casings and tropical island style ceiling fans. Custom built-in cabinetry with glass shelves are on both sides of the room reveal the exceptional attention to detail taken to build this home. The living area flows from the high ceiling to a cozy, lower ceiling feel for the dining area. It has beautiful wood coffered ceilings and sits giving a wonderful view out the back French doors leading to the main level balcony.

The gourmet kitchen in this home is no less impressive. There are high-end stainless steel appliances and the dishwasher is at waist level for easy loading. The cabinets are custom built with glass windows and the ceiling adorned with tongue-and-groove wood panels and outlined heavy crown moldings.
There is a fine wine captain, beautiful decorative tiles and hardwood floors as well. You even have fine views from this part of the home- the window above the sink has a great view out to the water.

Fully furnished with all needed cookware and stemware.

There are 4+ bedrooms throughout the house.

The master bedroom is located on the 3rd level and includes French doors that go out to your private balcony with expanded views of the water. The bed is a brand new luxury king size. As with the rest of the house, it has fine custom touches including crown moldings, hardwood floors, fine furnishings and fixtures. Also includes a new flat screen TV with Bose wall speakers for surround sound. An over-sized walk in closet tops it off. It is a true master suite in that there are two sets of doors. The first set leads into a hallway with ornate shelves, then through another door into the main room- the ultimate in privacy.

Bedroom 2 is a master suite as well and has many of the same custom touches and king size bed. It is an octagon shaped room with high vaulted ceilings trimmed out with wood beams in a "spider web" effect. It is a real visionary delight! It too has hardwood floors and its own private bathroom. Deluxe furniture and fixtures offer a refined finishing touch.

Bedroom 3 also is a suite with its own private bathroom. A slightly smaller room, it contains a queen bed and there are custom built cabinets/shelves that adorn one entire wall.

Bedroom 4 also is a suite with its own private bathroom. This room is more children oriented with two twin beds and a bathroom full of colors. Also with hardwood floors, crown molding and fine furnishings.

Lazy Daze is truly special, offering many great options for guests. The pool is full sized and heated as is the spa. The dock is perfect to sit and relax on while getting some fishing in. Bring your boat and keep it at your fingertips for those fun boating days. You are less than 5 minutes to open water down the wide canal.

The pool has two over-sized covered porch areas. One is on the ground level by the pool and features plenty of seating. The second level porch is the length of the back side of the house and has an over-sized table, perfect for having that large gathering meal outdoors. The garage is for 3 vehicles. The beautiful tropical foliage throughout the property creates that special tropical island feel.
