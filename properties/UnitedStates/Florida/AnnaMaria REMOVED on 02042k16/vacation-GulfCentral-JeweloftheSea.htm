Jewel of the Sea, Holmes Beach, Florida Vacation Rental

 

Jewel of the sea is the pinnacle of Holmes Beach Coastal Living. This palatial 8 bedroom, 5.5 bathroom is something out of your dreams. A home that truly has it all with beautiful hardwood floors, crown moldings, fine fixtures, granite counter and so much more. And that is just the construction. This home real beauty is what it brings to you. The home is only a short 2-3 minute stroll over to the famous white sands of the Gulf of Mexico. You have a magnificent view of those pristine blue waters from the roof top deck. What an incredible place to enjoy a cocktail and sunset.

Then there are the amenities. A pool that is something from a exotic tropical island resort. It has a waterfall, hot tub, swim up Tiki hut and a bridge that spans over the pool and is taylor-made for photographs. Next to the pool is the covered lanai area with it's full wet bar, refrigerator and TV- it will be like hanging out at your own private cabana bar! And it does not end there. Just off the pool lanai is the indoor recreation room. Air Hockey, billiards and a ping pong table will be fun for the entire family. In addition, the house has a variety of pool and beach toys and beach accessories.

The sleeping arrangements include 3 king beds, 3 queen beds, 2 sets of bunk beds and a pull-out queen sleeper sofa.
