By The Sea, Anna Maria, Florida Vacation Rental

 

By The Sea is the epitome of Island luxury! Offering a rooftop deck with sweeping Bay AND Gulf water views, a private heated pool, hot tub, dock and so much more you simply won't want to leave! Offering a fantastic location, you are just a short 30 second stroll from the pristine Gulf beaches that Anna Maria Island is known for! . The home is not your average rental. It will feature detailed crown molding, granite, travertine and marble throughout, custom Anderson windows, custom hardwood flooring in much of the living areas, high-end stainless steel appliances, high-end furnishings and fixtures and so much more! The home will have a British West Indies Luxury theme with an elevator and all the amenities you would expect in a luxury home.

This direct Bay front home has something for everyone to enjoy in your group! Gather around in the backyard adorned with the private heated pool with a hot tub-all of this while overlooking the gorgeous views of the Bay. Take advantage of the summer kitchen on the porch, grill up the catch of the day on the BBQ grill, bring your boat for loads of fishing adventures or simply relax pool-side, soaking up the Florida sunshine. Spark up some friendly competition during your stay and utilize the pool and foosball tables available for your use! Also will feature X-Box gaming systems and old style stand-up arcade style video game with over 200 different games including Mrs Pac Man, Galaga and Donkey Kong. *There is a 6 seater custom GEM cart (golf cart) available as part of the rental for guest use- restrictions apply

Featuring 2 full gourmet kitchens, 7 bedrooms, 5 full bathrooms and 2 half bathrooms, this home has been designed with convenience in mind without compromising the luxurious details you would expect from a high-end custom home. Ideal for multiple families or large family get-togethers, you will find enough space for everyone to be able to enjoy spending quality time together without sacrificing your privacy. The sleeping arrangements include 6 King-sized beds, 2 sets of twin beds (2 bunk beds) and 1 Queen sleeper sofa in the office room. Sleeps 16 in beds, 18 total. All bedrooms will have their own flat screen TVs. Most mid-level bedrooms will have Bay water views and many of the upper level bedrooms will have views of Gulf water and/or Bay water.

This property participates in our Professional Linen Program. In our continuous effort to bring only the best quality experience to our guests, we are proud to offer a professional linen service at this property to better enhance your enjoyment while staying here. This luxury amenity provides you high quality linens that are in place and ready for your use upon your arrival. These high-quality linens are washed in a commercial setting using the latest and best technology.
