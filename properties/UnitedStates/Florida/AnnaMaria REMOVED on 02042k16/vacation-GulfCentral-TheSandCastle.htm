The Sand Castle, Anna Maria, Florida Vacation Rental

 

Look at this brand new direct Gulf front rental!! The Sand Castle provides everything you could possibly dream of.. This brand new 6 bedroom and 4.5 bathroom direct Gulf front luxury home lives up to its name. Indulge in unobstructed views of the crystal clear blue waters of the Gulf of Mexico from the numerous levels of this elevated property, walk over to the pristine white sandy beaches by crossing your private dune walkover, cool off in the resort style deluxe pool with spa, or simply enjoy the sights and sounds of the Island from one of the open-air decks overlooking the beaches.

This luxury home offers only the finest furnishings and fixtures including a professional chef's kitchen with a double oven, 6 burner gas range top and a built in 48" refrigerator and freezer. Equipped with 2 living areas on separate levels, this home is an ideal getaway for multiple families. Large enough for each member to enjoy their privacy yet remain under one roof! You will fall in love immediately with this home when you walk up to the main living level featuring an open floor plan facing the stunning beaches. This home ideally embodies comfort and convenience without compromising any of the luxury. The second living area located on the next level up features a kitchenette for your convenience, as well as more views of the beautiful beach.

This property participates in our Professional Linen Program. In our continuous effort to bring only the best quality experience to our guests, we are proud to offer a professional linen service at this property to better enhance your enjoyment while staying here. This luxury amenity provides you high quality linens that are in place and ready for your use upon your arrival. These high-quality linens are washed in a commercial setting using the latest and best technology.

The pool area, which will be on the Gulf water side of the home, will be a really fantastic gathering place for all members of your family and friends! Relax in the tropical atmosphere with the resort style private heated pool with spa and let all of your worries slip away. Enjoy a delicious meal outdoors with the convenient pool-side outdoor kitchen-this home truly offers it all! Sand Castle offers 2 Master suites with incredible Gulf front views. Both suites offer King-sized beds, Gulf facing balconies and spacious en-suite bathrooms. Bedrooms 3 and 4 also feature King-sized beds and private balconies and share a bathroom. Bedrooms 5 and 6 both offer bunk beds featuring a twin-over full combination and private balconies. Both of these bedrooms share a bathroom. Each bedroom has its own private balcony and brand new flat screen TV.

Explore all that Anna Maria Island has to offer without ever turning on your car! Simply take advantage of the free Island Trolley service that runs from the North end to the South end of the Island from early morning until 10pm at night. Both South and North traveling Trolley stops are located right in front of the home!
