Oyster Pearl, Anna Maria, Florida Vacation Rental

 

Oyster Pearl is a stunning brand new elevated and luxury Island home located on the prestigious Northern end of Anna Maria Island. This lovely home is located West of Gulf Drive and just 1 minute stroll down the street from a direct beach access to the Gulf of Mexico and beaches. So convenient luxury home is equipped with a private heated pool with spa, a covered pool-side lanai with outdoor seating area and a BBQ grill for your convenience! Offering the best of both worlds, you are just a simple walk away from the beach and have the pool in your private backyard!

The home has 6 rooms, sleeping 12 in beds. The sleeping arrangements include 5 King-sized beds with 3 of the rooms offering private en-suite bathrooms. The six room doubles as an extra living area by day and the trundle bed pulls out to set up 2 additional twin size sleeping beds at night. This room is perfect for the kids to hang out in and give some separation to the adults when using the main living area and can still comfortably sleep 2 additional people.

This versatile homes offers something for everyone-a private backyard ideal for spending quality time together by the pool, fantastic location to the beach and it is closely located to the popular Pine Avenue and all of its quaint Island shops and restaurants. Come relax and unwind and find out for yourself why Anna Maria Island is considered Florida's hidden gem!
