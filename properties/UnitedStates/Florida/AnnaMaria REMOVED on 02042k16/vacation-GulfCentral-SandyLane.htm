Sandy Lane, Holmes Beach, Anna Maria, Florida Vacation Rental

 

Sandy Lane is a beautiful beach house that is only 3 houses away from the stunning Gulf of Mexico. Offering a fantastic location, private heated pool, lots of amenities including a gourmet and fully stocked kitchen and much more!

The sleeping arrangements for Sandy Lane include 2 Master suites, one on each living level, with King-sized beds, en-suite bathrooms and private balconies offering views of the Gulf waters. The Queen-sized bedroom also has a private en-suite bathroom and the Twin bedroom is located closely to the guest bathroom that is accessible from the hallway.

This property participates in our Professional Linen Program. In our continuous effort to bring only the best quality experience to our guests, we are proud to offer a professional linen service at this property to better enhance your enjoyment while staying here. This luxury amenity provides you high quality linens that are in place and ready for your use upon your arrival. These high-quality linens are washed in a commercial setting using the latest and best technology.

Take the fuss and worry out of packing your private sheets and beach towels knowing that when you arrive to your dream vacation rental, in place are high quality and commercially cleaned luxury bedding, bath towels, wash clothes and beach towels ready for your stay. Eliminate the guessing game, free up luggage space and breathe easy knowing that everything is already taken care of for you. This same philosophy extends even to your departure- nothing to wash, dry, fold or having to fool with trying to squeeze into an already tightly packed suitcase! A fantastic "must have amenity" for your AMI vacation experience.

If you love to cook than you will fall in love with this kitchen! Equipped with mixers, blenders, an espresso maker, coffee maker, waffle iron, Bosch appliances, Elkay sinks and two dishwashers-offering everything you might need during your stay!

This versatile home offers the best of both worlds-spend your days lounging around the private pool or on the 7-mile stretch of white sand beaches. Located closely to the Manatee Avenue Bridge you are just a short drive from the mainland and Island amenities such as the Island's only full grocery store, local restaurants and shops.

Come and indulge in ultimate relaxation with a getaway to Anna Maria Island and experience for yourself why it is considered to be Florida's hidden gem.
