Casa Dorada, Anna Maria, Florida Vacation Rental

 

Casa Dorada offers many fantastic amenities inlcuding a tropical backyard, adorned with matured tropical landscaping, a large private heated pool with a spa, an outdoor bar and covered lanai! Tucked away on a quiet residential street but only a few blocks away from the gorgeous beaches of the Gulf of Mexico-you truly have it all with this spacious 5 bedroom and 3.5 bathroom private home.


It's great location puts you close to some of the best things that Anna Maria Island has to offer-just a short 4 minute stroll to the white sandy beaches, a short bike or Trolley ride to the popular Pine Avenue and all of its shops and restaurants.

The fully stocked kitchen features stainless steel appliances and granite countertops. Cook or grill up a delicious meal at home to enjoy outdoors by the covered lanai or explore one of the local eateries!

The sleeping arrangements include 3 King beds and 2 Queens-ideal for a group of 10, you will absolutely love the spacious bedrooms.This home participates in our Professional Linen Program. In Anna Maria Vacation's continuous effort to bring only the best quality experience to our guests, we are proud to offer a professional linen service at this property to better enhance your enjoyment while staying here. This luxury amenity provides you high quality linens that are in place and ready for your use upon your arrival. These high-quality linens are washed in a commercial setting using the latest and best technology.

Take the fuss and worry out of packing your private sheets and beach towels knowing that when you arrive to your dream vacation rental, in place are high quality and commercially cleaned luxury bedding, bath towels, wash cloths and beach towels ready for your stay. Eliminate the guessing game, free up luggage space and breathe easy knowing that everything is already taken care of for you. This same philosophy extends even to your departure- nothing to wash, dry, fold or having to fool with trying to squeeze into an already tightly packed suitcase!
