Golden Pineapple, Anna Maria, Florida Vacation Rental

 

Golden Pineapple is a brand new construction 5 bedroom plus a den and 4.5 bathroom rental home on the prestigious Northern end of Anna Maria Island. This wonderful luxury home has so many fantastic amenities for everyone in your group to enjoy! Less than a 5 minute walk away from the pristine Gulf beaches this home is situated on a canal and offers beautiful water views.

Make a splash in the private heated pool and relax in the spa-this tropical backyard offers all you would expect out of an island getaway! A covered patio with outdoor seating, gas BBQ grill, outdoor Foosball and Ping Pong tables- you will simply fall in love and won't want to leave!!

Enjoy the multiple decks, all brand new furnishings and stainless steel appliances, two living areas-one on each living level, a fully stocked gourmet kitchen and so much more. This home is professionally decorated inside and out and offers multiple large flat screen TV's and only the best amenities.

Take the fuss and worry out of packing your private sheets and beach towels knowing that when you arrive to your dream vacation rental, in place are high quality and commercially cleaned luxury bedding, bath towels, wash clothes and beach towels ready for your stay. Eliminate the guessing game, free up luggage space and breathe easy knowing that everything is already taken care of for you. This same philosophy extends even to your departure- nothing to wash, dry, fold or having to fool with trying to squeeze into an already tightly packed suitcase! A fantastic "must have amenity" for your vacation experience.

If you are looking for a spacious luxury home, then look no further! This home is ideal for large groups and allows for everyone to be under the same roof yet still have their privacy!
