Villa Earnest, Anna Maria , Florida Vacation Rental

 

Villa Earnest is a gorgeous, brand new elevated Island house that is tucked away on a quiet residential street and only a short one minute stroll to the Gulf beaches. This 6 bedroom and 6.5 bathroom luxury home is equipped with a private heated pool, spa, BBQ grill and multiple balconies and a rooftop deck! Professionally decorated, this home has a beautifully sleek look and feel with some fine European touches.

Look at this fully stocked gourmet kitchen, equipped with brand new stainless steel appliances and features granite countertops- what a cook's delight. Highlighting the open floor plan, the kitchen overlooks the dining area as well as the living space and the back porch area.

Villa Earnest features a flat screen TV in every bedroom, multiple balconies and much more! Every bedroom has a private or en-suite bathroom. Enjoy the private backyard with its tropical heated pool, spa and BBQ grill or simply walk down to the Gulf beaches to enjoy the volleyball nets, children's playground, on-site concession and more! This fantastic home offers it all-great location and amenities!

** Private homes with pools will not be heated unless requested by guest. Please contact AMV for details. Pool heaters in our private homes are locked for your safety and controls must not be tampered with as they are monitored by the pool company. 80° is the highest heat temperature setting. Pool temperatures will probably stay around 75° during the winter months. Please note if cold days occur, water temperatures will drop and may stay cooler longer. Pool heaters will not operate and will automatically shut down if outside temperatures get too cold. While we take every precaution to ensure that pools are heated as specified, weather conditions may affect the temperature of the pool. Nothing can be done about this issue. Outdoor spas are not hot tubs. The temperature of a spa is typically set at the same temperature as the pool. Guests should not expect hot tub like temperatures for pool spas.

In the event of pool equipment malfunction during your stay, AMV will expedite repairs as quickly as possible. It may be necessary to close swimming pools for maintenance purposes. No refund or rent adjustment will be made for circumstances beyond our control, malfunctions or loss of use of equipment or amenities.
