Blue Bird Villa, Holmes Beach, Florida Vacation Rental

 

Blue Bird Villa is a fanciful and lovely accommodation offering 4 bedrooms and 3.5 bathrooms for your next holiday stay. Walk to beaches and enjoy the local culture and nightlife, truly a great space. The house is one of the newest partner collections through dreamexoticrentals.com and makes for a great escape. You will have a professional manager on call and a lovely set of area information upon arrival.

It has everything you want in an tropical island vacation rental property- lush landscaping, flat screen HD TV's throughout, hardwood floors, fun island-style furniture and fixtures and so much more.

The home features multiple balconies, including an over-sized one off the front on the 3rd level that is a simply fantastic place to go get lost from it all! The views of the island are so relaxing and comfortable- and don't forget about the soft island breezes you can catch. A perfect place to sit and enjoy a book for the afternoon.

Rather be in the sunshine? No problem. The tropical pool screams fun! With southern facing exposure, this area is bathed in sunshine much of the day. There is no better place to cool off after a long day at the beach. The covered lanai just off the pool offers plenty of shade if that is what is desired. Of course you can enjoy shade in the pool up under the umbrella in the shallow end as well!

4 bedrooms with 2 being master with en-suite baths, both with King beds. Full size bed and two twin beds are featured in the other two bedrooms.
