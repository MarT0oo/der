White Sands, Anna Maria, Florida Vacation Rental

 

White Sands is a truly breathtaking direct beachfront, one of a Kind, Holiday Rental in Anna Maria Florida. It’s beyond what you would expect, truly a wonderful escape. Take your shoes off and just put your toes through the sands, really spectacular. Never will you feel like escaping more than you do when it’s a sun filled, sunshine engaging, beachfront rental.

There are two porches facing the water in which to sit and enjoy the views. Your private beach is only a few steps from the back of the property. There is a very unique enclosed, private porch off the back that has louvered shutters as to continuously allow the cool ocean breezes to flow through. The water is calm, perfect for swimming and fishing. The garage is oversized, plenty of room for vehicles. There are bikes, beach chairs, towels and toys provided. Rocking chairs on both balconies and a porch swing facing the water on the ground level.

The home has a very cozy living room that looks off the back through floor to ceiling windows. The kitchen has high quality tiles, fixtures and appliances and is fully stocked with all cookware and dinnerware. There are two bedrooms on the main level, one with a queen bed and one with (2) bunk beds sets. The laundry/beach room is over-sized with a full size shower and direct access from the porch so as to allow for showing after coming right up from the beach. There is also a foot and full body shower outside as well.

The second level has the Master Suite with King bed and full views of the Skyway bridge. The is a common area with couch that is perfect for relaxing with a book. The 4th room is a bonus captains loft area with low ceilings and 1 day bed- a perfect area for kids to sleep and hang out away by themselves. There is a new flat screen TV in all 4 of these rooms as well as the 2nd level common area. The house is full of beach atmosphere- this is truly Anna Maria Island luxury at its finest.

Enjoy the Resort Amenities with a twist.
