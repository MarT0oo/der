Villa Playa, Holmes Beach, Florida Vacation Rental

 

Villa Playa is a luxury home in Holmes Beach near Anna Maria Island, with lovely views, amazing pool and luxury amenities. This luxury 4 bedroom, 4 bathroom are simply out-of-this-world! The floors are done in travertine, custom hard wood, premium fixtures and furniture you would find at a Ritz-Carlton- it just does not get any better than this on Holmes Beach.

Villa Playa has a wonderful exterior offering the traveler more than just a little comfort a heated pool with hot tub and covered pool lanai for relaxing with your favorite cocktail. Tropical landscaping surrounds the entire back area for privacy. There are water views from the main living area, sweeping views from the Master bedroom and multiple balconies, including the roof top terrace.

Bedrooms one, two and three all feature a King bed. Bedroom four features two twin beds. All rooms contain beautiful bead-board wainscoting, custom hardwood or travertine marble floors, flat screen HD TV's and wonderfully tasteful decorations. Soft inviting colors, fine linens and just the right finishing touches makes Villa Playa the luxury vacation destination you have been looking for on Holmes Beach Florida.
