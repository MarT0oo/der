Villa Olivia
6 Bedrooms | 6 Baths | Game Room | Pool | Gym
Delray Beach, Florida
From $14,000 per Week

Directly on the Ocean, this spectacular oceanfront estate on Delray's famed Atlantic Beach offers expansive ocean frontage yet retaining an intimate atmosphere. The Villa is ideally suited for those who appreciate the best in life and has been professionally designed by a renowned British interior designer to provide particular warmth exuding a timeless elegance throughout.

The house opens up as you enter in a beautiful foyer, with light filtering in from the many windows throughout the house.  The house is set like a Hacienda, with the pool in the middle, making it that much more interesting to walk from room to room enjoying the pool and ocean views. 

The house has a divine library that is great for those who like to sit and read or just sit and have morning coffee.  Reminiscent of what you would find in an old European home, this classic touch ads class and sophistication to this ultra luxurious beach house. 

The house also boasts an amazing Game/Gym room with Billiards table, Full Gym, and enough space for everyone to enjoy their time there.  In addition there is a Theater room for those who love to sit and watch a wonderful movie, in here you’ll feel like the Theater has come to you, with a full cinematic experience at your fingertips. 

For those who love to cook, there is a large Gourmet kitchen with butler's pantry, Informal dining area, adjacent family room with fireplace. The formal living room offers vaulted cypress ceiling opening directly to the ocean, a formal dining room with barrel vaulted ceiling, bar and a wonderful wine room.

The First floor ocean side master has a queen sized bedroom, en-suite luxurious bath and a private ocean front loggia.  There are five additional luxuriously designed bedrooms, including staff quarters & utility room.
