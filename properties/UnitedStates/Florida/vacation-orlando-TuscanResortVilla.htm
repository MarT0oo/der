Tuscan Resort Villa, Reunion Resort, Orlando, Florida Vacation Rental

 

This outstanding home has been a private residence on Muirfield Loop within the Reunion Resort and so boasts numerous upgrades throughout the home. The main house has five bedrooms; three on the ground floor and two upstairs. The separate guest house across the courtyard from the main house is perfect for in laws, older teenagers or staff. Having been a model home for one of Reunions preferred builders the home was decorated using bespoke paint techniques and furnished to the highest standard.

The upstairs has a large game room which will provide hours of entertainment for the children and adults alike with the advantage of a rear balcony overlooking the pool deck and the Jack Nicklaus golf course, one of three PGA courses throughout the resort.

The outside facade of this custom home is so appealing. The wrought iron gates lead into the courtyard where you can also access the private casita (guest house) with it's king-size bed and ensuite bathroom which has a tub shower combination. In the main house you enter the circular hallway with it's large chandelier into the open plan living area which incorporates a fully fitted kitchen with large granite island overlooking the casual dining area and bright spacious great room with feature stone fireplace all overlooked from the gallery landing above. Across the hallway and separated by an purpose built wine store and servery is the formal dining with it's custom decor and furnishing a perfect location for a dinner party or celebration. Sliding glass doors provide access and a view of the lovely pool deck and golf course beyond. There are three bedrooms on the ground floor, one king-size, one queen size with a shared bathroom. The large king-size master suite is located at the rear of the home with a seating area, fully fitted closet. Additionally, the beautiful bathroom having an oval tub, walk in shower, and twin vanities.

Upstairs are two further bedrooms, one with two twin beds and the other the second king-size master suite, both have ensuite bathrooms. Along the gallery landing at the rear of the home is the games room with pool table, comfortable seating, large wall mounted TV, and built in wet bar with refrigerator. Access onto the rear balcony will provide fantastic views of the pool area, golf course and resort making this a great space to relax at the end of the day.

The outdoor living area is mature with the heated pool and spa, covered dining area, ample lounging space and built in Summer Kitchen overlooking the golf course.

Located in the Reunion Resort, this beautiful home commands an enviable elevated position on Muirfield Loop and backs onto the Jack Nicklaus golf course with spectacular views of the Reunion Grande Hotel in the distance. Reunion is probably the most prestigious vacation rental community in Central Florida having every amenity that you could want to make yours a memorable stay, from restaurants, bars, swimming pools, 3 PGA golf courses, spa and water park though membership is required for certain amenities.

The resort is also ideally situated for all the major parks, Disney, Universal & SeaWorld with many local attractions situated on the US192 a ten minute drive away. Just 100 yards from the main entrance you will find a great selection of shops and stores that will meet your day to day needs and I4 is only two minutes away giving easy access to both the Gulf and Atlantic coast lines for great beaches. Travel time from local airports is 30 minutes for Orlando International and 50 minutes from Sanford International.
