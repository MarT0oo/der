Crystal Beach Diamond, Destin, Florida Vacation Rental

 

Crystal Beach Diamond will make all of your beach vacation dreams come true. From the moment you arrive at our spacious beach home you will be greeted with panoramic views of the Gulf of Mexico and the beautiful white sugar sand beaches that the Emerald Coast has become known for.

Crystal Beach Diamond boasts two full levels including two fully appointed kitchens, two living areas, 6 bedrooms, 4 baths and enough space for your large group to enjoy the spectacular view from either of the two expansive decks.

It is also partly handicap accessible and there are NO steps at the beach access.

Second floor

Enjoy morning breakfast from the top deck while watching the waves crash on the beach or have a magnificent sunset dinner overlooking the waters of the Gulf of Mexico. The upper deck has seating for 24 at two large patio dining tables or if lounging in the sun is your thing, there are 6 adjustable loungers on the upper deck for sunbathing or afternoon reading.

Inside you will be greeted by vaulted ceilings in every room starting with the upper common area which includes the Kitchen, Dining Area & Family Room.

You have seating for 10 at the large dining table and seating for another 9 at the kitchen island. The kitchen is fully stocked with all the amenities that you need to entertain the whole family, including stove, oven, microwave, coffee maker, toaster, dishwasher, large 4 door fridge, wet bar and large pantry to house all your food and snacks for the whole stay.

The family room area has comfortable seating to watch a movie or catch your favorite sports team on the 55" flat screen tv on the expanded cable package also free Wi-Fi!

The Second floor of Crystal Beach Diamond offers TWO Private King Master Suites both w/ private en suite baths, King Master Suite #2 has the added convenience of a walk-in shower.

Also, on the second floor is a Double Queen Suite w/ attached Jack & Jill bath that is adjoined by the Double Bunk room which sleeps 4 in the twin/twin & twin/twin bunks. There is also a convertible sofa in the Second floor family room. All bedrooms have large walk-in closets and the Second floor also has its own wash/dryer closet & large hall closet.

First floor

The lower deck includes a martini pool, perfect for an afternoon cocktail while the kids cool off in the pool after a long day in the sand & sun or for a late night relaxing dip in the jetted pool.

There is seating around the martini pool area and seating for 6 at the large patio table outside the gated martini pool area. For your convenience there is a rinse-off shower for when you return from the beach and there is also a gas grill on the first floor deck.The lower deck has direct access through your private gated walkway to the Scenic 98 activity path which leads directly to the beach access one short block east at the Shirah Street Beach Access.

Inside the first floor is a large common area that includes the second fully stocked kitchen, living area and dining area.

The dining area has seating for up to 8 people at the beautiful glass and stone dining table and seating for another 6 at the breakfast bar. The first floor includes a king bedroom w/ attached shared bathroom the first floor also features a double bunk room that sleeps 5 in the twin/twin & twin/full configured bunks. Both bedrooms offer large closets, the first floor has its own washer/dryer room, large pantry and direct access to the martini pool area through French doors.

All guests have access to 1 guest garage and there is extra parking for up to 4 cars on the driveway. The first floor can be rented alone during winter months!

Shopping, restaurants, grocery, outlet malls, Destin Harbor, Henderson State Park are all in close proximity and in some cases within walking distance.
