Palm Tree Villa, Fort Lauderdale, Florida Vacation Rental


A simply charming and upscale luxury beach house with 6 bedrooms and 5 bathrooms sleeping upwards of 12 guests or more.  The house sits just across from the ocean with these great views and amazing ambiance.  Much like a cottage style the house is set back and has a very personal pool and garden.  The house features large windows to let in light as well as wonderful new designer furnishings throughout. 

There is the main house and then the guest garden cottage, adjacent to the home.  The main home a 4 bedroom/3 bath is a corner lot with an awe-inspiring view of the ocean. The Kitchen has granite counter tops and stainless steel appliances. It houses the main entertainment with a wet bar and plenty of room for sporting yourself and your guests. The cottage house is an adorable home as 2 bedroom 2 bath. This home is perfect for your in-laws, or another family.

It’s called the Palm Tree Villa for the Palm Lined street and home, which are really lovely at night as a backdrop to the ocean.  It’s just a great place to escape with family and friends.
