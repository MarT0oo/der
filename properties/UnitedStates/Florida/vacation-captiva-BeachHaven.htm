Beach Haven, Captiva Island, Florida Vacation Rental

 

The warmth of hardwood floors welcomes you throughout this home. A large comfortable seating arrangement consists of a leather sofa and 2 chairs facing the flat panel television. Located off the living room is a wet bar with additional table and seating for four. Sliding doors open completely to screened in lanai with views of the Gulf of Mexico and overlooking the swimming pool.

This kitchen comes fully equipped with all the bells and whistles. A large open kitchen featuring granite countertop; stainless steel appliances, dishwasher and breakfast bar with seating for 4.

Dining Room, A gracious dining table with seating for 8+ along with scenic views of the Gulf of Mexico.

Bedroom One, An en suite Master bedroom surrounded with tranquility throughout; flat panel television that articulates whether you are in the sitting area or lounging in bed. Relax in the soaking tub or walk in shower.

Bedroom Two, A second master bedroom with your own private porch overlooking the Gulf of Mexico. Adjoining private bathroom with a large walk-in shower.

Bedroom Three, Hallway bath with walk in shower.

Bedroom Four, This nautical themed room is ready for sleepy pirates in their bunk beds. Hallway bath with walk in shower. Also has door for entrance to the guest apartment.

Bedroom Five, The Nautical theme continues in this child's room and the twin bed (with pull out trundle) shaped like a boat. Hallway bath with walk in shower is steps away.

Bedroom Six: Guest Apartment, A separate Nanny's quarters is filled with light; features include a comfortable living room with flat screen television, full sized kitchen, dining area. The bedroom is located off the living room and adjacent to the large bathroom with bath tub and shower.
