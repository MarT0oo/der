Seahawk, Captiva Island, Florida Vacation Rental

 

Upon entering the home you are welcomed by a large wrap around deck. Tropical themes and colors are featured everywhere in this home. The living room has a relaxing sectional sofa, large flat panel Television. This home has a sound system that can be for inside and outside the home. There is a patio located just off the living room that overlooks the swimming pool and the bay.

This kitchen is all you need for your vacation. Breakfast bar seating, a flat panel TV and an island prep area.

Dining Room, The tropical theme continues with seating for 8.

Bedroom One: Downstairs, Master bedroom has windows overlooking the patio and pool; a large walk in closet, private bath with walk in shower and double vanity.

Bedroom Two: Downstairs, Hallway bath with shower/tub combo

Bedroom Four: Upstairs, Children's room in sherbet colors has 2 twin beds. Hallway bath with shower/tub combo.

Loft Area Upstairs, Spacious loft can be used to write out post cards or quiet reading area; sofa also can convert to sleeper sofa.

An outdoor sundeck is located off the upstairs twin bedroom. This private retreat is surrounded by views of Bryant Bayou and glimpses of the Gulf of Mexico, making it a perfect spot for sun bathing or cocktails at sunset.
