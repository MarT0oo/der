Gourmet kitchen featuring granite countertops and stainless steel appliances, fully stocked. Large island featuring smooth, flat range top stove. Sunny breakfast nook with seating for seven, and additional seating for four at the bartop. Opens to dining room and patio, with great views of the bay

Dining Room, Spacious glass top table, with comfortable seating for 10. Easy access to kitchen, wet bar and living. Features panoramic views of the bay.

Bedroom One, Located upstairs, the master bedroom is spacious and airy, with vaulted ceiling and fantastic views of the bay.
