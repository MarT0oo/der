Heartfelt Escape, West Palm Beach, Florida Vacation Rental

 

Built and designed by owner 10 years ago as a mediterranean revival villa and located in Historic Flamingo Park, this spacious 3 bedroom/2 bathroom home features antiques and eclectic flare throughout. Here are a few of the specials features: Antique crystal & brass doorknobs from original home; handmade tiles in master shower to depict a grotto, with antique claw foot style bathtub for soaking under a skylight to the stars; historic wood floors in dining room and library from a barn in North Carolina and the library fireplace mantel was acquired from a 1920 Maurice Fatio estate in Palm Beach and restored by owner. This property has a beautiful library offering an array of genres allowing the guest to relax and read in a cozy environment.

Amenities include a custom built, spacious jacuzzi/pool, a fully equipped gourmet kitchen w/ coffee maker, microwave, blender, spices, cookware, etc; large screen TV in living area with HBO hundreds of channels, separate laundry room, pull out sofa-bed in library, TV and DVD player in master bedroom, high speed internet, outdoor grill with outdoor seating. A Pack & Play and high chair are available. This property does not allow pets.

Heartfelt Escape is located in the Flamingo Park Historic District within the West Palm Beach Arts & Entertainment District adjacent to downtown. The Kravis Center for the Performing Arts, Norton Museum of Art and the rejuvenuated downtown area of CityPlace, with its pedestrian zone of restaurants, shops and boutiques are all within 15 minutes walking distance. The nearest beaches are a 5 minute drive.

The Palm Beach International Airport in West Palm Beach is a 10 minute drive away.
