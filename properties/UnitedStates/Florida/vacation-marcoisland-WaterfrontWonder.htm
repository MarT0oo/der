Waterfront Wonder, Marco Island, Florida Vacation Rental

 

A stunning example of a luxury island dream home, summertime never ends at Waterfront Wonder. Professionally finished in 2018, this 5 bedroom 3 bathroom coastal villa promises to amaze you with its dazzling waterfront views, resort style pool and patio, and chic living spaces. Home to a soothing color scheme of driftwood browns, seaglass blues, brushed gold tones, and high-end Pottery Barn furniture, guests may find themselves choosing between strolling down to the new public beach access next to Resident’s Beach or enjoying the sunshine on the south facing lanai or waterfront dock from her chaise sun lounge chairs.

The open plan chef’s kitchen is outfitted with every amenity one would expect in a commercial style kitchen. Equipped with top of the line Themidor appliances, two refrigerators, two dishwashers, two indoor wine coolers, a gas range and more, this space is truly a place for those who love to cook. Watch the kids as they splash in the pool as you prepare a meal and serve a meal to those waiting in the elegantly appointed dining room located just steps away from the massive granite island. Set the mood with the 7 zone Sonos® HiFi wireless audio with expansive music library.

After your meal, gather in the great room and enjoy your favorite TV show, movie or sports game on the 70 inch 4K/UHD equipped with AppleTV, Xfinity cable with premium high definition channels and DVD/BD/UHD player.

When it is time to retire after a long day at the beach or pool, guests will appreciate the fine linens and appointments in all five of her bedrooms. Wake up to waterfront views in the Master Suite complete with its very own private spa-like en suite bathroom. The kids are sure to love the junior suite, home to two built-in bunk beds that overlook the water.

For those who like to entertain, Waterfront Wonder boasts a game room with an 8 foot Brunswick® pool table and an upstairs living space with a wet bar. Need to take care of business while you’re on vacation? Close the door to the private office where getting work done is a breeze.

The spacious laundry room boasts two washers and dryers for busy beach life!

Transition outside to immerse yourself in the finest example of modern Florida living. With highly desirable southern exposure, Waterfront Wonder is a premier luxury home for those who enjoy having fun in the sun. Whip up a meal in the outdoor kitchen overlooking the glistening blue pool. Soak up the sunshine from a chaise lounge on the sun deck. Or head out to the waterfront dock to watch as birds soar and dolphins play.

Waterfront Wonder truly has it all. This blue-chip waterfront Marco Island estate is in a class of her own, and satisfies the most discriminating of tastes ...
