This beautiful purpose built vacation home has the enviable position within the the brand new resort of Champions Retreat at Champions Gate. The living area is open plan and extremely spacious incorporating the main family living area with oversized sofas for viewing of the large wall mounted TV, a dining nook, fully equipped kitchen with granite counter tops, stainless stell appliances and breakfast bar perfect for casual meals. Adjacent to the kitchen is the formal dining room where celebration diners can be presented and enjoyed. The games room will provide hours of fun and entertainment having comfortable seating, a pool table and air-hockey. Off the family room is bathroom with walk in shower and the added convenience of also opening onto the pool deck.

Upstairs the loft area has been furnished with a large sectional sofa, the perfect space for a family movie Night, relaxation or entertainment with the large wall mounted TV, DVD and games console. Off this area is a queen size bedroom with ensuite bathroom, the second queen size bedroom, two bedrooms each having two twin beds and the family bathroom with twin vanities and a tub/shower combination. To the front of the home is the extremely spacious second king-size master suite, the bathroom having twin vanities, oval feature tub and walk in shower. All bedrooms have flat screen TV's and the home has Wi-Fi
