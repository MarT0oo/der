Beachcomber A101, Sanibel, Florida Vacation Rental

 

Very spacious three bedroom, two bathroom gulf front condo located on the East-End of Sanibel.

Furnished with a casually elegant look plus updated kitchen and bathrooms with granite countertops. Spectacular direct gulf views from the living room, master bedroom, and expansive lanai.

There is a King bed in the master bedroom, two twin beds in the guest bedroom as well as a queen sleeper sofa in the second guest bedroom. Please note that the second guest bedroom has pocket doors leading to the kitchen and dining areas and no closet. The sleeper sofa can be left closed with the room used as a den or office as well. There are three TV's, two DVD players, and high speed wireless internet access. Living room furniture has recently been updated as well as all new lanai/porch furniture. Beachcomber is an upscale property that is elevator serviced and offers covered parking along with a community pool (heated) and outdoor shower. Monthly minimum stays required.
