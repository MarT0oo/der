Castle Retreat, Reunion Resort, Orlando, Florida Vacation Rental

 

A beautiful custom built 3 bedroom home commanding an enviable position overlooking one of the three PGA golf courses that the Reunion Resort offers, Arnold Palmer, Tom Watson and Jack Nicklaus. The large pool and deck area is probably one of the best within the resort and is surrounded by a spacious terrace which makes this area perfect for family fun, entertaining or watching the golf.

The home as been presented with a contemporary style of furnishings making it extremely homely and perfect for families with children of all ages.

You enter the home into the open plan living area with it's vaulted ceilings which has two sections. First, a comfortable sectional sofa from which to watch the large wall mounted TV, a second a conversation area of comfortable arm chairs overlooking the pool deck. The fully equipped kitchen with it's granite counter tops, stainless steel appliances adjoins the dining area which also has access onto the pool deck. The ground floor also offer the laundry room and king-size master suite with a large ensuite having a deep tub, walk in shower and twin vanities and overlooks the pool area and golf course. The games room offers both foosball and a pool table to offer hours or fun.

Upstairs is a gallery landing with work station area overlooking the family room and two further bedrooms I which one containing the second king size and the other having two twin beds. Both bedrooms are served by the family bathroom that has a tub/shower combination. There is a large bonus room with sectional sofa, flat screen TV, games console and the addition of a queen size sleeper sofa to accommodate any extra guests.

Definitely the jewel in the crown of this home is the outdoor living area. It is one of the largest in the resort that it has to offer. it is complete with a large heated pool and spa, covered dining area, ample lounging space, quiet spots, lawn/shrubbery areas, and golf course views, truly amazing!

Located in the Reunion Resort, this beautiful home backs onto the golf course. Reunion is probably the most prestigious rental community in Central Florida having every amenity that you could want to make yours a memorable vacation from restaurants, bars, swimming pools, 3 PGA golf courses, spa and water park though membership is required for certain amenities. The resort is also ideally situated for all the major parks, Disney, Universal & Seaworld with many local attractions situated on the US192 a ten minute drive away. The main entrance is only two minutes from I4 giving easy access to both the Gulf and Atlantic coast lines for great beaches, Just outside the entrance you will also find a selections of shops, stores, and takeouts to meet most of your day to day needs.
