Reunion Jewel, Reunion Resort, Orlando, Florida


This beautiful home was a model home for one of Reunion's leading builders and therefore has numerous upgrades throughout the home which include panelling, built-ins, mouldings and decorative features. The current owners have just completely furnished throughout to the highest standard in a traditional style to ensure your every comfort and requirements are met.

The outdoor living area is amongst the largest in Reunion with a spacious travetine deck surrounding the oversized heated pool. There is a covered dining area alongside the built in Summer Kitchen and the whole area is covered by a screen which is a rare advantage in this resort and prevents insects and wildlife from pestering your realaxation,

Located on the prestigious Westside of the resort this home commands a quiet private position backing onto conservation. As you enter the main house there are two bedrooms off the main hallway, one having a queen size bed, bathroom with a walk in shower that can also be accessed from the hallway for convenience. The other is the first kingsize suite having a tub/shower combination and twin vanities in the ensuite bathroom. The hallway leads into the open plan living area which has a large kitchen incorporating stainless steel appliances, large range, granite counter tops and long breakfast bar. The seating area has comfortable leather sofas arranged to enjoy the large wall mounted TV housed in a bespoke built-in with surround sound, there is also a formal dining area with eight seater dining table. The whole of this area overlooks and has access onto the screened outdoor living area. At the rear of living area is the master kingsize suite with decorative panelling, access onto the pool deck and a huge ensuite bathroom having a jacuzzi tub, walk in shower and twin vanities joining a large fitted closet.

To the front of the home and completely independant is the queen size guest suite with it's ensuite bathroom having a tub/shower combination, this is the perfect accommodation for inlaws, older children or staff. Off the the hallway in the main house is the laundry room and the brand new movie theatre/games room with large wall mounted screen, projector, raised recliner seating, pool table and hightop to provide hours of fun and entertainment for the whole family. A stairway leading to a quite reading room with comfortable seating and two further further bedrooms, the first another kingsize suite and the other having two queen size beds, both bathrooms offer tub/shower combinations and twin vanities with the bedrooms being seperated by a huge walk through closet. All bedrooms have wall mounted TV and Wi-Fi throughout the home. The outdoor living area is the jewel in the crown of this fantastic home, a huge space paved in travetine and incorporating a large heated pool, ample lounging space, a covered dining area for those long al fresco meals, a built in Summer kitchen and large wall mounted TV for your realxation and enjoyment. This whole area is covered by a pool screen to keep the bugs at bay and backs onto conservation for your privacy.
