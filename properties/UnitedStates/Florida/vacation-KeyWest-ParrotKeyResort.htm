Keys Oceanfront Townhome, Key West, Florida Vacation Rental

 

Discover our waterfront double queen guest room with two queen beds, a white sand terrace, and water view porch. Step off your porch and feel the sand between your toes on Resort tranquil sunning terrace. Hedged by wild mangroves, along the Gulf of Mexico, the 375 ft² waterfront guest room is also just steps away from 4 sparkling pools. The room features 2 plush queen beds, a walk-in shower, mini-fridge, coffee maker, 27" flat screen TV, DVD player, a hair dryer, and property-wide Wi-Fi. Enjoy Key West style handmade wooden furniture and original artwork.

The connecting suite includes a master king room, second queen room, living room, and two balconies, overlooking the Gulf of Mexico. Everyone can congregate in the spacious living room featuring a 42" flat screen TV, dry bar area, mini-fridge, coffee maker and toaster. The master king and queen bedrooms have full bathrooms and their own 27" flat screen TVs, DVD players and Wi-Fi access.

The suite is accessed by a flight of stairs with the master bedroom on the second floor and the second bedroom on the third floor.

Our three-bedroom Key West villas offer both privacy and ample shared space for family or friends traveling together.
