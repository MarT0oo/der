Located in Davenport, Florida, just 10 miles from Orlando’s Disney Theme Parks, this is one of the prettiest homes in the area.  Davenport unlike other cities is not as congested and offers it’s vacationers some great restaurants and night time activities as well as being central to Orlando and only one hour to Tampa. 

 

The house is a large 5 bedroom, 3 bathroom home that can easily accommodate 10 guests  The Master Suite consists of a King size bed, with en-suite bath, and lovely designer decorated furnishings.  The guest suites are all done tastefully with One Queen, 4 Twins and 2 full sized beds.

 

In the living room you will find a large screen television and lots of space for family and friends to lounge around.  Additionally you have a great game room for the kids and adults alike.  The open kitchen allows you and your family to spend nights socializing around the cook of the house or just to grab a cup of coffee over breakfast.  No matter what you’re looking for in a vacation home, you’re sure to find it here.

 

One of the nicest features is the gorgeous pool area, equipped with bbq, and seating, for those who love the outdoors.
