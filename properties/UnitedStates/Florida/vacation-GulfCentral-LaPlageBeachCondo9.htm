LaPlage Beach Condo 9, Holmes Beach, Florida Vacation Rental

 

LaPlage Beach Condo 9 is the cream of the crop. Direct Gulf Front, high-floor views make this one of the most spectacular luxury condo's on all of Anna Maria Island. What you get on the inside is no less outstanding. The deep cherry wood island style furniture is the finest around. Granite countertops, crown molding and tasteful artwork make this a premium unit. The oversized balcony is perfect for watching a sunset while visiting with family and friends at the end of a long sunfilled day at the beach.

Other amenities include TV and DVD, wireless, free long distance, high-speed elevator, and a large open veranda overlooking the beach. As you would expect in a vacation rental of this superb standard, the provision of free high speed internet access allows you to keep in touch with business or family and friends.

Park in the cool private, covered garage, enjoy the large heated pool and spa, cook out with the Weber gas grill and picnic in the shade of the pergola beside the pool. This property provides the ultimate vacation destination.
