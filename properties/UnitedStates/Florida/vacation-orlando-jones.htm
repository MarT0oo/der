A Stunning 7 Bedroom, 5.5 Bathroom, Luxury Villa Rental with water views, located in Emerald Island Resort Community where you will have access to the Resort Amenities during your stay.  The Mediterranean Style home features 3 Master Suites with King Beds Ensuite, Two Queen Bedrooms which share a bathroom, and Two Twin Bedrooms that share a bath.  These rooms have all been decorated to provide comfort and fun during your Orlando trip, where most people come to visit the Disney Theme Parks year round.

 

The living area features a 51’ wide flat screen television, lots of comfortable sitting, and  a Home Theatre, DVD and VCR. The living room has yet another TV with a The Playstation 2 with games. If you want to keep in touch with friends and family at home, we provide you with free unlimited international and long distance phone calls. In addition we now provide free wi-fi high speed internet access at the house.

 

The family room has a magnificent cathedral ceiling which gives a wonderful feeling of spacious luxury. The kitchen is generously equipped for eating indoors or outside. The house is fully air-conditioned and has a games room with a pool table, foosball table (table football) and air hockey. The house leads onto a shaded lanai with tables, chairs and a barbeque grill. Beyond is a screened deck area with a heated south facing 30 foot pool and spa with sun loungers, overlooking a lake and a wooded conservation area. There are alarms on all doors leading to the pool and a child safety fence can be erected if preferred.
