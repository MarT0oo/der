Villa Abigail, West Palm Beach, Florida Vacation Rental

 

Located in the Grandview Heights Historic District adjacent to downtown West Palm Beach, this 1923 historically landmarked home has been recently restored, updated, expanded and luxuriously appointed by a professional interior designer.

This deluxe home sleeps up to 7 guests (including the day bed in the den). The 2 story house features 3 bedrooms, 2 1/2 bathrooms, a reading room, living room, dining room, den/office, a spacious kitchen, Wi-Fi, HDTV, HBO, audio system, an expansive outside dining terrace and pool deck, lounge chairs and a heated private pool. There are flat screen TVs in the living room and in the master suite.

This Vacation Property has recently received a landscaping facelift, with all new flowers, pavers, brick walkway, and an amazing, authentic "Beach Front" in the backyard with sand and lounge chairs to mimic a day at the beach.

The property is located just 10-15 minutes walking distance to CityPlace, the rejuvenated area of downtown with its pedestrian zone, restaurants, shops, department stores, outdoor cafes and movie theaters. The Kravis Center for the Performing Arts and Norton Museum of Art are also within 10 minutes walking distance. The beaches are within 5 - 10 minutes driving distance. The famous island of Palm Beach with its popular points of interest (Worth Avenue, Flagler Museum, Breakers Resort) are also 5 - 10 minutes driving distance.
