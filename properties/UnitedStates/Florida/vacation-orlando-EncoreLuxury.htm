Encore Luxury, Orlando, Florida Vacation Rental

 

This 12 bedroom villa, located at the Encore Club, is less than a 10 minute drive to Walt Disney World and their 4 incredible theme parks where you and your family can create magical and unforgettable memories that last a lifetime! You and your loved ones can also enjoy exclusive amenities right on property including a Aquapark with thrilling water slides, an arcade, multiple bars and restaurants, and an amenity center with a fitness room!

Sleeping up to 24 people, Encore Luxury has bedrooms across both floors including 6 King sized bedrooms and 2x children's bedrooms themed the beach and sailing!

The home theater is the perfect place for the whole family to watch a movie together or play friendly competitions of pool, air hockey, or video games in the loft area! The outdoor deck is also a great place to soak up some Florida rays in your very own private pool and spillover spa.

The Encore Club at Reunion is the Perfect Location!

The Encore Club at Reunion is just 10 minutes from Disney and within easy access of all of Orlando's main attractions.
Orlando International Airport is less than a 20 minute drive away and the Gulf and Atlantic coasts are less than an hour's drive from the Encore Club at Reunion.

With the fabulous tourism corridor of the 192, just 5 minute's drive away, you and your guests are never far from Orlando's best family friendly restaurants.

Reunion Resort's 'Eleven' and 'Forte' restaurants are also available for Encore guests to enjoy and the standard of cuisine will always impress.

Reunion Resort's Clubhouse and Sushi Bar Lounge are also great choices for high quality casual dining.

The Encore Club at Reunion is within easy reach of two large Publix grocery stores and within easy reach of Posner's shopping park that includes a wide range of specialist stores.

Best of all, you've got everything you need, right on your doorstep, but the beauty of Reunion, is that you'd never even know it as you relax in Orlando's premium vacation rental community!
