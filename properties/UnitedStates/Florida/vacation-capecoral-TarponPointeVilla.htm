Tarpon Point Villa, Cape Coral, Florida Vacation Rental

 

Tarpon Point Villa is a luxurious custom built home located in the SW area of Cape Coral. The villa is very close to Tarpon Point Marina. The 4 bedroom and 3 bath villa has 2 kings, 1 queen, 2 twins with western exposure. There is a flat screen TV in each bedroom for your enjoyment. The fully equipped kitchen with granite countertops has all major appliances. The spacious living room has a TV, DVD player and stereo sound system. Next to the living room you have a nice family room for guests to sit back and relax.

For your convenience, there is Wi-Fi available to connect your smart phones and laptops. The pool and spa are electric heated. The lanai is equipped with pool furniture, al fresco dining area and a gas BBQ grill. You have gulf access from the canal. The boat dock can be used to dock a rental boat during your stay. Please inquire about renting a boat. We can have it delivered to the villa.

Within a short drive from Tarpon Point Villa you have an shopping and great restaurants. The Miromar Outlet Mall is a half hour drive from the villa. Voted the Best Shopping Mall and Best Factory Outlet Mall in Southwest Florida. The outlet features more than 140 top designer and brand name outlets.

Cape Coral sits on Florida’s stunning Gulf Coast and is built around a system of over 400 miles of canals known as the “Waterfront Wonderland”. The city of Cape Coral lies between the Caloosahatchee River, part of the Intracoastal Waterway and the Gulf of Mexico. The city provides ample access to boating, fishing and other water sports activities.
