Palmer Paradise, Reunion Resort, Orlando, Florida Vacation Rental

 

This luxury 6 bedroom vacation home with its private courtyard heated swimming pool and spa is located in the exclusive community of Reunion Resort within the Liberty Bluff division overlooking the Arnold Palmer golf course, one of 3 PGA courses within the resort. All 4,040 ft² of the house has been decorated and furnished throughout to meet the expectations of this world class resort whilst still ensuring extreme comfort for your relaxation and enjoyment. Your family will love being only minutes from all the major attractions and theme parks that Orlando has to offer whilst still enjoying the tranquility of resort and it's host of amenities.

This beautiful 6 bedroom vacation home is the courtyard style home commands a fantastic position with breathtaking views across the Palmer golf course with the Reunion Grand Hotel in the background. On the ground floor of the mini house are two bedrooms, one having two full size beds and the other a queen, these two bedrooms share a bathroom with a tub/shower combination. The living area is open plan incorporating a comfortable seating area with large flat screen TV with surround sound and Blu-Ray, fully equipped kitchen and dining area with access onto the rear patio with golf views, a perfect setting for an evening cocktail. Upstairs at the rear of the property is the king-sized master suite and en-suite which includes a garden tub, walk in shower and double vanities with a private balcony. Two further bedrooms, one king-sized and one queen share a bathroom with a tub/shower combination, the laundry room is also located off the landing area.

Across the courtyard is the games room which will provide hours or fun and entertainment for the family with its pool table, table tennis, air hockey and foosball. Above is the guest suite comprises of a king-sized bedroom with the en-suite having a tub/shower combination and a separate living room with mini kitchen, dining area and sofa bed.

The pool area is spacious with a heated pool and spa, dining area and ample lounging space.
