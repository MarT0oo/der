Champions Exclusive, Orlando, Florida Vacation Rental

 

If you are searching for a large south facing pool home in the Disney Parks area of Orlando, this 8 bedroom villa at Championsgate is a great option for guests with a large number of guests in need of a luxury home.

Complete with access to Championsgate's water park, guests staying at the home can enjoy unlimited and exclusive access to the fabulous clubhouse, water park and lazy river.

With bedrooms on both floors, the home offers easy access and additional privacy to guests of all ages, making this a perfect opportunity to arrange a family reunion in the world's most entertaining destination!

With a south facing pool deck, the fun can continue after you return from the parks, as your family cools off in the fabulous pool!

The Retreat at Champions Gate is perfectly located for easy access to all of Orlando's major attractions.
Just 14 minutes away from Walt Disney World, and around 25 minutes from Orlando International Airport, you can easily reach the major routes within a few minutes of leaving this tranquil resort.
With fabulous restaurants of the 192 just a few miles away, this luxury home is also just a few minutes away from the legendary Manny's Steakhouse on route 27 near Davenport.
Fabulous grocery stores are a short drive away, enabling you to keep your supplies well stocked without making special trips.
A perfect home, with a fabulous location!
