Two Waters, Captiva Island, Florida Vacation Rental

 

Two Waters Retreat is a beautiful home featuring views of Pine Island Sound and partial views of the Gulf of Mexico. Located on the beach, the home's living area features flat panel television; two large, comfy sofas in the living and two chairs for additional seating. Opens to balcony overlooking the caged, pool deck

The spacious, gourmet kitchen is perfect for entertaining and cooking with the family. The kitchen features granite countertops, stainless steel appliances and is fully stocked with appliances, cookware, dinnerware and silverware

Dining Room, Located just off the kitchen and living room areas, the large dining room table comfortably accommodates up to 10

Bedroom One, Located upstairs, enjoy views of the bay and partial views of the Gulf of Mexico in the master bedroom which is equipped with a king bed and flat panel television. En suite bath features double vanities, Jacuzzi tub and spacious walk in shower with dual shower heads

Bedroom Two, Located downstairs, this guest bedroom features a queen bed, television and a hallway bath with shower/tub combination.

Bedroom Three, Located downstairs, this guest bedroom features a queen bed, flat panel television and en suite bath with shower/tub combination

Bedroom Four, Located downstairs, this guest bedroom features two twin beds, flat panel television, blu ray DVD player and hallway bath with shower/tub combination.

Loft area features a desk and a large, comfy sofa and chair. Opens to balcony overlooking pool deck.

Play Room is Located on the ground floor, the play room is perfect for entertainment on a rainy day. This room features a ping pong and poker table, television, an elliptical and a full bath for a quick rinse after playing in the pool and beach.
