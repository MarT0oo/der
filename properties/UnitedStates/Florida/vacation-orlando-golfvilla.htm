Villa Watson
5 Bedrooms | 4.5 Baths | Overlooking Course
Orlando, Florida
From $2,950 per Week

Villa Watson is located in the amazing reunion resort. It is an exquisite 5 bedroom and 4 and 1/2 bathroom home. It has one of the best locations in Reunion and is a two minute walk from the club house, Grande Hotel, Seven Eagles and the cove as well as overlooking the ninth hole of the Watson course. Golf House is a luxuriously designed and furnished transitional style home that has extremely spacious and comfortable rooms with a large outdoor terrace.

Accommodation: Fully equipped gourmet kitchen with breakfast area, dining room and living room and large den with pool table, TVs in all bedrooms, living room and den for a total of 7 TVs (all receive cable), wireless internet, large pool and patio area and 3 separate upstairs terraces.

Amenities: Air conditioned, telephone, wireless internet, Cable TV, 7x TVs , DVD, stereo, pool table, sun loungers, outdoor dining and outdoor dining service, BBQ gas piped, all linens provided including pool towels, safety pool system for children and laundry room.
