Flower Garden, Marco Island, Florida Vacation Rental


Flower Garden is a Marco Island villa waterfront with dock, great for families looking to get away during the winter months or just find a place to escape nearby. It is located just 1 mile from the beach and is walking distance from restaurants and shops in the area.

The Flower Garden is a two story 5 bedrooms and 3 bathroom Cottage style home, with all of the warmth and comfort of your own second home. The Master Bedroom is en-suite and has one King sized with lots of great views, while the additional four guest rooms are Queens and share 2 bathrooms. There is a wide open living room that opens up to the pool as well as a great family room for lounging and having your morning coffee with paper.  The dining room sits 8 and there is an eat in kitchen area for additional guests.

Marco Island is on the Gulf Coast of Florida just 15 miles from Naples and is clearly a boating community.  You can find many charters near the house or we can have a boat delivered to the house for your enjoyment.  Tigertail Beach is a great spot to bring the kids, as it allows the kids to play in the sand but also to note the conservation efforts Marco Island Makes.  As we are all well aware, Marco Island and the Gulf Coast were hit by storms in the past few years and it’s important for the community to give back.

No matter what you do, we hope you will enjoy our Flower Garden, truly a great home for your next trip to the Lovely Florida Gulf Coast.
