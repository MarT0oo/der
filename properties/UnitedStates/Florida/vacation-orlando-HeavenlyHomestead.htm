Heavenly Homestead, Reunion Resort, Orlando, Florida Vacation Rental

 

This home is a great vacation home for those looking to have fun, go to theme parks, and visit local restaurants and shops as with its convenient location, but also for relaxing after a long day, enjoying tranquil views, and spending quality time with family . The new decor and furnishings throughout will make this an outstanding vacation home having added a seventh bedroom which holds fourth king-size suite.

Located in Homestead, the most central part of the resort and overlooking the Tom Watson golf course, one of three PGA courses within the Reunion Resort gives this property an enviable location. multiple families will be guaranteed plenty of space with the floor plan of this home incorporating 7 bedrooms, 6 full and 2 half baths, formal and informal dining and seating areas, a movie theatre/games room, heated pool and spa with covered dining an lounging space. Breathtaking views can be enjoyed from the first floor rear balcony.

This large spacious home also offers a large driveway for ample off road parking. The decor and furnishings have been chosen to the high standards associated with the Reunion Resort. The open hallway features a sweeping stair case, and to the left is the bright formal dining area which leads onto the open plan living areas of the home. This incorporates the laundry room, a guest bathroom, an open plan fully equipped kitchen complete with large central island, breakfast bar, granite counter tops, and brand new stainless steel appliances. The kitchen area overlooks the family living area which includes casual dining and ample comfortable sofas surrounding the large flat screen TV. The family area has one wall overlooking the large pool deck with its heated pool and spa where you will be able to enjoy the extended lounging space. Also the shaded dining areas and picturesque views across the golf course, there is also a pool bathroom for your convenience. A second seating area off the kitchen has been designed to provide the perfect space for children to enjoy their games console, or for a guest to view their favorite program. The main master suite with its huge bathroom incorporating double vanities, a large corner tub, a wet room and having access onto the pool deck via French doors is the only bedroom suite on the ground floor. The movie theater/games room is located off the hallway and will provide hours of entertainment for the whole family.

Upstairs located around the gallery landing are three further king-sized bedroom suites each with a bathroom ensuite and two having access onto the rear balcony. Three further bedrooms, two having two twin beds in each share a bathroom and the third, a queen size has an ensuite bathroom, all bedrooms have flat screen TV's. The wrap around balcony is the perfect space for those social evenings or simply relaxing whilst taking in the amazing views and evening skyline

Located in the Reunion Resort, this beautiful home backs onto the golf course. Reunion is probably the most prestigious rental community in Central Florida having every amenity that you could want to make yours a memorable vacation from restaurants, bars, swimming pools, 3 PGA golf courses, spa and water park though membership is required for certain amenities. The resort is also ideally situated for all the major parks, Disney, Universal & Seaworld with many local attractions situated on the US192 a ten minute drive away. The main entrance is only two minutes from I4 giving easy access to both the Gulf and Atlantic coast lines for great beaches, just outside the entrance you will also find a selections of shops, stores and takeouts to meet most of your day to day needs.
