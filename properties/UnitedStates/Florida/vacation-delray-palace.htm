Coral Beach Palace
5 Bedrooms | 4.5 Baths | Beachfront | Pool
Delray Beach, Florida
From $8,000 per Week

Coral Beach Palace is a beautiful, luxury, oceanfront home that offers families the quality and serenity of a Resort with the pure privacy of a private home rental.  The blending of the two sides gives you access to one of the most beautiful homes and beaches on the Atlantic Coast, complete with 5 large bedrooms, 4.5 bathrooms and 1 crib for children.  The home also features many of the amenities you can expect from a luxury home including an outside pool, luxury pool furnishings, and elegant landscaping.

As you enter the community, 1 mile from downtown Delray Beach, where you’ll find shopping restaurants, and shopping, you’ll come to the community of Delray Ocean Estates.  This community is a wonderful piece of heaven, with tropical foliage, gorgeous garden view, and complete privacy in its own little world.   You have your own driveway with parking for 2 cars easily. 

As you enter the house you’ll find soaring ceilings and a winding staircase that draw your eye upward.  The house is filled with the sunshine coming in from the East, which give the house an amazing natural light, brining the house to life.  The living area is comfortable with a great space for reading or relaxing with family.  There are plush sofas and lots of light during the day in this wonderful area.  The house has a large media room, equipped with a theater where you can watch movies or TV, for those days when you decide to stay in.  The media room is reminiscent of a Theater with its Theater like seating and atmosphere. 

The gourmet kitchen, which is great for any novice chef,  has all the things you can dream of in a kitchen, including high line appliances, granite countertops, and hard wood cabinetry.  In addition there are dishes, cutlery, and stemware for use during your stay.  Everything you need to enjoy your vacation is on site.

The Master Suite with King sized bed, which is on the second floor has lovely ocean views, and a private terrace.  The bedroom also features en-suite bathroom with his and her vanities.  The guest suite (1) has a Queen sized bed and bathroom, the guest suite (2) has a Queen sized and a crib.  The guest suite (3) also known as the Au-pair room has a King sized bed and bathroom.  The guest suite (4) also known as the children's room has a Double bed and Bunk bed. There is also an additional half bathroom for guests to use.

The patio, which is where we know you’ll spend most of your days soaking in the sun, is just ideal.  Children love this area for its white sandy beaches and the pool area.  The design inspires you to just let go and relax, with the Mediterranean feel and modern architecture.  Like most who visit, the sounds of the ocean at night are bound to put you to sleep or in a state of complete relaxation, and that’s what you would expect from such a magical location. 

In addition there is a wonderful game room, where you have a bar and billiards table and plenty of seating.  This comes in handy when you have multiple age groups trying to enjoy the holiday.  You can escape to the beach or to your own private area to have fun, enjoy a pint, and watch some television with friends and family.

The house can be made fully catered with chef, maid, and spa services.  You can also hire a nanny and concierge during your stay to bring things together.  All you need do is ask.
