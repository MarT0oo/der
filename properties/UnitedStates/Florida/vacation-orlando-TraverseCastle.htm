Traverse Castle, Reunion Resort, Orlando, Florida Vacation Rental

 

This magnificent villa in Reunion Resort's luxury vacation home community offers the perfect location in the Disney area for a large group of up to 24 guests.

With a prime south facing location, this really is a perfect opportunity for a very large group of family, friends, wedding parties and corporate guests to relax and enjoy the beautiful Floridian weather.

With a huge luxury pool, fully equipped games room, private gym, theater room and even your very own putting green, your fun can continue day and night.

Located on the highly sought after Grand Traverseway area of Reunion's West side, you are only a few minutes away from Interstate 4, with easy access to the Orange County Conference Centre and main routes of Central Florida, and less than 12 minutes away from the main entrance to Walt Disney World.
