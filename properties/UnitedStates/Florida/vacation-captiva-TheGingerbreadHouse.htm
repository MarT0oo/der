The Gingerbread House, Captiva Island, Florida Vacation Rental

 

Welcome to The Gingerbread House on Captiva Island. This antique style cottage is set just 300 feet from the beautiful Captiva Island beaches. With 2 spacious bedrooms, 2 full bathrooms and a den that closes off to be a third bedroom, there is plenty of space for a family of six.

Stay for a week or longer and enjoy the full kitchen and washer/dryer to make your stay that much more accommodating.

The Gingerbread House has an expansive screened lanai facing to the west to sit, relax and enjoy a cold beverage or even a meal.

The master bedroom has a queen bed with en-suite bath while the guest bedroom has two comfortable twin beds. The den is set up as a family room with a TV and does close off for privacy with a comfortable queen sized sleeper sofa. High speed wireless internet is included as is beach equipment and beach towels.

We look forward to welcoming you to our Captiva Island Cottage that we call The Gingerbread House
