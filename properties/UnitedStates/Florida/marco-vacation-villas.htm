Visiting Marco Island, Florida

Marco Island is one of the many wonders of SW Florida’s Gulf Coast, although Hurricane Charley saw its way to make way through Captiva, many of the residents have since built up their beach homes bigger and better than ever.  Marco Island is truly a beach location.  The reason people go there is to escape from it all, the excitement is about the beaches and that is one thing you have plenty of.

Captiva is known to captivate the hearts of children, many of whom spend each summer growing to love digging for sea shells, enjoying the amazing oceanfront rentals, and escaping from the day to day with their families.  It’s no wonder it’s called Paradise! 

The things people love most about Captiva is that the local shopping, such as

        Perwinkle Place

        Tanger Outlets

        William E. Wilson Fine Jewelry

        make it feel more like home. 

For other shopping opportunities in the area, visit Shop On Sanibel.com. 

Additionally the restaurants are amazing, with some great local cuisine, as well as international flair from

        Keylime Bistro

        Sanibel Steakhouse

        Old Captiva House
        with some award winning cuisine .

No matter what you chose to do in Captiva, you’ll always do it with a smile!
