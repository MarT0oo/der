Bella Vita, Pompano Beach, Florida Vacation Rental


Absolutely stunning 3 bedroom home in very exclusive neighborhood in South Florida's finest spot for fun and sun. Step into this designer home with luxurious furnishings, state-of-the-art appliances, a gourmet kitchen with granite countertops, and, every modern amenity you may need to make your visit to South Florida an amazing experience. This exclusive 3 bedroom 3.5 bath home on an Interior Point Lot Surrounded on two sides by water with Spectacular Intracoastal Views and 280 ft of deepwater and a fully equipped dock that can accommodate up to 90ft yachts.

Home is equipped with king and queen-sized beds in each bedroom, modern baths with European fixtures and Jacuzzi tub in master bath. Enjoy a swim in our heated pool or just spend some time in our Spa. Totally updated, beautifully furnished, and has flat screen TV's in all rooms, marble flooring throughout the house.

The property is on the West side of the intracoastal waterway... just a quick drive over the bridge brings you to the a warm white sandy beaches of East Pompano Beach and its spectacular Beachfront areas... which are teen and kid friendly.

Close by are golf and tennis, numerous parks and libraries, Las Olas Blvd and to the south to world renowned South Beach. Visit the largest mall-Sawgrass Mills Mall. Also visit the Adventura, Galleria , Boca Raton Town Center and Bal Harbour luxury malls. And don't forget your ride on Ft. Lauderdale's Water Taxi, which takes you sightseeing and restaurant hopping all over Ft. Lauderdale and South to Hollywood. Visit the Oasis, the largest 6,000 passenger ship in the world at Port Everglades.
