Atlantic Horizons, Fort Lauderdale, Florida Vacation Rental


Be dazzled by the ocean sounds, relax on your private balcony, watch the waves and enjoy the sensational ocean breeze that will tantalize your every senses. May all your dreams come true at Atlantic Horizons. This enjoyable villa was designed for those who appreciate comfort and beauty. You can feel the ocean breeze from every room. The villa is situated across a beautiful sandy beach.

Whether you are looking for a family vacation getaway, need a home for a business engagement, entertaining guests, maybe some private sunbathing or just time to rejuvenate your life. Atlantic Horizons has all the amenities you would look for in a vacation rental; large flat screen TV, DVD player, surround sound system, pool table, heated pool, rainfall showers, and a private inside laundry room. Every essential for a relaxing beach vacation for everyone!

The Main House has two stories consisting of 4 bedrooms and 2 full baths with a half bath in the laundry room by the pool. The guest bedroom downstairs has a King size bed and ensuite bathroom. The master bedroom upstairs has a King size bed and a flat screen TV. All 3 bedrooms upstairs share one bathroom in the hall. The first guest bedroom upstairs has 2 Full (Double) size beds. The second guest bedroom upstairs has a Queen Murphy bed. The kitchen, dining area, and 3 bedrooms are located on the second floor. Downstairs you have an entertainment room with a pool table and 50” flat screen TV. Additionally, there is a refrigerator for your convenience.

Within walking distance to Ft. Lauderdale's best restaurants and the newly opened Auberge spa and dining. With a heated pool, expansive beach and ocean vistas, we have attempted to capitalize and emphasize the reasons you and your family selected Florida and Fort Lauderdale in particular as your vacation destination.

Walk the beach, kick the sand, ride the waves, soak the sun, and for early risers…witness exalting sunrises. The environment cannot be improved upon…anywhere. It's a photographer's paradise whose bejeweled setting makes everyone beautifully photogenic.

Atlantic Horizons is in the perfect location of Fort Lauderdale beach. Parking is no problem. Neither is traffic noise. The area is a renowned residential beach neighborhood…quiet, safe, and secure.

Quick access to Fort Lauderdale/Hollywood International Airport and freeways speed you to extended travel opportunity including a quick cruise to the Caribbean. Local entertainment includes Hard Rock Casino and Gambling, golf and all other recreational activities. Fishing is always great whether deep-sea or inland waters.

We welcome you to paradise!

Note: there are 3 bedrooms located upstairs including the kitchen and dining area. 4th bedroom is downstairs with ensuite bath. All bedrooms upstairs share a bathroom.
