Gulf Pearl, Marco Island, Florida Vacation Rental

 

Cool, crisp and contemporary, Gulf Pearl dominates her space on Marco Island's sparkling blue waterways. This custom home – built in 2013 by Andrew Hunter Homes – resides on a premium waterfront tip lot that leads to Caxambas Pass and the Gulf of Mexico.

Wide open and wonderful, the 28' x 13' pool is electrically heated year round, with a splash pad for toddlers and a stone hot spa for pure relaxation. Exterior and interior blend seamlessly, with soothing sand hues and new Modern European furnishings.

Artful architecture offers water views from most rooms. Stay entertained (and connected) with technology as sophisticated as the décor. Enjoy meals in Gulf Pearl's fully stocked gourmet kitchen – or dine out. This elegant waterfront villa is centrally located granting easy access to your choice of dining, entertainment and recreation venues.

Active couples and families will appreciate that Gulf Pearl is near Mackle Park; TripAdvisor reviewers have ranked it second of 26 attractions on Marco. One said it was "One of Marco Island's Best Features." He wrote "Mackle Park has evolved over the years into a first class facility that has something for everyone." There is a large lake surrounded by a paved track for biking, walking, roller blading etc. Islanders use the lake for racing remote controlled boats. Add in a fabulous kid's playground with an adjacent water spay area, soccer fields and large basketball court to boot! Inside the main building meetings are held and every form of indoor activity is available including a teen section with ping pong and billiards. "A great facility that keeps on getting better all the time."

All you need for the beach is in the garage – including adult cruiser bikes, cooler, and Tommy Bahama® brand beach chairs and beach cart. At end of day, a western exposure guarantees champagne sunsets, two of her four bedrooms feature en-suite baths, and finer linens assure blissful sleep.

Gulf Pearl of Marco Island is the epitome of "island chic." Make her yours for a week or more ...
