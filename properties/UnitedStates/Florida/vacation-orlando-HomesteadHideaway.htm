Homestead Hideaway, Reunion Resort, Orlando, Florida Vacation Rental

 

Located on Homestead within the Reunion resort and Spa, this home the ideal location for those wanting to explore the Resort on foot. The focal point, The Reunion Grand Hotel, with it's bars and restaurants along with the main Clubhouse and golf shop are a mere stroll away. The resort also offers a host of other amenities including 3 PGA golf course; Palmer, Nicklaus and Watson. There's also tennis courts, numerous communal pools, a water park, and cycling trails.

This perfect single story family home is one of the few to offer a screened pool and deck with the resort . The furnishings and decor have all been completed according to the Reunion standard and won't fail to satisfy you whilst still retaining a 'home away from home' feel.

This really is the perfect home for smaller families looking for first class accommodation. Beautifully decorated and furnished throughout with many upgrades, it is located on one of the residential streets in the centre of the resort in one of the best area. With everything conveniently planned on one level, as you enter the home the first bedroom with two twin beds is on one side of the hallway to the front. There are two further bedrooms additionally, one queen size and the other with two twin beds sharing the family bathroom that offers a tub/shower combination. The king-size master bedroom suite with large bathroom incorporates a corner tub, walk in shower, and twin vanities, and is separated from the other bedrooms by the living area All bedrooms include TV's.

The living area consists of a fully equipped kitchen which separates the formal and casual dining areas. There is a large family seating area with a comfortable sectional sofa and a large flat screen TV. This area has access to the pool deck which includes a heated pool and spa, covered dining area perfect for those summer evenings, and ample lounging space all screened to keep the bugs and wildlife at bay.

Located in the Reunion Resort, probably the most prestigious rental community in Central Florida having every amenity that you could want to make yours a memorable vacation from restaurants, bars, swimming pools, 3 PGA golf courses, spa and water park though membership is required for certain amenities. The resort is also ideally situated for all the major theme parks, Disney, Universal & Seaworld with many local attractions situated on the US192 a ten minute drive away. The main entrance is only two minutes from I4 giving easy access to both the Gulf and Atlantic coast lines for great beaches. Outside the main entrance of the Resort you will find a selection of stores, shops and takeouts to meet most of your day to day requirements.
