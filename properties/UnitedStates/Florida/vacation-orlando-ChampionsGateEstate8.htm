Champions Gate Estate 8, Champions Gate, Orlando, Florida Vacation Rental

 

Champions Gate is a vacation home community name you need to know. It’s like the Athens of the modern vacation home world. This location in particular is a 6 bed 6 bath paradise. Each bedroom is a minimum of 110 square feet (with the master bedroom being significantly larger), with everything you need, including an alarm clock and TV. Have kids? Well two of these bedrooms sports twin beds and comfortable Disney themes with items like Mickey Mouse covers.

The living room area looks especially trendy with its large TV and open layout. Soothing grays accent the earth tones of the room, giving the home a very cozy and friendly feeling. You’ll want to entertain company with the large kitchen and separate dining room, complete with a large wooden table - a large group will put this area to good use.

The first floor of this Orlando vacation rental has a game room. The game room features air hockey and billiards, in what appears to be a converted garage of sorts, with a rolling door to the outside. There is also a pool and spa outside, plenty large enough for a big group. Here you can sunbathe or just hang out in the pseudo-outdoor environment intelligently created by the large glass exterior housing the pools. This means privacy and comfort, without feeling stuck inside. The house also has a media loft with additional room for play or relaxation; it’s perfect for a movie night.

If you have a car, store it in the 2 car garage, or take it for a spin into town. This Orlando vacation home is central, right off the I-4 highway. It’s just a short drive to get to the major cities and do anything and everything. Many people visit Champions Gate and spend time in Walt Disney Land or at the beaches.

Don’t forget this is a resort style community, meaning you have a great environment within Champions Gate. If you simply walk to the Oasis Clubhouse, you’ll become infatuated with all the amenities. The large community pool is a joy itself, with its lap pool and mini water park - equipped with slides and water spouts. There’s cabanas to eat and drink under. This clubhouse offers a fitness center and large studio for the best of yoga or pilates. Last but not least, guests especially love the clubhouse restaurant and bar, offering great service and food and drink.
