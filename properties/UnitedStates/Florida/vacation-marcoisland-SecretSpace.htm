Secret Space, Marco Island, Florida Vacation Rental

 

Beauty and The Beach; it's easy to fall in love with Secret Space.

At Secret Space, it's an easy walk to the relaxing white sand beaches of Marco Island right at the end of our street. This gorgeous two-story waterfront villa is one of the few residential homes to offer such exceptional proximity.

Warm and welcoming, with graceful arches, rich woods and posh furnishings, all rooms on the main level lead to an open air heated pool and oversized hot spa. Sunbathe, read and post photos of tropical surroundings that will be the envy of friends back home.

Secret Space offers wide-open spaciousness for effortless entertaining, from great room and dining room to fully stocked stainless and granite kitchen. Her plush furniture is substantial, offering that rare combination of comfort and elegance. Choose between two master suites with epic beds and sumptuous fabrics. LCDs in every bedroom and Wi-Fi throughout the home will keep you entertained and connected.

While the main level encourages interaction, the second floor is pure leisure. For peace and privacy, ascend the gleaming wood stairway to the exquisite upper level balcony; savor the view with coffee or wine.

Secret Space's location in the highly desirable west central sector of Marco Island puts you within walking distance of our beautiful white sand beaches and exceptional pampering. Walk west on Maple and you'll encounter the Marco Island Spa; walk a bit further down for Marco Island's legendary white sand beaches. (You'll find all the essential beach gear in the garage!!) Turn left to explore the many amenities of the Marco Island Marriott Beach Resort, Golf Club & Spa.
