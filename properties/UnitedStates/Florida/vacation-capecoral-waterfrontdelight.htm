Waterfront Delight
4 Bedrooms | 3.5 Bathrooms | Pool | Waterfront | Boat
Cape Coral, Florida
From $3,800 per Week

A Beautiful 4 bedroom home with all of the perks a vacation rental needs and then some.  With it’s contemporary feel, this home features 3 car garage, waterfront views, dock, and comes with a boat for your use while in the villa.  No matter which way you slice it, Waterfront Delight is a great place to vacation.

The living room area is equipped with a television, standard with cable, for your viewing pleasure.  The area is perfect for those late nights with family and friends, enjoying a movie, or your favorite shows.  The living area opens up to the dining room, where you’ll find a beautiful glass table with room for 6 to 8 guests.  Whether you’re enjoying coffee or a nice dinner, this is a perfect setting.

The kitchen is wide open so that the chef can communicate with the family while cooking.  This makes for a very easy and fun loving floor plan, so that nobody gets left out.  From the living area you can find your view out to the pool and waterway, which is an amazing treat for those who love the nautical life, you truly feel like you’re at one with nature.

The master suite has a King sized bed with ensuite bathroom, a great escape for those who want to spend some quality time alone, to watch TV, or just to relax.  The guest Suites have a queen sized, full, and two twins for your convenience.  This is a great layout for those traveling with children or a larger family group.  There are also 3 main bathrooms and one guest bathroom that leads out to the pool.
