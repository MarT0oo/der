1071 Sandrift, Captiva Island, Florida Vacation Rental

 

Enjoy breathtaking views of the Gulf of Mexico from this spacious 2070 square foot three bedroom , three bath luxury villa. The open floor plan provides the perfect beach getaway with the living area opening onto a large deck with spectacular views. The fully equipped kitchen opens into the living area perfect for family evening or entertaining. Take a short stroll to the beach or take one of the trolleys to the Pointe Pool Complex for a day in the sun. The master suite is located upstairs with a beautiful water views. This condo accommodates up to 10 guests.
