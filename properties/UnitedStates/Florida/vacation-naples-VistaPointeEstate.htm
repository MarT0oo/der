Vista Pointe Estate, Naples, Florida Vacation Rental

 

The Vista Pointe Estate is one of the most beautiful in Naples, situated on over an acre of Naples Bay waterfront, with over 300' of frontage and a one-of-a-kind 64' cut-in boat slip for safe harbor, this Cooney designed, custom-built BCB home is the benchmark by which all homes in the area will be measured. The sprawling footprint, generous covered outdoor living areas, infinity edge pool, gourmet kitchen and prep-kitchen, and, 4-car garage are some of the high points this special home provides. Enjoy unobstructed, panoramic Naples Bay views that can't be matched. A gated entry and beautifully landscaped grounds complete the uniqueness of this offering.

The house is perfect for those seeking to be near the beach but also to have the lovely waterfront views that come with Waterfront Living. The rooms are all designer decorated with the best of luxury linens and hardwood designer furnishings. Completely done to the taste of the discerning traveler. The Master suite has a King Sized bed and en-suite bathroom, while the guests each have Queen beds plus their own private bathrooms. In total there are 6 bathrooms.

The kitchen is perfect for the novice cook in us all with granite counter-tops, stainless appliances and an eat in area for you enjoyment. We can also arrange a chef for the day or the week, our chefs are all professionally trained and can cook a variety of meals including culinary artistry for the gluten free pallets. The dining area is perfect for hosting your family dinners and you will always find a new way to enjoy the time together, unlike traditional vacation rentals. We strive to only offer the best in accommodations.

Outside you'll find a lovely pool area, with grill, perfect for enjoying the beautiful winter warmth that Florida Enjoys year round. If you would like a boat rental, tours or anything else, please let us know.
