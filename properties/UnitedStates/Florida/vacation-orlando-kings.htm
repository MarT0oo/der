Be among the first guests to stay in our luxurious, new, seven bedroom (six bathroom house) located in the prestigious Formosa Gardens Estates neighborhood. Formosa Gardens Estates is a gated and walled estate community located just three miles from Disney off of Route 192W. The neighborhood is in a quiet, tranquil area yet only a one minute drive to a grocery store, pharmacy, medical walk in clinic, and many restaurants including Outback Steakhouse and Carrabbas. Formosa Gardens is less than a 3 mile drive to Disney!

 

This home has been professionally custom designed throughout with over 4000 ft² of space. There are 7 spacious bedrooms designed for the most discerning guests, who simply want the utmost comfort and style!

For the Kings and Queens of your party, King Triton’s Palace invites you to stay in one of four Master Bedrooms, all with king-size beds and en suite bathrooms. All four master bedrooms are furnished with $1,200+ Spring Air pillowtop mattresses (A Consumer Reports Best Buy mattress). Each Master bedroom offers a 27inch tv, vcr and satellite tv. Each bedroom television is connected to its own satellite receiver so up to eight different programs can be watched in the house at the same time. 

 

Three of the master bedrooms are on the first floor and one of the master bedrooms is on the second floor. One of the first floor Master suites includes a full size crib (like you would have at home!).  For the Princes and Princesses or your party, King Triton’s Palace proudly offers three Disney themed bedrooms. The bedroom themes include The Little Mermaid, Cinderella and Toy Story.

 

The Toy Story bedroom and bathroom are located on the first floor next to the breakfast nook. Painted in vivid Toy Story colors, this room is sure to delight any young child. There is a wall size mural of Buzz Light-year on the wall across from the bed. For entertainment, this room offers a Thomas like train table and train set, a 13 inch tv/vcr combo, a bookshelf stocked with toys for all ages, and even a Buzz Light-year dress up costume! If it is time for bed, turn off the lights and watch the stars glow on the ceiling from one of three twin beds in the room. The furniture in this room includes a set of bunk beds and a trundle bed under the lower bunk. In total, there are three twin size beds in this room. The top and bottom bunk both have bedrails available for them. Photos do not show the pull out trundle but it is a full size twin mattresses. The bedding in this room is from the Disney catalog and is the ever popular Buzz Light-year bedding.

 

Our house was designed with families in mind. This stunning home has a South facing deck and a huge pool including a large swimming area, a baby area, and a heated spa (heats to 102 degrees). Following the theme of King Triton’s Palace, the pool is shaped like a fish. The fin of the fish is the spa, the body of the fish is the main pool (3 to 5 feet deep) and the tail of the fish is a toddler wading area (gradual slope from 2 - 12 inches deep). 

 

The swimming pool and spa also have fiber optic lighting for your own private "light show!" Surrounding the pool is a large deck area equipped with a gas barbecue grill, a covered lanai, and plenty of lounge chairs. The deck area also has three tables and chairs for outdoor dining. Child safe door alarms are provided for safety.
