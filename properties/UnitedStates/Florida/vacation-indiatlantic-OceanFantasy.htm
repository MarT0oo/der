Ocean Fantasy, Indiatlantic, Florida Vacation Rental

 

The Ocean Fantasy offers an unsurpassed vacation experience unmatched by any other hotel, resort, or vacation home with 100 ft. of Atlantic Ocean frontage, 1 acre of prime beachfront in the heart of the Space Coast in proximity to all amenities and directly on Atlantic Ocean!

The Ocean Fantasy has 4 bedrooms and 4.5 Baths with stunning 180 degree ocean views from a 12-foot wide upper deck spanning the whole east side of the house. The house will sleep up to 12 people with 1 king, 3 queen beds and 2 queen size sleeper sofas (in the upper floor living room). The views are breathtaking from this recently renovated house with quality finishing’s in pure luxury and high end brand new quality furniture.

Relax to the sound of the ocean waves by a wood burning fireplace, surf just in front of YOUR HOUSE, watch dolphins from the huge upper deck, cook out, observe rocket launches from YOUR beach deck, look at the stars during the night or walk down the steps to the white, sandy beach any time you desire. This is as close as you can get to your own private beach.

Ocean Fantasy is located in Indialantic between Melbourne Causeway and Eau Galle Causeway in the heart of the Space Coast, a 72 mile stretch along Florida’s Atlantic (East) Coast between Cape Canaveral and Sebastian inlet. The area beaches are closest to Orlando with some of the best surfing in the country and spectacular array of wildlife, unspoiled natural habitats, sanctuaries and preserves. At the same time you will be within an easy reach to everything – surrounded by nice restaurants, just an hour’s drive from Orlando and all of its attractions, less than half an hour away from Cape Canaveral and Kennedy Space Center and just two and a half hours away from South Beach in Miami.

Fishing, boating, sunbathing, hiking, walking, golfing, water ski, jet ski, treasure hunt, you can have it all! This is truly Florida beach experience for your whole party while staying in the Space Coast area!

Enjoy your stay with the Atlantic Ocean in your backyard – no streets to cross, no crowded hotel elevators, this is your own piece of paradise!
