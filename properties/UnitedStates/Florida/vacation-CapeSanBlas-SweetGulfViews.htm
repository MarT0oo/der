Sweet Gulf Views, Cape San Blas, Florida Vacation Rental

 

Sweet Gulf Views is the perfect vacation rental to enjoy a beach getaway in the Gulf of Mexico. Newly built in 2017, Sweet Gulf Views offers a great gulf view, and four-bedrooms in a quiet section of Cape San Blas. There are three-bedrooms and two bathrooms on the first floor, and the top floor features a fourth bedroom with a twin pullout along with a living space.

Each bedroom includes a new Smart TV. Each floor offers screened porches, perfect for lounging or a sunset dinner. Cook for the family in the fantastic kitchen or use the gas grill for a beach BBQ. The home is across the street from the beach and it's only about a two minute walk to the closest beach access.
