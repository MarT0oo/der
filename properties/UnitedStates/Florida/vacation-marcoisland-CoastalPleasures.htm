Coastal Pleasures, Marco Island, Florida Vacation Rental

 

A privileged, luxurious lifestyle awaits in the heart of the tropics at Coastal Pleasures, one of Marco Island’s premier mediterranean-style manors.

This stunning and spacious two-story, four bedroom island estate will enchant and captivate your seaside senses. Her resortlike atmosphere will embrace you from the second you walk through her doors. Sky-high ceilings stretch towards the sun and blue water views extend across one of Marco Island’s articulate waterways. Coastal Pleasures boasts magnificently landscaped outdoor spaces that will allow your family to make the most of life under the Florida sunshine.

Her generously proportioned common areas boast elegant decor with just the right hint of seaside flair. Meticulously detailed for the most comfortable island lifestyle available, Coastal Pleasures’s living spaces feature a gourmet kitchen with granite countertops, stainless steel appliances, electric fireplace, decorative island finishes and more. Her ground-level sliding glass doors lead directly onto a stunning waterfront lanai with a heated year-round pool and waterfall spa. Fish, moor a boat, relax or entertain on the large dock just beyond the lanai. With perfect western exposure, play, lounge and dine until the sun sets over the island.

Retire to one of her beautiful bedrooms for an afternoon siesta or full-night’s sleep. The elegant master bedroom is located on the ground floor and provides her guests with a spa-like ensuite master bath, as well as private lanai access. Upstairs, three large bedrooms await - two come complete with en suite bathrooms and birdseye views of outdoor Marco and the interior living space. The second story is also home to a delightful bonus room complete with a wet bar and walk out terrace with views down the waterway.

Coastal Pleasures is dressed with HDTV, WIFI, full compliment of finer linens throughout, fully stocked gourmet kitchen, electrically heated (year round) pool, beach gear, and far more!

From her spacious and stylish interior to her expansive outdoor areas, withdraw from the world and get acquainted and adjusted to island time at Coastal Pleasures, the perfect Marco Island vacation retreat.
