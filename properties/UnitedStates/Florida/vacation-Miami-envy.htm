Villa Envy, Miami, Florida Vacation Rental

 

This 4 story waterfront, ultra modern townhome catches your attention from a mile away. The captivating exterior wrought iron facade leaves the most esteemed traveler dazed and astonished by the sheer beauty and brilliance of the building. After a double take or two you realize you are nestled in the heart of Miami in a district that is quickly on the rise. 

A total of 7,663 sq ft of floor to ceiling impact windows, marble floors, and space galore amaze you as you walk through the unit. This 4 bedroom, 3.5 bathroom newly constructed (2006) waterfront gem includes a breathtaking fourth floor master suite with sitting area, enormous tub, and spectacular shower. Not to mention all new bathroom and light fixtures, as well as a beautiful modern kitchen with black marble countertops with wood, hidden storage compartments, and a Subzero fridge for your enjoyment.

You will enjoy a huge exterior 3rd floor terrace and an exterior rooftop deck with ocean views and several perfect tanning locations. Also on the rooftop lays a convenient heated spa for love under the moon.

This townhome also includes an elevator, a 2 car garage, laundry room, alarm system, wireless internet throughout, and is fully furnished. Don't forget about different thermostat controls for each floor, 5 strategically placed brand new A/C units throughout. This is a great spot for those looking for a large place to stay with privacy, yet something modern and inviting at the same time.  You can be in South Beach in just  under 10 minutes, the house is close to everything, which makes the idea of filming, photography, or longer rentals just seem perfect.
