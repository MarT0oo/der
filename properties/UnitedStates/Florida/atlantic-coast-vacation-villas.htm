About Atlantic Central Coast

You have stayed in many homes over the years we are sure, but there is so much more than the SE Coast of Florida to see.  There are homes that have Golden Beaches and soft Sands with unlimited family activities.  Those cities are sometimes the last people think about but they should be some of the one people visit most.

Vero Beach is very central and has become a great place for those who love the ocean and beachfront homes.  This area is great because you are only about 1 hour from Disney, you are close to Cape Canaveral, you can drive to Daytona and so much more.  It's a perfect blend for those looking for something special.

Indialatic offers a wonderful mesh of Sping Break Families and lovely surfing, sailing, and oceanfront villas for rent.  This location is great for those who want to come in through Melbourne Airport and want a short drive to their homes.  It's also great for family activities as well, especially if you want to visit the Kennedy Space Center or travel to a wonderful Zoo and local water park.

There are so many lovely locations in Central Atlantic, these are just a few of the cities not to miss: Astor, Cape Canaveral, Cocoa Beach, Daytona Beach, Fort Pierce, Melbourne, Merrit Island, New Smyrna Beach, Ormond, Palm Coast Holiday Rentals, Port St. Lucie, Stuart and Vero Beach.

You will enjoy a multitude of things during the summer and winter months and finally have a great vacation at a fraction of the cost of staying at a hotel.
