Windwood Bay Estate 3, Windwood Bay, Orlando, Florida Vacation Rental

 

Spend your tropical Disney vacation in this 5 bedroom 4.5 bathroom home located in the community of Windwood Bay. This home has three master bedrooms with ensuite bathrooms attached. Relax in your very own oversized private pool with no rear neighbors. Play some ping pong or a game of pool in the games room. pool facing North

The Windwood Bay community is a peaceful community with beautiful 3 -5 bedroom vacation homes.

The Windwood Bay community has the entire Champions Gate golf community in its backyard, with shopping plazas, restaurants and takes outs, the Omni Hotel landmark and of course the 5 golf courses within that resort and Reunion combined. The distance to the I-4 is less than 2 miles to access Walt Disney World theme parks as well as downtown Orlando, and Florida Beaches. Windwood Bay is 7 Miles to Disney, 21 Miles to Universal Studios, 2 Miles to Grocery/Shopping and 28 Miles to MCO Orlando International Airport.
