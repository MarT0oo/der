Captiva Breeze, Captiva Island, Florida Vacation Rental

 

Enjoy the breeze of the Gulf of Mexico from this four bedroom home’s rooftop sun deck after walks along the beach, swimming in your private, heated pool, dipping in the hot tub or gazing up at the stars.

Every guest will find their own way to unwind whether by the private pool, playing on one of the arcade arcade games, challenging someone to a game of pool or ping pong or by lounging in one of the many indoor or outdoor living spaces.

From top to bottom Captiva Breeze has been completely redecorated and upgraded with designer touches and will comfortably sleep up to 10 guests.

An exemplary, luxurious home awaits your arrival on private Wightman Lane. Just steps from the serenity of the Gulf of Mexico or the convenience of the Village Area of Captiva Island., Captiva Breeze is a four bedroom, four bathroom luxury vacation rental home with spacious indoor and outdoor living areas, a private heated pool and hot tub with waterfall and porches abound, including a rooftop sun deck, or captain's walk, with partial views of the bay and Gulf of Mexico.

On the main living level you will find a living area with cathedral ceilings, an expansive gourmet kitchen with high end granite counter tops with wine chiller, bar area, the large master bedroom suite, one guest suite with two twin beds, full bathroom accessible from the living area or twin bedroom as well as a full size washer and dryer. The kitchen and dining areas as well as the master bedroom suite all open up to the screen enclosed lanai overlooking the private pool.

The master bedroom suite includes an en-suite bathroom with a jetted tub large enough for two, a glass and tile oversized shower, dual sink vanity, walk-in closet and a separate commode. A private stairway leads you from the master bedroom suite to the private pool!

The second, or upper level of the home features two additional bedrooms each with their own private en-suite bathrooms. One bedroom has a king sized bed while the other spacious bedroom has a king bed as well as twin bunk beds with safety stairs, comfortably sleeping up to four guests in the one room. Both bedrooms have private access to the rooftop sun deck. Captiva Breeze's modern floor plan, multiple living areas and private elevator make this a fabulous Captiva Island vacation home for any family!

Outside Captiva Breeze is a screened-in, over sized and heated pool with hot tub and waterfall, four authentic 'Free Play' arcade machines, an outdoor pool table, ping-pong table, gas BBQ grill and lush landscaping for your own private tropical oasis!
