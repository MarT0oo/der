Keys Luxury Hideaway, Marathon, Florida Keys, Florida Vacation Rental

 

Keys Luxury Hideaway in Marathon was designed as a labor of love by John Ratliff, a serial entrepreneur. With over 10,000 square feet of amazing livable space spanning across the Main House and the Bali Guest House, Keys Luxury Hideaway has 6 bedrooms, 5.5 baths, 2 pools, a gourmet kitchen, a full outdoor spa, an expansive tiki bar with outdoor kitchen, an enclosed outdoor sports bar, a game room with billiards table, a fully stocked commercial style bar complete with 4 draft taps and a conference room with seating for up to 14 with full multimedia capabilities, and much more. Keys Luxury Hideaway is the perfect home for large gatherings of families, friends and executive teams, and can accommodate 14 to 18 people comfortably. Keys Luxury Hideaway is truly a slice of paradise that you and your group can just relax, rejuvenate and just get away from the hustle and bustle of everyday life... all in your own private oasis.

The center piece of the lower level of the Main Home is a massive outdoor (covered) sports bar that features 3 high definition televisions, as well as a pool that includes a swimmer’s treadmill. Once inside the Main Home you will find a full breakfast bar (complete with refrigeration and appliances) that opens up into a game room for fun gatherings for your entire group. Just off the Game Room in the Main Home there is a lower-level bedroom with 2 queen-sized beds and an ensuite bathroom.

The Guest Home, which is adjacent to the Main Home, is a Bali inspired structure that features a fully stocked bar area, large conference room and a half bath. The custom-built fold-away doors in the downstairs gathering room of the Guest Home can be opened up to create a 180 degree indoor/outdoor space overlooking the serene outdoor pool and spa.

The Upper Level of the Main Home is comprised of a beautiful gourmet chef’s kitchen, spacious dining area that seats up to 16 people.... plus seating for 6 additional guests on our covered dining space right off the kitchen!! The upstairs also boasts an airy, open concept living room with glass pocket doors that open up to a wrap around terrace, and 3 luxurious bedrooms. The bedrooms include the Master Suite, which features a rain shower and in-room service bar (with refrigeration), a second master suite, with a beautiful spa-style shower, and a guest bedroom with access to a separate full bath immediately next to the room.

The Main Home and Guest Home are connected by a walkway that overlooks the stunning grounds and natural setting that surrounds Keys Luxury Hideaway. Upon entry to the Upper Level of the Guest Home, the guest is greeted by an beautifully constructed lofted wooden ceiling. Just off the foyer inside the Upper Level of the Guest Home is a gorgeous master bedroom with three sided pocket doors that open onto a private deck. The Guest Home also boasts a second, spacious guest bedroom, and the two bedrooms in the Guest House share a spectacular spa-like bathroom that includes an 8 jet body spray rain shower and separate spa tub.

The Upper Level of the Guest Home includes a large loft area just above the landing, with 2 queen beds and a private seating area. It is a great play space for children and has plenty of room for adult guests as well.
