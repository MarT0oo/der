High Design Villa, Fort Lauderdale, Florida Vacation Rental

 

High Design is a new 3500 sq. ft home with 4 bedrooms and 4 baths located on the north fork of the New River in Fort Lauderdale. The home has a stunning 42 ft heated, salt water lap pool with spa overlooking the River. The home is very spacious with 12 foot high ceilings and large windows with custom plantation shutters in most rooms and custom remote control roll down shades in the great room overlooking the pool, spa and river. The home has a gracious front porch entry along with a second story large covered veranda off the second master bedroom, as well as a covered rear porch for al fresco dining. The rear of the home and three of the bedrooms overlook the pool, spa and river. The gourmet kitchen has granite counters, stainless appliances and the great room has a separate wet bar with wine storage. The great room has a 70 inch wall mounted smart flat screen TV, Blue-ray DVD and surround sound speakers, it overlooks the pool, spa, outdoor dining and entertaining areas and patios as well as the tranquil North fork of the New River.

The outdoor living area has dining for 8, outdoor grilling, music, 8 chaise lounges for sun and relaxation as well as a dock for relaxing, fishing and watching the river and sunsets. There is a separate large living room and formal dining room with seating for 8. There are 4 large bedrooms two have king size beds, and one has a queen size bed. Three of the bedrooms have views of the New River and the second master bedroom is at the front of the house with a large covered veranda over-looking the wooded neighborhood. The master suite has a 60 inch wall mounted flat screen TV with DVA and the master bathroom has a 42 inch wall mounted flat screen TV.
