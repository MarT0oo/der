Ocean View Penthouse
6 Bedroom | 4 Bathrooms | Oceanview
Tybee Beach, Georgia
From $3,000 per Week

Ocean View Penthouse encompasses the entire top floor of Tybee's newest South Beach condo development with unobstructed Ocean Views. Rent ONE SIDE of the Penthouse for up to 8 guests OR open the living room doors and rent BOTH SIDES of the Penthouse for up to 16 guests.

This 3,500 ft² family-sized Luxurious Penthouse is beautiful and functional for Tybee Beach living at its very best. The waterfront vacation living spaces have multiple seating areas for conversing and relaxing. For your entertainment, the penthouse comes complete with TV/DVD, cable and stereo. The spacious living/dining areas flow out onto 2 covered Ocean View balconies with dining space for 12. For family-sized inside dining you have 2 dining tables, 12 chairs, and 2 kitchen bars with 8 bar stools. When rented in its entirety, the Penthouse has 2 master bedrooms with king-sized beds, 2 bedrooms with queen-sized beds and 2 bedrooms with 2 twin-sized beds in each bedroom. There are also 2 queen-sized sleeper sofas in the living area; sleeping 16 guests total.

Enjoy this fabulous South Beach location only steps to the beach. Walk outside your door to Tybee's famous Pier, Pavilion, nightlife, downtown shops, restaurants, and the Marine Science Center. You never have to drive your car! Arrive, park and enjoy! All linens and towels provided except beach towels.
