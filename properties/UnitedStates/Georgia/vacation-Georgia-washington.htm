Washington Square House, Savannah Georgia Vacation Rental


Washington Square Collection can provide comfortable and private accommodations for up to 14 guests on the same property!!!

Luxury Living Savannah's Washington Square Collection is on Savannah’s picturesque Washington Square 2 blocks from River Street! This square, named for George Washington, is on Houston Street near the site of the old Trustees Garden.

This circa 1790 - 1810 Historic Washington Square House is an 'Award Winning' Historic Restoration. After setting vacant since 1992, miraculously retained the vast majority of its original details! The original stair case, heart pine floors, all doors and hardware, windows, bead board wainscoting, fireplaces and mantles, etc, etc.

The painstaking restoration was conducted by stringent guidelines laid out by the Department of Natural Resource in Washington D.C. These guidelines were followed in order to save the historic integrity of the home and its significant contribution to Savannah’s Landmark Historic District. All of this and and 2 blocks to River Street!

No expense was spared on the period correct modernization of this historic property. Fully outfitted gourmet kitchen with island seating for 3 people is next to the Formal Dining Room that can seat up to 8 diners. The Living Room has a large smart TV for group entertainment with historical influenced furnishings. The casual Sun Room enjooys the views of the tranquil courtyard and fountain for enjoying alfresco dining and relaxing, The 4 bedrooms all have en suite, spacious bathrooms!

And just a few steps away you have an addition 2 bedrooms and 2 baths in the private carriage house. The Carriage House is a free standing cottage behind the main house with its own private entrance off the lane. It is located on the 2nd floor over the garage. Like all carriage houses in Savannah, the carriage house is where the driver lived in the 1800s above the horses and carriages they cared for.

The Washington Square Collection comes with a highly coveted garage parking for 2 cars.
