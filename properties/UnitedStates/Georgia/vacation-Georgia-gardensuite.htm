Garden Suite, Savannah Georgia Vacation Rental


Walk out your door of the Wedding Mansion Garden Suite and see Savannah’s beautiful and popular Monterey Square. This charming street-level vacation rental apartment exudes historic elegance both in furnishings and original architectural details.


With high ceilings and two oversized bedrooms as well as luxurious bathrooms that offer contemporary amenities, guests can enjoy the historic ambiance of Savannah and the southern lifestyle but still revel in the modern offerings expected of a luxurious vacation rental. Enjoy a large plasma television (and a comfy sleeper sofa) in the Garden Suite’s living/dining area. And, a fully outfitted, gourmet kitchen, with cherry cabinets and Corian countertops, provides an enchanting view of the courtyard.


Step out of the living area to enjoy your own patio and Savannah’s year-round friendly climate. The Mansion’s courtyard features a stunning imported Italian Statue, Flora – the goddess of flowers, which graces a soothing fountain.
