Playa del Sueno, St. Simons Island, Georgia Vacation Rental

 

Play del Sueno absolutely lives up to its name. The Villa is a brand-new six-bedroom oceanfront estate that sleeps 15 in pure luxury. Situated on a compound at the edge of St. Simons Island's East Beach and overlooking Gould's Inlet, Sea Island, and the Atlantic Ocean; the Villa is a stunning example of Mediterranean architecture and decor. The ocean view from both the main house and the pavilion are simply breathtaking. Few properties on the Georgia Coast can offer this kind of beauty.

The Villa is equipped as you would expect from a property of this quality. The spacious entertainment pavilion features an outdoor entertainment system with iPod hookup, for you to dance and dream the night away. Playa del Sueno also has three kitchens, one of which is a gourmet cooks dream - complete with dual dishwashers and stainless steel appliances. The gorgeous infinity edge pool overlooks the ocean and has a heated spa that is perfect for the cool Winter and mild Fall and Spring evenings.
