Hills Architectural, Beverly Hills, California Vacation Rental


There is nothing like waking up in the morning in your own private mansion in Beverly Hills, where you can live like a celebrity and enjoy the atmosphere the dining and shopping.  This luxurious villa features a private swimming pool and is conveniently located in the 90210 Zip Code and just 5 minutes away from all of the luxury shopping and dining off of Rodeo Drive.  Don’t miss out on this completely perfect Villa Rental.

The house is 4,000 sq. feet and designed by a famous French Architect, who gave the house life with every curve and room placement in the house.  The house is decorated with a mix of Euro Styling and unique African antiques, which creates a perfect blend of new and old to give the house character.  The House has 4 main bedrooms and an additional area for the office and single guest Suite.  The Master Bedroom features a King sized bed and en-suite bathroom, the 2, 3, 4th bedrooms all feature Queen Sized beds and are also designer decorated.  Finally the very user friendly office features a twin which can be used for a child.  The room are large and give you a sense of Resort Living while being in the privacy of your own home.

Some of the additional features of the home include: A lovely salt water pool with swim jets which is great if you have children and prefer a more natural swimming experience.  A lovely manicured garden and patio area for you to enjoy during your stay.  Air conditioned rooms, wireless internet services, satellite televisions, free long distance phone calls, docking station for your iPod and stereo system throughout.
