Kamalame Private Island & Estate, Bahamas


This Private Island is a 96-acre private island hideaway with three miles of sugar-soft sandy beach stretching along its eastern shore just off the coast of Andros Island in The Bahamas. This barrier island is accessible only by our own private ferry or seaplane. This Private Island is the perfect choice for guests who value elegance, adventure, privacy, and the romance of a unique Caribbean experience.

Features Caribbean cuisine, beautiful beaches, a freshwater pool cut neatly into the Silver Palms that embrace the island, and some of the best fishing and diving found anywhere.

A handful of gracefully decorated private villas dot our island escape and face a rhythmic ocean surf that will lull you into an attitude that requires only bare feet, suntan lotion, and a cocktail, making this villa the perfect blend of romance and adventure. You can rent this Island by the villa or exclusively.  In order to assure you have your dates locked, please call us with enough advance notice, as this island is very popular. Here are just some of the great reasons to rent this private island

The Spa

The Spa on this island is the perfect refuge from the fast-paced, high stress world of today -- a place to concentrate on restoring and rejuvenating body, mind and soul. Our experienced Spa team will personalize a unique package of treatments to ensure you receive maximum benefit and complete relaxation from every visit.

Scuba Diving

The third longest barrier reef in the world is just a mile from the shores offering divers and snorkelers the perfect opportunity to enjoy the warm Caribbean waters while experiencing an exceptional underwater adventure.

Snorkeling

Snorkeling is an activity guests of all ages can safely enjoy on this Island. The water off the beach is shallow on the south side, perfect for wading, kayaking and hunting for shells. From the Spa North, the water is deeper providing the opportunity for a quick dip or a way to exercise off the afternoon cookies. Andros Island’s barrier reef is alive with an incredible diversity of beautiful fish and colorful coral.

The Great House

The Great House on the Cay serves as a perfect place to rendezvous for meals, cocktails, hors d’oeuvres and tales of the day’s adventures. This gathering place, with its inviting wrap-around veranda, is tastefully decorated with art, antiques, and a collection of Bahamian furnishings including comfortable oversized furniture. Enjoy our Great House, built in 19th century British tradition, and relax with a glass of wine while drinking in the tranquilizing panoramic view of the Caribbean.

Weddings and Honeymoons

We have been privileged to celebrate several weddings and honeymoons here. Let the romance of the Caribbean be the backdrop to this most memorable occasion. Our friendly and accommodating staff will be pleased to help you arrange the ceremony, your party’s accommodation needs, and any personal touch you desire to make this special event complete. Our chef will help you create a personalized menu for you and your guests. We welcome the opportunity to host your special day, and we’ll even decorate your wedding golf cart.
