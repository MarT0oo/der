Petit St. Vincent, Private Island, Grenadines Vacation Rental

 

Located in the southern tip of the Grenadines, our exclusive island retreat is more than just a luxury holiday—it’s an escape. Create lifetime memories with a loved one, friends, or family on our lush 115-acre tropical island. With just 22 spacious one bedroom cottages and two bedroom beach villas, you can enjoy the privacy you desire with gorgeous views of the Caribbean Sea.

Our accommodations are free from telephones, Wi-Fi and television. Instead, our signature flag system is a simple but effective way to communicate with our attentive staff. The yellow flag signals for room service, transportation, or a special request and the red flag for privacy.

With two restaurants, one hillside and one beachside, our gourmet chefs offer a variety of creative cuisine inspired by locally-caught seafood and our organic island-grown fruit and vegetables. During sunset, weekly beach barbecues with great local music make for a romantic dinner under the starlit skies.

Unwind with our low-key on-island activities: two yoga pavilions, a Balinese-run luxury spa and wellness center, and an array of land and water sports. Boat charters are available for day excursions to the nearby Tobago Cays marine reserve.

Let our island be that magical destination for a Caribbean honeymoon or a once-in-a-lifetime event. For large groups rent the entire island for the ultimate getaway.

Petit St. Vincent can be booked exclusively for just you and your invited guests for the ultimate island fantasy. Our private island resort is the perfect venue for a special anniversary, destination beach wedding, an executive event, or even a long-awaited family reunion.

With two restaurants and bars, an extensive wine cellar, a Balinese-managed hillside spa and wellness center, seven pristine beaches and over 130 staff members dedicated to you—we will ensure that your event will be memorable and extra special.

Our 22 ocean view cottages can accommodate groups of up to 56 guests for any occasion, however extra beds are available upon request for children.
