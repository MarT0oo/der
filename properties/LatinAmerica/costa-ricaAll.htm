Visiting Costa Rica

Traveling to Costa Rica in Central America is like going back in time when the rainforests, the beautiful hidden waterfalls, and the treasures of the world seemed pure and completely symbiotic with our own lives.  This richly natural and diverse culture is attracting people from all over the world to make Costa Rica their number one travel destination.  It is no wonder that Many of the older generations who are retiring are now choosing to retire in Costa Rica.  The beaches are pristine, the weather is amazing year round, and the local residents are wonderful.

While visiting try cities like: Manuel Antonio, Dominical, Peninsula Papagayo, Quepos, and so much more. These coastal cities are perfect for those looking for beach homes or cottages or even estate rentals. 

Additionally here are some fun things for family and friends to do:  Butterfly Gardens, amazing place to go with children http://www.butterflygardencr.com/, Adventure Tours http://www.adventurecostarica.com/, A different kind of bowling http://www.metrobowl.net/index2.html, Visit Natural Parks http://www.costarica-nationalparks.com/brauliocarrillonationalpark.html.
