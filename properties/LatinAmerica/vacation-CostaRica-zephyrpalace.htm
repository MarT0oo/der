Zephyr Palace, Jaco, Costa Rica Vacation Rental


The Zephyr Palace, a luxury mansion that shines like a jewel among others and is appreciated not only for its location, design and decoration, but also for the outstanding facilities and first class service.


Zephyr Palace Caletas expresses a truly unique combination of mountain and beach resort. Situated atop of a mountain at 350 feet above the Pacific Ocean, in the Central Pacific coast of Costa Rica, this luxury boutique hotel, manages to see stunning 360 degree views over the Gulf of Nicoya, from the islands of the Nicoya Peninsula, to the beautiful beaches of Jac and Herradura.


With a magnificent view from the mountaintop, the rainforest spreads toward the beautiful sandy beach. The dark-emerald green vegetation contrasts with the blue Pacific Ocean, and the tropical rain forest becomes the best complement for the most beautiful sunsets you can witness. At the bottom of the mountain, Zephyr Palace has its own private beach called Caletas. This area, one of the favorite regions for nature lovers, is best known for the colorful flocks of scarlet macaws.
