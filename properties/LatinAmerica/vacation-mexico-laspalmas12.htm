Las Palmas Villa 12
3 Bedrooms | 3 Baths | Pool House | Golf Community                          
Punta Mita, Mexico
  From $1200 - $4200 USD per night

Incredible, direct views of the premier ocean golf hole – “Tail of the Whale” (the only natural island green in the world) – are part of every gaze from your chaise by the pool or virtually any window.  Just a stone's throw away from the Residents' Beach Club, Golf Club House and tennis courts, this beautifully decorated Villa (decorated by the same designer who did the Four Seasons) is perfectly located.

From the terrace, with its outdoor Viking grill, you will have the perfect place to enjoy the beautiful nights of Punta Mita.  When you open the main door of this wonderfully designed 3 bedroom ocean view Villa, you will appreciate the beautiful indoor patio with water feature, look-through living room, spacious terrace, golf course, and ocean – in the blink of an eye. As soon as you have had the chance to do this, you will realize that there might not be a better place to spend your precious time than right here.

Sit on the patio and listen to the music of waves curling against rocks of the “Tail of the Whale” island green after a day of golf, surfing, whale watching, kayaking or snorkeling at the beach – just a hundred or so yards away.
   
These spacious homes also boast a gourmet kitchen with a Wolf gas range, outdoor shower, and state-of-the-art technology that includes fiber-optic wiring and wireless Internet.  Features include a courtyard entryway, granite slab countertops, exterior wet bar and grill, a SubZero refrigerator/freezer, and much more.  Also available are Vonage telephones which allow you to call almost anywhere as if you were in the States. 

There is a High Definition plasma TV in the main living area which includes a satellite connection from the U.S.   You will be able to enjoy TV with DVD and US channels. Plus, there’s a washer and dryer.   You will have everything you need to have the vacation of your life.  This villa is located on hole #4 of the world renowned Jack Nicholas-designed Four Seasons golf course which was voted #1 resort golf club by Conde Nast Magazine. The spectacular ocean view golf course villa includes over 3,700 square feet of living space and a two car garage. Includes 3 bedrooms (1 with a King size bed and 1 with two full size beds and 1 with two twin beds). Comfortably sleeps 6 or 8.
