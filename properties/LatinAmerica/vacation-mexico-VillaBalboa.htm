Villa Balboa, Conchas Chinas, Puerto Vallarta, Mexico Vacation Rental

 

This 12,000 square foot, contemporary , waterfront villa is minutes away from old town Puerto Vallarta and comfortably sleeps 18.

Behind the private entry gates are suites offering stunning views, all with private terraces and spectacular views of the bay and city; a private apartment; plus a staff or nanny quarters. Villa Balboa features many water elements including fountains, gardens and Koi ponds.

Comforts and amenities that guests will undoubtedly enjoy are the fully equipped state-of-the-art gourmet kitchen; the private screening cinema with seating for 20; the elevator; the private dance floor and the art-deco bar. To complete the experience, beds have dream foam toppers to make you feel like you are sleeping on a cloud.

The clean, contemporary design is the perfect setting for traditional and custom made one-of-a-kind pieces, and showcases original colorful art and decor, giving a sophisticated ambiance that embraces the flavors of Mexico.
