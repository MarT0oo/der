Villa EOS, Guanacaste, Black Beauty Village, Costa Rica Vacation Rental

 

This majestic two-story Costa Rican villa looks out over the Pacific Ocean from its lofty height above lush vegetation. This four-bedroom ultra-luxury home is over 8,000 square feet of covered living space. You’ll be enthralled with the sleek design, open layout, perfect blend of indoor and outdoor areas, generous number of private bedrooms, abundance of natural light and air flow, and postcard-perfect ocean views from every room. Eos’ clean, contemporary furnishings are complemented by tropical plants, artwork and decorative pieces thoughtfully placed with your comfort in mind.

This sublime Costa Rican luxury home is available for your next vacation and will create memories to treasure for a lifetime! Entering the ground floor, you’ll be impressed with the well-appointed gourmet kitchen complete with first-rate appliances, plenty of storage, a large island for prepping or dining, and an ocean view for the chef! The kitchen opens directly onto a dining room and spacious living room. There’s an outdoor living area right outside of the living room. There’s an outdoor dining area if you decide to take your meals al fresco, and a separate outdoor entertaining space with BBQ. There are two Queen Bedrooms on the ground floor, each with their own bathroom and clear ocean view.

Your vacation will begin the moment you enter Eos. This exquisite luxury home will delight you with its timeless comfort, surprising amenities and spacious floor plan.
