Villa Carina
12 Bedrooms | 13 Baths | Pool | Oceanview | Indoor Parking           

Mangarabita (Angra), Brazil

  From $9800 - $19600 USD per week

Villa Carina is a 12 bedroom villa in Rio de Janeiro state, Mangaratiba (Angra) offering a wonderful sea view.  This luxury accommodation is great for those searching for a large villa that can accommodate larger groups while traveling.  On the water, this house has a wonderful ambiance that invites you to stay in and relax. 

The Villa boasts 4 floors which include 7 Queen Bedrooms, 4 bedrooms with two twins in each and one bedroom with 3 twins.  The villa sleeps 25 guests in total and is extremely spacious with 993 sq. meters.  In addition there are 13 full bathrooms and 7 toilet rooms. 

There is a modern living room, TV room, playroom, leisure area and kitchen.  The villa boasts such amenities as:  bathroom heater, wireless internet connection, computer, air conditioner, DVD, cable TV and fireplace.

The outside spaces include:  veranda over the sea, barbecue and pizza oven, two swimming pools (open and covered), sauna, Jacuzzi, elevators, pier and garage.

This villa offers 4 permanent house keepers to assist guests in whatever they need.
