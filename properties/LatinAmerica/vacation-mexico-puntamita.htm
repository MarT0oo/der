Casa Joya del Mar, Punta Mita, Nayarit, Mexico


Casa Joya del Mar, a private vacation estate within the prestigious gated community of Punta Mita, is located a mere 45 minute drive from the Puerto Vallarta, Mexico International Airport (PVR). Punta Mita enjoys the prestige of being awarded one of the top golf communities in the world, by Conde Naste, and once you have visited you will understand why! Our villa is located within the inner gates of Lagos del Mar Estates, one of the private residential communities with Punta Mita Resort. This elegant private community of 24 custom estates enjoys stunning views of the Bay of Banderas as well as downtown Puerto Vallarta.

Our unique 6 bedroom villa, offers over 9,500 ft² of luxury living designed to cater to your every whim. Built by famed Architect Jose Luis Nino Preciado, and exclusively decorated by Alder and Tweed, Casa Joya del Mar is the essence of casual elegance. You will enjoy the soothing sounds of the villa’s 3 magnificent fountains, including an exotic stone wall fountain inside the inner courtyard. Although designed to be a haven from the world, you will find every amenity to afford a peaceful, carefree stay. From Wi-Fi, Vonage telephone to flat screen televisions with Dish satellite and a Sonos sound system, it’s all here for your enjoyment. Our motto is attention to detail, anticipating your every need. We invite you to enjoy this beautiful villa as your own private retreat!

Casa Joya del Mar is positioned adjacent the 7th fairway and 100 yards from the green of the Punta Mita Four Seasons Jack Nicklaus Signature Golf course. The ocean, just beyond the 7th hole offers stunning scenic views by day and the moon over the ocean by night. Providing its guests every luxury, in an unparalleled setting, the Villa offers panoramic ocean and golf course views from its 4 master suites, and is beautifully nestled in a private idyllic setting meant to afford its guests the ultimate in relaxation while enjoying the finest of indoor-outdoor living year round. Featuring a 40 ft. swimming pool with adjacent hot tub, you will be pampered as you swim and languish poolside while, Francisco, our concierge, refills your drinks!

As our guests, during your stay, you are afforded Premier member access to 3 beautiful beach clubs: The Residents Beach Club, The Kapuri Beach Club and the St. Regis Beach Club. In addition, you may also enjoy two 18 hole Jack Nicklaus Signature courses; Pacifico and Bahia, as well as the award winning tennis and fitness centers.

As part of your stay we invite you to enjoy the villa’s many amenities which include airport pick-up and drop-off, pre- arrival grocery shopping, as well as daily housekeeping and concierge services. Our full time staff will prepare breakfast and lunch, and we can arrange for a professional chef to prepare the evening meal. Also during your stay at Casa Joya del Mar you have use of the villa’s Suburban and the 6 passenger golf cart within the gates of Punta Mita. Francisco, our concierge, can arrange tour and excursion bookings (fishing, whale watching, zip line, etc.), tee times, as well as a visit to the house by a private masseuse.

WELCOME TO PARADISE!

Punta Mita is paradise. Many guests prefer staying close to the Casa to enjoy golf, tennis and beach activities offered through the Clubhouse or Residents Beach Club, all available via a free shuttle service. But other adventures await only minutes away. You can experience whale watching, surfing, snorkling, sailing, kayaking, rainforest touring, horseback riding, jeep safaris and day trips to Puerto Vallarta and Sayulita. Arrangements are offered through Punta Mita’s Residential Concierge or third-party companies. Many activities can be customized upon request. Take a stroll through…

Residents Beach Club

As a guest of Casa Joya del Mar, you’ll have access to two Residents’ Beach Clubs. Located within gated Punta Mita, Residents’ Beach Clubs offer pristine beaches, dining, swimming pools, kayaking, massage, snorkeling, comfortable beach furnishings and concierge services.

ProShop & Clubhouse

From the Four Seasons Clubhouse, you can tee off for a round of golf on the Jack Nicklaus Signature Course, enjoy the driving range or tennis courts, follow up your activities with a spa treatment, and then relax with drinks or a meal in the outdoor restaurant.
