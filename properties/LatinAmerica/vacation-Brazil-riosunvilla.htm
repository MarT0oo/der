Rio Sun Villa
5 Bedrooms | 4.5 Baths | Relaxation Pool | Office                             
Rio De Janeiro, Brazil
  From $14000 - $18200 USD per week

Inaugurated in 2005, our naturally luxurious five-bedroom villa, Rio Sun Villa, combines the style and charm of the Colonial period with modern comforts and conveniences. We offer short-term rental of the entire house, perfect for small groups of up to 13 people seeking privacy, tranquility or extra living space. The Solar’s décor combines custom-made furniture, natural textiles and our private collection of design objects with popular art handpicked by the owners on their numerous trips throughout Brazil.

The PROPERTY consists of:


• Over 500 m2 of interior space and over 2000 m2 of outdoor patios and gardens
• 4 bedrooms in the main house with queen size beds (total of 3 bedrooms with en-suite bathrooms, and two extra rooms), 3 of which open up onto a veranda with hammocks and panoramic views + 1 private Bungalow outside the main house with king size bed and 4 full bathrooms and 1 toilette
• Ample cozy living room and office space
• Fully equipped kitchen, as well as a kitchenette on second floor
• Dining room with 19th century rustic dining table comfortably seating up to 12
• Veranda and garden terrace, with permanent bar, outdoor lounge and dining area
• Lush tropical garden of plants and fruits trees, herb and vegetable garden
• Relaxation pool and sundeck
• 2-car garage
