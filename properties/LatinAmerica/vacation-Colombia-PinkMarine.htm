Pink Marine, Rosario Islands, Cartagena, Colombia Vacation Rental

 

This 4 bedroom island home equips you with all of the amenities necessary to have the relaxing vacation on the Caribbean that you were looking for. There is ample lounge space for you to enjoy the endless ocean and sun. The home is perfect for island living with wood beams and colors throughout you won't be disappointed.

From every window, you can catch a glimpse of the crystal waters, whether sitting in your dining room to enjoy a tasty meal or lounging in bed before your day starts. Take a dip off the dock or wade out in your two private beaches to throughout the day to cool off from the hot Colombian sun. This home comes with 4 bedrooms making it perfect for a small group of family or friends.

Each bedroom is equipped with air conditioning and comfortable beds. Pink Marine is the perfect place for enjoying the sun and the beaches of the fantastic Rosario Islands. The price includes cleaning as well as breakfast and lunch preparation from the in house cook. Groceries are not included.
