Casa Theodore
11 Bedrooms | 11.5 Baths
Cabo San Lucas
From $2,700 per Night

Casa Theodore is a spectacular 11 bedroom/11.5 bath Villa located in The Pedregal, a private gated community just outside of Cabo San Lucas. A mere five minute taxi ride can whisk you away from the hustle and bustle of town and deliver you to this private villa. The only thing topping the gorgeous blue water views of the Pacific, are the stunning sunsets that will take your breath away.

Walk down the curvy staircase and you're immediately surrounded by lush, tropical flowers complete with the soothing sounds of ocean waves crashing and flowing water from the home rock waterfall. Enter the oversized, hand-carved wooden doors and you've stepped into a beautifully furnished modern villa, complete with plush leather furniture and original paintings. Mexican artwork is provided by local artisans within the home interior giving it that contemporary yet authentic feel. Enjoy the incredible white water ocean views from every room within the Villa or on the resort styled luxury pool deck. Nothing comes close to the breathtaking views from the heated Infinity pool.

At 16,500 sq. feet, it is one of the largest private villas in the Pedregal. The main house can be rented in conjunction with the guest house and/or apartment and the locking central stairway allows complete access while still maintaining privacy. Perfect for a large group or a multi-generational family, Casa Theodore is sure to be the villa to serve your every need. Every room in the Villa encompasses a full granite bathroom. Each is oversized to give you freedom of space and complete privacy. Enjoy personal relaxation in a deep tub or rinse off in a separate walk-in shower. Spacious and invitingly styled with contemporary Cabo furnishings, the living and dining areas can be used to entertain all guests. Reservations for this Villa include complimentary wireless Internet access. Enjoy state-of-the art amenities in a setting characterized by serenity, comfort and pleasure.

Casa Theodore appeals to a wide range of travelers because of the many things to do within and around the Villa and Cabo itself – including the fine art of doing nothing at all. Explore the romantic, adventurous, luxurious, and relaxing way of life at Casa Theodore.
