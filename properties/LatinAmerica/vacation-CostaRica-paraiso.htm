This exquisite Guatemalan-style villa, located in the “Las Olas” neighborhood, is the largest and most prestigious home from Dominical to the South Pacific. The breathtaking architecture and elegant decor, amazing 180º white-water views and exclusive facilities make this house a truly unique vacation rental. Comfortably accommodating up to 10 people, Villa Paraiso promises to fulfill all of your expectations for a top-notch Costa Rican vacation.

 

This is an authentic Guatemalan architecture with over 8,500 square feet of living space with 5 bedrooms and 5.5 baths.

 

The upstairs master bedroom as a wraparound terrace, Jacuzzi and double shower.  The villa comes fully-furnished with custom furniture, antiques and commissioned artwork throughout house.  There is a beautiful gourmet kitchen and full outdoor kitchen complete with refrigerator and barbecue.  Additionally there is an infinity pool with two built-in chaise lounges and swim up bar with umbrella, wine cellar, a theater for 12 with full projection system, state-of-the-art surround sound and graded seating.  Additionally there is an open-air courtyard with fountain and rustic wood columns.
