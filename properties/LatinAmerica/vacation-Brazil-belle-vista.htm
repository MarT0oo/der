Villa Belle Vista
4 Bedrooms | 6 Baths | Pool | Gym | Barbeque                                        
Geribá Beach, Buzios, Brazil
  From $3850 - $9300 USD per week

Welcome to Bella Vista Buzios, built by renowned architect, Octavio Gabaglia.  The villa is the last house on the top of Geribá Hill, boasting ultimate privacy and fantastic panoramic views of Tuscans, Geribá and Manguintios, coastlines, bays and open sea.  Access to the primary beach of Geribá is a short walk down the hill.

Bella Vista Buzios has 4 bedrooms, all with en-suite bathrooms.  The master includes a private study/office area.  Most bedrooms have their own terraces.  The villa has a beautiful pool, deck and barbeque area with views all around.  It is close to golf, tennis, beaches, city center, cinema, night clubs, bars and restaurants and shops.
