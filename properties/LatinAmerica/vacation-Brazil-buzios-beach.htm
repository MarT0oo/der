Buzios Beach House
6 Bedrooms | 6.5 Baths | Pool | Beachfront                                           
Buzios, Brazil
  From $5625 - $16750 USD per night

This Buzios Beach House is a Luxury villa, ideal for your family and friends to spend their vacation, feeling as if in your own home.  Located in the charming town of Búzios, this 6 bedroom home comfortably accommodates 12. 

This house is situated at Ferradura beach, in Búzios, a charming fisherman's village, just 105 miles from Rio de Janeiro.

 

Ferradura's exclusiveness and uniqueness is due to the combination of its beautiful surroundings, clean and calm waters (the result of its horseshoe format), and the most refined residences of Búzios. One step out of the door and you are in the sand, a few more and you will be in the quiets waters of Ferradura. It's an invitation to people of all ages who love to practice water sports, swimming, or enjoying the sun.

 

It has a fully equipped kitchen, microwave, all dish and glassware, two refrigerators, etc., wide band internet, air conditioning, hot water, ceiling fans, cable TV, 6 persons outside Jacuzzi, BBQ grille, wet sauna, towels and linens.

Besides all this, there are two living rooms and a dinning room with panoramic beach view and a garage for 5 cars.

 

To support your stay, every day there will be one made responsible for cleaning, cooking and eventually making 'caipirinhas'. If you decide to go out, you can eat at refined restaurants at 'Rua das Pedras' (Stones´s Street/ in downtown Búzios), just a few minutes from Ferradura Beach.
