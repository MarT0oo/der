Casa La Laguna, Los Cabos, Mexico Vacation Rental


Casa La Laguna is the definitive Los Cabos Luxury Beachfront Villa. Located on a semi-private beach, this 7 bedroom luxury villa provides an incredible, caring staff of 9 including an amazing gourmet chef, assistant chef, in-villa butler daily from 12:00pm-10:00pm, in-villa spa with world class masseuse Tina at very fair rates, gym, media room, in-villa concierge services during your stay, and one round trip airport transfer, all included in the rate! We’re nearly the ONLY 7 bedroom beachfront villa in all of Los Cabos this is gourmet full service included in the rate. All other villas are villa with housekeeping only. So you need to add $600-$700 per day just for chef, assistant chef, and butler if outsourced, with no guarantee of quality.

Spacious and sprawling, Casa La Laguna is one of the largest and most luxurious beachfront homes available in Los Cabos. The 7 bedroom villa accommodates 15-18 guests and offers over 11,000 ft² of living covered living space, 9,000 ft² of which is air conditioned. Additionally, the estate offers over 16,000 ft² of terrace and outdoor living space. Bali meets Mexican Hacienda in this four level compound, all with unobstructed views of the beach, tide pools, and the Sea of Cortez; spectacular!

We have 7 Sea of Cortez view suites, and 9 bathrooms. The sparkling two level pool offers an upper wading pool, a 15 foot slide, and a lower inﬁnity pool large enough for lapping. The huge beachfront Jacuzzi sits directly above beach with space for 12-15 guests. World class restaurants and resorts with stunning settings, and high-end shopping are 11 minutes away at the traditional Mexican colonial town of San Jose del Cabo, with the more vivacious Cabo San Lucas also within easy reach.

Located 5 minutes from the well-known Los Cabos Farm to Table scene, which includes Flora Farms, ACRE, and Los Tamarindos; must stops during your stay at Casa La Laguna. Renowned Jack Nicklaus and Greg Norman golf courses and a world class marina (with Swim With the Dolphins, whale watching, and fishing/yacht charters) are also 5 minutes away at Puerto Los Cabos. ATV riding (brought to the villa) and horseback riding, swimming, snorkeling, surﬁng, and ﬁshing are all immediately adjacent.

At Casa La Laguna, its world class staff and incredible setting will exceed your expectations in every respect.
