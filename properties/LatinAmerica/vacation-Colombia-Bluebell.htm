Bluebell, Barú, Cartagena, Colombia Vacation Rental

 

Bluebell is the perfect place for an ideal stay with friends and family. This fantastic home out of the noise of the city was designed with privacy and rest in mind. An escape from the bustle of the city, this spacious three bedroom home is fully-equipped with air conditioning in all bedrooms. The first bedroom has a Queen-size bed, the second bedroom has 2 singles and one double bed. Both the first and the second bedroom share the same bathroom. Upstairs it is the master bedroom with a big King-size bed and ensuite bathroom.

The kitchen and dining room are designed with space and freedom in mind, allowing you to prepare your meals while spending quality time with your friends and family. The home has its own private pool as well as access to a tranquil private beach: a spa, tennis court, community pool and streets for leisure golf cart riding are also available in the neighborhood. To ensure that your stay is an experience to remember, both a butler and a maid are present to help you with any culinary or cleaning needs.

The price includes cleaning as well as breakfast and lunch preparation from the in house cook. Groceries are not included.
