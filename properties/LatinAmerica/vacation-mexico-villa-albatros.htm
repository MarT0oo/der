Villa Albatros, Cancun, Mexico


Villa Albatros offers luxury accommodations. Here where the Maya civilization embraces the blue and turquoise waters of the Mexican Caribbean, vacationers can revel in an incredible blend of colonial charm and outdoor activities. The combination of the villa's unique architectural design built on different levels will provide well-being, peace, relaxation and privacy. With 11,500 sq ft (1,100 m2) of total interior space, its handcrafted wood, glass works and majestic chandeliers will make you feel at home. The two remarkable colonial style fountains in the three-story high vaulted ceiling living room area, will give you an elegant and unique feeling. This fabulous villa offers all that is stylish about living in Mexico.

The master suite is located on the top floor. It features a king-size bed, walk-in closet, powder room, as well as, a large marble bathroom with a Jacuzzi and a private balcony overlooking the blue sea. The first floor offers a large luxury room with two queen-size beds, a private terrace with stunning views overlooking the ocean, a walk-in closet and marble bathroom.Â  On the second floor you will find our premium ocean view room with two queen-size beds, a private terrace with fantastic ocean views and its own bathroom. On the ground floor are our two water-view premium bedrooms featuring king-size beds, full marble bathrooms, shared kitchenette, walk-in closets and private patios. These two bedrooms have direct access to the garden, beach and swimming pool areas.

The Villa's garden apartment is fully equipped with its own complete kitchen, king size bed, sofa, dining room area, private entrance and garden views.
