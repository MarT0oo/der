Casa Luna, Guanacaste, Black Beauty Village, Costa Rica


An modern architectural masterpiece featuring spectacular ocean views from every room. This ultra-modern tropical villa has 3 comfortably appointed bedrooms, 2 ½ baths, outdoor showers and modern furnishings throughout. An open design separates the dining, living and play spaces. There are 46” flat screen TVs and Blue Ray DVD systems in the entertainment and living areas. The kitchen is spacious and fully equipped with modern conveniences. With a private infinity edge pool, and breathtaking views of the Pacific Ocean and lush mountains, Luna is the ultimate vacation getaway.
