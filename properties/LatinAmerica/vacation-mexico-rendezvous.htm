Villa Rendezvous
3 Bedrooms Ensuite | 3.5 Baths | Amazing Views
Punta Ballena, Cabo San Lucas
From $ 950 per Night

Villa Rendezvous is a 3,750 square foot, 3 bedroom and bath luxury penthouse condominium. It is located in the prestigious Auberge Private Residences in Punta Ballena, Cabo San Lucas, next to the Esperanza resort and offers an amazing view of the Sea of Cortez. This villa comes with a fully equipped kitchen, a spacious living room for intimate gatherings and a great outdoor space perfect for grilling. As an appreciated guest at Auberge Private Residences you will have exclusive access to the new Private Residence Clubhouse, restaurant and fitness facility.

From Villa Rendezvous, you are just a short walk to the Spa at Esperanza which is rated the #1 spa in Mexico and Central and South America by Travel+ Leisure Magazine in October, 2007. Renting this vacation home includes pre-arrival planning with your dedicated vacation planning, concierge services 24 hours a day 7 days a week while you are in Cabo and welcome drinks upon your arrival. Housekeeping is provided Monday-Saturday. Staying in Villa Rendezvous will definitely make you feel right at home.

Villa Rendezvous is located 40 minutes from the Los Cabos International Airport, 8 minutes from Cabo San Lucas, and 25 minutes from San Jose del Cabo.
