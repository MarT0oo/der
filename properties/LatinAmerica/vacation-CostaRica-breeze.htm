Casa Breeze, Guanacaste, Black Beauty Village, Costa Rica


The gold standard in sustainable eco-luxury... Breeze is an expansive, modern estate situated on a secluded large parcel tucked into the Costa Rican hillside with spectacular jungle and ocean views. Encompassing over 4,000 square feet of indoor and outdoor living space, the villa is an ideal home-away-from-home. Three sumptuously decorated bedrooms provide comfortable accommodations for up to 8 people. And the spacious open floor plan provides breathtaking views of the ocean and jungle.

 

The home’s master has a wonderful open view with en-suite bathroom and glass doors from top to floor.  The guests suites are all designer decorated and make you feel as if you are staying in a 5 star Resort.  And we can make you feel as if you are in a 5 star resort with all of the services we can offer you, including chef and grocery shopping as well as concierge services that can arrange day trips and so much more.  Costa Rica is known for its Eco Friendly residences and its nature preserves so while in town do not forget to tour this beautiful country.
