Villa Tierra, Guanacaste, Black Beauty Village, Costa Rica


Enjoy phenomenal views of the Pacific Ocean and ruby red skies at sunset. Tierra is a charming contemporary luxury home with spectacular ocean views that is fully immersed in its jungle setting. The house has 3 large bedrooms, and the master suite’s private deck is equipped with Jacuzzi tub. The villa gives the impression of being in an open air setting and affords plentiful views of the jungle, mountains and Pacific Ocean. You and your guests can awake to the thrill of various birds or the distant calls of howler monkeys. And the jungle setting offers a lot of privacy in which to enjoy your stay without unwanted interruptions. Tierra offers an ideal base from which you can relax poolside, explore the tropical countryside, or enjoy stunning sunset views over the Pacific.

Each morning you will wake up in your rooms with a wide open view of the lush and tropical landscapes.  The rooms are designer decorated and have every amenity you would find in a 5 Star Resort.  The lovely Euro Style kitchen with modern furnishings help bring the home together.  As well you will receive a first class manager and staff available if you need anything while in town.  There is a VIP package that can be added to this home to complete the experience.
