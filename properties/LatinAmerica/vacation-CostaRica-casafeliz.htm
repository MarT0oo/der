Casa Feliz, Manuel Antonio, Costa Rica Vacation Rental


This exquisite 4 bedroom, 4.5 bath luxury home provides the ultimate getaway in modern tropical living. Each bedroom suite provides top-of-the-line bedding and carefully selected furnishings to ensure comfort and luxury. All the rooms are separated from the main social areas in order to ensure privacy throughout your stay. The theme of peace and intimacy is maximized as each guest has their own private bedroom balcony, complete with furniture - and in the case of the Master Suite - your very own tree which was lovingly built around.


Casa Feliz is decorator finished with unique features from both local and international artisans. You will enjoy fully air conditioned interior living spaces while floor to ceiling glass doors and windows throughout the home, plus exterior covered walkways, provide optimum nature observing opportunities, allowing you the possibility to savor the sunset, appreciate the vibrant gardens, or spy a monkey jumping through the trees.


Casa Feliz is afforded the luxuries of a full-time staff, including housekeeping and concierge services, plus the possibility of requesting in-home dining experiences, led by our Executive Chef. The outdoor BBQ and entertainment area includes an infinity-edge pool overlooking the Pacific Ocean and is completed with a bi-folding window which creates a pool bar for you and your friends. This home is perfect for you to soak up the sun until sunset, barbecue, observe the exotic wildlife or just experience a little rest and relaxation.
