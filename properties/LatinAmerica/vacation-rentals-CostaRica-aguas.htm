Las Aguas Jungle Lodge, Costa Rica Vacation Rentals


Luxury lodge home in a beautiful, secluded mountain setting.   The villa is 12 miles down to the ocean at Dominical.

 

The main house (6000 sf/3 levels) sits in a private jungle reserve of 66 acres which has 5 large waterfalls, two rivers, spring fed creeks and kilometers of hiking trails. The house sleeps 10 guests; there is an in-ground pool and 9 people spa.  Giant chess is in the garden.  Separate laundry facility and work out area. Maintenance and housekeeping staff is on site. For a nominal fee, a cook can be arranged for breakfast, lunch and/or evening meals. Menus will be tailored to your preferences.  

 

Athena Espresso Bar offers a selection of locally grown and roasted coffees all day.  A well-equipped fitness center, a cinema/media room with nightly movie, and a library with Internet/Wi-Fi access are all in the main lodge. There is large hot tub in the gardens, perfect to enjoy the cool mountain evenings.

The adventurous guests can explore kilometers of hiking trails through the new and old growth forest, swim in a cool rushing stream, relax in a tranquil jungle grotto and play in the five waterfalls right here at the resort. Everyone else can take in the sun at the pool.
