Hacienda Corazon, Puerto Aventuras, Riviera Maya, Mexico


Hacienda Corazon, Puerto Aventuras is nestled in the soft white sands and lapis waters of the Fatima Bay Beach. Hacienda Corazon "House of Hearts" is an incredible one of a kind villa!

From the moment you walk through the villa gates you will be enchanted, the heart shaped tropical garden complete with cenote leads to the impressive villa. Entering the home you are transported to a world of luxury hacienda style. The center of the villa is a courtyard, from which you can see the true blue of the ocean beckoning you. On the lower floor, off the courtyard there are 2 living rooms, one of which has a huge flat screen TV with satellite, enormous gourmet kitchen with all modern conveniences and the Cariño Suite with 2 queen beds and ensuite bathroom. There is also a massage room, where you can enjoy blissful relaxing massages. There is a large ocean front covered terrace, where you will enjoy many delightful evenings with family and friends dining al fresco. There is a BBQ gas grill for those wishing to cook the catch of the day.

On the upper level you have 3 ocean front king bedded suites, Mi Amor, Mi Reyna and Mi Cielo. Each suite has ensuite bathroom and stunning ocean views. On the north wing of the villa there are 2 suites, Amorcito and Querida, each of these suites has 2 twin beds and ensuite bathroom. The west wing has 4 further suites, BomBom, Mi Vida, Mi Corazon and the Linda Suite. These suites have wonderful tropical garden views and have ensuite bathroom. The BomBom suite has a wonderful ocean view from the bedroom entrance.

Entertainment is closeby, here at the villa you have a Billiard table, Air Hockey and Table Tennis…hours of fun are guaranteed for the young and young at heart!

The villa staff are on hand to make sure that you have the perfect vacation. Cook services may be arranged additionally… try delicious local delicacies from our Mexican Cook or more international cuisine from one of our gourmet chefs, they are also masters at mixing your favorite cocktail of choice! The villa concierge is on call and will be happy to assist your throughout your stay.

Step from the downstairs terrace and feel the soft sand between your feet… relax beach side by the pool on chaise lounges or swim in the large swimming pool. There are kayaks for guest use. The Fatima Bay beach is great for swimming and snorkeling, there is lots to see in the sea world, the pretty fishes and coral will delight you.

Close by to the villa you have golf and tennis, gymnasium, the marina has international restaurants, boutiques, Coffee shops, dive stores, Dolphin Discovery, fishing charters, skate park and so much more. Puerto Aventuras is perfectly located to take advantage of the many attractions along the coast, Xpu Ha beach 5 minutes south, Playa Del Carmen is 15 minutes north with many restaurants and bars and the famous 5th Avenue, Hacienda Corazon is 15 minutes South, Eco Parks Xcaret and Xel Ha, the ruins of Tulum and Coba are a great day out. Experience beautiful beaches and cenotes along the coast…you are truly in paradise!

For larger groups, corporate Retreats, Weddings and Events Hacienda Corazon may also be rented with the sister villa Hacienda Del Mar next door. The 2 villas offer the perfect combination.

For the ultimate retreat, choose Villa Hacienda Corazon and be the king and queen of hearts!
