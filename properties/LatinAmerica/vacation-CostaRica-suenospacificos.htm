Villa Suenos Pacificos, Dominical, Costa Rica Vacation Rental


This truly magnificent private home, located in “Las Olas” community, offers stunning panoramic ocean views of Dominical and Manuel Antonio to the north and the Osa Peninsula to the south. Enjoy expansive views of the ocean while you cook in a gourmet kitchen with stainless appliances that are nestled in an octagonal tower that is unique to this fine property. This finely appointed vacation rental is built with the finest materials, is designed to embrace the views and embodies pure comfort to provide a magical experience!

 

This beautiful villa has over 4,000 square feet of living space, the Master suite and two additional bedrooms, 4 bathrooms each Fully-furnished with fine furnishings.  The spacious dining and living areas with abundant natural light throughout the home, there is Central air-conditioning, Chlorine-free infinity pool, Wireless internet connection, and the house has expansive deck areas.
