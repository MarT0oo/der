Roatan Retreat Two, Roatán, Honduras Vacation Rental

 

Gorgeous, spacious, and utterly unique, our two-bedroom villas put you right on the Caribbean Sea. Pricing is based on a 4 person occupancy in a full villa, but the villa can comfortably accommodate up to 8!

Each bedroom features two queen-size ultra-comfortable Heavenly beds, air conditioning and ceiling fans, and a private en suite bathroom with Balinese shower. The oceanfront villas are designed with separate private entrances for each bedroom. Between the two bedrooms is the central living area and gourmet kitchen. The living room features a full-size sofa and two chairs and has louvered doors along one wall that open up completely to the deck. Lounge on the covered deck, which features a patio set, or on the private oceanfront area with lounge chairs, and enjoy the gorgeous view of the Caribbean.

Secluded setting with exceptional service and personalized attention. A popular choice for honeymoons and anniversary trips the boutique hotel size also makes it ideal for group retreats, family reunions, and destination weddings.

Relax on the beach or by the pool, head out on kayaks, or explore the underwater world snorkeling or diving. This is your refuge, the Caribbean Sea your playground. The coral reef is at your doorstep.

One bead in a necklace of cays between French Harbour and Brick Bay, and is located safely behind the reef, keeping the waters surrounding the Cay calm and protected. The resort is positioned perfectly for the trade winds to create a tropical breeze over the entire cay.

The Resort is built around a privately owned 4-acre cay, located just off the main road on the south shore of Roatán, the largest of the Bay Islands in the Caribbean.

Delicious meals are a highlight of staying at the Resort. Meals may be served in the pool cabana (which includes the main floor dining room and upstairs Lookout Lounge) or pool deck, or enjoy room service to your bungalow, loft, villa, or boat. You can select from yummy preparations of local or imported foods and alcoholic or non-alcoholic beverages and blended drinks. Many of our guests have some meals here, as well as enjoying some of Roatán’s restaurants and preparing some meals in their rooms.

Other guests want us to prepare all of their meals, and opt for our inclusive meal package. We serve a variety of preparations that include local specialties, such as shrimp, lobster, and fish, as well as chicken and other meats.
