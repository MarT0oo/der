Casa Caiman, Peninsula Papagayo, Costa Rica Vacation Rental


With vistas that capture the precious gifts of the land on one side and a few steps away from the 13th green of the Arnold Palmer Signature Golf Course at Peninsula Papagayo on the other, Casa Caiman is the quintessential luxury estate home.  Blending rich indigenous materials, lush fabrics and modern entertainment amenities, Casa Caiman is exotically impressive; it is the place to entertain, relax and indulge in paradise.

 

Features of Casa Caiman include:

 

- 7,400 square feet of lavish and luxurious interior space

- 3,600 square feet of usable outdoor space including generous decks with shade, outdoor kitchen, wet bar, Jacuzzi, and expansive infinity-edge pool with a shallow wading area

- 5 king bedroom suites with spa-like private baths and private terraces; all bedrooms feature flat screen televisions and DVD player

- Nanny room with a twin bed. It is detached from the main house but accesses the main house through the garage

- Kitchen, dining room, living room and bar area features a 27 foot ceiling with Molejon ceilings, a stone native to Costa Rica

- Expansive indoor wet-bar with custom cabinetry and flat screen television

- Spacious and comfortable media room with flat screen television, card table, Xbox, and DVD player

- Desktop computer and printer plus wireless internet access

- Custom-designed interiors with bamboo and Saturnia marble flooring

- Gourmet kitchen equipped with custom cabinetry, sub-zero/Wolf industrial stainless appliances

- Bathrooms with Colombian and Italian marble

- Spacious two-car garage

- Maid service included. Caretaker and landscaping crew will also be on site to tend to outdoor and pool maintenance and landscaping needs. An IT service technician is on call to address issues related to internet service

- An in-home private chef and/or local cook is also available for an additional fee

- In-home spa/massage services are also available for an additional fee

- A private boat captain available upon request for sport fishing, diving, snorkeling, surfing and water taxi services

- Concierge service

 

Guests of Casa Caiman will have membership to the exclusive Prieta Beach Club, a 38,000 ft² open-air hideaway featuring a gym with state-of-the-art fitness equipment, lap pool, and children's pool. The Beach Club features dining at Marea Bar & Restaurant, Sports Bar and Snack Shop. Guests will also have access to Prieta's Peninsula Discovery Program where you can discover Pura Vida living with a dynamic array of tailored outdoor learning activities that range from outrigger canoe paddling adventures to surfing lessons with expert guides.
