Villa Ten, Guanacaste, Costa Rica Vacation Rental

 

This villa is perfect for large groups of couples or families traveling together. Each bedroom has its own private bathroom. The outdoor lanai (A porch or veranda) is a perfect gathering spot to enjoy meals or just relax together. Three of the bedrooms and the living area have views of the ocean.


Take in the breathtaking views and Costa Rican natural wildlife from your villa's private pool, just steps away from your bedroom. Enjoy the sunset and a cocktail while conversing with your partner, family, friends or colleagues, while your personal Cook prepares your next smeal.

Set amidst the trees and overlooking Bolanos Bay, this large contemporary home features an elegant dining and living room. Sliding glass doors lead you to the fully furnished patio and plunge pool. The outdoor stairs lead to an outdoor terrace and bar area with a stunning panoramic view of the bay and tropical lush forest. A private sitting room easily converts into a fourth bedroom for large groups traveling together. Furniture in each bedroom was handcrafted and stained to match the wood throughout the home.

Relax and unwind in your own private villa, with indoor comforts, full kitchen and laundry services. Daily housekeeping service tends to your air-conditioned villa while you enjoy your favorite shows on the Plasma TV screens with Direct TV and CD/DVD players.

Bedrooms: Two oversized queen beds offer comfort in two master suites upstairs, with adjoining bathrooms, featuring glass enclosed showers and double sink vanities. The third bedroom sleeps two in twin beds, and the fourth bedroom has three twin beds and one day bed. Each bedroom features iPod Hook-ups, and all have an attached bathroom with oversized bath towels and linens, and a hair dryer.

Maximum Capacity: 10 adults, or 10 people with a combination of adults and children

 


*Holiday Season All Inclusive Package includes all meals & non alcoholic beverages for all guests in the villa up to the maximum, private cook, all amenities onsite, airport transfers, private golf cart (one per villa). Please consult concierge regarding full list of inclusions.
