Casa Caiman, Peninsula Papagayo, Costa Rica Vacation Rental

 

Nestled atop Costa Rica's world famous Gold. Coast, Casa Caiman is located in Costa Rica's premier luxury development - Penisula Papaguyo. Papagayo is home to the infamous Four Seasons Hotel as well as the countries top golf course. The uniqueness of the peninsula provides some of the most spectacular vistas in Central America. Some of the world-class facilities at Papagayo include tennis, single track mountain biking, a marina, and a spectacular beach club.

Casa Caiman is one of the only 5 bedroom homes in Papagayo. Each room has a private ocean view terrace as well as large flat screen tv's and en suite full baths. The living room boasts a 27-foot ceiling with local sandstone and marble spanning walls and floors. A large deck and infinity pool with adjacent hot tub insures no sunsets go unseen.

Only a 45 minute drive from Liberia international airport means that you can fly direct to Casa Caiman from: Houston, Miami, L.A., Atlanta, New York Toronto, London, Dallas Minneapolis, Chicago, Calgary, Montreal and Denver.

Casa Caiman offers world class golf, fishing and surfing just a few hours flight from the states. The accessibility, luxury, world class amenities and natural beauty make Papagauyo and Casa Caiman a true Viridian Experience.
