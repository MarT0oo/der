Castle in the Sky, Manuel Antonio, Costa Rica Vacation Rental


The BRAND NEW, gated estate on 2.5 acres of rain forest offers a stunning 270-degree ocean panorama and a golden-sand beach is virtually footsteps away. Perfect for families, retreats & weddings, Your butler and his staff tends to every detail to ensure a memorable stay.

The uniquely designed, perfectly suited villa is nestled into the lush jungle teaming with monkeys, sloths and tropical birds. The multi-level complex with it's own elevator, is comprised of both the MAIN VILLA, a mansion of steel, glass and natural rock as well as a meticulously crafted ENTRY COTTAGE on our multi-acre rain-forest estate. 

We feature 10 boutique-style bedrooms all with their own uniquely tiled entrances in Italian Bisazza tile as well as their own private baths with locally produced organic bath products and rain showers. Villa amenities include a ballroom-disco off of the pool terrace and main level bar, a covered dining terrace and bar with sweeping views as well as an open-air roof deck w/ bar and Jacuzzi that's perfect for below-the-stars dining. It's also an outdoor movie theatre!

In short, the villa captures the essence of your own home by the sea with the event-sized social spaces, infrastructure and impeccable service of a small luxury boutique hotel. A daily chef prepared breakfast is also included and chefs can be arranged for exquisite lunches and dinners as desired. 

Our butler and his staff sees to it that his guests are impeccably attended.

Included: Bi-Lingual Villa Manager/Concierge, Chef prepared Gourmet "Tico" Breakfast including champagne Mimosas, Twice Daily Maid/Turndown service, on site Maintanence and 24 hour Security.
