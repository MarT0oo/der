Villa Morabeza, Playa Tamarindo, Costa Rica Vacation Rental

 

Welcome to Villa Morabeza, a five star luxury villa located in a private/gated community right on the beach in Tamarindo. This 8 bedroom, 8½ bathroom villa comfortably accommodates big families or a group of friends in a unique, private and safe location.

Morabeza is both a word and a concept, a lifestyle, a way of seeing and understanding things and life. The word says it all, namely open spirit, welcoming, hospitable, relaxed, etc... etc it is in a way very similar to what the word ALOHA means to Hawaiian islanders…

And there is plenty of morabeza in Tamarindo Beach: the air is soft, the breeze is fresh, and the ocean is clear and warm–just like Hawaii. Our location is unique as it offers on beach access to both Tamarindo and Langosta (less than one minute walk!) and a short 5-minute drive to town. The house sits elevated above the beach with no easy direct access, so it is very private. Guests have a “hidden” pathway to access the beach. Villa Morabeza features 4 master bedrooms with breathtaking ocean views, 1 guest bedroom with ocean and internal patio view and two kids bedrooms each with double bunk beds. Some showers are outdoor with ocean view.

On the property there is a separate studio with a full bathroom and kitchenette; ideal for family members who want privacy or staff visiting with you (babysitters, chefs, etc.)

The spacious living areas can be opened completely towards the ocean view. The inside living area contains one big fully equipped kitchen and prep kitchen, a bar seating at the island and sitting at an integrated table. The dining area includes a 18-foot long dining table that will comfortably sit 20 people. Villa Morabeza also includes lounge areas around the pool and a second dining table in front of the beach with spectacular views perfect for enjoying a cocktail while viewing an amazing tropical sunset. Staying in Villa Morabeza is a luxury experience and to make sure you have an unforgettable stay, we provide complimentary typical breakfast, concierge service and housekeeping.Our Property Manager, Francisco Bustos, will be available to address any issues that may arise during your stay. To get around we include 2 electrical fat bikes complimentary in your rental.

We have added two great amenities to Villa Morabeza. First we installed a MistAway system that protects this property from mosquitoes & other pests. The botanical, all-natural insecticide allows you to enjoy the comforts of the outdoors without the worry of bugs. We also installed a water system to offer complimentary still and sparkling water on tab. The water is pure, tastes very good and is super ECO friendly. No more dragging cases of water from the supermarket and no more polluting plastic trash.
