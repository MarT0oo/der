Villa La Escapa, Peninsula Papagayo, Guanacaste, Costa Rica Vacation Rental

 

You can't escape the ocean from this brand new, beautiful villa as nearly all rooms in the house feature tremendous views and terraces facing out to the majestic Pacific. Perched on the gorgeous Peninsula Papagayo, this home is surrounded by vibrant tropical flora and countless soothing water features. La Escapa is the perfect vacation - whether you chose to spend every day lounging by the pool and Prieta Beach Club or venturing out to explore the Guanacaste region's incredible national parks.

The villa's four suite-style bedrooms are immaculately furnished and provide all of the comforts of home with the added benefit of the perfect tropical setting! For kids, we have a large bunk room that sleeps four in twin beds and an additional two in a queen size pull out couch. Wake up to enjoy a spectacular infinity edge swimming pool overlooking the jungle and panoramas of the sunset 365 days of the year. With an outdoor wet bar and bbq, the good times can just keep on rolling. Indoors, enjoy a chef-grade kitchen, cool areas to lounge, and Wi-Fi/Netflix.

The interiors of La Escapa feature unique, exotic furnishings and a decorative scheme that conjures a Zen-like sense of tranquility. High ceilings and sliding floor-to-ceiling windows keep the feelings airy and breezy throughout this architectural gem.

Elsewhere on the Peninsula, enjoy the exclusive owner's beach club and fitness center, Arnold Palmer golf course, spa facilities, tennis, explorers club, and the perks of a fabulous children's program. You can also access any of Papagayo's eight restaurants during your stay. Your reservation also includes housekeeping and daily breakfast service.
