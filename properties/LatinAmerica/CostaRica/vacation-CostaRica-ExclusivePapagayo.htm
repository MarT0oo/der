Exclusive Papagayo, Guanacaste, Costa Rica Vacation Rental

 

Exclusive Papagayo is an all-inclusive property located in the exclusive beachside community of Ocotal, in the Guanacaste region of Costa Rica, overlooking the Gulf of Papagayo and the Pacific Ocean.

Exclusive Papagayo is just a few minutes south of the little beach village of Coco and only a 25-minute drive from Liberia International Airport. This gorgeous, newly-constructed villa is situated on a private hilltop that rises from the beach of Ocotal and offers breathtaking views of the Pacific to the west and volcanic mountains and jungles to the north, east, and south.

A perfect vacation spot for family or business groups ranging from 8 to 20 to experience all the fun and adventure Costa Rica can offer!

Exclusive Papagayo outstanding “Five Star” service will start from the moment you begin planning your trip to Costa Rica to the final good-bye at the end of the trip. Our staff consists of seven wonderful individuals including an on-site manager, chef and assistant chef, bartender/waiter/guest attendant, housekeeper, facility attendant, and night security guard. Management and staff will be available 24 hours a day to facilitate your stay in any way. Daily services include room cleaning; full meal service; computer, and internet services. In addition, infant and handicap support/access can be accommodated. They are focused on anticipating and responding to your every need!

Transportation: We will assist in any way possible to accommodate your specific transportation and guide requirements throughout the length of your stay at the Villa. In advance of your trip, we will work with you to customize a specific “package” that best meets the needs and desires of your group, including airport pick-up and drop-offs, local travel around Ocotal and Coco, and, of course, fun-filled excursions to any number of Costa Rica’s most exciting adventure spots. (transportation options subject to additional cost).

Meals: Your stay at Exclusive Papagayo includes three wonderful meals each day prepared by our local Costa Rican chef. We will review the proposed menu with you in advance of your trip with a goal of customizing meal choices to your group's preferences, including special dietary needs and libation preferences.
