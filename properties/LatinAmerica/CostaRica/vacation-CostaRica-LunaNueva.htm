Luna Nueva, Playa Azul, Costa Rica Vacation Rental

 

The spectacular Luna Nueva luxury rental home is situated near the playful coastline of Playa Azul. You can hear romantic sounds of waves crashing along the shore and the music of rare and exotic birds as your natural soundtrack right from the comforts of this beautiful modern villa. The ultra-modern Luna Nueva villa has 4 comfortably appointed bedrooms, 3 baths, outdoor showers and modern furnishings throughout.

With a private infinity edge pool and plenty of space for entertainment outdoors, complemented by breathtaking views of the Pacific Ocean and the lush mountains, Luna Nueva is the ultimate vacation getaway.
