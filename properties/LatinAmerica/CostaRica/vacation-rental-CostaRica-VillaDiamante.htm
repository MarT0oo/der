Villa Diamante, Los Sueños Marina & Golf Resort, Playa Herradura, Costa Rica Vacation Rental

 

With over 7,300 square feet of luxurious living space, a private 600 SqFt swimming pool, wonderful landscaping and superior finishes, this 4 bedroom single family villa offers an wonderful ocean view with a combination of a lush tropical environment, Villa Diamante means Diamond Villa, and this villa lives up to the beauty and uniqueness that a diamond portrays.

You will feel like royalty as you step info these absolutely breathtaking spaces. Carefully created to offer guests the ultimate Costa Rica luxury experience, you will find only top of the line furnishings; from the terrace, dining room and bedrooms, to the kitchen and bathrooms, you will never find another space as remarkable as Villa Diamante.

This four bedroom can also be rented as a 2 or a 3 bedroom, depending on your needs. For a group of single travelers, there is room for up to four people. Three of the bedrooms feature a King size bed, while one of the bedrooms one sofa bed with a pull out bed.

The villa includes a fully equipped gourmet kitchen with stainless steel appliances, an ample terrace, dining area, living area, central air with individual controls in each room and laundry room. Guests will also enjoy the convenience of wireless Internet access, surround-sound throughout the entire house, 48" Flat screen televisions in each bedroom and an entertainment center with a large flat screen television in the living area.
