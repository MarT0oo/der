Palms Villa, Playa Flamingo, Guanacaste, Costa Rica Vacation Rental

 

The Palms Villa is two stories with two bedrooms and three full baths, and finished with fine modern furnishings providing luxury and comfort in every room. The villa contains floor to ceiling double panel bi-fold doors which maximize your view of the amazing sunsets from both floors. There is also a quiet second story balcony at the front of the home. It has two full dining areas, one indoor and one outdoor. Go out the back doors and you are less than fifty steps to the white sandy beach!

Master Suite: 1 x King Bed
Guest Suite: Either 2 x Twin or 1 x King
We also have a brand new Queen sofa bed in the living room with black-out blinds, and a full bath on the main floor.
Due to local bylaws, the unit can sleep up to a maximum of 6 people.

The Palms condominium has 24/7 guards, gated privacy and two pools with a creek connecting them with attractive tropical landscaping throughout the complex. The main 100ft infinity pool is great for little ones, too! There is a fully equipped gym with a beautiful ocean view. The resort offers free non-motorized water sports equipment (kayaks, surf boards, boogie boards), and the staff are glad to facilitate anything from catamaran tours to wave runners and more. There is an on-site kitchen and bar serving up great breakfast, lunch, tropical cocktails and even sunset dinners on the beach! There is also a massage therapist on site who is amazing and very reasonably priced.

If you love to cook, you'll enjoy the fully stocked kitchen, Viking appliances, and a common barbecue area that boasts brand new Weber grills situated under an attractive Palapa roof.

The villa includes 100 mbps internet (wireless or wired) and a free Vonage home phone line. An amenities fee of $265/visit is payable at the office upon arrival that covers daily cleaning, daily towel service, paper products (toilet paper, paper towel and facial tissue) and bath soap/shampoo/conditioner. Please note that this fee will not be visible when you book via Dream Exotic Rentals.

This luxurious beach front Villa is located on the South end of the exclusive Playa Flamingo, and is THE ONLY beach front condominium complex on the gorgeous white sands of Flamingo Beach, Costa Rica's premier beach. Located on Costa Rica's northwest Pacific coast, this villa is just a forty five minute drive from Liberia airport, and twenty five minutes from Tamarindo's airport. The Palms is nestled among multi-million dollar private estates near the end of a secluded cove and is renowned for its soft white sand, and clear warm ocean water. Flamingo is also home to world class sports fishing. Located just offshore, the Catalina Islands offer outstanding scuba diving. Nearby Playa Grande offers some of Costa Rica's best surfing. Beautiful golf courses, a marina, and gourmet restaurants are all conveniently located nearby.

Few vacation destinations offer the stunning natural beauty of Costa Rica, where vast stretches of white-sand beach give way to lush tropical forest and misty mountain peaks, and where whale watching, marlin fishing, zip-lining, snorkeling, orchid hunting and sunbathing are tempting diversions.
