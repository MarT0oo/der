Villa Mel, Peninsula Papagayo, Costa Rica Vacation Rental

 

Welcome to magnificent Villa Mel, where the constant hum of traffic and city stress is replaced by the sound of howler monkeys, crashing waves and birdsong. Neighboring the Four Seasons Resort Costa Rica, located on the incomparable Peninsula Papagayo, and only steps away from the Arnold Palmer Golf Course, sits this luxurious contemporary Private Residence. Only minutes away from stunning beaches and lush nature trails, Villa Mel boasts elegant top of the line furnishings, as well as impressive collection of modern art.

In addition to the lush surroundings and breathtaking views this amazing 11,000 square foot luxury villa features 5 spacious bedrooms, 6 full bathrooms, powder room and service quarters. The large master suite offers a California King bed, oversized walk-in closet and an amazing bathroom with double shower and bathtub.

Of the additional 4 bedrooms, 2 have king beds and 2 have double queen beds. Each bedroom has a private terrace and 55” smart TV’s with apple TV and surround sound system.

The property includes three large sitting areas one of which is a large formal sitting area, a spacious family room with a 4K 75” TV with satellite system. This room integrates with the professional kitchen and has its own breakfast area. The third is an open social area with large comfortable furniture on the covered patio, it has its own dining area with a beautiful wooden trestle table and bench seating, as well as professional BBQ area and a full bathroom. In the hallway leading to the master suite there is a casual and comfortable open office area and state of the art gym featuring high end equipment and massage area.

Relax and unwind in the infinity swimming pool with adjacent heated Jacuzzi is surrounded by lush tropical gardens and has sensational sunset views over the Pacific Ocean.

The on-site staff of Villa Mel are happy to create original and tailor-made experiences for the guests, from organizing romantic sunset dinners, spa treatments, water activities, picnic on a private beach to unforgettable adventure tours.

The endless views of the Pacific Ocean and lush tropical forest are Mother Nature’s contribution to this masterpiece.
