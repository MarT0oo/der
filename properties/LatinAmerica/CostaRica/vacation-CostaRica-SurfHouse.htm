Surf House, Nicoya Peninsula, Costa Rica Vacation Rental

 

Our new SURF HOUSE is positioned on an adjacent lot beside Villa I with a spectacular ocean front vista. It is our most casual and "layed back" accommodation with an ocean view morning, noon and night that will take your breath away.

Converted from a modest private Costa Rican home, our newest space has three air-conditioned sleeping rooms; one with a small seating area and large refrigerator. All three rooms have doors opening to the sea, the breeze, and the sounds of the birds, monkeys and rolling waves. The surf house offers one community bathroom with a shower and a sunken bathtub that sits outside. There is an abundance of outdoor open space leading to the Pacific including hammocks and beach chairs for lounging, reading or sunset viewing.

The Surf House sleeps up to six and is perfect for one or two couples, one couple with up to four children, four to six single adults (surfers or getaway friends) or overflow space for teenage children and friends while mom and dad take a villa next door. Room one has two twin beds, room two has a king bed, and room three is equipped with two single day beds. All of Florblanca amenities are available to guests of the Surf House including room service, our beautiful spa Bambu, pool, Nectar Restaurant, tours, maid service, laundry etc.

Whether you come alone or with a group, the Surf House is yours for a wonderful Costa Rican get away. Come have the time of your life by the sea...the Surf House at Florblanca!
