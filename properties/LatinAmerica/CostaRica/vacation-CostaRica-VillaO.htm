Villa O, Playa Hermosa, Costa Rica Vacation Rental

 

Villa O is a two-story, 7 Bedroom, Adults Only boutique hotel in Guanacaste, Costa Rica. We are an adults only property, catering to couples who seek a quiet, romantic getaway. Our Villa is a 10,000 square foot mansion set on 2.5 acres of dry tropical forest. Architecturally designed to be open and spacious, the Villa is a blend of Mexican and Balinese styles. Situated high on a hill overlooking Costa Ricaâ€™s picturesque Papagayo Bay, the property provides guests with a sense of tranquility from the minute you step through the doors.

The interior design of the Villa was overseen by Luisa Carranza. Luisa has worked for over 10 years designing and fitting upscale hotels including the Four Seasons Papagayo. Her vision was to create a five element sanctuary, combining leather, silk and iron with traditional woods. We think you will agree that she succeeded.

The surrounding landscapes of greens and blues create a fantastic backdrop for your unforgettable vacation. It was our great desire to combine the luxury of a five star property with the natural beauty of the surroundings. To achieve this we have worked hard to landscape the tropical gardens. Calling on the talents of Joan Roca, we built a stunning two tier swimming pool and using natural stone helped to create a harmonious setting. To witness the real vision it is necessary to see a sunset from the garden when the orange and pinks contrast with the deep purple blues from the pool.

Our Guanacaste Costa Rica hotel is near the quiet port town of Playas del Coco and several nearby beaches. We are just close enough to the action, yet sufficiently removed that you will feel miles away from stress and worry. We have a fantastic and well-trained staff who is dedicated to providing you superior service throughout your stay.
