Castle Inclusive Resort, Puntarenas, Costa Rica Vacation Rental

 

Located on the Central Pacific coast of Costa Rica, this spectacular turn-key Ocean View Trophy Estate is just 15 minutes from white-sand area beaches and an hour from San Jose International Airport. The luxurious property is ideal for a private family resort, boutique hotel, private corporate retreat or second home in paradise.

Complete with 10 bedrooms, 11 bathrooms, large pool with expansive deck, swim-up bar, full spa, lush gardens, 200ft waterslide, putting green and tennis courts. Incredible views overlook the bright blue Pacific Ocean and the Gulf of Nicoya, which provide a stunning backdrop for Costa Rica’s famed sunsets.

Built for the Countess of Baillet-Latour by a noted German architect, this spacious 9-acre estate is known locally as “Castle Inclusive Resort.” The secure residential property boasts lavish gardens with over 43,000 square feet of buildings, including a 12,000 square foot main house and two separate guesthouses. Offering privacy and a host of activities for your family or group, this is both the perfect private retreat and an ideal place to entertain.

Features include cathedral windows; arched doorways; high ceilings; solid hand-carved Cristobal wood throughout; new commercial kitchen; marble walkway leading to the library and billiards room; audio system; air conditioning throughout; fitness and game room. Too many amenities to list.

Extraordinarily maintained and fully operational, there are 2 pure water wells on the property, which provide safe, pure drinking water, along with 7 septic tanks and reliable plumbing, electrical and telephone. Almost all furniture and appliances included. Conveniently located in Esparza, Puntarenas, in a gorgeous piece of the Pacific Central region. This luxurious, tropical estate is your own private Eden.

You will never have to lift a finger in the kitchen at Castle Inclusive Resort. Chef Minor (over eight years at the Castle) and his staff will confer with you to plan each days meals…. Breakfast lunch and Dinner. Our staff does all the shopping using the finest purveyors in Costa Rica and proudly serves memorable meals. Beverages, fine wines, and the freshest local produce and fruits are all available in abundance……

There are many dining areas to enjoy your service at the Castle. Our formal dining room can serve up to twenty guests at our large table, or smaller seating arrangements.

Many guests elect to have breakfast and dinner on the main level veranda. Framed by stunning surrounding views over looking the pool, golf greens, and the mountains and Gulf of Nicoya beyond. It is a exquisite location in the morning, and perfect for evening dining with beautiful views, cool breezes and overhead fans.

With a relaxing day at the pool, a golf tournament, or tennis match, lunch at the pool house (Rancho Dining) is cool and relaxing. The pool house provides seating for up to thirty guests (or more) and is also a perfect location for a special evening barbecue or dinner.
