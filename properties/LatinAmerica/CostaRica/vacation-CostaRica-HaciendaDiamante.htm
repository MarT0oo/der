Hacienda Diamante, Playa Matapalo, Guanacaste, Costa Rica Vacation Rental

 

Hacienda Diamante is a unique luxury estate that offers all the amenities of home plus much more. This exclusive estate, located within the Diamante Eco Adventure Park and Animal Sanctuary property, boasts stunning Pacific Ocean views. Spacious and comfortable, our 7,000 ft² gated and secluded retreat is a perfect getaway for up to twenty-two friends and family members.

Upon arrival, our friendly and courteous staff will be there waiting for you with refreshments in hand! The minute you cross through the grand entrance, you are greeted with breathtaking sights viewed through huge wall-to-wall sliding glass doors.

The inside of the estate offers a myriad of amenities, including a great room with vaulted ceilings, state-of-the-art kitchen with two breakfast bars for casual seating, twelve-person grand dining table, oversized sofas in the large living room, HD smart TV's, high speed wireless internet and a fully professional fitness center.

Enjoy waking to the sounds of nature in one of our five luxury bedrooms--each having its own private entrance to the outside terrace overlooking the infinity pool and ocean. All bedrooms have ample closet space, en-suite bathrooms, large HD smart TV's with access to guests own streaming accounts, air conditioning and ceiling fans. Both master suites offer walk-in indoor and outdoor showers along with giant soaking tubs.

The true gem of the property has to be the outdoor area, where the ocean meets the sky and nature surrounds you in all its beauty. You can bask in all-day sunshine at the infinity pool, enjoy lunch in our barbecue and bar area and watch the Costa Rican sunset while enjoying alfresco dining with your friends and family.

Let yourself be pampered by our experienced full-time bilingual house staff. Our team will cook delicious ethnic dishes, perform daily housekeeping duties and offer concierge services that will ensure you have the most memorable experiences. With perks like these, you may never want to leave Hacienda Diamante.

If you can pull yourself away from the estate, we encourage you to take time to visit Diamante Eco Adventure Park and Animal Sanctuary, conveniently located within walking distance. Here you can experience Zip Line courses, including the longest dual line in the country; our renowned Animal Sanctuary, home to the largest variety of species in one location; botanical gardens; horseback riding; ATVs; a beach; kayaking and groomed walking trails. With hundreds of acres to explore, this is one of Costa Rica's must-do experiences! (Guest of Hacienda Diamante will also receive a 15% discount at Diamante Eco Adventure Park and Animal Sanctuary)

Business meetings, executive retreats or team-building events? Let us help you host these events in a private and confidential setting.
