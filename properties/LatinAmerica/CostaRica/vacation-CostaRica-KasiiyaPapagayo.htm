Kasiiya Papagayo, Laguna Beach, Papagayo, Costa Rica Vacation Rental

 

Kasiiya Papagayo is a fully serviced private retreat. Built in the vibrancy of nature and evolving to meet the needs of today’s savvy, eco-friendly visitors. An extraordinary 123 acres on your own, with two beaches in the heart of Guanacaste, Costa Rica, is a captivating destination that sits effortlessly within its natural surroundings.

Serene and personal, it’s a perfect paradise, where respect for the local environment is as second nature as a world-class hospitality. Eco luxe at its finest, the villas and entire resort are solar-powered, and the water is obtained by rainwater harvesting.

Five gorgeous suites constructed from all-natural materials make for an intimate ambience amongst wild Costa Rican dry tropical forests and beaches.

Kasiiya means "find your pace" in Swahili and the Kasiiya experience focuses on mind and body wellness – guests can wake up to private yoga or movement sessions set to the song of the magpie-jay, embark on a wellness discovery with our in-house healer.

With a total area of 123 acres, Kasiiya Papagayo features just 5 sumptuous suites. Eco-friendly villas are fully equipped and feature private gardens, beach or tropical forest views and daybeds. All suites feature fans, air-conditioning, eco minibar, a small library, and Wi-Fi.

The suites feature a King-sized bed, large open-air living room with daybeds and a lounge area, indoor and open- air private bathrooms, an outdoor area with a porch including a dining area, couches and a hammock

Kasiiya Papagayo has two unique beaches, and is set in the forest, home to monkeys, whales, dolphins, rare birds, and thousands of other species.

The land spreads over 55 hectares and offers a bio diverse landscape, welcoming you to unwind, recharge and experience a world away from the thousand repetitions of an urban day.

One Double suite: Two bedrooms with a king size bed each, en-suite bathrooms with two showers outdoor and indoor, bathtub, air conditioning, sofa with reading area, living room with dining area seating six. Terrace with sun-loungers. 1800 square feet. Beach front

Three suites: King size bed, en-suite bathroom with outdoor shower and bathtub, air conditioning, sofa with reading area. Terrace with sun-loungers. 860 square feet.

Three beach front suites and two ocean view sunset suites.

The cooking team serves creative fresh local and international menus sourced by local farms, and uses organic ingredients in 90% of the dishes in our two restaurants. Experience dining in settings as memorable as the cuisine.

The restaurants offer a peaceful and natural environment to enjoy a relaxing sunrise breakfast, delicious lunch by the beach or a romantic sunset dinner. The gourmet and varied dishes cater to all requirements.

All the experiences have the same goal - to help the guests connect to nature in their own way, to allow a personalized unique experience. One of the best snorkeling spots on the coast is directly in front of the five beachfront suites, and whales can be seen from October to February.

The existing ecosystem features three types of monkeys, and spectacular bird life that can be enjoyed from platforms throughout the property.

A unique fitness platform amongst the jungle enjoys an expansive ocean view and is ideal for yoga and immersing into nature. On-site guides are on hand to organize tailored jungle tours, kayak tours, and special night ventures to follow the turtles first steps towards the sea. Other activities include stand-up paddle boarding, and excursions for tree-climbing, trekking, and zip-lining.

The Spa - Spa treatments will take place amid the jungle and incorporate local volcanic stones, oils and mud. The on-site healer will help guests "pause", and enhance the sense of connection to nature through local techniques and rituals.

Note: Hair dryers are not allowed at Kasiiya property due to our ecological solar electricity system.

Location

Kasiiya is located in Papagayo, Guanacaste, in the North West coast of Costa Rica, bordering the Pacific, a province well known for its warm seas, beautiful beaches and breathtaking scenery. The climate provides consistent heat all year round resulting in luxuriant, unspoiled tropical forests. Kasiiya is just 45 minutes from the Liberia international airport with direct flights to the USA and some major cities in Europe.
