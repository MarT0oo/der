Casa de las Cascadas, Quepos Point, Manuel Antonio, Costa Rica Vacation Rental

 

Casa de las Cascadas is an architect's dream. It's modern tropical design accents air flow, and light. It is true neo-tropical living, the indoor and outdoor spaces flowing freely. The vacation home's living area has twenty foot windows that capture the vista from sky to sea, jungle to mountain. An infinity-edge pool spills off into a slate cascade. Two and a half acres of rainforest and sculpted gardens set the stage for a true work of art. The ocean view villa's master and upper guest bedrooms are accessed via an outdoor breezeway, and each has its own bath. The vacation rental's downstairs suite is spacious and comfortable, with direct access to the lower pool. An outdoor path leads through the gardens to the rental home's studio apartment--complete with its own living area, kitchen and terrace.
