Mareas Estate, Osa, Uvita, Puntarenas, Costa Rica Vacation Rental

 

Have this world class 36-acre Villa surrounded by over 200 acres of wild life preserve all for your friends, family or as your personal retreat.

Spend a week or more at this stunning retreat, which is located in one of the most desirable areas in all of Costa Rica. Mareas Estate in Costa Rica beckons you with 36 acres Costa Rica Rainforest, surrounded by over 200 acres of wild life preserve, your own sanctuary and rain forest perched on a ridge overlooking the lovely Dominical Beach on the southern Pacific coast of Costa Rica. This beach borders the Ballena National Marine Park, also known as the "Whales Tail Reef". Ocean breezes and lush rain forests create a tropical home to toucans, monkeys, sea turtles and whales.

From mountains to ocean, Mareas Estate in Costa Rica offers eco adventures to awaken the wide eye kid in everyone; zip lining, sport and fly fishing, snorkeling, surfing, kayaking, water fall rappelling, scuba, horseback riding, bird watching, hiking, ATV tours, night tours and much more.

Each suite provides you an unparalleled level of splendor and luxury, tucked in the rain forest with privacy and tranquility, offering stunning views of the beautiful deep blue ocean.

Immerse yourself in our 1,700 sq ft luxurious infinity swimming pool, surfaced entirely in cobalt blue glass tiles, featuring shallow water lounging areas. Our spill over Jacuzzi and cascading waterfalls create a dramatic panoramic visual to the ocean below.

Mareas Wildlife Preserve invites you to explore nature trails right outside your suite, leading to our two waterfalls and the forest canopy, where one can experience paradise in a friendly villa in Costa Rica.
