Villa Encanto
3 Bedrooms | 3.5 Baths
Los Cabos
From $750 per Night

Let Los Cabos sweep you off your feet at Encanto. This beautiful vacation rental, whose name translates to “charm,” will romance you with its Old World-inspired interiors, shared amenities and a wonderful location in The Corridor, close to some of the best restaurants and golf courses in the region. Its three bedrooms can accommodate up to six on a family holiday, a getaway with friends or a Punta Ballena honeymoon.

Your stay at Encanto begins with a welcome beverage and includes daily housekeeping, as well as the services of a concierge and villa manager. Guests are welcome to use the community’s shared pool, hot tub, clubhouse, exercise facilities and tennis court, but the villa also has its own private outdoor living areas with a Jacuzzi and barbecue. Other amenities include satellite television and Wi-Fi.

The heart of Encanto is its open-plan great room, whose hacienda-style furnishings combine European elegance with a relaxed, inviting feel. Settle in to one of the plush sofas, gather around the artfully weathered dining table or perch at the bar in the well-equipped kitchen, which has a microwave, toaster, coffee maker, plus a blender for all your cocktail needs. This luxury property has two bedrooms with king beds, including one with a jetted tub, and one bedroom with two queen beds. All three bedrooms have en-suite bathrooms featuring Teva Naturalis toiletries, and, for your comfort, air conditioning and ceiling fans.

Encanto is just a 2-minute walk from the Auberge Private Residence gym and Punta Ballena tennis court, and a 5-minute walk from the Esperanza Restaurant, where you can enjoy a romantic dinner overlooking the water. By car, it’s 5 minutes to the Cabo del Sol golf course and 10 minutes to shopping and dining in the nearest downtown area.
