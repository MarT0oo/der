Casa Ramon, Costa Rica Vacation Rental


Casa Ramon enjoys its own private spa, infinity pool, covered parking, expansive views, 6 bedrooms, 8 baths, air conditioning throughout, 2 miradors (look-outs equipped with bbq facilities), modern kitchens, our regional cook and staff. Casa Ramon is waiting for you "more".

Captivating lush gardens and lava rock line the primary entrance to Casa Ramon. Costa Rican antique carved wood benches adorn the vibrant hallway lit at night by a natural crystal lamp leading you to the Grand Vista Apartment.

You enter through a large glass doorway into a stunning bistro sitting area redefining contemporary tropical style this first floor faces the Grand View Terrace. Granite counters define the fully equipped open area kitchen and chic indoor dining room with its bamboo columns.

Out on the Grand View Terrace you marvel at the breathtaking views and can easily reach the bamboo covered lookout point. Here there is an outdoor living space with an outdoor dining room and tasteful lounge areas.  The warm earth tones inside set apart a splendid relaxation area with woven Balinese Banana Leaf furniture.

The master bedroom is a statement in relaxation and comfort with a stunning marble bathroom that walks out to a patio by the pool area. The guest room is a delightful repose for company with warm soothing colors and its own blue bathroom.

Some of the Amenities at the Villa Include – Pool and Jacuzzi, Therapeutic Massage Spa, Covered outdoor exercise area, Outdoor showers and baths, Outdoor fountain area, Lookout point and lounge area, Gardens and trails, Covered Parking, Second floor entrance with bamboo staircase, Two bedroom – Deluxe Master Suite and Guest, 3 Bathrooms with Shower, Master bath Jacuzzi, Full kitchen with large granite counter dining, Indoor living area, Upstairs open air bar lounge and full kitchen area, Large bamboo roof covered terrace, Outside terrace lounge areas, Stereo, Satellite TV, Air-conditioning, Ceiling fans, Wireless Internet and much more.

For a week, live the ultimate dream… never having to leave Casa Ramon.
