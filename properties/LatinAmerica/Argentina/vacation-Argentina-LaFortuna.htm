La Fortuna, Salto, Argentina Vacation Rental

 

La Fortuna is a historical palace from 1902 that was completely restored in 2008. The property features antiques, art pieces and furniture from Argentina’s glorious Belle Epoque from the turn of the century. This stunning estate perfectly combines modern art and comfort with the traditional countryside to create a unique and sophisticated atmosphere..
.
The main house can host up to 16 guests in 4 master suites with queen size beds and 3 other suites with 3 twin beds each. All rooms have walk in closets and complete bathrooms..
.
On the ground Floor there is a grand living room, a study with a 2 story wood paneled library, a formal dining room for 18 people, the master suite with a private terrace, an apartment with a king bed suite and a second room with 3 twin beds, the main kitchen, the breakfast room and a large terrace..
.
The second Floor has 2 queen bed suites, 2 rooms with 3 twin beds each, a playroom for children, the billiard room, a service pantry and an observatory with a telescope..
.
The basement is host to an impressive wine cellar, a private cinema, a wine bar, and a baby grand piano. An elevator connects all the Floors in the palace.
.
On the gardens of the property there is a beautiful large swimming pool with an attractive solarium and relaxing patio area. The pool side cabana has a complete kitchen and a traditional Argentine style barbecue; the perfect place for an informal lunch.

La Fortuna is located in Salto, Buenos Aires province, 200Km from downtown Buenos Aires. La Fortuna used to be a property with important farming activity and is still surrounded by farms dedicated to such activity.

Guests who stay at La Fortuna can go for an antique carriage ride to the old Berdier train station, the closest little town from this magninificicent Palacio de la Pampas.

Transportation to and from Buenos Aires can be arranged in a private car or van.

La Fortuna is the ideal place to enjoy an exclusive and luxurious vacation with friends and family or host unique gatherings for special celebrations or business meetings.

This incredible property offers traditional Argentine cultural activities with a sophisticated touch and high standards of personalized service creating an unforgettable experience.

La Fortuna is available for exclusive and customized residential stays in which the staff takes care of all the needs of its guests in a dedicated and stylish way. Residential stays include full access to all the suites and rooms of the palace for up to 16 guests and all activities that the property has to offer as well as different meals and beverage options.

The chef specializes in traditional gourmet dishes and has ample knowledge in international cuisine as well. Every day she proposes for each meal three different menu options that will be selected by the guest with the help of the house concierge. A wine selection, Open bar, beer, and cocktails are available with an addition charge.

Pop Up Hotel

For those individual guests who wish to live the experience of a luxurious stay in the Palace, there is the opportunity to make a reservation for specisically scheduled dates in which the Pop Up Hotel will operate.

On these specicific thematic weekends, the property offers daily rates that include a variety of indoor and outdoor activities, four meals a day, non alcoholic beverages and personalized service. Events

La Fortuna is the ideal venue for corporate events as well as social gatherings. The unique combination of the traditional gaucho spirit, the beautiful gardens and the picturesque palace create an atmosphere like no other. The chef’s classic recipes plus the creativity, technology and attention to detail are what distinguish events carried out at La Fortuna.
