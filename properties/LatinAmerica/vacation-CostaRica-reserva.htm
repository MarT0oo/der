Balinese Reserva
4 Bedrooms | 4 Baths Ensuite | Pacific Views                                    
Manuel Antonio
  From $6,375 per Week

Pure luxury describes the most modern vacation villa in the Quepos and Manuel Antonio area. Casa Reserva is nestled in the hillside overlooking the new Quepos Marina. Relax on one of four balconies to watch the brilliant Pacific sunsets and exotic Titi monkeys, and toucans.

The modern Bali architecture of this four bedroom home defines the word comfortable. The smart-home features will make your stay truly relaxing as the house serves your every need.

The large kitchen, dining room, separate bar and living area, and the terrace with the pool and the swim up bar will provide you with more than enough room to entertain your family, friends, business associates, or that significant person in your life that you want to impress with a week of exotic amenities.

All rooms have plasma TVs and safes in the walk-in closets. From the moment you enter the stunning tropical entrance, you view the beautiful blue waters of the marina.

The laundry room has ample storage, a cot, large sink, and washer/dryer. Sky Cable and high speed wireless internet are provided.
