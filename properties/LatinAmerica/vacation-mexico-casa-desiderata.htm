Casa Desiderata, Lagos del Mar, Punta Mita, Nayarit, Mèxico


Although spacious, Casa Desiderata was laid out and conceived to be easy to live in with almost everything within easy reach. With a large living area open to the kitchen and the back patio, guests are encouraged to come together to a common area for companionship and pleasure. All rooms are able to be fully closed and air conditioned or open to the tropical outdoors at your discretion. Bedrooms were planned to allow the feel of a master suite with his and her sinks and walk in closets and full sliding doors opening to the outdoors. Decorating is warm, modern, simple and fresh. All sheets are 400 count or more and the kitchen is equipped with commercial appliances and amenities. The dining table indoors seats 8 and outdoors the patio table seats another 8. Located on the 9th fairway of the world famous Four Seasons Golf Course, this is an unobstructed oceanfront Villa allowing views of humpback whales frolicking by day and cruise ships twinkling by night.

Property rentals include: Personalized residence or villa orientation tour, Preferential tee times and a special greens fee rate, Use of the Residents´ Beach Club (subject to prior reservation and availability.

Residential Concierge services:  Pre-arrival itinerary planning, Airport pick-up and drop off, Pre-arrival grocery shopping, In-house or villa spa services, Baby sitter service, Car rental arrangements,  Restaurant reservations, Booking of tours and excursions.
