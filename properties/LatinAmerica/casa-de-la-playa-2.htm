Casa Salinas II, Puerto Vallarta Mexico


Enter the gorgeous villa, Casa Salinas II, through an antique door from India into a haven of elegance and unique art.  Ideal for active living and entertaining, the high ceiling living room flows to an expansive pool patio and an endless view of the bay. Beautifully furnished this 8,000 sq ft, open and airy luxury villa is located on a private sandy beach on the prestigious South Shore, just two miles south of town. The 4 spacious air-conditioned ocean view King bedrooms with private bathrooms en-suite that will sleep 8 are restful retreats with terraces overlooking the ocean. The 5th and 6th bedroom garden casitas sleep 4 additional persons. One with a King bed and the other with two Twin beds with their own ensuite baths. Guests can enjoy the privacy and all the comforts they could want in this lovely villa.

Impeccably managed and maintained by a staff of four and overseen by Juan the ever-affable exceptionally helpful and knowledgeable English speaking manager, all waiting to serve your every whim 6 days a week. They will service you with warm Mexican hospitality and you are assured of eating well with the chef’s gourmet cuisine. The maids will do guests personal laundry if requested. For those special celebrations Juan can arrange birthday cakes, flowers, Mariachis, manicure/pedicures, massages, etc.

 

The heated infinity pool offers wonderful views of the ocean just steps from the private sandy beach where guests can play and enjoy swimming, snorkeling, BBQ and candle light dinner. Charter Juan’s boat for a fishing trip, an excellent captain who knows all the best spots. If you want to scuba, water ski or jet ski we can arrange pickup by boat from our beach. Golf, tennis and family-fun activities are within a short drive. There is an on-site gym, DSL Internet access, satellite TV and more.

Casa Salinas II with its 6 spacious bedrooms that sleep 12 is an ideal setting for family gatherings, birthdays, anniversaries, other celebrations, and a relaxed and enjoyable vacation. For weddings and large groups, book both Casa Salinas II and Casa Salinas I its equally as charming 5-bedroom partner next door, for a total of 11 bedrooms that sleep 22. Children are welcome, childcare can be arranged.
