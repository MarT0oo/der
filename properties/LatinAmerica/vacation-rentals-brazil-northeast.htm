Travel to Brazil

Many people think of Brazil and their mind automatically goes to Rio. Rio is best known for its Copa Cabana Beaches, it’s amazing nightlife, and of course Carnival.  It is a place you can go to let it all go, in addition to being a wonderful hotspot, Brazil is also famously affordable in terms of vacationing.  You may spend a little more renting a luxury beach villa or a home in the Buzios region, but what you’ll spend on nightlife, food, and the rest of your stay will be quite affordable.  This is a truly amazing place to share time with family and friends.

The locals are very colorful and they really do care about their health, so you will find a gym on almost every corner.  Don’t think it will be a breeze to workout with your Brazilian counterparts, they are known to be athletic and very competitive, so your spinning class may turn into a competition.  You certainly will leave feeling invigorated.  We offer quality vacation rentals in mainly the coastal regions, Buzios, Rio, and Ubatuba.  We are adding more locations every day, so check back.  The Buzios Beach area is known to be the very affluent and it’s beaches are beautiful.
