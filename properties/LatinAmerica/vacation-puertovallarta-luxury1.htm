Casa Gran Dia, Puerto Vallarta, Mexico


Casa Gran Dia is not only a place to vacation, it´s a small piece of heaven brought down to earth for you to enjoy in this paradise called Puerto Vallarta. This house is perfect for holidays, vacations, corporate retreats, family getaways, special celebrations etc.

 

Experience waking up with the sound of singing wild birds, the summer breeze sprinkling your face, and that morning scent of fresh fruits and homemade coffee waiting for you in the ocean view terrace of this beautiful Villa.

The view of the Bandera´s Bay is fascinating; you can enjoy it in most of the bedrooms as well as from our game room and pool terrace. Other bedrooms have a view to the jungle which is also a sublime experience.

Puerto Vallarta Villa I is located on the south side of Puerto Vallarta, between Mismaloya and Boca de Tomatlan, two of the most famous beaches in Puerto Vallarta, with beautiful sandy beaches and water taxis always that lead to many other lovely beaches around the Bandera´s Bay

 

The house has 4 levels, 6 bedrooms, a TV room, a game room, 4 splendid balconies with magnificent views, a large living room, our triangle shaped dining room and a spacious kitchen.

 

The villa includes the services of a maid who will gladly clean the villa every day during your visit. We also have waiters and cooks at your service at an additional cost. Our concierge will gladly greet you and help you organize any activity or transport request you may have during your visit.
