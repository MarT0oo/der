This oversized unit with large open terraces was designed to be a dramatic, modern and cozy 3 bedroom coastal indoor-outdoor living space.  Punta Vista comes equipped with 3 bedrooms with king bed and full bath, bathroom amenities, fine linens, towels and robes, and a state of the art open kitchen with Viking appliances.  Punta Vista  offers all of the modern necessities available, such as Wireless Internet, Satellite TV, Phone, A/C, flat screen TVs.

Adjacent to the ultra exclusive Punta Mita Resort and away from the bustle of tourist swagger, Punta Vista 302 offers tranquility and privacy directly on the beach.  Playa Punta de Mita is on Puerto Vallarta’s North Shore near the tip of the Punta de Mita peninsula, a high end enclave and jewel of the Riviera Nayarit. This beach is special in being the last family friendly azure swimming waters protected from rough open ocean currents.

To the left are the astounding dream villas of Ranchos Estates, Punta Mita; a residential subdivision of extravagant beachfront villas within the Punta Mita Resort.  To the right is El Anclote beach, where the beach restaurants serve an ever broadening range, including fresh seafood, traditional Mexican, homemade Italian and world class high cuisine at Hotel de Artistes del Mar. Pangas wait to take you exploring the Marietas Islands, to a surf break or out to see whales and dolphins.  Fine shops and galleries are budding as an expat community grows. The Four Seasons and St. Regis Resorts at Punta Mita feature two Jack Nicklaus golf courses, and elevate the entire North Shore Puerto Vallarta, Riviera Nayarit region.

Daily cleaning and concierge service included in rental rates. Airport transportation is available. Private Chef and pre-stocking grocery shopping service is also available.
