Casa Melonal
4 Bedrooms | 4 Baths Ensuite | Pool Home | Resort Amenities       
Peninsula Papagayo
  From $1,950 per Night

Perched on the cliffs of the Papagayo Gulf, Casa Melonal is a luxurious, custom vacation estate that captures the untamed beauty of the Costa Rican tropic. With mesmerizing ocean views adorned with exquisite flora and fauna, the vistas of Casa Melonal call to those seeking an exotic and comfortable retreat that is truly “other worldly”.

In keeping with the integrity of Peninsula Papagayo, Casa Melonal is custom-crafted with distinctive natural textures and furnishings including bamboo, organic cottons, silk, and stone. With a palette of color that only nature can provide, luxury living has never been more inspiring.

Features of Casa Melonal include:

 

-5,400 square feet of lavish and luxurious interior space

-3,000 square feet of usable outdoor space including generous decks, zen gardens, Jacuzzi, outdoor dining areas and an expansive infinity-edge pool

-4 spacious bedroom suites with spa-like private baths and private terraces; 3 suites with a king bed and one suite with 2 queen beds; all with flat screen televisions and DVD players

-Expansive wet-bar ideal for entertaining

-Desktop computer and printer plus wireless internet access

-Custom-designed interiors with bamboo and Saturnia marble flooring

-Gourmet kitchen equipped with hardwood cabinetry and industrial stainless appliances

-Comfortable media room with flat screen television, Xbox, DVD player

- Maid service included. Caretaker and landscaping crew will also be on site to tend to outdoor and pool maintenance and landscaping needs. An IT service technician is on call to address issues related to internet service

- An in-home private chef and/or local cook is also available for an additional fee

- In-home spa/massage services are also available for an additional fee

- A private boat captain available upon request for sport fishing, diving, snorkeling, surfing and water taxi services

 

Guests of Casa Melonal will have access to Four Seasons amenities except during holidays and based on occupancy of Four Seasons. Casa Melonal is located in Peninsula Papagayo – a gated and very secure compound. Located only 30 minutes from the Liberia Airport.
