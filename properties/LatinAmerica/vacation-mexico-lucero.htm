Villa Lucero
3 Bedrooms Ensuite | 3.5 Baths | Panoramic Views
Punta Ballena, Cabo San Lucas
From $ 850 per Night

Villa Lucero is a sprawling 3,750 square foot 3 bedroom (2 king & 2 full) and bath luxury condo located in the prestigious Auberge Private Residences in Punta Ballena, Cabo San Lucas, next to Esperanza Resort and Spa. Furnished and designed in contemporary Mexican style, it is easy to feel right at home in this lavish estate. Enjoy panoramic views of the Sea of Cortez from the master suite, second bedroom, dining room, and terrace. A fully equipped kitchen awaits your culinary expertise.

The living room is spacious enough to hold an intimate gathering of your friends. Guests of Villa Lucero in Auberge Private Residences at Esperanza have access to the exclusive Punta Ballena, Cabo San Lucas Beach Club which includes a pool, hot tub, restaurant and bar, tennis courts and a fitness center with showers and lockers. Vacationers are well taken care of from the pre-arrival planning with your dedicated vacation planner and professional concierge services 24 hours a day 7 days a week while you are in Cabo.

Other services such as babysitting, spa, private chef are also available upon request so pack up your bags and head to Villa Lucero - an enchanting place to stay.

Villa Lucero is located 40 minutes from the Los Cabos International Airport, 8 minutes from Cabo San Lucas, and 25 minutes from San Jose del Cabo.
