Villa Verde, Aldea Zama, Tulum, Quintana Roo, Riviera Maya, Mexico Vacation Rental

 

Villa Verde is a new luxury home located in the eco-conscious Aldea Zama neighbourhood of Tulum. It has 4 bedrooms, modern clean lines, an open concept design and private 15m lap pool.

Villa Verde is an architecturally designed tropical retreat with care and attention in every detail to give you the ultimate sense of comfort, peace and tranquility. The beauty of the Mayan Riviera is at your finger tips. The home is close to Tulum beach, beautiful cenotes, and the town of Tulum.

Designed by Co-lab design team from LA, this home showcases the ultimate in tropical living. With long expanses of wide open space, a beautiful lap pool, and a gorgeous rooftop patio, it has all the features you could want in a luxury home.

There is a beautiful connection between the indoors and the outdoors. The living area fully opens to the pool. The master bathroom has an open air bathtub in the garden - soak and watch the stars. The open rooftop is the ultimate sunset spot with jungle views.

With so much greenery surrounding you, it is a birder's paradise.

The house is designed to relax and connect. The kitchen has a large island, the pool lights up beautifully, there is a custom gas/charcoal barbecue and a projector for watching tv or your favourite film as well as a built in sound system. The home has tasteful ambient lighting inside and out to enjoy the beautiful evenings under the moon.

The pool has multiple water features and so you can be calmed by the sound of flowing water and the birds that frequent the garden.

The 4th bedroom is a studio complete with a kitchen and private bathroom. It is a beautiful space with incredible views, perfect for guests who would like a little more privacy from the rest of the group.

All 4 bedrooms have king beds (one can be 2 twins by request), ensuite bathrooms, custom closets, and large opening windows that fully open to the cascading tropical plants and greenery that surround the house.
