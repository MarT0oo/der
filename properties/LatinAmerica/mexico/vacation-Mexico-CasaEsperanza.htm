Casa Esperanza, Pedregal, Los Cabos, Cabo San Lucas, Mexico Vacation Rental

 

Located in the dynamic hillside community of Pedregal, Casa Esperanza is a newly constructed luxury vacation rental with accommodations for eighteen. Unlike most other villas in Pedregal, Esperanza has direct walking access to the beachfront, a feature that is rare in this steep hillside area. And, while maintaining an exceptional level of privacy, it’s just five minutes from the heart of downtown Cabo San Lucas.

Whether you’re looking for a secluded place to sit and read, or a spacious environment for a full group hangout, Esperanza has the spot for you. The main living area is massive, and the domed brick-ceiling and numerous windowed openings give it an even grander feel. When the doors are open, the space is filled with fresh ocean breeze and natural sunlight and the line between inside and out is delightfully blurry. The lounge area has a plush “L” shaped couch and a couple leather arm chairs facing the dynamic ocean view. The kitchen features high-end stainless steel appliances, granite countertops, and a social breakfast bar that helps unify the entire open concept atmosphere. The formal dining table has space for ten and resides beneath its own brick-ceiling and an elegant chandelier.

Inside Esperanza’s walls you’ll find plenty to keep you busy and entertained, like Satellite television, a Sonos sound system, and Wi-Fi. On the terrace there’s an amazing infinity pool and hot tub combination, great for relaxing while looking out upon the ocean view. You’ll also find sun loungers and a fire pit on the balcony.

A great way to see Cabo’s dynamic landscapes is from the sea, and Cabo’s marina is the perfect place to charter a tourist or fishing boat for an afternoon of exploring. If there’s a golfer in your group, Cabo del Sol Golf Course is another scenic option. And, for delicious beachfront dining, boutique shopping, and an exciting nightlife, Cabo San Lucas can’t be beat.
