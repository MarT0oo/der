Laguna Encantada, Akumal, Yucatan-Mayan Riviera, Mexico Vacation Rental

 

Nestled away on the shores of the stunning Yalku Lagoon (1 mile north of Akumal), is the 6000 square foot villa Luna Encantada. This is an amazing villa with lots of space for family and friends. The villa is perfect for vacations, entertaining, weddings, events, yoga workshops and corporate retreats!

Villa Laguna Encantada has its own roof top deck with Pergola, here you have amazing views of the Yalku Lagoon and tranquil jungle! At night you can relax and stargaze its magic!

Entering the contemporary "zen" style villa decorated in creams and whites, with natural stone and wood you are greeted by the expansive open plan living area with huge sofa, just off this area is a den with flat screen TV and Stereo...perfect for catching a movie! The indoor dining area has seating for 12 guests and the gourmet fully equipped kitchen with top of the range appliances will please the most discerning chefs.

The glass hall way with tropical garden views leads to the 4 air conditioned bedrooms and luxurious bathrooms with ornate sinks with high quality fittings and stone features. The master suite has a king bed, couch for reading and ensuite bath with shower and shower tub. The guest suite has 2 queen beds and couch for reading and superb bathroom with an indoor shower and large indoor bath tub. The second guest suite has 6 singles and 2 queen beds (4 of these beds are loft style)...children will love this room!...the bathroom has indoor and outdoor shower. The 4th suite has 2 queen beds; the bathroom has an indoor shower and outdoor shower tub. All the rooms are huge with ample closet space.

Outdoors the enormous outdoor palapa has dining alfresco for 12. This area is perfect for entertaining! There is a flat screen TV, relax with your favorite drink and a movie or sporting event. The large swimming pool (30 x11 foot) is just wonderful, relax poolside on chaises embracing the tranquil Caribbean atmosphere. There is a BBQ grill for cooking up outdoors! From the beautiful garden surrounded by beautiful tropical plants you have a boardwalk to your own private deck with direct access into the amazing Yalku Lagoon, here you have fantastic snorkeling and swimming, you will see many colorful fishes, turtles and more.

We are sure you will have the most fantastic vacation here at Laguna Encantada, it's so relaxing, comfortable and spacious and lots of fun entertaining! Close by you have Akumal (1 mile away) with many restaurants, bars, dive stores, fishing, the beautiful Akumal Bay, along the coast you have beautiful beaches, the ruins of Tulum, Coba and Chichen Itza, eco parks Xcaret, Xel Ha, Xplor, the Sian Kaan Biosphere, beautiful cenotes, world class scuba diving and cave diving. There is so much to see and do you will visit again and again!
