Villa Austral, Punta Mita, Mexico Vacation Rental

 

Villa Austral is one of the stunning Punta Mita vacation rentals that make up the Four Season’s Resort. Built up on a hill, with unobstructed views across the ocean, this luxury villa is in one of the best vacation spots in Mexico.

The villa has a Zen-like feel as soon as you walk through the redwood door to find the stunning central water feature. Various outdoor spaces, from terraces to the dining area with an outdoor grill make this the most relaxing of vacation rentals in Mexico. The beautiful infinity pool is the perfect place to relax and enjoy the view.

With 4 beautifully appointed and deliciously cozy bedrooms and a guest studio, you will feel like you are home away from home. The housekeeper and chef will ensure you can simply relax and enjoy this most special of Mexico destinations.

Throughout this villa, which was designed to create a spacious and a userfriendly environment, you will feel the warmth and friendliness which made the Pacific coast of Mexico famous. The villa was built on a hill towering over the Four Seasons Resort, so you will have unobstructed views of the ocean from almost anywhere in the villa.
