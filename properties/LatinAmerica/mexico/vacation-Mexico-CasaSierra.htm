Casa Sierra, Puerto Vallarta, Mexico Vacation Rental

 

In the beautiful gated community of Sierra del Mar, just past famous Garza Blanca Resort on the South Shore is Casa Sierra, the perfect place families, friends, wedding parties or groups with special events seeking a larger property to vacation at.

This 11,000 square foot hacienda-style, old world villa is distinguished by soaring ceilings, wide hallways, incredible stone work, arches, interior fountains, and impressive gardens.

There are 10 suites with private bathrooms that are spread out throughout the villa to ensure privacy for the guests of Casa Sierra.

The living and dining room open up to expansive terraces complete with a large, salt-water, heated pool overlooking impressive views of the ocean and mountains. This same incredible view can be seen throughout the house and property.

Feel like a little beach time? Casa Sierra is located a short 5 min stroll from one of the finest beaches in the area, Playa Las Gemelas.

Feel like a trip into Puerto Vallarta to enjoy some of the fine restaurants, shopping or nightlife that this city has to offer?

Casa Sierra is a short 10 minute drive from downtown and the staff is available to organize transportation for the guests.
