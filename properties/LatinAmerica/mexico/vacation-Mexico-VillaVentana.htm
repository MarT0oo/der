Villa Ventana, Puerto Vallarta, Mexico Vacation Rental

 

Villa Ventana is a luxury six master bedroom, three level, media room, 10,000 square feet modern Mexican Villa completed in 2007 following two years of construction and meticulous finishing.

Villa Ventana is perched on a hill above Puerto Vallarta's romantic district (Los Muertos Beach area) in Las Amapas (just south of Old Town), with expansive and 100% unobstructed views of all of Puerto Vallarta and the entire Bay of Banderas and Pacific Ocean beyond.

Villa Ventana is the second home in Puerto Vallarta owned and managed by the owner of a five- star luxury and award winning boutique resort in the Fiji Islands, Qamea Resort & Spa, and as such we have a solid understanding of how to deliver a quality product. From comfortable beds with high thread count Australian cotton sheets, tasteful local and international art, a fully qualified (and trained in our Fiji spa) spa therapist, and a city and ocean view that is unsurpassed. We believe that Villa Ventana will fast become the premiere rental villa in Puerto Vallarta for discerning travelers.

Entry into Villa Ventana is from Calle Gardenias. When you step through the oversized wrought iron metal entry door, you will walk past an internal garden with a two-story waterfall and onto a landing overlooking the living room below, large heated infinity pool, and expansive views.

On the top floor of Villa Ventana is the first master bedroom, with wrap-around private balcony, en suite bathroom with bath and indoor and outdoor shower, and a 42-inch plasma screen satellite TV. Walk down the dramatic staircase into the living room with two+ story vaulted ceilings, gourmet stainless steel kitchen, and dining room. Bi-fold oversized wood doors open up the entire living and dining room onto a large deck that wraps around the entire second floor. On the deck, there is a large heated* infinity swimming pool, Jacuzzi spa, comfortable deck furnishings, and a second outdoor living area. This floor also boasts a second full master bedroom with plasma screen satellite TV and a media room/library, also with satellite TV, and leather sofa that converts to a full size bed if needed.

On the bottom floor of Villa Ventana is a third living room with two additional master bedrooms, each with two full size beds, private en suite bathrooms (with shower only), and balconies with expansive city and ocean views. All bedrooms are fully air-conditioned (remote control), and all water into the Villa goes through a state of the art water purification system.

Throughout your stay at Villa Ventana, you will be pampered by our house manager and personal spa therapist, Lette, in addition to a full time maid. Our chef is available to prepare two meals for you each day. You only need to pay for the groceries.

Connected to Villa Ventana, from the bottom floor, is a two bedroom, two bath apartment. The apartment is furnished and fitted out to the same quality as Villa Ventana, and is only available for rent as an add on to Villa Ventana
