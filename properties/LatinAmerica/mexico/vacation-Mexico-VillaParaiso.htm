Villa Paraíso, Las Palmas, Punt Mita, Mexico Vacation Rental

 

A luxurious four-bedroom retreat, Las Palmas 22 is nestled within the exclusive Las Palmas de Punta Mita residential enclave just a short walk from the spectacular sandy beaches of the Pacific Ocean. It embodies the true essence of indoor/outdoor living with modern Pacific Coastal Mexican architecture. A well designed and elegant courtyard welcomes you and the indoor/outdoor experience continues throughout with spacious covered and uncovered terrace areas, allowing for shaded and unshaded sitting and lounging areas as well as a private plunge pool. Guests are just a 5-minute walk to the Pacifco Beach Club, which is only accessible by members and guests of Punta Mita. The beach club offers ocean activities and services such as breakfast, lunch, dinner and spa treatments

The master bedroom with its elegant four post bed is located on the upper level, where you’ll enjoy a sitting area, large master bathroom, inside shower, Jacuzzi, walk-in closet and a private outside shower for that true tropical Mexican experience! The master bedroom offers panoramic views beyond the lush vegetation and westward towards the Pacific Ocean. The villa’s three luxuriously appointed bedrooms are located on the lower level in a peaceful tropical garden. Also on the lower level is a TV den with a large screen 3D TV and large sofa for the family to enjoy a movie should after a day in the ocean, on the golf courses or playing tennis. The three bedrooms have large walk in closets, and spacious bathrooms. A gourmet kitchen and outdoor gathering spaces enhance the vacation experience with family and friends.

Punta Mita, is Mexico’s most prestigious gated community and resort destinations, as well as a variety of experiences and memories. It is the sensual breezes of the Pacific Ocean and a clear evening sky peppered with stars. It is the gracious and attentive staff of Punta Mita Beach Clubs. It is Oceanside Jack Nicklaus Signature Golf Courses and fiery sunsets. It is families, friends and colleagues gathering to share adventures.

Punta Mita’s coveted address is enhanced by the familiar comforts that sophisticated residents expect. These include ease of travel, excellent medical care, as well as reliable safety and security. Punta Mita, situated in the Riviera Nayarit, is easily accessible with direct commercial flights from major airports throughout the U.S., Canada, Mexico and Latin America. Adjacent to the Puerto Vallarta International Airport is the Aerotron FBO, which serves private aircrafts. After arriving, the 28-mile ride to Punta Mita is less than 50 minutes by car.

Enjoy your vacation on your own terms at one of the coolest locations in Punta Mita!
