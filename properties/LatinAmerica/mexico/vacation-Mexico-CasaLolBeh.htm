Casa Lol-Beh, Soliman Bay, Tulum, Quintana Roo, Riviera Maya, Mexico Vacation Rental

 

Lol-Beh luxury vacation rental within the Riviera Maya Mexico is an exceptional twelve-bedroom beachfront vacation villa located in Soliman Bay, north from Tulum, offering incredible ocean and jungle views and full service staff at your disposition. The villa's architecture is a mix of Spanish and Mexican design, with high ceilings, marble floors and rich handcrafted furniture. It has a rooftop accommodation, multiple lounging areas around the pool and living rooms. Lol-Beh can accommodate up to 34 guests in a total of 12 bedrooms.

There are six beautiful bedrooms inside the villa and six exquisite bedrooms located in our guest houses. Each bedroom is furnished with luxurious hand crafted furniture, high quality and the most comfortable linens and mattresses, and an en-suite bathroom. We have an amazing staff that will be there to make up the beds and ensure you get the treatment you deserve and are met with a wonderful greeting every day. The ideal vacation is one where you do absolutely nothing during your stay and we fully understand that.

We have two living areas inside Lol-Beh. The main living area is an open space together with the dining area. It has a large seating area, perfect for gatherings, that is very comfortable and shares an air conditioning unit. It also has a smart flat screen television with satellite programming. The second living area is located at the entrance of the villa. It is a very zen area and ideal for quiet meetings without any interruptions.

Chef Services are included from Monday to Saturday. Let our Chef prepare your meals and snacks throughout the day. Enjoy them by the pool, on our roof terrace, or in our dining area. Our maids are available daily. They will clean your rooms, refill your water vases and make sure you have fresh towels. Our concierge will pass by during your stay to make sure you have everything you need. They will assist with booking activities, in house spa services, entertainment etc.. A complimentary arrival dinner will be served on the first day of your arrival.

A complimentary continental breakfast will be served daily. The Chef can always prepare additional items upon request! *Cost of groceries are not included. Whether you want to relax in the shade or in the sun, our pool is the perfect place to enjoy. Our pool is the ideal place to spend your days when you are not out discovering the Riviera Maya. Relax on the lounge chairs, sit under the palapa by the beach or have meals on our outside dining table. Make sure to catch the beautiful colors of the sunrise in the early morning while having your breakfast under the palapa by the beach.
