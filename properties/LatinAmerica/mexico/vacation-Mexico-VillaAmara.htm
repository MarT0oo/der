Villa Amara, Tankah Bay, Tulum, Riviera Maya, Mexico Vacation Rental

 

Amara is right on the beach. You’ll love it because of the beautiful views, the location, and the outdoors space.

The house harmonizes with the beauty of it’s surroundings with an effortless and casual sophistication. The passion for tasteful and simple, plus a very committed sense of quality has taken this project to the next level.

Located in Tankah, only minutes away from Tulum Villa Amara is the perfect setting for a family vacation, excursion with friends, or a quick romantic getaway. This luxurious villa offers enough space to comfortably accommodate up to 10 guests.

This secluded beach of Tankah is nested in between the cristal clear water of the cenote and the sounds of the crashing waves. It's a special ecological zone that restricts developments and has a special conservation provisions to protect the fragile ecosystem in the area. We also invite you into cooperation in respecting our natural surroundings and helping to preserve this unique slice of paradise.

Villa Amara includes 5 spacious bedrooms with kings beds. Each bedroom has private bathroom. The Villa offers living area with comfortable sofas and fully equipped kitchen.

Outdoor living space is marvelous at Villa Amara where you can savor the incredible views of the Caribbean. There is a wooden deck furnished with lounge chairs and oceanfront swimming pool.
