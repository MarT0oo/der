Sol y Luna, Playa del Secreto, Puerto Morelos, Riviera Maya, Mexico Vacation Rental


Villa Sol y Luna is a full-service villa conveniently located half an hour drive from Cancun Airport. Our on-site staff, a bilingual concierge and a chef will help you enjoy this unique location for a once-in-a-lifetime vacation.

Villa Sol y Luna is a large and spacious villa on one of the nicest beaches in the Riviera Maya. Playa del Secreto is a gated community with only a handful of private oceanfront homes that boasts a wide expanse of white sand beach. No rocks or sea grass will impede your view of the turquoise Caribbean Sea.

There are 4 bedrooms on the villa ground floor; all are air conditioned with ceiling fans, private closets and full bathrooms with hair dryers. All except 1 have ocean views. A safe box is available in 1 of the bedrooms.

There is an indoor family game room equipped with a pool table, 45″ flat screen TV, a stereo sound system, wireless internet and a bar counter with a fridge, microwave and sink. An additional full bathroom is also available. There is a laundry room with washer, dryer, ironing board and consumables.

Facing the ocean there is a large terrace with vertical awnings to provide shade from the morning sun, equipped with a sofa set and a Ping-Pong table. A ramp provides wheelchair access to the villa exteriors, by means of a pathway. Guests in wheelchairs have access to the pool, beach area and to parking situated in front of the villa.

9 people sleep on separate beds in the ground floor villa.

On the upper floor there are 3 bedrooms, all air conditioned, with ceiling fans, private closets, each with a full bathroom and a hair dryer. All except 1 have ocean views. The master bedroom has a reading room with a fold-out sofa and a safe box in the walk-in closet.

The dining room may seat up to 18 guests at two 5ft diameter dining tables, separated from the kitchen by a counter. The gourmet kitchen is fully equipped with garbage disposal, dish washer, semi-industrial stove with gas oven, coffee maker, blender, toaster and the basic consumables, so you don’t need to rush to the nearest grocery store for shopping when you check in. The living room has wireless internet, a 45” flat screen TV and a stereo sound system. The dining-living room and the kitchen are ocean view oriented with a nice sea breeze. A half bathroom is adjacent to the dining-living room.

You will enjoy a breathtaking view of the ocean from the wide covered terrace accessible from the dining room. This is a highly recommended place for dining and social gathering. This terrace has a vertical awning to provide shade from the morning sun and a cozy sofa set with hammocks to enjoy the sunset accompanied by your favorite drink.

6 people sleep on separate beds in the upper floor villa,and 2 more on fold-out sofas.

The lounge furniture on the roof top terrace can be assemble into a nice comfortable bed, offering a more intimate and relaxing atmosphere to enjoy the panoramic views of the aquamarine waters, the poignant intensity of the Caribbean sunset. At night you may witness the full splendor of the moon reflected on the ocean or, on a moonless night, a sky covered with stars.

To accommodate larger groups, there is a side apartment with 3 bedrooms and 1 full bathroom; all bedrooms have private closets, ceiling fans and are air conditioned. It has a living-dining room with a kitchenette equipped with a fridge, coffeemaker, sink and microwave oven. 5 guests sleep on separate beds.

A range of 20 to 26 people sleep at Sol y Luna. There are 2 roller beds and 3 cribs for families with babies and toddlers. The pool area is a wonderful gathering place at mealtimes. There is a thatched roof kitchen-bar with a bathroom, an electric cooler, gas barbecue, sink and a counter with wooden stools for cooking and enjoying a relaxing family barbecue.

The 15ft by 45ft lighted swimming pool with Jacuzzi is flanked by an invitingly shady palm grove and a sand floor playground. You may rest on the lounge furniture or patio furniture shaded by thatched umbrellas, or take a nap in one of the several hammocks. From the pool you may head toward the beach for a restful sojourn in one of our hammocks at the beach palapa.

Villa Sol y Luna has all the amenities you require to be comfortable: broadband wireless internet, satellite TV with movie channels, video games, and DVD movie library will fulfill your needs. The staff live on-site and are always ready to attend you while respecting your privacy all day long.

For those who may want to mingle with people, Cancun and its discos is only 30 minutes drive, Playa del Carmen, 20 minutes drive south, and the fishing village of Puerto Morelos, just 5 minutes. Each of these vibrant towns offers a variety of gourmet restaurants, cafes, shopping and nightlife.
