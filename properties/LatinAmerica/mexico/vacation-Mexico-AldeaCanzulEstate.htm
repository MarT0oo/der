Aldea Canzul Estate, Tulum, Hotel District, Quintana Roo, Riviera Maya, Mexico Vacation Rental

 

The Estate is a combination of the following three side-by-side villas - Casa Chakte, Casa Chukum and Casa Chechen:

Casa Chakté features three bedrooms with en-suite bathrooms, a modern and large living room with dining area, fully equipped kitchen, private terrace and a beautiful roof top with stunning views of the ocean and the jungle sunsets.

Casa Chechén features two separate bedrooms with en-suite bathrooms, a living-dining room area with a fully equipped kitchen corner and a private terrace with beautiful garden views.

Casa Chukum features a beautiful main house with private ocean front terrace and a roof top with a massive sun bed and spectacular views of the ocean and the jungle sunsets. Additionally, Chukum offers a pool house for a total of 4 bedrooms plus a Mezzanine room. The villa also features an outdoor bar and a private central patio with sun beds next to a cozy private swimming pool.

Aldea Canzul is the ultimate luxury property in Tulum beach, where Mexican architecture blends with warm rustic jungle elements. Features three unique houses surrounded by tropical gardens just a couple barefoot steps from the white sand & turquoise sea of the Caribbean.

The property offers privacy and exclusive services, allowing guests to escape from their daily life and reconnect with nature. It is, with no doubt, the perfect place to enjoy Mexico's finest destination.
