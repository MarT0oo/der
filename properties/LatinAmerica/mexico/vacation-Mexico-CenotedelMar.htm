Cenote del Mar, Tulum, Riviera Maya, Mexico Vacation Rental

 

Cenote del Mar within the Tulum Region of Mexico is the most luxurious 8 bedroom beachfront villa in all of the Mayan Riviera. It is located on the white sandy beaches of Tankah Bay. With 8600 sq. feet of space, high ceilings, spacious rooms, private pool, and a rooftop terrace which gives a 360 degree view of the entire bay. This villa is total beach lovers dream with every wonderful amenity that anyone could want during their stay. From the Canoes to the beach towels and a staff, tehre is nothing to make you want for more for your next beach vacation with family and friends.

Villa Cenote del Mar ensures you a worry-free, luxurious and full of amenities that go beyond standard Riviera stay. This 8 bedroom villa will give plenty of space for up to 20 guests. Enjoy large family gatherings or retreats without feeling that someone is invading your privacy. Each room is lavishly done with your hotel style bedding and comfy pillows to make you simply melt into your sleep. The bathrooms are en-suite and have everything you need, please do bring your own soaps and shampoos because every country is different and shampoos here may not be to your taste. When traveling make sure to keep your personal items in tact, not leaving any money within the rooms.

Cenote del Mar is located in the Riviera Maya, a beautiful area that offers a range of activities for any traveller, including scuba diving, fishing, golf and shopping. For the person that loves history, Tankah Bay is located near the Mayan ruins and for the ecologist, it is an easy access to many natural attractions and eco parks. At the end of the day, Tulum and Playa del Carmen offer nightlife, restaurants, dancing, music and much more.
