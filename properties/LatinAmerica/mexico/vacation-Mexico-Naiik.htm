Naiik, Sian Kaan, Tulum, Quintana Roo, Riviera Maya Mexico Vacation Rental

 

Naiik, meaning Casa del Aire in Maya, is a chic contemporary oceanfront oasis offering 4 luxury bedrooms, settled alongside the pristine Sian Kaan coastline and lush tropical jungle.

The brand new villa offers the perfect blend of comfort and elegance. Four bedrooms, 4 bathrooms and large living space make it an exemplary property to visit with friends or family who are looking to discover the beauty of the secluded side of the Riviera Maya. Relax on the rooftop deck, perfect for lounging and tanning or catch the incredible sunrise with your coffee from the living room patio. The location offers complete privacy with the dense tropical jungle of the Biosphere Reserve on one side and gentle Caribbean breeze on the other.

Enjoy the breathtaking views from the rooftop infinity pool, which feels connected to the rest of the house through the modern glass bottom feature. Alternatively take the day exploring the largest natural reserve in the region - The Sian Kaan. Villa Naiik is surrounded by natural beauty, tucked away 13km south of Tulum on unpaved roads, you won’t find a better place to escape the daily pressures of life.

Your rental includes our personal concierge services prior to your arrival and then relax from the moment you arrive with our complimentary airport transfers for up to 8 guests and full time chef services throughout the duration of your stay.

Naiik is a stunning holiday rental that will leave you mesmerized as you drift through its open rooms and spacious outdoor spaces. Feel the soft sea breeze while taking in the charming views of the Sian Kaan coastline and lush tropical jungle. Quiet and secluded, this private oceanfront retreat located in Tulum along the Mayan Riviera is the perfect antidote for any stress caused by the day to day grind of modern life.

Step through the panoramic glass doors and enjoy the many terraces at this holiday home. Or enjoy the stunning sunsets from the spectacular rooftop while splashing in the infinity pool with a “looking glass” bottom. Other outdoor features at Naiik include an alfresco dining set for six, hammocks and sun beds. Head to the private beach with the villa’s kayaks and snorkeling gear for some fun in the water. The indoor perks at Naiik are perfectly suited for the modern family. Enjoy a movie from the library on the overhead projection system and make use of the iPod dock, Wi-Fi and yoga mats.

This contemporary villa represents a fusion of Caribbean design and a sensibility for avant-garde luxury that creates a stylish beach hideaway par excellence. The living and dining areas offer an open plan and a stellar view of the ocean. The fully equipped kitchen features stainless steel appliances and a breakfast bar. Your reservation includes waiter, cooking and housekeeping services.

Four breathtaking bedrooms accommodate up to eight guests at Naiik. All of the bedrooms provide en-suite bathrooms, king size beds, dual vanities, Sky TV, air conditioning, ceiling fans and hammocks. The two master suites also offer stunning ocean views. There is also an additional sofa bed in each suite.

Optimal for family reunions or luxurious weddings, Naiik provides every perk to make this a truly memorable holiday in the sun.
