Villa Marinera, Puerto Aventuras, Quintana Roo, Riviera Maya, Mexico Vacation Rental

 

Villa Marinera, Puerto Aventuras is located on the ocean inlet in Puerto Aventuras. The nautically inspired home offers use of the owners luxury yacht with bi-lingual captain for snorkeling and fishing trips.

Entering the spacious and comfortable villa you are drawn to the view of the luxury yacht, Aceituna (named after the owners wife Olive) docked and ready to go fishing or snorkeling in the many wonderful locations here along the Riviera Mayan coast! You will see an abundance of sea life, colorful fishes, turtles and more! The fishing season for Sail Fish and Marlin is excellent from March to September, Barracuda, Tuna, Shark, Snapper and Groupers is fantastic year round!!

The villa has 4 bedrooms with ensuite bath and 1 bedroom in the little house in the grounds. From the large open plan living and dining room step out to the huge deck with swimming pool, bar area with BBQ…ready to celebrate and cook the ‘catch of the day’. The villa is very comfortable and well set up for entertaining; you will feel like you are at home. Villa Marinera pays homage to the owner’s keen fishing interest, including a picture of the owner with Gregorio Fuentes, the fisherman and first mate of the Pilar (Ernest Hemingway’s boat).

Close by to the villa you have the Fatima Bay beach, or visit one of the many beautiful beaches along the coast on Yacht Aceituna or by car. The San Carlos marina is under 5 minutes walk, here you have restaurants, bars, scuba diving, golf, tennis and more!

The villa staff is wonderful and will make sure you having the most wonderful time here! Ahoy there to all the sea enthusiasts, sports fishermen and families who will have the time of their lives here at Villa Marinera!!
