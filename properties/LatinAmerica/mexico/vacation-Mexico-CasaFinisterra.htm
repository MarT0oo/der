Casa Finisterra, Pedregal, Cabo San Lucas, Mexico Vacation Rental

 

Casa Finisterra is an incredible, modern, ocean view home situated cliff side in the famous and exclusive development of Pedregal in Cabo San Lucas.

Architect Steve Harris designed this unique and modern gem combining minimalist design with rustic touches. Literally built into the mountain, the indoors melds seamlessly with the outdoors and the outdoors blends into the indoors. Solid glass walls provide unobstructed views of the Pacific Ocean and allow you to connect with the abundant wildlife which is always visible in the waters around Cabo.

A getaway spent in Casa Finisterra will give you all that you seek from your Cabo vacation: rest, relaxation, and the ability to enjoy the ocean’s wildlife which have made this area famous. Whether you enjoy outdoor dining, relaxing in the heated pool, or enjoying the Pacific’s breezes by the fire pit, this villa will always put a smile on your face.
