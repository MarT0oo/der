Villa Lunada, Punta Mita, Mexico Vacation Rental

 

Villa Lunada sits right on top of El Faro, a high quality right point break reminiscent of Malibu, and is within walking distance of two other surf spots. The sweeping views from any and every part of the villa include the peninsula, the Jack Nicklaus golf course and the brilliant waters of the Pacific. For those considering the St. Regis or Four Seasons Resort in Punta Mita, Villa Lunada offers a private oceanfront estate less than a half-mile from the St. Regis for 30-60% less: our luxurious property features a crescent-shaped pool, spa and surfing just a few steps away.

If you are looking to find the best of Punta Mita Villas, Villa Lunada is the area’s premiere surfing and stand up paddleboarding vacation destination. A gorgeous compound located inside the prestigious La Punta Estates, Villa Lunada is less than one mile from the St. Regis Punta Mita Hotel. With 5,500 square feet of space, there’s plenty of room to roam, so whether it’s a guys or girls-only surf and SUP vacation with your favorite friends, your style won’t be cramped.

The main villa has a master bedroom, two oversized guest rooms and a bonus room which can be transformed into a media room, a teenager’s hangout or a king suite, depending on the guests’ needs. The master suite itself is a fabulous spread with an office, a library, and a master living room with views of the Bay of Banderas from the huge soaking tub.

Common areas include a state-of-the-art kitchen, dining ‘pod,’ and an open living room that leads out to the pool deck and unrestricted views. The entire building is designed to maximize indoor/outdoor living, with a surfer chic décor that echoes the brilliant hues of the Mexican sunset and furnishings that welcome tired, happy paddlers after a day out on the water.

Outdoors, an infinity pool with Jacuzzi overlooks the beach, a fire pit awaits guests on chilly evenings under the stars, and a beach volleyball court is ready for a quick game anytime. The compound also has its own onsite organic garden, for fresh “farm to table” cuisine served in the adjacent Napa-style palapa.

A stay at Villa Lunada includes use of the SUP (Stand Up Paddle) facilities and lessons at nearby Hotel Cinco, use of the Four Seasons golf course (green fees are extra), and guests can request staff and a chef, as needed. We also offer the opportunity to practice early morning yoga on the terrace. If you are wanting to learn or enhance your existing practice, The Villa has its own yoga instructor, Stephanie Bowlin.

There are many Punta Mita Villas to choose from, but Villa Lunada stands out as the perfect destination for groups or families who are passionate about surfing, stand up paddleboarding, golfing, or simply soaking in all the beautiful sites of the Bay of Banderas. Enjoy your vacation on your own terms at one of the coolest locations in Punta Mita.
