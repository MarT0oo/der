Villa Lucia, Amapas, Puerto Vallarta, Mexico Vacation Rental

 

The Villa Lucia experience unforgettable views, yet close enough to walk to everything. Located hillside in Upper Amapas, just above the popular Romantic Zone & Los Muertos Beach, with breathtaking ocean views of the Bay & town from every room. The spacious luxe villa offers the best of indoor / outdoor living, perfect for enjoying the balmy tropical climate. The contemporary designed villa has simple lines & serene organic details blending harmoniously with the nearby tropical rain forest of the Sierra Madre mountains.

The heated infinity pool has an expansive terrace with covered outdoor living area plus sun lounges. The main villa has 4 spacious Bedrooms, 2 equal Master bedrooms on the top level & 2 generous guest suits on the level below Main, all have ocean views, private balconies & ensuite baths. Add the Casita with a 5th & 6th Bedroom with additional living area with TV & 2nd pool.
