Casa Chanel, Palmilla, Cabo San Lucas, Mexico Vacation Rental

 

A veritable hamlet situated in the Palmilla area of Cabo San Lucas, Casa Chanel offers sweeping views of the Sea of Cortez and the surrounding landscape. With carefully curated furnishings and extra amenities such as complimentary chef services, Casa Chanel will leave you feeling pampered and rejuvenated. We recommend this sprawling vacation rental for a holiday with family and friends, a golf getaway, a corporate retreat or a special celebration like a wedding, birthday or anniversary.

The sprawling outdoor deck area, which spreads out over 6,000 square feet, is larger than the interior of most homes. Guests can take a dip in the large infinity swimming pool while enjoying the amazing views of the sea and Cabo. Even if you don’t fancy a dip, the resort-like furnishings of Casa Chanel’s outdoor living area comes with plenty of deck chairs and comfortable couches for getting a tan while enjoying a good book and an even better cocktail. An outdoor grill area, alfresco dining, sun loungers and a fire pit – everything’s covered!

From the chandeliers and high ceilings to the luxury furniture, every room inside Casa Chanel brims with sophistication and style. The neutral tones of the surfaces create a calm and refreshing ambience throughout the 12,000 square feet of interior space. Other amenities, such as a palapa and outdoor stereo system, are also great for intimate gatherings – especially at sunset!

Sleeping at Casa Chanel is a sublime experience. Each of the eight exquisitely designed bedrooms feature with satellite television, a private terrace and large, comfortable beds. High-speed Internet and Wi-Fi throughout the house make it easy to connect and get work done while in the privacy of your bedroom. Your reservation includes waiter, housekeeping, driver and butler services.

Nearby San Jose del Cabo is a picturesque colonial town that also offers some of the area’s best beaches for surfing. A little further northeast, you will find the East Cape – a top spot for fishing and exploring the coastal lagoons for amazing birds and other wildlife. In Cabo San Lucas, you can visit the awe-inspiring stone arch, El Arco, or enjoy the attractions of El Medano beach as well as the sizzling nightlife.
