Set at the edge of a tropical jungle, in between the trees and under the moving shadows, Tulum Treehouse is a collaborative space the brings together design with the artisanal traditions of the Yucatan region.

Tulum Treehouse inlcudes large common areas, several spacious wrap-around terraces, multiple spaces for indoor-outdoor dining, and five bedrooms with en-suite bathrooms, so it can accommodate up to 10 people comfortably.

Born from the passions and dialogue between a diverse group of designers and artisans, this villa is an ambodiment of their collective backgrounds, sensibilities, and philosophies to welcome diverse groups of friends, family or all kinds of people who wish to experience Tulum like in their own home.

Located on the main Boca Paila Road which crosses Tulum from North to South, Tulum Tree House offers it's guests the most central location possible in Tulum with some of the area's most famous restaurants (such as Hartwood!) within a short stroll from the Villa. Another benefit to it's central location is a privileged access to three of Tulum's top beach clubs at no entry cost nor minimum consumption. One of the beach clubs you may access is located directly across the road from the Villa meaning guests can enjoy a full serviced beach just a hop away from this beautiful property.

** Please note that due to it's proximity to one of Tulum's most sought after bar, El Gitano, the noise level on Friday nights can be elevated. We suggest you inquire for discounted tables for Tree House guests and join in on the fun!** .
