Casa Paraiso, Alta Vista, Puerto Vallarta, Mexico Vacation Rental

 

A breathtaking villa designed to include all the rich architectural details of a historical home with all the luxury comforts and amenities you expect. The villa has architectural details like vaulted ceilings with massive wooden beams and colourful hand painted tiles in the kitchens and bathrooms.

The hillside location above the town gives breathtaking views, yet is a short walk to popular Los Muertos Beach and Puerto Vallarta’s best gourmet restaurants, art galleries, shopping and nightlife.

Extra meal service available for an additional service fee plus cost of Food & Beverages. Note due to the number of steps this villa is not a good option for guests with mobility issues.

About the Area

Puerto Vallarta is a resort town on Mexico’s Pacific coast, in Jalisco state. It is known for its beaches, water sports and nightlife scene. Its cobblestone center is home to the ornate Nuestra Señora de Guadalupe church, boutique shops and a range of restaurants and bars. El Malecón is a beachside promenade with contemporary sculptures, as well as bars, lounges and nightclubs.
