Casa La Vida, Punta Mita, Mexico Vacation Rental

 

Enjoying a breathtaking beachfront setting within the acclaimed Punta Mita resort, Casa La Vida is sought for its location on the shores of the Bay of Banderas. Located in the gated enclave of La Punta Estates the designer appointed, six-bedroom residence offers welcomed privacy. Guests can look forward to the attentive service of a well-trained staff and a vacation to be remembered for a lifetime.

In addition to its views of the Bay of Banderas, the two-level residence accommodates a large family or several families that can gather together in various indoor and outdoor patios and common areas, yet can retreat to more private spaces to nap or get lost in a good book. Guests are also given privileges within Punta Mita, where a host of amenities await.

Guests of Casa La Vita experience service similar to that of a boutique resort hotel. The property manager is poised to book a sailing or sport fishing charter, set up an inresidence massage, make tee times or arrange a lively fiesta. Though familiar with traditional Latin American fare, our chef is also expert at preparing dishes* influenced by regions and countries from around the globe. Whether the poultry, seafood, produce, fruits and meats are from local farmer’s markets or other points on the compass, guests will find the chef’s inspirations praise worthy.

With its setting on the shores of the Bay of Banderas, Casa La Vida is a coveted retreat for the discerning traveler. Inside Punta Mita’s gated enclave of the La Punta Estates, it invites guests to press the pause button on hurried lives and instead get away to the beach to relax and recharge.
