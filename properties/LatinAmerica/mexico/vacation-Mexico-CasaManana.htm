Casa Mañana, Palmilla Norte, Mexico Vacation Rental

 

Casa Mañana is your beach front getaway located in the renowned and exclusive Palmilla residence area, situated on the finest swimming beach in all of Cabo. This unique villa will make you feel secluded in your own slice of paradise with the sounds of the surf adding unparalleled magic to your stay.

This incredibly spacious and well-designed open floor plan is unique with the living area, dining room and outdoor patio all wrapped together to become the perfect place for relaxing and entertaining. Every one of the rooms boasts magazine-worthy views of the turquoise waters of the Pacific Ocean. The five bedroom, five-bathroom luxury villa has unsurpassed privacy, with a two master suites each with en-suite bathrooms and terraces. With a separate media room that boasts expansive ocean views and additional accommodations for two, Casa Mañana is perfect for a family vacation or a Mexican getaway with friends.

Designed to embrace its natural surroundings, Casa Mañana delightfully blurs the line between inside and out with unique natural water features and native plants. The open concept living/dining/kitchen space features state of the art appliances, modern furniture and floor-to-ceiling openings stretching its entire length. Casa Mañana’s intricate stone work sets a charming rustic tone that redefines seaside elegance.

Outside on the terrace, guests will love the private atmosphere created by the carefully crafted seating areas, spectacular fire pit and the natural surroundings of being on the ocean. Snorkel on the reef a few meters away or enjoy the kayaks or paddle boards before cooling off in the pool/spa – all while enjoying the sweeping views.

Whether you choose alfresco dinner on the patio or dinner in the dining room - it all adds up to sheer joy.
