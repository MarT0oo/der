Casa Palapa, Hotel Zone, Tulum, Quintana Roo, Riviera Maya, Mexico Vacation Rental

 

Casa Palapa is an eco chic beachfront home completely open to the caribbean breeze with mexican colorful touches. It embodies a grand suite on top; a two bedroom suite and a double room on the ground level. The grand suite encapsulates two spacious bedrooms of which the master is bestowed with a double-king size bed. Each bedroom within the grand suite has its own private bathroom. They both share a private lounge appointed with a Jacuzzi overlooking a vast sun terrace.

The two bedroom suite downstairs has one bedroom furnished with a king size bed; while the second bedroom is a twin room with two single beds. The bedrooms connect through a lounge area and share one bathroom. The private double bedroom is located within a large space with a view, furnished with a double bed and appointed with a bathroom.

All rooms boast of their minimal design which facilitates one's appreciation of nature.

Floor to ceiling windows allow maximum light and a feeling of openness that brings the outside in and the inside out. The sliding windows wide open to intake the breeze bring the guests in the enjoyably physical contact with the astonishing infinity views in the early hours when the Sun proclaims the new day from below the horizon.

The casa derives its name from an enormous palapa attached to it. Its open-sided thatch-roofed space amounting to 400 m2 totaling the space up to 9,200 m2 serves as a giant lounge appointed with a full kitchen, generous 20 seater dining table, capacious wine rack, floor lamps, large sofas, colorful bean bag chairs, and other seating. All distributed around the area forming functional and comfort clusters. Naturally illuminated during the day, at night the light comes from 30 variously sized and shaped handmade wicker fixtures hanging from the 15 meters high ceiling.

The ocean side of Palapa is given in its entirety to a rivulet-like swimming pool. Long and curving, it seamlessly connects the palapa to the house, where the pool commences with a lesser depth. It culminates where the beautifully massive staircase of the palapa takes one directly to the beach.

At the rear of the Palapa House lies a pristine jungle gently transpierced with a pathway winding in between palm trees. A few steps away from Casa Palapa is an open through tropical chapel standing on the rocks overlooking the ocean. It is spacious enough to seat a congregation of 105 persons. Considering its size and secluded location, Casa Palapa is an ideal accommodation for large ceremonies, events or big family gatherings.

Casa Palapa is the outcome of collaboration by a number of architects, engineers, landscape designers and interior decorators united in their vision of organic continuum, living in space intimately interlinked with nature and culture. These two forces charge the structure with extraordinary energies, physical and symbolic, the effect of which is pleasantly astonishing. Experience nature and eco luxury in CasaPalapa.
