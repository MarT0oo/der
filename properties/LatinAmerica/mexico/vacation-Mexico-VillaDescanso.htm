Villa Descanso, Pedregal, Cabo San Lucas, Mexico Vacation Rental

 

Spacious Pedregal Villa with fabulous ocean views that span from Lands End to Finisterra Beach. A private sanctuary of welcoming luxury, Villa Descanso offers views extending to the Pacific Ocean and a wealth of top-of-the-line amenities for you and up to 19 of your friends.

Seamlessly blending modern style and the flavor of traditional Mexican, this villa is in harmony with its environment perched on a hillside. Upon entering, you will appreciate the attention to detail throughout including Travertine marble flooring, granite and marble countertops, wood accents and a color palette inspired by desert hues. The nine suites, some featuring ocean views and private balconies, offer individual media centers and plenty of space for each member of your group to relax in comfort. Inside, you'll also enjoy the lavish gourmet kitchen, an Internet study with multiple stations, and a large steam shower/bath.

Outside, you'll find everything you need to enjoy the spectacular Baja sunshine. The spacious terrace offers an infinity pool with swim-up bar, a Jacuzzi for 8 and 12 lounge chairs. Fire up the gas barbecue, turn on the outdoor fire-pit and settle in for a marvelous evening enjoying the colors of the sunset dip beyond the Pacific. If you can tear yourself away from this magnificent retreat, you're just minutes to all the fun of Cabo San Lucas.

The full-time staff provide the ultimate in personal attention and service, handling the indoor cleaning and maid services, outdoor cleaning and maintenance. They are knowledgeable of the villa and can assist with questions and problems should they arise. They are also excellent cooks, and for an additional fee (negotiated directly with them) are available for meal preparation and cooking.

The Pedregal is the 'Beverly Hills' of private residential gated communities in Cabo San Lucas. Despite 30 years of steady growth, the Mediterranean-inspired community still manages to hold onto its Old World atmosphere while simultaneously embracing the new. Built into a magnificent granite hillside surrounded by exotic desert vegetation, the area's 360 acres feature sweeping panoramic views over either Cabo San Lucas Bay or the Pacific. With cobblestone streets, fine restaurants and numerous recreational options, the emphasis is on privacy and a casual, tropical lifestyle. Exquisite showpiece homes abound. Excellent swimming is available at Mèdano Beach, less than a mile away. The Pedregal is an oasis of exclusivity just steps to all the action of downtown and the marina. The Pacific side of the Pedregal offers stunning ocean views, with whale watching right from the terrace of your luxurious private villa. The beautiful stretch of beach along the Pacific is perfect for sunbathing and beachcombing. Big Pacific waves make this beach unsuitable for swimming.
