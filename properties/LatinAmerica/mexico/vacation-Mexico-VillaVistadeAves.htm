Villa Vista de Aves, Puerto Vallarta, Mexico Vacation Rental

 

Villa Vista de Aves is a private 5 bedroom villa located just a few steps down to one of the best white sandy beaches of Conchas Chinas & approximately 2 miles from all the fun of downtown Puerto Vallarta. Located inside a Beachfront Residential complex, Villa Vista de Aves is completely private & self contained with private entrance & pool. Easy access to beach thru the complex garden & elevator and also enjoy the large common pool & Palapa covered open air living space on the beach.

The villa has over 5000 ft² of indoor & outdoor living space, heated infinity pool & a spectacular water feature with 2 giant palm trees to frame the view of the sunset. The main level is an open concept entertaining area with living, dining & very well equipped kitchen all flowing out to the main terrace with a heated infinity pool with lounging areas & outdoor dining.

The villa design is contemporary with simple lines, with an infusion of Mexican hand crafted textiles and one of a kind folk art. Villa amenities include access to the Club Alexandra residential gated complex private beachfront pool, beach chairs available & Palapa common area.
