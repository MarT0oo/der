Casa Familia, Los Ranchos, Punta Mita, Mexico Vacation Rental

 

With its generous 13,000 square foot floor plan and six spacious bedrooms, Casa Familia comfortably accommodates up to 18 adults. The four luxurious master bedrooms feature king-size beds, expansive ocean views, private baths, individual climate control systems, refrigerators, personal safes, and satellite televisions with DVD players. In addition, there are two bunk rooms with private baths, each easily sleeping up to six individuals.

Water purification, telephone/intercom, Wi-Fi, and state-of-the-art sound systems span the entire villa. The media/game room has a big screen television, poker table, and games. Additional features include an office/exercise room with a desktop computer, internet telephone, and a suite of work out equipment, including an elliptical machine, two stationary bikes, and free weights.

Stepping outside, the beautifully maintained yard offers a pool and hot tub overlooking the ocean, as well as a basketball court with two nets.

Casa Familia is located on a bluff twenty feet directly above the beach -- a perfect combination of privacy, magnificent views, and immediate beach access. The expansive and wide white sand beach is great for walking, running, shelling, and exploring. The ocean is very swimmable with manageable currents and waves and is fantastic for boogie boarding directly in front of the house. There are great tide pools to discover or paddle out to one of Punta Mita's best surfing spots, La Lancha.

Punta Mita is one of the world's premier destination resorts with no shortage of adventures to experience during your visit. This tropical playground is surrounded by miles and miles of stunning shoreline. Below are some of the activities you can sign up for.

Oceanfront Golf

Golf enthusiasts have two Jack Nicklaus Signature Golf Courses to choose from, Punta Mita Bahia and the newest, Punta Mita Pacifico. Test your putting technique on the Bahia Golf Course, with its undulating fairways and greens. Pacifico Golf Course offers stunning panoramic views of the Pacific. The Punta Mita peninsula’s tropical landscape of palms and flowering trees adds to the pleasure of playing this golf course, which is at once challenging for experienced golfers and friendly for beginners.

Terrific Tennis

Punta Mita is currently home to eight tennis courts, two of which are lighted for evening play. The Tennis Club also has a pro shop, towel bar and offers professional instruction..

Scuba Diving and Snorkeling

Los Arcos, considered one of Mexico's premier diving spots, is an eco-reserve along the coast of Banderas Bay. Combining excellent diving with an extraordinary beach, Las Caletas is a preferred locale. One of Mexico's most notable diving areas, the Marietas Island Marine Reserve is just off the northern coast of Bahia de Banderas.

Sailing and Windsurfing

An excellent way to experience the grandeur of the ocean and the shoreline. Day trips introduce you to humpback whales, dolphins, sea turtles and rays, while sunset cruises showcase the fiery sky and the twinkling lights of Puerto Vallarta. Or enjoy a great sense of freedom as your windsurfer skims across the water.

Sport Fishing

Sport Fishing enthusiasts try their luck at catching Blue, Striped and Black Marlin, Dorado, Amberjack, Sailfish, Wahoo, and many others in the waters off Punta Mita. Whether in Banderas Bay or the Pacific Ocean, the action awaits from the deck of a luxurious sportfishing boat.

Swimming with Dolphins

The adventure beings with an educational introduction to the dolphins' habitat and "way of thinking". What follows is an aquatic experience you won't soon forget.

Whale Watching

From December to April, Humpback whales frequent the Bay of Banderas. Watch them as they breach the water and splash down in their magical "sea ballet" and listen as they sing their mystical songs.

Sea Kayaking and Surfing

Spend a fun-filled day in a kayak navigating the waters and exploring the natural wonders. If you prefer, surf the waves on your own two feet or by riding a boogie board.

Canopy Tour

Adventurers visit a private eco-reserve in the heart of the tropical forest of the Sierra Madre Mountains. Traveling on a series of zip lines from platform to platform, people of all abilities are able to traverse high above the floor of the rainforest, enjoying remarkable sights including glorious flowers, colorful tropical birds, and interesting reptiles.

Jeep Safari

Guests travel through the Mexican outback of the Sierra Madre Mountains in a specially designed Mercedes Benz SUV. You'll venture through mountain streams and rugged terrain to "discover" forgotten villages and stand awestruck at the rainforest's phenomenal floral and fuana. Your journey also includes a visit to a secluded coastline, where you'll enjoy a delightful lunch.

Horseback Riding

Traveling with bilingual tour guides, equestrian enthusiasts enjoy a day of exploring while learning the area's diverse history and experiencing the natural landscape in an unforgettable way.
