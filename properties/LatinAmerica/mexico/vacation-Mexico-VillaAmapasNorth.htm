Villa Amapas North, Conchas Chinas, Puerto Vallarta, Mexico Vacation Rental

 

Villa Amapas is synonymous with excellence in Puerto Vallarta. The staff service is unparalleled and the space incredible. Sister property to Villa Amapas South next door, the two villas share a common wall and a guest house and can be rented together for a total of 8 bedrooms in one compound.

Fabulous artwork by famous artists, antique wooden doors, gardens with fountains, sculptures and stone courtyards, an elevator and a beach front infinity pool make this one of our favorite luxury villas. There is a sandy beach just below the pool area; the popular palapa restaurants on Los Muertos beach in the tourist district are an easy 10 minute walk along a beach path to town.

As if prime location and upscale amenities were not enough at this top of the line resort villa, it’s the excellent staff that keeps guests returning year after year.
