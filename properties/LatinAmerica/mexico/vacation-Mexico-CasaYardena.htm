Casa Yardena, Soliman Bay, Tulum, Quintana Roo, Riviera Maya, Mexico Vacation Rental

 

Whether you would like to take a swim in our 20' x45' swimming pool, or go snorkeling in the beautiful aquamarine waters of the bay, what is most satisfying is that you could relax afterwards under the palapas in our hammocks by the beach or on the lounge chairs by the pool. When it is lunch time, our chef could prepare a gourmet meal or even a BBQ in our outside kitchenette charcoal grill that is located by the pool so that you don' t miss out on the beautiful sun.

Later, have a cold refreshing lemonade in the large and beautiful garden at the entrance of Casa Yardena and enjoy the natural view and the fresh air while listening to the chanting birds.

There are five artistically designed air-conditioned bedrooms with fourteen foot ceilings inside the villa, two bedrooms are furnished with two queen size beds, two bedrooms are furnished with one king size bed and one bedroom is furnished with one queen size bed. There are also four lovely guesthouses located outside, two of the guesthouses are furnished with two king size beds and the two other guesthouses each have one king size bed. Each of the nine bedrooms are equipped with ceiling fans, satellite TV, a DVD player, and a security safe. Casa Yardena's magnificent decor was inspired by Mexican architecture and craftsmanship. Every Bedroom has its private luxurious marble bathroom and the Master bathroom has a Jacuzzi tub with an incredible view of the Caribbean.

Casa Yardena's observatory rooftop balcony is equipped with an impressive lounge seating area, parasols, bar and a breathtaking view of the bay, the ocean and the vast jungle. The enchantment is complete as the sun romantically sinks beyond the horizon after another fabulous day. Casa Yardena is truly elegant, deliciously secluded and incredibly spacious, beachfront paradise home that charms its guests with its beauty, comfort and luxurious design, an exclusive property located on 80 feet of the private white sandy beaches of Tankah on Soliman Bay in the Caribbean, the world's second longest coral reef, home for colorful tropical fish and many varieties of coral and sea plants.

The decor of this villa is absolutely outstanding. There are beautiful tile floors, huge windows and sliding glass doors that facilitate views of the ocean from all rooms. There are gracious Spanish archways, fabulous outdoor gardens and immense balconies with ocean views.

Its spacious living room offers a thirty foot ceiling, an impressive half circle lounger that can seat together its guests comfortably with a spectacular view of the pool and the beach, which is ideal for relaxing and entertaining. The pool area is also equipped with an outside shower for your comfort. Free internet access is available which makes it ideal for modern vacationers. Our manager also provides personal assistance that include planning day trips and booking authentic Mayan massages at the comfort of your dream vacation home. Casa Yardena has five full time caretakers and a security service is also included, a security service company provides your vacation home with a security night guard.
