Casa Roka, Kupuri Estates, Punta Mita, Mexico Vacation Rental

 

Located inside the gates of Punta Mita's exclusive resort, this oceanfront villa designed by renowned architect Manolo Mestre, is the perfect destination for friends and family vacation. This villa is perfect to accommodates up to 14 guests.

The pool, terrace & beach cabana steps away from the sandy beach of Litibú Bay, are the perfect spot to enjoy your vacation.

Scattered throughout the peninsula, your pick of golf, tennis, and beach clubs are included with your stay. Casa Roka is perfect for family vacations, reunions, or a getaway with your closest friends. A day trip to downtown Puerto Vallarta should definitely top your itinerary during your sojourn. From sunset cruises and tequila tastings to snorkelling and seaside horseback riding, there’s an exhilarating activity for everyone in your crew.
