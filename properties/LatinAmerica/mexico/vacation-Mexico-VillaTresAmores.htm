Villa Tres Amores, Porta Fortuna, Punta Mita, Mexico Vacation Rental

 

The blue ocean and a white-sand beach await just outside Villa Tres Amores. This oceanfront villa in Punta Mita’s Porta Fortuna offers panoramic views and an exclusive location next to the Sufi Restaurant and St. Regis Beach Club. Every attention to detail has been accounted for to ensure guests have the most spectacular vacation experience designed for the world traveler with no less than 5 star expectations. The views, service and style make this luxury property a favorite for guests returning to Mexico year after year. Your holiday at Villa Tres Amores includes cook service at breakfast and lunch, as well as an on-site private concierge who can help arrange activities and excursions or whatever your heart could desire. Villa guests are welcome at the pools, spa, restaurants and tennis courts at four nearby beach clubs, and the Pacifico + Bahia Golf Courses. If you’d prefer to relax in private, the villa has its own resort-worthy outdoor living area with an infinity salt water pool, hot tub, barbecue, and shaded lounging and dining areas.

Bright, open indoor/outdoor plans are perfect for entertaining the whole family or gathering to relive the day’s feats on the golf course. The spacious combined living and dining room can be opened to the terrace via fold-away glass doors, and features streamlined designer furnishings. The house Ipad with music selections can be used for entertaining throughout the house or by specified rooms. Adults will find the in house custom designed bar one of the best places for fine spirits or a Cigar as the nightcap to an amazing day. Refreshments served in the afternoon can take place in the open courtyard, or set out on the island in the fully equipped kitchen.

There are four bedrooms in the main house at Villa Tres Amores; all have en-suite bathrooms. The main house has two king bedrooms and one with two queen beds, while the last bedroom has two queen beds plus a set of bunk beds. The combination of warm wood and soft-toned bedding and upholstery gives each of the suites a modern yet welcoming feel.

A popular destination for honeymoons, warm-weather escapes and golf getaways, Punta Mita enchants with its sandy beaches, postcard-worthy rock formations and low-key vibe. Tap into the relaxed feel as you stroll outside the villa and directly onto the beach, or head next door to Sufi, Porta Fortuna Beach Club or St. Regis Beach Club. By car, it’s only 10 minutes to shopping and dining in central Punta Mita, and 20 minutes to the ocean-view Litibu Golf Course.
