Villa Las Arenas, Cabo San Lucas, Mexico Vacation Rental

 

This gorgeous beachfront property is a gated for top security and offers luxurious accommodations for you and loved ones. You will find yourself immersed in modern Mexican architecture, striking tropical scenery and impressive views of the warm deep blue ocean! This tucked away gem is not too far from all the fun and entertainment. It is just a short 20 minute drive to San Jose and 10 minute drive to Cabo San Lucas. Featuring 4 bedrooms each with their own bath, a fully equipped kitchen open to all the living spaces. The outdoors of this villa makes you feel like you’re on your own private island. Spacious gardens, several lounging spots. Enjoy a margarita, or two by the pool and end the night by the fire pit enjoying the warm ocean breeze while hearing the rhythm of the waves crash. This place is perfect for special retreats or events.

Perfect for large dinner parties with a dining area for eight, you can entertain your guests in style and luxury at Las Arenas. This beachfront villa provides all sorts of amenities including satellite television, Blu-ray player, stereo system, Internet, bar with wine cooler, air conditioning and purified water. This elegant villa provides you with daily housekeeping, a security guard and exclusive access to the Punta Ballena Beach Club so you can rest easy.

Gorgeous marble countertops and dark wooden cabinets adorn your kitchen as the magnificent Mexican sun filters light through the large casement windows. Stone, cactus and lush grass are regular sights at Las Arenas that contribute to a sense of calm and regal relaxation. Grab some shade under a palapa, or walk straight from your backyard into the ocean. Welcome to your dream beach vacation!

Las Arenas features two master suites, a king sized guest bedroom and a private ‘casita’ with a king bed. With a total of four exquisite bedrooms, Las Arenas can accommodate up to ten lucky guests. Each bedroom includes an en-suite bathroom with shower or jetted bathtub, satellite TV and DVD player. Relax with nothing but the sound of ocean waves as your soundtrack!

The region of Cabo is located at the sunny tip of Mexico’s Baja Peninsula, where the Pacific Ocean meets the Sea of Cortez at the iconic rock formation known as El Arco. Just a short flight from many major U.S. cities, Cabo is an easily accessible vacation spot with warm weather year-round. Cabo San Lucas offers luxurious accommodations, gorgeous beaches, great golf courses, world-class fishing, gourmet dining, a hot nightlife and so much more. However you spend your time in the soothing Mexican sun, you are sure to emerge refreshed and rejuvenated like never before after your stay at Las Arenas!
