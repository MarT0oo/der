Casa Fryzer, San José del Cabo, Mexico Vacation Rental

 

Perched on a cliffside along the southern Baja coast lies a secluded luxury oasis overlooking the Sea of Cortez. With its impeccable sense of style, Casa Fryzer at Villas Del Mar is the tropical escape you’ve been longing for.

Spend leisurely afternoons on an expansive ocean-view terrace. Refresh in a contoured infinity pool with swim-up bar and inset lounge. Inside the villa, a circular coffered ceiling with statement chandelier accentuates the living area’s curved design. A custom kitchen with high-end appliances welcomes discerning foodies. Or leave the cooking to your own personal chef, who will prepare gourmet meals to your liking.

This residence boasts six en-suite bedrooms with TVs and ocean views—many with direct access to a private veranda and spa.

When you want to spend some time away from home, exquisite shops and restaurants await within the gated Villas Del Mar community.

About the Area

Los Cabos is an area surrounded by arid desert, beaches, and turquoise waters. There is an eternal harmony here, and the world seems reborn every morning at sunrise. Los Cabos consists of two enchanting and distinctive cities, each with their own remarkable features, joined by a 20-mile corridor. San Jose del Cabo, an 18th Century mission town, still keeps all of its historic flavor and picturesque charm. Cabo San Lucas, on the other hand, is a modern, bustling town teeming with stores, restaurants and nightlife.
