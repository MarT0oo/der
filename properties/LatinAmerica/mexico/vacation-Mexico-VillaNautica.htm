Villa Nautica, Puerto Aventuras, Yucatan-Mayan Riviera, Mexico Vacation Rental

 

The gorgeous villa Nautica epitomizes blissful and luxurious vacationing by the sea side. The villa features a roof top palapa with a double chaise lounge and wet bar with seating not to mention an amazing multi level beachside pool, deck area, kayaks and much more!

Entering the villa you are greeted by a huge open plan living and dining area leading seamlessly to the outdoor covered terrace, deck and beach area! The fully equipped gourmet kitchen will delight aspiring chefs, from here walk out to the grill area on the terrace…perfect for cooking the ¨catch of the day.¨ The ocean views from the villa are just stunning and you will embrace the nautical feeling Caribbean style!

The villa boasts 4 sumptuous bedrooms. The huge master suite with ensuite bathroom with double vanity, double shower and walk in closet is located on the ground floor. From the master suite you walk out to the Jacuzzi and deck area! A further 3 suites with ensuite bathroom and walk in closets are located on the upper floor.

The upper floor has a large media area with huge flat screen TV with satellite. Relax on comfy sofas and watch your favorite movie….when you aren’t watching a movie you will be mesmerized with the amazing ocean views that stretch horizontally the entire length of the living room. From here stairs lead to the amazing roof deck with palapa, wet bar, full chaise lounge and seating area with cushions.

The villa Nautica staff are wonderful and you will feel truly pampered during your stay. Complimentary Mexican Cook services for Breakfast and lunch are included. Our dedicated concierge will greet you on arrival and be of assistance during your stay.

A short distance from the villa is the San Carlos marina with many restaurants, bars, boutiques, delicatessens, golf, tennis and more!

Villa Nautica is one of the few exclusive 4 bedroom private villas located on the sandy Fatima Bay beach. We recommend early booking to secure your preferred dates.
