Villa Punta Cana, Punta Cana, Mexico


Welcome to a beautiful Ocean View Villa, located at a Golf Course. Just 5 minutes away from Punta Cana International Airport. The Beach is just 300 meters from the Villa and so is the Club House, with 2 restaurants, 1 Bar and the Six Senses Spa.

This stunning house was design with high standard of comfort and relaxation of our guest's. This 5 bedroom 5.1 bath homes comfortably sleeps up to 12 people & is ready for your arrival with high thread count linens and towels, a fully equipped gourmet kitchen & 5-star hotel quality amenities and extras.

You have free access to the Punta Cana Beach Resort. Sports such as sailing, wind surfing, tennis, snorkeling, scuba diving, horseback riding, kite-surfing, fitness-center, water ski and Golf are being offered.
