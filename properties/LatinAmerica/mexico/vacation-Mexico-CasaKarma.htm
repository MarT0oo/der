Casa Karma, Puerto Vallarta, Mexico Vacation Rental

 

Casa Karma in Puerto Vallarta Mexico is a vacation rental beachfront featuring 8 stunning individual Guest Suites, each with their own en-suite bath. The villa features a full staff of Chef, houseman, concierge, and maid to see to guests needs. Casa Karma is perfect for families, reunions, special events or just those looking to unwind, relax and enjoy the natural beauty of Puerto Vallarta! One of Puerto Vallarta’s premier villas, Casa Karma is a unique beachfront property that offers 6 remarkable king guest suites with ensuite bath and private ocean view terraces. The 7th king bedroom with ensuite bath has an interior water garden view and is located within easy access to the main living and dinning areas.

The new 8th Penthouse features an ensuite bath with vessel sinks and Kohler whole body shower. This special suite features a sitting area with large screen tv and private plunge pool on the oceanfront deck. Below the pool and on the beach – fire pits can be set alight for an evening of Oceanside fires, wine, and romance. Our elegant water garden offers an oceanfront mandala and endless fountains for strolling in your private garden or for up to 200 guests for weddings, parties, performances and special events.

It’s the combination of a great full staff, tasteful art and designer touches, along with elegant amenities, gourmet cuisine, luxury and endless guest pampering that make Casa Karma so special. Enjoy the heated infinity pool or jacuzzi and have a sunset cocktail after dinner at the poolside bar. Casa Karma also offers the simple pleasure of being able to dip your toes in the warm sand and cool waters of Banderas Bay just steps away from your villa. You can walk along Conchas Chinas Beach into Old Town in 15 minutes or take a short 3-minute cab ride and enjoy the best of what Puerto Vallarta has to offer!

We do weddings for everyone! Are you dreaming of a beautiful, romantic and affordable destination wedding? Traditional weddings are expensive, hectic and you spend very little time with your guests. For less than the price of a one-day traditional wedding in the US or Canada, you can have a 4-5 day wedding vacation with your family and friends at Casa Karma! The Event Terraces at Casa Karma are the perfect oceanfront venue for your special day with space for up to 200 guests. Our event space is also perfect for small weddings. Our Wedding Salon is unique on the south side of Puerto Vallarta. It is a beautiful oceanfront wedding dressing room for the wedding party. The entrance from the Wedding Salon can become a dramatic and beautiful entrance down the staircase onto the lower event terrace where the wedding party and guests will be watching and waiting.

All 3 of our terraces have romantic fountains and lush landscaping. Our garden terrace is a beautiful setting for photographs before or after the wedding and a perfect setting to have an outdoor lounge area for your guests before or after the wedding. Our main terrace has a fireplace, wood-fired pizza oven, and a service bar as well as air-conditioned public restrooms for your guests. Our new Catering Kitchen has plenty of space for your caterer to provide excellent food, beverage, and service for the reception or dinner afterward. Our lower terrace is perfect for a sit-down dinner for up to 140 people following a reception on the main terrace. After dinner, dance the night away on the main terrace or the lower terrace, both of which are pre-wired for sound and light systems.
