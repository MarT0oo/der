Playa Mismaloya, Puerto Vallarta, Mexico Vacation Rental

 

Tucked along the coastline about 20 minutes south of Puerto Vallarta is Playasola, accessible only by boat and perfect for those seeking ultimate seclusion. With a private beach, private sports complex and full staff, this villa is the definition of retreat! Playa Mismaloya, which translates to “lone beach,” boasts a 3-acre estate with a 9000 square foot villa.

Experience the feeling of vacationing on a private tropical island at this fabulous beachfront rental property in Puerto Vallarta. This private villa is located on the pristine shore of Las Animas Beach and is accessed by a 10-minute boat ride via private water taxi from Boca Pier.

With all of the amenities of a private resort in Mexico, this magnificent property is ideal for family vacations and destinations weddings in Puerto Vallarta. Included in the rental rates of this Las Animas beach house is a staff of seven including a private cook and waiter for breakfast and lunch, a professional chef and waiter/bartender for dinners, a housekeeper, groundskeeper and nighttime security guard.

Nestled on three acres of private beachfront property and surrounded by Puerto Vallarta’s verdant rainforest, this lavish vacation rental features an expansive beachfront entertainment deck that truly impresses.

Begin the day with a quiet breakfast on the terrace while taking in the breathtaking views of Las Animas Beach. Read your favorite novel while relaxing in one of the padded lounge chairs overlooking the ocean.

After soaking in the sun, jump into the glimmering infinity-edge pool that features a swim-up cocktail bar. Step onto the private beach for a refreshing swim in the ocean or enjoy an afternoon of snorkeling on your very own piece of paradise.

The spacious thatched-roof palapa is the perfect setting for sunset BBQ’s with the entire family while on vacation on Puerto Vallarta’s Las Animas Beach. The palapa is equipped with a full outdoor kitchen and dining for 10 guests. Take a nap on one of the padded lounge chairs overlooking the beach beneath the shade of the palapa.

This luxurious 9,000 square foot beachfront villa with 8 Bedrooms and 9 bathrooms comfortably sleeps up to 10 guests. Each air-conditioned bedroom features an en-suite bathroom and a private balcony with gorgeous ocean views.

Additional amenities included with the rental of this private villa in Puerto Vallarta include a Jacuzzi, fitness center and a sports complex with a tennis court, basketball court and a beach sand volleyball court.
