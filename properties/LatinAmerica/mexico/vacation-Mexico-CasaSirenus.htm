Casa Sirenus, Playa Del Carmen, Rivera Maya, Mexico Vacation Rental

 

The upscale beach front Casa Sirenus enjoys the best location in the private gated community of Playacar. A beautifully curated villa with 7 suites exuding an opulent eclectic luxurious style.

Feel enchanted entering Casa Sirenus with the waterfall and tropical indoor garden. The reception area greets you with sumptuous sofas and bar area. Our butler will welcome you with refreshing lemonade and cocktails. The open plan gourmet kitchen boasts the highest quality amenities, here you have indoor dining for 12. There are 6 sumptuous suites in the main villa and a further suite located in the grounds. The suites are named “Hey You, Breath and Listen to The Beat, Here and now, Surrender and Flow¨. On the second floor you have a living area with satellite TV. As you walk through Casa Sirenus you will be delighted by all the unique details. Beautiful carpentry, luxuriant fabrics, granites, stones and decorations.

The swimming pool and jacuzzi is on the beach side deck area, here you have sofas for relaxation. The large beach front palapa with bathroom, bar, kitchen with oven and grill, indoor dining and lounge area. Enjoy your favorite cocktail, dine alfresco, relax, celebrate or watch the sunrise here. By the palapa there are steps leading to the beach.

Feel the soft white sand underfoot and take a dip in the majestic turquoise ocean. Here the swimming, kayaking and paddle boarding is beautiful.

Our full staff of butler/bartender, gourmet chef, concierge and house staff are on hand during your stay. Our chef will tempt your palate with dishes with local and international inspiration. Our mixologist will delight you with your favorites and experiential creations.

Close by to the Famous 5th Avenue, you have many restaurants, boutiques, cafes, bars and trendy nightlife. Golf and tennis is closeby. Along the coast you have many places of outstanding beauty, beaches, cenotes, eco parks, World class scuba diving, the Mayan ruins and much more. For an extraordinary vacation experience choose Casa Sirenus.

There is a large lawned area right on the beach. Weddings and events are considered – please inquire.
