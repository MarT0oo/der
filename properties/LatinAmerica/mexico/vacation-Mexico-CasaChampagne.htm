Casa Champagne, San José Del Cabo, Mexico Vacation Rental

 

Wait till you see the award-winning décor in Casa Champagne! The exquisite, flawless architecture creates an ambiance of luxury and true elegance. The family room includes a large sitting area with table seating for 8 guests. The gourmet kitchen is fully equipped with top-of-the-line appliances and granite counter tops.

Casa Champagne is located in the private, prestigious community of Palmilla – Caleta Loma. You are only 3 minutes from the famous Palmilla Golf Course, 5 minutes from the quaint town of San Jose del Cabo and only 1 block from the renowned Palmilla beach.

This 4 bedroom/4.5 bathroom villa offers over 7,400 sq ft (interior) and over 5,000 sq ft of outdoor space & gardens. The master suite has great ocean views and a private hot tub. The other 3 bedrooms have HD TV satellite receivers and Blue Ray. Don’t miss the media room with 60” HD TV, top-of-the-line audio equipment, IPod docking, Sirius satellite radio, CD changer, DVD and Blue Ray.

Relax & Enjoy the sunset and relish in the spectacular elegance of Casa Champagne!
