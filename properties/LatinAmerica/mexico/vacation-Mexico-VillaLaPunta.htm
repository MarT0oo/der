Villa La Punta, Punta de Mita, Mexico Vacation Rental

 

Villa La Punta, on a perfectly private peninsula at the ultra lux Punta Mita Resort, is marvelous modern Colonial Pacific Mexican architecture by Alex Pössenbacher. This masterpiece of a home was featured in Architectural Digest, commands stunning panoramic views of the cascading waves, the lights of Puerto Vallarta and the Marieta Islands. Villa La Punta, located within the gates of Punta Mita in the exclusive La Punta Estates, is completely private and affords the utmost in security for its most discriminating guests. Both large groups and extended families will enjoy this secluded beachfront compound – Villa La Punta is not located on a golf fairway nor overlooked by neighboring properties.

The villa itself represents a modern take on Mexican colonial architecture. A grand palapa, red tile roofs and wood pergolas, combine with classic Mexican elements including a Talavera tile-clad kitchen, rooms with vaulted brick ceilings, hand blown glass pieces by Ernesto Cruz, and walls adorned with artwork by Sergio Bustamante and Joan Miro.

The 9-bedroom estate comprises a curvaceous, airy main house with five oceanfront, ensuite bedrooms, a separate two-bedroom guest apartment, and private two-bedroom casita, on over an acre of lushly landscaped and manicured grounds. The master suite encompasses the entire 2nd floor of the bedroom wing. All bedrooms in the main house contain ensuite baths and private ocean view terraces.
