Casa Liberty, San José Del Cabo, Mexico Vacation Rental

 

A blend of traditional Mexican architecture and contemporary interior design, this elegant villa features not only a stunning design, but is also steps away from the golf course, the sand and the blue waters of the Sea of Cortez.

Situated in the exclusive new development of Puerto Los Cabos, Casa Liberty takes luxury living to extremes.

For those who love to entertain, this villa features a large gourmet kitchen complete with top of the line stainless steel appliances, granite counter-tops and clean straight design cabinetry, adjacent living and dining rooms include multiple pocket doors to create open family space.

The resort style outdoor living also includes a solid Gazebo aside of a thrilling bonfire; large outdoor grilling and dining area. Infinity -style pool and Jacuzzi surrounded by lush gardens and the orchard. Casa Liberty is hidden treasure in Los Cabos dessert for those who enjoy exquisite design.
