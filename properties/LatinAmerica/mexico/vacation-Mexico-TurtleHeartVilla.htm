Turtle Heart Villa, Tulum, Quintana Roo, Riviera Maya, Mexico Vacation Rental

 

Turtle Heart Villa is a luxury residence situated directly on a beach where hundreds of marine turtles—loggerheads, greens and hawksbills—annually nest. This spectacular location combines both breathtaking beauty and the natural splendor of an intriguing environment unlike any other along the Mayan Riviera. Whether you are planning to capture the magic of nesting turtles from April through December or just searching for the ideal getaway from winter, this bijou villa is sure to grab your heart and soul!

If luxury, privacy, and service suit your desires, Turtle Heart Villa offers the finest of all amenities. Boasting a spacious floor plan, this 5500 ft² “Smart Home” is designed to surpass the desires of the most discriminating vacationer. You will also be treated to your personal staff, complete with daily maid and garden/pool service. Chef service available on request for a nominal fee.

For your in-house entertainment there are two large living areas, one with a surround-sound large flat-screen cable TV and DVD player. Mood lighting, a whole-home audio system, with mp3 capacity, is throughout the villa, inside and out. Wi-Fi is fast; connect easily and anywhere on the grounds.

Lounge poolside in a hammock amid lush, tropical vegetation while enjoying an icy drink, surfing the net with wireless connection or catching up on your reading. The sundeck and pool area is a great venue for a BBQ fiesta. The exercise enthusiast can enjoy a full gym available upstairs in a private studio. Spa services such as massage, yoga, manicure and pedicure can also be arranged during your stay.

The rooftop patio displays breath-taking panoramic views of the Caribbean. Complimented with a grill, plenty of casual seating, sunbathing beds, lounge bar, massage table and the romantic charm of a million stars in the evening. The simple, yet sophisticated, design is the place to totally relax in the sun or shade with complete privacy. This is one of the premiere places in the world for viewing turtles where they nest and hatch. Many guests consider the roof “the best room” in the house.
