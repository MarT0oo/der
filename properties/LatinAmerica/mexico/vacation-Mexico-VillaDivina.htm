Villa Divina, Conchas Chinas, Puerto Vallarta, Mexico Vacation Rental

 

Villa Divina is an oasis of peace and tranquility right by the heart of Puerto Vallarta. This self-contained villa is perfect for everyone: from individuals, and couples to groups. Groups can rent the entire hotel with its seven rooms, pool, gym, and kitchen; including the penthouse which includes an additional 2 rooms and pool / jacuzzi. Daily services include. This place is a perfect option, not just for vacations, but also for small weddings, corporate retreats or social events. Alternatively, small families and couples can submerge in luxury by renting the penthouse: our premier room with its own private balcony, infinity pool, living areas, and kitchen.

Villa Divina is so aptly named, as it truly is divine! Beautiful artwork, hardwoods and colorful linens are highlighted against a dreamy white background. Every detail of Villa Divina has been thought out and executed perfectly. This villa also features an incredible 2 bedroom Penthouse suite with a private infinity pool and jacuzzi. Located in the most charming and historic area of Conchas Chinas with lovely views of the Bay of Banderas.

Our in-house food service offers succulent dinners prepared by our chef on demand. Overlooking the ocean and with the option of eating both indoors and outdoors there is no better way to start your day. Breakfast is prepared daily by our staff, and we’ve decided to let you choose what and how you want to have lunch. If you need help arranging lunch we can take care of that too. Please note that groceries for meals are not included in the rate.
