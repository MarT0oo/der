Villa La Datcha, Cabo San Lucas, Mexico Vacation Rental

 

La Datcha Cabo San Lucas is located on the Pacific coast at the southernmost tip of Mexico’s Baja California Peninsula. This place is a true haven for the fans of offshore fishing and water sports. Inspired by the traditional Mexican hacienda, the villa features brightly colored and textured interiors, terraces, shady patios and fountains, as well as many locally crafted pieces of furniture and decor.

Boasting a beachfront location, La Datcha directly overlooks the ocean. The property is made up of three interconnected buildings, two or three floors each. Most rooms are designed in the Mexican-American style, which comes through clearly in the kaleidoscope of textiles and mosaics, paintings, whimsical sculptures and woven decor. The US- and European-made furniture provides the high level of comfort it is known for, while the unlikely marriage of Russian patterns with the Mexican ornaments works remarkably well highlighting the Russian roots of the Datcha’s host.

The villa can easily accommodate up to 20 people as it has two large and eight regular-size bedrooms, each with an ensuite bathroom, a toilet and a walk-in wardrobe. The two large bedrooms each have an adjacent private terrace — one with a fireplace and the other with a swimming pool. The latter bedroom also features a study. The villa has several dining areas that can accommodate all the guests. They may gather at a big table in the spacious hall or have a fire-lit dinner in a special sitting area inside the pool.

La Datcha Cabo San Lucas is designed in such a way as to make it easy for guests to combine sports and relaxation. The villa is fully equipped to cater to your every whim. Shape up with the help of highly qualified trainers and brand-new fitness equipment in the villa’s gym, and wind down in the spa area equipped with a sauna, Turkish bath and massage parlor. The spa also has what could probably be Mexico’s first ever snow room — a snow-filled retreat kept at a nippy 14 °F.

The ocean is just seconds away from the villa door. There are two sunbathing and relaxation zones with sun loungers, one on the beach and the other by the poolside on the large terrace in front of the main building. The water edge of the infinity pool visually merges with the ocean on the horizon. The Gulf of California is a breeding ground for blue and humpback whales. From November to April, witness these majestic animals’ mating rituals from the comfort of your La Datcha bedroom or pool. Sit by the isle of fire in this borderless expanse of blue, enjoy a glass of wine from Oleg Tinkov’s private collection, and feel the magic of La Datcha Cabo San Lucas as all your worries and cares melt away.

Cabo San Lucas is the perfect spot for offshore fishing. The catch here is not limited to the standard tuna, snappers, sailfish or swordfish. This place is known as the «Marlin Capital of the World», and for good reason: it was here that in spring 2018 a US tourist caught a 12-foot blue marlin weighing 970 pounds! Specimens like this are quite rare, but you can always count on encountering 200–450 lb. large marlins. La Datcha Cabo San Lucas will help you make your dream catch a reality — a high speed fishing boat and an experienced captain are on us. And so is the fishing gear — we will take care of everything, if you let us know in advance.

Cabo San Lucas is also an exceptional place for surfers: the swell here is always great thanks to the distinctive floor relief. The coast near the villa is known for an astonishing variety of wave types: sometimes the waves can reach ten to thirteen ft in height, while on other days the water is quiet and still. There is no such thing as a bad day for surfing on the Baja California Peninsula. May to October are best spent surfing in the Gulf of California (also known as the Sea of Cortez), while from November to April you’d be better off going for the Pacific. We will provide you with all the necessary equipment and a surfing instructor to give you a tour of the best spots around.

La Datcha Cabo San Lucas can only be booked as a whole for vacations with family, friends or partners.
