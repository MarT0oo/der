Casa Ikal, Sian Kaan, Riviera Maya, Mexico Vacation Rental

 

CASA IKAL is a new stunning beach front villa inside the biosphere reserve of Sian Kaan, a mile north of Boca Paila bridge and 20 mins away from Tulum; the Caribbean beach-front brings you to a rich coral reef at swimming distance and the lagoon front goes directly to ancient Mayan canals, one of the most spectacular and exclusive places on earth!

This villa is now offered with exclusive high-end services from the same managers of very successful Casa Nalum: full staff, transfers, all meals, snacks all included in the rates.

Casa Ikal is a very contemporary house designed by world renown De Yturbe architects www.deyturbe.com and sits on a 5 acres estate that has 300 feet of pristine seafront and 300 feet of lagoon front, 300 sq meters of roofed space and another 350 sq meters of exteriors. The house has big open-plan living/dining areas, all with fine marble floors, fully fitted kitchen, storage room, 4 bedrooms and 4.5 Bathroom (4 en-suite) and plenty of exterior decks. The rooftop has hardwood decking, green roofs and a double infinity swimming pool with fiber optics lighting, and with spectacular views of sea, lagoon and the lush vegetation all around.

Casa Ikal also sets new standards of sustainability and it is completely auto sufficient - sun, wind power and a very silent backup generator - zero carbon property, but with all the modern comforts such as AC in all bedrooms, 120" cinema projections, SKY TV, wi-fi internet, etc.

Special attention is given to food and services with personalized dietary and hospitality requirements organized prior to your stay and with emphasis to a very attentive, but discrete service for a very relaxed and effortless vacation. This is the ultimate villa in the Mexican Caribbean's for the most discerning travelers!
