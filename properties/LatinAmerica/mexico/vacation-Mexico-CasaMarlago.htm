Casa Marlago, El Encanto, Punt Mita, Mexico Vacation Rental

 

5 Bedroom 6 1/2 bath 2 story home located in the new El Encanto development, within the exclusive gates of Punta Mita. Over 9,000 square feet of interior and exterior living space, including five bedrooms for up to 10 guests. Features ocean and lake views on three sides.

Four Ocean and Lake view Bedrooms with full en suite bath and Lower floor (basement) bedroom.

The villa's outdoor terrace borders one of the two Jack Nicklaus Signature Golf Courses in Punta Mita, and features a stunning view of the Bay of Banderas and Pacific Ocean. Comfortable loungers overlook the swimming pool, while the outside dining area and adjacent lounge are perfect for evening meals and cocktails. The outside fire pit will keep you warm on cooler nights when the Mexican sun goes down.

The living room, surrounded by glass windows on both sides that fill the space with ocean light, features comfortable, simple furniture underneath vaulted wooden beams with a nautical feel. Next door is the dining room, with ocean view and an oval stone table seating ten, and a fully equipped kitchen with a breakfast bar for three. There is also a TV room with wet bar.

Cross the villa's inner courtyard to the bedrooms. The master suite features a king size bed facing French doors that open onto the outer terrace, a beautiful ocean view, and an ensuite bathroom with stand-alone shower and bathtub. Of the four other bedrooms, three feature king beds while the fourth has two queens, and all have ensuite bathrooms. Varying color palettes, charming decorative touches, and traditional art make each room unique and memorable.

Casa Marlago is literally steps from a world-class, beachfront golf course, and you can ride the 6-person golf cart just 12 minutes to Punta Mita Residents Beach Club. By car, drive about five minutes to Punta Mita Village or Kupuri Beach.

The El Encanto development is particularly exclusive, featuring gorgeous villas such as Casa Marlago. With ocean, lake, and golf course views, this spectacular vacation rental is ideal for families and friends in search of a private spiritual sanctuary with romantic evocations of Old Mexico.
