Villa Mia, Puerto Vallarta, Riviera Maya, Mexico Vacation Rental

 

This elegant ocean view Villa resort is an undeniable treasure only the truly fortunate will experience. Plan your next dream vacations, wedding, business convention, and all kind of special events at Villa Mia.

The secluded 54,000 sq/ft beachfront Villa Mia Resort is located in the south side of Puerto Vallarta, about 20 minutes from downtown, where the green of the jungle and the blue of the ocean offer you a “special frame” for an unforgettable stay. The 115 Bedrooms have the most beautiful and eccentric views of ocean and exotic gardens, you will experience nature as you never had before.

By special arrangement Villa Mia has been made available to select qualified groups. The Villa is a privately owned estate and is not open to the general public.

Two huge terraces with palapa bars, one in the main area and the other one by the pool , are a unique design statement of this exclusive Villa.

Feel the ocean breeze while you are sipping a Margarita in the infinity pool or in one of the two large palapa bars. The Jacuzzi is so close to the ocean that you can almost taste the salty water.

There is a pathway to a large terrace in the ocean built with rocks used as an altar for weddings or as a rest area to enjoy the sunsets and the magnificent views of the Bay.To the right side of this area, there is a custom made pool (with natural rocks of the area) where you can enjoy the ocean water without any worries of the waves. From this point you can start a unique adventure with the Kayak that the Villa also provides at no extra charge.

Villa Mia can accommodate up to 34 guests, 9 rooms are the minimum requested for a minimum of 3 nights
