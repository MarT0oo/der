Casa San Sebastian, Puerto Vallarta, Mexico Vacation Rental

 

Casa San Sebastian, nestled majestically amidst cliffside boulders in a sheltered Pacific cove on Puerto Vallarta’s exclusive south shore, represents one of this coast’s most spectacular examples of contemporary “new palapa” architecture.

Visit our Puerto Vallarta villa and you will be surrounded by sweeping views, sunshine, color and open spaces. The architecture and rich Mexican colors are as dazzling as the remarkable setting.

An 8,000 square foot oceanfront luxury villa in Puerto Vallarta, Casa San Sebastian was inspired by the Mexican Pacific Coast architectural style that originated in nearby Careyes and has been made famous by renowned Mexican architects. This new palapa style emphasizes a sensual blending of local colors, textures, and materials in a manner that renders the Casa an integral part of its environmental surroundings.

Life at this Puerto Vallarta villa is nature. Ocean waves spill over massive boulders beneath one’s feet, brightly colored parrots and parakeets serenade from the mountainside jungle that is the front door, and (to adopt the romantic phrasing of the travel writer) a soothing tropical sun warms a visitor’s body while soft Pacific breezes gently caress one’s soul.

Five thousand, five hundred square feet of living area, including an 1,800 square foot self-contained servants quarters for four and an oversized two-car garage, are contained under roof. Terraces, both open and covered, provide another 2,500 square feet of living, relaxation, and entertainment area. Modern conveniences are the finest available. The villa features air conditioning throughout, a satellite TV reception “dish”, high-speed Ineternet access, unlimited long distance calling to the United States and Canada at no charge from the VOIP telephone, electricity and potable water from the modern Puerto Vallarta municipal systems, and wiring throughout for telephones, and CD stereo system. Even the built-in drawers and closets in the dressing areas are cedar.

And despite its dramatic cliffside jungle setting, Casa San Sebastian is only ten minutes from the international glamour of sophisticated Puerto Vallarta’s famed restaurants, sports fishing fleet, and the trendy nightclubs and bistros of the seaside “Malecon.”
