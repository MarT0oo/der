Hacienda Caracol, Soliman Bay, Tulum, Quintana Roo, Riviera Maya, Mexico Vacation Rental

 

Hacienda Caracol is one of the most luxurious private villa in in all the Riviera Maya. (9,150 sq.feet of living space) Located in Soliman Bay, the most charming beach located along the second largest coral reef in the world. It is a Mexican Hacienda style with a touch of Italian design. Enjoy the tranquility and most relaxing vacation in our villa whether it is between friends or family.

Let our Chef cook exquisite meals for you, in our fully equipped professional kitchen, while you enjoy them on our outdoor marble table by the 45x21 feet pool, or on the shaded rooftop sofas which give a magnificent view of the entire bay.

With plenty of areas for entertaining or relaxing, you will always feel comfortable in our villa.

Hacienda Caracol can accommodate up to 24 guests. All ten beautiful bedrooms have a 12 ft ceiling.

In the main house, there are eight luxurious bedrooms. Each bedroom has it's own flatscreen TV with satellitte and en suite bathroom. The stunning bedroom of both guesthouses give a breathtaking view of the beach and have their own kitchenette. Each bedroom of the guesthouse has a king size bed and a double bed.

Our staff works from Monday to Saturday. They are here to pamper you in a week of heaven. They will make sure your have everything you need in order to make your stay a memorable vacation. Let our staff know if you want them to book for you additional service such as massages, yoga, etc.. Let them know if you want to do Kayaking or Snorkeling on your own beach.

Enjoy a delicious complimentary arrival dinner so that you do not have to worry about anything on your first day. We want you to feel worry-free as soon as you take your first steps into our villa. We will also prepare a complimentary continental breakfast every morning, from Monday to Saturday and except the departure date. Let our Chef do the rest of the cooking for you, he will even help you pick the groceries you need! (Kosher, Gluten-free, Vegetarian meals available on demand)

Our villa is the perfect luxurious vacation spot to enjoy the most relaxing vacation in paradise!
