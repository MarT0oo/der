Punta Mita Celebrity Estate, Punta Mita, Mexico Vacation Rental

 

Welcome to Punta Mita Celebrity Estate. Punta Mita Celebrity Estate is exclusively for those special guests with the most discriminating taste who expect nothing but the best. It is the only 5-star private residence in Punta Mita, Mexico.

This magnificent private beachfront retreat, newly renovated in 2013, was originally built by media entrepreneur Joe Francis and designed by world-famous designer Martyn Lawrence Bullard. Punta Mita Celebrity Estate is nestled amid acres of lush tropical gardens in Punta Mita, Mexico. It combines natural beauty, warm ocean water, priceless seclusion, incredible 5-star international cuisine, endless activities and unparalleled luxury into a getaway destination unlike any other place on earth.

With a discrete, yet attentive bilingual staff available to accommodate any request and a list of amenities to satisfy the most discriminating wish list, Punta Mita Celebrity Estate provides a carefree environment for relaxation and recreation that is the very definition of "paradise."

The name Aramara comes from the Huichol Indians who originally occupied the magical land where Punta Mita Celebrity Estate now sits. Aramara is the Huichol Goddess of Life. The Huichol believe that all life forms were created from her.

This private luxury resort is just over two hours by air from Los Angeles, but feels like a million miles from anywhere. It’s truly one of the most exclusive properties in the world. Those few who are fortunate enough to experience it, will never forget their time spent at Punta Mita Celebrity Estate.
