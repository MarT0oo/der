Casa Jaguar, Puerto Aventuras, Riviera Maya , Mexico Vacation Rental

 

Casa Jaguar is a beautiful 4 bedroom/4 1/2 bathroom residence located on the main avenue in Puerto Aventuras, Riviera Maya. Contemporary style, luxurious finishes, elegant decor and high security system are some of its main features.

Comfortable rooms and dining seating for 12 guests.

One of the bedrooms is a studio with separate entrance for full privacy if required as it may also be accessed from the inside of the house if desired. The studio has its own kitchen, microwave, dvd player, air conditioning unit, 42" plasma display, XBOX, king size bed, additional futon and luxurious bathroom.

The large kitchen comes with granite counter tops and fully furnished with: dishwasher, air conditioning unit, fan, plasma TV, wine cooler and breakfast bar for 10 people.

Enjoy the views of the garden and the pool from the rooftop terrace all from your comfortable resting chairs. all 3 bedroom balconies offer a view of the pool, garden, lush neighborhood vegetation and the golf course.

Don´t lose the opportunity to enjoy romantic evenings in the lounge located on the upper terrace, where you can use the large jacuzzi and comfortable lounge chairs.

There is an outside barbecue and garden furniture where you and your family can also enjoy the pool that is suitable for children due to a shallow depth.
