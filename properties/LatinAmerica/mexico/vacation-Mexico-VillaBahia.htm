Villa Bahia, Puerto Vallarta, Mexico Vacation Rental

 

Villa Bahia Puerto Vallarta is a beautiful vacation rental designed by renowned Mexican architect Álvaro Gómez Flores, Villa Bahia in Puerto Vallarta is a 12,000 sq ft private oceanfront property available as a 4 or a 5 bedroom rental. All of our master suites enjoy ensuite bathrooms and closets in addition to magnificent panoramic views of the Banderas Bay and our a¿magical blue ocean. All of our suites are air-conditioned and carefully appointed to maximize comfort and privacy.

Panoramic glass windows and sliding doors invite your eyes to gaze outside and fall in love with our jaw-dropping views. The interiors are equally stunning, carrying its custom furnishings, original works of art from renowned mexican artists and a welcoming architectural style, with the use of the finest tropical woods and regional crafts of unparalleled quality.

Villa Bahia boasts over 12,000 ft² of sheer luxury and elegance in the exclusive South Bay of Puerto Vallarta, coupled with the whole array of amenities for your entertainment either indoors or outdoors, and the attentive service of our professional staff that will cater to your every whim during your stay.

Our main gourmet kitchen features top of the line stainless steel appliances, the perfect setting for our private chef, who prepares every meal to your particular tastes and needs. Our 38-ft saltwater, heated infinity pool and 6 person hot tub will allow you to soak in the magical sunsets Puerto Vallarta has to offer.

Our wonderful staff team is made of stars that will provide everything to satisfy all your needs with the biggest smile. Menus can be customized to your taste upon your reservation or upon arrival. Our manager can hire any other services and professionals to pamper you during your time at Villa Bahia. All made for a perfect and relaxing getaway.
