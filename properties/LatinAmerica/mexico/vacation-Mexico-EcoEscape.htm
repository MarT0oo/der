Eco Escape, Sian Kaan, Tulum, Yucatan, Mexico Vacation Rental

 

Eco Friendly Lodge features a wonderful open air concept, with air flowing through the house. No the house has no A/C and the furnishings and all the decor are locally harvested and Eco materials. The house is a wonderful blend of new and rich friendly harvested woods with a local wonderful glazed tile throughout. The house is made to be at one with nature and not destroy that which is around it. The beaches are sandy and pristine and you are close to everything without being too close to the neighbors and things that may disrupt your stay. Focus instead on beautiful surroundings during your holiday with us.

The maids will come daily and if you like the maid can cook breakfast, for a nominal fee we can also have lunch and dinner made by a cook. The beds are very comfortable and you should find all you need We do recommend you rent a car if you plan to travel to the areas with the nightlife or luxury shopping. We are more of a beach escape and here you will feel you are away from it all.

Eco Escape is completely ecological and auto-sufficient, using the latest alternative energy technologies to minimize the impact on the environment, but without taking away all the modern comforts such as internet, SKY TV, iPod stations and water pressure. At Eco Escape we can arrange your transfers, daily tours and lots of great activities in and around the area. Tulum offers late night activities such as bars and nightlife, shopping and lots of great bars and art scenes. Engage your senses and enjoy some surfing or boating. The ideas are boundless.
