Villa Bahia Chileno, Cabo San Lucas, Mexico Vacation Rental

 

The spectacular oceanfront' is exquisitely located on one of the tucked away mountains surrounding the peaceful Chileno Bay. The prime location of Villa Bahia Chileno, is just under 10 miles east from the fun nightlife in downtown Cabo and 12 miles west from the peaceful San Jose del Cabo with their multiple galleries, restaurants and the famous Art Walk, making this the perfect spot.

This stunning villa overlooks the Chileno beach, which is one of the cleanest and low-key beaches in Cabo. Great for snorkeling, diving or just simply relaxing. The residence offers a fully equipped chef’s kitchen and huge indoor-outdoor living room space. Four charmingly designed bedroom suites, each with a spacious signature bathroom, ample closets and private garden area or terrace. However, we’re sure you will be spending most of your time poolside at the infinity pool, which conveniently has a palapa attached to it with a full equipped grill and dining area to enjoy some good food and drinks while you soak in your own private piece of paradise.
