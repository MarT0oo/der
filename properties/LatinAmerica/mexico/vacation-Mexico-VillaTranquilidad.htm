Villa Tranquilidad, San José del Cabo, Puerto Los Cabos, Mexico Vacation Rental

 

The extraordinary opulence of this villa is seen in every significant feature. From the classic workmanship to the location, views, furnishings, amenities, condition and service. Step into luxury at this spectacular oasis set just steps to a scenic stretch of beach in the prestigious community of Puerto Los Cabos. Aptly named, Villa Tranquilidad surrounds you with an atmosphere of refined relaxation, offering a host of top-of-the-line amenities to complement the vistas of the Sea of Cortez and the soothing sound of the waves.

This magnificent villa boasts a spacious interior with lavish bedroom suites, wood-beamed ceilings, Travertine and granite counters, and an expansive living area opening to an unbelievably scenic terrace. Step outside, and you're greeted with palapa-shaded patios, an inviting outdoor dining area, an oceanfront swimming pool, a large Jacuzzi, outdoor entertainment system and so much more.

Can be rented as a 6 bedroom option where both Casitas are locked off (sleeps 12 max) and also as a 7 bedroom option where one Casita is locked off (sleeps 14 max).

This villa is approximately 5.8 miles to town. Puerto Los Cabos is a luxurious development located just outside of San José del Cabo offering gourmet dining, art galleries and quaint boutiques. Enjoy the world-class marina alongside the traditional fishing community of La Playita. Puerto Los Cabos features breathtaking oceanfront homes, an excellent golf course, beautiful views of the Sea of Cortez, dining, resorts and more. Whether you're planning a once-in-a-lifetime celebration or a family vacation to rival all others, Villa Tranquilidad sets the scene for romance, relaxation and recreation.
