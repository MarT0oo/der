Villa Tesoro del Mar, Puerto Vallarta, Mexico Vacation Rental

 

Villa Tesoro del Mar is a stunning 7 bedroom, 11,000+ sq. ft home tucked perfectly into the hillside of Sierra del Mar, an opulent gated community with 24 hour security just 10 minutes south of downtown Puerto Vallarta.
