Casa Naah Payil, Cabo del Sol, Mexico Vacation Rental

 

Casa Naah is a twist on a sophisticated dessert escape. Very minimal yet elegant in it’s design. The villa features spectacular outdoor living areas with breathtaking views the Sea of Cortez and lushes greens of the Golf course. The five bedrooms, five baths accommodates twelve of your guests. Making it the ideal place for families, large groups.

Every room has tall, sleek windows creating a wonderfully expansive space for relaxation and entertainment. Savor the Pacific Ocean views and breeze while relaxing poolside. Prepare tapas on the master barbeque grill; then join your friends and family for an alfresco lunch.

Unwind Baja style at the charming contemporary Casa Naah Payil. This stylish Cabo del Sol vacation rental has everything you need for the ideal Los Cabos holiday: views of the Sea of Cortez; welcoming indoor-outdoor living spaces; and a location in The Corridor close to beaches, golf and more. Four bedrooms plus the option to use a fifth room mean you can soak up the Cabo sun with up to twelve friends or family members.

Watch fishing boats and catamarans ply the ocean from the comfort of a private terrace with a heated pool, hot tub, lounging and dining areas, and a wet bar. As the sun sinks toward the horizon, heat up the barbecue and firepit and linger outdoors. Inside, you’ll find a TV and sound system, plus Wi-Fi and a wine fridge.

Open on two sides to the terrace and a courtyard, the villa’s great room is bright and inviting. Natural wood and woven chairs lend organic appeal to the sitting area, while there’s plenty of room around the large dining table. Even the fully equipped kitchen has a breakfast bar where you can perch for the morning’s first coffee or an afternoon snack.

This luxury property has two bedrooms with king beds, one bedroom with two queen beds and one bedroom with two double beds plus a bunk bed. Guests also have the option to use a fifth bedroom with a bunk bed. All five bedrooms have private en-suite bathrooms.

Casa Naah Payil is a short drive from swimmable Medano Beach, where you can play in the water or sample the cocktails and seafood at Tabasco, and from tennis courts and the nearest golf course. For lively restaurants and nightlife, head into Cabo San Lucas; for a gallery walk or quiet honeymoon-worthy dinner, drive into San Jose del Cabo.
