PAVR 001, Puerto Aventuras, Mexico Vacation Rental

 

PAVR 001 is nestled amidst lush gardens filled with tropical flowers and tall swaying palm trees, uniquely embodies the lifestyle of the Riviera Maya Mexico. Imagine hearing the soft sounds of the waves breaking onto the shore, waking up to a glowing sunrise....Located in Puerto Aventuras, this luxury gated villa offers comfortable elegance in a serene tropical environment with endless Caribbean views.

This stunning 5,600 ft² two-level villa floor plan flows seamlessly, the main house entrance opens to a wide center hall and steps down to the living and dining area enhanced with 15 ft. high ceilings. Special attention to detail is evident and includes lots of upscale appointments, the kitchen is fully equipped and detailed with custom mahogany cabinetry, the walls are dotted with interesting original art. The living and dining French doors open out to the covered terrace, pool and private seaside sundeck.

This vacation villa comprises 3-bedrooms, 3-baths, and master bedroom with large covered terrace facing the ocean, ensuite full bath and walk-in closet. Guest bedroom with ocean views, adjacent full bath. Large ground floor guest bedroom with French doors open to a small terrace offering relaxing garden views.

In addition to main house, there is a two story guest house; the ground floor is a living room/game room and above it a guest suite with full kitchen and ensuite bath with shower. Private parking for two cars.

Should you like the extra pampering, you can hire your own private cook to ensure your stay offers all the five-star hotel comforts you expect in your Riviera Maya vacation home, Relax in your poolside terrace, gaze at the horizon and the twinkling stars over Fatima the bay, PAVR 001 is perfect for those who like the tranquility of a private villa rental near Playa del Carmen.

Puerto Aventuras marina mall restaurants, cafes and boutiques, 9 whole golf and tennis courts located within walking distance. Fishing, snorkeling, eco-parks and Mayan ruin sites close enough for you to go on day trips.
