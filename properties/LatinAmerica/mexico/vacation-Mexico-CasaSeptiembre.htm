Casa Septiembre, Conchas Chinas, Puerto Vallarta, Mexico Vacation Rental

 

This charming 8 bedroom beachfront villa, which may also be rented with a minimum of five bedrooms, was built in the traditional Mexican “Beach House” style, has been decorated in a rustic and colorful, eclectic décor with lovely antiques, religious artifacts and period pieces giving the home a sophisticated European look.

Located approximately one and one half miles south of the city of Puerto Vallarta on the beautiful Conchas Chinas beach, this home was formerly owned by a famous film director and has hosted many Hollywood celebrities. With seven air-conditioned bedrooms, and an additional room perfect for teens or a nanny, this villa can sleep up to sixteen guests. All bedrooms overlook the large swimming pool and beautiful white sand beach below, six of the bedrooms have their own bath, while the others have a shared bath.

The enticing pool and sun terrace are perfect for entertaining with a large palapa bar, lush tropical plants, and fantastic views. A full staff including cook, houseman, maids and gardener assure you that your every need will be met with style and comfort.
