Casa Dewi, Bahia de Banderas, Punta Mita, Mexico Vacation Rental

 

Located in the prestigious beach of Paradise Coves in Punta Mita, a secluded and exclusive area north of Bahía de Banderas, Casa Dewi is a dream property, with spacious and comfortable spaces that have been decorated in the style of a Balinese sacred home. In the middle of its breathtaking landscapes, from its terraces and windows you´ll be able to admire humpback whales spectacular fertility rites during winter. Surrounded by exuberant vegetation, facing the most beautiful and private beaches of Mexico´s Pacific coast, endowed with luxurious services and just a few minutes away from the best eco-tourism and adventure attractions, Casa Dewi is the sanctuary for a perfect getaway.

The property has 4 bedrooms and a full family room, and is prepared to accommodate up to 12 guests. Its outdoor lounge terraces and deck are perfect spots for relaxation, an entertainment family room and all the amenities are available. Its infinity pool, overlooking the breathtaking Mexican Pacific ocean, a paradise private beach and Jacuzzi complete the experience

This architectural gem is situated in the prestigious Paradise Coves enclave of Punta de Mita, steps from one of the finest private beaches on Mexico's Riviera Nayarit. The home was designed in the style of a sacred Balinese site, with exquisite traditional artwork and pavilion-style structures set amid lush gardens. Expansive vistas of the Bay of Banderas encompass the spectacular fertility rites of humpback whales during the winter months. The villa's exceptional private staff and amenities provide resort-style pleasures in privileged seclusion. With four ample bedroom suites (two with king beds, two with pairs of queens), Casa Dewi accommodates families, destination wedding guests, and groups of friends up to ten in size.

Enjoy a refreshing swim in the infinity pool, and relax on plentiful lounge chairs in the warm sunlight.
