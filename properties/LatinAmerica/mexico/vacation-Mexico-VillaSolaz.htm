Villa Solaz, Nayarit, Punta Mita, Mexico Vacation Rental

 

Villa Solaz is a home that is sophisticated and modern with all the creature comforts, yet closely integrated with nature. The scene is set upon entry, where an elegant water feature beckons you to follow. You wind your way through immaculate exotic gardens – the sound of water all around. And once you arrive at the elegant open-air living palapa, you are greeted by spectacular views of the Pacific Ocean and the Marietta Islands ahead.

A seamless flow between living spaces – pool, beach, fairway, ocean and house – follows, all equally inviting.

This immaculate, bright, airy home will delight on many levels. It’s perfectly suited to the golfer, the surfer, the most sophisticated host or hostess, the fashionista or the beach bum. With incredible attention to detail and a charming staff ready to serve with a smile, this home offers the perfect vacation to all.

Your stay at Villa Solaz includes villa host, chef and daily housekeeping service, as well as access to the beach club and restaurants at the nearby St. Regis Punta Mita Resort. Of course, the villa’s oceanfront terrace, infinity pool and lounge area make it feel like your own private resort. A barbecue, firepit and hot tub invite you to linger outside into the evening. Inside, you’ll find a media room, wet bar and pool table for entertaining, and an exercise room for keeping up with your routine.

This expansive property is laid out over a central palapa and adjoining pavilions for an airy, modern Mexican feel. You’ll want to spend most of your holiday in the sitting and dining areas in the palapa, savoring the warm breeze and listening to the waves lap at the shore. But the indoor living spaces are equally beautiful, from a sitting area backed by floor-to-ceiling artwork to a dining area with a chic blond-wood table. Not only does the exquisite fully equipped kitchen sit under a vaulted ceiling, it opens to the outdoor barbecue area for convenience.

The villa’s six bedrooms can accommodate up to twelve friends or family members, and all have en-suite bathrooms. There are five bedrooms with king beds, including a master suite with a lounge area, terrace and ocean views, and one bedroom with two queen beds.

A popular destination for family trips, warm-weather weddings and honeymoons, Punta Mita is an enticing mix of upscale residential communities, five-star resorts and—of course—lush scenery. And you can see it all by golf cart, with the St. Regis Resort and the town of Punta Mita just 5 minutes from Villa Solaz, and the Pacifico golf course just 10 minutes away.
