Villa Tierra Adentro, San Miguel de Allende, Guanajuato, Mexico Vacation Rental

 

Perched on a hill overlooking the mountain valley of San Miguel de Allende, Tierra Adentro luxury villa offers a private escape only 15 minutes from the city center with complimentary daily van and driver, a thermal mineral rich pool, private citrus orchard, daily housekeeping, hot breakfasts, and breathtaking panoramic views

Designed by architect David Howell, the contemporary 2-level hacienda style Mexican villa features 5 spacious master suites all with ensuite bathrooms, a large kitchen, a shallow and deep water pool, rooftop lounge and deck, interior courtyard, dining room, and living room. The grounds feature lavish landscaping, mesquite trees, olive trees, lavender hedges, and palm trees.

The property sits on 8-acres and overlooks the stunning mountains and valley. The UNESCO World Heritage site of San Miguel de Allende is a short 15-minute drive away, offering incredible architecture, culture, culinary delights, shopping, nightlife and more.

San Miguel de Allende is considered the most beautiful city in Mexico and in 2008 it was recognized, along with the Sanctuary of Jesus of Atotonilco, as a World Heritage Site by UNESCO. Its attractive and cosmopolitan appearance makes it one of the favorite destinations for art enthusiasts. There is plenty of sites to visit, and they all stand out the city’s historical and architectural heritage. For the second year in a row, Travel + Leisure recognized the city of San Miguel de Allende as 2018 World’s Top City!

Come and experience everything San Miguel de Allende has to offer from her cobblestoned colonial streets to the warmth of her people. You will not be disappointed!
