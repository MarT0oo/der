Sueno del Mar, Soliman Bay, Tulum, Quintana Roo, Riviera Maya, Mexico Vacation Rental

 

Sueno del Mar Riviera Maya villa rental is conveniently located on the pristine beaches with turquoise blue water of the Mexican Riviera Coastline. The area offers a wide range of activities for all travelers, such as diving, fishing, golf and shopping. Located at just 15 minutes drive from Tulum's Mayan Ruins for the history buff and close to a few eco parks and other natural attractions for the ecologist.

There are five luxurious bedrooms located inside the villa and one charming guest house located in the villa's garden. The decor of this villa is absolutely magnificent. There are lovely tile floors, large windows and sliding glass doors. There are beautiful Spanish archways, a fabulous outdoor garden and large balconies with ocean views.

There are two living areas at Sueno del Mar. The first one can accommodate all guests comfortably with a spectacular view of the pool and the beach that is ideal for relaxing and entertainment. The pool is also equipped with an outdoor shower for your comfort. The second living area is located at the villa's entrance. Free internet access is available, which is ideal for modern travelers. Our manager also offers personal assistance, which includes planning and booking authentic Mayan excursions, and massages in the comfort of your dream vacation home. We also offer full-time caretakers.

Our observatory rooftop is equipped with an incredible lounge seating area, bar and a breathtaking view of the bay, the ocean and the vast jungle. Sueno del Mar is an elegant, secluded, spacious beachfront vacation home located in the world's second longest coral reef, Soliman Bay, home of many colorful tropical fish and varieties of corals and sea plants.

Your own personal Chef will prepare delicious meals for you in our fully equipped kitchen. Enjoy your meal in our dining area or outside by the pool. The Chef is dedicated to create gourmet recipes that will satisfy your appetite. Cuisines range from authentic Mexican to traditional European meals. All you need to do is provide us with a list of your favorite foods and we're happy to cook them, as well we can provide you with some menu ideas and always are happy to cook local cuisines for you and your guests. This is about feeling free to vacation and live life to the fullest and we want you to know that we believe in that idea.
