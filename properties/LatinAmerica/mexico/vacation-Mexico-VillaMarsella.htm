Villa Marsella, Punta Mita, Mexico Vacation Rental

 

Nestled into the hillside above the Four Seasons resort, this unique villa enjoys total privacy from everywhere. With the lush jungle on one side and amazing views of the Pacific Ocean, one will feel like they have left the real world behind. Our home is unique at the Four Seasons. After extensive renovations we have created a more home like feeling father than a hotel setting.

Wake each morning to the sounds of birds singing in the trees and cool breezes. Enjoy your morning coffee, prepared by Angeleana, our full time housekeeper, on any of the three private sitting areas. In the evening enjoy a glass of wine while the amazing sun is setting right before your eyes. It will take your breath away.

With this Villa you will enjoy each and every amenity the Four Seasons Resort offers. If it’s a relaxing message you are after or dinner delivered to your table, it can all be yours! The Resort offers 2 of the most magnificent beaches in all of Punta Mita, one family pool, one Adults Only pool and the magnificent Lazy River. Sip on a Margarita while the river gently moves you through lush vegetation.

Enjoy 18 holes of golfing on the one and only ocean green in the world designed by Jack Nicklaus. On any given day you could be the only person out there. It’s like having your very own private golf course. If it’s tennis you wish then the tennis/ golf van will pick you up and deliver you court side. The hotel also offers a fully equipped gym with Yoga on the rock overlooking the ocean each morning and a basketball court.

Kids For All Seasons Club is available for the children to take a break from the parents and engage in an arts and crafts activity or a game of soccer from 9-7 each and everyday.

Access to the Sea Breeze Beach Club is open to all Club Punta Mita Members. Subject to capacity limitation and minimum consumption fees. Note: Current minimum consumption fees are $80/per adult, $40/per child (ages 5-12). Children under 5 are free. Fees are subject to change at anytime. Available on a first-come, first-served basis.
