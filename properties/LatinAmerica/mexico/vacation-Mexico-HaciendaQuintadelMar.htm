Hacienda Quinta del Mar, Puerto Aventuras, Yucatan-Mayan Riviera, Mexico Vacation Rental

 

Feel the sun and gentle ocean breeze as you relax on chaise lounges in front of the Mexican Caribbean... Welcome to the stunning Hacienda Quinta Del Mar, Puerto Aventuras! An upscale luxurious hacienda style villa with 4 suites plus den!

As you enter through the main gates you are greeted by a very pretty tropical garden. To the left you have the casita (little house), here you have a studio with 2 single futons and the third guest suite upstairs with ensuite queen bedroom with mini kitchen.

The main villa entrance leads to the open plan living, dining and gourmet kitchen, from each area you have gorgeous blue ocean views! This luxurious villa is tastefully decorated…. beautiful Mexican furnishings, art work and authentic Saltillo floor tiles. The living room has huge comfy sofas and chair, with a large Flat Screen TV with Dish Satellite (US programming). The spacious gourmet kitchen is fully equipped and boasts a central island, breakfast bar and seating for 2. The dining area has eating for 10. Stepping out from the living room you have the multi level private swimming pool flanked by chaise lounges. There is an outdoor sofa with cushions and Mexican Artesanal table for relaxed al fresco dining under the covered terrace.

The ocean view master ensuite bedroom on the upper level is truly wonderful!... with amazing ocean views to wake up to each day. The luxurious suite features a four poster bed, walk in closet and private covered terrace with coffee table, 2 chairs and outdoor sofa with cushions. Even the large ensuite bathroom has ocean views! Also on this level you have the first guest suite with king bed and ocean view, next door you have a full bathroom. The third guest suite is on ground level with king bed and has a pretty garden view. This suite uses the beautifully decorated full bathroom off the hallway.

Each morning our Mexican Chef will prepare a delectable breakfast for you!... a delicious start to the day! Chef services may also be arranged additionally for lunch and dinner service…our chefs have delicious local recipes and more international cuisine! Our bilingual concierge will greet you on arrival and be of assistance during your stay.

Close by to the villa you have the San Carlos Marina with many restaurants, boutique, dive store, Dolphin Discovery, the sandy Puerto Aventuras Beach (Bahia Fatima) and much more! Experience your dream vacation at the beautiful Hacienda Quinta Del Mar!
