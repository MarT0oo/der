Mexico Mansion, Acapulco, Guerrero, Mexico Vacation Rental

 

The Mexico Mansion is one of the most famous villa rentals in all of Mexico. The mansion has been recently expanded to 13 suites with a rooftop penthouse party terrace. The mansion can accommodate up to 32 guests or can be rented with access to less bedrooms for smaller groups.

The mansion includes a full staff (Chef, Maid, Butler, Bartender and Waiters) to satisfy all your needs. There are amazing panoramic bay views from multiple areas of the estate. There are 2 pools available for your please. The infinity edge pool on the 5th floor has an integrated bar with panoramic views and can be heated for your convenience. The ground level pool is larger and has a spacious area for sunbathing. There are a total of 10 Kings and 6 Queen beds in 13 well-appointed suites.

The Mexico Mansion is one of Acapulco’s largest and most prestigious properties. The spectacular bay and ocean views from every angle of this 22,000 square foot palace have impressed famous business owners, celebrities and athletes from around the world. Your personal chef, will prepare your gourmet meals either from our extensive house menu, or customize your menu to suit your individual tastes. The Mansion also has a professional masseuse on call for ensuite massages. This Mega Mansion is perfect for bachelor parties, couples vacations, weddings and family reunions. Acapulco boasts the best weather in the world! Unlike the Caribbean, it never rains between the months of November through June. The weather year round is 88 to 90 degrees with a cool ocean breeze. The panoramic views of the bay are breathtaking by day or night.

Acapulco is a great beach destination, where you can fully enjoy the sun, sand and sea. Its beaches are ideal for water sports and are the perfect place to rest and relax while you listen to the sound of the waves as the sun shines down upon you.

However, Acapulco also has attractions for those who wish to enjoy more than the beaches and the sun. This exciting city has many centuries of history and culture to discover as it has survived the conquest, independence and modern times. Acapulco offers a great number of restaurants along the bay. Many beach restaurants put out umbrellas, chairs and tables in the sand, so you can enjoy your food and drinks right by the water!

Acapulco has much more to offer including world class night clubs, cliff diving, golf, deep sea fishing, jet skiing, parasailing, scuba diving, sailing, zip line adventures and much more.

Bring your entire family or 30 of your closest friends for a vacation of a lifetime!
