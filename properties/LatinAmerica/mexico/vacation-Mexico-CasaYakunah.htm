Designed to showcase the majestic natural scenery, Casa Yakunah boasts large windows and doors that let the outside in. Nestled on a jungle dune, with the ocean in both front and back, Casa Yakunah is a dream destination of the Mayan Riviera situated in an integrated environment that will amaze and delight.

With multiple vistas of the turquoise Caribbean Sea and the emerald Caapechen Lagoon, you will soon understand why the Mayans knew Sian Kaan as the place “where the sky was born.” Within this exquisite natural setting, you will discover a rooftop infinity swimming pool, a gas grill and lovely alfresco dining and lounging areas both in the sun and the shade. Your reservation includes housekeeping and a manager. Feel free to ask about the all-inclusive package that features food, drinks, a cook service and one massage session per guest each week.

The interiors of Casa Yakunah are bright and airy. The high palapa-style ceiling above the living room provides that tropical je ne sais quoi. Contemporary furnishings have been well selected and placed throughout the property. The kitchen comes fully equipped with gas hobs.

Five immaculate bedrooms accommodate up to ten guests at Casa Yakunah. Children are welcome. All of the bedrooms include air conditioning, ceiling fans, Sky TV and iPod docks.
