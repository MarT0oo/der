Casa Maya Kaan, Sian Kaan, Tulum, Quintana Roo, Riviera Maya Mexico Vacation Rental


Casa Maya Kaan is exclusive luxury within Sian Ka’an superb wilderness sanctuary. This private residence rests on one of the most pristine hidden white-sand coves facing the spectacular emerald Sian Ka’an lagoon. Untamed lush jungle vegetation around the property provides comforting shade enhanced by birdsongs. Casa Maya Kaan is the ultimate space where one can breathe and escape the daily routine where it feels like your home away from home.

The spectacular estate offers various spaces for meditative relaxation while gazing at the vast horizon. Casa Maya Kaan has been designed to provide its guests the comforts and pleasures of a beachfront setting that embodies the Caribbean unique appeal. Casa Maya Kaan is a two-level open concept home & retreat with over 360 square meters of casual living space that includes a crafted wooden deck terrace: here up to 40 guests can enjoy casual lunches and tropical starry evening meals on banquette seating and tasteful textiles.

Whether guests are looking for a private place where they can sit and read or a spacious environment for a full group hangout, the large living area has a spot for everyone. The open-designed kitchen is fully equipped, and the dining tables accommodate up to 14 guests. Organic elements and traditional Mayan artifacts reinforce the sense of place and add to the ambience of the natural flair and energy. The generous living space accurately celebrates the tropical indoor-outdoor lifestyle and takes full advantage of the ocean and the unique surrounding view.

Casa Maya Kaan offers a collection of spacious and air-conditioned Rooms and Suites finely decorated in traditional Oaxacan style, where craft and attention to detail combine to achieve an appealing and intimate nest. Each room is designed to provide space, discretion, and serenity. An abundance of in-room amenities including plush pillows and breathable bed linens, soft and oversized bath towels, and eco-friendly toiletries are available to all guests.

Casa Maya Kaan boasts three fabulous bedroom suites with private bathroom and a fourth deluxe ensuite double bedroom with independent entrance. In addition right on the beach, 7 glamorous Bell tents equipped with a queen-size sprung mattress made up with crisp white sheets, blankets, and decorative pillows, offer ample space to share the luxury with all the friends or the extended family crew!

Casa Maya Kaan recreates a serene oasis amid airy gardens and the shade of swaying palms. At night candles and fairy lights magnificently illuminate the grounds, and the aroma of scented flowers in the air creates an unforgettable dreamscape. At Casa Maya Kaan the sound of the ocean waves and tropical birds in the surrounding jungle foster utter relaxation and serenity. Daybeds and private gazebos are waiting for those who choose to laze by the ocean on the white-sand beach. A picturesque private wooden jetty offers a romantic setting where guests can enjoy beautiful sunsets over the stunning Sian Ka’an lagoon.

Casa Maya Kaan endows with fine amenities across the property and offers serene comforts that allow the guests to reconnect with nature, rekindle the spirit and reignite the soul.
