Cielo Mar, Cabo San Lucas, Mexico Vacation Rental

 

Cielo Mar is translated as “House in the Heavens”, this gorgeous 9 bedroom 12 bathroom luxury villa sits high atop the prestigious Pedregal Community. There are panoramic views from every bedroom of Cielo Mar, including the Marina and out to the Sea of Cortez and the Pacific Ocean. The attention to detail by the architect and design team comes through from the moment you descend the entry stairs and enter through the double doors. Every consideration was made, from the game room with pool table, professional grade poker table and bar, to the comfort of the seating arrangement in the home theater. The state of the art gym is equipped with the serious gym enthusiast in mind, with the best available cardio equipment, hand weights and flat screen TV’s to keep you motivated all the way through your workout. Each romantic bedroom suite features luxurious premium linens and bedding, walk in closets, private baths (some with luxurious oversized Jacuzzi tubs), and private sitting areas for space and relaxation. The large gourmet kitchen inspires the chef within to create magnificent meals and entertain, and the bi-level patios and pool area feature multiple comfortable lounge areas and a large Spa.

For the corporate getaway, or just to keep in touch back home, there is an elegant office space, equipped with wireless internet and VOIP services at your fingertips.

Privacy and Luxury come together in this glorious villa designed for the senses, and there is no Villa in all of the Pedregal that can compare to the Elegance and Beauty, where land and sky meet, at Cielo Mar.
