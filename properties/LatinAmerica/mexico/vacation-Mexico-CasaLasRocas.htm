Casa Las Rocas, Cabo San Lucas, Mexico Vacation Rental

 

Breathtaking... Exotic... Amazing... are just a few of the words guests have used to describe Casa Las Rocas Cabo San Lucas! No more shared pools like other Cabo San Lucas hotels! Private Beach Houses settled on the beautiful Sea of Cortez with miles of sand and rolling waves lapping the shoreline are the new demand in Cabo.

Rest easy as your night drops to the sounds of the sea a few yards away from your master suite. Watching the sunrise directly from your bedroom lounger with a fresh mimosa in your hand is something many will never experience this same way from luxury vacation rentals like this.

The Guest Reviews, Photos, & Videos of this compound do not even come close to how it feels when you first enter the private gates, but it does give you an idea of how amazing this property really is so at least have a look!

Head down to the Main Palapa with hand carved grand double doors opening up to a spectacular view to the Sea of Cortez.

Grab a famous Margarita from your hosts, Lucero and Rodolfo to begin the orientation of your vacation oasis. Each suite has amazing views, comfortable beds, private kitchens, personal loungers and much more.

The property is built to house multiple individual casitas as to give your guests the experience of having their own private villa with kitchen units.
