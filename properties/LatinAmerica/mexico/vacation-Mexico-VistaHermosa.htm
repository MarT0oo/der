Vista Hermosa, Playacar, Playa del Carmen, Quintana Roo, Riviera Maya, Mexico Vacation Rental

 

Welcome to Vista Hermosa! An 8 bedroom abode nestled up to the idyllic white sandy beach and beautiful tranquil turquoise water! The moment you enter into the villa, you will experience a captivating view of the water and talc-white sand from many of the rooms. Vista Hermosa features 8 suites each with it's own large private bathroom. Some of these bathrooms are comprised of both showers and bathtubs. There are 4 large king suites each with one king size bed. The remainder of the bedrooms consist of 4 double queen suites, each with 2 queen size beds.

Vista Hermosa boasts a large size kitchen and open living and dining area that welcomes your entire group to commune in one area, eat, relax, watch some TV after a day of sun and sand. Vista Hermosa is equipped with outdoor living and dining areas and huge plunge pool. These great areas of the home invites you to plan a group event such as a afternoon lunch or an evening BBQ under the starry sky.

In addition to being just accros the street from the beach, Vista Hermosa is located only 12 minutes walking to distance to town or most specifically to La Quinta's (5th Avenue), restaurant, shopping and nightlife district!
