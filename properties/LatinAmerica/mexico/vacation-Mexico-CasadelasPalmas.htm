Casa de las Palmas, Tulum, Riviera Maya , Mexico Vacation Rental

 

Casa de las Palmas is a stunning full-service vacation rental on Tulum Beach. Our because rental home has three bedrooms and sleeps 8. One bedroom is on the main floor with a queen sized bed and a balcony. Upstairs there are two more bedrooms with balconies, sea views, and private bathrooms. One upstairs bedroom has a king bed and the other has a queen bed plus 2 single beds. Natural elements and artistic touches throughout the house reflect the lovely laid back vibe and feel of Tulum.

Shade palapas and loungers are on the beach for you to enjoy. Sea kayaks and boogie boards are also available.

Families are more than welcome - there is a swing for small children and the world's best sandbox at your doorstep!
