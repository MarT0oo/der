Los Elementos Cabo, Cabo San Lucas, Mexico Vacation Rental

 

Los Elementos helps you to imagine the vacation of a lifetime, nestled in luxury on a soaring cliff 260 feet above the Pacific Ocean!

From your dramatic perch you will be delighted with stunning 200-degree views from Lands End to Solmar Beach to the vast Pacific and the cliffs further up the coast. Every room, including the seven ensuite bedrooms, offers a dazzling ocean view, perfect for whale watching or simply calming your soul.

As you approach the entrance to the villa, you know immediately that you are about to enter a new world of beauty and soul-soothing luxury. Lush landscaping envelopes you as you walk the entry path to the main entrance. As you near the entry, you’ll see on your left a serene tranquility garden, perfect for personal meditation or simply getting away from the activity that your guests will be enjoying inside.

The new spacious interior on the main level offers a gourmet kitchen with a wine refrigerator, contemporary cabinets and counters, a dining area with seating for 14, a great room with fireplace and a built-in 75-inch ultra high definition TV. Additional seating around the kitchen island lends a convivial air to any entertaining situation. Step outside to the patio right off the dining area and drink in the Pacific view, or see the action on the pool level, one floor below.

The pool level itself is designed for the ultimate in al fresco living. Cool off in the beautiful infinity pool, or relax in the hot tub or on one of the many chaise lounges on the pool deck. Immediately adjacent to the pool is a recreation room with a bar, full kitchen, bathroom, lounge chairs, pool and game table and television. Fire up the built-in gas barbecue, turn on the outdoor fire pits and enjoy a sumptuous repast in the covered outdoor dining area. Outdoor living reaches its zenith when you find yourself in Los Elementos.

When your day is finally at end, you can retire to the comfort of one of the villa’s seven ensuite bedrooms. One of these bedrooms is a luxurious 1,565 square foot master suite, with its own private balcony and hot tub. All of the bedrooms offer private baths, high-definition televisions and spectacular ocean views, and five of the seven suites have balconies with outdoor seating.

Concierge service is available to arrange for any needs you might have, whether its transportation, in-villa dining, restaurant reservations, grocery service or activities planning. If you can tear yourself away from this magnificent retreat, you’re only minutes away from the marina, downtown Cabo San Lucas or one of Cabo’s fabulous beaches.

Once you’ve experienced this beautiful Villa, your foremost desire will be to return and experience it again. Come and enjoy the best that Los Cabos has to offer.
