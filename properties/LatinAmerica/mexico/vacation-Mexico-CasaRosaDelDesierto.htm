Casa Rosa Del Desierto, Quintana Roo, Riviera Maya, Mexico Vacation Rental

 

Blissful vacationing is guaranteed at “Casa Rosa Del Desierto”, Villas Del Mar 1 Condo B305!….A gorgeous beach front apartment (with elevator), 3 large suites with ensuite bathrooms and much more!

Casa Rosa Del Desierto is beautifully decorated by the owner and you will feel right at home! The open plan living area leads to the beachside terrace with chaise lounges and palapa with al fresco dining and BBQ. The kitchen is well equipped with all modern conveniences. On the main level you have a half bath with washer dryer and king size bedroom with ensuite bathroom.

Villas Del Mar 1 has a huge multi level swimming pool, tennis court and beach side with palapas and chaises. A very short walk takes you to the San Carlos marina with the many restaurants, boutiques, delicatessens, bars and more they have to offer you in Puerto Aventuras.

Casa Rosa Del Desierto will truly delight you!!
