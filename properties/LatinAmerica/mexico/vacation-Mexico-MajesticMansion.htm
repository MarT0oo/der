Majestic Mansion, Las Brisas, Acapulco, Guerrero, Mexico Vacation Rental

 

The Majestic Mansion is a five star property surrounded by nature and privacy. It is located in the most prestigious gated community in Mexico, Las Brisas. There is a beach that only has access from this private community and it is only a 5 minute walk from the villa. Guests have the use of several private clubs with tennis courts, gyms, La Concha beach club and more.

We have recently added a spa penthouse level which features a steam room, 8 person Jacuzzi, tiki bar for 10, lounge area, garden with fountains and premium sound system. This penthouse spa level has an incredible view of both the ocean and Acapulco Bay. The entire property is serviced by a new 6 person elevator.

The master bedroom is on the first floor, right next to the large 50 foot infinity edge pool and bar (heating optional). This suite is perfect for those with limited mobility. All rooms have high definition TV’s. The penthouse owner’s suite features a separate TV sitting lounge area with a 75” TV, Jacuzzi, large granite bathroom and private terrace. The Royal family suite has a King, Queen and Double beds so that the kids can sleep in the same room with parents.

The neon blue & magenta pool and accent lighting are exceptional. The Havana bar is set up for watching TV, playing games or socializing at the 12 seat table. The rotunda with Italian chandelier and 4 large sofas makes for the perfect cocktail area before and after dinner. Dinner can be served in either the air conditioned dining room with 75” TV, or on the roof deck to enjoy the breathtaking views. The villa has 2 kitchens and 2 rooms for nannies or service help.

Unlike the Caribbean, it never rains between the months of November through June. The weather year round is 88 to 90 degrees with a cool ocean breeze. The panoramic views of the bay are breathtaking by day or night.

Acapulco is a great beach destination, where you can fully enjoy the sun, sand and sea. Its beaches are ideal for water sports and are the perfect place to rest and relax while you listen to the sound of the waves as the sun shines down upon you.

However, Acapulco also has attractions for those who wish to enjoy more than the beaches and the sun. This exciting city has many centuries of history and culture to discover as it has survived the conquest, independence and modern times. Acapulco offers a great number of restaurants along the bay. Many beach restaurants put out umbrellas, chairs and tables in the sand, so you can enjoy your food and drinks right by the water!

Acapulco has much more to offer including world class night clubs, cliff diving, golf, deep sea fishing, jet skiing, parasailing, scuba diving, sailing, zip line adventures and much more.

Bring your entire family or 18 of your closest friends for a vacation of a lifetime!
