Casa Nikki, Playacar, Playa del Carmen, Quintana Roo, Riviera Maya, Mexico Vacation Rental

 

Casa Nikki is a charming privately-owned villa in the exclusive gated residential community of Playacar Phase 1, in the heart of the famous Mayan Riviera. You cannot help but enjoy a relaxing, charming and cozy vacation to emerge rejuvenated like never before. Just a few steps away, you can revitalize your senses in the crystal-clear turquoise ocean and the finest white sands of the Mexican Caribbean. A short walk will take you to downtown Playa Del Carmen, one of Mexico’s fastest growing resort towns, comprised of international restaurants, trendy bars, beach clubs and chic boutiques.

The magnificent three-level Mexican-style villa offers you easy access to the lovely private pool and garden. Jump into the glorious swimming pool and let the smooth water flow over your body in utter relaxation. Your villa includes satellite TV, DVD player, iPod dock, sound system, Wi-Fi, alfresco shower and rooftop terrace with lounging area. Relax in the sauna and steam off life’s day-to-day stresses. A barbecue sets the tone for lovely alfresco dining. Your reservation includes housekeeping and 24/7 security and concierge services.

Palapa-style living and state-of-the-art architecture leave Casa Nikki feeling sleek and tropical with a touch of avant-gardism. Relax on the white leather couches or feast in style in the dining area for eight next to the magnificent kitchen. White brick walls adorn the living and dining area completing the themes of minimalism and edgy design. The fully equipped kitchen includes a breakfast bar and is perfect for the culinary genius in the group.

With a total of five bedrooms, this villa can accommodate up to ten guests. Enjoy the master bedroom with king sized bed, en-suite bathroom with shower, air conditioning, garden view and a Sky TV. Bask in a tropical oasis with nothing but the cool ocean breeze tickling the tips of your hair.

Playa del Carmen is distinct among Yucatan beach towns both culturally and commercially. While its beaches are similar to other destinations, its nightlife and hotel culture is hipper in the European and American styles, blending the traditional Mexican locale with a more permissive and opulent array of tourist options. Go diving, snorkeling or take yourself back in history by visiting the Mayan Ruins. Whatever your style, Playa Del Carmen has something for everyone. Time to take advantage and unwind in paradise at Casa Nikki!
