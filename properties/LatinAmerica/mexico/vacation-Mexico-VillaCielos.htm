Villa Cielos, Playa del Carmen, Yucatan-Mayan Riviera, Mexico Vacation Rental

 

Situated in Playacar Phase 1 and just a 2 minute walk from the picturesque white sand beaches of the Riviera Maya, Casa Cielos is a tastefully renovated 3 level home offering a beautiful terrace on each floor. The property is wrapped in tropical foliage creating a tranquil and exclusive villa experience and includes a large swimming pool complete with outdoor furnishings for poolside meals or just unwinding. The roof top terrace makes a great vantage point for viewing sunrises, sunsets, or bird watching.

This villa includes 3 bedrooms with king and queen size beds and 3 full bathrooms. Total living space is a generous 3000 ft² and the all-inclusive packages include support staff, chef services, beach side staff support, round-trip private airport transportation and much more. Mayan ruins are just a short walk away along with many other interesting local points of interest.
