Hacienda Isla Capitan, Nuevo Vallarta, Nayarit, Mexico Vacation Rental

 

This place makes you forget everything. Discreetly located on an island in one of the most beautiful and immaculate marinas on the Pacific Ocean, the exquisite Hacienda Isla Capitan is a gorgeous villa with easy access to the most pristine beaches of the Bahía de Banderas. It is surrounded by a mature palm grove of various species including mango trees, citrus, and a garden of aloe vera and other tropical plants and herbs. In the overhead branches one can observe iguanas, butterflies, and a variety of colourful and exotic birds. Here plants and wildlife come together to create an oasis of escape from the chaos and stress of everyday life.

This and much more is what we offer you.

Hacienda Isla Capitan is a place of extreme relaxation. It can accommodate eight persons in total comfort. It is divided into two areas: the bedrooms (completely equipped and air conditioned), and the communal areas (living room, dining room, recreation area, and kitchen), such that you can socialize with your companions without compromising the privacy of the bedrooms. All of the finishings are of stone, tile, and fine hardwoods; the roofs are of palapa and tile. It is an organic house, 100% crafted by hand.

All of the water in the plumbing passes through a purification system and is potable and safe. The villa has a security system 24/7.

The property has two wharves which can accommodate vessels of up to 100 feet. Its gazebos and gardens alongside the sheltered harbor waters offer picturesque corners and cozy retreats to enjoy.

A luxury 28 ft. Sea Ray launch will carry you to unspoiled beaches away from tourist crowds. A late model chauffer-driven station wagon is available to explore the island and discover its most beautiful spots.

We tailor our services and cuisine to accommodate your preferences and needs. Our chef offers gourmet Mexican and International meals especially prepared for your taste. As well we can provide vegan and vegetarian meals—even detoxification diets—all with a holistic touch. You may choose your options at the time of your reservation. You can find everything we offer in detail under “Amenities”— a listing of everything that is included during your stay at no extra charge.

If you need anything else during your stay help is at hand 24/7. Our administrator / concierge is always available, as are the rest of our staff including chef, housekeeper, waiter, captain, and masseuse/therapist. In-call medical and rehabilitation services are readily accessible.

We are animal lovers so feel free to bring your pets along for a small additional fee and deposit.

We are looking forward to serving you with respect, kindness, and discretion—with your comfort and security foremost in mind—to make your vacation time spent with us an unforgettable experience.
