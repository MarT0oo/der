Villa Captiva, Cabo San Lucas, Mexico Vacation Rental

 

Located in the Corridor, this stunning beachfront 6 bedroom villa is surrounded by breathtaking views of the Pacific Ocean (Sea of Cortez) all throughout the home, which inspires a soothing and relaxing ambiance. The spacious living and dining areas offer comfortable and stylish furnishing, making this single level layout, perfect for entertaining and gatherings. It’s conveniently located 10 minutes away from San Jose del Cabo.

Located on a pristine white sand beach with calm turquoise waters is the gem of Mexico, Casa Captiva. Situated in Cabo San Lucas, this jewel of Mexican architecture is a favorite among travelers who love ocean breezes and tropical sun right at their door. Surrounded by lush gardens within the exclusive development of Punta Bella, you will recognize this landmark property for its more than hundred palm trees, including coconuts and Washingtonians, as well as the variety of bougainvillea.

All sorts of modern amenities inhabit this abode including a multi-zone stereo system with satellite radio, iPod dock, Wi-Fi, dining area, TV room, fitness facility, piano and fireplace. Grab a cool beer and kick back with a game of pool in your own personal sports bar. Want in-house spa services? Let the staff know, and we’ll set you up with the perfect remedy in the private massage room. With daily housekeeping and a security guard, you’ll feel free, safe and secure, so you can enjoy your vacation, your way. Perhaps a splash in the massive infinity swimming pool? It never closes!

Traditional roof tiles, hardwood floors, verandas and unique interiors transport you to the early 1900s and an old world tropical charm. The finest Mexican furniture from colonial times is the final touch to make Casa Captiva the villa that emulates elegance and grace. Enjoy your fully equipped kitchen with breakfast bar that includes fruit painted bar stools, so you wake up each morning to a fun and funky atmosphere.

Accommodating up to twelve guests in six bedrooms, you and your guests will have plenty of space to rest and relax. The bedroom suites include king or queen sized beds, en-suite bathrooms, four en-suite satellite televisions, three DVD players, four terraces and ocean views. Step off the beach and jump into your fluffy bed for full recovery from your day to day.

Known for it’s amazing beaches, scuba diving, the sea arch El Arco and abundant marine life, Cabo San Lucas is one the top five tourist destinations in Mexico. With beach access to the Sea of Cortez, you can wake up and take that refreshing morning swim you’ve always wanted, or maybe a brisk walk along the sands. Come take a journey at Casa Captiva and allow this Mexican vacation to be the magic you’ve always wanted.
