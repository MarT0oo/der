Casa Uh Ka´ai, Sian Kaan, Mexico Vacation Rental

 

Tranquility, luxury, serenity. These qualities sensuously combine at Uh K'aay “the house of the singing moon” on the white powdery sand beaches in the Sian Ka'an Biosphere Reserve, a UNESCO jewel near Tulum. Set in a pristine, verdant jungle with a private beach, ocean at the door and no neighbors, Uh K'aay is a secluded oasis in a setting of rare natural beauty.

Hang around at the scenic infinity pool or relax in the adjacent Jacuzzi on the rooftop, while sipping on your favorite sunset cocktail served by the butler. As the sun starts to set into the lagoon, post your favorite moments of your stay over Wi-Fi.

Uh K'aay offers spectacular views from every angle, but none more stunning than the panorama from the roof. Choose your vista: the shimmering turquoise waters of the Caribbean; the lush, vibrant greens of the jungle; the muddy blues of the mangrove lagoon or even the bright stars in the ebony night sky.

The rooftop was designed for opulent living and features a magnificent 30’ infinity saltwater pool, an 8-person jacuzzi, a fireplace lounge, a star viewing area and even a large shaded table for cocktails and dining “above it all.” Created for harmonious living with nature, Uh K'aay features retracting walls, open corridors and multiple terraces providing seamless indoor/outdoor living. This luxurious 4-bedroom villa was carefully planned to work in tandem with ocean breezes, sky, and shade to maintain a fresh atmosphere with an ever-changing play of color and light. The interior design is inspired: clean and simple with wood, white and blue. The furniture, décor and art have all been created by Mexican artisans, beautifully integrating local heritage into this amazing home.
