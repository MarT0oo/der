Villa Camaleon, Merida, Yucatan, Mexico Vacation Rental

 

Our beautiful beach house is 4 acres private property with 600 coconut palms and hundreds of different tropical plants with full time gardener and housekeeper.
The house has 150 feet of oceanfront. It is divided in two Palapas, which are connected by an open wood bridge beautifully decorated.

The Main Palapa Is the social area of the house, it has the main entrance, living room, dining room. bar, playing table, kitchen with ocean view, big terrace, pool and a sandy beach bar with grill and wood oven. Walking through the 40 feet bridge you get to the Family Palapa, wich has a family living room, sat TV and video games.

It also has four big and nice suites, each of them has a small living room, bathroom, walk-In closet and terrace. White sandy beach where you can take long walks,and warm calm safe sea, ideal for swimming, fishing, skiing .sailing, etc.

You will watch beautiful sunsets. Almost every evening the sky turns red orange and pink. Chixchulub is a fisherman Village with a Central plaza, Church, park. bakery, ice cream parlor, small restaurants with local food, drugstore, fresh market and grocery stores.

At 15 min. Distance you have the Port of Progreso, which is famous for its beautiful long bridge that was built by French people a long time ago. There you can find everything you need: bank, hospital, supermarket, marketplace .movie theater, restaurants and more.

It also has a Malecon. a long sea side walking street, where the restaurants are and people get together. On Wednesdays there is a Mexican market on the Street, where you can buy typical Mexican handmade products. It is also the day when the cruise arrives. Ten minutes walking distance from our home, you will find a Flamingo reserve and in seasons you are able to watch many of the them. There is also canopy trails and small ruins named Xcambo.

We are located at 40 minutes from Merida the capital city of the state of Yucatan where the international airport is. There is every day flights coming from Houston, 3 days a week coming from Miami and several daily flights coming from Mexico City and from other Mexican states.

MERIDA is a very nice, peaceful, colonial, traditional and historical city founded in the year of 1542 by Spanish conquerors. It has a lot of fine restaurants, museums, theaters, shopping malls, a beautiful main plaza with and old Spanish Cathedrals, Zoo, night clubs, haciendas, cenotes and one of the best golf club in Mexico: the Yucatan Country Club, a Nicklaus signature.

Merida is recognized as the number one safest city in Mexico, it is also called The Peace City. Halfway between Merida and Progreso there is a world recognized magical Mayan city: DZIBICHALTUN. The place where the moon equinoctial takes place. The moon stands in the middle of the principal temple of the Mayan city.

The dates that this phenomenon happens are: September 21 and march 21th The place gets full of people coming all over the world to spend the night between the ruins waiting to see how the moon reflects the ruins in a special way as a sign to show the Mayas that it was the right time to start seeding the soil. Dzibilchaltun also has a Cenote where you can swim in fresh and clean water.

YUCATAN IS A BEAUTIFUL MEXICAN STATE VERY WELL KNOWN AND RECOGNIZED FOR ITS EXCELLENT REGIONAL FOOD AND FOR ITS NICE AND FRIENDLY PEOPLE.
