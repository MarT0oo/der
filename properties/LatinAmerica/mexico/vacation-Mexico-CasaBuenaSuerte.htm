Casa Buena Suerte, Soliman Bay, Tulum, Quintana Roo, Riviera Maya, Mexico Vacation Rental

 

This exquisite home is located on the Caribbean coast of Mexico, just north of Tulum on the Riviera Maya.

Located in in Soliman Bay, this lush tropical paradise offers visitors a haven of peace and tranquility under the coco palms with the of a vast coral reef and warm sandy beach at your doorstep.

Hacienda style architecture with generous living spaces and luxurious appointments are the hallmark of this boutique property. Designed for those select vacationers who appreciate the special things that life has to offer!

Casa Buena Suerte is a stunning Hacienda style villa that overlooks the majestic beaches and reef of Soliman Bay and the Caribbean Sea. Designed for fun and relaxation, the hacienda will comfortably accommodate 12 adults and up to 6 children. With numerous areas for entertaining, it's the perfect venue for traveling with friends or family. Enjoy kayaking and snorkeling on your own beach, or a just short ride to one of the Riviera Maya's aqua parks or the Tulum Ruins.

The hacienda has terraces and levels of entertaining areas for guests to enjoy the company of others, or seek out a special area where you can enjoy a moment of seclusion. Step down into the circular outdoor living room where you can entertain guests and family even on a windy day. We designed this room for easy living with an outdoor kitchen and comfortable seating for plenty of guests.

The housekeeping staff is on duty Monday through Saturday to make your stay comfortable; luxury linens, Mayan spa amenities and a delicious continental breakfast to start the day. If you like, we can arrange a dinner service for you and your guests to be served in the villa, or we can make reservations at some of the best spots on the Riviera Maya; just let us know your needs and we will make every effort to assure they are fulfilled.

The details make the difference. Details such as hand carved onyx sinks in the bathrooms and our own line of spa products made by Mayans, with fresh local ingredients. Details like handmade tile and locally mined stone for the floors, mahogany furniture designed with simple yet timeless style. These are the details that make Casa Buena Suerte memorable.

Warm colors and contemporary furnishings provide a relaxing environment to unwind any time of the year. All of the five guest suites have balconies with stunning views, warm tropical breezes and of course luxury bedding. All of our suites have their own bath and all have a luxurious king size bed. A satellite kitchen on the second level allows guests to have a midnight treat without going downstairs. And our library of current books, DVDs, CDs and games is always available.

The swimming pool at Casa Buena Suerte is the largest pool on Bahia Soliman. This exquisite pool is completely blanketed in handmade cobalt blue tile giving it a deep, rich color and silky smooth surface. The pool has a zero edge so the water rises right to the level of the deck giving the appearance of spilling into the Caribbean. There have several rafts so you can soak up the sun while relaxing in the water, or if you prefer, relax in a chaise lounge on our pool deck. Just across from the pool deck is our outdoor living room and kitchen; drinks and snacks are always nearby.

The villa boasts a beautiful and private garden area making it the ideal venue to host events such as yoga retreats, corporate retreats and weddings!
