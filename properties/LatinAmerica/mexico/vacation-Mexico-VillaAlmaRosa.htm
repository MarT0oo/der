Villa Alma Rosa, Akumal, Yucatan-Mayan Riviera, Mexico Vacation Rental

 

The Alma Rosa in Akumal, Riviera Maya magnificent beachfront private fully staffed villa Alma Rosa, embraces the bohemian chic of Tulum. Located in an exclusive area of Jade Beach on the Mayan Riviera, this upscale home is exceptionally spacious, boasting 4,000 square feet of luxurious beachfront living. The four luxury suites with ensuite baths, each have a terrace and amazing ocean views.

Villa Alma Rosa was designed for a French artist and her family. The villa embraces the serenity of the ocean, nature and beach; combining stunning architecture, vibrant luxurious furnishings and beautiful art.

Your personalized concierge will greet you on arrival and be of assistance during your stay. Gourmet Chef and butler services are included for 3 meals a day. Our chefs use the freshest ingredients and have regional, international vegan, vegetarian dishes to delight you!

The spacious open-plan first floor is always bright and airy. Slide open the doors to the terrace, let the Caribbean breeze in and extend the living and dining areas to the outside patio and pool. The large sofas in the living room invite you to sink into their comfortable cushions and relax. The indoor and outdoor dining tables have seating for 12. The gourmet kitchen will please the most discerning of chefs!

The heated saltwater swimming pool is perfect for swimming laps or you may choose to relax poolside in a hammock under the pergola. Chaise lounges flank the swimming pool. The pool area has an outdoor shower and seating area. Alma Rosa has direct beach access, with kayaks and snorkeling equipment for guests use.

The stairway with waterfall feature leads to the upper hallway with futon sofa bed and large flat screen TV. There are three upstairs bedrooms each with king-size beds, a day bed, terrace and ocean views. Alma Rosa has a wonderful roof deck and wet bar. Here you have amazing beach views and to the rear beautiful jungle views. The sunsets and sunrises are spectacular!

For wellness retreats our chef will tailor your menu. Relaxing massages to the sound of lapping waves, sound healing, facials, yoga, Mayan Ceremony can be arranged additionally. Focus your mind, body and soul. Alma Rosa is available for weddings and events of up to 50 guests, celebrate your special moments here in paradise.
