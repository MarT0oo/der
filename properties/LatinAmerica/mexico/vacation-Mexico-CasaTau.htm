Casa Tau, Punta Mita, Mexico Vacation Rental

 

Prepare yourself for a spontaneous gasp of delight upon arrival to this spectacular beachfront villa crowned with an iconic Mexican palapa. With butlers on call, wait staff attending to each and every taste bud craving and a chef cooking up mouth-watering creations, this artfully designed 12-bedroom home is more akin to a multi-starred boutique hotel.

One of the obvious highlights is its enviable location right on a sandy beach. Not only can you pop down for a quick dip in the sea, you can relax in a private sandy recess designed to look like a golf bunker. Feeling thirsty? Ask your waiter for a fresh fruit cocktail or traditional margarita on the rocks as you lounge poolside!

Design wise, the estate has been masterfully crafted with locally sourced materials and custom-made furniture, where attention to detail can be noted throughout, from the polished concrete floors with intricate motifs to the owners’ private collection of artworks.

If you feel like venturing to the outside world, Punta de Mita village is only 5 minutes away. Likewise, you can access facilities within the exclusive Punta Mita development, enjoying full membership privileges as Casa Tau’s guests, which include resident beach clubs, a kids’ club, Lazy River, two Jack Nicklaus golf courses and a world-class spa
