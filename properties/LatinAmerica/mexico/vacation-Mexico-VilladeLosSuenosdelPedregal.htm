Villa de Los Suenos del Pedregal, Los Cabos, Mexico Vacation Rental

 

A memorable vacation awaits you at Villa De Los Suenos. This newly constructed 6 star platinum villa boasts unparalleled amenities, comfort and breath-taking ocean views. Majestically positioned atop a mountain in the gated community of Pedegral. It's contemporary hacienda style design offers 11,250 sq. feet of centrally air conditioned indoor living space with three master suites in the main house, three detached casita suites and a two bedroom guest house. The great room with it's beamed cathedral ceiling, extends to an expansive covered patio overlooking the negative edge pool where you can enjoy sipping a tropical drink while enjoying your own private Jacuzzi tub overlooking the beautiful panoramic ocean views.

The Kitchen is large and well equipped and has an adjoining dining area that accommodates 16. The comfortable spacious living room features an entertainment system with TV, and surround sound system, as well as iPod docking station. You can enjoy a spectacular ocean view sunrise breakfast on the marble decked veranda adjoining the beautiful pool patio. The villa features several entertaining areas, including indoor and outdoor bar, small gym, pool table and "yes" a putting green.

Chef services is available upon request and a "One Time" refrigerator pre stock is available a no additional charge.
