Casa del Quetzal, Conchas Chinas, Puerto Vallarta, Mexico Vacation Rental

 

A spectacular private villa with mesmerizing ocean views across the infinity pool, located hillside in Conchas Chinas a short 5 minute drive to the Bohemian charm & energy of the Romantic Zone & Los Muertos Beach. The villa has 5 almost equal Master suites each with ensuite bath, walk in closet, king size beds & most with Ocean views. The Villa was designed with impressive architectural details of a traditional Hacienda, with all the amenities & comforts of a luxury villa with full staff.

Casa del Quetzal is a world into itself, inside the walls of the property enjoy the owners thoughtfully curated collection of Museum quality folk art, artifacts & Paintings chosen from the best of Mexico’s history & culture, all just a few minutes from the Beach & downtown Puerto Vallarta

Perfect for family groups, couples, corporate retreats & a breathtaking location for a wedding or family celebration.
