Villa Kupuri, Kupuri, Mexican Riviera, Mexico Vacation Rental

 

Relax on the sunny terrace of this secluded Punta Mita Estate in Kupuri, where rich, beige stone and wood interiors helps ensure guests can savor an exceptional sun filled holiday. From the palm-lined, terraced exterior, take in the local scenery and amazing ocean view, before socializing in the, maritime-themed living room. Spas, beaches and golf courses are all very close by.

Throw open the wooden shutters of the spacious bedrooms of Villa Kupuri to take in the wonderful shifting Pacific beyond, with sun and clouds creating a stunning panorama of surf and rugged hillsides. Begin the day with breakfast in the pleasant outdoor dining area, with the suntrap patio allowing full enjoyment of the Mexican sunshine. With a huge, fully-equipped kitchen, guests will be served delicious meals after a bracing session in the villa’s excellently outfitted gym.

Stroll the broad, tiered terrace to find the perfect relaxation spot, swing in the sun hammock as the sea breeze blows through, or gather around the welcoming firepit as clouds roll in. Villa Kupuri ensures that guests can take in uninterrupted views and seclusion, while still being close to the renowned facilities of this established resort. Guests also have access to the resort’s famous beach and ocean clubs, and can tee off on perfectly manicured and highly-rated golf courses, play tennis or have a spa session, or cruise out to the natural beauty of the nearby Marietas Islands.
