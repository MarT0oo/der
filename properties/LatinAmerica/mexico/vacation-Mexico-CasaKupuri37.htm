Casa Kupuri 37, Kupuri Estates, Punta Mita, Mexico Vacation Rental

 

Luxurious and expansive brand new 3 level, 8 bedroom villa with spectacular ocean views. Enjoy the Punta Mita lifestyle with open plan living and dining, media storm room and dinner seating for 16 people.

Each bedroom is spacious with tasteful interiors and feature ensuite bathrooms, some offer sun drenched balconies and magnificent views of the Pacific Ocean and Kupuri beach. Pamper and spoil yourself with the spa and sauna on the lower level or work up an appetite in the gym. Otherwise there is plenty of outdoor furniture to enjoy the garden and pools either day or night.

If you can force yourself to leave, the Kupuri beach club is ideally located close by to experience one of the finest beach clubs in Punta Mita. This Villa has everything you need for a magical vacation and to cater to the most discerning clientele.

One of the most recent additions to the Punta Mita’s residential community array, Kupuri has quickly developed into a success story and become one of the hottest markets in luxury real state in the area. This gorgeous development is one of only two Punta Mita communities overlooking the lovely and more intimate Litibu Bay, instead of the huge Banderas Bay. That’s because of Kupuri’s location on the north side of the Punta Mita peninsula. This distinctive feature, gives the community a sense of distinction, a peculiarity if you will, that sets it apart from the rest of the Punta Mita world.
