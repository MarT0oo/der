Casa Ixchel, Tulum, Quintana Roo, Riviera Maya, Mexico Vacation Rental

 

Casa Ixchel A white pearl, nestled in between the intense hues of the Caribbean Sea and the old-growth jungle of Tulum, a regal estate that impresses even the most lavish of travelers

Casa ixchel is a 6 bedroom property boasting a pool, jacuzzi, gym, massage area, spacious indoor & outdoor lounges, state-of-the-art- kitchen, TV room and perfectly manicured garden, as well as a thatch-roofed dining and lounge area right on the white sand shores of Tulum, Mexico.

Casa Ixchel, designed by arquitect Alberto Schutte, with its clean-cut, minimalistic decoration and the open nature of the living areas, induces a sense of calm, power and contemplation. “Ixchel” in native Mayan refers to the moon goddess of love, medicine, fertility and harvest.

Casa Ixchel also sets new standards of sustainability. The Villa is a completely auto sufficient building, powered by sun, wind and an ultra-silent backup generator. The zero carbon property offers all the modern comforts such as AC, SKY TV, wi-fi internet, as well as all essential beach gear for snorkeling, kayaking and beach toys for kids.

Special attention is given to food and services, with personalized dietary and hospitality requirements and with emphasis to a very attentive, but discrete service to make you feel in your own home. This is a great villa in the Mexican Caribbean for the most discerning travelers!

About The Area

Surrounded by tropical jungle and endless views of the turquoise waters, Casa Ixchel offers privacy and seclusion from the busy beaches of the Tulum hotel zone, although close enough to enjoy Tulum’s wide range of archeological site and eco-tourist attractions.

Casa Ixchel is the last property before the entrance to the Sian Ka’an biosphere, at the southern end of the hotel zone, which is approximately 15-20 minutes driving from Tulum town. Rent a car at the Cancun Airport or arrange for us to have a you picked up. The drive to Casa Ixchel is aboput 2 hours from Cancun.
