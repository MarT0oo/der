La Ceiba, Costa Careyes, Jalisco, Mexico Vacation Rental

 

La Ceiba is set on a private estate, accessed by a high security gate in the ultra-exclusive Costa Careyes. The stunning luxury villa rental is located in a renowned enclave of Costa Careyes, Mexico. Its location between the popular resort towns of Puerto Vallarta to the North (166 km) and Manzanillo to the South (119 km) means that it is easy to get to but sumptuously secluded. This Mediterranean inspired, ultra-private, 2-acre architectural gem sits on a tropical jungle filled cliff, boasting unobstructed views of the Pacific Ocean, it is the perfect hideaway to relax. Majestic sunset after majestic sunset never gets tiring.

Designed by acclaimed architect Diego Villaseñor, the villa boasts soft lines, arches and many stark white walls. The effect is a tranquility and Zen-like energy that flows through the entire property and allows the powerful ocean to take center stage. With seven bedrooms and eight and half bathrooms, this villa provides the perfect setting for a Mexico family vacation or a retreat-type holiday with friends.

Step outside to enjoy the infinity-edge pool, tennis courts and the private yoga studio. The two rooftops boast 360-degree views across the stunning landscape all around this luxury Careyes villa.

High-tech amenities and discrete professional staff will let you relax and enjoy this most special of trips to Mexico.

A one-of-a-kind exclusive and chic Mexico destination for your enjoyment!
