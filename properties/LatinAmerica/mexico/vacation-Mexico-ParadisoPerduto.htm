Paradiso Perduto, Cabo San Lucas, Mexico Vacation Rental

 

Perched 180 feet above sea level, Paradiso Perduto and its breathtaking views of the steel blue and turquoise Sea of Cortez is an experience you won’t soon forget. Enjoy spectacular sunrises and sunsets from the villa’s grand, lagoon-like pool. The outdoor dining area, large enough for16, is a perfect place to start the day with a seaside breakfast. At night, enjoy your gourmet dinner in the large indoor formal dining area. A state of the art gym, glass enclosed and lit paddle tennis court, and lit bocce court are just steps away for you to enjoy.

The villa resides in a private double gated community, on two expansive lots, boasting a lush garden space that can accommodate up to 200 people for weddings, celebrations, reunions and corporate retreats, or just a friendly game of afternoon croquet.

Our latest addition is Turtle Creek; our six tee, eighteen hole chipping course has already been respectfully dubbed, “The toughest half acre in golf.” The par 54, 465 yard layout features a stacked sand trap, meandering water hazard, and 2100 square foot green with six pin locations. Turtle Creek will test the abilities of even the most skilled players while also providing fun for beginners and casual golfers alike.

Life is a collection of experiences. Let Paradiso Perduto be one of your most memorable.

Paradiso Perduto's luxurious, amenity-rich accommodations make for an unforgettable experience. The spacious 11,825 square foot floor plan boasts 8 elegant and well appointed bedrooms, including two junior masters, with eight full and three half bathrooms.

The villa sits on two private lots 185 feet above sea level with breathtaking views of the Sea of Cortez and the stunning topography of the San Jose Del Cabo area. The Greg Norman Signature Golf Course also dots the views from the many seating areas that make Paradiso Perduto a continual exploration of spectacular sights. The view only gets better as you turn your attention back to the sea to watch majestic whales and dolphins dance in the distance.

Our butler and gourmet chef are there to enhance your stay. Full concierge, spa and child care services are available on demand and just a few of the reasons why a Paradiso Perduto vacation is an experience unlike any other.
