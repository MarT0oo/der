Las Palmas 19, Las Palmas, Punt Mita, Mexico Vacation Rental

 

Located in the gated Las Palmas development, behind the gates of Punta Mita Resort. This villa is surrounded by Jack Nicklaus signature course, between the Four Seasons, and St Regis Hotel. 5 minute walk to the private Residents Beach Club on a pristine white sand beach, 5 minute drive to Kupuri Beach Club. The house includes Premier Sports Membership that gives you access to Kupuri, Pacifico and Porta Fortuna Beach Clubs.

Punta Mita, is Mexico’s most prestigious gated community and resort destinations, as well as a variety of experiences and memories. It is the sensual breezes of the Pacific Ocean and a clear evening sky peppered with stars. It is the gracious and attentive staff of Punta Mita Beach Clubs. It is Oceanside Jack Nicklaus Signature Golf Courses and fiery sunsets. It is families, friends and colleagues gathering to share adventures.

Punta Mita’s coveted address is enhanced by the familiar comforts that sophisticated residents expect. These include ease of travel, excellent medical care, as well as reliable safety and security. Punta Mita, situated in the Riviera Nayarit, is easily accessible with direct commercial flights from major airports throughout the U.S., Canada, Mexico and Latin America. Adjacent to the Puerto Vallarta International Airport is the Aerotron FBO, which serves private aircrafts. After arriving, the 28-mile ride to Punta Mita is less than 50 minutes by car.

Enjoy your vacation on your own terms at one of the coolest locations in Punta Mita!
