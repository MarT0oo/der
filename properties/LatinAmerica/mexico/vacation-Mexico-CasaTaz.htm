Casa Taz, Cabo San Lucas, Mexico Vacation Rental

 

Casa Taz is a custom oceanfront, Mediterranean-styled home tucked away at the foot of the Baja Peninsula between Cabo San Lucas and San José del Cabo. You can enjoy the lovely view of the Sea of Cortez from every single room in the villa. In fact, the house is only a hundreds yards away from a secluded beach that will delight your senses.

A regal staircase leads to the pristine, curved infinity swimming pool that offers breathtaking ocean vistas. Comfortable sun loungers are featured on two levels. One of the best areas in Casa Taz to just chill out is at the outdoor games area with ping pong and air hockey tables nestled among the lovely hammocks. You will also find a billiards and foosball table. Tennis equipment is provided, and the courts are five minutes away. When you return, you might opt for a soothing soak in the outdoor Jetted tub. From an alfresco shower by the beach, to wonderful outdoor dining and lounging areas, this villa optimizes your enjoyment of tropical life. Indoors, you will also find a home theatre, fitness equipment, baby gear and Wi-Fi access.

The cozy living area makes a great place for cards or a board game with nothing but the beautiful sea to gaze upon through the sliding glass doors. The formal dining area with seating for eight also overlooks the ocean. The large, fully equipped kitchen comes with a lovely, semi-circular, yellow-tiled center island bar and beige stools.

Five magnificent bedrooms accommodate up to ten guests at Casa Taz. All of the bedrooms include en-suite bathrooms, air conditioning, ceiling fans, TVs and access to a private verandah. Fine beddings and accoutrements provide restful evenings with nothing but the ocean waves as your soundtrack.

When you venture into Cabo San Lucas, keep a look out for the lovely shops and restaurants, as well as the famous El Arco rock formation. El Medano beach is great for swimming and water sports. Sister city, San José del Cabo offers a chance to admire old colonial architecture. Golfers will find many top options in Los Cabos Corridor. Come take a break in the therapeutic Mexican sun at Casa Taz!
