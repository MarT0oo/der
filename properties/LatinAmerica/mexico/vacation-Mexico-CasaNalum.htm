Casa Nalum, Sian Kaan, Riviera Maya, Mexico Vacation Rental

 

Casa Nalum sits on one of the nicest beaches worldwide, gazing across the sea with gracious open spaces and generous grounds for reading, relaxing or sunbathing, this Villa offers complete privacy with high-end service only 10 minutes from Tulum's restaurants and night-life.

Casa Nalum is the inspiration of all Nalum Private Retreats’ Villas. The Villa has become known through the years for its beauty, incredible location and its warm and welcoming atmosphere.

Nalum, meaning Mother Earth in Maya, is a place to reconnect, relax, gather and simply enjoy the beauty of life. Casa Nalum was the first sustainable building within the biosphere, making way for a new concept of authentic luxury travel experience in natural surroundings.

The property sits on 2 acres of land and it boasts over 3,500 sqft of living space + 3,000 sqft of terrace.

Casa Nalum is surrounded by the beauty of the Caribbean Sea and the cool waters of Chunyaxche Lagoon, tucked away in the heart of the Sian Ka’an biosphere – a UNESCO World Heritage site, approximately 5.3 km of unpaved road or about 15- 20 minutes drive from Tulum beach town following the entrance to the Sian Ka’an biosphere.

Hidden between tropical jungle and endless views of the turquoise blue waters, Casa Nalum offers privacy and seclusion away from the busy beaches of Tulum, although close enough to enjoy Tulum’s wide range of archeological sites, cenotes and ecotourist attractions.

The Villas is perfectly nestled between Tulum beach and the fishermen town of Punta Allen. (Punta Allen is about 45 minutes drive on unpaved road).
