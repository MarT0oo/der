Villa Marmol, Playa del Carmen, Yucatan-Mayan Riviera, Mexico Vacation Rental

 

This amazing new villa is the very definition of residential opulence. – Ideally situated in the exclusive private Playacar Phase 2 region of Playa del Carmen, Villa Marmol is a brand new, ultra spectacular luxury villa designed to satisfy even the most discerning tastes. "Magnificence in Marble" barely begins to describe the architecture of this stunning new home with its 5 opulently-appointed bedrooms, each containing its own bathroom.

In the master bedroom, there is an adjoining theater room with multiple speakers, a huge wall-screen, and high-def viewing for your personal movie nights. In the master bathroom, there is a shower that looks straight out of a Hollywood movie set with its rich, dark tones of imported African marble-- complete with 5 water-jets — these are the elevated levels of luxury and exclusivity that embody the standard of Villa Marmol.

Downstairs you your own private spa with an indoor jacuzzi, spa, and sauna-room.

The living area is a high-elevation veritable oceanic expanse of luxury, with vistas over the pool and garden area and enough space to entertain a post-Oscars party.

Underground car parking and rooftop, ocean-view lounging areas complete this thoroughly modern residence that will truly become your very own piece of paradise during a retreat of relaxation right in the heart of the Riviera Maya
