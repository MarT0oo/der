Villa Las Estrellas, Los Cabos, Mexico Vacation Rental

 

Villa Las Estrellas is a 3,750 square foot luxury penthouse condominium located in the prestigious Auberge Private Residences in Punta Ballena, next to the Esperanza Resort and Spa. This stunning vacation rental offers high end amenities and amazing views of the Sea of Cortez. Enjoy breathtaking panoramas from virtually every angle of this private holiday home in Cabo San Lucas!

The terrace features a sparkling oceanfront view, shaded lounge spaces, a barbeque and an alfresco dining set for four. Interior perks include an ice maker, air conditioning, ceiling fans and Wi-Fi. This penthouse unit also includes satellite television and a wonderful sound system. Guests of Villa Las Estrellas will also have access to the exclusive Punta Ballena Beach Club, which features a swimming pool, hot tub, restaurant, bar and fitness center. Tennis courts are also located nearby. Your reservation includes daily housekeeping.

High ceilings and overhead fans keep the feelings airy and breezy inside. The great room features a living area with comfortable furniture, a formal dining area for six and a fully equipped kitchen with stainless steel appliances and a breakfast bar for two. The design and layout of this delectable penthouse unit creates a seamless indoor/outdoor holiday experience!

Three exquisite bedrooms accommodate up to eight guests at this non-smoking villa. All of the bedrooms provide en-suite bathrooms, air conditioning, ceiling fans and access to a private balcony or terrace. Guests will have their choice of a king sized bed in either of two suites or one of two double beds in the third suite. The master suite includes a dual vanity, jetted tub and dressing area. We recommend the penthouse for a family getaway or an exciting honeymoon.

Known for its nightlife and the fabulous El Arco rock formation, Cabo San Lucas is a dream destination. An outing during your stay could include San Jose Del Cabo, a picturesque colonial town that also offers some of the area’s best surfing beaches. A little further north, you will find the East Cape – a top spot for fishing and exploring the coastal lagoons for amazing birds and other wildlife. You will also be just minutes from several championship level golf courses along the Los Cabos Corridor
