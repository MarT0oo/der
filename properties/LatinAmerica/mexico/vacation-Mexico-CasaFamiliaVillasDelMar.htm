Casa Familia Villas Del Mar, Quintana Roo, Riviera Maya, Mexico Vacation Rental

 

Blissful vacationing is guaranteed at “Casa Familia”, Villas Del Mar 1 Condo B304!….A gorgeous beach front Penthouse (with elevator), 3 large King suites with ensuite bathrooms and much more!

Casa Familia is beautifully decorated by the owner and you will feel right at home! The open plan living area leads to the beachside terrace with Jacuzzi, chaise lounges and palapa with al fresco dining and BBQ. The kitchen is well equipped with all modern conveniences, On the main level you have a half bath with washer dryer and King Bedroom with ensuite bath.With wireless internet and vonage telephone for guest use.

The upper floor leads to two further King suites and a wonderful terrace with a large palapa and sofa area with views over the jungle …perfect for relaxing!!

Villas Del Mar 1 has a huge multi level swimming pool, tennis court and beach side with palapas and chaises. A very short walk takes you to the San Carlos marina with the many restaurants, boutiques, delicatessens, bars and more that Puerto Aventuras offers.

Casa Familia will truly delight you!!
