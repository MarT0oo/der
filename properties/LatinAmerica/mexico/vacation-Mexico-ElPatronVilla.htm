El Patron Villa, Cancun, Quintana Roo, Riviera Maya, Mexico Vacation Rental

 

Chic and contemporary; this luxurious villa looks out over the lagoon to Cancun's energetic hotel strip. El Patron Villa offers the perfect luxury getaway for the discerning traveller looking to take advantage of everything the region has to offer. Gather with friends and family on the rooftop terrace where you can admire Cancun’s busy skyline or enjoy cocktails, prepared by your personal butler, on the lagoon jetty.

El Patron Villa is designed for up to 12 guests, spanning across 1,000 square metres that include 2 swimming pools, sauna, home cinema and a rooftop terrace with a bar. Separate bedrooms and living rooms allow for intimate gatherings, while ensuring personal space and tranquillity for all. Contemplate taking your vacation to the next level with a luxury yacht rental, anchored in your own back garden (inquire for more details).

Lastly, take advantage of the villa’s great location through private Expeditions. With Chichén Itzá, Ek’ Balam and Holbox just a short drive away we can show you another side to the ancient civilization that others won’t see.
