Vatulé, La Punta Estates, Punta Mita, Mexico Vacation Rental

 

Vatulé Punta Mita Mexico is a 6 Bedrooms plus Bunk bedroom for Kids, 7 Bathrooms, two powder rooms, Sleeps 22 Adults and 12 Kids, Infinity Swimming Pool, Outdoor Jacuzzi, Oceanfront, Surf Break La Bahía, Golf Court front. Vatulé Punta Mita Mexico is located inside the exclusive Punta Mita Development La Punta el Faro, Riviera Nayarit Mexico.

Escape to sunset views, sea breezes and chic modern style at Vatulé. Named for the owner’s three children—Valen, Tulah and Leighton—this oceanfront vacation rental makes a gorgeous family retreat or a resort-style setting for a getaway with friends. An enviable location in the exclusive La Punta Estates neighborhood puts guests close to golf, sandy beaches and five-star amenities at Punta Mita resorts.

Your holiday at Vatulé includes cook service at breakfast, chef service at dinner, and access to the St. Regis Punta Mita resort. But the villa is well appointed enough to feel like your own private resort, with a beautiful heated infinity pool, hot tub, a shaded living and dining area next to the pool, an outdoor kitchen with a barbecue, and even a large playing field for soccer or bocce. A sound system and wet bar make it easy to entertain, and a home theater is the perfect place to relax after a day spent playing in the water.

The villa’s thatched roof is a nod to traditional Mexican architecture, but inside, it’s all contemporary luxury. A vaulted ceiling and oversized fashion photograph overlook an open-plan great room with crisp white sofas atop a natural-fiber rug and a formal dining area that can seat twenty. Mirrored doors slide open to reveal the bistro-inspired, fully equipped kitchen, which has lively tile floors and a breathtaking marble backsplash.

The main house at Vatulé has six bedrooms with en-suite bathrooms, five of which are honeymoon-worthy master suites with king beds and ocean views and one of which is a children’s room with six bunk beds, its own media room and a terrace.

Take the included adults’ and children’s bikes or the provided golf cart to the three beach clubs, two golf courses, fitness center and tennis club at nearby resorts. For low-key shopping and dining, make the 10-minute golf-cart trip into the town of Punta Mita.

Vatulé Punta Mita Mexico boasts a 72 foot infinity pool with plenty of wide shallow stairs for young children. There is also a jacuzzi that sits 14 people. It has a 50 yard playing field (for soccer, badmitton, cornhole, bocci, etc.) overlooking the 17th fairway (Nicolas’ favorite hole).

Vatulé Punta Mita Mexico has sunset and island views overlooking the Pacific Ocean with the best surf view in all of the Punta Mita development. Vatulé Punta Mita Mexico has 2 separate living/dining rooms both with ocean views. It also has a shaded outside living/dining area with a wet bar and BBQ next to the pool. In addition, it has 2 roof top terraces. The home was designed for outdoor entertaining and the 27 outside speakers provide ambient sound through control four operation. Vatulé Punta Mita Mexico has plenty of shade options with the palapa day bed and four 8×8 foot adjustable umbrellas. If you desire to work while on vacation, there is immaculate Wi-Fi throughout the property and office with ocean view.
