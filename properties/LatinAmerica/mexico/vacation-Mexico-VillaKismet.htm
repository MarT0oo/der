Villa Kismet, Puerto Vallarta, Mexico Vacation Rental

 

This stunning architectural estate offers generous attention to detail that is unmistakable. Beautiful contemporary design surrounded by the lush jungle setting found at the Sierra del Mar at Los Arcos prestigious gated community is breathtaking. Beachfront living combining Mother Nature and an incredible lifestyle are generously offered in this special home. Also included are a common area Beach Club with Gym and Pool.

Casa Kismet features a dramatic interior garden courtyard, simple clean lines in design, floor to ceiling glass, marble flooring, quality handcrafted cabinetry, and very special design features that have been created especially for this estate.

Spacious entertainment areas and oceanfront terraces, no edge infinity pool, covered Palapa dining area, access to Zen style oceanfront gardens and enforced oceanfront areas, elevator to all floors, gym or spa room, and a complete separate one bedroom ocean view guest apartment for visitors. The master suite includes terrace, office suite, and gorgeous master bath with use of glass and wood finishes, all overlooking the sea. This is a jewel on the South Shore Riviera and a must see for anyone looking for quality construction, solid infrastructure, and detail in design all overlooking Los Arcos in the bay.
