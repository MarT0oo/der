Mantea Casa Cabo, Cabo San Lucas, Mexico Vacation Rental

 

There truly is no better place to wow guests than at Mantea Casa Cabo, with amenities of 5-star resort caliber, featuring a fully staffed villa with Gourmet Chef and Butler included in every stay. Other amenities include the heated infinity pool – with its cascading 12-ft high waterfalls - swim up bar, poolside outdoor kitchen and barbecue, outdoor TV and breathtaking ocean views. At Mantea Casa Cabo, our team works exclusively for the villa, offering a more personalized luxury boutique hotel level of service. The residence comes equipped with unmatched amenities including luxury linens as well as Bvlgary bathroom amenities. Our personalized service is to the highest of standards, starting with welcome drinks upon arrival and evening turn down service with nightly surprises. Our general manager, who is dedicated solely to Mantea Casa Cabo, coordinates any and all experiences including; reservations and experiences in the destination, yacht excursions, private transportation and anything else you wish.

The 8 bedrooms in the villa offer luxurious accommodation for up to 16 guests and have been individually decorated in a sophisticated modern design to create the ultimate sense of wellness and relaxation. Mantea Casa Cabo’s state-of-the-art property features also include a sauna with ocean views, full commercial quality gym as well as an in-house spa completed with a waterfall and a zen garden. The ocean breeze and the sound of the waves can be enjoyed while relaxing on the villa’s spacious terraces, where you can whale watch using our telescope or binoculars. Other modern amenities include Smart House Control 4 technology, controlled access with security cameras, and home theater with 72” flat screen TV.

Located in Cabo’s most exclusive community of Pedregal, Mantea Casa Cabo becomes a resort of your own, redefining luxury hospitality in a unique mix of privacy with pristine service and amenities.

The general manager will assist to coordinate your entire travel experience upon booking. From 24-hour butler’s service or a private chef always willing to fulfil your culinary desires, airport transportation or private flights, we are here to assist in any way possible. Our dedicated cleaning and maintenance staff will keep all areas flawless for your delight during your stay. Your complete comfort is our everyday goal. This effortless lifestyle is the essence of our everyday experience.
