About Argentina

Argentina Vacation Rentals from the Skiing Destinations on the mountains to the desert areas of Patagonia. Argentina provides the best of the best in quality and family vacations, from adventure  in the rapids to lovely self-catered lettings in the city of Buenos Aires. All that you need within a city that provides you with the ease and comfort you are used to within your own country at a reasonable price for luxury.

The outdoors and rapids are a huge draw here, in a country where nature and the equestrian farmer take first fiddle. You can stay in one of our Lodge Style Villas and enjoy the opportunity to commune within nature, enjoying growing your own foods and roughing it for a while, with your own private guide. Or you can enjoy you’re the beaches of Mar del Plata.

Some other areas to discover in Argentina are Cordoba and Mendoza where you have a variety of great restaurants and shops, enough to satisfy any traveler.
