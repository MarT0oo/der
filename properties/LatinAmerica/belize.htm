Villa Travel Belize

Belize is a place to go to get away from it all, with 40% of the region protected forestry, it makes this an eco friendly travel destination.  If you are searching for that perfect blend of Beaches, Eco Friendly activities, and a Caribbean feeling than this it the place to go.  Nowhere else will you find tranquility and “getting away from it all” as much as you will in this Southern Jewel. 

When visiting try: Visiting the Mayan Ruins, if you have never tried you must go, try tour operator http://belizeinthesun.com/contact.html.  Visiting Cayo where there are great beaches is a must.  Then Northern Belize for its great trekking paths, Stann Creek for a little history, and Toledo for some sailing. 

Then you can also try Belmopan , Belize City, Corozal, Crooked Tree, Dangriga, Orange Walk, Placencia, Punta Gorda and so much more.  People come for the Snorkeling, beaches, and wonderful times with family and friends.
