Casa Buzios
5 Bedrooms | 6.5 Baths (5 Ensuite) | Pool | Patio                                  
Ferradura Beach, Buzios, Brazil

True luxury in the St. Tropez of Brazil. This brand new luxury villa is located in a quiet location near Ferradura Beach, Buzios, 100 miles east of Rio de Janeiro. Designed by well-renowned architect Hélio Pellegrino, the villa is located inside a small, exclusive condominium with only 8 individually designed villas. Private pool and sauna. Oversized (1100 sq.ft.) living room. The entire villa has sophisticated interior with imported furniture/decor and elaborate flooring. 55 inch Led TV, B&W Zeppelin iPod Speakers, DVD, Cable, Grill, Bar and Wireless broadband. Fully equipped kitchen with washing machine and dish washer. Beautiful Ocean view from all rooms (except kitchen) as well as from pool area and sauna. Everything in this villa is truly of highest quality. The condominium has total security. Buzios has all the beach activities and night life you can dream of and is one of the sunniest places in the Rio de Janeiro state. This resort town is very safe with an abundance of activities. The villa is located in a tranquil area. Close to Feradura Beach and the breathtaking views of Ponta da lagoinha. Adjacent to hotel Pedra da laguna, with restaurant.

Four beautifully decorated bedrooms with ocean view. These are all equipped with AC (spring Carrier), fans and comfortable beds. One additional bedroom is more simple and has no view but with comfortable bed and fan. All ACs are brand new Spring Carrier and the beds are also brand new and very comfortable. Cleaning 3 times a week is included and there is no additional charge for final cleaning. Electricity not included.
