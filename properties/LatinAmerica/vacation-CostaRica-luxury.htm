Costa Rican Luxury
2 Bedrooms | 2 Ensuite Baths | Private Beach                                   
Manuel Antonio
  From $3,067 per Week

Located in the exclusive Buena Vista Villas Resort at Thlemar in Manuel Antonio, Costa Rican Luxury is a luxurious 2-bedroom retreat nestled into the jungle hillside. It offers the Tulemar Resort amenities in a privately owned house, 180 degree panoramic views of the Pacific Ocean and lush jungle from its 450sq.ft. wrap around veranda. Costa Rican Luxury 405 is just a short walk away from the finest and most private beach in the entire area.
 
The house is luxuriously furnished and has high end appliances in the kitchen, and a whirlpool Jacuzzi for you to enjoy. Costa Rican Luxury includes 2 bedrooms, each with 2 queen beds, luxurious mattresses, cable TV, DVD player, and air conditioning.  Each bedroom has an en-suite bathroom with stone counters and open-air raindrop showers. Use of the resort pool is included as well as the Tulemar private beach.
