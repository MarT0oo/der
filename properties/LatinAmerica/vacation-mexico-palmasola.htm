Palmasola, Punta Mita, Mexico Vacation Rental


Punta Mita, a 1,500-acre gated resort, boasting 9 miles of beachfront, offers a combination of luxurious amenities, convenience and security unrivaled by other destinations. We are a short 45 minute drive from the Puerto Vallarta International Airport, and are fortunate to have one of the most pleasant climates in the world, sharing the same latitude as the Hawaiian Islands.

Palmasola is located on the premier lot of the 3 Signature Estates located within the gates of Punta Mita Resort. Sharing a restricted access beach with the Four Seasons and right next to the world¹s only natural ocean island hole  "The Tail of the Whale".  The legendary Jack Nicklaus has designed two spectacular Jack Nicklaus Signature courses at Punta Mita.

An attribute completely unique to Palmasola is that our guests receive full privileges at the award-winning Four Seasons Resort Punta Mita. They are treated just as guests of the hotel with full access to pools, spa, dining, children's club and various activities.

Palmasola has built a reputation of incredible design and excellent service that has resulted in numerous accolades from publications such as: Town & Country Travel, The Travel Channel, Robb Report and others.

Our professionally trained General Manager, Abigail Rivera manages a staff of 15 including: housekeepers, waiters, drivers, concierge and three incredibly talented chef¹s (one Mexican trained, one Columbian trained and one European trained). More than a Villa, Palmasola is on par with having your own private luxury hotel.

Master Residence

El Sol is our 2,400 square foot master bedroom suite located on the upper floor of the Master Residence. El Sol has its own fully equipped study, exterior terrace with breakfast table, day bed and Jacuzzi, and a very private roof terrace with exceptional views. El Mar and La Tierra bedroom suites are located on the ground floor and share an Estarcito (living room, breakfast table and small kitchenette) between them. Both suites feature a pair of full size beds and La Tierra is wheelchair-friendly and has direct access to the shallow portion of the swimming pool.

Guest Residence

The upper level features 2 bedrooms, El Caracol and Las Mariposas, separated by an Estarcito (living room, breakfast table and small kitchenette). The lower level is home to both Los Delfines and Las Tortugas bedrooms; also sharing an Estarcito. All of the bedrooms in the guesthouse include a king-sized bed and all but El Caracol can accommodate additional beds for children. El Caracol and Las Mariposas both have their own private terraces including an outdoor shower. The guesthouse also features a private rooftop terrace.

Garden Suites

The garden suites are our most spacious rooms, very popular for families with children and are located in the rear of the property. El Viento and El Cielo are outfitted with mini fridges, coffee set-ups and large sitting areas that are convertible into extra sleeping accommodations. The suites share a raised patio, which has its own seating area and table.

Service

Palmasola was created to be the ultimate beachfront retreat for friends, family and colleagues to relax while enjoying nature, fabulous activities, fine dining and each other’s company. It is a fully staffed, single-client five star luxury retreat managed by Abigail Rivera, our professionally trained Hotel General Manager, where even the person who is most prone to be the planner in your group will have the freedom to take a well deserved vacation. Our hope is that you will surrender to Palmasola and let its staff take care of you, allowing you a brief escape from the cares of the world.

Dining

An amazing feature at Palmasola is the way different spaces are magically transformed into ever changing dining venues. The Dining Palapa provides a more formal setting with elegant mirrors to ensuring everyone has a view. (2–14 guests). Las Estrellas, rooftop terrace is an exquisite al fresco setting under the stars. (2–20 guests). The Palapita, presents intimate covered space right on the beach. (2-8 guests). The Beach provides many casual yet dramatic venues for dining: whether assembling a very large table on the open sand for a starlit dinner, creating a more intimate space under the mangroves, complete with hanging paper lamps, or setting up a special candlelit path to lead you to a romantic dinner under the signature Palmasola. (2–40 guests). The Main Palapa is a favorite lunch spot or a more formal setting for a cocktail reception. (2-40 guests). The Lawn provides an elegant al fresco option in a lush tropical landscaping setting. (2-40 guests). The Pool Deck is a great place to relax on the luxuriously large chaise lounges and enjoy casual poolside snacking. (2-40 guests). The Parilla features tableside grilling under a mature rubber tree. This area provides a unique, jungle-like atmosphere with a relaxed change of pace. (2-20 guests). The Master Residence Terrace provides an incredible view for a quiet breakfast or intimate dinner. (2-4 guests).  And so much more…
