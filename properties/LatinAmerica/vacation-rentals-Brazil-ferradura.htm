5 Bedrooms | 5 Baths Ensuite | Pool | Oceanviews

We are happy to present, Casa Ferradura Beach, perhaps the finest home in Buzios, Brazil. Located on a cliff overlooking Ferradura Beach, this house was just featured on MTV's "Real World - Road Rules" this past season. With a 270 degree view which includes both ocean and beach vistas and nightly sunsets over Ferradura Beach. The house is a short 10 minute walk to Ferradura Beach and only a 10 minute ride to Buzios.

 

The Master Suite is on the Second Floor and has it’s own bath, the second and third bedrooms also have their own bathrooms. The master is amazing with it’s wide open views. From the Master Bath enjoy the full beauty of Buzios. The home is situated so perfectly on the hilltop, making you feel as if you’re floating on air. Each room gives you the sense that you are truly in a special house. As you dine, you will enjoy the spectacular ocean views, which will make the idea of eating in an experience indeed.

 

The room has been modernly decorated, from the kitchen and it’s Very Euro Style design and high end Stainless Appliances, to the Living room, where you can sit and relax the day away in your own private Media Room. It is safe to say this is one of the most unique and stunning homes in Buzios. This unique experience will be perfect for Honeymoons, Birthday Escapes, Celebrity Escapes, Private Family travel, and just simply the makings of the perfect vacation.

 

You will be guaranteed privacy as the house is located on a point of land. The infinity edge pool looks directly toward the Atlantic Ocean. The kitchen and bar includes top of the line Viking equipment. There is a Jacuzzi in the master bath which overlooks the ocean. The house includes a caretaker and a cook, and most special requests can be taken care of. You can also have security come to the house daily for your stay at $75 - $150/day. There is an additional charge for pool heating at $60-75/day. Ask for anything additional you may need, we are here to help with any requests.

 

Bookings for this outstanding home have been taken up to one year in advance for high priority dates so if you have interest please send an email with your request as early as possible.
