Villa Vallarta, Punta Mita, Mexico


Villa Vallarta enjoys a privileged location within the exclusive Punta Mita residential area of the Four Seasons Resort, Punta Mita. Set hillside, among lush gardens, the villa provides vivid ocean views out to the Marietas Islands and the Pacific Ocean. Guests enjoy access to resort services and amenities, including the Four Season's beach just a five minutes golf-cart ride away.

Carved antique Mexican doors welcome guests to the the private entry courtyard. Open plan living areas are spacious and feature cathedral ceilings, hardwood beams, marble flooring, and Four Seasons quality and detailing with a contemporary Mexican coastal flair. The great room and dining areas open to outdoor living and dining areas, and to the private infinity edged pool and jacuzzi. The kitchen is fully equipped and features an eat-at bar. There are also laundry facilities, a guest powder room, and a garage for one car plus the complimentary golf cart. Villa Vallarta is fully air conditioned and provides satellite TV in the great room and each bedroom, CD and DVD players, as well as high speed Wi-Fi access.

This two level villa hosts four bedroom suites. All bedroom suites boast en-suite bathrooms. Two host king sized beds, one a queen sized bed, and the fourth, two double beds. Three suites open to terraces or balconies. The expansive master bedroom opens to a private garden terrace fronting the jacuzzi, and has a separate living area. The master bathroom features both a large soaking tub and a walk-in shower, as well as an outdoor shower.

Guests at Villa Vallarta will enjoy at-home comforts in luxurious privacy, just minutes away from the amenities and services of the Four Seasons Punta Mita. Punta Mita enjoys well-earned fame for world-class sport-fishing, as well as for a host of watersport activities such as snorkeling, whale watching, and, of course, swimming and sunning.
