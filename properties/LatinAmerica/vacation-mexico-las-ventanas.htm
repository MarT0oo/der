Las Ventanas al Mar, Puerto Vallarta Mexico Vacation Rental


Set in a lush, jungle mountainside eco-refuge surrounded by fabulous trees and wildlife, this newly constructed villa offer breathtaking views of Puerto Vallarta and the Bay of Banderas from one of the highest points in Puerto Vallarta.
Professionally designed and decorated, Ventanas al Mar or “Windows to the Sea” has incredible views from each and every corner. All the bedrooms offer the ultimate in privacy with large spacious terraces and seating areas.  Outdoor living is the key theme to this property.

Rock fountains, exquisite Indonesian furnishings, handmade linens, teak wood trim and accessories and granite countertops are just a few of the amenities that give these spectacular villas dramatic modern touches while maintaining soothing organic design.

The villa is located in the prestigious upper Conchas Chinas residential neighborhood only 5 minutes from downtown Puerto Vallarta and public beaches.

Four inviting suites, large terraces, beautiful "infinity" swimming pool, extra large Jacuzzi, pool lounge pergola, gourmet kitchen, elegant dining room, relaxing living room and well stocked wine cellar make this villa a paradise location to live or to retire, or simply, a matchless place to spend an unforgettable holiday.

Las Ventanas al Mar is a tranquil oasis located hillside just a few minutes’ drive from the beach & Bohemian charm of the Romantic zone & downtown Puerto Vallarta. This spectacular private villa is a perfect combination of simple contemporary design balanced with organic details & breathtaking ocean views. The 4 bedroom villa includes a full staff including cook to pamper you, plus all the amenities & comforts for the perfect get away.

The spacious villa is designed for outdoor living with a spectacular outdoor living area on the main level, breathtaking rooftop lounging area. Perfect for groups of friends or couples, families & corporate retreats
