Casa Papelillos, Puerto Vallarta, Mexico


Located 5.5 miles south of Puerto Vallarta, in the lush wooded area, where the mountain joins the sea, a natural rainforest sanctuary bordered by the calm blue waters of the Pacific Ocean.

Casa Papelillos boasts an unbeatable location in Banderas Bay, in the exclusive subdivision El Paredón, designed by the renowded architect Ricardo Elias in total privacy, serenity and security. The beauty of nature at its finest. This magnificent residence was built on an area of 13,600 square feet, with an architectural design that incorporates the existing thousand- year old trees in their areas.

Main features of Casa Papelillos:
5 suites with full bathrooms
Large modern living room with marble floors and a spectacular Bay view
Formal dining room for 12
Terrace with sitting area and table for 10
Estate of the Art kitchen with an island that serves as a breakfast bar for six, fully equipped with stove, dishwasher, electric oven and microwave, refrigerator, freezer, telephone for internal and external calls and a large pantry.
