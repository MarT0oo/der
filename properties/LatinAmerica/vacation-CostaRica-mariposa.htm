Mariposa, Guanacaste, Black Beauty Village, Costa Rica


A fabulous contemporary villa nestled in a tropical paradise. Mariposa is a gorgeous modern eco-estate situated on a lush hillside above Costa Rica’s Northern Pacific coast with spectacular ocean views. This villa’s luxury eco-design and spacious, open floor plan offer sublime views of the ocean and the beaches of Playa Ostional and Nosara Bay. You and your guests can enjoy a dip in the infinity pool or relax in the master bedroom’s Jacuzzi tub. A fully stocked kitchen provides everything you could need to prepare a sumptuous feast fit for a king. And the living and entertainment areas feature state-of-the-art multi-media systems. With a multitude of opportunities to explore the splendors of the tropics, this villa is the perfect place to relax and unwind from a hectic lifestyle.
