Villa Casa Genoveva, Ubatuba, Sao Paulo, Brazil Vacation Rental

 

Casa Genoveva overlooks the Atlantic Ocean and three different popular beaches in the resort city of Ubatuba. It’s centrally located offering great access to the neighboring resort towns of Parati (1 hour), Angra dos Reis (2 hours) and Ilha Bela (1 hour). The house itself is an open-concept, multilevel Mediterranean style villa designed for indoor and outdoor living. Each level of the villa is surrounded by a terrace from which you can see a variety of spectacular views in three different directions. The terraces and balconies offer areas for group activities and private corners for you to enjoy in quiet solitude. The house has 10 dormitories, 6 suites and numerous luxury bathrooms bedecked with the finest imported Italian marble.

The sunny kitchen features wall-to-wall cupboards and a large, walk-in pantry.

It also features Cable TV SKY, in both TV’s as well as through the home-theater. It includes a games room which contains brand new pool and ping pong tables. The 13-meters long heated pool is surrounded by heat dissipating “Pedra de Minas” stone and lush gardens.

You will have full access to the sauna, gym and a separate jacuzzi with breath-taking sea views in total privacy. The unique combination will ensure that a visit to this Villa leaves a memorable impression.

Casa Genoveva will also accommodate the most exciting gatherings, weddings and celebrations.

This house can easily accommodate 32 people. It is also possible to rent single suits to conveniently accommodate smaller parties.
It contains four fully air-conditioned suits and another two suits which are cooled by overhead fans. The four air-conditioned suits, as well as one of the fan-cooled suits can each comfortably sleep four people. Also our family suit consists of two separate rooms connected by a bathroom.

For newly-weds, the honey-moon suit situated at the highest place of the house will provide an unforgettable, spectacular view of the surrounding area.

Perfectly situated in between Rio de Janeiro and Sao Paolo, Ubatuba is one of the last unspoilt places in Brazil. The most beautiful beaches, spectacular nature and a friendly Community offers everything to the holidaymaker.

With more than 100 beaches on offer in Ubatuba you can discover something new every day and the variety is sure to cater for every taste; be it desire for solitude, crowds of people, for surfing, the rolling waves or for just for enjoying the warm South American sun.
A must-see are the many waterfalls with their crystal clear water. Professional guides can lead you to the most beautiful places in the Atlantic Rain forest and you will have the chance to discover fascinating tropical vegetation and colourful birds in their natural environment, preserved in a unique nature reserve. But you need not travel that far to see the wonders Brazil has to offer. You can spot Oncas and Turtles directly from the beautiful house in which you reside!

Everything is at your doorstep..
