Villa Santa Teresa, Villa Santa Teresa, Rio de Janeiro Vacation Rental

 

Now available for the first time for private rental, this 7 bedroom villa is located on the grounds of the private estate of one of Rio’s oldest and most notable families hidden in the heart of Santa Teresa.

A complete haven in the midst of the city, silent save for the sound of birds and the breeze in the almond trees, the 1970’s modernist white mansion sits enveloped in Rio’s tropical Mata-Atlantica rainforest, while below tumble priceless views over Rio’s Sugarloaf Mountain, cityscape and glistening Guanabara bay.

This is a unique opportunity for a group of friends or families to experience Carnival in Rio in the one of the city’s most beautiful houses. Recently renovated, the space is beautifully understated with spectacular views spanning the city from the swimming pool and deck. The 7 ensuite bedrooms offer views over the garden and century old mango tree, or Rio’s city scape, and can be made into doubles or twins.
