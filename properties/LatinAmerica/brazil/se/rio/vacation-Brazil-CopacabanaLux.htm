Copacabana Lux, Copacabana, Rio Vacation Rental


Elegant and sophisticated penthouse with breathtaking ocean views, directly on Copacabana Beach. This penthouse has recently been completely renovated from top to bottom, turning it into Rio's most anticipated and ultimate penthouse experience.

For many, Rio de Janeiro is a once-in-a-lifetime, bucket list experience. For others, they come back year after year. Either way, what better way to experience one of the most vibrant, exciting and beautiful cities in the world, than sky-high living in this sprawling penthouse overlooking the beach, ocean and all of Copacabana.

This amazingly spacious 1000m2 (11,000 sqf) penthouse is the utmost in style. There is a large bright living room with floor to ceiling windows facing the endless blue of the ocean, letting in great sunlight all throughout the day, and at night the lights of Copacabana far below make you feel like the city is a masterpiece that been painted only for you. This stunning property features a modern American style kitchen, and has been divided into 4 spacious bedrooms, all with en-suite bathrooms, along with another living space if you need a bit more room. This smaller extra bedroom area also comes with en-suite bathroom. All the rooms have been fully kitted with everything that is needed to define an exquisite stay - from the amenities in the living room, to the en-suite bathrooms, to the modern kitchen.

The views of the ocean, beach and mountains are around you everywhere in this property. All bedrooms have views, and when you step out onto the over sized terrace, you'll feel the true beauty of Rio. There is a large pool overlooking the beach and ocean, a sauna, and the terrace is wrapped in a large "U" shape around the pool and the penthouse, making outdoor living very much part of your holiday. This is the perfect location for some small entertainment, or hosting an elegant dinner party, where you can have no better backdrop than the city of Rio de Janeiro herself. If you don't feel like the crowds of the beach some days, stay in and enjoy your local luxury living, by lounging in the pool with your own butler serving you caipirinhas and cold chopp.

A stay at this penthouse includes daily tropical Brazilian breakfast prepared for you by our in-house team, daily cleaning, and of course, where we can arrange anything for you, from private chef, exclusive city and beach tours, luxury transportation, and helicopter and yacht rentals. We are committed to making your stay in Rio an experience you'll treasure for years to come.
