Beautiful Joa
4 Bedrooms |4 Baths

This tranquil villa is simply stunning in its decor, design and privacy. On your way to this tropical villa you will climb Rio's mountainside until you reach the private gated entry. As you approach the house the first thing you will notice is the stunning private heated pool - with a shallow pool for just keeping cool, and a larger part of the pool where you can swim or play. The pool is surrounded on two sides by a custom wooden deck, with large oversized patio furniture. The main entrance to the villa is off the terrace that surrounds the pool. Whether you are in the pool, relaxing on the patio furniture, or inside the villa in the living area, the view is just spectacular as you oversea the blue waters of the Atlantic ocean just below you.

The main floor of the villa has large windows on all sides, to give you lots of light which allows you to feel the nature around you. There is a large stylish living room with grand piano and another comfortable living space which both face the ocean. The main floor is very open in design, and has a dining area off of the living room, where you can entertain your dinner guests. The very large and beautifully designed kitchen is on this level as well, with another terrace with dining table if you choose to host a dinner outdoors.

The staircase is the centrepoint of this main floor, as it is in the middle of the open concept floor plan and leads up to the 4 en-suite bedrooms above. 3 of the bedrooms face the jungle of Rio's mountains, and offer the perfect space for you to relax. The bedrooms are all very private with large en-suite state of the art bathrooms. The main bedroom is at the front of the house and has an immense walk out private terrace that overlooks the pool and ocean below. The bedroom and en-suite bathroom are innovatively designed as one room - with an oversized shower area which gives you breathtaking ocean views from wherever you are.


If you are seeking privacy and luxury in a Rio de Janeiro villa, look for further than this exquisite property.
