Taratuga Paradise, Manguinhos, Buzios, Brazil Vacation Rental

 

This is not just a vacation villa, but rather an entire estate covering 8000m², and with private beach access it guarantees a truly exclusive Buzios vacation experience. The main property itself is constructed on 540m² and the simple lines and minimalist decor ensures that your attention is not distracted away from the surrounding natural beauty.

The 6 bedrooms all come complete with air conditioning and 5 star quality beds for your upmost comfort. 4 of the bedrooms have their own private hammock area for unwinding after an exhausting day on the beach. The master bedroom, found on the 2nd floor, boasts a private terrace with commanding ocean views.

The piece de resistance is the canopied sun room with expansive wall-to-wall windows blurring the lines between inside and out, flooding the room with sunlight. Outside there is a spacious terrace, sauna courtyard and swimming pool area ideal for a spot of sunbathing while soaking up the views.

If you feel the need to head to enjoy the famous Buzios nightlife or try some of the spectacular restaurants you’re only 15 minutes away from downtown. Whether you want to host an outdoor barbecue or cool off with a swim at the end of the day you will have everything you need here at your luxury vacation estate.
