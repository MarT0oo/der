Ipanema Palace PH, Ipanema, Rio de Janeiro, Brazil Vacation Rental

 

This modern 3 bedroom duplex penthouse at the top of the Ipanema Palace provides you with modern comfort and an incredible terrace view of Rio’s natural beauty. This property is distinguished by its sheek interior design and excellent location in the heart of Ipanema next to the famous Rua Farme de Moeda.

Upon entering the property you will step into the carefully furnished and stylish common area. A modern HD television and comfortable seats await you for whenever you desire to relax in-doors. As you walk further into the room you will see the dining table set and fully equipped kitchen perfect for preparing and eating fine cuisine.

The bedrooms are exquisitely furnished with luxury quality beds and furnishings. After a long day exploring the many wonders Rio has to offer you can sleep with the comfort and quality that you deserve. The rooms are fully air conditioned with the latest equipment to ensure you have a cool and tranquil retreat from Rio’s hot sun.

One of the bedrooms opens up directly on to the property’s stunning terrace, complete with an astonishing view of the Corcovado and Tijuca forest. With your own private swimming pool, the large and well-furnished outdoor space is a unique highlight of the property. It’s the perfect place to take in the immense natural beauty of Rio, whether by yourself or inviting friends over to share the view with you.

As with all of our Rio Exclusive luxury properties the price includes cleaning service 2 times per week by our professional staff as well as the support of the Rio Exclusive concierge service. We are happy to organize additional services for you and we will work with you to custom tailor your luxury vacation experience.
