Villa Ferradura, Ferradura, Buzios, Brazil Vacation Rental

 

This two-story luxury vacation villa has an incredibly spacious main floor with an open-plan living room, dining room, and kitchen. The feeling of space is augmented by an almost seamless separation between the indoor and outdoor areas where you will find plenty of seats and lounge chairs surrounding the borderless pool, positioned on the front of the property, and facing the ocean.

The property comes equipped with the latest in kitchen appliances, beautiful furnishings combining bold and sophisticated pieces with rustic ones, and wooden outdoor furniture on the patio. A wood-fired pizza oven and barbecue grill are also available near the pool area. The villa was built on 4000m2 property land of tropical vegetation and exotic natural beauty.

The second story is dedicated to five large bedrooms, all of which have direct access to a wide balcony. The master suite has a large private bathroom with a Jacuzzi overlooking the ocean. The property has 7 bathrooms all of which are completely renovated, large, and stylish.

With all the amenities one could wish for, and a fantastically furnished collection of living, dining areas, and private bedrooms, this property is perfect for any group or family looking to relax in spacious comfort. Located in the most exclusive part of Buzios, this property is an excellent choice for the discerning client seeking a beautiful vacation rental in which to relax and enjoy the world.
