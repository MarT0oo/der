Leblon Penthouse, Leblon, Rio Vacation Rental


This dream of a luxury Penthouse is located in the Rio de Janeiro district Leblon, Rio de Janeiro's most exclusive neighbourhood, only 50 meters away from the beach, and 5 minutes walking distance from Rio de Janeiro's fanciest restaurants and shopping areas.

On the lower floor, this duplex penthouse features an 80m2 luxury living room, with two different sitting areas, a Home Cinema and an adjacent dining room. The Home Cinema area has a glass ceiling from where you can see the pool. On this floor, you'll also find a fully equipped kitchen, 3 large suites with individual bathrooms, big cupboards and beautiful indirect lighting.

On the upper floor of the penthouse you'll find a huge terrace with its private pool. From here you'll have a wonderful panoramic view of the sea. Adjacent to the terrace, there is an opened living room and a fully equipped summer kitchen with barbecue.

The master bedroom with its direct view over the mountain scenery called Dois Irmaos is also located on the upper floor. Next to it, you'll find the office, the master bathroom with a two-person bathtub and a large walk-in closet.
