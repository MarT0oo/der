Leblon 4, Leblon, Rio de Janeiro Vacation Rental

 

Large 4 bedroom apartment in Leblon, an upscale neighborhood in Rio de Janeiro.

Located one block from the beach , one block from the metro and two blocks from shopping Leblon this modest apartment is located in a lively area with many exquisite restaurant and bars. The apartments has 4 bedrooms, three of the four bedrooms have double beds and one with a king double which can be spilt into twin singles at the clients request. There is an single spare bedroom at the back of the apartment for larger groups if needed.

The building has one apartment per floor, 24 hour doorman and the apartment comes with two parking spaces. The kitchen is fully equipped, living room and all bedrooms except the spare room have airconditioning.

This apartment is perfect for large groups and ideal if you want to be close to the Olympic action.
