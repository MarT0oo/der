Enjoy a stay in one of Brazil’s most desired luxury vacation rental, located in the exclusive neighborhood of Joatinga. This 5 bedroom property is ideally located in Joatinga, which is a noble neighborhood of Rio, situated between Sao Conrado and Barra da Tijuca. There is a secure, 24 hour security, directly at the entrance to the property as well, for your added safety and comfort. The villa is 800m2, and is designed with modern luxury in mind, comprised of metal, glass, wood and burnt cement finishes.

This 5 bedroom villa also has 8 washrooms, a home office, a home theatre, a BBQ area, large outdoor terrace, and a magnificent infinity pool which is surrounded by a lush green forest. The views from this villa – from inside and out on the terrace – is simply breathtaking, and one of the most awe inspiring sites of Rio and its famed coastline that you can experience.

The master bedroom itself is over 1000ft2, and has views which will leave you wanting to return to Rio time and time again. The master bedroom is attached to a spa, with a vertical garden containing over 1800 plants, and a spectacular waterfall shower. For those looking for an exclusive neighborhood, with privacy and discretion, and the utmost in luxury, this villa is the ideal vacation rental for your stay. It is also available to rent for the 2016 Rio Olympic Games.
