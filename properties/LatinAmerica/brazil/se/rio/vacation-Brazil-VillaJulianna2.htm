Villa Julianna, Buzios, Brazil Vacation Rental

 

Villa Julianna is a luxury residence in Ferradurra Beach only 30 Meters to the beautiful sands where you can lay and enjoy the amazing sites and sounds that have come to be known as Buzios Beaches.

Villa Julianna sits on two floors with vast views of the hillside strewn with luxury multi-million dollar homes.  A lovely seating area sits around the modern and very creatively placed outdoor pool, with views of the beachfront.  There are  10 suites, one additional  small guest house with an independent entrance. There is A/C in the rooms and living areas with televisions placed in most bedrooms.

Outside you have a lovely sitting area wonderful for outside dining and gathering, where the staff can bring you drinks and dinner under the moonlit sky, complete with bar and  refrigerator.  There is also an ample garage (7 cars),  swimming pool on 3rd, wet bar and wet beach bar, Jacuzzi for 6 people in deck, sauna and bathtub for hydro therapy, barbecue and oven of pizza. Total infrastructure of comfort and leisure for 28 people.

The  house is furnished using USA Purchased furnishings of high end standards.  This is great for those seeking the comforts of home while in Brazil.  There is wonderful cooling throughout the house including in the halls and in-room video which we have a 60” Television. There is a guest suite with a private entrance on the outside of the house that is for staff use of well equipped with air and everything and I can also affirm that in accordance with the existing space for parking can park four cars in other places but covered in yard without problem.
