PH Leblon, Leblon, Rio Vacation Rental


This two story, 4 bedroom penthouse has been recently renovated to provide guests with the ultimate luxury experience. A stunning terrace, sun lit rooms, open layout and artfully opulent interior design have transformed this space into one of the best properties in Rio’s upscale Leblon neighborhood.

The gorgeous living room creates a bright and welcoming environment for you as you enter the property through the front door. An intricately crafted hardwood floor sets the stage for beige, white and crème colored walls and furnishings. Artisan quality wood chairs are put alongside luxury couches in an environment made resplendent by the natural light.

The kitchen provides a large modernly equipped space for you or your team of service staff to prepare fantastic quality meals. You can count on having the very latest and very best appliances and equipment available.

The bedrooms all come with comfortable 5 star quality beds, perfect for resting after long days spent enjoying the many brilliant sights that Rio has to offer. Our careful attention to detail and interior design has made each room a luxurious and elegant space in which to recover and prepare for a new day.

The outdoor terrace is a highlight of the property. It gives you a beautiful view of the peak of the Dois Irmaos mountain and provides you with a fantastic outdoor space in which to relax, socialize and enjoy the sun. The outdoor furnishings and built-in dipping pool make it a perfect location to sunbathe, cool off in the water or enjoy a late night glass of wine under the star studded Rio sky.

The property’s location at the center of Leblon is an excellent advantage. You will be both a short distance from one of the Rio’s most elite beaches as well as within close reach the finest restaurants, shopping centers and entertainment options in the City.
