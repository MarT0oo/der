Joatinga Villa
4 Bedrooms | 5 Baths                                                                              
Joatinga, Rio de Janeiro, Brazil
  From €19,250 USD per week

Magnificent 4 bedroom villa with stunning, unique views over the ocean, mountains and city of Rio de Janeiro.

This villa is Rio's newest addition to the luxury market, and is beautifully designed so that each of the 4 suites have amazing views out over the Atlantic ocean. This villa was recently renovated to the highest international standards by renowned architects and design team. It is within a gated community, so has the utmost in security and safety. The location of this villa is along a winding road which hugs Rio's ocean side mountains, and has access to a peaceful and secluded private beach. The theme throughout the entire villa is white - from the furniture, to the marble bathrooms to the imported flooring. Each of the 4 luxury suites face the ocean, and two of them have private balconies. The sun rises over the Atlantic and mountains in the distance, so that each morning, whether you are in bed, taking breakfast on your private terrace or enjoying a morning swim in the large infinity pool, you will enjoy the sunrise.

All 4 suites have staircases that lead down to the jewel of this villa - the shared terrace and infinity pool. There is a wooden terrace which surrounds the pool - which is equipped with lounge chairs and has ample space to spread out. This is the perfect spot to have some drinks in the sun or for cooling off from Rio's heat and going for a swim, or to have dinner or a cocktail party under the night Atlantic sky. The design of the infinity pool makes you feel that you could swim right into the Atlantic from your very own pool, and even with other houses close by in the neighborhood, once you're in the villa or enjoying the pool, you feel total serenity and privacy.

This luxury villa is set in a great location - the neighborhood of Joatinga is very exclusive in Rio, and very safe, yet you're only a few minutes away by taxi to the restaurants and beaches of Sao Conrado, or 15 minutes away to Leblon and Ipanema.
