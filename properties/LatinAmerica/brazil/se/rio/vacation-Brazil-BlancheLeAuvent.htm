Blanche Le Auvent, Ipanema, Rio de Janeiro, Brazil Vacation Rental

 

This stunning 2 story penthouse is equipped with 2 suites, luxurious common areas and a spectacular outdoor terrace complete with swimming pool. With all the amenities you expect from luxury living this creative penthouse will help you experience the best of Rio de Janeiro.

Entering through the living room you will immediately be struck by the wall-length view of Rio´s famous Christ the Redeemer statue. The expansive view includes Tijuca National Forest and Rodrigo de Freitas Lagoon, providing plenty of space for the eye to wander and take in the natural beauty of the ´Marvelous City´. A tasteful mixture of high quality furniture and modern art makes the room perfect for relaxing in the day or socializing with guests. The kitchen and dining area are comprehensively equipped with the most modern and efficient appliances. Whether you enjoy cooking yourself or prefer the service of a private chef you will have everything needed to produce and enjoy culinary masterpieces.

Next to the kitchen is also a small additional room perfect for another guest or for live-in staff service. The room is comfortably furnished and has a great view from the shower.

The secondary suite comes with a large, luxury bed and its own fantastic window view of the city and ocean. Expertly furnished with seating, a work area and a built in television it contains everything you need to relax in peace at the end of a long day exploring the city.

The master suite is located upstairs and is designed to bring the most exclusive level of comfort and enjoyment for you during your stay in Rio. A spacious bed leaves plenty of room for a single or couple to sleep well. A huge closet for your personal wardrobe gives you room for everything you´ll want to bring to and buy in Rio. Your own private bathroom complete with Jacuzzi awaits you. Finally there is an excellent work station with a window view right next to your private entrance to the property´s spectacular outdoor terrace.

Entering the terrace from the master suite or through the shared common area, you´ll be taken aback by the unmatched panoramic view. The vista reaches from the Dois Irmaos Mountain at the end of Ipanema beach, past the Corcovado out towards the bay of Copacabana. Whether you prefer to lounge in the sun or cool down in the swimming pool the terrace has everything you need to relax or entertain guests.

Like all our Rio Exclusive luxury properties this penthouse comes with a tropical breakfast prepared every morning, daily cleaning service from our in-house team and the support of the Rio Exclusive luxury concierge service. We´re committed to making your stay in Rio an experience you´ll treasure for years to come.
