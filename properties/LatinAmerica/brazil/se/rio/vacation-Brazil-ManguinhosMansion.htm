Manguinhos Mansion, Manguinhos, Buzios, Brazil Vacation Rental

 

The structure’s elegant use of sustainably sourced laminated wood as the primary building material helps it blend seamlessly into the surrounding natural environment. With large windows and spatial openings on to the rest of the estate wherever possible, the design opens the building up and integrates it with the whole 39000 m² property. Gardens built around the suites yet within the structure lend the design a further natural touch, and the spectacular inside-outside pool, capped off by its’ own gourmet kitchen, completes the artful mix with befitting grandeur.

The two master suites are separately located on the 2nd floor giving each a substantial degree of privacy. Each master suite comes with its’ own private living room, walk-in closet and veranda overlooking the rest of the estate. Both are luxuriously furnished with the most comfortable beds as well as artfully selected furnishings which retain the natural aesthetic that distinguishes the estate.

Descending from either of the two master suites one arrives at the spacious, opulently lighted and immaculately designed living room. The living room contains ample space and seating for the whole household and is more than capable of hosting large groups of guests in comfort. With a high ceiling and towering framed windows replacing plain walls the space possesses an ambiance overflowing with light and positivity, creating the perfect environment for casual socializing.

Heading towards the rear wing you will find 8 of the similarly well-furnished en-suite bedrooms mixed with direct access into the garden. Each bedroom has its’ own unique style and method of integration with the building and outside environment; all of them however keep guests in sumptuous luxury. The suites are divided at the halfway point by the special cinema room, a perfectly equipped miniature movie theatre in the comfort of your home.

Passing through the second intersecting wing you’ll stop by the artfully crafted reflecting pond. On one side and connecting to the pool is the lengthy swimming pool which is perfect for exercise and escaping the summer heat. A small gourmet kitchen at the end of the pool provides an opportunity for a snack or drink before or after any swimming session.

The other side of the wing contains 2 more bedroom suites as well as the personal gym and a personal spa. The wing is perfectly equipped for all your physical well-being and relaxation needs. Like in every part of the villa only the very best equipment and artfully selected furnishings are employed.

Beyond the second wing you will find a tastefully designed traditional dining room as well as a more casual dining and bar area next to the major kitchen. The kitchen is fully equipped with everything necessary to cook for an entire household, and there is a secondary kitchen especially equipped for Seafood.

The wider property itself contains a tennis court, garage and boat garage for your use. The 39000 m² which make up the property as a whole is replete with local flora and fauna, giving you access to the surrounding natural beauty of Buzios. Located right next to the beach it’s an easy walk to the ocean, however the layout of the terrain and the architectural design of the Villa also ensures a high level of privacy and low visibility from the beach itself.

With all the amenities you could wish for and a fantastically furnished collection of suites and rooms, this property is perfect for any group or family looking to relax in spacious comfort. With integrated access to the beautiful beaches and environment of Buzios, this property is an excellent choice for the discerning client seeking a beautiful vacation rental in which to relax and enjoy the world.
