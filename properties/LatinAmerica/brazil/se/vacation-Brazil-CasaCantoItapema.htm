Casa Canto Itapema, Itapema, Santa Catarina, Brazil Vacation Rental

 

Welcome to Casa Canto Itapema. You'll feel surprisingly delighted when walking in the front door -- the view is truly unique! It's a luxury 5-bedroom private vacation home with floor-to-ceiling glass windows . The open design is enhanced with 12 ft. high ceilings. We built in a residential area right on the ocean, overlooking and a short walk from the warm and friendly beach of Itapema City.

This beautiful vacation home is built with the finest materials, is designed to embrace the views and embodies pure comfort to provide a magical experience! With its four levels and outdoor poolside deck, it cascades down the hillside towards the ocean below. The bedrooms are on the top two levels for maximum privacy and offer views of the ocean and the surrounding tropical foliage from balconies. Casa Canto Itapema features and unique stone deck designed to complement the natural surroundings. The pool includesa kids pool area and jetted spa on one side, which connects a granite slab pedestrian bridge to an adult pool area with waterfalls, a bar with two in-water stools, and a shallow wading area. We’re just 15 minutes south of the second most popular tourist Beach in Southeastern Brazil, Balneario Camboriu.

Each bedroom suite provides fineline bedding and custom designed furnishings to ensure warmth and style. You will enjoy fully air conditioned interior living spaces.
