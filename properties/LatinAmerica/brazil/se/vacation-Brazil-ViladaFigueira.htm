Vila da Figueira, Ilha Bela, Sao Paulo, Brazil Vacation Rental

 

The Villa is located inside the most elegant and private residential condominium at Ilha Bela -Residential Ponta da Figueira -home of 12 luxury homes.

The private area of the villa is 43,065 square feet, it has a main house with 4 suites, a guest house with 2 suites, a large office area (with Wi-Fi internet and a large colonial style working table bought at an action, perfect for working, studying and meetings), and another house with 2 rooms where the house keeper lives and eventually the nanny or drives sleeps.

At the main house the lounge and rooms have high ceilings and the decoration is inspired on the 19 Century Brazilian Coffee Cycle, with authentic materials and objects from this period.

There are 2 entertainment TV areas; one of them is a cinema area with a 70 inches screen where the entire family can enjoy high quality movies.

The main house also has an incredible sofa lounge, main dining table that seats 10, complete kitchen without walls that gives the guests an opportunity to follow the great cooking, sound. Systems, a large covered outside deck with another dining table that seats 8 (perfect for having breakfast watching the ocean) and a lounge area with sofas and design chairs.

The Villa has a full wine cellar and a tasking room inside a rock cave (the wines have competitive prices and are available for paid consumption); one 50 ft. swimming pool; another smaller pool with a water cascade; Jacuzzi; 2 private piers; buoys to park overnight two boats; basketball court with one hoop; fire pick area for luaus; lounge by the pool with barbecue and pizza oven; lounge by the main house with wood oven, pizza oven and barbecue spot; boat house to store the 2 kayaks and water sports accessories; many private trails and areas to enjoy a romantic and beautiful view of the ocean; CD/DVD/Blue Ray players; satellite TV with English channels; 2 music entertainment systems, one by the pool lounge and another at the main house; and a fully equipped home theater lounge with a 70 inch screen.

The property is right in front of the ocean, where you can swim, snorkel and kayak. The nearest beach is Praia do Curral, 1,5 miles away (3 minutes’ drive), considered one of the most beautiful l beaches at the island. The center of Ilha Bela with its colonial architecture is located 14 miles by car (20 minutes’ drive).

Enjoy the comfort of a 5 star hotel with the privacy of your own place. The swimming pools and ocean front are perfect for expending the entire day sun bathing and relaxing. At the Villa you will enjoy the luxury, privacy, peace and harmony with the nature of the island. The 3 house employees are well trained, educated, discrete and helpful.
