Arraial d’Ajuda, a peaceful Bahia village is located on a plateau which overlooks a captivating coastline.Arraial has the dual advantage of being close to all the facilities and infrastructure of Porto Seguro while retaining its village like charm and peace.

This is probably the reason for Arraial D’Ajuda real estate being a hit with the affluent property seekers as well as wealthy tourists. Due to its unique location and charm, it is destined to become one of the most sought after properties in Bahia.

Arraial is in the Northeast of Brazil, in the south of Bahia state. Formed by places such as Trancoso, Caraíva and Vale Verde, Arraial is a district of Porto Seguro. It belongs to the region known as Discovery Coast (Costa do Descobrimento), where Portuguese explorers arrived in 1500.

Arraial D’Ajuda is a lively town with a unique feel, many different Boutique hotels and restaurants. Tourists visit from all over the world and quickly fall in love with its charm. There you have access as well to an Eco Resort that comes with its own aquatic park as well as many other attractions. Very close by you will find the Terravista Golf Course and Condominium complex, which has its own landing runway for private aircrafts and an stunning 18 hole golf course
