Casa Monte Belo, Natal, Rio Grande do Norte, Brazil Vacation Rental

 

Casa Monte Belo has the base aesthetic of a luxury Brazilian beachfront coastal home combined with European comfort and amenities.

The house offers 5 en-suite luxury bedrooms in a combination of king beds and single beds that can hold comfortably 10 people at any given time.

The unique location right on the white sandy beach of Praia de Porto Mirim with the home's slightly elevated location brings amazing views of the sea and surrounding bay. Combine this with all year round warm climate, sea temperatures and excellent service and you will have an amazing experience in Casa Monte Belo.

What are you waiting for? Experience the real Brazilian beach paradise and book your unforgettable stay at Casa Monte Belo!

Designed by well-known European architect Claes Feder in rustic, colourful Brazilian style with an elegant European touch and craftsmanship, using first class materials throughout the villa inside and out. Beautifully decorated using bamboo and Brazilian hard woods, attractive stones, paintings and art pieces from various Brazilian artists, paying attention to every detail. A jewel!

A warm welcome to Casa Monte Belo at Praia do Porto Mirim, Jacuma, north of Natal, Rio Grande de Norte, 26 km from new international airport Sao Gonçalo de Amarante.

This fabulous holiday villa is situated right on the white sandy beach, surrounded by whispering palms and the turquoise ocean, with its own infinity pool and spa.
