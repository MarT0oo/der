Trancoso Elegance, Trancoso, Bahia, Brazil Vacation Rental

 

A stunning property in a secluded tropical setting, perfect for a large group. There are 6 bedrooms, 7 bathrooms – with occupancy for up to 12 people – in this luxury Brazilian getaway.

The villa is located in the charming and tranquil beach side town of Trancoso. It's in the state of Bahia, which is the large Brazilian state that runs all along the east coast of Brazil. Here you will find some of the most beautiful beaches in the world, and a true Brazilian way of life. The state of Bahia is very well known within Brazil, with its own easy going and fun culture, mixed with very friendly locals and a delicious cuisine with African influences.

This property sits on a large green lot – with tropical vegetation and nicely manicured lawns surrounding your villa. You have ocean views from the villa, and are only a short walk to the beach. The villa is centered around a resort style pool – complete with lounge chairs, covered eating area, and outdoor sofas. The pool is nicely finished with blue tile, and is perfect for relaxing and lounging the afternoon away.

The main house has been completed in a pavilion style with vaulted roof and wood beams. Inside you'll find a decor and style that perfectly matches the Bahian beach life. The entire main floor is surrounded by floor to ceiling windows and doorways opening to your pool and terrace area. If you leave the doors open, you'll feel that you've invited the outdoor life into your living room and sitting areas. It's a beautiful design, enabling the guest to use the villa for both indoor and outdoor use. There are giant white wood beams throughout the villa, and the furniture is over sized lounge furniture - perfect for a hot Brazilian day. You also have a full kitchen, and a dining table that can easily seat 12 people.

The bedrooms are impeccably done - with romantic mosquito netting and wooden window shutters, which adds to the exotic feel of the villa. The bathrooms are all beautifully finished as well, with Brazilian wood and tile.

The villa is a perfect example of luxurious Bahian life, and is a great getaway for those wanting an authentic Brazilian experience, a little off the well-worn path.
