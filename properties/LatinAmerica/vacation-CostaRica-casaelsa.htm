Casa Elsa, Manuel Antonio, Costa Rica Vacation Rental


Casa Elsa is an exclusive luxury vacation rental home located in Manuel Antonio, Costa Rica. Casa Elsa combines 7500 square feet of tropical paradise with the luxury and amenities that are sure to satisfy even the most discriminating holidaymaker.


Casa Elsa's uniqueness lies in its ability to offer an open concept view of the Costa Rican jungle and its wildlife while maintaining complete privacy. The vast, modern and open spaces means that the only visitors you can expect are from nature itself. Daily visits from birds, monkeys and a lazy sloth, along with a refreshing steady breeze from the Pacific Ocean will make your visit to Casa Elsa an everlasting and unforgettable experience.


If you would like to preview the Casa Elsa experience, view the video below. You will see that Casa Elsa is not just located in the Costa Rican Jungle - the jungle is Casa Elsa and Casa Elsa a part of it.


Casa Elsa is an open concept house. There are very few walls or doors in the house itself, except for the 4 bedrooms and ensuite baths. The house sports several floors, each offering a unique view of the Costa Rican jungle. Casa Elsa's design also exposes one to the elements. Visitors will feel the cool coastal breeze blow into every room of this luxury home. During the day, Casa Elsa is brightly illuminated by indirect sunlight which compliments the artistic elements of the house such as the acid-etched concrete floors and brightly decorated walls.


Tourist Attractions


During your stay at Casa Elsa, there are numerous activities and attractions that are sure to keep you and your family entertained. Casa Elsa is close to Manuel Antonio National Park, where visitors can hike through the rainforest or even take a guided tour.


In addition to the rainforest, there are numerous activities for visitors of all ages and interests. Popular activities include horseback riding, mangrove tours, kayaking, snorkeling, dolphin and whale sighting, sunset sails, sport fishing, etc. For those seeking adventure, don't miss the opportunity to zipline through the rainforest canopy or consider an underwater diving tour or white water rafting.


The nearby city of Quepos features several tourist attractions and is within bus or taxi distance from Casa Elsa. Closer to Casa Elsa, visitors can shop, visit a local art gallery, walk through the butterfly farm, and have lunch or dinner at one of many restaurants. Dancing and live music are available at several nightclubs. You can also press your luck at a casino.


The concierge at Casa Elsa can assist you in recommending activities. Furthermore, most attractions, excursions and tours provide transportation to and from Casa Elsa. With so many things to try you are sure to be entertained during your visit.
