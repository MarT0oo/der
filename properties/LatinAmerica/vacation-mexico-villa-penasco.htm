Villa Peñasco, Pedregal, Cabo San Lucas, México


This impressive oceanfront villa occupies the most enviably positioned site in this private and gated enclave of Pedregal, nurse capturing views of the sea. Perched high on a cliff overlooking Land’s End, where the Sea of Cortez meets the Pacific Ocean this villa offers the ultimate in natural beauty and first-class luxury.On the spacious main terrace take in the magnificent ocean view from the jacuzzi or infinity pool as you listen to the soothing sounds of its waterfall.

The terrace also features a palapa shade structure complete with an elegant bar, ampoule refrigerator, and barbecue, all perfect for outdoor entertaining.

Inside, the villa offers a state-of-the-art gourmet kitchen. The scenic dining room seats 16, and fully opens to the main terrace. After dinner, relax around the large stone fireplace in the living room. Each beautiful suite has a full marble bath, private terrace, TV, refrigerator, safe, and fabulous view.

To meet your business needs, Villa Peñasco provides a boardroom with a large-screen, surround-sound theater system, computer, high-speed, wireless Internet, 300 DVD movie collection, library, and fax machine. Other special amenities include a fitness room and keycard security access system.

Villa Peñasco’s staff is on hand to meet your every need. Conveniently located in the exclusive, gated community of Pedregal, this premier estate features the best in luxury, security, service, and scenic splendor.
