Casa Kupuri, Punta Mita, Mexico Vacation Rental

 

Occupying a coveted location within Punta Mita and in the community of Kupuri, it is located on among the most privileged sites within Punta Mita.

The Main House features the 1,000 square-foot Great Room with inviting seating, complete home theatre/entertainment center and wet bar, as well as a large dining area and open exhibition kitchen. Oversize glass pocket doors fully open up to the interior water wall as well as overlook the pool and expansive ocean views.

The center of activity at this house is its 904-sq ft, infinity-edged swimming pool and adjacent covered lounge area. The wood-decked living space has all the required comforts including a service bar with a 36" Viking grill, wet bar, mini-fridge, icemaker, and powder room. At night, a striking fire pit provides dramatic lighting.

Notable details of the home include its use of intelligent lighting systems, as well as energy-efficient roofing system and air-conditioning systems, all designed to minimize energy consumption. Use of sustainable wood furthers its eco-sensitivity. Throughout the home is a distributed, zoned audio system, as are iPod docking stations.

Service areas include an industrial-quality laundry room, separate 2-bedroom caretaker's house, and a 3-car enclosed garage. The residence offers five exquisitely designed bedroom suites, each with a spacious signature bathroom, ample closets and private garden areas or terraces.

Located in the Main House, the Master Suite has near 180-degree ocean views and direct access to an outdoor Jacuzzi—also accessible from the Great Room. The Master Suite's oversize bath has a double rain shower, steam option, massage tub and patio with outdoor shower. An interior garden connects this Suite to a flex-room with full bath.

A separate structure is home to the remaining three Bedroom Suites, each thoughtfully integrated with the lush landscape and topography of the home site. The largest is a double-level Suite complete with a pergola-covered living and dining area with wet bar, grill, mini-fridge and icemaker. Each of the three Suites feature ocean views.

A private pathway extends from the home down a secluded path to an oceanfront, palapa-topped beach cabaña, providing direct access to sandy Kupuri beach.

This exquisite estate residence includes Premier Membership in the private Club Punta Mita, with its two Jack Nicklaus Signature golf courses, Tennis Club, Fitness Center and the private Residents' Beach Club, as well as future planned community amenities.

Additional benefits of its Kupuri, Punta Mita location include high-end infrastructure, 24-hour dual gated security, and world-class amenities, including additional planned amenities for owners within the Kupuri community.
