Nekupe, Nandaime, Nicaragua Vacation Rental

 

Nekupe Sporting Resort and Retreat is an intimate, eight-room, luxury property located on an 1800 acre nature reserve in Nicaragua’s Pacific countryside; just 30 minutes from the charming, colonial city of Granada and only an hour from the Pacific Coast.

Offering a seamless confluence of sustainable, nature-based, Feng Shui inspired design with rich Nicaraguan culture and warm hospitality; the property flawlessly blends with the environment to nurture exploration, adventure, well-being, and peace.

Experiences include: horseback riding, daily yoga, organic gardening, and cooking classes among others.

Nekupe is the vision of Don Alfredo Jr. and Doña Theresa Pellas, avid travelers and adventure enthusiasts, whose commitment to nature is exhibited through the resort’s core principles of respect and stewardship of the environment. The result is a socially responsible destination that enriches an outdoor sporting lifestyle and creates an external retreat to inner peace.

Sophisticated, modern, and transcendent. These are the ways one could describe the architecture of Nekupe. Rooted in the core principles of respect and stewardship of the natural beauty in which it is set, Nekupe’s design strikes a balance between the authentic Nicaraguan life and culture with the modernity which today’s upscale travelers have become accustomed. Rooms feel like an escape from the everyday, with warm, minimal décor, thoughtful design and open layouts that work together in perfect harmony.

The Heart of Nekupe. The Casa Club serves as the central lobby lounge for the resort and houses the restaurant and pool court. To top it off, you enjoy a spectacular view of the Mombacho Volcano.

Built on the highest point of the property with views of the mountains all around, La Ermita is an oasis for contemplation and reflection, whether you are renewing your vows, celebrating a wedding or baptism, or reflecting on one´s self and one’s life.

Our Executive Chef introduces guests to traditional Nicaraguan fare with a modern spin, using a bounty of native ingredients sourced from Nekupe’s on-site garden and local purveyors.

Aiming to provide authentic Nicaraguan hospitality and locally sourced cuisine, Nekupe offers a fresh, farm-to-table spin on traditional Nicaraguan dishes.

Whether you are having dinner at Don Alfredo’s or an outdoor picnic, our culinary options are sure to please every palate.

Nekupe Spa is a place to both burn off steam and indulge in an array of rejuvenating spa treatments. Guests will find the purest form of relaxation by coexisting harmoniously with nature and will enjoy massages, facials and scrubs with the highest quality ingredients, destined to fully rejuvenate the body.

Nekupe offers opportunities around the entire reserve for guests to exercise, engage, and challenge themselves through physical activity. Located on a top of a mountain with unobstructed views, the Fitness

Center features a full-service gym. with the best interactive equipment for a full body circuit training, from cardio and weights, to personal training, fitness paths, and swimming.

Nekupe helps guests rejuvenate their bodies and reconnect with themselves in different ways, enjoying our inspiring and serene spots, receiving the vibes and energy from nature and all of our settings. Yoga instructors are available upon request to aid you in the stengthening of your body and gym relaxation of your mind.

Whether you are having an unforgettable vacation or celebrating an important life occasion at Nekupe, La Residencia de Doña Theresita is your home away from home. This 24,000 square foot residential compound, perfectly suited for one family or groups traveling together, features four serene rooms; two Grand Suites and two Suites, each one enjoying spectacular views of the forest, stunning design, peaceful courtyards, and modern amenities.

The villas feature four individual and self-contained spaces that can be rented by a single couple, several couples or groups traveling together. The villas are arranged so guests are immersed in nature and delighted with fresh breezes, a beautiful garden and breath-taking views. Three villas are 1,000 square feet and a fourth is 700 square feet.

Nekupe is located in Nandaime, “the place of abundant streams.” It is just under two hours from the Augosto C. Sandino International Airport in Managua, 30 minutes form the charming, colonial city of Granada and only an hour from the Pacific Coast.
