Rancho Majagual, Playa Majagual, San Juan del Sur, Nicaragua Vacation Rental

 

Imagine a place where the the trees grow tall and green above you, and you can wander amongst them in the dappled sunlight, listening to the calls of parakeets and monkeys. Filling your lungs with smell of the lush greenery and tropical flowers, feeling the warm air on your skin. Imagine stepping out of this paradise and walking right onto soft white sand beaches, flanked by rocks and cliffs, where warm, blue ocean water gently laps at the beach and your toes and the sun shines down upon you.

Rancho Majagual is this place. Set on the shores of raw, undeveloped white sand beach of Playa Majagual in San Juan del Sur Nicaragua. Known to the locals as "The Secret Beach" Playa Majagual is the backdrop to this magical location. With over 120 acres of privately owned land, Rancho Majagual is woven into a blend of jaw dropping landscapes. Five hundred meters of pristine beach front; lush, green tropical gardens and acres of native jungle hardwoods, all filled with flowers, trees, monkeys and dozens of bird species. Gated, private and secure, this sanctuary offers guests the utmost privacy, security and discretion to let down their guard, and connect with their surroundings. Set amongst these natural surroundings, nestled into the trees you will discover;

El Rancho- the large, classically designed five bedroom guest house sleeping up to 10 guests.
Three luxurious stand alone one bedroom Villas, Ranchito Guanacaste, Ranchito Palermo and Ranchito Jicaro.
The Clubhouse- The social heart of Rancho Majagual, this palapa is tropcial living with a vaulted palm roof, sitting area, dining area, bar, kitchen and 1/2 bathroom. It is the perfect place to gather for meals, cocktails and fun.
The beach front Infinity Edge Pool - located just outside of the Club House and steps from the main dwelling this space is filled with sunshine and has plenty of recliners for lounging and pool toys for enjoying.
Over 500m of direct beach frontage
Walking paths winding through the tropical gardens
An estuary that fills at high tide, a favourite spot for native bird species
Helicopter Landing Pad- perfect for those looking to simplify their arrival and stay
Our dedicated team of staff working behind the scenes to ensure a memorable stay
