Ranchito Jicaro, Playa Majagual, San Juan del Sur, Nicaragua Vacation Rental

 

Three intimate, private, beach front villas are located on the grounds of Rancho Majagual. Named for native trees that grow on the property, Ranchito Jicaro, Ranchito Palerma and Ranchito Guanacasta are located just steps away from the ocean. Wake up every morning walk out your door, and place your feet directly onto the soft white sand of Playa Majagual.

The Ranchitos each consist of a comfortable king sized bed with soft, high end linens. A private, ensuite bathroom with hot water, a living room (with a queen size futon couch), and front porch with lounge chairs complete the space. Fall asleep to the sounds of the waves meeting the sand on Playa Majagual. Step out of your villa for a moonlight swim under the night sky in the infinity pool, just steps from your bed. Wake up to the sounds of the Mono Congo monkeys and birds, welcome the day.

Guests of El Rancho will enjoy access to the Club House - a large, airy, social hub with a vaulted palm roof is the perfect place for families and groups to gather for meals, cocktails and fun. The Club House includes a large sitting area, dining area, bar, kitchen and 1/2 bathroom for your convenience. Located right near the beach and only a few steps away from the Club House is the properties large, infinity edge pool. Offering spectacular views and multiple loungers, we dare to suggest this might be the best spot to relax in all of Central America.

Our location directly on Playa Majagual, our guests enjoy unparalleled beach access. The warm, tranquil waters of this protected bay are ideal for swimming, fishing and walking at any time of the day. Located only a short walk from Playa Majagual are the two nearby beaches of Playones (Playa Matildas) and the famed surf spot of Playa Maderas. Staying here at Rancho Majagual gives you direct and easy access to all three of the of the San Juan del Sur areas best beaches.

We want you to be able to disconnect and unwind on your stay, to truly escape. Our full time staff include an on-site Property Manager, Gardeners, Housekeepers and 24 hour security. Our team connects will connect with you prior to your visit to ensure you have everything you may need in order to have an effortless and relaxing experience. Our onsite manager Claudia (fluent in Spanish & English) is available to assist travellers with any needs that may arise. Enjoy the complimentary breakfast cooked for you by our onsite private chef and if you like, our Chef is available to prepare all 3 meals for you while you simply relax or explore the surrounding area. (additional fees do apply).

We have access not only by land, but sea and air as well. Access to the property from the town of San Juan del Sur can be done via a water taxi (20minutes) or car (25 minutes). We can also assist with arranging a helicopter from the airport in Managua or Costa Esmerelda with your landing pad located directly on the property. Enjoy the beautiful views of the Costa Esmerelda Coast line as you arrive in style and comfort.
