Bighorn, Revelstoke, British Columbia, Canada Vacation Rental

 

The impressive timber-framed building was designed with indulgence in mind. Imagine winding down after a day of skiing in the triple height great room, indulging in gourmet canapes in front of the roaring log fire. Dinner can be taken in the elegant formal dining room, or in the more casual setting of the kitchen, where you can watch the chef prepare your food on the teppanyaki grill.

The house also features a hi-tech cinema and eight spacious guest suites, each with its own custom bathroom. A world class spa area offers everything you need to restore body and mind after a hard day's skiing Take your pick of the indoor current pool, sauna, fully equipped gym, massage rooms or the outdoor hot tub, where you can relax and enjoy the integrated sound system and sweeping views across the valley.

A mountain retreat like no other; Bighorn is both a heliski lodge and a 5 star ski chalet. We have taken the best elements from each concept and created a new definition of luxury. Bighorn offers a truly bespoke experience with private heliskiing from the house helipad and access to unbeatable ski terrain at Revelstoke Mountain Resort. Bighorn is the finest private accommodation in North America, serviced by an award winning European chef and an unobtrusive, dedicated team of staff.
