Compass Pointe Estate, Whistler, British Columbia Vacation Rental

 

Compass Pointe Estate sits above the Whistler village overlooking both Blackcomb and Whistler Mountains. This contemporary villa offers 6,200 square feet of modern living in the mountains all the while being walking distance into the village of Whistler. The property features a 1 master bedroom, 4 main bedrooms with 4 bathrooms plus a powder room. It also features a large salt water outdoor pool, heated driveway, 3 car parking garage and an extra living area (bedroom and bathroom) in the finished basement. (perfect for housekeeping/security staff) Property also features a comfortable theatre/media room, kids entertainment room(with stage) and a ski and snowboard storage room.
