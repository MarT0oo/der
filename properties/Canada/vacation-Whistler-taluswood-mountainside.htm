Taluswood Mountainside Luxury
4 Bedrooms | 4 Baths | Private Hot Tub | Private Deck
Whistler, British Columbia
From $3,465 per Week

Taluswood Mountainside Luxury is our Ski in / ski out four bedroom luxury town home on Whistler Mountain perched high above Whistler Creekside village (home of the 2010 Olympic downhill races). Vaulted ceilings framed by double story cedar logs and large windows let you enjoy the incredible mountain and lake views; a true mountainside retreat. Have dinner in the separate dining room and then relax in the living room curled up on the comfy sofas while enjoying a glass of wine next to the wood-burning fireplace. Enjoy the luxury of granite counters in the kitchen and bathrooms and the hideaway private hot tub on its own deck.

The house is completely designer inspired with rich oaks and amazing comfortable seating throughout.  The master suite includes a plush King Sized Bed, fireplace, and luxurious bath with his and her vanity.  The Guest Suites are all well appointed with two suites serving as children’s or singles rooms with Bunk Bed Configurations and their own baths.  This is a great location for kids and we welcome families who want to share the Whistler Experience.

 

A wrap around deck and Jacuzzi offer an especially nice touch for those looking for more than your ordinary stay.  You’ll have a great time entertaining your family and skiing on one of the Best Mountains in the World.
