Whistler House, Canada, British Columbia Vacation Rental


Welcome to Eagle's Nest - Whistler's most luxurious vacation rental home. This magnificent home features easy ski access, stunning vistas, and superb amenities including an outdoor heated pool, home theatre, and easy ski access.

Find your alpine escape amongst the trees. This Kadenwood property features stunning views of Whistler Peak and the Coast Mountain Range, also overlooking a natural green space.

Built by an esteemed team of designers, architects and builders, this home features timber frame construction with open decking and rafters. Enjoy the afternoon sun from the open concept living space, complete with vaulted ceilings and immense picture windows. The grand room is punctuated with organic materials including stone and local timers for a rustic, yet modern feel.

With 4 bedrooms and 5.5 baths, including two master suites, the home has much to offer. Enjoy a steam shower or soak in the outdoor hot tub while taking in the wonderful vistas. The home is complete with the finer touches such as Frette bath towels, robes and linen.
