Horstman Luxury Log Chalet, Whistler, British Columbia Vacation Rental

 

Whistler Luxury Chalet at Horstman Estates is a private sub-division of luxury exclusive Whistler homes. Horstman 4930 is an elegant mountain ski chalet, a perfect Whistler vacation home rental. It offers a prestigious mountainside location in Blackcomb Benchlands exclusive subdivision

This exclusive five bedroom five and half bathroom chalet features vaulted ceilings, wood log beams, hardwood floors and a huge stone fireplace enhancing the contemporary West Coast theme. It offers over 3800 sq. ft of living space with an open style gourmet kitchen; dining room with seating for 14; sunken living room with French doors; full laundry suite with washer/dryer/sink and a den with a state-of-the-art home theater including a 103" projection screen/laser disc player/DVD/CD/iPod stereo which connects to TV's in each of the bedrooms with satellite reception.

For your convenience, there is a heated driveway with two-car garage. After a day of skiing or golfing, invigorate yourself in the home-spa feature with a Eucalyptus Steam Shower and Jacuzzi bathtub in the master en-suite, or have a soak in the private outdoor hot tub located on a large balcony overlooking the valley.

Horstman Estates is close to Whistler's Upper Village. These are the only free standing luxury homes in Blackcomb Benchnlands. Their proximity to Whistler's Upper Village offers convenient access to Whistler's amenities including ski lifts, shops and restaurants.

Horstman is only 350 metres from the base of Blackcomb mountain, and features a ski-in access trail off of Blackcomb's Cruiser. The ski in access trail is directly across from this home, Horstman 4930.
