Belmont Estate, Whistler, British Columbia Vacation Rental

 

The Belmont Estate has two spectacular master bedrooms. Both rooms offer large en-suite bathrooms. One room has a massive Hammam style steam shower. There are also three double bedrooms with ensuite bathrooms in the main house. An additional charming two bedroom, two bathroom guest house is located next to the main house. Enjoy this haven for guests with the same classic luxury style as the main home. The living room is large with spectacular views. Enjoy the modern and functional gourmet kitchen. Dine at an extra large dining room table, with an exceptional view.

The estate also offers a state of the art media room, playful family room, and a private office. These spaces provide ample living and entertaining room. Retreat to the gym and spa building to relax with a sauna, a shower or workout with the finest in exercise equipment and audio-video electronics. Located in the back courtyard is a 12 person hot tub and a large 15m x 5m outdoor, heated swimming pool. Accompanying this space is also a wood burning fire pit with comfortable sitting, overseeing a beautifully landscaped garden. A mountain treasure, this estate offers the ideal year-round use for holidays and vacations. Additional amenities include a professional prep kitchen with a service food elevator, which brings the dishes to the main floor area. Forced air, radiant heating and air conditioning are supplied by an eco-friendly, efficient geo-thermal pump. Three beautiful fireplaces are also positioned to warm you up on a cold winter day.

The Helicopter landing pad is at your door to reach the powder of untracked slopes of the amazing mountain range surrounding you. No delays, no line up. Keep in mind that you are a mere five minutes away from the base of Whistler and you can ski your heart out on the perfectly groomed trails as well… the choice might be difficult some days! Whistler is North America best choice for snow and powder, let The Belmont Estate be the host of the magic and memorable moments you will experience here.
