Timber Luxury Chalet, Whistler, British Columbia, Canada Vacation Rental

 

Natural timbers extenuate this beautiful open-concept 5 bedroom, 4.5 bathroom home. Located within walking distance to Whistler Village this home boats 3,500 sq.ft of living space over 3 levels. Enter into the grand foyer with vaulted ceilings that opens up to the main top floor from above. The main level features the master bedroom with walk-in closet and private en-suite, second bedroom, also with private en-suite and 2 additional bedrooms with a adjoining bathroom. A 2 car garage and large laundry room complete this main floor.

A showcase curved staircase wraps around a feature cedar post up to the top level of this home. The open design blends the living room, dining room and expansive deck that wraps around the home to the kitchen and additional sitting area. A covered deck and vaulted ceiling overhead are anchored by a outdoor wood burning fireplace. A full bathroom, sitting area and large multipurpose room round out the other side of the this level. The bottom level of the home boasts the fifth bedroom and full bathroom. Groups and families will feel at home in this perfect home away from home. With mountain views and southern exposure deck you will enjoy your Whistler Vacation being within minutes from the main Village and less than a 5 minute drive to the base of the Mountain.
