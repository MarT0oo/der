Harmony Chalet, Whistler, British Columbia, Canada Vacation Rental

 

Spectacular executive home located on the side of Whistler Mountain. Breathtaking panoramic south-west views showcase the surrounding mountain peaks and valley lakes. This superior quality home features huge cedar beams, natural rock, slate & tile, and extensive woodworking. Top quality furnishings and appliances complete this plush and spacious mountain home retreat. With a billiard room, indoor resistance pool, steam room & outdoor hot tub, this slope-side home will pamper you during your Whistler visit. 8000 square feet of luxury on 4-levels. In-floor heating throughout.

Basement (Bottom) Level: 6th bedroom with 3 twins and full en-suite bath. Large stretching/exercise area, plus ping-pong table, running machine. Full home theater media room with 72” screen, 20 leather ascending seats.

Ground Floor (Pool Level): Entertainment area with full bar/kitchen, 4’x8’ billiards table, 42” flat screen, gas fireplace. Opens to a gorgeous indoor 15’x30’ resistance pool with waterfall, full bath and steam shower plus outdoor hot tub on large deck. 3 bedrooms with 1 king and 2 queens, all with full en-suite baths plus half bath.

Main Floor (Entrance Level): 3 bay garage and spacious foyer leading to a gorgeous open-plan kitchen, dining and living area. Gourmet kitchen with high-end gas appliances and all the accouterments, with pass-thru to adjacent dining area seating 10 around a circular table. The kitchen-dining area flows to the living area with ultra comfy seating around a huge rock, wood-burning fireplace. Large wrap-around deck off the entire area. Stunning circular staircase to top floor. Vaulted ceilings, extensive woodworking and large expanses of glass complete the stunning design of this home. One bedroom off the main level with King bed and private ensuite bathroom.

Top Floor (Master Suite): Expansive master bedroom area featuring 2 bedrooms with full en-suite baths. Master has king bed, 42” disappearing flat screen tv, huge soaker tub with gas fireplace, walk-in closets, double vanity.The nanny suite has queen bed and full en-suite bath. Lacks for nothing to pamper you on your vacation.
