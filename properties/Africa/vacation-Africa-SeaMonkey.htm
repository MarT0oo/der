Sea Monkey, Machabee, Mahé, Seychelles Vacation Rental

 

Located on the northernmost coast of Mahé, known for its abundant sandy beaches, coral reefs and tropical vegetation. Sea Monkey is all about exceptional privacy and exclusivity in your tropical home away from home. Sea Monkey Villa, Designed by Jean Marc Eynaud Architects, has been awarded the Africa’s Best Beach/Coastal Boutique Hotel Winner 2016.

Sea Monkey is located in the exclusive region of Glacis if you are looking for enjoying days spent on the beach or simply enjoying the villas own facilities, yet Vibrant Beau Vallon bay with its many restaurants, bars and local markets, is just a stone’s throw away

Built on a secluded rocky peninsula with panoramic views over the Indian Ocean, Sea Monkey is the definitive luxury retreat. The villa is nestled amongst lush vegetation, just metres away from the sea lapping on sculptured granite rocks. Pristine grounds and immaculate interior decor combine to make Sea Monkey an unrivalled paradise escape.

Besides relaxing beach days, diving, fishing and boat excursions can be arranged. A trip out to snorkel with the whale sharks with the professionally run Underwater Dive Centre situated on Beau Vallon beach is recommended between August and October. For some gentle land-based distraction, there are a number of small artists studios and some pleasant gardens to explore at Le Jardin du Roi in the mountains.

Sea Monkey is located in the exclusive region of Glacis where Ian Fleming got the inspiration for his book For Your Eyes Only. The area is known for its abundant sandy beaches, coral reefs and tropical vegetation.

Vibrant Beau Vallon bay with its many restaurants, bars and local markets, is a stone’s throw away. The capital Victoria and the airport can be reached within 20 minutes.

The capital Victoria and the airport can be reached within 25 minutes from Sea Monkey Villa. Self-driving is easy, with relatively short distances, good maps, easy signposting and a good network of tarmac roads on the island.
