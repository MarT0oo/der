La Casita Blanca, Casa de Campo, Dominican Republic Vacation Rental

 

The house is called “La Casita Blanca” because it’s white walls, floors and roofs make the house feel very airy as if it where floating up in the clouds. The villa is located along a beautiful The Links Golf Course, which with its lakes, trees, landscaping and migrating birds provide a picturesque backdrop that mimics the tropical landscape found all over the island.

A beautiful brick paved deck surrounds the house and together with the open floor plan of the living room they provide optimal indoor-outdoor living space. The layout and space planning of the house allows for it to act like a chameleon, it is great for a fun family vacation where playing kids can be seen from most areas of the house, as well as provide amazing entertainment space for guests while still providing the warmth and comfort necessary when you want a cozy intimate getaway. Great Staff.

Casa de Campo is located on the south eastern shore of the Dominican Republic, next to the town of La Romana and just 70 miles from the capital city of Santo Domingo, the oldest city in the new world. Casa de Campo is a luxurious 7,000 acre ocean front community offering world class golf courses which include the famous Teeth of the Dog Golf Course, Dye Fore and The Links Golf Course plus polo fields, skeet shooting centre, tennis centre, equestrian centre, water sports, spa treatment centre, fitness centre, business centre, private beach, a la carte restaurants, Altos de Chavon (artist village) and Casa de Campo Marina, all of which will please almost any visitor.
