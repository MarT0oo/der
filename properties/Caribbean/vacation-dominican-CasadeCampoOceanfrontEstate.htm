Casa de Campo Oceanfront Estate, La Romana, Casa de Campo, Dominican Republic Vacation Rental

 

The dramatic view and proximity of the sea immediately greets you upon entering this Caribbean oceanfront estate where a gem of a sunset can be seen every evening! Inspired by tropical motif in the original Casa de Campo style and kept to the highest standards, this 11 bedroom sprawling villa is the perfect property for a Large Family, or a Corporate group looking for their unique tropical hideaway.

Directly across from hole number 13 Par 3 on famous Teeth of the Dog golf course, this private compound features large bedrooms and bathrooms, plenty of covered and open social areas, an exceptional gazebo with newly thatched roof, a newly refinished, large swimming pool with swim up bar and a 15 person Jacuzzi plus sea stairs for swimming and much more.
