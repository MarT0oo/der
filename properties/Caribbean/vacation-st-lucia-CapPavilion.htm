Cap Pavilion, Ranch Site, Cap Estate, St. Lucia Vacation Rental


A Warm Welcome: This is a beautiful villa, inspired by Oliver Messel’s Caribbean designs, and constructed in an open architectural style from coral stone rendering, with cool green shutters and detailing. It is situated in a secluded location, on its own knoll high in Cap Estate and enjoys 360 degree views. Cap Estate has a championship 18 hole golf course, a racquet club offering both tennis and squash and two sandy beaches with good snorkeling.

Main House: The house has been professionally interior decorated to a very high standard with the use of bright tropical fabrics and antique furniture. The house is very much a part of the landscape with many open areas, which lead directly into the garden. Part of the house faces the Atlantic coast and so is always cool and breezy.

The villa consists of three wings built around a central courtyard with covered walkways and coral rendered arches connecting each area.

First floor: 1 double bedroom and a second double bedroom, with either 2 twin beds or a double bed. Both rooms have ensuite bathrooms, both with shower/tubs. Casual sitting / games area with Cablevision TV. There are two spacious sun decks affording spectacular views and a wonderful place to appreciate the tropical sky at night.

Downstairs: guest wing with a double bed and en suite bathroom (a tub) and a private outside area, master bedroom with king-size bed and en suite bathroom (a shower with glass windows). All four bedrooms have ceiling fans with two also having air conditioning.

The large sitting room has louvered French doors leading to the courtyard and the tropical gardens, and glazed quadruple doors opening out onto an airy covered verandah. The formal, open dining room can seat up to 12 people.

The full size swimming pool is situated on a lower level and is surrounded by rocks, trees and palms, offering privacy and shade from the sun. A small gazebo is situated near to the pool.

The house is situated in four acres of grounds, with the area around the house fully landscaped with lawns and tropical flowers.

Resident staff quarters are situated below the fully equipped kitchen.

Household pets – two cats
