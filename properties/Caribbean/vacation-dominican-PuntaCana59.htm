Punta Cana 59, Punta Cana, Dominican Republic Vacation Rental

 

Majestic evergreen surroundings, a never-ending breeze stroking the palm trees dancing softly and with the evening calm enjoying the silence in a privileged and secluded spot, yet next to a bursting day and nightlife that celebrates with joy each moment of the year. Welcome to the perfect recently built place that gathers taste, design, space, and color across all it commonplaces, galleries, bedrooms, and foyer: Punta Cana 59. Perched in a distinguished location inside the worldly acclaimed Punta Cana Resort, this subtle masterpiece salutes you right from the main entrance after walking through a magnificent coralina promenade and a water mirror. Once the doors open, the main foyer with all it´s governing white empowers an ambiance of smooth lines and a sober decor making you feel as you were inside an artwork.

The outlook inside wants you to go to all places at the same time. The savvily chosen chandelier in the dining room sheds a candid light, enhancing each piece in the place. The good smells will lure you into the fully equipped kitchen, with all the pantry and gadgets you need to make a perfect dinner or meal.

Stepping into the outdoor area will welcome you with the functional palapa with an alfresco dining and BBQ area next to the infinity pool and sun lounges. But a good vacation is perfect when you have features for scattering at the house, hence on the second floor, you will find a pool table and a foosball table to challenge your friends or family. Not into this kind of amusement? Step into the home theater to watch your favorite movie with the incredible Sonos sound system in the main house and all bedrooms.

Once the long and exciting day is over find your bedroom. In the main house, go upstairs to get to the master bedroom with a King bed, and an adjoining bedroom with a bunk bed and a pullout bed only for children, up to three (3 twins). The second bedroom has a King bed and an adjoining bedroom with a bunk bed and a pullout bed also only for 3 children max. (3 twins). The third bedroom has 2 queen beds. On the ground floor is the fourth bedroom with a king bed. Next is the guest house a fifth bedroom with a King Bed, a sixth bedroom with 2 queen beds and a seventh bedroom with a sofabed. All bedrooms have a walk-in closet and an ensuite bathroom.

This beautiful villa is located in the exclusive gated community of Punta Cana Resort & Club, a 26-square mile paradise that is blessed with a temperate year-round climate, light rainfall, and soft breezes. It's colorful coral reefs, calm crystalline waters and white-sand beaches are breathtaking. The Resort provides a matchless experience for all who visit this tropical paradise, and its tropically chic lifestyle is ideal for those who appreciate high standards of quality, comfort and aesthetics. As guests of the villa, you have access to all of the resort amenities and activities.
