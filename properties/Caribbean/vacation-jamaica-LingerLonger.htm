Linger Longer, Montego Bay, Jamaica Vacation Rental

 

Linger Longer is a lovely three-bedroom villa with a warm Caribbean charm and wonderful views of the 13th hole of Tryall Club’s golf course and the sea.

With a 35 feet swimming pool, beautiful surrounding gardens and its wide roomy veranda overlooking the beautiful fairways, Linger Longer is a great fit for families or as a couple’s getaway. The added benefit of a completely level floor plan also makes this villa ideal for guests with some mobility challenges. The villa sits on the plains along Mahogany Drive, overlooking the 13th hole of the magnificent Tryall Golf fairway. Built in a modern colonial bungalow style, Linger Longer has a distinctive courtyard with swimming pool and it is just a few minutes golf cart ride from the Club’s facilities, private beach and golf course.

The master bedroom has a king-sized bed and a spacious en suite bathroom with a state of the art multi -jet walk-in shower and a Jacuzzi tub. The guest bedrooms in the east wing are fitted with twin beds which may be arranged as kings if needed. Each has an en suite bathroom with walk-in shower. One bedroom opens onto the spacious verandah.

The open living spaces are a celebration of Caribbean style with warm parquet flooring and smart, child-friendly furnishings. The indoor dining room comfortably seats 8 guests and for al-fresco dining there is a spacious terrace.

The villa staff comprises a cook, housekeeper, laundress and gardener
