Russian Amethyst, Shoal Bay West, Anguilla, Caribbean Vacation Rental

 

While relaxing in any part of The Russian Amethyst you will feel comforted by the interior design that enhances the visual excitement of the furnishings and artwork as it seamlessly complements the magnificent architecture of the structure.

The Russian Amethyst is handicap accessible and has five bedrooms for up to ten guests. The villa is fully air-conditioned but also has ceiling fans for those who prefer the natural breezes of Anguilla.

The interiors of Altamer have been designed by June Goldfinger within the dramatic spaces created by her husband, internationally renowned architect Myron Goldfinger. June has a deep understanding and love for his bold use of geometry, which is reflected in her interiors. Her touch is distinguished by her commitment to the notion of excellence and uniqueness, which will be seen in The Russian Amethyst's interiors. Altamer guests will find that the furnishings are of the highest quality of which many have been specially commissioned for The Russian Amethyst.

The Living Room is truly spectacular with 21' high ceiling. Relax while looking out to the Caribbean Sea and the swimming pool. The indoor furniture has been designed by June Goldfinger in geometric shaped rattan that has jeweled colored raw silk cushions that you can just melt into your comfort zone.

The magnificent creation of glass and light are combined in the rare Artistic Scavo Lights hand made by a Master Artisan from Murano, Italy. When the lights are switched on, the artwork comes alive in beautiful colors. Ten of the lights grace the walls in scenes of sea life.

An elevator is available from the 1st through the 3rd floors that will accommodate wheelchairs but also for those who find stairs difficult or for guests tired from all the activities of the day.

Your entertainment center is for watching movies, shooting a game of billiards, a game of ping-pong or air-hockey, playing games and sipping drinks. Custom built pool table with teakwood and stainless steel finishes. Flat Screen TV with surround sound stereo (5 speakers), DVD player, VCR, cable programming and movie library with over 300 movies. Relax and enjoy the stereo system throughout the villa on 23 speakers throughout 7 regions with a CD library of over 500 titles. You can listen to Island Music or your favorite song in the Game Room while your travel companion is enjoying jazz at the poolside. Use special LCD remote panels that can operate music and entertainment center from anywhere on the property controlling different music for each of the 7 zones.

Sit back at the wet bar adorned stainless steel and marble finishes. The bar area has full views of St. Martin and the Caribbean Sea. Card and game table (card and games provided including poker chips, chess and favorite board games) Book library of over 200 titles.

The Skywalk is accessed from the second floor to allow you to lounge, bird watch or catch the sunrise on the sea's edge.

The Master Bathroom is decorated with all the finest amenities. You are surrounded by carefully selected Travertine marble placed in this architecturally exciting bathroom. As you immerse yourself in the Jacuzzi for two you can watch television, listen to music, enjoy the walk in Shower also designed to accommodate a couple. The skylights create natural lighting and stargazing from the Jacuzzi.

The Master Suite also has an attached fourth floor balcony that includes a private bar area, office or a optional crib for babies . The complete wet bar has marble finishes and offers exceptional views of St. Martin and the beach area. The office is complete with a personal computer including Internet access, printer and copier. In villa fax machine to send and receive confidential faxes. A cordless telephone is provided to roam villa. The office can alternatively be set up in one of the guest room locations if the guests are not using all rooms.

All guest rooms have full views of the Caribbean Sea and their own marble bathroom with Jacuzzi. Each room has its own telephone and television with VCR. Children's safety bars can be added to windows as requested. Guest rooms on the third floor feature spectacular high angled ceilings with skylights
