Oceanfront Caleton Villa, Cap Cana, Punta Cana, Dominican Republic Vacation Rental


This Luxury Villa in Cap Cana, Punta Cana, Dominican Republic, has its own pool and sits directly on the ocean. You are 100 yds from your own private beach and it is adjacent to the Caleton Beach Club which includes its own spa, bar, restaurant and exotic 100 meter kidney-shaped pool. The villa is staffed with a butler/chef, maid and landscaping team – all included in the price. The villa has an expanded patio and gazebo and sits between the 16th and 17th hole of the Punta Espada Golf Club -- the Caribbean and Mexico's #1 golf course as voted by Golfweek for the last two years. The course has hosted 3 PGA golf tournaments; a number of celebrities have stayed at the villa including: Denzel Washington, Matt Lauer, Jennifier Lopez, David Ortiz and Fred Couples.

The resort is filled with many wonderful restaurants and sports a world-class marina. But if eating out is not your thing, just give the butler your eating preference and the chef will prepare your exact multiple-course meal to be enjoyed in the villa’s intimate setting while listening to the crashing waves. Fishing, golfing, tennis, cycling or running are fun things to do at the villa. There is also Escape 2 Cap Cana which includes zip lines, dune buggies and a paintball gallery.

Cap Cana is a 30,000-acre development in the Dominican Republic that overlooks three miles of fine white, powder-like sandy beaches. The resort is set in a series of coral limestone bluffs from the coast to 200 feet above sea level which gives the resort magnificent views of the Caribbean Sea.

Cap Cana is located on the eastern most part of the country, perfectly communicated by the Punta Cana International Airport, which is only 10 minutes away and has direct flight services from the USA, Latin America and the most important European cities.

Cap Cana includes two Jack Nicklaus Signature Golf Courses, the biggest marina in the Caribbean with over 1,000 slips accommodating yachts of up to 250 foot, elegant boutiques and restaurants, white sand, clear blue beaches, world-class spas, swimming pools and tennis courts, scuba-diving, fishing, polo and riding fields.

Come and enjoy all the facilities that make Cap Cana the world's next great destination.
