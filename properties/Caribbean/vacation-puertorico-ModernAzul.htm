Modern Azul, Humacao, Puerto Rico Vacation Rental

 

Rising majestically above endless oceans, the Modern Azul resort home is a cornucopia of privacy that harmoniously celebrates the stunning scenic beauty of Palmas del Mar with seamless indoor/outdoor living and an extensive use of glass for endless views. The bold architectural of this extraordinary home creates a most mesmerizing resort experience mere steps to a private beach. Dramatic staircases, voluminous living spaces and an extravagant indoor/outdoor pool flow into one another creates generous terraces and patios, totaling four outdoor lounge areas, ideally accentuated with private gardens and unforgettable scenery.

Entertain in grand style in the living and family rooms graced by soaring ceilings and generous windows that open to the invigorating salty sea air. The top-of-the-line chef's kitchen is clad with premium finishes and fixtures including commercial-grade stainless appliances, large center island and high volume ceilings, accompanied by elegant formal dining, aesthetically lit with chandelier and overlooking the sapphire waters of the ocean. A moving glass wall system open up the home to even more light and air.

Each bedroom suite is an exquisite work of art with papering amenities and beamed vaulted ceilings, but it is the master suite that truly exhibits the most remarkable character. At the end of the day, retire to the decadent master; a showpiece of luxurious proportions with private terrace and separate entrance. A pièce de résistance to this peaceful retreat, the ensuite is streamlined for pampering with Jacuzzi tub, His & Her sinks, steam shower and private outdoor shower.

The extensive lower level expands your living with such features as full bath, indoor/outdoor bar, ice machine and foosball table.

It is outdoors where ultimate relaxation occurs. Bask in the sunlight on the generous pool deck or enjoy the views from several shaded alfresco dining and lounge areas, fully furnished and awaiting your every pleasure. A cascading waterfall leads out to the tranquil waters of the indoor/outdoor pool, where rippling, crystal clear waters beckon you to enjoy their cool, refreshing depths. Abundantly landscaped and boasting extravagant tropical expanses, this private oasis indulges the senses in every way. Experience your luxury vacation at Modern Azul and feel the stress of your busy life melt away to pure bliss.

This property is professionally managed by Surfside Palmas Resorts. Our office is open seven days a week and available at all times for late check-ins, repair issues and assistance with transportation, car/golf cart rentals, excursions, in home chefs, and catering.

If you are looking for the inside info on the best attractions and leisure activities, the best restaurants, nightlife and shopping venues, the best of everything – you have come to the right place. Our staff is well prepared to provide you with personalized recommendations and service to ensure a holiday that matches your needs and dreams.

The Resort Community of Palmas Del Mar resides on the east coast of the Caribbean Island of Puerto Rico. The Resort has two golf courses, the largest tennis center in the Caribbean, a Wyndham Hotel and Casino, 20 restaurants, equestrian center, marina and yacht club and a 5-acre beach club complex all set on 2750 acres with 3 miles of coastline with strict access control and 24 hour security.
