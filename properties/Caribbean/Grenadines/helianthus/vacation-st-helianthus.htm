Helianthus, Mount Pleasant, Bequia, St Vincent and the Grenadines


As the ex Prime Minister's home, Helianthus enjoys a unique privacy and position overlooking the sunflower plantations after which it has been named. Nestled luxuriously in 9 acres of mature cedars, mahogany & fruit trees and located at Mount Pleasant, this fabulous Bequia vacation base boasts breathtaking scenery, with an uninterrupted view of the Caribbean Sea. The seclusion of this villa offers simply the sounds of the tropical breezes blowing through the trees, the local birds in the established gardens that surround this stunning classical villa and the distant sounds of the surf below. Helianthus is just a 10 minute drive away from two of Bequia's most beautiful beaches, Princess Margaret Beach and Lower Bay Beach. You can take a dip, top up your tan or make the most of the great snorkeling spots on Lower Bay.

Designed by the renowned Italian architect Luigi Vietti of Costa Smeralda fame, its interior was decorated by French and Italian designers in a Caribbean ambience. Helianthus is actually the Latin term for sunflower. A lovely gazebo nestled amidst the tropical fruit trees in the garden offers and ideal place to get away and read or just appreciate the peace and quiet.

This villa has an enviable infinity pool, with amazing views of the Southern Grenadines. From Helianthus you can look out at Mustique, Balliceaux, and Battowia. Beside the pool, there is a central flower garden, courtyard and wide terraces cooled by the sea breezes and offering a unique vantage point for you and your guests to enjoy breathtaking views over the Sunflower plantations and beaches below. There are sumptuous sun loungers available for you to stretch out and enjoy the tropical sun whilst the large veranda is spacious and cool, and from here you can sit and take in the ex-Prime Ministers favourite views of the island. There are benches that look out onto the sea, and there is also a lovely outside dining area.

The living room is cool and beautifully furnished by the Italian and French interior designers who managed to mix their continental style with a Caribbean finish. There is satellite tv and video, dvd, internet, telephone and fax providing all the modern amenities connecting you to the rest of the world whilst this beautiful villa preserves your privacy and escape. The expansive luxurious white sofas stretch around the living room for your total relaxation whilst the tall vaulted ceilings maintain a cool and serene atmosphere.

Under construction currently is an air conditioned media suite featuring a Bose sound system and comfortable relaxed seating, an ideal environment for guests to chill out during the occasional tropical downpour.

The real benefit of this villa is the excellent service the staff provide as part of your holiday with a team of house staff including a cook, maid, launderess, driver and gardeners. There is a lovely kitchen area where the cook prepares great meals which leads onto the stunning dining area where you can enjoy the local and continental dishes prepared. Eating al fresco is always an option with the spacious terraces overlooking the ocean and gardens.

This wonderful Bequia villa rental has 4 large bedrooms each with its own spacious and stylish bathroom. The master bedroom, along with 2 of the other rooms, has a luxurious king size bed, and the fourth bedroom has a queen size bed. The large light bedrooms decorated in classical colonial style have high vaulted ceilings with cooling fans, beautiful spacious beds and stunning views. 2 of the bedrooms have air conditioning.

Airport pickup and use of a car or jeep are included in the rental. A team of well trained and welcoming staff await your arrival ready to prepare all your meals and assist generally during your stay. Transfers to other islands can be arranged by the Grenadine Team as well as sailing or any other water sports you might like to try. Whilst the villa is secluded and is surrounded by established gardens and beautiful beaches the town of Port Elizabeth is not far away offering bars, shops and restaurants.
