Amitabah, Bequia, Grenadines Vacation Rental

 

Perfect blue sea steps from this villa on the calm west coast. Perched a short skip above the sand of Lower Bay in a private location this is perhaps the finest villa in Bequia. It is the ideal seaside holiday house, designed around expansive out door living spaces, glamorous interiors and spa like bathrooms. Complete with a multitude of family friendly games like snooker and tennis, Amitabah is very much like a private hotel.

A 180 degree panoramic west facing balcony gives birds eye views of the west coast. Newly built, the villa is superbly fitted with stunning furniture and high spec technology. The sunset and green flash views are magnificent from all rooms. At Amitabah you can truly relax as your every need will be looked after by the charming and helpful staff. Ideal for couples and families you will find it hard to leave the quiet, relaxing atmosphere of Amitabah but you if you want to venture further afield the beach is just below and a little farther along the road you will find another idyllic beach named aptly after Princess Margaret.

This is area of Bequia is arguably one of the best and most private locations in the Grenadines The villa has a lovely, large dining room, with huge doors that allow you to look out at the property's views as you dine. This room, in keeping with the rest of Amitabah House, is fabulously furnished with a huge wooden table and comfortable chairs. This is an ideal place to enjoy your meals, especially with the cool sea breeze traveling in from outside. Full service is available for breakfast & lunch preparation and maid service daily. A full pre-provisioning service is available at an additional cost

Amitabah House has 6 bedrooms, all with en suite bathrooms that all have a shower and WC and hot water. All bedrooms also have AC and lovely, comfortable beds.The master bedroom is slightly different - the en suite bathroom has a bath tub, and the added luxury of a flat screen TV with cable and DVD player

Poolside bar, 50 ft swimming pool, Baby football, Snooker table, Tennis court, Basketball, Keegans Bar and Restaurant with large outdoor film screen, Mangos restaurant and De Reef bar all close by, Wireless Internet, Gas BBQ, Dvd Player, Cable TV, ipod dock, Rowing machine, Cross trainer, weight machine, yoga mats, work out balls, three mountain bikes.

Aside from all the sporting and swimming activities at the villa and the beach, if you like hiking there are trails around the island which you can take bonafide forests, waterfalls and nature at its best. Snorkelling and Scuba diving are excellent here and there are several dive sites close to the villa and in all around 12 dive sites around Bequia, 64 in the whole Grenadines chain. Children will enjoy visiting the Turtle sanctuary, kayaking, learning to sail, fishing, seeking out iguanas, playing football, tennis, cricket and volley ball on the beach, drinking from fresh coconuts and having local fish and chips, jerk chicken or conch souse at the beach. Bequia is recognised widely in the press as an ideal family holiday destination

Fully Equipped staff can serve breakfast, lunch and dinner at an additional cost. Bar service. Restaurants on the island are good and varied (Italian, Swedish, French, Caribbean, American)
