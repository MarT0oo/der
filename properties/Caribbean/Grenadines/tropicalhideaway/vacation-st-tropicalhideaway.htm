Tropical Hideaway ,Bequia, Grenadines.


Raising the quality of villas in the island of Bequia - this is a stunning new property built to high quality with really enchanting character and layout.

Very flexible spaces ideal for mixed family groups with very comfortable barefoot luxury standards throughout. The property is unique and built with thought and care using local crafts and skills of wood & stone. The property has wonderful finishing touches such as the huge bath caved from a solid piece of stone and spiral curved interior walls.

stunning location with breathtaking views & infinity pool
Tropical Hideaway boasts perhaps the largest infinity pool on Bequia which in turn offers some of the best panoramic views over the entire Admiralty Bay.

58ft Infinity edge swimming pool
Cooling off at this property there is a superbly well designed pool including a pool bar and bar stools to enjoy a cocktail or refreshing beer...

Perfect property for weddings, anniversary and group events on Bequia
This is a wonderful villa for an event such as a wedding or anniversary reunion. The spaces are dramatic with the striking back drop of the ocean view behind and personal with the creative spaces and beautiful tropical landscaping and established planted beds.

The Infinity Bedroom with huge natural stone bath tub is certainly worth a mention - hand carved by tribesmen from a massive boulder in Indonesia and carefully transported to Bequia. The tub weighs in at an impressive 3 tonnes!

Please Note: all water on the island is collected rainwater and at certain times of year the supply can be restricted - therefore the use of the large stone bath may be limited to one experience per couple per stay / week. If there is severe shortage it may not be possible to fill the tub.
Water is heated by solar energy so certain conditions may also make the bath impractical to use. Staff will do their best to make sure the experience can be enjoyed at least once during your stay. (This obviously does not effect your use of the shower.
