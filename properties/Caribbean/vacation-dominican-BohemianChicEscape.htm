Bohemian Chic Escape, Casa de Campo, Dominican Republic Vacation Rental

 

A Tropical Chic Retreat situated on the hills of Casa de Campo overlooking the first hole of the Dye Fore Golf Course. This gorgeous six bedroom villa offers a large cathedral ceilings providing open spaces throughout the property. This beautiful villa hosts a large living area and a formal dining room and an outdoor living and casual dining areas to entertain and for those special outdoor meals.

Four bedrooms are located on the top floor where you will find two master bedrooms featuring an outside terrace with view of the golf course, one of them feature a private Jacuzzi, and two guest bedrooms. The remaining two bedroom are located below where you will find a guest bedroom, living area and the children bedroom. The large backyard is perfect for families and small children to play and enjoy the day.

Casa de Campo is located on the south eastern shore of the Dominican Republic, next to the town of La Romana and just 70 miles from the capital city of Santo Domingo, the oldest city in the new world. Casa de Campo is a luxurious 7,000 acre ocean front community offering world class golf courses which include the famous Teeth of the Dog Golf Course, Dye Fore and The Links Golf Course plus polo fields, skeet shooting centre, tennis centre, equestrian centre, water sports, spa treatment centre, fitness centre, business centre, private beach, a la carte restaurants, Altos de Chavon (artist village) and Casa de Campo Marina, all of which will please almost any visitor.
