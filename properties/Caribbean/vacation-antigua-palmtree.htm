Palm Tree Villa
7 Bedrooms | 7 Baths | Pool | Jacuzzi
Gallery Bay Heights, Antigua
From $22,000 per Week

This Luxury Villa majestically dominates the hill and it occupies 13,000 sq. ft of living/entertaining space, located at Galley Bay Heights and enjoys 360 degrees views. As you drive towards the villa you can’t help but to notice the architectural natural stone wall bordering the property. On the inside of this work art of beauty is a flamboyant tropical Caribbean garden flourishing with Bougainvilleas, Palm Trees and other indigenous flowers. On the path to the main building you will see on the left a Wimbledon style tennis court, equipped with tennis rackets and balls.

The villa has two levels. On the higher level you find the living room with TV lounge and dining area, the kitchen, the Master Bedroom and 4 bedrooms. On the lower level you find 2 bedrooms, the laundry room and the gym.

The Villa with its huge glass sliding door, vaulted ceilings, bay windows and an open floor plan gives a feeling of openness and  brighten the home with natural sunlight, if you take just a minute to turn around you would be amazed at  the most stunning views of the horizon and the bay below. The main entrance to the villa opens up to the living room that is tastefully furnished with the most comfortable sofa set, a square coffee table, accent furniture and abstract art paintings on the walls, all these come together to accentuate the area.

With a flowing motion you will stumble upon the formal dining area, on one side of this area is an oversize rectangular dining table with chairs.

A family/entertainment room is comfortable furnished with home style theatre furniture, a Plasma HDTV and surround sound entertainment system. The villa has a play-station that allows three players with a vast choice of games. There are also a lot of books, DVDs and table games for more entertainment.

Although the kitchen is not open up to the living and dining area it is incredibly spacious and it sits on over 600 ft² Stunning views of the garden through the large windows in the kitchen add charm to the meals you are preparing. In the centre of the kitchen is an island with a vegetable sink, four island’s chairs are also place on one side of the island. The kitchen is fully furnished with stainless steel professional grade appliances, the cabinets were custom built and topped with granite and a wine rack is built in near the refrigerator. 

The villa has 7 beautiful bedrooms and can comfortably accommodate 14 guests.  All the bedrooms have an iPod docking station. The Master Bedroom is fitted with lovely furniture: a king-sized bed, a walk-in closet and the room also has a relaxing area with Plasma television and couch. The Master Bathroom has a spacious walk-in shower offering stunning Caribbean Sea Views, an L-Shaped vanity with double sink and a make-up area with seating. Next to the Master Bedroom there are two double beds bedrooms with wardrobes, one with king-sized bed and the other with two single beds, ideal for the children. Both bedrooms share a veranda and a bathroom with a walk-in shower and bath tub. On the eastern side there are two en-suite bedrooms with king-sized bed, walk in closet, walk-in rain shower and balcony. 

The lower level of the Villa has two en-suite bedrooms, walk in closet and walk in rain shower; the laundry room fitted with both washing and dryer machines.

The outdoor Jacuzzi and infinity pool are flanked by a huge tiled deck ideal for sunbathing in the day and relaxing in the night. The outside dining area, fitted with table and chairs covered by a roof, is the best place to lunch and dinner with your friends and family.

For the sport lovers Palm Tree Villa offers a fully equipped gym with stationary bicycle, weights, treadmill, Yoga-math with private bathroom at the lower lever of the villa and an open space tennis court with lights.
