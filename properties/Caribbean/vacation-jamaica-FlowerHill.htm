Flower Hill, Montego Bay, Jamaica Vacation Rental

 

Welcome to Flower Hill, one of Montego Bay’s most luxurious vacation home rentals. The Flower Hill villa sits on four and a half expansive acres of fragrant flower gardens and lush, verdant landscapes. The spectacular property measures an abundant 20,000 square feet, just perfect for accommodating up to six (6) couples, or groups up to seventeen (17) people. Located just a few minutes from Montego Bay Airport (MBJ), Flower Hill makes a memorable impression from the moment you arrive. Upon entering our gates, you’ll be greeted by a chorus line of stately palm trees whose immense fronds sway effortlessly in the tropical breezes.

When you’re not relaxing end enjoying the amenities of Flower Hill, you’ll be just a few minute’s drive to the some of the major highlights of Jamaica’s second largest city, Montego Bay (known to the locals as “Mobay”). Fishing, diving, snorkeling, horseback riding, golf, guided plantation tours, shopping, nightlife, and zip-lining, are just a few of the many attractions that you will discover in and around this vibrant Caribbean hotspot.

There’s nothing like sipping a fruity afternoon cocktail or smoothie while inhaling the fresh tropical air at the Flower Hill estate. Relax in a chaise lounge or swing in a hammock, right alongside our large infinity pool (30” x 55”).

After you’ve finished relaxing by the pool, why not take a stroll around the scenic Flower Hill property, enjoying its enchanting manicured lawns and soaking in the picturesque views of the Caribbean? After all, you have nothing but time!

Want some more activity? Head over to our outdoor gaming and event area to have some more “fun under the sun.” Need a little privacy? No worries, Flower Hill’s vast 10-acre property has plenty of secluded seating areas strategically hidden within our beautifully groomed orchards and flower gardens.

Soak up the sun, gaze up at the blue skies, and enjoy the stunning backdrop of Flower Hill’s oversized infinity pool. The 30’ x 55’ pool comes equipped with a therapeutic jetted hot tub, a still-water cooling tub, and a cascading waterfall that empties into a half-circle splash pool. Snap a few photos of the arcing fountain jets, and burn off some calories by swimming laps or using our swim jet.

Our professionally trained onsite staff will do whatever they can to make your stay comfortable and unforgettable. You’ll be pampered and spoiled by two housekeepers, two butlers, a laundress, and a private chef who will prepare scrumptious meals to your specifications.

You’ll never be bored at Flower Hill Montego Bay! We have a wide range of fun and engaging onsite amenities and activities that will keep you occupied all year round.

If you’re a fitness-minded guest, you’ll enjoy staying in shape at our fully equipped gym, complete with cardiovascular machines, weight stations, fitness trainers and masseuses (upon request), and a relaxing and therapeutic cedar-lined steam room.

Looking for something to do during an unexpected rain shower? Head on over to the Serengeti games room where you can snuggle in and catch a movie, practice your ping-pong skills, have a shuffleboard tournament or become a “pinball wizard.” Once the sun comes back out, it’s time to enjoy a volleyball or soccer game or play a little croquet!

After dining on sumptuous evening meals, you’ll have plenty of ways to create memorable nights at one of Flower Hill’s two exclusive bars. Your visit to Montego Bay won’t be complete until you sample some of Jamaica’s famous tropical rum cocktails, mixed and poured right in front of your eyes.

Flower Hill is pleased to offer a multitude of on-site amenities and recreational activities that will keep you and your housemates busy and entertained in any season.

Some of our indoor options include a relaxing spa, a fully equipped gym, a well-stocked game room, flat-screen TVs throughout the property, (including a 100” projection TV), high-speed wireless internet, state-of-the-art stereo system, and much more.

There is virtually no limit to the fun times that you and your fellow guests will have on Flower Hill’s breathtaking 4.5 acres of beautifully manicured grounds and gardens. If you’re feeling super-athletic, our lawns are quickly transformed into soccer fields and volleyball courts. If you just want to get the blood flowing a bit, then why not enjoy a friendly game of croquet?

We can say this with confidence – at Flower Hill, you’ll never be bored!
