Elysian Plain, Montego Bay, Jamaica Vacation Rental


Elysian Plain is a waterfront villa which has a wonderful majestic, tall palm tree which guards the entrance of this villa. The unique feature of this villa is its location which sits literally at the edge of the clear, blue Caribbean Sea. The views from here are spectacular. "Elysian Plain" is aptly named after the resting place of the Greek Gods.

The outdoor areas of this villa are spacious with new outdoor comfortable thick cushioned lounge chairs for sunning or reading your favorite book. All bedrooms are well appointed and very comfortable. They are furnished with lovely tropical mosquito nets which may be use for convenience or just left as part of the bedroom d√©cor.

The guest bedroom has its very own private and tranquil open courtyard. The Master bedroom is located in a separate cottage capturing the wonderful views of the Caribbean sea. The dining room has high beamed ceilings with large doors opening out to the pool area and this also brings in the cool tropical breezes carried over the Caribbean Sea.

You can choose whether to dine inside or on the pool terrace. This terrace gives views of the water edge swimming pool, grassy knoll and the spectacular ocean views.
