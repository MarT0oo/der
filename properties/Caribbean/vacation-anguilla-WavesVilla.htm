Waves Villa, Shoal Bay, Anguilla, Caribbean Vacation Rental

 

Waves Villa places you at the center of paradise with a breathtaking view of Shoal Bay East, one of Anguilla’s finest beaches. Pairing small town appeal with the ultimate natural beauty of pristine white sand, intense blue-green waters and soaring blue skies. This remote yet charming two miles of beach feels like a secret discovery .

Recently ranked as the #1 island in the Caribbean on Travel and Leisure’s World’s Best list, Anguilla is an “off the beaten path” destination with world class beaches. The island is a favorite among discerning travelers who cherish both natural beauty and private, luxurious accommodations.

With three bedroom suites, all with en-suite bathrooms, an oceanfront location, a gorgeous private pool and deck, and outdoor dining patio, Waves Villa is one with the natural surroundings, allowing guests to savor every sunset, beach walk and tropical cocktail. Clean modern lines, touches of warmth and large windows facing the sea make it the perfect place to unwind.

Fully equipped with amenities including a gas grill, gourmet kitchen, private pool, outdoor shower and hammock, Waves Villa offers a great beach escape vacation.

Direct beach access is less than a five minute drive to Shoal Bay where beach lovers can drink in the view and enjoy a fresh seafood at interesting local cafes and bars.

Make your Caribbean fantasy vacation come true at Waves Villa on the world-class island of Anguilla!
