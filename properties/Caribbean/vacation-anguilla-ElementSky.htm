Element Sky, Anguilla, British West Indies Vacation Rental


The iconic experience of the Caribbean is one that involves the white sands, the turquoise sea, and the brilliant blue sky. This is the essence of a stay at the Villa Element Sky, a luxury villa with private beachfront along Anguilla's Long Bay area.

Part of a group of luxurious holiday retreats, this five-bedroom property is literally filled from top to bottom with some of the finest amenities, services, and features anywhere in the region. Not only can you enjoy round the clock staff to provide for your needs, but you are surrounded by the loveliest architecture, d√©cor, and style as well. There is an open floor plan to the shared living spaces, and it relies on high ceilings, a pale palette, and excellent design, eliminating barriers to the outdoors. Large windows and arched doorways frame the lounge, living room, formal dining area, and enormous gourmet kitchen.

Stepping outside, visitors can enjoy time on the terrace where an al fresco dining gazebo, large infinity edge pool, Jacuzzi and several well-furnished lounging spots are found. Strolling down the terrace and through the landscaped gardens full of swaying palms, you then reach the beachfront.

The five bedrooms of this villa are as luxurious and feature-rich as the rest of the property, and each is home to a king sized bed treated with luxury linens, an en suite bathroom with a tub and shower, high-end toiletries, and many modern technologies that include WiFi, VoIP phones, and Internet access. This property also provides guests with use of a tennis court and fitness center, it comes with daily continental breakfast service, and there is even a full media room in the villa to ensure everyone has something fun to do at all times.

Whether traveling with a group of discerning friends or a large family, the Villa Element Sky is the perfect spot to just unplug and relax.
