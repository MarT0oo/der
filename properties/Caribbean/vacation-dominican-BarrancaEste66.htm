Barranca Este 66, Casa de Campo, Dominican Republic Vacation Rental

 

Barranca Este 66 located inside the amazing Casa de Campo resort community with award winning design. A stunning plantation-style home located in the exclusive Barranca Este neighborhood of Casa de Campo, overlooking La Romana Country Club golf course and the Caribbean sea. This beautiful eight-bedroom, fourteen thousand square feet villa, is perched overlooking much of the resort, boasting the most incredible hundred and eighty-degree ocean view. A view which can be enjoyed from every room in the house, from the entrance to the bedrooms, to the living areas, wherever you go the view follows you.

Designed by Carlos Lebinsky, famous for his work in Argentina and Uruguay, this villa creates the unique tropical home, so at one with nature that the colourful flowers of the bougainvillea lining the outside terrace are blown indoors on the breeze. The family room is off of the dining room and offers an informal dining and TV area. Children-friendly with trampoline and garden toys. The home's 8 bedrooms feature 6 King Bedrooms, one with Two Full beds and one with 2 twins. So you can arrange the entire group as you wish to make it the most amazing trip for you and your family.

Minitas Beach, Casa de Campo Marina and Altos de Chavan are all only a five-minute drive away, while the La Romana Country Club golf course is literally at your doorstep for early morning or late-afternoon walks. A true gem in the glittering jewel that is the Casa de Campo resort. Ask about all of the amazing tours inside Casa de Campo Resort, there is a daily fee per person for use of the club facilities, as well as a registration fee per guest. Please be advised prior to booking that this fee may exceed 400 per guests stay.
