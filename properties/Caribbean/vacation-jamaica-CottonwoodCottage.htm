Cottonwood Cottage, Bluefields Bay, Jamaica Vacation Rental

 

Our most nature-integrated villa, this home has two bedroom suites on four beachfront acres purchased from the Jamaican government. Dense tropical foliage has been fully-preserved, so guests enjoy full sun and also shaded seating under “tree umbrellas” on the lovely white sand Bluefields Beach. Snorkel and sea kayak in crystal-blue calm waters of the leeward South Coast before enjoying gourmet meals prepared according to your specifications up at The Treehouse or in a more intimate setting as shown above. The Treehouse, located below Cottonwood, is a bar, living, dining space overlooking the beach and sea with incredible views. This space is often used for the Monday cocktail party and Wednesday Jerk Chicken Luncheon offered to all guests. This luncheon also can be enjoyed in our new Jerk Chicken Grove situated next to the barbeque itself.

Cottonwood Cottage’s master bedroom offers expansive sea views with the stately tree that gives the villa its name in the foreground. With an authentic king-size four-poster canopy bed and other special pieces from the Moncure collection of Jamaican furniture, this bedroom suite has a private patio overlooking the sea and an exotic marble shower opening out to a tropical garden. The second bedroom suite has two twins that can be easily and fully joined. And the villa boasts an infinity-edge private pool looking out at the Caribbean with a lap lane, seats with whirlpool jets, and space for children to frolic.

Located south of Montego Bay and east of Negril, Bluefields Bay is the perfect escape from the commercialized tourist traps that plague far too much of Jamaica.

With its unspoiled natural beauty, Bluefields Bay has a calm and purity unlike the rest of Jamaica. Experience its unparalleled tranquility as you gaze at the scintillating Bay from your private exclusive villa. Now a Protected Fish Sanctuary, the sea is without activity — unlike the coastlines teeming with tourists and boats of most other holiday locations.
