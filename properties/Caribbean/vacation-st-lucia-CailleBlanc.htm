Caille Blanc, Cap Estate, St. Lucia Vacation Rental


A one-of-a-kind private villa in St. Lucia complete with a 65 foot infinity pool and waterfall facing the Caribbean Sea and a World Heritage site with the majestic Pitons.

This villa is situated on the Anse Chastanet road and is about one and half miles outside of Soufriere town with its drive in volcano and botanical gardens and a 5 minute drive from the pristine Anse Chastanet beach with its renowned reef and snorkeling.

Guests can soak in the amazing 65 foot infinity pool and enjoy the view of the Pitons and then they can gather together to dine on the spacious Pavilion courtyard or in the separate gourmet kitchen. Enjoy sumptuous meals daily, prepared by our skilled St. Lucian cook, Marilyn, well versed in local creole cuisine.

Our staff includes daily cleaning, cooking and security.Caille Blanc Villa is the creation of its owner, a world class New York designer, who selected the finest materials, antiques and furnishings from Afghanistan, India, China, Japan, and the United States. Local St. Lucia trees were forested for wall paneling and beautiful, hand-cut stone facades come from local quarries.

From the gated car park area, guests enter the Treetop Pavilion reception, the center point of the villa compound. It offers a shaded area for dining and relaxation beside the pool. There are five beautiful suites each with en suite bathrooms, plus an additional bedroom with ensuite bathroom.

Each bathroom boasts granite countertops and premium fixtures. All rooms have one or more ceiling fans and air conditioning.

POOL SUITE - This is a large, comfortable room facing the infinity pool and Pitons with a four-poster, King size bed, combined with living room, couch, club chairs and extra large bathroom. There is also a four poster day bed for your additional comfort.

TEMPLE SUITE - The Temple suite has hand-carved, antique palace doors imported from India, decorative stone-carving motifs, and hand-painted Indian metal tiles covering its vaulted ceiling. This Suite has two levels. The first floor of the Temple features a small gym and a separate bedroom with three four-poster full size beds, which is ideal for young people and a bathroom.

A stairway connects to the upstairs living room, bathroom and king size bedroom with a beautiful perspective of the Pitons. The Temple living room has an authentic Kiva fireplace reminiscent of those found in the ancient Pueblo village of Taos, New Mexico and decorative, antique, carved-wood bedroom entrance doors.

SUNSET SUITE - This suite is part of the original two-bedroom villa purchased by the owner twenty years ago. It is fully redesigned with the latest furnishings and bath fixtures. It connects to a separate family style living room/entertainment center and its own private patio facing the Pitons.
This is one of the most desirable locations to watch the sunset. It has a queen size, hand-carved mermaid bed by a well-known local artist/sculptor.

BOUGANVILLA SUITE - Across from the Sunset Suite.
The Bouganvilla Suite has a four-poster king size bed and a four poster day bed, with a private sheltered patio facing the Pitons. It also connects to the family style living room/entertainment center shared by the Sunset Suite. The Bouganvilla suite also features a four poster full size day bed.

GROTTO SUITE - This is the ownerowner’s private suite, located 18 ft beneath the pool level and constructed into the cliff side on a private stone patio with a cantilevered green wood deck. The Grotto comprises handmade, wood-paneled walls with a four-poster king size bed, an adjacent living room section with couch and chairs and a small kitchen and an ensuite bathroom. This suite has an individual Japanese soak tub outside the bedroom doors on the deck and the waterfall from the pool cascades into a small wading pool in the same area.
