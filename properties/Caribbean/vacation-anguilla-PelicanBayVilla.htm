Pelican Bay Villa, Pelican Bay, Anguilla, Caribbean Vacation Rental

 

Newly offered Pelican Bay Villa is a pristine waterfront vacation home on Anguilla, overlooking the sparkling Caribbean with breathtaking views of St. Martin.

The main house features three king bedrooms — two with sea views and one overlooking the garden. The poolside guest house has a king bedroom on the top floor and a ground-floor game room — complete with a pool table. Conveniently located a few minutes from the ferry port, this immaculate four-bedroom villa rental is a short walk to the sandy beach at Blowing Point. You can also experience and explore some of Anguilla’s world-famous beaches just a short drive away.

With a large open floor plan, guests can open the doors and let the sea breezes flow in for “no worries” relaxation and the ultimate island vacation experience. Newly remodeled white interiors, azure design accents and floor-to-ceiling windows echo the gorgeous seaside surroundings. Luxuriate by the pool, play outdoor games by the sea, or set up a beautiful outdoor dinner on the lanai. There are so many ways to savor your Caribbean getaway at Pelican Bay Villa!

Anguilla is recently ranked as the #1 Caribbean Island in “Travel and Leisure’s 2018 World’s Best Awards.” The island is an off-the-beaten-path destination known for stunning beaches, friendly locals and a wide offering of luxury villas and vacation homes.
