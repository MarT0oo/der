About Jamaica & Villa Rentals

Jamaica boasts some of the best beaches and friendly residents that you will ever find.  Not only is Jamaica considered a treat to visit but affordability is also a great part of why Jamaica stays on the map for vacations.  Most people love the idea of traveling to the Caribbean and finding that right mix of sun and fun, so why not make it a Jamaica Holiday this year.


When you visit Jamaica, the best places are Kingston for those who want to go back in time and see the way Jamaica started out, Montego Bay for it’s nightlife and culture and amazing strips, Negril for its family friendly atmosphere, beaches, and lovely skyline, Ocho Rios for the waterfalls which people love to see, Port Antonio which draws the rich and famous to its magical coasts, and the South Coast for the  local hotspots and great sunsets.


Some of the beaches include, Seven Mile Beach, Doctors Cave in Montego Bay, Aquasol, James Bond in Ocho Rios, Duns River Falls, Treasure Beach on the South Coast, Frenchman’s Cove in Port Antonio, and the Blue Lagoon.


No matter when you travel remember the climate changes in late May and we hit Rainy Season.  So, traveling between November and early May are great choices.  You can find some great deals during the summertime however, so check back often.  Those traveling internationally, we highly recommend you take out Traveler’s Insurance, as these are by owner vacation rentals.


Jamaica offers holiday homes from beachfront to Oceanside, these homes are great for families traveling together and wanting to explore somewhere different, there are great resorts and homes throughout Jamaica.
