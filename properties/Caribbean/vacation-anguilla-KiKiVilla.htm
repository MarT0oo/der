KiKi Villa, Anguilla, Caribbean Vacation Rental

 

Relaxed and comfortably designed, this cozy 4 bedroom new build is perfect for a large family or a group of friends. Situated on the hillside of Shoal Bay East. Kiki Villa offers a 180 degree view of the beautiful, turquoise blue sea. Just a 10 minute walk from the villa to Shoal Bay beach, with its perfectly white powdery sand and clear blue waters

The villa is spacious, open and airy, with high ceilings, offering both ceiling fans and air conditioning throughout. Designed with the comfort of our guests in mind and all that is required to keep you connected to the rest of the world.

The interior has an informal feel, furnished with both designer and locally made bespoke furniture. Fully equipped kitchen, with custom made solid Sapelle Mahogany fittings and tastefully tiled walls using carved local rock.

The exterior, all patio and window frames are trimmed with handpicked, cut and polished Anguillian rock. The exterior offers a pool, beach chairs, umbrellas, tables and chairs for outside dining and a gas barbeque grill.

Grounds have been newly landscaped featuring, Frangipani, Coconut trees, Cacti, Desert Rose, Ginger Thomas to name a few, fenced and with a frontal wall and electrically operated gate with video intercom system to ensure your privacy.
