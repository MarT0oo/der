Zion Hill, Montego Bay, Jamaica Vacation Rental


From its splendid location 300 ft above sea level, this luxurious 7 bedroom villa offers bewitching, unobstructed views of the ocean, its own private spa and Turkish bath, a putting green and a cinema.

Zion Hill comprises 14,000 square feet of stylish accommodation amidst six acres of immaculate garden on the summit of Ocean View Close. It is surrounded by scores of fruit trees and over 29 species of palm tree. The accommodation is spread between three distinct buildings one built on two levels and the other two a single level. Tastefully and comfortably furnished, the interiors are complemented with a collection of artwork from the ownerâ€™s travels. There are three bedrooms in the main house including the first master bedroom which has a king sized bed and French doors that open to a private ocean-facing terrace. The second bedroom also has a king sized bed and opens to views of the ocean. The third bedroom is designed for children with a queen size bed as well as bunk beds.

Two bedrooms on the lower floor of the second building have one queen bed and two full size beds respectively with private balconies that face the forests and sea.

The remaining bedrooms comprise two additional master suites separated by a sitting room which are in a fully self-contained cottage that is peacefully located at the top of the property. Each room opens to a private, beautifully furnished patio with spectacular views of the valleys and the ocean.

All bedrooms feature luxury private en suites bathrooms with walk-in showers and Jacuzzi tubs.

Cut stone stairs lead down through the manicured lawns to the infinity pool and teak sun deck and a collection of bicycles enable guests to explore the gorgeous gardens and discover the putting green before relaxing in the outdoor sauna and steam room. Children have their own playground and there is a well-equipped games room and cinema with a 153 inch screen, popcorn machine and comfortable recline and sprawl seating for 16. Those who enjoy keeping fit will gravitate to the gym and the outside yoga area is a perfect way to start each day. Needless to say, incomparable views are all around.

Meals can be enjoyed in the elegant dining room or outside in the gazebo which is also a splendid place to sit and watch the sun set over the distant horizon.

An experienced team includes an internationally trained Chef, Butler, Housekeepers and team of gardeners.
