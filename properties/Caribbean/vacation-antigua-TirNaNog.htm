Tir Na Nog, Jumby Bay, Antigua, Caribbean Vacation Rental

 

Situated on the highest point of the island, Tir Na Nog at Harbour Heights offers sweeping panoramic views of the Caribbean Sea from every room with soothing Trade Wind breezes. The private, modern hillside oasis offers 6 bedrooms amongst 12,000 square feet of exceptional living space set in 5 acres of enchanting tropical gardens. The interiors are elegant and relaxed, featuring custom made sustainable teak and acacia hardwood furnishings handcrafted in Thailand in a palette of neutrals with vibrant accents in shades of blue ranging from aqua to navy with hints of soothing sea-blue. The estate features expansive indoor and outdoor living and dining areas, a modern kitchen and study comprise the heart of this sophisticated estate.

The king-bedded master suite features an inviting terrace overlooking the pool, sea and beautifully landscaped gardens with a luxurious bathroom and a refreshing outdoor shower. Located in a detached cottage, a second king-bedded master suite features a quiet terrace overlooking the gardens to the sea and a spacious bathroom with an outdoor courtyard shower. Two additional terraced cottages offer two bedrooms each, each with ensuite baths, one with a king bed and one with a set of double beds.

Above the expansive swimming pool, a grand pagoda provides a magical place to relax or enjoy a deliciously healthy, fully customizable meal created by your private chef and accompanied by gorgeous views of the Caribbean Sea. A sunset yoga terrace presents yet another opportunity to soak in the serene atmosphere while meditating on the views. Stairs descend from the pool area to a footpath which winds its way to the white sands of Harbour Beach.

From its central location, Tir Na Nog also affords effortless access to the island’s other beaches and the resort’s amenities.
