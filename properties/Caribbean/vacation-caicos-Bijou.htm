Bijou, North Shore, Providenciales, Turks and Caicos Vacation Rental

 

Bijou, a Sophisticated Townhome in Turtle Cove, Providenciales, Bijou is a beautifully private two levels property that is a few minutes walk from famed Grace Bay beach and a short stroll from five of Provo’s most popular dining spots. It is a perfect vacation home for honeymooners, two couples, or a small family.

The main level has a large entrance hall and a two-piece bath, an outstanding granite kitchen with stainless appliances and a large breakfast bar, a generous dining room and a contiguous large screened porch overlooking your very own private garden, which is perfect for lounging or for grilling on your own BBQ

Upstairs you will find two full bedrooms with baths ensuite. The east suite features a king bed, flat screen TV, a balcony, double closets and a granite bathroom. It also has a television and DVD player. The west suite has twin beds, double closets and a granite bathroom as well.

From the garden, follow a charming path through the lush landscaping to a gorgeous communal pool for the complex complete with a cabana overlooking the canal. Perfect spot for cocktails or an al fresco lunch

Bijou is ideally located for those who may not want to rent a car. One can walk to the beach (five minutes), to a large grocery store (20 minutes), or to several restaurants or coffee shops a few minutes walk away. Bicycles are also available for rent at Bijou Cyclery. They will deliver
