Aqua Sunset, Grace Bay, Providenciales, Turks and Caicos Vacation Rental

 

Aqua Sunset is proud to introduce The Residences, their first-ever stand-alone residential villa enclave in Turks and Caicos. Dubbed a “micro-resort,” The Residences offer all the handmade comforts of a private home with the management and luxury services of a five-star resort. Paired with the hospitality group’s personal concierge service and unrivaled attention to detail, Aqua Sunset Beach exemplifies the luxurious lifestyle and experience in the Caribbean.

The Residences is comprised of three luxury homes and combines sophisticated, elegant design with the highest level of service and exclusive amenities. Each of the custom-designed homes is located on a 95-foot-wide freehold parcel of private beachfront and offers over 6,000 square feet of indoor and outdoor space, four bedrooms, four and a half bathrooms, and 2,600 square feet of external area with private deck surrounding an infinity pool and outdoor shower. The two oversized master bedrooms provide oceanfront views, while the master bathrooms boast freestanding tubs, glass showers, and double vanities. Expansive living and dining areas offer 14’ ceilings, solid wood kitchens with appliances from Sub-Zero and Wolf, and the highest quality of imported fixtures and finishing. A guest cottage offers a fifth bedroom, kitchenette, and bathroom.

The Residences are designed by Coast Architects based in the Turks & Caicos.

In addition to calling Grace Bay Beach home, guests are provided access to the resort property’s common areas and amenities, including an elegant owners’ library, a reception area where you can check in and access on-site management and concierge staff, and a fully equipped fitness center.

In-villa services include: spa treatments*, in-room chefs*, concierge services, housekeeping, maintenance and beach services, as well as preferential access to the award-winning Grace Bay Club, including signing privileges at all restaurants and bars.
