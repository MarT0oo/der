Bright Idea, Chalk Sound, Providenciales, Turks and Caicos Vacation Rental


This elegant Turks and Caicos vacation rental is located in a prime residential area overlooking the tranquil turquoise waters of Chalk Sound. Bright Idea is set on 1.8 acres of tropical landscaping and includes a fabulous two level, two bedroom main villa coupled with two private and fully equipped one bedroom guest cottages. This Caribbean paradise blends beautifully and naturally with the surrounding tropical flora, white sand beach, and native island beauty that permeates everything about the Turks and Caicos Islands.

In the main Providenciales villa, enjoy the gourmet kitchen and dining area, coupled with a living room to laze, relax, read or enjoy a movie. There is a powder room on the main floor for your convenience. The two bedrooms, both with en-suite bath, are on the second floor. Four Bedrooms: three with King size beds and one with two twins.

Both the main villa and guest cottages are equipped with Cable TV, air-conditioning (which is included in the price of rental) and ceiling fans. There is also high speed wireless internet for those who want to stay in touch.

 

The infinity-edge pool is sunk into the sundeck in front of main house and between the two cottages. The sundeck is perfect for catching the cooling trade wind breezes while tanning on lounge chairs or dining al fresco.
