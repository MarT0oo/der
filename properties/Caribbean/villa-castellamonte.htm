The famous Villa Castellamonte, noted as one of the top ten villas in the Caribbean, continues to be cited as the Dominican Republic's most exclusive luxury vacation villa rental. Our dedicated professional staff will cater to your every need while you are in residence. Meals are prepared by our chef, served by the staff and even your drinks can be made to order. Our villa concierge can help arrange any variety of activities including horseback riding, wind surfing, snorkeling, deep sea fishing, ground transportation, excursions or other adventures and even bring in cigar rolling or professional spa services right to the villa.

Villa Castellamonte del Mare, located on the Dominican Republic’s unspoiled and pristine northern coastline, offers the most exclusive, luxurious and complete Caribbean vacation experience. Designed by a world renowned, award-winning architect using individually selected materials from throughout the world, this 15,000+ ft² Tuscan-style rental villa is nestled atop a spectacular sea cliff with its own direct access to the semi-private 600' white sand beach. Within the town of Cabrera, Villa Castellamonte is close to other popular tourist destinations such as Cabarete, Sosua, Puerto Plata and Samana Bay. Villa Castellamonte offers 8 bedrooms with ensuites, each one with its own unique oceanfront view and pool access. Every bedroom also has its own terrace, most being over 18' deep, offering a welcoming shady resting place to enjoy the peace and solitude. Custom designed interiors and tailored hand-made furnishings; each room in the house offers its own unique views, features and comforts.

Villa Castellamonte offers a completely new experience in vacation villa rentals within the Dominican Republic. Our designers have created the perfect mixture of Italian elegance combined with Caribbean luxury. The villa rental offers a variety of packages, including all-inclusive vacation packages, and every rental offers world-class cuisine, fine vintage wines, premium liquors and the local pilsner, Presidente beer!

For those interested in Caribbean golf vacations, Villa Castellamonte is located only 7 miles from the acclaimed Playa Grande Golf Course. Designed by Robert Trent Jones, Sr. as his crowning achievement in the Caribbean, the Playa Grande Golf Course is considered by many to be the "Pebble Beach of the Caribbean." As an added plus, the villa has its very own private putting and chipping greens so you can sharpen your short game!

In addition to the Caribbean golf destination, within a few minutes’ drive are some of the most beautiful beaches in the Dominican Republic. Playa Grande Beach, Playa Diamonte, Playa Caleton, Playa Entrada and others all offer unique characteristics with warm, azure blue ocean waters. Whether you want to stroll the beach, boogie board, snorkel or build sand castles, we have the perfect beachfront vacation villa for you this year!
