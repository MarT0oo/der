Whispering Waters, Discovery Bay, Jamaica Vacation Rental


Whispering Waters is an elegant, 12,000 square foot, two storey home. It is the ultimate in comfort and style with seven air-conditioned bedrooms with en-suite bathrooms that is beautifully decorated to the highest standards. The home provides the very best with its combination of privacy and luxury and has entertained the most discerning clients.

Set on its own large and completely private white sand beach cove, Whispering Waters is impeccably maintained and has exquisite gardens with colorful lush flowers hanging from the trellises. Beautiful fabrics and original art grace the interiors with vibrant tropical decor, cool stone floors downstairs and native wooden floors in the bedrooms.

The home features a large living room downstairs with a dining area to seat 14 at a beautiful mahogany table. On the spacious verandah, there is comfortable seating, a large table for informal dining and a wet bar with TV. The verandah leads down to the 16'x34' pool with adjoining Jacuzzi and children's shallow pool, and to the large 100-foot secluded private white sand beach. Beyond the pool the garden leads onto the gazebo and beyond to a stone deck over the sea - a wonderful romantic setting for evening cocktails or a candlelit dinner.

A wooden stairway leads upstairs to a spacious family room furnished with overstuffed sofas, card table, 42" LED TV, computer and fax/printer, VOIP telephone for free calls to USA, Canada and the UK landlines, as well as games. Two master bedrooms are located on the second story together with three other bedrooms.
