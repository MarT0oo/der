Necker, Sir Richard Branson’s private island paradise, is situated in the British Virgin Island, a stunning and unspoilt area of the Caribbean. At 74 acres, Necker sits in turquoise waters surrounded by coral reefs and fringed with beautiful white sandy beaches. Necker can become your own private hideaway; away from the stresses and strains of daily life.  With a maximum capacity of 28 guests Necker consists of the 8 bedrooms in the Great House and six beautiful private Balinese Houses. Necker can be hired exclusively or if you're not looking to hire the Island privately, you can still enjoy the Necker experience during Celebration Weeks when individual rooms can be booked by singles or couples to enjoy our fabulous Island paradise ’house party’ style.

Necker Island BVI

The Necker Island BVI are made up of a few islands open for rentals through private owners and some islands are privately owned, this makes for a wonderful time in the BVI.  These islands are Virgin Gorda, Jost Van Dyke, Tortola and Anegada.  The private islands are those that have been purchased and used for the owners private retreat or as an island available for rental, those are Necker Island, Peter Island, and Little Thatch.  No matter which Island you chose there are always wonderful things to see and do in The Necker Island BVI.

We offer some amazing beachfront homes in the BVI, including the wonderful Aquamare Villas which are beachfront and offer a host of services, Baraka Point Estate which can be rented fully catered and is a little oasis, and Sol y Sombra which is a gorgeous home right in the Little Trunk Bay area.  The homes are available self and fully catered and the optimal times to go are during the winter months.  Yet you can find wonderful deals during May – November.  Don’t forget that if you are planning a wedding to make sure you make all arrangements in advance.
