Villa Diana, Sosua, Cabarete, Dominican Republic Vacation Rental

 

A Dream Villa with exquisite space and location. This 4 bedroom luxury Caribbean property is priced on outstanding quality and location, not a spirling speculative market. A classic beach villa with symmetrical architecture, soaring ceilings and Mediterranean roof lines.

The elegance of this villa is not sacrificed by its relaxed attitude and open flow plan. Its interiors harmonize sophistication and laid back beach living with native river stone interior floors and crisp cut coral pool decking.

A vintage inspired yet contemporary kitchen with Viking appliances features counter tops in rich coal granite with spectacular fosforescent blue flecks that contrast with fresh vanilla painted cabinetry. An expansive terrace provides an ample entertaining space or retreat with on one of the many oversized couches. A nearly sound proof t.v. room with cherry floors with large screen provides comfortable t.v. viewing. All four elegant yet unpretentious bedrooms are tastefully finished in sage and amber hues. An eclectic mix of local art in serene palettes and vintage black and white local photography connect the home to the local colors and environment. All bedrooms have ensuite bathrooms finished in carrara marble vanities, crisp white tiles and Frette towels with appointed storage and hooks for wet suits.

The living and dining area open to terrace for seemless indoor oudoor living. More than 20 royal palms grace the lush gardens. Serenely located on a quiet cul de sac that leads to the Sea Horse Ranch beaches. A timeless Caribbean villa completely furnished in the best residential address in the Caribbean.
