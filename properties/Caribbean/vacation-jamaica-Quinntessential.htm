Quinntessential, Montego Bay, Jamaica, Caribbean Vacation Rental

 

Offering what is undoubtedly the most magnificent ocean view in Tryall from a commanding position atop Garden Hill; Quinntessential is one of the finest private homes in Jamaica where meticulous attention to detail and designers flourish result in the epitome of sophisticated living without compromise.

The handsome white façade with its light-reflecting glass is a magical combination of contemporary design and the principles of 18th century Georgian symmetrical alignment. It is rare for an architect to accomplish this with such aplomb and the effect is breathtaking. The imposing front door with its graceful fanlight – based upon that at the Tryall Great House – opens into a perfectly square paneled entrance hall. Looking down through the reception room and to the veranda beyond, the magnificent infinity edge pool is in direct alignment with the front door and the elevation gives the impression that the ocean is flowing into the pool.

Inside or outside, elegant living spaces furnished in crisp white with accents of blues instill relaxation; dine outside on the covered veranda or inside at the elegant round Georgian dining table. But of course the pool terrace is the magnetic draw with the heated salt water infinity pool and integrated hot tub. As the evening air cools, enjoy drinks from the bar at the fire pit or adjourn to the pub below the pool with its adjacent putting green.

Ten guests are accommodated in five bedrooms. The magnificent master suite in the main house comprises a private sitting room, bedroom and terrace with infinity-edge plunge pool. In the adjacent cottage which is a mere four or five steps from the house are three bedrooms, two on the upper level and one downstairs with an adjacent sitting room or snug. The fifth bedroom is privately located adjacent to the the pool. All bedrooms face the fabulous ocean panorama; four have king beds and one twin beds which combine to form a king. The exquisite bathrooms are fitted by Waterworks throughout; two have a tub as well as walk-in shower and the remaining three have showers. Anticipate individuality in each bedroom with bespoke furniture custom made for each room.

Outside, a classical white garden surrounds the villa, immaculately maintained and affording wonderful privacy.
