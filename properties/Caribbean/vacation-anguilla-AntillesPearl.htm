Antilles Pearl, Shoal Bay West, West End, Anguilla Vacation Rental

 

Three floors of extravagance featuring a Master Suite with a private terrace, four guest rooms, and a 50 ft. “skywalk” that culminates in a triangular viewing area protruding right over the sand, trailing to the turquoise Caribbean Sea and rooftop terrace. The massive and bold geometric estate includes a modern lift for seamless transport between floors, a fully equipped gourmet kitchen with doors leading onto the terrace, a beautiful living room with a twenty-one foot high ceiling. This property includes personal butler, daily housekeeping and bespoke laundry services. The adventure of a lifetime begins here.

The Pearl features a twenty-three speaker sound system with seven LCD control panels for unrestricted music access from anywhere on the property. There is also a full entertainment center featuring ping-pong, air hockey, poker table, and a custom-built pool table with a teak wood and stainless steel finish. Outdoor features of the Pearl include a private beachfront, swimming pool, a 50 foot skywalk (an architectural feat that makes you feel as if you are floating above the ocean), a rooftop terrace with a barbeque for alfresco dining, plus tennis, volleyball and basketball courts.

A rooftop terrace with a stunning 360-degree view of the Atlantic Ocean, Caribbean Sea and St. Martin. A serene slice of paradise where you’ll find amenities exclusive to the world’s most luxurious resorts, provided in the comfort of your own private villa. Let your cares wash away into the crystal blue waters of the Caribbean as we provide you with a bespoke vacation experience.

With contemporary artwork from emerging and mid-career artists situated throughout the villa, it has been said that it is like having the opportunity to inhabit a sculpture. Carefully curated pieces highlight the architecture of the space and invite the eye to take in its beautiful surroundings. An emphasis on color, gesture and rich tones works to enhance the natural bright skies and blue waters of Anguilla.

Your personal butler can arrange an extensive list of concierge services including (but not limited to) a private chef, in-suite or ocean front massages, live entertainment, private boat charter, day trip to St. Barths by air, water sports and activities such as jet skiing or sailing lessons, nanny services and more.
