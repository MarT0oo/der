Kid Friendly Features:

Punta Cana Resort offers a supervised children's program for ages 4-12. Activities include boat rides, water skiing classes, horseback riding, games and crafts. Kids can also enjoy a petting zoo.

Recreation at Punta Cana Resort:

On-site water sports include sailing, windsurfing, kayaking, snorkeling, and boating (complimentary), plus scuba and deep-sea fishing. Just offshore is an eight kilometer coral reef and a sunken 220 foot cargo ship. On land, Punta Cana offers a private nature reserve with a swimmable spring, petting zoo, tennis, two swimming pools and horseback riding. Adjacent oceanfront La Cana Golf Course features 18 holes designed by P. B. Dye. A Tom Fazio oceanfront golf course opened in 2010


Spa and Fitness Center:

Punta Cana Resort offers a fitness center as well as Six Senses Spa, with a holistic and wellness focus and eight treatment rooms, plus four additional spaces outdoors. This is the first location Six Senses Spas has opened in the Caribbean. The "Planet Earth Core Treatment" incorporates minerals such as gold, silver, crystals and aromatic clays as well as native flora and fauna. Children under 12 have a special half-price spa menu.
