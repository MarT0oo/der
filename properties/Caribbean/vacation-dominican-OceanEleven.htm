Ocean Eleven, Cap Cana, Dominican Republic Vacation Rental

 

Modern, minimalist, avant-garde and luminous are the correct concepts to embody a 3-bedroom masterpiece perched in a very exclusive spot in one of the most worldly renowned with unbeatable ocean views and outlooks of the marina in Cap Cana.

Ideal for small group of family or friends and also for devoted fishing lovers, this state-of-the-art villa has its very own speedboat moored next to it ready to take you to the natural water pools, located in front of the Westin Resort, just a 7-minute ride.

The facade is governed by white coralline tiles and as you step inside a fully equipped kitchen with a quartz countertop welcomes you ready to prepare the most traditional Dominican meals. Next to the living room, marvelous water views invite you to the outside to lay back and enjoy the surroundings. A wonderful coralline floor guides you to the pool joint the wooden deck with sun loungers and a glass railing.

Going up to the second floor, all three bedrooms are waiting for you. The master bedroom is large with an incredible picture window covering all the front, letting the sunlight come in to enhance every detail. It has a king bed, ensuite bathroom, walk-in closet, and TV. The second bedroom features a queen bed, full bathroom with dual vanity, closet, and TV. The last bedroom has two queen beds, a full bathroom with dual vanity, closet and TV.

On the rooftop, the best is waiting for you. A great outdoor living, with BBQ area, alfresco dining, sun loungers, and a heated jacuzzi. With a cold beer and enjoying the incredible views, you reached the top!

This beautiful villa is located in the exclusive gated community of Cap Cana, a 26-square mile paradise that is blessed with a temperate year-round climate, light rainfall, and soft breezes. It's colorful coral reefs; calm crystalline waters and white-sand beaches are breathtaking. The community Resort provides a matchless experience for all who visit this tropical paradise, and its tropically chic lifestyle is ideal for those who appreciate high standards of quality, comfort and aesthetics. As guests of the villa, you have access to all of the resort amenities, casa club and activities.
