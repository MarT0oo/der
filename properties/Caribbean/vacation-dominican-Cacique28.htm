Cacique 28, Punta Cana, Dominican Republic Vacation Rental

 

Tucked yourself, your family and friends into this incredible 5 bedroom villa, nestled in one of the most requested and exclusive gated village in the whole Caribbean.

Right from the start, as you access the main door you will walk into a incredible foyer well decorated and aligned, which has a great outlook of the backyard with a large pool, galleries and garden furniture.

Walk outside to find a spacious ambiance to have an incredible time with your beloved ones and if you want to relax, jump into the jacuzzi.

Prepare breakfast on the fully equipped kitchen and have it on the exquisite palapa or the large terrace with eclectic, yet balanced furniture.

The villa has 5 bedrooms with AC, 3 of them with a king size bed, all with ensuite bathroom, the remnant 2 bedrooms have two queen sizes bed each, also with ensuite bathroom.

Lay back and enjoy this amazing property amid paradise!
