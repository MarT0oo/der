Villa Vieux, Chalk Sound, Providenciales, Turks and Caicos Vacation Rental

 

Nestled amongst lush gardens this Villa is set on tranquil Taylor Bay with stunning views across Chalk Sound National Park and Caicos Banks. This villa reaps the rewards of all day sun and breathtaking sunsets.  This magnificent ocean home invokes a feeling of an era gone by. The delightful architectural design with an exterior of hand cut native stone combine to make the Villa a truly exceptional estate. The high vaulted ceiling with exposed beams benefit from cooling trade wind breezes. Antiques, eclectic and unique furnishings provide both stylish and comfortable accommodation.

The Villa provides ideal accommodation for families or a group of friends wishing for a peaceful and luxurious retreat by the sea. This home is ideal for weddings or celebrations offering more than comfort for up to 14 adults.  Apart from the private pool terrace a deck area is ideally situated overlooking the ocean and beach - perfect for sunbathing and shade, pre-dinner drinks or to absorb the stunning sunsets. 

The main house which accommodates up to 10 adults comprises of a well equipped large kitchen opening out to a dining area and spacious sitting room.  All five master suites in the main villa have large ensuite shower rooms. The ground floor suite has an ensuite bath/ shower, and all have separate air conditioning and ceiling fans. (air conditioning is charged locally as used). To enhance your privacy each room has a television and music centre.

Two luxurious guest suites are available in addition to the main house to accommodate parties of up to 14 adults. Positioned on a lower level, approached by a private entrance with a gentle ramp access and opening onto the waterside, these beautiful suites are ideal for a little privacy. One suite benefits from discrete enhancements for guests who are less able bodied. The large walk in shower would be accessible to a wheelchair user. A small sitting area with a mini kitchen equipped with a microwave, refrigerator, kettle, toaster and coffee maker separates the two suites. A large deck area opens out from the suites affording ocean views. Please note that there are 2 steps into and out of the main villa and shallow steps into the pool. 

The pool terrace has a plentiful supply of sun loungers and provides alfresco dining with a large gourmet BBQ and wet bar. Steps lead down to the lower deck and dock area. 2 private docks provide access to the water and a brief swim to the shores and fine sands of Taylor Bay.
