Perched hillside offering panoramic vistas of Crocus Bay and its five-thousand-foot stretch of pristine beach and shimmering turquoise waters, CeBlue Villas and Beach Resort celebrates its world-class hallmarks of hybrid-luxury villas, boutique hotel full services, privacy and ultimate tranquility on the island of Anguilla.

Modern resort architectures flanks landscaping designed of native plants to preserve and enhance the beauty of its natural surroundings and Caribbean heritage.

LOCATION

CeBlue Villas & Beach Resort is situated on Anguilla’s exclusive northwestern coastline. The property is 10 minutes by car from Clayton J. Lloyd International Airport on Anguilla (AXA) servicing inter-region flights and private air. For international jet carrier flights the nearest airport is St. Maarten (SXM) and take speed boat from the airport ferry terminal.

One of the Caribbean’s most coveted destinations, Anguilla is the northernmost island of the Leeward Islands and offers 33 of the world’s best beaches.
