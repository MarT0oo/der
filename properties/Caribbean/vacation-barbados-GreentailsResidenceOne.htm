Greentails Residence One, Barbados Vacation Rental


Greentails; a private gated community of 7 villas (5 single story and 2 two story homes), is comfortably nestled within the center of Sion Hill, nearby the lovely mahogany forest.

Residence 1– one of the nicest villas in the development- is a 5000 sq ft 2 story villa that has 4 bedrooms in the main house and an attractive 500 sq ft 1 bedroom self contained cottage. The entire upstairs is the master suite, where partial sea views can be seen. Further 2 double bedrooms are located by the pool side. The final room is a study which will be used as the owners' personal storage.

Greentails Residence 1 captures the never ending blue skies of Barbados with its open living area which leads unto a very spacious covered terrace where you can enjoy Al Fresco dining, looking unto the large infinity pool.
