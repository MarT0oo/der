Las Palmas Sea, Sosua, Cabarete, Dominican Republic Vacation Rental

 

This 4 plus bedroom villa is a quintessential vacation home beautifully finished with walnut hardwood doors, louver windows and Mexican tiles. Bathrooms tastefully done in customized natural slate stone and decorative Mexican ceramic. Living area completely opens to the tropically landscaped yard and lovely free form pool with beautiful rock waterfall. The oversized master bedroom with a king size bed and en suite bathroom also has a sweet balcony for morning coffee overlooking the pool.

Two guest rooms have two full beds and one queen beds and share a bathroom. The fourth bedroom with queen bed has its own en suite bathroom. The mezzanine bonus room has two twins which can be used as a fifth bedroom and or children’s play den. Air conditioning and ceiling fans available in every room. This villa is one of our most tropical villas for year round indoor outdoor living. It is beautifully furnished with plenty of dining options inside and outside, perfect for BBQ’s watching the kids jump off the waterfall. This is the perfect villa to disconnect from a hectic lifestyle although Wi-Fi is available as with all our villas.
