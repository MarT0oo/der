Punta Minitas 10, Casa de Campo, Dominican Republic Vacation Rental

 

Located in one of the most renowned gated villages in all the Caribbean, this amazing bayfront villa is your best match to enjoy some incredible holidays whether with friends or your entire family.

Bestowed with an utter sober decoration this 8 bedroom mansion with its large and spacious common places will grant everybody the perfect stay. All bedrooms are equally distributed in two stories where the master bedroom is on the second floor with an incredible oceanview terrace. Next to it you have another bedroom with 2 queen size beds and the third and last bedroom from upstairs has a queen size bed. Then, if we go down again you will find a bedroom with three full beds and an external private hot tub, two other bedrooms have a king size bed each and the last bedroom has a queen size bed. All bedrooms have ensuite bathroom and except one on the first floor with a king size bed all bedrooms have a television.

Regarding common areas, you will find on the exterior a beautiful large pool next to a giant green tree with amazing views into the bay and the Caribbean Ocean. Rest in our spacious gallery feeling the gentle sea breeze or prepare yourself a snack in the fully equpped open kitchen next to a exquisite big living room.
