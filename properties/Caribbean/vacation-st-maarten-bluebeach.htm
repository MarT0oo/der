Blue Beach, Terres Basses, St. Martin Vacation Rental


With a superb location directly on magnificent Baie Longue Beach, this comfortable beach home offers an ideal vacation beachfront base. Its generous pool, expansive deck, covered terrace and inviting gazebo were all designed to take advantage of its fabulous Terres Basses location.

The living room features high ceilings and comfortable rattan and hardwood furnishings along with satellite TV, DVD and a stereo CD system. The living room opens to the large, covered terrace with additional seating and a large dining table. The inviting seafront gazebo offers an additional dining option. The fully equipped kitchen features granite counter tops, stove, oven, microwave, refrigerator, freezer, icemaker, dishwasher, blender, toaster and coffee maker. There is an outdoor charcoal BBQ.

All four bedrooms of Villa Blue Beach are air-conditioned. Two bedrooms are located in the main house. The master bedroom has a king bed and a private bathroom and the second, smaller bedroom, has a queen bed with a bathroom across the hall. A new building at the opposite end of the pool houses two additional bedrooms, one with king bed and private bath, the other with twin beds, bathroom and a small kitchenette. Both of these bedrooms have window screens and ceiling fans. There is a stereo CD system in this new building.
