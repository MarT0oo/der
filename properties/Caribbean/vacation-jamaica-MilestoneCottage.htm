Milestone Cottage, Bluefields Bay, Jamaica Vacation Rental

 

Milestone Cottage has always been the honeymoon favorite, although also popular for small families with its two bedrooms. Built many years earlier, it was purchased in 1986 and extensively remodeled and personalized. This is when its luxurious cliffside plunge pool with underwater seats was added—the perfect place for soaking up sun. Guests also enjoy heading down a dramatic staircase beside a massive fig tree to its seaside pavilion. This recent addition offers a way for guests to be close to nature in a totally private place, and there are sea kayaks nearby for adventuring along the remote South Coast shoreline. This pavilion is an ideal spot for seaside lunches, and is the perfect location to enjoy tropical drinks and admire the flaming colors of the sunsets.

Milestone Cottage has an intimate feel, making it cozy and comfortable for small families who want quality time together, but who also want some privacy. Its living room opens completely to the grassy lawns and sea views and pool, so children with nanny can play for hours and parents can remain out-of-sight at the seaside pavilion or on excursions. Reconvening for fantastic meals served by the headman is always a terrific treat.

Located south of Montego Bay and east of Negril, Bluefields Bay is the perfect escape from the commercialized tourist traps that plague far too much of Jamaica.

With its unspoiled natural beauty, Bluefields Bay has a calm and purity unlike the rest of Jamaica. Experience its unparalleled tranquility as you gaze at the scintillating Bay from your private exclusive villa. Now a Protected Fish Sanctuary, the sea is without activity — unlike the coastlines teeming with tourists and boats of most other holiday locations.
