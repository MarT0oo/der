Rose Hall Villas, Montego Bay, Jamaica Vacation Rental

 

For years Rose Hall Villas have played "home away from home" for many discerning clients. These 28-individual villas with private swimming pools, provide the ultimate Caribbean style escape. For the ultimate in luxury, we invite you to discover one of our Royal Villas, where you can enjoy full access to the amenities and activities of a world-class resort while still enjoying the privacy and comfort of your own Caribbean villa.

Each villa comes with its own personal butler, cook and housekeeper, who are there to attend to your every need. These exceptional staff members, who take pride in your happiness and comfort, will stock your pantry, make dinner reservations, arrange excursions, take care of your laundry and even assist in special celebrations.

With four, five, six or seven bedrooms, all with keyed entrances and en-suite bathrooms, the Rose Hall Villas are ideal for gathering friends, family or colleagues for an unforgettable Caribbean vacation. Four bedroom villas starting at $1,620 per night.
