Arrecife Royale, Punta Cana, Dominican Republic Vacation Rental

 

After the success of Yarari Royale we have chosen Puntacana Resort & Club on an amazing golf front location to set up our second flagship property with beautiful Lake Views and walking distance to the La Cana Club House where you will find a pristine white sandy beach, beachfront pool, restaurant and free chairs for anybody staying here, at Arrecife Royale!

Entering the final stage of its construction, this property is being built with the most advanced architectural techniques under rigorous supervision. Once accomplished, this massive 9 Bedroom villa will become a landmark throughout the Caribbean, based on the following pillars: modernism, minimalism, and avant-guard served with the internationally recognized professionalism of Volalto's team.

As you enter through the main entrance, a highed-ceiling with a gentle wind coming from the garden welcomes you to a large and sober foyer with modern touches and governed by a minimalistic outlook. The wooden panels on the main window sprinkle a fresh and natural touch to the villa.

Along with the villa's horseshoe structure, every piece of decor and functional furniture was cleverly positioned all over seeking the ideal balance to offer a relaxed ambiance for all guests. Note the adage here is just to lay back and enjoy all its unique amenities, outstanding concierge service, and dining experience.

When going upstairs to the second floor you will find 7 of the 9 bedrooms. There are three master bedrooms with a King bed and an en-suite bathroom each, including a hot tub on the terrace. Then, you have 3 double bedrooms with 2 Queen beds and en-suite bathroom each and the seventh bedroom, ideal for families, has 2 Queen beds and 2 Twin Bunk Beds with its en-suite bathroom. Finally, when going downstairs you will find the eighth bedroom, with a King Bed and en-suite bathroom next to the last bedroom, with 2 Queen beds and en-suite bathroom.

After a long beach day, the outdoor pool is the ideal break to cool off and then, sit on the large alfresco dining to enjoy a Dominican drink while planning your next day´s activities while marveling the lake and golf. After sunset, gather with your beloved ones around the firepit or inside the hot tub, to talk about have you did during your day. Also, for entertainment, you have a wide array of options available: pool table, foosball or ping pong.

Another option is to pump or simply keep your shape in one of the most advanced home gyms you will find in the Caribbean. This fitness room was equipped with the best dumbells, barbells, treadmill, etc.

This beautiful villa is located in the exclusive gated community of Punta Cana Resort & Club, a 26-square mile paradise that is blessed with a temperate year-round climate, light rainfall, and soft breezes. It's colorful coral reefs, calm crystalline waters and white-sand beaches are breathtaking. The Resort provides a matchless experience for all who visit this tropical paradise, and its tropically chic lifestyle is ideal for those who appreciate high standards of quality, comfort and aesthetics. As guests of the villa, you have access to all of the resort amenities and activities.
