Mount Edgecombe Plantation, La Resource, Grenada Vacation Rental


A self-contained and historic example of a traditional estate owner’s house. Completely and beautifully restored, the Plantation House comprises an entrance hall, drawing room, snooker room, study, kitchen/dining for 10, master bedroom (with king size bed) with an ensuite bath, dressing room, together with an additional 3 guest bedrooms (one queen, one double, and one triple single) with two bathrooms.

The most stunning feature of the Plantation House is the extensive, wrap around, covered living/dining verandah offering 180 degree views of the Caribbean Sea, just 100 meters (109 yards) away with the tropical gardens in the foreground.

The Plantation House sleeps 9 but there are 4 additional nutmeg rooms providing accommodations for a further 10 guests. Mount Edgecombe Plantation provides very personal service for up to 19 guests. Exclusive use of the entire estate provides the dream location for an intimate wedding, family reunion, and private event of special interest groups.

NUTMEG ROOMS

Located in the original plantation spice storage house with nutmeg and cocoa drying racks (which are still in operation). The Nutmeg Rooms overlook the cocoa plantation and Caribbean sea views with yachts making their way to and from the neighboring islands of the Grenadines.

The four nutmeg rooms (2 superior with King bed and single day bed, and 2 standard – 1 King bed and 1 twin) are very spacious, unique and luxury in style. Just the sounds of the birds, the tree frogs and sea breeze to enjoy.

GREAT ROOM

Adjoins the stunning double infinity swimming pools and provides the perfect dining space for meals with produce from the estate, complete with the ‘Little Bamboo Bar’. This is where you can relax after exploring Grenada or just relaxing here at the Mount Edgecombe Plantation. The views, sunsets and fireflies need to be experienced.

FOOD AND BEVERAGE

A live-in team provides housekeeping and can cater for individual preferences of food and beverage with freshly prepared local dishes using produce from the estate, and the opportunity to choose freshly picked fruit for breakfast.

Rates include bed and breakfast. Drinks are available at the Bamboo cash bar; and meals (lunch and dinner) can be booked on request.

ABOUT THE AREA

Places to visit and activities close by include: gorgeous, soft-sanded beaches; picturesque harbour city of St. George's, Grand Etang National Park with miles of hiking trails in the lush tropical rainforest; Seven Sisters and Concord waterfalls; Natural wildlife sanctuaries, Turtle and whale watching (Nov-May); fishing, sailing, snorkelling and scuba; nutmeg factories and rum distilleries; and farmer cooperative chocolate factory using cocoa harvested on the estate.

GETTING AROUND

Arrangements can be made for airport transfers from Maurice Bishop International Airport (60 minutes), trips to the capital St. Georges (45 minutes), as well as private excursions including fishing, boating, scuba, island tours, car hire, and island trips. We can assist with car hire, private excursions and nearby island trips.
