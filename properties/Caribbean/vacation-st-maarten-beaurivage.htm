Villa Beau Rivege, Terres Basses, St. Martin Vacation Rental


Located on beautiful Bay Rouge beach, Beau Rivage enjoys magnificent sweeping views of the beach, ocean and Anguilla. Beau Rivage's huge loggia/veranda boasts a sparkling pool, and is surrounded by comfortable single and double chaise lounges, plush seating, and stone mosaic tables. Enjoy dining in the beach side gazebo, only feet from the waves, or in the luxuriously appointed great room. Inside, antiques are combined with elegant furniture selected or designed by Francis Mayes, the best-selling author of "Under the Tuscan Sun", bringing a romantic and old world warmth to this special villa. Top of the line Sony TVs, DVD, and satellite systems and high speed wireless internet keep you connected to the world. Each of the three master suites has a Sony 40 inch flat panel TV, and the Great Room media lounge sports Sony's newest 46 inch Flat LCD and an IPOD dock. Every room is air conditioned, and enjoys views of the ocean. The three private master suites surround the pool, each outfitted with European furniture, and the heavenly W Hotel's king size beds. The designer kitchen is fully equipped, and opens to a pool bar with views of the ocean and terraces.

Minutes by car from the French port of Marigot with fine dining, sailing, deep sea fishing, and numerous activities, or a short walk to two cozy and fun beach bars. From the moment you drive through the private gate, and into the lush gardens, you'll be taken by the beauty, privacy, and fun of Beau Rivage.
