Kishti on Meads, Meads Bay, Anguilla Vacation Rental

 

Experience modern indoor/outdoor living in these sparkling new villas situated side-by-side on the pristine white sands of Anguilla’s Meads Bay. Floor to ceiling windows immerse guests in this breathtaking, world class location. Walk to top restaurants, charming beach cafes and enjoy a cocktail along a fabulous mile-long stretch of beach anchored by 5-star resorts on each end. Lounge on outdoor canopy beds, cool off in the private infinity pool or relax in a beachside hot tub. Kishti Villas on Meads Bay offer the pinnacle of luxury and pampering in the heart of one of Anguilla’s most gorgeous beaches.

Six bedrooms in calming blues and neutrals echo the surrounding tropical beauty in Kishti East and the West villa has five bedrooms with a larger master suite. Book both villas for a combined 11 bedrooms accommodating up to 22 guests. Each villa has rooftop and patio gardens, guest and chef kitchens, home theater and gym. The exceptional private staff will attend to each detail and request, perfecting this memorable and serene vacation experience on Anguilla.

Your stay at Kishti includes cook, butler, housekeeper and security service—meaning there’s always someone to bring you a drink on the beach. Spend your stay relaxing in the infinity swimming pools and hot tubs, or explore the bay with the provided sea kayaks, snorkeling equipment and stand-up paddleboards. Prepare local seafood on the barbecue, watch the waves over dinner at the al-fresco dining areas and enjoy a glass of wine under the stars from the loungers and daybeds on the terrace. The interiors are just as well-appointed, with fitness and media rooms, home theater systems and Wi-Fi.

Designed by renowned firm Cecconi Simone, the interiors of Kishti on Meads are both memorably stylish and beachy and comfortable. Each villa is laid out around a great room with a living area, dining area and kitchen that opens to the terrace via a folding glass wall, so the space is bright and airy. Wood floors, neutral upholstery and hurricane lanterns throughout the room have relaxed appeal. For your convenience, each villa has two fully equipped kitchens.

There are six bedrooms in the east villa at Kishti and six bedrooms in the west villa; all have king beds and en-suite bathrooms. The master suite in the east villa also has its own al-fresco shower. Though the bedrooms feature pared-down palettes, natural textures of wood and linen give the spaces a soft, organic feel.

Thanks to the location of Kishti on Meads Bay Beach, you’re just a 9-minute walk from Turtle Cove Beach, a 10-minute walk from the shops and restaurants along Meads Bay, a 1.5-mile drive from Barnes Bay Beach and a 15-minute drive from Anguilla’s Lloyd International Airport. You can also opt to fly to St. Maarten; it’s an 11-minute drive from the villa to the Blowing Point ferry terminal, a 25-minute ferry ride across to St. Maarten, and a 2-minute drive from the ferry dock to Princess Juliana International Airport.

Anguilla, one of the most northerly of the Leeward Islands in the British West Indies, offers an enticing combination of attributes for those who truly wish to escape. The main island is only sixteen miles long and three miles at its greatest width. With its flat topography, Anguilla doesn’t attract rain clouds the way mountainous islands do. The result is one of the most habitually sunny climates in the entire Caribbean

Add its friendly people and pristine beaches, and you have a favorite vacation destination for discerning travelers who prefer “off the beaten path” privacy; but still enjoy a world-class experience and easy proximity to excellent shopping, invigorating nightlife and exceptional dining options on St. Martin — all a brief crossing away.

Think you can land a wahoo? This is a great place to find out. Feel like a game of golf? You’re in luck. Are you an avid scuba diver? There are over 30 shipwreck sites awaiting your underwater exploration. In recent years, there’s even a burgeoning nightlife with opportunities to dance to reggae under the stars to your heart’s content.
