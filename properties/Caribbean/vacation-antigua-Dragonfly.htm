Dragonfly, Jumby Bay, Antigua, Caribbean


Located on the island’s southeastern plateau, Dragonfly is an enchanting tropical estate that enjoys both refreshing natural breezes and stunning views of Buckley Bay and the islands beyond. Mirroring the color of the Caribbean Sea, the home's signature aqua-hued doors and windows beckon to guests on arrival. A fresh island-inspired palette of whites, neutrals and blues is carried throughout the light and airy residence, complementing chic furnishings and an eclectic collection of artwork.

Entering Dragonfly, guests pass through a welcoming courtyard filled with bougainvillea, featuring tranquil fish ponds, and outside seating under a large frangipani tree. Stepping inside, a spacious great room opens to a wrapping veranda overlooking a glistening infinity pool. Along the pool deck, large cantilevered umbrellas provide shade for comfortable teak chaises where guests can take in sweeping panoramic views of the sea. A languorous tropical atmosphere accentuates the home’s array of outdoor living spaces, and additional dining areas, including a dining gazebo, and a BBQ courtyard with a calming water fountain.

Dragonfly offers exceptional leisure amenities. A combination den and media room features a pool table, and large screen media center. Just steps away, an indoor/outdoor garden room with table tennis is available. For more sporting pursuits, the estate offers an all weather flood-lit tennis court for daytime and evening play. Golf enthusiasts will enjoy the home’s professionally designed new putting green and bunker; and indoor golf simulator where they can practice and perfect their skills on myriad golf courses of their choice.

Designed to encourage relaxation, Dragonfly’s beautiful accommodations include three spacious master suites. The largest is located in the west wing and features a four-poster king bed, wet bar, two terraces, indoor and outdoor showers, and an outdoor soaking tub for two. The master in the east wing offers a four-poster king bed, terrace, outdoor shower and soaking tub. Located on the second level, the third master features an open wet room shower and soaking tub. A king-bedded room and a room with two small double beds and a window seat which can double as a third bed are also located on the second floor. All of the upstairs bedrooms open to relaxing terraces with captivating views of the island and surrounding aquamarine waters.
