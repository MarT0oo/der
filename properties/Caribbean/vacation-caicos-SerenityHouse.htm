Serenity House, Grace Bay, Providenciales, Turks and Caicos Vacation Rental

 

This 4,000 sq. ft luxury vacation villa is situated directly on Turks & Caicos' beautiful white sand beach of Grace Bay - rated among the top ten in the Caribbean. Swim and snorkel just steps from your door in the clear crystalline waters of Provo's north coast.

A short walk along the beach from the villa are Smith's Reef and Coral Gardens, two of the Island's finest snorkeling reefs within swimming distance from shore.

The spacious and supremely comfortable holiday home consists of two levels, with 6 bedrooms, each with ensuite bathrooms, air conditioning, cable television and telephones.

A formal dining room accommodates 12, and adjoins a lavish and beautifully outfitted gourmet kitchen, with breakfast bar.

Stepping through the entrance foyer, is a great room with wet bar, and separate den/TV room; floor-to-ceiling glass doors lead to the outdoor covered patio facing the Grace Bay beach, a perfect area for entertaining, alfresco dining, or poolside lounging. From the quiet privilege of Serenity's beachfront patio, take in the view of Provo's northshore reef, yachts and fishing boats beyond, windsurfers and paragliders breezing overhead in the distance.

The villa's upper level has 2 master suites (King beds) overlooking the beach and ocean; while the third (King) and fourth (Queen) bedrooms overlook the front gardens.

Lower level contains additional two garden-front bedrooms, one with 2 twins, the other with Queen. Guest powder room just off the Great Room, laundry room with 2 washers and dryer complete the accommodations.

Serenity House is surrounded by colorful tropical foliage in a large and private garden. A perfectly situated beachfront vacation rental, the house combines privacy with proximity to Grace Bay's attractions - shopping, fine restaurants, golf, fishing, diving and watersports activities.
