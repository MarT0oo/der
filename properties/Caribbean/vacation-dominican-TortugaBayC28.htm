Tortuga Bay C28, Punta Cana Resort, Punta Cana, Dominican Republic Vacation Rental

 

Sitting right alongside the coveted Cana Golf Course in the Punta Cana Resort, the Villa Tortuga Bay C28 is a perfect spot for an unforgettable holiday in the Dominican Republic. You do not even have to appreciate golf to appreciate the beauty of this property. Offering a stunning mix of indoor and outdoor living spaces, it is the epitome of luxurious living.

The unprecedented views from this villa are best enjoyed from the pool area. This includes the elegant stretch of pool that is framed by a stone surround, and a gazebo that offers lounging, a barbecue and al fresco dining. There is also the massive covered portico with its stone pillars, terra cotta floors, and truly elegant d√©cor. An outdoor living room and a more formal outdoor dining room are located here.

Step inside and you enter the two-story atrium that is open to a second floor balcony. The main floor features the great room with its formal living room, soothingly decorated in pale colors, wood and sea grass furnishings, and subdued accents. The formal dining room is nearby, and it leads you into the large and airy kitchen. With modern appliances and marble counters, it is a dream workspace. A separate TV lounge is available upstairs too.

The five bedrooms are divided between the two floors. A master suite is on the upper level with king bed and balcony access in addition to a large and opulent ensuite bath. The remaining bedrooms feature either one or two queen suites, and all have private baths. A stay in the Villa Tortuga Bay C28 includes the services of a housekeeper and gardener, as well as providing access to many of the resort's impressive amenities.

Dating to the 1970s, the Punta Cana resort now covers a stunning 26 square miles including Tortuga Bay, The Westin Punta Cana Resort & Club, Four Points by Sheraton Punta Cana Village, Six Senses Spa, the Punta Cana Ecological Foundation and high-end residential neighborhoods. Visitors enjoy a full service marina, gourmet restaurants, shopping, and two professionally designed golf courses in addition to other resort services and amenities.

This beautiful villa is located in the exclusive gated community of Punta Cana Resort & Club, a 26-square mile paradise that is blessed with a temperate year-round climate, light rainfall, and soft breezes. It's colorful coral reefs, calm crystalline waters and white-sand beaches are breathtaking. The resort provides a matchless experience for all who visit this tropical paradise, and its tropically chic lifestyle is ideal for those who appreciate high standards of quality, comfort and aesthetics. As guests of the villa, you have access to all of the resort amenities and activities.
