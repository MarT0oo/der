Castle Lime Stone, Anguilla, British West Indies Vacation Rental


Castle Lime Stone is a secluded beachside Tuscan inspired 10,000 ft² 4 bedroom villa; on the North Shore of Anguilla with magnificent views of the ever-changing shades of blue of the Atlantic Ocean.

On the second level there is a large Grand Master bedroom with en-suite bathroom with a bathtub and private outdoor shower and a large balcony overlooking the beach and sea, along with an en suite fitness centre.    There is also a Master bedroom in another wing, on this level with en-suite bathroom and shower and a large balcony overlooking the sea.

On the first level there are two more bedrooms each with magnificent sea views and en-suite bathrooms.  Each bedroom is air-conditioned and has ceiling fans.  There are large areas for entertaining either outside around the infinity pool or at the Gazebo with its BBQ and a wonderful view of the beach and the sea beyond. Or one can entertain more formally inside in the air-conditioned cool of a large Great Room with its adjoining dining area.   There is a Media room with a computer area, just off the Great Room.
