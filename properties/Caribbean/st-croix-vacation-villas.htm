About St. Croix

St. Croix is part of the USVI, you can get to St. Croix through http://airporthelper.com/stx , Alexander Hamilton Airport STX.  You can easily get a flight from NEW YORK or other parts of the USA daily.  You can also Island hop from many USVI to other islands such as St. Thomas and St. John and Puerto Rico.  There are plenty of things to do.  If you are looking to rent a car in St. Croix see Ace - Olympic Rent-A-Car Toll Free: 888-USVI-CARS or Phone: 340-773-8000 or visit www.OlympicStCroix.com. 

If you plan on getting out, you need to visit the many beaches, such as Cane Bay and Butler Bay.  If you plan on Golfing there are three Golf Courses Buccaneer Golf Club www.thebuccaneer.com/golf.htm, Carambola Golf Club www.golfcarambola.com, The Reef Golf Course.

If you plan on Going out at night try Rumrunners in Christiansted http://www.rumrunnersstcroix.com/.

So much to do in St. Croix, Enjoy your stay!
