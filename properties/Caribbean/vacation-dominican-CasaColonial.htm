Casa Colonial is a luxury 4 bedroom villa with creamy marble floors, vaulted natural wood ceilings and gorgeous wrought iron work. Casa Colonial has two guest wings and extensive indoor and outdoor living areas. It boasts the largest pool of any rental villa at Sea Horse Ranch. The beautiful interior with its garden vistas creates a serene and rejuvenating living experience scarcely found elsewhere in the world.
