As you turn right into the driveway from Copperwood Ridge and begin to ascend, you will wonder how much more of the ocean panorama will be seen once you alight at the top. Your sense of anticipation will not be disappointed as your eyes take in the 180 degree uninterrupted seascape and the undulating Hanover Hills. You are not dreaming, this is the beauty of Anticipation Villa.

The architect of this magnificent villa seized upon the stupendous location and created a home on one level where every single room looks out and opens out to the view of the sea, celebrating the beauty for which Jamaica is so renowned. The ultimate luxury of space - a space that has been imaginatively designed to free-flow from west to east, following the line of the bay so that wherever you stand or sit on the amazing terrace, that spans the total width of the house, the sea is ever-present. Views are framed by the orange flowers of a Poinciana tree and by the natural curve and inlets of the Hanover coastline. Anticipation Villa is designed to enable everyone to come together and enjoy the fun while also providing enough space to allow everyone to find his or her own private spot to relax. It is a house in which to entertain, or simply come together as a family. Anticipation Villa is a fine example of classical Caribbean architecture. Inside, it is a showcase of contemporary interior design that not only emphasizes the height and space of the rooms but also creates an atmosphere that is calm, harmonious and sophisticated. The talent of Jamaican master craftsmen is evident throughout, in the custom made furniture and irresistibly tactile pieces throughout. Lines are clean, straight and angular, spectacularly punctuated by the sensuous curve of glass sculpture, beaten silver circles and the translucency of water cascading down. The blue of the sea is ever-present, complemented by warm earth tones with a dash of the vibrancy of reds and greens. The home is a work of art surrounded by the beauty of nature.

The endless vista of the ocean is mirrored in the spaciousness within the walls of Anticipation Villa. The grandeur of the common areas melds into the tranquil elegance of each of the six bedrooms. All face the ocean and open to its own private section of the terrace. Four bedrooms have king size beds and two have two double beds which can be configured as kings.
