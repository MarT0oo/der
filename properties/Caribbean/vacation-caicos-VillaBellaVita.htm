Villa Bella Vita, North Shore, Providenciales, Turks and Caicos Vacation Rental

 

Named for the “The beautiful life”, Villa Bella Vita is located in the heart of the Turks & Caicos Islands, only 10 minutes from the airport and 5 minutes from Grace Bay.

Recently completed in Sept 2011, Villa Bella Vita is a very unique vacation property located on the island of Providenciales. Villa Bella Vita enjoys what is considered to be one of the best beaches in the world.

Situated in Turtle Cove on 120 feet of pristine ocean front beach, this 8000 square foot, 7 bedroom villa offers an exotic and exclusive luxury escape for up to 14 guests. This spacious, sophisticated villa, from its state of the art gourmet kitchen to the sumptuous bedroom / bathroom suites to the oversized pool terrace and infinity pool, has been furnished with the finest appointments from around the world.

No detail has been spared in the design and creation of this beachfront palace by Antonio Tadrissi B.Sc MArch of Prototype Design Lab. Upon entering the villa you will immediately notice the many rich, exotic design elements including the original hand carved wooden doors imported from India as well as a custom lace-patterned grand staircase.

These luxurious finishes are carried through out the villa and can be enjoyed in every room of the property
