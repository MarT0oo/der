Villa Lara, Casa de Campo, Dominican Republic Vacation Rental

 

A beautiful, modern and sophisticated seven bedroom villa located just steps away from Minitas Beach, the resort private beach.

As you walk in you are welcome with a a beautiful water feature that sits in the middle of the house. Open living area. Both dining room and Studio offer air conditioning.

Two large master suites featuring open plan bathrooms and courtyard. Each master suite provide internal access to the next room, ideal for families that want to share the space and be close. The fifth bedroom feature private bathroom with outside access to the swimming pool and garden.
