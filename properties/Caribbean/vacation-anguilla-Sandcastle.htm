Sandcastle, Limestone Bay, Anguilla, Caribbean Vacation Rental

 

Sandcastle Villa is an absolute dream home and its grandeur and exclusivity has delighted the most demanding guests, including A list celebrities. ​

When you are a guest at Sandcastle, our 24/7 concierge extraordinaire www.leviticuslifestyle.com will greet you at the ferry/airport and welcome you to our private escape. This newly built Villa is one of the few homes located directly on the beach, chairs and umbrellas await! Our staff will take care of your every need including cold towels on arrival, laundry service daily, and ensure that the villa is absolutely spotless at all times. You will have abundant and high quality linens, cookware, and tableware, and the staff will cut and arrange fresh flowers and coconuts. During your stay, you will have access to concierge service for booking all your activities including restaurants, boat charter, diving, spa, massage, private chef, and more.

Sandcastle is your private palace right by the sands of the Caribbean Sea. Welcome to sunny Anguilla, a magical island known for its watersports, nightlife and friendly local vibration. Take in the magnificent views of the ever changing hues of the Atlantic Ocean and tropical horizon right at your doorstep. An idyllic boutique villa awaits!

The outdoor oasis at Sandcastle includes an expansive second story infinity swimming pool with plenty of deck seating, beach chairs and umbrellas. The consummate entertainer won’t be able to resist whipping up some fabulous cocktails at the poolside bar with a stellar ocean view. Lovely alfresco dining can be a daily event with a barbeque and outdoor dining set under the shaded lanai. The children will love the fenced in and colorful playground with swings, slide, rock climbing wall and lookout fort. Indoors, you will find a media room and kiddie lounge with Xbox, Wii, movies, games and toys. The villa also includes and exercise room so you can keep on top of your workouts while away.

The vaulted ceilings and bright interiors foster an airy feeling throughout Sandcastle. The interior dining area makes a sophisticated setting in which to share a special announcement with your loved ones or entourage. The gourmet kitchen comes fully equipped with a center island breakfast bar. A black baby grand piano foreshadows moments of musical whimsy in the living area.

Four exquisite bedrooms with en-suite bathrooms accommodate up to eight guests at Sandcastle. A private stairway takes you to the grand master suite with a private balcony. The suite features a king size Tempur-Pedic mattress, a large down sofa for movie nights and an alfresco shower. All of the suites include air conditioning, ceiling fans and televisions.

While your villa sits right on the sands of the picturesque North Coast of Anguilla, you will find an excellent golf course just minutes away. In nearby Sandy Ground, “the entertainment capital of Anguilla,” you will discover a sleepy village by day that lights up at night with imaginative beach bars and hot night spots. Anguilla’s exceptional Caribbean beauty combined with an effortless living experience make the island an ideal getaway destination
