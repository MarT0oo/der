Villa Windsong, Cabarete, Puerto Plata, Dominican Republic Vacation Rental

 

Villa Windsong captures the essence of relaxation, making the quintessential beach home a reality. Lush tropical gardens filled with native fruits and countless palm trees provide an exclusive and private settings. Asian influences are seen in the raised wooden entrance and the outdoor walkways, as well as the beachfront gazebo facing the sunrise for yoga, reading or meditation. Native coralina floors containing dramatic shell shaped fossils paired with local river stone inlays inspire you to relax and live in harmony with the sound of the ocean.

Enjoy surround sound music and movies in the air-conditioned media room, or relax in the expansive great room with its living and dining areas featuring a twenty six foot ceiling. A gourmet kitchen with adjacent herb garden and BBQ overlooks the ocean. Unique features including the wood fired pizza oven and the beach side bonfire pit provide an oasis for casual entertaining. Surf, paddle surf, windsurf and snorkel from your own beach! For the water sport enthusiast, every detail has been thought of for equipment storage and usage. A tropical masterpiece where the line between outdoor and indoor living disappears creating an ambiance of nature and enjoyment rarely available in residences around the world. Truly, a unique and exotic vacation villa that doubles as a functional residence.
