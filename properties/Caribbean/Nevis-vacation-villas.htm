Visiting Nevis Island

Nevis is a wonderful Island in the West Indies, near St. Kits and St. Martin.  It’s a great place to escape and relax because it has not been spoiled like so many other destinations.  The lush greenery and the wildlife roaming through the trees are often seen as a sign of how Nevis takes the care of this precious jewel very seriously.  The blue waters are the most remarkable feature and draw your eye in every time.  The amazing ocean views that go on forever and the island hopping you can do while visiting really round out the vacation.  Learn to water-ski, paraglide, go snorkeling, surfing, or just relax on our white sandy beaches.
