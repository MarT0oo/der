Smugglers Cove 7, Barbados Vacation Rental


At Smugglers Cove, the elegance of times gone by has been reinterpreted for a postmodern world, enhanced by cool tropical breezes and panoramic sea views. Indulge in 6,200 sq ft of luxuriously comfortable living space, accented by the finest fixtures and finishes, including cool marble floors, vaulted ceilings and grand doorways, as well as a cutting-edge kitchen and lavish bathrooms. Leading designer Lynne Pemberton created the stunning classic-modern interiors. Italian loungers line the 60ft wide terrace, which also houses the private Jacuzzi pool. Enjoy impressive views of the Caribbean Sea, where the aquamarine horizon meets the bluest of skies.

Smugglers Cove is located on Paynes Bay - one of Barbados’ best beaches - just a short walk from the world-famous Sandy Lane Hotel. Swim, play and relax on the stunning white sands or in the tropically landscaped gardens. There is a wide range of amenities within a few minutes' drive, including boutique shopping, oceanfront dining and golf at the Sandy Lane and Royal Westmoreland golf clubs.
