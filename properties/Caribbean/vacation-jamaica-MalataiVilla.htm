Malatai Villa, Ocho Rios, Jamaica Vacation Rental

 

Malatai Villa is located in beautiful Ocho Rios on the north coast of Jamaica. A magnificent 10,000 ft², 8 bedrooms with 8 ensuite marble bathroom villa right on the glistening warm waters of the Caribbean Sea, only a 90-minute drive from Montego Bay.

The Villa is nestled in the midst of breathtaking gardens on an incredible natural white sand beach; surrounded by lush tropical flowers and greenery, this is truly your own piece of paradise…Private, yet situated only minutes away from the island’s best attractions and sites including: Dunn’s River Falls, Chukka Cove, Dolphin Cove, Duty-Free Shopping, and much more.

Malatai has a wonderful air-conditioned family room with flat-screen television with cable and Netflix and SONOS wireless speakers. There are also board games for adults and children. Downstairs are three bedrooms with private patios with sea views, and ensuite bathrooms. Upstairs the villa has 5 bedrooms overlooking the sea; with balconies and ensuite bathrooms. All bedrooms are air conditioned, and have ceiling fans, flat-screen TVs with cable, Netflix and SONOS speakers, telephones with intercoms, and safes. Bedside tables have electrical outlets and USB ports: bathrooms are also equipped with USB ports.

The grounds boast a stand-alone gym with a spectacular view of the ocean, fully equipped with A/C and ceiling fan, PRECOR treadmill, 3 top of the line Spinner NXT cycles, free weights, DVD’s and more.

Whether you go for a dip in the heated Jacuzzi or have the kids check out the bubble pool within the pool, this villa is suitable for the whole family or just an adult getaway, you’ll find everything you need at Malatai Villa to make this your best vacation ever.

The chef is ready to prepare mouthwatering home cooked meals from his beautiful granite and stainless-steel kitchen. Starting with your breakfast right through the day from hors d'oeuvres to delicious desserts, or as you desire.

The Ian Fleming International Airport in Boscobel is only 10 min from Ocho Rios and a 20 min drive to Malatai Villa. This exclusive airport is for private planes and charters only and ideal when staying at Malatai Villa.

…it’s time to relax, dream and enjoy.
