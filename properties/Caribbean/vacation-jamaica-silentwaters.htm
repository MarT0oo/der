Silent Waters, Montego Bay, Jamaica Vacation Rental

 

Silent Waters Villa Rental is a superb location for a family reunion, corporate retreat, destination wedding (seating 54 guests) or just a small group of friends seeking a most unusual holiday experience. Nestled in the hills of Jamaica, overlooking pristine forests and the spectacular turquoise waters of Montego Bay sits a truly remarkable private Jamaica resort property .... an enchanting heaven.

Silent Waters is one of the most glamorous luxury rental villas in Jamaica. This award-winning Jamaican holiday home is distinguished by world-class architecture, design and decor, breathtaking location, and exquisite accommodations and service.

The private tennis court on the pinnacle of the hilltop commands breathtaking views of the Caribbean, as well as the Great River which is the second largest river in Jamaica. Four PGA class golf courses are within a 30-minute drive. Several beaches are located within a 15-minute drive from the villa.

This luxury Jamaican villa rental includes the gracious staff - property manager, office assistant, head chef, assistant chef, butler / bartender, housekeepers / food servers, maintenance supervisor, gardeners and security guard… 14 in all.  The property is selected, perhaps more than any other private villa in Jamaica, by brides for a destination villa wedding. Additionally, other luxury villas are located within close proximity for hosting other guests within the wedding party.

Silent Waters Villa is 6 miles west of Montego Bay on the North Coast Road. Located on one of the highest mountaintops overlooking Montego Bay and the Caribbean Sea, the site consists of 18 acres. Twelve buildings, helicopter pad, private tennis court and two swimming pools occupy five acres. Five villa suites (9 air conditioned bedrooms) accommodate as many as 18 guests. With the addition of the owner’s private villa (1 air conditioned bedroom) up to 20 guests may be accommodated.

Villa discounts are frequently available, so be certain to contact us for your upcoming holiday or special events needs.

Pavilion

The main feature of the villa property is the pavilion. Here guests gather for friendly conversation, enjoy watching sports or a movie on the large screen television, delight in music from the baby grand piano, read or just take in the incredible views of Montego Bay. 100 feet of sliding glass doors disappear into pocket walls to open the central Pavilion to a breathtaking panorama of sea, sky and mountains.  The Pavilion opens to a marble terrace, infinity-edge swimming pool and sunning area overlooking the Caribbean Sea and Montego Bay.

A pair of 17th Century Buddhist temple attendant statues stand guard over the Main Pavilion to ensure guests’ peace and serenity… as well as an 18th Century Thai Bible Chest and 17th Century Burmese Buddhist monastery bell.  Paintings and other arts and antiquities adorn this main gathering area as well.

The Pavilion also includes a home theatre with LED (HD)/Internet TV and surround sound system , DVD, 200 CD – disk programmable surround-sound music system and Logitec IPOD docking station with speakers for those who want to bring their own music. Windows XP computer and wireless Internet is also available to guests.

Another special feature at Silent Waters is the Yamaha Disklavier DC2 II XG 5’8” baby grand piano of highly polished dark walnut.  The Mark II SG series system utilizes standard MIDI files on floppy disk or CD ROM.  This is the worldwide most acclaimed player piano … the same system composers and recording artists use to write and record.  Recording and composing software are available in the adjacent computer. 

A number of yoga groups have found Silent Waters Villa to be an ideal venue for their retreat in the Caribbean. The peace and serenity of the pavilion has complemented the activities of the yoga instructors making this a unique vacation villa experience.

Guest Suites

Each of the Guest Suites at Silent Waters Villa provides the utmost in comfort and luxury with a host of amenities. The suites are uniquely and individually designed and located to capture the best views. Guest Suites 1, 2 and 3 capture the pristine jungle forests and views of the Great River. At night it can become magical to see the fog and mist drifting down the river as a blanket only to be lifted high up in the valley into your bedroom by the gentle breezes. Guest Suites 4 and 5 provide views of the sea and Montego Bay.

Guest rooms consist of 5 individual detached luxury villa suites, each under separate roof, which can accommodate a total of 18 guests. Sleeping accommodations: 3 king-size beds, 6 queen-size beds, 1 queen-size sofa bed, 2 full size sofa beds.
