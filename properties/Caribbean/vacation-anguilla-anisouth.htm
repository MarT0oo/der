Ani South Villa, Anguilla, British West Indies Vacation Rental


Like an ivory sculpture on the majestic cliffs overlooking Anguilla’s Little Bay, the six bedroom Ani South has been designed to offer an unforgettable Caribbean vacation. Unique to Ani South is its configuration, accessible to guests via an elevator, roll-in showers, pool lift and wheelchair access – all subtly designed within the home’s conceptualization. At Ani South, the amenities of a world class resort and the privacy of an enclave combine to offer you the ultimate carefree and sun soaked holiday!

Have a splash in the heated infinity swimming pool. Or perhaps a soothing soak in the dramatic cliff-edge hot tub? Other resort-themed perks include a roof lounge, golf carts, mountain bikes, pool lift and a night-lit tennis court with cabana lounge. A gas grill sets the tone for exquisite alfresco dining. Indoors, you will find a home theatre, a shared gym, billiards table and games room. Wi-Fi access and baby equipment are also provided. Ani’s impeccable service will have you hooked. The staff includes a breakfast chef, master chef, two butlers, a cocktail specialist and a tennis instructor. Beach bags are packed with snorkel gear and the staff ‘magically’ get there before you to set up. How about a gourmet meal with a white-linen dining table right by the ocean?

The sleek furnishings and contemporary finishes provide clear views of the Caribbean Sea from almost every angle at Ani South. Wake up to breathtaking sea and sky hues from your bedroom each new morning. The interiors are clean, bright and modern, with naturally hewn wooden furniture serving as accent pieces throughout. Breeze through indoors and out for a casually elegant island experience!

The six stunning bedrooms at Ani South accommodate up to twelve guests. All of the bedrooms feature en-suite bathrooms, air conditioning and ceiling fans. All three master bedrooms (with direct outdoor access) as well as the indoor and outdoor living spaces are oriented towards the breathtaking western view down the Anguilla coastline and towards evening sunsets.

The experience at Ani South is further enhanced by Anguilla’s unsurpassed collection of shockingly white sand beaches, crystal clear turquoise waters, an amazing assortment of local and fine dining restaurants alongside a warm and welcoming local population. Anguilla delivers sophisticated luxury travel while retaining its laid back island charm. The cliff-edge wedding deck also makes Ani a great venue for special events. Come experience a fresh tropical tempo!
