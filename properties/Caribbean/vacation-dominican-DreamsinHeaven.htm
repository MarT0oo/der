Dreams in Heaven, Casa de Campo, Dominican Republic Vacation Rental

 

Dreams in Heaven in Casa de Campo Dominican Republic is a mix of the beautiful Spanish feeling and the amazing local architecture. Beautiful bright colors and lovely amenities make this a one of a kind experience. Bring your entire family and feel right at home. A curated home from our new Casa De Campo Collection.

A true Casa de Campo villa. The beautiful five bedroom villa was built right in the middle of a beautiful bamboo forest, palms and Embauba trees in a five thousand square meter homesite.

The villa is divided in three pavilions where the main one feature the formal social/dining area and the other two hold the bedrooms. As you enter you are surrounded by lush vegetation along each corridor. The serenity and atmosphere of the villa made an special habitat for birds where you can hear them sing every morning.

A very private setting where you can relax en enjoy the beautiful views of the swimming pool, perfect garden and eleventh hole of the Teeth of the Dog Golf Course. Great staff.
