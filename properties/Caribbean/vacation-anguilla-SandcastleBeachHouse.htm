Sandcastle Beach House, Limestone Bay, Anguilla, Caribbean Vacation Rental

 

Located directly on the beach, Sandcastle Beach House is the perfect spot for active families that don’t want to miss one minute of vacation time indoors! With kayaks, paddle boards, bikes, and a “toes in the sand” location, this is true beach living. The ocean is your pool, and at the end of an active day the jacuzzi will sooth your muscles. We have indoor entertainment covered as well with a shuffleboard table, card table, huge eat in kitchen, long table dining for family dinners, and lots of comfy seating for reading or watching movies on the 65” TV, complete with Sonos surround sound…and we think you will agree, the views from inside cannot be beat!

SBH is designed to attract those who are more focused on being active, and connecting with family through the organic experience of living on the beach. The home is very well appointed, with 3 king size bedrooms, en-suite bathrooms, large comfortable seating inside and out, and the great room concept is conducive to making lasting family memories!

It’s no coincidence that just next door to SBH sits Sandcastle Villa!(www.anguillasandcastle.com). Sandcastle Villa and SBH can be rented together, for the ultimate multigenerational family reunion, small wedding, anniversary, or any other reason you can think of to celebrate life! The two homes together sleep 16 guests, and with both homes you will be in heaven with the kids running between the homes on the beach, jumping in the pool, playing on the playground or in the kids Xbox lounge in the evenings…the beach can be lit up from both properties allowing you to maximize your beach time!

For special events or simply an action packed week of fun with the whole family, let us plan all your activities, chefs, spa services, beach bonfires, beach barbecues or whatever you dream up to make your own memories…we will do our best to make it happen here!
