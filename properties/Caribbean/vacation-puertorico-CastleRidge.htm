Castle Ridge, Humacao, Puerto Rico Vacation Rental

 

This is a beautifully renovated luxury home located on the cliffs of the resort with panoramic views of the Caribbean. Tastefully decorated in an eclectic mix of Caribbean and Southern decor, relaxation can be found in every room of this home.

The seven bedrooms; all with their own bathrooms, provide plenty of room to comfortably sleep 16 people. The bunk room off the game room allows a great nook for the teens or children. The dining room has plenty of seating area or you can enjoy a coffee on one of the many outdoor decks.

With a game room , multiple entertainment areas and TV's, a private pool, jacuzzi, and out door cooking area, you could spend your entire vacation on this property. You may find yourself napping in one of the dens, gazing over the Caribbean sunset on the patio or enjoying a movie on the 60" TV in the game room. Maybe some family ping pong, a swim in the pool or some karaoke for the kids is more your taste. Or; take a ride through the resort on the Golf Cart to one of the many restaurants. Either way this Puerto Rico Vacation Rental provides plenty to do.
