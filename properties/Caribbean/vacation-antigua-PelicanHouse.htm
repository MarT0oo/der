Pelican House, Soldiers Bay, Blue Waters, Antigua, Caribbean Vacation Rental

 

On the North West corner of Antigua among 17 acres of tropical hibiscus and palm trees, Blue Waters resort perches alongside the Caribbean’s radiant turquoise sea. Dainty pagoda roofs and white parasols line the sandy bay, making up the multiple award winning and one of the longest standing luxury resorts in the Caribbean.

To experience the full royal treatment Blue Waters has to offer, Pelican House is a good place to start. Available as either three or six bedroom accommodation, Pelican House is both luxurious and spacious with outstanding views of the Caribbean Sea. The décor is crisp and airy, made up of immaculate white linens and cool pale stone floors given a contemporary lift by distressed wood furniture and glass panelling. Outside, the private pool and generously sized patio are perfect for social gatherings as the sun sets over the ocean, and for enjoying breakfast in the sunshine the next morning as it rises.

Whilst staying in Pelican House, staff are always on hand to help you with every need, and guests are even issued with a mobile phone on arrival to contact the Blue Waters team from anywhere in the resort. However, you are also welcome to enjoy all the hotel facilities on the resort, including the cliff top Cove Restaurant, and the regular favourite Beach Bar which serves a fantastic selection of cocktails to sip on whilst relaxing on the sandy bay.
