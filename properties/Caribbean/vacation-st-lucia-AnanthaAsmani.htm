Anantha Asmani, Cap Estate, St. Lucia Vacation Rental


At the top of the cliffs, situated in Mount du Cap in Cap Estate is the exclusive private residence called Anantha Asmani.

This stunning 4 bedroom villa is part of the Mount Du Cap development, a private and gated community.

This exquisite villa has been designed to capture the essence of Caribbean lifestyle yet exudes the elegance of modern day life.

Anantha offers seclusion, tranquility and beauty in its surrounds. The direct translation, Infinite Blue is indicative of the views one can enjoy from Anantha.

The panoramic view of the ocean is breath-taking and never compromised. The infiniti pool and patio area are strategically located to maximize on these views and afford stunning views out to Martinique and out over Rodney Bay and its surrounds.

At Villa Anantha, nature never takes a holiday. It is the epitome of relaxation and tranquility where one can experience sunsets each evening in the privacy of Anantha.

Ideally situated, Anantha enjoys easy access to the surrounding services and amenities of various hotels, beaches, golfing and much more.

Only a 10 minute drive away, is Rodney Bay which is the hub of activity where on can enjoy fine dining, a casino, duty free shopping, banking, bars and nightlife.

Ideal for couples and young adults, Anantha has 4 stylish bedrooms, featuring 1 king and 3 queen bedrooms, all boasting beautiful sea views

Each bedroom is beautifully appointed and comes with its own ensuite bathroom, each cleverly designed to afford maximum comfort and convenience.

The amenities of Anantha include Wi-Fi internet access, cable vision, washer and dryer, plasma TV, DVD and CD player to mention a few.
