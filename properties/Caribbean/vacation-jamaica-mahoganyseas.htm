Sea Salt Villa, Montego Bay, Jamaica Vacation Rental


A brand new addition to the exclusive Tryall Club, Sea Salt is one of the most welcoming private homes that grace the northern coast of this Caribbean paradise. Inside, you'll find classic Jamaican architecture and 7,700 ft² of living, dining and entertaining space. Outside, you're surrounded by 5.4 private acres, meticulously landscaped with native trees and indigenous plants.


Elegant amenities await you at every turn ... Italian travertine walkways and baths, native mahogany flooring, Italian marble vanity tops, flat screen TVs, wireless internet connectivity in every room, a high quality sound system and Sirus satellite radio. There's even a 1,500 square foot practice putting green, two Sea Salt golf carts, and a regulation shuffleboard court.


The expansive main living area is perfectly designed for relaxation and entertaining. From here, the main veranda opens to a 25' x 70' pool with three infinity edge sides and fiber optic lighting for evening enjoyment.
This five-bedroom home has three bedroom suites, each with its own luxurious master suite featuring a king sized bed, private veranda and private bath. Two of the suites also feature an adjoining guest bedroom with a king or twin beds, private veranda and private bath with a tub and shower. One suite has an outdoor garden shower off the master bedroom, while another features a private sitting room that's perfect for children or nanny.


Near the center of the home is a full commercial kitchen staffed by our own professional chef to cater to your dietary needs and gourmet desires. A private herb garden adds just the right seasoning to the island's abundant fresh fruits, vegetables and seafood.


It's been said, "There's no place like home." After visiting Sea Salt, you may choose to differ.
As part of the Tryall Club, one of Jamaica's premier private clubs, our guests enjoy all the privileges, privacy and security of membership. Tryall offers a broad range of activities and amenities, including an 18-hole, par-72 championship course designed by Ralph Plummer and former host to such prestigious international events as the Johnnie Walker World Championship.


Nine tennis courts, (two lighted for night play), a pro shop, hitting partners, instructors and weekly tournaments greet the tennis enthusiast.


If you're looking for pure relaxation, Tryall offers a pristine beach with beachside cafe, while the Great House Restaurant offers breakfast, high tea, candlelight dining and is a favorite gathering place in late evening.
The fitness center features the latest in exercise equipment plus an activities guru who leads power walks, yoga, Aquacise, Reggae lessons and more.


And just because a little pampering never hurt anyone, Tryall has its own salon and massage center offering a full range of beauty and spa treatments.


Because the Tryall Club offers such a wide variety of activities and amenities, many guests never leave our grounds during their stay. Others choose to visit duty-free shops and restaurants and venture into nearby Montego Bay, or afar to Negril's stunning beaches, or excursions to nearby waterfalls and other points of natural beauty.
