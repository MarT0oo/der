Cinnamon Reef Mansion, Anguilla, British West Indies Vacation Rental


Set within a private tropical garden of approximately 1.5 acres, Cinnamon Reef Mansion is the perfect holiday hideaway. Located within idyllic Little Harbour, the villa is sheltered by Cinnamon Reef, providing unrivalled panoramic views of the stunning Caribbean sea and the islands of St Bart’s and St Martin. Within the sea-side garden there is a dining pavilion with seating for 10 and barbeque dining area on a private beach with beautiful white sand and direct access to the crystal clear sea.

Built with loving attention and comprising of 10 Bedrooms with en-suite bathrooms, tennis court, pavilion and gym, this beautiful villa is a unique place to relax and enjoy the ultimate in island living. Indigo is truly a Caribbean paradise.

About  the Mansion:

The Villa is arranged over three buildings (the Main House, the Courtyard Garden House and Little Indigo) which are linked by the some of the most beautiful gardens in Anguilla. It boasts a wonderful selection of dining and relaxation facilities and is a private and perfect location for either a family or a romantic vacation.
