Villa Julia, Las Terrenas, Dominican Republic Vacation Rental

 

Set on a small hill overlooking the sea, a few steps from the village of Las Terrenas, Villa Julia is the perfect hide-away for the most exacting ones, seeking the perfect balance between the quietness of an exuberant nature, the freedom feeling of a wide view over the ocean and the excitement of being in the middle of one of the most beautiful paradise of beaches of the Caribbean.

Located in Las Terrenas, on the north coast of Dominican Republic, Villa Julia is a luxury hideaway, nested in an outstanding environment, far from the excess of mass tourism.   This ancient small fishermen village has grown up smoothly since its discovery by the French 30 years ago, and still offers its wonders to its guests without losing any charm or beauty.

This little dream village, elected by trendy people, offers to upscale and aware guests the beauty of its immaculate beaches, the authenticity of its fishermen village and the luxury of some of its most researched villas; but all of this comes also with a natural and relaxed atmosphere and friendly contact with the inhabitants.

A few steps away from the village and its shops (the Paseo is at about 400 meters), Villa Julia is located on a small hill overlooking the ocean and the village houses, at an altitude of about 100 meters; it will easily host up to 10 guests in the greatest comfort, among a contemporary luxurious decor which combines sobriety and refinement.
