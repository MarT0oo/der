Villa Gordon, Hideaway Beach, Cabarete, Dominican Republic


Villa Gordon is a very new and beautifully decorated luxury villa located in the secure, gated community of Hideaway Beach. As the name implies, Hideaway Beach is a secluded expanse of paradise located between Cabarete and Sosua. Villa Gordon is a stunning vacation villa rental providing six (6) spacious bedrooms, each with en suite bathroom, elegantly furnished and fully air conditioned.

 

Getting to Hideaway Beach is easy; just walk out the back door and down onto the beach! Sweeping away in both directions is an excellent golden sand beach. Play, swim or stroll the beach and enjoy the unspoiled, pristine nature of the Dominican Republic. Villa Gordon is American owned and boasts many top-of-the-line products throughout the villa. Each bedroom offers its own television set and the main media room has a 61” flat screen. Relax, unwind and keep up with the games back home while you enjoy the Dominican Republic.

 

Another bonus, Villa Gordon is literally next door to Villa Celeste. Rent them both for a very large family getaway and save a fortune over comparable hotel rooms. Villa vacations are rapidly becoming the most popular, visit these villas to learn why. Hideaway Beach is located only about 25 minutes from the Puerto Plata (POP) international airport. Located between Sosua and Cabarete, Hideaway Beach has easy access to both cities including all of their amenities. Banks, shopping, beauty parlors, spas, restaurants, bars, discos, casinos and much more are only a few minutes from your front door.

 

Villa Gordon offers a professional manager, onsite security and daily maid services dedicated to helping you plan and enjoy the vacation of a lifetime. Spread out and enjoy this wonderful oceanfront villa rental. If you need anything, from food to excursions, just ask the villa management and they will be happy to assist you.

 

Price is for villa rental only. Additional staff, such as a private chef, driver and villa concierge, is available for a nominal additional fee. Spa services, shopping services and more are also available for additional fees. Staff gratuity is required. Please inquire for more information on any additional services.
