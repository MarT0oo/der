Castillo Del Mar, Cabrera, Dominican Republic Vacation Rental

 

Castillo del Mar is situated atop a 60 foot cliff overlooking the ocean with steps down to a secluded 600 foot white sand beach. It is a magnificent 16,500 square foot, three story Spanish Colonial style, villa with a wedding chapel and resort swimming pool measuring 85'X65'. Enjoy incredible oceanviews and sunsets. As you walk through the front door, the spectacular ocean views, and the spacious great room with 15 foot cathedral ceilings will be the first thing to wow you. As you explore more, you will find that the custom furnishing and decoration will provide the finishing touches to truly make your vacation a memorable one.

The villa offers 10 bedrooms and can comfortably accommodate 25 guests. Villa Castillo del Mar boasts 8 master suites, and 2 junior suites featuring 5 king and 6 queen beds and one twin bed. All of the bedrooms have Flat screen TV's and all have luxury 800 thread count Egyptian Cotton sheets and 900GSM Egyptian Cotton bath towels. All the bedrooms are air-conditioned. The dining room comfortably seats 24 people with plenty of outdoor seating for al fresco dining. Perfect for weddings, entertaining and large gatherings, this grand residence is spacious and well-proportioned.

The villa boasts every imaginable luxury and amenity. The luxury spa includes 2 massage, facial rooms with 4 massage tables, manicure pedicure station, and hair station. The Gym room includes 2 treadmills, one elliptical machine, free weights, excursive balls and bands, and full Golds Gym weight station. The game room includes a football table, table tennis, and shuffleboard table.

Neighborhood
