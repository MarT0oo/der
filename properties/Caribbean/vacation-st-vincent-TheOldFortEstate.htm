The Old Fort Estate, Mt. Pleasant, St Vincent and the Grenadines Vacation Rental

 

The Old Fort is the only fully restored historic plantation in St. Vincent & the Grenadines which can be rented for various different sized groups and functions. Constructed with cobblestones and wood beams in the classic French-Mediterranean mansion-house style, it is set high on the top of Mt. Pleasant and offers sweeping views overlooking the Grenadine Islands that stretch far Southward into the distance. The estate grounds fall down to the ocean with its golden meadows billowing with hay and tropical breezes that flow from every corner. The kitchen draws on fresh local produce and is famous for its fantastic cuisine with the dining area that turns into a magical setting at night.

Lush grounds are covered by numerous species of palms and fruit trees that surround one of the most spectacular pools you will ever see, framed by bougainvillea and a profusion of tropical exotica. Recommended over the years by numerous international media it has been transformed into the kind of property you will never want to leave. This exclusive estate is ideal for guests seeking a relaxed laid-back atmosphere, whilst at the same time is still conveniently located only a short distance from the best beaches and places of activity. Together with its amazing team of employees it forms the center of an incredible experience that allows one a vacation of a lifetime!

The Old Fort consists of various spacious suites and can be booked for various different groups of different sizes. It comes with a large kitchen with an inside bar and lounge area, a magnificent infinity swimming pool with kids pool, a large circular outside bar, acres upon acres of magnificent gardens, a variety of various outdoor & indoor dining areas, and its very own private powerboat.

The Sugar House (2 Bedroom Private Villa)

The Sugar House is a luxurious property steeped within the walls of an 18th Century Sugar mill that is part of the Old Fort Estate and included in the rental rate. It features several grand ensuite bedrooms, a spacious open plan dining & living area, a modern kitchen, a magnificent infinity swimming pool with kids pool (next door, upon request), luxurious private gardens and BBQ facilities.v Additional Services:
