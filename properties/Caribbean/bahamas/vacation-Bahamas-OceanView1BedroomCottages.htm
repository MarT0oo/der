Ocean View 1-Bedroom Cottages, Harbour Island, Bahamas Vacation Rental

 

Imagine your own private cottage on a spacious estate with an ocean view so spectacular, it looks like you can reach out and touch it. Close—you are just a private pathway away from your own piece of paradise—our dazzling pink sands beach. All enjoyed from your private 600-square foot patio.

Whether you choose the one- or two-bedroom option, you are always overlooking the stunning azure waters of the Atlantic Ocean. The one-bedroom ocean view is perfect for two adults. The two-bedroom, two-bath ocean view cottages is spacious enough for four adults, and features two full bathrooms and a sitting room lounge area that separates the bedrooms.

A jewel of the Bahamas. Lush haven. Secluded tropical sanctuary.

Tucked away in 20 acres of a flourishing garden-like setting on tiny Harbour Island is the legendary Pink Sands Resort. Private winding palm-shaded paths meander their way through lush rambling gardens full of flowering hibiscus, frangipani, and fruit trees, leading to 25 exquisite one-story luxury cottages. Each one is characterized by a British Colonial, old Bahamas aesthetic with a beachy feel, modern amenities, and first-class service. We grow a lot of our own fresh fruit, vegetables, and herbs in an organic garden right here on the property, which then make their way to your plate at our iconic restaurants.
