Lily Pad, Harbour Island, Bahamas Vacation Rental

 

Perched above Harbour Island’s magnificent Pink Sands Beach, this four-bedroom, four-bathroom home designed by the award-wining design team consisting of de la Guardia Victoria Architects and Urbanists and Lindroth Design Company fuses the classic elements of Caribbean-Colonial style with modern technology and conveniences. In addition to a living room, there are two oversized covered terraces for entertaining and relaxing. Each terrace is connected to the living area, to provide seamless indoor and outdoor living.

Lily Pad employs an inverted floor plan to maximize views. The Kitchen, Great Room, and Master Suite which has a king-sized bed and private bathroom, are all on the second floor. The ground floor houses two guest suites with king-sized beds and their own bathroom, one guest suite with two twin beds that can be configured to a king-sized bed if required, a living area, bathroom and laundry room. There is also a spacious patio surrounding a private swimming pool, and one of the world’s best beaches is just steps away.

About The Area

Harbour Island, in the Bahamas, is one of the most celebrated destinations in the Caribbean. On the Atlantic side of the island are the famous pink sand beaches. A three-mile stretch of indescribable beauty, the sand here is truly pink. Sifted and smoothed by generations of waves, the sands are coloured by finely crushed coral. A light rose blush by day, they become a vibrant pink at sunset. With its gentle sands and the ribbons of blue in the sea, it is often called the world’s most beautiful beach.
