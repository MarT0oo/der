Lindon Villa, Fowl Cay, Exumas, Bahamas, Private Island Vacation Rental

 

Designed for honeymooners or as a romantic retreat for two, Lindon is spacious and located idyllically on a low ridge above North Beach. Open-plan, bright, breezy and framed with large windows and glass doors, Lindon is a breath of fresh air. The kitchen area features a welcoming breakfast bar, and the living-dining area is well-furnished with all the home creature comforts you'd expect.

The large back terrace overlooks spectacular powder-white sands, the sand bars of Gaulin Cay and an unusual chain of limestone-coral cliffs across the bay. Tied to the trees, a hammock, will rock you to sleep or follow the wooden steps which lead down to your very own private shoreline.

The Villas at Fowl Cay Resort

Dotted around the Eastern perimeter of Fowl Cay are six gorgeous villas for you to use as your home away from home in the Exumas. Our one, two and three-bedroom villas all have fully equipped kitchens that are stocked with breakfast, lunch and snack items before your arrival (all included in the price). All have ocean views and are lined with French or sliding glass doors that open to beautiful patios overlooking the breathtaking water below.

Each villa includes your own personal motor boat (gas too!), a golf cart and use of all the equipment on the island. The boat allows you get out and explore the beautiful central Exumas while Fowl Cay provides the ultimate home base to return to at the end of the day. You make your own breakfast, our chef makes you a picnic lunch to take on your boat, your villa host packs a cooler and brings your fresh clean towels and you're are off for a day on the water! Enjoy fishing, trawling, snorkeling, water sports, and more. When you return, jump in your luxurious shower and head up to the Hill House for cocktail hour and a gourmet dinner of local seafood and delicious desserts. It's all there for you to enjoy and we are there to make it as relaxing and enjoyable as possible.

Offers wheelchair access. Access to the restaurant can be made from the rear service road by golf cart to avoid the stairs at the front entrance. Access to Fowl Cay is made by boat and that there is no easy way to get from the dock onto and off the boat again, the resort cannot be held responsible for any injuries that may occur as a result.

All of the villas have fully stocked and equipped kitchens that are stocked with food for breakfast, lunch and snacks before you arrive. When you make your reservation we will send you a list of foods and drinks available in your villa. You will also be able to request any special items that are not on the list. We always do our best to accommodate special requests but occasionally due to the remoteness of the island we are not able to provide all special request items. For dinner we eat in the Hill House every night of the week. Cocktail hour is at 6:30pm a great meeting spot to mingle with some of the other guests or enjoy the stunning sunsets.

Everything you eat and drink while on the Cay is included in the price. All the food and beverages stocked in your villa in addition to all the food and drink served to you at the Hill House for dinner is included; House wines included and premium wines are available at an additional cost. Your cupboards and fridge will be restocked at no extra charge.

Getting to the Island

There are many options for travel to our Exumas private resort. Scheduled flights, as well as private chartered flights are available direct from Ft. Lauderdale, Florida (1h 30 minutes) or Nassau, Bahamas (40 minutes) into Staniel Cay Airstrip. Once on the ground at Staniel Cay airstrip (a mere 1.25 miles from Fowl Cay), a member of our resort team will meet you personally, take care of your luggage and bring you to the dock, where you will be transferred by a breathtaking 7 minute boat ride to the Cay.

Airport transfers for guests arriving from Staniel Cay airstrip to Fowl Cay Resort are complimentary. You are also welcome to travel to Fowl Cay with your own boat. Private planes may land at Staniel Cay's 3,030 ft. paved airstrip. You may also charter a sea plane near out resort.

Please note that check in time is 3 p.m. and check out time is 10 a.m. - Please plan your inbound and outbound charter flights accordingly.
