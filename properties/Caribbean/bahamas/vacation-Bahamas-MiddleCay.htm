Middle Cay, Private Island, Bahamas, Caribbean Vacation Rental

 

Middle Cay is a luxurious, private island just off the coast of Harbour Island. With a coconut tree grove, two villas, and daily housekeeping. This two-acre island combines five-star amenities with a secluded location for the ultimate Caribbean getaway. The island has two docks, so guests can dock their sailboats, yachts, or rentals with ease. One dock even has an automatic boat lift.

A working lighthouse adds nautical charm, a walkway winds around the entire island, and a fire pit makes s’mores a cinch. Fishing gear, a canoe, a standup paddle board, and three kayaks are perfect for spending the day on the sea. The surrounding islands are known for their pink sand beaches, coral reefs, and shipwrecks, so take advantage of Middle Cay’s snorkeling equipment to discover an underwater paradise. Our 22 foot boat is yours daily for 7 hours, after that you pay an hourly rate for our captain. You are responsible for the fuel. Harbour Island, which offers jet ski, boat, bicycle, and golf cart rentals, as well as several restaurants and beaches. We offer complementary water tubing, water skiing, feed the pigs in Meeks Patch, go underwater diving with a Yamaha Sea Scooter, or tour nearby islands.

Middle Cay has two homes, and both have WiFi and air-conditioning. The Main House has an open kitchen and dining space with stainless steel appliances, granite countertops, and a dining table that seats 8. Its living room has a flatscreen cable TV and opens to a private patio. Outside, there’s a heated pool and an outdoor shower.

In the Cottage, there’s a common area with beautiful, exposed wooden beams and tile floors. The living room has plush furnishings and a flatscreen cable TV, and there’s dining at the bar and main table. It opens to the patio and has three walls of windows that reveal ocean views. The kitchen has stainless steel appliances, a coffeemaker, and a mixer. The Main House has four bedrooms and the Cottage has three bedrooms. In the Main House, there’s a spacious master suite with a king bed, three walls of ocean views, a private patio, a lounge, and an ensuite bathroom.

The second suite has two double beds, a window seat, and an ensuite bathroom. The third suite has two twin beds, ocean views, and a hall bath. The fourth suite has a king bed and an ensuite bathroom. In the Cottage, there’s a king suite with exterior access, ocean views, and an ensuite bathroom. The second suite has two double beds, exterior access, and an ensuite bathroom. The third suite is ideal for children, with a twin bunk, trundle bed, and a half bath in the hall.
