Serendip Cove Bahamas, Luxury Rental Bahamas


In the heart of New Providence Bahamas, only 10 minutes from Nassau is Lyford Cay where you will find Serendip Cove.. A magical home tucked into the exclusive corner of Clifton Bay, with more than 600 feet of beachfront, this private estate is filled with character and charm.

The splendor of the tropical gardens combined with the luxuriously appointed accommodations provide guests with the finest traditions of island living enhanced by its proximity to world class clubs such as Lyford Cay, Old Fort and Albany house. This is an exclusive location for those looking to be near everything but still privately tucked away as the owners had intended for their own use. The large spacious rooms make the stay enjoyable and the beautiful views day and night give the house a magical feeling.

The pool cottages, next to the pool and ocean, have eight private rooms, each with its own bathroom. More tropical in style then the main house, the pool cottages have a well equipped kitchen, family room with cable TV, games, fireplace, ceiling fans, wireless internet, out-door dining (on a covered porch) and an out-door shower. This is a traditional and beautiful Bahamian home, you will not want for anything more.

This house is a one if a Kind, the owners utilize the property annually for their own stays. During your stay you will be taken care of by a very professional and courteous staff who have your best vacation in hand. No where else in the Bahamas do you have a house that can accommodate a large group and have positive reviews by guests, often not prepared for the level of relaxation.
