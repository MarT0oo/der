Ocean House, Harbour Island, Bahamas Vacation Rental

 

Ocean House welcomes you with fully air-conditioned spacious rooms, and a private swimming pool with plenty of outdoor patio space to relax and enjoy. The main house and cottage has over 2,500 square feet with interior design by Amanda Lindroth, perfectly aligned with the island feel of comfort, breeze and accents of pink.

The main house has two king bedrooms with en-suite bathrooms, one on each floor, with direct access to the veranda. In addition, each level provides a large living room, which opens up to 1,110 square feet of outdoor space of patio and private pool where you can take in open views of the beach and ocean. The main level provides an open-concept full kitchen with modern appliances, prep-island, and wine cellar. Other amenities include washer/dryer and guest powder room on this level. Only steps away, the adjacent pool cottage has one ocean view king bedroom and garden view room with twin beds with their own patio.

Ocean House has private access to the beach and an outdoor shower. There is Wi-Fi throughout the house. Housekeeping is provided twice daily and a full time concierge is on staff to accommodate any additional requests.

About The Area

Harbour Island, in the Bahamas, is one of the most celebrated destinations in the Caribbean. On the Atlantic side of the island are the famous pink sand beaches. A three-mile stretch of indescribable beauty, the sand here is truly pink. Sifted and smoothed by generations of waves, the sands are coloured by finely crushed coral.
