African Sapphire, Anguilla, Caribbean Vacation Rental

 

The African Sapphire villa, a 8 bedroom villa is ideal for those looking for two Master Suites. The living and dining space is ideal for a group of friends, one or two families vacationing together, or other larger group.

The 14,000 ft² villa is named for its furnishings, including a chandelier and table centerpiece handmade in Africa from half a million beads.

As you enter the main room you will be amazed at the sheer size with luscious views of the Caribbean Sea and St Martin from all directions. A dining room for 18 people is one of the focal points to the room that has many varied areas for relaxing, talking to you friends or just resting your eyes while listing to music from the resort musical collection. As you exit the building to the balcony you will be see your private 18' x 45' pool with a bridge extending from the 2nd floor over the center of the pool complete with a floating Jacuzzi for an experience to remember.

The entertainment pieces in the villa are of the highest quality. The hand made stainless steel Blatt billiard table is one of the highlights of the room complete with a home theatre offering a TV, DVD, Video projector system bringing movies and sporting events to life for all to enjoy. In addition to the adult areas, the villa has a children's play room to keep the younger ones challenged and having fun.

Relax and enjoy the stereo system throughout the villa on 23 speakers throughout 7 regions with a CD library with thousands of titles. You can listen to Island Music or your favorite song in the Entertainment Area while your travel companion is enjoying jazz poolside.

There are two Master Bedrooms, each decorated with all the finest amenities. The en-suite bathrooms are surrounded by carefully selected Travertine marble placed in this architecturally exciting bathroom. As you immerse yourself in the Jacuzzi for two you can watch television, listen to music, enjoy the walk-in Shower also designed to accommodate a couple. The skylights create natural lighting and stargazing from the Jacuzzi.

Each Master Suite has an attached fourth floor balcony that includes a private bar area, office or a optional crib for babies. The complete wet bar has marble finishes and offers exceptional views of St. Martin and the beach area. The office is complete with a personal computer including Internet access and printer. The office also includes an in villa fax machine to send and receive confidential faxes.

The four guest bedrooms each have direct views of the sea and St Martin and have a full en-suite bathroom swathed in Travertine marble and a specially designed vertical spa system and tub designed for two. Each bedroom has a TV with DVD and Internet access.

The villa includes a self contained gym featuring all the newest equipment.
