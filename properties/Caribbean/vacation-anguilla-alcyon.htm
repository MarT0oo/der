Alcyon, Anguilla, British West Indies Vacation Rental


Alcyon is a magnificent 8000 square foot 5 bedroom Mediterranean-style villa located seaside on the beautiful island of Anguilla. Its location allows for spectacular sunrises and sunsets to be enjoyed from the expansive travertine terraces surrounding the stunning infinity edge swimming pool. From every vantage point of Alcyon, the unique design provides breathtaking views of the Caribbean Sea, the islands of St. Martin, St. Barths, Saba and St. Kitts, while capturing the prevailing tropical trade winds of this undiscovered paradise.


Alcyon's elegantly designed interior features a grand two story living area, exotic hardwood ceilings, custom Italian metalwork, travertine tiling as well as marble and granite surfaces throughout. You will experience elegant, yet comfortable, indoor and outdoor living and dining areas, two indulgent master bedroom suites with whirlpools and grand walk-in closets, two guest bedrooms, state of the art appliances in a "cook's kitchen", and luxurious bed and bath linens. Alcyon also offers an expansive ocean view suite with kitchen ideal for guests requiring complete privacy or separate accommodations for a nanny, chef, etc.


Alcyon includes a mid-size car rental, complimentary continental breakfast, 24 hour property management, maid service, gardening and swimming pool services. The following services may also be arranged - transportation, including airport pick up, additional car rental, chef, masseuse, personal trainer, childcare services, tour guide and other personal services. Alcyon awaits you - please inquire how to enjoy your quiet elegance by the sea and reserve your private Caribbean getaway in Seafeathers Bay, Anguilla.

Pool and Terraces

Alcyon's unique location allows for spectacular sunrises and sunsets to be enjoyed from a total of 1,400 square feet of natural travertine tiled covered terraces and sun deck surrounding the stunning infinity edge swimming pool. For comfort and elegance, the area is completed with Lloyd & Flanders designer furnishings and includes large lounge chairs, ottomans, dining, and chaises.

Exterior

Alcyon is a beautiful 8000 square foot Mediterranean style villa located on the waterfront. Upon entering its grand double mahogany doors, Alcyon's unique design provides breathtaking views of the Caribbean Sea, the islands of St. Martin, St. Barths, Saba and St. Kitts, while capturing the prevailing tropical trade winds.

Living Area & Mezzanine

This grand two story living area features an exotic hardwood ceiling highlighted by a stunning bronze chandelier that showcases the custom Italian railing with solid mahogany handrail along the mezzanine. The living area's 16' opening provides an open air feel along with spectacular views of the Caribbean and neighboring islands. The room is tastefully designed in an elegant and inviting West Indies style of dark woods and golden highlights.

Dining Room

Elegant seaside dining room with 180 degrees of ocean views. Furnished with conversation in mind, the dining room features a round deep walnut dining table for 10, Ficks Reed rattan and woven wicker buffet, travertine tiling, terrace with Caribbean hardwood pergola and spectacular sunsets.

Kitchen

A Mediterranean inspired kitchen framed by stately columns and archways, featuring ocean views to the west, east and south toward the adjoining Caribbean islands, as well as ample space to accommodate work areas for several cooks. It features custom cherry hardwood cabinetry, granite countertops, a spacious island which seats three, a six burner dual-fuel professional range, large refrigerator, beverage cooler, dishwasher, and microwave.

Entertainment Room

An intimate air conditioned entertainment room designed in the colors and natural woods of the Caribbean with a wide screen tv, internet and netphone

Main Level Master Bedroom Ensuite

Seaside main level king size master bedroom ensuite with furnished covered balcony. Ivory, teal and sand decor, custom natural woven shades, luxury linens, full dressing room, double marble vanity, whirlpool, custom cabinetry, and large sea glass and travertine open shower.

Upper Level Master Bedroom Ensuite

Seaside upper level king size master bedroom ensuite with furnished covered balcony. Ivory, tan and hibiscus pink decor, custom natural woven shades, luxury linens, full dressing room, double marble vanity, whirlpool, custom cabinetry, and large travertine open shower.

South Guest Bedroom

Seaside upper level queen size bedroom with furnished covered balcony. Tropical Caribbean decor with custom natural woven shades and luxury linens. Shared guest bath including double sea glass vanity, imported Spanish tiling, and custom cabinetry.

North Guest Bedroom

Upper level extra long twin, or combined to a king size bedroom, with spectacular west facing ocean views. Classic royal blue and white decor with custom natural woven shades and luxury linens. Shared guest bath including double sea glass vanity, imported Spanish tiling, and custom cabinetry.

Sea Level Fifth Bedroom Suite

Expansive ocean view suite with full kitchen, living and dining area, extra long twin, or combined to a king size bedroom, full bath with aquarmarine glass tiling and covered terrace. This living space is ideal for guests requiring complete privacy or separate accomodations for a nanny, chef, etc.
