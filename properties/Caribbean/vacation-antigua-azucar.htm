Azucar Villa, Galley Bay Heights, Antigua


Azucar Villa is the jewel of Galley Bay Heights, on the west coast of Antigua. It is clad with an exquisite majestic architectural hand crafted natural stone wall and it is bordered by a roof top garden flourishing with tropical flowers; this garden continues on the ground levels with native palms and various tropical flowers.

Azucar Villa split level design spreads over 10,000 square feet of living and entertaining space. The fully equipped kitchen boast professional grade stainless steel appliances and a beautiful and functional island topped in Italian marble and housing a six burner cook top. Its custom made cabinets are imported from Italy. The internal dining area has a mahogany, round dining table and the living room has two over size sofas.

The oversize vaulted ceiling gives the distinct impression of being out into the open and it is further enhanced by the overhead sky window bringing the natural light in. Azucar Villa style of living continues into the Entertainment room. This room houses a 60" Plasma HDTV Television with state of the art sound system to ensure an unforgettable entertainment experience while comfortably lounging on a beautiful large L-shaped orange sofa.

Comfort living continues with six bedrooms; three located in the main floor, two located in the lower floor and one in the romantic cottage. All the bedrooms have private bathroom, ceiling fans, A-C and spacious wardrobes. Five bedrooms have double beds, while one bedroom has two single beds. All the rooms offer breathtaking views of the Caribbean Sea.

Outdoor living and relaxing will make up a great part of your stay at Azucar Villa. The villa offers you an outdoor living room setting warmly furnished with comfortable external furniture, just perfect for relaxing and taking in the breath taking panoramic views of the bay. The Pool Deck is home to a large professional grade BBQ grill & bar, a dining area with outdoor dining furniture, lounge chairs and sofas. The veranda has the infinity edge swimming pool and the Jacuzzi.

Azucar Villa's careful lay out makes it the place to be for ultimate relaxation, comfort and fun
