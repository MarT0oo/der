Brazilian Emerald, Anguilla, Caribbean Vacation Rental

 

The Brazilian Emerald is an architectural masterpiece designed for entertaining and relaxation. It is a 13,000 square foot villa with 5 bedrooms each with an en-suite bathroom and two additional powder rooms for a total of 7 bathrooms.

The living and dining space is ideal for a group of friends, a family gathering or entertaining your business associates. As you enter the room through a rounded hallway, a beautiful curved bar is the centrepiece for the flower petal floor plan offering interesting visual stimuli designed to offer views of the sea and St Martin from any direction.

From the table for 14 to the grand piano, this is a space you will never forget. As you exit the building to the balcony you will be delighted with an 18' x 45' pool and an outdoor Jacuzzi for 12 people. Many special evenings under the stars will be had by you and your guests.

The entertainment floor is simply a dream come true. The views are endless to the Caribbean Sea as well as being able to gaze down to the open area of the living and dining areas. Again, a full bar is the centerpiece to the room flanked on one side by a hand made stainless steel Blatt billiard table and on the other a custom stainless steel and Brazilian wood gaming table. As you move to the back of the area, you will enter the home theatre offering a TV, DVD, Video projector system bringing movies and sporting events to life.

The next floor contains the master bedroom, four guestrooms with additional walk in closets as well as a self contained gym featuring all the newest equipment.

Relax and enjoy the stereo system throughout the villa on 68 speakers throughout 7 regions with a network music library with thousands of titles. You can listen to Island Music or your favorite song in the Entertainment Area while your travel companion is enjoying jazz poolside.

The villa interiors are highlighted by pieces of art and sculpture from Brazil in addition to the specially designed Italian linens, Murano glassware and silverware forming the small details that make Altamer so special.

The villa has its own dedicated lighted tennis court with basket-ball hoop for those looking for some outdoor exercise to break up the day.
