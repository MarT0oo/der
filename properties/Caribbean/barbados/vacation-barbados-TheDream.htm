A luxurious and spacious beachfront villa located in the exclusive neighborhood, ‘The Garden’, St James, The Dream mixes contemporary, cutting-edge design with Caribbean chic, offering an opportunity to indulge in island living at its absolute finest. The comfort, facilities and staff offer a lifestyle and holiday experience unparalleled on the West Coast of Barbados. Set at the end of a private drive, The Dream offers a quiet and tranquil retreat whilst still within easy reach of the West Coast’s top amenities, including world-famous restaurants such as ‘The Cliff’, chic boutiques, trendy night spots and of course a selection of competition golf courses.

Recently renovated and fully upgraded, the contemporary, open-plan villa is beautifully appointed with high-end furnishings and finishes carefully selected from across the globe. There are five luxurious en suite bedrooms featuring air conditioning, security safes, flat screen televisions, DVD players, iPod docking stations. The magnificent master bedroom suite, located on the upper level overlooks the enticing turquoise waters of the Caribbean Sea. The ample living space with bar extends out onto the covered terraces and sundeck surrounded by beautifully landscaped grounds and manicured lawns, tropical flowers and swaying palm trees.

The relaxed media room includes a 60” flat screen TV and the property comes complete with fast speed wireless internet connection throughout. Additional amenities including a pool table and DVD library are also available.

The villa features a stylish 45'' infinity edge swimming pool and sunbathing terrace with oversized sun loungers and umbrellas. The outdoor seating area, complete with pizza oven, BBQ, bar and lounge area, provides guests with ample space to enjoy outdoor living at its very finest, both day and night, with glamorous lighting illuminating the terraces once evening sets.

Concierge services and an attentive villa staff ensure no detail is left unattended; The Dream comes standard with a house manager, two maids, a laundress, butler, chef and maintenance man. A night watchman is also available on request at an additional cost to client
