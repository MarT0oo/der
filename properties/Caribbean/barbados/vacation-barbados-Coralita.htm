Coralita, Sandy Lane, Barbados Vacation Rental

 

Coralita is a luxurious 7 Bedroom Villa located in the heart of the exclusive Royal Westmoreland Golf and Country Club.

This grand, colonial style villa offers magnificent views of the Resort and the Caribbean Sea beyond.

All bedrooms are en-suite and are located on the lower 2 levels, with the exception of the private and spacious master bedroom suite. The lower level includes a games/TV room as well as 2 guest bedrooms with private terraces and access onto the gardens leading up to the pool area. This beautiful villa flows from the courtyard entrance to the spacious living room which then opens out onto the pool terrace where you can spend your days basking in the sun, sitting at one of the comfortable seating areas or dine al fresco style.

A property manager is also on hand to assist with anything you may need during your stay including restaurant reservations, excursions and airport transfers.

All guests staying at this villa will receive access to all resort facilities.

Please note that this villa is only available as either a 6 or 7 bedroom.

The twin room is cool and spacious with its twin beds (easily converted to a double) and an attractive vista over the mature tropical gardens.
