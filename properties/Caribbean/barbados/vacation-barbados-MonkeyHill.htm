Monkey Hill is a luxury rental villa situated in the parish of St James on the exclusive West Coast of Barbados. The elegant, sprawling eight-bedroom property sits on nearly 2 acres of undulating land on one of the few ridge front lots within the exclusive Sugar Hill Community. Monkey Hill is around 8,000 ft² and offers its guests a unique blend of indoor and outdoor living.

Almost every part of the villa enjoys wonderful views of the North Atlantic Ocean, giving spectacular sights of the sunset and island life, making your stay at Monkey Hill an unforgettable one.

Monkey Hill is the perfect location for family holidays, entertaining, idyllic romantic breaks and golfing trips. Its prestigious surroundings provide everything you need for flawless events and celebrations with personnel, butlers, chefs and housekeeping services available upon request.

Monkey Hill has 8 en-suite bedrooms. 6 are located in the main house and 2 are in the cottage.

Main House Upstairs:

Junior Master Suite – Silverback Suite, with a King size bed, balcony and Media room. Reef Room, and the Alligator Room have Queen beds.

Main House Downstairs:

Master Suite – Coral Suite, with a King size bed and walk in wardrobe and a patio garden with sitting area. Turtle Room and the Seahorse Room have Queen beds and patio areas with seating.

The dining room is situated adjacent to the kitchen and living room and offers excellent entertaining possibilities with direct access to the outdoor terrace.

The stylish kitchen features all high end appliances, breakfast bar and counter tops of granite throughout. Separate access is provided for services as well as powder room, laundry and storage for housekeeping.

Monkey Hill has an extensive pool deck with infinity swimming pool and outdoor dining under a charming gazebo – like setting.

The Cottage:

The Cottage provides two further bedrooms at Monkey Hill; the White Room and Oak Room. They both have twin beds and their own en suite bathrooms. The Cottage itself is a self contained unit with its own kitchen, WiFi, TV, living area and garden with outside seating and delightful views of the Island flora.

The White and Oak bedrooms, located in the cottage. have twin beds that can be made up separately or as a king size bed. The cottage has it’s own separate garden area.

A Cook/Chef and housekeeper will be included.

Housekeeper / Laundress generally work 6 days per week from 9:00am – 5:00pm. An additional laundress will be provided depending on the level of occupancy.

Cook/Chef and Butler will be included at no additional cost when main house or house and cottage is booked. Based on a 7 night minimum stay in Winter and 14 night minimum at Christmas.

Limegrove Lifestyle Centre: Limegrove Lifestyle Centre has emerged as the most stylish place to shop, dine, and lime for all visitors and residents of Barbados. The Centre is home to many of the world's leading retail brands such as Armani, Louis Vuitton, and Ralph Lauren. An open courtyard with a stunning waterfall feature lies in the middle of the centre. A number of dining options awaits all visitors, from Thai, Greek and Italian. There are also 2 grand cinemas to catch a movie while munching on delicious treats.

Tides Restaurant: culinary art awaits you at the water’s edge of the Platinum Coast offering the perfect blend of ambience and mouth-watering culinary creations.

First and Second Street: this venue offers a great mix of food, lounging, and party spots. You can visit Piano Bar to sip drinks while listening to lovely singing and piano playing, or if you want something more upbeat visit Elbow Room on Fridays to have a great time. First and Second street have over 6 locations to dine and over 5 spots to lounge/party.

Chattel Houses: bright and quaint, traditional Chattel Houses provide clothing, jewelry, fine art and small eateries to grab a relaxing meal while shopping and walking around.

Massy Supermarket: this supermarket offers just more than basic necessities when compared to other supermarkets in Barbados. Distinctive features include flowers, specialty foods and a pharmacy.

Beach House: this complex includes shopping, The Beach House Restaurant and The Drift Bar.

Zaccios: A beachfront restaurant serving international cuisine and is open for lunch and dinner. Known for their yummy pizzas, be sure to try this out!

Coffee shops: with 2 cafés available in this area, guests can choose from Café Moya or Coffee Bean where yummy café lattes and little eateries are served.

Banks: there are 3 banks available, these include The Royal Bank of Canada, First Caribbean International Bank and The Bank of Nova Scotia.

Sunset Crest Property Owners Beach Access: persons who are staying in select Sunset Crest properties have access to the swimming pool and sun bathing deck at The Beach House Restaurant. Members also benefit from direct beach access from the pool deck unto a white sandy beach.

Saturday's farmers market: open every from 9:00 a.m. until 2:00 p.m. Set in the grounds of the historic Holders House, located at Holders Hill, St James and overlooking the beautiful Holders Polo Field, our market is alive with the hum of music and the chatter and banter of locals and visitors alike mingling around the individual stalls filled with arts and crafts or food products such as jams, jellies and chutneys.

Discovery Bay Hotel: Discovery Bay consists of charming plantation-style houses set in beautiful landscaped gardens. Located on the fashionable West Coast of the island. Days at this hotel are centred around the beach, the pool and the Sugarbird Bar and Grill.
