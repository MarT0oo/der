Blue Shutters, Royal Westmoreland, St. James, Barbados Vacation Rental

 

Blue Shutters is a stylish 4 bedroom property located on the 16th hole of the Royal Westmoreland Golf course. This charming open plan coral stone villa boasts high ceilings, one of a kind antique pieces and full time staff that will cater to your every need. The main house contains 3 spacious bedrooms with en suites. The one bedroom self contained cottage is fitted with a King bed and en suite bathroom. A private terrace affords seating for two with views over the golf course. The tastefully furnished wrap around terrace affords you amazing views of the golf course and the sea. This terrace with dining and sitting area, bar and BBQ also overlooks the large, enticing infinity edge swimming pool with several loungers and umbrellas on the deck for your enjoyment.

Kid’s Club: a convenient kid’s club where loads of activities and camps can be found for your young ones.

Royal Pavilion Restaurant – Palm Restaurant: Embrace open air dining while eating tantalizing Caribbean food.
