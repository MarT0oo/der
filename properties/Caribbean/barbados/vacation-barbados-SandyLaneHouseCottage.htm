Sandy Lane House, located within the heart of the prestigious estate from which it takes its name, exceeds every expectation of the quintessential island getaway. Set within two acres of manicured gardens, the 10-bedroom property features a stunning main residence meticulously finished with the contemporary Caribbean lifestyle in mind. Completed in 2014, the house features sleek, modern lines that capitalize on the juxtaposition of interior and outdoor living space. An abundance of natural light, cool breezes and ambient sound create an airy atmosphere that is amplified by the use of glass balustrades, chrome finishes and striking Crema Marfil polished marble flooring. Innovative glass walkways, designed to unite the two levels of the home by offering views from above and below, enhance the flow of light throughout.

The main house features four bedrooms, each with trey ceilings, flowing white drapery, handmade cabinetry, ceiling fans and air-conditioning. The master suite, located on the upper level, features a king size bed and direct access to the balcony overlooking the pool area and gardens. A spacious walk-through closet leads to the en suite master bath, featuring a stand-alone glass shower and stunning tub made with single-slab imported marble, perfect for a relaxing soak. The double vanity, also made from single-slab marble, offers ample counter space and storage.

There are two guest bedrooms on the upper level; one features a king size bed and the other a queen size bed, both have en suite bathrooms with showers. One also offers direct access to the balcony overlooking the pool. The fourth bedroom, featuring a king bed, is located on the lower level; it offers an en suite bedroom and direct access to the pool area by separate entrance, and can serve as a second master suite if desired.

The entertaining areas include an upper level lounge comprising a covered, open-air sitting room with vaulted ceiling and skylight and an uncovered terrace overlooking the inviting swimming pool below. Two sweeping stairwells lead to the lower level, which features a spacious central living area with built-in bar and climate-controlled wine caves, an impressive soundproof media room and extended dining area that opens on to the pool terrace.

A powder room, storage closet and private office are accessible from this central area. The kitchen is outfitted with white cabinetry, sleek chrome fittings, slab marble countertops and top of the line GE Monogram appliances. A two-car garage and maid’s quarters with laundry room, storage and separate bathroom complete the ground floor living space.

Two gazebos flank the generously sized infinity edge swimming pool: one featuring a large Jacuzzi tub and lounge chairs; the other outfitted for outdoor dining and entertaining, with a large Master Forge BBQ and single slab marble dining table.

Two guesthouses complete the pool area; one featuring two bedrooms whilst the other is used exclusively for massages. Both poolside guesthouses afford two bathrooms and a kitchenette. Well appointed with a large fridge, two-burner stovetop and every day appliances, the guesthouses offer the perfect retreat for extended families and friends, providing privacy and ample space for larger groups to relax.

The second house on the property, affords privacy from the main house by the garden landscape, offers four additional bedrooms, ample living space and its own swimming pool. The second residence can be rented separately or in conjunction with the main villa for large parties requiring additional living space. Each residence has its own entrance with electronic gate access, as well as ample outdoor parking. The gardens feature mature palms, vibrant flamboyant and frangipani trees and tropical foliage.
