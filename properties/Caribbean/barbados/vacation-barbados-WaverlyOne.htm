Waverly One, Gibbes Beach, St. Peter, Barbados Vacation Rental

 

Located on the fabulous Gibbes Beach in St. Peter, this charming and secluded townhouse is ideal for those looking for the perfect get-away. Relax in comfort by the idyllic plunge pool, or stroll through your private tropical garden to reach the beachfront access - this is paradise at your doorstep.

Inside Waverly One you'll find a spacious open-plan living and dining area - perfect for entertaining and spending time together. Whip up a meal in the kitchen, or enjoy one prepared by a talented local cook who looks after the townhouse with the help of a housekeeper/laundress - so you shouldn't have to lift a finger! There is also an al fresco dining option for those who wish to enjoy their dinner under the stars, and a BBQ and built-in wet bar which is perfect for relaxing during the day. The contemporary furnishing downstairs has a touch of tropical flair, while upstairs you'll find cooling tones in the three air-conditioned bedrooms (two of which have their own balcony).

With a postcard-pretty beach just steps away, and a plunge pool set in your very own tropical oasis, this will be an unforgettable holiday with endless memories. We look forward to welcoming you to our Barbados beachfront vacation rental!
