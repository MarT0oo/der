Oyster Bay, St.James, Barbados Vacation Rental

 

Oyster Bay is a four bedroom villa with a swimming pool located on the beautiful Lower Carlton Beach on Reeds Bay, West Coast. This property is fully enclosed and offers extensive beach frontage and excellent swimming in front of the villa.

All bedrooms are air-conditioned. There are three bedrooms on the first floor and two offer great sea views. There is also a sitting room upstairs with cable TV, overlooking the pool and beach.

On the ground floor you will find an open plan living area, dining room, built in bar and the 4th bedroom. French doors open to the inviting swimming pool and spacious surrounding pool deck which is equipped with sun loungers.

Outdoor living can be further enjoyed at the poolside dining gazebo where there is a sit up bar and built-in Bar-B-Q.
