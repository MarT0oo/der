Capri Manor, St. Peter, Barbados Vacation Rental

 

Located just across the street from the world famous Gibbs Beach in Barbados, Capri Manor is truly a little slice of paradise. Large, lush tropical foliage surround a private garden Jacuzzi and a full family size swimming pool. The pool is surrounded by a huge deck with ample patio furniture and a built in gas BBQ pit. As you enter the large foyer you get a true nautical feel from the natural teak coloured hardwood floors. There is a conveniently located interior sitting room as well as covered outdoor patio dining area all with lovely views of the back gardens and pool.

All 4 bedrooms are en suite with the master bedroom boasting his and her vanities. Ample closet space in each room adds to the spacious feel of this lovely home. The house is fully air conditioned and has cable TV in 3 of the 4 bedrooms.

There is a casual outdoor dining area located just off the well appointed, modern and spacious kitchen that is a perfect for a casual mid morning brunch. There are granite counter tops and brand new stainless steel appliances and the nautical theme continues with whimsical deck chairs surrounding the casual eat in kitchen table.

Capri Manor is a great spot for a family getaway and would make a wonderful spot for a small garden wedding.
