Coral Cove 2, St. James, Barbados Vacation Rental

 

The Mahogany Tree - Coral Cove 2 is a luxurious modern beachfront apartment ideally located on the beautiful Paynes Bay Beach in the parish of St. James. Paynes Bay beach is one of the most popular beaches in Barbados and offers a variety of water sports, from jet-skis to glass bottom boats. This is the perfect vacation retreat for those who desire a relaxing, fun filled vacation surrounded by lush beautifully aged mahogany trees and stretches of fine white sands.

This ground floor apartment is elegantly decorated to create space and style and enjoys spectacular sea views from the living room and master bedroom. Its open plan, air-conditioned, living/dining area conveniently leads onto its own private and spacious garden/patio, featuring lounge chairs, a casual dining area and private plunge pool. This large patio provides guests with the ideal spot to relax and enjoy the captivating beauty of the shimmering Caribbean Sea beyond.

The Mahogany Tree contains two enchanting en suite bedrooms that are fitted with ceiling fans and are fully air-conditioned. The master bedroom is stylishly designed with modern furnishings and opens up onto the patio, which has direct access to the sandy shores beyond. The second bedroom has twin beds which can be made up as a king.

Fully equipped with private beach chairs for guests and a short distance away from the renowned Sandy Lane Hotel, The Mahogany Tree is guaranteed to cater to all of your vacation requirements.

Sun loungers are provided on the wooden deck area and a secure beach gate leads out on to a quiet sandy beach where there is swimming and reefs for snorkeling.

The towns of Speightstown and Holetown are less than 10 minutes away by car, where shopping, restaurants and entertainment can be found. Golf, tennis and other activities are close by, while water sports and other water activities can be arranged for you.

Coral Cove 2 is very popular as a family villa rental, as well as for weddings - the sunsets are spectacular.
