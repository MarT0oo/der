Coral House, Gibbs Beach, St. Peter, Barbados

 

The Coral House is a luxurious, spacious and pleasing property enjoying a quiet and secure residential location, set in its own tropical gardens. It is within easy walking distance to what is undoubtedly one of the most beautiful beaches on the island.

This villa can accommodate up to eight guests in four bedrooms. There is also a fold-away bed for one additional person. The house is spacious, bright and airy, with interesting and attractive internal architectural features and a galleried landing.

As its name implies, The Coral House contains a number of coral stone features (most notably the magnificent staircase located in a circular Coral Stone tower), and boasts a particularly attractive pool area, terrace and bar, ideal for entertaining and for those never-ending summer lunchtimes.

Shops in nearby Holetown and Speightstown are within easy reach, as are the best restaurants on the region. The property is only half an hour by car to the island's capital of Bridgetown.
