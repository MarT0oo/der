Blue Point, St. James, Barbados Vacation Rental

 

The breathtaking view of the Caribbean Sea from the balcony of the master bedroom, tempts you and is an ideal for an early morning dip or soak beneath the stars.

This elegant four bedroom beachfront holiday villa is set in lush tropical gardens and located south of the famous Gibbes Beach on the West Coast of Barbados.

Blue Point is tastefully furnished in a style that combines classic comfort with oriental influences. The open plan living area leads out onto a spacious wrap around covered terrace with lots of seating areas to relax with friends or read a book. Three bedrooms are also located on the ground floor of the villa. Upstairs, the master bedroom opens out to a large balcony overlooking the pool, allowing the cooling Barbados breezes to flow through the room.

The swimming pool and sun loungers are just a few steps away and the fully enclosed property offers excellent privacy for sunbathers and those taking a cooling dip. There is a natural water course running through the property and a beach gate opens to the white sands just south of Blue Point, offering good swimming and snorkeling off the reef.

Blue Point is located on the southern end of Greensleeves estate and may be rented separately or together with Greensleeves House (9 bedrooms). Greensleeves House and Blue Point Cottage combine to feature a total of 13 bedrooms comfortably accommodating up to 30 guests. Greensleeves estate truly is the ideal holiday retreat for large families and groups. View our brochure for the entire estate below.
