Bohemia, Sandy Lane, St. James, Barbados Vacation Rental

 

This elegant home is located on 1 1/2 acres of ridge land at Sandy Lane Estate with wonderful ocean views. Immaculately maintained, Bohemia consists of 5 bedrooms and boasts an amazing 50 ft 'horizon' swimming pool. A day bed located poolside provides an immaculate view of the Caribbean Sea.

Top quality fittings and furnishings, all marble floors, entertainment extras include a pool table, piano, 67" TV with DVD, DirecTV, stereo and each bedroom is equipped with a TV, DVD players, safe, mini fridge and hair dryer.

This impressive home is run by an efficient Butler and well trained staff.

As this home is situated within the Sandy Lane Estate it affords guests of Bohemia privileged access to the Sandy Lane Property Owner's beach facility - this does not include the Sandy Lane hotel.
