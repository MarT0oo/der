Umoya, St. James, Barbados Vacation Rental

 

UMOYA … A South African word for a breezy place. And that it is!

Set on one of the highest ridges on the Apes Hill Club golf course, this stunning 5-bedroom villa faces west to the Caribbean Sea and enjoys cooling trade wind breezes. With amazing golf and ocean views Umoya features expansive outdoor spaces ideal for family and friends to spend the holidays together making lifelong memories.

A fabulous infinity edge pool with expansive coral stone deck is the perfect backdrop for an early morning swim, daytime frolic or an elegant evening at sunset with cocktails to celebrate your Barbados vacation. The large terrace has both lounging and dining areas over looking the pool and the home's open plan kitchen, outdoor bar and BBQs make entertaining effortless. The Chef and Butler will handle any and all meal requests with ease, creating the ideal setting for guests to relax, unwind and simply enjoy their Barbados villa holiday.

The master suite opens onto the main terrace on the ground floor with sensational views over the pool to the West Coast. Light contemporary décor and a free flowing floor-plan is comfortable and modern with a dressing area and adjoining bathroom featuring a soaker tub, walk-in shower and his 'n hers vanities. Set off the media lounge and a small office nook this area also has an east-facing patio for those who rise early to enjoy a morning cup of coffee or tea at sunrise.

Four bedrooms with en-suite bathrooms and lovely coastal views are set on the first floor. Each with spacious accommodations and simple stylish décor, make this home-away-from-home a perfect option for families with older kids or friends seeking a holiday away in the sun. All bedrooms have king beds and one bedroom has the option for the bed to be split into two twin beds if required.

Apes Hill Club is a world class Golf and Polo Community set on 470-acres and the common facilities are easily accessible by guests. These include their new clubhouse and bar, tennis courts, a fully equipped gym and an enormous lap pool with sun loungers and spectacular views. Our Concierge can arrange golf for guests, with a weekly package especially arranged for Umoya.
