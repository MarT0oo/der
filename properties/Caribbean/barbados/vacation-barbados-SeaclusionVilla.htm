Seaclusion Villa, Derricks, St. James, Barbados, Caribbean Vacation Rental

 

Seaclusion Villa Barbados is a brand new super luxury Villa located on the exclusive Platinum Coast of Barbados offering stunning panoramic sea views and an unparalleled secluded beachfront experience.

Positioned within a leisurely stroll of the world renowned Cliff Restaurant, the Villa offers effortless private beach access and a world class level of sophistication and elegance rarely available in the Caribbean. Located within a short drive from Holetown offering shopping and a variety of fine dining restaurants, whilst up and coming Speightstown can be reached within fifteen minutes.

The Villa is fully-staffed with a world class chef, attentive butler, efficient house keeper, gardener / maintenance man and even a night watchman for peace of mind. Providing six uniquely distinct bedroom suites, five of which offer sea views from their private terraces. From large covered terraces to multiple dining and lounging areas the property is ideal for entertaining and spending quality time with friends and family.

Seaclusion Villa Barbados blends classic high-end luxury with state of the art conveniences. Built to the owner’s exacting standards and architecturally designed with classic colonial features in mind, the Villa embraces the character of the tropics whilst meeting modern day demand for cutting edge technology and conveniences.

The experience guests will enjoy at Seaclusion is of paramount importance which is why a dedicated contingent of staff including a world class chef and butler team are ready to impress with their skills from healthy breakfasts by the pool and tasty lunches in the gazebo to sundown drinks with canapés and a sumptuous three course dinner under the stars.

Seaclusion’s full complement of staff along with the Villa management and concierge team will strive to meet all your expectations whilst allowing you to truly relax in your very own secluded paradise.
