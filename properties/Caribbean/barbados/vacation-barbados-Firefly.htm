Firefly, Paynes Bay, St. James, Barbados Vacation Rental

 

For anyone looking to spend their vacation on a sandy beach leading into turquoise waters and are looking for uncompromising quality and luxury – Old Trees 6 offers everything one could wish for in the perfect tropical retreat.

By adjoining two adjacent units in this beachfront development, Old Trees 6 allows for guests to embrace superb leisure in this newly renovated five bedroom unit. Guests have the option to use all five en suite bedrooms with no Media Room or four en suite bedrooms with the Media Room as a possible fifth bedroom option (suitable for children only)*. Enjoy island living in this expansive apartment offering two private terraces with unobstructed sea views while providing top quality and uncompromised luxury. The Old Trees development offers a communal swimming pool and direct beach access on to one of the best beaches on island where an enviable beach service awaits.

This lavish apartment is the only unit within the Old Trees development boasting five bedrooms and two sea facing covered terraces as two adjacent units were joined to create one impeccable apartment encompassing the entire first floor of the North building.

Upon exiting the elevator on to the first floor, a large pointed and blocked coral stone wall entices you with twinkling light fixtures as you enter through the main door. Vaulted pickled ceilings accompanied by warm recessed lighting welcome you into a modern yet welcoming apartment. The expansive living space with multiple seating options has been fitted with a black grand piano, coffee table, television and plush sofas, all embracing clear skies and turquoise waters.

The attractive en suite bedrooms are divided on either side of this illustrious unit. The Master bedroom, Bedroom 2 and 3 are located to the left of the condo, while Bedroom 4 and 5 situated to the right. Guests have the option to use all five en suite bedrooms with no Media Room or four en suite bedrooms with the Media Room as a possible fifth bedroom option (suitable for children only)*. The Media Room is equipped with a sofa couch that converts into two singles beds, and acts as a possible fifth bedroom option with a powder room. Please bear in mind for those looking to use the fifth bedroom, the inviting lounge area has a flat screen television equipped with Sky TV and cable and all of the bedrooms have televisions equipped with Apple TV and DVDs.

Set back from the living area is the extensive, well equipped kitchen finished with marble walls, clean cupboards and stainless steel appliances. The kitchen includes two refrigerators, two wine coolers and two bar stools.

Featuring two separate sea facing terraces; the first sea facing terrace benefits from a well-sized plunge pool, two sun beds and personal stairs leading you directly to the tropical communal gardens with a freeform swimming pool and waterfall feature right onto the golden sands of Payne’s Bay Beach where exclusive sun chairs, umbrellas and beach butler service awaits. The other sea facing terrace is equipped with a bar and marble stone dining table which seats up to 14 persons.

Your every wish will be accommodated by the housekeeper and chef and the on-site dedicated Villa Manager. Our Concierge Specialist can arrange your car rental and airport transfer to groceries and restaurant bookings – simply let us know what you need organizing, sit back, relax and enjoy your stay.

Scarlet Restaurant: ‘eat, drink, play’ is this electric restaurants' slogan where superb food, fantastic cocktails and good fun lies.

Daphne’s Restaurant: is a part of Barbados’ acclaimed Elegant Hotels Group and has drawn tourists and celebrities to this elegant Italian fine dining restaurant from around the world.

Holder’s Hill Polo Field: known as the main Polo venue in Barbados Polo enthusiast can cheer for their team from November to May.

Paynes Bay Fish Market: one of the smaller fish markets on the island where you can buy fresh local fish or watch as the fishermen dock their boats and offload.

Mr. Vic: this is a casual restaurant that is children friendly and is a popular local hangout which dish up a combination of Caribbean/British food.

The Cliff Restaurant: incredible setting matched with creative cuisine, a must see in Barbados.

Paynes Bay Vendors: every Friday you can catch a sumptuously fresh grilled fish, served with traditional Bajan seasonings. Of course there’s more, the vendors have a variety of Bajan delights including grilled chicken, macaroni pie, rice and peas and to wash it down Bajan rum is available along with other drinks.

The House Hotel: is a chic, tranquil retreat for adults to unwind and reconnect in a setting of understated elegance.

Tamarind Cove Hotel: featuring 104 newly renovated and contemporary guest rooms on a prime 750 sq.ft. stretch of serene beach, this luxury hotel is an ideal Barbadian destination for couples, families, reunions and retreats.

Treasure Beach Hotel: this Caribbean resort has airy and contemporary suites. Enjoy fresh local ingredients as you dine under the stars in this award-winning restaurant, where our dinner menu changes on a nightly basis.

Cariba Restaurant and Bar: Internationally trained chefs provide modern Caribbean cuisine with an Asian twist in a traditional chattel house setting.
