Coconut Grove 6 - Heliconia, Royal Westmoreland, Barbados

 

Coconut Grove 6 is a large 4 bedroom, open plan villa situated in the prestigious award winning golfing resort of Royal Westmoreland which encompasses 500 acres in a lush and visually vibrant setting.


"Heliconia" consists of a kitchen, dining room, living room, four bedrooms, laundry room, storage room, private garden and private swimming pool that sits on a large terrace.


The Villa backs onto the golf course and provides access to a private golf cart making this property ideal for the avid golfer.


With Stylish decor and furniture throughout you are offered both luxury and comfort.


All the bedrooms are en suite and air-conditioned and the Master opens out onto the garden where a beautiful Frangipani tree grows, adding its wonderful fragrance to the air.


The resort exudes a calming sense of peace and privacy, and includes 24 hour security. It offers a complete range of facilities along with Health Spa, Tennis Courts, Clubhouse, Outdoor leisure pools and much more.


Residents of the Royal Westmoreland Resort have access to the resort facilities as well as special golf rates and beach access organised by Royal Westmoreland.
