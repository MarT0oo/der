The Reef, St. James, Barbados Vacation Rental

 

Situated overlooking the turquoise Caribbean Sea The Reef will take your breath away with its sleek architectural design and decadent interiors. This modern villa is ideal for those who love to entertain, with several dining options and a lovely open air terrace providing lots of opportunity to capture the magnificent sunsets with friends.

The chic entryway flows through to a generous living area with plush furnishings, statement chandeliers and full entertainment area. The ultra modern kitchen opens up to the formal dining area and while this floor has central air conditioning the sounds of the ocean can be heard teasingly beyond the French doors. On the spacious covered verandah there is casual seating, a shabby chic super sized dining table that seats up to eight and gorgeous views of the bay. A guest bedroom can be found on this floor near the entrance and features twin beds, spacious walk in closet and en suite bathroom.

On the second floor you will find the second guest bedroom with king size bed, built in wardrobes and stunning en suite bathroom. The decadent master bedroom is resplendent with elegant wooden beams suspended from the ceiling and lit up with warm recessed lighting that instantly creates an ambiance of romance and intrigue. The breathtaking king size bed features a repurposed church door as the headboard that exudes old world glamour and commands the room. There is a bright and airy walk in closet, gorgeous en suite bathroom with marbled flooring and a Jacuzzi bath and a cosy seating area with entertainment system for those who want a little quiet time. French doors offer the opportunity to completely open up the room to the spacious covered verandah and spectacular views of the bay. This verandah has causal loungers, dining table and a wet bar offering yet another option for entertaining on a more intimate level. Expect sunsets that will stay in your memory forever.

From the main floor you descend to the ground floor where you will find a convenient self enclosed office ideal for working holidays and an exquisite dining area with seating for ten. A fourth bedroom is tucked away to the rear offering privacy for guests and the entire level feels like a self contained cottage of its own with kitchenette, guest bathroom off the office and a cosy lounge area with entertainment console. The terrace is accessed from this floor and offers lots of casual seating areas, a barbecue pit and more dining options for al fresco evenings under the stars with the sounds of the waves in the background. The terrace is fully enclosed for security and has a private gate and stairway leading down to the gorgeous sands of the calm bay below.

The Reef is nestled just off the coast road, set on a stunning cliff with nearby five star restaurants, chic luxury boutiques, golf courses, trendy bars and nightclubs all within easy reach. There is a tidal beach and amazing snorkeling just steps below the villa.
