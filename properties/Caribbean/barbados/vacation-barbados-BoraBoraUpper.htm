Bora Bora Upper, St. James, Barbados Vacation Rental

 

Bora Bora Upper is a bright and airy holiday apartment, located directly on the popular Paynes Bay Beach, on the West Coast of Barbados. Guests can enjoy easy access to a variety of watersports, from jet skis to glass bottom boats. Restaurants are within walking distance, and the nearest shopping center is found in Holetown, a short five minute drive away.

This first floor apartment which has been tastefully decorated, features cool tropical colors. The fan-cooled, open plan living and dining room area features high ceilings and opens on to a covered balcony overlooking the beach and ocean. The kitchen is well equipped.
