Bora Bora Lower, St. James, Barbados Vacation Rental

 

Bora Bora Lower is a bright and airy holiday apartment, located directly on the popular Paynes Bay Beach, on the West Coast of Barbados. Guests can enjoy easy access to a variety of watersports, from jet skis to glass bottom boats. Restaurants are within walking distance, and the nearest shopping center is found in Holetown, a short five minute drive away.

This ground floor apartment which has been tastefully decorated, features cool tropical colors. The fan-cooled, open plan living and dining room area opens on to the uncovered terrace where guests can relax and enjoy the warm tropical sunshine as well as watch the world go by. The kitchen is well equipped and a small barbeque is available for guest use. Both bedrooms are air-conditioned – the master offers an en suite bathroom, whilst the second bedroom is adjacent to the other bathroom.

Bora Bora is the perfect choice for those who wish a casual, relaxing, and fun filled vacation in Barbados. Bora Bora Lower and Bora Bora Upper can be booked together to make a great four bedroom rental option.
