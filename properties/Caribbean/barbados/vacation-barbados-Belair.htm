Belair, St. Peter, Barbados Vacation Rental

 

Belair is one of our most popular rental holiday homes located on the white sands of Mullins Beach on the West Coast of Barbados.

The bright and tastefully decorated entrance leads you into the spacious living room with its columns of natural stone and breath taking panoramic views of the turquoise Caribbean Sea.

This recently refurbished vacation home offers 4 spacious and comfortably furnished bedrooms in the main residence - there are two further bedrooms in the adjoining guest house. All the bedrooms are air conditioned and are stylishly furnished with fresh tropical tones. With the modern home entertainment system, high speed broadband and Wi-Fi it's easy to stay tuned in and equally easy to tune out and relax to the sound of the ocean lapping the beach just steps away. Alfresco dining is offered on the beachfront terrace which is a wonderful place to start or end the day - watching the sun set with a refreshing cocktail in hand.

Belair has an experienced team to take care of your needs and the Cook and Housekeeper/Laundress offer excellent levels of service and care ensuring everyone in the party gets a holiday.

Vacation rental guests at Belair can also enjoy the exclusive Sugar Hill Club, complete with tennis courts, gymnasium and swimming pool.

This beautifully presented beachfront villa is the perfect retreat to recuperate, rejuvenate the mind, body, soul and to create lasting memories.
