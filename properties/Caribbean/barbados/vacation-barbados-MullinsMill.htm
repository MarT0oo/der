Mullins Mill, St. Peter, Barbados Vacation Rental

 

Mullins Mill is a home of unique character nestled on eight acres of landscaped grounds.

The main house is constructed around a 17th century sugar mill offering privacy in the two adjoining guest cottages. A cosy gazebo on the cliff side has sweeping views of the coastline, and the property has a ridge side infinity swimming pool with a beach house and separate Jacuzzi located a short walk through the tropical gardens (about 100 yards) south of the main house. East of the main house is a floodlit tennis court, garden and orchard. Mullins Mill is the ideal choice for families - two bedrooms are located in the main house (one at the top of the mill), two in the adjoining cliff cottage and four more in the gate cottages on the grounds. A spacious sitting/dining verandah overlooks the gardens and cliff side gazebo and offers fantastic ocean views. You can view a detailed floor plan of the estate here.

Guests have use of the owner's private beach house right on the famous Gibbs Beach (just a 5 minute drive from Mullins Mill). Mullins Mill can accommodate parties of up to 12 in 6 air conditioned bedrooms each with en suite bathrooms. Mullins Mill is run by an efficient staff who will do their best to make your holiday a memorable one - the chef skillfully prepares some incredible local delights. This property is the perfect location for exotic, tropical weddings and special occasions - view images. Please note a venue fee will apply. Kindly enquire for more details.
