Saramanda, Sandy Lane, St. James, Barbados Vacation Rental

 

Saramanda is a luxurious and spacious five-bedroom villa with a large swimming pool located in the heart of the prestigious Sandy Lane Estate in St. James on the West Coast of Barbados. All the bedroom suites are air-conditioned, with ceiling fans, en suite bathrooms and walk in closets. They are also outfitted with cable televisions, telephones and safes.

There are two master suites on the first floor of the villa and each suite has a private covered patio that overlooks the pool deck and beautiful landscaped gardens. The masters also have both tubs and showers. The other three bedroom suites are on the ground floor and have showers only.

The villa features a large airy atrium with comfortable oversized sofas and French doors that open up to the covered terrace. The overall design of Saramanda is a seamless blend of indoor and outdoor living. All the living and dining rooms open to the pool and garden.

Adjacent to the living room is a cozy media room, which features a wide screen HDTV, cable and DVD. The media room also opens up to the pool deck.

Saramanda also has an elegant indoor dining room with a large coral stone table that seats 10 persons and a large, fully equipped kitchen. The friendly staff will provide you with three meals a day.

Outside, the expansive outdoor area features a partially covered deck that leads to the pool, with outdoor dining and lounging areas; the pool deck has comfortable sun loungers and is set in beautiful tropical gardens.

The villa is 5 minutes away from all the amenities of Sunset Crest and Holetown, including duty free shopping, restaurants and supermarkets.
