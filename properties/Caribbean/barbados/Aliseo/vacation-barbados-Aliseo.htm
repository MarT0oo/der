Aliseo, Sandy Lane, Barbados

 

Set in pristine and manicured grounds high atop exclusive Sandy Lane Estate is the spacious and magnificent Aliseo, a stately three-story residence that is just five minutes away from the fine white sands of exclusive Sandy Lane Beach.

This expansive ultra-luxe residence offers Colonial-inspired furnishings, colorful and exotic art pieces and opulent finishes. Multiple indoor and outdoor living spaces provide you and your guests with a variety of choices of where to sit, chat or unwind. The villa boasts a large and perfectly equipped kitchen with a cook, butler and housekeeper on site to tend to your every whim and ensure your stay is as comfortable and relaxing as possible. Enjoy delicious meals in the dining room or eat al fresco on your expansive terrace. The lush yet manicured grounds of Aliseo contribute to your feeling of well-being - take a dip in your private swimming pool, or just rest on the comfy cushioned sun chairs that festoon the deck area. The cozy gazebo or one of the secluded seating areas in the roofed terraces can also provide perfect shady nooks. At night, challenge a loved one to a game of pool or take in a movie in the media room.

Boasting nine sumptuous bedrooms Aliseo is easily able to accommodate large groups of up to 18 guests. All suites feature opulent en-suite bathrooms and air conditioning. The Master Suite is set apart from the other rooms and features your own private patio. This flexible and delightful residence presents a wide choice of sleeping arrangements with bed sizes ranging from twins to king size.

One of the many rewards of your stay at magical Aliseo is its close proximity to the fine white shores of Sandy Lane Beach. This exclusive area is well loved by the famous and star spotting can be a fun activity. And if you’re here between July and October you can also see the beaches other famous residents, the Hawksbill turtles! You’ll also enjoy one of the best swimming beaches on the island and have access to the Sandy Lane Beach Bar where you’ll find drinks and light meals to tempt you. While the eponymous luxury Sandy Lane Hotel is within a stone’s throw. Avid golfers will love being close to popular greens, including the Royal Westmoreland Course. It is just 2 km to Holetown and 11 km to Bridgetown, with Grantley Adams International Airport (BGI) 25 km away.
