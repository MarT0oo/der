Whitegates, The Garden, St. James Barbados

 

Whitegates is a 4-bedroom, beachfront villa with spectacular views, located on a cliff-side area of the island's Gold Coast. Beach access is via a private stairway leads to the tranquil turquoise water ideal for swimming.

This well-appointed villa has two indoor dining rooms; one is formal and the other informal. Both dining rooms have large arched French doors that open out to the property's tropical gardens. Next to the formal dining room is the living area that is bright and airy with many arched windows that capture sea views.

The living room opens up to a covered terrace and a large kidney-shaped swimming pool. The terrace has alfresco dining and a wet bar making it a lovely place to have lunch in between cooling dips in the pool.

There is a smaller terrace right by the edge of the cliff where you can lounge and watch breathtaking sunsets.

This home comes with an efficient and friendly staff whose goal is to make your vacation a relaxing one.
