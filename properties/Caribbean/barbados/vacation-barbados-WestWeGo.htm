West We Go, Royal Westmoreland, St. James, Barbados Vacation Rental

 

Located on the famous Sandy Lane beach with wonderful views of Sandy Lane bay and only a short walk up the beach from Sandy Lane Hotel, West We Go offers guests extensive gardens and beach frontage.

This spacious home has three A/C bedrooms in the main house and there is a 1 bedroom, self contained cottage in the garden with its own beach views. The main house consists of three bedrooms and bathrooms, a sitting room with cable TV, dining room and verandah with bar. There are two sitting areas in the garden, shaded by mature trees each with a gate opening to the beautiful white beach for excellent swimming and snorkeling.

A friendly and efficient staff will prepare delicious meals and ensure that your holiday is an enjoyable one!
