Villa Tamarindo, Between Sandy Lane & Holetown, St. James, Barbados Vacation Rental

 

Villa Tamarindo is an ultra-luxurious two-storey villa set within four and a half acres in the exclusive Villas at Beachlands. Ideal for discerning guests, this exclusive beachfront retreat features the most exquisite indoor and outdoor areas perched in one of the best locations in Barbados, 500 metres from Sandy Lane and 100 metres from Holetown.

The design of Villa Tamarindo draws upon the elegance of traditional West Indian plantation houses balanced with world class, contemporary interiors seamlessly spread across 5 bedroom suites, 5 bathrooms and 2 powder rooms. The villa sits against a backdrop of carefully landscaped grounds for guests to escape and relax when not enjoying the 44ft swimming pool or expansive beach views visible from almost every room.

This home is private, fully enclosed and gated, with a state-of-the-art security system and nightly security service in place. Each ensuite bedroom is fully air-conditioned and offers unrestricted views of the spectacular ocean from an expansive terrace and sundeck. The en suite bathrooms all feature unrivaled finishes. Within the property there is also a yoga studio, media room and high-specification chef's kitchen where a private chef will cater to your every need.
