Nirvana, St. James, Barbados Vacation Rental

 

This elegantly furnished 4 bedroom beachfront villa is located in Fitts Village, St. James and features an intimate swimming pool and wooden deck with sun loungers added in the garden.

Nirvana is designed to enhance the 'laid-back' lifestyle of the Caribbean. The coral stone building consists of 4 bedrooms, one located on the ground floor and the other three on the first floor. The master bedroom opens to a balcony with expansive views of the garden and shimmering ocean.
