Nelson Gay, Speightstown, St. Peter, Barbados

 

Located on a half mile of pristine, sandy beach with incredible swimming and a reef at one end that is ideal for snorkeling, Nelson Gay occupies two acres on a beach front site on the outskirts of Speightstown with spacious landscaped gardens and a stunning swimming pool. Originally built in the 1960's for a US Ambassador and constructed entirely of coral stone in the grand Palladian manner, Nelson Gay retains the charm of a bygone era. The property consists of the main house which contains 5 bedrooms, a cottage with 1 bedroom and by special arrangement, the media room in the main house has a day bed and there are also 2 self-contained Chattel Houses.


2 covered terraces on the north and south wing bedrooms offer additional privacy and comfort, while the living room is open and airy with 14ft. ceilings. This family friendly villa has numerous relaxing and dining areas and an elegant garden gazebo equipped with wet bar and fridge. A large area immediately in front of the house is roped off with buoys for safe swimming.


The staff and Villa Manager will meet every need for a relaxing and exclusive family vacation.
