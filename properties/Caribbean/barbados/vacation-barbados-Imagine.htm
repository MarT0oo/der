Imagine, Prospect, St. James, Barbados Vacation Rental

 

Set on the beautiful west coast of Barbados, in Prospect St James, this modern styled, 4 bedroom, 4.5 bathroom stand alone, oceanfront villa offers breathtaking views of the tranquil, turquoise-blue waters of the Caribbean Sea.

Imagine is a three storey villa that has been recently renovated with a stylish interior as well as a keyless entry that is fully equipped with security cameras and an alarm system. On the ground floor you have a lovely kitchen equipped with all amenities, laundry room that includes a washer and dryer, powder room, and a dining area to seat 6. Next to the dining area, there is a spacious living room that opens out onto the covered terrace where you’ll find a generous sized lounge area with a salt water pool to sit out, read a book or enjoy the rays of sunshine.

On the second floor, there are 3 substantially sized bedrooms all inclusive of their own three piece en suite bathrooms. The first bedroom has a queen size bed, while the second bedroom has 2 single beds. The third bedroom includes a king size bed with a stunning view of the Sea that you can indulge in from the standing glass balcony. All bedrooms have closet space located inside the rooms as well as an additional closet located in the hallway for linens.

The entire third floor is occupied by the voluminous master suite, which has vaulted ceilings and wall to wall glass doors that brings in the captivating natural sunlight. The master suite includes a king size bed and a flat screen TV. There is a walk through closet that leads into the 3 piece en suite bathroom. With a grand balcony and tasteful furnishings located off the master suite, this balcony is perfect to lounge and take pleasure in the delightful tropical sunsets.

Each room is designed in simplicity with a radiant pop of colour to complement the rich colours of the Sea. For your convenience there are air conditioners and ceilings fans in each room for those warm nights, as well as access control, and burglar bars for your safety. The entire villa is supplied with Bose Professional Audio, Lighting, keyless entry automation, along with wireless internet.

Housekeeper, personal chef, and around the clock concierge are all included with this villa.

With its ideal location, Imagine is close to all amenities such as Jordan’s supermarket, Gas stations, as well as being situated right next to the renowned Mediterranean restaurant; Cin Cin by the Sea. On each side of Imagine, are beautiful white sandy beaches such as Batts Rock Beach, Paradise Beach and the beach at Waves Hotel and Spa. For just a short distance walk you can enjoy a day in the sun and a swim in the Sea.

The Cliff Restaurant: incredible setting matched with creative cuisine, a must see in Barbados.

Il Tempio Restaurant: said to have ‘the best Italian food in Barbados’ by the late Lucian Pavarotti, this authentic Italian. Restaurant is situated beachfront where you can view the tranquil sea.

Waves Hotel and Restaurant: Waves Hotel is one of the most idyllic destinations on the island. The restaurant serves a variety from Asian-fusion, to classically Mediterranean and traditional Caribbean specialties. The Waves Hotel Spa offers excellent and rejuvenating spa treatments by amazing staff.

Jordan’s Supermarket: providing all the items you need to make your stay feel at home.

Cin Cin Restaurant: refreshing and contemporary; featuring a cool lounge bar and alfresco dining, amazing views of the West Coast and Quintessential Mediterranean style food with a Caribbean twist.
