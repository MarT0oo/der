Westhaven, Royal Westmoreland, St. James, Barbados Vacation Rental

 

Westhaven, a luxurious beachfront villa of two storey, is gently lapped by calm waters from Gibbs Beach of the West Coast.

A mere 5 minutes away is the historic little town of Speightstown – with the most delightful and casual restaurants tempting visitors with their authentic Caribbean cuisine and sea vistas. Don’t forget to check out the Gallery of Caribbean Art and Arlington House - a 'must see' interactive museum with audio-visual features of 'Old Barbados'.

Lush gardens usher you through a gateway opening onto the pool deck with a gorgeous view of the sea. The shimmering pool is surrounded by sun loungers with a trickling water feature in tune with the soothing sounds of washing waves. Loungers can be taken down to the seashore through the private direct access to the beach. Serene, crystal waters beckon you to swim, to snorkel and to take advantage of several water sport activities. Gibbs Beach meets Mullins Beach to the north allowing for romantic strolls or brisk walks.

For entertainment the comfortable living room on the ground floor is outfitted with flat screen television, complete with cable. This area opens onto the expansive patio – just the spot for sipping cocktails while admiring a superb sunset. The kitchen’s large window opens outwards to the dining area, making it convenient to serve meals, snacks and refreshing beverages throughout the day. Even partake of a scrumptious meal on the pool deck al fresco - prepared by the villa’s chef.

The bedrooms - which have outdoor patios, air-conditioning and en suite bathrooms – are all located on the first floor except for one situated on the ground level. The master – also equipped with cable television - and 2nd bedroom suite share a balcony overlooking a magnificent view of Gibbs Bay. The smaller balconies of the third and fourth bedrooms offer lovely views of the garden. The villa can accommodate valuables in its two safes.

This tropical haven offers you privacy, peace and everything you need to relax.
