Coral Cove 3, St. James, Barbados Vacation Rental

 

Coral Cove 3, otherwise known as "Green Flash" meaning the "perfect day" is located on the beautiful wide beach of Paynes Bay. The swimming is idyllic with clear waters and good snorkeling. The turtles often swim in the area and we have reports of many people seeing them when they are snorkeling.

The villa, "Green Flash", is located on the ground floor resides on its own wing. There is a private garden that is a stone's throw away from the Caribbean Sea and beach. Beach chairs are set out daily for those in residence as well as having your own personal patio and outdoor dining area. This spacious villa contains two bedrooms and is finished and designed with elegant furnishings. All bathrooms are marbled and each bedroom has its own bathroom. All bedrooms are air conditioned as well as containing ceiling fans. "Green Flash" can sleep five, as there is a day bed in the second bedroom that can easily convert into a comfortable twin bed. The master bedroom contains an attractive king size bed with pleasing fabrics and patio.

The open plan kitchen is fully equipped and has all the amenities of a modern home. All appliances are of the highest standards with a wine fridge and stainless steel finishes. For those wishing to dine inside, there is a wonderful "mango" wooden table that will seat six comfortably. The living room contains a 52" High Def. LCD television, DVD player as well as a music station dock to play your favorite sounds. In both bedrooms we have televisions and in the second bedroom there is also a play station 3 video game blu-ray console for those looking for something other to do than sunning, swimming, outdoor water sporting or just walking the beach.

"Green Flash" is only a five minute drive to the famous Sandy Lane Hotel as well as the Holetown area where shopping, tennis and golf are all available. This unique villa is ideal for families as well as those looking for luxury without the big price. The sunsets are sure to "wow" you the very first night you come!! "As the sun sets into the Caribbean Sea after another perfect day in Barbados; you too, may witness the magic of the "green flash".
