Villa Caprice, Reed's Bay, St James Barbados Vacation Rental


Enjoy the warmth of our Caribbean welcome as you relax in elegant luxury and comfort. Make each day your own as you wake up to the gentle sounds of the surf - whether choosing to have fun with the family or explore the island with friends, there is something for everyone.

Caprice is a four bedroom oceanfront home located on a rocky bluff with reef on Barbados' Gold Coast. Steps lead down onto the south boundary to a beach where there is good swimming. There is a large indoor sitting room which opens onto a covered dining veranda and another sitting area, both leading onto the open terrace. There is a plunge pool with waterfall, a private sun deck as well as a more spacious, enclosed sun deck facing the ocean. It is equipped with lounge chairs for relaxing during the day, or star gazing at night.

Our attentive and caring staff includes a dedicated villa manager who will answer all of your questions and make suggestions for your stay with us - just relax and let us take care of all your needs.

Caprice Villa is a 5 minute drive to Speightstown and restaurants, a 10 minute drive to Church, Tennis court, Golf course, Grocery store & Sandy Crest Medical Clinic, a 30 minute drive to Queen Elizabeth Hospital, and a 45 minute drive to Grantley Adams International Airport.

The Caprice experience extends beyond simply a vacation, to creating unforgettable memories.
