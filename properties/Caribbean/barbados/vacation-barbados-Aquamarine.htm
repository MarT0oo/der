Aquamarine, St. Peter, Barbados Vacation Rental

 

A relaxed beachfront holiday home situated literally on the water's edge at Mullins Beach, St. Peter, on the West Coast of Barbados. Aquamarine's spacious patio offers wonderful views of Mullins beach and the Caribbean Sea, not to mention spectacular sunsets. The open plan dining and sitting room leads onto the deck which also has chairs to relax, to sunbathe and a dining table for al fresco meals.

The bedrooms of this vacation rental are comfortably furnished and have plenty of storage space for your holiday wardrobe! There is an en suite bathroom for the master bedroom with bedrooms 2 and 3 sharing a bathroom. The deck is partially covered offering shade for those who are taking a break from the Barbados sunshine. Step off the deck onto the soft white Barbados sand and in a few short strides you are at the water's edge -perfect for cooling off or taking a swim or a snorkel to check out the brightly coloured tropical fish on the reef.

Aquamarine is perfect holiday rental villa for families or groups of friends looking for cheerful ambiance and lots of beach!
