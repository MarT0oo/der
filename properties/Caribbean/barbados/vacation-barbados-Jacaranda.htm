Jacaranda, Gibbes Beach, St. Peter, Barbados Vacation Rental

 

Located in the residential area of Gibbes Glade, St. Peter, Jacaranda is a short walk from the lovely Gibbes Beach.

This charming three bedroom, four bathroom villa has been designed around a central atrium featuring an inviting plunge pool. The spacious living room opens to a large partially covered deck and sitting area.

Perfect for families to sun lounge on the deck or relax in the beautifully maintained gardens. The outdoor swimming pool is nestled in well landscaped gardens.
