Smugglers Cove 4, Paynes Bay, Barbados

 

Classical Caribbean architecture meets today's demand for elegance and style. This exclusive and low density development offers spectacular unobstructed sea views.

Smugglers Cove is located on arguably the best beach in Barbados and offers a beach bar, safe swimming, water sports and is within a short walk of the World famous Sandy Lane Hotel. Golf at Sandy Lane or Royal Westmoreland and numerous fine dining ocean front restaurants are also just a few minutes away, so whatever your pace of life Smugglers Cove offers the best in West Coast lifestyle.

The apartment is built to the highest of standards and include luxury bedrooms and quality European kitchens. Great care has been taken in designing this outstanding beach front home, using natural woods, coral stone and marble along with attractive architectural features and wide open living spaces and terraces.

The building includes 24hr security and the tropically landscaped grounds include a communal pool and sun deck.
