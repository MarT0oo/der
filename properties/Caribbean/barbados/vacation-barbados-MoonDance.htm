Moon Dance, Sandy Lane, Barbados Vacation Rental

 

Moon Dance is a stunning private residence set in almost two acres of beautifully landscaped gardens at the heart of Barbados’ renowned Sandy Lane Estate. The villa faces west and overlooks the 8th hole of the famous golf course. Only a short walk down the 9th fairway, past the Old Nine Clubhouse & Hotel Tennis courts, and across the road is the main entrance to The Sandy Lane Hotel and the Sandy Lane Property Owners beach facility on the island's finest west coast beach.

With renovations completed for the 2015 season, the villa offers total privacy in its very quiet and safe location at the end of a "no through road" behind electric gates and security fencing. It's contemporary interior design blends natural and vibrant colours harmoniously... along with spacious indoor and outdoor living areas make it the ideal holiday home for a large family or group of friends.

The main villa comprises of four luxurious double bedrooms that face the garden aspect with French doors to let the breeze through! Additionally they are air conditioned with ceiling fans to ensure you are kept at exactly the right temperature. All four feature en-suite baths and separate showers. The newly configured large master suite also features an air-conditioned bathroom. The main guest suite with a private balcony (at centre of house) has a twin beds large walk-in-closet and large bathtub with views over the garden. King "four poster" beds are a feature of the final two bedroom suites (one of which is on the ground floor).

A spacious open plan living and dining room, with seating for 8 people, opens on to a sizable covered patio, overlooking the swimming pool and gardens, with further lounge seating and a 10/12 seat dining area for enjoying al fresco meals. A further poolside dining table seats 6. A fully equipped and air-conditioned Italian Scavolini kitchen fitted in 2015 and if you really have to watch TV there is a separate air conditioned TV room with Apple TV equipped 40 inch television and DVD player completes the main house. The house has Internet Wi-Fi, Vonage Internet telephone & fax machine.

At the bottom of the garden is a separate self contained 2 bedroom (air conditioned) cottage with its own kitchen/lounge area and Mahogany shaded patio. This is an ideal place for extra guests looking for a little more privacy.

Guests at Moon Dance can enjoy relaxing around the beautiful Japanese mosaic pool and jacuzzi, and the pool has the added benefit of having low chemical salt water. You will struggle to find a swimming pool that is so warm and inviting! Surplus sun-loungers, two gazebos, one with a bar and one with bathroom and shower facilities, ensure you have all you need to spend an uninterrupted day by the pool.

The beautiful Sandy Lane beach is just a few minutes’ drive away (its less than 0.5 mile) and guests of Moon Dance have Membership to the Sandy Lane Property Owners Beach Facility with parking, restrooms and sun loungers & shades will be provided free of charge by friendly staff.
