Casablanca, Sandy Lane, Barbados Vacation Rental

 

Located inside the exclusive neighbourhood of Sandy Lane, this elegant estate is an ideal retreat for a group of friends or large family, making it one of our top Barbados villa rentals. When you travel to Barbados, you’ll experience the fine beaches, rich culture and pleasant climate the island is known for.

This estate is bursting with amenities—keep up your exercise regime in the villa’s equipped gym or play a round on the private tennis court. During the day, swim in the 58-foot, infinity-edge pool and then lounge on one of the sun chaises with umbrellas, or wander through the landscaped gardens. In the evening, the media room has a flatscreen with Apple TV, games, books and an entertainment bar.

Inside, this classy home features a minimalist but inviting ambiance with white décor, punches of colour, high-quality furnishings and vivid artwork. The spacious, open kitchen boasts all stainless steel appliances and modern cabinetry as well as an island with two wine coolers. Choose to enjoy a meal in the cozy dining area or in the more formal space, complete with a circular table under an iron chandelier. And there’s always the option of dining al fresco—the terrace has a dining gazebo as well as seating areas with plush couches and a crystal chandelier.

Up to 14 guests can comfortably stay here. The main home’s five bedrooms include en-suite bathrooms and have beds ranging from kings to bunks. The master suite has a sitting area and views of the pool along with a private patio overlooking the golf course. It also boasts a king-sized bed, walk-in closet and an en-suite bathroom with marble tiling, a rain shower and a marble tub. Two more bedrooms with king beds are in the guest cottage.

As a guest, you’ll have access to Sandy Lane Beach Facility’s golf course and private beach cabana. Five minutes away are the high-end shops and fine dining options of Holetown.
