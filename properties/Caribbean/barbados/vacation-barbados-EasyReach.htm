Easy Reach, St. Peter, Barbados Vacation Rental

 

Easy Reach is an impressive beach house offering 3 bedrooms with en suite bathrooms just a few feet from the sea on the white sands of the famous Mullins Beach. The house was totally refurbished in January 2009 to an excellent standard.

It is decorated simply in white with wood pickled ceilings. It has modern beach house style furnishings, lovely bathrooms and a brand new kitchen. The spacious living room patio doors open onto a covered dining terrace. The covered terrace leads to a spacious wooden deck where you can relax on sun loungers and enjoy the spectacular views of the Caribbean Sea.

The upstairs master bedroom has French doors that open onto an elevated spacious outside deck with breath taking north and south panoramic views over the entire beach area. The second bedroom opens directly onto the covered terrace and the sun deck.
