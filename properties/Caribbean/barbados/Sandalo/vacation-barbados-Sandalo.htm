Sandalo, Gibbs Beach, St. Peter, Barbados

 

Architect Ian Morrison has overseen the total renovation and refurbishment of Sandalo, an exceptional property situated on a beachfront site shaded by many mature mahogany trees on the island's renowned Gibbs Beach.

Sandalo offers a total of five bedrooms - four in the main house (all on the upper level) as well as a self-contained bedroom in the guest cottage on the villa grounds. Three of the bedrooms in the main house open to balconies and all have wonderful views of the pool, gardens and Caribbean Sea.

There is a media room equipped with state-of-the-art plasma TV, electronic games and surround sound. The air-conditioned kitchen is custom designed with cabinets by Joshua Jones of the UK.

The swimming pool is surrounded by a partly covered spacious terrace ideal for entertaining. There is also a delightful poolside gazebo suitable for alfresco dining. A gate leads to the beautiful white beach with another seating area at the gate.
