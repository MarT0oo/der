Reeds House 5, Lower Carlton, St. James, Barbados Vacation Rental

 

This spectacular ground floor beachfront condominium is situated in the quiet Reeds House complex and offers up a tropical oasis with spectacular views from every room.

Elegantly furnished with a cool Caribbean style it has a wonderful relaxed atmosphere with an open plan kitchen and living area that leads out to a spacious open air dining and lounge area under the covered terrace. With an understated luxury and a cool Mediterranean style guests will enjoy the tranquility of the private garden, the sheltered spa pool and the tropical foliage that sets the scene for relaxation. Access to the soft white sands of the bay is just in front of the condo and the waters are great for snorkeling while the sands are shaded by swaying coconut trees.

There is a separate dining area adjacent to the spa pool where many memorable star lit meals can be enjoyed and the sunsets on the West are simply spectacular!

The cool coral stone interiors enhance the tropical ambiance of the apartment and the two air conditioned bedrooms open out to the patio and gorgeous views of the picturesque bay beyond. In keeping with the spectacular views of the lush palm trees, sparkling blue waters and soft white sands, the decor is elegantly simple with tropical colours, island inspired furnishings.

Golf, water sports, tennis and a plethora of island excursions can easily be arranged by the on site concierge and the busy hotspots, restaurants and luxury shops of the West are only a short drive away.
