Clearwater, St. Peter, Barbados Vacation Rental

 

Clearwater, a spacious coral-stone holiday villa set in tropical gardens, is located on the beautiful Gibbs Beach, St. Peter on the West Coast of Barbados.

The villa is furnished with lovely antiques throughout, and has an open plan dining and living area which opens on to a large, covered patio overlooking the spacious gardens, lily pond and the sparkling Caribbean Sea.

All four bedrooms are air-conditioned as well as having cooling ceiling fans. Upstairs, the master bedroom features a queen size bed, en suite bathroom, and a large picture window with excellent views of the sea and gardens. Downstairs, the sea-view bedroom has twin beds and en suite bathroom. The remaining two bedrooms overlook the gardens and have twin beds and share a bathroom.

Spend your days relaxing on the private patio, where you can enjoy the company of family and friends in picturesque surroundings. Enjoy a drink in the sun or in the shade of the tamarind tree after you take a quick dip or a swim in the Caribbean Sea. Clearwater's extensive beachfront patio provides direct access to Gibbs Beach where you can walk on pristine white sands or sit and watch the glorious sunsets. It's a short drive to Speightstown, the nearest shopping area.

Let peaceful Clearwater be your tropical getaway for a week or two... or even longer.
