Villas on the Beach 205, Holetown, St. James, Barbados Vacation Rental

 

Villas on the Beach is located on a beautiful West Coast beach in the heart of Holetown, St. James and designed by well known architect, Ian Morrison. Just a short walk away are all of the excellent amenities that Holetown has to offer - world class dining and duty free shopping, an excellent grocery store, 24-hour medical centre, spas and salons, and lots of nightlife! In addition, for the golfer at heart, we are located just a short drive away from three world renowned golf courses - Apes Hill Club, Sandy Lane and Royal Westmoreland.

Apartment 205 is located on the first floor with a spectacular view of the clear Caribbean Sea from the private patio. This modern two bedroom, two bathroom villa offers fully air conditioned living areas with ceiling fans throughout. The master bedroom has been designed to take advantage of the amazing view so that you can wake up to the waves each morning of your stay! All bedrooms have en suite bathrooms.

This apartment offers guests the added benefit of membership to the in-house gym located conveniently in the basement of the complex. Your holiday experience will be made that much better by the attentions of your friendly housekeeper who will prepare local meals for you and help with the laundry!

Come enjoy the ideal vacation at Villas on the Beach 205!
