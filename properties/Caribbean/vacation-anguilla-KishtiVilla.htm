Kishti Villa, Anguilla, Caribbean Vacation Rental

 

The word KISHTI in Urdu, a language derived from the Arabic and Hindi languages, means a boat, an ark, or a canoe. It is quite frequently used in poetry and verse, set to Indian music to reflect life, love, God, and possibly other things left to the writer's imagination.

Villa Kishti's design by renowned international designers Cecconi Simone, Inc. and architecture by Frank Hamilton have resulted in a contemporary villa with stunning design and architectural details that will offer an enriching, enlightening, and rewarding "lifestyle" experience. Fronted by the villa's volcanic rock shoreline, large infinity pool, Grotto with a hot tub, giant shaded bed, music, and a bar, the villa's angled and sloping roofs are Kishti's signature features, quite unique on the island.

The spirit of Villa KISHTI is captured in the simple luxury afforded by an exceptional location, blended with a very desirable environment. It has embraced its secluded setting with a modern villa and Asian sensibilities, to fulfill that very spirit and deliver a promise of tranquility, delight, and complete privacy. The villa will provide a setting of natural beauty in which excellent service and guests' privacy are paramount.

A spacious living room with a spectacular view of the water, an indoor-outdoor dining area to seat eight to twelve, and a modern kitchen will enhance one's comfort and overall experience. There are four bedroom suites with an office and exercise room, including a private terrace that also serves as a recreation room. The villa may be rented as a one-bedroom, two-bedroom, or three-bedroom as well. The placement of three suites, each with its own private terrace and panoramic views of the Atlantic Ocean, assures individual privacy for guests. The master bedroom suite has its own private terrace, an outdoor shower, and an individual hot tub, all overlooking the Atlantic waters. Each suite, individually decorated, has its own refrigerator and coffee service.

Anguilla's distinct Caribbean beauty, combined with an effortless living experience, make the island an ideal getaway for the most discriminating travelers. Kishti Villa will provide for a memorable visit to Anguilla by such a traveler.
