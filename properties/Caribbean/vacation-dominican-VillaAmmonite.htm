Villa Ammonite, Punta Cana, Dominican Republic Vacation Rental

 

Carved in natural coraline stones and rectangular stone walls with a majestic blue ammonite shape, high-ceiled wooden the entrance will take all guests to one of the most envied and fabulous views of the Caribbean ocean. The way goes along a large aisle next to water mirrors with colorful fishes. To the left is the large and imposing dining room with a 22-seats table, modern art expressions and lighted up by a massive and genuine 3-levels chandelier. The fully-equipped kitchen with dual fridge, large kitchen island, breakfast bar, marble countertops, and dual sinks is always ready to get the most delicious local and international dishes from the high-skilled chef. The vast and ample living room is located at the end of the hall with sophisticated tapestry, high wooden apex ceilings, Italian design sofas, a wet bar, and access to the large garden, pool, and jacuzzi.

The outdoor furniture under the pergola next to three hammocks swinging gently to the beat of the sea breeze offer the guests the summit of relaxing. From the pool and the jacuzzi, the views are simply mesmerizing and can we enjoyed from the loungers inside the water while the kids have fun in the playground. For entertainment, everybody has multiple options: from a pool table game to watch a movie inside the comfy home cinema or for loosening up, the massage room and the fully-equipped gym have all the equipment guests need.

On the ground floor there a bedroom with a queen bed. On the second floor, 5 bedrooms are ready for a nap or an excellent sleep. The master bedroom has a king bed, 3 bedrooms with queen beds, the fifth bedroom with 2 twin beds. They all have ensuite bathrooms and air conditioning, and open to a balcony or terrace. Guests can get to the second floor, gym, and massage room using the elevator.

This beautiful villa is located in the exclusive gated community of Punta Cana Resort & Club, a 26-square mile paradise that is blessed with a temperate year-round climate, light rainfall, and soft breezes. It's colorful coral reefs, calm crystalline waters and white-sand beaches are breathtaking. The Resort provides a matchless experience for all who visit this tropical paradise, and its tropically chic lifestyle is ideal for those who appreciate high standards of quality, comfort and aesthetics. As guests of the villa, you have access to all of the resort amenities and activities.
