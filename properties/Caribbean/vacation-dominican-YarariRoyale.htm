Yarari Royale, Cap Cana, Dominican Republic Vacation Rental

 

Discover a new concept of luxury holiday, where good taste, relaxation, fitness, modernism and minimalism are blended in such a way you will have a unique experience at the new Yarari Royale. Recently built with the most advanced architectural techniques under an avant-garde vision, this stunning 9 bedroom estate rises above the rest. Located inside Cap Cana, one of the most renowned gated villages throughout the Caribbean, within a 5 minute golf cart drive to the renowned Eden Roc Beach Club (complimentary access).

As you enter the villa, a large foyer with high ceilings greets guests, with cool garden breezes from the terrace. In the main living areas, and throughout the property, the villa is sprinkled with subtle modern touches and governed by a minimalistic outlook. The furniture is cleverly positioned to find the perfect balance, giving a relaxed ambiance for all guests to provide a relax ambience enabling guests to fully enjoy its unique amenities.

When going upstairs to the second floor you will find 7 of the 9 bedrooms. There are two master bedrooms with a King bed, an ensuite bathroom and Jacuzzi on the terrace. Then, you have 4 double bedrooms with 2 Queen beds and ensuite bathroom each and the seventh bedroom, ideal for families, has 2 Queen beds and 2 Twin Bunk Beds with its ensuite bathroom. Finally, when going downstairs you will find the eighth bedroom, another master with a King bed and ensuite bathroom next to the last bedroom with 2 Queen beds and ensuite bathroom.

After a long beach day, the outdoor pool is the ideal place to cool off and gather with friends, or sit on the large alfresco dining to enjoy a drink, and appreciate the sunset while planning the next day’s activities. Alternatively, you can make the most of the pleasant evenings and relax in the outdoor hot tub with a nice glass of champagne, either in peace or with good company. For those active guests on holidays, they can keep in shape with the most advanced home gym you will find in the Caribbean, bestowed with equipment from the world renowned brand TECHNOGYM.

Equipment: Dumbbell Rack with Dumbbell Pairs from 1kg to 10kg, Adjustable Bench, Excite®+ Vario Elliptical Cross Trainer, SkillMill™ Console, Jog Treadmill, Kinesis PERSONAL HERITAGE BLACK (This is the ultimate piece of designer gym furniture for the home and wellness spaces. It allows you to rediscover and improve strength, coordination, flexibility, posture control and breathing control), Arke Functional Kit Med on wall holder.

The community Resort provides a matchless experience for all who visit this tropical paradise, and its tropically chic lifestyle is ideal for those who appreciate high standards of quality, comfort and aesthetics. As guests of the villa, you have access to all of the resort amenities, casa club and activities.
