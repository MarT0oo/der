Dos Sols Vacation Rental, Virgin Gorda, BVI


Located in one of the quietest sections of Leverick Bay, Dos Sols Villa is known for its design, privacy, proximity to the beach, heated pool, and exceptional amenities. A unique custom architectural design separates each bedroom from the main villa, allowing your family and friends to gather on the large covered verandah, Great Room or pool deck, while providing privacy and intimacy of separate bedroom cottages. Regardless of the size of your group, while at Dos Sols Villa you have exclusive use of the entire villa.

There are 3 bedrooms with accommodations for up to 7 individuals, each with air-conditioning and Caribbean-style outdoors showers overlooking the water. Bedrooms are clustered on different levels around a 10,000 gallon freeform pool (depth 3 ft - 6 ft) and native stone deck. The pool is comfortably heated during winter months - when shorter daylight hours and brisk breezes can make pool water too cold to enjoy.

Bedrooms include two separate cottages and one lower level main villa bedroom located adjacent to the pool. "Sea Aria" - the master suite cottage - is designed for privacy and intimacy with a king-sized bed, sitting area, mini-refrigerator, coffeemaker, covered verandah and outdoor furniture. TRAVELING TO VIRGIN GORDA:


If you're arriving by air, keep in mind that there are no direct flights from the USA, Canada, Europe or South America to the British Virgin Islands main airport - the Terrence B. Lettsome Airport (EIS). When booking air travel remember to request flights to Tortola or use the airport code, EIS. The airport code for the Virgin Gorda Airport is VIJ. If you're arriving by sea, ports of entry are located in Road Town and the West End on Tortola, Great Harbour on Jost Van Dyke and St. Thomas Bay in Virgin Gorda. All vessels must clear Customs and Immigration immediately upon arrival and a valid passport and boat registration papers may be required.

All roads lead to the British Virgin Islands. Connecting airports surround the BVI, making arrivals and departures an easy part of your travel itinerary.
