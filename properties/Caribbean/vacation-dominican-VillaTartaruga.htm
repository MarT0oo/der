Villa Tartaruga, Punta Cana, Dominican Republic, Caribbean Vacation Rental

 

Behold and marvel one of the brand-new and modern properties built inside the exclusive Puntacana Resort & Club. Erected in one of the last remaining oceanfront lots, Tartaruga offers enough space to host 20 guests in any of its 8 comfy and large bedrooms.

Like coming out of one of Picasso's masterpieces, the main entrance takes you through a long corridor next to a crystal-clear pond and rounded white columns while discovering the cubic facade that takes you to the main foyer of this splendid two-stories villa. The marble tiles on the floor and the coralina stones on the wall will guide you to the living room through a high ceiling with outstanding wooden beams and surrounded by evergreen palms and Caribbean vegetation. There, the breathtaking view of the blue-emerald ocean is perfectly balanced with the rattan chairs, couches and sitting places composing a smooth atmosphere. Stepping outside welcomes everyone to plunge into the large pool with a hot tub and to have a clean out-view to the endless Caribbean Sea. The diverse exterior living areas offer multiple options to enjoy the comfortable sea breeze. Next to the living room is the dining room with exquisite decor and a large table with enough space for 22 seated guests, The eyes are naturally led to the mesmerizing outdoor views and light is provided by a suspended rectangular and modern chandelier.

For exercising and shaping bodies the villa has a full gym with Technogym Equipment, dumbells and more. After the training session, the massage room offers all you need to relax and recover strength. For those who are not big sports fans, the property has a unique 12-seat cinema to watch your favorite movie while having popcorns or the media room on the second floor with 4K HD TV. For the ones who like to bet, on the guest house, there is a bar with 4 TV's and a poker table to try out luck and strategy.

When the sun goes down, energy needs to be pumped up! On the second floor of the main villa, that can we reached with the elevator, you have the master bedroom with a king bed, ensuite bathroom with a tub and amazing views to the garden and ocean. The second and third bedrooms have a king bed and an ensuite bathroom each. On the first floor, the fourth bedroom has 2 queen beds and the fifth and the sixth bedrooms have 1 king bed each. They all also have an ensuite bathroom each. Finally, in the guest house on the second floor, we have the seventh bedroom with a king bed and the eighth bedroom with 2 queen beds. Both have an ensuite bathroom each.

This beautiful villa is located in the exclusive gated community of Punta Cana Resort & Club, a 26-square mile paradise that is blessed with a temperate year-round climate, light rainfall, and soft breezes. It's colorful coral reefs, calm crystalline waters and white-sand beaches are breathtaking. The Resort provides a matchless experience for all who visit this tropical paradise, and its tropically chic lifestyle is ideal for those who appreciate high standards of quality, comfort and aesthetics. As guests of the villa, you have access to all of the resort amenities and activities.
