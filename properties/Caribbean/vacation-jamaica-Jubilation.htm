Jubilation, Tryall Club, Jamaica Vacation Rental

 

This magnificent villa is located on Mahogany Drive with the entrance driveway on Ocean View Drive. Constructed to the highest of standards and quality and with lovely sea views, Jubilation sits surrounded by a mature, beautifully landscaped garden that has become the favourite habitat of birds. With soaring ceilings and an abundance of free-flowing space, Jubilation offers two master bedroom suites on the upper level and four guest rooms on the lower level. The villa kitchen is regarded as being one of the best-equipped at Tryall, the swimming pool is huge and the sheer amount of veranda and patio space make it a perfect home for entertaining in.

Built on two levels, the villa is a celebration of Jamaican architecture and detailing including intricate fretwork and indigenous Mahogany beam vaulted ceilings. Throughout the villa you will find magnificent plant specimens, a vibrant collection of art and a happy all-pervading spirit. On the main level of the house are two master bedrooms and on the lower floor are four guest bedrooms. The large master bedrooms feature king sized four poster beds with lavish en suites complete with travertine tiles, marble vanities, copper sinks and exotic claw foot tubs. Both bedrooms open onto the full length terrace which overlooks the pool and with ocean and garden views. Each bedroom has a 42 inch flat screen television with cable programming and ipod docking stations.

The four guest bedrooms have two four poster queen beds each and immaculate en suite bathrooms with walk-in showers. These bedrooms flank the spacious pool deck and have views of the sea or gardens.

The spacious living and dining areas are furnished with interesting works of art with doors that fold back allowing the breezes to come in. A separate entertainment room on the lower level comes with a 60 inch television, games, music system, books as well as an adjoining bathroom and door that open to the lower verandah, bar and pool area. Throughout Jubilation there are enchanting little nooks and alcoves that provide perfect escape spots for reading or a lazy nap. Outside, the villa is noted for its large heated pool and surrounding terrace.
