Villa Overlook, Montego Bay, Jamaica Vacation Rental


Overlook is one of Tryall's newest additions to their collection of luxury villas in Montego Bay.  With spectacular views of the Caribbean Sea and Tryall's famous golf course, Overlook villa lives up to its name with memorizing views. There are 5 wonderful bedrooms all with air conditioning for comfort.

Fully staffed villa with a butler, cook, housekeeper, laundress and pool-person/gardener, you will be totally pampered this vacation.  Outdoor dining on the verandah overlooking the golf course and Caribbean Sea.  Large spacious living room opens out to the Verandah with spectacular view of the Caribbean Sea.

Built on one of the prime sites at Tryall and conveniently located in easy walking distance of the Great House, gym and tennis courts, with total privacy and magnificent landscaping, Overlook Villa (formerly Fern Hill) was completed in November of 2006.

Designed to afford one hundred and eighty degree views of the golf course and Caribbean Sea from all of its rooms, its pool deck and its one hundred foot long veranda; it is fixture with all of the modern amenities. It was decorated by a noted interior designer to combine the best of contemporary and native furnishings and art in a distinctly Jamaican flavor. The expansive Great Room, with its 25 foot high peaked roof, houses a fifty inch plasma television and state of the art sound system which plays throughout the villa. All five of its bedroom suites are air conditioned as is the dining room, which opens to the pool.

Its three master and two deluxe suites have luxurious en suite bathrooms (with glass enclosed showers) and plasma televisions and DVD players. Each master suite has its own distinctive appeal. The Ridge Suite contains a sumptuous bathroom with a Jacuzzi tub as well as a private veranda; the Palm Suite contains a romantic nine foot shower for two; and the Pool Cottage, in addition to its high octagonal ceiling, sits across a small bridge like an island astride the pool “overlooking” the sea.

The villa's fifty foot heated swimming pool is framed by mature royal palms. It contains Jacuzzi platforms and is surrounded by a large deck bathed in sunshine throughout the day.

An experienced staff of five provides complete, friendly and efficient service. Guests are provided with two new complimentary golf carts. The 8,000 square foot villa is also equipped with an emergency generator and an auxiliary water supply.
