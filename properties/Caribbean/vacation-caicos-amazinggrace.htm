Amazing Grace, Grace Bay, Providenciales, Turks and Caicos Vacation Rental

 

Located on the pristine white sand beaches of Providenciales Island, Amazing Grace is conveniently situated within a 15-minute drive to all local amenities.

Amazing Grace was constructed in 2007 on a private 18-acre lot with a full renovation completed in 2010, making this villa the perfect destination for all types of vacations. With over 9000 square feet of living space, five bedrooms each with a private en-suite bathroom, plenty of rooms for entertaining and all types of technological amenities to keep you as connected to the outside world as you wish, Amazing Grace strikes a perfect balance between laid back relaxation and 21st century beach villa Caribbean Turks & Caicos lifestyle.

From the moment you walk into Amazing Grace, be prepared to be spoiled.

Enjoy the services of a housekeeper during the day and a security guard each night. A Villa Manager is available 7 days a week to answer questions and make your stay enjoyable.

Let each day bring with it the delights of beachfront living on Provo. Refreshing morning breezes carrying the clean scents of sea and sun wind prepare you for the start of another day of doing exactly what you want. Enjoy your day lingering by the pool, cooling off from time to time with a dip. Or plan for an afternoon beach swim or snorkel in the serene blue sea. Take in a luxurious mid-day nap where you are lulled to sleep by the rustle of palm trees and the scents of frangipani and bougainvillea.

Surrender to the rhythms of the ocean, the tranquility of island life, and the unhurried pace that puts you at the top of your own to-do list. And after your day of sun and play and an evening spent lingering over sunset views and local seafood, let the sultry night air wrap you in its indulgent embrace...a reminder that tomorrow is a brand new day and that Amazing Grace is your personal playground.
