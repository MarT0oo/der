Villa La Pinta, Terres Basses, St. Martin, Caribbean


The wide open design of this spacious four bedroom villa is in complete harmony with its natural setting. The view from the living room stretches from the tastefully furnished deck to the distant horizon giving the impression of even more space.

All the bedrooms have a king-sized bed and private bathrooms with showers, air conditioning & ceiling fans, plasma TV & DVD player.  A covered porch with outdoor dining and lounge areas gives Villa La Pinta an authentic Creole flavor. Villa La Pinta also features sunset views, lovely gardens, a tennis court, and a gym room.
