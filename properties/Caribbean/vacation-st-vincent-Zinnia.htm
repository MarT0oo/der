Zinnia, Mustique, Grenadines, Caribbean Vacation Rental

 

One of the largest original Oliver Messel villas, Zinnia enjoys a commanding hillside position with far reaching sea views of the Grenadine islands. A vibrant collection of objets d'art and spectacular trompe l'oeil provide the perfect backdrop for spectacular house parties and family gatherings

Zinnia offers spectacular spacious open-plan living, withe extensive terraces for reading and relaxing, and numerous dining options. A separate media/entertainment room with an encyclopedic film and music library provides the ultimate entertainment retreat

Guests climb a grand circular staircase to reach four master bedrooms; two king size with en-suite bathroom, shower, dressing room and double basins, and two queen size with en-suite bathrooms, shower, and single basins.

The property can be let with or without the two bedroom guest cottage which features one queen size and one twin, complete with its own covered verandah

Originally designed and built by Oliver Messel in 1977. Refurbished in 1997

Meet the Chef

Joycelyn Robertson has been at Zinnia since December 1, 1997. She started out as a maid but was always in the kitchen. She is an "all rounder" but specialises in sushi and Italian cuisine. She received some formal training from some FCI (French Culinary Institute NYC) courses held on the island but mostly she is self taught.
