Arabian Nights, Casa de Campo, Dominican Republic Vacation Rental

 

Arabian Nights in Casa de Campo Dominican Republic is a mix of the beautiful Spanish feeling and the amazing local architecture. Beautiful bright colors and lovely amenities make this a one of a kind experience. Bring your entire family and feel right at home. A curated home from our new Casa De Campo Collection.

Spectacular, modern and beautiful villa designed and decorated by renowned italian architect Gianfranco Fini. The property is located in front of hole number nine of La Romana Country Club Golf Course and close to Minitas Beach. Great work and attention to details and quality furniture.

A large collection of works by contemporary Italian and international artist can be seeing throughout the villa. Two sided gardens, large lounge and dining area, TV room, study/library, covered terrace, gazebo, pool deck, infinity edge pool and Jacuzzi are part of the amenities. The villa comes with with four bedrooms that include two master suites with a king size bed, dressing room a large bathroom and an outdoor shower. The remaining two guest suites each feature on-suite bathrooms and outdoor showers. Great place to enjoy with family and friends.
