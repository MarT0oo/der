Grenada Luxury Estate, St. George, Grenada Vacation Rental


The Grenada Luxury Estate is a one of a kind experience unlike any other. You will find yourself treated like Royalty from the moment you arrive, private cars will come and pick your group up and bring you back to the villa for your stay. You will arrive to an awaiting staff, ready to assist in unpacking, bringing your welcome cocktails and showing you around the most unique and luxurious property you'll ever encounter.

The property only ever takes one booking at a time so you will always have exclusive use of the now 12 bedroom property, which can be booked for exclusive use with 6, 8, 12 or (from Summer 2018) with 17 bedrooms. It is set in 6 acres of completely private tropical gardens and comprises of the following accommodation:

Main mansion house - 4 huge bedrooms, 3 of which have a completely private balcony with sun beds, these rooms open up completely on one side. These rooms are all luxurious and over-sized and have fantastic sea views. They overlook the large infinity swimming pool which is just a few steps away. Top 3 View suites - are set on their own at the top of the property with truly wonderful views. Each of these suites opens up completely onto a private deck. The Folly suite - set on its own.

4 bedroom Beach House with private pool - set directly on white sand beach with its own sitting room, each of the bedrooms of the Beach House are luxurious and over-sized. From Summer 2018 an additional 5 newly built luxury bedrooms will be available set in beautiful gardens with fantastic sea views. Each bedroom has a super king sized bed (6 foot x 6 foot 3 inches) imported from Europe, 7 of the rooms also have king sized sofa beds.

All rooms have a luxurious en-suite bathroom with luxury toiletries, fluffy white towels, dressing gowns, slippers. The bed linen is all imported from Europe and is an amazing 1000 thread count. Goose down or non-allergenic pillows, there is even a pillow menu! All rooms are air conditioned, have ceiling fans, all bedrooms have private stocked fridges, tea and coffee making facilities, all rooms have large screen smart TV with the most extensive cable package available in the Caribbean. The property also has a cinema facility which can be moved outdoors for ‘movies under the stars’. The property has a huge Great room with windows on 3 sides with extensive patios overlooking the water. It has indoor and outdoor dining capability for large groups with a choice of venues on the property. It has a Tiki bar, a swim up bar and a full bar. Cocktails are a specialty!

The property Manager has over 15 years of managing and creating and experience for it's luxury property guests. Michael graduated from one of the top Hospitality schools in Switzerland and has made his mark on the Luxury Boutique Private Island and Luxury Villa Market. He is ably assisted by an internationally trained Head Housekeeper, one of the top Chefs in the Caribbean (British trained) and a team of not less than 18 staff. The Security Manager is former British Special Forces. The property can provide ultra luxury service if required including 24x7 room service (premium rate bookings). The property has multi-lingual staff.

Located on the beautiful island of Grenada with easy daily flights from the USA, and frequent flights from Europe this beautiful property is very private. It has the benefits of a private island but with no access difficulties as it is situated just 15 minutes from the International airport. Situated on a privately owned peninsula, it is a 6 acre gated Estate with 24 x 7 security. Grenada is one of the safest Caribbean Islands with very little crime but this Owner values her privacy and security.

The property has its own generators, private water supply, desalination plant and is fully gated. Although Grenada is considered to be outside of the hurricane zone (being the farthest south of the Caribbean Islands that we offer) this property is built to hurricane code.

The property has a 185 foot private dock, wraparound completely private (man made) white sand beaches set with swaying palm trees, a large (90 foot) infinity swimming pool with swim up bar and stunning views over the Bay. It has a spa pool also with a fantastic view. There is also a large swimming pool on the beach as well as steps from the beach into the crystal clear waters of the Mount Hartman Bay. There is fantastic snorkelling directly from the property and the dock provides a wonderful backdrop for hundreds of colourful tropical fish.

The property has 2 boats for guest use (with Captain) and this is at additional rate plan. The 31 foot motor cruiser has an onboard fresh water shower and WC and is perfect for exploring and fishing whilst the 19 foot jet boat is perfect for all watersports. The property has extensive water sports facilities including water skis, wakeboard, knee board, kayaks, tubes and lots of fun toys such as floating islands (with their own bar!) that can be attached to the dock. The Owner owns the dredging rights to the Bay around the property and there is a 150 foot privacy cordon from the water side of the property. There are also 3 luxury vehicles for guest use (with driver) including a 14 seater luxury mini van, 2 seven seater SUVs.

Guests who recently booked this property did not leave at all for 10 days, the property really has it all! However if you want to explore the beautiful Island there is a full concierge service available and plenty to see and do.
