Cinnamon Heights, Grenada, West Indies Vacation Rental

 

One of Grenada's most luxurious villa properties with every service and facility of the Mount Cinnamon Resort, this villa is unique.

With 6,000 sq ft of indoor space, terraces and decks, the villa has been designed to take maximum advantage of the breath-taking views of the Caribbean Sea from all rooms. Stylishly decorated with contemporary classic furnishings, the villa is set in private tropical gardens. Every comfort and amenity has been incorporated into the design including a large infinity pool, separate outdoor jacuzzi tub, fully fitted kitchen and laundry with latest appliances and a separate dining room. The villa has BOSE home entertainment system, IPOD Docs, Wi-Fi, Cable TV, DVD Player.

The master bedroom suite has a large study sitting room with a sleeper sofa-bed and generous dressing areas and ensuite bathroom. There are two further guest bedrooms with twin/king beds and ensuite bathrooms. Reception areas have additional powder rooms.

There are large reception areas inside and out. Fully air-conditioned.
