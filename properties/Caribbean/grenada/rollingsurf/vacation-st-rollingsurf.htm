Rolling Surf, L’Anse aux Epines, Grenada, West Indies.


This superb new stunning bespoke beachfront villa brings you closer to the water’s edge than you ever imagined possible. Bordering Prickly Bay, one of Grenada’s favourite anchorages, Rolling Surf can sleep up to 6 guests in three bedrooms, including a magnificent master suite and two further pool side bedrooms.

There is a self-contained apartment, which can be available upon special request. Located in L’Anse aux Epines, the most sought after residential area in Grenada, this luxurious villa is the definitive waterside setting for your best Caribbean holiday. Just yards from a beautiful beach, with the famous Calabash Beach bar and Gry Rodes Restaurant and the ScubaTech PADI dive centre, with a shop, hairdressers, spa and other restaurants within walking distance. What more would be on the wish list of a discerning traveller or guest?

Rolling Surf offers sophisticated comfort in a convenient location. The villa has a very spacious kitchen adjoining the living and dining room area, a breezy covered patio area and three air conditioned bedrooms with en suite bathrooms. The 6 foot, four poster master suite is enhanced by a large lavish master bathroom with whirlpool bath for two, fully tiled walls, deluxe shower and a dressing room. Sliding bi-fold doors lead out to the stunning, private covered veranda with uninterrupted views to the sea.

The second & third bedrooms are situated in the guest area with direct access to the pool deck. The apartment which is available on separate request, offers excellent privacy with a very separate bedroom as it is below the master suite and is only 15 feet way from the sea. A hardwood staircase leads to the sea and beach access. We also have an exercise area with workout equipment and a TV Room with 40” LCD TV, Cable TV and international multi region DVD player, and A computer equipped office,.

All guests at Rolling Surf are entitled to 10% discount on all Food and Beverage consumed at The Calabash Beach Bar & Restaurant. A mini market is located close by in L’Anse aux Epines. A short drive away is picturesque St. George’s, Grand Anse beach and the airport.

Rolling Surf is well situated to catch cool tropical breezes and lovely sunset views of Prickly Bay and yachts at anchor. Cherrie- anne, Rolling Surf’s housekeeper, is there to make your stay in Grenada a relaxing, carefree, memorable holiday.

We will meet you at the airport and take you to the villa, and introduce you to Rolling Surf with a welcome drink. Your first evening’s dinner with wine will await you as well as a welcome basket with breakfast items. Cherrie-anne is also available for babysitting and can stay late or a weekends at an additional hourly rate. Cherie-anne will be at rolling surf 8am to 4pm every day.
