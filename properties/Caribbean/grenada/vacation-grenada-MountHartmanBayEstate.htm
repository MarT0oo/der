Featured in Harper’s, Conde Nast Traveler and a host of other big name titles, this property offers world renowned views, one of a kind architecture and unparalleled service. The estates architecture mixes traditional, modern, organic and futuristic. This charming property exudes an unmistakable air of elegance, sophistication and luxury.

The property only ever takes one booking at a time so you will always have exclusive use of the now 15-bedroom property, which can be booked with 8, 12 or 15 bedrooms. All bedrooms have a luxurious en-suite bathroom with luxury toiletries, fluffy white towels, dressing gowns and slippers. The bed linens are all imported from Europe of amazing 1,000 thread count. Goose down or non-allergenic pillows, there is even a pillow menu! All rooms are air conditioned with ceiling fans. All bedrooms have private stocked fridges, tea and coffee making facilities. All rooms have large screen smart TV with the most extensive cable package available in the Caribbean. The property also has a cinema facility which can be moved outdoors for ‘movies under the stars’. The property has a huge Great Room with windows on 3 sides with extensive patios overlooking the water. It has indoor and outdoor dining capability for large groups with a choice of venues on the property. It has a Tiki bar, a swim up bar and a full bar. Cocktails are a specialty!

The property Manager has over 15 years of managing and creating an unforgettable experience for its luxury property guests. Michael graduated from one of the top Hospitality schools in Switzerland and has made his mark on the Luxury Boutique Private Island and Luxury Villa Market. He is ably assisted by an internationally trained Head Housekeeper, one of the top Chefs in the Caribbean (British trained) and a team of 14 staff members (more for larger groups). The property can provide ultra-luxury service if required including 24/7 room service (premium rate bookings). The staff is multilingual.

This resort is set in 6.5 acres of completely private tropical gardens and comprises of the following accommodation:

Main Mansion House
4 large bedroom suites, 3 of which have a completely private sea view balcony with sun loungers. All have ensuite baths with tubs and separate walk-in showers. One has a 2 person Jacuzzi tub. The suites can comfortably take additional beds as they are an average of 1,000 square feet each. These rooms are all over-sized luxurious with imported super king size (6-foot x 6 foot 3 inches) beds and breathtaking sea views. They overlook the large infinity swimming pool which is just a few steps away. For larger groups with children there are 8 very high-end pocket sprung rollaway beds that can go into any of the bedroom suites. The Main House has a fully equipped air-conditioned gym with a treadmill, cross trainer, stationary bicycle, professional multi-gym set, power plate and lots of free weights.

Top 3 View Suites
Walking up one outside flight of scenic stairs (lit with fairy lights and stunning views) to the top of the property you come to the 3 Top View Suites. Each has bi-fold doors opening one whole side of the suite onto a private deck. The rooms average 700 square feet each with ensuite baths and imported super king size (6-foot x 6 foot 3 inches) beds. All suites are large enough to accommodate a rollaway bed. One suite has a private kitchen suitable for a Nanny to use. Additionally, there is a large kitchen separate from these suites that is available for a Nanny needing to use a kitchen.

The Folly Suite – in the poolside tower with its own private entrance you will find The Folly Suite, with over 850 square feet spread out on 2 floors, has a large bathroom/dressing room on the first floor and then a lovely bedroom with an imported super king size (6 foot x 6 foot 3 inches) bed with absolutely stunning views on the top floor. This is the only room not able to accommodate an additional bed.

4 Bedroom Beach Villa with Private Pool
From the main mansion house, walking past the main infinity swimming pool, and down onto the private beach you come to the 4-bedroom Beach Villa. Each bedroom in the Beach Villa can be accessed independently and are very large. The top 2 bedrooms are 1,200 square feet and the bottom 2 are 900 square feet each with super king size (6-foot x 6 foot 3 inches) beds. Each of these bedrooms has a large en-suite bathroom (bathtub, shower, bidet, etc) and an additional outdoor shower for 2. One of the bedrooms opens up directly onto the private Beach Villa swimming pool. Another opens up onto a private garden. The Beach Villa has a small kitchen, outdoor Tiki kitchen, private deck and seating for 20 people comfortably. There is also a sitting room with doors that open up fully onto the beach and an additional half bath. In addition, each bedroom has a king size (6-foot x 6 foot) sofa bed, additional beds can easily be placed in these rooms and additional beds can also be placed in the sitting room.

4 Bedroom Bay View Villa
Crossing a private road on the gated Estate you come to a brand new villa. This villa is very contemporary, with white quartz floors and bathrooms. There are 4 very large bedrooms all with en-suite luxury bathrooms. There is a large open plan kitchen onto a sitting room with sit up bar. Every room in this villa has floor to ceiling glass onto wonderful sea views. Two suites have imported super king size (6-foot x 6 foot 3 inches) beds. The two additional guest suites have 2 imported twin size (3-foot x 6 foot 3 inches) beds in each which can be combined to make a 6-foot x 6-foot 3-inch bed or be separate twins. In addition, this villa has a 20-person fire pit and a 20-person hot tub. Brand-new infinity pool completed December 2018.

Located on the beautiful island of Grenada with easy daily flights from the USA, and frequent flights from Europe this beautiful property is very private. It has the benefits of a private island but with no access difficulties as it is situated just 15 minutes from the International airport. Situated on a privately-owned peninsula, it is a 6.5 acre gated Estate with 24/7 security. Grenada is one of the safest Caribbean Islands with very little crime but this Owner values her privacy and security.

The fully gated property has its own generators, private desalination plant and water filtration systems. The estate is capable of running completely ‘off grid’. Although Grenada is considered to be outside of the hurricane zone (being the farthest south of the Caribbean Islands that we offer) this property is built to hurricane code.

The estate has a 185-foot private dock, wraparound completely private white sand beaches (manmade) set with swaying palm trees, a large (90 foot) infinity swimming pool with swim up bar and stunning views over the Bay. It has a spa pool also with a fantastic view. There is also a large swimming pool on the beach as well as steps from the beach into the crystal-clear waters of the Mount Hartman Bay. There is fantastic snorkeling directly from the property and the dock provides a wonderful backdrop for hundreds of colorful tropical fish.

Mount Hartman Bay Estate (MHBE) Private Resort has a boat for guest use (with Captain) and this is at additional rate plan. The 31-foot motor cruiser has an onboard fresh water shower and toilet and is perfect for exploring and fishing. Boat is charged at $500 per day plus cost of fuel; Boat is included with estate buy-out (15 bedrooms); Cost of fuel used is additional. The property has extensive water sports facilities including wakeboard, knee board, kayaks, tubes and lots of fun toys such as floating islands (with their own bar!) that can be attached to the dock. The Owner owns the dredging rights to the Bay around the property and there is a 150-foot privacy cordon from the water side of the property. There are 3 luxury vehicles for guest use including a 12-seater luxury minibus and two 7-seater SUV’s; Resort staff provide chauffeur services.
