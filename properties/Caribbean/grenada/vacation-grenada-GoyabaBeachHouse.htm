Goyaba Beach House, Carriacou, Grenada Vacation Rental

 

Take a few steps through the garden and you are on one of Carriacou’s natural, unspoiled coves with rocky outcrops and sandy inlets – the perfect hideaway with warm swimming and good snorkelling. This delightful home has colourful rooms with names like “sapotilla”, “mango” and “lime”. The house is set on a grassy slope in a delightful tropical garden with many mature trees.

There are three double bedrooms, a living room and spacious dining/kitchen area and a study, all opening out onto a large veranda overlooking Hillsborough Bay. The master bedroom has a 4’6” poster bed and an “en suite” bathroom. The two other bedrooms, each with a 4’6” poster beds, share a second bathroom. There is a second covered veranda at the rear of the house which provides an ideal shady location for afternoon siestas. It opens on a flowery patio which is the perfect spot for breakfast and evening drinks. The well equipped kitchen has a stove, fridge/freezer, and all you need to cater for 6 persons. The house is furnished in beautiful, locally crafted Mahogany.
