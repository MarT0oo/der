Moondance, Anguilla, Caribbean Vacation Rental

 

This beautifully appointed two-bedroom villa is privately located on a small bluff near Long Pond, just minutes from the valley. This ideal location ensures our guests have easy access to the finest dining the island has to offer while staying at the most intimate retreat for romantic and restful moments.

The villa is outfitted with amenities to suit the most discerning of travellers. Enjoy fresh espresso on your balcony terrace or a cool glass of wine by the plunge pool after a long day on the beach. A gourmet kitchen is fully equipped with modern appliances and everything you might need to enjoy a market fresh meal.

Moondance provides total privacy for up to two couples while offering luxurious shared spaces to dine and relax. Each master bedroom offers a private ensuite, euro-linens and towels as well as air conditioning to offer the utmost in relaxation and comfort. The Villa is furnished with the highest quality custom furnishings in vibrant Caribbean colours.

Every detail has been attended to, including all the modern conveniences seasoned travellers have come to expect. Leave your computer at home, yet stay connected with your loved ones with our in-house computer. Wireless internet is available throughout the home should you wish to connect any of your devices to the world beyond the tranquil environment of Moondance. Apple TV and Sonos sound systems offer a variety of entertainment options to engage your every musical or entertainment desire.

The villa is surrounded by natural island landscaping. Bird lovers will enjoy listening and watching for rare birds in the bird sanctuary beside the villa. Nearby beaches provide endless opportunities for walking, jogging and enjoying the native flora and fauna.
