Awa, Grace Bay, Providenciales, Turks and Caicos Vacation Rental

 

Enjoy the wonderful views of the white sands and sparkling waters of Grace Bay at this one of a kind villa in Turks and Caicos. Here stands a contemporary and decadent beachside bijou villa that raises the concept of seamless indoor/outdoor living to a crescendo. From beach loungers to kayaks, paddleboards, pool floats and snorkeling gear, Awa offers everything you need for fun on the water. Get the beach holiday you deserve with Luxury Retreats!

A volcanic rock entrance pond on one side of the main living space is balanced by an infinity edge pool on the other. These beautiful water features act in harmony with the trade winds to cool the space as a fully glazed wall disappears completely from view. Outdoor lounging is enhanced by a predominantly native landscape and through a variety of perks including sunken fireside seating, a hot tub and a deck fringed with palm trees, in-pool loungers, an outside lounge area and a beachside deck with hammocks. Ample alfresco dining and lounging spaces scatter the patches of light and shade cast by the architecture.

Awa blends uber-contemporary European luxury with outstanding warmth. The interiors are effortless and elegant, creating a unique sensation at every turn. Native stonework and locally milled woodwork and cabinetry create a perfect balance of softness and comfort, while custom ceramics, marble and unique wallpaper finishes offer a striking modern note.

Six crisp and pristine bedrooms accommodate up to twelve fortunate guests at Villa Awa. All of the bedrooms include en-suite bathrooms. One of the master suites features a rain shower, and the other provides an alfresco shower. All of the four suites on the second floor offer amazing ocean views. Sublime rejuvenation awaits!

Your reservation at Awa includes a friendly staff with personal chef, housekeeper, butler and pool attendant. Whether it’s time for a movie night in the media room equipped with Netflix or just a casual board game between family and friends, the creators of Awa have thought ahead to ensure that guests’ every whim is satisfied with a little panache. In a country known for its exciting ripsaw music and succulent seafood, this residence represents that ever illusive piece of paradise. Soak it in!
