Nevis Modern Villa, Nevis Island, BWI Vacation Rental


At Nevis Modern Villa you will find world class luxury accommodations - a totally unique, private tropical island paradise. Weekly and Long Term Rentals, Group stays for 1-10 or more Guests. Rent the Cottage Additionally inquire for rates.

All Weekly, Long Term and Group rentals, regardless of number of guests in party, will enjoy exclusive use of all accommodations during their stay at Nevis Modern Luxury Villa Nevis. We currently have five full, luxury Villa Suites and three swimming pools in a two building, multi-level, walled and gated hillside complex that insures intimacy and privacy, all in close proximity to local beaches, the main island road and excellent restaurants.

Beginning Summer of 2010, our Garden Terrace level will be available with three additional villa suites (for a total of eight), a forth swimming pool, and our own on-site, solar powered Hydroponics Greenhouse, for Guest parties of 1-16 or more.

Lush tropical courtyard gardens, waterfalls and water features are integrated into the landscape so that the healing power of water can be enjoyed virtually everywhere on the estate.

At the Upper Courtyard Garden level are entrances to our Great House with two bedroom suites, multiple galleries or porches and swimming pool, and charming Nevis Modern Luxury Cottage (directly adjacent to Great House) with front and rear, garden and sunset galleries and private pool. One level down, on our Grand Terrace Pool Deck level are East and West Poolside Suites and a third swimming pool. Smaller Guest parties of 1-4 may wish to use our Poolside Suites as bedroom quarters while enjoying Nevis Modern Luxury Villa's Great House for daytime activities - for the ultimate 'Owner's experience'.

Standard Rental includes: Housekeeping, Help with getting to Villa, complimentary use of cell phones, complimentary high speed wireless internet, Evaporative air conditioners in all suites, Flat screen LCD TV's with DVD players in all suites. Use of villa's DVD lending library, queen beds in all suites, kitchenettes in all suites.


Inclusive Weekly Rentals include:

• All amenities listed in Standard Weekly Rentals.
• Concierge services including making reservations for your party prior to your arrival.
• Pick up upon arrival at point of entry into St. Kitts or Nevis, local transportation to Eden Villa Nevis. For Guests arriving in St. Kitts, this includes taxi ride from airport to water taxi, then water taxi to dock at Oualie Beach, then taxi up the hill to Eden Villa Nevis. All Guests will be met at check-in by a member of our staff.
• Arrival night catered dinner served in Great House Dining Kitchen.
• One evening (per stay) of live local entertainment, 'out under the stars' on our Grand Terrace Pool Deck.
• Full use of two vintage, four door Suzuki jeeps. Our jeeps are maintained in "excellent" or "new" condition. Damage waiver and temporary local license required. Minimum age 25.
• Use of two mountain bikes.
• Use of two Sunfish Sailboats - in "new" condition (for qualified, experienced sailors only). Damage waiver required.
