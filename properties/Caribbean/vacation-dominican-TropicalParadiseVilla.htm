Tropical Paradise Villa, Casa de Campo, Dominican Republic Vacation Rental

 

One of the most beautiful villas in Casa de Campo. This magnificent villa is surrounded by the Caribbean Sea, lush gardens and views of Casa de Campo Marina and Catalina Island. Relax and enjoy a cool tropical cocktail on the beach or bask in the warm Caribbean sunshine.

Cozy and rustic decor make the villa feel like you’re at home with all the modern luxuries of a dream holiday. A total of nine well appointed bedrooms, four of which feature beautiful sea views. Step into the master bedroom and enjoy en-suite bathroom, air conditioning, ceiling fan, safe, TV and sea view terrace. The formal dining room offers breathtaking views of the Caribbean Sea and seats ten. The villa also includes a swimming pool, jetted tub, oceanfront sanded solarium, barbeque and TV room. The villa is fully equipped kitchen features marble countertops and stainless steel appliances perfect for the culinary mastermind of your entourage. Kick back and relax with a glass of wine and a good book in the living room and enjoy the comfortable furniture with colorful popping accents.

A great villa for groups, families or corporations looking to get away and have the accessibility of everything Casa de Campo has to offer. A four (4) seater golf cart rental is also included for the lenght of your stay.

Casa de Campo is located on the south eastern shore of the Dominican Republic, next to the town of La Romana and just 70 miles from the capital city of Santo Domingo, the oldest city in the new world. Casa de Campo is a luxurious 7,000 acre ocean front community offering world class golf courses which include the famous Teeth of the Dog Golf Course, Dye Fore and The Links Golf Course plus polo fields, skeet shooting centre, tennis centre, equestrian centre, water sports, spa treatment centre, fitness centre, business centre, private beach, a la carte restaurants, Altos de Chavon (artist village) and Casa de Campo Marina, all of which will please almost any visitor.
