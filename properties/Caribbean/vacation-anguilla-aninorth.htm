Ani North Villa, Anguilla, British West Indies Vacation Rental


Like a towering ivory sculpture perched on the majestic cliffs overlooking Anguilla’s Little Bay, the four bedroom Ani North has been designed and fitted to offer an unforgettable Caribbean vacation. Unique to Ani North is the home’s dramatic stair atrium with massive skylight above and water feature below. At Ani North, the amenities of a world class resort and the privacy of an enclave combine to offer you the ultimate carefree and sun soaked holiday!

Have a splash in the heated infinity swimming pool. Or perhaps a soothing soak in the dramatic cliff-edge hot tub? Other resort-themed perks include a roof lounge, golf carts, mountain bikes and a night-lit tennis court with cabana lounge. A gas grill sets the tone for exquisite alfresco dining. Indoors, you will find a home theatre and a shared gym. Wi-Fi access and baby equipment are also provided. Ani’s impeccable service will have you hooked. The staff includes a breakfast chef, master chef, two butlers, a cocktail specialist and a tennis instructor. Beach bags are packed with snorkel gear and the staff ‘magically’ get there before you to set up. How about a gourmet meal with a white-linen dining table right by the ocean?

The sleek furnishings and contemporary finishes provide clear views of the Caribbean Sea from almost every angle at Ani North. Wake up to breathtaking sea and sky hues from your bedroom each new morning. The interiors are clean, bright and modern, with naturally hewn wooden furniture serving as accent pieces throughout. Breeze through indoors and out for a casually elegant island experience!

The four gorgeous bedrooms at Ani North accommodate up to eight guests. All of the bedrooms feature en-suite bathrooms, air conditioning and ceiling fans. All three master bedrooms (with direct outdoor access) as well as the indoor and outdoor living spaces are oriented towards the breathtaking western view down the Anguilla coastline and towards evening sunsets.

The experience at Ani Estate is further enhanced by Anguilla’s unsurpassed collection of shockingly white sand beaches, crystal clear turquoise waters, an amazing assortment of local and fine dining restaurants alongside a warm and welcoming local population. Anguilla is unique in its ability to deliver sophisticated luxury travel while retaining its laid back island charm. Come experience a fresh tropical tempo and enjoy your vacation, your way!
