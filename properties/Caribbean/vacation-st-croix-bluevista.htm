Blue Vista Villa, St. Croix Vacation Rental


Blue Vista, the name says it all. Sit back and relax in the Jacuzzi and let the trade winds carry all your cares away.  This majestic Villa is hilltop overlooking the beautiful sparkling Caribbean and the historic site where Columbus first landed in the new world.

As you enter the mahogany gates you are dazzled by the large pool and Jacuzzi; let your gaze drift to the covered dining area and on out to the blue Caribbean all the way to Buck Island.  Through the French doors from the pool deck is the dining room with the table that seats 12, and great room with conversation areas, correspondence desk and satellite TV. The fully equipped gourmet kitchen is just down the hallway that leads to the bar area complete with satellite TV and internet.  For our guests who must work, there is an office including PC, printer, phone, and broadband internet on the lower level.  Each bedroom has a private deck or patio, full bath en-suite and AC.  Blue Vista is furnished in West Indian plantation style with beautiful woods and soft colors; your comfort is our first priority.

The caretakers are on the lower level and are there only if needed, their main concern is your total privacy.
