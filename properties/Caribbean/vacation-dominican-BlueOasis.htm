Blue Oasis, Cap Cana, Dominican Republic Vacation Rental

 

Seclusion and privacy are the keys to this amazing 7 Bedroom oceanfront masterpiece. Nestled in Cap Cana, one of the most worldly renowned gated village in the whole Caribbean, this property is away from all, but has everything you need for an unforgettable experience with Blue Oasis.

Modern with avant-guard touches, the high-ceilinged foyer stands proudly with the futuristic spiral stairs with glass railing and two intriguing sculptures patrol the main entrance. A million drop-like glass form a spectacular chandelier welcoming everyone who visits the property.

On one wing, the elevated ceilings guide you to the lounge area with picture windows and a comfortable sitting area waits for the guests, while they think of choosing a margarita or martini at the adjacent wet bar. To the other wing, the large living room with the white-marbled piano is the ideal spot to discuss the next place to visit or prepare local dishes in the fully equipped kitchen. At dinner time invite all family member you could think of because they will find space at the 24-seats table, lighted by the classy glass chandelier.

Stepping outside means to be wrapped up by the gentle Caribbean breeze and marveled by the outdoor living and wooden ceilings. The stagged and unique pool and the hot tub offers fun and fantastic endless ocean views, always watched by the yellow sculpture enhancing all surroundings. After the palm tree barrier, a private beach waits for you.

After a long and exciting day, the modern staircase will take you up to all seven bedrooms. The master bedroom has an incredible view to the ocean with a large window and balcony, a king bed and ensuite bathroom with jetted tub and walk-in shower. Then, the second bedroom features a king bed with pool and ocean views and an ensuite bathroom with jetted tub and walk-in shower, TV and access to the balcony. The third bedroom with a King bed has a garden view and balcony access. The fourth and fifth bedrooms feature 2 queen beds each, garden view, access to balcony, TVs and ensuite bathrooms with bathtub and walk-in shower. The sixth has a king bed and the seventh has a queen bed with garden and ocean views, TVs and ensuite bathrooms each.

This beautiful villa is located in the exclusive gated community of Cap Cana, a 26-square mile paradise that is blessed with a temperate year-round climate, light rainfall, and soft breezes. It's colorful coral reefs; calm crystalline waters and white-sand beaches are breathtaking. The community Resort provides a matchless experience for all who visit this tropical paradise, and its tropically chic lifestyle is ideal for those who appreciate high standards of quality, comfort, and aesthetics. As guests of the villa, you have access to all of the resort amenities, casa club and activities.
