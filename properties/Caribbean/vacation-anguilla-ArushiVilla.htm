Arushi Villa, Rendezvous Bay, Anguilla, Caribbean Vacation Rental

 

Situated on the western end of the sparkling white sands and aquamarine waters of Rendezvous Bay, Anguilla, Arushi Villa brings private luxury to this stunning natural paradise. You’ll enjoy pampering and seclusion with the five-star amenities of Cusinart Resort at your service. And for golf lovers, Anguilla’s 18-hole Gregg Norman golf course is just steps away in this gated community.

Arushi, pristine white against Anguilla’s cerulean blue skies, offers five elegantly appointed master suites with marble bathrooms and air conditioning throughout. Modern luxury coupled with indoor/outdoor living areas are perfect for seclusion and relaxation. All butler and concierge services are included in one simple rate.

Savor the perfect tropical getaway surrounded in natural beauty with exceptional private service and amenities at Arushi Villa.
