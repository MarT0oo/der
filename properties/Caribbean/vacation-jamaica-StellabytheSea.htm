Stella by the Sea, Montego Bay, Jamaica, Caribbean Vacation Rental

 

This is without doubt the finest oceanfront location at Tryall. If a private beach, the choice of two pools, a spa, extensive gardens and rooms that open to the sight and sound of the waves are top of your list then Stella by the Sea ticks all the boxes.

With 6 bedrooms, a large Italiante courtyard, a private state-of-the-art movie theatre and a gourmet kitchen complete with a wood-fired pizza oven this really is a one-of-a-kind villa and with a location that is second to none.

You will be hard pressed to find a finer location. Set amidst over four acres and with a breathtaking oceanfront that includes a lovely golden sand beach, Stella by the Sea is a true escape offering tranquility, the luxury of space and the gentle cooling touch of the trade winds.

With a very successful melding of Caribbean and Italian influences, the architecture cleverly brings the outside in and from wherever you are you not only see but hear the ocean. The villa features two ocean facing master suites one with a fabulous en suite bathroom with Japanese tub and two ocean facing guest bedrooms in the main house, all equipped with cable TV and DVD players. Two additional bedrooms are in the self-contained guest cottage which features its own living area, kitchen and patio dining area. The principle bedroom in the cottage is an escapists dream with walls to the north and west that open out bringing the sea virtually inside. En suite bathrooms throughout the villa are exceptional in terms of the quality of fittings and space.

One of this villa’s outstanding features is the choice of ‘perfect spots’. Guests can play catch-up over drinks at the bar or admire the sunset from the stone table vantage point directly on the sea. The dining room in the main house comfortably sits 14 guests and opens on 3 sides to welcome in the breeze. An additional covered dining patio seats 12 and there is a gazebo on the lawns overlooking the water, perfect for breakfast or late afternoon tea. A spacious courtyard with a lily pond is a relaxing retreat to finish a good book, or beat your partner at a game of chess. There are two swimming pools, one salt water and the other chlorine-free fresh water as well as an outdoor Jacuzzi. Sonos is fitted throughout the villa. Movie buffs will be overjoyed to find the movie theatre which is equipped with the very latest technology.

Guests at Stella By The Sea are looked after by an expert staff of eight.
