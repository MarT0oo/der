Lounge in the water while our dedicated villa staff serves you drinks or pampers you with a wonderful ocean view massage.

Plus, each of the villa bedrooms has its own private balcony, ceiling fan, air conditioning, full ensuite bathroom and elegant tropical designs.

Take your meals overlooking the ocean, take a romantic walk amongst our gardens, or just sit around reading and snoozing the day away.
