About St. Barth's Villa Rentals

St. Barths is a wonderful place to come and visit if you want a laid back Caribbean Holiday.  Located in the Lesser Antilles close to St. Martin.  This island is a favorite for those who want privacy and has long been a place where you can find high end retailers and boutique shops as well as Entertainers escaping the eyes of the paparazzi.  This is a great place for anyone looking for an island they can truly find serenity.

The Beaches are one of the biggest draw in St. Barthelemy, some of them include Colombier beach, St. Jean, Flamands, Grand-Cul-de-Sac beaches and Shell Beach which is popular with the locals. 

There are many spectacular villas to rent in St. Barths and we recommend this island to anyone looking for a high end home and a great time.
