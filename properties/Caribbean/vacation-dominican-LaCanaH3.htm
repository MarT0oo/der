La Cana H3, Punta Cana, Dominican Republic Vacation Rental

 

Hop into this unique luxury villa to spend some quality time with your family or friends.

Within the excel gated village Cap Cana, this property is the ideal fit for those connoisseurs of life who want to have some unforgetable holidays.

From the wooden deck to the hammock tied to Caribbean palm trees, the backyard of the villa is very spacious, with a large pool to cool off and a jacuzzi to lay back and relax. It has a unique landmark, and its a palm with an L form leaning over the swimming pool.

On the palapa with an ecological hay roof you can find several chaise lounges to have a nap or rest while your kinds are jumping around and splashing into the water.

Inside, you will find a fully equipped kitchen with an exit to a gallery with table and chairs next to a BBQ.

All decorations were carefully selected to match with an ecological ambiance and if you upstairs you will find the master bedrooms with a king size bed, ensuite bathroom, TV, private balcony and jacuzzi.

Going back downstairs you will find the other 3 bedrooms, one with a queen bed, another with two double beds and the last one has a king size bed with TV.

All bedroom have a private bathroom.
