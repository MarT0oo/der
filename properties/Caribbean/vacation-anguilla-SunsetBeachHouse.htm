Sunset Beach House, Anguilla, Caribbean Vacation Rental

 

A true tropical paradise, Sunset Beach House is nestled in a floral garden, designed by Anguilla's award-winning gardener. Drive thru the iron gates and down past the limestone rock floral walls which light up this incredible property in the evening. Fall asleep to the sound of the waves breaking on the sea rocks and wake to a panoramic view of the turquoise water and blue sky that welcomes the day.

Enjoy morning breakfasts and sunset cocktails while watching boats sail by and fisherman cast their nets off the sea rocks of Black Garden Beach. The area is known for dolphin and whale sightings and the chances are high that you will see rainbows arching over the sea.

Facing sunsets on the Atlantic Ocean and a 150ft walk to Black Garden Beach, Sunset Beach House, provides it all. Four ocean-front, ensuite king-sized bedrooms each have private balconies. An open floor plan makes entertaining easy with the gourmet kitchen leading into the living room and into the outdoor eating area. An outdoor TV room with Satellite TV leads to the heated-pool/Jacuzzi and outdoor shower. Steps lead down to the water and to the beach. This central location, just around the bend from Little Bay, provides easy access to restaurants and beaches on both ends of the island. All while maintaining the privacy that big hotels simply cannot provide.
