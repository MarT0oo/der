La Magnolia, Hillside Terres Basses, St. Maarten Vacation Rental

 

Magnolia is an ideal vacation escape for either families or groups of couples. This compact property lies in mature hillside gardens with views south west over the ocean and Saba. The comfortable living room and adjoining, well equipped kitchen lead out onto a generously proportioned shaded terrace. With its bar set up, dining area and casual lounging area, the terrace makes for perfect Caribbean outdoor living and is complimented by the sunny terrace surrounding the pool.

 

The Master Bedroom has outside access off the shaded terrace. The 2 other bedrooms can be accessed also from the terrace but have interconnecting doors; ideal for children, but that can be locked making them both suitable for couples.

 

The two single beds in the 3rd bedroom can be converted to a queen as needs be.


Further details feature Wi-Fi internet access, electric gate access and with no neighbor overlook, excellent privacy.

This villa offers the perfect vacation venue for families, groups or couples alike.  Next to La Pergola (4 bedroom) and Always (5 bedroom) making this ideal for a combination stay for larger groups.
