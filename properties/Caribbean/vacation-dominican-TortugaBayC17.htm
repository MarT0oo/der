Tortuga Bay C17, Punta Cana Resort, Punta Cana, Dominican Republic Vacation Rental

 

Escape to Tortuga Bay C-17, a luxury villa with spectacular views and a truly relaxing atmosphere. This beautiful home is located inside the exclusive enclave of Tortuga Bay in Punta Cana Resort. Home to luminaries such as Oscar de la Renta, Mikhail Baryshnikov and Julio Iglesias, this property is within easy reach of three award winning golf courses and the top-rated Bamboo restaurant.

From the shade of the outdoor lounge to the pristine waters of the swimming pool – this is how many of the illustrious guests of Tortuga Bay C-17 will choose to spend their days in the therapeutic Dominican sun. You will also find plenty of sun loungers around the pool deck. A barbeque sets the tone for some lovely alfresco dining in the well-shaded dining area. Indoors, you will find a home theater system, a media room and Wi-Fi.

Tortuga Bay C-17 integrates seamlessly into the landscape with natural elements including rocks, Coralina limestone and exotic woods. The entire home is beautifully furnished with either imported or custom-made furniture, accented with pieces collected by the owners on their trips around the Dominican Republic, Europe and Puerto Rico. The home has a sophisticated, yet rustic flair while maintaining a comfortable tropical elegance. Surrounded by lush vegetation, enjoy total privacy and serenity.

Five impeccable bedrooms accommodate up to fourteen guests at Tortuga Bay C-17. The villa is non-smoking and children are welcome. All of the bedrooms feature en-suite bathrooms and either king or queen size beds. Fine linens and accoutrements help provide excellent nights of rest.

Overlooking the 8th hole of P.B. Dye’s stunning La Cana Golf Course, this villa is a tropical dream come true. Wiggle your toes in the soft, white sand at Punta Cana, or take part in a myriad of aquatic activities including sport fishing, scuba diving, snorkeling, windsurfing, surfing and sailing. As part of the gated community of Punta Cana Resort, guests of Villa Angelina - Tortuga Bay C-17 will enjoy access to the tennis courts, the beach & golf club and the Six Senses Spa. Ultimate leisure, positive vibrations and simply elegant living make this villa the destination of choice for sun seekers with refined tastes.
