Santosha Villa Estate on the British Caribbean island of Anguilla is more than a villa. Set on the sugar sands of secluded Long Bay Beach and convenient to the CuisinArt Golf Resort & Spa and top West End restaurants, Santosha Villa Estate is a private, luxury resort that you enjoy all to yourselves. The sprawling, former personal residence is nestled in a lush garden just 10 minutes from Blowing Point Ferry Terminal and 15 minutes from Anguilla’s airport. Hidden away from the crowds, beach hoppers often miss this wide ribbon of silky sand on Anguilla’s north coast. From Santosha Villa Estate you can access it in mere seconds, and although all Anguilla beaches are public, you’re likely to have it all to yourselves. Laze in a beach chair in the cool shade of an umbrella as you sip the perfectly-timed icy drink offered by your personal butler. Or swim, snorkel and play in the warm waters. Choose your pace. Take a leisurely stroll, or get your heart pumping and kite-board the waves. Build sandcastles and memories.

Our oasis is rented as a full estate and sleeps 21 in a grand villa, three guest houses (including a deluxe honeymoon hideaway) and a nanny-apartment suite. Paved terraces wind through tropical gardens and wrap-around verandas cooled by gentle trade winds frame dramatic sea views. Refresh in the oceanfront pool or hot tub. Work out in the full gym or get in a game of tennis or basketball on the private courts. Dine at sunset in the romantic beach-side grotto or under the pavilion on delicious meals prepared on the outdoor grill or in the estate’s commercial kitchen.

For larger groups or events, add Sand, Sea and Sky Villas at our sister property Long Bay Villas. Located next door on the same beach, this trio of recently-transformed, five- and six-bedroom luxury villas feature private pools, hot tubs, a shared gym, covered dining area to seat 30 and a beach bar. Combined, the two estates sleep 54 people with room on and off the sand for hundreds of guests.
