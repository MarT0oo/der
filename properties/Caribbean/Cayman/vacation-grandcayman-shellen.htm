Shellen, East End, Grand Cayman Vacation Rental

 

Tuck into your own private mini-resort at Shellen, a 3-bedroom (plus sleeper sofa), 2-bath villa poised at the edge of the rocky, dramatic Caribbean coastline. Amenities include an ocean-view outdoor deck with pool, a sunken hot tub, and plenty of lounge chairs under tall palm trees. Not to mention the private snorkelling and wading beach, easily accessed from your own stairway.

Shellen is totally secluded, with no neighbours and, on clear nights, an unpolluted sky full of stars. In the evening, this northwest-facing villa is afforded spectacular sunset views. Stir a tropical cocktail, settle into the hot tub, and watch the sun sink into the sapphire waters of the Caribbean. Then watch the sun be replaced by the twinkle of a million stars.

Cathedral windows in the living area and dining sunroom reveal big views of the sea and sky. A more formal dining room seats 6 in comforts that will carry your meal long into the evening. When bedtime arrives, sleeping arrangements include a king-sized bedroom, two queen-sized bedrooms, and a sofa bed in the private den, which sleeps two more for a total of 8 people. A fully-equipped kitchen waits for you to cook breakfast for your group… there’s a full-service grocery store just 3 minutes away by car, or head to one of the restaurants in the same area.

The natural beachfront just beyond Shellen Villa may not be ideal for sunbathing because of its rocks and pebbles, but it more than makes up for it by offering some truly exceptional, warm-water snorkelling and wading. Catch some sun on one of the deck’s lounge chairs then head down to the beach for a dip. Best of all, Shellen Villa fits a family-sized budget—so you can enjoy all the top-notch amenities of a (private!) Caribbean resort, without stressing over the price tag.

The most remote and picturesque part of Grand Cayman can be found in the Eastern Districts of Grand Cayman, including the quaint Caymanian village of East End. This side of the island also boasts the highest elevations, which makes the peaceful drive around this side of the island even more scenic - with many stretches overlooking the bluff.
