On The Rocks, South Coast, Grand Cayman Vacation Rental

 

Proudly resting on the Beach Bay bluff over the shimmering azure Caribbean waters. This beautiful “On the Rocks” offers a rare elevation for Grand Cayman, making it a scenic paradise. Its elegantly furnished, sumptuous and spacious rooms in this deluxe villa are a delight to all. The private positioning allow for a quiet setting - yet it’s only a 2-minute walk to a lovely sandy beach that you’ll often be the only ones inhabiting it!

Having everything anyone would need on vacation, it can comfortably accommodate up to 12 persons in the 6 gorgeous bedrooms. It has a large BBQ grill, perfect for a seaside cookout while the family relaxes on this magnificent property, lounging pool side

Located just 20-minute drive to the airport, George Town and the famous Seven Mile Beach and the new upscale shopping area at Camana Bay. The state of the art kitchen will give endless joy to any chef and hungry vacationers. The large pool and deck area will provide many pleasant hours of soaking in the warmth of the Caribbean sunshine with the occasional quiet swim to cool the senses.

The South Coast of the island describes the area between George Town as it stretches along towards Bodden Town up to Frank Sound. This beautiful coastline is more rugged than Seven Mile Beach, yet has many stretches of white sandy beach, many of which are commonplace for snorkeling, kayaking or even kiteboarding and surfing during windy days.
