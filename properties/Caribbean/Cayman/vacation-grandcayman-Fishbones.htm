Fishbones, East End, Grand Cayman Vacation Rental

 

Fishbones is located on the Northeast of the island, just about 2 miles west of the Morritt's and Wyndham Reef Resorts. Fishbones is truly an ultra-luxe villa inside and out. Professionally designed and expertly decorated with exquisite furniture, drapes and decor this home will make you feel like royalty.

Step through the foyer and into an expansive living, dining and kitchen area that leads onto a large screen porch for gorgeous water views. The screened lanai overlooks a stunning pool that leads down to a private path for excellent reef-protected snorkeling and swimming over a shallow, sandy bottom. This home does not have a sandy beach, however, there is a sandy cove on the nearby adjacent vacant lot, plus two resorts open to the public that offer sandy barefoot beaches.

Fishbones enjoys amazing elevated Caribbean views of multi-colored waters with rare spottings of eagle rays and turtles in the shallow reefs. Views are interrupted only by the occasional dive boat mooring on a popular dive site just outside the reef. The villa's windowed 3rd floor cupola is a great place to watch sunsets and allows sunshine to pour into the villa and down the spiral stairs. Trade wind breezes can keep the house cool during winter evenings when you want to turn off the A/C and open the villa to the sound of waves crash on the shore below.

With a first floor king master suite, and two second floor queen bedrooms with shared bath, Fishbones accommodates up to 6 adults comfortably.

Stunning elevated pool and pool deck! Poolside loungers and hammock cabana are perfect for soaking up the sun, or napping in the shade.

Stone pathway down to excellent, reef-protected snorkeling. A healthy marine environment awaits so we advise guests to wear pool shoes or fins when they go exploring. Living area features a large, comfortable sectional and large HDTV for everyone to enjoy after a day of sun and surf.

Indoor and outdoor dining tables seat six—one in the dining area, and one on the screened porch for al fresco dining with a view. Modern gourmet kitchen with top of the line appliances. Keurig coffeemaker, dishware, utensils, and cookware provided.

The first-floor oceanfront master king suite opens onto the pool deck and comes furnished with large HDTV and an oversized soaking tub in private bathroom. The two second-floor queen bedrooms share a large bath and open onto the second-floor balcony offering panoramic ocean views. Upstairs loft area features a small office with desk for guests who need to work during their stay. Third floor Captain’s Watch cupola provides brilliant views and is perfect for sunset watching.

Gated and walled property offers guests plenty of privacy.
