Twin Palms, North Side, Grand Cayman Vacation Rental

 

Twin Palms Villa is directly oceanfront in the Rum Point district and enjoys a sandy beach and protective coral reef with excellent snorkeling. No neighbors on either side ensure guest privacy. New conTwin Palmsorary 18' x 54' pool and infinity hot tub was built this summer. Pool table and shuffleboard table added in December.

This deceptively large 6,500 sq ft single story conTwin Palmsorary home was built specifically for entertaining larger families. Twin Palms offers stunning glass-wall views and has a great open concept feel.

The villa enjoys a deep sandy beach, 2 tandem kayaks and 10 loungers for sunbathing. We enjoy a small area on the beach that is sandy barefoot entry to the water as well for children.

Owner does not charge an extra fee for spa heat. Pool itself is not heated--even the Ritz-Carlton and Westin hotels do not heat their pools in winter this far south.

Weddings and events permitted. Event fee and additional refundable damage deposit apply.
