Cayman Castle, East End, Grand Cayman Vacation Rental

 

Cayman Castle is directly oceanfront on a private 2-acre gated and walled estate on an elevated point of land and enjoys a wide white-sand beach with excellent snorkeling and sunbathing. Infinity pool, multi-tiered oceanfront terraces, panoramic roofed balconies and a covered first floor veranda provide guests plenty of room for spreading out, dining or enjoying the views; shaded or in the sun. Cayman Castle can also be rented with the optional 2 bedroom guesthouse, which sleeps an additional 6 people (4 adults max).

This grand estate is not only one of the largest rental villas on the entire island it also is a notable landmark. Virtually anyone on the island will know of the Castle in the East End. While we are neighbors to the Wreck of the 10 Sails Park and the police constable's residence, this property enjoys no residential neighbors to the north or the south for well over 500 feet. With the gate shut in the evenings, guests enjoy their privacy.

The owner maintains the property quite well making upgrades every fall to keep the experience not only refreshing and pleasant for the repeat guests, but for newcomers to Cayman Castle as well.

Cayman Castle also makes an intimate wedding venue for small ceremonies. Owner requests no invited guests, limited to 18 guests total.
