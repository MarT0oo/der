Villa La Playa, Rum Point/Cayman Kai, Grand Cayman Vacation Rental

 

This authentic Caymanian gem in the quiet residential neighbourhood of Cayman Kai will charm you with its waterfront location, swimming pool, traditional island architecture, distinctive paint colours, and surprisingly spacious interior with 4 bedrooms, 4.5 baths and an open-concept floor plan. All situated on a tranquil inlet on Grand Cayman’s northern shore!

Walk to the ever-popular Rum Point Club from this ideally located villa. At Rum Point, locals and visitors alike gather for food, drinks, and fun on a great beach with acres of glassy, shallow water for wading and snorkelling. If you’d rather, you can boat to Rum Point—Villa La Playa has its own private boat dock reaching into the tranquil inlet.

Another standout feature at Villa La Playa? Its sublime outdoor area, poised at the edge of the balmy Caribbean inlet. Here, a glittering waterfront swimming pool seems to merge with the white, sandy beach beyond it, a covered gazebo provides refuge from the sun, and a sprawling porch with wicker furniture coaxes you into cocktails and conversation on a long, lazy afternoon.

Guests at Villa La Playa tell us its quaint appearance on the outside can be deceiving—they’re surprised to discover just how spacious it is inside! Find 2 living rooms (one downstairs and one in a lofted space upstairs), a dining table that seats six, and a big kitchen with lots of room for multiple cooks to congregate and make dinner. The 4 bedrooms at Villa La Playa include one king-sized bedroom, 2 queen-sized bedrooms, and one bunkroom with a twin bed bunked on top of a full-sized bed. Your group will never feel squeezed into this space, with each room—even the bathrooms—providing plenty of room to breathe!

If you are arriving by car, you’ll know you are almost at Rum Point/Cayman Kai by the ‘vacation-style’ homes that begin to appear with cute signs named by their owners in fun island-style names such as; ‘Kailypso’. This area of the island truly feels like a vacation village – even for those who live here. Similar to a cottage town in North America only in the Caribbean and still very restful...
