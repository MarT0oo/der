The Penthouse at the Seafire Residences, Seven Mile Beach, Grand Cayman Vacation Rental

 

With elevated ceilings and views from more than 100 feet above sea level, this stunning penthouse sets the precedent for luxury oceanfront living with unobstructed views of Grand Cayman. This spacious corner unit features breathtaking ocean vistas from every bedroom, with the entire Seven Mile Beach crescent stretching out from its deep, wraparound terraces. Professionally designed with a refined coastal interior, this sky-bound estate speaks to guests through its rich finishes, soft textures and soothing colors.

Sunbathe on the property’s rooftop terrace, or access all the rich amenities of the adjacent Kimpton Seafire Resort + Spa, with its three distinctive restaurants, award-winning spa, and glorious pools and beach.

No matter how vivid your imagination, seeing Seven Mile Beach in person can’t be matched. There’s a reason travel magazines consistently rank this crescent-shaped shoreline on their lists of top beaches. Time and the ocean have transformed white coral into soft sand that quietly merges into clear cyan waters. Who can resist taking a dip when water temperatures range from 79°F to 86°F (26°C to 30°C) throughout the year?

Located at the Kimpton Seafire Residences, we can’t imagine being anywhere else. Neither will you, because this resort fully engages your senses on an amazing stretch of sand. Grab a gourmet fish taco and rum cocktail from our right-on-the-beach restaurant. Try snorkeling, paddle boarding or kayaking. Take a sunset stroll on the sand or the boardwalk. It’s all laid out for you in unabashed beachfront splendor.
