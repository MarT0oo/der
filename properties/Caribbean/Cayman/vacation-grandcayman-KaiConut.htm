Kai Conut, Cayman Kai, Grand Cayman Vacation Rental

 

Kai Conut sits facing the sound on the shores of the southern peninsula in beautiful Cayman Kai. This one-story, 5-bedroom villa with a private sandy beachfront is perfect for vacationing families with small children.

Kai Conut's spacious living area, newly renovated kitchen, and dining areas are separated from the outdoors by walls of windows and sliding glass doors leaving the home filled with sunlight year-round. Whether you're enjoying breakfast at the dining table or reclining on the sectional sofa, you can always enjoy the tropical scenery surrounding the property.

Panoramic views to the northwest not only capture the azure waters off the coast but the boat traffic as well from guests motoring to and from Stingray City, the island's number one attraction.

Sitting at the end of Water Cay Road, with vacant lots on both sides, guests won't see anyone else for the duration of their stay if they so desire.
