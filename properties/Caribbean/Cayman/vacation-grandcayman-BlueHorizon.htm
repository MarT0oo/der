Blue Horizon, North Side, Grand Cayman Vacation Rental

 

Welcome to Blue Horizon, a newly-built 2,200 square foot oceanfront villa located on Grand Cayman’s north shore. This ultramodern masterpiece balances stunning interiors with captivating ocean views to create an unforgettable vacation experience.

The living room’s floor-to-ceiling glass is sure to catch your eye and leave you breathless with turquoise sea views stretching out across the horizon.

The stunning silver kitchen sparkles with a vibrant backsplash, stainless steel appliances, and marble countertops. This contemporary kitchen is equipped with an infrared grill, wine fridge, coffee makers, and everything you need to prepare a gourmet meal. The elegant dining table seats six comfortably with both ocean and garden views.

Blue Horizon sleeps 6 guests comfortably across 2 king ensuite bedrooms and 1 twin bedroom with adjacent full bathroom.

Bedroom Layout:

Master Suite — ocean views, king orthopedic bed, day sofa, HDTV, sliding door access to the pool deck, and ensuite bath with walk-in shower and soaking tub.
Guest Suite — garden views, king orthopedic bed, HDTV, and ensuite bath with a walk-in shower.
Guest Bedroom — garden views, two twin beds, HDTV, hall bath with a walk-in shower. Twin beds can be made up as a king bed upon request.

The villa’s outdoor areas accentuate the best of beachfront living. With no immediate neighbors on either side, guests can experience seclusion among the tropical elements. A gorgeous mosaic-tiled pool overlooking the Caribbean Sea offers a retreat from the heat while the sectional sofa under the veranda provides the perfect spot for lounging and relaxing.

The beach here is pebbly and not sandy. The shallows are vibrant with marine life providing amazing snorkeling for active guests. You will need pool shoes or snorkeling fins for foot protection when entering the water. One of the best scuba-diving sites on the island (Babylon) can be found in the villa’s backyard.

Blue Horizon's Notable Features
Cool travertine floors throughout, both inside and out.
Two king suites with orthopedic beds for a comfortable night’s rest.
A home security system for peace of mind.
Veranda dining table for al fresco dinners with a view.
Secluded natural beach area with loungers and pergola for shade.
Spectacular snorkeling and diving directly behind the villa, stairs to the water's edge.
All beaches in Cayman are technically public, so if guests wish that experience, we have great sandy beaches just a short drive away at two water sports resorts that are open to non-guests.

Tukka, The Italian Kitchen and Over the Edge Restaurant are a few of our favorite restaurants and only a short drive away. One notable attraction nearby (5 minute walk) is the Crystal Caves. This incredible series of walkable caves is a great family activity that everyone will love.
