Kaia Kamina, Cayman Kai, Grand Cayman Vacation Rental


Kaia Kamina is a true seaside majesty. This remarkable Cayman Kai villa near Rum Point presents 10,000 square feet of modern West Indies style – a getaway that feels like your own boutique resort. Explore the private, child-friendly beach and gentle Caribbean waters by kayak, paddle board, snorkel, or bare feet. Or keep an eye on the kids from the heated beachfront infinity pool.

Kaia Kamina sleeps 18 across seven bedrooms. Keep the sea views close in three oceanfront, king-size master suites that share a breezy balcony. Two more garden-view suites in the main house aim to please our smallest guests with two twins beds in one bedroom, and two full-over-full bunk beds in the other. Two more detached, garden-view king-size suites with kitchenettes and laundry offer the ultimate privacy—perfect for young families or accompanying staff.

Find space to cool down from the sun (like a media room and great room) and work on your tan (like a poolside deck, steps from the beach). As the sun sets, a decision awaits: dinner indoors, with the chef’s kitchen and contemporary dining table…or head out to the outdoor kitchen, covered poolside dining table, and unforgettable views?
