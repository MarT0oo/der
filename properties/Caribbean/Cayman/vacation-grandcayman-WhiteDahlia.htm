White Dahlia, Cayman Kai, Grand Cayman Vacation Rental

 

Welcome to White Dahlia. This open-concept, multi-windowed floor-plan offers up to 10 guests an opportunity to spend their vacation free of clutter with smart home technology, water toys and luxurious amenities.

Soaring ceilings, walls of glass and the quiet cove & dock behind the villa creates a serene enclave for your family and friends. With a white sandy beach diagonally across the street, this brand new villa provides a welcome escape after a day in the sun at Rum Point, or out on the water.

Villa owner has arranged for his guests to receive a 25% discount towards Silver Thatch Stingray Snorkel Charters with an option of pick-up/drop-off at the villa's dock.

Thatch kayaks and stand up paddleboards (SUPs) available for discounted rental. Signed waiver and 5 day minimum required with 6th and 7th day free: US$39 per day per kayak/SUP ($195 per week/each). Paddles included.
