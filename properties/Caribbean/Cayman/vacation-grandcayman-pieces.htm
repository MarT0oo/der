Treasure Cove, Rum Point/Cayman Kai, Grand Cayman Vacation Rental

 

A fantastic place for reunions with friends or family at this secluded, crown-jewel property situated near land’s end beside the calm waters of Cayman Kai.

Treasure Cove offers the features that discerning guests expect: from an immaculate private pool to your own boat dock—even two kayaks and a paddleboard for exploring the inlet’s warm, inviting waters.

No commercial traffic reaches out-of-the-way Treasure Cove, and there are no high-rise hotels nearby. All of the neighbours are residential homes at least 100 feet apart and often serve as second homes, which virtually promises quiet days and nights.

If you do decide to socialize and meet locals, however, the legendary Rum Point Club is just a short walk away. Here, locals and tourists mingle, eat delicious seafood, sip cocktails, set off for watersport adventures and enjoy the legendary barefoot beaches.

AN EXTRAORDINARY SETTING

The light-filled, professionally decorated interior of Treasure Cove is a striking setting for your unforgettable vacation. A 12-person, glass-top dining table with contemporary leather chairs will encourage you to extend dinner well into the night. Several conversation areas comprise the huge living area, including an L-shaped leather couch that invites indulgent afternoon naps. A gleaming gourmet kitchen allows several chefs to work their magic at once.

In total, Treasure Cove can sleep up to 15 guests comfortably, with a sleeper sofa in the TV area plus 6 bedrooms-four with their own en-suite baths for stress-free mornings.

Speaking of stress-free: Treasure Cove welcomes small weddings and other special events. House your guests all in one place and host an unforgettable ceremony with the beautiful Caribbean inlet as your backdrop. Your group will leave feeling indulged and rejuvenated.

If you are arriving by car, you’ll know you are almost at Rum Point/Cayman Kai by the ‘vacation-style’ homes that begin to appear with cute signs named by their owners in fun island-style names such as; ‘Kailypso’. This area of the island truly feels like a vacation village – even for those who live here. Similar to a cottage town in North America only in the Caribbean and still very restful...
