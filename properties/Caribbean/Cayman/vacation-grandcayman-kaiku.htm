Kaiku, Cayman Kai, Grand Cayman Vacation Rental

 

You will find few villas like this one in the Cayman Islands. With the gentle Bioluminescent Bay on one side and fishtail palms creating a natural fence on the other – and sitting on a massive double lot – Kaiku feels a world away. Yet this villa, surrounded by secluded beach in Cayman Kai, is only a minute from Rum Point. Come to keep a low profile and launch your boat from our private white sand beach, or to have easy access to Cayman’s best entertainment and adventures.

Between the main villa and a detached carriage house, this tucked away estate accommodates 14 guests in eight bedrooms. In the main home, you’ll find a king-size master suite with soaking tub and shower and queen-size guest master suite with nursery – both with ocean-view balconies.

One double suite enjoys a pool and garden view, a queen-size bedroom shares a bathroom with the Zen Garden Nursery featuring two cribs for your youngest guests, and two additional queen-size bedrooms share a bath. The detached carriage house boasts a queen-size suite and a kid’s room with two twin bunks, the perfect option for privacy.

About the Area

From sunrise to sunset, there’s always something to do in Cayman Kai, a residential resort community where smiling neighbors will become your new friends.

Start your day by visiting Kaibo Beach Espresso for a cup of organic coffee and breakfast. Or, stroll or bike ride to the Rum Point Club – it takes just minutes. Stop by to enjoy the white sands and clear, shallow waters, or to explore the shoreline by wave runner, paddleboat or jet ski. When all that adventure leaves you hungry, the onsite Wreck Bar & Grill serves up lunch (and world famous Mudslides) on seaside picnic tables or will deliver dishes straight to your seaside hammock.

Although quiet and peaceful, Cayman Kai livens up in the evening – you’ll notice your neighbors making their way to spots like Kaibo Beach Bar or Kaibo Upstairs for great food and company. Every Tuesday they host a Barefoot Beach BBQ that attracts visitors and locals alike. After dinner, return to your private home and spend the evening surrounded by your loved ones. Little ones will love exploring Starfish Point Beach. Starfish dot the tideline at this pristine spot with gentle waters.
