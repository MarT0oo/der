Villa Emmanuel, Rum Point/Cayman Kai, Grand Cayman Vacation Rental

 

Gather your favorite people and head to 5-bedroom, 4-bath Villa Emmanuel, on the quiet shores of Cayman Kai, near Rum Point. A private pool and cabana at the edge of the sea provide the setting for lazy afternoons in the sun—cocktails in hand. When you want to explore the island, you’re in luck: some of Grand Cayman’s natural wonders are within easy walking distance. Observe Caribbean sea life at Starfish Beach and Stingray City—two photo-worthy beaches where these creatures gather.

Also close by are Rum Point Beach and Kaibo Yacht Club, which offer up not only some lovely beachfront, but also a smorgasbord of wining & dining options—from beach barbeque to tropical cocktails to gourmet cuisine. Not to mention water-bound activities like snorkelling, scuba diving, jet-skiing and boating. Kaibo Yacht Club is just a 15-minute walk, while Rum Point is a short drive away. A rental car is recommended for easier access to the airport (45 minutes away), George Town and Seven Mile Beach.

Make sure to return to Villa Emmanuel before sunset! The sun’s finale as it sinks into the Caribbean Sea is unforgettable—especially from your private swimming pool at the water’s edge. The residential area where Villa Emmanuel is located means you will get lots peace of quiet from blissful sunset to sunrise and back again. When it’s time for bed, your options are plentiful: 2 bedrooms with king-sized beds, 1 bedroom with a queen, and 2 bedrooms with 2 twin beds in each. A total of 8 guests can sleep very comfortably at Villa Emmanuel, making it an easy choice for family reunions or friend getaways.

In the morning, a fully equipped kitchen with stainless steel appliances and a spacious centre island waits to be filled with the scent of freshly brewed coffee, and a platter of crispy bacon. Barstools at the centre island, plus a breakfast nook that seats 6, welcomes everyone to begin the day together in paradise!

If you are arriving by car, you’ll know you are almost at Rum Point/Cayman Kai by the ‘vacation-style’ homes that begin to appear with cute signs named by their owners in fun island-style names such as; ‘Kailypso’. This area of the island truly feels like a vacation village – even for those who live here. Similar to a cottage town in North America only in the Caribbean and still very restful...
