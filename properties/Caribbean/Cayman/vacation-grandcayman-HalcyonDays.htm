Halcyon Days, Cayman Kai, Grand Cayman Vacation Rental

 

Welcome to Halcyon Days. Tucked away at the end of a tranquil cove, this villa features a private pool, shaded patio, and sunny décor. Launch kayaks or paddle boards from the villa's private dock, or charter a boat to pick you and your guests up right off the water. Nearby, the iconic Rum Point Club provides plenty of dinning options, plus bars and water sports activities.

Inside, the home's décor captures the Caribbean spirit with lots of color and bright artwork. With two bedrooms (king bed and double twin beds), plus a daybed in the den, Halcyon Days sleeps 5 guests comfortably.

Guests choosing to dine-in can enjoy cooking in a modern full sized kitchen equipped with standard appliances, dishware, cookware, and cutlery. The covered patio includes a charcoal grill and outdoor seating for al fresco dining.

Halcyon Days has the charm and personality to help make your Cayman vacation unforgettable.
