Fischer's Reef, Rum Point, Grand Cayman Vacation Rental

 

Located only 1 mile from the famous Rum Point Club, Fischer's Reef is a perfectly designed 8 bedroom oceanfront villa ideal for large families, or group getaways. Featuring multiple living and sleeping areas--4 king suites, 3 queen suites, a twin bunk suite, and pull-out sofa--the villa’s unique floor-plan allows guests to spread out in ample style. A wide staircase separates the floors meaning the noise from one floor won’t reach the others.

Renovations completed in 2014 include luxurious features such as an oceanfront infinity pool with optional heat, beach cabana, multiple indoor and outdoor dining areas, two separate 75-inch LED TVs and Sonos home theater system. All the attention-to-detail makes Fischer’s Reef a wonderful venue for entertaining and relaxation.
