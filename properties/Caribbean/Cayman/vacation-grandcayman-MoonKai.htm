Moon Kai, Cayman Kai, Grand Cayman Vacation Rental

 

Moon Kai is a luxury oceanfront villa estate, located on a stretch of the best beach in Cayman Kai. The main house and optional, beautifully-equipped coachhouse apartment can accommodate a total of 14 people--12 adults max--with 5 expansive oceanfront king suites and a separate queen master suite in the coachhouse. Each king suite offers a full bath and private, screened-in balcony, so every guest has the chance to stay in style. The 1,000 sq ft coachhouse offers a full kitchen, dining area, living area (TV) and screened porch. Many of our repeat guests draw straws for this private retreat!

Moon Kai is one of the most popular large rental villas on Grand Cayman, offering just the right balance of opulent interiors, extensive list of amenities and plenty of space to appeal to a wide range of guests. Many great memories and fantastic holidays are created at Moon Kai.

*2 Silver Thatch stand up paddleboards (SUPs) located on property for discounted guest rental. 5 day minimum required with 6th and 7th day free: US$39 per day per SUP, $195 per week.
