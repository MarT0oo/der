Villa Bellagio, Rum Point/Cayman Kai, Grand Cayman Vacation Rental

 

Nestled amongst whispering palms on The Cove in the popular Cayman Kai area on the north coast is a comfortable family home that welcomes all!

Arranged on two floors this is the perfect ‘home from home’. With 5 bedrooms and 5 bathrooms this beach house makes for the perfect family get-together or a group of friends for a relaxing vacation.

The ample pool and outside area allows for casual BBQ’s in the warm sunshine or cool moonlight, with a beautiful white powdery beach and hammocks to laze about on. This property has many ‘toys” to play with such as Kayaks to explore the cove, and if you are lucky enough you can paddle out at night to catch the amazing light show in the water produced by mother nature herself. The newly added dock allows for you to park your boat. You can easily rent a boat for a few days or your whole stay! There are also bicycles to explore the area ,and we suggest heading over to Star Fish Point for a swim.

Situated close by are Rum Point Beach and Kaibo Yacht Club, which offers some wonderful wining & dining options—from beach barbeques to famous mudslide cocktails to gourmet cuisine! You also have options for water-bound activities like snorkeling, scuba diving, jet-skiing, boating and trips to Stingray City.

Just a quick drive out of the area (5-15 minutes) will lead you to some of the best restaurants the island has to offer as well as a corner store, a full grocery store and some amazing activities like the Crystal Caves. A rental car is recommended as this property is about 45 minutes from the Airport.

After a full day be sure to head back to Villa Bellagio in time to catch the sun’s finale as it sinks into the horizon of the Caribbean Sea. It is truly unforgettable!

If you are arriving by car, you’ll know you are almost at Rum Point/Cayman Kai by the ‘vacation-style’ homes that begin to appear with cute signs named by their owners in fun island-style names such as; ‘Kailypso’. This area of the island truly feels like a vacation village – even for those who live here. Similar to a cottage town in North America only in the Caribbean and still very restful...
