Pease Bay House, South Coast, Grand Cayman Vacation Rental

 

Welcome to Pease Bay House, a luxurious gated estate only 20 minutes from the airport. This spectacular home sits along a private sandy point of beach facing southwest for exquisite sunsets views. Due to its unique location, Pease Bay House is one of Grand Cayman's best kept secrets for those seeking privacy while on holiday.

Spanning 6,000 ft² across 5 bedrooms (3 king suites, 1 queen and 1 twin bedroom) and 5.5 baths, this home also boasts a large infinity pool and a private tennis court. The new homeowner has redecorated and invested in TV/Audio technology, WiFi connectivity, and new artwork.

Walking through the front door of this home offers a true wow-factor view of the Caribbean beyond the infinity pool for an unforgettable first impression. Architectural amenities include travertine marble floors, mahogany paneling, shutters and window treatments.

This villa will have you feeling at home. There are two living areas: one on each floor and a second floor railed balcony with pergola accessible from three of the bedroom suites and upper great room with vaulted ceiling. A large thatched cabana on the beach with hammock will beckon those needing an afternoon nap. Children can comb the shoreline in each direction looking for crabs, lizards, and seaglass. It would be rare to see a soul on the beach with the exception of your immediate neighbor to the west. Access to this stretch of beach is very limited.
