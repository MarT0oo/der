White Cottage, Frank Sound, Grand Cayman Vacation Rental

 

Gather in a place where sea meets sand. With its own private stretch of child-friendly beach, our five-bedroom, three-bath White Cottage is an exceptional place to be together. For this modern take on a traditional beach cottage, we brought in statement décor to complement open, sea-view living spaces. A wall of windows surveys the pool, the gentle beach, and the kids as they play. Meanwhile, outdoor living areas, including a spacious screened lanai, welcome you after a day in the sun.

With room for 11, every family member will have their space. Five bedrooms include two first-floor, oceanfront masters that open to the lanai. One offers its own en-suite bath, while the other shares a bath with a garden bunk-room that will delight three young guests (and keep them close). Upstairs, two thoughtful queen-size bedrooms share a hallway bath.

Tucked on the south side of the island in Frank Sound, White Cottage puts memory-making destinations at your fingertips.

For a dose of local culture, take your pick between Rum Point, Cayman Kai or the East End (15-25 minutes away). All three offer postcard Caribbean beauty, charming beach bars and, of course, fresh seafood. Bring your towel to the coral sands at Seven Mile Beach – just a half hour away. Or catch sight of vibrant blooms and native wildlife at the nearby Queen Elizabeth II Botanical Park. Charming local art studios and gift shops dot the main road along with local vendors selling fresh fruit, jerk chicken and the day’s fresh catch.

A few minutes away, The Lighthouse Restaurant’s upscale menu of fresh seafood and Italian cuisine pleases all tastes (and ages). Or opt for a family dinner in your ocean-view dining area, perhaps prepared by one of our trusted personal chefs. You can also do your own meal-making: we take the stress out with pre-stocking and grocery delivery services, and nearby grocery and liquor stores make it easy to pick up that extra something on your way home from a day trip.
