Calypso Blue, North Side, Grand Cayman Vacation Rental

 

Calypso Blue has been fully renovated and redecorated, but is still at a reasonable cost for a larger family. Oceanfront freshwater pool with a sandy beach area lead to excellent coral reef snorkeling just steps from the back door. A large oceanfront screened porch is the perfect place to dine or relax after a day of sun and surf. New owner has replaced everything from windows and doors to all new furniture!

The 3,400 ft² open floorplan features a large brand-new kitchen with modern appliances, a large breakfast bar, and vaulted ceilings. The house has two living areas, both featuring large flat-screen TVs, comfortable seating and plenty of space for everyone to spread out and relax! The dining table is usually set up for 6, but the owner has 2 leaves to expand the table to seat 12. The screened porch offers two rectangular dining tables which will seat 12 guests total.

Calypso Blue features two bedroom wings, allowing multiple families to stay in privacy. 2 king master suites and 2 queen bedrooms with shared large bath, plus stairs to the 2 twin bedrooms and shared bathroom upstairs. 3 of the first floor bedrooms have oceanviews with access to the large screened porch. The 4th bedroom with queen bed has a private view to the east. Modern furniture and furnishings were imported to outfit Calypso Blue in wonderful style.

For guests who want to relax on the beach, porch or deck, enjoy the ocean and pool, and generally kick back in their own private beach home, Calypso Blue is ideal. Walking distance to a small grocery, post office and the house is also within a reasonable driving distance to watersports, restaurants, East End resorts and the famous Rum Point Club.

Calypso Blue can accommodate up to 12 people. Excellent 4 couple villa. The owner offers both a 6BR rate for up to 12 people and a 4 Bedroom rate (upstairs locked off) for up to 8 people.
