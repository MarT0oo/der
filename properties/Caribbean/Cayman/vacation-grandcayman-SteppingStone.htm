Stepping Stone, East End, Grand Cayman Vacation Rental

 

This brand new, Balinese-style oceanfront home is one of the most spectacular residences in all of East End. Every detail was meticulously planned and thought out by the owners after years of traveling the world.

Boasting outstanding views of the glistening Caribbean Sea, this world-class home defines the luxury vacation experience in Grand Cayman. Built with the most tasteful and quality materials from around the world, this contemporary abode utilizes the perfect mix of stone and varied natural hardwoods that beautifully compliment each other in a fusion of Balinese-Pacific Rim brilliance.

The seamless transition from interior to exterior living is enhanced by the naturally illuminating large pocket sliding doors along the ocean side of the home. Enjoy fun in the sun frolicking along your own beautiful, white sandy beach just steps down from the private infinity-edged pool.

Innately charming an intimate, the district of East End is far away from the every-day hustle and bustle of other more populated districts in Grand Cayman. Located about 30 minutes east of George Town, you will know you have arrived when you pass by the impressive, elevated coastline to your right, where the ocean shimmers and rolling waves crash onto the ironshore below.

This is an extremely quiet and peaceful part of the island with quaint cottages of every color standing side by side with churches and other historic sites. There are even remnants of ship wrecks still to be seen poking out of the emerald green sea at East End, about which the locals are more than happy to share tall tales passed down from generation to generation of the island’s extravagant sea-faring history. In East End there are plenty of opportunities to experience and appreciate authentic Caribbean life.
