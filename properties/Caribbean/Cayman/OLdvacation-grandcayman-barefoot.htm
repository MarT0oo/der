ABOUT THE CAYMAN ISLANDS:


The three islands which comprise the British protectorate of the Cayman Islands have a total area of 100 square miles, 76 of which make up the capital island; Grand Cayman.

Grand Cayman is unique for its banking and tourism industries: a multi-cultural sophisticated community with world class restaurants, diving, golf and surrounded by beautiful coral white sandy beaches.

The two other smaller islands (affectionately known as The Sister Islands) offer worldwide renowned scuba diving.  Cayman Brac is well known for its ecotourism, caving and a quiet peaceful loving community.  Little Cayman offers an experience of a private Caribbean island; you'll feel like you are escaping from civilization with its population of less than 100 residents.

Guests visiting the Cayman Islands can fly into Owen Roberts International Airport (GCM).
