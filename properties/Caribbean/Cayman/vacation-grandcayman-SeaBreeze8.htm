Sea Breeze 8, Seven Mile Beach, Grand Cayman Vacation Rental

 

A stunning sunset show awaits you at Seabreeze 8. This 4-bedroom, 4.5-bath, 4th floor unit provides a coveted vantage point for that perfect minute when the sun slides into the Caribbean—and many other unforgettable moments, too.

Claim your sunset spot on the balcony’s collection of plush outdoor furniture. Here, just at the tree line among rustling palms and tropical birds, you won’t feel separated from your surroundings but seamlessly a part of them.

CUSTOM-DESIGNED INTERIOR

Inside, a local designer created a classic and subtle seaside space with custom furnishings, carefully chosen art and soft shades of blue and beige. Cooking at Seabreeze 8 is, well, a breeze, thanks to thoroughly stocked cabinets and a private gas BBQ. A 10-person dining table waits to be filled with good conversation, a bottle of wine, and a dazzling meal prepared with local ingredients from the nearby grocery store. Set the mood with streaming music piped throughout the entire apartment.

A separate media room erases boredom on rainy days with a Smart TV, premium cable package, a collection of DVDs and a PlayStation 4 system with a library of games.

Four luxury bedrooms each have their own en-suite bathrooms, with luxury linens and towels provided. Three bedrooms boast flatscreen TVs with cable.

AN IDEAL LOCATION



Seabreeze 8’s location on the serene northern end of Seven Mile Beach offers quiet and seclusion, yet is within walking distance from beachfront restaurants, shops and quaint West Bay village. It’s not uncommon to have the pristine beach all to yourself here. Collect seashells, relax by the pool, or snorkel alongside sea turtles at one of the best snorkeling spots on the Cayman Islands.

Because this building is new and just beginning to generate buzz, Seabreeze 8 is one of our best-kept secrets. Come and see it for yourself!
