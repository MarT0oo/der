Kempa Kai, Cayman Kai, Grand Cayman Vacation Rental

 

This remarkable Cayman Kai villa near Rum Point boasts 9,000 square feet of modern Balinese style luxury—a private compound that feels like your own boutique resort, only steps away from idyllic Starfish Point. Escape to barefoot luxury in this new 7 bedroom, 6 1/2 bath, estate home with study where gentle sea breezes and toes in the sand replace frazzled mornings and traffic jams. In true Luxury Cayman Villas tradition, this villa is being built to suit parties of all shapes and sizes, including large families who desire room to spread out while still being able to gather together. A custom kids’ bunk room, custom built in furniture and a full butler’s kitchen are just a few of the many unique features of this bespoke villa where every detail has been meticulously designed.

Set on a nature preserve that is home to a plethora of sea life, explore the private, child-friendly beach and gentle Caribbean waters by kayak, paddle board, snorkel, or bare feet. Or, keep an eye on the kids from the deck of the beachfront infinity pool while you enjoy a sundowner, or relax in the rooftop jacuzzi as you take in some of the best sunsets in all of Grand Cayman.
