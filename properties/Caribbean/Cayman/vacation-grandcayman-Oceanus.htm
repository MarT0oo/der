Oceanus, Frank Sound, Grand Cayman Vacation Rental

 

Introducing Oceanus, where luxury, elegance and seclusion converge. This modern masterpiece has a contemporary design. The ocean-front mansion boasts four levels and includes its own elevator so less mobile loved ones can easily access all of the action. During your stay, you will enjoy your own private, seaside pool and white sandy beach where the calming sapphire-blue and emerald green waters gently advance and retreat from the pristine shoreline. Escape to this oasis where the surrounding fruit trees and local flowering shrubs form an impenetrable canopy of seclusion and breathtaking, natural beauty. Oceanus is extremely well-built with an open plan accented by magnificent cathedral ceilings and encapsulated by floor-to-ceiling glass for spectacular views.

Experience even more amazing views and fresh island breezes from the roof-top party deck, where, on a clear night you can see most of Grand Cayman’s illuminated shoreline dancing in the distance.

About the Area

Tucked on the south side of the island in Frank Sound, Oceanus puts memory-making destinations at your fingertips.

For a dose of local culture, take your pick between Rum Point, Cayman Kai or the East End (15-25 minutes away). All three offer postcard Caribbean beauty, charming beach bars and, of course, fresh seafood. Bring your towel to the coral sands at Seven Mile Beach – just a half hour away. Or catch sight of vibrant blooms and native wildlife at the nearby Queen Elizabeth II Botanical Park. Charming local art studios and gift shops dot the main road along with local vendors selling fresh fruit, jerk chicken and the day’s fresh catch.

A few minutes away, The Lighthouse Restaurant’s upscale menu of fresh seafood and Italian cuisine pleases all tastes (and ages). Or opt for a family dinner in your ocean-view dining area, perhaps prepared by one of our trusted personal chefs. You can also do your own meal-making: we take the stress out with pre-stocking and grocery delivery services, and nearby grocery and liquor stores make it easy to pick up that extra something on your way home from a day trip.
