George Town Villas 101, Seven Mile Beach, Grand Cayman Vacation Rental

 

George Town Villas #101 is an Ocean Front, 2-bedroom unit located on the ground floor of the complex, which allows for convenient water access. A few steps from your door and you’re there! The vibrant coral reef just offshore presents an exceptional snorkeling opportunity, and the rocky/sandy coastline is great for collecting shells or strolling alongside the water.

Inside George Town Villas #101, find bright and airy spaces with big windows and modern decor. A large common area comprised of the kitchen, living, and dining areas invites your group to spend time together. Two bright bedrooms feature a king-sized bed in one and a pair of twin beds (which can be converted into a king)in the other which allows for 4 people.

A shaded porch area looks out to the shimmering sea. Whip up a drink or a delicious meal in the new kitchen and settle in for an afternoon of relaxation and conversation overlooking the water!

At the George Town Villas complex, perks include a seaside swimming pool, tropical landscaping including spacious green lawns, a tennis court, free Wi-Fi.

In close proximity, you will find two more wonderful Cayman destinations: the upscale shops and restaurants of Camana Bay and the duty-free shops and business district of George Town. Also, there Worlds Gym just across the way. Or take a short walk to Seven Mile Beach, with its pristine stretch of wider beachfront for a long stroll.

The Seven Mile Beach corridor hosts many condos and resorts, hotels, shops and restaurants, combined with the Camana Bay development, on the opposite side of the strip, that provides high-end waterfront shopping, a large cinema complex, plus several restaurants, bars and a weekly outdoor local produce market every Wednesday.
