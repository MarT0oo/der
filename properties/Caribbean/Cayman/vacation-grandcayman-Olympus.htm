Olympus, Rum Point, Grand Cayman Vacation Rental

 

Welcome to Olympus, a majestic contemporary estate masterfully designed and perfectly positioned on a private oceanfront lot in Rum Point.

Designed with dramatic white angled walls, three levels of outdoor entertaining spaces, and an oceanfront living area with 40 feet of floor-to-ceiling sliding glass, Olympus is breathtakingly gorgeous. Perched on an elevated ridge, it is not difficult to see how the current owner came upon the name Olympus. At almost 8,000 ft² of living space for only 4-5 bedrooms, each room is a grand statement and offers views of the sea, the pool, and the expertly-landscaped grounds.

The L-shaped cerulean-blue swimming pool has tiled patios on both sides. The unique design of the house creates numerous shaded areas from which to escape the Caribbean sun's rays. The third-floor roof deck includes a private sundeck and open garden, plus a covered outdoor kitchen and dining area for sunset dinners, or late night cocktails. The owner has ordered a suite of new outdoor furniture for the decks and entertaining spaces.

The indoor features of Olympus rival the outdoors. Upon entering the villa, the modern floating staircase and tropical-wood wall of doors will draw your attention straight to the oceanfront great room that opens to an ipe deck, white sand backyard and the shimmering Caribbean waters beyond. A very large flat screen TV focuses on the living area, as well as another in the "media room" for gaming and home theatre use. Each bedroom also has an integrated wall-mounted TV with SONOS sound bar, Cable, Netflix, and Apple TV. A furnished small home office with complimentary WiFi is available for guests needing to conduct business or connect with clients while on-island. Technology is in long supply at this villa.

Olympus can comfortably sleep 8 adults in 4 en-suite master bedrooms, as well as accommodate another adult couple or 2 children on a Tempurpedic foam mattress (pull out sofa) in the media room. Another 2 children on a pull-out sofa in the first-floor master queen family suite makes 12 total; 10 adults maximum.
