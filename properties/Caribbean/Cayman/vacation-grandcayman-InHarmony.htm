In Harmony, Bodden Town, Grand Cayman Vacation Rental

 

Escape the stress of your daily life, but keep the comforts of your home. In Harmony Villa is our most down-to-earth option, with bright, sea-view spaces where families can gather and private nooks for tucking away with a new novel. Nestled on a wide, natural beach, this four-bedroom, four-bathroom villa embodies Caribbean breeziness with modern design and airy gathering spaces that flow seamlessly to outdoor living areas.

Guests will awake to sweeping sea views in three private oceanfront bedrooms – including a master suite with private balcony encompassing the entire second floor. Retreat to a private sitting area, greet the day from the oceanfront deck, refresh in an en-suite bath with soaking tub, or sink into an ocean-view bed. Two ground-floor, ocean-view queen bedrooms with en suite bathrooms complement a kid-friendly bunk room with a twin-over-queen bunk and separate twin and adjacent bath.

About the Area

Tucked on the Bodden Town of the island in Bodden Town, our home puts memory-making destinations at your fingertips.

For a dose of local culture, take your pick between Rum Point, Cayman Kai or the East End (15-25 minutes away). All three offer postcard Caribbean beauty, charming beach bars and, of course, fresh seafood. Bring your towel to the coral sands at Seven Mile Beach – just a half hour away. Or catch sight of vibrant blooms and native wildlife at the nearby Queen Elizabeth II Botanical Park. Charming local art studios and gift shops dot the main road along with local vendors selling fresh fruit, jerk chicken and the day’s fresh catch.

A few minutes away, The Lighthouse Restaurant’s upscale menu of fresh seafood and Italian cuisine pleases all tastes (and ages). Or opt for a family dinner in your ocean-view dining area, perhaps prepared by one of our recommended personal chefs
