Villa Caymanas, North Side, Grand Cayman Vacation Rental

 

Welcome to Villa Caymanas! This beautiful oceanfront villa is located in Old Man Bay along the north coast.

Boasting four full king suites, an oceanfront freshwater pool, and expansive balcony, Villa Caymanas is the perfect vacation retreat. Whether you prefer to explore the snorkeling reefs nearby or simply relax by the pool, itâ€™s all just steps off the back door.

The beach here is strictly pebbly (rarely sandy), but the owner has a very sandy backyard around the pool deck. The villa is directly in front of Babylon, one of the top 5 dive sites on the entire island. The snorkeling in this area is excellent, however, fins or pool shoes are necessary to protect your feet.

Villa Caymanas' modern, streamlined architecture features many oversized oceanfront windows that flood the interiors with natural sunlight year round. The kitchen, living, and dining areas open onto a second-floor balcony spanning the full width of the house providing stunning ocean views. Guests have the option of dining indoors or al-fresco off the balcony. A modern kitchen comes equipped with standard appliances, dishware, cookware, cutlery and coffee maker.

There are two king suites on the second floor each with an en-suite bath. One suite has ocean views and opens onto the balcony, while the other has garden views. Two additional king suites are located downstairs and open onto poolside veranda and sun deck. All bedrooms come furnished with flat screen TVs and cable programming. An exercise room and home office are also on the lower level.

With gorgeous views throughout, comfortable furnishings, and wonderful outdoor space, Villa Caymanas is sure to please!
