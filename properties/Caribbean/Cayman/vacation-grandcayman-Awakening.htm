Awakening, North Side, Grand Cayman Vacation Rental

 

Introducing Awakening! With uncompromisingly modern lines and bold architectural angles, few homes have excited as much interest as Awakening in Grand Cayman. Positioned upon a 15-foot sand ridge, on one of Cayman’s most spectacular shorelines, the futuristic beach house makes an audacious design statement.

Serene in its originality, this ultra-modern 8,000 square foot house is located in the picturesque North Side, with its 100 feet of prime beachfront and high elevation, allowing for spectacular views across the reef and cerulean sea. Inspired by the innovative minimalism of contemporary Spanish architecture Awakening emulates the sleek lines of luxury beach villas you might find on Ibiza or Mallorca. Awakening truly celebrates the natural beauty of Cayman’s north coast in a fresh way.

About the Area

The district of North Side is appropriately located on the northern coastline of Grand Cayman, about a forty minute drive from the airport and far away from the hectic life of George Town, the capital of the Cayman Islands.

North Side is a charming and secluded village with breathtaking views of the Caribbean Sea. The coastline here is unblemished and pristine. It’s impossible not to immerse yourself in the area’s natural beauty.

North Side residents are neighborly and seem to truly enjoy and appreciate their little slice of paradise. Despite its relative seclusion, you can still find some local shops and other amenities and the beaches and entertainment of Rum Point are just 5 minutes
