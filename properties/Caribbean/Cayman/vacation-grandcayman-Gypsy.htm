Gypsy, Rum Point, Grand Cayman Vacation Rental

 

Welcome to Gypsy! This beautiful oceanfront villa has been professionally decorated and includes a private pool with elevated views and close proximity to the famous Rum Point Club.

Decorated in an elegant Caribbean theme of beach cremes and soft hues, Gypsy is a luxurious island getaway. From the moment you enter this home's foyer, your eyes are drawn past the beautifully tiled floors and out through the tall glass doors to the pool area and Caribbean Sea in the background. We do not ever get a second chance at a first impression—and Gypsy won't disappoint!

Spanning over 4,000 sq ft of living space, with another 3,000 sq ft of outdoor space, Gypsy can accommodate 10 guests across 4 bedroom suites.

The oceanfront master suite comes furnished with a four-poster king bed with walk-in closet and private ensuite bath with double vanity, large tub and separate walk-in shower. The southwest master suite features vaulted ceilings with a king sleigh bed and ensuite bath with stand-alone shower. One room over is the queen suite with ensuite bath, single vanity, and combination tub and shower. The southeast guest bedroom also features vaulted ceilings and comes furnished with two queen beds and a shared bathroom with double vanity and walk-in shower.

Views from Gypsy's living areas are quite simply astounding. High ceilings, airy interiors and tall french doors flood Gypsy’s interiors with sunlight all year round. Comfortable sofas and elegant sectionals provide ample seating for guests to relax while taking in the views from the living room, or family room. The grand piano in the family room can be unlocked should you wish to play while on vacation. Alternatively, we can cover the piano if you do not plan on using it, or are concerned about potential damages to it.

Gypsy's gourmet kitchen comes equipped with modern appliances including a double-oven, built-in subzero refrigerator, Keurig coffeemaker, lots of counter space and a dining table with seating for 10. A propane grill is available off the pool deck for island cookouts.

The backyard pool and patio sits perched above the beach providing privacy from any beach goers walking by—a rarity in this residential area. Stairs off the pool deck lead down to a deep sandy beach perfect for sunbathing. Guests are free to use the folding beach chairs stored in the pool shed. Large hedges and grassy lawns separate neighboring lots and provide privacy during your stay.

Finally, Gypsy's homeowner has a 42' yacht certified luxury Formula boat that he offers at a special discount exclusively to villa guests. Guests save 10% on a half-day captained charter with a maximum of 10 guests. For more details, please visit ChartersoftheSea.com

Owner does not permit pets, smoking, or events with invited guests on the property.
