Casa del Sol, Casa de Campo, Dominican Republic Vacation Rental

 

A sophisticated and luxurious retreat that reflects that magical merging of inspiration and architecture. This ocean-front villa is perfectly designed to combine outdoor living with nature and to fully encompass the ocean view into every aspect of this incredible property.

Sprawling entertaining spaces flow outside to the outdoor living room to the stunning infinity pool and the beautifil outdoor gazebo. The divine master suites located on the second level presents tray ceilings, large glass sliding door windows, a balcony overlooking the the Caribbean Seas, and an opulent ensuite bath will a soaking tub and separate glass shower. The lower level houses the living room, formal dining room, a small office, study and a top-of-the-line chef's kitchen with premium finishes and fixtures and professional-grade appliances. Every detail was carefully selected and quality crafted.

This stunning six bedroom oceanfront villa is located within close distance to Minitas Beach and Casa de Campo Marina. The perfect spot to relax, listen to the lapping of the waves and enjoy a good glass of wine!
