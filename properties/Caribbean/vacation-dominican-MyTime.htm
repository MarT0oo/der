My Time, Casa de Campo, Dominican Republic Vacation Rental

 

A stunning three-story elegantly and professionally decorated ocean front villa fully equipped bordering the Teeth of the Dog Golf Course Signature hole.

As you walk in the property welcomes you with a view of the beautiful open living room, the outside swimming pool and ocean. Located on the ground floow you will find the formal living room, the formal dining room providing seats for eighteen guests and a small seating area to be use pre-meals, study and the fully equipped kitchen. This amazing property comes with six uninterrupted ocean view bedrooms suites, three of which are master suites and a large family room with a full bar.

Guests can also enjoy al fresco meals experiences, two gorgeous swimming pools, one of which sits on a rooftop deck with a full bar, seating area and bathroom plus a tropical landscaping gardens and indoor and outdoor flowing water bodies that are some of the many amenities the property has.
