Three Dolphins Villa, Blowing Point, Anguilla, Caribbean Vacation Rental

 

Three Dolphins Villa overlooks the sea with an expansive open floor plan that showcases the surrounding crystal clear waters against cerulean blue skies. This elegant, yet soothing rental villa offers sparkling views from every room — with designer touches throughout — so you can savor the sunny Caribbean beach location.

Three master suites with en-suite bathrooms in the main house are perfect for three couples. The adjacent guest house, with two king bedrooms and a shared bath, is great for a family of four or two more couples. A 40’ by 15’ fresh water pool, the centerpiece of an expansive deck, is a glorious place to relax, breath deep and savor the spectacular views of St. Martin. With a small beach just steps away, there is a perfectly secluded place to sunbathe, snorkel and rejuvenate by the sea. Or take in a different beach each day, as several of Anguilla’s powdery white, world-class beaches are just minutes away by car, including Meads Bay, Long Bay and Rendezvous Bay.

Contact our concierge to assist with any detail from airport transfers to dining reservations to childcare. Daily housekeeping, a staff gardener and a maintenance professional are always at your service so you can focus on this luxurious villa paradise and getting away from it all.
