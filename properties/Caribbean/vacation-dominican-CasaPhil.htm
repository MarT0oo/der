Casa Phil, Las Terrenas, Samana, Dominican Republic Vacation Rental

 

Caribbean luxury awaits you at Casa Phil, where modern European design is customized into an idyllic oasis. Whether you are lounging by the infinity pool or taking in the breathtaking views of the ocean, relaxation abounds at this stunning home. Perched atop the exclusive Loma Bonita in Las Terrenas Dominican Republic, the family-friendly villa comfortably sleeps ten.

The three-story villa has an open plan allowing for cool ocean breezes and breathtaking seaside views. All our four bedrooms feature Air Conditioning, Private Bathrooms, Wide Closets and well-appointed furnishings that mix antique with modern for a selective and stylized feel.

Master Bedroom

Located on the 2nd floor the Master Bedroom is the gem of the house and features his and her sinks and shower; a gigantic closet; private access to a porch and a lavish king size bed.

Second Master Bedroom

Located on the 1st floor, the Second Master bedroom, boasts a stone shower, fireplace and a beautiful metal forged bed coupled with a bunk bed to accommodate either romantic couples or adults and children together.

Guests Bedroom

The guest’s bedroom is located on the second floor with jaw dropping view of the sea and a private access to the terrace. It can flexibly accommodate a couple either in a king size bed or 2 single beds.

Children Bedroom

Located on the second floor, the “children clubhouse” has a dorm-like feel, with trendy bunks and a king size bed that can also comfortably accommodate couples with or without kids.

Living Room

Located on the 1st floor, the Living Room, open plan allows for cool ocean breezes and beautiful seaside views. Versatile shutters let guests open or close the house to the elements, creating a Robinson Crusoe-esque experience, with a dash of luxury. Perfect for lounging after a long day in the sun!

Outdoor Terrace

The ground floor outdoor terrace is one of the main features of Casa Phil. With a Balinese style infinity pool, adjacent hot jacuzzi, bar and pool table it is the natural social gathering place of the house. Enjoying the requisite 5pm cocktail on the wrap around the pool, listening to the waves crash at the nearby beaches.
