Villa Hall, Cap Estates, St. Lucia Vacation Rental

 

As you walk through Villa Hall’s majestic entrance the natural beauty of St. Lucia envelops you. The breathtaking view of the vast Caribbean Sea captures your imagination and awakens your senses. Villa Hall is surrounded by extensively landscaped gardens in beautiful St. Lucia’s Cap Estates. Only a five minute walk from two of the islands best snorkelling beaches, you can enjoy relaxing in the sun, swimming and snorkelling.

Nestled in the hillside, Villa Hall’s soaring ceilings and spectacular views are the perfect home away from home. Villa Hall offers six bedrooms, each with an ensuite bathroom, central air-conditioning and cable TV to make your stay as comfortable as possible. Three of the bedrooms open to walk-out balconies with wonderful views of the pool, gardens and the Caribbean Sea. The other three bedrooms have Juliette balconies overlooking the gardens, pool or sea.

Choose to dine inside or out. You can enjoy our air-conditioned dining room with easy access from our fully equipped kitchen and Great Room. You can also dine al fresco at the covered poolside dining area or the kitchen balcony, both overlooking the sea and lush garden. You can relax around Villa Hall’s infinity pool, laze poolside on sun loungers or cozy up with a book as you sway in the hammock.

The staff and Villa manager will satisfy your every need for a relaxing and memorable vacation.
