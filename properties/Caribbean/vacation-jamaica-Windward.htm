Windward, Montego Bay, Jamaica Vacation Rental

 

Windward is aptly named for the continuous cross current of trade winds that traverse the property. This graceful 6 bedroom home has the most spectacular 180 degree views from every room and from both levels. Airy interiors, well configured bedrooms and chic furnishings with eclectic accents make this villa a relaxing haven.

The villa looks out from the crest of the hill on Mahogany Drive and is built in classic Caribbean style, on two levels with spacious and contemporary interiors cleverly designed for family living. Windward’s four bedrooms on the upper level enjoy terraces which face the ocean and the two lower level bedrooms open onto patios with views of the stunning gardens. There are two master suites each with king sized Mahogany beds, private dressing rooms and en suite bathrooms finished with floor to ceiling travertine tiles, marble double vanities, walk-in showers and deep bathtubs. There is a guest bedroom adjacent to each master suite. One has a king size bed and the others have twin beds which can be configured as a king if required. The guest en suite bathrooms have walk-in showers.

The relaxing and spacious living room, dining room, and breakfast room are all on the main level with magnificent ocean views. An open plan design facilitates easy living and the colourful fabrics and comfortable furnishings give a spirited feel. Dinner on the great verandah makes for a spectacular occasion with views across Sandy Bay and the city of Montego Bay in the distance.

The villa’s lower level comprises a Comac heated infinity saltwater swimming pool which can be lit at night. The covered verandah on this level features a family area with ping pong table and other games, while an expansive yard includes small soccer field for family football matches.

The villas’ staff comprises a Chef, Butler and Assistant, Housekeeper, laundress and Gardener.
