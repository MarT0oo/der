Villa Nevaeh, Long Bay, Anguilla, Caribbean Vacation Rental

 

Welcome to our stunning villa Nevaeh, situated on the beachfront of the gorgeous Long Bay. Nevaeh is heaven spelled backwards and it certainly lives up to its name – it really is complete paradise. The villa is so spectacular and right in the middle of the island’s popular West End.

The villa is located on 1.35 acres of landscaped gardens that leads down to the pristine beach. As you enter Nevaeh, you are presented with the most gorgeous Balinese temple doors that open onto the beautiful garden and Japanese courtyard - simply the perfect place to unwind and relax while on vacation.

The fully equipped kitchen adjoins the main lounge and contains multiple refrigerators which are integrated into the decor and also feature six stools up by a granite topped counter which invites guest to sample snacks between meals. Nevaeh includes their very own chef in the Heavenly rate.

Nevaeh has a gorgeous terrace with a heated infinity pool that is color coordinated with the sea and cascades into a lower diving pool and grotto. A 12-person hot tub adjoins the pool and steps away a secluded half mile stretch of pristine beach awaits with an array of water sports on offer,

The villa offers plenty of shared and private living spaces. All the rooms are furnished with luxurious finishes. The entertainment and activity spaces are fantastic including a lighted tennis and basketball court, golf simulator with golf clubs, Techno gym, table tennis, beach volleyball, Xbox game console and not forgetting the 9 seater home theatre. There is so much to do at Nevaeh making it a great villa for families and large groups.

The villa has 9 bedrooms altogether with five bedrooms located in the Main House, three bedrooms within a Guest House nestled among the flowers and foliage, and one in the guest cabana located between the Main House and Guest House. The second floor of the main house is where two of the three Master Suites are located and they are separated by a pergola shaded cocktail deck that makes the most of the stunning ocean views. The free-standing pine wood cabana bedroom is positioned between the Main House and Guest House. An intimate, private accommodation with cedar-paneling throughout, the cabana can be set up with one king size bed. A bathroom with shower and single vanity, personal storage space and a beverage refrigerator completes the amenities. A wooden deck wraps around two sides of the cabana and ocean and garden views can be enjoyed from the rattan lounging beds positioned beneath the pergola.

The Master suite on the first floor features a jacuzzi on the terrace and has been designed to suit those with impaired mobility with convenient access to the beach. All the bedrooms at Nevaeh offer an elegant decor and oversized bathrooms. Some of the bathrooms feature pedestal tubs and outdoor showers and each provides a private terrace.

Nevaeh is complete Heaven for golfers as the villa offers a state of the art simulator that allows guests to play the world’s most challenging courses. There is so much entertainment on offer at this villa – it has to be the most tech oriented villas in the Caribbean. It is the ideal villa for extended families.

The villa staff at villa Nevaeh includes a property manager, villa supervisor, butler, housekeeper, houseman and on-call concierge and this ensures all our guests are well looked after throughout their trip.
