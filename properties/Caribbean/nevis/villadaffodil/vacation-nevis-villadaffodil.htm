Villa Daffodil, Nevis Island, Caribbean


Our magnificent villa was recently built by a Nantucket Island builder, with great attention to detail. The property is adjacent to the Four Seasons Resort Golf Course 12th hole and has panoramic views of the Sea. What makes the property especially unique is that it is a large (about 9,000 sf), multi-million dollar home, offering luxury and privacy, at a fraction of the cost of resort homes.

It is the perfect setting for families, couples, or groups. Larger groups love the incredible amount of space the villa offers, with 5 large bedrooms, 3 separate living areas and expansive verandas with tons of seating. Couples rave about the private location that is still less than a 10 minute drive to Town.

Further details for this vacation estate rental in Nevis

Wake up to see monkeys playing on the Golf Course. Spend the day relaxing by the pool, exploring, or going to the beach. Dine at one of the amazing plantation inns, or just stay in and cook fresh fish at the outdoor kitchen. The evening view is amazing, with a clear, beautiful view of the twinkling lights of downtown Basseterre.

The owners pay attention to making sure that every guest has a wonderful experience, whether you are visiting the Island for the first time, or are a regular. The property is meticulously maintained, inside and out, with regular thorough housekeeping and gardening. Every guest is greeted at the villa & as the owner is on-island year-round, you can be assured that any concerns you may have will be looked after. Please ask about our “hassle free service” which covers your taxi, boat trip (if arriving via St. Kitts), groceries waiting in the fridge & a rental car in the driveway! High-speed wireless internet, Directv and long distance phone.

The gardens feature a variety of lush, tropical plantings, Nevis Stone pathways, copper pots filled with fish, landscape lighting and an infinity edge swimming pool. You will find hummingbirds and monkeys as welcome visitors!

The interior of the home is finely finished and outfitted. Our large gourmet kitchen features high-end appliances like Thermador, Dacor, Bosch and Kitchenaid -- even a separate ice machine and wine fridge! There are large interior as well as outdoor dining areas, including bar seating. There are three separate living areas, with abundant seating, two of which having large-screen tvs with satellite tv. We offer three master suites and two other large bedrooms -- 3 king, 1 queen 2 full beds. Three interior living areas, one on the lower level and two on the main level. And something that photos don’t show … luxury hotel quality Sealy Posturpedic pillow-top mattresses, so you can truly have a relaxing night. Quality linens.

Five and a half bathrooms, including one bathroom with an outdoor shower covered in stone, with rain head shower and bench seating.

Built-in Nevis Stone and granite BBQ area featuring KitchenAid grill, under counter fridge and sink, with bar seating. 3 outdoor dining areas. Pool deck is outfitted with comfortable chaise lounges and side tables, perfect for pool-side relaxing. The villa sleeps up to 12 people comfortably.

For added peace of mind, our home is equipped with a generator and a back-up rainwater cistern, just in case of any outages. The home also has a security system and the property is gated. Two master suites offer safes.

Additionally, a cottage can be added, featuring 2 bedrooms – 1 queen and another bedroom with 2 twin beds. Cottage has a living area, full kitchen with island seating, powder bath and full bathroom with shower.
