Villa Capri , Galley Bay , Antigua


Villa Capri elegantly sits on over 7,400 ft² of living and entertaining space. As you drive towards this Antigua holiday home you will be amazed by the natural stone wall that hugs the property. On the inside of this majestic wall is an immense tropical garden thriving with tall mature trees, native palms and flowering plant life.

As you enter the villa vaulted ceiling, elegant floor tiles and huge sliding doors that open up to into the patio with infinity pool, the bay and the horizon give the feel of openness and a view that is incredible. This villa has an open floor plan, the living area is tastefully furnished with three modern European style couches and a coffee table, the accent wall is accentuated with floating paintings of the island of Capri, the owner's love for both islands inspire him to bring a touch of Capri into this villa. From this area the views through the open sliding doors gives a view for continuity as the infinity pool and the bay seems as though that are on one plain. Just off the living area is the formal dining area that is elegantly furnished with Italian furniture. From here a large sliding door opens up to the patio. Back on the inside, the functional Kitchen is fully equipped with professional grade stainless steel appliances, an island is added for extra space and chairs are set against it. This area is dazzling with an inviting atmosphere.

The family/entertainment room is tastefully furnished with sofa, HD Television, BOSE surround sound system, a desk with a chair and lamp. The floor is decked with natural hard wood, this area is bright and charming since a large window opens to the outside and brings in natural light.

This luxury villa has four en-suite bedrooms in the main villa with its own bathroom, veranda, closet and security safe. The master bedroom is furnished with a king size bed, night stands with lamps, walk in closet, large sliding door and huge windows bring in the natural light. The bathroom has his and her vanity, tub and walk in rain shower. The other three suites are also elegantly furnished and have their own bathroom. All the bedrooms have ceiling fan, air condition, and ipod decking station to listen to your favourite music.

Cottage Capri is self-contained with en-suite bedroom, bathroom, veranda, living area, minibar and its own access to the infinity pool.

On the outside of this holiday villa is the floating infinity pool, flanked by a huge wooden deck with comfortable lounge chairs for a relaxing or basking under the Caribbean sun. The Patio is furnished with outdoor dining furniture where also a stainless steel BBQ is standing to cook your fresh fish. This outdoor space is perfect for family gatherings, entertaining friends or just relaxing and enjoy the tranquility of nature.

The gym room has its own bathroom, a/c and ceiling fan.
