Villa Dominique, Nonsuch Bay, Antigua, Caribbean Vacation Rental

 

Villa Dominique is located on the gently sloping hillside of Emerald Cove. A very dramatic and luxurious home in a spectacular setting, in a gated residence. The Villa is located at the waters edge with a panoramic ocean view of the East Coast. It has three levels with an upper master suite, an entry-level second suite, a charming living room and patio, with den, and several dining nooks. The lower level has two bedrooms en suite.

There is alfresco dining on the lower patio and pool deck. Just a short walk takes you through the garden gates to your own private beach.
