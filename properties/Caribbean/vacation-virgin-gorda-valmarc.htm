Villa ValMarc Vacation Rental, Virgin Gorda, BVI


Our family would like to welcome you to Villa ValMarc. This luxury villa is a short 2 minute stroll to the pristine beach of Mahoe Bay, with its spectacular coral reef in the Caribbean Sea. Villa ValMarc is surrounded by lush flower gardens and has an extensive variety of fruit trees, including mango, papaya, banana, lemon, lime, orange and star fruit. Any of which are delightful for you to enjoy with your sunset beverage while in the free form pool.

This very private villa has four air conditioned bedrooms all with their own bath suites. Two of the bath suites include an outdoor glass block shower. The villa is built with two free standing pavilions. The main villa has a great room, dining room, entertainment area, as well as a custom kitchen including an island ideal for accompanying the serious chef while wonderful West Indian creations are being prepared. Living space is flanked by two bedrooms, each with their own walk in closet. All rooms open to a covered patio, ideal for outdoor dining and some shaded space for relaxing.

Wendy's Watch, the second pavilion, has two air conditioned bedroom/bath suites. The upstairs suite is a complete studio apartment with a king size bed, indoor seating area as well as a small kitchen. The covered patio offers complete privacy from the rest of the villa. Downstairs is another bedroom/bath suite that also has a walk out balcony.

Villa ValMarc offers many amenities to complete your perfect vacation. Some of these include satellite television that has ESPN, HBO and the NBA Ticket among the many channels selections. You will also find a VCR, DVD player, CD/radio, a well as an assortment of games and videos and a collection of books for your reading pleasure. Wi-Fi is available in some spots in Mahoe Bay. There are two barbeques - one gas and one charcoal. Also available for you are beach chairs, coolers, and the use of the Mahoe Bay tennis court. Each bedroom contains a complimentary safe for storage of valuables.

TRAVELING TO VIRGIN GORDA:


If you're arriving by air, keep in mind that there are no direct flights from the USA, Canada, Europe or South America to the British Virgin Islands main airport - the Terrence B. Lettsome Airport (EIS). When booking air travel remember to request flights to Tortola or use the airport code, EIS. The airport code for the Virgin Gorda Airport is VIJ. If you're arriving by sea, ports of entry are located in Road Town and the West End on Tortola, Great Harbour on Jost Van Dyke and St. Thomas Bay in Virgin Gorda. All vessels must clear Customs and Immigration immediately upon arrival and a valid passport and boat registration papers may be required.

All roads lead to the British Virgin Islands. Connecting airports surround the BVI, making arrivals and departures an easy part of your travel itinerary.
