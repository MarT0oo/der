Las Palmas Cap Cana, Cap Cana Resort, Dominican Republic Vacation Rental

 

Las Palmas Cap Cana is located in one of the most beautiful resorts in the world in Punta Cana Dominican Republic, easily accessed by plane, most airlines flying into Punta Cana easily from most places in the world. Cap Cana is the most prestigious of the Punta Cana resort with private beaches, luxury amenities, spas, golfing, and more. You cannot find a more amazing place to visit and play alongside some of the most impressive VIP's in the world.

You will find everything you want in the villa, made to impress with it's 4 large suites, modern decor and breathtaking backdrop. The house comes inclusive with maid and a wonderful staff, a cook can be arranged at a nominal rate. The housekeeper will keep things tidy and set the pace for your amazing vacation. The golfing is one of a kind so if you like getting up at dawn and golfing before the crowds, you're in the right place. Get up, jump into your private golf cart and enjoy the scenery. It's a very special place for very special travelers.

Las Palmas is situated on the 7th hole of the world-famous Punta Espada Golf Course, a Jack Nicklaus PGA Champions course, where you can chip on to the the 7th hole green from our back yard. The villa features spectacular ocean views and private pool and separately heated spa/hot tub. Naturally, our guests have access to all that Cap Cana has to offer as well as access to the resorts exclusive Cap Cana beach club.Cap Cana Oasis would like to congratulate Punta Espada Golf Course for being voted #1 Golf Course in the Caribbean and Mexico for 2010 by Golf Week for four (4) consecutive years. We are so happy to be located on the 7th hole of not only a PGA Champions course but also Golf Week’s #1 course in both the Caribbean and Mexico.
