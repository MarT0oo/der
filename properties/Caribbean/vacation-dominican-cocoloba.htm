Villa Cocoloba, Las Terrenas, Dominican Republic Vacation Rental


In the heart of adventure and stunning natural beauty, this unique Caribbean style villa offers breathtaking views of the Caribbean Sea and the surrounding coastal mountains.   This stylish and modern villa offers casual elegance at it's finest. It sits perched on a hilltop that gathers the warm sea breezes that offers its guest's peace and tranquility in a relaxed atmosphere.

In the morning you can wake up to brilliant sunrises and enjoy a cup of coffee on the veranda and in the evening you can sip your favorite cocktail while enjoying spectacular and colorful sunsets from the veranda and nearby infinity edge pool.  The unique floor plan of Cocoloba gives you over 650 m2 of living space and features 7 bedrooms and 6 baths on three levels. It also offers a large open kitchen and infinity edge pool, all surrounded by tropical gardens and over 400 m2 of terraces. The villa also gives its owners and guests a satellite TV with 2 flat screen TVs and Bose Stereo system in all main floor rooms of the Villa.

Master suite level

The master suite gives the owner amazing views overlooking the pool and the Caribbean towards the front and garden and mountain views to the back.
