No Le Hace, Montego Bay, Jamaica Vacation Rental

 

From its lofty vantage point, No le Hace has fabulous unobstructed ocean views and an ambiance that is delightfully Jamaican.

The recently renovated villa opens onto a central courtyard and is composed of a "great room", a dining area seating at least 10, a spacious patio and pool overlooking the ocean, a thatched roof bar, a separate gazebo, staff quarters and kitchen, 4 bedrooms, 5 baths, plus a separate two story, spacious master cottage including a bedroom, bar, living area and 1.5 baths. The en suite baths throughout the villa, three of which boast outdoor private showers, have all been completely renovated and feature travertine tile, granite countertops, stone flooring and Kohler fixtures.

The infinity pool facing the ocean and set into the large patio, is adjacent to the gazebo and thatched roof bar. The expansive area has an incomparable ocean vista and is the focal area of the villa. Each bedroom also has an ocean view and is outfitted with new smart flat screen tvs for your entertainment. An indoor/outdoor sound system is available for your pleasure as you sip cocktails while floating in the pool. The thatched roof bar includes a large, smart flat screen television with back lit LED. The villa is outfitted with panoramic Wi-Fi.

The No Le Hace staff includes five full-time employees who anticipate your every need - a chef, a butler, a laundress, an under butler and a housekeeper. Our staff is simply the best. The translation for No Le Hace is "No Worries" and that is how you will feel when you stay with us. Whether it is for a family get together or a vacation with friends, we look forward to accommodating you.
