Villa Belle De Nuit, Happy Bay, St. Martin Vacation Rental


Villa Belle De Nuit on the hillside above Happy Bay beach is an architectural gem. Designed by Mauricio Lanari, the villa captures the cool trade winds and magnificent ocean and sunset views in a relaxed contemporary setting.

The spacious living room, with media area, has panoramic windows leading out to the cover terrace dining area and graceful pool terrace with oversized infinity styled pool and spectacular views of the ocean and nearby Anguilla. The villa has five beautifully furnished bedrooms and 5.5 bathrooms. The Master and 2nd Bedroom have interior access off the living areas while the three further bedrooms on the lower level, are accessed from the pool terrace.

A public footpath leads from the villa down to peaceful Happy Bay beach and to the more commercialized Friar’s Bay Beach. A perfect property for a group of friends or family get together, Belle de Nuit is a welcome retreat that mirrors the turquoise waters of the Caribbean beautifully.
