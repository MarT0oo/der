Ti Moune, Rendezvous Bay, St. John, Caribbean Vacation Rental

 

Ti Moune’s cheerful Caribbean aesthetic, lush tropical landscaping and simply magnificent views will instantly put you in an island frame of mind. You’ll want to grab your favorite book and settle in for a leisurely day on the sun-dappled decks. Or perhaps mix up a cocktail and go for a relaxing float in the pool. The well-appointed great room, gourmet kitchen, and cozy bedroom suites all make it delightfully easy to enjoy your stay.

For days spent out and about, Ti Moune is also ideally situated, only minutes from the easy bustle of Cruz Bay, which has great shopping, dining and entertainment options. And it’s just a few minutes further to the North Shore’s world-class white sand beaches. Whether you want a private island escape or trip full of adventure and exploration (or a happy combination of both), Ti Moune will make you feel right at home.
