Tropical Dream Villa at Cap Cana, Cap Cana, Dominican Republic Vacation Rental

 

Tropical Dream Villa represents paradise on every level. From lush tropical surroundings to its top-tier standards of luxury, this spectacular eight-bedroom villa promises unrivaled peace and tranquility. The property is located within the exclusive Cap Cana community; a 26 sq mi estate lined with white powder beaches and colorful coral reefs, where the Caribbean and the Atlantic merge.

The two-story contemporary island villa curves its way through grounds overflowing with outdoor living spaces. Have your private chef serve breakfast under the thatched gazebo, as morning light dances along the surface of three surrounding swimming pools. Designer chaise longues are positioned everywhere, allowing you to chase the sun all day. A multi-use sports court lets you get physical with your guests, while the wood pizza oven and outdoor sound system make entertaining a must.

Tall vaulted ceilings, open passageways and an abundance of sliding glass walls make the villa’s interior an extension of the outdoor bliss. The décor takes its cues from a variety of sources, from classic Caribbean tones, to California contemporary and even hints of Eastern zen. Multiple living rooms and games rooms present ideal spaces to gather with your guests in the evening. Give the chef a night off and test your skills in the gourmet kitchen. A private gym and squash court are at your disposal, should you wish to get the heart racing.

Up to eighteen people will sleep like royalty in any of Tropical Dream Villa’s eight bedrooms. Each one features its own spa-inspired en-suite, and air conditioning. Several rooms have direct access to either the central patios or balconies, and all feature lovely views. The beautifully tiled Master suite boasts its own living room with flatscreen TV. Wood vaulted ceilings and accents provide a cozy ambiance in numerous guest bedrooms.

The Cap Cana community is rich with sights and activities. Start with a quick walk to the Punta Cana Resort Beach for surf and sand. Punta Cana Village and the various clubs hold fine dining options. Contact our concierge to book a tee time at the award-winning Jack Nicklaus golf course, Punta Espada. Take a boat out at the high-end marina, or enjoy scenic walks through the tropical sanctuaries found at Cap Cana.
