Aquamarie Villa Kamique, Anguilla, Caribbean Vacation Rental

 

Sitting on Anguilla's South Shore, Kamique commands its own secluded cove beach and views towards the mountains of St. Marten. Kamique is a collection of 3 Luxury Villas on Anguilla's South Coast-6 Bedroom Triton, 4-bedroom Aquamarine and 4-bedroom Anani. A 180-degree full ocean view. The homes are nestled in the rolling landscape to form their own private enclave. Aquamarine Villa is wheelchair accessible as well.

A blend of woods, stone, and concrete creates a modern island-inspired environment. A touch of the tropics provides a sense of romance. The atmosphere blurs the line between inside and out, and orchestrates light and shade to create a peaceful oasis.

Aquamarie's 4-bedroom villa is a contemporary West Indian Style home that sits on a slight rise overlooking the sea. This villa has direct access to a cove that is protected by an outer reef. Umbrellas, beach chairs, and day beds are available for use.

Two master en-suite bedrooms are located on the first floor in separate wings offering amazing views. On the ground floor, guests will find a mini master suite with ocean views, en-suite bathroom connecting to a garden view bedroom also with en-suite bath. This bedroom is good for those guests with small children.

The indoor/outdoor living and dining area with full decks surround a large pool. There is a modern open plan kitchen. A media room with comfortable seating and a large television, and many videos games including a Wii and a powder room.

The villas range in size up to 10,800 square feet of constructed area. Each is centered on a wide open Great Room incorporating living, dining, and kitchen facilities, and indoor/outdoor entertainment options. The Media Room has 50-inch flat-screen TVs. The master suites have well appointed bathrooms and outdoor garden showers. The outdoor spaces include sun decks, private meditation, and dining areas and sea view pools. Charming touches like Jerusalem stone flooring, Indonesian furniture, Italian kitchen, and Egyptian cotton linens are but a few of the niceties found here. Modern amenities, appliances, entertainment, and wireless internet systems are outfitted throughout each villa.

Each villa holds its own full size pool. Please note the pools have no child security features and are to be used at guests' own risk.
