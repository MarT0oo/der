Villa Cabofino, Cabrera, Dominican Republic Vacation Rental

 

Situated on a Cliffside with incredible ocean views as the backdrop, this award winning 9 bedroom luxury estate will enchant you with its 12,000 square foot living space.  Play tennis in the full-size grass tennis court, or sunbathe by an alluring slate-bottom pool and soak in a free-form rock whirlpool Jacuzzi complete with a cave. Enjoy sunset cocktails at an authentic Tiki bar, and sample the delicious cuisine from our professional kitchen. Finish the night at the in-house Island Disco and discover why Cabofino is known as the best party villa in the Caribbean!

As you enter Villa Cabofino, you will be awestruck by the incredible size of this magnificent villa and the breathtaking panoramic ocean views that it offers. Our staff will greet you with welcome cocktails and snacks as you get ready to tour your new home. 

The kitchen is one of the busiest areas of the house. This is where your professional chefs will prepare delicious meals designed specifically to your desire. You need not lift a finger as our friendly wait staff will pamper you with five-star service. Sit back and enjoy our authentic Caribbean dishes in our 11-seat dining room, or al-fresco in our outdoor dining areas. 

 

Our staffed, fully stocked bar offers breathtaking cliff side ocean views and is the perfect place to enjoy your favorite cocktails. You'll be entertained watching videos on the giant screen satellite TV, playing pool on the billiard table, and listening to your favorite tunes on the powerful sound system. This is truly the perfect party villa. 

The beautiful master suite, featuring a king-sized bed, a living area with chimney, and a very spacious en-suite bathroom with a marble hot tub, offers relaxing panoramic ocean views from every window and a relaxing outside terrace.  The double-room pool suite offers a comfortable living area that leads through a door to a connecting bedroom suite. Both suites feature king beds, air conditioning, cable TV, and a shared bathroom. This is the ideal family suite. The Cliff Suite Cottage offers you privacy with direct ocean views and features tastefully decorated oversized suites with independent entrances and en-suite bathrooms. Each suite features a king-sized bed, cedar closets, dressing room, cable TV, small refrigerator, and A/C.

Located on the highest point of the property, overlooking the grass tennis court and ocean, the very spacious tennis master suite offers unobstructed 270 degree direct ocean views, a large en-suite living room and a beautiful natural tropical garden inside the suite. 

Steps from the entertainment room, the South Suite and nearby Coral Suite each feature a queen bed, en-suite bathroom, air conditioning, and cable television. The Dolphin Suite is a spacious double suite with connecting room, just steps from the entertainment and bar area. One room offers a king-sized bed and the second room features a queen-sized bed and a shared en-suite bathroom. Both rooms have A/C, ceiling fans, and cable TV.
