Bávaro Breeze, Bavaro Beach, Punta Cana, Dominican Republic Vacation Rental

 

This 3 bedroom apartment has an incredible location on the ground floor with an outstanding ocean view of the pool and beach. It has three private rooms, 2 with en-suite bathroom and one with bathroom just outside the room. Spacious Living room with kitchen and dining table.

The main bedroom has a Queen size bed, the second room a Queen size bed and the third bedrooms have 2 twin beds, all the bedrooms have air conditioning and ceiling fans. The kitchen is fully equipped with a gas stove and all utensils, and the TV's in the living room and bedroom have international cable.

Playa Turquesa is a beachfront condo community located on one of the top 10 Caribbean beaches. Take a swim in the most beautiful stretch of Punta Cana/Bavaro Beach and then relax at the Playa Turquesa pool.

Quieter than resorts or hotels, your apartment will quickly become your home for the week. The beach, grounds, buildings and pool are well kept and clean. The beach is never out of sight and located only steps away from the apartment.

Vibrant life around the area with activities for everyone. Water activities include parasailing, snorkeling, scuba diving, kite boarding, fishing, and boating. For golfers, there are numerous fine golf courses in the area to choose from.

Restaurants along the beach are just a short walk away. Also, there is a sports bar, bakery, pharmacy, and several mini-grocery stores are all within walking distance.

A short drive away is the Palma Real Village Mall, which boasts cinemas, the Hard Rock Cafe, Outback and a host of other stores. Casinos, nightclubs, and an ecological reserve are also a short drive away.

We are located 20 minutes from the international Punta Cana airport.
