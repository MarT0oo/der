Dragonfly, Montego Bay, Jamaica Vacation Rental

 

On Jamaica’s northern coast, just west of Montego Bay, The Tryall Club boasts some of the Island’s most prestigious luxury villas, resort amenities, and amazing ocean views. From its elevated perch, villa Dragonfly captures panoramic vistas of the lush green hillside and endless ocean. With an open-air design and seven king en-suite bedrooms, all with ocean views and outdoor access, this breezy beach house is perfect for a large family gathering, vacation with friends, or a retreat with colleagues.

Nearly every room in Dragonfly faces, and opens to, an incredible ocean view. The atmosphere is free-flowing, with barely an obstructions between inside and out. The spacious living room has plenty of comfortable seating for the whole group. If you feel like finding a more private space, the home theatre is perfect for a quiet night in. For a little excitement, Dragonfly has a poker table, table tennis, exercise room, and a basketball court. Outside, the terrace has an amazing infinity pool and hot tub. And, if you’re thinking of hosting, there are wet bars and dining areas inside and out.

Dragonfly comes equipped with a staff of housekeepers, butlers, a laundress, and a private chef, all there to help make your vacation as relaxing as possible. There’s also an on-property shuttle service to help you navigate Tryall’s beautiful estate. While you’re exploring, you’ll find their private beachfront and club, tennis and golf, beach cafes and restaurants, shops, the fitness center, and a kids club. And, if anyone is feeling adventurous, there’s complimentary access to Watersport equipment.

Twenty-kilometers to the west, you’ll find Montego Bay, where you’ll fall in love with their beachfront bars and restaurants, designer shopping outlets, and vibrant nightlife. If you’re traveling with children, the Aqua Sol Theme Park and Kool Runnings Water Park are exciting places to spend an afternoon. And, if you’d like to check out some of the public beaches in the area, Half Moon and Coral Beach are two of Jamaica’s finest, both within a short drive.
