Amanoka Villa, Discovery Bay, Jamaica, Caribbean Vacation Rental

 

At Amanoka the best of the Caribbean beauty and hospitality abounds. Within the villa’s walled compound, colors bursts from the tropical garden filled with exotic orchids and sensuous lily ponds. The garden’s manicured lawn flows onto the villas private white sand beach and turquoise bay. On the water’s edge, you will find an island gazebo with swinging “sky chairs,” perfect for lounging away the day or watching a glorious sunset.

Cradled in the lush embrace of the garden are the main villa and three large en-suite bedrooms. The entrance hall, great room, and dining area open onto a spacious deck complete with a comfortable bamboo seating pavilion, large heated jacuzzi and infinity edge pool. Linked to the main villa by a garden walkway is the four-bedroom guest villa, all with large en-suite bathrooms and opening onto balconies with views of the bay.

Amanoka’s interiors mirror the beauty of its surrounding. A tasteful blend of Caribbean and Indonesian style, the villa’s décor is pleasing to both the eye and soul. Teak, bamboo, and stone finishes accentuated by colorful fabrics, and perfectly placed orchids fill this elegant home. Artwork by both Jamaican and international artists adorn the walls ensuring that every room is a visual treat. The villa is outfitted with cable television in the great room and all bedrooms, a Sonos music system that filters soothing sound throughout the house and grounds and complimentary wireless internet access.
