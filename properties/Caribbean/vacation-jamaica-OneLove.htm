One Love, Montego Bay, Jamaica Vacation Rental

 

One Love is a one bedroom condominium perfectly with views of the Tryall Golf Course and the Caribbean Sea.

One Love sits on the crest of a hill at the heart of the Tryall Club and is adjacent to the elegant early 19th century Georgian Great House. It is only 2-3 minutes by golf cart or property shuttle to the Club’s private beach and the golf course. The tennis courts, Kid’s Club, swimming pool, shops and wellness centre are just a short stroll away.

One Love has a split level layout with a lovely terrace and the clever combination of French country style with Jamaican accents makes for an atmosphere that is warm and welcoming.

Air conditioned throughout, the bedroom is approached through a foyer on the main level. It has a grand four-poster king bed and a Jacuzzi tub enclosure. The en suite bathroom has a walk-in shower. Outside, the balcony is a perfect place to sip and savour an early morning cup of Blue Mountain coffee whilst admiring the magnificent countryside and Caribbean waters.

A short staircase leads down to the smartly furnished open-plan living room and kitchen. A full bed pull-out is included as well as a Murphy bed for extra sleeping space if required. Outside, a private terrace offers commanding sea and countryside views.

A full-service Housekeeper provides maid, laundry and breakfast service. Private Cook/Chef and/or butler services can be arranged for dinners.

Movement around the property is free courtesy of the the property shuttle and golf carts are available for rental.
