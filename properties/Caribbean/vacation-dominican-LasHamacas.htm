Las Hamacas, Cap Cana, Dominican Republic Vacation Rental

 

Discover a paradise like no other, nestled up against the sugar sand and sparkling Atlantic water. Located in the midst of luxurious Cap Cana estate, Las Hamacas Beachfront is an exceptional villa, whose reputation for refinement and luxury have earned it coverage in international magazines. The unique, 6-bedroom home finds its roots in Caribbean plantation style, and infuses it with safari tones and Eastern flavors.

Where Las Hamacas’ outdoor living areas begin and end is a matter of perception. The home is backed by an immense, two-level wood beam veranda, open on all sides, with lounge furniture for your enjoyment, and elevated walkways passing below its thatched roof. Pull up a chaise longue to the infinity pool and spend relaxed days basking in the sun, as the sound of lapping waves fill your ears. Hammocks hang everywhere - hence the villa’s name - including from palm trees on the beach. As the sun begins to set, serve champagne before lighting the barbecue for an al fresco feast.

Inside you’ll encounter eclectic décor of hand selected furniture, accessories and art that capture the vibrant spirit of island life. Fresh, ocean air pours in through sliding glass doors, while picture windows treat you to displays of colorful flowers and gardens. Multiple living rooms are at your disposal, as are high-end appliances when you’re ready to prepare a masterpiece in the gourmet kitchen. This property also houses the latest technology, allowing you to access Wi-Fi, and watch movies on a flatscreen TV.

The six unforgettable bedrooms are spread around the property, with several in the main house and a few more in an picturesque stilt guesthouse. In each one, you’ll find an original arrangement of imported furniture below a lofty thatched roof. The spacious Master bedroom is a villa unto itself, with a King bed, beautiful lacquered doors and a private sun room. All bedrooms have their own walk-in closet and spa-inspired en-suite.

From its state-of-the-art marina, to the Jack Nicklaus signature golf course, Cap Cana features everything you desire for a 5-star escape. Plunge into the water and discover a world of tropical fish or explore trails that lead from gardens to natural springs. Dine at fine restaurants, peruse the boutiques or just spend each day searching for the perfect stretch of white sand.
