Villa Amarilla, Anguilla, British West Indies Vacation Rental


Welcome to Villa Amarilla. This stunning vacation home is located on Anguilla's Leeward north side, on the Sea Rocks of Island Harbour.  Designed by Anguilla's own acclaimed architect Carl Richards, Villa Amarilla offers 9,000 square feet of luxury and comfort.  The Caribbean architectural design is accented with an Italian influence in its yellow stucco walls, turquoise shutters and red tile roof.  The scenic vista includes some of the island's most beautiful reefs with panoramic views of Shoal Bay Beach to the west and Scrub Island to the east.  Spacious indoor and outdoor living and dining areas offer ample room for a large group to gather or a variety of havens for an individual to find solitude.  Shoal Bay Beach, a two-mile stretch of powder white sand and crystal clear water is a short drive from the villa and a secluded beach is a short walk away.

Moroccan influences highlight the distinctive interior décor, designed by the owners in collaboration with award-winning interior designer David Salem.  Spacious indoor and outdoor areas offer ample room for a large group or quiet havens for individual solitude.  Villa Amarilla's spectacular setting, stunning design and décor, and elegant comfort create the perfect setting for your island escape. Iron and glass doors open to reveal a spacious entry with matching custom tables and green tile staircases leading to the four upstairs bedroom suites and outdoor living area.  A stunning shell chandelier highlights the entry and reminds guests of the natural beauty of the Caribbean.  The large open living room leads to the outdoor seating areas and includes a flat screen television for entertainment.  A separate air conditioned media room has a 60" flat screen plasma HD television, surround sound and a library of movies.  There is a Control4Audio system for playing a wide selection of music downstairs and outside by the pool. 

Villa Amarilla features state-of-the art GE Profile appliances including a five burner gas cook top, double ovens, microwave, dishwasher, spacious refrigerator and freezer and a separate wine refrigerator.  The indoor bar, equipped with an ice maker and beverage center is strategically situated between the kitchen and dining room and opens to the living room.  The custom dining table seats 12 guests and is accented by a coral chandelier.  The spectacular heated infinity edge pool is large enough to swim laps but also offers the most stunning panoramic views on the property.  Relax on the pool's viewing ledge with a refreshing beverage in hand or stretch out on one of the built-in lounges while the cool water surrounds you.  There is a spacious heated spa with equally stunning views that can be enjoyed day or night.  Villa Amarilla features an expansive patio area surrounding the pool with beautiful terra cotta tile and river rock.  The covered outdoor living space offers comfortably furnished seating areas.  Beautiful tile detailing in one of the covered seating areas reflects the richness of the Caribbean ocean and sky.  For outdoor dining in a stunning setting, the one of a kind tile table seats up to 10 guests.  The tiled bar area conveniently services the pool area and outdoor dining table.  The outdoor barbecue grilling kitchen is wonderful for preparing steaks and fresh seafood.  A large upstairs terrace and two private upstairs balconies provide the perfect setting for sunbathing or enjoying the sunset view in the evening.

The gym is conveniently located in a separate building adjacent to the main house.  The gym features an Octane Eliptical, a True Recumbent Bike, a 36" HD flat-screen television, iPod docking station, stereo, full bath and massage table.  There are also yoga mats, free weights, and body balls to enhance your workout.  Guests enjoy a magnificent view of the ocean and the lovely landscaped grounds in air-conditioned comfort.

The Pearl Master Suite is the only downstairs bedroom. The interior decor features subtle hues with Salamander fabric on the window treatments and stunning ocean views on three sides.  The king mahogany bed has luxurious Sierra linens and a 32" HD flat-screen television provides complete comfort.  The bedroom and bathroom are air-conditioned, but for those who prefer the ocean breeze, there are screens and a ceiling fan.  Guests may choose to shower indoors or outside under the stars in your private outdoor shower.


The upstairs Emerald Master Suite combines a beautiful green and white Lee Jofa fabric with a stained wood beam ceiling to create a warm luxurious bedroom.  The king mahogany bed has luxurious Sferra linens and a 32" HD flat-screen television provides complete comfort.  The bedroom and bathroom are both air-conditioned, but for those who prefer the ocean breeze, there are screens and a ceiling fan. The Turquoise Suite can be configured with twin beds or one king based upon guests' preferences.  With turquoise and white Lee Jofa fabric and beautiful Italian tile, the colors of the sea move from the lovely ocean front view into the ambience of the room.  Exquisite detailing and Sferra sheets complement the comforts of air-conditioning, ceiling fan and a flat screen television.  The bathroom has a large walk-in shower accented with beautiful tiles and natural lighting.  The Coral Suite can be configured with twin beds or one king based upon guests' preferences.  Rich Clarence House coral and white fabric and stunning ocean views create a vibrant, colorful ambiance in the room.  It has striking accessories, Sferra sheets, a flat screen television, air-conditioning and ceiling fan combine comfort with luxury.  Custom handmade tile and natural lighting accent the large bathroom.  The Sapphire Master Suite is another suite with stunning ocean views and early sunrise views.  Classic blue and white fabric, a stained wood beam ceiling and beautiful accessories bring a warm ambiance to the suite.  Guests will enjoy the choice of an indoor and outdoor private shower with beautiful blue and green tile in the bathroom.  The bedroom has custom Sferra linens, a flat screen television, ceiling fan and wood blinds which add comfort and luxury.
