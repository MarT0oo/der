Red Sun Villa, West Coast, Antigua Vacation Rental

 

Red Sun Villa is a modern 5 bedroom villa enjoying breathtaking views of Galley Bay's white sand beach and the calm turquoise waters of the Caribbean Sea. A large and very private salt water swimming pool is situated in the lush grounds and a jacuzzi is perfectly located on the main deck area, adjacent to the main loft style living area with its futuristic door/walls which open up at the touch of a button onto the al fresco dining area. The main living space also houses a lounge area with TV, a well-equipped kitchen and an indoor dining area.

A large wooden deck links the main living accommodation to the sleeping accommodation where 3 bedrooms are situated on the upper level and a further 2 bedrooms on the lower level. All bedrooms face the ocean and have en suite bathroom facilities.

The villa is furnished with great attention to detail and style -with a definite slant towards minimalism. All furnishings and finishes are Italian designer and high quality.

The house has both 110V and 220V power outlets with both US and European two pin sockets.

The villa has private access to Shell Bay, a small beach adjacent to Galley Bay and a short walk from Galley Bay Beach- a perfect spot to soak up the sun or enjoy some snorkeling in the pristine clear waters of the ocean
