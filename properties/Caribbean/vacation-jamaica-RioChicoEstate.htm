Rio Chico Estate, Ocho Rios, Jamaica Vacation Rental

 

From Jamaica with Love: this grand-scale James Bond style escape is a breath-taking hideaway which can be yours to enjoy! Probably the best kept secret in Jamaica, the Rio Chico Estate is the epitome of old-world charm and luxury and perfect for large gatherings of friends or families who are looking for the height of luxury and exclusivity to the extreme.

This villa is also available as a 6 bedroom option starting at $8,250 per night.

As you enter through Rio Chico's gates, you can't help but feel as though you've just stepped straight into a Monet painting: passing lily ponds and ivy covered stone bridges on your approach through this magnificent 14-acre estate. Located just outside of Ocho Rios, our Rio Chico Villa is without a doubt one of Jamaica's most exceptional and stunning properties. Manicured lawns, lush tropical and heavily fruited gardens, the landscape sings English-country-garden meets Jamaican tropical paradise. Welcome to the world of Rio Chico, no longer just a dream. Villa Rio Chico is undoubtedly one of the finest, most picturesque estates in the world, and it's no exaggeration to say that it's heaven on earth.

Experience a rarified tropical estate that combines true beachfront luxury with lavish gardens reminiscent of an Italian palazzo. This 14-acre gated property is a chic oasis framed by lush tropical vegetation, meandering streams two waterfalls, ivy-covered bridges, and manicured gardens adorned with classical Vicenza stone sculptures, all set along a 2,000-foot protected shoreline.

The interior of the main house and separate cottage evoke the ambience of a chic European boutique hotel. Step inside to discover a living room styled with billowing white fabrics, rich woods, and gleaming silvers. Rooms flow seamlessly from indoors to out, with a Zen-like feeling that melds naturally into the environment. And each of the eight spacious bedroom suites reflects a unique style and aura.

Sumptuous touches abound, like contemporary bathrooms with a refined attention to detail befitting an estate of this caliber, freestanding marble soaking tubs, unique designer light fixtures and furnishings, Sferra linens, and Frette bathrobes. And the staff of highly-trained butlers, private chef and sous chef, and housekeepers are attentive, unobtrusive; and most of all, masters at creating unique experiences that you can only find at Rio Chico.

Magnificent outdoor spaces have been designed with the same level of elegance. The bluff-top main pool overlooks the ocean, while a second hidden pool steps down directly into the ocean's waters. A third pool is a spectacular natural river pool sourced from the same waters as the nearby Dunn's River Falls.

Two private white sand beaches provide secluded spots for relaxation or exhilaration, with our water-sports guru ready to provide instruction for the sea kayaks, sailboats, paddleboards, and snorkel gear. A tennis court is lit for night play, and a billiards table, gym, and croquet are provided for your entertainment.

Dining at Rio Chico is an amazing experience in and of itself. From laid-back to formal and from five-course meals to quick afternoon snacks, whatever your food mood-of-the-moment is, our friendly staff at Rio Chico is there to make your vacation an unforgettable one.

Rio Chico is perhaps one of the most stunning properties in all of Jamaica and as such the people who take care of the daily operations and the guests that stay there, are equally as stunning. From the head chef who masterminds much of the daily experience inside the house to the head grounds keeper who maintains the 14 acres of exceptional beauty that surrounds Rio Chico, the attention to detail and warm and friendly atmosphere is second to none.

Guests at Rio Chico can expect world class gourmet meals from the moment the wake up to the last meal they eat under the stars. The butlers at Rio Chico are professionally trained masters of detail that work behind the scenes setting the mood at every meal and making sure you want for nothing during your stay. You can rest assured that your stay with us will be the most relaxing vacation in the most unforgettable setting imaginable.
