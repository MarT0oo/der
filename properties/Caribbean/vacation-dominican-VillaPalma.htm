Villa Palma, Punta Cana, Dominican Republic Vacation Rental

 

Nestled and surrounded by a dense coat of dancing evergreen palms, Villa Palma offers seclusion and privacy to all its guests. Facing the golf course from the pool area, this 5-bedroom horseshoe-shaped property is the perfect match for large families or a group of friends who are looking to forget about the bustling city for a while.

Accessing through the main entrance guests will be guided to the heart of the villa, a large patio with beautiful and exuberant palm trees and views to the balconies on the second floor. Crossing the main hall is the living room with a sober and sophisticated taste that guides to the garden, the outdoor living, and the pool. On one of the wings is the fully equipped kitchen with marble countertops, a modern finish and a kitchen island with a hob. Next to the living room is the dining room with a 10-seats glass table and a remarkable chandelier with over 30 bulbs.

On the second floor is a family room, with a wide TV, studio with desk and distinguished turbine-like fans. On this floor, there are four out of the five bedrooms, with access to the interior balcony. Two of them have 2 queen beds, a TV and an en-suite bathroom each. The third and the fourth bedrooms have a king bed, TV and ensuite bathroom each. On the first floor is the master bedroom with ample space, TV, desk, en-suite bathroom and access to the garden.

This beautiful villa is located in the exclusive gated community of Punta Cana Resort & Club, a 26-square mile paradise that is blessed with a temperate year-round climate, light rainfall, and soft breezes. It's colorful coral reefs, calm crystalline waters and white-sand beaches are breathtaking. The Resort provides a matchless experience for all who visit this tropical paradise, and its tropically chic lifestyle is ideal for those who appreciate high standards of quality, comfort, and aesthetics. As guests of the villa, you have access to all of the resort amenities and activities.
