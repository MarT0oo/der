Afternoon Delight, Casa de Campo, Dominican Republic Vacation Rental

 

Afternoon Delight in Casa de Campo Dominican Republic is a mix of the beautiful Spanish feeling and the amazing local architecture. Beautiful bright colors and lovely amenities make this a one of a kind experience. Bring your entire family and feel right at home. A curated home from our new Casa De Campo Collection.

A beautiful five bedroom Balinese style home featuring expansive social areas with views to the gardens, Casa de Campo Marina and the Caribbean Sea.

As you walk in guest are welcome with a corridor and lush vegetation that lead you to the living areas, the infinity swimming pool and gazebo. Bedrooms are located on the ground level and second level. The large master bedroom sits on the second level offering views over the Marina and the Caribbean Sea. It feature a small social area and a large bathroom. Each bedroom with their private bathrooms and dressing areas, over look the interior gardens.

The villa is just step away from Casa de Campo Marina where restaurants, shops and supermarket are located.
