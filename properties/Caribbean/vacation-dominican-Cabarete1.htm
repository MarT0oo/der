This 'DRAMATIC OCEAN FRONT CARIBBEAN VILLA' on the north coast is in the prestigious ocean front section of the exclusive Sea Horse Ranch. Cabarete 1 is a 250 acre exclusive, gated development on the North Coast between Cabarate and Sosua. It is a fifteen minute drive from the International airport at Puerto Plata. Besides a large lap pool at the villa there is a community pool available for your use in a park like setting overlooking the ocean. Also at Sea Horse Ranch there is tennis and horseback riding in this gated and prestigious development.

Sosua is a quaint town with a bohemian flair sporting lots of out door cafes, a wide variety of foods and an active night life including three casinos. Sosua beach is set in a protected cove and sports many bars and restaurants and has long been one of the favorite beaches on the north coast. Cabarete has a beautiful wide beach where the restaurants and bars are built directly on the sand. Cabarete is host to the world kite and wind surfing championships. Sea Horse Ranch is 15 minutes from Puerto Plata International Airport, 5 minutes to Sosua town center, and 10 minutes to Cabarete Beach.

Arrangements can be made to be picked up at the airport. Exquisite craftsmanship with lavish appointments in Mexican terracotta tile floors, mahogany woodwork, granite counter tops, stainless kitchen and marble bathrooms. Guests are welcomed in a dramatic foyer to 35 ft beamed ceilings. Just beyond, the elegant living room is graced by an impressive iron chandelier and inviting overstuffed furnishings.

Art lovers will especially appreciate the extensive collection of original art, a small example of the attention to detail for a vacation full of grace and charm.
