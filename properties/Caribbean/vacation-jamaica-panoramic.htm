Bluebird Villa, Montego Bay, Jamaica Vacation Rental


Bluebird is a four-bedroom villa overlooking a magnificent tropical garden and the glittering panorama of the Caribbean Sea. A spacious, living room lifts your spirit with soft furnishings in up-tempo hues of blue and yellow accented with crisp white.
