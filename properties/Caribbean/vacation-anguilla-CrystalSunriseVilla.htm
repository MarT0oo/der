Crystal Sunrise Villa, Sandy Hill Bay, Anguilla Vacation Rental

 

Separated from the ocean’s shore by Anguilla’s fascinating; limestone rocks, towers Crystal Sunrise Villa. Coastally aligned, this beauty over the horizon offers you the most picturesque views; from its roof top, open deck panoramic patio to the poolside. Why sleep indoors? When you can sleep under the solidarity of the stars and be awakened by the serenity of the sunrise across the ocean.

Be greeted by the natural auras of the ocean’s breeze as you merrily go about your morning. Take advantage of our modern styled kitchen and its utilities or allow us to wine and dine you with some of the best chefs in the region. Partake of your dinner indoor or outdoor in luxury. No need to worry about the calories you’ll put on because when you’re ready to burn them off; you can do so privately and comfortably in our indoor gym; after which you can proceed to relax and unwind in the heated pool.

Once you have rested and rejuvenated behold some of nature’s greatest treasures by taking part in outdoor activities such as swimming, fishing, snorkeling, bird watching or even sightseeing of the surroundings and neighboring islands like ST. Barths and St. Martin from a distance; without even having to leave the premises. Whilst indoors, why not gather the family and head to the entertainment room where a pool table is there just waiting to be used by you; not forgetting the kids as there is a vast amount of space remaining for them to utilize.

Space is one of things that this villa is not shy of; for this 13000 sq ft building boasts the highest qualities in its amenities. Hosting five of the most beautifully garnished bedrooms, four of which are adorned with king size beds and one with a sofa bed; complimented by four of the most modern and luxurious bathrooms ; as well as one half bath. Whilst all four bathrooms are lustrously equipped with a shower and toilet, only two of them include a bidet and only the master bathroom has both a tub and a jetted tub.

Crystal Sunrise Villa is a true personification of what home away from home should feel like. Now, can you envision staying anywhere else?

This property is in close proximity to the ocean, is newly built and has all the necessities and amenities. This property can also accommodate a large family or a group of friends and associates.
