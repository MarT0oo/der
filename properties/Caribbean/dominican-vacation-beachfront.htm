Villa Flor de Cabrera, Cabrera, Dominican Republic Vacation Rental


Flor deCabrera beachfront luxury vacation villas are set on a pristine, unspoiled beachfront within the Orchid Bay Estates – a secure, gated community within the town of Cabrera. This enclave is the home of many of the most beautiful Dominican Republic vacation villas. When you rent this Private Resort the entire property and its full-service dedicated staff is totally committed to your vacation pleasure. You never share the facilities with another group.

Flor de Cabrera offers complete privacy. The primary features and luxury amenities of Flor de Cabrera are of the highest quality. Furnishings of custom mahogany, cherry and walnut complement the custom-designed interiors in every room. Outside, the chaise lounges and terrace seating are equal to the majesty of the villa and designed for immediate relaxation and enjoyment.

Dedicated, Highly Trained Staff – Our professionally trained staff is ready to craft and deliver an exceptional experience customized to satisfy your needs and highest expectations. We have on hand a resort concierge which has a dedicated bi-lingual concierge is available to help you with all your wishes, excursions and plans on or off the property grounds. Our Kitchen staff of Private Executive Chef and our Executive Chief will delight with amazing international and local dishes and will be pleased to bow to any requests or suggestions. We have a full time Butler / Host these young men and women are highly trained bi-lingual ‘butlers’ that are at your beck and call throughout the day and evening. The Maids / Laundress are on site all day for all your needs, and discretely move throughout the villa to keep everything in perfect order. As well we cannot forget our Gardeners / Maintenance We have dedicated gardeners who daily groom and trim the garden.

If you're looking for relaxation, the Spa Massage will melt away your stress. Let our masseuse unknot those muscles while the soothing aromatherapy works its magic. You can have the massage in the resort spa, in the privacy of your own suite or, as many do, on the terrace overlooking the ocean. Our masseuses are trained for various massage styles, facials, pedicures, manicures and soothing aromatherapy. It’s heaven!

Luxury Bedroom Suites Each one of our ten magnificent bedroom suites offers air conditioning and beautiful, captivating vistas Villa Flor de Cabrera even has two master suites, one within the main house and the other discretely separated, perched on the edge of the beach for the most romantic beachfront suite possible. Spacious, Elegant Dining Our international and local gourmet dishes and an impressive selection of fine wines are orchestrated by the executive chef. Private Luxury Swimming Pool – Designed in a free-form style with a luxurious Pebble-Tec finish, the private pool overlooks the sweeping golden sand beach and azure blue waters beyond. A large spa area nestled right under the terraces of the villa leads to a fantastic waterfall feature with a manicured tropical garden surrounding it.

Secluded Golden Sand Beach – Our villa is located directly on the secluded Orchid Bay beach, full of deep golden sand and with phenomenal swimming and playing areas. Over one mile of beach is right at your feet, just waiting for those sand castles or that beach volleyball game or moonlit dinner.

Manicured Tropical Gardens –There are plenty of beautiful tropical gardens to frame the stunning ocean and beach vistas. Stroll through the gardens to see the spectacular array of lush vegetation and a harmonious blend of flowers and tropical plants.

Flor de Cabrera is owned by a charitable family trust through the PONA Foundation. One hundred percent (100%) of the profits from Villa Flor de Cabrera is fully dedicated to education and community programs sponsored in the greater Cabrera area by the Trade for Travel program.

Every aspect of Villa Flor de Cabrera has been thoughtfully considered for a rich and memorable experience.
