Trinity Villa, Montego Bay, Jamaica Vacation Rental


Trinity's exquisitely appointed great room opens out to the top patio area where the lap pool is located. There is a formal dining room for elegant candlelit dinners, complete with a beautiful custom made table. For more casual dining options, Trinity has a terrace dining table, as well as a covered gazebo that over looks the dramatic waterfalls. All of the public space rooms have wall size sliding glass doors allowing the magnificent views of the turquoise sea to be enjoyed at all times. The doors can be recessed into the walls, allowing for the enjoyment of the warm sea breezes and tropical air inside. Gorgeous Italian tile can be found throughout the villa, from the cigar room to the media room.


Each of the 5 bedroom suites has its own lavish bathroom made from the finest Italian tiles, complete with double sinks and huge walk in showers with 3 shower heads. Each exquisite bedroom is situated to take advantage of its own fantastic view of the Caribbean ocean and the beautifully landscaped grounds. When opened, the Plantation shutters in both the bedroom and the bathroom allow the views in. Walk in closet, individual safes, air conditioners and flat screen TVs complete the palatial suites.
