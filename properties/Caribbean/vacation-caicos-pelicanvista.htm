Pelican Vista Villa, Grace Bay Beach, Providenciales, Turks & Caicos Vacation Rental


Your Caribbean dream vacation awaits you at Pelican Vista, one of the newest and most spectacular multi-million dollar homes to be developed in the exclusive residential area of Leeward, on the island of Providenciales. This magnificently appointed 4 bedroom, 4.5 bath main house and 2 bedroom, 2 bath guest house is the epitome of a Caribbean tropical paradise, with its birds-eye view of the famed Turks and Caicos shimmering turquoise-blue waters and white sand, combined with the magnificent year-round average temperature of 80 degrees. Pelican Vista is the perfect location to create vacation memories of a lifetime.

Main house has four identically-sized and luxuriously appointed master bedrooms with ensuite bath and private toilet, and also closets and a safe. Two king bed suites and two suites with extra-long twins that can be set up as kings on request.

The Guest House has a luxuriously appointed master bedroom with ensuite bath and private toilet and closet, safe, mini fridge as well as an exterior privacy shower and a patio. The second bedroom offers 2 sets of bunk beds which is suitable for 4 children only and includes flat screen TV with an ensuite bath/shower.

Find yourself in the perfect environment to create incredible Caribbean meals (cook your own bonefish catch!) using the gourmet kitchen is crafted from exotic zebra wood and includes wall to wall glass doors. Round it all out with a dishwasher, microwave, oven and warming tray, and the beautiful outdoor wet bar and grill at poolside. Even enjoy your food outside in the screened, pool-side gazebo. Make yourself at home with the included washer and dryer, wireless internet, security system and the televisions with DVD players in each room.

Take in your surroundings on the second story verandah and third story cupola/crow’s nest which offer breath-taking views of the turquoise waters from the comfort of the house. Just 150 paces from one of the world’s finest beaches, you will find yourself torn between the warm, alluring waters and the steamy Jacuzzi and private villa pool!
