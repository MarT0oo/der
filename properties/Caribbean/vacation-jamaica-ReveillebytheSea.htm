Reveille by the Sea, Montego Bay, Jamaica Vacation Rental

 

This updated three bedroom property, is an elegant, family- friendly villa that sits right on the water’s edge, with a unique private pool deck overlooking the water and beautiful views of Montego Bay in the distance.

A contemporary beach cottage with elegant interiors and its own secret stairwell leading down to water, Reveille by the Sea is a very special place for families, groups, or couples seeking a relaxed and romantic getaway.

You will find Reveille by the Sea on the plains of the Tryall property, adjacent to the Club’s private white sand beach and just a short stroll from the Tryall Club’s legendary Golf Course. A quick jog up the hill or 2 minutes’ by golf cart takes you the world class tennis courts, the fitness center, massage center and much more of the Club’s amenities.

The villa is contemporary in design with clean lines and hints of classical Jamaican architecture. Inside, the style is warm and tropical with serene wall colours that instill a feeling of calm. This villa may also be rented together with the lovely 4/5 bedroom villa, Retreat which is directly across the path, therebymaking a total of 6-8 bedrooms. The master bedroom has a queen bed and opens to a private sea facing patio. The second bedroom has two full beds and opens to a private patio overlooking the pool and ocean. The third bedroom also has 2 full beds and is a convenient distance from bedroom two,for families with small children.

This is a very comfortable child-friendly villa with deep sofas and large louver windows that can be thrown open to let in the sea breeze.

For a memorable evening, have dinner served on the pool deck, which sits right over the sea - an experience that mirrors dining on the deck of a ship. If you decide to skip golf, sit back and savour a Jamaican Rum Punch as you spend a lazy day on the long shady verandah watching the waves roll to the shore.

The welcoming staff comprise a cook, housekeeper/laundress and gardener
