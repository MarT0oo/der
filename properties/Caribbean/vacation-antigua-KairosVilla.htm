Kairos Villa, Jumby Bay, Antigua, Caribbean


The name Kairos, Greek for "idle time," is truly fitting for this 8,703 ft² private beach home.

The main house and guest quarters are separated by a courtyard filled with palm trees enclosed by green mosaic tiles. Here, guests enjoy a light and casual Caribbean ambiance with warm-coloured walls, hand-carved light fixtures and wicker chaise lounges draped in white fabric. Opening to a lush tropical landscape and ocean views, Kairos includes a living room, cozy den, upstairs deck with ocean and courtyard views, 46' infinity-edge pool, indoor dining room seating 12, outdoor dining area with ocean views, and a fully equipped kitchen complete with granite countertops and stainless-steel appliances including a microwave, wine cooler, beverage cooler, ice machine and two dishwashers.

With its interconnecting spaces and seven bedrooms, Kairos is ideal for groups and families. The two guest cottages each have three bedrooms: one with king bed; two with two twin beds. An additional bedroom located up the hill offers a full daybed. Every accommodation has a private bath/shower and cable TV/DVD player.
