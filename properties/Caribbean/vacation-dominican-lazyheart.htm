Villa Lazy Heart, Cabrera, Dominican Republic Vacation Rental

 

Villa Lazy Heart offers 10 beautifully appointed air-conditioned bedrooms with en-suite bathrooms spread out in separate, independent cottages that surround the house. Each cottage suite offers comfort and privacy. A spacious master suite is located in its own private cottage, a few steps from the main house. It features a deluxe king-sized bed, his-and-hers bathrooms and a dressing room. Mahogany doors open onto a covered wraparound terrace that offers stunning ocean views. All suites come with an amenities kit and the linens and towels are of the best quality. All accommodations are equipped with cable TV, wireless internet in selected areas, Satellite TV in the master and entertainment rooms, and walk-in closets.

 

The main building is comprised of the Great Room, Kitchen and Dining Rooms, and Entertainment Room. Lazy Heart's Great Room is an ideal place to sit down and play a game of chess, read a book from our book collection, or get together with family and friends. The kitchen is one of the busiest areas of the villa. Our chefs will prepare your meals using Italian, French, Dominican, Vegetarian, or any other influence you desire. You can be assured of high quality, high quantity, and mouth-watering delights. You need not lift a finger as our friendly wait staff will pamper you with five-star service. You and your family will enjoy quality time together in Lazy Heart's Entertainment Room. Enjoy a game of pool, watch DVDs, or choose from over 400 Satellite TV channels on our big-screen TV. 

 

It’s true, not all villas are created equal. Villa Lazy Heart sets itself apart from the others with its attention to your safety, well-being and enjoyment. With a fully trained staff of 13; including a masseuse and chef de cuisine, you will never have to make your bed, pick up your clothes or make a cup of coffee. Your every wish is our command, literally, and we commit to doing our utmost to satisfy your every whim.

 

Speaking of whims, here are just a handful of the wonderful features and amenities available at Villa Lazy Heart:

Full Maid Service, Full Chef Service w/professional kitchen, Daily Gardeners / Pool Cleaning, 24-Hour Security Guards (for peace of mind), Massage, Pedicure/Manicure, Hard Clay Tennis Court, Riding Stables w/5 Horses, 45’ x 60’ Private Heated Pool, Secluded White Sand Beach (semi-private), Beach Toys for Kids, Snorkeling Gear, Satellite TV w/VHS & DVD, Large Video Library (latest hits), Regulation Pool Table, Spotting Scope for birds, whales, etc., Deep, Shady Terraces for Relaxation, Hammocks, Discount Golf Packages, more…
