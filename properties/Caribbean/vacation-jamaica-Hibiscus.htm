Hibiscus, Montego Bay, Jamaica Vacation Rental

 

This beautiful split level one bedroom condominium, gives guests the pleasures of the tropics while maintaining the warmth of home.

Couples looking for a getaway will be totally enraptured by the incredible views from this suite, which offers privacy while still being just a short stroll away from the hub of Club amenities.

Hibiscus is nestled on the ridge of the hill at the heart of the property, with a fabulous view of the golf course and the Caribbean Sea and is adjacent to the Georgian Great House. A short stroll takes you to much of the Club’s amenities including tennis, fitness centre, infinity edge swimming pool, Kid’s Club, Massage centre, beauty salon and much more, or take 2 minutes by golf cart or shuttle to the beach and Golf Shop.

The design is modern Georgian, with a split level floor plan similar to a city condominium. The bathrooms and kitchen are fitted with modern amenities and the suite is air conditioned throughout. The foyer on the suite’s main floor leads to a spacious bedroom, simply and elegantly furnished with colourful tropical accents. There is a king sized bed and the cozy patio extending from the bedroom is a magical setting for daiquiris at sunset.

The en-suite bathroom has a walk-in shower and granite finishes. A short staircase, accented by a chic chandelier and colourful wooden sculptures leads to the dining area, kitchen, second bathroom and living room on the suite’s lower level. The living room opens to a patio so you can sunbathe privately, or dine al fresco while enjoying magnificent views. The suite has table settings for six, a pull-out sofa and bar-style kitchen counter. A full-service Housekeeper provides maid, laundry and breakfast service and a private Cook/Chef and/or butler services can be arranged for dinners. Movement around the property is free, just call for the property shuttle, or rent transportation carts for added independence.
