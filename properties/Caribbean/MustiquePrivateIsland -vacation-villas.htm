About St. Vincent & The Grenadines

 

These islands are located in the Lesser Antilles Chain in the Windward Islands, in the Atlantic Ocean, near St. Lucia and Barbados.  These Islands are a wonderful place to escape to and enjoy the remarkable history of the natives and the beautiful landscapes that move over densely populated forests and low lying mountains.  The areas like most islands in the Caribbean are best visited during the winter months, when the hurricane season is over.  These islands have endured over the years from having been ravished by storms, yet have rebounded beautifully.

Enjoy the lovely villas in this area, you can find upscale mansions on the beach or cottages, whichever you desire.  This is a great place for brining your friends and family, very safe and inviting.

How to Get There

Airport Information – The Largest Airport is http://airport-authority.com/SVD  E.T. Joshua Airport, near Kingston.  This will fly into St. Vincent.  However you can also fly into neighboring airports, such as Mustique Island Airport with Airport Code MQS, Canouan Island Airport at the heart of the Grenadines with airport code CIW, or you can fly into neighboring International Airports such as Grenada’s Airport and then catch a commuter flight to your Island Destination.  It is best to land in the larger airports to better get a handle on the transfer.

If you need more information about arrivals and transfers to these islands, please consult your travel agent.
