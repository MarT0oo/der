Villa Capri, Cape Estate, St. Lucia Vacation Rental


Perched on a hillside overlooking the azure waters of the Caribbean and framed by lush tropical gardens is this large private home away from home – Villa Capri St Lucia. St. Lucia is also known for hosting ABCs The Bachelor for the final rose ceremony!

Formerly an Embassy, accommodation in this beautiful villa is comfortable and well appointed. Villa Capri is offering nine-air-conditioned, sea view guest rooms, all with en-suite facilities and TV. You are guaranteed a friendly welcome and personal service throughout your stay.

Unwind on the sundeck, take a refreshing dip in the pool, enjoy a massage in our pyramid retreat, or stroll down to one of the two sandy beaches on our doorstep.

Cap Estate is the home to St. Lucia’s only 18-hole golf course. There are nature trails for hiking and horseback riding and the Gros Islet and Rodney Bay centers of tourist activities are only minutes away.

Villa Capri St Lucia is the largest private villa on this 850-acre residential estate, historically a sugar plantation at the northern tip of the island. In this secluded location you can enjoy peace and tranquility. This villa is perfect venue for special occasions like birthday and anniversary celebrations, weddings, family reunions, small business retreats, yoga retreat or scuba course, or just a group of friends vacationing together.

Are you getting married? Our coordinator can arrange everything for you worry-free.

The upper floor of Villa Capri St Lucia features four bedrooms (Calabash, Cashew, Tamarind, Nutmeg) the kitchen and buffet area, a TV corner with DVD and VCR set up, an extra powder room, the main dining room including a terrace overlooking the Caribbean Sea. One of the most outstanding features of Villa Capri is the newly added sunset deck. By extending the bar area it offers beautiful sunset views in the afternoon and is ideal for candlelight dinners and star gazing at night.

Our large dining room can accommodate a group of 20 people dining on one large table or, if preferred, the set up can be split up into three tables of 6-8 people. Also located in the dining area is a large flat screen TV and a stereo with iPod capacity.

The catering-size kitchen and buffet area of Villa Capri St Lucia is set up with microwave, toaster, coffeemaker, deep fat fryer, blender, food processor, six burner commercial stove with two ovens, salamander and grill plate, two large freezer/fridge combinations, chest freezer, a quadruple sink, and self-serve counter with coffee maker and small wine cooler.

The lower floor of the Villa contains five bedrooms, terrace, bar with large wooden open-air deck, library corner with stereo with iPod capacity, credit card phone and we have high-speed wireless internet access all throughout the house.

Surrounded by a wooden sun deck our pool invites you to laze away the day, read a book or enjoy a drink in the gazebo at the edge of the deck while taking in the beautiful views of Anse du Cap Bay.

The romantic hammock retreat, the large couple’s hanging basket, and wooden as well as stone benches all over the property make for many quiet enclaves to enjoy by yourself or with a loved one.

Very popular with many of our guests is the relaxation and treatment pyramid on the highest point of the property. Here, encircled by beautiful Caribbean plants, the massage therapist will relief you of tension and stress.

First floor
Calabash, our beautifully decorated bridal/master suite with four- poster bed, island furniture, small fridge, cable TV, A/C, dressing area and en-suite bathroom with separate shower and bathtub is located in the right wing of the top floor of Villa Capri.

Tamarind, next to room Calabash, with a four-poster double bed, en-suite bathroom, cable TV, A/C, features a hammock on its balcony overlooking the Caribbean Sea.

Nutmeg, located in the left wing of the upper floor, features a queen-sized bed, en-suite bathroom, cable TV, A/C, and a hammock on its balcony overlooking the Caribbean Sea.

Cashew, next to room Nutmeg, with a king-size bed, en-suite bathroom, cable TV, A/C also offers excellent views of the blue sea through any of its three windows.

Ground Floor
Mango, located to the far left, directly below room Cashew with two double beds, en-suite bathroom, cable TV, A/C is decorated in Caribbean colors.

Pawpaw, a room with one double bed, cable TV, A/C and en-suite bathroom, is situated between Mango and Guava.

Guava, featuring two double beds, en-suite bathroom, cable TV and A/C is identical to room Mango. Almond, our family room, has three double beds, en-suite bathroom, cable TV, A/C and can be used for various purposes including: business meetings, group sessions, boys or girls room for wedding parties, or just about anything else like a Super Bowl Party or Slumber Night.

Carambola, the room nearest to the library corner, with a connecting door to Almond, is ideal for families with small children and has a double bed, en-suite bathroom, cable TV and A/C.
