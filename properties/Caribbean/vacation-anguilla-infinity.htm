Infinity Anguilla, Anguilla, British West Indies Vacation Rental


Overlooking the Cove and only two minutes away from pristine Maundays Bay, is Anguilla's newest luxury villa enclave in chic West End - Sheriva. Situated on a lush 10-acre alcove with stunning views of the Caribbean Sea and neighboring St. Martin, the property offers ultimate privacy, yet swift access to the island's finest beaches, boutiques and restaurants. The villa names - INFINITY, HARMONY, and MYSTIQUE - eloquently reflect the aura of Sheriva. Nestled behind an artfully designed wall, this residence-style trio is the discerning traveler’s choice for luxury escapism.

Infinity boasts 14,000 square feet and offers 8 bedrooms, 8.5 baths, and 2 pools. Fine finishes of marble, granite and exotic hardwoods flourish throughout the oversized suites. The furnishings and interior design successfully blend distinctive Caribbean charm with contemporary sophistication. All bedrooms are air-conditioned with ceiling fans, screened doors and most have plasma TV's and DVD players.

The master bedroom, fittingly called the Grand Villa Suite, is an elegant retreat with double showers, a signature infinity-edge bathtub, generous dressing closet and a sitting nook with daybed for enjoying afternoon tea or simply relaxing before a sunset massage. In addition to the Grand Villa Suite, the main floor has a quaint room with two twin beds and a queen ensuite – all of which open onto the infinity pool deck. The Loft Suite sits atop the villa with a private balcony, kitchenette, and direct access to the rooftop terrace.

On the ground floor of Infinity, one finds a sizeable 2-bedroom pool suite and a secluded garden suite. Both have kitchenettes and comfortable sitting rooms. On this level, the fitness and entertainment room share a common space. Stay in shape with state-of-the-art exercise equipment. For entertainment, the 60” plasma TV, Bose music systems, myriad board games for kids or adults, and video games make our guests feel right at home.
