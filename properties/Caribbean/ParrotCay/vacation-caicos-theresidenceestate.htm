The Residence Main House, COMO Parrot Cay, Turks & Caicos Resort, Vacation Rental


Located on a 7.37 acre lush estate located directly on the beach.  The main house is close to 5,000 sq feet in size interior space and comprises of 5 bedrooms/ 5 ½ bathrooms, the master is complete with its own private steam room and has views towards the island’s wetlands and ocean beyond. Owned by one of our most valued guests,  The Residence is for people who seek extreme comfort and a private stretch of beach that offers peace and tranquility, the ocean is so close you can hear the waves lap against the sand.  The 60ft by 45ft infinity pool is stunningly dramatic.

Villa Facilities

Bedrooms:5 bedrooms, four-poster king in four bedrooms and two twins in one bedroom (which can be converted to a King),  with white netting, air conditioning, ceiling fans, a 63" satellite plasma television (master suite) and 55" satellite television (guest suites) with DVD players, an in-room safe, direct dial telephone with voicemail, radio, CD player/ipod docking station, umbrella.

Bathrooms: 5 ½ bathrooms, bathtubs, interior standalone and outdoor showers (steam shower in master), magnified make-up mirrors, hairdryer, bathrobes, flip-flops, COMO Shambhala bathroom amenities

Kitchen: toaster, espresso machine, oven, stovetop, microwave, dishwasher, 2 full size refrigerators

Living/Dining Area: two and three-person sofas and reading areas, dining table for 12, media room with plasma television with DVD player, direct dial telephone, Wi-Fi Internet access
