The Sanctuary is a unique beach property made up of three houses on the private island of Parrot Cay.
Nestled beside a swatch of powder-white sand, The Sanctuary has views of both the island’s lush vegetation
and turquoise sea. Inside and outside blur with large windows that bring the landscape in.

THE SANCTUARY MAIN HOUSE


The Sanctuary Main House is 8.947 sq feet in size, with three bedrooms, each complete with their own en-suite bathroom which include a separate bathtub, shower, his/her sinks, plus outdoor shower and Jacuzzi.
