Villa Amber, Rio San Juan, Dominican Republic Vacation Rental

 

If you are looking to get together a large group for a vacation that is truly luxurious, you will not be disappointed by your stay at Villa Amber.

This six-bedroom estate is perfect for getting together the entire family for a memorable vacation. Located on its own peninsula with 270 degrees of ocean views including coves, beaches, and mountains, you will be surrounded by beauty for your entire stay. The design is sleek and stylish with a pleasant balance of contemporary finishes and Dominican influence.

The facility is fully staffed and serviced so that you will always be taken care of. You will have a private chef and 24-hour butler service so that you are living at the height of luxury. Take a dip in the pool or enjoy the water slide when you feel like making the most of a beautiful day. You can also get in a tennis match, go work out at the gym, or treat yourself to some time at the spa. However you want to enjoy your vacation, you will be able to do it.

Ani West – Amber is located in a more laid back, rural part of the Dominican Republic so your family can enjoy a relaxing vacation. It is only minutes away from beautiful beaches and a Trent Jones cliff top golf course reminiscent of Pebble Beach, so you can still get out and enjoy yourself when you feel like exploring the area.

While visiting, you will have access to all day snacks, cocktail service, and the mini bar. You will be aided by a full staff, which includes the general manager, a private chef, butlers, spa therapists, security, and a children’s activity coordinator. Whenever you need assistance, just say the word and somebody will be there to make sure your vacation is the best that it can possibly be.
