Why choose Kamalame?

Magical and mesmerising, Kamalame Cay occupies the Bahamas’ largest and least developed island, a lush and breezy landscape dotted by gumbo limbo trees, bougainvillaea and West Indian-style bungalows, cottages and villas.

Gracefully designed with an emphasis on recreation, sport fishing and utter relaxation, this exquisite retreat features the Bahamas’ only overwater spa, tennis courts, indoor-outdoor dining, freshwater pool and more – all accessible to just 60 guests, who lounge in isolated beachside bliss, sauntering over to the Great House to mingle or ducking over to the Nassau for shopping, then racing back to the reconnect with Kamalame Cay’s own brand of barefoot chic.
