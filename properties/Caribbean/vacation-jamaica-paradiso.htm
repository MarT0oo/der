Villa Paradiso, Mammee Bay Estate, Ocho Rios, Jamaica Vacation Rental


Villa Paradiso is situated on a spectacular white sand beach in Mammee Bay Estates. Surrounded by 1.75 acres of lush tropical gardens and lawns the villa comprises a large main house with 5 bedrooms and a separate 2 bedroom cottage.

Enter the main house through a spacious entrance foyer which leads directly into an elegantly furnished, 2-storey, 45ft. vaulted ceiling living room. The room opens to ocean views through French doors to the covered verandah. Off the living room is a private indoor formal dining room. The first floor also has 2 large bedrooms, 1 opening to the pool.

The covered first floor verandah fronts onto the pool terrace and overlooks the sea and beach. Comfortable seating and a separate dining area create a relaxing atmosphere for outdoor activities.

The second floor has 3 bedrooms, 2 with private seaside balconies. This floor also has a large family room, overlooking the main floor, equipped with TV, computer with high speed internet access and a small library.

The separate air conditioned gym comes complete with table tennis, weights, treadmill, stationary bike and excise ball to help work off the extra pounds from Leila's cooking.

The 2-bedroom cottage is a short walk (20 yards) from the main house. Each bedroom shares the bath and comfortable living room. There is a small kitchen in the cottage for Night time refreshments or early morning coffee.

The eight bedroom is situated adjacent to the pool terrace and is a King bedroom with ensuite bathroom and private patio.
