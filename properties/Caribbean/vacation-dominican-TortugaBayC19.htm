Tortuga Bay C19, Punta Cana, Dominican Republic Vacation Rental

 

Whether you are looking to have some deserved vacations with your family or you want spend some quality time with your friends, this dapper 8 bedroom luxury villa in the exclusive Tortuga Bay neighborhood is the right place for you!

With a broad outlook to one of the most rewarded golf courses in the Caribbean, this property has a large lawn with a swimming pool next to an exquisite palapa with a hay roof where you can hang out with your beloved ones while enjoying the incredible weather.

This villa is inside the gated village Punta Cana Resort and close to any place you need to go. It has a fully equipped kitchen with all necessary cooking utensils and a sober decorated living room with a unique style to charge your good vibes.

On the upper floor, you have 3 bedrooms, one is the master bedroom with a king size bed and the other two with a queen-size bed each. All three bedrooms have their own ensuite bathroom and flat TV. Going downstairs you will find the other bedrooms. Two of them have a king-size bed each, another one has two queen size beds. Those bedrooms have their own ensuite bathroom and flat TV. The seventh bedroom has 2 twin beds and the eighth bedroom has a queen bed. Both have an ensuite bathroom each.

This incredible property is next to the Tortuga Bay Beach, which is only a 5 minute walk or 1 minute golf cart drive away! Please remark that the beach doesn't offer any kind of services or beach chairs.
