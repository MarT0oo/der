Beach House, Meads Bay, Anguilla, Caribbean Vacation Rental

 

Winner of the 2012-2013 International Property Awards for the Best International Architecture – Single Family for 2012-2013, Beach House on Meads Bay in Anguilla is a stunning 17,000 ft² contemporary luxury masterpiece.

Ian “Sugar George” Edwards, Architectural Designer and Property Developer of Sunset Homes Anguilla accepted the exclusive global award that marks the pinnacle of excellence in the fields of architecture and property development worldwide at an Awards Ceremony in London on December 7, 2012.

Designed to capture the natural Caribbean beachfront beauty of Anguilla, the stunning residence sits on the powdery white sands of the island’s west end with an exclusive oceanfront location adorned with spectacular sunsets and ocean views.

In the Caribbean, architecture styles vary greatly from island to island and the beautiful island of Anguilla features some of the most interesting architecture found anywhere in the world. Now the addition of the stunning new Beach House takes Anguilla contemporary architecture to a whole new level.

“It is a contemporary design like you will see in the video. We try to focus on the geometric shapes, nice straight clean lines, just simplicity at its best,” said Designer/Builder Ian “Sugar George” Edwards. Designed and built by renowned Anguilla architectural designer Ian “Sugar George” Edwards, Beach House at Meads Bay features over 16,000 sq.ft. of simple elegance with amazing amenities, a stunning infinity pool, the latest in electronics technology, a home gym, a studio, movie theatre, and unique water features spread throughout the property.

“It’s one of the most beautiful beaches in the Caribbean right on Meads Bay. So what I tried to do when we were doing the design is incorporate a lot of the water feel into this property. You will realize that many of the places that you walk around in this property you will see either a fountain or a waterfall. So wherever you are on this property you can either see water or hear water.”

“I went all out in terms of the home automation on this house wherever you are in the world you can actually view your house. We have about twelve cameras throughout the entire property, you can control music, lighting, everything from just the touch panel,” said Edwards.

The property also features a palm tree lined drive, tennis court, commercial laundry room, two separate guest houses, huge bathrooms in every suit featuring specular outdoor showers and views, and gracious living and entertaining areas throughout.

“When you come in you feel like you are actually on the ocean even before you step foot on the sand so when you do step foot on the sand it like another game in itself,” said Edwards. Situated in the center of the beautiful mile long stretch of Meads Bay Beach, the property features the privacy of a single family home, yet sits between two of the world’s finest resorts in either direction. “Viceroy Jaqala on one end and Malliouhana to the next. The other resorts you’re on a resort, this one is your own private home. You have your own tennis court and you own everything,” said Edwards.

With its stunning contemporary architecture and modern features, The Beach House is another prime example of why the island of Anguilla enjoys the reputation as the “Hamptons of the Caribbean.” “We are very proud of this property. At first I saw it in my mind, but many people probably did not see the vision, but now that it’s done I feel really, really fortunate, proud and blessed to be part of something like this in Anguilla,” added Edwards.
