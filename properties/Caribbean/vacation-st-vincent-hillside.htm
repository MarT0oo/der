Belmont Hillside Luxury
3 Bedrooms Ensuite | 3 Baths | Pool | Spa | Grill                                       

Bequia

 From $3,200 per Week

Belmont Hillside Luxury provides guests with the ultimate Caribbean escape.  The villa is situated on the hillside in Belmont, giving elevated views of Admiralty Bay and is only a few minute’s drive from the shops, restaurants and local beaches. As well as a private infinity pool the villa also has its own Air conditioned gymnasium and spa treatment area. If you are looking to explore the other islands then a trip on our crewed 63ft sailing yacht can be arranged for your private use. The villa also offers windsurfing, kayaking and snorkeling equipment for your use at the beach and tuition is available. Evenings can be spent sipping cocktails around the pool or watching a movie under the stars on our outdoor projector screen.

The Villa can accommodate between two and six people.

The main living area has an open plan design with the kitchen, dining room and living room flowing into one elegant space. From the lounge and dining area there is direct access to the main veranda, terrace and pool area through four sets of French doors making the whole area beautifully light and open with stunning views of the whole bay. The kitchen is fully equipped with all modern appliances, including American style fridge/freezer with ice-maker, dishwasher, microwave, and Smeg hob and oven.

Three double bedrooms, all with private en-suite shower rooms and French doors leading directly onto the terrace and pool area. Each bedroom is comfortable and tastefully appointed.

The outside area consists of the veranda, terrace, tropical garden, infinity edged pool, spa, barbeque and dining area. There is plenty of space for relaxing whether you choose a hammock, day bed or a sun-lounger by the pool. Or if you are feeling energetic spend some time in the air-conditioned private gymnasium.

The staff is always on-site to assist you with any questions.
