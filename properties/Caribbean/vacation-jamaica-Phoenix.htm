Phoenix, Tryall,Sandy Bay, Hanover, Jamaica Vacation Rental

 

Built in the traditional Caribbean style, Phoenix features a fabulous extremely private location on the sea with two private sand beaches and extensive seafront verandas that take full advantage of the splendid ocean views and ever present trade winds.

Situated on Country Club Drive East, Phoenix is a brief one minute walk to the First Tee, Driving Range and Club Beach. Constructed on one level, the villa is welcoming to all ages. Grab bars and shower benches in the bathrooms provide an extra measure of comfort. All areas of the villa are accessible under protection from rain or sun. The ultimate ‘home away from home’, Phoenix has been lovingly maintained to the highest standard. Four seafront bedrooms feature King Beds and access to the veranda and beach through French Doors. Two of the remaining bedrooms have proximate sea views. These bedrooms can be made up either as King or Twin. All bedrooms, oversized, have flat screen televisions and ensuite bathrooms with translucent custom shower enclosures. A seventh bedroom with garden is also available upon request. SONOS is available throughout the villa.

The East Wing Living Pavilion opens on all sides with French Doors providing access on three sides to an extensive wrap around veranda and a stunning private beach, bathed in the shade of a casuarina. Columned arches on the fourth side open to the circular Swimming Pool.

The West Wing Living Room is open on two sides with French Doors providing access to another extensive veranda and the second private beach, sunny all day. Dining at Phoenix is a movable feast. Choose candlelit formality under the crystal dining room chandelier or seaside informality on the veranda with Montego Bay aglow in the distance. Furnishings throughout are elegantly simple in wicker, glass and wrought iron, a perfect complement to the beachfront location.

During the summer it is possible to rent the 4 bedroom east wing and upon request during the winter.

Staffing consists, at a minimum, of Chef, Butler, Housekeeper/Laundress (2) and Gardener. Additional staff, as needed.
