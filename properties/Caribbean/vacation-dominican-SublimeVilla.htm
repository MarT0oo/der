Sublime Villa, Casa de Campo, Dominican Republic Vacation Rental

 

Sublime Villa is a spectacular villa in Casa de Campo luxury community, along the southern coast of the Dominican Republic. Nestled amid lush gardens and trees, the villa enjoys sublime views from its expansive pool terrace and palapa, while its straight lines and elegant interiors provide an enchanting setting for relaxation and entertainment. Four fabulous bedrooms provide sumptuous privacy for EIGHT guests, plus a sofa bed in the TV room where 2 additional people can sleep for a total of ten, making this a perfect vacation rental for one or two families or a large group of friends seeking a unique yet resort-like experience of this island paradise.

The immaculate white governs over the mesmerizing interior evoking the consummate elegance of the Caribbean pattern with high ceilings of rich wood, gorgeous marble floors and two unique chandeliers enhancing the glass dining table for 14 commensals.

The ample terrace of polished stone surrounds a gorgeous swimming pool and a jacuzzi, its surface meshing gracefully with the sublime panorama of evergreen and sky. Here, enjoy enchanting days in utter seclusion, sunbathing on loungers, sipping cool drinks in the palapa, and refreshing body and mind in the hot tub. Relish an alfresco lunch and linger outdoors at sunset and at night, savoring cocktails under the moonlight kissed by the dreamy breeze.

The worldly renowned Minitas Beach is within walking distance, less than 1/4 mile from the villa.

The villa features one king bedroom with walk-in closet, three rooms with two full-size beds. All feature full bathrooms, TVs, and ceiling fans. Three bedrooms are on the second floor and only one with two full beds is on the ground floor. For extra bedding, there is a Sofa bed in the Media Room. Décor, style and superb bedding evoke the finest Dominican tradition, casting an autochthon ambiance for an unforgettable stay.

This property has a Hot Tub with independent pump and heater.
