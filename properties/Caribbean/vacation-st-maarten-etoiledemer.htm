Etoile de Mer, St. Maarten Vacation Rental


The aptly named Etoile de Mer or in translation “Ocean Star”, is a dazzling 4 bedroom villa, located directly on the water, at the far end of Cupecoy Beach in Dutch St. Maarten.

Like an abstract work in blue, the ocean and sky provide a stunning backdrop to the gleaming white, columned pool terrace and later in the day the glorious Caribbean sunsets set the palette alight.

The covered terrace provides plenty of shade for cool al-fresco dining and poolside lounging and an upper terrace provides further outdoor lounging. The terrace area blends effortlessly into the high-ceilinged and light-filled interior spaces comprising a large, stylish living room and gourmet kitchen space, both enjoying the stunning ocean view.

The four beautiful king sized bedrooms offer luxurious en-suite bathrooms, high-end electronics and ocean views. One bedroom is located on the main floor and a curving staircase ascends to the second floor and the three remaining bedrooms.

Etoile de Mer is located within the gated enclave of Shorepointe and just a minute’s walk from the marina of Porto Cupecoy with its gourmet restaurants and boutiques.
