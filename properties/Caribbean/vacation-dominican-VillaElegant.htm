Villa Elegant, Punta Cana, Dominican Republic Vacation Rental

 

All our lives are marked by our childhood memories and remembering them make us see the bright side of our days as adults. Combining these memoirs with a relaxed mindset is the best formula to experience the best holidays you could ever have!

Thus, this radiant 5 Bedroom villa perched inside the elegant and chic Punta Cana Resort has all the condiments to spend an unforgettable holiday and has a slide in the pool to let those memories as a kid to come back!

As you enter the villa through its outstanding foyer you will immediately feel the balance between a minimalistic style with smooth colors, the open indoor spaces grant a great visual of each part of the house, including the second floor. To the right is the large living room with a gentle touch governed by squares, picturesque lamps and big windows with hardwood frames, inviting all members of a family to lay back and enjoy the evergreen garden views. On the other wing, the fully equipped kitchen is ready to fulfill all your whims with an isle in the middle to prepare the most delicious meals with your kids.

Savvily executed, this property is the ideal place for a family because you will find everything for your kids, especially for little girls: a room full of toys, a tent, a small car and even strollers to take their dolls for a promenade and more!

The benevolent weather in Punta Cana is always shiny, hence plunge into the swimming pool while enjoying a golf view of the magnificent La Cana golf course. And remember to hop on the slide to feel reborn again or enjoy a drink under the fancy palapa.

When it´s for everybody´s rest at night there are two bedrooms on the first floor, one with a king bed and the other with a queen bed. Both have TV, full bathroom with shower and walk-in closet. Taking the stairs to the second floor you will stumble upon the remaining three bedrooms. One has a king bed, the second two full beds and the third one a queen bed. All have TV, a full bathroom with shower and walk-in closet.

This villa state is located in the exclusive gated community of Punta Cana Resort & Club, a 26-square mile paradise that is blessed with a temperate year-round climate, light rainfall, and soft ocean breezes. It's colorful coral reefs, calm crystalline waters and white-sand beaches are breathtaking. The Resort provides a matchless experience for all who visit this tropical paradise, and its tropically chic lifestyle is ideal for those who appreciate high standards of quality, comfort and aesthetics. As guests of the villa, you have access to all of the resort amenities and activities.
