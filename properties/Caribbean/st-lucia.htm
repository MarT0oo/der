Visiting St. Lucia

When you think of St. Lucia, you should think of spending time with your family and relaxing.  This is probably one of the most amazing islands for small children and their families to enjoy serene time with one another.  Parents often love to take their kids to the Marina Islands Nature Reserve where they can learn about different wildlife and discover the little explorer within.  The Pigeon Island Museum and Interpretive Centre offers you a chance to explore history with  your children while learning something yourself about this amazing island.

For the adults we  have activities from Whale watching to snorkelling and sailing.  As well St. Lucia offers some great nightlife, such as: Pulse Nightclub, Upper Level Nightclub, Mangos Bar, and Bliss just to name a few.  You also have Restaurants such as Charthouse, The Lime, Memories of Hong Kong, and various other restaurants that you can find on stlucia.org. 

No matter why you come to St. Lucia be sure to visit our beaches, Reduit Beach, Jalousie Beach, Anse Chastanet, Pigeon Island National Park, Grande Anse.  Our home rentals can be found on the beach or on the cliffs within walking distance, always check back with us for more information on new properties to be added.
