South Seas Villa, Chalk Sound, Providenciales, Turks and Caicos Vacation Rental

 

Panoramic views of the Caribbean Ocean provide the best sunrise and sunset viewing anywhere on Providenciales. Guests experience a level of comfort and privacy that is rare with any Caribbean villa. The South Seas Villa property spreads across four and a half acres of tropical landscaping and palm trees, with lush rock gardens leading down to the waterfront.

South Seas Villa is a private Caribbean hideaway directly on the water in the exclusive neighborhood of Silly Creek Estates. This air-conditioned four bedroom, four and half bath Turks and Caicos property includes the two bedroom main Villa and a two bedroom Guest House. The property can also be rented as a 5 bedroom villa which includes the lovely Carriage House.

The fully screened Gazebo is found at the end of a winding path that also leads down to the ocean shore, perfect for scuba diving. This house also includes bicycles and a kayak for two for afternoons of exploring the island of Provo.

The private swimming pool is a sculptured work of art, winding around the curved exterior of the large Villa Sun Room. Enjoy a relaxing dip after a day of Turks and Caicos shopping, fishing, and beaches. Enjoy cocktails on the terrace, the very private Gazebo, or the screened in Sunroom with its amazing views of Caribbean sunsets and turquoise seas. Tennis privileges are included and are nearby.

The architecture of this Turks and Caicos luxury villa is truly exceptional, with unique curves and lines, nooks and niches, terraces and balconies. From the road, South Seas Villa is secluded, with entry via a winding walkway concealed artfully with foliage from casual strollers on Silly Creek Lane. The view of the house from the shoreline is breathtaking, showcasing the great talent of the architect to its best advantage.

South Seas Villa features a master king bedroom suite and second king bedroom, both with attached bathrooms. The master bedroom houses a luxurious king-size bed and rich fabrics. The second bedroom also has a king bed and is decorated with cheerful country blues and yellows.
