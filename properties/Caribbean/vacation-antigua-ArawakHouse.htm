Arawak House, Emerald Cove, Antigua, Caribbean Vacation Rental

 

Arawak House is located on the gently sloping hillside of Emerald Cove, a very dramatic, luxurious home with special furnishings, in a gated residence. The house has a panoramic ocean view of the East Coast and Nonsuch Bay. There are two levels, the entry-level is by way of a wooden deck that surrounds the house, which leads onto several patios and a dining Terrace. The living room and second dining area is combined in a charming space with a small balcony.

There is a bedroom en suite, with a private patio. The lower level has two bedrooms en suite each with private entrances and sun decks and beautiful views of the azure water. There is al fresco dining on the patio just off the very modern and well-appointed kitchen which leads to the lap pool and pool deck. Just a short walk takes you through the garden gates to your own private beach.
