Villa Las Hamacas, Punta Cana, Dominican Republic Vacation Rental

 

This exceptional and exquisite beachfront luxury villa is situated amid paradise. It has also been featured in important international magazines such as Elle Decor and Architectural Digest of Germany.

A fully equipped property with an eclectic decor and style and fine furniture ready to fulfill any whim of its guests. The villa reflects the Caribbean spirit compared to a 5-stars hotel. The Master bedroom has an enormous king-sized bed, a spacious bathroom and an escritoire which outlooks the pristine waters. Then, other 2 bedrooms have 1 king-sized bed each and their private bathrooms. A forth bedroom has another big king-sized bed, the 5th bedroom has 2 charming double beds and the last bedroom, ideal for toddlers, has 2 full sized beds. They all have en suite and equally equipped with their own flat screen TV, cable, DVD, their own private bathroom, toilet, shower and walk-in closets.

The villa’s common spaces are an abundant exhibition of superb taste and details. Hammocks are everywhere (thus the villa’s name) and the infinity pool is ready to receive the splashy guests.
