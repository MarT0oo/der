Following Seas Villa, Montego Bay, Jamaica Vacation Rental


Following Seas is a one-of-a-kind vacation hideaway offering the ultimate in luxury, comfort and breathtaking décor. Following Seas' casual elegance seamlessly balances original artwork with classic Jamaican architecture, inviting the outside in through large windows and open air verandas. There is a sense of graceful harmony in this private home balanced easily between delightful tropical gardens and glistening sea.

Perched atop one of the highest points of the historic Tryall Club on Jamaica's northern coast, Following Seas' seven extensively landscaped and manicured acres provide visitors 180 degree panoramic views of the crystal clear Caribbean, as well as lush mountain views of the western Jamaican landscape.

Welcomed by majestic island palms, guests enter Following Seas through a dramatic spiral staircase leading to the centerpiece of the home - an expansive, 900 ft² living and entertaining space which opens onto an expansive covered terrace with dining area, all of which overlook the sparkling pool and glistening ocean. In addition, a charming dining gazebo built around natural coral rock is an elegant setting for up to 35 guests. A full commercial kitchen, swim-up bar, spa and large infinity outdoor pool and deck area combine to make Following Seas the perfect destination for parties large and small.

Breathtaking tropical designs with bright cheerful accents compliment this home's island feel. Custom cabinetry graces every room, and there are flat screen TV's in every bedroom and living area. Other conveniences include air conditioning, satellite radio, intercom system, high-speed wireless internet in every room, and Netspeak phone lines allowing free calling to the US, Canada and most of Europe.

The Main House offers four luxurious bedroom suites. The master suite features a hand-carved four poster king bed and two private 'his' and 'her' bathrooms and dressing areas. The remaining suites feature one king bed, two full beds and two twins, respectively. All bedrooms have private double bathrooms adorned with the elegance of marble and granite.

And for added privacy, the Guest Cottage, just off the Main House, features 2 bedrooms (1 king; the other with 2 full beds), living room, kitchen with breakfast area, and a veranda with seating and dining area. All rooms enjoy full ocean views. The Guest Cottage was extensively renovated as well as redecorated over the summer of 2014. The Guest Cottage now boasts its own private splash pool and the remodeled Master Suite now has a secluded outdoor shower.

A completely new Media Cottage, located between the Main House and Guest Cottage, was also constructed in 2014. The Media Cottage features ample comfortable seating and a huge flat screen television for family and guests to enjoy sports or movies together. For added convenience, Following Seas is pleased to provide its guests with the complimentary use of four golf carts.
