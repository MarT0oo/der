Villa Moderne, Las Olas, Cabarete, Dominican Republic Vacation Rental

 

This spectacular villa is appx 890 sq. meters. 6 bedrooms, two masters….one on the first floor and one on the second floor. Each Master has dual showers, featuring shower jet systems. All bedrooms feature en suite bathrooms. One guest rooms has two showers, one inside and one outside! One of the six bedrooms could be utilized as a TV / office room.

Features such as two 1/2 baths, one off of the living room and the other off of the dining room show how well thought out this villa has been designed. Top quality fixtures and lighting throughout! Very large living room, dining room seating 10, gourmet kitchen, and casual dining area off of the kitchen. Superb woodwork, designer furnished! Top quality ceramics and stone features throughout, as well as Persian carpets! Two level pool with cascading waterfall, separate children’s area.

The kiosko with its “espejo de agua” or reflection pond, and large outside decks are great for entertaining. The second level features an incredible roof garden with lots of space to entertain or just enjoy the cool evening breezes. You can view the sea and the mountains all from that roof! Totally turn key, just bring your toothbrush…..
