Round House Villa, Montego Bay, Jamaica Vacation Rental


Delightfully whimsical with wonderful ocean and golf course views, Round House offers classical Jamaican and American style with plenty of space and exuberant gardens.

Located on a brow of Copperwood Ridge, Round House is an easy walk to Tryall’s world class tennis courts, its gym and other amenities, and a 3-minute ride by the villa’s complimentary golf cart to the championship Tryall Golf course and private beach.

Designed on two levels, the house is built in a quintessential Caribbean style with wide verandas and lofty cathedral ceilings. Interior spaces are warm and inviting with furnishings that combine rich Jamaican heritage with classic American charm. There are eight bedrooms in total at Round House; some command ocean views, others enjoy colourful garden vistas. There are six king-bedded rooms (including the master) and two twin-bedded rooms which can be converted into king beds. Three of the bedrooms on the lower pool level interconnect with each other and come with a small private sitting room with flat screen television. Each bedroom has a private en suite travertine marble bathroom..

The main level’s spacious living room and bar open to a wide terrace with magnificent views of the sea. Here on the veranda are plenty of chairs in which to relax including delightful rocking chairs. To the left is a fabulous circular dining gazebo which unsurprisingly given the magnificent views is the favoured dining location.

The lower terrace level has a pretty heated swimming pool with plenty of sunbathing areas as well as a further gazebo for shady relaxation.

Round House comes with a staff of six - a Butler, Cook, Housekeeper, Laundress and two Gardeners.
