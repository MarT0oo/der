Casuarina, Montego Bay, Jamaica Vacation Rental


This lovely Jamaican villa is situated in the famous Tryall Club estate. Nestled among some of the finest homes found in Jamaica.

Casuarina has been newly refurbished and this 4 bedroom villa is a new addition to Jamaican Treasures Villa collection. All guests at Casuarina have a complimentary membership to the Tryall facilities. The beach is only a 2 minute golf ride away.

Casuarina is a remarkably welcoming four-bedroom villa which offers the most magical views of the Caribbean Sea from its prestigious vantage point on Barnes Hill. The entrance foyer is a celebration of exotic ferns with pleasing tropical flowers brought in fresh from the gardens in anticipation of your arrival.

From all indications, the stage has been set to welcome you in true Jamaican hospitality: warm smiles, gracious service, an inviting circular pool and a gorgeous living room mark your first moments of initiation into this private paradise.

For friends and families alike, Casuarina’s living room is the place to be re-invigorated. The lively, colourful décor sets the mood for days of endless relaxation. To sit in this room is to feel refreshed while reclaiming all your senses in enjoyment of the natural beauty of the setting.

There is a flat screen television available with surround sound, as well as DVD and CD players. The villa is equipped with wireless high-speed Internet access. Children will relish the adventure of sunny gardens bordered from below by tall, arching palm trees, banana trees and of course, Casuarina trees.

Casuarina has four bedrooms which are all equipped with televisions and air conditioning. There is one master bedroom with a king-sized bed and three bedrooms with twin beds each. All bedrooms have en suite bathrooms.

Dining options are determined by your whim – whether for an elegant evening or a light meal served al fresco by the poolside. Casuarina is pleased to provide its guests with the use of two complimentary golf carts. Your private, welcoming villa staff is comprised of a superb team of cook, butler, housekeeper, laundress and gardener.
