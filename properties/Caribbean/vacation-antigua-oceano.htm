Oceano Harbour Villa, Jolly Harbour, Antigua


This spacious beach front villa is situated in a quiet position on a beautiful white sand beach just a few steps away from the calm turquoise Caribbean Sea, lapping gently along the shore. At the front of the property is 90' of bulkhead and your private boat dock on the deep water of Jolly Harbour. There is a 20,000 pound rated boat lift and two 1,000 pound rated Jet-Ski lifts..

The property is located on the coveted South Finger in the gated community of Jolly Harbor which also boasts an 18 hole golf course, 4 tennis courts, a squash court, an Olympic size swimming pool, supermarket, bank, several bars and restaurants and much more... all within the gates, and accessible by golf cart

Newly built, great care has been taken to achieve perfection, from the very tasteful mix of contemporary and romantic designs, the very high standard of furnishings to the spacious and generous layout giving maximum privacy to all of the four air conditioned bedrooms with en-suite bathrooms

As you enter through the spacious elegant entrance into the foyer, you will enjoy unobstructed views of the ocean through the custom designed pocket doors that retract into the walls creating a 20 foot expanse connecting the outside gallery and the inside living room as one open space.

Gourmet Kitchen The gourmet kitchen has everything you could possibly wish for and just that little bit more. From ice makers, to the wine cooler and enough ovens to have a small banquet. There is also a large amount of storage space for groceries

In the lounge there is a 63in flat panel TV with full surround sound and Cable TV, along with a SONOS stereo system which can also play ITunes/IPods as well as stream live music over the high speed Internet. From this living area there is a corridor leading to two further guest rooms, both with stylish en-suite bathrooms and private terraces.

Master Bedroom The master bedroom and bathroom is located at the front of the house overlooking the pool and ocean. The master bedroom is hexagonal in shape with views all around. The bed has its own TV coming up from the bed frame for your viewing pleasure and then down again when you have finished. The en-suite bathroom is absolutely exquisite with two showers, lots of space and a wonderfully relaxing bath tub

The living room and kitchen patio doors open up completely so that you have continual trade wind breezes and uninterrupted sea views. It's pure heaven. The covered veranda has a dining table for 8 people and a smaller table for 4. There is also a large day bed with comfortable cushions situated under one of the 12 ceiling fans throughout the home for relaxing into after a hard days sight-seeing.

Swimming Pool and spa There's an invigorating Jacuzzi which has a waterfall into the fresh water swimming pool, and four bar stools adjacent to the Bar-b-que / rotisserie and outdoor kitchen area for cold cocktails in the pool. Upstairs you will find a spacious sun terrace for sunbathing in private, or for casual drinks at sunset

The BBQ is fully equipped with sink, ice cooler and fridge plus lots of storage and preparation room. So catch your own fish from the dock in the front of the house, or out on your boat, and come out back to grill it up that night for the freshest dining experience you can imagine.

Outdoor dining Just steps away from the pool is the soft white sand of the beach and the turquoise waters, making it irresistible, a must for a daily swim. There are two moorings at the shoreline to easily latch the Jet-Skis when you break for lunch.

Oceano Harbour Villa is located just 5 minutes from Castaways restaurant which serves local foods, as well as Sushi on Thursday night
