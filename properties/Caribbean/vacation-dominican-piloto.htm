Casa Piloto
6 Bedrooms | 7 Baths | Pool | Beachfront
Confesi, Puerto Plata, Dominican Republic

Casa Piloto Villa is a beautiful ocean side clandestine complex just right for family gatherings or large groups. The villa is marvelously crafted with interconnecting gardens and privately gated beach access. Large palm trees grace the lawn and encircle the beautiful swimming pool adorned by large stone walkways, market umbrellas and comfortable verandah furnishings. Two grand staircases lead from the house to this amazing swimming pool. The stone walkway continues from the swimming through the manicured lawn and shady palm trees to the beautiful white sands and sapphire blue waters of Cofresi Beach. Casa Piloto Villa was a dream villa built by the original developer of the Hacienda Resort complex. Casa Piloto Villa was once the premier gathering place of celebrities from around the world.

This Dominican Republic Villa presents guests with large spacious rooms finely decorated for comfort and leisure. The main living room offers overstuffed cushions only enhancing this tranquil setting. Guests of Casa Piloto Villa will enjoy spacious bedroom suites in vibrant island colors with grand bedding and well appointed private terraces. Each bedroom has lovely appointed en suites rendering gorgeous ocean and garden views. All rooms have large ceilings. The large kitchen is fully equipped with the latest amenities. Guests will indulge in outdoor dining on the sweeping verandah adorned with stylish furnishings while delighting in the sound of waves breaking upon the beach, a treat among Dominican villas.

Casa Piloto Villa is a perfect choice when deciding to rent Caribbean villas. Presenting its guests with beautiful Dominican architecture and lovely furnishings amongst the serene beauty of its landscape. Casa Pilot Villa is perfect for families vacationing together or small groups. Water activities are offered including, snorkeling, swimming, fishing, wind surfing, kayaking the list is endless. Fine restaurants, fabulous shopping and exciting night life are a moment away from Casa Piloto Villa. If golf and tennis are calling, a wide selection of facilities is close by for enjoying. Please join us to experience the warmth, hospitality and comfortable accommodations guests will come to enjoy at Casa Piloto Villa, Dominican Republic.
