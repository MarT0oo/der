Inifnity Villa, Montego Bay, Jamaica Vacation Rental


Among the newest Premier villas at the renowned Tryall Club, Infinity is a halcyon haven overlooking the Caribbean Sea. Its rooms are large and tastefully furnished -- some traditional, some eclectic -- with a designer’s eye to comfort throughout. Named for its seemingly endless private pool, Infinity is a vacationer’s paradise for golf and tennis enthusiasts, spa weeks, family reunions or merely relaxing into the good life for a week or more. While extraordinarily private and placid, the villa is located in the tiny community of the Tryall Club, named by Condé Nast on its Gold List of “the best places to stay in the whole world.” Belying its posh location, life at Infinity is in an environ of barefoot living between pool and hot tub, and easy excursions by golf cart between beach and mountain for sea and sun.

In this casual ambience, mealtimes are memorable. Linger late over candlelight dinners created by Infinity’s talented chef and served by your butler. A well-designed and fully-outfitted sunken bar adjoins the living room. Open on two sides, the bar provides access to thirsty guests in the living room and family room, and also from the hot tub, lawn and pool terrace. The bartender is competent and the drinks menu is extensive. Notable at Infinity is the massive heated pool, uniquely divided into sections for wading, lounging and laps. Child friendly, Infinity features a soft grassy lawn and shallow entrance to the pool. The center core of the villa (living room, family room, dining room and bar) can be closed against the tropical heat and air conditioned on request.
