Karma Bay, Montego Bay, Jamaica Vacation Rental

 

For a taste of paradise where the waves lap the shore and the trade winds caress the palm trees, Karma Bay has all the attributes that make for an unforgettable experience. With six private bedrooms, this magnificent waterfront villa melds influences from Asia and the Caribbean together with vibrant colours and an emphasis on luxury living.

Karma Bay sits on the Tryall plains, along Country Club Drive.

The villa is a brief stroll to the Club’s private white sand beach and the legendary Tryall Golf Course. A short golf cart ride will take you up to the Great House, tennis courts, shops, Kid’s Club and gym. Karma Bay’s six bedrooms are all air-conditioned with individual media systems and private en suite bathrooms featuring waterfall showers and two of the en suites boast extended al fresco shower areas. Four of the bedrooms have king-sized beds, while two have twins which may be configured as kings. The waterfront facing bedrooms all open out onto a private terrace area. Four of the bedrooms are joined to the main house by elegant covered pavilions that offer views of the lawns and the ocean.

The large and luxuriously decorated living, dining and verandah offer fabulous views and the extensive pool deck and sprawling lawns offer plenty of space for relaxing.

Karma Bay is pleased to provide its guests with the use of a complimentary golf cart. Your private, welcoming villa staff is comprised of a superb team of cook, butler, housekeeper, laundress and gardener.
