Del Mar, Anse Marcel, St. Maarten Vacation Rental


Introducing Del Mar, a fabulous new addition to our rental listings at Dreamexoticrentals!  A luxurious, brand new, 5 bedroom home in the locale of Anse Marcel, commanding stunning views over the bay and marina, Del Mar has a marvelous contemporary décor with a retro touch.

There are 5 spacious bedrooms each with an en-suite designer bathroom, walk-in closet and flat screen TV and DVD. Ideal for families the villa has 4 of the 5 bedrooms that are accessible from the main house and one of these bedrooms has a special décor that the kids will love!

The sizeable living area is very comfortable and well equipped and opens onto an extensive terrace with a large pool and lounging area perfect for enjoying the spectacular view. Villa Del Mar is just a 5 minute walk from the beach at Anse Marcel and is within easy reach of Grand Case and popular Orient beach.
