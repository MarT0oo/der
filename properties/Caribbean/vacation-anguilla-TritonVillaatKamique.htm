Triton Villa at Kamique, Little Harbor, Anguilla, Caribbean Vacation Rental

 

Sitting on Anguilla's South Shore, Kamique is on its own secluded cove beach and has stunning views towards the mountains of St. Martin. Kamique is a collection of 3 Luxury Villas - 6-bedroom Triton, 4-bedroom Aquamarie and 4-bedroom Anani. There's a 180-degree full ocean view, and the homes are dramatically nestled in the rolling landscape to form their own private enclave. Aquamarie villa is wheelchair accessible as well.

A rich blend of woods, stone and concrete creates a sensual yet modern island-inspired environment. A touch of the tropics provides a sense of romance. The atmosphere blurs the line between inside and out, and orchestrates light and shade to create a peaceful oasis.

Triton Villa is a 6-bedroom contemporary West Indian Style villa. The villa overlooks the Caribbean Sea and the mountains of St. Maarten and beyond.

Two master bedrooms with magnificent views are located on the main floor in separate wings. Each have a full en-suite bathroom and large outdoor shower.

There is a spacious indoor/outdoor living and dining area with full decks surrounding a large infinity pool, and a modern open plan kitchen.

The Media room offers comfortable seating with a computer and large television; there are many videos games, including Wii, and a powder room.

On the ground floor in one wing there is an ocean view mini master and en-suite bathroom connecting to a garden view bedroom also with en suite bathroom (this bedroom is particularly good for those with small children).

There are two more ocean view connecting bedrooms in a separate wing of the villa on the ground floor.

This villa, along with the whole of Kamique, is wonderful for multi generational families, destination weddings and executive retreats.

Each Villa has its own full size pool. Please note the pools have no child security features and are to be used at the guests' own risk.

Additional Amenities include:

24-hr concierge; special signing privileges available at KU and Cuisinart; 37" - 42" Flat Screen TV in bedrooms; choice of Tempur or Serta Enrapture mattress; pillow selection; Italian design Egyptian cotton bed linens; L'Occitane personal care amenities; crib and high chair available; espresso maker; all-clad cookware; fully outfitted kitchen; pre-arrival grocery shopping; private beach pavilions; secluded cove with beach loungers; umbrellas and two shaded gazebos; snorkeling gear for adults and kids; media rooms in each villa with flat screen TV; surround sound; Nintendo Wii; various board games; poker games; a small DVD library.
