Caribbean Casa de Campo, Casa de Campo, Dominican Republic Vacation Rental

 

A luxurious eight bedroom villa located within walking distance to Casa de Campo Marina, near Altos de Chavon and minutes away from the many amenities that Casa de Campo has to offer. Enjoy beautiful and panoramic views of the Caribbean Sea and the golf course from an outdoor gazebo and the large backyard offering an infinite edge pool and Jacuzzi.

Open floor plan and beautifully decorated with lots of comfortable furniture, marble floors and baths, double height ceilings. All bedrooms are air-conditioned with modern bathrooms, walk-in closets, LCD TVs and Blu-Ray.

An expansive climate controlled family entertainment living room with TV/DVD/iPod dock radio, opens to a covered terrace overlooking the ocean and connects to the outer deck and pool. Home theater with 120” big-screen with high fidelity surround system. Fully equipped kitchen, interior formal air-conditioned dining room seating 16 guests. Lots of fun for kids – swings, trampoline.
