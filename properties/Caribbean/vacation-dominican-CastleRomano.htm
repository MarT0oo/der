Castle Romano, Las Terrenas, Samana, Dominican Republic Vacation Rental

 

We offer an amazing villa in Las Terrenas, Samana, Dominican Republic for large family reunions, anniversaries, honeymoons, weddings, corporate meetings, social gatherings, and other social functions. The Castillo Romano has 13 bedrooms, 4 kitchens, 3 living rooms, 7 bathrooms, etc. on four levels plus a swimming pool, jacuzzi, and a BBQ area. It was built with a Mediterranean decor in mind inspired by a Roman design. It is luxurious, elegant and refined, grand majestic and very comfortable.

The floors are in granite, the staircase in travertine marble, the woodwork is in Cahoba wood throughout. Our past guests have complimented the Castillo in stating that it looked like the house in Scarface with Al Pacino and that it deserved to have a movie made in it. Built with quality imported materials, it has a unique and original feature of offering superb comfort that can hold up to 33 people. The staff is simple, friendly, efficient, always ready to help, and above all trustworthy. The adventure tours and expeditions in the area are exceptional and provide lots of fun and interesting things to do such as the grots, El Limon waterfall, Los Haitises National Park, zip lining, sail boating, sail kiting, whale watching, scuba diving, deep sea fishing, to mention just a few activities throughout Samana.

The natural beauty is astounding and the beaches are awesome and the castle is precious which makes for a perfect vacation that will be memorable, fun, and everlasting.

Castillo Romano invites you and it will be an honor to host you... See you soon!
