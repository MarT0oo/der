Cielo, Montego Bay, Jamaica Vacation Rental

 

From its high perch atop Barnes Hill, this luxury villa commands a truly breathtaking view over emerald lawns, an infinity pool and the broad expanse of Tryall Club’s legendary golf course. To complete this magical picture are infinite views of the turquoise Caribbean Sea.

Elegant yet casual, Cielo is the perfect choice for a family or group of friends to delight in each other’s company. Spacious and airy, the villa can accommodate up to eight guests. The living room features cable television, CD & DVD player and stereo system for your entertainment pleasure. Wireless internet is also available. A unique place to catch your breath and invent new dreams, Cielo is your escape to easy, tranquil moments. From its tastefully decorated rooms and shaded verandah, the most seductive views of intimate gardens and the distant horizon may be enjoyed.

Dining is a premier vacation experience at Cielo with the best cook on the island, and exceptional staff. Pampering touches are all in the details, and in service completely inspired by your wishes.

Three of Cielo’s bedrooms are located in the main villa. Two have king-sized beds and one has two twin-sized beds. The fourth bedroom is located in a private cottage adjacent to the pool. The cottage has its own verandah and is equipped with a king-sized bed. All bedrooms at Cielo are air-conditioned, with en suite bathrooms and cable televisions. The study may be converted to a fifth bedroom using a pull-out sofa bed.

The villa is pleased to provide its guests with the use of two complimentary golf carts. Your private, welcoming villa staff comprises a superb team of butler, cook, housekeeper, laundress and gardener.
