Magnolia, Kamalame Private Island, Bahamas Vacation Rental

 

Tucked in a lush, flowering tropical garden, Magnolia’s stylish, sun-splashed rooms offer 20-foot peak ceilings throughout its open concept, living-dining space. This expansive villa opens onto a well-appointed 1,500-square-foot beachfront terrace with panoramic views of the Atlantic.

Ideal for up to eight adults, this airy and stylish villa was conceived, constructed and designed by Kamalame Cay’s owners. Enveloped in a soothing coastal palette of linen and white accented with graphic Ikat prints and bright splashes of blue and turquoise, interiors blend modern conveniences with a fresh, contemporary take on classic West Indies charm that balances elegant designer fabrics and furnishings against global accents sourced from Bali and India.

Each of Magnolia’s four en suite bedrooms is outfitted with rain showers. Lined with French doors with lofty spaces, the villa’s bright and spacious open-concept Great Room offers 1,200 square feet of living and dining space. Magnolia’s generous outdoor gathering space is equipped with a shaded al-fresco dining area with a modern gas grill, sun loungers and spectacular vantage points.
