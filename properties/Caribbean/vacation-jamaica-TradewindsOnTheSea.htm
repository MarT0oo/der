Tradewinds On The Sea, Montego Bay, Jamaica, Caribbean Vacation Rental

 

One of just a handful of Tryall villas which has its own private beach (depending upon the tide), Tradewinds On The Sea has the most spectacular sea views with an interior style that is the epitome of modern beachfront living.

The villa is set right on the shoreline and is built in the classic Caribbean style with 21st century enhancements. Built on one level with a separate detached cottage, Tradewinds affords an enviable ocean frontage with an extensive lawn to the east. Every area of the villa takes advantage of the ocean panorama and the overall design invites the outside in. Light, airy and with a lovely free-flowing sense of space, Tradewinds offers exceptionally comfortable accommodation with an immediately appealing oceanfront style.

The open-plan sitting room and dining room flow into the adjacent media room with all areas having the most stunning panorama of the ocean. Soft watery colours such as eau de Nil and pale blue reflect the tones of the sea and sky. The beautiful new swimming pool with integrated hot tub directly overlooks the sea and the surrounding terrace catches the sun all day long. To the right of the sitting room is a very spacious veranda with relaxing furniture and a flat screen television.

The bedrooms all enjoy sea views and have immaculate en suite bathrooms with walk-in showers. Three bathrooms also have outdoor showers and one has a tub. All of the bathrooms are air-conditioned. The master bedroom has a separate small sitting room with the most idyllic sheltered view over the bay.

The villa staff comprises a butler, chef, housekeeper, laundress and gardener.
