Arrecife 23, Punta Cana, Dominican Republic Vacation Rental

 

Imagine yourself in the middle of paradise, a gentle sea breeze against your face while enjoying in one of the most exclusive and luxury places.

This 4 bedroom oceanview villa in the Arrecife neighborhood inside the prestige village Punta Cana Resort is the perfect option to have some well deserved holidays.

From the pool you can see the Caribbean Ocean and one of the most lushy golf courses in the Dominican Republic.

Once you enter the villa you'll find an exquisite decorated foyer which leads you to a large living room and a fully equipped kitchen with an island in the middle.

Downstairs you have a bedroom with a king sized bed, television and its own ensuite bathroom. When you go upstairs you will find a bedroom with 2 queen size beds, a twin bed, television and en suite bathroom, another bedrooms is endowed with a king size bed, television and its own en suite bathroom and, finally you have the spacious master bedroom with a king size bed, an integrated hot tub, television and ensuite bathroom.

When you step outside you can cool off in the private swimming pool while making a BBQ and enjoy a drink on the bar in the gallery.
