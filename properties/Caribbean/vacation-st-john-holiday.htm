Chocolate Hole, St. John, Caribbean

This one of a kind, palatial, Mediterranean-style retreat, perched on a beautifully landscaped bluff overlooking the secluded Chocolate Hole Bay on the island paradise of St. John, U.S. Virgin Islands.


A dream come true, this stunning villa welcomes you with gentle tropical breezes, warm and delicious sunlight to bathe in, sweet floral scents of the tropics and the soothing music of waves caressing the white-sand beach below.
This Villa is one of only a few beachfront villas on this exclusive eight mile stretch of utopia. The rare presence of beachfront villas on St. John is the Villa’s most precious quality—three-fifths of its breathtaking beauty is protected as part of Virgin Islands National Park.


The island’s shoreline protects secluded bays of crystal blue-green waters pulsing with coral reef life, tropical forests, coconut palms and more than 800 botanical species.


This magnificent villa, with its British West Indies ambiance, is one of only a few six bedroom villas on the entire island of St. John and is available for those seeking a fantasy vacation of which dreams are made.


Stunning, warm and welcoming, the Villa is available as a six-bedroom villa or, in the low season, can be reserved as two separate villas. The master villa is comprised of four bedroom suites and the guest villa of two bedroom suites.  Both the master and guest villas have their own living and dining room areas and newly renovated kitchens with rich mahogany cabinetry and graphite counter tops.


One of the most unique features is that all of the six bedrooms are 650 square foot master suites (the largest on the island), each with their own balconies, offering panoramic views of Chocolate Hole Bay, the Caribbean and a picturesque inlet that accommodates spectacular yachts, a pristine beach and the rhythmic sound of lapping waves of crystal clear water from the Caribbean.
