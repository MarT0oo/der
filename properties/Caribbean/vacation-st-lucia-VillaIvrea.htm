Villa Ivrea, Mount Hardy, Cap Estate, St. Lucia Vacation Rental


Villa Ivrea is located at the northern tip of Cap Estate and boasts breathtaking views of both the Atlantic Ocean and the Caribbean Sea.

Designed by Dan F. Sater II and built in 2009, it offers all the amenities required by discerning travelers, beginning with luxuriously appointed bedrooms and social areas, together with outdoor features like infinity pool, panoramic verandas, pergola and terraced gardens.

The main house consist of 4 well appointed bedrooms, all located on the top level of the house. There are two bedrooms with ensuite shower rooms and a bathroom is shared by the other two.In addition to the Main House, Villa Ivrea offers a fully independent, one-bedroom Cottage that sleeps two and includes a full kitchen, central air conditioning and a veranda with a splash pool.

The Cottage can be rented separately, especially by couples looking for an intimate haven with lovely ocean views and all the modern comfort or as an add on to the main house.The renowned St. Lucia Golf & Country Club is just a 5 minute drive away, while a 15 minute drive will put you in the heart of Rodney Bay with all its different attractions, including excellent restaurants, a world-class marina, two duty free shopping malls, pharmacies, post office, banks, etc.

All this is just the beginning, as the rest of this beautiful island awaits being explored by those interested in snorkeling, hiking, bird watching, scuba diving, sightseeing, mountain biking, deep-sea fishing, sailing and more.

"Saluti" from Villa Ivrea, an elegant taste of Italy, where the enchanting warm waters of the Caribbean Sea meet the crisp waters of the Atlantic Ocean and the dancing lights of Martinique greet you from the veranda.

Please note that our housekeepers are not trained nannies and should not be expected to look after and/or babysit infants and children. If you do make babysitting arrangements with the staff, please be advised this is at your own risk.
