Chavon Villa, Casa de Campo, Dominican Republic Vacation Rental

 

This spectacular ten bedroom villa sits on a privilege area of Casa de Campo offering commanding views of the Dye Fore Golf Course, the Chavon River and the Caribbean Sea.

A soothing water feature filled with exotic fish welcomes you as you walk into the living areas of the villa. High ceilings, formal dining room. spacious open air living & dining areas and private studio office. There is one master suite and nine guest suites. Bedrooms are distributed in two wings, on one side the master suite and three guest bedrooms and on the other six guest bedrooms. All bedrooms are air conditioned and feature on-suite bathrooms, plasma TVâ€™s and safe box. Large patio, gazebo and a beautiful infinity swimming pool. An ideal gateway villa for a large family, a corporate retreat or wedding.

Casa de Campo is located on the south eastern shore of the Dominican Republic, next to the town of La Romana and just 70 miles from the capital city of Santo Domingo, the oldest city in the new world. Casa de Campo is a luxurious 7,000 acre ocean front community offering world class golf courses which include the famous Teeth of the Dog Golf Course, Dye Fore and The Links Golf Course plus polo fields, skeet shooting centre, tennis centre, equestrian centre, water sports, spa treatment centre, fitness centre, business centre, private beach, a la carte restaurants, Altos de Chavon (artist village) and Casa de Campo Marina, all of which will please almost any visitor.
