Sea's Edge, Rendezvous Bay, St. John, Caribbean Vacation Rental

 

The view across St. John's south shore is breathtaking, and during your stay in Sea’s Edge you won't miss a second of it. Sea’s Edge sits on a cliff side point 60 feet above Rendezvous Bay, surrounded by water on three sides. With beautiful island-inspired architecture, charming indoor and outdoor living spaces, and complete privacy, Sea’s Edge is a haven, the perfect place to escape the stresses of civilization. This villas waterfront location makes for lots of lovely island breezes, with stairs leading to a rocky trail that brings you to a private protective cove perfect for taking a leisurely float or setting off on a rental kayak. .

Spread out between a main house and separate cottage, the five bedroom suites are richly furnished and each has a private balcony with direct sea views as well as en suite bathrooms and televisions for those nights you may just want to cozy up in bed with a good movie. Additionally, the gourmet kitchen and luxurious main living areas and are equipped with every modern convenience, including a state of the art sound system.

The outdoor living spaces are an elegant extension of the indoors with a comfortable dining area, a large 42’ invisible edge pool that disappears into the Caribbean Sea, and plenty of space for just enjoying the sun and the breeze. And for days when you'd rather lounge on the beach than lounge at home, Sea’s Edge is just a short drive to the white sands of the North Shore.
