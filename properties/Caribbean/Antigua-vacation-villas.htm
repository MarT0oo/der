Antigua & Barbuda

Antigua & Barbuda are two “twin islands” in the middle of the Caribbean with spectacular views and  lovely bays opening up to white sandy beaches.  The islands have been inhabited for thousands of years and have some of the oldest history in the Caribbean.  With its richness and diversity these two islands have recently become the hideaways for the rich and famous, for their natural wonders, amazing beaches, and very relaxed and friendly residents.  Year round you will find the island filled with visitors throughout the world seeking either to scuba dive, fish, or to sun themselves on the shores.  This is why you’ll find some of the most beautiful homes hillside on Antigua.  From the sea you can spot the mansions, villas and cottages decorating the hillside which makes it a spectacular place to visit.

When thinking about the islands you may also want to bring your finances with you.  You can find some of the most prominent banks in the world with offices in Antigua, such as Bank of America and Barclays.  What this means is that not only are the islands rich with tourism but that you can count on them to be there for any fiscal needs as well.  Truly a great place for an investment home or vacation home.

Flying In

When visiting you will fly into V.C. Bird International Airport, located on the northeast corner of Antigua.  Major airlines are flying into this destination daily, so it’s very easy.  Flying times: New York--4 hrs.; Miami--3 hrs.; Baltimore--4 hrs.; Toronto--4 hrs.; Puerto Rico--1 hr.; London--8 hrs.; Frankfurt--9 1/4 hrs.; Paris--8 hrs.

Ferry
