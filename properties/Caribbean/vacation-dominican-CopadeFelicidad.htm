Copa de Felicidad, Casa de Campo, Dominican Republic Vacation Rental

 

Copa de Felicidad is a beautiful 6 bedroom home within one of the most prestigious Golf Communities in the World. Known for it's simple hospitality from start to finish, to it's gorgeous beach, golfing for the best of players, amenities and fitness centers. Ask for more information in booking excursions during your stay. Golf Carts are typically between 50-100 per. day, unless the home is equipped by owner with private cart. We also provide maid, chef services, each house is different so please make sure to ask about your services. Food is additional if chef is provided, food will typically cost 30-65 dollars per. person per. day.

Located adjacent to the tourist town of La Romana and above Casa de Campo Resort. The airport, which opened in December 2000, is about 68 miles (110km) from the capital, Santo Domingo. Punta Cana International is about a 45 minute drive from the Airport

A newly constructed six bedroom villa offering modern style architecture and furnishings located by hole 6 of La Romana Country Club.

Spacious bedrooms each with large bathrooms. Marble floors throughout the house with mahogany doors and windows. The kitchen is large with stainless steel commercial appliances. A separate dining room that will seat 12 people. Complete with central air conditioning in all bedrooms, dining room and TV/media room.

Large swimming pool and Jacuzzi with lots of deck space. The gazebo accommodates a dining area and a living area. The villa is facing east with cool breezes all day and night.

**Note** At any time we may need to put you into another similar/better home if these homes become unavailable. The Homes are privately owned but like any other home may experience damages, electrical issues, or may become uninhabitable. We take all precautions to ensure your vacation is perfect, but these are things beyond our control.
