Round Rock Estate, West End, Anguilla Vacation Rental

 

Round Rock Estate is a contemporary 6-bedroom villa on the idyllic island of Anguilla. Completely renovated in 2019, no expense was spared to make the most of the breathtaking waterfront location. Sit by the private pool under pristine white umbrellas and gaze at the cool Caribbean Sea against the enchanting views of neighboring St. Martin. Everything you might desire is poolside — covered seating and dining, a heated pool and jacuzzi, full bar with ice maker, BBQ grill, ample lounges and a large terrace. Indoors enjoy a new modern kitchen and all new designer furnishings throughout, making Round Rock one of Anguilla’s most chic new villa experiences. Large common living areas and three bedrooms also offer spectacular views of St. Martin. Workout at the new on-site gym and rock out to the state-of-the-art Sonos home sound system.

Round Rock Estate is centrally located — minutes from the ferry and the airport —and near Rendezvous Bay, one of Anguilla’s premiere world class beaches. Don’t miss the chance to visit paradise and revel in the villa lifestyle at this hip Anguilla villa!

Anguilla is recently ranked as the #1 Caribbean Island in “Travel and Leisure’s 2019 World’s Best Awards,” making this the third year our island has taken the top ranking. In addition, Anguilla has been ranked #6 on “Travel and Leisure’s 15 Best Islands in the World,” for 2019. The island is a world class, off-the-beaten-path destination known for stunning beaches, friendly locals and a wide offering of luxury villas and vacation homes with exceptional privacy.
