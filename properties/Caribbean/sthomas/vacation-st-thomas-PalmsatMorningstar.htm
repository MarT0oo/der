Palms at Morningstar, Morningstar Bay, St. Thomas Vacation Rental

 

Beautifully designed and generously outfitted with the best of everything, Palms at Morningstar is one of our newest and most noteworthy luxury villas on St. Thomas. Located south of Charlotte Amalie, just a two-minute drive from Morningstar Beach at Marriott Frenchmans Bay Resort, Palms at Morningstar looks down on the beach and out to the Caribbean Sea and colorful tropical sunsets.

Just recently completed, Palms at Morningstar is completely air-conditioned and features two spacious bedroom/bath suites separated by the Great Room and gourmet kitchen on the Main Level.

The entry driveway is bordered with a stunning avenue of majestic palm trees, lighted at night. Walk from your car directly into Palms' Great Room. As you enter, the sun-sparkled expanse of blue ocean before you is breathtaking. Palms at Morningstar's light and airy Great Room shares space with the open-plan gourmet kitchen, and is furnished in arresting red leather accented by colorful modern art. The stainless-steel and granite kitchen is a chef's dream, featuring a granite counter with bar stools, a Sub-Zero refrigerator, Viking gas range, Miele dishwasher, espresso machine, wine cellar cooler, two sinks and a great selection of cook's tools.

Tall french doors open out to the Palms' outdoor lounge, kitchen, bar and pool and frame the relaxing sea views beyond. On one side of the covered deck is an outdoor kitchen with a wet bar, blender, icemaker, beverage center and a vented stainless steel gas grill. On the other side are cushioned chairs and a large TV, perfect for watching the game with cold drinks in hand. The infinity-edge swimming pool is set like a jewel in the center of the deck. The lounge/sun deck is furnished in cushioned teak, including an alfresco dining table, chaise lounges for tanning and sitting areas.

The plush king master suite is on the west side of the Great Room. It features a desk (if you must work on vacation), cable TV, and a beautifully-designed ensuite bath. A sliding frosted glass door opens to the stylish bath and its twin sink vessels, cable TV, a tiled shower, and a separate WC behind the vanity.

On the east side of Palms at Morningstar is the queen bedroom/bath suite with a tiled bath and shower in a glass enclosure. Both bedroom suites open out to the pool/lounge deck, perfect for a moonlight swim under the stars. Outside the queen suite, in the hallway, is the convenient laundry room.

This fully air-conditioned contemporary luxury villa showcases sleek and clean modern design inside and out, with added wow-factors of artistic lighting, a great floor plan providing maximum privacy for three couples or a family, great up-to-date electronics and amenities, all overlooking aquamarine ocean views and located just minutes from Morningstar Beach, Havensite Mall, Charlotte Amalie, restaurants and shopping - your fantastic place in the sun!
