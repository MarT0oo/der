CalypsoBlu, Frenchman's Bay, St. Thomas, US Virgin Islands


Awaken to a perfect blend of Caribbean style and life's expected pleasures at CalypsoBlu. Overlooking Morningstar Beach below and the Caribbean Sea beyond, this charming, fully-air-conditioned three-bedroom villa is located at the end of a cul-de-sac road on the south side of St. Thomas, convenient to nearby beaches, shopping and restaurants.

A tiled garden walkway and a red awning ushers you into CalypsoBlu's breezeway, which is set with comfortable, cushioned chairs for cool shady relaxation - the perfect spot for reading, conversation and sunset cocktails. The breezeway connects the villa's two pavilions. Stop there for a moment to savor the expansive views of white sand, aqua seas and blue skies stretching out beyond the sparkling pool.

To the right just off the breezeway is the air-conditioned Great Room with living and dining areas, a well-equipped open-plan kitchen and a powder room. Sliding glass doors open to a covered verandah overlooking the pool and sun deck. On the other side of the breezeway is the air-conditioned king master bedroom with an ensuite tiled bath and its own private verandah. Steps lead from the breezeway down to the pool deck and then down to CalypsoBlu's second bedroom, an air-conditioned king suite which also features a verandah.

At the pool deck are chaise lounges, a table with an umbrella, and a gas grill. A few steps from this deck lead down to CalypsoBlu's third air-conditioned bedroom suite, which offers two twin beds, an ensuite bath, and a mini-kitchen with a refrigerator.

The three bedrooms at this secluded home are well-separated for maximum privacy; each has its own outside keyed entrance, making this an ideal villa for couples vacationing together. Watch spectacular sunsets, yachts and cruise ships coming and going from Charlotte Amalie's harbor, or take short drives to shopping, restaurants and beaches. Live by the sun and love by the moon at CalypsoBlu!

Each bedroom has A/C, a small coffeemaker (the southern lower bedroom also has a bar sink, and the garden-level bedroom features a mini-kitchen with fridge, dishes + glassware.
