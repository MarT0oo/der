Villa Mas, St. Thomas, US Virgin Islands Vacation Rental

 

Situated on a hillside above beautiful Morningstar Beach and Marriott Frenchmans Reef Resort, this sleek, just completed four-bedroom home definitely offers "más" - "more" in Spanish! Villa Más is built on two delightful levels, offering full air-conditioning throughout and four stylish bedroom/bath suites, including two spacious king master bedrooms.

The driveway to Villa Más brings you right to the entry door for easy walk-in access into the living area and kitchen. Beyond an entire wall of glass screened french doors is the villa's swimming pool, sun deck, outdoor kitchen, gas grill and dining, and a 180-degree expanse of the Caribbean Sea before you - a sun-spangled ocean view that will take your breath away!

Villa Más's Great Room has a living area with plush sofa and chairs and a 60-inch cable TV with an Apple digital media player for movie, television, music and game technophiles. The lounge area shares space with the modern kitchen, well equipped with high-end stainless-steel appliances including a wine cellar cooler, gas range, and a granite-topped island with bar stools and a champagne trough for ice and drinks.

Just outside is the outdoor living space where you'll likely spend most of your time, a beautifully-designed and partially-roofed sun deck around an infinity pool boasting dramatic LED lighting for the evenings, a Bose Sound System, chaise lounges, a cushioned and shaded sitting area, tables and chairs, sun umbrella, and an outside kitchen with a professional gas grill, a wet bar, beverage center, commercial icemaker and alfresco dining table for meals with a view.

On the west side of the sun deck are two air-conditioned bedrooms with ensuite baths. The sophisticated king master suite features clean contemporary style, a private balcony overlooking a small garden area with a lawn with shade trees, a double hammock, and the sea view, and a spectacular adjoining bath with a double vanity with glass vessel sinks and a spacious tiled rain shower, all enhanced by creative lighting. Next to the king master is Villa Más's second air-conditioned bedroom, a smaller queen suite with cable TV and an ensuite tiled bath with a rain shower; this bedroom also opens directly on to the sun deck and pool.

The second air-conditioned king bedroom/bath suite is located on the opposite side of the sun deck, with direct access from the pool area. Widely separated from the other bedrooms for maximum privacy, this spacious king suite offers a double-sink vanity, a separate WC and a beautifully-tiled rain shower. From this bedroom, a hallway leads past a laundry room back to the lounge area and kitchen.

Villa Más's fourth air-conditioned bedroom is downstairs on the garden level, in a secluded world of its own for total privacy. It encompasses a spacious covered verandah, a small kitchen with appliances, a queen bed, a bath with tiled rain shower, and a washer and dryer.

Within its stellar floor plan, Villa Más is cleanly furnished with crisp contemporary flair, sleek surfaces, stainless steel railings, and finished in fine detail - truly, a work of art. This handsomely-executed and very well-equipped villa, located just minutes from one of the best beaches and resorts on St. Thomas, offers a chic, polished headquarters for quality family time or for friends vacationing together - get more - MÁS! - for your island stay - brand new, and truly spectacular!
