Sea Star, East End, St. Thomas, Caribbean Vacation Rental

 

Sea Star Luxury Villa is a unique villa located on the East End of the beautiful Caribbean Island of St. Thomas. Sea Star is designed to provide all the comforts of home with the serenity that can only be found in the islands. Take a deep breath and relax as you gaze out over our infinity pool onto the clear turquoise waters of Great Bay.

Sea Star Luxury Villa is conveniently located just down the beach from the Ritz Carlton resort however, at Sea Star, we have our very own private and secluded white sand beach, equipped with beach chairs, paddle boards, kayaks and snorkeling gear- all you need to enjoy the perfect day by the water!

Along with our pristine infinity pool, we have a large patio with lounge chairs, a stainless steel gas grill and two separate covered dining areas- seating a total of 20 people. In our covered lower level, we also have a 7-person jacuzzi, overlooking the ocean.

The villa itself has three bedrooms on the main level- each with their own full bath. In addition there are two suites on the lower level complete with a full bath, private lounge area, and pull-out sofa. We sleep 14 adults, and can provide air mattresses for additional guests if requested. We have had families of up to 20 people comfortably stay at Sea Star! The main floor is also fitted with a full kitchen and dining area perfect for large groups. We have a complete entertainment system with a bluRay player and movie library, a surround sound system, and Wifi.

Additionally for your convenience, we have a washer/ dryer, air conditioning in every room, and an automatic generator in case of a power outage (which can happen in the tropics).

Sea Star Luxury Villa strives to be an ideal place to spend the vacation of a lifetime. We are constantly upgrading and are in the process of expanding our beach to allow for more beach activities like beach volleyball and an outside fire pit. We are full service vacation providers and aim to ensure that all of our guests enjoy a stress-free stay. We will meet you at the airport and escort you to the villa and are more than happy to help organize any other services while you are here including boat trips, at-home massages etc.
