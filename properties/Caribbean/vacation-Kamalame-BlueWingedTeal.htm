Blue Winged Teal, Kamalame Private Island, Bahamas Vacation Rental

 

Redefining island living, our 2-bedroom cottage, Blue Winged Teal, offers the perfect oasis from everyday life. This 1,400-square-foot private cottage serves as a quiet retreat and sits just a few footsteps away from the water’s edge.

Featuring elevated decks that boast both east and west-facing panoramic vistas, vaulted ceilings, an inviting living area and elegant bedrooms, this cottage are the epitome of Bahamian luxury. With a stunning mix of modern and beach-infused décor, the cottage was designed to thrive on natural light and let in the cool sea breezes with beautiful French doors throughout the space.

A full kitchen is stocked with premium spirits and international wines, snacks, tea, and a French press. Ideal for up to six guests, this cottage can be adapted to accommodate either king or twin-sized beds
