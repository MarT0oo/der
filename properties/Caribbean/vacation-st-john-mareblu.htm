Mare Blu, Rendevouz, St. John Caribbean Vacation Rental


Exciting color, a great floor plan, and superb design, lighting, and finishes distinguish this spectacular new villa overlooking Rendezvous Bay! Mare Blu is considered to be one of St. John's most luxurious and stylish super-villas. Built in four spacious pavilions linked together by generous outdoor living spaces, the sparkling pool and tropical gardens, Mare Blu offers full air conditioning throughout the villa. Its excellent floor plan is designed so that all of the home's main living space is on one level. Six large bedroom/bath suites, a stunning indoor gourmet kitchen flanked by an outdoor kitchen dining and sitting area, and a beautifully-decorated Great Room are just a start at Mare Blu.

On the property's main level is the a pavilion offering the air-conditioned Great Room, a large covered and shady patio for outdoor living, a gourmet kitchen, and right outside through sliding screened pocket doors, an outdoor kitchen and alfresco dining area with a convenient screened pass-through from the kitchen. The handsome Great Room features a lofted cypress ceiling, a large plasma TV with cable and DVD player plus lots of DVD movies and games for everyone, a card or game table, and two sitting areas. A powder room is right around the corner, clad in travertine marble from top to bottom. Adjoining is the wonderful gourmet kitchen and pantry, masterful in its razzle-dazzle color scheme - a sleek, glass-tiled accent wall behind the gas cooktop, skylights, custom cabinetry of exotic African makori burl wood and stainless steel, and great selections of dinnerware, glassware and chef tools for your dining pleasures. The kitchen offers an island with prep sink, two dishwashers, a microwave/convection oven, a standard oven, a large three-door refrigerator and a small desk to the side.

Sliding pocket doors lead from the kitchen to Mare Blu's second kitchen just outside, which boasts a stainless steel professional gas grill, a second oven and an icemaker. Outside on this large L-shaped covered patio is an alfresco dining area flanked by lots of cushioned seating for cocktails and conversation. This shady pavilion flows into a large open sun deck furnished with chaise lounges at pool's edge; Mare Blu's outdoor furniture is designer Dedon, first class and comfortable all the way.

The sun deck continues to the pool's covered loggia, a shaded poolside lounge area where more cushioned seating, a 48-inch TV with cable, DVD player and movies, refrigerated drawers to keep your drinks and snacks cold while you swim and tan, a closet with pool floats, and a cheerful glass-tiled powder room are located. The laundry room is also off the pool loggia, where a supply of fluffy beach towels is handy. A striking stainless steel palm sculpture sits across the pool. Beyond the loggia is the two-level bedroom pavilion, where four roomy and air-conditioned bedroom/bath suites await. On the pool level are two suites: the two-twin (or 1 king) Indigo Suite and the Orange Suite with its king sleigh bed and an exotic alfresco shower. On the garden level below are the queen Yellow Suite, and the Pink Suite with two twin beds (which can also be made up into a king bed with your advance request). Glass sliding doors open from both the Orange Suite and the Yellow Suite to screened porches where you can read and watch the blue colors of the Caribbean Sea with your sweetheart, with privacy provided by etched glass panels depicting a fantasy of undersea life. All four bathrooms feature double vanities, and all are completely tiled in handsome gray granite from floor to ceiling; the Pink Suite's bathroom also offers a bathtub, and the Yellow Suite features a semi-alfresco shower.

Outside this pavilion are stepping stones through a ribbon of green lawn which take you past flowers and palm trees to the Ginger Suite, a very secluded bedroom/bath suite tucked under the Great Room's pavilion. This air-conditioned hideaway offers a four-poster king bed draped with netting, a gray granite ensuite bath with a double vanity and shower - and just outside is a private patio for sitting and admiring the ocean view. Mare Blu's sixth bedroom/bath suite, the Blue Suite, is located in the third pavilion, above the main pavilion in its own private realm overlooking the sea vista. This air-conditioned queen suite has its own mini-fridge, coffeemaker and a balcony just outside sliding glass doors.

This fantastic, well-equipped property makes the most of its locale, with every room overlooking Rendezvous Bay, and features exciting Caribbean color, sophisticated design, in the aluminum gates, top-of-the-line fixtures and finishes, luxury hypoallergenic bedding, and the best of everything for your vacation pleasures. Privacy is well maintained at Mare Blu by tall treelines and tropical landscaping. Just a short drive to Cruz Bay and to the famous National Park beaches beyond, Mare Blu is well-located and offers plenty of space for everyone to relax and enjoy. Book early to get the dates you want at this five-star villa...magnificent in every way!
