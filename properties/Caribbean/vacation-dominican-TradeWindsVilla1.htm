Trade Winds Villa, Casa de Campo, Dominican Republic Vacation Rental

 

An iconic private beachfront villa located in one of the most exclusive neighborhood in Casa de Campo. Enjoying beathtaking views on it's spacious ten bedrooms, two dining rooms, and two living rooms.

Conceived as a sanctuary and homage to nature, the villa was designed to maximize the effect of the trade winds. The property private white coral sand beach is unique in Casa de Campo and sheltered by a coral reef and a manmade stone breakwater. The formal dining room is built over the seawater offer a romantic touch under a thatched palapa roof.

The villa achieves a harmonious balance with a thoughtful choice of furniture, plantings, art, decorative objects, colors, and textures that harmonize with the Caribbean Sea and it's gardens. Natural materials are the hallmark of this villas outdoor design including the stonework. Great staff and excellent choice for a large family looking for a vacation to remember.

Casa de Campo is located on the south eastern shore of the Dominican Republic, next to the town of La Romana and just 70 miles from the capital city of Santo Domingo, the oldest city in the new world. Casa de Campo is a luxurious 7,000 acre ocean front community offering world class golf courses which include the famous Teeth of the Dog Golf Course, Dye Fore and The Links Golf Course plus polo fields, skeet shooting centre, tennis centre, equestrian centre, water sports, spa treatment centre, fitness centre, business centre, private beach, a la carte restaurants, Altos de Chavon (artist village) and Casa de Campo Marina, all of which will please almost any visitor.
