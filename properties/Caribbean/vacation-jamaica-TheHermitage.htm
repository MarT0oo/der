The Hermitage, Bluefields Bay, Jamaica Vacation Rental

 

Architect-designed and lauded as a prized jewel of our luxury Caribbean resorts, this distinctive home was completed in 1991. In building The Hermitage, major emphasis was put on using local workers and Jamaican materials (stone, marble, mahogany, cedar) to reproduce features of authentic and traditional Jamaican homes. Superb craftsmanship creating mahogany armoires and arches, handmade curved-top 8′ tall glass French doors and classic West Indian louvers marks this testimonial to the continuing richness of Jamaican building traditions.

Set on 4 acres of beachfront land, this villa has two spacious master bedroom suites and two smaller bedrooms. Each has breathtaking views, western-facing terraces perfect for sunsets, gorgeous canopy beds, and other special furnishings. The Hermitage’s Jamaican furniture is part of a larger collection assembled by the Moncure family to be enjoyed here in Bluefields. Below is a 1000′ long private waterfront with many “hideaways” where it is easy to enjoy seaside seclusion. The site opens to the 1/2 mile long Bluefields Beach. The combination of our Jamaica luxury all inclusive villa with its adjacent beach is what keeps bringing our guests back.

Located south of Montego Bay and east of Negril, Bluefields Bay is the perfect escape from the commercialized tourist traps that plague far too much of Jamaica.

With its unspoiled natural beauty, Bluefields Bay has a calm and purity unlike the rest of Jamaica. Experience its unparalleled tranquility as you gaze at the scintillating Bay from your private exclusive villa. Now a Protected Fish Sanctuary, the sea is without activity — unlike the coastlines teeming with tourists and boats of most other holiday locations.
