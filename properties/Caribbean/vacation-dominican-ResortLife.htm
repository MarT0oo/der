Resort Life, Casa de Campo, Dominican Republic Vacation Rental

 

A superior two story five bedroom villa beautifully decorated on a French country style with pastel colors.

On the ground floor you find the living areas, studio, formal dining room and two guest bedrooms. The large dining room overlooks the garden and swimming pool. The studio comes with a large LCD TV and stereo system for your entertainment. Both dining and studio are air conditioned.

On the second floor you will find the master suite and two other guest rooms. The master suite feature its own private studio with a balcony. All bedrooms offer en-suite bathrooms and air conditioning. Great cook and butler.
