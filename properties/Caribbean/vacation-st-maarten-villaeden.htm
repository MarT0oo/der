Villa Eden, Baie Longue, St. Martin Vacation Rental

 

Villa Eden is a NEWLY REDESIGNED and FABULOUS Beachfront Villa with the most breath-taking views you’ll ever find in the Caribbean. Located on one of St. Martin’s finest beaches, Baie Longue, where some of the world’s most beautiful villas and hotels line the shores, this is an absolutely perfect place for anyone looking for a high end luxury rental.

 

This luxury estate has undergone a major renovation to offer our luxury renters a premier beachfront escape. The Villa offers 5 bedrooms with en-suite bathrooms, air-conditioned living areas, covered terrace & dining area and fully equipped BBQ area. The pool, which can only be called a seafront dream, is large enough to lose yourself in, all while you have the majestic sea sitting right at your feet. You will feel as if you’ve found paradise from the moment you arrive.  There is also a tennis court that is fully equipped; an air-conditioned private gym completes this beach oasis.

The villa offers four immaculate guest accommodations with flat screen TVs and private bathrooms. The master suite is truly something for the books, with a private Ocean View sitting area and an indoor and outdoor shower. The villa also features a unique tree house guest suite situated on the edge of the sands, making this a truly exceptional place for any guest wanting to feel as if they are on the sand.  The villa offers it all: exceptional beachfront location with turquoise waters, a gym and tennis courts to maintain an active lifestyle and a family friendly estate that will create memories for your family year after year.
