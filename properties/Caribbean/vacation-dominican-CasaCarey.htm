Casa Carey, Punta Cana, Dominican Republic Vacation Rental

 

Casa Carey is a stunning beachside villa located in Arrecife, one of most exclusive and private residential enclaves of the world-renowned Punta Cana Beach Resort & Club.

Another spectacular masterpiece of acclaimed Mexican architect, Manolo Mestre, Casa Carey’s design approach harmoniously combines old-world architecture with the contemporary while complementing nature’s beauty. Set amidst blue waters, 48-holes of internationally designed championship golf courses, five-miles of pristine private beach and on a 1,500-acre ecological reserve, the beach and golf course front estate is a spacious 20,000 square feet, offering 6.6 bedrooms and 6½ bathrooms. Perfect for groups up to 16.

Casa Carey’s enchanting beauty epitomizes comfort, laid-back luxury, and elegance. Winding pathways and the property’s perfectly manicured gardens bring a sense of warmth and tranquility to the home. The swimming pool, in itself, is a masterpiece as the infinity edge seamlessly blends with the undisturbed view of the Caribbean Sea. The attention to detail is flawless. From the main living room’s wooden structured ceiling to its handcrafted and etched concrete floors, each room is a meticulously thought out and designed work of art.

Fashioned with both luxury and comfort in mind, all 6 rooms are designed as large master suites with views of the gardens or sea. Ground floor suites have exterior showers and sitting terraces. Second floor suites have indoor showers and outside lanai terraces with hammocks.

The house boasts several exquisite living areas. At the end of the pool sits a wonderful “Palapa,” which has a year-round breeze that makes it an ideal outdoor living room from sunrise to sunset. The outdoor terrace is where most socialize and go to read, swim, listen to music, have a drink, or enjoy a day in the sun. The main living room, off the terrace, is just as impressive and its entertainment control center and Baby Grand piano make it a perfect living area for intimate evenings with friends and family. For a quieter escape, guests can also unwind in the large lounge bed on the second floor balcony, overlooking the golf course and Caribbean Sea.

Whether you choose to dine in the main dining room, outside terrace area, rooftop, or in any of the other rooms at Casa Carey, the staff is ready to cater to your requests.

If you are looking for a little friendly competition, look no further than the home’s game room. End your day with a game of pool, Ping-Pong or dominos. Guests also enjoy getting caught-up on the latest TV shows and movies in the home theater room. Even a tropical vacation can benefit from a bit of modern pleasures and technology.

Climate: With Punta Cana’s tropical climate, guests can expect the temperature to average around 80 degrees during the year. Summer months, July to September, are normally warmer with temperatures reaching into the high 80s and low 90s. October through January are the cooler months with daytime temperatures in the mid 70s to low 80s and windy nights with temperatures from the mid 60s to low 70s.
