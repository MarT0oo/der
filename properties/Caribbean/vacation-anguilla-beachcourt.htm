Beach Court Villa Holiday Rental, Anguilla


On one of Anguilla's greatest beaches sits one of its greatest homes: Beach Court Villa at Shoal Bay East, a 10,000 square foot luxury villa with swimming pool and tennis court on sloped land that goes directly to the western end of Shoal Bay. It is one of the very few houses on all of Shoal Bay whose property brings you right to the sand.

Built on a hill, the house sits about 40 feet above the water, maximizing views and breezes. Yet—as you sit on its spacious decks—you are a short walk from the magnificence of Shoal Bay, its blues and greens, great for walking or snorkeling (enter the sea right in front of the house for some of the finest snorkeling you will ever experience).

Go through the lush gardens, past the tennis court, to the water; turn right, and over 1½ miles of beach lay before you. Swimming, snorkeling, and leisurely walks are all right outside the gate that insures your privacy (and keeps out the goats).


Visit Beach Court Villa through this site. After that, you will visit it in person. Your greatest vacation has just begun.
