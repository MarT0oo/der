Carisbrooke, Jumby Bay, Antigua, Caribbean


Recently reconfigured and redesigned, Carisbrooke has been entirely re-imagined. The villa is styled in a contemporary Caribbean fashion with a white, cr√®me and pastel color palette to enhance the home's grand spaces. Tiled marble flooring, driftwood-hued finishes and white Italian slipcover sofas combine for an overall fresh and elegant impression. Tongue-and-groove wood ceilings are painted white and feature state-of-the-art recessed lighting. On arrival, the open floor plan provides a view through to the stunning blue sea beyond. Spaces flow effortlessly from one to the next, from the dining area, to the living room, to the verandah and pool.

From its third floor location, the king-bedded master room presents a 270-degree view of the glistening sea from its wraparound terrace, complete with loungers and a pergola on one side. The spacious master bath offers a dressing area with large granite double vanities, a wet room with free-standing soaking tub and two rainfall shower heads, and a private balcony to relax on after bathing. The second-floor guest room features two queen beds, a balcony with loungers and a spacious bathroom offering a layout and amenities similar to the master suite. A ground-level guest room is outfitted with two queen beds, a private veranda, spacious bathroom with dressing area, granite double vanities, wet room with two rainfall shower heads and tub, and a private outdoor garden shower area.

Outside, under the cool shade of a large cantilevered umbrella, loungers surround a sparkling swimming pool. From this poolside vantage point, guests enjoy gorgeous views of the ocean, and direct access to a small beach.
