Harmony, Casa de Campo, Dominican Republic Vacation Rental

 

Designed by the best Architect team in Casa de Campo this new modernist beauty is breaking records in elegance and charm. As you enter the best of locations facing Teeth of The Dog and the Caribbean sea, the villa opens to a very long, elegant pool and gazebo, displaying breath taking views.

All six bedrooms were designed to be very private, facing the sea and intimate interior gardens. Additionally, they each have their own private bathrooms with gray quartz flooring, modern showers, huge windows that allow Caribbean light to bathe the counter tops and basins, and luxurious walk-in closets fully lined in cedar. The most pleasant feature of these rooms is the combination so beautifully achieved, of beige hues, light greens, and woods, plus organic, drift-wood lamps and art pieces, resulting in total elegance and relaxation.

Two large master suites dominate the second floor, as well as a large and spectacular terrace. Both have breathtaking views of the Caribbean Sea and Teeth of the Dog Golf Course. TVs, DVDs, Music and Safety boxes on all Bedrooms. The remaining four guests suites are located on the first floor, one of them a wonderful master suite with direct golf and ocean view.

The villa is surrounded by luscious, meticulously landscaped tropical gardens, displaying hundreds of palms.
