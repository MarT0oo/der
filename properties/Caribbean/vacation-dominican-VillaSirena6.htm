Villa Sirena 6, Punta Cana, Dominican Republic Vacation Rental

 

Villa Sirena 6 is located In the exclusive Arrecife neighborhood of the Punta Cana Resort & Club. The resort features beachfront hotel accommodations, three miles of white-sand beach - the longest in the Caribbean, a beachfront Club House 0.1 mile walking distance from the Villa, plus a host of other activities close by such as water sports, horseback riding, tennis, nature reserve, children's activities and evening entertainment.

Located 5 minutes from the airport and 3 minutes from the spectacular 0.1-mile beach and sparkling turquoise waters.

This idyllic Caribbean Villa presents you with one of a kind accommodation experience!

The master bedroom includes a spacious bathroom and outdoor terrace. All rooms include air conditioning, private bathroom and closets. Fully equipped kitchen, extensive living room terrace & dining room, private courtyard with swimming pool.

This beautiful villa is located in the exclusive gated community of Punta Cana Resort & Club, a 26-square mile paradise that is blessed with a temperate year-round climate, light rainfall, and soft breezes. It's colorful coral reefs, calm crystalline waters and white-sand beaches are breathtaking. The Resort provides a matchless experience for all who visit this tropical paradise, and its tropically chic lifestyle is ideal for those who appreciate high standards of quality, comfort and aesthetics. As guests of the villa, you have access to all of the resort amenities and activities.
