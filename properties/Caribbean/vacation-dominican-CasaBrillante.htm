Casa Brillante, Los Corales, Dominican Republic Vacation Rental

 

This gorgeous Mediterranean villa with modern appeal is brand new and one of the best rentals in the Caribbean!

Guests enjoy comfortable teak furnishings in the living room and bedrooms and large overstuffed wicker and rattan on the pool terrace. An enormous 2nd level master suite features a spa bathroom with tempered glass walk in shower with rain fall shower heads, an outdoor terrace with jacuzzi and additional sun deck with trellis. You may not want to leave this master suite. Adjoining the bedroom is a television suite featuring a luxurious “L” shaped oversized couch. The second story landing features yet an additional outdoor deck used for a private reading nook or morning coffee retreat. A second master suite is a private pool bungalow with generous bathroom, private terrace and two showers- one in and one under the sun or moon.

The professional kitchen is a dream for cooks: stainless appliances, granite counters, a large bar area and a breakfast nook. The living room and dining room open completely to the terrace and starry nights. A perfect setting for a perfect Caribbean vacation! Two guests suites located off the living area are attractively appointed in dark wood furnishings, refreshing Caribbean linens and tasteful local art. The generous pool is large enough for laps.

Beds of ylang ylang, Caribbean anthuriums and lush ferns frame views from the pool and terrace. Dark wood, crisp white floors and Mediterranean style furniture harmonize perfectly with a touch of modern in this immaculate villa. This villa features many private areas for a group stay with diverse routines or habits. Master suites have king size beds, guest suites have queen size beds.
