Keela Wee, Discovery Bay, Jamaica Vacation Rental

 

Windows and trellises are draped with flowering vines, bougainvillea, hibiscus, shrimp flowers and oleander, attracting delicate hummingbirds and the yellowbird renowned for its song. Interiors are colorfully furnished in tropical colors and art.

Stepping stones lead 35 feet across the velvet lawn to Keela Wee's fine sand 100' beach and crystal clear water. Complimentary sea kayaks, paddle boards, snorkelling equipment and an offshore sunning platform enable carefree hours of sport and relaxation on the azure water just steps from the verandah.

From the living room, wide doorways open to a casual verandah and wet bar with TV. Just beyond is the glistening private pool with Italian-tiled circular seating at the deep end and adjacent hot tub. Keela Wee's tile floors, louvres, verandahs and flower-draped balconies evoke a Mediterranean ambiance ~ enhanced by views along the bay of a fishing village nestled by the water and white cottages dotting the hillside beyond.

The vernadah dining tables seat 12 guests for delicious home-cooked meals. Eating al fresco on the large seaside wooden deck with friends and family is a favourite of many. As a special treat, the staff will plan a torchlight beach party ~ chicken, lobster or fresh-caught fish grilled on the outdoor barbecue.

A gleaming wooden stairway and color-washed walls lead upstairs to a second sitting room with a large flat screen television, computer with printer/scanner, comfy overstuffed sofas and card table.
