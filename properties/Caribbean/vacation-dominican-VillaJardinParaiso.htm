Villa Jardin Paraiso, Sosua, Cabarete, Dominican Republic Vacation Rental

 

A Caribbean vacation villa that beautifully blends tropical living with clean modern architectural lines. The large main room and terrace is where guests enjoy breezy cross ventilation and casual dinners from the gourmet kitchen.

The large terrace features tropical lounge furnishings for when guests retire from the refreshing lap pool. The television room features a large screen projector television and central air conditioning. The layout is designed for rental privacy with two bedrooms upstairs and two bedrooms downstairs, all with bathrooms en suite, cedar closets and television. Upstairs rooms each have a king beds, downstairs one guest room has two full beds, and one guest room has a queen bed. Tropical art, a state of the art Italian kitchen, modern Mediterranean inspired design and an excellent location just a couple minutes walking to the Beach Club make this villa a fabulous Caribbean rental.
