Villa Blanche, Casa de Campo, Dominican Republic Vacation Rental

 

Villa Blanche, a hand picked house with all of the bells and whistles, 6 large bedrooms amazing grounds and enough room for family and friends. Amazing ocean views and use of the facilities of Casa de Campo Dominican Republic, what else can you ask for. Your personalized welcome and great staff will make this trip more than enjoyable

Walk into the soothing sound of the ocean on this beautiful two story six bedroom beachfront Caribbean style villa offering plenty of comfortable areas to enjoy a relaxing holiday. The living room sits right in the center, connected to the outside thru a series of sliding louvered doors, offering guests gorgeous views of the outdoors. The formal dining area, adjacent to the living room, seats fourteen guests and round breakfast table for eight is just outside. On the ground level you will find a guest master suite, two guest bedrooms and studio.

The ground bedrooms feature sliding louvered doors that open to the outside bedroom corridor where you can enjoy every morning amazing views of the property grounds and the Caribbean Sea. The spacious master suite, the third master suite and the kids bedroom are located on the second level. The outside gazebo, on top of the swimming pool, and a private beach are also part of the amenities.
