Cove Spring House, The Garden, St. James, Barbados Vacation Rental

 

Cove Spring House is the most coveted vacation home on Barbados; unique in its prime Platinum Coast location, atop coral stone cliffs which wrap around a secluded beach; sumptuous in its stunningly furnished interiors; intimate, private and secure in its magnificent walled and manicured gardens; architecturally designed for aesthetic all-day, outdoor living, entertaining and relaxing.

Every great house has its admirers. Among those who have chosen Cove Spring House for vacations are Oscar winners, supermodels, captains of industry and Forbes List financiers. And it's not just the absolute privacy, superlative luxury and five-star attention that appeals to discerning guests.

Ten exquisitely appointed, air conditioned en suite bedrooms with hand-picked Ralph Lauren furnishings can accommodate a party of up to 20. Expansive verandahs furnished with sofas and armchairs and cooled by ceiling fans offer a dozen private spots to while away an afternoon reading. An air conditioned sitting room for reception of guests prior to further entertaining, or privacy and reading. A magnificent private cinema with 20 reclining viewing chairs. Fine art throughout the property, including photographic prints by the renowned Terry O'Neill. An air conditioned fully equipped gym to tone bodies, or a business centre for when work calls. Wi-Fi facilities and an equipped office to ensure you maintain communications - when you want to - but only if you want to.

Many houses in Barbados are built at the water's edge of the island's all-public beaches. But very, very few can claim to have a beach all to itself. The natural coral stone cliff on which Cove Spring sits, encloses a safe, picture postcard sandy beach, protected by an offshore reef. The half-moon shaped fresh water swimming pool and adjoining spa is ringed with Balinese loungers and tropical planting. A meandering terrace to the water's edge offers secluded day beds, shaded by palms, or sun-deck loungers with wrap-over umbrellas. Dine at one of two sea side gazebos and chef station for al fresco lunches or butler-served pool snacks and attentive staff to replenish your glass. An intimate romantic dinner for two in one of two gazebos at sunset, or a lavish dinner party for 20 seated formally at the handmade ironwood table on the terrace or canapés and a cocktail party around the pool? Cove Spring House has the space, the expertise and the staff to give guests a five star service.

A professional house manager will discuss your daily requirements and organize everything, from a massage to dinner reservations, excursions or a private jet. Our dedicated staff of an on-site manager, chefs, butlers, maids, gardeners and attendants are trained to provide for your relaxation, comfort and dietary needs while ensuring your privacy and security when staying at Cove Spring. Cove Spring House is located on the most desirable stretch of Barbados' fabled "Platinum" west coast, where beach homes sell for up to $90million on the calm, palm-fringed Caribbean side of the island that now rivals St Tropez, Malibu and The Hamptons as one of the world's most upscale destinations. Mere minutes from a dozen world-renowned restaurants that rank alongside the best that London, Los Angeles or New York has to offer. Three championship golf courses, two spas and tennis clubs are less than two miles from Cove Spring House's gates. Twenty minutes from the airport with direct international flights to Miami, New York, London, Manchester, Toronto, Philadelphia, Montreal.
