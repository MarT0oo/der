Villa Jasmine, Taylor Bay, Chalk Sound, Providenciales, Turks and Caicos Vacation Rental

 

Villa Jasmine is a lovely Mediterranean style villa where indoors and outdoors meet effortlessly. Jasmine is built on high ground for the maximum amount of privacy and air circulation, which is why the main living area of the house is on the second floor. There is also a tower and rooftop sunning deck with 360° views and great ocean breezes.  Ideally situated on a narrow spit of land, this villa has a pool and is surrounded by the most beautiful colored water. The villa is a short two minute walk to the prestigious Taylor Bay Beach.

 

The upper level has a spacious terrace with wonderful views of the ocean and   spectacular sunsets. It has both open and shaded areas, a   16 x 20 pool and a wet bar, living room with one wall completely open to the terrace, large gourmet kitchen, which opens onto a back terrace   and views of Chalk Sound, master bedroom with a king-size bed, ceiling fan and en suite bathroom with a large shower.  French doors open onto the front terrace, which has a   great ocean view.  The second bedroom has a king-size bed, ceiling fan and en suite bathroom with a large shower. It too has French doors leading to the terrace and an equally good ocean view.  The third bedroom has ceiling fan and en suite bathroom  with a large shower. It's French doors lead to a balcony   looking towards the tennis courts. 

 

The Lower Level Features a bedroom with en-suite bath as well, it’s separated from the main house with it’s own entrance.  Amenities include A/C in all bedrooms only, beach towels, local hone calls, DVD Player, CD Player, TV with Cable, and crib.
