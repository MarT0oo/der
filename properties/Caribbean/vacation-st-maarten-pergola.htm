La Pergola, Terres Basses, St. Maarten Vacation Rental

 

A classic French West Indian vacation villa with fantastic views over looking Baie Longue Beach, the Caribbean Sea and Saba. This well designed property set in mature gardens, features an extensive terrace with shaded plantation style seating area, a large casual dining and BBQ Gazebo and an infinity-edged swimming pool that cascades down to a second pool.

 

The interior space is made up of 4 air-conditioned spacious bedrooms each with private bathroom and ocean view, a large and well equipped kitchen and a beautifully decorated living room and formal dining room.

 

Further details that add to the quality of La Pergola include: guest laundry facilities, ice maker, Wi-Fi internet access, living room with 40” flat screen satellite TV/DVD and superior stereo with indoor/outdoor speaker system, electric gate with security camera and utter privacy.

 

The perfect villa for friends and couples vacationing together, a family wanting to get away or just a quick honeymoon destination for those wanting a large home with all the amenities of a resort vacation.  You can also fully cater the villa if you would like to make your stay more enjoyable.
