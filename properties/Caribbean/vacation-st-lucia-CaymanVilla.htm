Cayman Villa, Cap Estate, St. Lucia Vacation Rental


Welcome to Cayman Villa: A luxurious villa situated high on Sea Breeze hills in Cap Estate. Cap Estate is a residential area with private homes and an 18 hole championship golf course, three hotels and restaurants and 2 beaches. Cayman Villa overlooks the Atlantic Ocean and is perfectly situated to enjoy the breathtaking views of Martinique (on a clear day) and the lovely Atlantic breeze.

The entrance to the house is into an open area which leads to a well equipped kitchen, dining room with a dining table to seat 8 persons and a living room. The open space is made complete with a bar, stocked with a beer and wine chiller, ice maker and an array of glassware. Just off the central living room is a media room with a 52 inch plasma TV, DVD player, Ipod dock and Sonos sound system. The sitting room opens out through glass doors onto a walkway with steps leading down to the large patio and pool area.

The main house comprises 3 well appointed bedrooms with a fully self contained cottage, adjacent to the house, which acts as the 4th bedroom. The spacious master bedroom suite has a four poster kingsize bed, flatscreen TV and ensuite bathroom with both a shower and a bath.
