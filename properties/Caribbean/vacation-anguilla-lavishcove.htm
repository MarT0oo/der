Reputed to be the island’s largest villa at 21,000 square feet, Lavish Cove Villa sits above the Cove Lagoon and is home to 4 Grand Villa Suites, 1 Loft Suite with a private sun terrace, 9 bathrooms, 3 pools, and a large state-of-the-art gym. Each lavish master suite has an air-conditioned walk-in dressing room, double European-style jet showers, infinity-edge bathtub encased in marble, double sinks with granite countertop, and bathroom lounge area with daybed for reading or simply lounging.

The two ground floor grand villa suites come with their very own pool for added privacy. Additionally, there is an adjacent Junior Garden Suite, accessed by a separate entrance with kitchenette, which is perfectly suited for a guest’s personal staff or additional guests. With two unique “bridges” leading into the main floor living areas and expansive travertine pool decks, Lavish Cove Villa defines the art of luxury escapism by offering unparalleled privacy and seductive elegance.
