Villa Stella, Montego Bay, Jamaica Vacation Rental

 

Villa Stella offers views of the Caribbean Sea and the Tryall Club estate from its two levels of spacious terraces.

Villa Stella is situated on the brow of Copperwood Ridge. It is an easy walk to Tryall’s world class tennis courts, its gym, spa and other amenities, and a 3-minute ride by the villa’s complimentary golf cart to the championship Tryall Golf course and private beach.

Designed with classic Caribbean architectural features, the villa is on two levels with lofty ceilings, expansive verandas and open living spaces. The spacious master suite has a queen-sized bed with a large en suite bathroom. Five other bedrooms have twin beds each which may be converted to king size if required. En-suite marble bathrooms equipped with waterfall showers accompany each bedroom. All bedrooms have air-conditioning, cable television and DVD players.

A wide entrance on the main floor leads to the living room that overlooks the sea and countryside. A covered terrace wraps around the house and is perfect for parties with a bar and a built-in stereo system. There is a formal dining room as well as an informal dining area on the upper veranda. The kitchen includes a wood burning pizza oven. A games room on the lower level with pool table, football and an eight foot HDTV projector provides entertainment for the young as does the large heated swimming pool and separate Jacuzzi tub outside.

The staff is comprised of a Chef, Butler, laundress, Housekeeper and gardener.
