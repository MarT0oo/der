Villa Le Bleu, Anguilla, British West Indies Vacation Rental


Seafront Estate is simply the most exciting and glamorous new villa in Anguilla (opened December 09).  This 10 bedroom villa (all en-suite) is located in approximately 2.5 acres of beautiful tropical gardens with two swimming pools, two small natural beaches, beach pavilion, tennis court, gym, personal cinema and beautiful water garden.

Seafront Estate is unique in both location and design. Spacious and modern - the experience it provides is one of fresh air, blue skies and the myriad colors of the sea.  The Villa offers fabulous open style living in a superb private location.

Seafront Estate and it’s Mini-Estate accommodation and facilities are sited in 2 areas. These areas are linked by attractive landscaped walkways which traverse the property. The spectacular living and entertaining areas are situated on the upper level of the Estate - which is accessed by walking through an exquisite water garden and ascending the imposing master staircase.

Seafront Estate:
6 king-size master bedrooms, all en-suite with magnificent ocean views – two upstairs on pool terrace, 4 with private terraces and direct access to garden/sea.

1 twin room (can be converted to king) with private terrace – also en-suite – links to   1 queen-sized master bedroom (en-suite) overlooking water garden.


Additionally:
If you have a large group you can rent both the Seafront Estate and the Cinnamon Reef Mansion and hold a Family reunion, large wedding or large corporate retreat.  Between both homes you can easily sleep 40 guests and more on cots and roll away beds.  This is a great option for those seeking a private and serene getaway.
