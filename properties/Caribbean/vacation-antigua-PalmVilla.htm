Palm Villa, Galleon Beach Resort, Antigua, Caribbean Vacation Rental

 

Located in the convenient yet private community of Galleon Beach Resort is newly built Palm Villa a luxury 4 -bedroom, 5-bathroom and self-contained studio v designed with elegance in mind.

Built into the hillside, the spacious living area has two living rooms to include television and office area with casual seating it opens to verandas on two sides. The comfortable outdoor dining area is located under the covered terrace, with seating for twelve and breakfast table seats 6

The two-story villa has four bedrooms on the main floor, with lower level living, it offers casual sofas, chairs, tables, dining, and pool, shower, lounging area with spectacular gardens surrounding the patios and balcony that overlooks the historic Nelson’s Dockyard and English Harbour. Also located on this level is a well-equipped cook's kitchen with every appliance and a dramatic bar, equipped with wine cooler, fridge, and small sink and bar stools, there is also a changing room with toilet & basin

The beach is just steps away at Galleon Beach where guests may use the facilities and amenities, including water sports and hourly ferry to Nelson’s Dockyard.
