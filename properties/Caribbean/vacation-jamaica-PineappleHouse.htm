Pineapple House, Montego Bay, Jamaica Vacation Rental

 

Luxury living is all about the details. Pineapple House has undergone a complete renovation, and it is prepared to please the most discriminating vacationers that appreciate luxury living. Distinctive in its architecture, Pineapple House’s extensive fret-work and ‘Oliver Messel’ inspired design is defined by its Messel-green shutters, coral stone exterior, authentic Saltillo terracotta tile and rustic elegance that is only found at the best Caribbean resorts. Pineapple House is a spacious villa located on Copper Ridge with a magnificent 180 degree view of the Caribbean Sea located on Jamaica’s exclusive North Shore.

The name 'Pineapple House' is an accurate description of the setting – warm, welcoming with plenty of privacy, peace and quietness. You’re located above the Tryall Club 18-hole golf course and the 180 degree views of the Caribbean Sea in the foreground are absolutely gorgeous.

With capabilities to comfortably sleep 10, Pineapple House is a premier villa located in the exclusive Tryall Club overlooking the ruins of the historic sugar plantation and the Caribbean Sea. In this unspoiled setting in the midst of world-class golfing and beaches, you're surrounded by a naturalist's world of sea, sky and pristine land. A short 5-minute walk from Pineapple House are the 18-hole golf course, tennis, fitness center, great house, kid’s club, and private beaches. If you value tranquility, natural beauty and rustic elegance, this is heaven on an island.

Accommodations:

Bedrooms: The five bedrooms are equal in size (about 250 ft²) and each includes a private balcony or deck facing the Caribbean Sea with elegant custom-designed cabinetry, Saltillo-terracotta tiled floors and ensuite bath. There are four master bedrooms outfitted with King-sized beds and one bedroom outfitted with twin beds that would appeal to the kid in all of us. The lower level master bedroom is handicap-accessible. All bedrooms are equipped with air conditioners with individual thermostats and ceiling fans. Smart televisions with capability to surf the internet, listen to music or check emails are installed in each suite.

Kitchen: The professional-grade kitchen is defined by granite, a mahogany-coffered ceiling, and lovely views to the front waterfall and rear pool, gazebo and Caribbean Sea. The kitchen is outfitted with a Vulcan 6-burner stove, grill and oven, side-by-side refrigerator/freezer, dishwasher, microwave, toaster, blenders, coffee maker, and abundant cookware and dinnerware.

Outdoor Living Area: As you enter the secluded courtyard of Pineapple House, you are welcomed by a massive waterfall and English garden. One of the most popular design features in an island home is the Lanai (outdoor living/dining area). Pineapple House takes great advantage of its lovely setting with its comfortable 2,500 square feet Lanai. This is more than a deck with furniture; it’s an outdoor living area with comfortable furniture and a 10 person dining table that protects you from the sun and the occasion island shower.

Pool: Heated for your comfort, your kids will love the 2 foot deep kiddy section, while the avid swimmer can practice their strokes in the 8 foot deep section.

Hot Tub: Rekindle friendships or enjoy your solitude with a glass of champagne in the hot tub. With audio and lighting special effects, the nighttime romance can be jump started right here.

Outdoor Bar: Your butler, Sanford can make you a mean Jamaican Rum Punch or enjoy our signature drink, ‘The Pineapple Painkiller’ at the outdoor mahogany bar. From the outdoor bar, you look at the 8-foot deep heated diving pool, hot tub, dining gazebo and beautiful views of the Caribbean Sea.

The photos leave no doubt that Pineapple House is a spacious, beautiful, and very comfortable luxury villa. Everything about this villa is appealing; from the bedrooms to the great room to the outdoor living area. This is a professionally designed, very tastefully decorated home with a distinct sense of island elegance. Pineapple House is an excellent vacation choice for large families of 10 or for 4-5 couples, and this romantic and beautiful setting is also well suited for honeymooners and lovers.
