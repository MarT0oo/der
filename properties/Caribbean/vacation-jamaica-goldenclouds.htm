Golden Clouds Estate, Oracabessa, Jamaica


Golden Clouds villa is a luxurious 12-suite estate situated in a park-like 7-acre setting. It is a spectacular oceanfront villa located on Jamaica's fabled North Coast in the picturesque village of Oracabessa. Located a fifteen minute drive from Ocho Rios, the villa overlooks a private palm fringed, white sand beach and the Caribbean Sea. Surrounded by manicured lawns and gardens, the property consists of a main villa with 10 suites and a garden cottage with an additional 2 suites and its own kitchen and living area. Each building has a large veranda and its own swimming pool.

Main features of the villa include private beach, 2 swimming pools, tennis court, gymnasium, 1/2 basketball court, volleyball, and shuffleboard.

About Us: With an 80% return rate for guests, Golden Clouds is recognized as one of the top villas in Jamaica. It is renowned for its excellent service staff, luxurious accommodations, superb and varied kitchen skills and preserving the simple island beauty in its natural state. This 5-star private villa is available for your pleasure, retreat, reunion or getaway. It is perfect for families on holiday, weddings or reunions, a business workshop or incentive trip, private retreats or whatever you may need. The relaxing environment and amenities offered can be your home away from home in a tropical paradise.

Golden Clouds has world-famous snorkeling directly in front of the property with multiple access points to the sea from the beach and by ladders, and features it’s own swimming platform located offshore for your convenience. Other recreational facilities include a tennis court, volleyball court, shuffleboard, basketball half-court, and a modern fully-equipped exercise room.

Accommodations: All suites at Golden Clouds have the same level of meticulous service and attention to detail that is found at 5-star hotels. The main difference in the various suites at Golden Clouds is the location on the property itself – with some suites having majestic ocean views, while others have limited garden or pool views.
