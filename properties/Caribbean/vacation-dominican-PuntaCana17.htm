Punta Cana 17, Punta Cana, Dominican Republic Vacation Rental

 

Punta Cana 17 is Equipped With 6 Bedrooms, 6.5 Bathroom, All Rooms With A/C

Escape and indulge in Punta Cana 17, a luxury villa with spectacular views and a truly relaxing atmosphere. Ultimate leisure, positive vibes and simple yet elegant living is sure to be the destination of choice for the weary traveler. Make your dreams come true in this private tropical paradise!

Punta Cana 17 integrates well with the beautiful Punta Cana environment and natural elements including rocks, Coralina limestone walls/floors, exotic woods and exciting landscapes. Surrounded by tropical vegetation, this hidden gem is perfect and unique for your long desired privacy and peaceful comfort.

This beautiful home is located inside the exclusive enclave of Tortuga Bay in Punta Cana Resort & Club. Home to such famed luminaries such as Oscar de la Renta, Mikhail Baryshnikov and Julio Iglesias, this property is within three award winning golf courses, two designed by PB Dye and the third is the world renowned Tom Fazio-designed Corales Golf Course. Also on the grounds is the only five star rated resort in the Caribbean, Tortuga Bay Hotel, with its award winning restaurant, Bamboo.

The 6 bedrooms, 6.5 bathrooms Punta Cana 17 has been stunningly sited in mature gardens by an award-winning Dominican landscape architect. The entire home is beautifully furnished with either imported or custom-made furniture and accented with pieces collected by the owners on their trips around Dominican Republic, Europe and Puerto Rico. The home has a sophisticated rustic and rural flair while maintaining a comfortable tropical elegance. It is surrounded by tropical vegetation and hidden between beautiful flora ensuring extreme privacy and calm atmosphere.

The full-time Villa staff includes the housekeeper and a handyman. The home also has full-time concierge service to assist you with all your needs. There is a golf cart included for transportation on the grounds of the resort with no extra charge.

Meal preparation is available at Punta Cana 17 for an additional charge and, if you choose to use this service, our concierge will work with you prior to arrival to arrange this so that your culinary needs are met. There are also eight restaurants within the resort, with many additional dining options located in Punta Cana Village outside the Resort, including international cuisine.

All bedrooms are cleaned daily and all laundry will be collected each morning and returned the same day washed and ironed upon request.

Punta Cana 17 is a high-end, luxurious six-bedroom beautiful home overlooking the 8th hole of P.B. Dye's stunning La Cana Golf Course and located in an exclusive, gated community within Punta Cana Resort & Club/Tortuga Bay.
