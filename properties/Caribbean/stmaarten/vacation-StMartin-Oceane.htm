Oceane, Terres Basses, St. Martin, Caribbean Vacation Rental

 

The Villa Oceane on St Martin is located in the exclusive gated community of Terres Basses just minutes away from the pristine Plum Bay Beach. This charming, family friendly villa is set amongst a gorgeous, flowering luscious green garden and the villa itself commands sweeping, stunning views of the nearby Caribbean Sea. Mediterranean in architecture, the interiors are spacious and elegantly furnished with a combination of wood, rattan and iron and glass pieces.

The home is truly defined by a light and airy ambience, clean design, spacious rooms and fabulous bathrooms. Then there is the state of the art kitchen fully equipped with all the necessary utensils to help you prep for the ultimate gourmet meal, offering bright blue tiled countertops and a delightful breakfast bar with sharp wrought iron bar stools. Just outside, there is a large furnished terrace just perfect for reading, napping or dining. A built-in table at the shallow end of the pool provides an ideal spot for just "hanging out". Beautiful Plum Bay Beach is located within a five minute walk of the villa.

Three of the bedrooms are fairly equal in size while the fourth bedroom (with twin beds) is somewhat smaller. Each of the four bedrooms has their very own private ensuite bathroom and three have king sized beds while the other has two twin beds which can be pushed together to form a king. Three bedrooms have interior access making villa Oceane an ideal choice for families with children.

Villa Oceane features 4 bedrooms and 4 1/2 bathrooms. The master suite features a king bed and en suite bathroom. The bedroom has exterior access to the pool area and interior access. The bedroom connects with a second bedroom that features 2 twin beds with it's own ensuite bathroom.

The remainging two bedrooms are located on the left side side of the villa, each with a king bed and ensuite bathroom. Bedroom three has interior and exterior access. Bedroom four only has exterior access.
