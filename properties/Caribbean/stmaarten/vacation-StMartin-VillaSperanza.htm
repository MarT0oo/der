Villa Speranza, Terres Basses, Saint Martin, Caribbean Vacation Rental

 

Speranza, this three bedroom contemporary villa is located on a hillside facing the Simpson Bay lagoon, and the Dutch side of St. Maarten, with captivating views both day and night.

Relish in lush green mountains, and the bright blue waters of the lagoon by day and the shadowed mountain peaks and bright city lights by night. Designed with this view in mind, you can enjoy it from every room in the villa.

Spend your days poolside on the outside terrace that extends the width of the villa featuring an oversized pool, and lounge chairs for six. No need to go inside to eat, connected to the terrace is a covered area for outside dining enclosed by flowing white drapes, and lit by harmoniously colored glass lights. From the terrace, step into the main living space that features an open-concept design with high ceilings, coastal home décor and a seamless transition from kitchen, dining and living area. Marble countertops and a breakfast bar await you in the full-equipped kitchen.

A total of six guests can easily stay in this villa’s three bedrooms that all have direct access from the pool terrace, and each suite boasts its own view, with its own distinct, ocean inspired furnishings. Two of the three bedrooms have king-sized beds, and the third bedroom has two twin beds. All suites have TV, air conditioning and en-suite bathrooms.
