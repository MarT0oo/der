Tiaris - La Samanna Villas, Baie Longue Beach, St. Martin, Caribbean Vacation Rental

 

Tiaris at La Samanna St Martin. The residences of La Samanna have already earned a sterling reputation for their beauty, elegance, and luxurious amenities. The all new Villa Tiaris at La Samanna lives up to this reputation, and even improves upon it. Set as it is overlooking the sea and positioned between the harbor town of Cupecoy and the coveted Baie Longue Beach, it offers some of the loveliest scenery and sea breezes imaginable.

Visitors to the four bedroom villa will appreciate the emphasis that the design of this property places on the outdoors. A wraparound infinity pool and terrace is stocked with daybeds, sun loungers, an al fresco dining area (with gas barbecue), and a range of open and covered spaces. Countless hours of leisurely relaxation are possible whether you are a sun worshipper or hoping to curl up in a shady nook to enjoy the latest best seller.

The interior of the villa is equally as impressive and has been decorated with a professional hand. Oversized rooms spill into one another to create a fluid living space. You will find the enormous gourmet kitchen (with convenient breakfast bar) spilling into the large, formal dining room. Columned archways visually divide spaces but never impede movement or comfort. These frame the entrance to the sunken living room where a vast banquet sofa encourages hours of conversation and relaxation. There is a full entertainment system here.

The four bedrooms of the villa are as luxurious as the rest of the property and feature private terraces, exquisite en suite bathrooms with luxury linens and toiletries, WiFi and more.

Villa Tiaris features four beautiful air conditioned bedroom suites with three king beds and one bedroom with two twins beds which can be switched out for a king bed. Each bedroom has an en suite shower. Three of the bathrooms have bathtubs. Each Bedroom has a TV, DVD player and CD Player.

With an onsite restaurant offering daily breakfasts as part of your rental rate, you can head right to the beach or just enjoy delicious local foods and then spend the day in your private paradise. The Villa Tiaris at La Samanna also comes with airport arrival and departure services, champagne and flowers on arrival, daily maid service and a host of other luxuries that make it one of the finest locations on the island.
