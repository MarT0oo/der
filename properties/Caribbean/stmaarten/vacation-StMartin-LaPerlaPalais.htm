La Perla Palais, Terres Basses, St. Martin, Caribbean Vacation Rental

 

Villa La Perla Palais St Martin. One of three villas in the La Perla Collection, the Villa La Perla Palais on St Martin can be easily described as the leading property in the group. Although all three have been exquisitely designed by Lulu by YSL Interiors, the settings cannot be ignored. Set on one of the most desirable and picturesque beach areas of St. Martin known as Baie Rouge, the Villa La Perla Palais uses the amazing scenery as the perfect backdrop for its excellent design.

Imagine entering the beautifully scaled property by crossing the life-sized outdoor chess game that is tucked into the private gardens. Head up the elegant stairs and you enter the living area. Open to the terrace, the view provides a remarkable sampling of all that is best about the Caribbean - air, sky, and the gorgeous hues of the sea. The unusual design of the pool leads the eye along the terrace and to the sands and beach beyond. You can enjoy a daily swim in the chlorine free pool, but you can also take a few steps and reach the sea.

The pool is flanked by lounging and dining areas ideally designed for up to six guests. There is also a media room and fitness area, a Jacuzzi, and the option for on-premises fitness support. The villa features a gourmet kitchen along with a smaller, fully equipped service kitchen. There is also a private spa room for professional massage treatments if desired.

Interestingly enough, the three La Perla properties can be booked together, providing up to five suites in all. The Villa La Perla Palais features three of those suites, each with a luxury king bed and large en suite bath.

Whether you want to enjoy a private and romantic experience in exquisite surroundings or to host a large event, look to the La Perla Palais and its sister properties for an exclusive and unforgettable retreat.

This villa can be rented on its own as three suites or alongside its two sister villas: La Perla Classic and La Perla Bianca providing 5 suites in all. Located within the gated residence of Baie Rouge in Terres Basses with 24 hr security.
