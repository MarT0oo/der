Bahia Blue, Terres Basses, St. Martin, Caribbean Vacation Rental

 

Welcome to Bahia Blue, a four bedroom tropical paradise nestled in the verdant landscape of Terres Basses in the locale of Baie Rouge, on St. Martin’s northern coastline.

The South American vibe permeates the house with its two-level cascading jungle-pool, edged with tropical foliage and sleek interiors accented with hardwoods, natural stone, and bright acid colors to heat up the mix.

The house is set up for outside living and entertaining with a beautiful pool deck area; a haven for sun worshippers and a palm-fronded poolside gazebo providing a cool respite from the noon-time sun.

The outside dining area and fully equipped wet bar area with large gas grill make “al-fresco” entertaining a breeze. At night the terrace is transformed with dramatic lighting to “jungle” with a backlight of Marigot Bay. A staircase leads up from this main terrace to a rooftop sunning deck with oversized loungers and stunning views over Marigot Bay and the hills in the distance.

High cathedral ceilings provide cooling airflow to the light-filled open-plan interiors: a sleek modern kitchen with central work island and high-end appliances and a comfortable TV viewing area.

All four bedroom suites* feature sumptuous en-suite bathrooms, private access, and terrace area and large walk-in closets. The master suite also features a hot tub in its own private garden.

Bahia Blue offers an idyllic setting and exceptional amenities, if you can bear to leave the house, the beach is only a 10-minute walk!
