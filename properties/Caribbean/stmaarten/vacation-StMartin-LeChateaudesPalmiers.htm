Le Château des Palmiers, Plum Bay (French Side), St. Maarten


Le Château des Palmiers is a world-class beachfront estate that includes a 6-bedroom Ocean-side Villa and a 4-bedroom Garden-side Villa. Situated on the white sand beach at Plum Bay, this fabulous property provides an intimate, relaxed and attentive environment within the walls of a gated enclave.

The many amenities include a heated pool, an open air and air conditioned fitness center, a tennis court, and covered outdoor bar, billiards and dining areas.

The Ocean-side Villa features 6 king size bedrooms with full ensuite baths and 3 half baths, including a two-story master suite with a master bath with jacuzzi tub and two private balconies facing the pool & ocean. One of the guest bedrooms has 2 king size beds which is great for a family of 4. All bedrooms are fully-airconditioned and equipped with Wi-Fi access, flat screen TV + Apple TV, phone and a safe to store your personal belongings.

The remaining interior spaces include a fully equipped kitchen, a formal living room, a formal dining room seating 10 and a casual outside dining area and a media room.

The 4 bedroom, 4 full/1 half bath Garden-side Villa features the “Jungle Room” with king size bed and small living room, the “Palm Room” with king size mahogany bed and a 2 bedroom suite with queen size beds, a shared living room and cozy dining room. There is also a huge commercial catering kitchen. All bedrooms are fully-airconditioned and equipped with Wi-Fi access, flat screen TV + Apple TV, phone and a safe to store your personal belongings.

Other amenities include air conditioning in all rooms, an outdoor sound system and internet access. All bedrooms have independent DSS TV receiver & VCR, and the media room and master bedroom have large satellite dish & DSS. There is an on-site Chef and experienced security staff available along with a state-of-the-art security system. Enjoy the beautiful Caribbean sunsets from this stunning one-of-a-kind Château and experience the vacation of a lifetime.
