Bahari, Cupecoy, St. Maarten, Caribbean Vacation Rental

 

Bahari in Shore Point is a stunning condo located in the exclusive Shore Point community in Cupecoy. Shore Pointe is directly across the street from Porto Cupecoy. Clients who rent Bahari are only steps away from boutiques, restaurants, a white glove marina, and a supermarket.

Bahari is a 3 bedroom property that has been designed to offer two floors of amazing indoor and outdoor living space. The ground floor leads to the pool which is directly facing the Caribbean ocean. The pool features a lounge and bar perfect for a relaxing day at the villa. The second floor is covered which is the ideal spot to enjoy the view and sea breeze of the ocean. There is also a hot tub located on the terrace.

As you walk through the entrance of Bahari you will come to an open floor layout that includes a large sofa, a dining table, and a fully equipped gourmet kitchen. The marble countertops and modern appliances will make it a delight to prepare a meal. The first floor also has an office, a media area, and laundry room.

The first bedroom is on the lower level, which gives to the terrace and the pool. The remaining suites are upstairs and each have a king bed, private ensuite bathrooms and private terraces.

Bahari is located directly Cupecoy Beach which is a clothing optional beach.
