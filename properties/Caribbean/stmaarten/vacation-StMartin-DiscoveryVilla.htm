Discovery Villa, Orient Bay, St. Martin, Caribbean Vacation Rental

 

Discovery Villa is a stylish and contemporary 3 bedroom villa located in the heights of Orient Bay in the gated residential area of Mont Vernon III with views east to the Atlantic Ocean.

The villa has its own private infinity pool and attractive sun deck, like the deck of a yacht, with a shade sail and stainless steel and glass surround.

Designed for a seamless flow between the interior and exterior, the sleek white interior spaces open out to the covered terrace for outside dining and lounging. The beautiful gleaming white kitchen is very well-equipped with top-of-the-line appliances and features a central island with hob and also serves as a breakfast bar.

The three king size bedrooms have air-conditioning and access to the pool terrace. Each room has its own color accent of plum, lemon yellow and orange, contemporary art and luxurious en-suite bathrooms with chic Italian stone tiling.

The residence is located within a short driving distance to Orient Bay beach with its beach bars, restaurants and water sports. The commercial centre of Hope Estate is close by for shopping and dining.
