Terre Azure, Terres Basses, St. Martin, Caribbean Vacation Rental

 

Caribbean living at its best in this beautiful 4 bedroom home in Terres Basses, St. Martin. Terre Azure enjoys a fabulous waterfront location offering exceptional sunset views across the Simpson Bay lagoon and beyond to the Caribbean Sea and the island of Saba.This beautiful residence has all the modern amenities and features over 5000 ft² (500 sq.m) of luxurious interiors including 4 bedrooms and 4 & 1/2 bathrooms, a large living room, a media room, an office and a fully equipped kitchen.

Extensive terraces surround the large 50ft x 30ft. (15m x 10m) infinity pool and Jacuzzi and a poolside gazebo area provides ample space for shaded outdoor dining and lounging.

All four bedrooms feature large en-suite baths with showers and two are part of a "Master Suite" which includes a private outdoor terrace and large outdoor Jacuzzi tub on a deck overlooking the lagoon. The remaining 2 bedrooms are located on the opposite side of the house, facing the infinity pool and overlooking the lagoon with views of Saba in the distance. Each of these two independent bedrooms has its own full bath and can be accessed by guests from either the poolside or the gated driveway carport arrival area. An additional 1 bedroom, 1 bath unit is located on a lower level below the pool.

The property also features a boat dock for access to the Simpson Bay lagoon.
