Anegada, Tamarind Hill, Gibb's Bay, St. Maarten, Caribbean Vacation Rental

 

Located in the heights of Tamarind Hill, in Dutch St. Maarten, this beautiful four bedroom, four and a half bath home offers spectacular ocean views over nearby Gibb’s Bay and in the distance, the neighboring island of St. Barth.

The fabulous view can be seen in all its glory from the large columned pool terrace running the length of the house, housing a 30ft. (9 m) long pool, with comfortable shaded lounging and areas for sunbathing.

Large glass sliding doors open from the terrace into an oversized and fully air-conditioned living space equipped with a dining area for 8 persons, a comfortable lounge and TV viewing area and an open-plan designer kitchen equipped with stainless steel appliances and a large marble-topped breakfast bar.

Each of the four king size bedrooms features beautiful Italian stone tiled bathrooms with large open showers and double vanities.

Villa Anegada is situated just a few minutes from beautiful Gibb’s Bay and the Dutch side capital, Philipsburg is a just a 15-minute drive away. Anegada is also close to Orient Bay and Grand Case where you can find some of the best restaurants on the island of St. Martin/St. Maarten.

All of the bedrooms are equally sized and situated under one roof making the house perfect for families or couples traveling together.
