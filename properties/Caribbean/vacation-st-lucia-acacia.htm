Villa Acacia, Becune Point, Cap Estate, St. Lucia Vacation Rental


Villa Acacia is a light, cool and spacious villa, which has been extensively renovated by its current owners and is situated at Becune Point, Cap Estate.Â  The villa is completely fenced and gated and is situated on a hillside in an acre of lush landscaped gardens and gently sloping lawns. The villa enjoys a wonderful breeze all year round with fabulous views out to Pigeon Island National Park, and across the Martinique Channel towards the French island of Martinique.

You enter the house into an expansive living/dining room, which is well furnished and equipped with Cablevision TV, DVD, Stereo and an I-pod speaker system. A spacious front veranda leads off the sitting room and is fully furnished with lounge chairs and a dining suite for alfresco dining. Relax here and enjoy the wonderful views. The back patio is extensive and very private, with a covered sitting area and further shaded and open areas. There is a large swimming pool, an 8 person Jacuzzi and a covered bar area with a built in barbeque.

The first floor comprises of three air-conditioned double bedrooms; all with ceiling fans. The master suite features a four poster king size bed, Cablevision TV and a spacious en-suite shower room. Bedroom II has a queen size bed with an en-suite shower room and bedroom III has a king size bed and an adjacent shower room.

A separate ground floor annex is available for those guests wishing to house a larger party. This comprises two air-conditioned double bedrooms with shared bathroom facilities. A separate air conditioned room is available upon request. A sitting area and kitchenette finish off the ground floor annex accommodation. There is a large covered patio area with views of the lush garden area in front of the house.
