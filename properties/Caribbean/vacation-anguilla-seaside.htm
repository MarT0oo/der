Villa Paradise, Anguilla, British West Indies Vacation Rental


Villa Paradise is an exquisite 7,000 square foot, gated vacation home surrounded by 3.5 acres of magnificently landscaped gardens overlooking the sea- the home is one of the most luxurious villas in the Caribbean as well as one of the most beautiful luxury homes on Anguilla…a preferred hideaway for celebrities and royalty / the rich and famous / the less known but well-travelled.

Villa Paradise combines natural beauty with quiet elegance while offering unrivaled views across the crystalline Caribbean Sea to the majestic mountains of St. Maarten, making it one of the most sought-after luxury villa rentals in Anguilla. The villa's protected harbor with boat dock and small, private cove are located in front of the villa while the adjacent coral reef offers some of the best snorkeling opportunities in Anguilla. Villa Paradise is completely walled in and gated for highest level of security.

The spacious living room is located on the upper level and opens onto two terraces with stunning views. Amenities included a 55” LED TV, Bose sound system, an office area equipped with computer, internet, printer and fax. The villa boasts a German manufactured MIELE kitchen with GE Monogram appliances and granite work tops which will please even the most discerning tastes-dining options include the formal indoor dining area that seats 8 or the outdoor covered dining pavilion that seats 12.

In the main house, there are a total of six bedrooms and six bathrooms on two levels, both levels having lovely sea views. On the upper level there are three bedrooms: The Grand Master Bedroom has a King size bed, en suite marble bath, and a large private terrace. The second bedroom has a Queen bed and the third bedroom has three Twin beds. On the lower level there are three additional bedrooms: Another Master Bedroom with a King size bed and en suite master bath – a second bedroom with a King size bed and a third bedroom with two Queen beds each of these also having en suite baths. In addition on the lower level, there is a seventh bedroom located in a separate studio apartment with a private entrance and terrace, kitchenette and King size bed.

All bedrooms feature pillows by Ralph Lauren and are equipped with the finest amenities, air-conditioning, ceiling fans, LED TV, DVD players and stereos. All bathrooms are fitted with custom-made marble and granite counter tops and feature accessories by Rolex, Kohler, and Villeroy Boch.

Villa Paradise was updated and renovated in 2015. A significant part of the renovations includes a gracious 28’ x 69’ infinity edge pool spilling over to the Caribbean Sea and luxurious hot tub surrounded by an ivory stone deck with remarkable views. In addition, there are shaded outdoor areas featuring double hammocks…all in all, a marvelous opportunity for personal use with impressive rental options.
