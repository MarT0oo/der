Bon Vivant Villa, Sandy Lane Estates, St. James, Vacation Rentals Barbados


Paradise is waiting for you on the west coast of one of the most luxurious islands in the world. Imagine a private villa nestled on a two and a half acre tropical garden, appointed for leisure and sports, and staffed to serve your every need. This is no dream. This Bon Vivant, Barbados.

Recognized by "The London Times" as "one of the most gracious getaways for the well-heeled," Bon Vivant is a world class vacation villa located in the renowned Sandy Lane Estates. At Bon Vivant, you'll enjoy colorful sunsets, warm gentle breezes and the soothing warmth of the radiant Caribbean sun.

Barbados' English heritage and tradition will enchant you. The fragrance of frangipani, hibiscus and bougainvillea will surround you. Built for recreation, Bon Vivant will satisfy your needs for leisure or sports. Relax or exercise in the private Jacuzzi or fifty foot swimming pool. The flood lit tennis court is perfect for cool evening play. There's an indoor air-conditioned racquet ball court, a traditionally-styled billiards room, a fully equipped gymnasium, terrazzo ping-pong table, shuffle board and trampoline.

Of course there is life beyond Bon Vivant. A three minute walk brings you to an eighteen hole golf course and the Sandy Lane Hotel. Here you can relax on a private beach at Bon Vivant's own beach cabana complete with sun chairs, refrigerator and the crystal clear waters of the Caribbean. At the Sandy Lane Sports Shop you can arrange to go sailing, wind surfing, snorkeling or scuba diving. In Barbados there are historical sites to see, excellent shopping, superior dining, outrageous night-clubbing or just enjoying the beauty of the coral sand beaches.

It's a world of difference beginning with an elegant and stately villa. The ten room main house features four bedrooms decorated with beautiful floral and tropical prints. Each bedroom has its own private bath. Relax after a day in the sun in the coral stone living room. This open room is perfect for calm, quiet evenings or gracious entertaining. Dine in the villa's richly appointed dining room in an elegant setting of fresh island flowers, candlelight and soft music. The perfect complement to the main house is the guest villa. Each of its two bedrooms has its own bath. This villa is complete with a living room, dining room and kitchen. French doors open from the living room to the pool and tennis court.

Bon Vivant is truly where all your needs are met. We want to spoil you at Bon Vivant. An English housekeeper, cooks, butleress, maids and gardener see to your every need. You'll be treated with well-mannered charm in the tradition of the island's English heritage. Enjoy freshly cooked Barbadian gourmet delicacies; be delighted by tropical fruit and flowers picked daily on the estate.
