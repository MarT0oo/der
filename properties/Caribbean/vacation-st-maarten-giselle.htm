Giselle, Terres Basses, St. Maarten Vacation Rental


Villa Giselle St Martin. The Terres Basses community in St. Martin is one of the most coveted for its beauty and its scenic location, and the delightful Villa Giselle provides visitors with an idyllic place to enjoy this area in luxury and style.

This five-bedroom gem sits in its own private gardens, surrounded by mature trees and plantings, and offers a stunning range of indoor and outdoor living spaces that can easily create your ideal holiday getaway. The focal point for most visitors is the infinity-edge pool and its surrounding terrace. Paved in silky smooth stone, the terrace wraps the pool, and it all faces the west and provides an unbelievable daily sunset view.

From the terrace, you can enjoy a swim, step into the whirlpool tub, bask in one of the many sun beds, swing from the hammock, or step beneath the portico area where al fresco dining, barbecuing, or lounging are all possible. The porch spaces are generously furnished and are open to the terrace as well as into the great room and shared living spaces.

These include the oversized, gourmet kitchen that provides uninterrupted views along with a bevy of high-end appliances. There is a breakfast bar, formal dining room, and several lounging niches spread throughout the air and contemporary great room. High ceilings, modern and stylish furnishings, and a soothing palette make this a fantastic area for gatherings and relaxation. A media room is tucked into one corner too.

The five bedrooms are also designed in this same airy and contemporary style, offering neutral palettes and colorful accents that make each space a welcome retreat. Four of the suites offer king beds, generous living spaces, sea views, terrace access and luxurious ensuite baths. A fifth bedroom suite is set apart and is a self-contained area ideal for staff or teens.

Bedrooms 1 - 5: Bedrooms with a king-size bed, A/C, ceiling fan, TV, DVD, walk-in closet, ocean view, hair dryer and en-suite bathroom with shower, vanity and WC. 2 guests maximum.

The impressive Villa Giselle provides you with the perfect base from which to enjoy the island, or it can be your destination all on its own!
