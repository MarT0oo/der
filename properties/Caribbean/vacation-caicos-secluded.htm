Secluded Caribbean Villa, Salt Cay, Turks & Caicos, B.W.I. Vacation Rentals


On a remote Turks and Caicos Island, a half an hour flight from Providenciales, exists a rare opportunity to rent a 19th century British Colonial, 3 bedroom, 2.5 bathroom house.  Secluded Caribbean Villa is located on Salt Cay, one of the last holdouts of the Old Caribbean, where strong Bermudian architecture and relics, mingle with rustic vegetation, and azure blue water.  Antique stone walls keep out donkeys and cows that freely roam the streets.

With spectacular ocean views to the west and calming salina views to the east, Secluded Caribbean Villa is situated in the heart of historic Balfour Town.  The villa is within walking distance of 2 restaurants, a grocery store and the island dock, yet secluded and private.

Newly restored, with great attention to quality and detail, the house offers such 19th century d√©cor, as beamed ceilings, ship knee supports, wood lined walls and multiple large shuttered doors.  A new, modern kitchen and baths, satellite TV and high speed internet connection, bring the house into the 21st century.   In keeping with the original origins of the house, it is decorated with antiques and historical tropical decorations.  The house is elegant and stylish and abounds with comfortable lounging areas indoors and out.  Read in the ocean front gallery, or relax on the east veranda, the house and grounds are private and spacious.

Secluded, quiet, peaceful, and serene, Salt Cay is more than a Caribbean vacation, it is an experience of tranquility, character, and soul.  It amuses, delights, enraptures, captivates and relaxes.  Come to the Secluded Caribbean Villa, for a one of a kind adventure, in a one of a kind house, on a one of a kind island.
