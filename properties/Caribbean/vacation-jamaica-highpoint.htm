Point of View, Montego Bay, Jamaica Vacation Rental


Point of View is a truly spectacular deluxe villa perched on the top of Barnes Hill and located within the famous Tryall estate. This villa boasts breathtaking views of the Caribbean Sea from every point. The wonderful spacious veranda is a lovely focus point for relaxing, entertaining and casual dining. From here, views to the sparkling swimming pool seems to be at the edge of the property, then views beyond to the famous Tryall golf course and the blue azures of the ocean.

The interiors of the villa are designed for comfort and elegance. Furniture of rich mahogany and exotic imported teak feature in this home. The bedrooms are large and spacious, all are fan cooled, sitting on the hill, enjoying the tradewinds from the Caribbean sea. Each bedroom enjoys their own private bathroom. Master bedroom suite is large, luxurious, has king sized bed, own private veranda, lovely sitting area and adjacent bath.

Other four bedrooms are also spacious and well appointed, all open to spectacular views. Special to this villa is the fitness room with exercise climber and weight machine. There is also an office complete with desk and fax machine. There is surround sound stereo system which can be heard on the outside verandas. There are spacious verandas that run the whole length of this house and each special area has wonderful lounges and hammocks for total relaxation.

For the business-minded, Point of View features an office, complete with a fax machine. The villa offers many options for leisure time. The private swimming pool with its dramatic edge finished in hand-cut antique brick is the perfect place for days of easy living. For the fitness conscious, an exercise room features a versa climber and health max weight machines. With your comfort in mind, there is stereo surround sound available throughout the villa.

Point of View is pleased to provide its guests with the use of two complimentary golf carts. Your experienced and friendly staff of ten will ensure the perfect retreat. They include a welcoming team of two butlers, cook, security officer, two housekeepers, laundress and three gardeners.

This luxurious and private villa is very comfortable graces with wonderful gardens which are tended by 3 gardeners. Audrey is the head of house hold and will make sure all runs smoothly at Point of View. The complimentary Tryall club membership is there for your enjoyment. A complimentary shuttle will take you to the resort and its facilities which is only a few minutes away.
