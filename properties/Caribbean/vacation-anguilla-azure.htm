Azure Villa, Anguilla, British West Indies Vacation Rental


Designed by world renowned Caribbean architect Carl Richards to take advantage of one of the most spectacular views in all of the Caribbean with a location overlooking Anguilla's best beach, this gated estate offers luxury vacationing with all the sumptuous amenities.


Villa Azure is a 5 bedroom Mediterranean architectural masterpiece with four Levels and six decks and terraces, offering and true indoor and outdoor living in 9,000 square feet of spacious comfort. Located in the center of Anguilla's largest marine park with the islands best snorkeling and swimming directly in front of the Villa, you will be mesmerized by a thousand of shades of blue, and soothed by the sounds of the waves at this waterfront retreat.

Overlooking Anguilla's fabulous Shoal Bay Beach, its powder soft sands are available to you any time you desire. You will be swept away by the amazing natural beauty, privacy and total comfort. Villa Azures coveted location, exquisite attention to detail and tailoring to the desires of our guests has cumulated in the perfect holiday retreat.  If you start with a truly amazing island that is the chosen destination of the luxury traveler, and painstakingly find the ideal location with the most incredible view imaginable, next to the beach voted the "best beach in the world" by Cande Nast Traveler, USA Today and Fodors, how do you compliment and perhaps even elevate Mother Nature's breathtaking creation? At Villa Azure, we created an oasis for all of the senses, where guests are pampered with genuine hospitality and service, touched by the sweetness of local warmth and charm, with an unparalleled commitment to the finest experience possible.  Villa Azure was created to provide the ultimate in private escapes- away from the crowds of large resorts without sacrificing the service and care the luxury traveler has come to expect. Villa Azure is about luxury, in a relaxed warm way.

The entire design of the Villa was developed to completely transform and relax you. From the precise placement of the walls of glass that open to allow the tropical scented trade winds blow through, to the enveloping furnishings, you will feel the stress of the real world melt away as you take your first steps through the eighteenth century hand carved doors.

Five spacious, sumptuously appointed bedrooms are havens of ultimate comfort and supreme elegance, fully air-conditioned with ceiling fans and Italian handcrafted Egyptian cotton linens. You and your guests have the option of renting as many bedrooms as you need depending upon the size of your party. Enjoy the amazing vanishing edge pool, large covered and open-air decks, heated spa, romantic thatched roof bar and steps to the sea. All bedrooms face the soothing azure ocean and open on to a deck or terrace.


Shoal Bay beach is over 2 miles long and is rated one of the best beaches in the world, with "the whitest sand on earth" and is only a short distance away. Perfect for long walks on its perfect powder soft sand, you can enjoy its wonderful restaurants, amazing snorkeling, and scuba right off the beach.

Although Anguilla has always been known for its stunning beaches, it is the people that will truly endear you to this island. It is the Caribbean you always dreamed of.
