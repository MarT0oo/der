Jamoon, Sandy Lane, St. James, Barbados Vacation Rental


Jamoon is a very stylish 7 bedroom villa in Barbados with interiors that mix classical and contemporary styles influenced by the Italian owners. It is located on the prestigious Sandy Lane Estate with access to the property owners beach facility. 

 

There are five spacious bedrooms on the ground floor with 2 of them interconnecting and sharing a large bathroom - the other bedrooms, including the 2 on the first floor all benefit from their own marble en-suite bathrooms. There are a 2 large master bedrooms, one on the ground floor and the other upstairs.  All the bedrooms have safes and are air conditioned with ceiling fans.  The bedrooms offer an assortment of King, Queen and Twin beds.

 

This villa in Barbados is built around an impressive swimming pool (16m x 10m) that has been specifically designed at the shallow end for pool volley, badminton etc and the deep end connects directly to the lounge area of the villa and is connected by small wooden bridges. As the sun sets, the lighting changes color complementing the setting and adding to the magic of the Caribbean night.

 

The main lounge is very spacious with high ceilings and tasteful furnishings including a baby grand piano. Off the lounge at the back is the informal outside lounge and dining area with sea grass sofas and chairs. The house also has a separate study with broadband internet access, fax and copier included and also a separate television room with big screen Cable television, DVD and Video.

 

Guests staying at Jamoon have access to the Property Owners beach facility at Sandy Lane beach which includes sun loungers.
