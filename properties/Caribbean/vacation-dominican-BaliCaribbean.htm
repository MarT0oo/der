Bali Caribbean, Casa de Campo, Dominican Republic Vacation Rental

 

Bali Caribbean in Casa de Campo Dominican Republic is a mix of the beautiful Spanish feeling and the amazing local architecture. Beautiful bright colors and lovely amenities make this a one of a kind experience. Bring your entire family and feel right at home. A curated home from our new Casa De Campo Collection.

A magnificent and beautiful four bedroom villa located right at the top of the Chavon River offering spectacular views of the river and the Caribbean Sea.

The villa was designed in a Balinese style offering great areas of relaxation throughout the house. Divided in three areas the villa provide you with all the amenities you require, from a master bedroom with it's own private balcony and a whirlpool bath with amazing views of the Chavon River to spacious living areas and study to a modern infinity edge swimming pool with Jacuzzi, lounge gazebo, BBQ and a pool bar for the entertainment of your guest and family.

The villa also offers a car with driver and a 36 feet boat for guests use. Restrictions apply.
