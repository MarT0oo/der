Eagle’s Nest, Montego Bay, Jamaica Vacation Rental

 

With over 2000 square feet of living space, Eagle’s Nest is the largest of Tryall’s one bedroom condominium suites and offers airy accommodations with fascinating views from its elevated promontory.

Honeymooners, couples and small families will find Eagle’s Nest has ample places to steal away, or come together for family fun, while being just a stroll away from the hub of the Club’s activities.

Eagle’s Nest projects from the crest of the hill at the heart of the Tryall Club, adjacent to the elegant 18th century Georgian Great House. It is only 2-3 minutes by golf cart or property shuttle, to the Club’s private beach and of course, the legendary Tryall Golf Course. The world class tennis courts, fitness centre, swimming pool, Kid’s Club, Massage centre, and beauty salon are just a short stroll from the suite. The entire suite is fully air conditioned. The bedroom has a king size bed, and downstairs, the living room has two queen sized Murphy beds for extra sleeping. The en suite bathroom has a large shower/tub combination and the bedroom’s balcony has sweeping views of the forests, sea and Tryall Golf Course.

A small staircase leads to the suite’s spacious lower level fitted with an open plan living area. A fully equipped kitchen, bar and dining area for 6, as well as a second full bathroom are on this level. The living area opens to a comfortably sized lounging terrace which is perfect for al fresco dining and with the same mesmerizing views as from the bedroom.

A full-service Housekeeper provides maid, laundry and breakfast service. Private Cook/Chef or butler services can be arranged for dinners.

Movement around the property is free by the property shuttle and golf carts are available to rent.
