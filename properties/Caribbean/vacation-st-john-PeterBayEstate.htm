Peter Bay Estate, Upper Peter Bay, St. John, Caribbean Vacation Rental

 

Peter Bay Estate is a magnificent five bedroom Italian style villa overlooking the breathtaking Caribbean beaches & bays on the north shore of St. John, Virgin Islands. Located at Peter Bay Estate, St. John’s most prestigious address, Peter Bay Estate offers luxury, exclusivity, and total privacy amidst spectacular vistas.

With beautiful art, HDTV’s and a luxurious living room set, Peter Bay Estate is a magnificent 5-star Caribbean villa for sophisticated travelers.Peter Bay Estate enjoys a spacious ocean-view deck with breathtaking views, pool, alfresco dining, Caribbean views from every room, tropical landscaping, and a beautiful arched stone veranda for elegant outdoor living and entertaining.

As VIP guests at Peter Bay Estate in Upper Peter Bay, you have access to private Peter Bay beach, as well as being moments away from the world class virgin beaches of the north shore such as Trunk Bay, Cinnamon Bay and Caneel Bay. Peter Bay Estate is exclusive, tranquil, private, and exceptionally scenic.
