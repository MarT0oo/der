Casa Grande, Grace Bay, Providenciales, Turks and Caicos Vacation Rental

 

Come, relax and enjoy this private walled and gated Spanish style beachfront villa. Casa Grande is ideally located on the north shore of Providenciales , on the award winning Grace Bay beach which offers 12 miles of powdery white sand and crystal clear turquoise waters. A true paradise!

You wouldn't want to be anywhere else except on the breathtaking beach or relaxing on the pool deck. Enjoy walking on the beach, swimming in the warm turquoise water, watching gorgeous sunsets from the covered patio deck and nightly skies filled with shining stars, from the rooftop lounge.

Completely remodeled and expanded in 2011 and privately set on an acre of incredible landscaped grounds, this fabulous home is conveniently located just minutes away from restaurants, bars, attractions, and fantastic snorkeling sites.

Every possible amenity has been provided for complete enjoyment inside and outside the home. From bikes to kayaks to movies and games, you'll never run out of things to do, just time to enjoy them. Open concept living provides the ideal atmosphere for socializing, or you can simply retreat to one of the secluded sanctuaries when privacy is desired.

Fully furnished and air conditioned with 5 bedrooms including 1 master bedroom suite, 5 en-suite bathrooms and 1 powder room. Luxurious linens, beds with plush mattresses, and feather-down pillows ensure a great night rest. It's a great way to end a busy day filled with fun activities. Make your reservation today and enjoy the vacation of a lifetime.
