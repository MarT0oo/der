Carissa, Mustique, Grenadines, Caribbean Vacation Rental

 

Created by Mustique's leading architect and builder, the late Arne Hasselqvist, this chic plantation-style villa set in six acres of lush gardens was refurbished in 2014/15. The villa now boasts a contemporary interior design and all modern amenities, including an infinity pool with a fully-stocked gazebo bar and a floodlit tennis court. Centrally located, Carissa offers its guests unsurpassed western views of Britannia Bay and southern views of The Grenadines

Contemporary open living spaces, shaded verandahs, a dramatic dining pavilion, an office, a television room, indoor bar complete with billiards table for guests' comfort and enjoyment
