Villa Punta Cana, Punta Cana, Dominican Republic


Welcome to a beautiful Ocean View Villa, located at a Golf Course. Just 5 minutes away from Punta Cana International Airport. The Beach is just 300 meters from the Villa and so is the Club House, with 2 restaurants, 1 Bar and the Six Senses Spa.

This stunning house was design with high standard of comfort and relaxation of our guest's.
