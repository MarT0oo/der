Turtle Cottage, Soldiers Bay, Blue Waters, Antigua, Caribbean Vacation Rental

 

On the North West corner of Antigua among 17 acres of tropical hibiscus and palm trees, Blue Waters resort perches alongside the Caribbean’s radiant turquoise sea. Dainty pagoda roofs and white parasols line the sandy bay, making up the multiple award winning and one of the longest standing luxury resorts in the Caribbean.

The latest addition to the Blue Waters collection of beach accommodation is Turtle Cottage, a four bedroom villa with stunning views of the Caribbean Sea. With fresh, contemporary interiors and décor reflecting the sandy palette of the cove below, Turtle cottage combines the Caribbean beach lifestyle with elegance and modernity. Outside, the private pool and balcony with scenic ocean views will ensure you don’t forget that you are at a world class relaxation spot thousands of miles away from home.

Whilst Turtle Cottage is one of Blue Waters more secluded accommodations, guests are welcome to venture out of their villa to enjoy the services and facilitates at the hotel resort. It is hard to resist the call of the smooth blue water vista stretching out in front of you, and Blue Waters offer a range of non-motorised water sports with complimentary introductory lessons for beginners. And if you are unsure of what to do with yourself next, your concierge, just a quick phone call away, will be more than happy to help you decide.
