Dieppe Bay House
5 Bedrooms | 5 Baths | Pool
Falmouth Harbour, Antigua
From $6,000 per Week

Ideal for either family or adult groups, Dieppe Bay House has been furnished with elegant colonial-style antique teak and mahogany furniture. The five bedrooms can sleep between ten to fourteen people. Dieppe Bay House is a beautiful ‘off the beaten track’ sea front villa - the perfect place for a fabulous idyllic Caribbean escape.

With its own swimming pool and large tropical gardens, the house sits on the western shore of Falmouth Harbour in the south of Antigua. There are stunning views across the sea to the distant masts of the Antigua Yacht Club and out to the open ocean.

The Dieppe Bay House staff is there to make sure you have a relaxing holiday. A housekeeper comes every weekday and there is an on-call property manager, gardener and pool service.

Dieppe Bay House can be found on Dieppe Bay at the mouth Falmouth Harbour in the south of Antigua. The house is five minutes drive off the main road and from Falmouth village where the nearest shops and restaurants can be found. A greater choice of shops and restaurants are accessible ten minutes away in English Harbour and Nelson’s Dockyard. The VC Bird International Airport is 30 minutes drive from Dieppe Bay House.
