Villa Sand, Anguilla, British West Indies Vacation Rental


Terraces that open to the infinity edge pool and the ocean beyond induce a natural lightness of being, while ivory, rust, burgundy, pewter and muted green interiors keep guests happily anchored to earth. Featuring four master suites, a two-story design with vaulted ceilings and spacious interiors at 6,500 sq ft., this villa will certainly not disappoint those looking to vacation in a relaxing, luxurious setting.

Long Bay Villas consists of three distinct, sparkling white villas, inspired by the design themes of sea, sand and sky. This is the ultimate in luxury and indulgence. Each secluded island hideaway has its own infinity edge pool with Jacuzzi, an outdoor dining pavilion and private access to beach with an ocean front beach house, and is perfectly private. The entire 3 villa Estate can be rented to accommodate up to 26 guests. Perfect for large family reunions or Corporate retreats
