Cap Cana Penthouse, Cap Cana, Punta Cana, Dominican Republic Vacation Rental

 

Leaving the hustling and bustling city to have some relax time, but you do not want to lose sight of crowded and busy spots full of people who, like you, are looking for a great time?

Hence this unique and amazing fully equipped 4 bedroom penthouse is what you are looking for!

Located in the fancy and fashionable marina of Cap Cana (one of the most renowned gated villages in the Caribbean), this modern and sophistically decorated penthouse has 4 bedrooms, two on each floor. When you walk into the property along the foyer, you will have an amazing view of all the marina and all in common spaces, openness prevails, having full sight of the spacious living room and the professionally equipped kitchen with a counter top.

On the first floor you will find a two bedrooms with a queen size bed, full bathroom with shower and walk-in closet each, yet one of them has a TV. When going upstairs you will get mesmerized by the majestic master bedroom with a king size bed, bathroom with double shower, walk-in closet and TV. The forth bedroom has a queen size bed, full bathroom with shower and walk-in closet, finally there is a fifth room with a queen bed, full bathroom and wall closet.

On the roof-top there is a open patio, with BBQ and a hot tub with an incredible view to the whole marina.

The location of this penthouse is unique, because you are only steps away from the best restaurants, bars, night clubs and a small grocery store.

The community Resort provides a matchless experience for all who visit this tropical paradise, and its tropically chic lifestyle is ideal for those who appreciate high standards of quality, comfort and aesthetics.
