Bird of Paradise Villa, Anguilla, British West Indies Vacation Rental


The Paradise Villa, located on Sandy Hill Bay beach, Anguilla, offers a unique blend of exquisite detail, spacious comfort, luxury, and quiet ambiance. It was designed by Wilson Associates, award-winning architects of luxury villas and resorts throughout the world. The Bird of Paradise is designed in an Asian Tropical style with meandering, lagoon-style pools that weave between the four buildings to create an intimate compound that is lush with vegetation.

The Villa is located on a small bluff overlooking the Caribbean Sea and the islands of St. Barth's and St. Martin. The beautiful, crescent Sandy Hill Bay beach is a short walk down a path and the sheltered bay and beach offer excellent swimming and snorkeling as well as a safe harbor for a small yacht.

The award-winning Paradise Villa offers a large interior living room with fold-back walls, as well as several exterior living areas, one with a fireplace. There are four dining experiences. Anguilla's outdoor ambiance is captured in large verandahs and terrace patios, both shaded and open. A large Indonesian bale, or daybed, is situated in a grove of palm trees overlooking the sea and is perfect for massages and naps.

There are four bedroom suites with an office that can serve as a fifth bedroom. The villa may be rented as a one-bedroom, two-bedroom, or three-bedroom as well. The placement of the suites, each with its own verandah and equal, panoramic views of the beach and Caribbean Sea, assures individual privacy for guests. Each suite, individually decorated, has its own refrigerator and coffee service. Frette linens and Bvlgari amenities enhance the luxury of this tropical retreat.

Perfect for luxury holiday retreats, romantic weddings or honeymoons, family reunions, or extraordinary family vacations, the Bird offers a magical home away from home with up to five bedrooms large enough to sleep 8 adults and two children, as well as spacious seating areas and fabulous vistas. Go, ahead -- pamper yourself!
