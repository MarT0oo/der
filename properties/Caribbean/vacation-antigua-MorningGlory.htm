Morning Glory, Jumby Bay, Antigua, Caribbean


A fabulous colonial style villa set in eight private acres of lush, well-stocked tropical gardens, Morning Glory offers four suites in the main house and two private cottages on the grounds, each including two suites. Decorated to an exceptional standard and elegantly furnished, the property offers a modern, fully equipped kitchen with breakfast patio, two dining areas in the main residence and a dining gazebo adjacent to the beach. All bedrooms offer air-conditioning, ceiling fans and en suite bathrooms.

With its unique layout, this estate offers flexibility to cater for a party of any size, large or small. It can be rented as just the four-bedroom main house or with one or two cottages.

The home also features an indoor and outdoor loggia, great room, TV/family room and study as well as cable TV, DVD player and broadband Internet. Additionally there is a fully equipped gym, spa treatment suite, flood-lit tennis court and outdoor games area, including chess, draughts and croquet.

Opening directly onto a show-stopping white sand beach with exceptional views of the sea and the 20-meter-long infinity swimming pool, this is the perfect private retreat for both families and larger groups.
