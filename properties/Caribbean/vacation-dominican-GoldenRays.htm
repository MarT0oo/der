Golden Rays, Casa de Campo, Dominican Republic Vacation Rental

 

This extraordinary villa is located on the third hole of the Links Golf Course offering a spectacular view overlooking the entire resort of Casa de Campo with a 180 degree view of the Caribbean Sea with an unobstructed view of spectacular sunsets each evening.

Upon entering, your gaze is drawn to the breathtaking ocean views, of the Caribbean Sea and nearby Saona and Catalina Islands that seem to fall directly from your sixty foot infinity pool and Jacuzzi. The villa boasts two story interior gardens that provide the unique Casa de Campo indoor/outdoor living experience.

All this scenic beauty is surrounded by a 3000 ft outer deck and living area with beautiful gazebo, perfectly designed for outdoor entertaining and dining. A fully equipped kitchen with commercial restaurant appliances, interior formal air-conditioned dining room and a specially climate controlled family media/entertainment center. All living areas and the master suite command sensational views.

You can marvel at the spectacular sunsets while sipping cocktails from the patio, gazebo or any of the other numerous balconies of this property.

Casa de Campo is located on the south eastern shore of the Dominican Republic, next to the town of La Romana and just 70 miles from the capital city of Santo Domingo, the oldest city in the new world. Casa de Campo is a luxurious 7,000 acre ocean front community offering world class golf courses which include the famous Teeth of the Dog Golf Course, Dye Fore and The Links Golf Course plus polo fields, skeet shooting centre, tennis centre, equestrian centre, water sports, spa treatment centre, fitness centre, business centre, private beach, a la carte restaurants, Altos de Chavon (artist village) and Casa de Campo Marina, all of which will please almost any visitor.
