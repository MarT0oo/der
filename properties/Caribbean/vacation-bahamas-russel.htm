Mrs. Russel's House
3 Bedrooms | 3 Baths | On Squire's Estate
Governor's Harbour, Eleuthera, Bahamas
From $520 per Night

Mrs. Russel's House was built during the early and mid 1800's and keeps its character that can be described as a New England Colonial style mixed with a hint of Georgian flavor. During the recent restoration all of the original and historic architecture was preserved while still accommodating the needs of modern luxury living.

 

The essence of the past can really be felt as you feel immersed in the intimate and nostalgic interior décor. Mixing original furnishings with a unique blend of creatively researched pieces recreates a sense of colonial nostalgia.

Mrs. Russel's House consists of three master bedrooms. Two rooms contain king sized beds and one room contains two full beds. The house has a living room, dining room, fully equipped island kitchen, and terrace. The furnishings include state of the art appliances, flat screen TV, air conditioning, ceiling fan, wireless internet and cordless telephone.

 

The estate is surrounded by a lush garden which is home to many fruit trees, Bougainvillea, Hibiscus, Aloe and Coconut palms. Those are just a few of the plants that grace this serene property. The infinity pool serves both Mrs. Russel's House and Toad's Hall. The pool deck is dramatic with its views of the harbour and adds to the delight of these well-manicured grounds.

 

You may also rent the entire Squires Estate which includes 10 bedrooms and 6 different cottages: Belmont House, Mrs. Russel’s Cottage, The Stone House, The Cottage at Belmont, Toad’s Hall, and The Jr. Cottage at Belmont.
