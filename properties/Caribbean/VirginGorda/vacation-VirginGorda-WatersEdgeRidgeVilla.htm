Waters Edge Ridge Villa, Ridge Villas, Virgin Gorda, British Virgin Islands Vacation Rental

 

Presiding atop the Ridge Villa neighborhood, Poseidon's Perch is a 4-bedroom villa with turquoise views of the Atlantic Ocean, Anegada, Oil Nut Bay and the resort community. This finely crafted water palace’s décor is contemporary and stylish, featuring striking natural granite and stone materials.

Embracing indoor-outdoor living in this British Virgin Island luxury rental is easy with large pocket doors and terraces that frame the brilliant Atlantic Ocean and Bay views. This luxury villa rental is excellent for outdoor lounging with infinity edge pool and palapa lounge area, jacuzzi and pool bar. It features a welcome alcove that leads to the great room and bedroom levels, each with private entrance, en-suite bathroom and terrace. The outdoor space is accentuated by a turtle shade structure for lounging with ample terrace space for al fresco dining and barbecue grilling.

The Master Bedroom is complete with king bed, walk-in closet, en-suite bathroom featuring an outdoor shower and private path to palapa lounge area. One guest bedroom features queen and twin bunk beds with en-suite bathroom, while another guest bedroom has a king bed and en-suite bathroom with outdoor shower. An additional guest room features a king bed with en-suite bathroom which overlooks the pool and bay beyond. A cart is included with this villa.

Dramatically shaped, the island reminded Christopher Columbus of a reclining woman, so he named it Virgin Gorda. Beach-lovers will enjoy the beautiful stretch of pristine beaches, particularly the Baths, a beach made up of giant granite boulders that form hidden pools and grottos. Vacationers will also enjoy the unique indigenous plants that flourish in the National Parks and can admire the exotic birds and ruins at the Little Fort National Park. This property is located on Oil Nut Bay which is a coral reef bay described as "headland attached linear reef". The beach at Oil Nut Bay stretches for about 0.5 miles (0.80 km) and the coastline extends to Leverick Bay. Virgin Gorda is truly one of the most beautiful destinations in the Caribbean, where you can truly get it away from it all and simply relax.
