On the Rocks Villa
4 Bedrooms | 4 Baths
Little Trunk Bay
From $7,500 per Week

On The Rocks is a contemporary, well-appointed and luxurious vacation villa. Its happy repeat guest clientele attests to the fact that it is a perfect home-away-from-home in a great location!

Situated within private Little Trunk Bay Estate, this 4 bedroom villa accesses three amazing beaches. In closest proximity – literally one minute stroll from the villa – is Little Trunk Bay which is known for its excellent snorkeling. The beach to the right: Valley Trunk has been named one of the TOP TEN MOST BEAUTIFUL BEACHES IN THE WORLD for its long powdery-white-sand beach and smooth-swimming turquoise waters. To the left of the villa is Spring Bay, also an amazing beach. Just five minutes away is The Baths, world famous for its enormous granite boulders, and hidden caves and warm ocean pools.

On The Rocks was masterfully designed to incorporate the granite boulders native to the southern end of Virgin Gorda. The villa’s pool features a charming waterfall crafted from the rocks and boulders. The villa is accessed by a sunlit stone walkway which ends at the artistic blue doors. The Great Room is light and airy. It is circular in shape ensuring a wide panoramic ocean view that includes the islands of the Sir Francis Drake Channel: from Peter Island and St John to Tortola and The Dog Islands. The Great Room is topped by a richly stained exposed-beam ceiling making the room tropical and warm. Sea grass furniture, accented by rich cobalt blues creates a perfect meeting place for family groups or long-time friends with a yearly tradition of vacationing together. The outdoor terrace includes a dining-table cooled by a charming white umbrella and lounge chairs perfect for lazy afternoons. The pool terrace is the perfect place to sip a refreshing rum-punch while admiring the most spectacular sunsets worldwide. This is the right place for perfectly memorable evenings.

The fully-equipped contemporary kitchen includes a Wolf range, granite counter tops, Liptus wood cabinets, stainless steel appliances and halogen task lighting. A wet bar with built-in ice maker is located just off the living room.

On the Rocks provides indoor and outdoor dining complete with a custom-built outdoor charcoal grill and is the ideal setting for intimate gatherings, a casual dinner or delicious barbecue.

The four bedrooms are luxurious and exude elegance with a Caribbean flare. Each bedroom has air conditioning, plush Euro-Top mattress and rich linens. Blackout drapery allow for sleeping late. Flat screen TVs; DVD players and Ipod docs are also included.
