Cheemaun Beach Villa, Beach Villas, Virgin Gorda, British Virgin Islands Vacation Rental

 

Cheemaun Beach Villa is a gorgeous three-bedroom villa situated directly on the beach. The luxury villa encompasses 3,250 square feet (302 square meters) of easy island elegance. Featuring three bedrooms, two powder rooms, a gourmet kitchen, dining area and large great room all under one roof, this villa is ideal for families of all ages. Two master bedrooms face brilliant views of the crystal blue waters with king beds, generously sized bathrooms with soaking tubs, and indoor and outdoor showers. The third guest room features two queen beds for the ultimate in flexibility.

High ceilings feature in the Great Room, with luxury furniture and fine interior finishes throughout. A spacious outdoor terrace. The outdoor terrace is accessible through floor to ceiling sliding windows in the Great Room and all bedrooms. The terrace has a built-in grill and luxurious outdoor furniture for lounging; It overlooks a private pool and spills out to the beach and calm waters of the bay. A cart is included with this villa.

Close to the Beach Club, Arrival Dock and Resort Center, Cheemaun Beach Villa offers an exquisite location to experience the luxury and pristine beauty of Virgin Gorda. Cheemaun Beach Villa is a non-smoking property.

Dramatically shaped, the island reminded Christopher Columbus of a reclining woman, so he named it Virgin Gorda. Beach-lovers will enjoy the beautiful stretch of pristine beaches, particularly the Baths, a beach made up of giant granite boulders that form hidden pools and grottos. Vacationers will also enjoy the unique indigenous plants that flourish in the National Parks and can admire the exotic birds and ruins at the Little Fort National Park. Virgin Gorda is truly one of the most beautiful destinations in the Caribbean, where you can truly get it away from it all and simply relax.
