Tingalayo Virgin Gorda Vacation Rental


‘Tingalayo’ was designed and created by owners with a lifetime of experience in villa development. The result is a truly remarkable property, where every detail and guest’s wish has been catered for. As part of the recovery from the storms of 2017 the property has recently been enhanced and upgraded with new furnishings throughout, state of the art technology and additional living spaces added. Spending time at Tingalayo is restorative and invigorating and once experience you will want to come back time after time!

As soon as you walk onto the immense coral stone patio, you can’t help but be overwhelmed by the stunning views. Here you can gaze almost 360 degrees, taking in Jost Van Dyke, Smugglers Cove, Apple Bay, Sopers Hole, St. John, St. Thomas, even Puerto Rico on a clear day! Sailboats, wildlife, lush hillsides and aqua waters, make this vista one you will never get bored of.

The villa is made up of seven brightly coloured buildings interwoven and connected with pathways through beautiful gardens. Dotted around the property are welcoming lounging alcoves, perfect for relaxing and reading. The buildings centre around an infinity edged pool which extends out to the breathtaking panorama beyond.

The Great House boasts a elegant living and dining open plan room with plenty of comfortable seating and entertainment. This perfect gathering space leads seamlessly to a modern kitchen, perfectly equipped for you or a private chef. A few short steps lead you out to a generous wooden deck, where al fresco dining with this view doesn’t get any better!

Each bedroom is separate and unique, giving you complete privacy. Open the windows to the breeze or enjoy the air conditioning featured in every room. The bathrooms are spacious and beautifully appointed.

The self contained guest cottage is perfect for families with children. The ground floor features a living room and kitchen that lead on to a private deck with plunge pool. On the top floor, two beautifully furnished bedrooms are separated by a large wooden deck.

The Games Room is fully equipped for kids both big and small! Here you will find a large flat screen TV, a selection of games including a Skeeball machine and a range of musical instruments. The nearby courtyard has a full size pool table. The Games Room can be air conditioned for those wishing to escape the midday sun and simply unwind.

There is also a hot-tub and private sun deck, a bocce court, a fully equipped gym and a stunning yoga pavilion. Tingalayo really is a once in a lifetime villa. The in-house team will customize your experience to create your perfect vacation.
