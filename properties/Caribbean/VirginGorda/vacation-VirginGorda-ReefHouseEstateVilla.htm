Reef House Estate Villa, Estate Villas, Virgin Gorda, British Virgin Islands Vacation Rental

 

The Reef House reflects the natural beauty of the surrounding terrain with pod-style living to provide ultimate privacy and highlight stunning views of Necker Island, Eustatia and the reef.

Overlooking the Marina Village, this villa consists of three bedroom 'pods' each with spacious bedrooms, indoor and outdoor showers, plus a tub 'with a view' in the king master bedroom. Each of the rooms are individually decorated to blend with the surrounding beauty of the island. Two of the bedrooms have king size Westin Heavenly beds, the third, having a king size vi-spring bed, is capable of splitting into two single beds. All the rooms are well separated from each other with their own balcony complete with lounging chairs.

The main living area, known as the Great Room, houses a large sitting and dining area along with a well-equipped kitchen. The stunning Great Room leads out onto an infinity lap pool. At each end of the swimming pool are tiki huts, both on platforms overlooking the ocean, one with comfortable lounging and one in close proximity to the kitchen and barbecue, ideal for shaded outdoor dining. The Great Room is designed very much as a seaside room. It is light and airy with beautiful artefacts from all over the world collected by the owners. The Media Room features a 66” Samsung TV and stocked bookshelf. The result is perfect simplicity: modern, yet comfortable.

Dramatically shaped, the island reminded Christopher Columbus of a reclining woman, so he named it Virgin Gorda. Beach-lovers will enjoy the beautiful stretch of pristine beaches, particularly the Baths, a beach made up of giant granite boulders that form hidden pools and grottos. Vacationers will also enjoy the unique indigenous plants that flourish in the National Parks and can admire the exotic birds and ruins at the Little Fort National Park. This property is located on Oil Nut Bay which is a coral reef bay described as "headland attached linear reef". The beach at Oil Nut Bay stretches for about 0.5 miles (0.80 km) and the coastline extends to Leverick Bay. Virgin Gorda is truly one of the most beautiful destinations in the Caribbean, where you can truly get it away from it all and simply relax.
