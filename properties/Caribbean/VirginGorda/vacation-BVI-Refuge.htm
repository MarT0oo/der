Refuge, Tortola, British Virgin Islands, Caribbean Vacation Rental

 

Very private and so stylish, The Refuge is the ultimate Tortola beach villa! A specious Great Room and three beautiful bedroom suites which can sleep up to six guests in style - the entire villa is centrally air-conditioned. All rooms look out to sea views, and each suite has its own private romantic balcony.

An electronic gate ushers you into this just-completed property; stairs descend to the villa's upper level entry. On either side of the upper-level entry foyer are two bedroom suites, a king master suite with its own kitchenette, and a two-twin bed suite. A curved staircase leads to The Refuge's main level, with the kitchen, dining area and Great Room on one side and the second king master bedroom/bath suite on the other. In the middle is a powder room and french doors which open out to a small deck set with two chaise lounges and the refreshing dip pool.

The high-ceilinged, very comfortable Great Room is the heart of The Refuge. Two large, plush sofas flanked by upholstered chairs provide gathering space for everyone, and a large 42-inch flat screen cable TV, DVD player and a Bose iPod dock are ready for your entertainment. There are even pull-down blinds to keep the bright sun out as you wish. Tall french doors open out to an ocean view verandah with cushioned seating on one side, and to the pool alcove on the other. The sleek kitchen and center island with bar stools is adjacent, featuring stainless steel appliances, anigre wood cabinetry and a warm cedar ceiling. Alongside is a round dining table for six, and just outside through french doors is another covered dining area, alfresco, bordered by tropical landscaping and overlooking the picturesque Bay.

The lower king bedroom/bath suite is a perfect private oasis for two. Screened slider doors open out to this suite's private covered patio. This suite features a stunning bath divided into three areas for excellent privacy - a vanity with two glass vessel sinks, a separate WC and bidet, and a tiled bathtub/shower area alongside another shower, just outside via a french door.

The Refuge's upper level offers the second king master bedroom/bath suite, this one also featuring its own covered verandah looking out to sea, and with bonuses of an equipped kitchenette with a microwave and mini-fridge and a washer and dryer. Across the foyer is The Refuge's two-twin suite, artfully designed in loft fashion, looking down into the Great Room and through a high, perfectly-situated window framing azure Brewer's Bay and blue skies beyond. Doors across the loft wall can be closed for privacy. A door opens to the twin suite's private patio, and the bathroom is a work of art, featuring an attractive vanity with two bamboo sinks, a tiled alfresco rain shower covered with a pergola just outside, a separate WC area, and a square bathtub with a window showcasing those mesmerizing sea views.

Exiting from the dining area, you can take a trail with railroad-tie steps (no railings or lighting) just 60 feet down to the perfect white sands of Brewer's Bay, where you can swim, snorkel and enjoy walks along the long beach. Sleek tilework and chic lighting throughout add to the clean, modern decor: organic but very elegant. The Refuge is an exciting new villa, distinguished by its easy access to one of Tortola's finest beaches - five stars!
