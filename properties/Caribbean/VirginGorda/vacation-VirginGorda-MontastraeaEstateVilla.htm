With an airy island-contemporary design and pod-style architecture, Montastraea Villa features four elegant bedrooms with private verandas, indoor and outdoor shower and stunning views of the reef below. Floor-to-ceiling glass doors completely retract in a Great Room that also features ample living space, a fireplace and gourmet kitchen. Dramatically shaped, the island reminded Christopher Columbus of a reclining woman, so he named it Virgin Gorda. Beach-lovers will enjoy the beautiful stretch of pristine beaches, particularly the Baths, a beach made up of giant granite boulders that form hidden pools and grottos. Vacationers will also enjoy the unique indigenous plants that flourish in the National Parks and can admire the exotic birds and ruins at the Little Fort National Park. This property is located on Oil Nut Bay which is a coral reef bay described as "headland attached linear reef". The beach at Oil Nut Bay stretches for about 0.5 miles (0.80 km) and the coastline extends to Leverick Bay. Virgin Gorda is truly one of the most beautiful destinations in the Caribbean, where you can truly get it away from it all and simply relax.
