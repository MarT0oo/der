Poseidon’s Perch Ridge Villa, Estate Villas, Virgin Gorda, British Virgin Islands Vacation Rental

 

Presiding atop the Ridge Villa neighborhood, Poseidon’s Perch is a 3-bedroom villa with turquoise views of the Atlantic Ocean, Anegada, Oil Nut Bay and the resort community. This finely crafted water palace is the epitome of high-rise living at Oil Nut Bay.

This luxury villa rental is excellent for outdoor lounging with infinity edge pool and palapa lounge area, jacuzzi and pool bar. It features a welcome alcove that leads to the great room and bedroom levels, each with private entrance, ensuite bathroom and terrace. The décor in this villa is contemporary and stylish, featuring striking natural granite and stone materials. The outdoor space is accentuated by a turtle shade structure for lounging with ample terrace space for al fresco dining and barbecue grilling.

The Master Bedroom is complete with king bed, walk-in closets, en suite bathroom featuring an outdoor shower and private path to palapa lounge area. One guest bedroom features queen and twin bunk beds with ensuite bathroom, while another guest bedroom has a king bed and ensuite bathroom with outdoor shower.

Embracing indoor-outdoor living in this British Virgin Island luxury rental is easy with large pocket doors and terraces that frame the brilliant Atlantic Ocean and Bay views.
