Villa on the Beach, Mahoe Bay, Virgin Gorda, British Virgin Islands Vacation Rental

 

A beachfront villa situated right on the sands of Virgin Gorda, Villa On the Beach stands ready to welcome travelers. This accurately named estate offers a tropical respite for groups of up to ten with its four plush bedrooms and vast verdant terraces that overlook Mahoe Bay. Villa On The Beach is the ideal Caribbean island getaway – a beautifully constructed villa, a dazzling ocean view and plentiful amenities, ensuring that guests will have memories from their visit that last a lifetime.

One can start their day by soaking up the sun in lounge chairs on the terrace or wandering down to the private slice of soft sandy beach. The enclosed garden boasts spacious tropical flowers amid ample room for both play and rest, not to mention a beautiful stone pool with raised edges, providing a safe space for young children. Evening in paradise includes stunning sunsets from the poolside patio with a ready grill or indoors with the large flat-screen entertainment unit. A daily housekeeping service is also included.

Both the interior and exterior of Villa On The Beach have been constructed with luxury in mind. The main living area has been furnished with rich hardwoods, local stone and only the finest furnishings and works of art. The kitchen has been made complete with marble countertops and modern appliances which can easily access either the indoor or alfresco dining areas, both of which have ample seating.

Three bedrooms each have king size beds with en-suite bathrooms that also have alfresco showers that are open to the fresh air yet completely sheltered. Two of these rooms have beautiful garden views, but one has breathtaking views of the ocean. A large and functional bunk room with its own en-suite bathroom can sleep up to four children across two sets of bunk beds. Sure to be a hit!

There are no shortage of nearby attractions on the small island of Virgin Gorda. The laid-back pace of the island offers the perfect excuse to take your time while doing some casual snorkeling in Devil’s Bay, or exploring the stone ruins of a nineteenth century copper mine. Hanging out in a hammock at a local tavern while sipping a cocktail is quite simply the ultimate in Virgin Gorda relaxation!
