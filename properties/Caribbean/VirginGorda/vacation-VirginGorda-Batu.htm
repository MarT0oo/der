Batu, Virgin Gorda, British Virgin Islands Vacation Rental

 

Nestled amongst the giant boulders that form the stunning Atlantic shoreline of Virgin Gorda, the recently completed Villa Batu (Indonesian for "rock"), provides a luxurious Caribbean hideaway. With refreshing tropical breezes, spectacular views and five-star amenities, Batu provides a most superior vacation rental experience. Just a few minutes' walk from Batu is the Baths. This world-famous geological rock formation is known for its giant granite boulders, rock pools, and white sand beaches. Batu was designed and constructed to utilize these incredible gifts from nature, and the result is truly awe-inspiring.

The villa has a modular design to maximize privacy for each of the four suites, and it follows the natural contours of the landscape. Each bedroom has an outdoor shower and, together with the main house, offers unsurpassed views of the Atlantic Ocean. The freshwater infinity pool is reached by following one of the many stone pathways that meander amongst the boulders and tropical gardens.

After entering the Indonesian hardwood doors that announce your entry into this calming Caribbean sanctuary, you enter the main house. Hardwood floors and unique pieces of handcrafted custom-made furniture adorn the open-plan dining and living room areas. Custom-made Mora wood dining tables provide seating for up to twelve guests both inside the main house and outside on the covered deck.

A fully equipped kitchen comprising of granite countertops and professional grade stainless steel appliances, including a refrigerator, range, oven, microwave, dishwasher, wine refrigerator, and high capacity ice maker provide the perfect opportunity to create family meals or gourmet dining. The air-conditioned entertainment room and study includes a 60" smart television with surround sound and a satellite entertainment system. Wi-Fi internet access and stereo sound system are available throughout the main house and pool area.

Lastly, the air-conditioned gym provides state of the art equipment for the exercise enthusiast, along with a newly added bathroom and murphy queen sized bed. Batu is your perfect home away from home!
