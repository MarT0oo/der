Hillside Villa
4 Bedrooms | 5 Baths | Pool | Library
Galley Bay Heights, Antigua
From $10,125 per Week

Hillside Villa is placed within a spectacular setting on the lush hillside of the beautiful Galley Bay Heights offering breathtaking panoramic views of Galley Bay and Deep Bay just below. Its Italian architecture mingled with its simple and attractive western furnishing gives Hillside Villa a warm and welcoming atmosphere while still offering all the comforts of a luxurious resort.

The villa has 4 bedrooms each with its own bathroom complete with both rain and hand-held showers and a private opening onto the spacious patio area furnished with comfortable loungers. Three bedrooms are set within the main house while one, known as the Cabana, is nestled within the tropical garden with direct access to the pool deck. The Cabana is the perfect private retreat elegantly furnished with a queen sized bed, bedside tables with lamps, ceiling fan and kitchenette with its own fridge and microwave oven. Two of the main house bedrooms have king-sized beds and one has two single beds. These three bedrooms have spacious walk-in closets, ceiling fans, air conditioning, mosquito nets and are equipped with large French doors ensuring their comfortable coolness day and night.

Hillside Villa offers you a cozy indoor dining area furnished with a table that cleverly transforms into a pool table should you feel the desire to challenge your family and guests to a friendly game of pool. However, Hillside Villa is built to let you enjoy the great outdoors of the Caribbean and thus gives you the opportunity to do just that with a comfortable and attractive outdoor dining area complete with a fine round teak dining table with chairs and conveniently equipped with a stainless steel BBQ and bar area adding to your dining outdoor experience.  The indoor kitchen is fitted with custom made Italian cabinets and marble counter tops and is equipped with the highest standard appliances, including 2 dishwashers, stainless steel refrigerator, cooking range, electric oven, microwave, toaster, coffee maker, electric kettle, blender and food processor.

But this villa has so much more to offer. You may kick back and relax on the sofa in the entertainment room conveniently arranged with a large plasma TV screen and your own private library complete with numerous DVD, CD, books and games to choose from. In this villa you may just feel tucked away in a cozy corner of paradise but not to worry Hillside Villa also ensures that you remain in contact with the rest of the world by providing you with easy internet access. To cool off you may be tempted to dive in the fabulous infinity pool ingeniously designed to give the swimmer the distinct impression of continuance in the stunning bay below. As the sun sets on Hillside Villa bathing its spacious pool deck in delicate red hues you may just find yourself sipping a pi≈àa-colada while sitting in the warm soothing waters of the outdoor Jacuzzi.

Uniquely for your convenience, Hillside Villa comes with maid and gardener service six days a week and upon special request a chef or nanny may also be provided. The villa also has a secure safe to assist you with caring for your valuables.
