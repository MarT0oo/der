Villa Ashiana, Marigot, St. Lucia Vacation Rental


A beautiful 5 bedroom Villa situated at Marigot, the yacht haven of St. Lucia. This villa offers fabulous views of Marigot Bay and wonderful sunsets can be viewed from the villa. This 2 storey villa has large open patios upstairs and downstairs to enhance the outdoor living typical of the tropics. There are large spacious bedrooms upstairs attractively furnished, all are air- conditioned and equipped with en suite bathrooms.

The large room facing east is the 5th bedroom equipped with twin beds. The living and dining room are open plan and furnished with a tropical finish. This Villa is situated on 15,890 sq ft of land and measures 7,747 sq ft. An attractive infinity edge pool compliments the villa.
