Incredible panoramic views of the Caribbean and St. Barth's! Hear the waves!

Villa Caribbean Jewel is located in the exclusive gated community of Tamarind Hill Estates on the Dutch side of St. Maarten, near the French/Dutch border.  A few minutes drive to Dawn Beach, Orient Beach, and Guana Bay Beach. For those who are in the mood for exercise it is also possible to walk down to Gibbs Bay Beach. Numerous outstanding restaurants are just 2 minutes away. Ten-Fifteen minutes away from the Dutch capital of Philipsburg and shopping.

This luxury Mediterranean-style villa features gated entry, lush gardens, extensive balconies and terraces, and a private pool with a disappearing edge. Four master bedroom suites feature king sized beds, private balconies, air conditioning, and in-suite baths - 4.5 total baths. Each bedroom has a beautiful view of the Caribbean.
