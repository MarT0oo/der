Punta Cana 97, Punta Cana, Dominican Republic Vacation Rental

 

Punta Cana 97 is a lovely luxury vacation rental within Punta Cana Resort. Punta Cana has been hailed as one of the Best Resorts in the World by various travel and trade magazines. Making it a hotspot on many radars for luxury travel.

The house is built to the standards of the luxury traveler with beautiful amenities and amazing host and concierge who will help with any of your needs. The master bedroom includes a king size bed, as well as the second bedroom. The third bedroom has a queen size bed and the fourth bedroom features two queen beds. All rooms include air conditioning, private bathroom and walk-in closet. Two of the bedrooms feature a flat screen TV. Fully equipped kitchen, extensive living room terrace & dining room, private courtyard with stunning swimming pool.

Want to rent a boat, you can. Want to throw a surprise party we can help. No matter the experience we're here as your experience experts. Come join us on your next vacation.
