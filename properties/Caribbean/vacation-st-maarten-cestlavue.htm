C'est La Vue
6 Bedrooms | 6 Baths Ensuites | Waterfront | Pool
Terres Basses (French Side), St Martin
From $9,500 per Week

Situated in the exclusive residential area of Terres Basses, C’est La Vue is a comfortably elegant waterfront home. Set at the end of a private road and built into the hillside, the villa nestles in beautifully landscaped tropical gardens lush with mature trees and brilliant bougainvillea. 

The property comprises 3 separate building. The main house has a comfortable living room, dining area and well appointed, sociable kitchen which all open out onto a sheltered covered terrace with generous open views and the pool & Jacuzzi terraces. 

C’est La Vue offers six beautifully decorated, attractive bedrooms all with air-conditioning, king-size beds and en-suite baths. All of the bedrooms are fairly equal in size and 5 are on one level. The Master bedroom is located in the main house and is accessible off the living room. Three further bedrooms are located close to the main house. Bedrooms 2 & 3 (both king-size) are contemporary in style and interconnect making this an ideal suite of rooms for parents traveling with children. Bedroom 4 is accessible via stone steps down to a private terrace and gardens & has the added convenience of a kitchenette. Bedrooms 5 & 6 are in a separate building close to the pool and interconnect via a private living room which again makes this ideal for parents traveling with children. 

A graceful infinity edged private swimming pool overlooks the Jacuzzi deck which in turn leads to steps down though the gardens to the private boat dock with boat lift on the Simpson Bay Lagoon. Guests staying at C’est La Vue are able to use the villa owner’s 2 jet skis for a nominal daily rental fee. 

C’est La Vue is a restful and quiet oasis of tranquility and peacefulness where guests may enjoy the soothing sounds of the rustling trees and enjoy the wonderful views.
