La Bougainvillea, Eleuthera, Bahamas


La Bougainvillea is a Moroccan style home with simple island charm. Beautiful Spanish tile floors throughout with accents of blue and green make each room feel warm and unique.  The Villa sits on 30 beautiful acres covered with native flowers and Silver Palms. La Bougainvillea is a true paradise getaway.

 

Welcome to this little piece of Heaven on Earth. You can Rent this private beach front estate located in Eleuthera, Bahamas with 5 beautiful bedrooms and 5.5 Bathrooms with1000 feet of beautiful pink sand beach!!The 30 acres of property is complemented with native flowers and silver palms!

 

A perfect place for snorkeling, diving, swimming and relaxing on the beach. The house is built to resemble Moroccan architecture, which makes it one of a kind. Come see why so many who visit Eleuthera and La Bougainvillea never want to leave!

 

The estate home is 4000 square feet including a king-sized master bedroom suite with an en suite private bathroom, a queen sized, full sized suites, fully equipped kitchen, dining room and a spacious living room.  The interiors are fashionably styled and spacious. Entrance is through dramatic French doors into a lovely foyer. The furnishings and appointments are of the highest standards and offer an air of whimsy as well.

 

You will feel the Villa's welcome everyday with glistening turquoise waters, tropical climate, and friendly community. Outside, there is a 60 foot infinity edge pool located with direct ocean view. It is shared with one other property located on the estate. The pool sits right along the pink sandy beaches - what a view!!
