Limin Da Coconut, Blowing Point, Anguilla, Caribbean Vacation Rental

 

Limin da Coconut is a new villa rental on the island of Anguilla in Little Harbour. This gorgeous villa has 4 private master bedrooms with en-suite baths and ocean views. Each suite has its own special look and decor.

When you enter the villa you immediately see the soaring ceilings and direct view to the gorgeous Caribbean Sea and our neighboring island of St. Martin. A large living room with sectional seating, gorgeous dining table with seating for 12. The kitchen is fully equipped with GE Profile appliances, island with granite counter top, and separate pantry.

Just a few steps down off the living area is the pool with outdoor lounge seating. A few more steps down and you are onto a small beach for swimming and snorkeling.

The bedrooms are located on multiple levels and all have privacy and water views. Steps from the 2nd floor lead up to the roof deck for a panoramic view of the southern coastline, gorgeous evening sky and breathtaking sunsets.
