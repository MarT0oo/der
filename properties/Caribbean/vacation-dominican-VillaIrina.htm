Villa Irina, Sosua, Cabarete, Dominican Republic Vacation Rental

 

Situated on a private cul de sac just a minutes walking distance to the Beach Club Restaurant and pools, this sprawling one story villa marries casual ranch style living with luxurious craftsmanship and construction. An enormous master bedroom is located privately at one extreme of the villa offering privacy and tranquility while the other is located on the guest wing with two other guest bedrooms. Each master bedroom has a spacious spa inspired bathroom finished in creamy colored marble counter tops, walls, shower and bath. The villa features gorgeous coral stone floors in the living room and hallways, Mexican terracotta floors in the bedroom and mahogany wood floor in the audio visual room. Extensive tile terraces from the main house wrap around the gorgeous free form pool to teak wood platforms and an outdoor pool shower. An open concept living room offers seamless indoor and outdoor visuals to enjoy the lush garden views graced by a ranch style white stone wall. Top notch matresses and bedding, luxurious overstuffed furnishings and hardwood beds, state of the art kitchen with stainless steel appliances. This villa has four bedrooms and 4 1/2 bathrooms. A fabulous vacation retreat or year round Caribbean residence!
