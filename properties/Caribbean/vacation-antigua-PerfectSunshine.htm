Perfect Sunshine, Nonsuch Bay, Antigua, Caribbean Vacation Rental

 

From the moment you step into our Perfect Sunshine luxury villa accommodation, the word “opulence” can be felt everywhere.

A huge, spacious reception room begins the experience; featuring Designer’s Guild sofas and matching floor decorations, sculptures perched artistically in wall niches and coloured vases to compliment the effect. The vast, glass sliding doors can be opened fully to turn the indoors into a sheltered extension of outside.

Then of course there is the 42” flat screen TV for when you want to settle down to a movie, or keep the kids occupied whilst concocting your own cocktails or culinary delights in the high-end, designer kitchen.

With its Jenn-Air oven, American-style fridge, other equally high-tech appliances, matching utensils and tableware, this kitchen will make even the most humble sandwich present itself with an air of immodest swagger.

When the sun is long gone, and your eyelids start to get heavier, it doesn’t mean that the luxury needs to rest as well. Choose from one of the 5 spectacular bedrooms; if you have free reign, then the Master suite is the obvious choice, with a super king-size four-poster bed, a sparkling en-suite bathroom complete with Jacuzzi, a walk in shower and mind-blowing, triple aspect views. If you have to share the opulence however, the second bedroom boasts a huge super-king sleigh bed, east-facing sea views as well as a bathroom and shower. On the next floor there is another bedroom equipped with king-size bed and large en-suite bathroom, and an additional twin room and double room which share a bathroom and shower.

But let’s face it; you won’t be spending much time indoors during the sunlight hours. Two spacious verandas, East and West, provide a perfect combination of shaded and sun-kissed comfort. Complete with luxurious lounging chairs around the pool, sturdy dining tables, deck chairs and even a comfortable hammock. Designed to catch the sun all day long as well as providing plenty of shade for those less inclined to sun bathe, the verandas offer different, yet equally phenomenal views. To the East, the picturesque Green Island rises from the azure waves, from which a gentle breeze keeps guests pleasantly cool in the scorching sun. Whilst to the West, completely sheltered from the breeze, but still a sun-trap, a view of a fjord-like bay fills the panorama, a perfect spot for watching the sun go down after a long, blissful day’s self-pampering.

A further feature of this paradise, is that you can rest assured in the knowledge that, as part of the exclusive resort below at Nonsuch Bay, the area benefits from 24 hour security.
