Rock Cottage, Soldiers Bay, Blue Waters, Antigua, Caribbean Vacation Rental

 

Towering above the Caribbean Sea, providing breath-taking views of the turquoise waters, Rock Cottage is a shining example of luxury Caribbean accommodation. Ideal for a family reunion, couple’s retreat or high profile guests, Rock Cottage will shower its guests with sea, sun and serenity

Seclusion meets decadence at Rock Cottage, one of the Caribbean’s most private and intimate accommodations.

Boasting unspoiled vistas of the turquoise ocean, which can be admired from your private terrace, Rock Cottage offers its guests the upmost in beauty and elegance. The interior of the cottage enjoys a classic beach décor with a modern twist, bestowing upon it a warm and authentic atmosphere. And with up to 5 bedrooms, a separate living area, kitchen and plunge pool overlooking the ocean, there is more than enough to keep you occupied during your holiday. Rock Cottage is available with either two or five bedrooms, depending on your requirements.

The two bedroom option features two master bedrooms with luxury bathrooms which include double showers, vanity basins and bidets. The two bedroom option is suitable for four adults and two children.

The five bedroom option features an additional two bedrooms, available with en suite bathrooms. There is also a smaller bedroom with a queen sized bed and en suite shower room suitable for children, young adults or accompanying staff. The five bedroom option is suitable for a maximum of nine adults and three children

Private dining, picnic or barbecue lunches are available for guests to enjoy whilst sunning on the beach, jetty or sun deck. Other facilities include a barbecue for outside cooking, a fully-equipped kitchen, satellite TV and DVD player with films, night watchman on site, and plunge pool, plus numerous sundecks and dining areas.

One of the defining features of Rock Cottage is the concierge service, which is available from 8am-6pm. No matter what you require, your concierge will be on hand to see your stay is made as wonderful as possible. Upon arrival, Rock Cottage guests will be issued a mobile phone to place calls to the concierge, food & beverage operations and the management team, so no matter where you are in the resort, you’ll only be one phone call away from added luxury.

Rock Cottage enjoys a unique location on a rocky outcrop which juts into Soldiers Bay, resulting in beautiful views of the Caribbean Sea.

With the ocean surrounding the cottage on three sides, guests can enjoy serene vistas and intimate seclusion whilst luxuriating in their accommodation
