Lime-Acre Villa, South Coast, Jamaica Vacation Rental

 

If you enjoy off-the-beaten-track adventures that transport you to another time and place, then this villa escape is your match made in heaven. You'll discover provincial coastal life on the island's tranquil southern shoreline, famous for its rugged unspoiled beauty. Lime-Acre villa provides a sanctuary for anyone looking to truly get away from their hectic busy lives. Pick ripe fruit from within the 4 acres of grounds bursting with lush mango, guava, plantain, fig, and of course, lime trees. Follow the original winding stone footpath that leads you from the historic property to your secluded strip of private beach, and nearby coral reef teeming with tropical sea life.

This villa is also available as a 3 bedroom option starting at $1,132 per night.

Inside this magnificent plantation house is every luxury you could possibly crave during your stay, all perfected by our doting villa staff - an extension of your family from the moment you step over the threshold. Welcome home to the splendid and peaceful Lime-Acre.

From the moment you enter Lime Acre you can feel the history of the home surround you. The main floor is open-plan with the living and dining areas combined in one large space. The original mahogany details on the main floor extend upstairs to the bedrooms whose windows open wide to let in the sea breezes that lull you to sleep every evening.

The wraparound porch upstairs and the stone verandah on the main floor make afternoon reading a must for every guest no matter which spot you choose. The Main House with three bedrooms and Plantation Cottage with two bedrooms together can accommodate between 10-12 guests.
