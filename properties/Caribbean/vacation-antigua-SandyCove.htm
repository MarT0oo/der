Sandy Cove, Jumby Bay, Antigua, Caribbean


Sandy Cove is a delightful beachfront retreat that makes any Caribbean dream vacation a reality. This extremely spacious and comfortable four-bedroom beach villa offers over 4,150 square feet of open-plan indoor-outdoor living space, a large terrace, an oceanfront infinity-edge pool with expansive sundeck and a private beach – all with captivating views across the bay and out to mainland Antigua.

Three of the four bedrooms in this beautifully furnished home face the ocean and afford spectacular views. The upper level master bedroom offers a king-size, four-poster bed, separate dressing area and spacious balcony, while the ground-level master bedroom opens to a relaxing terrace. The light and airy villa also provides a modern kitchen, choice of two dining areas and a lovely garden. Positioned at the end of the series of villas, Sandy Cove boasts unrivaled privacy and tranquility.
