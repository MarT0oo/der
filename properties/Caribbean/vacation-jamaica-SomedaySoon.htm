Someday Soon, Montego Bay, Jamaica Vacation Rental

 

This outstanding villa features Jamaican influenced architecture and furnishings with a wonderful collection of original paintings by well-known Jamaican artists such as Errol Allen, Messam, and Jeffrey Samuels. Hand-knotted Persian rugs and Jamaican grass rugs add a flourish to the floors. The beautiful flow of the living space is complemented with landscaping of exceptional quality creating an environment that encapsulates the charm and serenity of living in the tropics. Four spacious, equal-sized bedrooms are in the main house and a fifth bedroom is in a private wing – all with immaculate, large en suite bathrooms. The first floor has two bedrooms, each with two twin beds that can be converted into a king-sized bed.

The second floor bedrooms and private wing bedroom each have king-sized beds. Each bedroom has a flat screen television and iPod dock. The en suite bathrooms have a private WC and separate indoor and outdoor showers. Downstairs, the outdoor shower is part of a private garden and upper level showers have planters with lush tropical foliage.

Each bedroom is a private retreat with large covered private verandas that provide magnificent views of the Tryall golf course and the sea beyond. All are decorated in quintessential Jamaican style with dark woods, beautiful fabrics and Jamaican art.

The villa offers large indoor living and dining rooms as well as two separate living and dining verandas. Every room, including the infinity pool and huge Spa, offer gorgeous expansive views of the sea.

The large indoor living room opens to a spacious outside living veranda and the 50’ infinity swimming pool. Both indoor and outdoor living spaces have flat screen televisions.

The indoor dining room has seating for 12 or more and opens onto a separate covered dining veranda featuring a Jamaican hand-carved round dining table for 10 and offering spectacular views. The veranda is a lovely place to dine informally and experience the produce from the villa’s organic garden as well as fruit from the surrounding lime, papaya, ackee and breadfruit trees. Bees in the garden hive provide very local honey.

There is a fabulous heated infinity pool which features a 50’ swimming lane and a 6” deep lounging platform, complete with lounge chairs, and incredible views of the sea. An inset infinity edge spa is heated to a comfortable 100 degrees and can accommodate 12 persons. Walls and gardens provide a private enclave enclosing the indoor and outdoor living areas and pool.

Someday Soon is the most energy-efficient villa at Tryall featuring hidden solar panels that provide electricity, water heating and charge the electric golf carts.

Your personal staff of six includes a chef to prepare your favorite foods, a butler to serve drinks and meals when and where you choose and daily housekeeping and laundry services.
