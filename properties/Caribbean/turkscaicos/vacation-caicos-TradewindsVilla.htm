Tradewinds Villa, Turtle Cove, Grace Bay, Providenciales, Turks and Caicos Vacation Rental

 

Tradewinds Villa is a tranquil 3-bedroom 3-bathroom, pod styled villa. Newly built and designed, this villa seamlessly blends indoor/outdoor living with privacy and comfort. Located in Turtle Cove, this villa is just steps from Grace Bay beach and the spectacular snorkeling spot known as Smith’s Reef. Smith’s Reef is widely known as the best “off-beach” snorkeling in Providenciales with endless tropical fish and lazy sea turtles.

In less than 5 minutes walking you can be dining at any one of the 6 restaurants and café’s located in Turtle Cove. The islands largest supermarket, the Graceway IGA, is located less than 5 minutes from the villa by car. Guests can also walk the beach to the islands world famous Fish Fry, where local restaurants, craft shops, and musicians gather every Thursday at 5pm to offer their best food, music and culture.

Guests at Tradewinds Villa enter through private louvered doors to a courtyard with ample seating, an out-door dining area and private swimming pool. Immediately on your right is the self-contained master bedroom with queen bed, on suite bathroom and walk in closet.

To the left as you enter, you will find the main pod. Here you will find:

- A second master suite with on suite bathroom
- A large living room
- Fully equipped kitchen with island
- A washer & drier
- The 3rd or guest bedroom with bathroom. (not en-suite)

Although, connected each bedroom in this pod has its own private entrance direct from the bedroom to the pool deck and gardens. Tradewinds villa is the perfect blend of location and privacy. It is ideal for 3 couples or a small family looking for a peaceful escape.
