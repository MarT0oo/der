Grace Too Beach House, Grace Bay, Providenciales, Turks & Caicos Vacation Rental

 

Grace Too Beach House is part of a family estate directly on the beach in the heart of Grace Bay, Providenciales, featuring three homes over two buildings; The Beach House, The Quarterdeck and The Cottage. The Beach House, a 3 bed rental, is a large, contemporary, completely renovated (completed December 2016) beachfront home that can accommodate up to 7 guests.

They villas can be rented separately or all together for an amazing family vacay. We have presently locked down the property to do further amazing upgrades and amenities.

Grace Too Beach House has 100 feet of beach front access to renowned Grace Bay beach, with its 11 km of white, sugar-fine sand. There are multiple restaurants, bars and activities steps away from the villa in either direction.

The large, beachfront pool is surrounded by pristine white loungers, sofas, Adirondacks, and beautiful landscaping designed to provide privacy while allowing unobstructed views of the stunning turquoise ocean. The outdoor pool BBQ and shower round out this vacation-essential space. Best of all, from Grace Too’s beachfront, you can explore amazing snorkeling at the national-park-protected Bight Reef.

The Beach House sits directly on Grace Bay Beach. It can sleep seven over three bedrooms, has 2.5 bathrooms (one is Jack-and-Jill), a dream kitchen, elegant indoor dining area, and panoramic views from its wrap-around veranda. Air conditioning throughout all the bedrooms keeps the home a perfect temperature, although with the refreshing ocean breezes coming through the beachfront wall-to-wall sets of sliding doors, you may never need it. The home’s Hemingway style architecture ensures you are perched to capture those breezes and enjoy the unsurpassed views of the turquoise ocean and white sand beach just outside your door. The 360-degree wrap-around veranda easily accommodates beachfront outdoor dining for all 16 guests of the estate.

Beach level you’ll find the beachfront private pool with loungers, sofa, Adirondacks, and outdoor kitchen. Two barbecues — one on the living level and one at the outdoor kitchen ensures lots of grilling space. The outdoor shower ensures the sand stays where it belongs. The estate comes equipped with four kayaks (two are double), two paddle boards, an assortment of lifejackets, as well as portable beach chairs, in case you find the beachfront pool deck too far away from the beach (at only seven paces away, we highly doubt it!).

Please note that the Beach House is only available for rent separate fromThe Quarterdeck and Cottage during low season, and on a case-by-case basis. Please note that the Quarterdeck and Cottage have their own shared pool.

Optional air con throughout property: Should you opt to use air conditioning, meters are read with you on your arrival and departure. You will be charged the going island rate during the time of your stay. While the base rate is 26 cents/kwh, Fortis also has an additional charge for the fuel factor which fluctuates monthly
