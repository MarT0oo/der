Acajou Villa, Leeward, Providenciales, Turks and Caicos Vacation Rental

 

Welcome to Villa Acajou, a limited availability luxury villa constructed in 2015 and nestled along the canals of Leeward Going Through Providenciales (“Provo”). Acajou is French for “Mahogany," and you will find this exotic wood throughout the house in custom built cabinets, counters, floors, doors, and windows. Villa Acajou has been professionally decorated and appointed by one of the island’s top decorators.

The main house has four bedrooms (3 Kings, 1 Queen) and 3.5 baths. There is also a full bath with an outdoor shower in the backyard beside a covered gazeebo. The solar heated pool sits between the gazeebo and an outdoor bar and grill, all of which border the 50 foot dock along the canal. If additional space is needed, a 2 bedroom, 2 bathroom guest house with a full kitchen and sitting area is available. Every bedroom and sitting area (including the outdoor gazebo) has a television connected to a state of the art media system which includes cable television, Appletv, and a movie server containing more than 800 movies. Watch your favorite movie in the pool after dinner!

Other amenities include a cross trainer on the second floor of the main house, 2 standup paddle boards, and a kayak. Feel free to paddle or kayak the canals or adventure out into the shallow ocean waters less than a 5 minute paddle away.

Leeward is ideally situated at the east end of Provo and is less than 5 minutes from a local beach, as well as famous Grace Bay Beach (rated #1 best beach in the world on Trip Advisor) and Long Bay Beach (rated #10 best beach in the world on Trip Advisor). Acajou is also less than a 5 minute walk to Big Blue Adventures (for fun recreational activities) or the Blue Haven Resort where there are additional outdoor activities (Caribbean Cruisin and Catch the Wave), the Elevate Spa, 2 restaurants, and a market/coffee shop. Of course the heart of Provo with all of its exceptional restaurants and shopping is just minutes away.

Book this villa quickly because it is only available for rental for less than 25% of the year.
