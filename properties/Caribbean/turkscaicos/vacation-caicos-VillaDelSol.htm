Villa Del Sol, Grace Bay, Providenciales, Turks and Caicos Vacation Rental

 

The natural beauty of the Turks and Caicos Islands is astounding. But every now and then, something comes along that still has the power to just take your breath away. Here, on the Island of Providenciales, that ‘something’ is your first encounter with the magnificent Villa del Sol.

Located in the exclusive Leeward district of Providenciales, Villa Del Sol sits directly on famous Grace Bay Beach.

A three bedroom masterpiece, the villa was completely refurbished by the new owners and the pictures tell the story best. Villa Del Sol showcases sun, sand, azure-blue ocean waters, with direct walkout to a private pool, white sands, and outdoor living spaces of undeniable character and comfort
