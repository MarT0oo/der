Flip Flop House, Long Bay, Providenciales, Turks & Caicos Vacation Rental

 

Flip Flop Cottage, brand new to the rental market and perfect for kiteboarding enthusiasts or small families, is located in the beautiful Long Bay subdivision of Providenciales, Turks & Caicos Island. It is a wonderfully spacious 2 bedroom, 2 bath, 2 story home with astounding views of the ocean. It has a with a wonderfully breezy balcony with alfresco dining, as well as a terrace with loungers and a BBQ for your dining pleasure. Walk down the road and beach access to beautiful Long Bay beach or drive some 5 minutes to famed Grace Bay beach, as well as grocery stores, shopping, restaurants, night life and water activities.

The Owner provides linens, beach towels, kitchen equipment, TV, telephone, washer/dryer, maid service before and after the rental period. Cribs, high chairs, baby equipment, beach chairs, bikes, etc., are not included. Baby equipment can be rented through a third party on request at the time of booking at additional cost.

Starter size amenities only provided on arrival, such as shampoo, conditioner, soap, toilet paper, paper towel, sponge, dish soap and laundry detergent

Situated in Bringamosa, Long Bay Beach is on the East coast of Providenciales. You might want to drop by regardless of whether you're staying in the vicinity. Long Bay Beach stands out because it's perfect for getting away from vacation stresses, like overly crowded restaurants and attractions. Many of the other beaches in Turks and Caicos may be just as pretty, but tend to be busier because they're lined with some of the most famous hotels and restaurants.
