Footprints on the Beach, Grace Bay, Providenciales, Turks and Caicos, Caribbean Vacation Rental

 

Make your mark on famed Grace Bay Beach at Footprints on the Beach. Set on the exclusive Tranquility Lane on the beach’s eastern end, the six-bedroom villa has extensive outdoor spaces and 100 feet of oceanfront on Grace Bay Beach.

The villa is surrounded by an acre of landscaped grounds, and opens to a backyard with pool, terrace, fire pit and beach. Spend the day using the snorkel and swimming, playing on the outdoor pool table or ping pong table, relaxing on the beach chairs or hammock, then rinse off in one of the two outdoor showers. You will enjoy the services of your very own private chef creating delicious delicacies from his extensive repertoire. Any three daily meals prepared for you (groceries extra) or be catered by the on-staff butler. Gather around the fire pit to watch the stars or, if it’s a warm evening, retreat inside to the central air conditioning and satellite television.

Footprints has recently undergone a change in ownership and just completed (Nov 2018) a multimillion-dollar renovation to both the interior and exterior. The villa has been professionally decorated with all new contemporary furnishings, a new kitchen and a chef kitchen with an open design on the main floor.

Each of the villa’s six bedrooms has a full en-suite bathroom, air conditioning, satellite television, ceiling fan and is stocked with bathrobes and toiletries. There are five bedrooms with king beds (one wheelchair accessible bedroom), and one bedroom with two bunk beds (4 beds). There are two high chairs and two cribs available for the youngest guests.

Though Footprints on the Beach is tucked away from the bustle of the island’s resort area, it’s just 3.2 km by car to the golf course, and 4.8 km to grocery stores, a tennis court, the Salt Mills shopping area and downtown. Have dinner and drinks at the restaurants and bars along the busier areas of Grace Bay, then enjoy a quiet evening back on your own private stretch of sand.
