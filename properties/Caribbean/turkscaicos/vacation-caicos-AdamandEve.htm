Adam and Eve, Leeward, Providenciales, Turks and Caicos Vacation Rental

 

A Fun Villa with lots to do. Adam and Eve is comfortable, spacious and beautiful, perched on the peaceful canal front of Leeward, Providenciales, a minute drive to the Marina and 4 minute drive from Grace Bay Town Center, Entertainment, Shopping and Restaurant district. Surrounded by 4 amazing beaches - Pelican Beach, Grace Bay Beach, Long Bay Beach and Emerald Beach all within 4 minutes dive and not even 3 minutes walk to the TCI Ferry and the Dock, for Island hopping, fishing and jet skiing. Tour the Leeward canal on your very own private two singles and two double Ocean Kayaks, complete with cushioned seats, life vests and paddles directly from the private dock at the villa.

Boasting 4 Bedrooms and 4.5 Bathrooms the Villa sleeps 9 comfortably and is nestled close enough to all the activities, beaches, restaurants and shopping of Grace Bay yet far away enough to enjoy the privacy and relaxation of our beautiful island. The Villa, though fully air conditioned, enjoys a constant light breeze, has great natural lighting and is a writer's inspiration.

Adam and Eve offers everything you need for a wonderful vacation with family and friends, from a fully stocked gourmet kitchen and well-appointed bedroom suites, to a 50-foot curving edge pool, swim up bar, outdoor BBQ grill, 120 feet boat dock, plush sun loungers and beautiful interiors. All the bedrooms are decorated with the perfect mix of Caribbean colours.

Dramatic tall sliding glass doors in the living areas and bedrooms open to invite the cool Caribbean breeze, views of the outdoors, gardens, water and putting green. The pool deck enjoy full sunshine most of the day, great for sunbathing and the pool softens in the night with cool ever changing led lights. The gourmet kitchen has custom wood cabinetry, stainless steel appliances, double ovens, granite counter, wine cooler, water dispenser and ice maker and a separate 32 ft freezer. Every kitchen gadget you could want to prepare a small snack or a gourmet meal has been provided.

The gardens are beautifully landscaped around the Pool and features a Mini Golf course, ideal to practice your swing. The larger 18 hole Provo Golf Club, a Carl Litten designed course rated a one of the best in the Caribbean is four minutes drive away. Other activities close by are snorkeling, diving, paddle boarding, kite boarding, eco tours, island tours, the conch farm, jet skiing while you can kayak, swim or sail, directly from the boat dock.
