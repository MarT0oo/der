Always Summer, Turtle Tail, Providenciales, Turks and Caicos Vacation Rental

 

Welcome to Always Summer Villa. This lovely appointed 3 bedroom villa sits directly on Turtle Lake. The crystal clear water is, literally, at your door step, waiting for you to enjoy. Just jump in.

Always Summer Villa has over 180 degrees of fantastic waterfront views and is perfect for family fun or couples. The location is ideal for kayaking, paddle boarding, bone fishing or just watching wild flamingos meander lazily.

Hop in the provided kayaks (2) or on our paddle boards (2) and follow the canal to the open water of the Ocean in just 15 mins. Guests can also drive a short 1/4 mile to the beach, at the end of the street turn left and go a few feet.
