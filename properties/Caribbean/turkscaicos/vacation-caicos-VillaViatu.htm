Villa Viatu, Leeward, Providenciales, Turks and Caicos Vacation Rental

 

Somewhere along the sands of the Leeward Beaches, you'll find the best villa on the island of Providenciales. With it's modern touches and flair, beautiful living areas, amazing open flow of rooms and features a one of a kind experience with daily maid and a free chef for a day! The house is only a few minuets walk (210 yards) to the beautiful white sandy beaches, great for families looking to vacation together.

This house opens up to an amazing dining and living area. Creamy white living area with high gloss white modern Euro Cabinetry make for an amazing view. You'll get lost in the beauty once you enter this magical villa. The islands beauty and grace last year round. The house also has a very unique double kitchen, both top and bottom floors, offering guests the ability to take two families independently and make their space unique. A great idea also for Kosher clients needing two kitchens to prep.

The Villa houses 4 separate, self-contained suites arranged around a set of common areas and amenities, such as a pool, outdoor space, hot tub, BBQ area, fire pit and hammock grove. 2 of the suites feature 2 bedrooms, each with en-suite bathroom, a powder room, and a fully equipped kitchen. The other 2 are studio suites, with sleeping, living and dining areas, a sleeper sofa in addition to the bed, a private bathroom and a kitchenette, to afford the desired level of privacy.
