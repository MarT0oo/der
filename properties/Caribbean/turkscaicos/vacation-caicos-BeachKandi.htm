Beach Kandi, Turtle Cove, Grace Bay, Providenciales, Turks and Caicos Vacation Rental

 

Beach Kandi is a luxurious two-bedroom beachfront villa with incredible ocean views from every room. This villa is set as close to the beach as one could expect. All the rooms have floor-to-ceiling windows and doors to make the most of the panoramic view. On the ground floor, you can slide the glass windows open to connect the interior living area to the pool terrace. Walk onto the white limestone patio to the edge of the infinity pool. Stylish loungers, teak day beds, and bright red umbrellas complete the ambiance.

The two bedrooms feature photographic ocean views and king size beds. Each room has a walk-in closet, full en-suite bathroom with shower, double vanities, and a deep soaking tub. The glass bedroom doors welcome in the cool ocean breeze. The exterior doors of the ground-level bedroom open directly to the pool, while the second-level bedroom has its own covered balcony.

The living area has an open plan lounge, 6-person dining table, and full kitchen with stainless steel appliances and white marble countertops. A plasma television, Bose blue tooth stereo, and DVD player are found in the living room and in each bedroom. Modern amenities such as fast Wi-Fi, a pre-paid cell phone for local calls, Vonnage phone for free overseas calls (some limits apply), and an iPad mini with pre-loaded music are included. Ceiling fans and air-conditioning keep the villa cool and comfortable.

Be sure to pick up a striped beach towel and the villa’s snorkeling gear and step down to the shoreline. Swim out to the excellent snorkeling sights of Smith’s Reef to see colorful corals, sea fans, schools of fish, and maybe a spotted eagle ray or turtle.

Your pool enjoyment doesn’t need to stop at night. Turn on the outdoor lights and the pool heater. The terrace has lounge seating, a propane BBQ, and with the ocean just off your patio, you have the perfect scene for casual dining.

Turtle Cove Marina is just a 7-minute walk from your villa. Stroll along the dock and watch the yachts as they leave and return from their journeys. The marina has a selection of restaurants and boutique shops, a casino, and tour and activity operators.

If you’re looking for an elegant beachfront villa with a view you won’t forget, Beach Kandi provides just that.
