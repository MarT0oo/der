Beach Daze Villa, Leeward, Providenciales, Turks and Caicos Vacation Rental

 

Beautiful Beach Daze is located in the exclusive enclave of Leeward, on the island of Providenciales, in the Turks and Caicos Islands, on what is often voted as the best beach in the world, Grace Bay Beach.

Welcome to this beautiful Caribbean Chic designed, 3 Bedroom, 2 Bath home just and easy 4-minute walk to the water’s edge of famed Pelican Beach in Grace Bay, Providenciales, Turks and Caicos Islands. The villa offers a large fresh water, sport bottom, 32′ x 22′ private swimming pool with a tanning shelf, sleek waterfall feature and cool LED lighting. The living space is outfitted in tasteful modern Caribbean décor, large 8ft windows and beautiful views of the large pool, gardens and coconut palm trees.

Villa can sleep up to 8: King master bedroom, Queen bedroom, and a trundle bedroom that can be a King, and a Queen sofa in the living room. Additional person fee is $30 each plus tax per night after the first 6 persons.

Enjoy the comforts of the Pool level living room with exposed ceilings and bask in the great natural lighting from the 8ft high windows all around. A Samsung TV (with Blue Ray DVD with Smart TV apps) in the family room, adds to the entertainment and keeps you connected, though who would choose to surf the web when you can enjoy a lazy day by the poolside or beach combing a few steps away? The fully equipped kitchen with new stainless-steel appliances is ready for meal preparation, or you can BBQ on the pool patio and dine alfresco. Walk 4 steps upstairs to a spacious master bedroom with king bed and ensuite bathroom boasting a Jacuzzi tub and walk in shower. You can also enjoy the views of the blooming bougainvillea and swaying palms outdoors while relaxing in bed. All three bedrooms have new Samsung Smart TVs and Bluetooth enabled speakers. The oversized 44-foot-long covered patio under 11 ft. ceilings comes complete with a large sectional, outdoors fans and romantic lighting. The expansive pool deck accented by comfortable chaise loungers is the perfect way to enjoy the Caribbean sun. Additional touches include, outdoor Bluetooth speaker, hammock under the palms, unique pink umbrellas, alfresco dining, plush pool and beach towels and the most amazing stargazing you will find this side of Paradise.

True to the name BEACH DAZE, this vacation rental gem is located in Leeward, the upscale, gated (with CCTV and night security guards), beach community in Grace Bay, and at the edge of Grace Bay’s restaurant and shopping district. It is close to restaurants, golf course, tennis courts, casino, watersports, marina, shopping and so much more. For your comfort, the villa is fully fenced, gated, and monitored by our on-island security team as well as fitted with a house alarm system and automatic dusk to dawn outdoor lighting.

There are two separate air conditioning systems in this holiday villa, one for the master bedroom and the next for everywhere else. Beach Daze gets a comfortable, almost constant trade wind as well. Travelers’ Choice rated Providenciales the #1 island in the world. Dynamic growth continues to transform the demographic profile of the Turks and Caicos Islands in a very positive way, and as a result, we can never guarantee that there will be no new construction in the area.
