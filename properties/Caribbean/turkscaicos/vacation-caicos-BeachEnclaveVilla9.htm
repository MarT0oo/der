Beach Enclave Villa 9, Babalua Beach, North Shore, Providenciales, Turks and Caicos, Caribbean Vacation Rental

 

Ten acres of private gated property in Turks & Caicos welcome you into your own magical oasis when you book a stay at Villa Beach Enclave North Shore - Villa 9. Panoramic views of the gorgeous waters of the Caribbean wake you each morning, as you step out of your bedroom suite into the open floor plan of the huge living space. In true island style, this home blends airy indoor and outdoor living spaces with ease. You can fill up your coffee and then wander out to the manicured gardens, where the lily pond creates a gorgeous scene for your morning. The comfortable outdoor furniture is every bit as luxurious as the cozy seating indoors, so you’ll want to spend the day soaking up the sun. But if you want some peace indoors, your spacious media room offers plenty of ways for you to relax.

In the afternoon, take a dip in your private pool, or pack up your lunch and head down to the beach. You also have access to the beachside resort amenities, such as yoga, a pool, water sports activities, and more. Four bedrooms and four bathrooms comfortably suit up to eight guests in absolute privacy and luxury. With plenty of space to spread out (both indoors and out) and huge bedroom suites, this is the ideal spot for a family getaway, a vacation with friends, or even a company retreat. No matter how you choose to enjoy Villa Beach Enclave North Shore - Villa 9, you’ll love every inch of this rich property. Don’t miss a chance to walk along the white sand beaches and dip your toes in the warm turquoise waters of Turks & Caicos.

You’ll want to dine al fresco and take in the view of the sun setting over the water every night. Your stay is made even more relaxing thanks to the full staff that takes care of everything. You enjoy the services of a butler, housekeeping staff, a pool maintenance person, and the on-call property manager. Every modern convenience is provided, including Wi-Fi, cable TV, access to the pool from the bedrooms, a security system, and more. There’s also an outdoor shower, a sunbed, and other luxurious ways to enjoy the outdoors. Give your guests privacy and luxury thanks to the wide-open design of this beautiful villa with tons of amenities.
