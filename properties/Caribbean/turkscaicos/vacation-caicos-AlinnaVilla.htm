Alinna Villa, Chalk Sound, Providenciales, Turks and Caicos Vacation Rental

 

Four master suites are beautifully and tastefully appointed around Villa Alinna’s large swimming pool and jacuzzi to give guests the ultimate feeling of privacy and luxury while enjoying frontal views of Chalk Sound. The fifth bedroom is setup as a kid’s room with two bunk-style beds (two queen and two twin beds) with an ensuite bathroom. The interior living space is a large, open concept style. The kitchen boasts top of the line appliances; an electric cooktop, oven, microwave and small ice machine. The living room has a large sectional sofa that can comfortably seat the whole house and a large flat screen monitor with standard cable television. The villa also has an interior and exterior surround sound system that operates via an application that can be downloaded for free on a smartphone.

The house is a single level layout throughout with a shallow pool and easy access into the ocean which makes it absolutely ideal for families with young children and/or seniors. The annex above the garage offers families a game room with an exterior patio that has an outdoor pool table. There is also a mini-gym complete with a set of weights, a treadmill and a bowflex machine. Upon prior request and approval, the annex can be converted into an additional suite with an ensuite bathroom and ocean views.

Along the ocean guests are provided with access to the villa’s multiple tandem and single ocean kayaks and paddleboards to explore the many small islands located directly out in front of the house. The area is national park so there is lots of marine life to see. Guests of Villa Alinna are close to two of Providenciales’ most popular beaches; Sapodilla and Taylor Bay. Ask our daily Villa Host to prepare some foldable chairs and a cooler to take with you each day while exploring the island and it’s many beaches.
