Villa Skyline, Blue Mountain, Providenciales, Turks and Caicos Vacation Rental

 

This one of a kind newly built luxury Villa comes with over 4,000 ft² of outside entertaining areas set over 4 floors and offers ocean and island views from all angles of the villa.

The interior of the Villa boosts 6,000 ft² with 2 large bedrooms all with ensuite bathrooms located on the main floor and then located on the 3rd floor we have the master suite, which includes a private owners lounge and gym area together with a master bedroom, large closet, and vanity area.

The main living area on the 2nd floor is an open plan kitchen with dining room concept with a living room off the one side and a large entrance foyer.

All rooms have access to either the pool and decking area or to one of the many large entertaining balcony areas. On the ground floor of the villa, there is a separate maids quarters which has a separate entrance from the main house.
