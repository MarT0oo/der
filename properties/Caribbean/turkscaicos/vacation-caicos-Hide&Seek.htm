Welcome to Hide and Seek villa! Hide and Seek villa is a new luxury home in Long Bay, filled with amenities. Guests enter the private compound via a winding private driveway to get a first view of the stunning pool and lush gardens hosting a variety of tropical plants.

Grace Bay resorts and activities are a 5-10 minute drive away, depending on where you want to go.

Interior living spaces feature a color palette, reflecting the tropical brilliance of Turks and Caicos. The open concept floor plan integrates seamlessly from indoor to outdoor living courtesy of sliding glass hurricane windows.
