Coral Pavilion, Grace Bay, Providenciales, Turks & Caicos Vacation Rental

 

Coral Pavilion in the Turks and Caicos is among one of the best luxury vacation rentals in the world. The unparalleled location is just 5 minutes from the heart of Grace Bay with its restaurants, amenities, and shopping. The home is adjacent to Coral House, one of the island’s most celebrated estates and a complimentary neighbor. Set within approximately 1 acre of manicured tropical gardens with 120’ of lineal beach frontage, Coral Pavilion features stately Bajan architecture including a five-bedroom villa and a one-bedroom staff cottage spanning more than 8,000 ft² (under roof) Coral Pavilion offers a generous mix of indoor and outdoor living space ideal for entertaining, whether it be large parties, or an intimate family gathering.

Upon entering the Coral Pavilion you'll find a pair of coral stone stairs leads you over a Frangipani framed fountain, through the entry pavilion, and into an impressive courtyard graced by the beautiful coral stone facade of the two-story main house. The living room opens onto an expansive covered terrace, offering commanding views of the turquoise sea and the grand 55’ pool flanked by manicured lawns. At the end of the lawns, the beachfront gazebos feature the quintessential “Messel green” lattice work, surrounded on either side by large palm trees. A water feature and outdoor grille station frame this expansive area.

Coral Pavilion has been impeccably detailed by its creators. A soft color palette with the Barbadian coral stone, carved and shaped by expert craftsmen, provides a complimentary match to the painted hardwood windows and doors. Turkish sandstone flooring, handmade custom tile, and black and white marble round out the materials.

The hardwood louvered shutters and awnings evoke the great houses of Barbados, designed by the famed Oliver Messel, a favorite of the British aristocracy. Painted in the same “Messel” green hue, the cabinetry in the home’s glamorous kitchen spaces includes a full chef’s kitchen and a butler’s pantry. A formal dining room capable of seating 14 is separated from the kitchen by a serving area. The lower level is completed by a spacious ocean-facing sitting room, also accessible from one of four gracious guest bedrooms – each with its own large en-suite bathroom and beautifully appointed closets.

A sweeping staircase and double height space featuring magnificent custom French lanterns leads to the master suite which enjoys the entire upper level, spanning more than 1,500 ft² It features white-washed floors and entirely timber clad walls and curved ceilings, with built-in window seats. The master bedroom terrace is detailed with elegant balustrades and coral flooring with turquoise glass tile insets to play off the ocean colors. The master bathroom enjoys gorgeous views from the wet room rain shower and cast-iron soaking tub. A private study and large walk-in dressing room complete the master suite.
