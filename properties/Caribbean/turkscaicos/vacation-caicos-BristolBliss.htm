Bristol Bliss, Turtle Tail, Providenciales, Turks and Caicos Vacation Rental

 

Welcome to Bristol Bliss, an immaculate four bedroom all-suite villa located in the prestigious Turtle Tail region of Providenciales, in the Turks and Caicos Islands.

This 3,200 square foot home offers guests four frontal ocean view suites with large ensuite bathrooms all centered around the home's open concept 'Great Room'. The Great Room has a large, well equipped, contemporary kitchen, complete with a full chef's selection of appliances and cooking utensils. Located in the Great Room is also a large interior dining room that accommodates eight comfortably as well as a spacious living room with a large 65" flat screen television. The interior/exterior zoned surround sound system is powered by Sonos and can wirelessly be controlled by phone or tablet by simply downloading an app.

The interior space can be easily opened up to the exterior pool deck via the villa's large sliding doors. The pool deck offers guests lots of exterior seating in the form of plush sofas and lounge chairs. There is a large eight seater exterior dining table that is covered under the villa's roof and positioned conveniently beside the outdoor propane barbeque. The shallow but large salt water pool is ideal for families with young children, seniors or guests that are less comfortable in a deeper pool.

As you walk down to the beach from the pool deck, guests have a manicured and lit walkway to the beach as well as beach deck with chairs and ample shade. The beach will feel like your own private stretch of sand, with next to no foot traffic from neighboring homes. Bristol Bliss' location on the South side of the island, mean guests can enjoy the shallow and clear Caicos Banks. For as far as the eye can see (roughly 50 miles out and 90 miles across) the depth does not exceed 8' to 10'.

To enhance your luxury beachfront experience, Bristol Bliss also provides guests with a double kayak and two paddleboards to use throughout their stay complimentary. The small island 'Cays' located in the ocean roughly a quarter mile in front of the villa make for a kayakers dream location to explore.
