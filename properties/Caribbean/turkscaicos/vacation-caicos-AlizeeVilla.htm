Alizee Villa, Taylor Bay, Chalk Sound, Providenciales, Turks and Caicos Vacation Rental

 

Alizee Villa sits on 1.9 acres, with 220' of beach front on the south shore of Providenciales. Taylor Bay beach is accessible via a private pathway from the house to the beach. At the beach you will find your own private deck with loungers. This Villa features four bedrooms all with en-suite baths.

In addition to a screened sitting and dining area, there is a formal living and dining room, and a fully equipped kitchen that has a pass through to the screened porch. Villa Alizee is equipped with all major appliances.

The large pool is surrounded by a grand terrace and a covered gazebo, perfect for sun or shade bathing.

The Villa is comprised of one main building that has the Master bedroom and twin bunk beds in the second bedroom, the two King Pod are located on both end of the pool and an optional 5th king bedroom guest house, easily accommodating 10 adults.

Complimentary use of private Sunset Bay tennis courts a short walk away from the villa
