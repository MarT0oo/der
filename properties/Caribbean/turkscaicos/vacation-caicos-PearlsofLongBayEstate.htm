Pearls of Long Bay Estate, Long Bay, Providenciales, Turks and Caicos Vacation Rental

 

Spend tranquil days swimming, kite surfing, snorkeling or simply walking on the pristine beach as the cooling waters lap underfoot. Or luxuriate under the island sun on beautiful pool decks furnished with designer loungers, or plunge into soothing waters to get refreshed. Whatever your choice, the spectacular setting of each Pearl will provide you with an unforgettably unique vacation.

Located along Provo’s stunning Long Bay Beach, welcome to the Pearls of Long Bay Estate, a group of three equally incredible seaside villas. The perks at this luxury vacation rental have been set to overdrive for fun in the sun and pleasant repose. Get the rest and relaxation you deserve, next holiday, at this exclusive oceanfront property in the Turks and Caicos!

Each of the three villas includes two swimming pools and a children’s wading pool. Or perhaps it’s time for a soothing soak in the therapeutic bubbles of the hot tub? Take your pick at each villa. Decked out for the consummate entertainer, you will also find the following in every residence: alfresco dining sets, pool bars, Japanese teppanyaki cooking stations, barbeques, sun loungers, umbrellas, beachside sound systems and children’s play sets. The interiors of these elegant residences are equally impressive with stunning home theater rooms with popcorn machines, cable TV, indoor/outdoor sound systems and other divertissements including Netflix, Apple TV and a DVD/Blu-ray players. Your reservation includes housekeeping, and a private chef.

The Pearls of Long Bay Estate offer guests uninterrupted views of the turquoise waters spanning the Caicos Banks while marrying contemporary design with Caribbean barefoot luxury. These stunning homes seamlessly harmonize the idea of indoor/outdoor living with beautiful landscaping, decks and lagoons throughout, each with 12,000 square feet of sophisticated living space. High vaulted ceilings and plenty of overhead fans keep the feelings airy and breezy.

A whopping total of 23 bedrooms suites accommodate up to 54 guests at this non-smoking vacation property. Some suites open directly to a swimming pool via a set of steps right at the door. All of the suites offer dual vanities, televisions, Netflix and Apple TV. Sleep soundly with nothing but the ocean waves as your soundtrack!

The Pearls of Long Bay Estate offer first class amenities reminiscent of a five star resort. Continue your workout regime in the state of the art 1,000 square foot gymnasium or get together for some tennis on the championship court. Relax in the Turkish steam bath, sauna or alfresco massage pavilion. The Provo Golf Club is also just a few minutes away. This estate makes a great bet for corporate retreats, weddings and celebrations, or an epic reunion of family and friends!
