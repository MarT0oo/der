Lindia House, Long Bay beach, Providenciales, Turks and Caicos Vacation Rental

 

Spend tranquil days swimming, kite surfing, snorkeling or simply walking on the pristine beach as the cooling waters lap underfoot. Or luxuriate under the island sun on beautiful pool decks furnished with designer loungers, or plunge into soothing waters to get refreshed. Whatever your choice, the spectacular setting of each Pearl will provide you with an unforgettably unique vacation.

Beautifully landscaped gardens surround these gated, picturesque and private homes, allowing you to bask in the Island’s beauty. Framed by the ocean and by a championship tennis court, the Pearl's also offer a shared fully equipped, air conditioned gym, Turkish steam bath, sauna, and an alfresco massage are for two, as well as a bathroom and shower area. Delight in the joy of unparalleled luxury and comfort as you while away blissful hours in privacy.

Discretely located directly on the silky sands of world renowned Long Bay beach in Providenciales, Turks & Caicos Islands, The Pearls are marvelous beachfront homes that offer unforgettable views of the azure blue Atlantic. These stunning 8 bed 8.5 bath beachfront villas marry the essence of the Caribbean with extreme luxury, resulting in 12000 square feet each of simple splendor, sophistication and seclusion. Whether visiting for a romantic getaway or to escape the real world with your family and friends, welcome to the magical world of The Pearls of Long Bay Estate.

At dusk, you’ll be treated to memorable sunsets. And as darkness sets in, beautiful custom lighting will cast a magical glow around you as music wafts through the air via the speakers that are discretely located, with keypads throughout.

While the soft contemporary furnishings ensure your comfort, the spacious 700 ft² living room with sliding wall to wall glass windows, allows you to bask in natural light and fresh breezes, while enjoying breathtaking views.

Each has a fully equipped interior kitchen that would make a chef proud. Top of the line appliances (fridge, freezer, ice maker, oven, dishwasher, microwave, wine cooler) are combined with Nespresso coffee makers, blender, toaster...and basically everything one might need to produce gourmet meals.

Their cinema rooms each have 10 cinema seats, along with a popcorn machine, fridge, DVD player, Blu-ray player, Apple TV and a stunning Hi Definition 95 inch TV.

The outdoor living oasis at the center of each Pearl connects the front deck with the lagoon area. Each lagoon has two beautiful decks for alfresco dining, as well as a pool and waterfall, perfect for lounging on the designer patio beds surrounded by regal palms and Caribbean flowers. These lagoons are also the location of gourmet alfresco covered kitchens; equipped with custom built Tepanyaki, BBQ, side burners, ice maker, two fridges, 2 small wine coolers and a cocktail machine. These dreamy lagoons will be the perfect place to enjoy a starlight suppers or cocktail hours.

For the pleasure of Pearl guests, each villa is equipped with kite surfing and snorkeling equipment, as well as a playground with swings, seesaws and climbers. Or enjoy a wonderful game of tennis or basketball on the shared court

For the fitness minded, there is a shared 900 sq ft. air conditioned gym equipped with the latest model of treadmill, Stairmaster, dumbbell rack and 14 other workout devices.

Or for relaxation, you can book a couples massage, or simply enjoy a Turkish Steam bath or sauna in the shared spa area, which also offers an open air shower and bathroom. If you opt to include a private chef and daily maid service, they will endeavor to make your stay as simple and carefree as possible. For your personal use, or for staff should you opt to have them, the laundry rooms have both washers and dryers for your convenience.

Each Pearl has eight stunning bedrooms with bath ensuite, that capture the magical beauty of Turks & Caicos. The spacious master suite, with sliding glass doors, opens to a beautiful balcony with astounding views. The remaining guest bedrooms, likewise luxuriously appointed, also offer ensuite bath, ceiling fans, air conditioning, a 55 inch flat screen TV, Apple TV, Netflix, Wi-Fi, safe and a fridge.

Each 600 ft. front deck is divided in half and connected with a 16 ft. bridge with infinity pool, Jacuzzi and kiddie pool. Each Pearl has a 70 ft. wooden path leading to beach decks landscaped with beautiful palm trees. These stone decks are equipped with designer patio beds, as well as a canopy bed. Imagine listening to your favorite music wafting overhead through discretely placed speakers, making stargazing a magically romantic experience.

Providenciales is home to several of the world’s most renowned beaches, with sugar-fine sand and azure blue waters, and we encourage you to explore them all. Or perhaps a horseback riding along the white sands in Long Bay will appeal to the nature lovers in your party. Or maybe you will want to explore the reef-protected waters of the Princess Alexandra National Park with your family and friends. But whatever your choices, please do not miss out on visiting the enchanting streets of Grace Bay; where you’ll discover wonderful shops, world class restaurants, and a vibrant nightlife.
