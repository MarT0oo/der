Ocean Villa 5, Amanyara, Turks & Caicos, Caribbean Vacation Rental

 

Our Beach Villa 5 Bedrooms has direct access to Amanyara’s private powdery white-sand beach and turquoise waters.

Lying on the western shore of Northwest Point and bordering the pristine reefs of Northwest Point Marine National Park, Amanyara offers four-bedroom villas for rent. Every villa stands on a little over an acre and a half of flush landscape and is framed by an intimate reflecting pool or an ornamental lake.

Surrounded by the timeless, tranquil elements of the Caribbean, you can opt for a view overlooking serene ponds or the ocean. Perfecting the indoor/outdoor style of living, each villa is made up of pavilions encompassing 8,000sq ft (743sq m) in total. There are four indoor/outdoor master bedroom pavilions (plus two staff bedrooms), a spacious living room and dining room pavilion, a fully outfitted kitchen, an infinity edged swimming pool (in black volcanic rock surrounded by extensive hardwood decking) and an outdoor dining sala.

Built with floor-to-ceiling glass and with an abundance of natural finishes in wood and stone, the overall effect is understated, calm and exclusive. The dedicated living/dining pavilion has a vaulted ceiling and sound systems. Many also have an internal pond. Guests can leave the seclusion of their private villa to make use of the wide range of resort facilities available to them.

A personal cook and housekeeper take care of all guest needs. Villas come with two four-seater golf buggies, and guests may enjoy access to the resort’s facilities and services.

Every aspect of the operation of the villas is overseen by Aman resorts, including staff management, general maintenance, security, housekeeping, gardening and guest amenities.
