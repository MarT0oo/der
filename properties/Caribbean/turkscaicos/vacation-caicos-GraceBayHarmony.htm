Grace Bay Harmony, Grace Bay, Providenciales, Turks and Caicos Vacation Rental


Harmony 4 (4) Bedroom Beach Pool Level Residence (4500 ft²): ocean front living and dining room with terrace, two ocean front master bedrooms with terrace, ocean view and garden view bedrooms with terrace, four and a half bathrooms. Outdoor shower on master bedroom terrace with plunge pool, media room

Discerning travelers agree: after your holiday at this Estate Beach Level Residence, the Caribbean will simply never be the same. This luxury residence is part of the illustrious Grace Bay Resorts, one of the first developers to discover this jewel destination on Turks and Caicos. Overlooking the sparkling turquoise waters of the sea, little could compare to the sublime vibrations to be experienced at this oceanfront bijou villa.

Just steps from the door, you will find sun loungers and the pristine waters of the private plunge pool. Rinse off in the alfresco shower. With an established reputation for superlative service and innovation, enjoy the many amenities of Grace Bay: swimming pool, welcome drinks, pool and beach set up alongside complimentary daily American breakfasts, start-up bar and shuttle service. Housekeeping is included and a babysitter, activities, spa services and a chef can be arranged for a nominal fee.

The interiors feature high vaulted ceilings that offer that quintessential Caribbean atmosphere. Furnishings are modern and plush. The open plan dining, kitchen and living area offers an easy and breezy feeling. The kitchen includes a center island breakfast bar for ultimate convenience. The villa also includes a home entertainment system, an office space and Wi-Fi access.

Four fabulous bedrooms and additional bedding accommodate up to eight adults and two children at this non-smoking villa. All bedrooms feature en-suite bathrooms, ceiling fans and direct access to the terrace. You will find king beds in three suites and a queen bed in the fourth. Only the finest linens, bedding and accoutrements have been provided to help ensure soothing nights of rest. Wake up each new morning to the shining sun and glimmering sea!
