Salacia, Grace Bay, Providenciales, Turks and Caicos Vacation Rental

 

Providing the perfect contrast to the vibrant blues and greens of Grace Bay’s tropical environment, villa Salacia invites its guests to relax in luxury with its elegant white color palette. Highlighted by wood and stone accents, Salacia’s interior is both fun and formal, making an ideal host for any occasion. Spacious openings and numerous windows make sure the ocean view is never far away when your inside. For a closer look, head out the two-level oceanfront terrace, which is decorated with covered and uncovered lounge areas and an outdoor kitchen.

Decorated with lush tropical greenery and mature palms, this stylish oceanfront villa offers incredible views of the Caribbean Sea from its luxuriously adorned waterfront terrace and spacious six bedroom residence. Located in Grace Bay, the home of nearly all of the island’s luxury restaurants and shopping destinations, Salacia offers an incredible amount of privacy, despite being so close to many attractions and amenities. Nearby, you’ll find the Blue Haven Marina, Grace Bay Beach, and one of the Caribbean’s best courses at Provo Golf Club.

Salacia is an absolutely perfect place to host an unforgettable beach getaway. With indoor and outdoor dining, an in-house bar with a pool table and pinball machines, a swimming pool and hot tub, and a spectacular ocean view, you and your guests will find something new and exciting every day. Some of Salacia’s other features include, air conditioning, Wi-Fi, a gym, a security system, and all six bedrooms feature en-suite bathrooms.

Currently, Grace Bay Beach is ranked the “World’s leading beach destination” by the World Travel Awards. And, it is Trip Advisor’s “second-best beach in the world.” Perhaps its most important feature, Grace Bay Beach is incredibly consistent. Thanks to the barrier reef, about one mile from the shore, Grace Bay is protected from the swells of the Atlantic, granting it crystal clear, calm waters with no rocks, seaweed, or pollution. Nearly all of Grace Bay’s restaurants, shops, beach bars, and tourist activities are within walking distance of the beach, which is just four kilometers from Salacia.
