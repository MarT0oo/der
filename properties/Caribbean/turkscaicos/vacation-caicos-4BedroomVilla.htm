4-Bedroom Villa, Turtle Tail, Providenciales, Turks and Caicos Vacation Rental

 

Tucked into an elevated hillside overlooking the bay at Turtle Tail, this oceanfront villa is the ultimate way to vacation in Turks & Caicos. This villa is stunning and spacious with unobstructed panoramic views of the turquoise southern waters of the island. Featuring contemporary indoor-outdoor architecture, with two private pools, and courtyards framed by signature Caicos stone walls. Expansive indoor-outdoor living areas with floor-to-ceiling windows provide the ultimate in casual elegance for gatherings or intimate and private evenings. Includes sunken outdoor fire pit lounge area; Over-the-water swimming platform with direct ocean access; Private oceanfront infinity pool and interior courtyard pool with waterfall feature.

A private entrance and courtyard with swimming pool which leads to the main living room, dining room and kitchen all delicately decorated in soft tones not to distract from the beauty of the ocean. The ocean front terrace leads to an infinity edge pool, sunken outdoor lounge with fire feature, and staircase to swim platform, which is ideal for relaxation and tanning. This Villa also features a covered, open-air den and sitting area with television for gatherings.
