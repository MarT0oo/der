Seafoam Villa, Leeward, Providenciales, Turks and Caicos Vacation Rental

 

After all you deserve to be pampered. Directly situated on the turquoise waters of the Leeward Channel this villa spreads across 3600 square feet of internal living space, boasting 3 King Bedrooms with ensuite baths, plus 1 bonus room with 2 Twin beds that can be converted to a king; Queen Sleep sofa in Living room; large open plan living and dining room with a massive kitchen fit for a banquet; pantry, laundry and over half an acre of outdoor space.

Rates quoted for 6 persons see additional guest fee for over 6 persons when you do an instant quote or ask for details.

Wi-Fi internet throughout this beautiful waterfront Caribbean villa and a 60" flat screen TV with satellite stations keeps you connected. The outdoors are accented with a beautiful boat dock, water and power installed; in-ground pool facing the canal and enjoys full sun, outdoor shower, BBQ grill, pool chaise loungers, outdoor dining and tasteful landscaping.

Accommodations -The villa can accommodate up to 8 persons. Master Bedroom - on the East Wing - King bed, pool and water view, ensuite shower, soaking roman tub, double vanity, double closets, TV, AC, ceiling fan, walks out to pool and canal.

Guest Bedroom 1 - on the West wing - King bed, pool, water and garden views, ensuite shower/tub combo, vessel sink, closet, TV, AC, ceiling fan, walks out to pool and canal. Guest Bedroom 2 - on the West wing - King bed, water and garden view, outdoor shower, ensuite shower, vessel sink, closet, TV, AC, ceiling fan, walks out to pool and canal. Den - on South side off Kitchen - 2 Twin beds, garden views, closet, TV, AC, walks out to garden and front entrance. A half bath in the Living room serves as a Powder room. There is 1 Queen Sleep sofa in the Living room.

Kitchen - fully stocked with all the gadgets you need for a simple salad or a gourmet meal. Top quality appliances, double ovens, custom cabinetry, sub zero refrigerator, ceramic toop stove and more cool features makes this a chef's playground. Enjoy amazing Sunset Views through the pines while preparing supper.

Seafoam Villa, a new modern waterfront, beach-style home built on the idea of enjoying the water and the beauty that surrounds it, boasting 4 Bedrooms with pull out sleep sofa bed. You will not want to be any place else than at Seafoam soaking up the sun or on the beach.

Pelican beach also known as Leeward beach is a short 3 min drive away and over 8 miles of white powdery sand beach, gin clear turquoise water and romantic sunsets. Known to be off the beaten path this beach is not overrun by beach goers or condo guests, sometimes the only footprints you will see is your own.

A short walk around the corner is the amazing Salt restaurant and Bar for the most delicious freshly caught, blackened snapper you will taste in the islands. Also in the same location is a Supermarket, Deli, Spa and gourmet Fire and Ice restaurant. Big Blue unlimited watersports is on the same canal for diving, eco-kayak tours, paddle boarding, sailing and snorkelling.

Things to Do - For the water we provide 2 kayaks each with double seats and 2 standup paddle boards so you can enjoy the waterways as much as you like; complimentary this Summer. Just a few seconds away are boat charters that pick you up directly from your dock for fishing, sailing, scuba diving or snorkel tours. Should you decide to be more active, jet skiing is a short 4 min walk away at Caribbean Crusin or kiteboarding on Long Bay Beach with Kite Provo 6 min drive.
