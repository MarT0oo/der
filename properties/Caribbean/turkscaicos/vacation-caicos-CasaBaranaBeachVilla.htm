Casa Barana Beach Villa, Blue Mountain, Providenciales, Turks and Caicos Vacation Rental

 

Casa Barana is a superb, modern Tuscan-style beachfront six-bedroom villa located in the private gated community of Thompson’s Cove, directly on 135 feet of tranquil, oceanfront paradise. The West wing consists of five large ensuite bedrooms. The spacious upstairs Master suite has a bar sink, refrigerator and a patio with an outstanding ocean view. Adjacent to the master is a large bedroom with two twin beds (also convertible to a king) perfect for kids. Enjoy the peace & serenity of the huge upstairs deck area overlooking the beach as well as the entire property. The three bedrooms on the main level each have their own entrance, beautiful ensuite bathrooms and patios. The Villa's East wing consists of an ensuite bedroom, high-end kitchen, a gorgeous dining room and great room, half -bath, and laundry room off the kitchen with a back door leading to a luxurious outdoor shower. Each bedroom has satellite TV. The huge great room offers more than enough space for the entire family to gather together for drinks or to watch the big flat screen TV.

The large outdoor courtyard area has multiple seating options for families to gather and enjoy the warm sunshine by the pool and jacuzzi or a casual BBQ at the end of the day. With lush gardens and a multitude of fruit trees, the private property is surrounded by almost two acres of lovely grounds.

Enjoy lazing by the ocean on the beachfront patio. Play with the numerous beach toys or take a kayak out and discover the tranquility of the nearby cove. Go snorkeling and see a multitude of sea creatures’ right outside your doorstep. Situated close by, only a ten-minute walk down the beach is the world-famous Da Conch Shack and Kalooki’s restaurants. Only a short walk from dining and entertainment!

After a full day, be sure to catch the sun’s finale as it sinks into the horizon of the beautiful turquoise sea!
