Aqueous, Leeward, Providenciales, Turks and Caicos Vacation Rental

 

The fabulous Aqueous Villa is located within Sunset Beach Villas, a private gated enclave of villas within the Leeward Community gates. Only 200m from World Famous Grace Bay Beach.

This casual contemporary villa, designed by Simon Wood Associates Ltd., has captured the essence of privileged, comfortable and modern seaside living. The inspired designs emphasize elegant open concept indoor/outdoor living with private keypad entrance to the signature courtyard creating private outdoor living space in the front of the villa. The back of the villa opens up to an expansive outdoor area with Ipe’ wood decking, beautiful pool area and elegant native landscaping. The villa is private & secure while providing for seamless transitions between outdoor and indoor living from the front right through to the back of the property. 1000’s of developed outdoor square feet make living outdoors both luxurious and comfortable.

Sunset Beach Villas is a gated community within Leeward Estates which is also a gated community. The villa is fitted with electronic keypad entrance to the private courtyard and alarm system. Security personnel on site.

Your Island Dream Home is located only 200 meters from world famous Grace Bay Beach which has won the Travelers Choice “Best Beach in the World” award.
