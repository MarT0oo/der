Alta Bella, Chalk Sound, Providenciales, Turks and Caicos Vacation Rental

 

Discretely located directly on renowned Chalk Sound in Providenciales, Turks & Caicos Islands, Alta Bella is a marvelous oceanfront home that offer unforgettable views of the shimmering turquoise waters of iridescent Chalk Sound. This stunning 6 bed, 5.5 bath villa marries the essence of the Caribbean with extreme luxury, resulting in 3700 interior square feet, 1400 square feet of shaded outside living area, and over 2000 square feet of decks and pools of simple splendor, sophistication and seclusion. Whether visiting for a romantic getaway or to escape the real world with your family and friends, welcome to the magical world of Alta Bella.

Beautiful natural gardens surround this gated, picturesque and private home, allowing you to bask in the Island’s beauty. Framed by the ocean, Alta Bella offers a large heated tiled pool, glass tiled Jacuzzi, an exterior shaded lounge area, as well as a bathroom and shower area. A luxurious outside top floor living room with glass railing to enjoy the panoramic views of uninhabited Cays of Chalk Sound, a bird watchers paradise. Delight in the joy of unparalleled luxury and comfort as you while away blissful hours in privacy.

Spend tranquil days swimming, kite surfing, snorkeling in the cooling waters of the Sound. Or luxuriate under the island sun on beautiful pool decks furnished with designer loungers, or plunge into the Jacuzzi to get refreshed. Whatever your choice, the spectacular setting will provide you with an unforgettably unique vacation.

At dusk, you’ll be treated to memorable sunsets. And as darkness sets in, beautiful custom LED pool lighting will cast a magical glow as you relax in front of the fire pit coffee table.

While the soft contemporary furnishings ensure your comfort, the spacious living room with sliding wall to wall glass windows, allows you to bask in natural light and fresh breezes, while enjoying breathtaking views.

Alta Bella’s kitchen/ dining area was built for entertaining a crowd with a 55 inch TV , with a large dining table seating 12, and additional island seating for 6. The fully equipped kitchen with custom built cabinetry and quartz counters, is equipped with top of the line appliances (fridge, freezer, ice maker, oven, dishwasher, microwave) are combined with lots of All-Clad cookware that will make any chef very happy. Reed & Barton flatware and Fiesta tableware service for 20, coffee makers, blender, toaster, juicer, waffle iron…and basically everything one might need to produce gourmet meals.

And the beautiful multi-leveled exterior decks provide plenty of room for everyone to while away the hours and are perfect to enjoy starlight suppers, cocktail hours, or fun-filled family time.

For the pleasure of Alta Bella guests, the villa is equipped with concrete stairs for ocean access, and has lots of snorkeling equipment.

Alta Bella has six stunning bedrooms that capture the magical beauty of Turks & Caicos. The spacious master suite, with sliding glass doors, opens to a beautiful balcony with astounding views. The remaining guest bedrooms, likewise luxuriously appointed, also offer four bedrooms with ensuite bath, and two smaller queen bedrooms with a shared bath, all with ceiling fans, air conditioning, flat screen TV’s, Safes are available for securing your personal effects.

Providenciales is home to several of the world’s most renowned beaches, with sugar-fine sand and azure blue waters, and we encourage you to explore them all. Stunning Taylor Bay is an amazing beach for children where they can have hours of fun. Or perhaps go for a horseback riding along the white sands in Long Bay will appeal to the nature lovers in your party. Or maybe you will want to explore the reef-protected waters of the Princess Alexandra National Park with your family and friends. But whatever your choices, please do not miss out on visiting the enchanting streets of Grace Bay; where you’ll discover wonderful shops, world class restaurants, and a vibrant nightlife.
