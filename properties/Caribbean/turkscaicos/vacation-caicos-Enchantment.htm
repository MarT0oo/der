Enchantment, Amanyara, Turks & Caicos, Caribbean Vacation Rental

 

Enchantment is a shimmering example of a stunning tropical residence perfect for large families, diverse groups and corporate retreats. Located on the isle of Providenciales in the Turks and Caicos Islands, this residence borders the pristine reefs of Northwest Point Marine National Park that are renowned for some of the world’s best wall and reef diving. In a country known for its exciting ripsaw music and succulent seafood, you will be perfectly poised for the ultimate Caribbean holiday!

Step onto your swath of white sand, just steps from the black infinity-edge swimming pool. Lounge chairs and parasols are in abundance, both on the hardwood pool deck and on the beach. Whether you want to relax at the spa, chill at the beach club, lounge on the sun deck, peruse in the library or get some exercise at the fitness center and tennis courts, full access to the resort facilities is complimentary for all guests. Amanyara offers a variety of water sports equipment including Hobie Cats, boogie boards, paddleboards and sea kayaks. Time for some much needed sun!

Enchantment comprises a series of elegant pavilions, each set apart from one another, and benefitting from outdoor lounging and dining areas, collectively raising the idea of seamless indoor/outdoor living and privacy. Vaulted ceilings, Balau accents and ceiling fans keep the feeling easy and breezy as you glide through this architectural masterpiece. The formal dining area makes a magnificent setting for a special announcement. The combination of Asian and contemporary decor is simply stunning. Alfresco dining, stargazing or just lounging about in this setting is a pure delight!

The juxtaposition between Asian style and modern luxury extends into the chic and plush bedrooms. All six bedrooms feature floor to ceiling glass sliding doors, en-suite bathrooms, televisions and air conditioning. The master suite is the crown jewel of this property, with its king size bed, beachfront location and extended master bathroom, this elegant enclave has been thoughtfully configured to cater to a lifestyle of effortless consciousness, with direct access to an enlarged outdoor shower which opens to a private pathway connecting the master suite to a private Pilates studio and Zen garden. An adjoining media room is ideal for movie watching.

Two other guest pavilions feature king size beds with the final bedrooms located in a distinct guest suite. Six twin beds create the ideal space for young children and leave space for a nanny suite. The garden and ocean views provide feelings of calm and serenity for the distinguished guests at Enchantment. The residence accommodates up to fourteen fortunate guests and includes housekeepers as well as complimentary use of two four-seater golf buggies.

If you are looking to find the polar opposite of the daily hustle and bustle, your reservation at Enchantment may just be the best choice you made all year. Whether you are whistling a tune or playing a silly board game with loved ones, the meditative ambience at this residence is sure to heighten your sensations. Have your holiday your way in the Turks and Caicos!

Complimentary Private transfers are provided to/from Providenciales International Airport (PLS) if flying commercial, Signature – Blue Heron FBO if flying private and all Providenciales Marinas if arriving by private or charter yacht. For guests arriving and departing privately via Provo Air Centre (PAC), however we can assist in booking transfers via a 3rd party provider at USD $175.00 + 12% government tax per vehicle per way (maximum 4 passengers).
