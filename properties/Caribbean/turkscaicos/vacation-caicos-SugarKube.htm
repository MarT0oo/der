Sugar Kube, Turtle Cove, Grace Bay, Providenciales, Turks and Caicos, Caribbean Vacation Rental

 

Sugar Kube offers contemporary luxury living in a private setting. This stylish one-bedroom beach villa features two levels of spacious rooms and a rooftop lounge. As soon as you step into Sugar Kube, you’ll be in an open-plan living area with sliding glass doors leading straight to your sparkling pool. The modern white kitchen is fully equipped with stainless steel appliances.

The bedroom features a king size bed, walk-in closet, full bathroom with shower, double vanity, and a deep soaking tub. The sliding glass doors at both ends of the bedroom lead onto covered balconies. Open the doors wide and you’ll have a breeze blowing through your room.

Up on the rooftop terrace, you’ll be faced with a panorama of Turtle Cove and an amazing view of the unique blue ocean in front of your villa. Be sure to come up here in the evenings to see boats pass by, watch wonderful sunsets, and admire the stars.

This villa is inviting, but the tropical sun will draw you outside, so make your way to the palm-bordered infinity pool. Sunbathe or relax on the poolside lounge under the large umbrella.

Take the complimentary snorkeling gear and walk down the short private path to the beach where you’ll have a view of Grace Bay. Smith’s Reef is the best shoreline snorkeling spot on the island and it's just off the beach. Snorkel around the reef to see marine life ranging from colorful parrotfish and angelfish to spiny red lobsters and green sea turtles.

Once you’ve had enough sun, step back inside and you’ll be kept cool with ceiling fans and air-conditioning. There’s a selection of entertainment amenities including fast Wi-Fi, a pop-up plasma television, Bose sound system, DVD player, and a pre-paid cell phone for local calls.

Out on the pool terrace is an al fresco dining area and gas BBQ for casual dining. For additional dining options, Turtle Cove is just a 7-minute walk and offers several restaurants, along with shops, a marina, casino, and tour and water sport operators.

Whether it’s for a honeymoon, anniversary, or a relaxing beach vacation, Sugar Kube offers a luxurious destination for two.
