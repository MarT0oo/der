Ocean Pavilion 115, Amanyara, Turks & Caicos, Caribbean Vacation Rental

 

Providenciales lies just within the Atlantic Ocean, beside the Caribbean Sea, overshadowed for years by its celebrated neighbors resulting, fortuitously, in an untainted and refreshingly unaltered coast and hinterland. Nature lovers, diving enthusiasts, lovers of tennis, golf, fishing and sailing, gentle beach strollers, and passive admirers, can all relish in the rich pickings that both nature and Amanyara can offer in harmonious abundance.

All timber-shingled pavilions are identical in layout and design. An overhanging roof line creates shaded outdoor areas on the pavilion & three terraces which are accessible through large sliding glass doors that open to catch the cooling sea breezes. Arrival to a pavilion is via an entrance terrace that houses two daybeds. Sliding glass doors open to reveal the room interior which features a central king-size bed behind which sits a writing desk and chair. Beyond is a cabinet that contains a personal bar with refreshments.

Positioned in the corners of the room are a reading chair with a footstool and an entertainment unit comprising a flat screen television and DVD/CD player with surround sound system. This combined bedroom and living room also opens onto two additional terraces, the first with twin banquettes for lounging or dining and the second featuring a sunken table with cushions and bolsters and two sunning mats on an exposed timber terrace.

The bathroom area is separated from the room by a decorative wooden screen and features an elegant free-standing bathtub with an adjacent ottoman. Twin vanities are located on either side of the bathroom as well as a separate shower room and toilet. Accessed from the bathroom, there is a dressing area with hanging and storage space and a personal safe. Measuring a total of 1,250ft; the pavilions capture a tropical aesthetic by incorporating a simple palette of colors and materials. The floor is polished sand-colored terrazzo, with teak inlays and sisal matting used throughout pavilions. Each pavilion is situated on the edge of tranquil ponds with a timber sun deck extending over the water. These pavilions are nestled in the vegetation along the shoreline. Ocean Pavilions are positioned along the oceanfront with views of the ocean, these pavilions are elevated approximately 15-20ft above the waterline and have pathways leading to the rock formations above the sea. Pavilion 115 enjoys sweeping ocean vistas and has direct access to a sandy beach cove.
