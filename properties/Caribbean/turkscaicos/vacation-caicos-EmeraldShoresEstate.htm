Emerald Shores Estate, Chalk Sound, Providenciales, Turks and Caicos Vacation Rental

 

All rooms have gorgeous water views. The main house overlooks the Chalk Sound National Park, and you can see for miles. It has 5 bedrooms, three on the upper level and two on the lower level.

The upper floor must be rented before the lower floor, although they can be separated as needed by a doubled door on the stairway. We do not rent the lower floor if only the upper floor is rented. (This policy ensures renters of their full privacy.) The upper level has a breakfast nook and dining area. The lower level has a kitchenette.

An oversized 40 ft. kidney shaped pool serves as the Main House’s center of fun and relaxation. The pool has a water seat built into the center of the wall where you can enjoy drinks. A wet bar and barbecue area entice alongside.

The windows and doors all have screens, and in the upper level there is an extra screened porch, a rarity among many island villas. Each bedroom has exquisite in-suite bathrooms, with showers in the upper level and shower-bathtubs downstairs. Each floor also has a powder room, making a total of seven bathrooms in the house.

Washer and dryer rooms are located on each floor, and there are clotheslines outside if you need them. We have a telephone for local calls, and there is adequate parking for at least 5 cars.

Each floor has one bedroom with a queen sized bed, and the rest have two twin sized beds in each bedroom (can be put together to make a king sized bed if needed).

The Guest house has four bedrooms, two on the upper level and two on the lower level. Each has the same layout; one of the bedrooms has one queen sized bed and the other has two twin sized beds (can be put together to make one king sized bed if needed). Each floor has its own living room, kitchenette and full bathroom.

Second-floor bedrooms occupy the highest points on the property. From private balconies you can see Chalk Sound, Silly Creek Marina, and the Caribbean Sea. The ground floor unit has beautifully colored gardens outside their windows and is wheelchair accessible.

Tucked away amid lush vegetation that provides complete privacy, the Guest House has its own private pool, phone, laundry room, car park and barbecue area. The houses were built to be close enough for families, but secluded enough to give plenty of privacy.

If only one of the Guest House units is rented, it must be the top unit first. We will not rent the lower unit, in order to ensure guests total privacy.

Taylor Bay beach is just a short walk or a 2 minute drive down the road. This white sandy beach is so secluded it’s like having your own “Robinson Crusoe style beach,” as one of our guests describes it. The water is so warm and clear you can go in chest deep and look down to see your toes wiggling in the sand.
