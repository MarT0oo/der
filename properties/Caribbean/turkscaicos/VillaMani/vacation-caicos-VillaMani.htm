Villa Mani, Turtle Tail, Providenciales, Turks and Caicos Vacation Rental


Villa Mani has all you could want in a Turks and Caicos luxury villa hideaway. Just a 20 minute drive from the Providenciales International Airport, or step from your yacht onto the 350 foot, deep water dock in a private marina. The villa is located on the southern (quiet) side of Providenciales, you will enjoy complete privacy and the elevated views of the turquoise Caribbean from most rooms and patios. Villa Mani is a large high end destination option with all the luxury amenities you would expect in a fine resort, including an unusual cove protected ocean beach with a huge shallow swimming area, a three level infinity pool cascading down to the ocean with chaise lounges built in to lower pool, and a three story tower lounge/bar with a grand view of the entire island of Providenciales in Turks and Caicos Islands. Villa Mani is not a "here's the key, have a good time" vacation villa. You will also have at your disposal right at the Villa Mani private dock a 33 foot express cruiser (air conditioned) for island touring, snorkeling, fishing, and beach picnics. Built-into the boat is the latest diving equipment, an advanced SNUBA system, no tanks or heavy equipment strapped to your back, no certification needed, depth to 50 feet, never run out of air.
