Samsara Estate, North Shore, Providenciales, Turks and Caicos Vacation Rental

 

Samsara Estate is a new beautiful beachfront vacation home in Turks and Caicos. The home features King suites arranged like private villas around the home. The Rooms are each private and meant to lure in the luxury traveler into a new world of vacation rentals, private owned and operated. A large luxury pool and your own private white sand beach await you. With the wonderful sun decks and beautiful decor the guests will enjoy the moments of absolute bliss and privacy. Samsara Estate is a private home on the beach that offers exclusive vacation experiences for a group of up to 24. Guests can enjoy the white sands, clear, turquoise waters and pristine coral-heads; 180° view, sunrise to sunset, while dining, swimming or enjoying a cocktail at the swim-up bar.

The Luxury Villa accommodates 22 (10 adults and 12 children) with the option to rent the Guest House and its granite plunge-pool and accommodate 24. Samsara Estate living is all about family fun and sunshine. The owners have created a total paradise for you and your family members to enjoy. The essence of the experience is to enjoy the warmth of Samsara Estate as a home; a place to relax and create lasting memories.

There are five king suites with outdoor showers, highest-quality furnishings and views of the ocean. At the heart of each suite is a central king-sized bed joined by comfortable seating, entertainment unit with Smart TV and Apple TV. Each suite is individually air-conditioned.

Then we have an amazing bunkhouse which can accommodate up to 12 children. Each bedroom has double showers, double basins, toilet facilities and separate access to the bunkhouse “loft“ living room. The “loft” has comfy seating, four balconies, Smart TV, games consoles and a range of DVD’s. An outdoor lounge with sofas and television enables children to socialize and enjoy playing on the fireman’s pole between the two living areas.

There is also an additional Guest Room with King Suite just in case you bring another family along or private couple. In total with guest house the Villa sleeps 24 people
