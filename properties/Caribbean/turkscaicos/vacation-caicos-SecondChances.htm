Second Chances, Leeward, Providenciales, Turks and Caicos Vacation Rental

 

This over-the-top luxurious 5-bedroom villa allows guests to choose from a large variety of thoughtfully appointed areas both inside and out. A fountain-garden courtyard, pool side beds and loungers, 2 balcony seating areas with garden and ocean views, outdoor lounging and dining under roof, and plush settings in almost every corner. Additionally, each bedroom is designed to be a private personal space equipped with a television, arm chair and ensuite bathing room.

Second Chances is located within Sunset Beach Villas. A private gated enclave of villas located only 200m from World Famous Grace Bay Beach

Sunset Beach Villas is a gated community within Leeward Estates which is also a gated community. The villa is fitted with electronic keypad entrance to the private courtyard and alarm system. Security personnel on site.

Your Island Dream Home is located only 200 meters from world famous Grace Bay Beach which has won the Travelers Choice “Best Beach in the World” award.
