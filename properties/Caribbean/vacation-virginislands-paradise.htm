The Gestalt, Tortola, British Virgin Island Vacation Rental


The Gestalt's charm begins to work its magic as you enter via the stone walkway through a flowering garden courtyard surrounded by lofty palm trees. This just-remodeled and updated home features open-air Mediterranean architecture showcasing stunning living and dining areas on two levels, where elegant furnishings lend a stylish tropical air incorporating cool tiled floors, a grand mahogany dining table, and where shady seclusion looks out to verdant plantings and serene ocean views. A brand-new kitchen has just been installed, featuring an arched pass-through to a counter lined with bar stools.

The adjacent bedroom wing with its four air-conditioned, screened bedrooms and four baths overlooks the very private pool and a large sun deck with cushioned teak patio furniture and a hammock. A private path leads down to The Gestalt's oceanside dock for diving and snorkeling.

Masterfully designed to combine maximum livability with style and unparalleled privacy, The Gestalt may also be combined with Steele Point villa, 1/3 mile down the Estate road, and/or with next-door Blackbeard's Hideaway (a new 2-bedroom villa on Steele Point Estate), to accommodate up to 22 persons.

Location, Location, Location!

Oh and did we mention the view? The Steele Point peninsula is one of the many headlands that make up the Sir Francis Drake Channel. Beyond the channel is picturesque St. John, however the view from Steele Point is of the 7,000+ acres of undeveloped National Park. If the aforementioned views were not enough, from Steele Point you can also see BVI National Parks, Great Thatch Island, Sandy Cay, and Sandy Spit along with eight other islands. Another unique feature of this property is being situated on the very point of the peninsula where quite literally the Atlantic and Caribbean converge.

The Steele Point Estate is directly in the center of not just the U.S. or BVI, but all the Virgin Islands. Everything wonderful that the Virgin Islands has to offer is equidistant and easily accessible. Lastly, because the peninsula is a gated estate you have the privacy of a private island, but the convenience of being on BVI's main island.
