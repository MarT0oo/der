The Cottage at Squire's Estate
1 Bedrooms | 1 Baths Ensuite | On Squire's Estate
Governor's Harbour, Eleuthera, Bahamas
From $190 per Night

Situated near the Belmont House with a spectacular view of the Harbour and Cupid Cay is The Cottage which consists of one master bedroom (king size mattress), en-suite bathroom with extra large shower. Amenities include indoor living area with mini-bar, flat screen TV, air conditioning, ceiling fan, wireless internet and cordless telephone. There is also a separate outdoor patio with sea view.

 

You may also rent the entire Squires Estate which includes 10 bedrooms and 6 different cottages: Belmont House, Mrs. Russel’s Cottage, The Stone House, The Cottage at Belmont, Toad’s Hall, and The Jr. Cottage at Belmont.
