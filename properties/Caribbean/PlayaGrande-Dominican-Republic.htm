About Dominican Republic

So many people are making their way to one of the newest Caribbean Destinations to offer upscale rentals, the homes offered in this area are found in mainly the Santo Domingo, Punta Cana, Puerto Plata, Playa Grande, Sousa, Cabarete, Samana, Sousa, and a few other coastal cities.  When traveling to Dominican Republic you do have a main airport and some smaller private airports.  The drive from the main international airport to most of the villas are 1 hour to 2 hours long.  This is information necessary when making travel plans.

People traveling to Dominican Republic you can find themselves in some of the largest homes as well in the Caribbean.  Cabarete and Cabrera offer homes as large as 10 bedrooms and that means you can bring your entire family or enjoy an intimate wedding on the sands of your villa.  Often we can also arrange catering at these villas but it is not necessary, you may however want to have the staff purchase foods prior to your arrival as they know where to go and find the best produce and the foods for your stay.  The staff can also help with meal prep for most of the villas. 

Some of the things to do include oceanfront golfing, yachting expeditions, jetski rentals, birthday rentals, whale watching tours, snorkelling, and so much more.  Some of our villas include Castellamonte, Casa Kimball, Villa Lazyheart, Piloto, and so many more.  Let us help you find the right home for your needs this year.
