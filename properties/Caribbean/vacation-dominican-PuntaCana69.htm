Punta Cana 69, Punta Cana, Dominican Republic Vacation Rental

 

Located in one of the most exclusive and secluded spots in the whole Caribbean, this architectural masterpiece is the ideal place to dwell and relax, ensuring a unique and well deserved vacation days.

As passing along the majestic foyer you will get mesmerized by all the dense tropical vegatation that decorates the main outdoor hallway, making you breath the scent of the evergreen palms dancing with the gentle sea breeze.

This golf front 6 bedroom villa has everything you need to have an amazing holiday. On the ground floor you will find the first bedroom with a king size bed, private bathroom and walk-in closet. The second and third bedroom has two full size beds each, bathroom and walk-in closet each. Note that the third bedroom has also two individual sofa beds. Then, next to the pool there is a distinguished guest house with a king size bed. When you go up the stairs, on the second floor are the remaining bedrooms. The master bedroom has a king size bed, full private bathroom with double shower and walk-in closet and the 6th bedrooms has two queen size beds, full bathroom with double shower and walk-in closet.

Common spaces are decorated with valuable and unique Italian furniture, the kitchen is spacious and fully equipped, large living room. When it´s lunch or dinner time, you can choose among a wide array of possibilities, whether in the terrace next to the pool, in the garden or on the second floor terrace.

The villa is also equipped with SONOS multi zone audio system and last but not least a private pool to cool off after a long beach day and full of exciting activities.

About The Area

This beautiful villa is located in the exclusive gated community of Punta Cana Resort & Club, a 26-square mile paradise that is blessed with a temperate year-round climate, light rainfall, and soft breezes. It's colorful coral reefs, calm crystalline waters and white-sand beaches are breathtaking. The Resort provides a matchless experience for all who visit this tropical paradise, and its tropically chic lifestyle is ideal for those who appreciate high standards of quality, comfort and aesthetics. As guests of the villa, you have access to all of the resort amenities and activities.
