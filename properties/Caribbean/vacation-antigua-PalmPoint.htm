Palm Point, Antigua Vacation Rental

 

Surrounded by fine white sands, tropical gardens and the turquoise waters of Antigua, Palm Point is the ultimate luxury beachside villa for up to fourteen guests. Chic and contemporary in style, the large privately-owned property is the place to relax and unwind in total privacy

Nestled on an exquisite private beach in beautiful tropical gardens, Palm Point is the perfect destination for up to fourteen guests to unwind. Fresh, modern and elegant with splashes of British personality, each of the seven bedrooms at Palm Point feature vaulted ceilings, all-white interiors and French doors with views of the brilliant blue Caribbean Sea and exotic gardens.

Contemporary design with clean white lines and grey shutters to flatter and engage with the surrounding gardens.

The beautiful villa is situated on its own private beach allowing guests to enjoy pampering and comfort at its finest. Everything about Palm Point provides a perfect holiday location.

The seven en-suite bedrooms, each with king-size beds, are enough to satisfy any need. Spacious and calm, six of the magnificent bedrooms are arranged around the central communal areas allowing everyone privacy and a true sense of space.

The master suite, the seventh room, is truly unique. Positioned on the tip of the peninsula, away from the property, the circular suite is surrounded by water, with 360 degree views of the ocean – perfect for anyone looking for a secret, seaside hideaway.

Featuring the finest linens, Philippe Starck bathrooms, large walk-in wardrobes and plenty of personal touches, Palm Point is a contemporary, luxury villa for a Caribbean getaway you’ll never forget.

Lying to the north west is Palm Point’s private beach, only a few short steps from the expansive living areas and terraces of the villa. Lined by palm trees and leading out onto the peaceful waters of the inlet, the beach is the perfect spot to soak up the warm Caribbean sun.

The white sandy beach is the place to do as much or as little as desired. Let time melt away with a good book and a cool drink in the shady cabana, or make a splash in a sea kayak, on a paddle board or simply bobbing along in a retro pedalo.

With its triple aspect views of the ocean, the private beach offers guests absolute seclusion, save for passing boats and the odd tropical bird.

There is an excellent team in place at Palm Point to cater to guests’ every need. From a concierge to arrange activities and excursions to a local fisherman to drop off the catch of the day, our hand-picked team means that time spent at Palm Point is about more than just the place.

It is the team’s genuine warmth combined with their extensive local knowledge that keeps guests coming back time after time and gives Palm Point a true sense of place. Everyone takes a real pride in the villa and will go the extra mile to ensure your stay is truly special. Whether it’s the Housekeeper, Rhoni, bringing in flowers from her garden each morning or Sylvan the Gardener serving fresh coconuts from the trees – nothing is too much trouble.

Palm Point has an array of premier facilities that make a stay at the villa one of the most luxurious Caribbean holiday experiences on the island of Antigua. Our accommodation is designed to allow for families, groups and couples to holiday as they wish.

Palm Point has seven en-suite bedrooms with enviable views, all leading into expansive living and dining areas, a kitchen and outdoor terrace. Within the villa there are two separate lounge areas, two outdoor dining areas, kayaks and a private space to moor a yacht.

Guests can enjoy serenity and privacy with the exclusive infinity pool and Jacuzzi, with uninterrupted views of the sea.

Complimentary Wi-Fi and cable are available to all Palm Point guests, allowing you to remain connected should you wish.

The villa boasts a state-of-the-art kitchen and a choice of shaded outdoor dining areas with stunning views out to the ocean or of the swimming pool. The large terraces are ideal for casual breakfasts, laidback lunches and glamorous parties, should you wish to entertain at Palm Point

Warm, steady trade winds and a beautiful coastline make Antigua a prime sailing destination. Whilst at Palm Point, take to the ocean in our 72-foot Sunseeker Predator sports yacht from the villa’s own private mooring. Head out with the crew to snorkel on the local reefs, explore the island or discover a hidden beach for a romantic picnic.

Also available to hire is the Palm Point speedboat. Able to seat up to six, the boat can be taken out to the bays on either side of the house for snorkelling, water skiing or family fun riding the doughnut.

The beautiful island of Antigua lies at the centre of the Leeward Islands in the Eastern Caribbean, approximately 17 degrees north of the Equator. This fabulous location ensures plenty of year-round sunshine, as well as gentle, cooling breezes.

On the western tip of the island is Palm Point, within easy walking distance of the bustling marina of Jolly Harbour and only a twenty-minute drive from VC Bird International Airport. To ensure a seamless journey, allow us to arrange private airport transfers

The private villa offers complete privacy and seclusion within a gated complex. The property is fenced off with night-time security each evening and 24-hour security available on request.
