Agave, Camaruche, St. Barts, Caribbean


Agave is an extraordinary luxury villa vacation rental situated on the heights of Camaruche in St Barts in the French Antilles.

Agave is one of the most private and calm villas, offering a 180° view on the ocean and the islands of Saint Martin, Fourchue, Bonhomme and Frégate.

With a modern and contemporary style, the villa is not only very well equipped but also very pleasant to live, being on one level

The bedrooms, all of identical sizes offer a great view. Two bedrooms are situated on each side of the living and the third in an independent bungalow for more intimacy.

Agave takes advantage of a maximum sunshine and will reveal you at the end of the day the magnificent warm colours of the sun going down behind the hills.

For more comfort, you will find a fitness room with a treadmill and a bench with some weightlifting.

This villa rental is ideal for friendly vacations.
