Avenstar, Camaruche, St. Barts, Caribbean


"Avenstar" is one of our "Extraordinary villas for Extraordinary people"

Overlooking the ocean and the sunset, this brand new contemporary chic villa is of star quality with its contemporary furniture, long heated infinity pool, water fall stone garden, and ultimate sound system.

This villa has five large bedrooms, a large living room which opens onto the terrace and a sunny deck.

Avenstar is a magnificent luxury villa rental in Camaruche in St Barts in the French Caribbean.

 

We are short of words to describe this luxurious villa…

- Two heated swimming pools looking onto the sea.
- The contemporary, sophisticated and comfortable decoration focus on the white with, in each room a touch of a different colour for more elegance.
- Big volumes.
- Very private.
- Very pleasant outside area overhanging the sea.

Avenstar is ideal for St Barthelemy vacation with family or between friends.
