Fabrizia, Gustavia, St. Barts, Caribbean


Fabrizia is the vacation property rental the most out of the way of the largest villa of the private estate of Soleil Couchant located on the heights of Gustavia in St Barthelemy in the Caribbean. It is also the largest villa.


The villa overlooking Gustavia’s harbour, the half circle pool and Jacuzzi are facing the sunset…


Surrounded by fountains, Japanese gardens, this very private villa extends on several levels all connected to each other by stairs and terraces.


Successful Creole type building, living room connected by a covered terrace to a dining room and a fully equipped kitchen, bedrooms scattered over the tropical garden, which make this luxury villa unique on the island.


The private estate of Soleil Couchant: Is located on the hillside of Gustavia in St Bart’s facing the sunset. This exclusive estate extends on 6 acres with only four villas including Angelina, Fabrizia, and Serenity. Gustavia with its restaurants and shops as well as Shell Beach is only at few minutes walk.
