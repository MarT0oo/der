Eternity, Flamands, St. Barts, Caribbean


Villa Eternity is a luxurious 5-bedroom villa on the Flamands hillside - the ideal vacation home for those who love to entertain in style. The outside area has plenty of deck space, a surround-sound Bose stereo system and 2 heated pools: one on the upper level with swim-up bar, measuring 65 feet long, and a smaller pool on the lower level. There is a heated jacuzzi located close to the master bedroom.

The air conditioned master bedroom, located in a separate building next to the living area, has a large flat screen TV with DVD player, twin closets, massage area, and en-suite bathroom with a double vanity, sunken tub, and a very private outdoor shower.

There are 4 other bedrooms, all with classic Hollywood movie star themes. Two are located on the upper level, each with king bed, en-suite bath with shower and double vanity. The lower level bedrooms are identical to those above, with the addition of a sofa bed in each that could accommodate a young child. There is a small pool located just across the deck from these two lower rooms, and a shady garden to the side. There is an air conditioned fitness room on the lower level.

ETR - Villa Eternity is a great solution for someone looking for plenty of contemporary-style space with superb views, amongst luxurious surroundings.
