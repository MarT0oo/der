Valentina Property, Pointe Milou, St. Barts, Caribbean

 

" Valentina property " is composed by the villas " Valentina " and " Bonbonnière ".

 

The villa “Valentina” is located on the private community of “La Pointe Milou”.


This very private villa is very original, the outside living room is constituted by a ceiling and columns of a former Indian temple, took apart in India and gone back up on the island, has a lot of success among our clientele. A villa which we highly recommend you…

 

We love particularly in the outside living room, the Buddha and his fountain, which incites to the rest and to the peace … The living room fully air-conditioned in front of the ocean view, ready to receive friends or remain in family… the bedroom which looks onto the swimming pool and those spacious who gives onto the garden, with its bathroom like a health resort …

 

“Bonbonnière” is facing the ocean view and the island of Tortue, this villa offers everything you need for a perfect stay: Ocean view, privacy, nice pool, gourmet kitchen, fully air conditioned villa with large sofas in the living room, and a large bedroom…


“Bonbonniére” is a perfect villa for a couple.

 

Few words about " La Pointe Milou ": " La Pointe Milou " is a private community located between Lorient and Marigot. It is one of the most requested locations for villas to rent on the island. “La Pointe Milou” has one of the biggest and nicest villas of the island. It is one of the best places of Saint Barth for the jogging.
