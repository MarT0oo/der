Summer Breeze, Grand Cul de Sac, St. Barts, Caribbean

 

“Summer Breeze” is a villa located on the heights of Grand Cul de Sac, at a short walk from the beach of the lagoon.
The villa overhangs the lagoon of Grand Cul de Sac and the ocean. The colour of the Lagoon goes from the white flashing to the turquoise blue, and the deep blue. It is a true delight for the eyes!


The ocean view on the island of Tortue shows the waves from the Atlantic Ocean beaten the coral reef.


The villa is spacious, very luminous with a contemporary decoration embellished of exotic wood piece of furniture.

 

The swimming pool and the deck which overhang the lagoon are very relaxant.


For the tennis player the tennis court of “Le Flamboyant” are at few minutes from the villa.
