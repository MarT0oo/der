Le Manoir de Lurin, Lurin, St. Barts, Caribbean

 

This stylish colonial estate combines the soul of the plantation mansion, with the state of the art of the contemporary housing.


Hidden on the hillside of “Lurin”, on a central location at few minutes from the “capital” “Gustavia” and the beautiful pristine beaches of “Salines” and “Gouverneur”, this estate offers one of the most beautiful manicured tropical garden of Saint Barthelemy.


The view will take your breath away, as this exceptional estate as one of the best ocean and sunset view of the island.

 

Housing:
Colonial house:
The main colonial house is surrounded by a covered terrace. A large living with a billiard, a gourmet kitchen, and a screening room with a flat screen, dvd, sat tv, are located on the main floor.


The first floor is reserved to the master bedroom, with its own private bathroom, a private desk with computer, and a balcony overlooking the tropical garden and the ocean view.

 

Garden Pavilion:
One roomy ensuite bedroom is located in the garden pavilion. The bedroom includes two queen size beds, a large bathroom, and a kitchen.

 

Sunset Pavilion:
The sunset view pavilion is located at the edge of the property, facing the ocean and the sunset view. The sunset pavilion has a has a bedroom, and a separate living room. The private bathroom offers a tub, and an outside shower. Both rooms are oriented toward the ocean view.


The second bedroom of this pavilion is located underneath.

 

Guest Pavilion:
The guests pavilion is positioned on the entrance of the compound. It has a bedroom with a private bathroom. In addition, it has an alcove with an extra bed. This pavilion is perfect for guests, or children, or for staff.

 

Pool area:
The pool is situated within a large deck and terrace that provides uninterrupted views on the ocean, the sunset, the hills surrounding, and the islands in the background… The long lap pool measures 50 ft by 13 ft.


The pool area offers the perfect location to have a party and to receive friends: large deck, outside covered dining, kitchen, BBQ, large sofas, sun chairs and sun beds.

 

In conclusion, “Le Manoir de Lurin” provides an ideal occasion to taste an exceptional tropical paradise.
