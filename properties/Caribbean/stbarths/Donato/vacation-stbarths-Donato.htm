Donato, Colombier, St. Barts, Caribbean

 

The “Domaine des Etoiles” is a private gated estate located on the heights of Colombier. With only 05 beautiful villas, including “Donato”, and an access reserved only to members of this area, it represents the quintessence of the privacy.


Villa “Donato” stretches into several levels and terraces, all facing the extraordinary view on the ocean, the islands around, and towards the sunset.
A majestic entry brings to the living, the dining and the pool area. The gourmet kitchen and the dining are ready to welcome 10 guests…


The villa has three spacious bedrooms, each overlooking the extraordinary view on the ocean. In addition to the large pool near the living room, one bedroom has a private swimming pool, and one a Jacuzzi.
