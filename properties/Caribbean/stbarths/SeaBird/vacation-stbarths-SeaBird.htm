Sea Bird, Mont Jean, St. Barts, Caribbean

 

“Sea Bird” is an elegant Mediterranean villa located on the exclusive estate of “Mont Jean”.
A feeling of serenity and tranquillity is given by the view toward the ocean. The sunny terraces, the large semi circular pool and the Jacuzzis widely open on the ocean increase this feeling.


Inside this villa is roomy. A large living room has white sofas with an “Opium” coffee table. The gourmet kitchen is separated from the living by a high bar.
The Master bedroom located in the main villa has a ocean view. The 2 other bedrooms located in a separated bungalow have an amazing view on Marigot Bay and the lagoon of Petit Cul de Sac.

 

Few words about “Mont Jean”: The private estate of Mont Jean is located in La Pointe Milou. This exclusive gated estate includes only large properties like “Sea Bird”, “Arrowmarine” and “Armor”.
