Papaye, Petit Cul de Sac, St. Barts, Caribbean

 

One of our best villas, one of the most private villas, one of our villas with a direct access to the sea!
“Papaye” shares with the community the two private tennis court on the beach.


Villa “Papaye” is located in the private estate of “Domaine du Levant” along the bay of Petit Cul de Sac, at only few steps from the beach.


The villa is facing the sunset, and has a lot of privacy.


This spacious and luxurious four bedroom villa is overlooking the bay of Petit Cul de Sac.


From the large living room open on a circular pool, the view on the garden and the turquoise bay of Petit Cul de Sac is magnificent. This manicured villa has four large bedrooms, a large pool and a large gazebo overlooking the water.

 

About "Le Domaine du Levant: The “Domaine du Levant” is a small community villa, located on the beach of Petit Cul de Sac. This community includes only 15 exclusives villas, with a private access to the beach of Petit Cul de Sac.
