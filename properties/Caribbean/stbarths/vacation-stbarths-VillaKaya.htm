Villa Kaya, Vitet, St. Barts Vacation Rental

 

Kaya luxury villa rental in St. Bart's is a villa built by an enthusiast of decoration and architecture. This villa built on the heights of Vitet offers a breathtaking view of the ocean. It is surrounded by decks and terraces overlooking the ocean view. The contemporary furniture has been perfectly mixed with the warmth of the exotic wood found throughout this villa.

The living room consists of large contemporary sofas overlooking a covered terrace furnished with large armchairs and coffee tables. The pool is between the living room and the bedrooms. One of the bedrooms has a direct view through a window on the inside of the pool. The house is really setup to offer guests that Casita feeling of being in the Indonesian ocean yet with the experience of the Caribbean. A truly wonderful experience for those seeking to get away from it all. The home is setup with a King in the Main Villa, a Queen located under the Villa, and two more King Suites located in another Bungalow. You feel immediately how the detail went into the layout and feeling.

The home features do not even begin to compare to why people still journey to St. Bart's as their main destination every year. It is truly one of those places you have to experience at least once, making it a bucket list vacation every time. We hope to see you at Kaya soon.
