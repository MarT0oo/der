Mapou, Petite Saline, St. Barts, Caribbean

 

“Mapou” is a villa perched on the heights of Petite Saline, near Lorient.

 

The villa has a magnificent view on the ocean and on a country side of the island.


This exceptional view emphasizes the views on green hills, blue sea, and yellow cliffs …


The owner privileged the exotic wood and the stone to give in this spacious villa a determinedly colonial look.


Wide decks surround the villa, and connect between them bedrooms, living, and the pool.


The living room is extended by a covered terrace and a deck overhanging the view on the sea.
