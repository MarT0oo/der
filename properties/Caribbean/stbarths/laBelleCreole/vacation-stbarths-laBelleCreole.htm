La Belle Créole, Saint Jean, St. Barts, Caribbean

 

La " Belle Créole " is a brand new villa located hillside in Saint Jean.

Nothing was forgotten to complete this new 6 bedroom villa, a large sunny terrace extended by a new large pool facing the ocean view.
