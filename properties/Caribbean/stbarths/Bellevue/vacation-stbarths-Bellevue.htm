Bellevue, Marigot, St. Barts, Caribbean


Bellevue villa is located on the hillside of Marigot in St Bart, at only few minutes from Grand Cul-de-Sac lagoon, its restaurants and water sports.

This holiday house to rent in St Bart, recently built, offers an exceptional view on the ocean and Marigot bay. The living room opens onto the pool, the terrace and the view.

The decoration of this villa in St Bart, is contemporary, simple and plain. For sporty people, a fitness room; for cinema enthusiast, an air-conditioned TV room is at your disposal.
