Axis, Petite Saline, St. Barts, Caribbean

 

One of the most beautiful ocean views of the island !
This very private villa is perched on the top of Lorient hill, facing the sunset.
“Axis” offers an incredible ocean view on the bay of Lorient, Saint Jean, and the islands of Saint Martin, Anguilla and Toc Vert…


All the rooms in the villa have an absolutely magnificent view, either on the ocean or the mountains.
A spacious living room is opened onto a terrace and the pool.


Separate from the living, but with an ocean view, the air conditioned TV room has large sofas to enjoy.
On the pool level, two spacious bedrooms are overlooking the ocean.


The third bedroom is perfect to accommodate children and a nanny.
“Axis” is located in a very quiet and exclusive area.


The infinity pool has a gazebo with a high bar.
