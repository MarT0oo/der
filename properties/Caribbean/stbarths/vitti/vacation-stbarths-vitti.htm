Vitti, Shell Beach, Gustavia, St. Barts, Caribbean


Perched over Shell Beach, this is one of the most prestigious and unique villas in St. Barts. A cantilevered infinity pool offers sunset and ocean views, while the spacious terrace is an ideal place to enjoy the Caribbean breeze.

The five stylish ensuite bedrooms face the ocean and the corridor to bedrooms 3, 4 and 5 is lined with portholes through to the pool. Shell Beach is accessed by a private staircase which leads down from Villa Vitti to the shore.

Completely private yet only a 2-minute drive from Gustavia's restaurants and boutiques, this contemporary and chic villa benefits from a truly enviable location.
