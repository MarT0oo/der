Luz, Lorient, St. Barts, Caribbean

 

“Luz” is a luminous white villa located on the hillside of Lorient.
The villa has a very nice view on the ocean and a large part of the hills in Lorient.
The living room with air conditioning could be opened on a large terrace perfect for addict suntan.


Large sofas are outside under a covered terrace.
A long lap pool is located on a side of the villa. A nice tropical garden is surrounding the villa.


The villa is perfect for a large family or a group of friend who would like to celebrate a special event in Saint Barthelemy!
