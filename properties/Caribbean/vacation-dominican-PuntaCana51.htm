Punta Cana 51, Punta Cana, Dominican Republic Vacation Rental

 

This four and a half bedroom villa is close by P.B. Dye's stunning La Cana Golf Course, and located in an exclusive, gated community within Punta Cana Resort and Club, home to Oscar de la Renta, Julio Iglesias and Mikhail Baryshnikov.

It includes a private swimming pool and private garden with BBQ area. Located 3 minutes from the spectacular 3-mile beach and sparkling turquoise waters.

The master bedroom located on the second floor includes a king size bed, en suite bathroom and Flat screen TV. There is a second bedroom next to the master with one twin bed, and has to use bathroom of either the master bedroom or one of the other two bedrooms downstairs. The third bedroom has a queen bed and fourth bedroom three twin beds. The last two rooms include en suite bathrooms and closets. All rooms have air conditioning.

Fully equipped kitchen, extensive living room terrace & dining room, private courtyard and swimming pool.
