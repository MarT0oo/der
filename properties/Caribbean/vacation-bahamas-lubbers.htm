Lubbers Cliff Top Luxury
4 Bedrooms | 3 Baths | Pool | Oceanview
Abaco, Bahamas
From $350 per Night
Lubbers Cliff Top Luxury is located on Lubbers Quarters Cay, a small island between Great Abaco Island to the west and Elbow Cay and Tilloo Cay to the east.  Hope Town on Elbow Cay with its candy striped lighthouse is probably Abaco's most well known place.   Marsh Harbour International Airport (MHH) is where you fly into unless you come on a boat.

Lubbers Cliff Top Luxury sits about 50 feet above the Sea of Abaco.  The home has four bedrooms, two fully equipped kitchens and three full baths; can accommodate up to 12 people; and has its own dock and a slip in the marina.  The home also has a swimming pool (20 feet by 10 feet) built into the deck facing the ocean.  The location is opposite from Tilloo cut, which takes you out into the Atlantic Ocean from the Sea of Abaco

Huge Living Room / Kitchen with cathedral ceiling with 16 foot sliding doors which opens up to 10 foot wide deck with an unbelievable view. Four bedrooms, three full baths.

Master bedroom has a King size bed; attached bathroom with two vanities, shower, toilet and bidet. Two guest rooms with shared bathroom; shower, vanity and toilet. East guest room has two twin beds bed. West guest room also has two twin beds. Downstairs apartment has a king size bed with attached bathroom. In the apartment there is also a pull-out sofa, which makes a Queen bed in the living room / kitchen plus and additional Queen bed
