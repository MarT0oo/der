Belle Etoile, Terres Basses, St. Martin Vacation Rental


Villa Belle Etoile on St Martin is set cliffside in the exclusive gated community of Terres Basses. A large and manicured estate resembles a personal resort with it's long welcoming driveway, elegant grandeur and incredible amenities. One of the loveliest villas around, it is a personal and decadent retreat for vacationers. This stunning cliffside villa delivers the most breathtaking scenery including some heart stopping sights of the glorious blues of the Caribbean Sea and the phenomenal golden sunsets.

Just moments away from pristine Plum Bay Beach and featuring a gorgeous infinity edge heated pool with an island Jacuzzi and a large island deck for unwinding after a long day in the sun, this is the perfect villa for a group of friends to all chill out in. This amazing pool is child-friendly and is designed to maintain a sense of idyllic wonderment. A private lagoon on a tropical island.

Inside, the decor is neutral with colorful accents, giving off a sweet combination of Caribbean casual and continental elegance. This highly private villa includes a top rated chef that delivers customized gourmet meals if you choose not to make full advantage of the state of the art kitchen which has been filled with all the latest appliances to aid you in making the ultimate, mouth watering gourmet meal.

Then there are the gorgeous four bedrooms that are located in the main house and the fifth bedroom is located in a sweet cottage separate from the rest of the property, accessible by crossing over an idyllic bridge and koi pond. Each bedroom has their very own private en-suite and are fully air conditioned. The four bedrooms in the main house offer a king sized bed, dressing and tub terrace while the cottage bedroom has a lovely queen sized bed, lovely inside and outside shower, private patio and extensive terrace.

Villa Belle Etoile has 5 bedrooms and 5 en suite bathrooms. Four bedrooms are located in the main house and the fifth bedroom is located in a delighful garden cottage. All bedrooms have air conditioning.

Master bedroom has a king size bed, dressing, shower and tub terrace. Mini master bedroom has king size bed, dressing, shower & terrace.

Bedrooms #2 and #3 have king size bed (one can be arranged as two twin beds),dressing, shower.

The separate cottage has a queen size bed, double shower (inside outside), patio & terrace. The cottage is located across from the koi pond and offers a private sanctuary. The cottage is set in a garden and the bedroom is smaller. All rooms offer Hermes Paris Amenities.

Villa Belle Etoile features a heated saline pool set to 86 degrees Fahrenheit (30 degrees Clesuisu). The inset jaccuzzi is set to 93 degrees Fahrenheit (34 degrees Celsius). Please note that the actual temperatures may vary due to the trade winds and weather elements.

Belle Etoile features one of the best chefs on island that will cater to your every whim. Prior to arrival, the villa requires an advanced provisioning allowance of $4000 for the chef services.
