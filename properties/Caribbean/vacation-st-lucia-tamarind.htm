Villa Tamarind, Windward Ridge, Cap Estate, St. Lucia


This is a spacious house, which has been recently renovated to a very high standard with much of the exterior in an attractive coral stone finish. The villa is situated on Windward Hills and enjoys views of both the Atlantic and Caribbean Seas. The house is cooled by a constant breeze from the Atlantic Ocean. The villa is situated in an acre of landscaped garden that is fenced with access through a remote controlled gate. The villa is a short drive from an 18-hole Championship Golf course and Cotton Bay and Le Sport beaches. The numerous shops and restaurants situated in Rodney Bay are about a 10 minute drive away.

The first floor has 4 air conditioned double bedrooms. Two of these have king-sized beds and open onto a high level patio, where guests can enjoy wonderful views. Each of these bedrooms has an en-suite shower room. The other two bedrooms have two single beds each, which can be converted into a king bed. Both bedrooms have en-suite bathrooms – one with a tub and one with a shower.

Downstairs there are 3 more bedrooms, which are cooled with ceiling fans. The Atlantic and Caribbean bedrooms have a king-sized bed and en-suite shower, and the middle bedroom has two single beds and an en-suite shower.

Ample space is provided for relaxation and entertainment with a large sitting / dining room with a TV which is connected to cablevision + a DVD and a stereo. There is a long dining table which can seat 12 persons and another round table which can seat 8 persons.

A wooden patio runs the entire length of the house furnished with tables and chairs for alfresco dining and sofas and loungers for relaxing.

The patio is adjacent to the pool deck, swimming pool and a large wooden deck for sun worshippers. There is also an attractive gazebo with built in seating.

At the back of the house is the kitchen, equipped with cooker, fridge, microwave, coffee maker etc., a laundry room (with a washing machine and a dryer) and a small drinks fridge, a small child’s bedroom cooled with a ceiling fan and another shower room (available at a small additional nightly fee) The house is equipped with a baby’s crib and high chair.

The watchman/ general maintenance man lives in a cottage at the bottom of the garden separated from the house by mango trees and lawn.
