About Turks & Caicos Islands

The Turks and Caicos has long been a place in the Caribbean people go to enjoy and relax while on Vacation.  Homes here range from Beachfront Bungalows to the Large Oceanfront Estates, but each home has a unique feel to it that is unique unto itself.  The islands which there are 40 of, but only 8 are inhabited, are one of the Caribbean’s last natural untouched beauty. 


You can take a boat out once you arrive at either the Providenciales, Grand Turk, Salt Cay, North Caicos, South Caicos, Middle Caicos, West Caicos, East Caicos, Parrot Cay, Pine Cay, Ambergris Cay, and Dellis Cay.  Join one of the tour guides in the area such as www.suncharters.tc and make your way around all of the Islands for your enjoyment.  Take a day trip and scuba dive with Big Blue at www.bigblue.tc, or enjoy a wonderful restaurant such as Anaconda Restaurant in the Providenciales or call Caicos Catering and have the food brought to your door.


Turks and Caicos Villa rentals range from beachfront to oceanfront rentals and can be rented by the week catered or self catered.  Some of the home rentals include Waterden Villa, Bajacu Villa, The Stargazer Villa, and Jasmine Villa in the Caicos Islands.  You can arrange to also have your wedding there and it’s a beautiful place for family reunions. 


There are tons of things to do, remember the weather is best from end of November through Early May and after that we do hit the rainy and hurricane Seasons.  You can find the prices drop according to season and most holidays during Christmas, New Years have a two week minimum.  If traveling from overseas we recommend taking out Traveler’s Insurance.
