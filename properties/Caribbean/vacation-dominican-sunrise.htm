Sunrise Villa, Cabrera, Dominican Republic


Sunrise Villa accommodates 16 adults, or up to 26 adults and children, in an array of six air conditioned bedroom suites, each with a full, private bath and ocean views. The bedrooms in our oceanfront rental are located in four separate houses, each strategically clustered around a crystal clear pool and hot tub. In addition to an ocean view, every suite in our exclusive Caribbean villa comes with an attitude that says, "This is your vacation, so start acting like it."

 

Dominican Republic luxury villa rentals, such as Sunrise Villa, offer unique and luxurious accommodations located on the beach front. Ours is a one of the most beautiful Dominican Republic villas, offering eight (8) luxury accommodations, each with a unique view of our semi-private beach front and our private villa swimming pool. Relax with the privacy, serenity, indulgence and pleasure of your own Dominican Republic villa this year, at Sunrise Villa!

 

1. The Diamante Suite

Located on the second floor of the Main House, the Diamante Suite has a king size bed, private balcony, sitting area, and full bath with a two-person shower and Jacuzzi.

2. The Presidente Suite

Yes, we named it after the outstanding local lager. This enchanting suite is nearly 1,000 sq ft and has separate sleeping and living areas. There's a king size bed in the bedroom and a queen size sleeper in the living room, so comfortably accommodating four people is not out of the question. This suite is ideal for a family-style Caribbean vacation and includes a two-person Jacuzzi, a separate shower, a bar, and private balcony with ocean and pool views.

3. & 4. The Royal Palm and Samana Suites

Imagine waking up and the first sight you see from your king size bed is a spectacular ocean view! These ever-popular suites are separate, yet identical. They’re each situated on a 20-foot cliff which looks out towards the ocean and includes a private veranda, a full bath with Jacuzzi, and a very unique outdoor shower.

5. & 6. The Piscina Suite

Perfect for a family Caribbean vacation, the Piscina Suite is a two-bedroom, two-bath suite with a connecting living room. Each bedroom boasts a queen size bed and twin sleeper. The living room also has a full size futon, a TV with VCR, and a Sony PlayStation. This poolside suite even has a private veranda with pool and ocean views.

7. & 8. The Garden Suites

Two luxurious King bed suites, each 875 sq ft. Sitting rooms with queen sleeper sofas, shower and jacuzzi tub, balcony. Very private

 

*All bedrooms feature safes for locking valuables, roomy closets, and executive refrigerators. The Royal Palm, Samana, Presidente, Garden and Diamante suites also offer hair dryers and a special make-up area with mirrors in the baths.
