La Bella Vita
4 Bedrooms| 5.5 Baths | Pool | Ocean Views
St. John, Caribbean
From $6,000 per Week

La Bella Vita, in the Virgin Grand Estates, overlooks beautiful Great Cruz Bay. Fourteen islands and numerous cays provide breathtaking sunset views year round. You'll also enjoy constant tradewinds from the expansive deck. With four air-conditioned master suites, a gourmet kitchen designed by a professional chef, spacious great room with media center and expansive decking La Bella Vita offers the finest accommodations for your St. John vacation.

Bedrooms features 4 King Beds and 1 room with 2 twins, all with their own bathroom.  As well the house comes with a lovely staff that is there to help you through your every day, concierge to guide you and Linen Service Once Weekly.  Enjoy the wonderful island and the lovely beaches while you are in town, just a short drive from the villa.

Review

This house is absolutely amazing. I stayed here on 2 separate trips & I would do it again. The Ocean views just smack you in the face as soon as you open the front door. Everything is beautiful, all of the en-suite bathrooms are beautifully identical. The outdoor wet bar, the bathroom & outdoor shower right by the pool are so convenient. The house stereo pumps all throughout the house & downstairs by the pool. so just plug in your IPod & your set. All types of pretty tropical flowers surround the property. The views are great from every bedroom, bathroom & balcony. The house has everything you can possibly think of - Beach coolers, chairs, towels, you name it. You'll love it.
