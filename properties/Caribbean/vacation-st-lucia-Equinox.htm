Equinox, Cap Estate, St. Lucia Vacation Rental


Welcome to Equinox! This newly built villa was recently completed. Equinox is situated at the prime location of Sea Breeze Hills in Cap Estate and enjoys uninterrupted views out to sea. Its prime location affords complete privacy for its occupants. This sprawling villa was designed to create a social atmosphere while still affording its occupants areas of seclusion and privacy if so desired.

There are 4 bedrooms each with ensuite bathrooms. Each bedroom has been artistically decorated by local interior designers, Inside Out.

The modern yet Caribbean flair they have chosen is evident through out the house, extending to the living and dining area and out to the pool and deck area.

The pool is a haven of tranquility, where one can hear the sounds of the waves crashing below and also where you might spot migrating whales! The pool area is complete with a swim up bar.

Equinox also has a plunge pool located directly off the master bedroom, the perfect spot for couples or honeymooners or for little tykes! Come let Alina your housekeeper dazzle you with her repertoire of local dishes which can be enjoyed in the main dining area or outside under the stars.

Equinox is the perfect villa and is suitable for all, couples/honeymooners or families.Maid and cook service provided 6 days a week, Monday to saturday, 8:30 am - 3 30 pm. Please note that our housekeepers are not trained nannies and should not be expected to look after and/or babysit infants and children. If you do make babysitting arrangements with the staff, please be advised this is at your own risk.
