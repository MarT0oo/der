Villa Atlantica
6 Bedrooms | 7 Baths | Pool Home | Beach Front
Sosua, Dominican Republic
From $800 per Night

Upon entering, you will be enchanted by the lush tropical gardens surrounding the pristine blue waters of the private pool. The villa features six bedrooms, all furnished with king or queen sized beds, satellite television, air conditioning and private bathrooms. The unique rustic Caribbean design blends with the modern amenities to provide a true tropical paradise.

Lush tropical gardens surround the crystal clear waters of the uniquely designed pool. A small wading pool is a great place for children to play or adults to lounge and relax. Inviting lounge chairs tempt you to spend the day in this Caribbean treasure while you enjoy the gentle ocean breezes and the sounds of the ocean. 

Relax in the hammock in the large living room that opens to ocean views and ample sun deck, or just enjoy the view surrounded by friends and family. The living room features satellite television, a full-sized pool table, and air conditioning.  

Rustic decor and incredible oversized kitchen facilities make this villa a favorite for the DR's most demanding guests. A breakfast bar overlooks the unique dining room, which offers incredible ocean views, a gentle crosswind, and exotic decor. 

Unique decoration combined with modern amenities make the bedrooms of Villa Atlantica special. Each spacious bedroom features a king or queen bed, air conditioning, satellite television, and its own bathroom and shower. The bedrooms of Villa Atlantica provide absolute comfort and convenience.
