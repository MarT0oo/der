Maralago 426, Palmas del Mar, Puerto Rico Vacation Rental

 

Beautiful and bright, these villas enjoy one of the best views in Palmas Del Mar. Landscaped nature, sounds of falling water and turtles picking their heads provide inner peace and visual pleasure. Relatively close to the pool and gazebo, but far enough from unwelcome noises, privacy is not sacrificed here. Across Palmas del Mar Beach Club and walking distance to the beach. Bring vacation back to your vacation.

Renting a home or Villa is a great way to vacation but without Resort Services it is easy to lose the relaxation from your vacation. Grocery shopping, tours, transportation, childcare and many things to do.

Our beautiful Villa comes complete with full concierge service you have come to expect when on vacation. We have full time maintenance and housekeeping staff to make sure everything is to your satisfaction and if you decide to pamper yourself we can even provide private chef service, en suite massage service or butler service ? or you can be left to enjoy the sound of the waves in solitude. Beautiful Caribbean scenery in a full service resort makes Palmas Del Mar the perfect place to explore the Caribbean. If you are looking for a family place where you can relax and get away from the noise without being stranded; this is the place to vacation. There is no amenity that is not a phone call, golf cart ride or short walk away from your front door. From zip lining to kayaking, snorkeling, diving or a small restaurant or casino ? this is just the beginning. Golf, tennis, bike riding, cliff diving or tours and excursions; how about a day trip to Vieques? Or even St Tomas? We are located in a resort that offers the best of both worlds peace and tranquility with easy access to everything you could possibly need.

Private transportation or a rental car will greet you at the airport. Groceries can be waiting you in your villa. We have golf carts so you can take a more relaxing approach to resort transportation or we can arrange a $40 flight to Vieques for you. Most importantly when renting this villa you get peace of mind that your villa will be well maintained and professionally cleaned upon your arrival.
