Baraka Point Estate, Nail Bay, Virgin Gorda, British Virgin Islands Vacation Rental


Thank you for your interest in Baraka Point Estate. The property is operated as a boutique retreat within the setting of a private luxury estate villa. However, please note thatour rental rates give you exclusive use of the entire property and all amenities as we do NOT rent to more than one group at a time so you have the whole place to yourselves.
Some of the highlights of Baraka Point include panoramic views, spacious accommodation in several copper-peaked Pavilions, complete privacy, 5-star staff service and direct beach access through our extensive tropical gardens-with superb snorkeling on the reef off our point. This beach has loungers and umbrellas exclusively for the use of Baraka Point guests as well as an outdoor grill for Baraka Point's Signature Gourmet Beach Barbecues.

Baraka Point also has access to two further beaches, one a 2 minute golf cart trip down the road; the other 5 minutes drive on Baraka Point's complimentary Beach Buggy (golf cart). We also have a wonderful private waterfront area accessed by stone steps, set amidst the giant granite boulders that are Virgin Gorda's hallmark. This area boasts large lounging cushions and a fire-pit for Caribbean-style marshmallow roasts! A beautiful wooden deck overlooking the water with hammocks swaying in the breeze is the perfect spot on the island to enjoy spectacular BVI sunsets.

Baraka Point has seven full time, dedicated staff members including two chefs, one from New Zealand and the other from Canada, who offer our guests superb gourmet food throughout their stay. We also have a wood burning pizza oven from Italy, and a well-stocked wine cellar for wine lovers. Meal pricing includes all ingredients, the staff’s time, restaurant-style table service and cleaning up so you needn’t lift a finger throughout your stay. There is no fixed meal plan-you just let the chefs know what you feel like eating and they cook accordingly whenever you wish, or they can surprise you with their creations or suggest a daily menu to save you having to think too much while on holiday. After all, Baraka Point is all about relaxation!

We also have a full time spa therapist who offers a range of Spa Treatments, Yoga & Pilates classes in Baraka Point’s Spa & Wellness Pavilion and on our beach.  Guided Adventure Programs can be designed to take in Virgin Gorda’s many beautiful views and sights; hike the hills, boulder the Baths or snorkel Savannah Bay! We offer Water Sports off the beach in our ski boat (wakeboarding and waterskiing, donuting, skurfing, tubing etc.), snorkeling trips and beach drops on our 25’ Nautica RIB with beach ramp and we have a Hobie Cat sailboat for experienced sailors. Our Concierge can arrange day trips, sailing, diving, fishing, and other activities on your behalf. The services of a nanny are available and we have a number of age-appropriate children’s activities such as face painting, hair braiding, games and arts & crafts. Treasure hunts, Baraka Point Beach Olympics and Sand Castle Mania mean there is no chance of anyone feeling bored (and Moms & Dads get to relax!).

For those guests wanting to stay in touch with the outside world, Baraka Point has a Guest Office Area and high speed wireless internet access so you can log on poolside. We have satellite TV, a large DVD collection and book library, Playstation 2, and an indoor/outdoor SONOS music system in the Main Pavilion (feel free to bring your own iPODs and plug in). We have iDocks, Sony Bravia flatscreen TVs and DVD players in ALL FIVE suites. There is a well-equipped Sea View Gym with one-on-one personal training available plus a number of land and water-based activities, sea kayaks, snorkeling sets, beach loungers, and water toys for children.

Baraka Point accommodates 14 guests (plus 3 infants) with a maximum of 10 adults in a group . Our Suites are located away from the Main Pavilion in the gardens with their own entrances, decks and/or sun terraces. All suites have king size beds, ocean views and air conditioning; two have alfresco as well as indoor showers, and one of our three Luxury Master Suites has its own plunge pool. Our two Family Suites have a central sleeping area with adjoining twin bedded rooms so that parents can have their privacy and be able to relax in the knowledge that their children are close by.
