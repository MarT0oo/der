Whispering Palms, Jumby Bay, Antigua, Caribbean


Spacious, comfortable and beautifully furnished, Whispering Palms offers a welcoming blend of Caribbean luxury and charm. The private villa features over 5,000 square feet of indoor-outdoor living space, a large lushly landscaped terrace and private infinity-edge pool with fantastic views across the sea. Guests will enjoy the rolling lawn that flows from the pool and terrace down to an intimate beach.

Decorated in a classic Caribbean style, Whispering Palms off-sets dark wood finishes and furnishings with a soothing neutral color palette accented with vibrant bursts of color. Offering cathedral ceilings and terracotta tiles throughout, the villa comprises four bedrooms, a modern state-of-the-art kitchen, a dining area with seating for eight, a family room with TV and a tiered living area with windows that capture the bright sunshine and open the home to a fantastic panorama dotted by Bird Island and several other uninhabited islets.
