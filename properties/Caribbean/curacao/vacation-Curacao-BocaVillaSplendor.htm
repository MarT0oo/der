Boca Villa Splendor, Jan Thiel, Boca Gentil Resort, Curacao Vacation Rental

 

Beautifully decorated and child friendly holiday house with private pool (salt water). You can enjoy the marvellous sunsets from the porch of this villa.

The villa has a nice livingroom with TV and a Bose Bluetooth speaker, four air-conditioned bedrooms, three bathrooms, a very spacious living room with TV and a luxurious kitchen with many kitchen appliances like an American refrigerator, oven, dishwasher, microwave, Nespresso coffeemaker etc.

The porch has a large dining table, comfortable chairs, a lounge area and at the swimmingpool are sunbeds. There is a special safety fence for small children so they can't go to the pool by themselves. Free Wi-Fi is available.
