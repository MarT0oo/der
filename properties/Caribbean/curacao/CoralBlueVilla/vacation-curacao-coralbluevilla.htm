Coral Blue Villa, Curacao, Caribbean


This beautiful tropical villa is located in one of the best and quietest neighborhoods of Curacao; a few minutes away from idyllic beaches, restaurants, attractions and a big local supermarket.

Downtown Willemstad with his famous colorful houses, the Emma bridge and many shopping area’s are only 15 minutes by car. The villa has a completely new swimming pool surrounded by a tropical garden with palm trees, a large Palapa with a lounge-sit. The porch has a sitting area, diner-table for 8 persons connected to the open kitchen. Kitchen appliances are modern and according to European standards; there is a microwave, dishwasher, washing machine, American refrigerator, a gas-oven, Senseo coffee machine, water-cooker, etc. The villa is fully equipped. If you want absolute privacy, this villa has it!

The sitting room has a flat-screen-TV , DVD, a prepaid connection for internet, 3 bedrooms, two new bathrooms and separately a nice guesthouse with 2 beds and a third bathroom. All the bedrooms are air-conditioned. The porch in the back near the swimming pool can be fenced; so it is very save if you have small children. This villa is a typical tropical house, build ‘on the wind’, an alternative for crowded holiday-parks. The villa is fully walled, with carport for two cars and build on 1200m2 private ground.
