Casa Curacao, Willemstad City, Curacao Vacation Rental

 

This ocean front house sits on top of the beginning of one of the best coral reefs in Curacao it features 3 bedrooms and 3 bathrooms and its fully airconditioned.

The best part of this house is the access to the Caribbean sea with its staircase which leads you down to the ocean. Just 20 steps down you will have your own ocean reef. Swim miles and miles of the most pristine waters without having to rent a boat. This villa sits 100 feet from Harris Hole one of the best diving spots in the whole island of Curacao. In this Ocean Paradise you can do whatever you want: Swim, snorkel, scuba dive, fish form your own backyard, this all is possible in Casa Curacao.

Casa Curacao is located about 10 miles from the airport and 12 miles from the the world famous Willemstad. It's remote location makes car rental an absolute necessity, but the total tranquility and peace of mind is well worth it.
