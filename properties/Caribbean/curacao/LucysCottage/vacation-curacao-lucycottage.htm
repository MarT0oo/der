Location

This cozy waterfront retreat is located at Santa Martha, on the southwestern side of the island. It is located right at the main gate of Sunset Waters Beach Resort, just before you drive down to this all inclusive hotel which is nestled on a magnificent stretch of pristine white sand beach. It offers easy access to Curacao's premier diving sites, including the one and only Mushroom Forest and spectacular snorkeling and scuba in the lagoon.

The drive from the airport to Casa Lucy is approximately 25 minutes. The nearest supermarket to Casa Lucy is approximately 20 minutes drive away. Otrabanda and Punda (Curacaos historic town center) are located approximately 35 minutes away. Within 15 minutes drive is Christofell Park, a protected wildlife preserve and garden covering 4,500 acres of land. It features great hiking, mountain biking, car tours and nature walks.

This location can be best described as serene and secluded. Its best suited for people who enjoy a quiet and laid back atmosphere. Its ideal for example for couples seeking a romantic retreat, people who need a quite place for inspiration and privacy (such as writers), and divers who want to be close to Curacaos best diving spots, accessible by boat or from shore.

The house

The fenced garden (approximately 15000 sq ft.) is rocky with indigenous fauna, cacti and beautiful bougainvilleas. Casa Lucy can sleep up to 4 people. It has 2 bedrooms, 2 en-suite bathrooms, an open living/dining and kitchen, a large covered side terrace with BBQ facility, an overhanging back terrace with a most spectacular panoramic view, and a private side terrace right next to the second bedroom, where full privacy is guaranteed! The total area of the house is 968 square ft.
