Kas Kinikini, Damacor, Curacao Vacation Rental

 

Kas Kinikini is a luxurious colonial villa surrounded by a beautiful tropical garden with its own private swimming pool. The villa has three comfortable bedrooms, three bathrooms and is suitable for 6 persons. All bedrooms are air-conditioned with fans. Bedrooms 1 & 2 have cable TV and en-suite bathrooms. A spacious screened-in porch creates a peaceful outdoor ambiance and is the center of this tropical accommodation. A gentle trade wind blows across the porch, where you have a magnificent view of the garden and pool, free of bugs. The porch is furnished with comfortable chairs and a dining room table. Outside you can lie on a deck chair to soak up the sun around the pool, or sit in the shade of the gazebo and enjoy the garden surrounded by birds.

The villa is located in Damacor, a safe and quiet residential area in the eastern part of Willemstad. The house is within walking distance of Barista Coffee Shop, Bar-Restaurant De Tropen, Take Away Lin Hong, Pizzeria IL Forno, Car Rental Nordstar, the Zoo and Centrum Supermarket, the best-stocked supermarket on Curacao. A few minutes away, one finds trendy bars, restaurants and shops at Zuikertuin Mall and Bloempot. Kas Kinikini is located just east of the Willemstad Ring Road. A car is highly recommended, to fully experience the island. The UNESCO listed Punda and Otrabanda, in the historical center of Willemstad, are ten minutes away. The Sea Aquarium beaches are 15 minutes away. The beaches of Jan Thiel, Blue Bay and Kokomo are 20 minutes away.
