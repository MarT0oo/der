Villa Spanish View, Curacao, Caribbean Vacation Rental

 

Villa Spanish View has a splendid view over the Spanish Water. The villa has just been completely renovated and decorated.

It has four airconditioned bedrooms with ceiling fans and three bathrooms. The fourth bedroom can also use the bathroom upstairs (max. 8 persons). Upstairs is a fully equipped kitchen with an American refrigerator, dishwasher, oven, microwave etc., an airconditioned office with printer and a spacious air-conditioned livingroom with a lovely sofa and a 75"Smart TV.

Outside is a large terrace with two large diningtables and a lovely lounge area with Smart TV. It's a very nice place where you can enjoy the warm breeze and you have a wonderfull view over the boats on the Spanish Water.

At the swimmingpool (3x3m) are several sunbeds. On top of the roof is a large sundeck. For the children is a playroom with all kind of games, a DVD-player and Playstation. Free Wi-Fi available.

The villa has mainly American outlets (110V) and some European outlets (220V). Adapters available Villa Spanish View is located near Restaurant The Boathouse and Pro Sail (for sailing and motorboats rentals/trips).

About The Area

Curaçao, a Dutch Caribbean island, is known for its beaches tucked into coves and its expansive coral reefs rich with marine life. The capital, Willemstad, has pastel-colored colonial architecture, floating Queen Emma Bridge and the sand-floored, 17th-century Mikvé Israel-Emanuel Synagogue. It’s also a gateway to western beaches like Blue Bay, a popular diving site.
