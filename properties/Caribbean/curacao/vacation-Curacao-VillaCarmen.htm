Villa Carmen, Willemstad City, Curacao Vacation Rental

 

This beautiful and charming villa is located in a very central neighborhood called Hoenderberg. This former traditional country house is the perfect choice for travelers looking for relaxation and comfort. The villa is just minutes away from some great Curacao attractions, just a short car ride to the finest restaurants and the famous Jan Thiel beach and Spanish water.

From this location you are less than 10 minutes from Salina (shopping area) and only 15 minutes drive from down town Willemstad (Punda & Otrobanda). There are supermarkets, restaurants, and gas station conveniently located within 5 to 10 minutes drive. The nearest beaches are Caracasbaai, Baya Beach, and Jan Thiel Beach.

The theme of an old traditional country house (rustic, warm, and comfortable) is carried out throughout the villa. The spacious living and dining room is open and inviting. The villa has an open modern kitchen equipped with refrigerator, dishwasher, stove, oven, lots of storage compartments, and all the basic things you need to prepare your own meal. It features two dining tables one for 6 and one for 8 persons, comfortable seating area, TV with cable and DVD.

The house sleeps a maximum of 8 persons; it has four bedrooms, which are all air-conditioned. With the exception of one room, all have their own private bathroom -en suite. The master bedroom has a king size bed, two rooms have queen size beds and one room has two twin beds, which can be configured as one large bed. There are four complete bathrooms in the house plus an outdoor shower in the pool area.
