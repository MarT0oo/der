Villa Paradiso, Curacao, Caribbean


Villa Paradise is a beautiful villa with a marvelous view above the Caribbean Sea at Coral Estate. Coral Estate is a private estate on the southwest coast of Curacao. On the one hand it is located on an inland lake, which is adjacent to salt pans and on the other side by the Caribbean Sea. It is only twenty minutes’ drive from the airport and thirty minutes from the colorful and historical UNESCO heritage city Centre Willemstad.

The resort features a bar & restaurant, a dive shop / dive center, a gym, a small beach and a nice pool.


The beach (a coral beach, excellent for snorkeling and diving) is less than 10 minutes’ walk or less than 5 minutes’ drive. There are two larger beaches within 10 minutes’ drive from this location.

The Villa

You could use one word to describe this home; stunning!

However one word is simply not enough to show what makes this home so special. If you want high end luxury, this home has it, if you want spacious bedrooms, this home has it, and if you want beautiful sea views from all rooms on the sprawling three floors of this Caribbean mansion? well yes you guessed it, this home definitely has it!

The master bedroom commands its own wing of the house. You awake in the morning and take your coffee on you large covered private balcony and feel the gentle breeze, while you watch the rolling waves, pure heaven.


Your private bathroom and dressing room is finished with same high quality materials that run throughout the house.

There are three bedrooms in total all of which again have their own feel of privacy and individual charm.

The kitchen is perfectly equipped for entertaining with quick access to the dining room and a short step to the huge main porch which brings the outside in and vice versa. BBQs by the pool or cocktails while watching the sunset are what is required in this area of the house.

This house also feels fun and welcoming; there is a vast games room where there is, at present, full size billiards table where you can relax while enjoying the gorgeous view above the ocean.
