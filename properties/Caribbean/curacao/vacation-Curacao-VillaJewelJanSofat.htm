Villa Jewel Jan Sofat, Curacao, Caribbean Vacation Rental

 

This spacious and child friendly villa is located in the villa Jan Sofat, in the Southeast of Curaçao and is surrounded by a beautiful tropical garden with several terraces. The Villa is characterized by space and light through his unique way of building.

After entering the villa you will see the luxurious decorated living room with a leather couch. The open kitchen has a cooking island. The kitchen is equipped with many appliances like an ceramic cooker (5-rings), dishwasher, oven, microwave, Senseo coffeemachine and an American refrigerator with ice cube and cold water dispenser. There is a dining table for 8 people where you can enjoy your meal together.

The villa has a master bedroom with a walk in closet, television and private bathroom with double sinks. From the 3 bedrooms, 2 bedrooms are sharing a bathroom.

When you would like to cool down, you can relax at the terrace or take a cooling dip in the private pool. In the swimmingpool is a sittingarea where you can read a magazine or book. You can enjoy the sun in a refreshing way. There is also an outdoor shower.

There is a special safety fence for small children at the pool. Free Wi-Fi is available and the villa has also an own parkplace. If you like comfort an luxury is this vila the ultermate choice!

About The Area

Curaçao, a Dutch Caribbean island, is known for its beaches tucked into coves and its expansive coral reefs rich with marine life. The capital, Willemstad, has pastel-colored colonial architecture, floating Queen Emma Bridge and the sand-floored, 17th-century Mikvé Israel-Emanuel Synagogue. It’s also a gateway to western beaches like Blue Bay, a popular diving site.
