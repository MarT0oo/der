Ocean Sky, Curacao Ocean Resort, Curacao Vacation Rental

 

Ocean Sky is a lavish-styled, ocean front apartment with a modern decor. The large balcony overlooks the Sea Aquarium and the Lions Dive lagoon. The view is spectacular by day and by night. The modern living room, dining room and open plan kitchen have elegant high ceilings and fans, creating a sumptuous feeling of space in which to relax after a wonderful day at the beach. You can dine either inside at the big dining room table, or outside on the balcony where a trade wind always blows. Both bedrooms have high quality air-conditioning, flat screen TVs and and suite bathrooms. Ocean Sky is situated on the first floor, in a small complex of 4 apartments, in Curacao Ocean Resort. The balcony is secluded from other apartments providing privacy. The resorts small private beach has sand, deck beds and palapas.



Ocean Sky is located in the Curacao Ocean Resort (Apt 6-4), which is a gated resort. The turquoise blue water of the lagoon is 50 yards from the penthouse, which faces onto the Sea Aquarium where you can swim with the dolphins or watch the seals being fed. A stones throw away is Ocean Encounters Dive School, Hemingway Restaurant and Lions Dive Beach. Continue on the beach path to the newly renovated Mambo Beach Restaurant and Shops. Next to it you will find the Wet & Wild Bar, famous for its Sunday Beach Party.

The path then leads to Cabana Beach, which has live entertainment on Friday evenings. Next to the resort you will also find a fitness club, a taxi stand and a casino. The Sea Aquarium area (Bapor Kibra) is the best beach location on Curacao because the beaches have sand (not pebbles), deck beds, palm trees providing shade, excellent food and beverages and even shops. The best part is that these beaches are within walking distance of Ocean Sky. The nearest supermarket is 5 minutes away. The World Heritage UNESCO listed areas of Punda and Otrabanda, in the historical center of Willemstad, are ten minutes away.
