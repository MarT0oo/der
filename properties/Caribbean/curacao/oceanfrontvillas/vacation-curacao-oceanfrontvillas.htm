Ocean Front Villas, Baoase Resort, Curacao, Caribbean


Two beautifully appointed villas featuring an interior of Asian elegance, offering three bedrooms with two luxury bathrooms, one uniquely outdoor open-aired shower/bath. The air-conditioned Master Suite features a warm hardwood floor and shuttered doors on two sides under a high roof. The modern furnishings include a king-size bed, a chair and a desk.

Similar in style to the Master Suite but with shuttered doors on one side, bedrooms 2 & 3 are located on the top floor, and are furnished with queen beds, cool hardwood floors and are equipped with air-conditioning, reading lights, safe deposit boxes, desks and chairs. These bedrooms are complemented by a spacious timber balcony, which looks across the pool or lush tropical garden to the ocean beyond.

The modern equipped kitchen with dark grey cabinets and glassless shuttered windows is equipped with an electric stove, oven, a full size fridge, coffee machine and other high tech appliances. There is even the provision of a sound system. Dining is either enjoyed in the upstairs inside air-conditioned dining area, or on the one-sided open terrace, that offers access to the mineral pool and is arranged with handcrafted lounge and dinning furniture.

Nestled amidst tropical gardens and caressed by the azure water of the Caribbean Sea, the Baoase Luxury Resort is an idyllic retreat tucked away on the stunning island of Curaçao. The five star plus resort is located on 300 meters of stunning beachfront property. Offering sweeping views of the Caribbean Sea with most colourful and exciting coral reefs.

Situated on the south side of the island between the capital Willemstad on the UNESCO world heritage list and the and popular Curacao Sea Aquarium, the resort is just a few minutes away from the shops in the Punda city centre and the night life of Willemstad.

The Baoase experience is a unique one, from in-villa well being treatments, to in-villa cooking to just relaxing by your own private pool or strolling the beach it is meant to make memories for a lifetime. Each guest can enjoy the pool, private beach, restaurant and beach bar or indulge in one of the many spa services. For the more active traveler, let our Guest Relations department help arrange a day full of activities. From water sports to sailing, diving or fishing excursions, your every request will be taken in to consideration to help make your vacation the most memorable. Baoase luxury Resort will surely bring Heaven to Earth.
