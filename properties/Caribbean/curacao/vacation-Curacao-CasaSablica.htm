Casa Sablica, Sunset Heights, Curacao Vacation Rental

 

Casa Sablica is a Mediterranean styled, double story villa bordering on a nature reserve. The upstairs terrace looks onto the north coast and the Christoffel Mountain. The villa has three bedrooms upstairs and one bedroom downstairs and is suitable for 8 persons. All bedrooms are air-conditioned and bedrooms 1 & 2 have cable TV. Two screened-in porches provide protection from bugs and at the same time create a wonderful outdoors feeling. The downstairs porch is furnished with a big wooden table, sun loungers, chairs and faces directly onto the pool and wooden deck. At night, the lit up pool creates a romantic ambiance. A gentle trade wind from the sea blows across the property.

The villa is situated in the hill top neighborhood of Sunset Heights, a gated community. Below in Sun Valley are a number of mini markets and a gym. Close by is Restaurant Don Quijote and Truk'i Pan Pinguino de Oro (old trucks which sell food until late) and Burger King. Within 10 minutes from the villa you will find the best-stocked Supermarket on the island and trendy bars, restaurants and shops at Zuikertuin Mall. A rental car is essential since public transport is inadequate. The UNESCO listed Punda and Otrabanda, in the historical center of Willemstad, are 15 minutes away. The beaches Pirate Bay, Blue Bay and Kokomo Beach and Sea Aquarium are 20 minutes away.
