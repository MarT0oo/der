Curacao Royal Villa, Curacao, Caribbean Vacation Rental

 

These four luxurious and modern villas are appropriate for 8 people (10 people on request) and are designed by architect Eric Kuster. The villas are nicely decorated and located in the residential area Vista Royal, within walking distance of Jan Thiel beach, the beachclubs and restaurants.

This small resort with four indentical villas are very comfortable. The villas have seaview and are surrounded by a tropical garden with a large palapa with a loungeset, a large dining table with kitchen and BBQ, refrigerator and wine cooler. On the first floor is a private pool and a very nice pooldeck with outdoor shower and two sunbeds.

This villa has a living room with a TV and sound system and four air-conditioned bedrooms, all of them with bathrooms and TV. One bedroom is on the first floor and the other three are on the ground floor. One of them is located separate of the villa. Downstairs is also a separate TV room.

The modern kitchen has a cooking island and is equipped with all luxurious appliances like a Nespresso coffeemaker, dishwasher, blender, oven, refrigerator with icecubes and a wine cooler. The villas have a alarm system, electricity 220 volt and a private parkinglot.

You can use the gym and game room with Playstation, pool table, table tennis and table football, which are located in the center of the resort. Ideal for children and free from charge!

The management is happy to assist you with private excursions, massages and babysitting.

About The Area

Curaçao, a Dutch Caribbean island, is known for its beaches tucked into coves and its expansive coral reefs rich with marine life. The capital, Willemstad, has pastel-colored colonial architecture, floating Queen Emma Bridge and the sand-floored, 17th-century Mikvé Israel-Emanuel Synagogue. It’s also a gateway to western beaches like Blue Bay, a popular diving site.
