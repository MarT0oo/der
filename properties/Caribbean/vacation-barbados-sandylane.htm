Happy Days, Barbados Vacation Rental


Overlooking the fourth fairway of The Old Nine at Sandy Lane, Happy Days is gracious in every aspect. This privately owned villa was completed in 1990, and is let complete with all equipment and utilities. While the owners' first floor suite is retained for their use only, the entire ground floor of the house is available for rental.

A sweeping drive through manicured gardens leads to the portico front entrance. An impressive hall with a cloakroom opens into the lounge and dining room, and then on to the spacious pool patio. In addition to the main pool guests have the added benefit of a plunge pool/jacuzzi. The extensive landscaped gardens extend toward the private tennis court and its adjacent pavilion. In addition, Happy Days has a private beach cabana on the Sandy Lane Property Owners Beach Facility.

The villa comes equipped with cable television, and stereo system as well as an ADSL internet connection. Happy Days is located approximately 1/4 mile from excellent swimming on the Sandy Lane Beach and other West Coast beaches, 1/4 mile from a supermarket, shopping and banking facilities in Holetwon, 5 miles from the capital Bridgetown, and 12 miles from the Grantley Adams International Airport.

This property may be rented as a 3 bedroom or a 5 bedroom villa.
