Villa Paradisso, South Hills, Cap Estate, St. Lucia Vacation Rental


Welcome to Villa Paradisso, a 4 bedroom villa situated on a hill top in Cap Estate. Paradisso's unique location affords its occupants panoramic ocean views which is enhanced by the constant cool tropical breeze flowing through this villa. The villa is situated a few minutes drive away from Cotton Bay beach, Le Sport Resort and Spa and the St. Lucia Golf and Coutry Club with its 18 hole championship golf course.

Rodney Bay with its shops, restaurants, bars, night life and medical facilities is about a 10 minute drive away.

This modern, three story villa has been constructed to a very high standard. The ground level entrance leads into a spacious living/dining room, opening through French doors onto a verandah, which overlooks the Atlantic Ocean and enjoys a constant, cooling breeze. The modern kitchen, with granite worktops and modern appliances including a 5 burner gas Hob, fridge/freezer and wine cooler, is situated next to the dining area. There is also a utility room with a washer/dryer and a guest bathroom. A garage completes the ground level facilities.

Upstairs there are three air conditioned bedrooms. The master bedroom has a kingsize bed and an ensuite shower room with a walk in shower and a jacuzzi. Bedrooms 2 and 3 each have a queen size bed: both bedrooms have ensuite shower rooms. All the bedrooms open onto a generous family entertainment area with a sofa bed, books, TV and DVD.

The lower ground floor can be accessed from the ground floor verandah or from an external staircase. There is another spacious patio with covered and uncovered areas for alfresco dining, barbeques and relaxing by the pool. There is a separate studio room at the back of the patio with an American Queen bed and an ensuite shower room.
