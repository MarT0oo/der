Blue Moon, Golf Ridge, Cap Estate, St. Lucia Vacation Rental

 

Blue Moon is a spacious newly constructed villa situated on the fairway of the 18 hole Championship golf course. The villa enjoys wonderful views of the golf course and out to the Caribbean Sea. Every window has an amazing view! There are 4 double bedrooms comfortably sleeping 8 people. All bedrooms have en-suite bathrooms and are equipped with air conditioning.

Conveniently located 5 minutes drive from the shops, bars and restaurants of Rodney Bay. The villa is built on three levels. You enter into an open plan sitting room with a well equipped kitchen. The sitting room has comfortable sofas and chairs for relaxing, listening to music or watching TV. This room opens out onto a spacious patio with a table that can seat 8 persons for alfresco dining. At the far end of this patio is a small solar heated pool, the perfect spot for enjoying a cool, tropical cocktail.

Also on this level and opening out onto the patio are two double bedrooms with en-suite shower rooms. Each bedroom is air conditioned and has a fitted hair dryer. Bedroom 2 has two single beds which can be zipped together to make a king bed, and TV. Bedroom 3 has a queen bed. There is also a guest bathroom on this level. Upstairs is a spacious master bedroom with a king size bed and en-suite shower and bath room. This bedroom is also air conditioned and has a clock radio, wall mounted TV and hairdryer. The bedroom opens onto a private patio for quiet lounging and enjoying the views.

At the lower level is another patio with a swimming pool and loungers for relaxing in the sun. From here you can access the large games room - fully equipped with a pool table which can be converted into a table tennis table, bar, TV lounge and stereo. Bedroom 4 is off this room and is also air conditioned with an en-suite shower room. It has two zip and link single beds which can be joined to make a king bed. A laundry room is situated at the lower level off the garden (access only for the housekeeping staff). Blue Moon is situated in nearly an acre of landscaped garden, filled with tropical fruit and palm trees. Pick your own mango, papaya, banana, lime or one of the many other exotic fruits growing in the garden which is both fenced and gated for security and privacy. The garden is a lovely feature of the villa.
