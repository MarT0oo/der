Sandcastle Point Villa, Anguilla, British West Indies Vacation Rental


SandCastle Pointe Villa, in the heart of Anguilla's famed north coast, is a unique property of stylish contemporary design in one of the world's most beautiful tropical settings. Adorned with modern contemporary art and thoughtfully chosen furnishings which blend effortlessly into the villa's carefully planned decor, SandCastle Pointe was lovingly conceived and designed to appeal to the senses and sooth the soul.

 

Located in Shoal Bay East, Anguilla, British West Indies, this majestic 4 bedroom villa sits on one acre of stunning oceanfront property and is a short stroll to one of the finest beaches in the world. With more than 7000 square feet of indoor and outdoor living space, SandCastle Pointe is designed for relaxation, entertainment and privacy in a striking contemporary style. An architectural showpiece, SandCastle Pointe features soft arches, grand spiral staircases, lighted domes and curved lines throughout. The top floor of the villa boasts two master suites that overlook the breathtaking turquoise waters of Shoal Bay East. Each air-conditioned suite is tastefully decorated in minimalist style and features king-sized beds, Ralph Lauren bedding, eclectic art pieces, whisper quiet ceiling fans and large ensuite bathrooms.

Two additional guest bedrooms with ensuite bathrooms reside off the Main level with equally gorgeous views of the ocean and surrounding grounds. These rooms feature queen sized beds, air conditioning, ceiling fans, rare art pieces and soft lighting. The main floor of SandCastle Pointe offers ceiling to floor "windows to the sea" that automatically open at the touch of a button and disappear into the walls to reveal sweeping views of the large pool deck, infinity edged heated pool and the endless blue of the ocean just beyond. A beautiful wraparound veranda is the perfect spot for lounging or al fresco dining, day and night.

The villa's design allows continuous cooling trade winds from the east to meander through the spacious living and dining areas adjacent to the pool deck as well as the state of the art Poggenpohl kitchen. A large inviting mid century sofa adorns the living room as well as a collection of unique objet d'arts. The dining table seats up to ten in style and is ideal for dinners served in tropical elegance. The spacious Spanish red hi-gloss kitchen is every chef's dream and features Miele as well as Sub Zero appliances.

A billiard/entertainment room complete the main floor. Guests can relax in comfort with a full bar and 50 inch plasma flat screen television. SandCastle Pointe guests also enjoy a complimentary car rental during their entire stay (4-seat SUV. Insurance and Anguilla driver's license not included).
