Villa Ambiance Terres Basses, St. Martin Vacation Rental


Villa Ambiance St Martin. As soon as you get a glimpse of Villa Ambiance in the Terres Basses area of St. Martin, you will understand why it is one of the most frequently sought out properties in the region. Like an island adrift in the sea and sky, it is a remarkable example of world-class design and architecture. Set on a hillside overlooking the sea, the property is surrounded by a sweeping and open landscape. Open walls and covered porticos give the immediate sense of a classical structure more than a villa, and yet the property is packed with amenities.

Four large and luxurious en suite bathrooms are like private getaways. Opening to the surrounding terrace, they have views, and three of them feature outdoor showers too. The terrace leads to the swimming pool area, but this is not the heart of the home. There are actually many spots where you will enjoy water, lounges, and spots to relax in private or with a group.

Bedroom 1 (Connected with Bedroom 2): Bedroom with a king-size bed, A/C, ceiling fan, WiFi, TV, DVD, safe, outdoor shower and en-suite bathroom with shower, vanity and WC. 2 guests maximum.

Bedroom 2 (Connected with Bedroom 1): Bedroom with a king-size bed, A/C, ceiling fan, WiFi, TV, DVD, safe, outdoor shower and en-suite bathroom with shower, vanity and WC. 2 guests maximum.

Bedroom 3: Bedroom with a king-size bed, A/C, ceiling fan, WiFi, TV, DVD, safe, outdoor shower and en-suite bathroom with shower, vanity and WC. 3 guests maximum. (There is a small room attached to it with a single bed).

Bedroom 4: Bedroom with a king-size bed, A/C, ceiling fan, WiFi, TV, DVD, safe and en-suite bathroom with shower, vanity and WC. 2 guests maximum. (This bedroom is handicap and wheelchair accessible).

Interior Access Bedrooms: 2 Exterior Access Bedrooms: 2

The use of the many covered porticos allows the open floor plan in the shared living spaces to provide constant access to the surrounding scenery, the sea breezes, and the many amazing sights. A large, gourmet kitchen leads to a dining area on the terrace as well as a more formal spot indoors. An enormous living room opens to a covered lounge, and this leads to the pool area. Furnishings in all rooms are clean, contemporary, and inspired.

Whether it is with your family, friends, or even for a small, destination event, the hillside beauty of Villa Ambiance will provide you with all you need for a truly unforgettable experience. Minutes from shops, restaurants, and beaches, you may discover you do not want to leave the grounds or to miss one minute of the ever-changing scenery that this impressive setting provides.
