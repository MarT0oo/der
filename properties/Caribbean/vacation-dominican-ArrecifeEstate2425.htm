Arrecife Estate 24/25, Punta Cana Resort, Punta Cana, Dominican Republic Vacation Rental

 

Arrecife Estate 24/25 is a chic tropical vacation rental located in the private gated community of Punta Cana Resort & Club in the Dominican Republic. This exclusive setting consists of a twenty-six square mile paradise blessed with a temperate year round climate and soft ocean breezes. Here you will find colorful coral reefs, calm crystalline waters and breathtaking white sand beaches. Perfect for a honeymoon, holiday or wedding, welcome to a luxury villa ideal for guests who appreciate high standards of quality, comfort and aesthetics!

This idyllic estate is composed of two separate villas, one with four bedrooms, and the other with five. Each of the properties offers a swimming pool, sun loungers, alfresco dining, a barbeque and a balcony. Indoors, you will find cable television, air conditioning, ceiling fans and Wi-Fi. Your reservation includes housekeeping and butler services.

With fountains and overhanging vines, all the details of the exterior spaces of Arrecife Estate 24/25 have been chosen and arranged to provide a tranquil setting for conversations, board games or a round of cards. The interior spaces are palatial, characterized by a two-story ‘inner courtyard’ with vaulted ceilings and surrounding balconies from where family members and friends can check out all the action below. Rich artworks and furnishings lend an airy gravitas to the atmosphere.

With a total of nine stunning bedrooms, Arrecife Estate 24/25 is an ideal site for a posh corporate retreat or a large family reunion of up to twenty guests. All nine bedrooms at this tropical villa feature en-suite bathrooms, dual vanities, televisions and ceiling fans. Three bedrooms provide jetted tubs. And access to the outdoors is a breeze!

Just minutes from the ocean and the beach, come see what everyone is talking about in Punta Cana. The villa is ideally situated to experience many exciting outdoor activities including golfing, kite surfing, water skiing, wake-boarding, snorkeling, paddle boarding and kayaking. The property is also just a jaunt from two PGA-rated golf courses, a Six Senses Spa and Punta Cana Village. Be sure to check out the Indigenous Eyes Ecological Park and Reserve that features natural pools and twelve freshwater lagoons!
