Callisto, Terres Basses, St. Maarten Vacation Rental


Located on a hillside in the Lowlands of St. Maarten the Villa Callisto has superb sunset views of La Samanna and Bay Long. With a beautiful contemporary style decor, this 3 bedroom villa lends itself to lazy days and refreshing salt water pool dips.  It’s a completely amazing place to spend a holiday away from home.

The gourmet kitchen is perfect for those who love to sit around chatting and entertaining as they cook.  The eat-in style with plenty of barstools is a wonderful treat.  Let your hired chef create a masterpiece while you sample and watch or serve your family breakfast and talk about the days plans.  The granite countertop and completely contemporary style makes this an easy place to feel at home.

The large master suite features a stunning en-suite bathroom, fantastic 4 Poster Bed and is designer decorated.  The room has Air conditioning and a beautiful view.  The second guest suite has a Beautiful Queen sized bed and en-suite bathroom with beautifully designed bedding and accents.  The third guest mirrors the second with wonderful accents and complete privacy.  The guest suites are designed to make you feel like you’re truly living in a resort style villa.

The living room has satellite TV, stereo and piano and opens directly onto a terrace with large salt water pool and gazebo and outside dining and seating area. You can basque the days away in the sun or entertain on this amazing terrace that offers wonderful views.  The loungers are also large and comfortable for those days that you just want to sit around reading a book.  The outside eating area also offers you the feeling that you’re one with your surroundings.  The view of the ocean as you’re eating, the soft breezes coming off the seas and the sunsets that seem to go on and on.  This splendor is what makes St. Maarten a place to discover year after year.
