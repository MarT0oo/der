About Puerto Rico

When thinking of a place to go that combines the beautiful beaches of the Caribbean and the amazing weather yet gives you the sense of a lager state with its international airport hubs, big cities like San Juan and the amazing coastal regions like Rincon, Puerto Caribe and Porto Del Sol.  Puerto Rico is the heart of the Caribbean and offers travelers a great range of Resorts, Villas and private mansions to chose from.

If you want to go somewhere amazing we highly recommend Rincon, Puerto Rico.  The amazing beaches and small town feeling really draw a wonderful mix of surfers, international travelers and Americans year round.  Many people have made this a destination of choice for their weddings and it’s been a wonderful treat for those looking for beachfront ceremonies.  The ocean, the sand and the warm weather year round make this a perfect paradise.  Remember rates are always higher during the winter time and the summer is off season.  You can find some great places for less money during the low season, which start in May.

Other regions to explore are: Parador for the small intimate beaches and private escapes, Porta Del Sol on the West Coast of Puerto Rico where you’ll find lots of amazing smaller municipalities like Quebradillas, Isabela, San Sebastián, Moca, Aguadilla, Aguada, Rincón, Añasco, Mayagüez, Las Marías, Maricao, Hormigueros, San Germán, Sábana Grande, Guánica, Lajas and Cabo Rojo.Porta del Sol (people come here for the beaches), Porta Caribe (South Region) offers you the opportunity to visit some amazing  historical sites and some of cities to explore are Ponce, Patillas, Arroyo, Guayama, Salinas, Aibonito, Coamo, Santa Isabel, Juana Diaz, Villalba, Jayuya, Peñuelas, Guayanilla, Adjuntas and Yuaco (Here you’ll find amazing golf). 

Explore the cities or caves or the amazing historical cities, once you visit you’ll see plenty of reasons to keep coming back.
