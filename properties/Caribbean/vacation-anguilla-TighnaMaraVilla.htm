Tigh-na-Mara Villa, West End, Anguilla Vacation Rental

 

Tigh-na-Mara Villa (House by the Sea), centrally located on sunny Anguilla, exudes old world West Indies charm inside and out. If you love sophisticated, retro island style you will adore this immaculate and beautiful property. Trimmed in teal and white shutters and lattice work, this storybook island villa immerses you in a delightful ambiance of days gone by, combined with modern necessities including high speed internet, a recently renovated chef’s kitchen and air-conditioning throughout. Caribbean charm is woven into every elegant detail from peak-roofed terraces to cedar paneling to wicker and hardwood furnishings.

Three cozy bedrooms in the main house feature king sized beds and en suite baths. The pool house offers another en suite king bedroom complete with wet bar, screened porch, 2 separate baths and a shower for rinsing after your dip. The sparkling lap pool is your own private paradise — lined with large wicker chaises for 12 guests.

A very unique estate, Tigh-na-Mara Villa is nestled in lush landscaping with magnificent views of St. Martin from nearly every room, and offers access to Elsie Bay Cove.

Anguilla is recently ranked as the #1 Caribbean Island in “Travel and Leisure’s 2019 World’s Best Awards,” making this the third year our island has taken the top ranking. In addition, Anguilla has been ranked #6 on “Travel and Leisure’s 15 Best Islands in the World,” for 2019. The island is a world class, off-the-beaten-path destination known for stunning beaches, friendly locals and a wide offering of luxury villas and vacation homes with exceptional privacy.
