Villa Stella, Westerhall Point, St. David, Grenada Vacation Rental

 

As they arrive at the Villa, Guests will enjoy the beautifully manicured gardens and the private sun deck overlooking the sea. The view towards the Atlantic Ocean is unobstructed, and there is a nice cooling breeze off the sea. There are two beaches within easy walking distance, both nicely secluded.

With an amazing view towards the interior of Westerhall Bay, and the Fort Jeudy Penninsula, you may find yourself simply relaxing on the patio and enjoying the view, rather than taking advantage of the opportunities to explore the area, by foot and by water. However, there is no need to feel rushed, as you can set your own pace: relax and enjoy this opportunity to recharge your batteries. Should you decide to go snorkeling along the tropical reefs just yards offshore the Villa Stella, you can access the shore via the staircase and deck built alongside the bluff.

This comfortable 5-bedroom features spacious closets and dreamy, well-appointed bathrooms. Natural light flows via the windows, which look out on our gardens and the view of the Bay. All bedrooms are en-suite. 3 bedrooms are located on the upper level and the other two on the lower level of the villa. The vaulted ceilings also add to the spaciousness of the rooms, and the glass bloc sections on the exterior wall let in natural light without the bright sunshine, while the ceramic tiled floors keep your feet cool and comfortable. Our bedrooms are also fully air conditioned for your comfort. The lower floor of the villa is self-contained with its own separate living room and kitchen.

Entertainment
The interior furnishings of Villa Stella successfully blends antiques with modern furniture from a variety of sources worldwide. Guests can relax and enjoy the movies of their choice on plasma televisions, or choose from a selection of DVD’s, books and CD’s. For your convenience, there is access to the Internet, and both phone and fax are on hand should you wish to keep in touch with your business or family. Should you wish to play your own musical selections from your iPod on the villa’s sound system, an iPod dock is provided. For those who would like to be more active while vacationing at Villa Stella, table tennis is available, as well as kayaking in Westerhall Bay, just a few short steps from the villa. The bay itself is well sheltered, and has a healthy mangrove nursery along the shore, where you can see much of the local wildlife.

Kitchen and dining
Meals can be prepared in the Miele, fully appointed kitchen, which offers the convenience of oven, dishwasher, nespresso machine, ice maker, microwave and Fhiaba fridge/freezer. The living area and sun deck also has a BBQ grill, so guests can enjoy evening barbecues. For your convenience, Villa Stella comes provided with a housekeeper and caretaker. The housekeeper is an excellent cook and can prepare your breakfast, lunch and diner. The caretaker can be sent on groceries or errands on your behalf so you can enjoy your time at the villa.
