Villa Las Palmas, Punta Cana, Dominican Republic Vacation Rental

 

Villa Las Palmas is a high-end, luxurious four-bedroom villa which overlooks the 14th hole of P.B. Dye's stunning La Cana Golf Course and is located in an exclusive, gated community within Puntacana Resort & Club/Tortuga Bay, home to Oscar de la Renta, Julio Iglesias and Mikhail Baryshnikov.

The villa is situated on an elevated lot with sweeping views of the golf course from the rear terrace and private 40' x 20' infinity-edge pool with smaller zen pool fountain and cascade. The two main houses are separated by a landscaped walk and garden. Adjacent to the master suite is a private stone-walled garden with outdoor shower and handmade tiled jacuzzi.

The property has exceptional landscaping including 30 coconut palms, 12 foxtail palms, 110 areca palms, birds of paradise, gardenias, ginger, jasmine, and fruit trees.

Located 5 min. from the airport and 3 min. from a spectacular 3-mile beach and sparkling turquoise waters. Fulltime maid, gardener and concierge. GOLF DISCOUNTS!! GOLF CART AND AIRPORT TRANSFERS INCLUDED!!!

Villa and Resort Amenities: Villa Las Palmas is located in an exclusive, gated community of Punta Cana Resort & Club, a 26-square mile paradise that is blessed with a temperate year-round climate, light rainfall, and soft breezes. It's colorful coral reefs, calm crystalline waters and white-sand beaches are breathtaking. The Resort provides a matchless experience for all who visit this tropical paradise, and its tropically chic lifestyle is ideal for those who appreciate high standards of quality, comfort and aesthetics. As guests of the villa, you have access to all of the resort amenities and activities.

Along with golf, guests of the villa can enjoy tennis, sailing, windsurfing, snorkeling, scuba, deep-sea fishing, horseback riding, fitness center and a 1,000 acre Ecological Nature Preserve with walks through woods and pristine springs and crystal-clear ponds.

Dining options include La Yola, an open-air thatched roof restaurant located on the water next to the 70-slip marina, plus 6 other restaurant

La Cana Golf Course: GOLF DISCOUNTS!!! Golf Magazine has declared it the 'Number one course in the Caribbean.' Need we say more? Within a ten minute drive, there are also stunning golf courses designed by Jack Nicklaus and Tom Fazio.

Six Senses Spa: The world-famous Six Senses Spa offers a broad range of holistic wellness, rejuvenation and beauty treatments to balance the senses in 8 indoor salons and 4 outdoor salons, all managed under the guidance of expert therapists from Thailand.

Indulge yourself. Make this your next spa getaway.

VIP Services: Rate includes escorted airport transfers, golf cart, maid, handyman, concierge. Chef and nanny available for hire.

Villa guests have unrestricted access to all resort activities as well as all restaurants, beaches and pools. Golf discounts are also included with a stay at the villa.
