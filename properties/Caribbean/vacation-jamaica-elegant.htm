Rose Cottage, Montego Bay, Jamaica Vacation Rental


Rose Cottage sits quietly yet dramatically on a plateau affording exceptional mountain and ocean views. Designed by Nancy D. Maffasanti in the old traditional Jamaican style, the house is decorated in an elegant arrangement of themes influenced by Asia, Jamaica and Europe…it is both elegant and comfortable and meant to be enjoyed.
 
Rose Cottage, two swimming pools, spacious landscaped tropical gardens. Rose Cottage’s many pavilions exhibits elements of design which promotes efficiency of cooling and warming through natural ventilation. Additionally, the rooms all have ceiling fans and/or air conditioners.
 
The main structure houses four bedroom suites, a private garden dedicated to enhancing the tropical shower experience, a formal living room, an open study and an open veranda/great room which views of Montego Bay.
 
The Master Pavilion Bedroom suite, the fifth bedroom suite with its sitting room equipped with the audio/visual equipment, beverage refrigerator/icemaker, audio and visual equipment sits a-top the formal dining room, has a 360 degree view of the property a spectacular sunset view over Sandy Bay.
 
Rose Bud, the sixth bedroom suite is a 1,900 ft² one bedroom cottage with a full master bathroom (Jacuzzi/ shower), a sitting room equipped with audio/visual equipment, beverage refrigerator/icemaker, a powder room with a shower and a verandah and a bar equipped with a refrigerator/icemaker and bar sink. In addition to the 75ft. infinity lap pool and its gazebo there is a large garden pool which envelopes the south side of the gracious dining room, its’ beautiful pavilion where many meals are served while you are able to enjoy the spectacular sunsets.
 
Rose Cottage is equipped with the modern conveniences: telephones, computers, wireless internet, cable and various audio/visual toys, etc. We are pleased to provide guests with two complimentary golf carts and your private villa staff, comprised of a chef, butler, housekeepers, laundress and gardeners.
 
Rose Cottage is the perfect destination for the ultimate pampering experience, where you can put a little distance between you and the rest of the world.
