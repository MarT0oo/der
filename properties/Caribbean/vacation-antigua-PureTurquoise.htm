Pure Turquoise, Jumby Bay, Antigua, Caribbean


Taking its name from the incomparable sea views, Pure Turquoise is undoubtedly one of the finest Estate Houses in the Caribbean. Providing complete privacy and space in equal measure, this unparalleled property combines the intrinsic natural beauty of its beachfront location with exquisite luxury.

With a 200 metre private beach, magnificent 25m infinity pool and set upon 6.2 spectacular water front acres, the property was completed in 2017 as the ultimate retreat. The interiors, designed by a top English designer, include furnishings from World leading brands; all contributing to a tranquil yet sophisticated atmosphere.

Set in a village-style arrangement, the buildings are thoughtfully aligned and elevated to deliver all-encompassing sea views. The main pavilion is open to refreshing breezes and features formal and informal seating areas, a TV/games room and an elegant dining area. There is also a spacious English hand-built kitchen where your private chef will prepare your meals.

Each guest villa has a large sea-view verandah, a spacious and luxurious fitted main bedroom with super king bed and a dressing room with extensive walk-in wardrobe space. In addition to indoor and outdoor showers, each en suite bathroom provides an outdoor tub for bathing under Jumby Bay’s gorgeous star-lit skies.

The estate’s focal point—a stunning 25-meter infinity pool—blends seamlessly into the turquoise sea beyond. Recognized as the Caribbean’s finest by The New York Times, the pool is surrounded by an expansive deck and beautifully furnished gazebo which provide ideal settings for deep relaxation and al fresco dining.

From the pool, steps lead down to a private secluded sandy beach where the Hawksbill turtle nests in season. You will discover an array of marine inhabitants while snorkeling around the reefs located off the beach. As you recline on luxurious daybeds and chaise lounges, you will witness the magnificent aeronautical displays of tropical birds, including pelicans, frigate birds and sandpipers in their natural habitat.

To sum up Pure Turquoise, we will leave you with the words of one of our guests; ‘Your eyes fall onto the horizon, your mind settles into bliss; if you’ve never been to paradise, paradise is this.
