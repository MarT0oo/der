Mariposa, Terres Basses, St. Maarten Vacation Rental

 

Terres Basses is home to Mariposa, a superb, newly constructed private home with 180º views of the crystal blue waters of the Caribbean and the setting sun. The spacious indoor and outdoor living areas offer ideal opportunities for relaxing and entertaining, complete with the gourmet kitchen and covered outdoor dining area with gas BBQ. All four exterior access, air-conditioned bedrooms (3 with queen-size & 1 with 2 twins) are equal sized, are graced with ocean views, and give access directly onto their own private terrace with lounge chairs and sun parasols. The en-suite deluxe bathrooms feature oversized double showers and twin vanities.

 

Further details that add to the quality of Mariposa include the large heated pool, ice machine, fine quality dinner & glass ware, living room with 40” flat screen satellite TV/DVD and superior stereo system.

 

Located only a few minutes’ walk to Plum Bay beach, the vacation dream is sealed as the sun romantically sinks beyond the horizon after another perfect day. This villa offers the ideal vacation venue for older family groups or couples alike.
