Villa Vistamar
6 Bedrooms | 6 Baths | Pool | Gated
Sandy Lane, Barbados
From $2,000 per Night

A spacious and luxurious villa, Vistamar was previously a home and has never before been offered for rental. Designed to be a truly Caribbean “home away from home”, Vistamar can accommodate up to twelve guests in six bedroom suites: four in the main house and two in a self-contained cottage.

There are four air-conditioned bedrooms and three bathrooms in the main house. Two are with en suite bathrooms, while the other two bedrooms share a bathroom. The self-contained cottage has two air-conditioned bedrooms with en suite bathrooms, and an extensive veranda that stretches the length of the cottage. The indoor living room features oversized comfortable sofas and French doors that open out on to a covered terrace with casual outdoor seating, wet bar and a large dining table that seats 12 where everyone can gather together and enjoy quality time.

Vistamar is 5 minute walk to tennis court, a 5 minute drive to the Beach, Church, Clinic, Restaurant, Grocery store, Golf course & Holetown, a 30 minute drive to Queen Elizabeth hospital, and a 45 minute drive to airport.

At Vistamar, there is plenty of space for guests to relax and unwind. It is the perfect choice for families or groups of friends!

Your Experience - by day

Start each day with a refreshing dip in the swimming pool while breakfast is prepared; we can organise a menu that suits whatever your heart desires, or to your specific dietary needs.  Your villa manager can make any bookings or reservations and give you advice and information about any activities you may be interested in. Book a round of golf or two at the prestigious Sandy Lane Golf Club or tennis lessons from a resident pro; learn to surf, scuba dive or water ski; take an island tour and visit some of the heritage sites on the island such as St Nicholas Abbey or enjoy an in-house spa treatment while the kids play in the pool.

Just relax and switch on to island time, and let us take care of the details.  We are happy to help you organise the perfect vacation and make it as relaxed or as adventurous as you would like.

Your Experience - by night

Gather together for an evening meal of delicious, homemade food prepared by the cook and order your choice of fine wines or champagne to celebrate your holiday or special occasion.  Stay close to home by visiting Holetown (5 minutes to the north), with its lively atmosphere on Second Street, with plentiful bars and a variety of restaurants or venture further north, to Speightstown, for some authentic local flavour and freshly caught fish.

Travel further afield to the south coast, where you can explore jazzy bistros in Bridgetown, find nightclubs that will take you into the wee hours, stop off at any of the bars and restaurants dotted along the coastline and take a walk through St Lawrence Gap. On a Wednesday or a Friday, head to Oistins, where the catch of the day is grilled right in front of you, island-style.

Your villa manager is happy to offer recommendations or make reservations for you; if you have any specific requests, we will do our best to organise them for you.
