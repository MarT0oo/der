Big Blue Ocean 38, Canouan, St. Vincent & The Grenadines, Resort


Big Blue Ocean also has a private path directly onto Point De Jour beach where you can swim in the turquoise sea or where a romantic dinner can be enjoyed. External finishes are a blend of stone, adobe painted cement walls and hardwood windows and doors. Roofs are wallaba shingles which blend naturally into the environment and gives you a feel of been surrounded only by nature.

Through the main entrance you enter the foyer and then into the delightful and large great room with beautiful views. You can relax in the air-conditioning or open the huge stackable glass sliding doors which when fully open bring the outside in while capturing the prevailing tropical trade winds of this undiscovered paradise.The spacious kitchen comes with stainless steel SMEG appliances and is very well appointed, conveniently located off the kitchen are the laundry room and service bathroom.

The right wing of the villa has 3 bedrooms, the master bedroom which is just three steps up from the great room and has private media room adjacent to it. The master bedroom also has a sizeable walk through closet and is set up to enjoy the picturesque scenery of the ocean. A few steps down from the great room are two more bedrooms which both have en-suite bathrooms, large closets and a private veranda with views of the ocean.

In the left wing of the villa you will find the study/media room which can also be converted into a bedroom as it has a fold-out- sofa bed. There are two more bedrooms in the left wing, one has double beds and the other a king size bed, they both have en-suite bathrooms, central air conditioning, ceiling fans and private verandas. You will experience elegant yet comfortable indoor and outdoor living with an ambience that is relaxing and romantic.
