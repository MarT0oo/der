Villa Ferrari, Canouan, St. Vincent & The Grenadines Vacation Rental

 

Dreamexoticrentals is happy to introduce the Very unique and privately owned Villa Ferrari on Canouan having the capacity to accommodate up to eighteen adults in various fully equipped bedrooms:

The six guest bedrooms share the same floor plan. The rooms are fully appointed with air condition, a double bed , en suite bathroom with a shower and vanities. Two of these bathrooms have a bath. Handmade Middle East rugs are by each side of the bed. These bedrooms are all equipped with sliding glass door that open to the garden. There is free wireless and MP3 compatible docking station. There is also a beverage fridge, espresso maker. tee pot and ample closets for storage. Two chairs and a table are outside each room by the sliding door. Shower chairs are available on request.

The master bedroom in the resident area with another two bathrooms both having a bath has a studio room for children next to it and another fully equipped bedroom on the upper deck may be used out by groups where extra space is required. Private living room with a desk is a perfect office for private meetings or calls. In case of need this room can be converted to the ninth bedroom.

The great club room features a bar, HIFI stereo and opens via louvre doors to the pool area with a grill. On the other side of the pool there is a more formal dining area and a fully equipped kitchen.

The pool is surrounded by a sun deck with pool chairs having also a children’s shallow part for the younger set.

The whole house has a wireless

There is a permanent exhibition of the works of the architect Antonio Ferrari all around in the villa: in the guest bedrooms, corridors and other spaces there are hanging his framed visions and drawings remembering this extraordinary man. Precious Middle east carpets all around the house on the floors, Nomad pillows, African Makonde ebony pieces of art, big two floor chandeliers in both club and dining rooms and wall lights all being made from wrought iron together with other ancient artifacts and wooden furniture create a unique atmosphere.

With all of these Villa Ferrari provides a perfect retreat for families or big groups of friends. It offers a graceful balance between private areas and opportunities for communal sharing. Transitions from indoor to outdoor living areas are seamless. This villa allows its guests to fully relax in pampered style. The clarity of the surrounding waters of the southern Antilles, the white sand beaches, and the varied sporting opportunities ensure that travelers of all stripes and ages encounter activities to suit.

BIKING
Mountain Bikes available to explore the island.

FISHING
St Vincent & The Grenadines is a paradise, where a fisherman’s dream can turn into reality as its waters offer the enthusiast a chance to hook a prize catch such as Blue Marlin, Spearfish, Wahoo, Jack fish, Sailfish, Billfish, Giant Black & Yellow fin tunas, Mahi Mahi and the ever present Barracuda. Please note that Deep Sea Fishing Boats do not allow larger groups than ten (10) persons per boat (3 boats available) with a minimum of four (4) persons per boat. The waters of St Vincent & The Grenadines are teemed with sea life including a variety of dolphins and rare whale species.

HIKING
Hike to the highest point on the island Mount Royal and take beautiful pictures of the Grenadines especially at sunset!

SNORKELLING
Snorkeling inside the bay, protected by one of the largest coral reefs in the Caribbean.

TENNIS
Three Astro- Turf Flood Lit courts are located on the resort (next to the Kids Club). The Tennis Club has complimentary racquets and balls for use by residents of all ages during their stay.

WATER Sports
Non-motorized watersports such as hobie cats, wind surfs, sea-kayaks, Pedallos and paddle- boarding are available at Shell Beach.
