Maison Tranquille, Canouan, St. Vincent & The Grenadines, Resort


Maison Tranquille is a 3 bedroom single level villa compromising 370 square meters of covered space including a Guest Cottage, an external pool and deck, overlooking exquisite views of the sea.

External finishes are a blend of stone, adobe painted cement walls and hardwood windows and doors. Roofs are wallaba shingles which blends naturally into the environment, offering the perfect essence of tropical living.

The spacious kitchen comes with stainless steel SMEG appliances and is very well appointed, conveniently located off the kitchen are the laundry room and service bathroom.

The spacious great room features spectacular views through the large sliding doors. From the terrace the infinity-edge pools blends into the backdrop of the sea. Floors are Travertine marble and the grand vaulted ceiling which has exposed rafters and wallaba shingles.

All bedrooms enjoy en-suite bathrooms, central air conditioning, ceiling fans and private verandas overlooking the lush landscape of The Grenadines Estate Golf Course and the turquoise water of the Caribbean Sea. The master bedroom is large and spacious with a unique master bathroom which has a “chimney-style” shower which allows you to enjoy an open air feeling.


You will experience elegant yet comfortable indoor and outdoor living with ambience that is relaxing and romantic.
