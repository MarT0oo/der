Tradewinds, Montego Bay, Jamaica Vacation Rental


Tradewinds is a beautiful villa with an absolute waterfront location, within the famous Tryall Club resort. This cosy home sits on two acres and is only a few minutes’ walk from Tryall’s famous beach club, pro shop, driving range and first fairway. You may use the villas own private golf carts to roam around the 2200 acre resort.

This villa is right at the water’s edge which over looks out over a private beach and the blue azures of the Caribbean Sea. From this private beach you can swim out to the reefs and snorkel around to your delight. This villa offers a huge outdoor sunning patio which has beautiful views of Jamaica’s north coast.

The living room is the center of entertainment with cable TV, CD, Video, sound system. Ten glass-panelled doors open to the spacious veranda and pool terrace which is furnished with sunning lounges, tables and chairs for your comfort. Meals can be served here. The master bedroom has a king sized four poster bed with ensuite bathroom. There is a television, sitting room with a daybed, lovely ocean views. The two other bedrooms connect through a small interior passageway. These bedrooms are great for families as parents and children can be close and share this two bedroom connecting rooms. The fourth bedroom which is located in the separate octagonal cottage is on the edge of the pool terrace.

This four bedroom, four bathroom villa is very comfortable, with the dining and living room faces the pool and wonderful sea views. The professional staff will look after your every need. There is also a complimentary shuttle to take you to all the resort facilities that the famous Tryall Club has on offer. All this combined together will make sure your holiday in Jamaica is very special
