Dunes Villa, Grace Bay, Providenciales, Turks and Caicos Vacation Rental

 

Striking a balance between environmental responsibility and contemporary design, the two freestanding private beachfront villas of The Dunes have set a new standard of excellence for luxury villa homes on the Turks & Caicos Islands. Enjoy breathtaking vistas from the 3 ocean view bedroom suites in the main villa (including two master suites), or the utmost privacy in 2 additional one-bedroom executive pool suites—each with outdoor shower—situated adjacent to the spectacular pool oasis that forms the heart of each villa. The property’s 5 bedrooms and 5.5 baths complement expansive indoor/outdoor living space totaling more than 6,700 square feet, all situated within a gated, micro resort community. Meanwhile, The Dunes’ onsite, personal concierge will ensure no wish or whim goes unfulfilled.

In the heart of the villa, delight in the contemporary style oasis-like pool area featuring a “floating” outdoor dining palapa and bold fire feature. Oceanside, sliding doors disappear to seamlessly connect the main villa’s indoor living space to the full-width beachfront terrace. The sumptuously appointed terrace flows out on to unique "nestling areas," located within your own private sandy dunes, overlooking the famed Smith’s Reef. Behind the scenes, multi-zone air conditioning systems, solar water heating, gray water recycling and BIONEST waste water treatment provide the utmost comfort while utilizing advanced technologies carefully selected to reduce the villas’ environmental footprint.
