Skye House, Galle, Sri Lanka Vacation Rental

 

A private modern beach front villa close to the historic town of Galle with 5 en-suite bedrooms, a large pool, a play ground and direct access onto one of Sri Lanka's best beaches.

A grand example of the amazing villas available for rent along the Sri Lankan coast, Skye House will take your breath away. Wake up to the sound of waves crashing along your private beach. Savor delicious meals prepared exclusively for you by your own personal chef. Skye House offers guests the opportunity to spend their days in paradise.

Skye House easily accommodates large parties. The house comes with two master bedrooms each with their own king-size bed and three additional bedrooms, one of which contains a queen bed, another a twin bed, and third three bunk beds. The vast, luscious green lawn situated next to a stunning L-shaped infinity pool offers guests both a great place to hold a gathering and picturesque views from the ocean-facing terrace. The villa also features convenient ensuite bathrooms and remarkable water features.

Guests can relax and let the professional staff take care of their every need. Skye house is equipped with state-of-the-art technology including Wi-Fi, iPod speaker docks, satellite TV and a local phone. Those seeking to explore the amazing sites of Sri Lanka can kick-off their adventure by checking out the famous city of Galle and its well preserved fortress, which sits only 10 kilometers away. This well-located piece of paradisaical perfection gives guests a ticket to luxury living complete with an unforgettable Sri Lankan experience.
