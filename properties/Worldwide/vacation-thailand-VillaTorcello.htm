Villa Torcello, Kamala, Phuket, Thailand Vacation Rental

 

Villa Torcello features a tasteful blend of modern architecture and contemporary Asian style fused beautifully together to produce a unique and distinct character. It is a masterpiece on its own merits, with every area and aspect of the villa beautifully intertwined with meticulous design and outstanding functionality. In other words, Villa Torcello is unrivalled and unsurpassed in terms of its beauty and splendor.

Villa Torcello features well-appointed rooms with floor to ceiling glass walls allowing seamless integration of outdoor scenery and the interiors, and cathedral style roofs stunningly matched with dark walnut timber flooring.

Refreshingly luxurious marble floors and countertops define the bathrooms, with walls and cubicles accentuated with cool mosaic tiles.

Villa Torcello boasts of unrivaled landscape design and mood-defined lighting system with the latest Vantage Automated Home System which allows the villa’s transformation from any subdued relaxed ambiance to a vibrant and lively party experience with just a button click. Mood lighting system is installed in every room as well as an integrated sound system.

The estate’s gentle slopes and flat plateau complements the family-friendly environment that envelope the entire villa. Keeping a “floating” effect on three levels, Villa Torcello enjoys a seamless flow of spaces throughout the rooms and walkways. The top level houses the Owner’s Retreat while the other 2 lower levels form a U-shape around a central lily pond greeting guests at the entrance level, and to an infinity swimming pool further down.

The meticulous spatial planning allows the rooms to enjoy fantastic views of the Andaman Sea, with refreshing options to relax in cool shaded areas, sun basking terraces and pavilions.

The grand master bedroom suite is on its own elevated level together with 4 other bedrooms, a relaxing family room and office, a spacious living room, a game room with pool table and Xbox, a walk-in wine cellar, a fully-equipped European kitchen and dining area and a 12-person spa set into well-manicured and landscaped gardens.

The European-inspired kitchen features a state-of-the-art imported Varenna-designed kitchen assembly, including an enormous breakfast island, Corina work surfaces and custom-built storage units.

The equally stylish European elegance extends to the formal dining room which is wrapped with floor to ceiling glass concertina doors which open up completely to allow the surrounding courtyard and ocean views into the dining space with indoor or outdoor dining options.

Villa Torcello is designed with the concept of providing generous living space for socializing and entertaining, without compromising privacy and harmony with the natural surroundings.

The unrivalled luxury and privacy offered by Villa Torcello is set in a very unique environment, a stone’s throw away from Kamala village.
