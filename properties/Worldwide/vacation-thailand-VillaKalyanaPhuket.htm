Villa Kalyana Phuket, Phuket, Thailand Vacation Rental

 

The sun rises on the world-class yachting destination that is the Royal Phuket Marina, home to luxury yachts and the impressive 5-bedroom Villa Kalyana, the most luxurious address in Phuket. Right on the edge of the marina’s exclusive waters and upscale Villa Kalyana, close to Phuket’s Andaman Sea, comes with its own private, 23-meter berth; a special stay for yachties and those who appreciate the beauty of boating and the splendid scenery and sailing scene in Phuket.

Exquisitely homely, beautifully luxurious, and sophisticatedly designed Villa Kalyana is defined by its incredible location. Take a cool dip in the villa’s salt-water pool which is 25 meters long and extends elegantly to a wonderful sala with in-water loungers and Japanese style seating right by the marina. The three-story villa with a two-story wing is designed to promise a delicious sense of privacy yet a homely intimacy too that inspires total relaxation.

The dark hardwood floors and traditional Asian villa design, décor, and artefacts blend perfectly with the light-reflecting, minimalist white walls and the modern features of Villa Kalyana. These include open plan spaces, see-through balconies, and a master bedroom with walls of glass looking out to the pond and garden surrounding it. The effect is effortlessly soothing, wonderfully inviting, and luxuriously glamorous all at the same time. The relatively new-build villa was renovated in 2016 and the sound and theatre systems are cutting edge. To follow the delicate touches of Japanese and Thai design flair throughout the villa, the living and dining room playfully boasts karaoke too for fun evenings of musical entertaining.

An open plan lounge with big sofas and a round-table dining area with a Lazy Susan, plus a full kitchen with a wine cellar create a welcoming and enjoyable environment indoors; with three staff, including a private chef, on hand to look after every need. Keep active within the grounds of the exclusive gated estate of the Royal Phuket Marina with fitness and health club facilities, a spa and a tennis court nearby. Meanwhile, the beaches of Bangtao and Surin are just a short drive away

Lounge by the pool on comfy, beach-club style sofas or refresh in the whirlpool looking out to the marina. While right by the pool, a barbeque plus an island bar and more beds set in their own water-feature-style mini pool are ideal for balmy evenings entertaining and dining outdoors or even a massage by the pool as the sun goes down; fantastic features to really experience the exuberance of the marina-side lifestyle Villa Kalyana so expertly exudes

What sets Villa Kalyana apart is not simply its individual marina setting, but the attention to design details that create rooms that all offer their own special, unique charm. The 110 square meter master bedroom has a huge lounge area and its own pond, whilst stone walkways lead to a Japanese master bedroom and garden views. Each room has its own ensuite bathroom with a whirlpool bath and whilst the main master bedroom opens to the green outdoors the other rooms each have their own private balcony with stunning views of the pool, garden, and marina.

The Royal Phuket Marina is designed with upmarket waterfront lifestyle in mind too and is an attractive setting. The boardwalk boasts restaurants and bars including a patisseries, prohibition-style speakeasy lounge, and artsy Vietnamese eatery too. Kids Mania engages youngsters with indoor and outdoor play areas, promising grown-ups an opportunity to relax along too.
