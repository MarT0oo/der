Casa Blanca
5 Bedrooms | 3 Baths | Sleeps 10 | Pool | Ocean Access                 
Port Douglas, Australia
      From AUD $3850 - $4200 per wk

Casa Blanca is one of Port Douglas' most beautiful luxury holiday houses. This peaceful setting provides the perfect base from which to enjoy everything Port Douglas has to offer.  Set in the exclusive enclave of Solander Boulevard this two storey mansion is designed with easy living in mind and is dedicated to space and privacy.  Casa Blanca sleeps up to 10 guests in “king size” comfort. This luxury holiday house caters for everyone: large families, families & friends or couples wishing to share a memorable holiday.

Casa Blanca is a unique property within the township of Port Douglas and is within only 200 leisurely footsteps of a quiet area of the beach.  The main street of Port Douglas is a few minutes drive away by car or luxury limousine.  Every comfort has been thought of for your tropical holiday.  This spacious freeform pool—10 metres one arm and 10 metres the other—wraps around the house and is designed for year round pleasure. Sun decks surround the solar-heated pool where you can relax on day beds or snooze in a hammock.  Uninterrupted views of the pool and garden are ensured by the state-of-the-art stainless steel pool fence … and when you tire of lazing by the pool, stroll through the beach track and let the warm waters of the Coral Sea entice you to swim there.  Glimpse the breathtaking beauty of the Ulysses butterflies as they flit through the rainforest canopy. Snooze in a hammock under the shade of towering palm tree.

Casa Blanca’s gardens set it apart from any other retreat in the township of Port Douglas, giving you the space and privacy you desire in a tropical sanctuary.  Discover the numerous exotic flowers and plants throughout the Casa Blanca grounds.  Pick a bunch of fresh herbs from the pot, a lime for a cooling G&T from the lime tree or inhale the heady perfume of gingers at dusk.  The lush lawn area is big enough for a game of cricket or footy!  (or in your case baseball, gridiron!) Or just indulge in a massage in the private gardens of your own retreat.
