Heart of Queenstown
3 Bedrooms | 3 Baths | Kiwi BBQ | Lap Pool                                      
Queenstown, New Zealand
  From NZD $1430 – $2850 per night

Spacious, immaculate and carefully considered 42 sets a new standard for luxury accommodation in New Zealand. Situated only a block back from Lake Wakatipu and facing The Remarkables mountain range, the natural expanse is truly breathtaking. With the shops and restaurants of downtown Queenstown an easy five minute stroll away, Forty Two is perfectly placed.

The property is laid out is over three distinctive floors and its three spacious bedrooms can comfortably accommodate up to 6 adults. The study also has a sofa which converts to a comfortable double bed for a larger group of 8 if necessary. Make use of the state of the art home entertainment system and benefit from the convenience of a fully functional office. Enjoy the outside entertainment area by having a traditional Kiwi BBQ or a swim in the lap pool.

Combining native New Zealand materials with a contemporary edge, this Vacation Rental blends seamlessly with its spectacular surroundings. From the beech wood paneling specifically selected and milled for the property to the handpicked river stones that make up the fireplace, the home offers a luxury experience unlike anything else in Queenstown.  

We look forward to introducing you to a very special corner of New Zealand and sharing the magic of Heart of Queenstown.
