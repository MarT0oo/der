Villa Kalyani, Canggu, Bali, Indonesia Vacation Rental

 

Welcome to Villa Kalyani, our luxurious five-bedroom villa in the heart of Canggu. We designed Kalyani (which means beautiful or blessed) to offer all things to all people, and as well as an exceptional range of hi-tech entertainment facilities, our tropical home comes complete with ample spots for rest and relaxation. From the dining room to the playroom, from the open-sided living room to the sunken balé lounge, the air-conditioned home theatre, the outdoor cinema and the 22-metre swimming pool to the rooftop with its 360-degree views, we are sure you will find a niche to call your own.

Luxuriously appointed with king-size beds and ensuite bathrooms, the five bedrooms are leisurely spread out across the property to allow you plenty of privacy. Each is equipped with air-conditioning and audio-visual facilities, while the two-bedroom guest pavilion also presents a living area and 7-metre plunge pool.

Kalyani is perfectly set up for families or groups of friends in search of a chilled-out five-star holiday experience, as well as being wired for sound for those looking to host weddings and parties. We have two bars, a dance floor, a professional DJ booth, a large rooftop reception terrace, and generous lawns for gatherings of up to 80 people.

Our professional staff will go out of their way to ensure that your stay at Villa Kalyani is as memorable as can be, providing polished service and gourmet food. Furthermore, if you wish to head out to explore the island’s many delights, a car and driver are available at a special daily rate.

Less than two kilometres from Bali’s south-west ‘sunset’ coast, Villa Kalyani’s location is ideal for those who wish to play at the beach or revel in Bali’s rural charm, while still being close to Seminyak’s vibrant social scene.

Villa Kalyani comprises a large two-storey, U-shaped building housing three bedrooms and the entertainment areas and a single-storey guest pavilion at the base of the garden with two bedrooms, a living area and a plunge pool. Between these two buildings is a 22-metre swimming pool and lawn. There are myriad entertainment areas on the first and ground floors including a formal dining room, kitchen, a games lounge with pool table and bar, a party area with professional DJ booth and dance floor and bar, a media room and sun decks both on the roof and beside the swimming pool.

Master 1
With a high-pitched wooden ceiling suspended over hardwood floors, together with a king-size bed, comfortable soft furnishings and balconies on two sides looking out over the pool and garden, the master bedroom will induce nothing but extreme contentment for those lucky enough to draw the winning straw. The jewel in its crown, however, must be the gargantuan ensuite bathroom with its vast soaking tub and private garden.

Master 2
Similar in size to the master bedroom, this second upstairs master room is equipped with a king-size bed and its own wrap-around terrace overlooking the pool and garden. With its earthy tones, hardwood floors, comfortable furnishings and very spacious bathroom, this suite is bound to impress.

Guest bedroom 1
Furnished with a king-size bed which converts into twins and large ensuite bathroom compete with soaking tub, this guest bedroom is discreetly tucked away behind the dining room. The room looks out across a neat lawn to a waterwall and ornamental pond, allowing guests to drift into dream-time to the soothing sounds of falling water.

Guest bedroom 2 & 3
Located at the far end of the garden, either side of a covered but open-sided living area, these two super-comfortable rooms have king-size beds, ensuite bathrooms and great views out over the lawn and pool. A 7 x 3 metre plunge pool adds to the luxury of this guest pavilion.

Play room & bar
Open to the garden and equipped with a pool table, a bar and a sitting area with yielding couches, this chill-out spot offers the best of both worlds for competitive types and those who prefer to simply sit back and watch.

Media room
Equipped with surround-sound system, large sofas, a Nintendo Wii and a massive projector screen (which can also be assembled for movie nights in the garden), this media room is set up to provide a gold-class home cinema experience.

Party room
There are not many villas in Bali that offer a purpose-built party space complete with a bar, professional DJ booth and dance floor. Open-sided and facing the garden Villa Kalyani’s party room is most definitely the place to get your groove on all night long. And when you need to take a break from dancing, there’s an adjacent lounge area with soft-seat couches for up to 15 people.

Dining room
Centrally positioned in the main building and furnished with a large wooden table for 12 people, Villa Kalyani’s dining room can be closed off for air-conditioned comfort and provides the perfect setting in which to savour the exceptional cuisine prepared by the villa’s private chef and his team.

Outdoor living
At the far end of the inviting, 22-metre swimming pool is a wooden deck topped with three double sun loungers. When the sun gets too much, the adjacent ‘sunken’ gazebo provides respite. It’s all especially beautiful at night when the trees and gardens are lit up and a fire bowl glows from the end of the pool.

Between the upper level master bedrooms are two fun spaces; one with a swinging cushioned daybed suspended from the ceiling, and the other presenting a wooden sundeck with two double sun loungers for private tanning.

More top-level outdoor living space can be found above guest bedrooms 2 & 3, where an expansive roof terrace offers broad views while a balé bengong (relaxation pavilion) provides shade. The terrace is ideal for receptions and especially good at sunset or after dark when the temperature has dropped.
