Bondi Beach Penthouse, Elizabeth Bay, Sydney, Australia Vacation Rental

 

Created by leading architects and interior designers, this luxury penthouse carves out a space on Sydney’s iconic Bondi Beach like no other. Offering sweeping views across the beach and dramatic clifftops, this three bedroom apartment gives you access to everything the Harbour City has to offer—and allows you to retreat in style when you wish to relax.

The open living area really embraces its location with sunlight and the ocean breeze filtering throughout. Stylish furnishings make the most of the abundant natural light, while a double open kitchen with marble benches and state-of-the-art appliances is ideal for entertaining.

Each of the bedrooms have blockout blinds, and open out onto that view, so no one has to miss out on the pleasure of waking up to it. Heated towel racks for your beach towels, fogless mirrors and an an outdoor shower for those returning from a swim at the beach. There are also Sonos speakers throughout, and a small office space, meaning that guests will be left wanting for nothing.

Nature delivers its own work of art with the 180 degree view of Bondi Beach directly in front of you. But you won’t be staying indoors for long: the highlight of the property is undoubtedly the spacious alfresco entertaining area, with several day beds, couches, and a barbecue and dining area giving you a front row seat to the best of Sydney without having to leave the house.

Watching the day come and go from the alfresco entertaining space overlooking the beach. Take a short stroll to one of the boutique local grocers or farmers’ markets to stock up on fresh ingredients, and make use of the outdoor barbeque for a quintessential Australian entertaining experience.

This architecturally stunning penthouse features an expansive 236 sqm internal area, framed by 80 sqm of terrace space, and an additional 19 metre frontage to Bondi Beach. Whether working from home, entertaining guests, or having a romantic evening in, you’ll never tire of the majestic views of the ocean and headland.

Make your sightseeing an exclusive experience by having our concierge rent you a boat to cruise around the landmarks for the finest views, and take dips in some of the secret beach coves spread throughout the eastern suburbs. After sightseeing, you’ll be firmly in the mood to explore Sydney’s culinary landscape. Begin by visiting neighbourhood stalwarts such as Bondi Icebergs and Sean’s Panorama, before branching out to nearby Catalina on Rose Bay, and award-winning city haunts including Bennelong, Rockpool, and Aria.
