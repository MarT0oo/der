Tranquility, Mosman, Sydney, Australia Vacation Rental

 

Tranquility is one of the most special Sydney luxury homes in Mosman. As you approach you’ll pass the double garage and off street parking, and open the front door onto three levels of stylish luxury.

Head straight downstairs to discover one of the living rooms that leads to an outdoor dining table, then onto the breathtaking outside space. As you walk down the lawn you’ll pass the outdoor kid’s swing/slide/climbing equipment as well as the trampoline, where little ones can laugh, play and make memories to last a lifetime, until you reach the stunning horizon pool surrounded by lush greenery and with incredible water views. Here you’ll wile away the hours gazing out over the glistening water of Quaker’s Hat Bay before lighting up the BBQ or pizza oven on the terrace, the perfect place for outdoor entertainment.

Once you’ve explored the garden, head back into the large open plan house to discover the rest of the numerous entertaining areas, with natural wood, stone and glass, stunning artwork and natural stone floors throughout. The decadent kitchen is a dream come true whether you love to cook or just to entertain. The black granite is offset with crisp white, and the warm wood tones show off the Miele appliances, coffee machine, steam oven and grill. There’s even a butler’s kitchen with additional entertaining oven, fridge and sink. As well as the downstairs outdoor dining table, you have the choice of the elegant dining table just off the kitchen, and a beautiful outdoor dining balcony with a fan and heating bars so you and your guests are always perfectly comfortable. When the day draws to a close you have the choice of four beautiful bedrooms, with the fifth being a delightful nursery complete with a cot for the little one.

About the Area

With bay views in the distance and pretty streets lined with glamorous homes, Mosman is a stylish shopping and entertainment destination with a multitude of Sydney luxury homes. Military Road and surrounding streets have plenty for the keen shopper, from fashion and homewares to accessories and antiques. Grab a morning coffee at Café Mosman before heading out to hunt for a bargain at Mosman markets, or explore some local bush tracks. For little ones there’s so much to do, from snorkelling to playing on the beach, and the whole family can end the day with some delicious food at The Public Dining Room.
