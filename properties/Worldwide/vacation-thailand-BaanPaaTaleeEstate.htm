Baan Paa Talee Estate, Kamala, Phuket, Thailand Vacation Rental

 

This exquisite oceanfront property is spectacularly situated high on a headland overlooking the Andaman Sea on the picturesque Cape Amarin between Kamala and Patong on Phuket's scenic west coast. “House on the cliff by the ocean” is the appropriate translation of the Thai name for this superb nine-bedroom luxury villa designed by Paris-based architects Arte Charpentier.

Baan Paa Talee has an abundance of indoor and outdoor living and dining areas – all with sweeping panoramic views – and a gorgeous 25m infinity pool and jacuzzi overlooking the ocean. With living and sleeping pavilions distributed generously over four levels of beautifully designed gardens that merge into the natural landscape, interspersed with soothing water features, Baan Paa Talee Estate's accommodation and facilities rival those of the most lavish resorts in the world.

The main villa comprises seven spacious ocean-view bedrooms, arranged in flexible configurations to suit families, couples and independent groups. Uniquely, this property also boasts a self-contained two-bedroom Ocean Villa with an open-plan kitchen, dining and living area, and a wide balcony perched just above the sea. While Baan Paa Talee Estate provides the perfect setting for a wedding for up to 80 guests, the Ocean Villa makes a dream honeymoon hideaway.

A 20-seater dining room, indoor and outdoor lounges, media room, alfresco bar, barbecue and games area make up some of Baan Paa Talee Estate's many relaxation areas, and there’s a well equipped gym and beach house with watersports equipment for the energetic.

A large team of dedicated staff, including a guest relations manager, housekeepers, service staff and a villa manager are on hand to ensure that guests want for nothing and a gourmet chef prepares superb Thai meals on a par with the best restaurants in Phuket, but within the comfort and convenience of your own villa.

Centrally situated on Phuket’s west coast, Baan Paa Talee Estate is just a 15-minute drive from the beaches of Kamala and Patong; 20 minutes from Surin and less than an hour from the international airport. Laguna golf course and the fascinating markets and shops of Phuket Town are also within easy driving distance.
