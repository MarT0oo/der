Eagle Spirit, Russell, New Zealand

 

Tucked into the hillside, Eagles Spirit offers peace and relaxation in a sheltered, sun drenched location.

Nestled into a sea of native bush, the tranquillity and seclusion of this private villa, from the moment you walk through the front door, the panoramic ocean vistas overlooking the historical grounds of Waitangi will capture your heart and imagination.

Watch dolphins play just below you, while unwinding in the outdoor heated spa pool, or on the sunlounger on the deck.
