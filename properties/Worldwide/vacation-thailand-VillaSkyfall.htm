Villa Skyfall, Tambol Bophut, Thailand Vacation Rental

 

You cannot beat the spectacular views over Koh Phangnan and Ang Thong Marine park! With both modern Thai and antique Asian furniture complementing one another, the Villa is an interior designer's dream and is unique in its architectural design. This Villa is much more than just a luxury shelter by the sea, it is an artistic manifestation that takes its natural environmental attributes as palettes for its architecture, where everyday materials around it become part of the canvas, including sand, rocks, terrain, and other natural features.

Large and intimate relaxation spaces
The villa itself enjoys numerous areas to relax, from large spaces combined with spectacular views, to cozy individual nooks and crannies, which allow guests to decide if they want to entertain just the closest of friends or to hold parties for large events. These spaces include a study, a luxurious indoor movie theatre, an indoor lounge bar with a pool table, and an outdoor pool bar.

Varied entertainment options
The shared spaces include the estate tennis court, an indoor bar that overlooks the infinity pool, and hosts a pool table and 2 large TVs, ideal for watching sporting events with friends and family. On top of this the villa’s lighting, music system and theatre are state of the art, operated by Ipad, whilst at the same time letting guests connect their own choice of music from individual Ipods and computer systems.

Delicious meals in stunning surroundings
There is a sunny outside dining terrace, seating up to 8 people, and an indoor dining room with large plate glass windows and doors, that create an indoor/outdoor dining experience. The villa has a fully equipped kitchen, however a personal chef is included in the daily rate, meaning that you can relax entirely.

Swim with a view
This magnificent private 26 square meter swimming pool has a deep swimming section and a shallow seating/children’s play area, and is surrounded by stunning views will create that perfect holiday atmosphere for everyone.

Luxuriant and comfortable bedrooms
Each of the 8 en-suite bedrooms enjoys satellite fed television and DVD, whilst being able to access the large music library on the centralized server. There is one large master bedroom, a further six doubles, and a twin; the master bedroom and two of the doubles have their own private balconies. Lift access to bedrooms (except lower floor rooms) not only means that luggage delivery on arrival is painless, but also that elderly relatives have access to the whole house from the car porch itself.

Varied local activities
Villa Skyfall is located in Choeng Mon, the top East-Northern corner of the island. The Estate is just 10 minutes from the airport. Beautiful Choeng Mon beach is within walking distance and both the popular Chaweng beach, with its shops and nightlife, and Bophut Fisherman's village with its vibrant restaurants, are only a fifteen minutes’ drive away.

Staff and services
Resident staff are on hand 24/7, and additional staff, including dedicated chefs, bar staff, caterers and even musicians can be hired to make your stay unforgettable.
