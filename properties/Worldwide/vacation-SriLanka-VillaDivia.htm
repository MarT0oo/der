Villa Divia, Dikwella, Sri Lanka Vacation Rental

 

Hugging close to the coastlines of Dikwella, Ani Villas Sri Lanka is an expansive beachfront villa complex containing two individual villas - the eight bedroom Villa Monara and seven bedroom Villa Divia. Set upon a sprawling tropical garden, the villa complex sits atop a bluff on the central south coasts, and commands a stunning view of pristine white shores and azure blue waters. A luxury vacation rental like no other, Ani Villas Sri Lanka boasts lush amenities, fine interiors and impeccable service.

Situated within a spacious garden, Villa Divia’s scenic grounds are dotted with tropical blooms and shady palms. With plenty of cosy facilities, guests at the villa are spoilt for choice when it comes to picking a spot to relax and unwind at. The open plan living sala is perfect for lazy afternoons, while the pool bar serves as an excellent spot for enjoying sundowners. Adults will not be able to resist taking dips in the beachfront pool, while the little ones will have the time of their lives making merry at the kids’ pool, complete with a water slide.

Built for rest and relaxation, the exquisite interiors of Villa Divia are a pleasing and comforting sight. Wooden fixtures and light colours create an ambience of cosiness, while tasteful ornaments spice up the soothing simplicity of its interiors. Guests can enjoy a quiet evening browsing through their favourite books in the library, or indulge in a cheerful session of games and film watching in the entertainment room.

At the end of an enjoyable day, it is time to sink down into a deep slumber. Holidaymakers at Villa Divia will gladly retreat into the well-appointed bedrooms for a restful night. Four of the bedrooms are capacious master suites, while the remaining three are suites.

Travellers residing at Ani Villas Sri Lanka will have a plethora of travel activities to choose from. Water sports enthusiasts will have a fine time riding the waves on a surfboard, or venturing out to the sea for a fascinating session of whale watching. Nature lovers may embark on an insightful visit to one of Sri Lanka’s gorgeous animal reserves, while cultural buffs will delight in taking an expedition to explore tea plantations located close by.
