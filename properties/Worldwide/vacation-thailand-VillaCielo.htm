Villa Cielo, Natai Beach, Phuket, Thailand Vacation Rental

 

The stylish six-bedroom Villa Cielo offers a fantasy tropical haven for families or groups. Situated on the lovely Natai Beach, just 30 minutes to the north of Phuket International Airport Cielo is ideal for both a relaxing holiday and an exquisite wedding or special event. Enjoy the five-star service of a friendly team of staff including a villa manager and chef, and ample facilities including a 25-metre pool, wide lawns, chic indoor and outdoor entertaining areas, a TV room, guest kitchen, trampoline, pool table, foosball, surfboards and barbecue. Relax and celebrate in style at Villa Cielo.

Embracing its centrally positioned 25-metre pool on three sides, Villa Cielo offers endless sea views and full immersion into the Natai Beach tropical idyll. The open-plan living area, set just back from the pool, features floor-to-ceiling glass doors that slide open fully to let the ocean breezes flow through. Access to all areas of the villa, both indoors and out, is via this pivotal space. The TV room off to one side leads to the villa’s single-storey wing housing three bedrooms including a master suite that extends along the pool’s length towards the sea. The dining area and guest kitchen are on the ground floor of the two-storey wing opposite, with a second master suite and two guest bedrooms upstairs.

Indoor living

Cielo’s bright, spacious living areas offer a stylish setting to relax or entertain in style. Extending across the central section of the villa is the vaulted living room, furnished with a pool table and a soft sand-toned sofa positioned for optimal views. This bright room opens completely to the pool terrace in front and the reflecting pool behind to create a breezy indoor-outdoor connection, or it can be closed and cooled with air conditioning.

The living area connects to the dining room and modern guest kitchen where floor-to-ceiling sliding glass doors on three sides let in natural light to bathe the room in a tropical glow. Meals at the stately deep-red dining table for 14 feel like a special occasion, while the breakfast bar with four stools offers a more casual place to dine. The stylish TV/family room adjacent to the living area is designed with some eye-catching art and a roomy sofa with entertainment choices for all ages including a foosball table, home theatre, board games and kids’ wheel-boards. This can be sealed off from the living area by sliding wooden doors for added privacy.

Outdoor living

With 3,800 square metres of gorgeous beachfront setting, Cielo offers ample space for staging events or simply playing in the tropical outdoors with family and friends. Its 25-metre infinity pool extending towards the sea is surrounded by a spacious furnished sundeck and a shaded barbecue dining area. At the front of the beachfront lawn is a peak-roofed sala offering a prime sea view, while kids are sure to flock to the lawn behind the villa with its trampoline and hobby horses, plus plenty of space for football or volleyball play.

Master suite – upper level

This lavishly designed top-floor suite is a romantic haven for couples with fabulous sea views. Featuring a dramatic peaked-roof and furnished with a king-sized bed, daybed, TV and cosy armchairs, this is a room for total private relaxation. Or step out onto the balcony to enjoy the views and the sea breeze. Added touches of luxury include a large walk-through wardrobe and ensuite bathroom.

Master suite – ground level

Offering gorgeous views of the pool and sea, this chic king-bedded master suite sits at the front of the poolside wing under a vaulted roof. The suite is stylishly furnished with a leather lounge chair, work desk, TV, walk-through wardrobe and ensuite bathroom with a sunken terrazzo soaker tub.

Guest bedrooms

Two sea-and-pool-view guest bedrooms share a bathroom with a sunken terrazzo tub on the ground fllor, while two more guest rooms, each with private ensuite bathrooms, are on the upper floor above the dining area. Each peak-roofed room, two with twin beds, one king-bedded and one with a queen-sized bed, is a comfy retreat with wardrobes and colourful art accents. One of the upstairs guestrooms is also equipped with a TV and DVD player.
