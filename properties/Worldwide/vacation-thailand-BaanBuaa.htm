Baan Buaa, Samui Beach Village, Koh Samui, Thailand Vacation Rental

 

Baan Buaa is a 3 bedroom luxury villa to rent that will comfortably sleep 6 adults. This beach villa rental comprises a 2-storey main building and an adjacent separate guest building. It is stunningly furnished with many antiques and richly decorated to reflect Thai/Balinese architecture. Baan Buaa has been designed to reflect tropical indoor and outdoor living and offers a peaceful and luxurious setting for your tropical island holiday in Samui. The villa is omly a few steps away from the white sand of Nahai Beach and offers sea and beach views from the upstairs bedrooms and balconies.

The indoor living room complements the outdoor living space. The terrace doors can be opened to form a living area for social occasions and to enjoy indoor and outdoor living with ease.

For entertainment options there is a large 50 inch plasma TV with DVD player & DVD movie library and a comprehensive live TV international channel package (True Visions Gold).

Baan Buaa has its own private pool with a large sandstone pool terrace and a pool shower. The Thai sala pavilion with its elevated platform provides lounging space in the shade close to the pool. The villa is air conditioned throughout. Indoor and outdoor ceiling fans and large sliding windows and doors maximize natural cooling.

For entertainment options there is a large 50 inch plasma TV with DVD player & DVD movie library and a comprehensive live TV international channel package (True Visions Gold).

The indoor dining area comfortably seats eight. The terrace doors connect to an outdoor dining area and to the pool terrace.

The outdoor dining area overlooks pool and sandstone terrace. Protected by its wide roof, it is open to cooling gentle breezes. Baan Buaa has a fully fitted western kitchen so you can entertain in your villa, cook meals and enjoy all the comforts from home. A large Australian gas barbecue allows for great outdoor cooking and grilling.

The master bedroom is on the first floor. It has a king size double bed, walk in dressing room, ensuite bathroom and a private balcony with furniture.

Like all bathrooms in the villa, the master bathroom is fitted with double washbasins, an open shower with rain-shower head, and a wc.

Bedroom 2 is also on the first floor. It has a king size double bed, walk in dressing room, ensuite bathroom and a private balcony with furniture.

Bedroom 3 is in its own building, with direct access to sunbathing terrace and pool. It has a queen size double bed, open plan bathroom with ensuite shower room/wc, its own lounging terrace, and a private garden outdoor shower.

Baan Buaa is an individually designed and decorated beach villa in a unique location on Samui Beach Village which combines the best of tropical living with all the amenities of a luxury home for an unforgettable tropical island vacation.

Samui Beach Village also offers a unique hotel-style service for our guests. A team of 40 staff is on hand to provide the top quality service you would expect to find in a luxury 5 star resort. Unlike most villa rentals where the service is limited, we pride ourselves on giving our guests the best holiday experience possible. Samui Beach Village is a superb location for people looking to lose themselves in the beauty of Koh Samui's gorgeous beaches and lush tropical landscape. The village - which is set right by the beachside - is a small luxury living development close to the main tourist areas of Samui.

It adjoins an unspoiled stretch of sandy beach in the relatively undeveloped south-eastern coast, and has been built amid a profusion of coconut palms and tropical landscaped gardens. Two neighbouring islands - Koh Tan and Koh Mudsum - well-known for their fantastic fishing and snorkeling, are only a short, 20-minute boat ride away.

It is only ten minutes from Lamai Beach, 30 minutes from Chaweng and Samui's top golf course, and 35 minutes from the airport, so you are never far from the action. But while there is easy access to shopping, restaurants, nightlife and other activities, back in Samui Beach Village you will feel you are on your own tropical island paradise.
