Rockstar Villa, Sydney, Australia Vacation Rental


You will gasp when you see the views from this 5 bedroom tri-level luxury house. You might even feel that you are bathing in the Pacific Ocean itself, as you slip into the wet edge pool and absorb the panorama. This high end property is perfect for travelers who enjoy the finer things in life.

This executive property has 5 bedrooms, 4 bathrooms on 2 self contained levels to allow for private spacious living. Ideally suited for 6-8 adults, 2 family groups or a larger family who need to spread out.

There are eating areas from BBQ al fresco to formal dining with an ocean backdrop. The 2 kitchens provide plenty of cooking options from casual to master chef!

This house is newly refurbished in fresh beach colors and boasts modern appointments include air conditioning, limestone floors with heated tiles in winter, 2 cable TV's with Foxtel and home surround entertainment for great movie viewing.

Palm Beach is Sydney’s beach home to the rich and famous. Sydney CBD is a 60 minute drive or a 15 minute seaplane trip. Attend to your city business or shopping and be home for dinner in paradise.
