Waterfront Watson's Bay, Watson's Bay, Sydney, Australia Vacation Rental

 

If you’re after an authentic Sydney holiday experience, a home offering coastal and casual modern elegance, and an exclusive beach side location then you’ve found it right here.

We are really excited to be able to offer this home for luxury accommodation rental and know it offers a truly unique Sydney holiday experience. Welcoming exceptional views from all levels, sea breezes, outstanding entertaining and relaxing zones, and magnificent accommodation, we would highly recommend this home for a fabulous holiday. The outdoor options are boundless, and the convenience of local restaurants, entertainment, ferries, walks and transport are all just walking distance from the front gate.

The home-owners love the casual and easy outdoor lifestyle offered by the home location. They really relish the outdoors whether paddle boarding, walking to nearby Camp Cove, dining out locally or having fish and chips at the park. They are also providing paddle boards, bicycles, golf clubs and small children’s canoes to ensure you really get a taste of the lifestyle!

This stunning 3-level home offers unparalleled views overlooking Watson’s Bay, the sea pool and famous Watson’s Bay Hotel in the near distance. Boasting a modern, coastal aesthetic this home offers the perfect setting for a relaxing holiday while watching the action of the Sydney Harbour and Watson’s Bay beach-goers.

A tropical garden pathway leads inside to awe-inspiring views from the magnificent open-plan living, kitchen and entertaining level. Modern furnishings in warm tones, sumptuous wide spotted gum floors, and glass stacking doors that open to bring the outdoors in, all combine to create a stylish, carefree and casual coastal feel. The kitchen is contemporary and understated, with a sleek, central stone-topped island, and all the latest appliances. Artworks, impressive sandstone features and a magnificent sandstone fireplace all add to the beauty of this space. A separate entertaining area with comfortable outdoor lounge and seating is the perfect place for taking it all in.

The over-generous master bedroom on the top level is modern and tranquil with warm grey and yellow Scandinavian influences. Offering full sea views, sliding doors to the balcony, wooden floors and coastal ship lap joinery – this bedroom will be hard to leave behind! An adjoining en-suite complements that of any hotel bathroom. Soak in the bath and watch the boats sail by.

The lower level is equipped with a sauna, gym, pool table, outdoor shower and entertaining zone flowing out to a tiered garden overlooking the water. Outdoor dining and lounges to sit at and overlook the bay, and a garden pathway leads down to a separate entertaining area at the waters edge. From there a gate to the water makes for an easy swim, paddle board or a walk on the beach. A fire pit and BBQ also make for a great vibe down at the water.
