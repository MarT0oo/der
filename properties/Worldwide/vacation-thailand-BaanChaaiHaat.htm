Baan Chaai Haat, a stunning Koh Samui villa that has something few of the world's most exotic locations has to offer - your own private Luxury villa right on the edge of a wide expanse of silvery sand stretching on either side as far as the eye can see.

This beachfront luxury villa in Koh Samui with private pool and sundeck is set in its own tropical garden with a magnificent view overlooking the calm waters of the Gulf of Siam. It is one of the jewels in the crown of Samui Beach Village, a new and exclusive beachside development situated in the south east corner of the island of Koh Samui.

Baan Chaai Haat is a four bedroom Koh Samui luxury villa that can comfortably accommodate groups or families up to eight people, with extensive outdoor living facilities including a large private pool with sun-decking, set in spacious landscaped gardens. It offers direct access to the tranquil sandy beaches - the perfect setting for a trouble-free holiday.

Despite its tranquil location it is only 35 minutes from the picturesque airport of Koh Samui. On arrival you will be met by our driver who will bring you to your Samui Beach Village luxury villa, where you will be greeted by our manageress, Fiona, and her experienced staff. Throughout your stay they will be at your service, with just one aim: to ensure you have a relaxing and memorable stay.

Luxury Beachside Living In Koh Samui Luxury Villas Of Samui Beach Village

This Koh Samui beach villa is a large integral lounge and dining room, together with a fully furnished kitchen with all the modern conveniences for dining. There are three large bedrooms with king-size beds, all with en-suite bathrooms, and there is a fourth bedroom with a queen-size bed.

The rooms of Baan Chaai Haat luxury villa have been designed with your personal comfort in mind, with air conditioning appropriate to the sunny tropical climate complimented by stylish ceiling fans. All the windows are inter-changeable between glass and mosquito netting to create an ideal ambience at all times. This Koh Samui beach villa is also equipped with the latest in modern technology including cable TV and DVD/CD players.

The large lounge and living area is the heart of Baan Chaai Haat, with TV entertainment system, dining room and gourmet kitchen. As an additional service we can provide you with your own chef, Khun Eak, who will prepare and serve your own choice of both International and Thai cuisine.

From the main living area, large patio doors lead to the poolside terrace and a large pool that is ideal both for swimming and as a play pool for children. On either side terraces and sundecks tempt you with sun loungers and parasols, perfect for relaxing under the tropical sun. There is also a large gas BBQ plus an outdoor dining facility to complete your tropical living experience.

Baan Chaai Haat beach villa enjoys one of the finest positions to be found on any tropical island. From the pool and sundeck and the outdoor dining facility, just a few steps lead down to the white sand, with the ocean stretching away to the horizon. From here you have magnificent views to the north and south of Samui. Far removed from the busy Samui tourist areas, the beaches are always quiet, only visited by occasional tourists and a few fishermen. During the winter months the beaches and shallow sandy lagoon become an ideal location for kite-surfing.

Samui Beach Village is a superb location for people looking to lose themselves in the beauty of Koh Samui's gorgeous beaches and lush tropical landscape. The village - which is set right by the beachside - is a small luxury living development close to the main tourist areas of Samui.

It adjoins an unspoiled stretch of sandy beach in the relatively undeveloped south-eastern coast, and has been built amid a profusion of coconut palms and tropical landscaped gardens. Two neighbouring islands - Koh Tan and Koh Mudsum - well-known for their fantastic fishing and snorkeling, are only a short, 20-minute boat ride away.

It is only ten minutes from Lamai Beach, 30 minutes from Chaweng and Samui's top golf course, and 35 minutes from the airport, so you are never far from the action. But while there is easy access to shopping, restaurants, nightlife and other activities, back in Samui Beach Village you will feel you are on your own tropical island paradise.
