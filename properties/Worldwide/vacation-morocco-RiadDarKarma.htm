Riad Dar Karma, Marrakesh, Morocco Vacation Rental

 

In the historic center of Marrakech , the Riad Dar Karma combines tradition and modern comfort, combining luxury, calm and volupté.Idéalement located in the heart of the ramparts of the Medina, the Riad Dar Karma is a high place of the Kasbah district, A stone's throw from the Saadian Tombs and the two Royal Palaces.

This centuries-old residence belonged to the French translator of his Majesty King Mohamed V. Here, everything has been thought for the comfort and privacy of its guests, this charming house with very beautiful volumes is articulated around 2 patios, The traditional one, the orange patio with a wall fountain lined with small lounges, whose traditional decoration has been preserved and a patio swimming pool with a more modern scenography, An invitation to rest and gentleness with its lounge area, sheltered from the tumult of the Ocre City.

A splendid terrace entirely done up and sported gives a true space of life to the rhythm of the sun, it benefits from a very big solarium, misters and a shower. This place of rest also offers its guests the privilege of taking their breakfasts and lunches with an unobstructed view of the Medina and the Atlas Mountains, sometimes punctuated by the storks flight.

After long days of discovering all the hidden treasures of the medina and its souks, you can relax in the summer by the pool and in winter enjoy the lounge around a mint tea with a good fire. During your stay , We invite you to discover the oldest Oriental beauty ritual, the hammam. Indeed, heated 24h / 24h, we offer you free access to the hammam , this ritual is an integral part of the Moroccan art of living, it relaxes the muscles, releases the toxins and purifies the skin.
