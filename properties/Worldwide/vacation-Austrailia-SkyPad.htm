Sky Pad, Sydney, Australia Vacation Rental

 

Sky Pad is the epitome of sleek city living. Located in the trendy and desirable Darlinghurst suburb, this one bedroom apartment sits on the doorstep of Sydney cafe culture. The pad has a prevailing atmosphere of sexy sophistication that will empower you as you walk through the door.

Sky Pad delivers and excels at meeting the needs of an independent traveller, this stunning apartment ticks the boxes of lovation, style, and convenience.
