Villa Canggu North, Canggu, Bali, Indonesia Vacation Rental

 

Contemporary-styled, two-in-one Villa Canggu, complete with swimming pools and an exciting selection of artwork, is located 100 metres from the beach near Canggu, which is fast evolving as Bali’s hippest seaside scene. The villa is divided into two separate residences which can be rented jointly or independently: four-bedroom Villa Canggu South is adjoined by two-bedroom Villa Canggu North, offering guests the choice of a two, four or six-bedroom renting option.

The villa is well suited for groups of friends or families with plentiful facilities and a professional team of staff including a manager, butlers, security, a car and driver, and a private chef. It is also ideal for weddings and events for up to 40 guests.

If you’re looking for a versatile, out-of-town villa, close to the beach, with plenty of family-friendly facilities, yet also ideal for groups of friends or for weddings and events, then our beautiful, contemporary-styled holiday home could be just the villa for you.

We describe Villa Canggu as versatile because it is actually two villas in one: the main four-bedroom Villa Canggu South complete with its 20-metre swimming pool can be rented independently or in conjunction with sister Villa Canggu North, offering two more bedrooms, an additional living and dining area and an second swimming pool.

We pride ourselves on being family friendly with plenty of facilities for children including a kiddies’ pool. At the same time, we think groups of adult friends will enjoy the sophisticated architecture and décor, relish the luxuriously comfortable and spacious rooms, and be captivated our private collection of artwork, which includes paintings, installations and murals by some renowned Indonesian artists.

Canggu is a once-sleepy village now fast evolving as the hippest beachside scene in Bali with a great choice of cafés, a few boutiques, spas galore, and activities including a surf school, yoga venues and a dance school. Our villa lies on the borders of Canggu in a quiet, close-to-the-beach location with the inclusive service of a car and driver should you wish to explore further afield.

Our friendly team of full time staff, which includes a villa manager, personal butlers and a superb chef, make it likely that you will never want to leave.
