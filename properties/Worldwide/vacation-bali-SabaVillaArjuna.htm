Saba - Villa Arjuna, Canggu, Bali, Indonesia Vacation Rental

 

Looking for a love nest made for two? Say hello to Arjuna. This intimate villa is perfect for honeymooners and anyone wanting the ultimate romantic Bali experience. Located in the coastal town of Canggu, in Bali’s southwest, the villa is part of the larger Saba estate. The open sided living, dining and kitchen space overlooks the private 8 metre swimming pool, as does the delightfully tropical garden bathroom.

If you’re travelling as part of a group, Arjuna can be connected to Bima and/or Nakula, giving you the option to take part in fabulous group festivities. At the end of the day, however, you can simply close your door to enjoy the bliss of peace and solitude.

The villa is wonderfully located - its private car will take just ten minutes to drop you at vibrant Seminyak with itsworld-famous restaurants, cafes and shopping. If you’re keen to maintain your exercise regime then the Canggu Club, where you have complimentary access, is just a few minutes up the road. Or if it’s waves you’re after, the legendary break at Berawa Beach is on your doorstep. The options at Arjuna are endless.
