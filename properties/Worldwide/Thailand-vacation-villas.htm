Visiting Thailand

Most people traveling to Thailand are seeking to enjoy either the coastal regions, such as Phuket.  Phuket is truly the area to visit if you enjoy gorgeous white sands and amazingly kind and courteous locals.  This is an area that is well traveled by people throughout the world for it’s amazing and colorful landscapes and gorgeous feel.  Other locations such as Kathu, Thalang, are also popular in this area. 

Others who travel to Thailand enjoy  Koh Samui in the South East Region.  This area is really great for those who are seeking a resort like setting with a Caribbean feel.  The white sand is amazing for those traveling with children, it’s truly a family friendly location.

Some other locations to consider are: Thailands Central Region, Southern Thailand, and Bangkok.
