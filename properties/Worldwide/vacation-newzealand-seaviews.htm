Sea Views Penthouse
4 Bedrooms | 4 Baths | Gym | Outdoor Pool                                        
Aukland, North Island, New Zealand
      From NZ $2500 per night

This truly stunning 4 bedroom, 4 bathroom, luxury apartment is located on Auckland's Viaduct Basin - the perfect base for experiencing everything the City of Sails has to offer.  With magnificent sea views and just a short stroll from the yachts and ferries as well as the vibrant city centre, this luxurious penthouse offers the very best in central city accommodation.

At around 300 meters out to sea, you'll still find five star service and facilities in the world-class Hilton hotel. Enjoy the state of the art gymnasium and heated outdoor pool. Savor contemporary cuisine at White Restaurant, or sip an expertly made martini with a view at the Bellini Bar.

You'll find a myriad of other options to wine, dine or dance. A generous serving of international eateries matched with world famous kiwi hospitality ensures a memorable evening on the stunning Auckland waterfront - now the epicenter of Auckland's varied nightlife.


The apartment has monitored, secure parking for your safety and convenience. If you'd rather leave the car at home, the nearby Fullers Ferry terminal is your gateway to the pristine island of the Hauraki Gulf. And the Britomart transport hub's buses and trains are just 5 minutes walk.

For some therapeutic retail, take your time browsing the fine art, fashion and design stores that line Custom, High and Queens Streets.  Of course, no 'city of sails' worth its salt would be complete without superb boating facilities. The Viaduct precinct boasts world-class refit facilities, a wealth of marine chandlery services and a vast range of moorings virtually on your doorstep.
