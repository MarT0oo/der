Villa Kalyana- Your Private Super-Luxurious Villa Resort

Idyllic beachfront holiday villas in Asia don’t come much better than Samui South Beach - there is something infinitely special about this Absolute Beachfront Villa - probably the most incredible hideaway in Thailand

Privacy comes first: This Estate is completely Private - Private Villa - Private Beach - Private Bay...

The total living area is more then 1500 m2 on 5000 m2 land and 40 meters private beachfront, 300 m2 Pool....

The Villa-Resort is separated into four 75 m2 beach bungalow suites; a 90 m2 master suite and another four 45 m2 luxury accommodations in the main building; all within footsteps of the 300 m2 infinity edge swimming pool with two islands - just meters from your cozy private beach area.

This is a perfect example of a villa that successfully combines location, style, luxury and convenience all rolled into one.

When entering the villa for the first time, you’ll be immediately impressed by the huge covered terrace and its lounge area, which comprises a 250 year old hand-carved wooden "Dance Sala" literally floating above a water basin. Straight away, you will be able to observe those hypnotic sea-views down the garden where the bungalows are positioned around the extensive pool.

Merging contemporary Balinese design with Modern Asian elements, it is spectacular in every way. Enjoying four bungalows, one eccentric master suite and additional four bedrooms of the highest order, fifteen in-and outdoor bathrooms and a spacious open plan living terrace, this Koh Samui villa rental provides a vast amount of space and privacy for an occupation with up to 20 guests.

Weddings and parties can flawlessly support 100 + guests and their hosts.

Four 85 m2 bungalows around the pool have indoor and outdoor bathrooms, a kitchenette, all usual 5-star equipment such as flat screen TV, minibar, 4 poster throne-beds, and 7 meter folding doors, opening to the beach... The master suite overlooks the whole scenario, with 2 walk-in dressing rooms, an indoor bathroom with sea view, a private outdoor spa terrace with Jacuzzi and massage sala, well sorted bookshelves and much more....The other rooms do all have sea view, a filled minibar, private terrace areas, desk, sofa, walk-through wardrobes and floor to ceiling sliding doors to the garden.

The Villa is fully equipped with modern technology, Wi-Fi (fiber optic) all over the compound, 11 flat screen TVs from 30 to 65 ", HD video projection in the cinema room, quietest possible air-conditioning (total 20 aircon units ) central hot water, rain showers,

Over 120 m2 air-conditioned dining area is equipped with a massive 7 meter wooden table seating 22 persons, a European breakfast kitchen, a bar and a chill out area. Directly connected is an outdoor barbecue terrace for those "dining under the stars" arrangements.

Main terrace of over 150 m2 with 12 meter high wooden pyramid-shaped dome ceilings hosts the 14 seater lounge sofa floating over a water basin within an antique hand carved wooden pavilion.

Spa/massage area, fitness, cinema room, entertainment room with bar and snooker table, and a large poolside terrace with 4 round outdoor sofas.

We have full time staff of 10 for your convenience, European Guest Host, Thai Villa Manageress, Thai Chef, 3 maids, driver, and 3 gardeners. The Host will arrange all kinds of activities you can imagine.

Cars, motorbikes, jet skis, boats, canoes, mountain bikes are available at request.

This will be the essence of a holiday you will never forget!
