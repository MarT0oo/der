Villa Amanzi, Phuket, Thailand Vacation Rental

 

Villa Amanzi is a lavish hillside haven with fabulous views over Kata Noi beach. Its six ensuite bedrooms, friendly staff and plush indoor and outdoor living areas make Amanzi an ideal holiday escape for families or groups.

Amanzi is the Zulu word for water, an element whose restorative properties prevail within and around the villa: cool down in the 15-metre infinity-edge pool, enjoy a soothing massage to the sound of ocean waves or take a refreshing soak in the bathtub or jacuzzi of the two master suites. Amanzi’s atmosphere of cool tranquillity is further enhanced by the trickling stone waterfall feature and koi-pond garden to the rear of the villa’s living and dining room.

A holiday at Amanzi could be spent in complete peace simply lounging on the comfortably furnished terrace, indulging in some in-villa spa treatments, or taking a morning stroll along the soft sands of Kata Noi, just a 10-minute walk away. Or, for a more social escape, Amanzi’s top-notch entertainment systems, modern kitchen, barbecue sala and glorious setting make it a charming venue for a party or special celebration. Sports enthusiasts can play table tennis or pool in the privacy of the villa, visit the estate clubhouse for its tennis court and fitness centre, or book a round at one of Phuket’s top golf courses.

Amanzi is easily accessible to guests of all ages, with a lift connecting the two main floors, flexible bed configurations, and a good selection of kids’ equipment and facilities. The ultra-private downstairs bedroom suite is attached to a TV lounge room with a rollaway sofa bed, while a cosy room on the top floor could serve as a nanny’s room or a playroom for the kids.

Among Villa Amanzi’s staff is a charming and knowledgeable English-speaking manager, a housekeeping and service team, and 24-hour estate security. Meals chosen from a menu of mainly Thai dishes are prepared by your private chef, while more dining and leisure choices are just a short distance away at Kata Noi and Kata beaches.

Spread over three levels, with its spacious outdoor living areas and pool extending from the middle level, Amanzi’s design optimises the sea views. The villa’s entrance is on the top floor, along with a covered parking space, foyer, first master suite and two guest bedrooms. Descending via the lift or the wide staircase brings you to the large living and dining room that opens to the pool terrace and barbecue sala.

The second master suite and another guest bedroom are also on this floor, while the fourth guest bedroom and adjacent TV lounge are found on the lower floor. A second set of stairs from the dining area leads down to the kitchen.

Amanzi’s generous living spaces were built for entertaining. An open-plan living and dining room with a fully equipped granite bar, study desk and a TV / Bose entertainment system take up most of the villa’s middle level. Elegant sofas are orientated to optimise sea views to the front and the waterfall feature to the rear. A dining table for 10 is set at the far end of the space, where stairs lead down to the kitchen.

The huge, bright kitchen is fitted with modern appliances and a set of floor-to-ceiling windows fold open to welcome the sea breezes. This makes a lovely spot for breakfast and to observe the chef at work.

On the villa’s lower floor is a TV lounge (adjacent to a bedroom suite) that opens to a sea-view balcony and walled garden, while another room on the top floor serves as a nanny’s bedroom or playroom.

With outdoor speakers and plenty of space to unwind, Villa Amanzi’s pool terrace, 15m infinity pool and barbecue sala – all with stunning views over Kata Noi beach – make an exceptional hub for fun and relaxation. The fan-cooled sala lies just a few steps up from pool level and is perfect for sunset cocktails and evening grills, with a professional barbecue, dining table for 10, pool table and comfy sofas.

More sofas are strewn along the shaded terrace that runs the length of the pool, while an open spa room and sauna face the sala.

Amanzi’s six air-conditioned bedrooms offer flexibility and comfort to groups and family guests. All have ensuite bathrooms, and all but one come with sea views. The two master suites and one guest bedroom have TV entertainment systems, while the lower-floor guest bedroom has a well-equipped TV lounge attached.
