Villa Katrani, Taling Ngam, Koh Samui, Thailand Vacation Rental

 

Spectacularly secluded with stunning cliffside vistas of Koh Samui’s beautiful blue waters and natural beauty; the relaxing 7-bedroom retreat of Villa Katrani promises all the incredible features of a luxury resort within a private villa setting. Looking out over white sands close to the charming fishing village of Thong Krut, full villa staff and a personal chef create a comfortably superior holiday. Relaxation and an exhilarating villa vacation experience is inspired day and night with an in-villa spa with sauna and in-villa nightclub with a bar and DJ booth.

Spacious rooms with high ceilings, timber floors and green, tranquil spaces create glamorous open terraces for sun worshippers, leafy terraces and shaded cloisters with hidden nooks for quiet moments sitting alone contemplating the wonder of the world. Villa Katrani exudes soothing sophistication with its stylish Moroccan design touches and Eastern minimalist décor, while the striking, contemporary art injects a sense of daring adventure. Elephant trunk waterfalls and water features give Villa Katrani a lost-in-time, secret-garden ambience. A rock-pool style bath and ornately carved four-poster bed in the main bedroom add finishing touches to the villa’s sense of style.

Lounge by the infinity pool bordered by burnt orange ottoman sun beds with a poolside lawn and sala, until exquisite sunsets are revealed. Sink into the cool interior of the lounge looking out to the pool, with four luxuriously comfortable sofas. Dine indoors or al fresco, with a main dining table surrounded by greenery and full-length window doors leading out to a patio with a mesmerizing ocean view; a real sense of jungle safari eating as the indoors extends outdoors.

Entertainment, adventure and activity await with a games room and media room, as well as and an outdoor tennis court and fitness room, this holiday home promises a perfectly balanced, unforgettable luxury villa stay.

Villa Katrani expertly fuses the best of a private villa and a luxury resort. This beautiful hilltop property offers a sophisticated, Zen-like atmosphere. Located in Taling Ngam, a natural haven, the vacation rental offers spectacular views of Koh Samui’s turquoise waters and white sand beach from every angle. From the tennis court with its dreamlike view of the island and sea, to the almost interminable ceilings in the great room – get the holiday you deserve!

The grounds feature a long swimming pool great for doing laps. Or relax in the villa’s outdoor garden space. A lounge area, an alfresco dining area and a barbeque pit offer the setting for memorable moments. Or just forget about it all in one of several daybeds facing the sea. Guests can also celebrate evenings in the villa’s own nightclub, which features a bar that seats ten and a lounge area for eighteen. With a state-of-the-art surround sound system, a fun lighting system and a DJ booth, make it a private party!

This villa also features a fully-equipped gym, a spa with two massage beds and a steam room. Other features include a media room and a game room with a billiard table. The dramatic great room with two story ceilings and rich textures of wood and leather is no doubt the social heart of this villa. Book it for a family holiday or a wedding!

The master bedroom is an impressive space with high ceilings, magnificent ocean views, a king-size four-poster bed, a walk-in closet, an en-suite bathroom with rain shower and vanity and a rock-pool style bath. Each of the bedrooms feature either an ocean or garden view with timber flooring and minimalist decor. Air conditioning, flat screen TVs and a desk space are other available features.

Health aficionados will appreciate a visit to the Marga Yoga center for a realigning and meditative experience. You can also try your chances at Top Cats Fresh Water Fishing to hook a big one to take back home for the whole family. Since the island is so small, it’s also a reasonable drive to the Santiburi Golf Course, a key stop on the PGA tour. Whether discovering the treasures of Koh Samui or just sipping a cool drink at home, your visit to Villa Katrani will truly be one for the books!
