Lassana Kanda, Galle, Sri Lanka Vacation Rental

 

Lassana Kanda meaning Beautiful Mountain is a 7 bedroom luxury villa located a few km from the historic southern capital of Galle and tropical palm fringed beaches. The Chef, who speaks fluent English, is one of the best in the area and has trained and worked in Australia and UK so prepare for feasting. He is supported by a team of professional staff who have at their heart the desire to make your stay as wonderful as possible.

Perched on a high hill with south westerly views down to the ‘rice paddy cultivated’ valley floor, Lassana Kanda is extremely private. The main house has 4 bedrooms (3 dbls and 1 twin) upstairs, all ensuite and air con. Two of the doubles lead out to a balcony that overlooks the pool and distant tropical canopy. Downstairs there is an open plan sitting and dining area; fully equipped kitchens and an air conditioned study / TV room as well as guest bathroom.

The sitting area has floor to ceiling timber windows that completely fold back to reveal the 20m pool and covered pagola with dining table and built in sofas. One side of the pool has an infinity edge.

Underneath the pool is constructed further accommodation and consists of an ensuite family bedroom with king size double and twin beds; a fully equipped gym; a TV/DVD and games room with table tennis table; and a massage room. All these rooms look out through french style windows onto a landscaped lawn.

There is also a Garden House with two ensuite bedrooms (double and a twin) as well as private seating area.
