Ivory House, Galle, Sri Lanka Vacation Rental

 

Ivory House is located a few km from Galle and set elevated overlooking rice paddy fields.

It is a blend of Dutch colonialism and modern living and has 4 dbl bedroom suites.

Ivory House is a villa that takes largely its inspiration from Dutch colonialism era, infused with the modern, simple and contemporary aesthetic. This villa is located in Galle and it was designed so much so that we could view the stunning rice paddy fields with the least obstruction.

This villa has 4 double bedroom suites consisting of two master suites and two guest doubles. The two guest doubles has bathroom with outdoor shower, making a mundane activity for once very special and unique.

Ivory House is located at Habaraduwa, a few kilometers from Galle and it has a generous 20 meters infinity pool, suitable for those wanting to burn some calories by swimming for a few laps. The pool is also comfortable enough for those who just want to float around.
