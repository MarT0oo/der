The Gap, Watsons Bay, Sydney, Australia Vacation Rental

 

This property boasts 270 degree uninterrupted views extending from the city and Harbour Bridge through to and beyond North Head. The Gap is a perfect place to view the New Years Eve fireworks and the spectacular start to the Sydney to Hobart yacht race.

Comprising interconnected kitchen, living, dining the entry level is a social hub. The lowered sitting area is super spacious with a comfortable plush lounge and an outdoor terrace that enhances space and light within the room.

The kitchen design with stainless steel bench tops, Miele appliance and large marble island bench/breakfast bar reflects the homes ability to cater for all occasions. From small family gatherings to large dinners, the contemporary dining table seats up to ten guests.

Retractable sliding glass doors connect all living spaces with the large grassy backyard, which features a gas heated swimming pool, sun drenched terrace, outdoor BBQ and the perfect setting for alfresco entertaining. Also on this level is a guest bedroom with ensuite, which doubles as a home office. Beside this room is where children can spend hours playing Nintendo Wii, Apple TV and Foxtel on the large flat screen TV.

A highlight of this home is the unique interiors by Zahra Designs which complements the modern architecture, constructed by the award-winning Mil Constructions.

Upstairs are three eye-catching bedrooms, the first bedroom has a king size bed and ensuite. The remaining two bedrooms have king single beds and a shared bathroom. These two rooms feature an adjoined balcony. The master bedroom occupies the third level of the home with views from city to ocean, perfect for watching sunrise over the sea and sunsets over the city. The office on the entry level can be turned into a 5th bedroom.
