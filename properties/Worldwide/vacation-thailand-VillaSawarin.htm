Villa Sawarin, Cape Yamu, Phuket, Thailand Vacation Rental

 

Big, bold and beautiful, Villa Sawarin glitters brightly from its hilltop position among the flame trees at exclusive Cape Yamu on Phuket’s east coast. This palace of peace and tranquility, designed by Philippe Starck and Jean-Michel Gathy, blends modern grandeur with the charm of alfresco Thai living, providing eight luxurious bedrooms, huge gardens sweeping down to the beach, and superb guest facilities for those looking to reconnect and relax.

Only 20 minutes from Phuket International airport, Villa Sawarin is within striking distance of some of Asia’s best golf courses and the Royal Phuket Marina, and is less than 30 minutes’ drive from the shops and restaurants of Phuket Town. Villa guests also have access to the dining, sports and wellness facilities of the award-winning five-star resort hotel, Point Yamu by COMO, including the hotel’s private beach club on nearby Koh Naka Yai. But, with a stunning 24-metre infinity pool, smart outdoor dining sala and sitting area, spa room, beachside boathouse with yoga deck, games room with home theatre, well equipped gymnasium and private putting green, Villa Sawarin provides a dream escape where guests are unlikely to feel much need to venture further afield.

Five star service awaits at Villa Sawarin, whose staff includes a gourmet chef, a knowledgeable concierge, villa manager, 24-hour security and a team of housekeepers
