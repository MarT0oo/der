Villa Samira, Kamala Beach, west-coast Phuket, Thailand Vacation Rental

 

From the moment you step into its grand vaulted entrance, Villa Samira embraces you with modern elegance and tropical splendour. With glorious sea and sunset views, this six-bedroom retreat found in a secured estate near Kamala Beach is perfect for entertaining or simply unwinding in plush and peaceful surrounds.

Samira, which means ‘cool breeze’ in Sanskrit, is stylishly designed in mostly blue, turquoise and white tones inspired by the owner’s love of the sea. Families and groups of friends have plenty of space to gather and celebrate, with Samira’s inviting aquamarine-tiled 19-metre pool and jacuzzi at the heart of the villa surrounded by a wide terrace incorporating alfresco dining and lounging areas. Indoor living areas, which include a chic living room, kitchen and dining room, and games room with a pool table, all open to the pool terrace, while the media room is tucked away behind the games room. And those seeking total restoration will want to book some massage treatment time in Samira’s custom built spa room.

Occupying the entire top floor of one of the villa’s two large pavilions, the king-bedded master suite is a romantic escape with a jacuzzi plunge pool overlooking the sea on its private terrace, plus a double bathtub set discreetly to the rear of the terrace. Two ensuite guest bedrooms lie on the top floor of the second pavilion, while three bedrooms on the lower floor offer private bathrooms with showers. Beds in the five guest bedrooms can all be set up in king or twin configurations, offering greater flexibility.

Samira is staffed by a dedicated team including an English speaking villa manager and a skilled Thai chef, available to prepare all meals. With its superb setting and top quality facilities, Samira is a fantastic venue to stage a picture-perfect wedding or special event. Beyond the villa, the best beaches, dining spots and leisure attractions of Phuket including laid-back Kamala Beach and the nightlife of Patong are within easy reach by car.

Villa Samira is set over three levels with a pair of two-storey pavilions flanking the grand entrance hall and a spa room and three guest bedrooms situated below the pool deck.

All the indoor and outdoor living spaces, including the wide pool terrace, games room, media room, living room, dining room and kitchen, are on the villa’s entry level. The large master suite occupies the entire top floor of one pavilion while two king-bedded guest rooms lie on the top floor of the opposite pavilion. All six bedrooms have sea-view balconies.

Flanking the villa’s grand entrance and pool terrace are two pavilions housing Samira’s living areas.

Floor-to-ceiling sliding glass doors offer great views and plenty of natural light. In the larger pavilion is the living room, an elegant space with a fluttery white chandelier and two lounging areas, one facing the front for pool and sea views, another oriented towards a TV entertainment unit. Buddhist-themed paintings, oversized lanterns and soft blue and white tones add to the room’s tranquil atmosphere.

Sliding doors lead to the kitchen and stately dining room, where 12 blue suede chairs frame a solid wood table. The open kitchen, with breakfast bar, wine fridge and modern appliances sits at the back of the room while across the entire front are glass doors that slide open to the terrace, furnished with a small outdoor dining table. The staff kitchen, where the chef prepares divine meals, is tucked away behind the guest kitchen. In the other pavilion is a games room with a pool table, bar and entertainment unit adjacent to the media room, which is equipped with a projector screen and furnished with soft sofas.

Villa Samira’s outdoor living spaces extend from the grand entrance hall, with a decked terrace bordering the pool, and a chic white poly-rattan sofa set for relaxing in the shade. The 19-metre pool and jacuzzi is a prime spot for taking in the sea and sky views. Six sunbeds are set on the raised wooden deck along the pool’s length. In front of the games room, at one end of the pool, is a dining sala for enjoying alfresco meals, while at the other end is a sitting area with a fire pit – the perfect spot for post-swim sunset cocktails.

Master suite this romantic retreat boasts a huge wraparound balcony with a private jacuzzi plunge pool, sunbeds, a reflecting pool, double bathtub and outdoor shower. This bright, king-bedded room occupying the entire top floor of a pavilion is smartly furnished with an aquamarine loveseat and ottoman. Hidden behind the room’s study desk is a walk-in wardrobe and the luxurious ensuite bathroom where the rain shower’s glass enclosure gives it an outdoor feel.

Two large king-bedded bedrooms share the top floor of a pavilion, each with fabulous sea views, private terraces, wardrobes and lavish ensuite bathrooms with double bathtubs. One of the bedrooms has a side terrace with a reflecting pool and outdoor shower.

The three lower-floor guest bedrooms each feature king-sized beds, balconies and ensuite bathrooms with rain showers. Spacious, bright and elegant, these rooms will appeal to kids and adults alike.

All five guest bedrooms are furnished with king-size beds that convert to twin beds if preferred, giving great flexibility for couples, families and friends.
