Bondi Boheme, Bondi Beach, Sydney, Australia Vacation Rental

 

This newly finished modern apartment block is perfect whether you’re looking for last minute holidays or long term stays. Adjoining the Adina Hotel, you can use their pool facilities (including a fantastic 25m lap pool and gymnasium) whenever you feel like a change from heading down to the iconic Bondi Beach. The entry to these Sydney apartments entry is a dark sleek foyer, where you can admire the cool artwork before taking the lift up to your floor – and don’t forget to leave your keys in your personal key locker here when you hit the beach!

As soon as you enter the beautifully appointed living area (with a stunning Provst chair!), you’ll realise that this room forms the heart of this home, with floor to ceiling glass surrounding you. Step across the wide oak floorboards and open one of the doors onto the balcony, adorned with an abundance of potted green plants and with a concrete outdoor table setting overlooking the crystal waters of Bondi Beach. Luckily you don’t have to go too far to grab a glass of something cold to enjoy while making the most of the view, because the sleek, state of the art chic kitchen is just a few steps away as you head back inside, and comes with a marble island benchtop, Miele appliances and wine fridge.

Further inside you’ll find the three bedrooms, including the main with huge ensuite with bath and its own WIR. Each bedroom opens onto a sun-dappled wraparound balcony, and the glorious views of blue skies and even bluer waters are complemented by the classic and elegant décor in each bedroom, crisp white walls accented with colourful bedding and carefully chosen artwork. There’s even a powder room, a study nook for catching up on any work you’ve brought with you, and secure basement car space for your vehicle.

About the Area

Perfect for last minute holidays in the sunshine, you’re only 300m from the incredible Bondi Beach and 10km from Circular Quay. As well as being heaven for sunworshippers and water sports enthusiasts (the surf culture here is second to none), it’s paradise for foodies. With Bills restaurant, China Diner, Gelato Messina and Harris Farm Market all on your doorstep, you’ll struggle to decide where to try next! For a perfect coffee, any time of the day, you must visit the Atlas Kiosk, and what would be the best restaurant in the area? We hear it’s a toss-up between Icebergs and Harry de Wheels Bondi.
