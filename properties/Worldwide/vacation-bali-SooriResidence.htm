Soori Residence, Tabanan, Indonesia Vacation Rental

 

The Soori Residence features a generous layout, with spacious living areas and six en-suite bedrooms spread over two levels. A haven of privacy, this masterfully designed villa opens up to unobstructed views of the Indian Ocean, making for the ultimate Bali residence. Several large outdoor decks, pavilions and lawns with barbecue and bar facilities create a perfect backdrop for open-air entertaining. Complete with an infinity pool, gourmet kitchen, private gym and spa therapy room, this villa is perfect for an undisturbed and elegant getaway.

Overlooking the unending Indian Ocean and with the eternal Mount Batukaru in the backdrop, The Soori Residence is a dream vacation villa in Bali. Much like the legendary Indonesian island itself, The Soori Residence is highly artful both in its sleek architecture and stylish decor that effortlessly melds with the tropical gardens of the estate. The villa’s five lavish suites accommodate groups up to ten, each of whom will be captivated by the estate’s private and privileged beachfront.

The Soori Residence has countless amenities, everything a traveling guest would want to have while on their tropical dream vacation. The zero-edge swimming pool produces a great optical effect of seemingly merging with the ocean and is conveniently located next to the inviting alfresco dining area, grill and wet bar which are all just screaming for a party. Indoors, guests are encouraged to take full advantage of the cutting-edge Apple TV and Bose sound system. An appointed butler, housekeeper and 24-hour room service will keep guests happy as proverbial clams.

The villa’s open air pavilions and decor not only compliment each other, but also the lush tropical gardens. The rooms feature designer light fixtures and a largely black-and-white color scheme with splashes of eye-grabbing ochre and chestnut brown. The interior dining space features a long and elegant rustic table, next to the gourmet kitchen.

Three of home’s five en-suite bedrooms have been equipped with plush king size beds, while the remaining two rooms have two comfortable twin size beds. Each room also has a sound system hooked up to iPod docks and balconies with dazzling ocean views. Additionally, the master suite boasts an alluring terrazzo bath tub and a large walk-in closet.

The Soori Residence is only a five minute drive from Pan Pacific Resort, offering excellent tennis and golf facilities. Other encouraged activities include a climb up Mount Batukaru, which should be reserved for experienced hikers, through the island’s largest wetland rainforest. Sangeh Monkey Forest should also be on everyone’s list, not just for a chance to see the monkeys, but also to see the 17th century Bukit Sari Temple. Experience peace of mind and tropical bliss like never before in Bali!

Located on Bali’s south-western coast, Soori Bali is nestled within the picturesque Tabanan Regency, where volcanic mountains loom above verdant rice terraces, and striking black-sand beaches delve gently into the Indian Ocean.

Soori Bali lies 25 minutes away from the majestic Tanah Lot Temple, one of Bali's most sacred and venerated temples. The resort can be reached in a little over an hour from Ngurah Rai International Airport.
