Seafields
3 Studios | 1 Cottage | 1 Suite (all with ensuite baths)                       
Aukland, North Island, New Zealand
      Please inquire for rates

Seafields luxury coastal accommodation can accommodate up to twelve guests in five separate suites. It is the ideal bed and breakfast location to recuperate after a long journey or just take a break from a hectic city schedule.  It also offers the perfect backdrop for your intimate wedding, special occasion celebration or small conference.

Every attention to detail has been made to ensure your complete comfort. Guests can relax and unwind in complete seclusion, surrounded by antiques, oriental rugs and original works of art, while enjoying complimentary port, tea and coffee and home baking.

Seafields five luxurious suites (The Pavilion, Garden Studios (2), Seaview Suite and The Cottage) are all beautifully appointed to the highest standards with magnificent sea views creating the perfect retreat within a retreat.

Seafields is a semi rural 5-star retreat nestled amongst beautiful landscaped gardens on the shores of the Hauraki Gulf at Whitford, just 20 minutes from Auckland’s International Airport and 30 minutes from Auckland city, by car or by ferry from nearby Pine Harbour Marina, Beachlands.

Escape and unwind with the solitude of an estuary walk, a relaxing soak in the Jacuzzi, indulge in the sauna or enjoy an invigorating massage (by appointment).  For the more energetic, enjoy a myriad of activities from kayaking, sailing, fishing, heli-fishing, skydiving, gliding, polo or perhaps a round of golf at one of New Zealand’s best known golf courses - the choice is unlimited!
