Visiting Victoria

Victoria is a lovely coastal region offering visitors amazing drives up and down its shores.  It’s probably most well known for the scenic routes that reach from The Murray to Gippsland, The Great Ocean Road and the Grampians to name a few.  Most people who travel here love to visit Melbourne, the area offers amazing city sights and sounds an is home to the Melbourne Food and Wine Festival http://www.melbournefoodandwine.com.au/ (largest in the world), The Melbourne Comedy Festival http://www.comedyfestival.com.au/2010/season/, as well as having some amazing theaters www.showbiz.com.au.  No matter whether it’s shopping, dining, or living, Melbourne is the city to do it in.

The Great Open Road is another area well traveled by tourists.  This area boasts amazing dining such as: Athelstane House http://www.athelstane.com.au/, Fisherman’s Pier http://www.fishermenspier.com.au/, and Oakdene Vineyards Restaurant http://www.oakdene.com.au/.  For tours of the area you should try: A day Outdoors Touring http://www.adayoutdoorstouring.com.au/ or Wedgetail Tours http://wedgetailstours.com.au/.
