Pacific Place, Palm Beach, Sydney, Australia Vacation Rental

 

As you set foot on to Pacific Place you’ll feel as if you’re arrived at your own luxurious coastal resort. And nothing says resort living like the lush gardens and the unforgettable huge wooden entry doors that greet you at Pacific Place. When you open those doors there’s no doubt as to why this home is a level of luxury above most others.

The expansive largely white living areas are positioned to take full advantage of the views with large bi-folding doors looking out across Palm Beach and the Pacific Ocean. A long timber table is perfectly placed to take in the views while the contemporary kitchen also sits in the open plan area – you can even cook with a view!

As you’d expect, the remaining living rooms also provide plenty of room to relax with a soft white palette, minimalist styling and high-quality practical furniture choices. While the bedrooms also come with a view! The stunning master suite is conveniently located on a separate floor while the remaining three bedrooms each have an ensuite, ensuring complete privacy for all guests.

Those magnificent ocean views are a feature outside too where you’ll find it hard to resist taking a dip in the infinity pool or sipping a drink pool-side on the timber deck. There’s even room for the kids to burn some energy on the beautifully manicured lawns and also back inside there’s a children’s retreat with TV room. Indeed, Pacific Place makes it so easy to actually enjoy an ultra-luxurious family holiday.

About the Area

Pacific Place is ideally located to discover the best of Palm Beach. You’re just a few minutes drive to the beach and a short walk to the Palm Beach ferry wharf. There’s a variety of outdoor activities close by including kayaking and hiking, while Palm Beach golf course is only five minutes away. You can choose from many cafes and restaurants - if you’re after a meal with a view Dunes Restaurant is a good place to start. If you’d prefer to venture into the city or discover more of the northern beaches, public transport is available.
