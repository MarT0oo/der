Baan Banburee, Samui Beach Village, Koh Samui, Thailand Vacation Rental

 

Baan Banburee is a spacious 4 bedroom, three-storey luxury villa that will sleep up to 8 adults plus 2 children.

The villa is stunningly decorated to reflect Thai/Balinese architecture and offers a peaceful and luxurious setting for your tropical island holiday. There are stunning views of the sea, beach and hills from the second floor roof terrace.

With all modern conveniences such as Wi Fi, TV/DVD with satellite channels, ceiling fans, air-conditioning, a fully fitted western kitchen and dining room - this villa provides the perfect home away from home in a tropical paradise.

Surrounding by stunning tropical flora, privacy is first and foremost so you can lounge around your own swimming pool on sun-loungers and parasols for those who seek some shade.

With the beach just seconds from your doorstep, this villa is perfect for friends and families for a truly relaxing island escape.

We pride ourselves on the standard of comfort we provide throughout the villa, from air conditioning, mosquito-net doors for enjoying the sea breeze and fans found in all rooms through to the quality of service assured by our villa manageress and staff.

Samui Beach Village also offers a unique hotel-style service for our guests. A team of 40 staff is on hand to provide the top quality service you would expect to find in a luxury 5 star resort. Unlike most villa rentals where the service is limited, we pride ourselves on giving our guests the best holiday experience possible. Samui Beach Village is a superb location for people looking to lose themselves in the beauty of Koh Samui's gorgeous beaches and lush tropical landscape. The village - which is set right by the beachside - is a small luxury living development close to the main tourist areas of Samui.

It adjoins an unspoiled stretch of sandy beach in the relatively undeveloped south-eastern coast, and has been built amid a profusion of coconut palms and tropical landscaped gardens. Two neighbouring islands - Koh Tan and Koh Mudsum - well-known for their fantastic fishing and snorkeling, are only a short, 20-minute boat ride away.

It is only ten minutes from Lamai Beach, 30 minutes from Chaweng and Samui's top golf course, and 35 minutes from the airport, so you are never far from the action. But while there is easy access to shopping, restaurants, nightlife and other activities, back in Samui Beach Village you will feel you are on your own tropical island paradise.
