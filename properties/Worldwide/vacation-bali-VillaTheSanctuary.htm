Villa The Sanctuary, Bali, Indonesia Vacation Rental

 

Villa The Sanctuary Bali is designed and built with an intimate resort feeling and spread out over 30.000 m2 beautiful land overlooking paradise. It is a wonderful retreat and everybody looking for an exclusive holiday villa can stop looking!

Villa The Sanctuary Bali, open to magnificent views and a beautiful private tropical gardens, is the essence of luxury tropical living. Villa The Sanctuary Bali is finished with the finest materials combined with exclusive furnishing and contemporary art.

Loyal Balinese staff provide a unique and personal service to pamper you in every way!

Villa The Sanctuary Bali has a spectacular location bordered by jungle, trees, rice fields, tropical gardens and impressive river. It is the only villa in Bali with unobstructed infinity views over Bali’s beautiful nature. Once you enter the villa, you enter a new world: a paradise of your own!

Villa The sanctuary Bali is hidden away in the authentic Balinese village Cepaka. Cepaka is part of Canggu which is considered to be the high end living area of Bali! Canggu beach, Pererenan beach, Seseh beach and Ecko Beach are all known as famous surfing beaches and renowned for their legendary sunsets. They are only five kilometer away from the villa so within very easy reach. We provide free bicycles to ride to the beach…

A swim in our pool, a surf at the beach, a treatment in our Spa, a workout in our Gym, a magical bike ride in the rural village or a game of golf are all a matter of steps away!

It is 5 minutes to the popular Greg Norman designed “Bali Nirwana Golf Course” (recently voted as one of the top ten golf courses in the world): www.nirwanabaligolf.com/en/golf and 5 minutes to the “Pererenan Horse Riding Centre”: www.kudapstables.com

Villa The Sanctuary Bali offers total privacy and exclusivity, yet is still very close to the most wanted area of Bali: Seminyak. Seminyak is 25 minutes away with all its trendy shops, chic boutiques, world-class restaurants, vibrant bars and funky beach clubs. The most famous and wanted shopping and nightlife area of Bali is very close by! Your driver brings you to venues such as Ku De Ta, Potato Head, W Hotel, Sarong, Metis, Sardines, Hu’u Bar, The Living Room and La Lucciola in less than 25 minutes.

The airport is 40 minutes away depending on traffic
