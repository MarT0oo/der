Designed by renowned architect Michael Wyatt and constructed with great sensitivity to the surrounding natural environment, Coronet Peak Mountain Retreat was completed in 2009.  The property sits in more than 200 acres of rolling parkland, has stunning 360 degree views and offers kilometers of walking trails for the outdoor enthusiast.


Winner of the 2010 House of the Year competition for the Southern Region, the home is open and pacious, sleeping up to 10 guests in a relaxed ambience. The house sleeps 8 guests in supreme comfort with three king size double beds and two single beds in four large bedrooms, each with en-suite.
