Baan Tongsai, Phuket, Thailand Vacation Rental

 

Set within the exclusive Royal Phuket Marina, 'the' gateway to Phuket's paradise white sand islands Winner of many awards for style, luxury and design, and voted 'the best marina in Asia', Baan Tongsai luxury apartment is ideally situated within the exclusive Royal Phuket Marina. This exquisite location ensures privacy and luxury, whilst affording convenient access to 5-star facilities, Phuket town's incredible restaurants, trendy bars and attractions, and the breath-taking stretches of white sand beaches on the secluded islands within Phang Nga Bay.

Luxury, elegance, comfort and convenience Newly built and meticulously furnished to a very high standard, no expense has been spared to ensure the maximum luxury and comfort for guests. A stunning 20m infinity pool, gleaming teak wood floors, polished marble bathrooms and captivating sea/marina views await you. This ultra-luxurious property offers three spacious and luxurious bedrooms all boasting outstanding views over the water. A huge 9m x 6m living room with enormous windows opens out onto two balconies – perfect for enjoying sunset cocktails overlooking the sea, marina or feature pool.

Every 5 star amenity you could wish for The Royal Phuket Marina has so many facilities, you may never leave the resort. To list a few there is a private beach, plus several islands and beaches just 10 minutes by boat taxi, several world-class restaurants, a luxury spa, a sports bar, cafes, shops and designer boutiques, tour agencies, tennis courts and a fully equipped gym.

Three luxurious bathrooms and three luxurious bedrooms, all with spectacular views All three bedrooms are large, luxurious, and fully-equipped with both air conditioning and silent running ceiling fans, 32" HD TV's with 200+ international satellite channels, dressing tables and comfortable seating areas or sofas. Memory-foam topped mattresses and the finest Egyptian cotton linens ensure a wonderful night's rest.

All three decadent en-suite bathrooms are spacious, filled with natural light and beautifully finished in gleaming marble with hand-carved solid marble sinks, large walk-in showers and floor-to ceiling shower glass walls. The master bedroom's en-suite bathroom also features a twin shower, and separate free standing double-ended twin person Jacuzzi bath.

Baan Tongsai luxury apartment is just the beginning of your luxury holiday experience Whether your holiday at this stunning property is a family break or a romantic getaway, our location specialists and in-country concierge team will ensure that your every wish is catered for. Special requests for everything from private chefs to VIP reservations, private yacht charters or custom pre-stocking of the apartment can be taken care of at your request, to ensure your holiday starts, the moment you arrive.
