Manly Cove, Fairlight, Sydney, Australia Vacation Rental

 

If you’re looking for the best in boutique stays Sydney for your luxury holidays or short breaks, this stunning home will give you and your party all of the home comforts you could possibly wish for.

With three spacious bedrooms all decorated with high end touches, you’ll feel like you’re in your very own five star hotel when you retire for the night. The master suite, with its superior king size bed and stylish ensuite complete with shower and bath – this and the main bathroom mean that nobody will have to queue for their early morning shower, or soak in the tub!

If you like to use technology as well as making your own entertainment, you’ll be spoiled for choice at Manly Cove. As well as the Foxtel and Wi-Fi available for you, there are three televisions (so no need to fight over the remote!), as well as a docking station for your own music, Bluetooth speakers and a fantastic DVD collection for cosy nights in.

If you or one of your party is a keen cook, the two fully equipped kitchens are sure to delight you, and you’ll love the huge range of options for where to eat. You could have a morning pastry on the balcony, complete with stunning water views, a formal meal at the dining table or enjoy a BBQ in the manicured gardens with both open air and undercover areas so you can choose between catching some sun and cooling off in the shade.

You’ll have no problems with access or getting around, because this wheelchair accessible property offers you both garage parking and on-street parking, so if you do decide to venture further afield, you can bring your own transport.

About the Area

Choosing a home for rent in Fairlight lets you enjoy the best of both worlds – a thriving, friendly community with ocean views but only a short distance from buzzing city life. Hop on the ferry at Manly and enjoy a night at the Opera House followed by dinner at Aria or treat the kids to a day at Taronga Zoo without jumping in the car! With such a short walk to Manly there is so much sightseeing, shopping, restaurants and health/beauty treats on offer, it’s the perfect place to spend luxury holidays.

If you’re looking for an active holiday, there is a fitness centre and gym, golf, tennis, water sports, swimming, surfing and fishing. Live like the locals by dining out at the fantastic Garfish Manly, or grabbing a coffee at Moorish Balgowlah, perfect for people watching on a lazy Sunday morning.
