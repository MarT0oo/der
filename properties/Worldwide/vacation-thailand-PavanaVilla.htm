Pavana Villa, Koh Samui, Thailand Vacation Rental

 

The undeniably beautiful Pavana Villa offers a luxurious beachfront locale, within an exclusive bay, on Koh Samui’s southern coastline. The cutting edge architecture with impeccable interior design offers a contemporary Balinese feel, in a private beachside retreat, perfect for luxury travellers.

Offering far reaching views across to neighbouring islands of Koh Tan and breathtaking sunset vistas over the mainland, Pavana Villa is located in an exceptional setting. The open plan design with seamless divisions makes the most of it’s pristine beachfront location and tropical climate. The huge sense of space is enhanced by the vaulted ceilings and oversized doors and windows which feature throughout the expansive villa and it’s free flowing design. Six spacious bedroom sanctuaries with an additional bespoke kids bedroom/playroom makes this an ideal setting for families and friends.

With 20m of panoramic beach frontage and an impressive 18m long infinity edged pool flanked by the shores of Laem Sor and its turquoise waters, a cooling dip has never felt so refreshing. Large decks with sun-traps and shades with cooling fans, create multi dimensional spaces engulfed in breathtaking island canvases. The shaded kids pool is a unique feature and ideally located close to the beach with surrounding decks for easy supervision.

Guests of this luxury destination will enjoy first class service and amenities with a discreet yet attentive feel for a villa experience that will always be remembered.

Family haven ...

A luxurious, intimate home-away-from-home feel, Pavana Villa is an incredible family haven offering island style freedom and space, without compromising on luxury. Families can enjoy all the benefits of this fully staffed villa with high-end services and a dedication to ensure all family members have one of the most memorable holidays.

The secure nature of the villa, the expansive spaces and its beachfront location allow children to thrive in its vast tropical charm. Benefitting from the villa being all on one level and unfolding onto the shallow golden sands beach, enables kids to enjoy the effortless movement between rooms and the ability for parents to feel at ease, knowing their children are close by.

The 6.9m x 2.7 m shallower children’s pool with thoughtful sun shade is a great kids pool and the large surrounding sundeck also offering a shady section is ideal for parents and nannies to keep a closer eye on the smaller ones of the group. The larger impressive 18m pool overlooking the azure shores of Laem Sor beach is a great lap and lounge pool for adults and older children.

The quiet secluded beach with its azure sea is a great playground for all. The initial shallow nature of the coastline is a welcome respite for parents of smaller children. Wading out to deeper shores, snorkeling off of the coral reef can be a colorful discovery, or perhaps paddle boarding or canoeing is something that may entice other guests to explore this stunning bay.

The attentive service team is an essential ingredient in this successful villa. The Villa Manager offers a wealth of experience and knowledge in assisting guests with their holiday experience. The experienced Chef is there to tempt the discerning travellers with mouthwatering menus, delicious culinary treats and even special tailored meals for children. With a true understanding that holidays are something for all, Pavana Villa offers an excellent nanny service for that extra pair of hands and eyes.

Sleep in…

Six en-suite bedroom suites are designed in separate bedroom pavilions. The master poolside pavilion shares views of the infinity pool and bay beyond. The sheer size of the master suite offers a spacious king sized bed and large sofa seating area with 40” TV screen. The vaulted ceilings, clean lines and stunning design exude an impressive and fresh style. A spacious contemporary grey terrazzo bathroom with double lovers rain shower and lovers bath tub, opens up to its own private back garden. The outside chill zone with sofa seating provides a private deck for guests to enjoy some personal down time.

For peace of mind when travelling with children, one of the bedroom pavilions is adjoining door to the 6th bedroom for younger guests. Each bedroom enjoys this incredibly spacious feel with sumptuous bedding, and inside and outside chill zones, impeccably designed for relaxation. Creating a serene and relaxing atmosphere with contemporary beach style.

The children room is aptly located furthest away from the poolside, providing extra safety for young ones. Offering the children a dream escape of their own, the playroom has been designed as a fun kid’s dormitory with built-in bunk beds that can house up to 4 children. It has a spacious play area with a cozy snug filled with cushions, perfect for down time whilst watching movies and playing games.

Should guests choose to bring nannies with them or use the villa's nanny service, an additional Staff bedroom with kitchen and dining area adjacent to the children’s room is available.

Indulge in ...

Upon arrival, the team of Pavana Villa has but one mission statement in mind; guests must relax, enjoy and experience a truly unforgettable holiday. With this ethos the Pavana Villa team works behind the scenes adding all the extra hidden touches to make the holiday run as smoothly and effortlessly as possible.

Exquisite food is prepared each day by a dedicated chef, and attentive yet discreet service from your Villa Manager and team. Using local ingredients, the Chef can tailor menus to each guests taste. Breakfast includes homemade mueslis and freshly squeezed juices, and lunch can be a variety of colorful salads, sizzling fish and barbequed meats. In the evenings, delicious courses can be served with varying levels of spice and children can enjoy specially tailored meals to suit every type of appetite.

The onsite nanny service is here to be enjoyed should guests need that little bit of extra time for themselves, with once again discreet and attentive staff. The in-villa spa service is an unforgettable indulgence. Guests wishing to enjoy the extensive menu can choose from the differing deck areas, the beach or the privacy of their own bedroom sanctuaries, to enjoy a wonderful holiday treatment. The driver is at hand should guests wish to head out and explore the beautiful isle of Koh Samui or perhaps even to simply head to a supermarket for some home from home provisions. Having an experienced driver who knows the island extensively makes getting around that much easier.

The garden sparkles with the simple elegance of exotic beachside living. Live orchids are attached to each palm tree as indigenous plantings flourish in this lush tropical heaven. The scents of the widely planted jasmine and frangipani trees fill the warm evening breezes as kaleidoscopic colors streak the sunset sky.
