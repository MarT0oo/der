Baan Fah Sai, Samui Beach Village, Koh Samui, Thailand Vacation Rental

 

Baan Fah Sai is a large luxury 3 bedroom villa with its own private swimming pool, designed in tropical style and adorned by Thai finishings; this Samui villa offers guests a feeling of lightness and comfort.

You enter the luxury villa through a beautiful entrance into the large main living area. The open plan living room has sliding doors and mosquito nets with cooling ceramic floor tiles with all areas of the villa having soft down lighting, up lighting and ceiling fans.

A teak wood kitchen, handmade by Samui craftsmen, occupies one corner of the living room with a breakfast bar surround to make even food preparation a social event. This has been designed with all European features including dishwasher, double sink, hob, oven and cooker hood – perfect for those who like to entertain.

Flowing to the right of the living room (with flat screen TV & DVD, as well as high speed Wi Fi throughout) is a unique bench-style dining area with seating for 6. The dining area overlooks an outdoor wooden-decked space through double sliding doors, with a further table and seating surrounded by lush tropical foliage which is perfect for relaxing, dining or unwinding in the shade.

Forming part of the dining room’s charm are large exquisite thatched sliding doors that reveal a sizeable bedroom with a queen size bed and stunning decorations including a whimsical white flowing canopy that drapes over the bed. Yet another sliding glass door forms part of this room, allowing for a remarkable view of and access to the pristine pool. With its own private en-suite complete with a large rain shower head, this en-suite doubles as a guest bathroom for the pool through a dual-door system. A great bedroom if you seek complete privacy for guests or fantastic for children to have their own space.

Upstairs you find the stunning master bedroom, complete with its own walk in wardrobe that leads to a spacious and stylish bathroom with a large bath. The luxurious king size bed sits underneath an exquisite piece of Thai art and ornate hanging lights, which grace each side of the bed. The bedroom extends to outside with a large wooden balcony where you can sit and unwind, with the breeze of the ocean passing by.

A second bedroom of the luxury villa sits adjacent to the master bedroom, just past a desk area (for when you need to write those postcards home!) which comprises of two single beds and more stunning Thai artwork. A very large and spacious room, this bedroom is ideal for friends and children. Like the master bedroom, it contains a walk in wardrobe, large en-suite and expansive balcony area. With beautiful polished wood floors, vaulted wooden ceilings and subtle ambient ceiling lighting – the upstairs bedrooms are a happy combination of traditional Thai culture and 21st century living.

Fah Sai is the ideal luxury Koh Samui villa for a family or group of friends with ample sun-decking and sun-loungers to enjoy the sun or shade, and the large sala gives a further outside eating area or just a place to relax. The landscaped gardens provide privacy and a lush environment to swim in the freeform swimming pool or take a 30 second stroll down the lush coconut lined lane to a stunning stretch of white sandy beach. Paradise!



































Samui Beach Village also offers a unique hotel-style service for our guests. A team of 40 staff is on hand to provide the top quality service you would expect to find in a luxury 5 star resort. Unlike most villa rentals where the service is limited, we pride ourselves on giving our guests the best holiday experience possible. Samui Beach Village is a superb location for people looking to lose themselves in the beauty of Koh Samui's gorgeous beaches and lush tropical landscape. The village - which is set right by the beachside - is a small luxury living development close to the main tourist areas of Samui.

It adjoins an unspoiled stretch of sandy beach in the relatively undeveloped south-eastern coast, and has been built amid a profusion of coconut palms and tropical landscaped gardens. Two neighbouring islands - Koh Tan and Koh Mudsum - well-known for their fantastic fishing and snorkeling, are only a short, 20-minute boat ride away.

It is only ten minutes from Lamai Beach, 30 minutes from Chaweng and Samui's top golf course, and 35 minutes from the airport, so you are never far from the action. But while there is easy access to shopping, restaurants, nightlife and other activities, back in Samui Beach Village you will feel you are on your own tropical island paradise.
