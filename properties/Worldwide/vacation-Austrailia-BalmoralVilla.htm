Balmoral Villa, Mosman, Sydney, Australia Vacation Rental

 

A superb setting with magical views this large three level, five bedroom much loved family home is the ideal holiday destination.

The magnificent vista and intimate connection with Middle Harbour out through the Sydney Heads to the ocean beyond makes this home truly exceptional. Combined with a spectacular architectural home that was created to maximise the enjoyment of the water and panoramic views guests have a sublime sanctuary.

The grand design of the home functions extremely well with multiple breakout spaces, a home gym, ample outdoor BBQ areas, four light filled bedrooms on the lower level, formal living & dining area on the main level and a day to day living and dining area on the main level. Merging with these spaces is the superbly appointed marble kitchen on the main level with breakfast bench, Miele appliances including a gas cooktop, ovens and fridges.

The serene master retreat occupies the top level of the home boasting a large outdoor terrace, Jacuzzi, en-suite and home office.

Brimming with brilliant natural light, this home offers the perfect balance of convenience and privacy, with easy walking distance to the Mosman shopping strip and restaurants such as Bathers Pavilion, Public Dining Room, Ormeggio and Ripples Chowder Bay (to name a few).

For a lovely family day out; Balmoral Beach is a mere nine minute stroll straight to the golden sand and turquoise waters.

The home has been oriented to create a visual feast where the 180 water views can be enjoyed from every level, including the great breakfast bar for the morning coffee and paper.

The lower level is a fantastic getaway for the kids or separate guest accommodation (including travelling staff).
