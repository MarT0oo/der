Alchemy, Phuket, Thailand Vacation Rental

 

Set on an elevated beachfront land along Phuket’s serene and exclusive luxury estate of Cape Yamu, Villa Alchemy ensures that guests have the best seat in the house to witness the captivating landscape of this stunning peninsula. Its four-floor construction features multiple balconies, each offering enticing views of the rocking waters crashing against the sun-kissed shoreline.

The topmost, open-air balcony provides an excellent terrace for sitting back with a book in hand under the glowing sunset or simply lounging with friends and family beneath the romantic moonlight. A viridescent, well-manicured garden perfectly completes the villa’s breathtaking exterior.

Villa Alchemy is exclusively marketed and managed by The Private World, and is part of its premium collection of luxury villa rentals in Phuket.

Moving inside, the contemporary furnishing and décor embodies graceful elegance. Hints of traditional inspiration is evident in the predominantly modern vibe that concentrates around the main living/dining area, and ripples out across the lower floors, and throughout the rest of the villa. Aesthetic wooden accessories, metal embroidery, and a plethora of chic chandelier designs add to the interior’s brilliance, which is mostly characterized by precious stone and lovely teak floors.

The villa’s 10 ensuite bedrooms sets itself in a league of its own for having the most number of bedrooms out of any villa in the Cape Yamu estate. For days when you feel like enjoying a film or two, you can sit back in the cinema room equipped with a large 3x2m screen for the most immersive viewing experience. The pool table and bar in the entertainment area also adds to the indoor fun. This space also features an adjacent seating area that opens up to the middle infinity pool with a commanding view of the Phang Nga Bay.

In terms of essential amenities for convenient living, Villa Alchemy offers it’s guests everything they could ever want. From fast Wifi Internet, a jacuzzi, to a fully-equipped American kitchen, Villa Alchemy is as complete as they come. There’s even a safety deposit box in every room for your peace of mind and a spacious garage for four cars.

Keeping fit often spirals to the bottom of our minds during vacations; however, this villa ensures it stays right at the top. The well-ventilated indoor gym comes with all you need for your daily workout, offering much in the way of treadmills, dumbbells, and whatever else you prefer in your fitness routine. Villa Alchemy ensures your outdoor amusement isn’t lacking either, offering two big infinity pools for your indulgence, including a safer kid-friendly accompaniment. Its beachfront location provides a relaxing day on the heavenly eastern coast of Phuket, picnicking, or having a seaside bonfire to keep off the cold of dusk.

A dedicated team of 6 staff members including an in-house villa manager and a private chef is available to cater to your very needs. Daily housekeeping, laundry services, car rentals, in-house spa treatments, and guest experience services are also available to deliver personalized services specifically tailored to meet your preferences.

Villa Alchemy plucks wishful thinking into a real-life, unforgettable luxury villa vacation experience like no other.
