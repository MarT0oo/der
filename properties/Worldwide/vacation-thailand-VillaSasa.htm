Villa Sasa, Koh Samui, Surat Thani Province, Thailand Vacation Rental

 

A modern designed villa set in tropical surroundings close to the beach in the village of Ban Rak, with views to the neighbouring island of Koh Phangan. Villa Sasa Samui is an elegant property that is very spacious, and furnished with style and sophistication, creating an environment for peaceful relaxation and rejuvenation.

This villa is a two-storey villa that offers 3 spacious bedrooms, all with en-suite bathrooms. All of the bedrooms are fully air conditioned and furnished with king sized beds. 2 bedrooms also have satellite TVs and DVD players.

The villa runs the length of the luxurious pool, which is complemented with an inviting sala for guests to relax and enjoy their time outdoors.

Villa Sasa has an open plan living/dining room, which creates a very social atmosphere, accompanied with wood and tiled interiors, which boast comfortable furnishings through out the villa. The villa is great for evening dining outside by the pool, whether it is a traditional sit down meal or a BBQ, with the combined ambient lighting and surround sound from the music system, you truly feel that you are on vacation.

The Kitchen is a modern masterpiece with the latest amenities and fittings, including an ice water-dispensing refrigerator, a refreshing and welcome feature during the hot months.

What makes Villa Sasa even more attractive is its location, barley 200 meters from a very quiet beach with spectacular views to Koh Phangan. You are only a few minutes walk from an excellent selection of shops, restaurants and bars, 10 minutes drive from Chaweng which is the main hotspot on Koh Samui and 5 minutes from the famous Fisherman’s village in Bophut. Fisherman’s village has become a favourite with guests for its Friday walking street market, where you can experience a variety of traditional foods, trinkets, music, and local hospitality.
