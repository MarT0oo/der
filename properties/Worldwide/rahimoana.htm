Somewhere beneath the stars known as the Southern Cross... 

 

On an Island in the South Pacific you will find what people around the world call Heaven on Earth. This is that place, and Rahimoana—Sun God over the Ocean—epitomizes that feeling.  This is a place where the rich and famous make their escape, to enjoy the solitude and beauty of one of the most amazing homes in the world.

 

When you say location, you cannot be speaking on anywhere else.  Rahimoana offers any superstar the absolute best privacy and luxury, with no one around to take away from the sheer magic of this holiday escape.  Most have called it a retreat from heaven, offering it up as a place to find yourself again or loose yourself for a while.  Let us pamper you, from the moment you arrive till the moment you step back onto your plane.  If you can’t find rest and relaxation here, than you will not find it anywhere. 

 

Rahimoana, somewhere between those seven stars and heaven, is infused with warmth and light, surrounded by the sapphire seas and untouched emerald coves of the Bay of Islands.  In sub-tropical Far North New Zealand, this sublime work of art is the essence of serenity, peace, and timeless beauty.

 

With exquisite food and wine and unsurpassed service and amenities, Rahimoana offers everything you need for the ultimate luxury experience of a lifetime.
