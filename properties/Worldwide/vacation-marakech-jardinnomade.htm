Villa Jardin Nomade, Marrakech, Morrocco Vacation Rental


Do you fancy a fairy garden with roses and olive trees? Then this Moorish–style architect villa with a view of the Atlas Mountains will suit you with its pure lines and its ethnic interior design in a warm and trendy atmosphere. The Villa Jardin Nomade is a quiet and refined gem for a stay with your friends or family in Marrakech, only 7 minutes from the buzz of Djema el Fna square and its colorful souks.

Facing the Atlas mountains, on a beautiful 10 000 m2 terrain planted with orange trees, mandarin trees, palm trees, hundred–years–old olive trees and thousands of roses, the Villa Jardin Nomade proposes A 450 m 2 Villa, ready to welcome 8 adults and 2 to 3 children.  There are 4 bedrooms with a unique pure design, about 50m² each with vast bathrooms, dressings, and separate toilets.  There is also a private terrace for each room with an outside lounge and solarium. 

The house features a lounge with a fireplace corner.  This is wonderful for those days where you just want to sit in and sip on a glass of sherry and enjoy the wonderful views.  Additionally there is a gourmet kitchen that is fully equipped, a place where you can find plenty of what you need to make this a second home for you.  Our dining room has an outside view for those who love to dine and feel as if they are really enjoying the idea of the “perfect” vacation home.  We can also arrange a full staff if needed for an additional cost.

There is a library lounge with satellite TV (Canal +), CD/DVD player et Bose sound.  We have left nothing out so that you can come and just unpack your bags and begin your Moroccan Journey.  There is an expansive terrace near the swimming pool, with a lounge and a dining corner.  Most of our guests truly enjoy this part of the house, it’s a place they come to get away from it all and sit and relax at night or with friends and family after a meal.

This is the perfect blend of perfect holiday and spectacular living.
