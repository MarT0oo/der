Balmoral Modern, Mosman, Sydney, Australia Vacation Rental

 

This striking contemporary residence takes pride of place footsteps from Balmoral Beach. Close enough to hear the sound of lapping water and smell the quintessential Australian beach side fragrance of sunscreen & salty air.

Made up of three levels, the light filled and airy entrance shares the floor with the four bedrooms. The lower level is a great retreat for kids with a TV/rumpus room (and kitchenette) and sun-drenched grassy area. The upper level is the focal point of the home with a spacious design layout inclusive of an open plan living, dining, kitchen area.

A welcoming atmosphere is created with the kitchen, dining and lounge room flowing seamlessly into the sun-drenched entertainers balcony (with BBQ) perfect for those long summer evenings whilst watching the spectacle of yachts sailing by.

Simply styled with neutral colours, bathed in sunlight and an elevator to all levels, this home has everything you need to relax and unwind at your fingertips.

Once you’re there, there’s little reason to leave with plenty of shops along Military Road, stunning scenery and even the iconic Taronga Zoo to keep you amused.

Kick off a summer’s day watching the sunrise with a stroll down to Balmoral Beach. Sure, it means getting up early, but it also means catching some natural beauty before the day gets hotter and the crowds descend.

Once the sun is up and things start to get hot, cool off with an ocean dip. For swimming, opt for the north end (to your left if you’re facing the sea) where the water is always crystal clear and you’ll be away from docked boats and the naval base down at the southern end of the beach.

Holidaying in Balmoral really isn’t complete without a stop at the iconic Boathouse for breakfast.

The owners built this home with the purpose of bringing the family together. Relish in the sociability of the homes features with long BBQ lunches on the alfresco balcony overlooking the beach.

Designed to please, the sleek modern kitchen has everything you need to prepare a family meal at home. This property is ideal for those who like to entertain family and friends.

Light, airy and designed to capture the ocean breeze, what more could you want when it comes to a seaside escape?

The calm waters of Balmoral Beach paired with endless children’s activities this destination is a young family’s dream.

Enjoy the many coastal and foreshore tracks, Taronga Zoo and the city ferry only a stones throw away.

Balmoral Beach offers a more relaxed atmosphere, with parks, swimming and beach side cafes; this is a holiday not to miss.
