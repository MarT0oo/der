Palm House, Pererenan, Bali, Indonesia Vacation Rental

 

Experience luxury villa living at this contemporary retreat located amongst the picturesque rice fields in Bali’s Canggu area. The Architect and Interior Designer, Felipe Gonzalez (SUKYF & Architects) and Ros Hemley (The Interior Space Australia Pty Ltd) have created a unique collaboration that is sophisticated yet family friendly. With a dedicated and discreet team of staff to cater to every whim, the villa has five double rooms, plus the option to convert the gym and office into additional bedrooms for up to 18 guests. The Palm House boasts a 40-meter pool for real swimming and the perfect Jacuzzi corner for sunset drinks. The air-conditioned gym and spa are always open, while the view of lush green lawn with rice paddies and palms beyond will bring ultimate tranquility and relaxation to the most discerning guest.

Located in the rural district of Canggu, just minutes from some of Bali’s most revered surf beaches, The Palm House offers incredible sunsets with glimpses of the mystical Batukaru volcano in the distance.

Minimalist horizontal lines accent this contemporary sanctuary set in almost an acre of tropical gardens overlooking the rice fields. Close to the excitement of Seminyak, but far enough away to ensure a traditional Balinese experience. No detail has been overlooked - every piece of furniture has been custom designed specifically for the villa, and each piece of unique artwork, personally chosen by the Villa owners.

The Palm House can accommodate up to 18 guests, King mattresses in each room that can also be configured to twins, and stunning tropical views from the picture windows of every bedroom.

Families and sport fanatics will enjoy the 40-metre pool, air-conditioned gym, spa services and a Yoga pavilion overlooking the pool and rice paddies. There is a shallow end of the pool, which is shaded until midday – perfect for little ones to have some splash time. And at the other end, there is a Jacuzzi that begs to be used for sunset drinks. Make use of the car and driver and visit some of Bali’s temples and historic sites, play a round of golf at the nearby Nirwana course, or stroll through the streets of Seminyak, just 25 minutes away.

The outdoor sunken living room and BBQ area is an alternative for casual dinners and takes advantage of the breeze in Canggu at night. And with almost 200m square metres of neatly manicured lawn, The Palm House is the perfect location to celebrate special occasions and sophisticated events. The Palm House has an in-house bar man and resort quality chef at your disposal - kick back with a Vespa Martini, or have Chef create and prepare a 10-course degustation menu exclusively for you and your guests.

The Palm House has five en-suite bedrooms enveloped in air-conditioned comfort. Each room has large picture windows that frame the tropical green views, along with a terrace or balcony. All five rooms are of a similar size and have flexibly configured beds offering both twin and king options. We also have luxury rollaway beds that can be added upon request. For larger groups, the gym and office can be converted to a sixth and seventh bedroom.

The designs of the bedrooms are understated luxury – softly muted colors mixed with antique woods. Unique pieces of antiques and artwork adorn the walls. The wardrobes are walk-in and the bathrooms feature a mix of marble and teak wood, an oversized freestanding bathtub, as well as spacious shower stalls. With the exception of the Children’s room, where there is a double head massive shower, in the space of the tub.

The mattresses are hotel-grade, thick and luxurious, wrapped in Egyptian cotton bedding. A comfortable sitting area is furnished with a custom-made sofa and desk. Sheer curtains ensure total privacy while enjoying the light, but for those that want to sleep in, every bedroom is equipped with black out curtains.

CHILDREN
The children’s room has 4 single beds built into the wall – these beds can hold both children and adults comfortably. For children occupancy, we would prepare the room with small table and chairs, arts and craft supplies, toys, books and DVDs. The room also features a lovely reading swing, perfect for looking out onto the tropical views.

The outdoor lounge and dining room on the ground floor opens seamlessly onto the manicured grass lawn with expansive pool and jungle views. This formal dining area seats 12 guests.

The entertainment room is just off of the main stairway and houses 2 oversized modular sofas with plenty of pillows to go around. This is a perfect place to wind down in the air conditioning while watching your favourite movie, or playing from our selection of board games.

The enclosed lounge and dining room directly off the Chef’s kitchen is the heart of the villa. With a dining table for 12 guests where breakfast is normally served, and a superbly comfortable lounge sofa for taking in the sights at the Palm House, many of our guests end up spending most of their time in this part of the house.

For further outdoor entertaining, there is a sunken living room flanked by the jacuzzi and a large stone fire pit - great for cocktails and casual dining nights. This space, which has a lovely breeze at night, has a large built-in barbecue, sink and fridge alongside a bench style cocktail bar.
