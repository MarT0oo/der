Villa Napalai, Surin, Phuket, Thailand Vacation Rental

 

Napalai means ‘a place in heaven’, and Villa Napalai’s location is certainly heavenly.

Set on a private hillside estate with generous views of the Andaman Sea, the villa lies within easy walking distance of Surin Beach, which boasts Phuket’s best beachfront dining scene, boutique shops and a growing number of beach clubs. Golf courses, marinas, water sports and other outdoor leisure activities are all within easy reach of the villa.

Boasting an impressive range of facilities, including a games room with pool table and bar, a sunny playroom for the children, a spa room, a well-equipped gym, plus an infinity pool and private walled garden, a heavenly experience is also assured.

A blend of modern and Thai accents add colour and life to each room, while the pool terrace is a stylish place to cool down and catch those fabulous island sunsets. Every detail has been designed to ensure a worry-free and ultra-comfortable stay.

Enjoy sea views from the private balcony or the lavish double jacuzzi tub of the romantic and spacious master bedroom. Three guest bedrooms (one twin-bedded and ideal for children) each offer the utmost privacy and comfort, with ensuite bathrooms, cosy seating areas and luxurious design touches. The sleek, modern kitchen is well equipped, and guests can entertain with ease at indoor and outdoor dining settings and a sophisticated built-in barbecue area in the landscaped garden.

The villa is staffed with a full-time Thai chef who prepares delicious meals daily, two maids and a villa manager who will be pleased to assist with all holiday requirements, including golf tee-off times, dining reservations, yacht charters, island tours, in-villa Thai massage treatments and more.
