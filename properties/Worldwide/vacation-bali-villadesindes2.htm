Villa Oost Indies, Bali, Indonesia Vacation Rental


Villa Oost Indies is a 2 bedroom Bali villa, a new addition to the Des Indes Collection and is situated opposite the original residence. Large ancient Chinese doors open out into a cleverly carved Joglo which serves guests as a living and dining area.


Relaxing lounges and plantation chairs and a circular dining table rest in the Joglo area alongside interesting coffee table books on Indonesia. The Joglo fringes the lagoon style swimming pool and opens out onto a tropical garden with an expansive 900sqm manicured lawn which is perfect for Bali Weddings and Events.

Villa Oost Indies has the same immaculate attention to detail as the original residence. The walls are hung with original Indonesian works of art, batiks and antique hand carved reliefs. You will rest your head on Egyptian cotton sheets within an antique four poster Balinese bed. Grand bathrooms house pedestal washbasins, free standing bathtubs and spacious rainwater showers. Children are welcome at Villa Oost Indies.

This Bali 2 bedroom villa can has two additional single rooms which are above the kitchen area which house queen size beds. These are perfect for singles or guests traveling with staff. The use of these rooms results in a maximum capacity of 6 adults.

Villa Oost Indies, Luxury private villa with 900sqm garden in Seminyak, Bali

One of the keynotes of this luxury villa in Bali is its extremely spacious 900sqm lawn area fringed by tropical palms. While resting in the Joglo, your view looks out over this expansive garden area. Perfect for families and friends in the daytime, we can lay out sun loungers, set up garden games or hang a hammock or two.

Private villas in Seminyak are great places to host parties and dinners and the garden of Villa Oost Indies is perfect for entertaining. Bali’s traditional Kecak (fire) Dance is often performed in this garden for guests with up to thirty local performers followed by a traditional Balinese Ristaffel Dinner set up under the stars.

Both Seminyak luxury villas are available to rent for events such as Bali weddings and anniversaries.
