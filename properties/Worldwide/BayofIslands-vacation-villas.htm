Russell Bay of Islands

Russell Bay of Islands is a place where sunsets seem longer and days seem to go on and on.  People often travel here to “get away” from the hustle and bustle and find themselves exploring new ways to enjoy R&R.  It has become one of the places even the rich and famous find plentiful for the much needed get away destination.  The Bay of Islands are easily gotten to by ferry from Opua Wharf or Paihia wharf.  The area is strewn with amazing luxury cottages, little white picket fences, and some of the loveliest sailing and boating activities you will find in the area. 

Lots of people who visit like to make sure they see Christ Church, New Zealand’s oldest Church and certainly a place filled with history.  Also try Long Beach for great strolls and the Walking Track to Flagstaff Hill.  We currently offer Luxury Holiday accommodations in this area, such as Rahimoana and Sacred Place.
