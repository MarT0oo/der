Grand Villa Noi, Phuket, Thailand Vacation Rental

 

Designed by celebrated architect Khun Duangrit Bunnag, Grand Villa Noi is chic and modern. The luxurious villa is a fine blend of contemporary touches and traditional Thai elements. Its edgy design with glass walls allows unobstructed views of the natural beauty around and enhances the overall luxury beach holiday experience.

Located on a 10- kilometre stretch of unspoilt beach in the semi-rural Phang Nga province, Grand Villa Noi holds prime position on the white sands of Natai Beach. The villa is a relaxing haven surrounded by lush foliage, tropical gardens and large, swaying trees. The sea views from the villa are outstanding, and sure to be enjoyed throughout the duration of your holiday.

One of the first things you will notice about the villa are the floor-to-ceiling glass sliding doors of the main residence. The doors look very chic, and ensure natural light floods the residence, but they are also functional. Slide them open to allow the gentle sea breeze to flow through the villa, or choose to dine semi-alfresco as you watch the sun set across the turquoise waters of the Andaman Sea.

Housekeeping staff will keep the villa in order while you sit back and relax. With a private chef at your disposal, expect gourmet meals when you want them. Nearby Pilai Village has a beachfront restaurant when you wish for a change of scenery and is a great place to witness local fishermen going about their daily life. A smattering of eateries can be found at Khok Kloi, at a distance of ten kilometres from the villa.
