Villa Keong, Tabanan, Bali, Indonesia Vacation Rental

 

If you are looking for a peaceful and secluded place to relax and wish to stay near the ocean surrounded by palm trees and rice fields, this amazing villa is just right. This exotic wooden 3 bedroom villa with a spacious tropical garden is located near the ocean in the District of Tabanan.

Villa Keong is built in mixed antique styles of Sumatra, Kalimantan and Flores cultural heritage with two buildings. The unique architectural design of the houses, made of iron wood and glass, will not leave anyone disappointed.

The buildings and the swimming pool are situated in a spacious green garden. One building is a two-story sumatran style house with 2 bedrooms upstairs, two bathrooms, living room, dining room, and well-equipped kitchen are downstairs. The second building, which is located near, provides one bedroom and a bathroom with a shower.

The road to the beach takes just 1 minute. A wooden pier is provided; part of which belongs to the villa. The beach with black volcanic sand is hidden from view and is very private. This beach is not very suitable for swimming as it is open ocean, however it is good for kids to jump in the small waves.

Nearby there is a hotel restaurant where you can order your tasty breakfast in the villa. If you decide to cook yourself, we advise you to bring food with you because the big local store is 30 minutes away. You can go sightseeing and visit nearest popular places such as Jatiluwih Rice Terrace, Tanah Lot Temple, Taman Ayun Temple, Ulun Danu Temple and many others.
