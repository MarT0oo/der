Villa Kalimaya IV, Seminyak, Bali, Indonesia Vacation Rental

 

Villa Kalimaya IV is a stylish, fully staffed luxury villa set within private gardens in the heart of vibrant Seminyak, featuring two bedrooms with king-size beds and a large twin bedded room all with marble ensuite bathrooms. With two living areas, an open-plan kitchen, dining room, terrace, infrared sauna, jacuzzi, private swimming pool and traditional Balinese water features, plus a thatched poolside balé in lush tropical surrounds, the villa makes an ideal base for relaxing balmy Bali days before heading out for a night on the town.

The three-bedroomed, two-storey Villa Kalimaya IV is a wonderful option for groups of friends and families. The ground floor contains the spacious open-plan living and dining room, a neat fitted kitchen and one of the lovely master bedrooms with glass doors that open onto the garden and pool area. Two further bedrooms, one of which has twin beds, open onto the upper floor balcony. Here guests may be served breakfast in the morning or just enjoy a coffee. There’s a second sitting area and entertainment room on this floor too – a lovely spot to read a book or just relax with a cool drink in air-conditioned comfort. Outside, a jacuzzi, sauna and 12 x 4-metre private pool ensure total relaxation.

This charming two-storey villa boasts two living rooms – one up and one down – which can be opened to embrace the warm tropical breezes or closed for absolute air-conditioned comfort. Both living rooms are equipped with satellite TVs and DVD players and surround-sound systems. The dining area, to one side of the downstairs lounge, seats six guests at its elegant table overlooking the garden and pool.

The sparkling swimming pool, set within the private walled garden, abundant with tropical palm trees and aromatic frangipanis, forms the centrepiece of this villa. Enjoy the tropical sun from one of the many lounge chairs on the pool deck; relax in the shade of the thatched balé; soothe yourself in the jacuzzi or detoxify and energise in the infrared sauna.

All of the bedrooms at Villa Kalimaya IV – two upstairs and one downstairs – have full-length glass doors that can be opened to create a sense of space and airiness. Downstairs they open directly to the garden and pool area and upstairs they open onto a balcony surrounded by bougainvillea and other lush tropical plants. Two are furnished with grand king-size four-poster beds, with the third bedroom offering twin beds (convertible to king-size), and all boast air-conditioning, an iPod dock, bar fridge, TV and DVD player. Each has a plush marble ensuite with rain shower and bathtub.

About The Area

Villa Kalimaya IVs located in the absolute heart of Seminyak, within short walking distance from some of the best known and iconic restaurants and bars on the island. Ku De Ta and Potato Head are literally at your doorstep and it’s just a stroll to the gorgeous beachside restaurant La Lucciola and the hot new dining favourite with both locals and expats, Petitenget. Take some time and explore some of the world-class boutiques and fabulous contemporary art galleries.

If you’re into surfing then the beach is just a few minutes away and boasts some serious world-class breaks and brilliant sunsets. Don’t miss out on a visit to Pura Petitenget, the fourth largest temple in Bali, where spectacular Balinese ceremonies may often be witnessed.
