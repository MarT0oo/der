The Darling, Darling Point, Sydney, Australia Vacation Rental

 

Come for the postcard-perfect view across to the Sydney Harbour Bridge, stay for the sundrenched open-plan apartment in one of the city’s most exclusive neighbourhoods.

The Darling compliments its stunning location with practical design and furnishings. This state-of-the-art modern security triplex combines the latest security technology with privacy and discretion.

Enter the generous open plan living room and be blown away by the sight of the glittering harbour in front of you, while the glass sliding door to the outdoor dining area means it remains uninterrupted day and night. A further dining area is indoors next to the sleek galley kitchen. Three bedrooms and bathrooms are located just off the main living room.

Guests won’t want to stay indoors for long, with The Darling designed as the perfect springboard to the best of everything The Harbour City has to offer.

Make the most of this fabulous harbourside location by throwing open the doors and windows to receive the ocean breeze from across the harbour and natural sunlight that the property has been designed to attract. When you want to get out and about, take a stroll to the adjacent neighbourhood of Double Bay, home to the city’s most exclusive shopping and dining, or explore natural spaces such as nearby Rushcutters Bay Park and Yarranabbe Park.

The alfresco entertaining area means guests can show off that superb view. The day bed means it can also double as a second living area, to give families more of a chance to spread out. Make use of the sophisticated galley kitchen and enjoy early morning coffees, leisurely brunches, and dinners that go well into the evening.

Perfectly situated in this exclusive Sydney Harbour enclave, the ocean is your backyard. The apartment is only a short walk to the gentle Redleaf Beach, or a short drive to iconic Bondi Beach. Charter a boat for a day exploring hidden beaches, and don’t forget to head over to see the Sydney Opera House in all of its glory.
