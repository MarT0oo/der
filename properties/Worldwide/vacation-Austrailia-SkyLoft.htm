Sky Loft, Sydney, Australia Vacation Rental

 

Find yourself in the very heart of one of the most adventurous and exciting cities in the world. The Sky Loft sub-penthouse is situated in Sydney’s desirable Darlinghurst suburb and is ideal for those who are after the excitement, convenience, and luxury of city life. You’ll be impressed by the sleek interiors and formidable views of the city lights framing the lingering sunset in the horizon from the spectacular balcony that runs the length of the apartment.

This three bedroom style oasis offers two en-suite rooms as well as fine timber finishes and elegant Carrera marble interiors. Your inner chef will feel at home in the Sky Lofts kitchen, which boasts Electrolux appliances, two ovens, a gas and induction cook top as well as a huge entertaining island bench. With two lounge rooms and a dining room that seats up to twelve people, this sub-penthouse location is perfect for entertaining guests, especially with the stunning views you are sure to get from the balcony.

With extensive cafes, fine dining, and night-life options to choose from, you will have endless entertainment opportunities to keep you amused.

Please note: Sky Pad is a 4th bedroom is across the hall and self-contained, not suitable for children without supervision – ie it is a 1 bedroom apartment in itself. This apartment can be booked in addition to Sky Loft to make 4 bedrooms, must be booked at the outset as they are two separate apartments.
