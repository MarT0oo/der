Bayside Darling Point, Darling Point, Sydney, Australia Vacation Rental

 

Impeccably positioned right on the water in one of the east’s most prized enclaves, this stunning home is one of only 7 house-sized havens in the block and boasts an enviable lifestyle of luxury, comfort and convenience.

On the doorstep of shops, transport & waterfront parks, this is the perfect destination for a luxury retreat or family stay.

The master bedroom includes a luxurious spa en-suite with with double vanity and a lovely study nook, along with the Queen guest bedroom including an en-suite and study nook. The third bedroom, which doubles as a rumpus area, is designed to allow to be divided to form two separate rooms.

Bayside Darling Point has a generous open layout that adjoins indoor and outdoor living around a huge covered terrace creating a seamless flow from inside to outdoor living.

The location! Darling Point is mostly residential and regarded as one of the most exclusive and prestigious suburbs in Australia. With the CYC only a stone’s throw, and Sydney’s best restaurants neighbouring in the buzzing hubs nearby, the decision to cook at home is an extremely difficult one!

This apartment has been beautifully refurbished. A clever use of space using sliding doors, ample storage and little nooks, this home is comfort yet decadence. The famous CYC across the road is perfect for an afternoon escape, a walk up and down Rushcutters Bay, or perhaps a spot of tennis in the afternoon.

Design, sleek interior, high ceilings, city skyline, Opera House and Harbour Bridge cameos hint at the wider delights available nearby. Whilst the rhythmic motion of nearby yachts on the adjacent marina remind you that this apartment enjoys an exclusive access to Sydney that most can only dream of.
