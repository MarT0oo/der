Luna2 private hotel, Seminyak, Bali Vacation Rental

 

Luna2 private hotel is a masterpiece of visionary, pop culture design located absolute beachfront in the heart of fashionable Seminyak. This villa delivers in every aspect – unparalleled location, award-winning interiors, world-class culinary chefs and around the clock service from a team of 20 dedicated staff.

Architect David Wahl and interior designer Melanie Hall, have seamlessly fashioned a five-bedroom residence with private terraces and gardens, a 20m pool styled with Marilyn Monroe mosaics all superbly positioned beachfront and overlooking the Indian Ocean. This is a serious celebration or party villa with direct access onto the renowned sands and surf-break of Seminyak Beach. With the ocean literally on your doorstep the open air balé adds the finishing touch to outdoor dining and your private chef is ever on hand to produce the ultimate in gourmet feasts. Despite every temptation to stay put, those wanting to explore will soon discover themselves bang in the heart of exciting Seminyak with all the restaurants, cafes, bars and boutiques at your feet. In the two-levelled living areas the ultra-modern, minimalist style is reminiscent of the great modernism of the past and all the furniture, carpets and fabrics have been custom designed.

Each of the five bedrooms is accented with its own colour theme with the decoration suggestive of modernist decadence with touches of 60’s pop and fuelled with innovations of the future. Two bedrooms have mesmerising ocean views and large private terraces overlooking the gardens, beach and waves, one overlooks the courtyard, whilst the other two enjoy views over sacred temple land.

Families will fall in love with the private theatre next door at the sister studiotel where kids can enjoy their own movie selection, whilst parents wanting a night out can enlist the free babysitter and slip on over to the guests’ nightclub or sensational rooftop bar. Luna2 private hotel’s media room is also fully loaded and ready to roll with TV, board games, music, and fine reading.

With metres of beach and ocean frontage, a team of 20 dedicated staff and an abundance of space, Luna 2 private hotel is a property that shouts out to those seeking an unsurpassed family holiday, a dream wedding and party celebration, or a perfect beachfront pad for a group of super savvy travellers.
