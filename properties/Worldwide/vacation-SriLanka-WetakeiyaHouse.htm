Wetakeiya House, Dikwella, Sri Lanka Vacation Rental

 

A lovely three ensuite bedroom family home over looking a rocky cost line. The elevated location offers wonderful views out to sea from the breakfast table, pool or yoga pavilion.

Situated 15 minutes away from TanDikwella, and an hour away from Dikwella, Villa Wetakeiya overlooks the rugged rocky coastline. The villa garden extends out to hug the shore, and it is a mere 5 minutes stroll to the magnificent ocean with perfect waves suitable for surfing.

There are 3 bedrooms in Villa Wetakeiya. The master suite has a romantic four poster King sized bed, en-suite bathroom, and French shutters that open up to the beautiful garden terrace. The second bedroom has a four poster queen sized bed with en-suite, whilst the third bedroom has 2 single beds with en-suite bathroom. Each bedroom is elegantly furnished, with the use of dark wood fittings for a warm and elegant vibe.

The living and dining area of the villa has plush sofas, entertainment sets, a large dining table and colonial inspired French windows that open up to gorgeous views. Outdoors, there is an in-built breakfast table that overlooks the villa plunge pool and ocean. Guests can enjoy their morning cuppa whilst admiring the glorious vista spread out before their feet. For meditation or yoga, there is also a private pavilion the very purpose.
