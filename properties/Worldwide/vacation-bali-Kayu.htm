Kayu, Bali Ethnic Villa, Umalas, Kerobokan, Bali, Indonesia Vacation Rental

 

Kayu, the Elegant, has 4 double rooms and their private open air bathrooms, situated along a pond of unbelievably blue water…

Welcome to your luxurious but traditional villa in Bali.

Kayu is inspired by “wood” which constitutes the structure of our villas in the tradition of the island of Java as well as of our statues “Timor”, the guardians of our superb and lush garden of 3000 m2 …. Respecting the concept of Feng Shui, the element “Batu” (stone) is represented in magnificent baths as well as in very beautiful pottery decorating the area around the majestic swimming pool. You’ll spend privileged moments relaxing in the Joglos living and dining rooms and TV corner with your family and friends.

Our friendly, discreet and competent team, the comfort of the place, the positive energies, the interaction of all these assets will help you escape into a successful holidays in Bali. Kayu’s originality … the luxury of playing billiard at the foot of a mini rice paddy specially designed for your pleasure.

The residential area of Umalas is just 35 minutes by car from Ngurah Rai International Airport and only 10 minutes from the shops and trendy restaurants of Seminyak.

Our property is nestled between rice paddies and consists of three great villas each with its majestic private pool. Built in the traditional Javanese style of antic carved teak wood, these “Joglos” are endemic to the island of Java. They welcome you for a relaxing break during your journey on the mysterious island of Bali. The secretive housing complex in ethnic style is set in a lush tropical environment scenting of jasmine where flowers, statues and ceramics blend together to create a peaceful atmosphere.

This atmosphere has been created by the French charm of Alexa Aguila Genoyer, traveler, photographer and artist; she has set up a combination of ethnic decoration, local materials, contemporary and cozy comfort which is topped by the friendly availability and hospitality of our Balinese staff.
