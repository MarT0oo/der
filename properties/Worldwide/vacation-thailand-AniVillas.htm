Ani Villas, Koh Yao Noi, Thailand Vacation Rental

 

The stunning beachfront estate at Ani Villas Thailand offers guests a magical Southeast Asian experience. Many activities and amenities are included in standard rates, allowing guests to enjoy as many relaxation, adventure, and cultural experiences as they desire.

Venture beyond the estate to explore the serene island of Koh Yao Noi, or simply settle in for quality time to reconnect. Premium activities and excursions are also available.

The Ani Villas Thailand team can help coordinate all aspects of your event, be it a wedding, milestone celebration, family reunion, corporate incentive or team building group. For more intricate productions we work with specialized event planners and celebrants.

Chef Yao, Ani Villas Thailand’s executive chef, delivers a world-class dining experience.

Chef Yao is a native Thai and wows our guests with a finer Thai cuisine that delicately balances a unique blend of flavor, texture, and spice, He holds extensive experience with international, fusion, and Western cuisine as well. Yao is also trained in floral arrangements, and his attention to detail clearly shows through with remarkable presentations.

Meals are served in any of the estate’s spectacular dining venues. The main dining sala floats above the pool at the beach’s edge. A café and instructional kitchen allow guests to learn from the chef and take home one of the greatest souvenirs: Thai recipes.
