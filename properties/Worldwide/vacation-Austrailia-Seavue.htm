Seavue, Eastern Sydney, Sydney, Australia Vacation Rental

 

Featuring sweeping views across North Bondi beach and the romantic cliff tops of Eastern Sydney, this luxury retreat is designed for those who travel in style.

The property interiors were designed by leading stylist Tamsin Johnson, reflecting a modern Australian sensibility with high quality furnishings and tasteful artworks. The kitchen boasts state of the art appliances and a clean design, content to play second fiddle to that spectacular view.

Out on the balcony, with an uninterrupted panorama of the coastline, guests can lounge on the daybed, cook on the barbecue, and entertain with a large dining table. The glass front allows the sunshine and sea breeze to filter throughout the property, ensuring that you maximise your Bondi experience even when you are indoors.

When it comes to exploring, you’re only minutes away from the vibrant beach culture, upmarket shopping, trendy restaurants, and buzzing bars of Bondi, as well as the more laid back pursuits of this exclusive location, such as golf courses and marinas.

See and be seen in the heart of trendy North Bondi, whether dining at celebrity hangout Sean’s Panorama or sitting down to a romantic meal on your balcony. Discover the best of the city’s beach culture with long cliff top walks, yoga classes, and clear blue waters on your doorstep. From there, it’s only a short car ride to see Sydney’s other attractions, returning to watch the sun set in private luxury.

The alfresco entertaining area means that you never have to miss out on the backdrop that is at once invigorating and relaxing. Complimented by stimulating contemporary artwork, this apartment hits all the right notes, whether you’re working from home, enjoying a summer holiday, or entertaining friends.

This home is designed for families who like the finest things in life. Johnson’s elegant design is functional and uncluttered, featuring furnishings of the highest comfort and quality and artwork scattered throughout the house. Guests will enjoy buzzing Bondi with the ability to get away from it all at only a moment’s notice.
