Cliffside Stunner
5 Bedrooms | 4 Baths | Stunning Views                                                    
Queenstown, New Zealand
      From NZD $1600 per night

This stunning 5 bedroom property spans over 3 levels and offers the luxury of space in a comfortable setting with an intimate feel.  Cliffside Stunner is draped in natural light with floor to ceiling windows that span the rooms from wall to wall, capturing 180 degree unobstructed views of Lake Wakatipu and Queenstown Bay from every room.  Nestled on a cliff face 2 minutes' drive from central Queenstown, this breathtaking private residence is unquestionably the most luxurious accommodation offered on Queenstown Hill.

Queenstown is a four season resort destination ideal for a family holiday, weekend break or romantic getaway.  Rightly known as the 'Adventure Capital of New Zealand', Queenstown offers a wide range of activities such as bungee jumping, sky diving, white water rafting, jet boating and many others.

Beyond the adventurous activities you can find world class golfing, alpine river and lake fishing and winery tours.  Our Concierge offers complimentary local information and booking services allowing you to relax and enjoy your stay. Whether you are looking for a romantic getaway or a family holiday, we can create tailor-made itineraries enabling you to make the most of your time.
