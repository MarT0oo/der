Kalua, Bali Ethnic Villa, Umalas, Kerobokan, Bali, Indonesia Vacation Rental

 

Kalua, the Exotic, and its lotus pond is the smallest of our 4 luxury villas with 4 bedrooms choice of queen or twin bed and their private bathrooms which are halfway in the open air, one of them with a beautiful pool of 20 m2.

Our bungalows in antic teak wood from the island of Java are arranged around a magnificent swimming pool in the form of a cloverleaf from where a “Dewi”, a goddess statue in lava stone, caresses you with her protective gaze. The moment you arrive in Kalua, you enter a fairy tale world….

A large garden of 2200 m2, perfumed and luxuriant, attracts you. You’ll be captivated by the decoration of the place, a mixture of contemporary and ethnic details in noble materials. Our Joglos living and dining room (no TV room) are at your disposal so that you can spend excellent moments together with your family or friends. A television and CD/DVD player is available upon request.

Our friendly staff, available for you at any moment, and the comfort of the place will make your stay in Bali pleasant and magic….Kalua …. its very particular touch … the view over the rice paddies….

The connection between Kalua and Ka , our 5 bedrooms villa , is a perfect setting to fuse the 2 villas , a place hosting up to 27 guests . Perfect for a group or a special event.

The residential area of Umalas is just 35 minutes by car from Ngurah Rai International Airport and only 10 minutes from the shops and trendy restaurants of Seminyak.

Our property is nestled between rice paddies and consists of two great villas each with its majestic private pool. Built in the traditional Javanese style of antic carved teak wood, these “Joglos” are endemic to the island of Java. They welcome you for a relaxing break during your journey on the mysterious island of Bali. The secretive housing complex in ethnic style is set in a lush tropical environment scenting of jasmine where flowers, statues and ceramics blend together to create a peaceful atmosphere
