Saba - Villa Bima, Canggu, Bali, Indonesia Vacation Rental

 

Just moments away from Berawa Beach on Bali’s southwest coast and ten minutes from the fabulously exciting Seminyak, lies Bima, a contemporary two bedroom villa, part of the incomparable Saba villas complex.

Within a minute’s notice, a sliding door within the garden can be opened to join Bima with its sister villa, Yudhistira. Guests will then have an extended tropical garden and not one, but two, sparkling 15 metre pools at their disposal, making this an ideal spot for families. Kids, especially, will love exploring the extra spaces. Alternatively, this adaptable villa offers the ideal spot for a truly sophisticated wedding or the type of chic celebration that Bali has become famous for; the choice is yours.

The villa has two delightfully spacious bedrooms boasting enormous open air ensuites, an air conditioned dining room which offers a retreat from the midday sun and an open air living pavilion overlookingthe swimming pool.

Experienced chefs are on hand to cook whatever you desire on state-of-the-art equipment, be it a burger or more sophisticated fare. Afternoon tea, with its tempting array of sweet treats, is served daily and will keep you going until your Sundowner with canapés or evening meal.

The world renowned culinary delights of Seminyak are just a short drive away and if you’re in the mood to shop then the villa’s car will drop you right to the epicentre of Bali’s fashionable boutiques and stores. A state of the art gym awaits you a few minutes away at the Canggu Club, and if surfing’s your thing, you are close to some legendary breaks.
