Villa Khayangan, Uluwatu, Bali Vacation Rental

 

A Beautiful and Magical Villa. This private Bali villa is indeed a “heaven on earth”, with many of our guests insisting that it is the best Bali luxury villa. The elegant Villa Khayangan is situated on the southern west tip of Bali and in magnificent gardens on top of a cliff, above a delightful white sand beach. It is hard to come across a luxury Bali villa that offers such panoramic views in Bali alongside a grand vista of the vast Indian Ocean the way this villa does.

The villa boasts of state-of-the-art facilities and lavish furnishings. It has the coolest bathrooms on the island, particularly in the 2-master bedrooms. Guests can choose from a six bedroom and a 3-bedroom renting option, thanks to the villa’s design. It further features a cold plunge pool, a business center, a spa, a cliff edge bale and big lap pools. Besides all these facilities making the villa an outstanding Bali holiday villa, they further make it a perfect place for a wedding Villa.

Villa Khayangan is a 6-bedroom luxury villa built atop a cliff with the ocean and a white beach on its front. Its exotic Javanese/Balinese design will sure to charm any discerning travelers' taste. It has 2 swimming pools with outdoor jacuzzi, mega spa, and spacious ground for wedding events. This villa is perfect for family and friends, as well as a venue for special events such as weddings and anniversaries.
