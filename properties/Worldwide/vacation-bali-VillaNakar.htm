Villa Nakar, Pantai Pasut, Tabanan, Bali, Indonesia Vacation Rental

 

This fabulous modern Villa is situated in a very close walking distance to the most fascinating beach of the island - Balangan. Balangan Beach is a long, low strand at the base of rocky cliffs. The view from the cliff to the turquoise ocean during the sunny day is stunning. The beach is covered with palm trees and fronted by a ribbon of near-white sand, picturesquely dotted with sun umbrellas.

Absolutely amazing and stylish villa is surrounded by a huge garden and a swimming pool, and has a beautiful sunset views not just from the big wooden gazebo on the roof but also in the front of the villa.

Shared space of kitchen, dining and living areas are in harmony and balance. The dining room is a perfect excuse for spending time with family and friends, relaxing and enjoying the table talk. The living area is quite spacious, comfortable and attractive, so it is a perfect place to hang out after a hot sunny day. Large moderate kitchen is equipped with everything you will need during your stay. Each bedroom is cozy and has each own stylish bathroom, trimmed by natural stone and wood. Modern, simple and very spacious space will be perfect for a big family or friends.
