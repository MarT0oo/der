Blue Heights, Dikwella, Sri Lanka Vacation Rental

 

A lovely house set in approx 2 acres of garden with four ensuite bedrooms enjoying a south facing elevated position leading onto a small horseshoe bay with seasonal swimming and bogie boarding.

The beach is accessed via the private gate and short 15 yard stroll.

Blue Heights is a villa located in TanDikwella; a lovely house in 2 acres of garden. This villa enjoys a south facing elevated position looking at a small horseshoe bay with seasonal swimming and boogie boarding. Blue Heights also has a sea view from the terrace that offers a calm and quiet environment, promising a splendid and peaceful holiday.

This villa has 4 bedrooms with modern and minimalist design, suitable for you who likes the feeling of high taste with simplicity. The villa itself is set on elevated gardens among coconut palms but still has direct access to the beach below.

Blue Heights is a unique villa designed by leading architect Amila del Mel with certain vernacular architectural characteristics in mind. To this end guests will notice a nuanced 1500 sq foot veranda lining the perimeter of the complex. Villa Blue Heights' large garden has a spectacular view that is further enhanced by this villa's private, beautiful deserted beach.
