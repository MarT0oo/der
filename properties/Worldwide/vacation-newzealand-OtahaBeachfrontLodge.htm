Otaha Beachfront Lodge, Bay of Islands, New Zealand Vacation Rental

 

Otaha Beachfront Lodge is a combination of exceptionally well curated and presented contemporary New Zealand design complemented by calming views of the ocean perched in the centre of New Zealand's most historically rich environment.

Built from the timber of local trees, Otaha Beachfront Lodge is a piece of New Zealand history both physically and geographically.

The indoor feel of Otaha Beachfront is clean, spacious and encourages refined relaxation. All of the floors have a rich warm wooden feel and the house reflects the breezy clean feel of the ocean.

With internal furnishings from Simon James and other internationally renowned designers Otaha Beachfront Villa's design has been considered from the front door to the back. With floor to ceiling windows there is no mistaking that you are in the middle of the Bay of Islands.

Five bedrooms, five ensuites and an additional room for a nanny or caregiver ensures you have optimal space for a family or group of travelling friends. The main living space has an internal facing outdoor decking area perfect for curling up and reading a book or for those summer evenings where you are looking to entertain outside. There is also an external beach facing deck for those mornings and evenings of contemplation with a coffee or glass of wine.

Only a two minute walk to the beach where you can enjoy surfing, floundering, walking tracks and a private area for friends and family.

Enjoy total privacy and immersion into your natural surroundings whilst still only being 15 minutes from the heart of Keri Keri
