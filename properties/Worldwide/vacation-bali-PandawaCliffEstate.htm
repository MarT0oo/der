Pandawa Cliff Estate, South Kuta, Bali, Indonesia Vacation Rental

 

Surrounded by a mesmerizing natural landscape on Bali’s southernmost tip and perched on a cliff top high above the Indian Ocean, the enchanting Pandawa Cliff Estate welcomes visitors to a world of unparalleled serenity, comfort and luxury.

Designed by internationally acclaimed Bali architect, Popo Danes, the estate consists of three equally splendid cliff-top villas - the Pala, Villa Rose and Villa Marie – offering a total of 21 Bedrooms amid beautifully landscaped gardens. The villas share access to a sports center with full size tennis court, squash court, gym and spa, thereby giving guests the opportunity to stay fit and get pampered during their stay. Complete with a professional staff team of forty, including a butler, private chef service and massage therapists, Pandawa Cliff Estate has all the attributes of a fabulous wedding or special event venue. Outside, a wide lawn extends from the terrace to the infinity pool, beside which stands a covered barbecue and bar area. Also close to the pool is a purpose-built amphitheater designed for staging wedding ceremonies and traditional Balinese dance performances.

The Pala – which means ‘Nutmeg’ – is Pandawa Cliff Estate’s crown jewel. This extraordinary six-bedroom villa, which boasts outstanding views over the ocean, offers the ideal setting for a special family holiday or an extravagant couple hideaway. The superbly- decorated lounge with ocean-view balcony and the dining room form the social hub of the house, while the bedrooms – a magnificent master suite with commanding views over the smooth white sands of Pandawa Beach, and five gorgeously appointed en-suite rooms – provide guests with their own private sanctuary. Additionally, a spa treatment room, fitness room and children’s playroom contribute to creating the ultimate getaway experience for families.

Emulating the traditional Balinese house compounds, Villa Marie offers a generously laid-out living space and comfortable outdoor dining area, with BBQ and kitchenette. Two luxurious master bedroom pavilions stand across a T-shaped swimming pool, featuring a shallow area for children. The lush landscaping, with its gurgling ponds and wooden decking, evokes an overall sense of harmony and peace, enhanced by spectacular ocean views.

Villa Rose brings a contemporary twist to traditional tropical design. Offering a combination of open-air and air-conditioned living spaces, it features impressive living and dining rooms with a cozy TV area, four elegant bedrooms over two floors, and everywhere, luxurious finishes and exceptional views.

Enjoying a secluded location, less than 30 minutes from Bali’s international airport, and within easy reach of several gorgeous beaches, Pandawa Cliff Estate makes a perfect Indonesian hideaway.
