79, Galle Fort, Sri Lanka Vacation Rental

 

A charming ancient house within the historic Fort walls that has been restored with authentic style and elegance.

There are two double bedrooms and a bunk room and two bathrooms. Master suite is upstairs and has access to roof top terrace with ocean views. Inner courtyard pond and garden view dining table.

79 Leyn Baan is a villa located inside the fort walls of Galle. The villa's authentic style and elegance evokes a strong Mediterranean feel.

Villa 79 has a total of three bedrooms including a bunk room which kids would adore. The master suite and an attached bathroom are located upstairs.

The rooftop terrace with fascinating ocean views looks out over the lighthouse to the Indian Ocean and beyond. There is a courtyard pond that adds to the fresh natural feeling of the villa. Guests can enjoy the sights of the garden while eating at the dining room table. The eclectic mix of art and furnishing sets apart this remarkable olde-style house.
