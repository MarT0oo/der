About the Area

Nestled along Snapperman Beach along the foreshores of Pittwater in the Sydney suburb of Palm Beach, this luxury holiday home is located an hour's drive north of Sydney and is reachable by car, public transport and even by seaplane from Rose Bay. It's a beautiful area with lots to do.

There are great restaurants and shops in the surrounding areas. Golf courses are everywhere - Palm Beach, Mona Vale, Bayview, Elanora Heights and even a driving range in Warriewood. Tennis courts are also nearby. Remedial massage is a walk down the road. Ferries leave from Palm Beach jetty to take you across to the Basin, Mackerel Beach or over to Patonga, Pearl Beach and beyond to Wagstaff.

Dining out is at your disposal. There are many cafes, restaurants and eateries around the area. Try The Boathouse, Pronto's, The Greedy Goat. For dinner try Boulevarde Bistro, Starfish, Barrenjoey House, Palm Beach RSL, Palm Beach Golf Club, Jonah's, Dune's.
