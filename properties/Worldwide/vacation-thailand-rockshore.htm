Rock Shore Villa
4 Bedrooms | 4 Baths | Cliff View | Infinity Pool                                        
Phuket, Thailand, B.W.I.
      From $2000 - $3500 per night

This Rock Shores Villa is a 4 bedroom, 4 bathroom Villa located in north-west Phuket-home to the island most exclusive and stunning coastline.  The Villa is perched on headland between two of Phuket is most beautiful beaches, Surin and Laemsingh beaches.

Superlatives cannot properly describe the vista and understated elegance of the Villa in the Laem Singh Villas estate situated on the exclusive oceanfront headland between Surin and Laem Singh beach.  All levels offer well-designed private spaces allowing each guest to find their own personal sanctuary.  With three large bedrooms this retreat is ideal for couples or large groups who also want privacy and romance.

The Master Bedroom has direct access to the pool with private bathroom and sea view deck.  It is also feature bathrooms with bath tubs that literally open onto 20 M. lap pool off its deck.  Take stylish and elegant tropical architecture that showcases Phuket's finest ocean views and sunsets.  Other 2 suites are spacious and offer private sea view decks.

How could one possibly what it feels like to float at the edge of an infinity pool that seemingly straddles an ocean cliff.  And what goes through your mind when you crawl  out of your private bathtub into an oceanfront infinity pool? 

Rock Shores defines tropical style and sophistication with a 3 level open plan that offers,  extensive indoor and outdoor living centered around the cliff side infinity pools and stunning panoramic views.  It's truly fit for those wanting the ultimate in privacy and luxury services like a personal chef, in-house maid service and driver for airport transfers and short trips to the beach and market.

Our team has been specially selected to assist you achieve your Phuket Dream.  On arrival you will be met by staff eager to offer you their warmest Thai welcome. A cool towel to contrast the warmth of Phuket, A cold drinks as we transport you to your villa for the period of your visit and you will begin to feel it all happening.

A gentle smile and a traditional “Wai” from our staff as you enter the villa will confirm the honor of your visit. Throughout your stay the team will be there to help you with your need for peace and comfort. If your wish is to take shelter in the villa for a Day they will be there to welcome you in the morning, prepare your meals, set up your rooms in the day and turn down your bed in the evening to welcome you Back.
