Baan Orchid, Samui Beach Village, Koh Samui, Thailand Vacation Rental

 

Baan Orchid Luxury Villa is a charming two-bedroom Samui villa set in a beautiful tropical garden with its own private swimming pool. It is just a few metres away from one of the best and most secluded beaches in Koh Samui.

The heart of the Koh Samui villa is a large living space which includes lounge, dining and kitchen areas all neatly interlinked. To the rear are the two bedrooms, while the front features lush tropical gardens, sun-decking and private pool.

You enter the property from your private parking area, through the profusion of tropical trees and flowers. The palm trees are surrounded by lilies, jasmine, bougainvillea, birds of paradise, and of course the beautiful orchids after which the Koh Samui villa has been named.

In front of the Koh Samui villa is your private pool with a large sundeck set out with loungers and umbrellas ready for you to enjoy the tropical sunshine that abounds in Samui. To complete the picture you have your own private outdoor dining area close to the Samui beach villa.
The entrance leads you directly into the lounge, complete with comfortable seating and a home entertainment system including satellite TV and DVD/CD systems. The villa also has wireless internet available for those who still need to keep in touch with the outside world while enjoying this piece of tropical paradise.

The dining room is conveniently placed between the lounge and kitchen areas giving you easy access for entertaining and a feeling of space for relaxation.

The kitchen is fully equipped with all modern conveniences for food preparation including oven, microwave, fridge and freezer, blender, coffee machine and more. We also have our In House Chef available for a small fee if you would like to enjoy a five-star dining experience in the privacy of your own luxury villa.

We pride ourselves on the standard of comfort we provide throughout the Koh Samui luxury villa, from air conditioning and fans found in all rooms through to the quality of service assured by our villa manageress and staff.

The master bedroom has a queen-size bed, bedside cabinets and built-in wardrobes with convenient outdoor access.
There is also a large modern en-suite bathroom with bath with a separate rain shower for your enjoyment.
The guest bedroom is well appointed and again has it's own en-suite bathroom and built in wardrobe. The bed can be arranged either as two singles or a large king.

The living area, which extends across the front of the luxury villa, has open views over the garden and swimming pool.

The large patio doors lead out to the sundeck furnished with quality teak patio tables, chairs and sun loungers.

Adjoining this is your private swimming pool where you can relax and enjoy a refreshing dip to cool down from the hot tropical sunshine.

Baan Orchid Koh Samui villa, just a few metres away from the idyllic white sand beaches that stretch along the coastline in both directions, offers one of the most accessible luxury villa holidays on the island at a very affordable price.

Samui Beach Village also offers a unique hotel-style service for our guests. A team of 40 staff is on hand to provide the top quality service you would expect to find in a luxury 5 star resort. Unlike most villa rentals where the service is limited, we pride ourselves on giving our guests the best holiday experience possible. Samui Beach Village is a superb location for people looking to lose themselves in the beauty of Koh Samui's gorgeous beaches and lush tropical landscape. The village - which is set right by the beachside - is a small luxury living development close to the main tourist areas of Samui.

It adjoins an unspoiled stretch of sandy beach in the relatively undeveloped south-eastern coast, and has been built amid a profusion of coconut palms and tropical landscaped gardens. Two neighbouring islands - Koh Tan and Koh Mudsum - well-known for their fantastic fishing and snorkeling, are only a short, 20-minute boat ride away.

It is only ten minutes from Lamai Beach, 30 minutes from Chaweng and Samui's top golf course, and 35 minutes from the airport, so you are never far from the action. But while there is easy access to shopping, restaurants, nightlife and other activities, back in Samui Beach Village you will feel you are on your own tropical island paradise.
