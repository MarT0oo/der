Tamara Wonderland, Sydney, Australia Vacation Rental

 

A luxury property whose beauty is matched only by its beach front surrounds, Wonderland Tamarama is the ideal home for a family vacation or a long weekend with friends.

This architecturally stunning new home has been completed with the finest materials and designed to create a feeling of light, space and privacy. The entire top floor of this three storey home is dedicated to living and entertaining spaces. The centrepiece is a fully equipped kitchen featuring Gaggenau and Miele appliances, Sub-Zero fridge, induction stove and walk in pantry. The sunken living and dining rooms open out to a large deck with panoramic views over Tamarama Beach complete with undercover seating. This floor also features a powder room and a small television room opening to the pool deck.

In the bedroom level below you will find the master suite and three bedrooms as well as a laundry and media/rumpus room. The laundry has indoor and outdoor drying facilities and features Miele appliances and a pull out ironing board. The children’s retreat play area, includes a television with PlayStation and a piano.

Finally, there is a self-contained granny flat, featuring a King en-suite with full kitchen, laundry, and mini walk in wardrobe. This floor has its own private entrance from the street below and a direct path access to the set of stairs that takes you down to the beach.

Other features of this magnificent home include a private, fresh water heated pool with spa and a fully equipped pool cabana with bathroom so you don’t have to enter the home when wet. A private side walkway and entrance to the home takes you directly down to Tamarama Park and on to Tamarama Beach.

Waking up to the view of Tamarama Beach and water without leaving the bed is undoubtedly the owners’ favourite thing to do in this incredible home. When the day gets going, they love to make the most of the undercover entertaining area, cooking up a storm for friends and family with the sea breeze in the air and the pool beckoning.

Living in the lap of luxury affords guests plenty of privacy. No expense has been spared to find the best of the best for everything from the kitchen to the outdoor pool. In addition to top-of-the-range appliances, the home is tastefully finished with concrete, brass, sandstone, marble, timber floorboards, steel throughout.

Known as ‘Glamarama’ to locals, it won’t take you long to understand why. You are only a short stroll to this exclusive beach, and only a little further along the cliff top walkway to the restaurants and boutiques of Bondi Beach. Hire a car to venture 15 minutes into the centre of Sydney, or along the coast to the glamorous eastern suburbs including Rose Bay, Double Bay, and Vaucluse.
