7 Bedrooms | 7 Baths | Heated Pool
Marrakech, Morocco

This magnificent Moroccan villa is situated just outside of Marrakech (4 miles southwest) within 1 hectare (2.5 acres) of land, and has a living area of over 700 sq. m.  There are large living areas, both inside and outside, and it boasts a large heated swimming pool.  Your needs are catered for by the cook/housekeeper and there is also a full-time gardener/pool man.


This Beautiful Villa boasts a spacious garden full of olive trees, a large private pool and has a beautiful view of the Atlas Mountains.  You are just a 5 minute drive from the centre of Marrakech and also 2 of the best golf courses in the area, several more courses being planned.

The villa can accommodate 12 adults (plus 4 children) and in brief comprises:  2 Master bedrooms (en-suite), 2 Double bedrooms (en-suite), 2 Twin / Double bedrooms (en-suite), 1 Childrens bedroom with 2 bunk beds (can sleep 4 children with en-suite).  There is also a tV room and separate Sitting room, dining Room (to seat 16), large central atrium with, retractable glass roof, large over-flow swimming pool, extensive outside living and dining areas , PC & internet are also included in your stay.
