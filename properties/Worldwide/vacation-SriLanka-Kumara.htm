Kumara, Weligama, Sri Lanka Vacation Rental

 

Close to the surf breaks of Welligama and a short tuk tuk ride to the beaches of Mirissa this stunning three bedroom luxury villa is a peaceful escape.

Set in over 2 acres of landscape gardens with excellent staff including an in-house manager.

Immersed in dense Sri Lankan forestry, the secluded Villa Kumara is actually a great place for surf lovers. The surf break of Weligama and the beach at Mirissa are only few minutes away. Villa Kumara offers a very peaceful and private ambiance, suitable for couples and family reunions.

Villa Kumara has a total of two bedrooms and both are master suites. The rooms are furnished with king sized beds and other basic amenities, as well as gorgeous additions of walled garden and outdoor bathing facilities. Each bedroom has its own private terrace where guests can relax and take in the fresh tropical breeze.

Approximately 2 acres of landscaped gardens surround the villa. Guests can relax in the infinity pool while gently gazing across the jungle canopy to experience harmony between manmade luxury and the raw beauty of mother nature.

The villa is slightly elevated allowing those within to gaze across the canopy of surrounding coconut palms and to enjoy the cooler breeze drifting off the ocean. The surf break at Welligama is less than 3 minutes away by tuk tuk and is amongst the best for all standards, with lessons available.

The villa complex is made up of large open plan pavilions. The three king size bedroom pavilions are set in private walled gardens with indoor/ outdoor bathing facilities and large outdoor terraces. The main living pavilion has floor to ceiling bi-fold doors and bi fold windows that open onto tropical landscaped gardens and alarge decked pool area with a thatched pergola (ambalamba) perfect for just relaxing or watching local wildlife , birds and monkeys especially, in the early mornings and afternoons.

The house staff includes cooks who are able to prepare delicious local and international cuisine, a private guest manager to arrange outings and house maids for daily housekeeping.
