Bellevue House, Eastern Sydney, Sydney, Australia Vacation Rental

 

Nestled in Sydney’s exclusive eastern suburbs, Bellevue House is its best kept secret.

Replete with tasteful luxury furnishings, walk through the front door and you’re immersed in a homely warmth, something you don’t find in every mansion in this well-heeled area.

Enjoy entertaining in the entry level, which features a lounge, double kosher kitchen, sitting room, laundry, and outdoor garden area. Move upstairs to see the property’s four bedrooms, beginning with the Master bedroom, which features an en-suite and sitting balcony.

Three smaller bedrooms (a Double, Single, and Twin Single) share the second bathroom, and more importantly, an adjacent a rumpus room for the kids. The final bedroom holds two King Single beds with its own separate bathroom.

When you’re finished deciding on rooms, step outside and discover the finest Sydney has to offer. Explore beautiful Rose Bay, go swimming at the child-friendly Redleaf Beach, set off sailing from the Cruising Yacht Club of Australia, or play a round at the Woollahra Golf Club.

Situated five kilometres east of the central business district, and only three kilometres from the world-famous Bondi Beach, Bellevue House is set in the perfect location to get the most out of your Sydney vacation.

If you can get past the location, we love the luxury fit-out. No small thing has been left to chance, allowing you to find a comfortable space and enjoy a glass of your favourite vintage while the kids take a dip in the outdoor pool.

Located on a quiet leafy street, it is still welcoming for families and children of all ages. The property has been designed as a children’s paradise, with a trampoline and a rumpus room in addition to ensure that are never bored. Cooper Park is only a four minute walk, while only a few minutes more in the car until you arrive at the glittering coastal strip, featuring Bondi, Tamarama, and Bronte—or set off on the stunning Bondi to Coogee coastal walk and enjoy them all!
