Baan Chirawan, Cape Panwa, Phuket, Thailand


A luxury villa nestled atop the ultra exclusive Sri Panwa resort, located on a private peninsular on the beautiful southern tip of Phuket. The winner of numerous awards for style and luxury including being voted within the top 5 Thai resorts by Conde Nast. The villa boasts outstanding views over the sea to both the east and west giving you truly unforgettable sunrises and sunsets. Frequented by royalty, movie stars and business leaders Sri Panwa boasts such a full range of facilities you might not leave the resort. Harper’s Bazaar Travel Guide said it has “every amenity you could wish for”. To list a few there is a private beach, 3 world-class restaurants (Gordon Ramsey commented “the chefs in Sri Panwa are a class act and would be a huge success in London”), a luxury spa, easy access to secluded white sand beaches with warm turquoise waters, a beach bar, nightclub, cooking school, tennis courts and fully equipped gym. Two villa staff take care of your housekeeping and general requirements..

All five bedrooms are large and luxurious, fully equipped with both air conditioning and handmade teak ceiling fans. The mattresses are very high quality ensuring a wonderful night’s sleep. The linen is also the finest Egyptian cotton. They all have personal mini bar facilities and spacious marble finished bathrooms furnished with both large showers and double ended baths. In addition, three of the bedrooms also have an private open air bathroom. Four of the bedrooms have private balcony/terrace, two of the bedrooms also have a double sofa bed in addition to the main king-size bed. All have separate seating/sofa.  The bathrooms are designed in a clean and contemporary style. They are all very spacious and light with floor to ceiling opening doors and windows allowing the sun and cool winds to flow through. They are all marble lined, with solid marble sinks carved by hand.

Perched high above the ocean the villa gives you breathtaking sunrise and sunset panoramic views of the Andaman sea and surrounding islands, including Koh Phi Phi and Koh Lon. Located in one of Thailand's most prestigious and exclusive private estates on the beautiful Cape Panwa peninsula, everything you'll need is literally at your door.

The estate itself offers you everything you need, from tennis, fitness, spa, world-class dining options including Phukets award-winning Baba, frequented by celebrities such as Gordon Ramsey, and even a private beach.

Leisure activities include, bird watching, jet skiing, climbing, snorkeling, caving, and whitewater rafting.
