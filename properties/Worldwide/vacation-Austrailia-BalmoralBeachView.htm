Balmoral Beach View, Mosman, Sydney, Australia Vacation Rental

 

The stylish, leafy and contemporary front entrance of Balmoral Beach View is merely a small taste of the divine home that’s hidden from sight. As soon as you approach the front door, you won’t want to leave this property. Panoramic views of Balmoral Beach dominate the expansive main living level. Indeed, if you don’t lose yourself in the views, you’re likely to do so in the superior features of this Popov Bass designed home: curved walls, columns, underfloor heating, internal lift, home automation system and meticulous styling.

In the living room there’s plenty of seating and with those ocean and garden views it’s hard not to feel relaxed. That relaxing vibe extends to the balcony where you have dedicated dining and entertaining zones. Back inside there’s also a large dining table which sits adjacent to the sleek, contemporary kitchen. Corian benchtops, high quality appliances and ocean views are sure to give you the motivation to whip up a scrumptious meal - but deciding on just one eye-catching spot to sit will prove challenging! You can even head down the staircase off the balcony and enjoy your meal by the outdoor pool.

Everything has been thought of in this home. On the lower ground floor, not only do you have direct access to the pool and landscaped gardens but you have a cellar, steam room, media room, break out area, bedroom and kitchenette. While on the third floor three bedrooms have been carefully designed to make the most of the spectacular vista. All bedrooms have ensuites while the master bedroom also has a large walk in robe, private balcony and free standing bath.

So if you enjoy the finer things in life this is the perfect luxury accommodation for you.

About the Area

Balmoral Beach is only a few minutes’ walk where you’ll also find popular beachside cafes and restaurants. You could stop for a coffee at Balmoral Boat House or for a meal at Public Dining Room or The Bathers Pavilion are a good start. If you’re a keen shopper, Mosman Village is close by. Accessing public transport and exploring the CBD is easy with a bus stop just five minutes away, while Taronga Zoo is only a short bus trip. There’s also various walking trails nearby where you can enjoy beautiful harbour views.
