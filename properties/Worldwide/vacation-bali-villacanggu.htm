Villa Canggu, Canggu Resort, Bali, Indonesia


Designed to maximise the natural light, and catch the cool breezes even at the hottest time of year, Villa Canggu, with four luxurious bedrooms and a spectacular 20 x5m swimming pool, offers a blend of air-conditioned comfort and tropical open-air living.

Nestled just 50 metres from the beach, there are also numerous relaxation areas, so guests will always find a private spot for peace and quiet. In fact, a large family or group of people could stay at the villa and still lose each other within its depths. In addition to this, guests have the choice of a number of different eating areas, all of which present a unique atmosphere.

Guests have full access to the prestigious Canggu Club for the duration of their stay. The Canggu Club is Bali’s premier international members club, providing first class facilities that include a Restaurant, Bar and Lounge , Library , Kids Club , Tennis courts ( flood lit and indoor) Air-conditioned Squash courts, 25meter swimming pool, Gym fitness and aerobics centre , Indoor sports hall, Sauna, and a Billiard lounge.
