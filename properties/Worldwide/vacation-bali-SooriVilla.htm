Soori Villa, Tabanan, Indonesia Vacation Rental

 

Designed by the prestigious Singaporean architect Chan Soo Khian, Soori Villa is an elite beach house located on the black sands of Bali’s beautiful Tabanan shore. This villa boasts three plush guest suites with space for six visitors and is awash in luxury amenities for them to enjoy. Experience Bali, Indonesia’s greatest bastion of culture and art while staying in the unparalleled Soori Villa!

Jump into the villa’s chic infinity swimming pool, or lounge around on the furnished cozy terrace next to the alfresco dining area and grill. Guests are also offered the chance to use a sleek new Apple TV, not to mention video games, a Bose sound system, iPod docks, as well as a spa and fitness facility. Both the villa and guests are looked after by a professional staff consisting of a butler, security guard and housekeeper.

The great room of Soori Villa is both generous in size and character, the result of a combination of a contemporary decor scheme juxtaposed with intricate though understated Indonesian objets d’art. Sliding glass walls allow for the splendor of the outdoors to be seen inside, as well as facilitating an ease of access to the gardens, pool and terrace areas. The dining area has a long and elegant rustic wooden table with room for fourteen creating an undeniably sophisticated atmosphere.

Soori Villa’s three en-suite bedrooms have been adorned with the bold color scheme found in the rest of the home, but with additional small accents of color that produce an especially dramatic effect. Two rooms boast plush king size beds while the third room has been equipped with two twin beds. Additionally, each room has a balcony with ocean views and the bathrooms have been stocked with Spa Alila natural toiletries.

Bali has been described by many as an island intoxicated with the arts, and this statement can be easily proven in any one of its many art galleries, such as nearby Subak Museum. Kelating Pan Pacific Resort is an elite resort providing the opportunity for a relaxing eighteen holes or a rousing game of tennis. For those who crave the hustle and bustle of lively streets, excellent dining and ample shopping, cosmopolitan Seminyak is the place to go!
Located on Bali’s south-western coast, Soori Bali is nestled within the picturesque Tabanan Regency, where volcanic mountains loom above verdant rice terraces, and striking black-sand beaches delve gently into the Indian Ocean.

Soori Bali lies 25 minutes away from the majestic Tanah Lot Temple, one of Bali's most sacred and venerated temples. The resort can be reached in a little over an hour from Ngurah Rai International Airport.
