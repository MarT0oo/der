Australia has always offered a bit of curiosity to the average traveler.  It is one of the most beautiful continents for its natural beauty, famous beaches, and colorful locals.  Nowhere else in the world can you see so much of the original history of a continent come to life, with so many locals still inhabiting the area.  It’s truly a place people love, most people visiting areas such as Sydney in New South Wales, Brisbane in Queensland, Melbourne in Victoria, Perth in Western Australia and Darwin in the Northern Territory. 

When most people think about travel to Sydney they think about the fact that it’s the largest city in Australia, located in New South Whales this area offers one of the most diverse and Big City experiences you will never forget.  If visiting Sydney don’t forget to visit Bondi Beach for the amazing white sands, Manly Beach for proximity to local shops, Woolong for it’s amazing views and topography, and Byron Bay famous for its whale watching and diverse cultural center.  These are just some of the great beaches, additionally people love to go to the Sydney Festival in January, attend the Film Festival or enjoy the Sydney Opera House.

Traveling to Queensland you have the City of Brisbane, a beautiful city filled with amazing nightlife, restaurants and museums, but additionally Queensland offers some of the Best Beach Tourism around.  Most people flock to Cairns, where you’ll find the area beaches and local shops, dining, activities to be very family friendly.  With Machans & Holloways Beaches for the quiet and those who love fishing, Trinity Beach popular for golden sands and family friendly atmosphere, Kewarra Beach for its resort style, Ellis Beach for the seclusion and why most chose this as a wedding destination.  In the area you also have Port Douglas, where families gather in beachfront villas year long to experience the crystal seas and beautiful atmosphere.  A place that attracts A listers, it’s truly a place to get away from it all.

When traveling to Victoria, most love to experience the nightlife and coastal beaches.  Melbourne offers the best draw and is the Capital of Victoria.  Melbourne is now well known and respected for its arts, commerce, education, sports and tourism.  Melbourne is also known to be a financial center in Australia, offering those who move their for business quite lucky indeed.  It is home to Ford and Toyota manufacturing plants as well a banking giant.  When getting around in the area you will also find a very intricate train system, which will make getting around both more affordable and convenient. 

If going to Southern Australia, take note of: Regions:  Adelaide Hills, Barossa Valley, Clare Valley, Eyre Peninsula, Fleurieu Peninsula, Flinders Ranges, Kangaroo Island, Limestone Coast Nullarbor Plain, Riverland, Yorke Peninsula.  Or Try: Adelaide, the capital of Southern Australia where you will find great shopping, entertainment, and history.  If going to Western Australia don’t miss out on going nature hiking and catch the fabulous array of birds, plants, and beautiful flowers. This is the perfect area for nature treks.  Some cities to consider are: Margaret River and Blackwood Valley.  If visiting The Northern Territory than you must visit Darwin, where you’ll find a city filled with young and vibrant residents, beautiful streets, modern architecture, amazing museums, historical sites, and the home of the Darwin Festival every year.
