Royal Davui Island Resort, Viti Levu Island, Beqa Lagoon, Southern coast of Fiji


Royal Davui Island Resort is a luxury Fijian experience. This exclusive adults only resort is situated in the Beqa Lagoon, off the Southern coast of Fiji. With only 16 Vales in total Royal Davui Island Resort ensures ultimate privacy and relaxation as there are never more than 32 guests on the island at any one time. The Beqa Lagoon and nearby Beqa Island are a very special part of Fiji, famous to the Fijians as the home to the Fire Walkers of Beqa Island. A unique breed of Fijians whom over time have been known for their abilities to walk over fire and hot coals.

This Fiji accommodation is the ultimate romantic getaway, whether it be for your wedding, honeymoon, anniversary, birthday or to share a wonderful Fijian experience with someone special - this is the island for you. All meals are included in your nightly tariff, the Banyan Restaurant & Bar serves sensational seafood and cuisine focused on the seasonal local produce daily. In addition to this, you may have your meals where you please, if you feel like having a picnic lunch on the beach, the Royal Davui staff can happily assist and this is still included in your nightly room tariff just as if you were eating lunch at the restaurant. Royal Davui Island Resort caters especially to romance, all rooms feature king beds, plunge pools, sun decks, double showers and spa baths and all non-motorised water activities such as snorkelling, hobie cat sailing and kayaking are complimentary.

Royal Davui Island Resort does not cater for children under 17 years of age.

There are 16 exquisitely appointed vales nestled in the lush tropical gardens, each with private plunge pool. The sun decks and plunge pools face the sea and are not overlooked by any other vale or common buildings on the resort

Island Plunge Pool Vale (4 Available): Located on the southern side of Davui Island, Island Vales enjoy views over white sand beach and Fijian Rock Fishtrap. Bedding is 1 King. Maximum room capacity is 2 Adults.

Deluxe Plunge Pool Vale (5 Available): Nestled on the eastern side of Davui Island, Deluxe Vales enjoy stunning views of Beqa Island or Beqa Barrier Reef. All Deluxe Vales are split level with open plan design. Bedding is 1 King. Maximum room capacity is 2 Adults.

Premium Plunge Pool Vale (6 Available): Features 2 large verandahs that as an extension of living area. Overlooks the Beqa Lagoon. Bedding is 1 King. Maximum room capacity is 3 Adults.

Royal Davui Plunge Pool Suite (1 Available): Very spacious with a small staircase that leads you to the large deck overlooking the Beqa Lagoon. Hidden paradise feel complete with indoor garden. Bedding is 1 King. Maximum room capacity is 2 Adults.

In-Room Dining: All meals are available for dining in your room, where the attentive staff can do a full pre-dining set up on the sun deck off your lounge, and then leave you to dine in ultimate privacy. Please Note: In-room dining service caters for meal times, breakfast, lunch and dinner.

Banyan Restaurant & Bar: Enjoy long lazy lunches under the shade of the Banyan Trees. Using the finest seasonal ingredients with strong emphasis on exceptionable local seafood, the Royal Davui Island Restaurant is open daily for breakfast, lunch and dinner, with fresh variety of fish featured on the menu daily.

Dining Experiences: Royal Davui staff can provide you with unique dining experience with private dinners for two or laid-back picnics set up on the beach or various resort locations of your choice.

Whether you are new to scuba diving or are a seasoned professional, Royal Davui Island has you covered. Beginners can partake in the Discover Scuba introductory course which begins with learning skills in the comfortable environment of the resort swimming pool with qualified PADI instructor. If you have dived before but are a bit rusty, there are also refresher courses also available. For the avid divers Beqa Lagoon is known for wide range of dive sites and dive styles in one location, dive pristine reef straight off the shore, soft coral caves, ship wrecks, deep dives, night dives and for the brave Big Fish Encounter dive with sharks.

Royal Davui Island Resort offers three styles of wedding celebrations, eloping, traditional or fijian. Ceremonies can be held on the pristine white sand beach or other location of your choice. Eloping is a casual intimate affair that can be witnessed by just one Royal Davui staff member. Traditional and Fijian wedding arrangements offer additional inclusions and encourage your family and friends to come and join in the unique tropical wedding experience. For pricing and details on Wedding Packages please contact us.

DISTANCE FROM AIRPORT: 20 minute fixed wing aircraft flight to Pacific Harbour followed by 30 minute water taxi transfer (Twice daily schedule basis booked with Royal Davui Reservations)
