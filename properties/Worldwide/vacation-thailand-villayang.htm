Villa Yang
4 Bedrooms | 4 Ensuite Baths | Ocean View | Infinity Pool              
Kamala, Phuket Island, Thailand
      From $10465 – $26950 per wk

Rejuvenate your body and soul in complete seclusion and privacy at Villa Yang. Situated on Kamala headland’s water edge, this spectacular half-acre site overlooks the breathtaking turquoise waters of the Andaman Sea.

Classic, soft-lined Thai design is mixed with the hard, crisp lines of modern design and luxury décor. Sleek materials such as dark hardwood terracing, marble and granite highlight the unique design statements introduced throughout the property: plate glass vanities and shower screens with pebble and bamboo inclusions, iridescent beaded bathtubs and glass stairways.

Villa Yang is spread over four different levels affording sea views from almost every room. The top entrance level, with reception and sea view sitting area, leads down to the second level where the large open-plan Minotti kitchen, dining and living area is situated. This indoor space opens onto the lounge deck that fronts a 17-meter infinity pool which seemingly teeters on the edge of the Andaman Sea. Down one more level are Bedroom 2, 3 and 4, each with a private en-suite and sea view deck. Further stairs lead down to the bottom level where the large, ultra private Master Bedroom (Bedroom 1) with en-suite with full bathtub and two private seafront decks surrounded by lush tropical gardens.
