Lilly, Koh Samui, Suratthani, Thailand Vacation Rental

 

Lilly has recently undergone extensive renovations and features 6 very large private en-suite bedrooms as well as a plethora of social spaces. The villa is an entertainers dream with air-conditioned lounge and dining area opening up onto an expansive pool deck with spectacular views of our coral cove.

A walled garden area is perfect for family picnics and the large infinity-edge pool is the star of the show. Centrally located on the estate, Lilly is a delightful walk down to the beach and overlooks the Samujana tennis court. The Master Bedroom, perched on top of the villa, commands the best views and features a walk-in closet, designer bathroom and private sun deck.

About The Area

Samujana’s name comes from the blend of Koh ‘Samui’ and the word ‘Jana’, meaning coral in Moroccan, due to its stunning hillside location overlooking a coral cove, with private beach access and uninterrupted views of Samui.

Samujana’s definition is very simple – 25 charming villas from 1 to 8 bedrooms, all with oversized private infinity pools, modern Thai architecture and breath-taking views, 5 star hotel services and details that make the world of differences, the perfect place for friends and family to gather and have a great time.

Samujana prides itself on providing an unparalleled luxury experience with our motto being, “Your Place. Your Time.” Each Villa comes automatically with its private Villa Manager and villa maid, state-of-the art leisure facilities and the latest in audio and media amenities, your private villa is your HOME away from home and if you really fancy having your own private chefs, you need just to ask and will take care of the rest.

For fun beyond our villas, Samujana offers an extensive range of land and water experiences. From boating excursions on our private sailboat to chauffeured tours of the beautiful island and waterfalls, our Experience Manager will help curate a bespoke adventure for you and your guests.
