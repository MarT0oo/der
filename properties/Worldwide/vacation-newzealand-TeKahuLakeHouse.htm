Te Kahu Lake House, Wanaka, New Zealand Vacation Rental

 

“Te Kahu Lake House is nestled into its environment at perfect peace with its 50-acre surroundings of tussock and natural bush. The home has timber cedar cladding inside out and blends beautifully with the outstanding natural landscape upon which it sits. All bedrooms have beautiful schist, tile and cedar ensuite bathrooms. Te Kahu holds an interesting collection of fine New Zealand art and a mix of interesting objects and furniture from the world over. Te Kahu won the New Zealand home of the year for architecture.”
