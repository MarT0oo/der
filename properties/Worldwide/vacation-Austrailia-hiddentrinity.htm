Hidden Trinity Treasure
4 Bedrooms | 3 Baths | Saltwater Infinity Pool                                  
Trinity Beach, Queensland, Australia    
      From AUD $400 per night

As you refresh in the cool infinity pool enjoy panoramic vistas of the beach and ocean below.   Designed by renowned architect Gabrielle Poole to embrace the tropical climate, the Hidden Trinity Luxury features two pavilions accommodating up to eight guests.   The master pavilion includes two levels featuring the kitchen, lounge and dining areas upstairs and the master bedroom with en-suite and entertainment lounge downstairs overlooking the pool.  Retreat to the back garden to find the second pavilion with three bedrooms and two bathrooms.

The gardens are beautiful and lush and highlight the stunning environment of the tropical north.  Take a short stroll down the garden path by the pool to the beach.
