Marrakech luxury villa with pool - Set on the outskirts of Marrakech but just a 15-minute taxi ride from the bustle of the centre, Villa Hana offers you the height of luxury and style.You will love the spacious atrium with its retractable glass roof, the beautiful views of the Atlas Mountains, the stunning infinity-style heated pool, the courtyards and mature gardens, and the vibrant Moroccan furnishings - all of which make this a very special villa indeed. Villa Hana is fully staffed, so you can take the opportunity to really unwind. The housekeeper will prepare breakfast and either lunch or dinner for you, organise any grocery shopping and make restaurant reservations as you require.

Marrakech is Morocco's second-largest city, an exclusive cultural destination. Often referred to as "the Red City" for the red ochre walls that surround it, the palaces, fountains and mosques all reflect its thousand-year-old history. The famous Jemaa-el-Fna square and market place in the medina (old city) is unforgettable.

If you want to explore a little further afield from this Marrakech luxury villa with pool, memorable day trips could include visits to the Toubkal National Park and Morocco's highest mountain peak, the Ouzoud waterfalls, the Ourika valley, Ouarzazate or the sandy beaches on the coast at Essaouira ... you'll be spoilt for choice! And of course, this Marrakech luxury villa with pool is very conveniently situated for activities such as quad biking, trekking, climbing and paragliding, which are all available in this area
