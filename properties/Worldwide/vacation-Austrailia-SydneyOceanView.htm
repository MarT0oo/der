Sydney Ocean View, Dover Heights, Sydney, Australia Vacation Rental

 

Situated atop Dover Heights’ romantic cliffs, this ocean-facing property whispers tranquility.

Enjoy unobstructed ocean views from almost every vantage point, whether over morning coffee at the breakfast bar, enjoying dinner on the sunken deck, or winding down with a glass of wine on the alfresco top deck as the day fades before your eyes.

Architecture by the wonderful Walter Barda and the interior design by captures the essence of the location, as polished dark hardwood floors are offset by sleek white furnishings with waves of neutral colour, combining the finest in nature and luxury. The contemporary Australian motif is completed with artwork by the late artist Brett Whiteley, whose intricate surrealism was influenced by his mentor Francis Bacon.

Guests will love the stone-fenced yard, along with the rumpus room and studio lounge room with open deck backlit by the eastern facing ocean vista. Upstairs the Master bedroom features an open en-suite with freestanding bath that also looks out across the Pacific, and three smaller bedrooms share the second bathroom. The Queen bedroom is situated downstairs with its own separate bathroom.

You could cook up a storm with state-of-the-art kitchen appliances and a sound system to match—but if you’re not in the mood, eat out, or dine in. Only a short ride to some of eastern Sydney’s finest restaurants, such as Catalina Rose Bay and Bondi Icebergs, or if you can’t pull yourself away from the view, have our concierge arrange for a top Sydney chef to create a personal experience for you without leaving the house.

The stunning architectural design puts nature on a pedestal and caters to all of the senses. Mornings are for the radiant sunlight highlighting the features of the property, while afternoons allow for you to turn off the air conditioning and enjoy the sea breeze drifting through and cooling the house naturally.

Take the five kilometre Federation Cliff Walk from Watsons Bay to Dover Heights, along the sandstone cliffs with panoramic views across the most sought over part of the city. Come down from up high to explore the serenity of the area’s small, secret beaches, without having to battle the crowds at Bondi each day.
