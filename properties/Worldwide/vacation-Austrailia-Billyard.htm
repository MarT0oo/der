Billyard, Elizabeth Bay, Sydney, Australia Vacation Rental

 

The Billyard is a spectacular contemporary apartment in Elizabeth Bay filled with natural light and intimate views over the water this serene residence also features a unique private harbour pool which guests can enjoy – all within minutes of the Sydney CBD.

This expansive two bedroom. two bathroom apartment features interior architecture by BKH and interior furnishings by Briony Fitzgerald who’s soft colour palate works in harmony with the ever changing colour of the harbour below.

The open plan living and dining areas open out to a large outdoor terrace – the perfect spot to enjoy your morning coffee as the sun rises over the harbour. There is a separate study area, guest powder room and gourmet kitchen equipped with everything for guests who love to cook at home – there are great local produce markets at nearby Kings Cross every Saturday morning and gourmet providores and deli’s in Potts Point to get all of your supplies.

The master queen bedroom features an en-suite with separate shower and bath with massage jets with the second queen size bedroom featuring and en-suite with shower.
