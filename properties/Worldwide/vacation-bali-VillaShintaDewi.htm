Villa Shinta Dewi, Seminyak, Bali, Indonesia Vacation Rental

 

Located within a secure complex of luxury villas in the heart of Seminyak’s fashionable Petitenget area, 500 metres from the beach and a short stroll from a wide choice of restaurants and fashion boutiques, Villa Shinta Dewi is a beautifully designed, four-bedroom villa that oozes with charm. Built around an 11-metre swimming pool and overflowing with tropical flowers, it is the perfect space for achieving total relaxation. The villa is fully staffed with a manager, private chef and personal butlers, and furnished to the highest levels of comfort. This is an ideal place to spend time with family or friends.

Located in the exclusive Laksmana Estate, just a few hundred metres from Petitenget Beach, four-bedroomed Villa Shinta Dewi is a peaceful and elegant retreat, shaded by sweet scented frangipani trees. The villa is within walking distance of the many boutiques, restaurants, beachclubs, nightclubs and spas of Bali’s hip and happening Seminyak area.

Villa Shinta Dewi is a contemporary expression of Bali-style, filled with comfortable, sophisticated furnishings, rich fabrics and all of the modern facilities needed to create a sumptuous holiday home. This predominantly two-storey property comprises three separate buildings positioned around an 11-metre swimming pool, showcasing smooth creamy-white stone, shingle roofs and polished marble bathrooms. A compact but well-equipped gym adjoins the master suite, which incorporates an air-conditioned bedroom with a king-size bed, a study/TV room that can be used as a fifth bedroom, and a glamorous bathroom fitted with a jacuzzi soaking tub and an outdoor rain shower. The remaining three bedrooms, equipped with TVs and DVD players, are housed within a two-storey sleeping pavilion. Living areas at Shinta Dewi include a casual open-sided lounge overlooking the pool and garden, as well as a wonderful, sunset lounge, which is also open-sided with a polished hardwood floor under a soaring roof. Next to this is a small rooftop terrace bordered by colourful bougainvillea plants, while below is a gourmet kitchen in cool shades of white and an open-sided dining room furnished with a table for twelve.

Villa Shinta Dewi is great for families or groups of friends and comes with a full team of highly professional staff, including a manager, personal butlers and a superb in-house chef.
