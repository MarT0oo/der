Alpha Villa, Tambol Bophut, Ko Samui, Thailand Vacation Rental

 

Spacious open plan interiors This villa is an excellent example of modern tropical architecture, built on three separate levels to maximize space, surrounded by carefully tended lawns and gardens. The main living, dining and kitchen area opens directly into the garden; the entire space drawing in the scenery and ocean breezes. This level also boasts a huge AV room that almost spans the length of the villa, complete with a home theatre system, large sofa and scatter cushions to relax on.

Private relaxation areas
As well as communal spaces to relax, there are individual areas that provide the perfect opportunity for a bit of me time and some pure relaxation. The lower level bedrooms all have their own private garden terraces, and the double guest bedroom occupies its own private corner overlooking the garden.

Dining with a view
A carefully considered design means guests can choose to dine al fresco on the pool terrace or in indoor air-conditioned comfort, without ever losing sight of the spectacular tropical surroundings.

Stunning infinity pool and gardens
Daytime pleasures centre around the large infinity pool, which literally pours into the view, set in the villaâ€™s beautifully tended gardens, and large sand washed sun terrace.

A perfect night's sleep
The spacious double master bedroom connects to the pool terrace and therefore benefits from breath-taking views. This attractive room also boasts an indoor and an outdoor bathroom en-suite, the latter with an indulgent tub and rain shower. A double and a twin guest room share a bathroom, making them ideal for a family, while a separate, second master suite features its own spacious bathroom with glass walled rain shower and skylight.

Local surroundings; beach and much more
Alpha Villa is located in Choeng Mon, the top East-Northern corner of the island, and is ideally situated; it is only 5 minutes from the beach and 10 minutes from the main commercial centre of the Koh Samui.
