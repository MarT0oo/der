Baan Flora is a lovely Luxury Samui Beach Villa with four bedrooms and five bathrooms, set in large landscaped gardens with spacious sundecks and a large private swimming pool. The villa nestles in a secluded and very private position within Samui Beach Village, a new gated luxury beach villa development in the south-east of Koh Samui.

The large living space of this luxury beach villa includes a comfortable lounge with TV/music entertainment centre, a dining room and kitchen areas all neatly interlinked. The bedrooms are situated to the rear, while at the front are tropical gardens, sun-decking and your own private pool, beyond which lies the beach itself.

The property is entered from the private gated parking area at the end of the small road leading to the beach. The entrance to the luxury beach villa is next to the large sun deck which also surrounds the very ample private swimming pool. The entrance leads you directly into the lounge, overlooking the pool and beach, with an adjacent dining area and modern kitchen facility to the rear.

The dining room is conveniently sited between the lounge and kitchen areas. The kitchen is fully equipped with all the modern conveniences for food preparation, including large fridge/freezer and microwave oven. We also have a gourmet Chef available for an additional charge if you would like to a enjoy 5-star dining experience within your own Koh Samui beach villa.

We pride ourselves on the standard of comfort we provide throughout the beach villa, from air conditioning and fans found in all rooms through to the quality of service assured by our villa manageress and staff.

Samui Beach Village is a superb location for people looking to lose themselves in the beauty of Koh Samui's gorgeous beaches and lush tropical landscape. The village - which is set right by the beachside - is a small luxury living development close to the main tourist areas of Samui.
