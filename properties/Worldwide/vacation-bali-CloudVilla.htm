Cloud Villa, Pantai Pasut, Tabanan, Bali, Indonesia Vacation Rental

 

This ultra-modern architecture consists of 5 massive geometric shapes, creating a futuristic and sleek one of a kind villa. This villa is like a great white floating cloud which is reflected in the turquoise water of the infinity pool.

The outdoor space includes a large garden area designed for weddings, and other special events that can accommodate more than 50 guests. The spherical shape of the villa creates an extraordinary feeling of harmony and peace

Precious solid wood and a variety of marble is found throughout the Cloud Villa that includes three bedrooms, four bathrooms, living room, kitchen, and dining area. The graceful staircase, soaring like a bird, will lift you up to the master bedroom which offers a magnificent view of the Indian ocean. A huge double bed, lounge area with chairs made of old teak wood, art objects made of onyx, bedside tables made from 15-million-year-old petrified wood completes the master bedroom. The five-meter high ceiling features custom lighting by the designers of the home and includes floating lights with hundreds of petals that makes for a truly unique bedroom atmosphere for you to slumber in the sky.

Cloud villa has two bedrooms located on the ground floor. Floor to ceiling windows offer views of themed gardens and private terraces. One bedroom includes works of art created by nature itself and the designers of the home; The dressing room and bathroom features the root of a giant tree that transforms into sculptures that are reminiscent of the fantastic flower.

Featuring Concierge Manager, full housekeeping, Butler, restaurant by menu, and unique spa by menu offered in a refined concept boutique hotel sensation. The futuristic design and architecture complete the top class of service.
