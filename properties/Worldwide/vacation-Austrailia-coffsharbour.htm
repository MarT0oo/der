Bedroom Two and Three....Suite with king bed, en-suite with shower, deep soak bath, air-conditioning, TV, ceiling fan, walk in robe.

Bedroom Four....Suite with king-split bed, en-suite with shower, free-standing bath, air-conditioning, TV, ceiling fan, walk in robe.

Bedroom Five...Three single king beds. Bathroom, ceiling fan, minibar refrigerator, TV. Although attached to the house, please note this room is not suitable for younger children due to the separate entry (not directly accessible from inside the house).
