Visiting New South Wales

When people thing of NSW in Australia they normally think of Sydney, rich in Culture and activities, Sydney is the Mecca of all Travel for City and Beach fun in Australia.  The culture is filled with amazing arts, financial institutions, as well as rich cities that are brimming over with nightlife.  This is truly the heart of New South Wales and offers visitors the chance to experience a city down under. 

The places most love to visit are:  Bondi Beach (Filled with exciting Boardwalks, fun nighttime clubbing, and amazing eateries), Manly (where you’ll be away from it all in no time, catch the Manly Ferry and discover paradise on this amazing island), Sydney Harbour Bridge (an amazing sight, people from far and near have loved this beautiful piece of architecture).  You can also discover the city center and the Sydney Olympic Park, it’s truly just a fabulous city to venture around in.  You’ll never tire of this place.

Other areas to consider are: Canberra (For its amazing architecture), Albury and Surrounds (for the Murray River), Yass and Surrounds (for its hiking trails and amazing views), and Armidale (for its cultural richness).  NSW offers some of the best holiday rentals, from Villas, to cottages, and luxury apartments.
