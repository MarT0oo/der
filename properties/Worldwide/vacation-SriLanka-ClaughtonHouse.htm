Claughton House, Tangalla, Sri Lanka Vacation Rental

 

Perched on a hill top amidst 8 acres of walled-in gardens Claughton House, designed by Geoffrey Bawa, is exceptional. 5 ensuite air conditioned bedrooms (one of which doubles as TV / Games room).

Claughton House rests atop a hill in Tangalla, set within a generous 8 acres of gardens. It enjoys the perfect location for a getaway, with magnificent views of the ocean and the gorgeous vista beyond. Guests at Claughton House also have access to a private sandy white beach, with plenty of opportunities to swim and surf as well.

There are three suites in Claughton House- a master suite and two junior suites. Each of these well-appointed suites is air conditioned, attached to ensuite bathrooms and has fantastic ocean and garden views. The suites are dressed in a colour palette of classic white with black trimmings, and evoke a modern, bright and airy ambience. The master suite has a King sized bed, whilst the junior suites have twin beds. The master suite and one of the junior suites also have a private garden, whilst the second junior suite has a built in bathroom. There is also an additional sofa bed in one of the suites.

Claughton House has plenty of living space, and the open plan dining area seats six, and overlooks the ocean, garden, as well as the pool. For alfresco dining, guests can choose to dine at the pool side dining pavilion, which also has BBQ facilities. To enjoy a spot of relaxation and rest, there is plenty of lounging space in the garden, on sun loungers by the pool, or in the covered yoga sala.
