Villa Ambra, Mengwi, Bali Vacation Rental

 

Villa Ambra exemplifies the paragon of a luxury holiday in a location that is an inbred element of traditional Balinese rural life. The villa has the best acclimatization for Bali’s fabled sunsets. On one side of it is the beach, on the other hand, is a river sanctuary, and inland are the beautiful rice fields.

It is an apotheosis of restful and peaceful living. One can wake up to the sound of the surf, and forge ahead onto the balcony to experience the ever-shifting frontiers of an inlet lagoon as the sea meets the waves, etching through the sand and redefining its way with the changing of the tides.

Guests can explore a plethora of relaxation areas, each one blessed with a spectacular view. Enjoy watching the fishermen working on the sea, watch the birds from a breezy verandah, or simply rest on a sun-lounger and allowing the wonderful staff to serve you excellent food and drinks. You can also intersperse with a few laps in the gleaming swimming pool and later gaze at a crimson sunset. An additional bonus is a private spa that offers a complete rejuvenation and high treatment. It is a paradise on earth.

The villa is stunning, with a large beach frontage and uninterrupted full sea views from all rooms. The decor is faultless and clearly the villa has been appointed to the standard of a luxurious home rather than just a luxury rental. The grounds are extensive and beautifully maintained and our favourite moment was sitting on the enormous sunset sofa (right on the beach), canapés and cocktails at hand,watching the gorgeous sunset. But what really sets this villa apart is the level of service provided, which was beyond what would be expected at a five star resort.
