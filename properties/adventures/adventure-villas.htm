Country House Cefalicchio
Location Beds Baths Sleeps Weekly Low Weekly High Weekly Peak
Canosa di Puglia 12 12 28 €7,000 €9,000Inquire

The Villa of Cefalicchio was originally built in the 18th century as a farmhouse, a “masseria”. In 1904, thirty years after it was bought by the Rossi family, it was turned into a country residence as a gift from the Rossi brothers to their mother, Antonietta Massari. The house is a typical example of the architecture of country villas dating from the period of King Umberto I and testifies to the predominating taste in the region between the last decades of the 19th and the beginning of the 20th centuries.

Visiting Bali

Bali is a one of the most friendly beach location in Indonesia, this small island is to some the heart of Indonesia, bringing in tourism from all over the world.  People come for the beautiful beaches, gorgeous modern villas, and the amazing hospitality.  Those who travel to Bali like to visit: Seminyak (where you’ll find amazing state of the art vacation rentals), Canggu (where the beaches are sandy and the area romantic), Sanur, Lovina, Nusa Dua, and Jimbaran. 

There are very beautiful homes to rent in this location, most people travel for the beaches, make sure to check your travel reports for any travel warnings to this region.
