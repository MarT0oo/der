If you have never experienced the beautiful Sunsets over Camps Bay or heard the sights and the sounds of Johannesburg, than you are missing out on some of the best in Villa Destination Travel in the World.  Not only is South Africa offering some of the best homes in the world for rental, but also offering some of the best pricing.  You can easily rent a home in Plettenberg Bay Garden Route, for the same price of a regular hotel in any country in the world.  It’s truly a magical place to visit for dozens of reasons.

Some Great Destinations include: Kruger National Park – A wonderful Safari Destination, where you can watch the wildlife with a qualified tour guide.  Cape Town/Camps Bay – A place where the beaches are sandy, there are great hiking trails, and amazing whale watching.  Sun-City – Where you’ll find corporate gatherings and people coming with friends for golfing and gambling which happens to be their premiere attraction.  Garden Route – Considered one of the most Family Friendly areas to travel, this areas main draw is the beach, sun and fun.  From Plettenberg Bay and it’s amazing beach homes, cottages, and oceanfront mansions to the Beachyhead locations and the Robberg Beach area just to name a few. 

No matter what you do in South Africa, always remember to pack for the weather which can range from Tropical to Cool depending on the seasons.  The Holiday Season during December/January usually have the highest population density, so book early.
