Our Responsible Charity Giving Programs:

 

Every year we donate to various charities throughout our Nation.  We try and choose those programs that actively make a difference in people’s lives, keep with high moral standards, and contribute at least 80% of their revenues to the charity.  We would like our client’s to know and anyone visiting that is it important to us as a company to leave this world better than we found it.

Please help us in supporting our charities and we will strive to do our best to keep our act of giving alive.  It only takes a moment to help in promoting positive changes worldwide, and in our Great Nation.  We believe that every dollar shared with a person or animal in need, every day given in selfless charity work, is a day worth living.
